### Best Travel Adapter Overall: Ceptics GAN 100W Universal Travel Adapter
### 最佳综合旅行转换插头：Ceptics GAN 100W 通用旅行转换插头

Jet off to more than 200 countries with these compact Ceptics adapters in your luggage, and charge all your gadgets. The classic slider design pushes out EU, UK, and US plugs, and you can rotate the pins for sockets in Australia and China. I love how securely the plugs lock into place; you must press down firmly to slide them back.
带上这款小巧的 Ceptics 转换插头，您可以前往 200 多个国家，并为所有设备充电。经典的滑块设计可以推出欧标、英标和美标插头，您还可以旋转插脚以适配澳大利亚和中国的插座。我非常喜欢它插头锁定的稳固感；您必须用力按下才能将其收回。

You have a universal input socket, an additional side socket for US or Japan plugs, and two USB-C ports and two USB-A ports on the bottom. But what makes this travel adapter my new favorite is the retractable USB-C cable. It’s around 25 inches long, the end sticks to the adapter magnetically, and it saves you from having to pack an extra cord. The cable can deliver up to 100 watts in total.
它配有一个通用输入插孔、一个用于美标或日标插头的侧面插孔，底部还有两个 USB-C 接口和两个 USB-A 接口。这款转换插头成为我新宠的原因在于其可伸缩的 USB-C 数据线。线长约 25 英寸（约 63.5 厘米），末端通过磁吸附在插头上，省去了额外携带数据线的麻烦。该线缆总功率可达 100 瓦。

### 亚军：Tessan GaN 通用旅行转换插头
