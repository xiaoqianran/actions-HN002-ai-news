# 8 Gemini tips for organizing your space (and life)
# 利用 Gemini 整理空间（及生活）的 8 个技巧

Google searches for “spring cleaning hacks” and “spring cleaning checklist” are spiking as people look for the most efficient ways to refresh their spaces. From deep-cleaning to decluttering, here are eight ways Gemini can help you tidy up.

随着人们寻求最高效的方式来焕新居住空间，谷歌上关于“春季大扫除技巧”和“春季大扫除清单”的搜索量激增。从深度清洁到清理杂物，以下是 Gemini 助你整理空间的八种方法。

**1. Generate a personalized cleaning checklist**
Kickstart your cleaning session by asking Gemini to build a customized checklist tailored specifically to your floor plan and lifestyle. Instead of a generic list, ask something like, “How can I organize my studio apartment to feel more open?” or “Create a room-by-room decluttering schedule for a busy family in a two-story home.”

**1. 生成个性化清洁清单**
让 Gemini 根据你的户型和生活方式量身定制一份清单，开启你的清洁工作。不要只使用通用的列表，试着问它：“我该如何整理我的单身公寓，让空间看起来更开阔？”或者“为忙碌的双层住宅家庭制定一份逐个房间的清理计划。”

**2. Audit your clutter**
Sometimes you might be too close to the mess to see the solution. Upload a picture of a cluttered drawer or closet and ask Gemini for spatial suggestions like, “How can I maximize this vertical space?”

**2. 评估杂物**
有时你可能身处混乱之中，反而看不清解决办法。上传一张杂乱的抽屉或衣柜照片，向 Gemini 寻求空间建议，例如：“我该如何最大化利用这个垂直空间？”

**3. Clear your refrigerator**
Reduce food waste and declutter your fridge by panning your camera across your shelves with Gemini Live. Instead of tossing mystery ingredients, simply ask, “What can I make with these leftovers?” Gemini will identify what you have and talk you through recipe ideas, helping you clear space while creating a delicious meal.

**3. 清理冰箱**
使用 Gemini Live 扫描冰箱货架，减少食物浪费并清理空间。与其扔掉那些不知名的食材，不如直接问：“我可以用这些剩菜做些什么？”Gemini 会识别你现有的食材并为你提供食谱建议，帮你腾出空间的同时还能做出一顿美味佳肴。

**4. Troubleshoot home repairs**
Don't let a plumbing problem or broken dishwasher stall your cleaning momentum. Instead of hunting for a lost paper manual or scrolling through endless PDFs, open your camera and ask Gemini Live, “What am I looking at and how do I fix it?” It will help identify the issue and act as your personal repair coach.

**4. 排除家居故障**
别让管道问题或洗碗机故障打断你的清洁进度。与其翻找丢失的纸质说明书或在无数 PDF 文件中滚动查找，不如打开摄像头问 Gemini Live：“我看到的是什么，该怎么修？”它会帮你识别问题，并充当你的私人维修教练。

**5. Streamline your supply run**
Use Ask Maps to simplify your errands and discover new places to go. You can ask, “Where can I drop off donation items on my way to work?” or “Find eco-friendly cleaning supplies on my way home.” Ask Maps will find nearby options along your route, factoring in real-time traffic and the store’s busiest times to help you avoid delays. You’ll also see insider tips like what items stores accept for donation and other products available for purchase.

**5. 简化采购流程**
使用 Ask Maps 来简化你的跑腿任务并发现新去处。你可以问：“上班路上哪里可以捐赠物品？”或者“在回家路上帮我找找环保清洁用品。”Ask Maps 会根据你的路线查找附近的选项，并考虑实时交通状况和商店的繁忙时段，帮你避开拥堵。你还能看到内部提示，例如商店接受哪些捐赠物品以及其他可购买的产品。

**6. Refresh your rooms**
Forget endless paint swatching or waiting weeks to see how a new couch might look in your space. If you want to turn a fresh page with a home makeover, let Nano Banana remove the guesswork. Upload a photo of a room, then ask Nano Banana to paint the walls a new color, place a sofa near the window, add bookshelves or show a brighter rug. Gemini works alongside you to only alter the parts of the photo you want to change and preserve the rest.

**6. 焕新房间布局**
别再纠结于无休止的油漆色卡，也不用等上几周才能看到新沙发摆在家里是什么样。如果你想通过家居改造开启新篇章，让 Nano Banana 帮你消除猜测。上传一张房间照片，然后让 Nano Banana 给墙壁换个颜色、把沙发放在窗边、增加书架或展示一张更亮眼的地毯。Gemini 会与你协作，仅修改你想要调整的部分，并保留照片的其他内容。

**7. Grow brighter, greener plants**
When indoor plants are drooping, identifying the root cause can be tough. Too much light or too little? Too much water or not enough? It can be confusing enough to make you want to get rid of the plant altogether. Instead, use Gemini Live to show your plant and its surrounding environment. Gemini can suggest where to position your leafy roommate for optimal light and teach you how to test for overwatering. You can even revisit the chat a week later to see how things have improved.

**7. 让植物长得更茂盛**
当室内植物枯萎时，找出根本原因可能很困难。是光照太多还是太少？水浇多了还是少了？这些困惑甚至会让你想直接扔掉植物。试着使用 Gemini Live 展示你的植物及其周围环境。Gemini 可以建议你将植物摆放在哪里以获得最佳光照，并教你如何判断是否浇水过多。你甚至可以在一周后回顾对话，看看情况是否有改善。

**8. Declutter your inbox**
You can use Gemini in Gmail to quickly summarize long, messy email threads and to find what matters, or ask Gemini to locate specific details from months ago — like a receipt — without manually digging through thousands of messages. Plus, if you’re an Ultra Subscriber in the U.S., you can opt into AI Inbox, which shows you to-dos and topics that matter most all in one place.

**8. 清理收件箱**
你可以使用 Gmail 中的 Gemini 快速总结冗长杂乱的邮件往来，并找到重要信息；或者让 Gemini 查找几个月前的特定细节（如收据），无需手动翻阅数千封邮件。此外，如果你是美国的 Ultra 订阅用户，还可以选择使用 AI Inbox，它能将你最重要的待办事项和主题集中显示在一个地方。

If your physical space isn't the only thing that needs a reset, Ultra Subscribers in the U.S can also use Agent Mode in Gemini for inbox decluttering. Just ask it to "help organize my inbox," and it will create a plan with smart actions like archiving clutter or creating tasks from reminders that you can approve with a simple click.

如果需要重置的不只是你的物理空间，美国的 Ultra 订阅用户还可以使用 Gemini 中的“代理模式”（Agent Mode）来清理收件箱。只需对它说“帮我整理收件箱”，它就会制定一个包含智能操作的计划，例如归档杂乱邮件或根据提醒创建任务，你只需轻轻一点即可确认执行。
