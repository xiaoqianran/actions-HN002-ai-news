# NovaVoice

NovaVoice is Your Voice OS that lets you work at the speed of thought. Typing is slow. Switching apps breaks focus. Formatting wastes time.
NovaVoice 是你的语音操作系统，让你能够以思维的速度工作。打字太慢，切换应用会打断思路，排版更是浪费时间。

Speak at 200+ wpm, get context-aware text. Hit hotkey, ask anything without googling.
以每分钟 200 字以上的速度说话，即可获得具备上下文感知能力的文本。按下快捷键，无需搜索即可询问任何问题。

Execute actions without switching apps (just with voice commands). NovaVoice remembers contacts, addresses, links.
无需切换应用，仅通过语音指令即可执行操作。NovaVoice 能够记住联系人、地址和链接。

NovaVoice writes, answers, and acts across your desktop.
NovaVoice 可以在你的整个桌面上进行写作、回答问题并执行任务。
