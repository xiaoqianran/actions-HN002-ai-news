Last November, we launched our first 5-Day AI Agents Intensive Course with Kaggle, reaching over 1.5 million learners. By popular demand, we’re bringing it back from June 15-19, 2026 — now with updated content, new speakers and a hands-on capstone project, all at no cost to registrants.
去年 11 月，我们与 Kaggle 共同推出了首期 5 天 AI 智能体强化课程，吸引了超过 150 万名学员。应广大需求，我们决定于 2026 年 6 月 15 日至 19 日再次举办该课程。本次课程更新了内容，邀请了新的演讲嘉宾，并增加了实操结业项目，所有注册学员均可免费参加。

This five-day online course dives deep into building powerful AI agents from foundational concepts to production-ready systems, especially with vibe coding. You’ll explore vibe coding workflows, where natural language becomes the primary programming interface, and learn how to create “10x agents” by integrating tools and APIs.
这门为期五天的在线课程将深入探讨如何构建强大的 AI 智能体，内容涵盖从基础概念到生产级系统的全过程，重点介绍“氛围编程”。你将探索“氛围编程”工作流——即以自然语言作为主要编程接口，并学习如何通过集成工具和 API 来创建“十倍效能智能体”（10x agents）。

Each day combines conceptual deep dives with hands-on examples. By the end, you’ll be ready to design, build and deploy robust agent systems — culminating in a capstone project that brings your ideas to life. You can register for the course on our website.
每天的课程都将概念深度解析与实操案例相结合。课程结束时，你将具备设计、构建和部署稳健智能体系统的能力，并最终通过一个结业项目将你的创意变为现实。你可以在我们的官网上注册报名。
