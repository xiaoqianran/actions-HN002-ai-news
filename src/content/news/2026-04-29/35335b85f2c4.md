# A new way to explore the web with AI Mode in Chrome
# 在 Chrome 中通过 AI 模式探索网络的新方式

Get fast, seamless help while you explore the web with a new AI Mode experience in Chrome — without switching tabs.
通过 Chrome 中全新的 AI 模式体验，您可以在浏览网页时获得快速、无缝的帮助，无需频繁切换标签页。

Finding information online can often feel like a constant game of “tab hopping.” You start searching in one tab, then open another to follow a specific idea or topic. Then you have to switch back to Search to keep your train of thought going.
在网上查找信息往往感觉像是一场永无止境的“标签页跳跃”游戏。您在一个标签页中开始搜索，然后打开另一个标签页来追踪某个特定的想法或主题，接着又必须切回搜索页面以保持思路连贯。

Our new Search experience in Chrome can change that. Today’s upgrades for AI Mode in Chrome make it easier to access and engage with content and dive deeper into what you find — all without losing your place or needing to switch tabs. It’s a more fluid way to discover and learn from the vast range of perspectives across the web.
Chrome 中全新的搜索体验可以改变这一现状。今天针对 Chrome AI 模式的升级，让您能够更轻松地访问和互动内容，并深入挖掘所发现的信息——这一切都无需离开当前页面或切换标签页。这是一种更流畅的方式，让您能够从网络上广泛的观点中发现并学习知识。

Introducing a better way to explore the web: Now, when you're using AI Mode on Chrome desktop, clicking a link opens the webpage side-by-side with AI Mode. This makes it much easier to visit relevant websites, compare details and ask follow-up questions while still maintaining the context of your search.
介绍一种更好的网络探索方式：现在，当您在 Chrome 桌面版上使用 AI 模式时，点击链接会以并排视图打开网页与 AI 模式。这使得访问相关网站、比较细节以及提出后续问题变得更加容易，同时还能保持搜索的上下文语境。

Let’s say you’re shopping for a coffee maker that fits in your cozy apartment and can make lattes. Just head to AI Mode in Search, describe what you’re looking for and you’ll get a range of options that fit your criteria. See a model you like? Open the retailer’s site alongside AI Mode and ask specific questions, like, "How easy is this to clean?" Using context from the page and from across the web, AI Mode will share the insights you’re looking for, helping you make an informed decision with ease.
假设您正在选购一台适合您舒适公寓且能制作拿铁的咖啡机。只需前往搜索中的 AI 模式，描述您的需求，您就会获得一系列符合条件的选项。看到心仪的型号了吗？将零售商网站与 AI 模式并排打开，并提出具体问题，例如：“这台机器容易清洗吗？”AI 模式将利用页面及全网的上下文信息，为您提供所需的见解，帮助您轻松做出明智的决定。

Or, perhaps you want to learn about the different McLaren Racing teams and how their pit crews train. With AI Mode in Chrome, you can easily explore related pages, like McLaren Racing’s official site, without breaking your flow. AI Mode is ready to handle your follow-up questions in real-time, creating a fluid process that helps you better digest each site you visit and uncover the next page to explore.
或者，也许您想了解不同的迈凯伦车队及其维修站工作人员的训练方式。借助 Chrome 中的 AI 模式，您可以轻松探索相关页面（如迈凯伦车队官网），而不会打断您的思路。AI 模式随时准备实时处理您的后续问题，创造一个流畅的过程，帮助您更好地消化所访问的每个网站，并发现下一个值得探索的页面。

Our early testers loved that they didn’t have to constantly switch tabs to get help with a comprehensive article or a long video. And they found that having both Search and the web side-by-side helped them stay focused on their tasks while exploring useful web pages.
我们的早期测试人员非常喜欢这种无需频繁切换标签页即可获取综合文章或长视频帮助的功能。他们发现，将搜索和网页并排显示，有助于他们在探索有用网页的同时保持对任务的专注。

Search across your recent tabs and more: We’re also making it possible to search across the Chrome tabs you’re already exploring. On Chrome desktop or mobile, you can tap the new “plus” menu in the search box on the New Tab page (or the existing plus menu within AI Mode) to select recent tabs and add them to your search. You aren't limited to one type of input, either: you can now mix and match multiple tabs, images or files (like PDFs) and bring that context into your AI Mode searches.
跨标签页搜索及更多功能：我们现在还支持在您已打开的 Chrome 标签页中进行搜索。在 Chrome 桌面版或移动版上，您可以点击“新标签页”搜索框中的新“加号”菜单（或 AI 模式中现有的加号菜单），选择最近的标签页并将它们添加到搜索中。您的输入方式也不再受限：现在您可以混合搭配多个标签页、图像或文件（如 PDF），并将这些上下文引入到您的 AI 模式搜索中。

Maybe you’re researching local hiking trails and have several related sites open already. You can add those tabs to your search and ask for similar kid-friendly trails in a different location. Or, if you’re studying for a statistics midterm, you can bring in context from open tabs with your class notes, lecture slides and academic papers and ask for more examples to illustrate a tricky concept. AI Mode will use those tabs to provide a tailored response and suggest more sites to explore.
也许您正在研究当地的徒步路线，并且已经打开了几个相关网站。您可以将这些标签页添加到搜索中，并询问其他地区类似的适合儿童的路线。或者，如果您正在为统计学期中考试复习，您可以引入包含课堂笔记、讲座幻灯片和学术论文的打开标签页作为上下文，并要求提供更多示例来阐述一个复杂的概念。AI 模式将利用这些标签页提供定制化的回答，并建议更多值得探索的网站。

Finally, powerful tools in AI Mode, like Canvas or image creation, are accessible wherever you see the new plus menu in Chrome. All of today’s updates are now available in the U.S., and we’ll expand soon to more places around the world.
最后，AI 模式中的强大工具（如 Canvas 或图像创作）在您看到 Chrome 中新加号菜单的任何地方都可以使用。今天发布的所有更新现已在美国推出，我们很快将扩展到全球更多地区。
