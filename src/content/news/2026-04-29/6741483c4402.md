# 3 new ways Ads Advisor is making Google Ads safer and faster
# Ads Advisor 通过三种新方式让 Google Ads 更安全、更快捷

We’re introducing three agentic safety features in Ads Advisor to troubleshoot violations, protect your account and manage certificates.
我们正在 Ads Advisor 中引入三项代理安全功能，用于排查违规问题、保护您的账户并管理证书。

Over the past few months, marketers have been using Ads Advisor, our AI agent in Google Ads, to spark creative ideas, unlock new insights, drive stronger performance and so much more. We're rolling out three new agentic safety features so you can spend less time managing campaigns and more time growing your business.
在过去的几个月里，营销人员一直在使用我们的 Google Ads AI 代理——Ads Advisor，来激发创意、获取新洞察、提升广告表现等。我们现推出三项全新的代理安全功能，旨在让您减少管理广告系列的时间，将更多精力投入到业务增长中。

**Proactive troubleshooting:** Flags complex policy violations without prompting and offers personalized guidance to resolve your issue.
**主动排查故障：** 无需提示即可标记复杂的政策违规问题，并提供个性化指导以解决您的问题。

**24/7 security monitoring:** Strengthens your account’s security using a personalized dashboard and suggestions.
**全天候安全监控：** 通过个性化仪表板和建议来加强您的账户安全。

**Instant certifications:** Transforms weeks of manual paperwork into instant approvals, built with Gemini capabilities.
**即时认证：** 基于 Gemini 能力，将数周的手动文书工作转化为即时审批。

### 1. Proactive troubleshooting to keep campaigns running
### 1. 主动排查故障，确保广告系列持续运行

We are introducing Real-Time Policy Reviews to provide instant, in-workflow guidance as you create and edit campaigns. But we know issues can arise at any time, which is why we’re evolving Ads Advisor to act as an end-to-end policy troubleshooter that fixes even the most complex violations. When issues arise, Ads Advisor will proactively scan your account and website to identify the problem, give you a clear path to resolution and then confirm the fix is made before you appeal.
我们正在引入“实时政策审查”功能，在您创建和编辑广告系列时提供即时的流程内指导。但我们深知问题随时可能发生，因此我们正在升级 Ads Advisor，使其成为端到端的政策故障排查工具，即使是最复杂的违规问题也能修复。当问题出现时，Ads Advisor 会主动扫描您的账户和网站以识别问题，为您提供明确的解决方案，并在您申诉前确认修复已完成。

### 2. 24/7 monitoring to protect your account
### 2. 全天候监控以保护您的账户

A key best practice for maintaining account security is auditing each user in your Google Ads account. This can be a cumbersome and manual process that can take hours depending on the size of your team. To help you move faster and improve security, Ads Advisor will soon monitor your account around the clock and share personalized recommendations, like reviewing flagged domains and dormant users. We’re also introducing a new security insights dashboard to show you how adopting these suggestions bolsters your account protection. For a snapshot of your current status, you can prompt Ads Advisor with “show me my security level.” We're also bringing passkeys to Google Ads to give you additional protection for your account — removing the hassle of passwords and codes while improving security.
维护账户安全的一个关键最佳实践是审核 Google Ads 账户中的每一位用户。根据团队规模的不同，这可能是一个繁琐且耗时的手动过程。为了帮助您提高效率并增强安全性，Ads Advisor 即将实现全天候账户监控，并分享个性化建议，例如审查被标记的域名和休眠用户。我们还引入了一个新的安全洞察仪表板，向您展示采纳这些建议如何加强您的账户保护。如需查看当前状态的快照，您可以向 Ads Advisor 发出指令：“show me my security level”（显示我的安全等级）。我们还将把通行密钥（passkeys）引入 Google Ads，为您的账户提供额外保护——在提高安全性的同时，免去输入密码和验证码的麻烦。

### 3. Instant certifications to eliminate paperwork
### 3. 即时认证，告别繁琐文书

While certifications are vital to maintaining a trusted advertising environment, applying for them has historically been a manual process. Ads Advisor will soon turn weeks of paperwork into instant approvals by proactively determining if your business needs a certificate based on your industry and country. Then it will either grant it automatically or, if more details are needed, help you submit your application in one click. As always, Ads Advisor will ask for approval before taking action in your account.
虽然认证对于维护可信的广告环境至关重要，但申请过程历来都是手动的。Ads Advisor 即将把数周的文书工作转化为即时审批，它会根据您的行业和国家主动判断您的业务是否需要证书。随后，它将自动授予认证，或者在需要更多详细信息时，帮助您一键提交申请。一如既往，Ads Advisor 在对您的账户采取任何行动之前，都会先征求您的批准。

We're constantly working to make our advertising platforms safer and more efficient. By harnessing the full potential of agentic AI, Ads Advisor provides the foundation for businesses to innovate faster and more securely. And this is just the beginning.
我们一直在努力使我们的广告平台更安全、更高效。通过充分发挥代理 AI 的潜力，Ads Advisor 为企业更快、更安全地创新奠定了基础。而这仅仅是个开始。

*Note: These new capabilities will launch in Ads Advisor in the coming months. Ads Advisor is available to all English accounts globally. More languages are rolling out soon.
*注：这些新功能将在未来几个月内于 Ads Advisor 中推出。Ads Advisor 目前面向全球所有英语账户开放。更多语言版本即将推出。
