### We're launching two specialized TPUs for the agentic era.
### 我们正在为智能体时代推出两款专用 TPU。

We're introducing two TPU chips to meet increasingly demanding AI workloads, including autonomous AI agents that work on your behalf to get things done.
我们正在推出两款 TPU 芯片，以满足日益严苛的 AI 工作负载需求，其中包括能够代表您执行任务的自主 AI 智能体。

AI agents need to reason, plan and execute multi-step workflows. TPU 8i is designed specifically to enable AI agents to complete this very quickly to provide a good user experience.
AI 智能体需要进行推理、规划并执行多步骤的工作流程。TPU 8i 专为助力 AI 智能体快速完成这些任务而设计，旨在提供出色的用户体验。

Complementing TPU 8i, TPU 8t is optimized for training and can run even the most complex models on a single, massive pool of memory.
作为 TPU 8i 的补充，TPU 8t 针对训练进行了优化，即使是最复杂的模型，也能在单一的超大内存池上运行。

Along with our full-stack purpose-built infrastructure — from networking to data centers and energy-efficient operations — they create the underlying engine that will allow us to bring highly responsive agentic AI to the masses.
结合我们全栈式的专用基础设施（从网络到数据中心，再到节能运营），它们共同构成了底层引擎，使我们能够将响应迅速的智能体 AI 带给大众。

To find out more, learn about how our TPUs are redefining what’s possible with AI.
欲了解更多信息，请深入了解我们的 TPU 如何重新定义 AI 的无限可能。
