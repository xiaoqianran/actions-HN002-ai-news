# 7 ways to travel smarter this summer, with help from Google
# 借助 Google，今年夏天让旅行更智能的 7 种方法

The latest tools from Google can help you plan trips, find a great deal and explore your next destination.
Google 的最新工具可以帮助您规划行程、寻找超值优惠并探索您的下一个目的地。

Summer travel season is right around the corner, and no matter where you’re headed, Google is ready to help each step of the way. Here are some of our latest tools to help you to plan memorable trips, find a great deal and dive into your next destination.
夏季旅游旺季即将来临，无论您要去往何处，Google 都已准备好在每一步为您提供帮助。以下是我们的一些最新工具，旨在帮助您规划难忘的旅程、寻找超值优惠并深入探索您的下一个目的地。

### 1. Build a custom trip plan with AI Mode in Search
### 1. 使用搜索中的 AI 模式构建自定义行程计划

AI Mode in Search can transform your scattered research into a cohesive travel plan. Just head to AI Mode, select the Canvas tool from the plus (+) menu and describe your ideal trip. AI Mode will craft a custom itinerary in the Canvas side panel, including options for flights and hotels, as well as local attractions laid out on a map. Looking for a different vibe or want to tweak the logistics? Just ask a follow-up question to refine the plan until it has exactly what you need. If you want a break from planning, AI Mode saves your progress automatically — just open your AI Mode history to pick up where you left off. Travel planning with Canvas in AI Mode is available for everyone in the U.S.
搜索中的 AI 模式可以将您零散的搜索结果转化为连贯的旅行计划。只需进入 AI 模式，从加号 (+) 菜单中选择 Canvas 工具，并描述您理想的旅行。AI 模式将在 Canvas 侧边栏中为您制作一份自定义行程，包括航班和酒店选项，以及标注在地图上的当地景点。想要换种风格或调整行程安排？只需提出后续问题，即可不断优化计划，直到它完全符合您的需求。如果您想休息一下，AI 模式会自动保存您的进度——只需打开您的 AI 模式历史记录，即可从上次中断的地方继续。目前，美国的所有用户均可使用 AI 模式下的 Canvas 进行旅行规划。

### 2. Save money with hotel price tracking on Search
### 2. 通过搜索中的酒店价格追踪功能节省开支

You can already track hotel prices at the city level, and launching today, you can now track prices for individual hotels, too. To get started on desktop, head to Search and look up a specific hotel by name, then tap the new price tracking toggle. On mobile, you’ll find the price tracking option under the Prices tab after you search. Either way, you’ll get an email alert if rates change significantly during your chosen dates, so you can jump on those price drops and snag a great deal. You can also track individual hotel prices through google.com/hotels. Select one of the listings and look for the price tracking toggle on the Overview or Prices tab. Price tracking for individual hotels is now available globally for signed-in users in English and Spanish.
您此前已经可以追踪城市级别的酒店价格，而从今天起，您也可以追踪特定酒店的价格了。在电脑端，只需前往搜索并按名称查找特定酒店，然后点击新的价格追踪开关即可。在移动端，搜索后您可以在“价格”选项卡下找到价格追踪选项。无论哪种方式，如果所选日期的价格发生显著变化，您都会收到电子邮件提醒，以便您及时抓住降价机会，获得超值优惠。您也可以通过 google.com/hotels 追踪特定酒店的价格。选择其中一个列表，并在“概览”或“价格”选项卡中查找价格追踪开关。目前，全球已登录的英语和西班牙语用户均可使用特定酒店的价格追踪功能。

### 3. Let Google take the hassle out of booking restaurants
### 3. 让 Google 帮您轻松预订餐厅

Trying the local cuisine is always a vacation highlight, but finding a table at the right restaurant can feel like a Herculean challenge. With agentic capabilities in AI Mode and Ask Maps you can focus on the food instead. All you have to do is describe your needs and preferences, like a table for five next Saturday at a place with Cuban food and live music. No matter what tool you’re using, we’ll search across multiple reservation platforms and websites to find real-time availability for options that meet your criteria. Then, you’ll see a curated list to choose from, with direct links to finalize your booking through partners like OpenTable and Resy. Agentic booking for restaurants is available on AI Mode in the U.S. and recently expanded to several more countries, including the U.K., India, Canada and Australia. It’s also available in Ask Maps, which is live in the U.S. and India.
品尝当地美食始终是度假的一大亮点，但在心仪的餐厅找到空位有时却难如登天。借助 AI 模式和 Ask Maps 的智能代理功能，您可以将精力集中在美食本身。您只需描述您的需求和偏好，例如“下周六预订一张五人桌，要求提供古巴菜并有现场音乐表演”。无论您使用哪种工具，我们都会在多个预订平台和网站上进行搜索，为您找到符合条件的实时空位。随后，您将看到一份精选列表，并可通过 OpenTable 和 Resy 等合作伙伴的直接链接完成预订。目前，美国的 AI 模式已支持餐厅智能预订，并已扩展至英国、印度、加拿大和澳大利亚等多个国家。该功能也已在 Ask Maps 中上线，目前支持美国和印度。

### 4. Ask Google to call nearby stores for last-minute shopping
### 4. 让 Google 代您致电附近商店，解决临时购物需求

Think of something you need for your trip, but don’t have time to order it online? Google can call local stores on your behalf to see who carries the specific thing you’re looking for, including relevant deals. This tool launched directly on Search last November, and it’s rolling out in the coming weeks on AI Mode in the U.S. Just describe what you need "near me,” like “I forgot to pack my prescription sunglasses, so I'm trying to find a pair of clip-on polarized ones that fit over my current glasses. Where can I get some nearby?” Our Gemini models and Duplex technology will make the calls and send you the details shortly after — giving you one less thing to worry about before takeoff.
想到了旅行中需要的东西，却没时间网购？Google 可以代您致电当地商店，查看谁有您需要的特定商品，包括相关的优惠信息。该工具于去年 11 月在搜索中直接推出，并将在未来几周内于美国的 AI 模式中上线。只需描述您“附近”的需求，例如：“我忘了带处方太阳镜，所以想找一副能套在现有眼镜上的偏光夹片。附近哪里可以买到？”我们的 Gemini 模型和 Duplex 技术将为您拨打电话，并在不久后将详情发送给您——让您在起飞前少一份操心。

### 5. Translate and communicate with confidence
### 5. 自信地翻译与沟通

With the Google Translate app and our latest update for live translation, your favorite pair of headphones are now your personal pocket translator for 70+ languages. Whether you’re navigating a bustling market or catching a local train announcement, Translate makes it easy to connect with the world around you by providing real-time translations directly through any pair of headphones. Built with Gemini’s advanced speech models, this feature doesn't just translate words. It also preserves the speaker’s original tone and cadence, helping you stay present in the moment throughout your trip. Live translate is available on both Android and iOS.
借助 Google 翻译应用和我们最新的实时翻译更新，您最喜欢的耳机现在就是您的个人袖珍翻译官，支持 70 多种语言。无论是在熙熙攘攘的市场中穿行，还是收听当地的火车广播，翻译功能都能通过任何耳机直接提供实时翻译，让您轻松与周围的世界沟通。该功能基于 Gemini 先进的语音模型构建，不仅能翻译词汇，还能保留说话者的原始语调和节奏，帮助您在旅途中全身心投入当下。实时翻译功能现已在 Android 和 iOS 上线。

### 6. Ask Maps for the best stops on your summer trips
### 6. 向 Ask Maps 咨询夏季旅行的最佳停靠点

Summertime exploration is easier than ever with Ask Maps, a new conversational experience powered by Gemini models that gives you answers to questions a map could never answer before. Say you want to go camping over the Fourth of July, but still want access to amenities. Tap on Ask Maps and say, “I’m looking for campsites with hiking, wildlife sightings and sunset photography, without compromising on access to clean bathrooms and food." Ask Maps analyzes Maps’ fresh information to give you all of the details you need, including a list of campsites and the amenities they have on site, like hot showers, parking, EV chargers and bathrooms. You’ll see insider tips from our community of 500 million contributors — letting you know about iconic hiking trails and the best spots to see sunsets and wildlife — details that would have taken hours of research to find. Ask Maps results are personalized, and it’s easy to turn your plans into action. Just add places to a list, make a reservation or navigate to your destination with a quick tap. Ask Maps is available to everyone in the US and India on Android, iOS and desktop.
有了 Ask Maps，夏季探索变得前所未有的简单。这是一种由 Gemini 模型驱动的全新对话体验，能为您解答地图以前无法回答的问题。假设您想在 7 月 4 日假期去露营，但又希望设施齐全。点击 Ask Maps 并说：“我想找一些可以徒步、观赏野生动物和拍摄日落的露营地，同时还要有干净的卫生间和食物。”Ask Maps 会分析地图的最新信息，为您提供所需的所有细节，包括露营地列表及其现场设施，如热水淋浴、停车位、电动汽车充电桩和卫生间。您还可以看到来自我们 5 亿贡献者社区的内部建议，了解标志性的徒步路线以及观赏日落和野生动物的最佳地点——这些细节以往可能需要花费数小时的研究才能找到。Ask Maps 的结果是个性化的，并且可以轻松将计划付诸行动。只需轻点一下，即可将地点添加到列表、进行预订或导航至目的地。Ask Maps 现已面向美国和印度的所有 Android、iOS 和桌面端用户开放。
