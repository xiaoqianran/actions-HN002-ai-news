# An update on GitHub availability
# 关于 GitHub 可用性的更新

Vladimir Fedorov is GitHub's Chief Technology Officer, bringing decades of experience in engineering leadership and innovation. A passionate advocate for developer productivity, Vlad is leading GitHub’s engineering team to shape the future of developer tools and innovation with a developer-first mindset.
Vladimir Fedorov 是 GitHub 的首席技术官，拥有数十年的工程领导力和创新经验。作为开发者生产力的热忱倡导者，Vlad 正带领 GitHub 工程团队，以“开发者优先”的思维模式塑造开发者工具和创新的未来。

Before joining GitHub, Vlad co-founded UserClouds, a startup specializing in data governance and privacy. He spent 12 years at Facebook, now Meta, as Senior Vice President, leading engineering teams of over 2,000 across Privacy, Ads, and Platform. Earlier in his career, Vlad worked at Microsoft and earned both his BS and MS in Computer Science from Caltech.
在加入 GitHub 之前，Vlad 联合创办了专注于数据治理和隐私的初创公司 UserClouds。他曾在 Facebook（现 Meta）工作了 12 年，担任高级副总裁，领导过超过 2,000 人的工程团队，涵盖隐私、广告和平台业务。在职业生涯早期，Vlad 曾在微软工作，并获得了加州理工学院（Caltech）计算机科学学士和硕士学位。

He currently serves on the board of Codepath.org, an organization dedicated to reprogramming higher education to create the first AI-native generation of engineers, CTOs, and founders. Vlad lives in the Bay Area and when not working enjoys spending time outside and on the water with his family.
他目前担任 Codepath.org 的董事会成员，该组织致力于重塑高等教育，旨在培养第一代 AI 原生工程师、首席技术官和创始人。Vlad 现居湾区，工作之余，他喜欢与家人一起进行户外活动或水上运动。
