# The next phase of the Microsoft OpenAI partnership
# 微软与 OpenAI 合作伙伴关系的下一阶段

The rapid pace of innovation requires us to continue to evolve our partnership to benefit our customers and both companies. Today, we are announcing an amended agreement to simplify our partnership and the way we work together, grounded in flexibility, certainty, and a focus on delivering the benefits of AI broadly.
创新的飞速发展要求我们不断演进合作伙伴关系，以造福我们的客户及双方公司。今天，我们宣布达成一项修订协议，旨在简化我们的合作关系及协作方式，其核心在于灵活性、确定性，并专注于广泛地交付人工智能带来的益处。

The greater predictability in the amended agreement strengthens our joint ability to build and operate AI platforms at scale while providing both companies the flexibility to pursue new opportunities. The agreement spells out:
修订协议中更高的可预测性增强了我们共同构建和运营大规模人工智能平台的能力，同时也为双方公司追求新机遇提供了灵活性。协议明确规定：

Microsoft remains OpenAI’s primary cloud partner, and OpenAI products will ship first on Azure, unless Microsoft cannot and chooses not to support the necessary capabilities. OpenAI can now serve all its products to customers across any cloud provider.
微软仍是 OpenAI 的主要云合作伙伴，OpenAI 的产品将优先在 Azure 上发布，除非微软无法或选择不支持所需的功能。OpenAI 现在可以向任何云服务提供商的客户提供其所有产品。

Microsoft will continue to have a license to OpenAI IP for models and products through 2032. Microsoft’s license will now be non-exclusive. Microsoft will no longer pay a revenue share to OpenAI.
微软将继续拥有 OpenAI 模型和产品的知识产权许可，直至 2032 年。微软的许可现已转为非排他性。微软将不再向 OpenAI 支付收入分成。

Revenue share payments from OpenAI to Microsoft continue through 2030, independent of OpenAI’s technology progress, at the same percentage but subject to a total cap. Microsoft continues to participate directly in OpenAI’s growth as a major shareholder.
OpenAI 向微软支付的收入分成将持续至 2030 年，该支付与 OpenAI 的技术进展无关，比例保持不变，但设有总额上限。作为主要股东，微软将继续直接参与 OpenAI 的成长。

While this amendment simplifies the partnership, the work we’re doing together remains ambitious. From scaling gigawatts of new datacenter capacity, to collaborating on next-generation silicon, to applying AI to advance cybersecurity, and more, we’re excited to keep partnering to advance and scale AI for people and organizations around the world.
虽然此次修订简化了合作关系，但我们共同开展的工作依然雄心勃勃。从扩展吉瓦级的新数据中心容量，到在下一代芯片上进行合作，再到应用人工智能提升网络安全等等，我们很高兴能继续携手合作，为全球的人们和组织推进并扩展人工智能的应用。
