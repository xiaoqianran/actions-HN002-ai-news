# Google Search queries hit an ‘all time high’ last quarter
# 谷歌搜索查询量在上季度创下“历史新高”

Google Search queries hit an “all time high” in the first quarter of 2026, according to a statement from CEO Sundar Pichai published as part of Alphabet’s earnings on Wednesday.
根据首席执行官桑达尔·皮查伊（Sundar Pichai）周三在 Alphabet 财报中发布的声明，2026 年第一季度谷歌搜索查询量创下了“历史新高”。

“Our AI investments and full stack approach are lighting up every part of the business,” Pichai says. “Search had a strong quarter with AI experiences driving usage, queries at an all time high, and 19% revenue growth.” He also notes that Q1 was “our strongest quarter ever for our consumer AI plans, driven by the Gemini App” and that the company now has more than 350 million paid subscriptions, with “YouTube and Google One being the key drivers.”
皮查伊表示：“我们在人工智能领域的投资和全栈式布局正在点亮业务的每一个环节。搜索业务表现强劲，人工智能体验推动了使用率的提升，查询量达到历史最高水平，营收增长了 19%。”他还指出，得益于 Gemini 应用的推动，第一季度是“我们消费者 AI 计划表现最强劲的一个季度”，目前公司拥有超过 3.5 亿个付费订阅用户，其中“YouTube 和 Google One 是主要驱动力”。

Alphabet also announced consolidated revenues of $109.9 billion, up 22 percent year-over-year from revenues of $90.2 billion. Google Services revenues overall were up 16 percent to $89.6 billion, including a 19 percent increase in Google subscriptions, platforms, and devices.
Alphabet 还宣布合并营收为 1099 亿美元，较去年同期的 902 亿美元增长了 22%。谷歌服务业务整体营收增长 16%，达到 896 亿美元，其中包括谷歌订阅、平台和设备业务 19% 的增长。

Wednesday’s earnings follow a busy start to the year for Google. The company launched many AI features, including Personal Intelligence for Gemini, task automation using Gemini on the Samsung S26 and Pixel 10 series phones, an “auto-browse” feature that lets Chrome perform multi-step tasks on your behalf, and an AI Mode for Gmail that infuses a whole lot of AI into your inbox. It launched the Pixel 10A, which we found to be a minimal update. Google also announced huge Google Play changes following Epic Games’ litigation and appealed the ruling that found the company to be an illegal online search monopolist.
周三发布的财报反映了谷歌今年繁忙的开局。该公司推出了多项人工智能功能，包括 Gemini 的个人智能（Personal Intelligence）、在三星 S26 和 Pixel 10 系列手机上使用 Gemini 进行任务自动化、让 Chrome 代替你执行多步任务的“自动浏览”功能，以及为 Gmail 推出的 AI 模式，将大量人工智能融入收件箱。此外，公司还发布了 Pixel 10A，但我们认为其升级幅度较小。在 Epic Games 诉讼案之后，谷歌还宣布了 Google Play 的重大变革，并对认定其为非法在线搜索垄断者的裁决提起了上诉。

Alphabet’s earnings beat revenue expectations, CNBC reports. Google Cloud also had major growth, with revenues increasing 63 percent year-over-year to $20 billion.
据 CNBC 报道，Alphabet 的财报营收超出了预期。谷歌云业务也实现了大幅增长，营收同比增长 63%，达到 200 亿美元。

Alphabet is set to host a call to discuss its Q1 2026 earnings at 4:30PM ET.
Alphabet 将于美东时间下午 4:30 召开电话会议，讨论其 2026 年第一季度财报。
