# Microsoft says it has over 20M paid Copilot users, and they really are using it
# 微软称其付费 Copilot 用户已超 2000 万，且活跃度极高

Despite the lingering perception that no one really uses Copilot, Microsoft said Wednesday that its user base and engagement are growing for the AI tool that’s baked into M365 apps like Word, Excel, and Outlook email.
尽管外界仍普遍认为没人真正使用 Copilot，但微软周三表示，这款集成在 Word、Excel 和 Outlook 等 M365 应用中的 AI 工具，其用户群和活跃度正在不断增长。

M365 Copilot now has 20 million paid enterprise Copilot seats, Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella said during the company’s quarterly earnings conference call. The company has quadrupled the number of companies paying for over 50,000 seats, Nadella said, noting that Bayer, Johnson & Johnson, Mercedes, and Roche have more than 90,000 seats. He pointed to the deal announced earlier this week with Accenture for over 740,000 seats. “Our largest Copilot win to date,” he said.
微软首席执行官萨提亚·纳德拉（Satya Nadella）在公司季度财报电话会议上表示，M365 Copilot 目前拥有 2000 万个付费企业席位。纳德拉指出，购买超过 5 万个席位的企业数量翻了两番，其中拜耳（Bayer）、强生（Johnson & Johnson）、梅赛德斯（Mercedes）和罗氏（Roche）的席位均超过 9 万个。他还提到了本周早些时候与埃森哲（Accenture）达成的超过 74 万个席位的协议，并称这是“迄今为止我们赢得的最大一笔 Copilot 订单”。

Plus, he insists that people are using it, engaging with Copilot as much as they do with email. “Copilot queries per user were up nearly 20% quarter over quarter. To put this momentum in perspective, weekly engagement is now at the same level as Outlook,” he said. “This is like a daily habit of intense usage.”
此外，他坚称人们确实在使用它，且对 Copilot 的使用频率与使用电子邮件相当。他说：“每位用户的 Copilot 查询量环比增长了近 20%。为了说明这种增长势头，目前的周活跃度已经与 Outlook 持平。这就像是一种高强度的日常使用习惯。”

He emphasized that Copilot is not dependent on any one model, like OpenAI. “You now have access in chat to multiple models by default, with intelligent auto routing in agents with critique and counsel, you can use multiple models together to generate optimal responses,” he said.
他强调，Copilot 并不依赖于 OpenAI 等单一模型。他说：“你现在可以在聊天中默认访问多个模型，通过智能代理自动路由功能进行评估和建议，你可以结合使用多个模型来生成最优响应。”

Microsoft 365 supports Anthropic’s Claude, for instance. In fact, Morgan Stanley’s Keith Weiss said on the quarterly earnings call on Wednesday, “Those Microsoft 365 Copilot numbers are super impressive and I think way ahead of most people’s expectations.”
例如，Microsoft 365 现已支持 Anthropic 的 Claude 模型。事实上，摩根士丹利的基思·韦斯（Keith Weiss）在周三的季度财报电话会议上表示：“这些 Microsoft 365 Copilot 的数据令人印象深刻，我认为这远超大多数人的预期。”

Agent mode is one area that is driving usage, noting that “as of last week, Agent mode is now the default experience across Copilot and Word Excel and PowerPoint.” Microsoft last week made its Copilot’s agentic capabilities generally available. This allows Copilot to take multi-step actions directly in the documents. “You now have a new way to delegate and complete work using Copilot,” Nadella said.
“代理模式”（Agent mode）是推动使用率增长的领域之一，纳德拉指出：“截至上周，代理模式已成为 Copilot 以及 Word、Excel 和 PowerPoint 中的默认体验。”微软上周正式向公众开放了 Copilot 的代理功能，这使得 Copilot 能够直接在文档中执行多步骤操作。纳德拉表示：“现在，你拥有了一种使用 Copilot 委派并完成工作的新方式。”
