# Splatoon Raiders preorders for the Switch 2 are nearly 20 percent off
# 《喷射战士：突袭者》Switch 2 预购优惠近 20%

Some may prefer the convenience of digital downloads, but physical versions are better in one big way: resale value.
有些人可能更喜欢数字版下载的便利性，但实体版在一点上具有显著优势：转售价值。

Nintendo recently announced a new pricing policy, which knocks $10 off the cost of digital versions of future first-party titles exclusive to the Nintendo Switch 2. Splatoon Raiders, for instance, is available for preorder ahead of its July 23rd release for $49.99 digitally or $59.99 for the physical edition.
任天堂最近宣布了一项新的定价政策，将未来 Switch 2 独占的第一方游戏数字版价格下调了 10 美元。以《喷射战士：突袭者》（Splatoon Raiders）为例，该游戏将于 7 月 23 日发售，目前数字版预购价为 49.99 美元，实体版为 59.99 美元。

However, Walmart is the exception in how it’s pricing preorders for the cartridge version. Right now, you can reserve the upcoming game for $49.94 — just a few cents cheaper than the digital version. Raiders isn’t a sequel to Splatoon 3, but rather a spinoff that has similar third-person, paint-splatting gameplay set in new locales.
然而，沃尔玛在实体卡带版的预购定价上采取了不同的策略。目前，你可以以 49.94 美元的价格预订这款即将推出的游戏，这比数字版还要便宜几美分。《突袭者》并非《喷射战士 3》的续作，而是一款衍生作品，它保留了类似的第三人称喷漆玩法，但背景设定在全新的场景中。

The same $10 discount is in effect for Yoshi and the Mysterious Book, a side-scrolling adventure with a gorgeous storybook art style that comes out on May 21st. The price of the physical version is $69.99, but Walmart is currently offering it for $59.88, which is similar to the digital edition’s price.
同样的 10 美元折扣也适用于将于 5 月 21 日发售的《耀西与神秘之书》（Yoshi and the Mysterious Book），这是一款拥有华丽绘本艺术风格的横版卷轴冒险游戏。其实体版定价为 69.99 美元，但沃尔玛目前提供的价格为 59.88 美元，与数字版价格相近。
