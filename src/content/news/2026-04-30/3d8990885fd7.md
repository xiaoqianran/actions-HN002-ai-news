# Motorola’s New Razr Folding Phones Command a Higher Price With Few Upgrades
# 摩托罗拉新款 Razr 折叠屏手机价格上涨，但升级寥寥

Like clockwork, Motorola is back with a new set of Razr folding flip phones. The formula is the same as last year, with three phones differing in specs and price: the Razr Ultra, Razr+, and Razr. But alongside these models, Motorola is finally launching its first-ever book-style folding phone, the Razr Fold, which it first teased at CES 2026.
摩托罗拉如期推出了新一代 Razr 折叠屏手机。其产品策略与去年如出一辙，依然是三款配置和价格各异的机型：Razr Ultra、Razr+ 和 Razr。此外，摩托罗拉终于发布了其首款书本式折叠屏手机 Razr Fold，该机型此前曾在 2026 年国际消费电子展（CES 2026）上预热。

The company announced the new handsets at an event in Los Angeles, where it also revealed a new pair of Moto Buds 2 Plus wireless earbuds that look eerily like Apple's AirPods Pro, but in blue; these will retail for $150 and will be available on April 30. Sadly, all of Motorola's folding flips are getting price increases, in line with what we're seeing from competitors like Samsung. The Razr Ultra is $1,500, the Razr+ costs $1,100, and the Razr starts at $800—that's a $200, $100, and $100 price bump over their predecessors, respectively. The new Razr Fold costs $1,900, landing between the Google Pixel 10 Pro Fold and Samsung Galaxy Z Fold7.
摩托罗拉在洛杉矶的发布会上公布了这些新机，同时还推出了一款 Moto Buds 2 Plus 无线耳机。这款耳机外观酷似苹果的 AirPods Pro，但采用了蓝色配色，售价 150 美元，将于 4 月 30 日上市。遗憾的是，摩托罗拉所有的折叠屏翻盖手机均迎来涨价，这与三星等竞争对手的趋势一致。Razr Ultra 售价 1,500 美元，Razr+ 售价 1,100 美元，Razr 起售价为 800 美元——分别比前代产品上涨了 200 美元、100 美元和 100 美元。新款 Razr Fold 售价 1,900 美元，定位介于 Google Pixel 10 Pro Fold 和三星 Galaxy Z Fold7 之间。

Preorders for all of the phones start on May 14, and they go on sale on May 21. Here's everything you need to know.
所有机型将于 5 月 14 日开启预订，并于 5 月 21 日正式发售。以下是您需要了解的所有信息。

### Flippy Razrs
### 翻盖式 Razr 系列

These new 2026 Razr flip phones see very minor spec bumps and no dramatic changes to hardware or design. The Razr Ultra's inner display is now a bit brighter, hitting a peak brightness of 5,000 nits. Motorola is also touting a new “Extreme AMOLED” display panel for these Razrs, though it seems largely a marketing term. Barring improved screen brightness on the Ultra, these phones still feature 10-bit screens with HDR10+ and Dolby Vision support and hit 100 percent of the DCI-P3 color gamut like their predecessors.
这些 2026 款 Razr 翻盖手机的规格提升非常有限，硬件或设计上没有重大变化。Razr Ultra 的内屏亮度有所提升，峰值亮度达到 5,000 尼特。摩托罗拉还为这些手机宣传了全新的“Extreme AMOLED”显示面板，但这似乎更多是一个营销术语。除了 Ultra 机型屏幕亮度的提升外，这些手机依然配备支持 HDR10+ 和杜比视界的 10-bit 屏幕，并像前代产品一样覆盖 100% 的 DCI-P3 色域。

There is one durability improvement with the Razr Ultra: It's the first phone to use Corning's Gorilla Glass Ceramic 3 on the outer display, which Motorola claims delivers over “75 percent better drop performance” over prior devices. The Razr+ and Razr have Gorilla Glass Victus on those outer screens, same as last year. The phones still have only an IP48 water-resistance rating, so they're not as dustproof as standard smartphones. Motorola says the base Razr is the only folding phone with a MIL-STD 810H rating, meaning it was tested to meet a specific set of durability standards, such as high altitudes and extreme temperatures.
Razr Ultra 在耐用性方面有一项改进：它是首款在外屏使用康宁大猩猩玻璃陶瓷 3（Gorilla Glass Ceramic 3）的手机，摩托罗拉声称其抗跌落性能比以往设备提升了 75% 以上。Razr+ 和 Razr 的外屏则与去年一样，采用大猩猩玻璃 Victus。这些手机的防水等级仍仅为 IP48，因此防尘能力不如普通智能手机。摩托罗拉表示，基础版 Razr 是唯一获得 MIL-STD 810H 军规认证的折叠屏手机，这意味着它通过了高海拔和极端温度等特定耐用性标准的测试。

Motorola isn't shy to play around with textures and colors, though this year's crop of Razrs doesn't seem to have the same breadth of options as the 2025 models. The Razr Ultra has an embossed Alcantara texture in Orient Blue, or you can grab it in Cocoa with a natural wood veneer. The Razr+ has a simple woven-inspired jacquard finish, and the base Razr adds to that with leather-inspired and acetate finishes.
摩托罗拉在材质和配色上向来大胆，不过今年的 Razr 系列似乎没有 2025 款那样丰富的选择。Razr Ultra 提供东方蓝色的压纹 Alcantara 材质，或者可可色天然木纹饰面。Razr+ 采用简约的编织风格提花饰面，而基础版 Razr 则增加了仿皮和醋酸纤维饰面。

Despite price bumps, the Razr Ultra and Razr+ have the same Qualcomm Snapdragon processor and RAM as their predecessors (Snapdragon 8 Elite for the Ultra and Snapdragon 8s Gen 3 for the Razr+). This is a playbook we've seen with some cheaper phones from Motorola and even Google's Pixel 10a this year, allowing them to stay the same price in this challenging economic environment without having to remove features. It feels especially disappointing here with the new Razrs, since they still cost more.
尽管价格上涨，但 Razr Ultra 和 Razr+ 使用的处理器和内存与前代产品相同（Ultra 为骁龙 8 Elite，Razr+ 为骁龙 8s Gen 3）。这是我们今年在摩托罗拉部分廉价机型甚至 Google Pixel 10a 上看到的策略，旨在在充满挑战的经济环境下维持价格不变，同时无需削减功能。但对于新款 Razr 来说，这让人感到格外失望，因为它们的价格反而更高了。

There is one slight change to the Ultra's chip, though. Motorola says it now employs a customized version of the Snapdragon 8 Elite used in last year's Ultra, which should deliver slightly speedier performance; it's hard to imagine you'll notice the change. “We always choose the best balance of features to deliver an immersive experience at a specific price point,” Motorola spokesperson Brendan Hall tells WIRED. “All three flip devices are powered by processors that enable fast performance and advanced AI experiences.”
不过，Ultra 的芯片确实有细微变化。摩托罗拉表示，它现在采用了去年 Ultra 所用骁龙 8 Elite 的定制版本，性能应该会略有提升；但很难想象用户能察觉到这种差异。摩托罗拉发言人 Brendan Hall 对《连线》杂志表示：“我们始终选择功能上的最佳平衡，以在特定价位提供沉浸式体验。这三款翻盖设备均搭载了能够实现快速性能和先进 AI 体验的处理器。”

The base Razr is getting a small bump to the MediaTek Dimensity 7450X; however, it offers only slightly better efficiency and AI performance, with no major changes to CPU or GPU performance.
基础版 Razr 的处理器小幅升级为联发科天玑 7450X；然而，它仅在能效和 AI 性能上略有提升，CPU 或 GPU 性能并无重大变化。

More bad news on the storage front: the base Razr no longer comes with 256 GB, but with just 128 GB. Motorola also cut the 1-TB storage option from the Razr Ultra, so your only choice is 512 GB.
