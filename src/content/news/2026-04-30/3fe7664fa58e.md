# KDE’s 30th Anniversary
# KDE 30 周年纪念

KDE is turning 30 this year! Three decades of passionate community effort against all odds; delivering control, privacy, and freedom to our users; and tons and tons of software.
KDE 今年 30 岁了！在过去的三十年里，我们的社区克服重重困难，始终怀揣热情；我们为用户提供了掌控权、隐私保护和自由；并开发了海量的软件。

CHECK BACK OFTEN! We will be updating this page frequently with new content, exciting 30th Anniversary news, things you can participate in, updated merch you can get, and much more!
请经常回来看看！我们将频繁更新此页面，发布新内容、激动人心的 30 周年纪念新闻、您可以参与的活动、最新的周边商品等等！

Read on and discover interesting facts you never knew, new merch you didn’t know you needed (but you do now!), how you too can help ensure we thrive the next 30 years, and where and how you can celebrate KDE’s birthday. Let’s start with that…
继续阅读，探索你从未了解的趣事、你意想不到（但现在绝对需要！）的新周边、如何帮助我们确保未来 30 年继续蓬勃发展，以及在哪里、如何庆祝 KDE 的生日。让我们从这里开始……

### 筹划你的派对 🎉

Join an event happening near you. If there are none, organize your own! Whether it is a meetup over drinks, a nice meal with friends, an installfest, or a full conference, let us know what, where, and when you are celebrating KDE’s birthday.
参加你附近的活动。如果没有，那就自己组织一个！无论是小酌聚会、与朋友共进美餐、安装派对（installfest），还是正式的会议，请告诉我们你将在何时、何地、以何种方式庆祝 KDE 的生日。

We’ll include your event in our list and it will show up in the map below. HOW TO ADD YOUR EVENT: Visit our wiki page and add your event using the template.
我们将把你的活动加入列表，它会显示在下方的地图中。如何添加活动：访问我们的 Wiki 页面，并使用模板添加你的活动。

### Help KDE…
### 帮助 KDE……

Most of our funds (70%!) come from private end users just like you. Become a Supporting Member and help ensure we receive a regular amount of money we can count on. This helps us plan and know what to expect for the next month, quarter, or year.
我们大部分的资金（70%！）来自像你一样的个人终端用户。成为支持会员，帮助我们获得稳定的资金来源。这有助于我们进行规划，并预估未来一个月、一个季度或一年的开支。

Use the box at the top of this page and select Become a Member to become a Supporting Member. Or make a one-time donation and provide us with emergency funds to get us through the following year. Use the box at the top of this page and select 1-time Donation to make your donation.

### Why donate
### 为什么要捐赠

We produce first-class software and your donation keeps us “in business” and our software sustainable for generations to come. We keep you in control and your donation allows KDE to remain truly independent.
我们生产一流的软件，你的捐赠让我们能够“持续运营”，并确保我们的软件能够造福后代。我们让你掌握掌控权，而你的捐赠使 KDE 能够保持真正的独立。

We reach people the tech industry left behind and your donation contributes to serving those who are ignored by the industry, and bring marginalized users into the community so we can grow the project for everyone.
我们触及那些被科技行业遗忘的人群，你的捐赠有助于服务那些被行业忽视的人，并将边缘化用户带入社区，从而让我们能为所有人发展这个项目。

We push to get Free Software into public institutions and your donation helps us adapt our software to what public institutions require, so your taxes go to fund Free Software, not some big tech corp.
我们推动自由软件进入公共机构，你的捐赠帮助我们根据公共机构的需求调整软件，让你的税款用于资助自由软件，而不是大型科技公司。

### How we use the money
### 我们如何使用资金

Our goals are ambitious and we need funds to carry them out. We need: a solid infrastructure for developers, translators, and other contributors; contractors (marketeers, event planners, lawyers, accountants) to carry out specialized tasks; to fund contributors travelling expenses so everybody has a chance to participate in the community; to pay to attend events and for material for booths; to commission artwork and designs; targeted development.
我们的目标宏大，需要资金来实现。我们需要：为开发者、翻译人员和其他贡献者提供坚实的基础设施；聘请承包商（市场营销、活动策划、律师、会计师）执行专业任务；资助贡献者的差旅费，让每个人都有机会参与社区；支付参加活动的费用及展位物料费；委托艺术创作和设计；以及定向开发。

### …and save the world
### ……并拯救世界

KDE contributes to cleaning up the world and you can too. KDE contributor Farid inspired us to take on the “30 for 30” challenge: for our 30th birthday, we are asking you to do something to help the environment and make the planet a nicer place to live in. Farid is planting 30 trees and we want you to come up with something similar.
KDE 致力于让世界变得更美好，你也可以。KDE 贡献者 Farid 启发我们发起了“30 for 30”挑战：为了庆祝 30 周年，我们邀请你做一些有益于环境、让地球变得更宜居的事情。Farid 正在种植 30 棵树，我们也希望你能提出类似的计划。

Film you and your crew carrying out your effort and we will promote your project on social media. Here are some more ideas:
拍摄你和你的团队执行计划的过程，我们将在社交媒体上推广你的项目。这里还有一些点子：

*   拯救 30 台（或更多！）电脑，避免它们被送往垃圾填埋场。
*   Upcycle 30 phones with a free mobile operating system.
*   为 30 部手机刷入自由移动操作系统，实现循环利用。
*   Clean up 30 hectares of woodland.
*   清理 30 公顷的林地。
*   Convert 30 people to a free operating system.
*   引导 30 人使用自由操作系统。
*   Take 30 techbros to court so they stop building AI datacenters.
*   起诉 30 个科技巨头，让他们停止建设 AI 数据中心。

### KDE’s history
### KDE 的历史

KDE has had a long and exciting history. Here we present a brief summary of what has happened over the last few decades, but if you want to see all the details, visit our timeline website, which gets updated every time something important happens.
KDE 拥有悠久而激动人心的历史。在此我们简要总结了过去几十年的历程，如果你想查看所有细节，请访问我们的时间线网站，每当有重要事件发生时，该网站都会进行更新。

*   **1995: Qt Created** – In 1995, the Norwegian company Troll Tech created the cross-platform framework Qt, with which KDE would be created in the following year.
    **1995：Qt 创建** – 1995 年，挪威公司 Troll Tech 创建了跨平台框架 Qt，KDE 将在次年基于此框架创建。
*   **1996: KDE Announced** – In 1996, Matthias Ettrich announced the creation of Kool Desktop Environment (KDE), a graphical interface for Unix systems.
    **1996：KDE 发布** – 1996 年，Matthias Ettrich 宣布创建 Kool Desktop Environment (KDE)，这是一个为 Unix 系统设计的图形界面。
*   **1997: KDE One Conference Held in Arnsberg** – About 15 KDE developers met in Arnsberg, Germany, to work on the project and discuss its future.
    **1997：KDE One 会议在阿恩斯贝格举行** – 约 15 名 KDE 开发者在德国阿恩斯贝格会面，共同开发项目并讨论其未来。
*   **1997: KDE e.V. Founded** – The nonprofit that represents the KDE community financially and legally was founded in Tübingen, Germany.
    **1997：KDE e.V. 成立** – 代表 KDE 社区财务和法律事务的非营利组织在德国图宾根成立。
*   **1998: KDE 1 Released** – KDE released the first stable version of its graphical environment.
    **1998：KDE 1 发布** – KDE 发布了其图形环境的第一个稳定版本。
*   **1999: Konqi Adopted** – A dragon is announced as the new animated assistant to the KDE Help Center.
    **1999：Konqi 被采用** – 一条龙被宣布为 KDE 帮助中心的新动画助手。
*   **2000: KDE 2 Released** – Featuring the Konqueror web browser and file manager, and the KOffice suite.
    **2000：KDE 2 发布** – 推出了 Konqueror 网络浏览器和文件管理器，以及 KOffice 套件。
*   **2002: KDE 3 Released** – Important additions included KDEPrint, translation into 50 languages, and educational applications.
    **2002：KDE 3 发布** – 重要新增功能包括 KDEPrint、50 种语言翻译以及教育应用程序。
*   **2008: KDE 4 Released** – Introduced the Oxygen theme, Plasma interface, Okular PDF reader, Dolphin file manager, and KWin.
    **2008：KDE 4 发布** – 引入了 Oxygen 主题、Plasma 界面、Okular PDF 阅读器、Dolphin 文件管理器和 KWin。
*   **2009: 1 Million Commits Reached** – The community reached the mark of 1 million commits.
    **2009：达到 100 万次提交** – 社区达到了 100 万次提交的里程碑。
