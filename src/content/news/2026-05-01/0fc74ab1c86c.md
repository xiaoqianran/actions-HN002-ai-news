### Sync-in

**Sync-in**

Sync-in is an open-source platform to store, sync, and share files while keeping full control of your data.
Sync-in 是一个开源平台，旨在存储、同步和共享文件，同时让您保持对数据的完全控制。

Self-hosted and privacy-first, it’s lightweight and fast, combining secure file management with real-time collaboration, granular permissions, and cross-device sync.
它采用自托管模式，优先考虑隐私，轻量且快速，将安全的文件管理与实时协作、细粒度权限控制以及跨设备同步功能相结合。

Built for teams that need sovereignty, security, and flexibility, without compromising performance.
专为需要数据主权、安全性和灵活性，且不愿牺牲性能的团队而打造。
