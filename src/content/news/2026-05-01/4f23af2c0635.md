# Legal AI startup Legora hits $5.6B valuation and its battle with Harvey just got hotter
# 法律 AI 初创公司 Legora 估值达 56 亿美元，与 Harvey 的竞争愈演愈烈

Nvidia has laid a new brick in its AI empire. NVentures, its corporate VC fund, has backed Legora, reportedly its first legal AI investment. Leveraging AI to help lawyers streamline their work, the Swedish-born legal tech startup is competing with U.S. player Harvey.

英伟达（Nvidia）为其 AI 帝国又添了一块基石。其企业风险投资基金 NVentures 已投资 Legora，据报道，这是该基金在法律 AI 领域的首笔投资。这家源自瑞典的法律科技初创公司利用 AI 帮助律师简化工作流程，目前正与美国竞争对手 Harvey 展开角逐。

Alongside Atlassian and other new financial investors, NVentures joined Legora’s cap table as part of a $50 million Series D extension that comes a month after the startup’s $550 million Series D. In the interval, this Y Combinator alum crossed $100 million in annual recurring revenue (ARR) — a milestone that contributed to its new $5.6 billion post-money valuation.

除了 Atlassian 和其他新的财务投资者外，NVentures 加入了 Legora 的资本结构，参与了其 5000 万美元的 D 轮融资延展。此前一个月，该公司刚刚完成了 5.5 亿美元的 D 轮融资。在此期间，这家 Y Combinator 的校友企业年度经常性收入（ARR）突破了 1 亿美元——这一里程碑事件助推其投后估值达到了 56 亿美元。

This brings Legora’s valuation just a tad closer to Harvey’s, which reached $11 billion last month when Sequoia tripled down on its investment. Andreessen Horowitz, Coatue, Conviction Partners, Elad Gil, Matt Miller’s Evantic, and Kleiner Perkins also participated in that round.

这使得 Legora 的估值与 Harvey 的差距进一步缩小。上个月，在红杉资本（Sequoia）追加投资后，Harvey 的估值已达到 110 亿美元。Andreessen Horowitz、Coatue、Conviction Partners、Elad Gil、Matt Miller 的 Evantic 以及 Kleiner Perkins 也参与了那一轮融资。

Legora, too, is backed by high-profile VCs, but it puts even more emphasis on the big names it secured as clients, such as Bird & Bird, Cleary Gottlieb, and Linklaters. According to the company, the platform it launched only 18 months ago is now used by more than 1,000 law firms and in-house legal teams across 50 markets.

Legora 同样拥有知名风投的支持，但它更强调其所获得的大牌客户，例如 Bird & Bird、Cleary Gottlieb 和 Linklaters。据该公司称，其 18 个月前推出的平台目前已被 50 个市场的 1000 多家律师事务所和企业法务团队使用。

Harvey has game in that area too. It claims 100,000 lawyers across 1,300 organizations as customers, ranging from global law firms like Hengeler Mueller and Latham & Watkins to corporate legal teams at companies like T-Mobile and Bridgewater.

Harvey 在该领域也实力不俗。它声称拥有来自 1300 家机构的 10 万名律师客户，涵盖了 Hengeler Mueller 和 Latham & Watkins 等全球性律所，以及 T-Mobile 和 Bridgewater 等公司的法务团队。

With global leadership as the end goal, the Harvey v. Legora rivalry is one they intend to play on each other’s home turf. Legora has opened multiple offices around the world with the U.S. a key focus for its expansion. Conversely, Harvey is pushing into Europe.

以全球领先地位为最终目标，Harvey 与 Legora 的竞争正延伸至对方的“大本营”。Legora 已在全球开设了多个办事处，并将美国作为其扩张的重点。反之，Harvey 也正在向欧洲市场进军。

With plenty of capital to spend on both sides, that battle has moved to mindshare. Not long after Winston Weinberg’s company Harvey signed a brand partnership with actor Gabriel Macht, who plays a high-powered lawyer in the TV series “Suits,” Legora launched an advertising campaign featuring movie star Jude Law under the slogan “Law just got more attractive.”

双方都有充足的资金投入，这场竞争已转向对用户心智的争夺。在 Winston Weinberg 创立的 Harvey 与在美剧《金装律师》（Suits）中饰演精英律师的演员 Gabriel Macht 签署品牌合作协议后不久，Legora 也发起了一场广告活动，邀请电影明星裘德·洛（Jude Law）代言，并打出“法律变得更有魅力了”（Law just got more attractive）的口号。

Both companies may be right to bet heavily on marketing. Rivalry aside, they are built on top of large language models made by AI giants that could well become their competitors. When Anthropic launched a legal plug-in for Claude not long ago, several publicly listed legal software companies saw their stocks drop.

两家公司在营销上重金投入或许是正确的选择。撇开竞争不谈，它们都是建立在 AI 巨头开发的大语言模型之上，而这些巨头很可能成为它们的竞争对手。不久前，当 Anthropic 为 Claude 推出法律插件时，几家上市法律软件公司的股价应声下跌。

Legora CEO Max Junestrand says he isn’t concerned. “Foundation models are improving quickly, but the real value is in how they’re applied,” he wrote in a statement. It also shows how the startup instills FOMO among its target users, stating that “the legal teams that embed AI effectively today will shape how the industry evolves.”

Legora 首席执行官 Max Junestrand 表示他并不担心。他在一份声明中写道：“基础模型正在快速进步，但真正的价值在于如何应用它们。”这也显示了这家初创公司如何向目标用户灌输“错失恐惧症”（FOMO），称“今天能有效嵌入 AI 的法律团队将塑造行业未来的演变方式。”

NVentures’ investment is also a signal that Legora might have enough of a moat to protect them from the model makers, and its bigger rival. However, Nvidia is also known for hedging its bets — after all, it invested in both Anthropic and OpenAI before deciding it has probably had enough.

NVentures 的投资也释放了一个信号：Legora 可能拥有足够的护城河，以抵御模型制造商及其更大的竞争对手。然而，英伟达也以“对冲押注”而闻名——毕竟，它在投资了 Anthropic 和 OpenAI 后，才决定可能已经足够了。
