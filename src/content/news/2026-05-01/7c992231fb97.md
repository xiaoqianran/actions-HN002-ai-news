# Best Gifts for Hikers, Backpackers, Outdoorsy People (2026)
# 2026年徒步、背包客及户外爱好者最佳礼物指南

Buying gifts for the outdoor enthusiast is damn near impossible. Your hiking/camping/cycling outdoorsy friends are often serious gearheads, meticulously poring over reviews, guides, and the perennial wisdom of ounce-counting Reddit users to find the exact right thing.

为户外爱好者挑选礼物简直难如登天。你那些喜欢徒步、露营或骑行的户外朋友通常都是资深装备控，他们会一丝不苟地钻研各种测评、指南，以及 Reddit 上那些精打细算的“克重党”们的经验之谈，只为找到那件最完美的装备。

Don't stress trying to pick that exact right thing yourself. The chances of you figuring it out and getting it for them as a gift is exactly zero. That doesn't mean you should punt and buy an REI gift card—although they might like that, especially with REI's annual anniversary sale coming up at the end of May. But gift cards are lame. Instead, get them something they totally didn't expect: one of these fun, useful, sometimes whimsical things that are almost guaranteed to not only delight them but actually get used outdoors.

别为了挑选那件“完美装备”而感到压力。你凭自己选对并送出礼物的几率几乎为零。这并不意味着你应该放弃，转而买一张 REI 礼品卡——尽管他们可能会喜欢，尤其是考虑到 REI 五月底即将到来的年度周年庆。但礼品卡太没新意了。不如送他们一些意想不到的东西：以下这些既有趣、实用，有时又带点奇思妙想的礼物，几乎保证能让他们惊喜，并且真的会在户外派上用场。

### Merino Wool Socks / 美利奴羊毛袜

Yes, you read that right. Socks. SOCKS. You should gift socks. Outdoorsy people can never have too many socks. We're hard on socks, which almost guarantees this gift will see some use. What your outdoorsperson especially needs are merino wool socks. Merino wool is a super fiber. It will change their lives.

没错，你没看错。袜子。袜子！你应该送袜子。户外爱好者永远不会嫌袜子多。我们对袜子的磨损很大，这几乎保证了这份礼物一定会被用上。你的户外朋友最需要的是美利奴羊毛袜。美利奴羊毛是一种超级纤维，它会改变他们的生活。

It's comfortable, has virtually no odor no matter how long you wear it, and helps your body thermoregulate, staying warmer when it's cold, cooler when it's warm. We love Darn Tough socks in particular because they're comfortable, stand up to considerable abuse, and have some of the highest merino wool content of anything we've tested.

它穿着舒适，无论穿多久几乎都没有异味，还能帮助身体调节温度——天冷时保暖，天热时凉爽。我们特别推荐 Darn Tough 品牌的袜子，因为它们舒适耐穿，能经受住严苛的考验，而且是我们测试过的产品中含毛量最高的产品之一。

### A Cozy Blanket / 舒适的户外毯

No outdoorsy person ever regrets owning a Rumpl puffy blanket. We used to use a Pendleton wool one, but it was heavy, got wet, and was hard to wash. A Rumpl puffy packs down light, like a sleeping bag. It’s made from 100 percent recycled plastic with a PFAS-free DWR. Coffee spills, puddles, dog hair, and mud just roll right off it—and if not, it’s easy to throw into the washing machine and have it air dry.

没有哪个户外爱好者会后悔拥有一条 Rumpl 充棉毯。我们以前用过 Pendleton 的羊毛毯，但它太重，容易受潮，而且很难清洗。Rumpl 充棉毯可以像睡袋一样轻便打包。它由 100% 回收塑料制成，并采用了不含 PFAS 的防泼水涂层。咖啡洒了、水渍、狗毛和泥土都能轻松抖落——即便弄脏了，扔进洗衣机洗涤并自然晾干也非常方便。

### Coffee, Coffee Now / 咖啡，现在就要咖啡

What is camping without brewing coffee over a morning fire? And what is coffee if not from a moka pot? This is the classic Bialetti moka pot on which all other moka pots are based. It's made of heavy-gauge aluminum that heats evenly and won't complain if you put it directly on the coals. It'll brew about a 4-ounce cup of very dark coffee—as close as you'll get to espresso outdoors.

没有清晨火堆上煮出的咖啡，露营还算什么露营？而如果不是用摩卡壶煮的咖啡，又怎能称得上是咖啡？这款经典的 Bialetti 摩卡壶是所有摩卡壶的鼻祖。它由厚实的铝材制成，加热均匀，直接放在炭火上使用也完全没问题。它能煮出约 4 盎司浓郁的咖啡，这是你在户外能喝到的最接近意式浓缩的口感了。

### The Best Knife for Fancy Hiking / 徒步必备的精致折刀

Opinel's classic folding knife practically begs to be taken on a hike with some good hard chorizo, soft Spanish cheese, and a nice baguette. It's the first step to hiking European style. I got my Opinel as a gift from a friend, and I love it. And I am an outdoor person, so there is your living proof that this is a good gift.

Opinel 的经典折刀简直就是为了徒步旅行而生的，带上它，再配点硬质西班牙香肠、软奶酪和法棍，这就是欧式徒步的第一步。我的 Opinel 是朋友送的礼物，我非常喜欢。作为一个户外爱好者，这就是它作为礼物绝佳的活证据。

One tip: Stick with stainless steel when gifting this. Carbon steel is great, but when you're throwing it in your backpack and possibly forgetting about it for long periods of time, carbon steel will rust.

一个小贴士：送礼时请选择不锈钢版本。碳钢材质虽然好，但如果你把它扔在背包里，很长一段时间都想不起来用，碳钢是会生锈的。
