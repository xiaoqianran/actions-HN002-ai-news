## Best Overall: Nolah Evolution
## 最佳综合奖：Nolah Evolution

One mattress made specially for side sleepers, coming right up. The design of the Nolah Evolution is meant to promote pressure relief, from the Euro pillow top to the pocketed coils in the very center of the mattress.

这是一款专为侧卧者打造的床垫。Nolah Evolution 的设计旨在提升压力缓解效果，从欧式枕头顶层（Euro pillow top）到床垫核心的独立袋装弹簧，无一不体现了这一点。

Of the three firmness levels offered, luxury firm does well with keeping the shoulders and hips level so that the lower back doesn't dip into odd angles (to prevent lower back pain). When it comes to lower-back support, the middle of the bed is purposefully firmer, thanks to zoned AirFoam right under the Euro pillow top and firmer zoned coils at the bottom. The pillow top conformed around my body, but overall, the mattress still retained a noticeably firmer feel. If you are a side sleeper who needs something softer, either for hip or shoulder pain, or that's just what you prefer, the plush soft version may be a better fit for you.

在提供的三种硬度等级中，“豪华适中”（luxury firm）能很好地保持肩部和臀部水平，从而防止下背部出现异常角度（以预防腰痛）。在腰部支撑方面，床垫中部特意做得更硬，这得益于欧式顶层下方的分区 AirFoam 和底部的分区弹簧。枕头顶层能贴合我的身体，但总体而言，这款床垫仍保持了明显的支撑感。如果您是侧卧者，且因为髋部或肩部疼痛需要更软的床垫，或者单纯偏好更软的睡感，那么“毛绒软”（plush soft）版本可能更适合您。
