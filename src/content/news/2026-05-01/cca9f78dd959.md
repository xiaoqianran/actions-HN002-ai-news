# Apple’s iPhone revenue jumps to $57 billion despite chip shortages
# 尽管芯片短缺，苹果 iPhone 营收仍跃升至 570 亿美元

Apple’s iPhone revenue jumped 22 percent to $57 billion over the past few months, despite supply chain issues impacting the device’s processor. In an interview with Reuters, Apple CEO Tim Cook said iPhone “demand was off the charts,” but there’s “a little less flexibility at the moment for getting more parts.” Cook added during an earnings call that supply constraints could come for Mac, too, “given the continued high levels of demand that we’re seeing.”

尽管供应链问题影响了设备的处理器供应，但苹果 iPhone 的营收在过去几个月中仍增长了 22%，达到 570 亿美元。在接受路透社采访时，苹果首席执行官蒂姆·库克（Tim Cook）表示，iPhone 的“需求极其旺盛”，但“目前在获取更多零部件方面的灵活性有所下降”。库克在财报电话会议上补充说，鉴于“我们所看到的持续高需求水平”，Mac 产品线也可能面临供应限制。

Apple earned $111.2 billion in revenue during the second quarter of 2026, its “best March quarter ever,” according to Cook. This latest earnings report comes a little over a week after the company announced CEO Cook’s retirement in September. John Ternus, the company’s head of hardware engineering, will take Cook’s place as he transitions into the role of executive chairman.

据库克称，苹果在 2026 年第二季度的营收为 1112 亿美元，这是其“有史以来表现最好的三月季度”。这份最新的财报发布于该公司宣布库克将于 9 月退休的一周多之后。届时，苹果硬件工程高级副总裁约翰·特努斯（John Ternus）将接替库克，而库克将转任执行董事长。

Even with Cook’s departure just months away, he’ll still have to lead Apple through the 2026 Worldwide Developers’ Conference (WWDC) in June, where Apple’s expected to take the wraps off a bunch of new features, including a more personalized Siri powered by Google’s Gemini AI model.

尽管距离库克离任仅剩几个月时间，但他仍需带领苹果完成 6 月份的 2026 年全球开发者大会（WWDC）。届时，苹果预计将发布一系列新功能，包括由谷歌 Gemini AI 模型驱动的、更具个性化的 Siri。

Apple had an announcement-filled March, revealing a budget-friendly MacBook Neo that caused a stir in the laptop space for its speedy performance and aluminium chassis. The device starts at $599, featuring a 13-inch display, an A18 Pro iPhone chip, and 8GB of RAM. Apple also announced a new iPhone 17E, an M4-equipped iPad Air, along with new MacBook Air and MacBook Pro models.

苹果在三月份发布了多款产品，其中经济实惠的 MacBook Neo 因其出色的性能和铝合金机身在笔记本电脑领域引起了轰动。该设备起售价为 599 美元，配备 13 英寸显示屏、A18 Pro 芯片和 8GB 内存。此外，苹果还发布了新款 iPhone 17E、搭载 M4 芯片的 iPad Air，以及新款 MacBook Air 和 MacBook Pro 机型。

The new devices helped boost Mac revenue to $8.4 billion, while iPads reached $6.91 billion. Apple’s services segment, which includes subscriptions like Apple One, Apple Music, Apple TV, and others, soared to another “all-time record” at $30.98 billion.

这些新设备助力 Mac 营收增长至 84 亿美元，iPad 营收达到 69.1 亿美元。苹果的服务部门（包括 Apple One、Apple Music、Apple TV 等订阅服务）营收飙升至 309.8 亿美元，再创“历史新高”。
