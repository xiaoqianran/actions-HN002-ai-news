# Google’s Gemini AI assistant is hitting the road in millions of vehicles
# 谷歌 Gemini AI 助手即将登陆数百万辆汽车

Google announced on Thursday that it will begin rolling out Gemini to cars with Google built-in, marking a significant upgrade from the current Google Assistant. The move signals Google’s push to bring more advanced, conversational AI into the driving experience.

谷歌周四宣布，将开始向搭载“Google built-in”（内置谷歌服务）的汽车推送 Gemini，这标志着其较现有的 Google Assistant 有了重大升级。此举表明谷歌正致力于将更先进的对话式人工智能引入驾驶体验中。

The announcement follows closely behind news from General Motors, which revealed yesterday that Gemini is coming to approximately 4 million vehicles from model year 2022 and newer, spanning brands like Cadillac, Chevrolet, Buick, and GMC. However, today’s announcement didn’t name specific automakers, suggesting that Gemini won’t be limited to GM vehicles. The rollout will begin in the U.S. with English-language support, with availability expanding over the coming months.

此前，通用汽车（General Motors）昨日刚宣布 Gemini 将覆盖约 400 万辆 2022 年及以后的车型，涵盖凯迪拉克、雪佛兰、别克和 GMC 等品牌。不过，谷歌今天的公告并未点名特定汽车制造商，这暗示 Gemini 的应用范围将不仅限于通用汽车。此次推送将首先在美国以英语支持的形式开启，并在未来几个月内逐步扩大覆盖范围。

Notably, Gemini isn’t just reserved for new vehicles, but also for compatible existing cars via software updates. Cars with Google built-in first launched in 2020. Now, according to Google, the new Gemini update enables a more natural, conversational way for drivers to interact with their vehicles. Soon, drivers will be able to speak more freely to complete tasks, explore ideas, or retrieve information.

值得注意的是，Gemini 不仅限于新车，兼容的现有车型也可以通过软件更新获得该功能。搭载“Google built-in”的汽车最早于 2020 年推出。据谷歌称，此次 Gemini 更新使驾驶员能够以更自然、更具对话感的方式与车辆进行交互。不久之后，驾驶员将能够更自由地通过语音来完成任务、探索想法或检索信息。

For instance, a driver could say they want to stop for lunch at a highly rated sit-down restaurant with outdoor seating along their route. Gemini can pull relevant suggestions using data from Google Maps, then handle follow-up questions like parking availability or menu options, including dietary preferences. Gemini can also complete tasks like turning on the heat, provide directions, recommend music, retrieve vehicle information, summarize incoming messages, and help drivers respond hands-free.

例如，驾驶员可以说他们想在沿途找一家评价很高、带户外座位的餐厅吃午餐。Gemini 可以利用谷歌地图的数据提供相关建议，并处理后续问题，如停车位情况或菜单选项（包括饮食偏好）。Gemini 还可以完成开启暖气、提供导航、推荐音乐、检索车辆信息、总结收到的消息以及帮助驾驶员免提回复信息等任务。

Additionally, there’s Gemini Live, which is currently in beta and allows for more open-ended, real-time conversations. Drivers can activate it by tapping a button in the interface or by saying, “Hey Google, let’s talk,” enabling brainstorming, learning, or general discussion while on the road.

此外，目前处于测试阶段的 Gemini Live 允许进行更开放的实时对话。驾驶员只需点击界面上的按钮或说“Hey Google, let’s talk”即可激活，从而在驾驶过程中进行头脑风暴、学习或进行一般性讨论。

Drivers who are signed into their Google accounts in compatible vehicles will be prompted with the option to upgrade. Once enabled, Gemini can be accessed through voice commands, the on-screen microphone, or steering wheel controls. Google says it plans to expand Gemini support to additional languages and regions. Future updates are also expected to deepen integration with services like Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Home.

在兼容车辆上登录谷歌账号的驾驶员将收到升级提示。启用后，驾驶员可以通过语音指令、屏幕麦克风或方向盘控制键来使用 Gemini。谷歌表示，计划将 Gemini 的支持范围扩展到更多语言和地区。未来的更新预计还将加深与 Gmail、谷歌日历和 Google Home 等服务的集成。
