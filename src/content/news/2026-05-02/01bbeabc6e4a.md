# Dreame — the vacuum company — just ‘launched’ its own phones
# 追觅（Dreame）——这家扫地机器人公司——刚刚“发布”了自己的手机

The robot vacuum manufacturer claims it’s launching a modular phone and a set of luxury devices, but I’m not convinced.
这家扫地机器人制造商声称正在推出一款模块化手机和一系列奢侈设备，但我对此持怀疑态度。

Dreame, a Chinese manufacturer best known for its robot vacuums but with ambitions to do much more, says it’s making smartphones now. I’m not sure I believe it.
追觅（Dreame）是一家以扫地机器人闻名的中国制造商，但它有着更宏大的抱负，并宣称现在要进军智能手机领域。我不太相信这一点。

The company showed off two phones at its own Next event, which took place in California this week, though both had previously been revealed in China in March. Neither phone has actually launched though — in China, the US, or elsewhere — and the company has revealed only a handful of specs about either.
该公司在本周于加州举办的“Next”发布会上展示了两款手机，尽管这两款手机此前已于三月份在中国亮相。然而，这两款手机目前尚未在任何地方（包括中国、美国或其他地区）正式发售，且该公司仅透露了极少数的规格参数。

Aurora Nex LS1 is the more interesting of the two, but also the less plausible. It’s a modular smartphone with a magnetic attachment point where the rear camera would normally be. Dreame has revealed five different modules that can be attached at that point: a triple camera that apparently includes a 1-inch-type sensor and a 115mm equivalent telephoto lens; an action camera; a fan; a satellite communications module; and a “Smart Agent Module.”
Aurora Nex LS1 是两款手机中更有趣的一款，但其可行性也最低。这是一款模块化智能手机，在通常放置后置摄像头的位置设有一个磁吸接口。追觅展示了五个可安装在该接口的模块：一个据称包含 1 英寸传感器和 115mm 等效长焦镜头的后置三摄模组；一个运动相机；一个风扇；一个卫星通信模块；以及一个“智能代理模块”（Smart Agent Module）。

We’ve seen modular phone designs before, but this is closest in approach to the version Tecno showed off at MWC this year. That phone was only a concept device though; Dreame is at least implying that the Nex LS1 is a real product that will really go on sale.
我们以前见过模块化手机设计，但这种方案与传音（Tecno）在今年 MWC 上展示的版本最为接近。不过那款手机只是概念机；而追觅至少在暗示 Nex LS1 是一款真正会上市销售的产品。

Dreame’s second phone is the Aurora Lux, though calling it a single phone is a little misleading. This is really a series of different phone designs, some quite radically different from each other, all unified by a garish sense of luxury. Some are coated in leather; others appear plated in gold. Many are plastered with gemstones. One seems to have an analog clock built into the rear camera island. And while most have matching circular camera designs, several are completely different, with the cameras sitting separately and flush with the rear, or separated from the rest of the phone by a Pixel-esque camera bar. In all, Dreame’s press release from the Chinese launch event claims that it has 29 different versions of the Aurora Lux, a number so comically large that I refuse to believe the company can seriously manufacture them.
追觅的第二款手机是 Aurora Lux，但称其为“一款”手机有些误导。这实际上是一系列不同的手机设计，其中一些设计差异巨大，但都统一在一种浮夸的奢华感之下。有的覆盖着皮革，有的看起来镀了金，许多还镶嵌着宝石。其中一款似乎在后置摄像头模组中内置了一个模拟时钟。虽然大多数手机采用了统一的圆形摄像头设计，但有几款则完全不同，摄像头独立且与背板齐平，或者通过类似 Pixel 的摄像头条与手机其他部分隔开。总而言之，追觅在中国发布会的新闻稿中声称 Aurora Lux 有 29 种不同版本，这个数字大得离谱，我实在无法相信该公司能认真地将它们全部生产出来。

Elsewhere Dreame has claimed camera capabilities including a 200-megapixel sensor, and support for Lofic — a new type of HDR processing so far only seen on the Honor Magic 8 Pro and Xiaomi 17 Ultra. It’s not clear which of the two phones those specs apply to though, and I would be as impressed as I would be surprised if Dreame was really committed to such powerful camera hardware on its first phones. Is a robot vacuum company really ready to release camera hardware that outclasses Apple and Samsung, the first true modular phone, and 29 different versions of a luxury, gem-encrusted handset, all from scratch?
此外，追觅还声称其摄像头具备 2000 万像素传感器，并支持 Lofic——这是一种目前仅见于荣耀 Magic 8 Pro 和小米 17 Ultra 上的新型 HDR 处理技术。目前尚不清楚这些规格适用于哪款手机，如果追觅真的在首批手机上投入如此强大的摄像头硬件，我会感到既惊讶又佩服。一家扫地机器人公司真的准备好从零开始，发布超越苹果和三星的摄像头硬件、第一款真正的模块化手机，以及 29 种镶嵌宝石的奢侈手机版本了吗？

Both of the phones will reportedly run Dreame’s own Aurora AIOS, “a proprietary operating system designed around proactive service rather than passive response.” Yes, it’s an Android skin with a load of AI tacked on.
据报道，这两款手机都将运行追觅自家的 Aurora AIOS，“这是一个围绕主动服务而非被动响应设计的专有操作系统。”是的，这本质上就是一个堆砌了大量 AI 功能的安卓皮肤。

Dreame hasn’t confirmed when or where either phone will launch, but has said that AIOS will launch in the second half of 2026. Unless it intends to release the software as a download for other devices, that presumably means the first Dreame hardware will release in the same time frame.
追觅尚未确认这两款手机的发布时间和地点，但表示 AIOS 将于 2026 年下半年推出。除非它打算将该软件作为其他设备的下载包发布，否则这意味着首款追觅硬件很可能也会在同一时间段发布。

The two phones were just one part of an eclectic Dreame launch event that also featured a rocket-boosted electric car, a laundry robot, and a TV with moving speakers. At least Dreame knows how to dream big.
这两款手机只是追觅那场“大杂烩”式发布会的一部分，会上还展示了一辆火箭助推电动汽车、一台洗衣机器人以及一台带有移动扬声器的电视。至少，追觅确实知道如何“做大梦”。
