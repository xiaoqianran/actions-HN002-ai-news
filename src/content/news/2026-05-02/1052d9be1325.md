# Lightweight Distillation of SAM 3 and DINOv3 for Edge-Deployable Individual-Level Livestock Monitoring and Longitudinal Visual Analytics
# 用于边缘部署的个体级畜牧监测与纵向视觉分析的 SAM 3 和 DINOv3 轻量化蒸馏研究

**Abstract:** Foundation-model pipelines for individual-level livestock monitoring -- combining open-vocabulary detection, promptable video segmentation, and self-supervised visual embeddings -- have raised the accuracy ceiling of precision livestock farming (PLF), but their GPU memory budgets exceed the envelope of commodity edge accelerators. 

**摘要：** 用于个体级畜牧监测的基础模型流水线（结合了开放词汇检测、可提示视频分割和自监督视觉嵌入）提高了精准畜牧业（PLF）的精度上限，但其 GPU 显存需求超出了商用边缘加速器的承载能力。

To close this gap, the 446M-parameter Perception Encoder (PE-ViT-L+) backbone of SAM 3 is distilled into a 40.66M-parameter multi-scale student through three mechanisms: a Feature Pyramid Network student encoder built on TinyViT-21M-512, a four-term direction-then-scale distillation loss, and backbone-substitution inference with sliding-window session pruning that bounds streaming GPU memory growth. 

为了弥补这一差距，研究人员通过三种机制将 SAM 3 中 4.46 亿参数的感知编码器（PE-ViT-L+）主干网络蒸馏为一个 4066 万参数的多尺度学生模型：基于 TinyViT-21M-512 构建的特征金字塔网络（FPN）学生编码器、四项“先方向后尺度”的蒸馏损失函数，以及带有滑动窗口会话剪枝的主干替换推理方法，从而限制了流式 GPU 显存的增长。

The DINOv3 family includes a pre-distilled ViT-S/16 variant (21.6M parameters) released alongside a 6716M-parameter ViT-7B teacher; the ViT-S (21M) variant is adopted as the per-individual embedder. 

DINOv3 系列包含一个预蒸馏的 ViT-S/16 变体（2160 万参数），与 67.16 亿参数的 ViT-7B 教师模型一同发布；研究采用了 ViT-S (21M) 变体作为个体嵌入器。

On the Edinburgh Pig dataset, the compressed pipeline reaches 92.29% MOTA and 96.15% IDF1 against the SAM 3 teacher (1.68- and 0.84-percentage-point losses), achieves a 7.77-fold reduction in system-level parameters and a 3.01-fold reduction in peak VRAM (19.52GB -> 6.49GB), and reaches 97.34% top-1 accuracy with 91.67% macro-F1 on nine-class pig behaviour classification. 

在爱丁堡猪只数据集上，该压缩流水线达到了 92.29% 的 MOTA 和 96.15% 的 IDF1（相比 SAM 3 教师模型仅分别下降了 1.68 和 0.84 个百分点），系统级参数减少了 7.77 倍，峰值显存占用降低了 3.01 倍（从 19.52GB 降至 6.49GB），并在九类猪只行为分类任务中达到了 97.34% 的 Top-1 准确率和 91.67% 的宏平均 F1 分数。

The pipeline fits inside an NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX 16GB envelope with 4.9GB of headroom, supporting a proposed -- but not yet empirically validated -- on-device embedding-pool re-identification mechanism whose per-individual footprint of approximately 94MB per animal per year produces a longitudinal visual record amenable to retrospective association with disease, lameness, reproductive, and growth outcome labels.

该流水线可适配于 NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX 16GB 的运行环境，并留有 4.9GB 的余量。它支持一种已提出但尚未经实证的设备端嵌入池重识别机制，该机制每只动物每年约占用 94MB 的存储空间，能够生成纵向视觉记录，便于后续与疾病、跛行、繁殖及生长结果标签进行回顾性关联分析。
