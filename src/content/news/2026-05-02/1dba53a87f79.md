# Towards Generalizable Mapping of Hedges and Linear Woody Features from Earth Observation Data: a national Product for Germany
# 面向地球观测数据的树篱与线性木本特征的通用化制图：德国国家级产品

**Abstract:** Hedges and other linear woody features provide valuable ecosystem services, particularly within intensively managed agricultural landscapes. They are key elements for climate adaptation and biodiversity amongst others not only due to a largely varying flora, but also as a feeding-, resting-, and nesting place for many animals and insects including valuable pollinators. Therefore, they require dedicated management, preservation, and attention.

**摘要：** 树篱和其他线性木本特征提供了宝贵的生态系统服务，特别是在集约化管理的农业景观中。它们是气候适应和生物多样性的关键要素，这不仅是因为其多样的植物群落，还因为它们为许多动物和昆虫（包括有价值的授粉媒介）提供了觅食、休息和筑巢的场所。因此，它们需要专门的管理、保护和关注。

Thus, systematic and large-scale mapping of these features from Earth observation data is of high importance. However, transferable and reusable workflows for linear woody feature mapping remain a key methodological challenge, given the diversity of sensor types, spatial resolutions, data acquisition conditions, and complex landscape variability encountered across study areas.

因此，利用地球观测数据对这些特征进行系统性的大规模制图具有重要意义。然而，考虑到传感器类型、空间分辨率、数据获取条件的多样性以及研究区域内复杂的景观变异性，针对线性木本特征制图的可迁移且可复用的工作流程仍然是一个关键的方法论挑战。

We introduce a modular workflow built around two independently optimizable components. Firstly, a flexible input data interface that consolidates heterogeneous Earth observation data into a binary woody vegetation mask, and secondly, a deep neural network trained to separate linear from non-linear shapes within these masks.

我们引入了一种围绕两个可独立优化组件构建的模块化工作流程。首先是一个灵活的输入数据接口，它将异构的地球观测数据整合为二值化的木本植被掩膜；其次是一个深度神经网络，经过训练以从这些掩膜中分离出线性与非线性形状。

We demonstrate the workflow by deriving three national-scale linear woody feature maps for all of Germany from three input sources by using a single trained model without retraining. Evaluation against refined reference data from four federal state biotope mapping campaigns and comparison with two existing linear woody feature maps demonstrate that the workflow produces competitive results across all evaluation sites on a national level.

我们通过使用单一训练模型且无需重新训练的情况下，从三种输入源中推导出覆盖全德国的三份国家级线性木本特征图，从而验证了该工作流程。通过与来自四个联邦州生物群落制图活动的精细参考数据进行评估，并与现有的两份线性木本特征图进行比较，结果表明该工作流程在全国范围内的所有评估点上均产生了具有竞争力的结果。

The modular design and its demonstrated applicability at national scale provide a foundation for scalable and generalizable linear woody feature mapping beyond Germany.

其模块化设计及其在国家尺度上的适用性证明，为德国以外地区可扩展且通用化的线性木本特征制图奠定了基础。
