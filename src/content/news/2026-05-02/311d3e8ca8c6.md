# TRUST: A Framework for Decentralized AI Service v.0.1
# TRUST：去中心化人工智能服务框架 v.0.1

**Abstract:** Large Reasoning Models (LRMs) and Multi-Agent Systems (MAS) in high-stakes domains demand reliable verification, yet centralized approaches suffer four limitations: (1) Robustness, with single points of failure vulnerable to attacks and bias; (2) Scalability, as reasoning complexity creates bottlenecks; (3) Opacity, as hidden auditing erodes trust; and (4) Privacy, as exposed reasoning traces risk model theft.

**摘要：** 在高风险领域中，大型推理模型（LRMs）和多智能体系统（MAS）需要可靠的验证机制，但中心化方法存在四个局限性：（1）鲁棒性不足，单点故障易受攻击和偏见影响；（2）可扩展性受限，推理复杂性导致瓶颈；（3）透明度缺失，黑箱审计削弱了信任；（4）隐私风险，暴露的推理轨迹存在模型被窃取的风险。

We introduce TRUST (Transparent, Robust, and Unified Services for Trustworthy AI), a decentralized framework with three innovations: (i) Hierarchical Directed Acyclic Graphs (HDAGs) that decompose Chain-of-Thought reasoning into five abstraction levels for parallel distributed auditing; (ii) the DAAN protocol, which projects multi-agent interactions into Causal Interaction Graphs (CIGs) for deterministic root-cause attribution; and (iii) a multi-tier consensus mechanism among computational checkers, LLM evaluators, and human experts with stake-weighted voting that guarantees correctness under 30% adversarial participation.

我们推出了 TRUST（用于可信人工智能的透明、鲁棒且统一的服务），这是一个包含三项创新的去中心化框架：（i）分层有向无环图（HDAGs），将思维链推理分解为五个抽象层，以实现并行分布式审计；（ii）DAAN 协议，将多智能体交互映射为因果交互图（CIGs），从而实现确定性的根本原因归因；以及（iii）一种由计算检查器、LLM 评估器和人类专家组成的多层共识机制，通过权益加权投票，确保在 30% 的参与者为对抗性的情况下仍能保证正确性。

We prove a Safety-Profitability Theorem ensuring honest auditors profit while malicious actors incur losses. All decisions are recorded on-chain, while privacy-by-design segmentation prevents reconstruction of proprietary logic. Across multiple LLMs and benchmarks, TRUST attains 72.4% accuracy (4-18% above baselines) and remains resilient against 20% corruption. DAAN reaches 70% root-cause attribution (vs. 54-63% for standard methods) with 60% token savings. Human studies validate the design (F1 = 0.89, Brier = 0.074).

我们证明了“安全-盈利定理”，确保诚实的审计者获利，而恶意行为者遭受损失。所有决策均记录在链上，同时通过“隐私设计”的分段技术防止专有逻辑被重构。在多个大语言模型和基准测试中，TRUST 达到了 72.4% 的准确率（比基准高出 4-18%），并能抵御 20% 的数据损坏。DAAN 的根本原因归因准确率达到 70%（标准方法为 54-63%），同时节省了 60% 的 Token 消耗。人类评估研究验证了该设计的有效性（F1 = 0.89，Brier = 0.074）。

The framework supports (A1) decentralized auditing, (A2) tamper-proof leaderboards, (A3) trustless data annotation, and (A4) governed autonomous agents, pioneering decentralized AI auditing for safe, accountable deployment of reasoning-capable systems.

该框架支持（A1）去中心化审计、（A2）防篡改排行榜、（A3）无需信任的数据标注以及（A4）受治理的自主智能体，为具备推理能力的系统的安全、可问责部署开创了去中心化人工智能审计的先河。
