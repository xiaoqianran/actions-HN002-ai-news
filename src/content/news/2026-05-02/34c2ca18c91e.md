### An Electric Bike: Trek FX+ 1
### 电动自行车：Trek FX+ 1

Electric bikes offer a lot of mobility around your grad's new city and campus. The Trek FX+ 1 is our favorite. It's backed by a lifetime warranty, has an extremely easy-to-use charger, and features dedicated turn signal buttons. The range doesn't quite hit the advertised 50 miles, but for a student getting to and from nearby places that doesn't want to rely on public transportation, this ebike is worth considering.
电动自行车能为毕业生在新的城市或校园里提供极大的出行便利。Trek FX+ 1 是我们的首选。它提供终身保修，充电器极其易用，并配有专门的转向灯按钮。虽然续航里程未完全达到标称的50英里，但对于不想依赖公共交通、需要在周边往返的学生来说，这款电动自行车非常值得考虑。
