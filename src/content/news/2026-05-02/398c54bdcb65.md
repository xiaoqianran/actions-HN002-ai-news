# Exploring the Limits of Pruning: Task-Specific Neurons, Model Collapse, and Recovery in Task-Specific Large Language Models
# 探索剪枝的极限：特定任务大语言模型中的任务专用神经元、模型崩溃与恢复

**Abstract:** Neuron pruning is widely used to reduce the computational cost and parameter footprint of large language models, yet it remains unclear whether neurons in task-specific models contribute uniformly to task performance. In this work, we provide empirical evidence for the existence and importance of task-specific neurons through a systematic pruning study on language models specialized for mathematical reasoning and code generation.

**摘要：** 神经元剪枝被广泛用于降低大语言模型的计算成本和参数占用，但目前尚不清楚特定任务模型中的神经元是否对任务性能有均等的贡献。在这项工作中，我们通过对专门用于数学推理和代码生成的大语言模型进行系统性剪枝研究，为任务专用神经元的存在及其重要性提供了实证证据。

We introduce an activation-based selectivity metric to identify neurons with low contribution to the target task and prune them while preserving target-task accuracy, and compare selective pruning with random pruning. Selective pruning consistently outperforms random pruning, indicating that activation-based selectivity provides a systematic advantage over random pruning.

我们引入了一种基于激活的选择性指标，用于识别对目标任务贡献较低的神经元，并在保持目标任务准确性的前提下将其剪除，同时将选择性剪枝与随机剪枝进行了对比。结果表明，选择性剪枝的表现始终优于随机剪枝，这说明基于激活的选择性方法比随机剪枝具有系统性的优势。

Reverse pruning experiments further show that removing a small subset of highly task-specific neurons (~10%) causes complete performance collapse, suggesting that there exist task specific neurons and critical task information is concentrated in a small portion of the network. In contrast, selective pruning of less critical neurons (~30% - ~35%) reduces accuracy but still preserves significant performance.

反向剪枝实验进一步表明，移除一小部分高度任务专用的神经元（约 10%）会导致模型性能彻底崩溃，这表明确实存在任务专用神经元，且关键的任务信息集中在网络的一小部分中。相比之下，对非关键神经元进行选择性剪枝（约 30% - 35%）虽然会降低准确性，但仍能保留显著的性能。

We also observed consistent reductions in parameters and runtime VRAM usage, along with improved inference throughput as pruning increases. Experiments on both 1.5B and 7B models reveal a robustness threshold around 15-20% pruning, beyond which accuracy loss and generation failures increase sharply. Fine-tuning substantially recovers performance across pruning levels, particularly for aggressively pruned models.

我们还观察到，随着剪枝比例的增加，参数量和运行时显存占用均持续下降，推理吞吐量也得到了提升。在 1.5B 和 7B 模型上的实验揭示了一个约 15-20% 的剪枝鲁棒性阈值，超过该阈值后，准确率损失和生成失败的情况会急剧增加。微调可以在不同剪枝水平下显著恢复模型性能，对于过度剪枝的模型效果尤为明显。

These findings provide empirical evidence of neuron specialization in task-specific language models and offer insights into pruning robustness, model redundancy, and post-pruning recoverability.

这些发现为特定任务语言模型中神经元的专业化提供了实证证据，并为剪枝鲁棒性、模型冗余以及剪枝后的可恢复性提供了深刻见解。
