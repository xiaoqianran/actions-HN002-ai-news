# Is your Purosangue SUV not sharp enough? Ferrari has you covered.
# 觉得你的 Purosangue SUV 不够犀利？法拉利为你准备了升级方案。

Did you know that SUVs now account for 6 in 10 new vehicles sold in Europe? That’s even higher than in the US or China, where market share for lifted hatchbacks currently runs at about 40 percent. So the fact that Ferrari decided to enter the segment with the Purosangue in 2023 should be seen clearly in that context.
你知道吗？目前欧洲每售出 10 辆新车中，就有 6 辆是 SUV。这一比例甚至高于美国或中国，后两者的“加高掀背车”市场份额目前约为 40%。因此，法拉利在 2023 年决定凭借 Purosangue 进入该细分市场，其背景显而易见。

Anyway, four-seat Ferraris aren’t entirely unheard of: I remain a big fan of the looks of the shooting brake FF and GTC4Lusso—if not the reliability of the latter. But the test drivers in Maranello (where Ferrari’s factory is) must have found something a little lacking with the way the Purosangue drove because they got to work on an upgrade for the SUV, which debuted this week.
话说回来，四座法拉利并非闻所未闻：我依然非常喜欢猎装版 FF 和 GTC4Lusso 的外观——尽管后者的可靠性不敢恭维。但马拉内罗（法拉利工厂所在地）的试车手们一定觉得 Purosangue 的驾驶表现还有所欠缺，因此他们着手为这款 SUV 开发了一套升级方案，并于本周正式亮相。

It’s a new Handling Speciale option, featuring new active suspension calibration that better resists the body’s roll, pitch, and yaw, something Ferrari says makes the Purosangue feel more compact than its 16.3 feet (4.9 m) might suggest. Expect Ferrari’s always-quick steering to feel even sharper, then.
这是一项全新的“Handling Speciale”选装配置，采用了新的主动悬挂调校，能更好地抑制车身的侧倾、俯仰和横摆。法拉利表示，这让 Purosangue 开起来比其 16.3 英尺（4.9 米）的车身尺寸所暗示的要紧凑得多。可以预见，法拉利一贯灵敏的转向系统将会变得更加犀利。

The control strategies for the double-clutch paddle-shift gearbox have also been improved, cutting shift times at the expense of a bit of refinement. But then that’s the point: If you want a soothing luxury SUV, many other companies will sell you one. Ferrari buyers want the feeling of the next gear engaging to be a little more brutal, particularly if they’re in one of the more permissive traction and stability control settings (or if those are disengaged entirely). In manual mode, that happens when you shift above 5,500 rpm, Ferrari tells us.
双离合拨片变速箱的控制策略也得到了改进，以牺牲少许平顺性为代价缩短了换挡时间。但这就是重点所在：如果你想要一辆舒适的豪华 SUV，市面上有很多其他品牌可供选择。而法拉利的买家们追求的是换挡时那种更具冲击力的感觉，尤其是在开启更宽松的牵引力和稳定性控制模式（或完全关闭这些系统）时。法拉利告诉我们，在手动模式下，当转速超过 5,500 rpm 时，这种冲击感就会出现。

To let people know you spent an as-yet-unannounced sum on the Handling Speciale option (though if you need to ask…), there are some styling tweaks like diamond-cut wheels, carbon-fiber logo shields on the side, and black accents instead of chrome. I note with interest that the wheel here has buttons, not capacitive panels. Hopefully we can arrange a test drive soon.
为了让别人知道你花了一笔尚未公布金额的费用选装了 Handling Speciale 套件（当然，如果你还需要问价格……），新车还进行了一些外观调整，例如钻石切割轮毂、侧面的碳纤维徽标盾牌，以及取代镀铬装饰的黑色饰件。我饶有兴致地注意到，这里的方向盘上使用的是实体按键，而非电容式触控面板。希望我们能尽快安排一次试驾。

Next up for Maranello is the Luce, its first-ever electric vehicle. So far, we’ve seen details about its powertrain, sound, and user interface, with the full reveal scheduled for May 25.
马拉内罗的下一个重头戏是 Luce，即法拉利的首款电动汽车。到目前为止，我们已经看到了关于其动力系统、声浪和用户界面的细节，全面发布日期定于 5 月 25 日。
