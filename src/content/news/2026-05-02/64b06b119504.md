# As Tim Cook steps down, Apple hit record sales — but a chip shortage looms
# 蒂姆·库克即将卸任，苹果创下销售纪录——但芯片短缺阴云笼罩

Apple reported a record quarter on Thursday. Yet outgoing CEO Tim Cook warned of some gathering storm clouds in the form of memory chip supply issues that could impact business in the near future.
苹果公司周四公布了创纪录的季度财报。然而，即将卸任的首席执行官蒂姆·库克（Tim Cook）警告称，存储芯片供应问题带来的阴云正在聚集，可能会在不久的将来影响公司业务。

“Today Apple is proud to report our best March quarter ever, with revenue of $111.2 billion and double-digit growth across every geographic segment,” Cook said during Thursday’s earnings call. “iPhone achieved a March quarter revenue record, fueled by such extraordinary demand for the iPhone 17 lineup.”
“今天，苹果很自豪地宣布我们有史以来表现最好的三月季度，营收达到 1112 亿美元，且所有地理区域均实现了两位数的增长，”库克在周四的财报电话会议上表示。“得益于 iPhone 17 系列的非凡需求，iPhone 创下了三月季度的营收纪录。”

Less rosily, Cook relayed that Apple spent more on memory chips in March than in previous quarters, though the company’s costs were offset by its ability to sell stockpiled inventory. But, he warned, the expectation is “significantly higher memory costs” in June and beyond — the likes of which may “drive an increasing impact” on the business.
情况不那么乐观的是，库克透露苹果在三月份的存储芯片支出高于往季度，尽管公司通过销售库存抵消了部分成本。但他警告称，预计六月及以后“存储成本将显著上升”，这可能会对业务产生“日益增长的影响”。

Cook was referencing what has commonly been called “RAMageddon,” the trend of the AI industry guzzling up memory chips with such astonishing gusto it is spurring shortages. This is driving up the prices of hardware. Apple is primarily a hardware company, so that’s obviously not great news for its core products.
库克所指的是通常被称为“内存末日”（RAMageddon）的现象，即人工智能行业以惊人的速度消耗存储芯片，从而引发了短缺。这推高了硬件价格。苹果主要是一家硬件公司，因此这对它的核心产品来说显然不是好消息。

Most notably, the chip shortage has impacted the iPhone. Despite the strong sales figures touted by Apple on Thursday, it has previously been reported that RAM costs have quadrupled — impacting phone production costs and putting John Ternus, Apple’s incoming CEO, in a less-than-enviable position. One possible result may be that Apple increases prices for the iPhone.
最值得注意的是，芯片短缺已经影响到了 iPhone。尽管苹果周四吹捧了强劲的销售数据，但此前有报道称内存成本已经翻了两番，这影响了手机生产成本，并使苹果即将上任的首席执行官约翰·特努斯（John Ternus）处于一个不太令人羡慕的境地。一个可能的结果是苹果会提高 iPhone 的价格。

“There’s just a little less flexibility in the supply chain at the moment for getting more parts,” Cook told Reuters on Thursday.
“目前供应链在获取更多零部件方面的灵活性确实有所下降，”库克周四对路透社表示。

Ternus, who has served as Apple’s senior vice president of hardware engineering, was on Thursday’s earnings call and praised Cook. “In my view, Tim is one of the greatest business leaders of all time. Stepping into the role of CEO is an incredible honor, and it means a great deal to me to have Tim’s trust and confidence,” Ternus said.
曾担任苹果硬件工程高级副总裁的特努斯出席了周四的财报电话会议，并称赞了库克。“在我看来，蒂姆是有史以来最伟大的商业领袖之一。能够担任首席执行官一职是莫大的荣幸，得到蒂姆的信任和信心对我来说意义重大，”特努斯说道。

He will obviously have his work cut out for him when he begins the job on September 1. But he will still have Cook’s supply chain experience to lean on for a while. Cook will become executive chairman.
显然，当他在 9 月 1 日上任时，他将面临艰巨的任务。但他仍可以在一段时间内依靠库克的供应链经验。库克将出任执行董事长。
