# Reconstruction by Generation: 3D Multi-Object Scene Reconstruction from Sparse Observations
# 生成式重建：基于稀疏观测的 3D 多物体场景重建

**Abstract:** Accurately reconstructing complex full multi-object scenes from sparse observations remains a core challenge in computer vision and a key step toward scalable and reliable simulation for robotics. 
**摘要：** 从稀疏观测中准确重建复杂的多物体场景，仍然是计算机视觉领域的核心挑战，也是实现可扩展且可靠的机器人仿真技术的关键一步。

In this work, we introduce RecGen, a generative framework for probabilistic joint estimation of object and part shapes, as well as their pose under occlusion and partial visibility from one or multiple RGB-D images. 
在这项工作中，我们引入了 RecGen，这是一个生成式框架，用于从单张或多张 RGB-D 图像中，对物体及其部件的形状，以及在遮挡和部分可见情况下的位姿进行概率联合估计。

By leveraging compositional synthetic scene generation and strong 3D shape priors, RecGen generalizes across diverse object types and real-world environments. 
通过利用组合式合成场景生成技术和强大的 3D 形状先验，RecGen 能够泛化应用于各种物体类型和真实世界环境。

RecGen achieves state-of-the-art performance on complex, heavily occluded datasets, robustly handling severe occlusions, symmetric objects, object parts, and intricate geometry and texture. 
RecGen 在复杂且存在严重遮挡的数据集上实现了最先进的性能，能够稳健地处理严重遮挡、对称物体、物体部件以及复杂的几何结构和纹理。

Despite using nearly 80% fewer training meshes than the previous state of the art SAM3D, RecGen outperforms it by 30.1% in geometric shape quality, 9.1% in texture reconstruction, and 33.9% in pose estimation.
尽管与之前的最先进模型 SAM3D 相比，RecGen 使用的训练网格（meshes）减少了近 80%，但在几何形状质量上仍超越其 30.1%，在纹理重建上提升了 9.1%，在位姿估计上提升了 33.9%。
