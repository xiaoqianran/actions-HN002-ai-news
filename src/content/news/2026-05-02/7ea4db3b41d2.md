# Unpacking Vibe Coding: Help-Seeking Processes in Student-AI Interactions While Programming
# 解构“氛围编程”（Vibe Coding）：学生与人工智能在编程交互中的求助过程

**Abstract:** Generative AI is reshaping higher education programming through vibe coding, where students collaborate with AI via natural language rather than writing code line-by-line. We conceptualize this practice as help-seeking, analyzing 19,418 interaction turns from 110 undergraduate students.
**摘要：** 生成式人工智能正通过“氛围编程”（vibe coding）重塑高等教育编程教学。在这种模式下，学生不再逐行编写代码，而是通过自然语言与人工智能协作。我们将这种实践概念化为一种“求助行为”，并分析了来自 110 名本科生的 19,418 次交互记录。

Using inductive coding and Heterogeneous Transition Network Analysis, we examined interaction sequences to compare top- and low-performing students. Results reveal that top performers engaged in instrumental help-seeking -- inquiry and exploration -- eliciting tutor-like AI responses.
通过归纳编码和异构转换网络分析（Heterogeneous Transition Network Analysis），我们研究了交互序列，以比较高绩效学生与低绩效学生的差异。结果显示，高绩效学生倾向于进行“工具性求助”——即通过询问和探索，引导人工智能给出类似导师的反馈。

In contrast, low performers relied on executive help-seeking, frequently delegating tasks and prompting the AI to assume an executor role focused on ready-made solutions. These findings indicate that currently generative AI mirrors student intent (whether productive or passive) rather than optimizing for learning.
相比之下，低绩效学生则依赖“执行性求助”，频繁地将任务外包，并促使人工智能扮演执行者角色，直接提供现成的解决方案。这些发现表明，目前的生成式人工智能更多是反映了学生的意图（无论是积极的还是被动的），而非针对学习效果进行优化。

To evolve from tools to teammates, AI systems must move beyond passive compliance. We argue for pedagogically aligned design that detect unproductive delegation and adaptively steer educational interactions toward inquiry, ensuring student-AI partnerships augment rather than replace cognitive effort.
为了从单纯的工具进化为学习伙伴，人工智能系统必须超越被动的顺从。我们主张采用符合教学法的设计，能够识别非生产性的任务外包，并自适应地将教育交互引导向探究式学习，从而确保学生与人工智能的合作能够增强而非取代学生的认知努力。
