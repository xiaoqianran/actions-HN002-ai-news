# Cross-Lingual Response Consistency in Large Language Models: An ILR-Informed Evaluation of Claude Across Six Languages
# 大型语言模型中的跨语言响应一致性：基于 ILR 的 Claude 六语言评估

**Abstract:** This paper introduces a systematic evaluation framework grounded in the Interagency Language Roundtable (ILR) Skill Level Descriptions and applies it to Claude (Sonnet 4.6) across six languages: English, French, Romanian, Spanish, Italian, and German. 

**摘要：** 本文引入了一个基于“跨部门语言圆桌会议”（ILR）技能等级描述的系统性评估框架，并将其应用于 Claude (Sonnet 4.6) 在六种语言（英语、法语、罗马尼亚语、西班牙语、意大利语和德语）中的表现。

We administer a battery of 12 semantically equivalent prompt clusters spanning ILR complexity levels 1 through 3+, collect 216 responses (12 prompts, 6 languages, 3 runs), and analyze outputs through a two-layer methodology combining automated quantitative metrics with expert ILR qualitative assessment. 

我们执行了一组包含 12 个语义等效的提示词簇，涵盖了 ILR 复杂度 1 到 3+ 级，共收集了 216 条响应（12 个提示词、6 种语言、3 次运行），并通过结合自动化定量指标与专家 ILR 定性评估的双层方法对输出结果进行了分析。

Quantitative analysis reveals that French responses are approximately 30% longer than German responses on identical prompts, and that creative and affective clusters show the highest cross-lingual surface divergence. 

定量分析显示，在相同提示词下，法语响应比德语响应平均长约 30%，且创意类和情感类提示词簇表现出最高的跨语言表面差异。

Qualitative analysis, conducted by a six-language professional with 12 years of ILR/OPI assessment experience, identifies five cross-lingual variation patterns: systematic differences in pragmatic disambiguation strategies, aesthetic and literary tradition divergence in creative output, language-internal technical terminology norms, cultural calibration gaps evidenced by the absence of culture-specific content in favor of culturally neutralized templates, and language-specific institutional referral behavior in emotional support responses. 

由一位拥有 12 年 ILR/OPI 评估经验的六语言专家进行的定性分析，识别出了五种跨语言变异模式：语用消歧策略的系统性差异、创意输出中审美与文学传统的背离、语言内部的技术术语规范、因缺乏文化特定内容而倾向于使用文化中性模板所导致的文化校准差距，以及在情感支持响应中特定语言的机构转介行为。

We argue that ILR-informed expert judgment applied to LLM outputs constitutes a novel and underreported evaluation methodology that complements purely computational benchmarks, and that cross-lingual output variation in Claude is interpretable, domain-dependent, and consequential for equitable multilingual AI deployment.

我们认为，将基于 ILR 的专家判断应用于大语言模型输出，构成了一种新颖且尚未被充分报道的评估方法，它补充了纯计算基准的不足；同时，Claude 的跨语言输出变异是可解释的、依赖于领域的，且对于实现公平的多语言 AI 部署具有重要意义。
