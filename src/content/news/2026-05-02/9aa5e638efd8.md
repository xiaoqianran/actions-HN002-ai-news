# Spotify adds 'Verified' badges to distinguish human artists from AI
# Spotify 推出“认证”徽章，以区分人类艺术家与 AI

Spotify is introducing a 'Verified' badge to help users identify when artists on its platform are human, not AI-generated.
Spotify 正在引入一种“认证”徽章，旨在帮助用户识别平台上的艺术家是人类，而非 AI 生成的虚拟形象。

The world's most-used music streaming service said the 'Verified by Spotify' text and green checkmark icon would appear next to artist names when they meet "defined standards demonstrating authenticity". This could include having linked social accounts on their artist profile, consistent listener activity or other "signals of a real artist behind the profile," the company said, such as merchandise or concert dates.
这家全球使用率最高的音乐流媒体服务商表示，当艺术家符合“证明真实性的既定标准”时，其名字旁将显示“Spotify 认证”文字和绿色勾选图标。该公司称，这些标准可能包括在艺术家个人资料中关联社交账号、保持稳定的听众活跃度，或其他“证明个人资料背后是真实艺术家”的信号，例如周边商品或演唱会日期。

In its blog post, Spotify said "more than 99%" of the artists listeners actively search for will be verified, representing "hundreds of thousands of artists".
Spotify 在其博客文章中表示，听众主动搜索的艺术家中将有“超过 99%”获得认证，这涵盖了“数十万名艺术家”。

It said the process would prioritise acts with "important contributions to music culture and history", rather than "content farms," with the platform rolling out verification and badges over the coming weeks.
该公司表示，认证流程将优先考虑那些对“音乐文化和历史有重要贡献”的艺人，而非“内容农场”。该平台将在未来几周内逐步推出认证和徽章功能。

With Spotify targeting AI-generated music and personas, some on social media have pointed out a verified account would only prove an artist was human, not that the music was made without utilising AI.
随着 Spotify 将矛头指向 AI 生成的音乐和虚拟形象，社交媒体上的一些人指出，认证账户只能证明艺术家是人类，并不能证明其音乐创作过程中没有使用 AI。

Ed Newton-Rex, a campaigner for creators' rights and former AI executive, said Spotify's approach could "punish real human artists who don't have some of the markers the verification is based on," like touring or selling merchandise.
创作者权利倡导者、前 AI 高管 Ed Newton-Rex 表示，Spotify 的这种做法可能会“惩罚那些不具备认证标准中某些特征（如巡演或销售周边）的真实人类艺术家”。

Instead, he suggests Spotify could be "automatically labelling any AI-generated music" as some other streaming services do.
他建议，Spotify 可以像其他一些流媒体服务那样，“自动标记任何 AI 生成的音乐”。

Professor of Music at the University of Durham Nick Collins said Spotify's decision was "unsurprising" given the "ongoing furore around generative AI" but added it would be a trickier task if it ever tried to label the music itself.
杜伦大学音乐学教授 Nick Collins 表示，考虑到“围绕生成式 AI 的持续争议”，Spotify 的这一决定“并不令人意外”，但他补充说，如果 Spotify 试图直接对音乐本身进行标记，那将是一项更棘手的任务。

"AI usage is not a binary position between 'entirely authentically handmade' and 'fully AI generated' but can have lots of in-between cases," he said. "We can probably welcome some sort of tagging system like this, though it may favour the more commercial and successful artists already active rather than new independent artists."
“AI 的使用并非‘完全纯手工制作’与‘完全 AI 生成’这种二元对立，中间存在许多模糊地带，”他说。“我们或许可以欢迎这种标签系统，尽管它可能更偏向于那些已经活跃且商业上成功的艺术家，而非新兴的独立艺术家。”

### Spotify's AI problem
### Spotify 的 AI 困境

Spotify has come under fire in recent years for its approach to AI-generated content on its site. One Leipzig-based software developer built his own tool to label and block AI music on the platform.
近年来，Spotify 因其处理平台上 AI 生成内容的方式而备受抨击。一位来自莱比锡的软件开发者甚至开发了自己的工具，用于标记和屏蔽平台上的 AI 音乐。

Posts by users in its community forums have frequently requested a "clear label for AI-generated songs" or for the site to "explain why subscribers should pay full price while the platform fills up with AI-generated music".
其社区论坛中的用户经常发帖要求为“AI 生成的歌曲提供清晰的标签”，或者要求平台“解释为什么在平台充斥着 AI 生成音乐的情况下，订阅用户仍需支付全额费用”。

In 2023, the then chief executive of Spotify Daniel Ek told the BBC he had no plans to completely ban content created by artificial intelligence from the platform.
2023 年，时任 Spotify 首席执行官的 Daniel Ek 曾告诉 BBC，他没有计划完全禁止平台上由人工智能创作的内容。

Meanwhile in 2025, a band called The Velvet Sundown, which had a verified page on the network with 850,000 monthly listeners, prompted accusations they and their music were AI-generated when it transpired they had never given interviews nor had any record of performing live.
与此同时，在 2025 年，一个名为 The Velvet Sundown 的乐队在平台上拥有认证页面和每月 85 万的听众，但当人们发现他们从未接受过采访，也没有任何现场演出记录时，引发了关于他们及其音乐是否为 AI 生成的质疑。

However, their profile now identifies them as a "synthetic music project... with the support of artificial intelligence," with 126,000 monthly listeners.
然而，他们的个人资料现在已将其标识为“一个在人工智能支持下的合成音乐项目”，目前每月拥有 12.6 万名听众。
