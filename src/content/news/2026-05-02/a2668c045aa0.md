# HQ-UNet: A Hybrid Quantum-Classical U-Net with a Quantum Bottleneck for Remote Sensing Image Segmentation

**HQ-UNet：一种用于遥感图像分割的量子瓶颈混合量子经典 U-Net**

***

**Abstract:** Semantic segmentation in remote sensing is commonly addressed using classical deep learning architectures such as U-Net, which require a large number of parameters to model complex spatial relationships. 

**摘要：** 遥感中的语义分割通常使用经典的深度学习架构（如 U-Net）来解决，这些架构需要大量的参数来建模复杂的空间关系。

Quantum machine learning (QML) provides an alternative representation paradigm by mapping classical features into quantum states, but its direct application to high-dimensional images remains challenging under near-term quantum hardware constraints. 

量子机器学习（QML）通过将经典特征映射到量子态，提供了一种替代的表示范式，但在近期的量子硬件限制下，将其直接应用于高维图像仍然具有挑战性。

In this work, we propose HQ-UNet, a hybrid quantum-classical U-Net architecture that integrates a compact parameterized quantum circuit at the bottleneck of a classical U-Net. 

在这项工作中，我们提出了 HQ-UNet，这是一种混合量子经典 U-Net 架构，它在经典 U-Net 的瓶颈处集成了一个紧凑的参数化量子电路。

The proposed design uses a non-pooling quantum convolutional module to enrich highly compressed encoder features before decoding, while keeping the quantum component shallow and parameter-efficient. 

该设计使用非池化量子卷积模块，在解码前丰富高度压缩的编码器特征，同时保持量子组件的浅层结构和参数高效性。

Experiments on the dataset show that HQ-UNet achieves a mean IoU of 0.8050 and an overall accuracy of 94.76%, outperforming the classical U-Net baseline. 

在数据集上的实验表明，HQ-UNet 的平均交并比（mean IoU）达到 0.8050，总体准确率为 94.76%，优于经典的 U-Net 基准模型。

These results suggest that compact quantum bottlenecks can enhance feature representation for remote sensing image segmentation under near-term quantum constraints. 

这些结果表明，在近期的量子限制下，紧凑的量子瓶颈可以增强遥感图像分割的特征表示。

This highlights the potential of hybrid quantum-classical designs as a promising direction for parameter-efficient dense prediction in Earth observation.

这凸显了混合量子经典设计作为地球观测中参数高效密集预测的一个有前途的方向的潜力。
