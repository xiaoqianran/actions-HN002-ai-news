# AttriBE: Quantifying Attribute Expressivity in Body Embeddings for Recognition and Identification
# AttriBE：量化身体嵌入中用于识别与鉴定的属性表达力

**Abstract:** Person re-identification (ReID) systems that match individuals across images or video frames are essential in many real-world applications. However, existing methods are often influenced by attributes such as gender, pose, and body mass index (BMI), which vary in unconstrained settings and raise concerns related to fairness and generalization.

**摘要：** 人员重识别（ReID）系统在跨图像或视频帧匹配个体方面，在许多现实应用中至关重要。然而，现有方法往往受到性别、姿态和身体质量指数（BMI）等属性的影响，这些属性在非受限环境下会发生变化，从而引发了关于公平性和泛化能力的担忧。

To address this, we extend the notion of expressivity, defined as the mutual information between learned features and specific attributes, using a secondary neural network to quantify how strongly attributes are encoded. Applying this framework to three transformer-based ReID models on a large-scale visible-spectrum dataset, we find that BMI consistently shows the highest expressivity in deeper layers.

为了解决这一问题，我们扩展了“表达力”（expressivity）的概念——将其定义为学习到的特征与特定属性之间的互信息——并利用一个辅助神经网络来量化属性被编码的强度。我们将该框架应用于大规模可见光谱数据集上的三个基于 Transformer 的 ReID 模型，发现 BMI 在深层网络中始终表现出最高的表达力。

Attributes in the final representation are ranked as BMI > Pitch > Gender > Yaw, and expressivity evolves across layers and training epochs, with pose peaking in intermediate layers and BMI strengthening with depth. We further extend the analysis to cross-spectral person identification across infrared modalities including short-wave, medium-wave, and long-wave infrared.

最终表示中的属性排序为：BMI > 俯仰角（Pitch） > 性别 > 偏航角（Yaw）。表达力会随着层数和训练轮次的增加而演变，其中姿态在中间层达到峰值，而 BMI 则随深度增加而增强。我们进一步将分析扩展到跨红外模态（包括短波、中波和长波红外）的跨光谱人员识别。

In this setting, pitch becomes comparable to BMI and attribute trends increase monotonically across depth, suggesting increased reliance on structural cues when bridging modality gaps. Overall, the results show that transformer-based ReID embeddings encode a hierarchy of implicit attributes, with morphometric information persistently embedded and pose contributing more strongly under cross-spectral conditions.

在这种设置下，俯仰角的重要性变得与 BMI 相当，且属性趋势随深度单调增加，这表明在跨越模态鸿沟时，模型对结构性线索的依赖性增强。总体而言，研究结果表明，基于 Transformer 的 ReID 嵌入编码了一个隐式属性的层级结构，其中形态测量信息被持续嵌入，而姿态在跨光谱条件下贡献更为显著。
