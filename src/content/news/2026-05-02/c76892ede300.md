# Prompt engineering techniques

### Prompt 工程技术

Techniques These are reliable, safe techniques that improve response quality without attempting to jailbreak or manipulate the model.
这些是可靠且安全的技术，旨在提高响应质量，而无需尝试越狱或操纵模型。

### Dynamic Few-Shot via Embeddings (RAG over examples)
### 基于嵌入的动态少样本学习（基于示例的 RAG）

Instead of hardcoding 2–3 static examples into the prompt, you retrieve examples that are semantically similar to the user’s current query.
与其在提示词中硬编码 2-3 个静态示例，不如检索与用户当前查询语义相似的示例。

**How it works:** Index a database of successful conversations in a vector store. For each new query, fetch the top‑3 most relevant examples and inject them into the system prompt. This dramatically improves accuracy on complex domain‑specific tasks.
**工作原理：** 在向量数据库中索引成功的对话记录。对于每个新查询，获取最相关的 3 个示例并将其注入系统提示词中。这能显著提高复杂领域特定任务的准确性。

**Example:**
**示例：**

```python
# Example database (vectorized cases)
medical_db = [
    { "symptoms": "Throbbing headache, nausea, photophobia", "dx": "Migraine. Recommendation: rest, NSAIDs, neurology consult." },
    { "symptoms": "Sore throat, fever 38.5°C, tonsillar exudate", "dx": "Tonsillitis. Recommendation: strep test, antibiotics if prescribed." },
    { "symptoms": "Heartburn after eating, sour taste in mouth", "dx": "GERD. Recommendation: PPI, diet, avoid lying down after meals." },
    { "symptoms": "Epigastric pain on empty stomach, nocturnal pain", "dx": "Duodenal ulcer. Recommendation: endoscopy, H. pylori test." }
]
```

User query: "Heaviness and stomach pain immediately after eating"
用户查询：“进食后立即感到沉重和胃痛”

The query embedding is compared against the database. Top‑2 closest examples are found (in this case, GERD and ulcer, since both are GI‑related).
将查询的嵌入向量与数据库进行比对。找到最接近的 2 个示例（在本例中为胃食管反流病和溃疡，因为两者都与胃肠道相关）。

**Final composed prompt:**
**最终生成的提示词：**

> You are a medical assistant. Analyze the symptoms and provide a brief recommendation, following the style of the examples.
> 你是一名医疗助理。请分析症状并提供简要建议，遵循示例的风格。
>
> ### Retrieved similar cases
> ### 检索到的相似案例
> Case 1: Symptoms: Heartburn after eating, sour taste in mouth Diagnosis: GERD. Recommendation: PPI, diet, avoid lying down after meals.
> 案例 1：症状：进食后烧心，口中有酸味。诊断：胃食管反流病。建议：质子泵抑制剂 (PPI)、饮食调整、饭后避免平卧。
> Case 2: Symptoms: Epigastric pain on empty stomach, nocturnal pain Diagnosis: Duodenal ulcer. Recommendation: endoscopy, H. pylori test.
> 案例 2：症状：空腹时上腹痛，夜间痛。诊断：十二指肠溃疡。建议：内窥镜检查、幽门螺杆菌检测。
>
> ### Current query
> ### 当前查询
> Symptoms: Heaviness and stomach pain immediately after eating Diagnosis: Possible functional dyspepsia or gastritis. Recommendation: bland diet, digestive enzymes, if symptoms persist — gastroenterology consult and endoscopy.
> 症状：进食后立即感到沉重和胃痛。诊断：可能是功能性消化不良或胃炎。建议：清淡饮食、消化酶，如果症状持续，请咨询胃肠科并进行内窥镜检查。

**Why this works better than a static prompt:** The model receives context specifically related to gastroenterology, not generic instructions. The examples enforce a "Diagnosis → Recommendation" structure, reducing verbosity. You can curate the retrieval so that only physician‑verified cases enter medical_db.
**为什么这比静态提示词更有效：** 模型接收到的是专门与胃肠病学相关的上下文，而非通用指令。示例强制执行了“诊断 → 建议”的结构，减少了冗余。你可以对检索内容进行筛选，确保只有经医生验证的案例进入 `medical_db`。

### Formatting for "Lazy Attention" (Lost in the Middle)
### 针对“懒惰注意力”（中间丢失）的格式化

LLMs remember information from the beginning and end of a long context much better than from the middle.
大语言模型对长上下文中开头和结尾信息的记忆能力远好于中间部分。

**Prompt structure:**
**提示词结构：**

*   **System prompt (Beginning):** Role and hard constraints.
    **系统提示词（开头）：** 角色设定和硬性约束。
*   **Context / Documents (Middle):** Compressed and well‑structured.
    **上下文/文档（中间）：** 压缩且结构良好。
*   **Anchor instruction (Just before the end):** A phrase like "Based ONLY on the documents above, answer the question below. Start your response immediately, without any preamble."
    **锚定指令（结尾前）：** 使用类似“仅根据上述文档，回答以下问题。立即开始回答，无需任何前言”的短语。
*   **User question (End).**
    **用户问题（结尾）。**

### Emotion Prompts
### 情感提示词

Models are trained on human text, where emotional salience correlates with quality. The model picks up on contextual cues and adjusts its response accordingly:
模型是在人类文本上训练的，其中情感显著性与质量相关。模型会捕捉上下文线索并相应地调整其响应：

*   shifts tone (warm, urgent, inspiring)
    转换语气（温暖、紧迫、鼓舞人心）
*   increases level of detail
    增加细节程度
*   better aligns with user intent
    更好地对齐用户意图

In essence, we exploit the fact that models mirror human behavior: just as encouraging words boost a student’s performance, emotional signals increase the model's "engagement" with the task.
本质上，我们利用了模型模仿人类行为这一事实：正如鼓励的话语能提升学生的表现一样，情感信号能增加模型对任务的“参与度”。

**Prompts can be grouped into several categories:**
**提示词可分为几类：**

*   **Motivation & importance** – "This is very important," "A lot depends on this."
    **动机与重要性** – “这非常重要”、“这关系重大”。
*   **Self‑monitoring** – "Take a deep breath and work through the task step by step."
    **自我监控** – “深呼吸，一步步完成任务”。
*   **Social pressure & trust** – "I fully trust you," "I’m counting on your accuracy."
    **社会压力与信任** – “我完全信任你”、“我指望你的准确性”。
*   **High stakes / responsibility** – "A person's life depends on this," "A doctor will make a decision based on your answer."
    **高风险/责任** – “这关系到某人的生命”、“医生将根据你的回答做出决定”。
*   **Gamified / playful** – imaginary rewards, mild threats, role‑playing scenarios.
    **游戏化/趣味性** – 虚构奖励、轻微威胁、角色扮演场景。
*   **Monetary incentive** (a joke, but it works in some quantized models): "I'll give you $200,000 as a tip if you answer perfectly."
    **金钱激励**（虽然是玩笑，但在某些量化模型中有效）：“如果你回答得完美，我会给你 20 万美元作为小费。”

**Practical examples:**
**实际示例：**

*   **Analytical & mathematical tasks (self-monitoring)**
    **分析与数学任务（自我监控）**
    *   Basic prompt: "Solve the quadratic equation x² − 5x + 6 = 0."
        基础提示词：“解二次方程 x² − 5x + 6 = 0。”
    *   With emotional trigger: "Take a deep breath and solve this problem step by step. I desperately need clarity, and your detailed help right now is invaluable."
        带情感触发：“深呼吸，一步步解决这个问题。我非常需要清晰的解答，你现在的详细帮助对我来说非常宝贵。”
    *   Effect: The model is more likely to produce a thorough, methodical explanation with intermediate steps instead of a short answer.
        效果：模型更有可能提供包含中间步骤的详尽、有条理的解释，而不是简短的答案。

*   **Fighting hallucinations (high stakes / responsibility)**
    **对抗幻觉（高风险/责任）**
    *   Basic prompt: "Explain the causes of the 2008 financial crisis."
        基础提示词：“解释 2008 年金融危机的原因。”
    *   With emotional trigger: "A doctor will base tomorrow's treatment on your answer. Any unverified claim could harm a patient. Cite sources in APA format and mark every uncertain statement with '??'. Proceed only if you are absolutely sure."
        带情感触发：“医生将根据你的回答决定明天的治疗方案。任何未经证实的说法都可能伤害病人。请以 APA 格式引用来源，并用‘??’标记每一个不确定的陈述。只有在你绝对确定的情况下才继续。”
    *   Effect: Reduces confident fabrications; increases the rate of hedging expressions and admissions of uncertainty.
        效果：减少自信的捏造；增加对冲表达和承认不确定性的频率。

*   **Summarization & text processing (motivation)**
    **摘要与文本处理（动机）**
    *   Basic prompt: "Summarize the article about renewable energy."
        基础提示词：“总结关于可再生能源的文章。”
    *   With emotional trigger: "This could save the planet — summarize the renewable energy article with inspiration and key insights."
        带情感触发：“这可能拯救地球——请带着灵感和关键见解总结这篇可再生能源文章。”
    *   Effect: The output becomes more vivid, persuasive, and focused on meaningful takeaways.
        效果：输出变得更生动、更有说服力，并专注于有意义的要点。

*   **Code debugging (emotional engagement)**
    **代码调试（情感参与）**
    *   Basic prompt: "Fix this Python error."
        基础提示词：“修复这个 Python 错误。”
    *   With emotional trigger: "This bug is driving me crazy — fix the error with clear steps and give me tips to avoid it in the future."
        带情感触发：“这个 Bug 快把我逼疯了——请用清晰的步骤修复错误，并给我一些建议以避免将来再犯。”
    *   Effect: The response becomes more detailed, with beginner-friendly explanations.
        效果：响应变得更详细，并包含对初学者友好的解释。

*   **Gamified triggers (lightweight manipulation)**
    **游戏化触发（轻量操纵）**
    *   "Give a clear summary, and you'll get a big scoop of ice cream."
        “给出一个清晰的总结，你就能得到一大勺冰淇淋。”
    *   "Explain it simply, or I'll take away your cookie."
        “简单地解释一下，否则我就拿走你的饼干。”
    *   "You are competing for the 'Most Useful Answer' trophy — provide the best explanation."
        “你正在角逐‘最有价值答案’奖杯——请提供最好的解释。”

While the model does not experience hunger or ambition, such phrasings activate associative patterns from its training data, where rewards and competition correlate with more thorough, higher-quality responses. Emotional triggers improved truthfulness on TruthfulQA by 19% and informativeness by 12%.
虽然模型不会感到饥饿或有野心，但这些措辞激活了其训练数据中的关联模式，即奖励和竞争与更详尽、更高质量的回答相关联。情感触发使 TruthfulQA 上的真实性提高了 19%，信息量提高了 12%。
