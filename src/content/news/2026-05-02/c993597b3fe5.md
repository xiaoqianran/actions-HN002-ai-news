# Musk v. Altman is just getting started
# 马斯克与奥特曼的对决才刚刚开始

Elon Musk spent the better part of three days on the witness stand this week in his lawsuit against OpenAI, and it’s already getting messy. Emails, texts, and his own tweets are surfacing in court, and there are plenty more witnesses to come.

本周，埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）在针对 OpenAI 的诉讼中，在证人席上度过了大半个三天，案件审理已经变得错综复杂。电子邮件、短信以及他本人发布的推文正在法庭上被逐一披露，而后续还有更多的证人即将出庭。

Musk’s argument against OpenAI? By converting the company to a for-profit model, Sam Altman betrayed the “nonprofit for the benefit of humanity” mission Musk signed up to fund. As Musk keeps reminding the courtroom: “You can’t steal a charity.”

马斯克针对 OpenAI 的论点是什么？他认为，通过将公司转变为营利性模式，萨姆·奥特曼（Sam Altman）背叛了马斯克当初注资时所支持的“造福人类的非营利性”使命。正如马斯克不断提醒法庭的那样：“你不能窃取一家慈善机构。”
