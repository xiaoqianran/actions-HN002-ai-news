# ChatGPT Images 2.0 is a hit in India, but not a big winner elsewhere, yet
# ChatGPT Images 2.0 在印度大受欢迎，但在其他地区尚未取得显著成功

India has emerged as the largest user base for ChatGPT Images 2.0 since its launch last week, OpenAI said on Thursday. However, third-party data reviewed by TechCrunch points to a more measured global response, with limited overall growth alongside sharp spikes in select emerging markets.
OpenAI 周四表示，自上周发布以来，印度已成为 ChatGPT Images 2.0 最大的用户群所在地。然而，TechCrunch 审查的第三方数据显示，全球范围内的反应较为平淡，整体增长有限，仅在部分新兴市场出现了激增。

ChatGPT Images 2.0, OpenAI’s latest image-generation upgrade, is designed to handle more complex prompts and produce detailed visuals, including accurate text across multiple languages. Early patterns from the company suggest users — especially in India, its largest market — are using it to create personal visuals such as avatars, stylized portraits, and fantasy-themed images.
ChatGPT Images 2.0 是 OpenAI 最新的图像生成升级版本，旨在处理更复杂的提示词并生成精细的视觉内容，包括准确呈现多种语言的文本。该公司观察到的早期使用模式显示，用户（尤其是其最大市场印度）正利用它来创作个人视觉内容，如头像、风格化肖像和奇幻主题图像。

Data shared by Sensor Tower and Similarweb with TechCrunch suggests the rollout has led to a more mixed global response. ChatGPT’s app downloads rose 11% week-over-week following the launch, per Sensor Tower, but overall engagement gains were modest, with daily active users and sessions up only around 1%. Similarweb data also shows a limited increase in ChatGPT’s global web traffic, rising about 1.6% week-over-week during the same period.
Sensor Tower 和 Similarweb 与 TechCrunch 分享的数据表明，此次发布在全球范围内引发了褒贬不一的反应。据 Sensor Tower 数据，发布后 ChatGPT 的应用下载量环比增长了 11%，但整体参与度提升并不明显，日活跃用户和会话量仅增长了约 1%。Similarweb 的数据也显示，同期 ChatGPT 的全球网络流量增长有限，环比仅上升了约 1.6%。

However, Sensor Tower data indicates some emerging markets — including Pakistan, Vietnam, and Indonesia — saw sharper spikes in ChatGPT’s app downloads, with increases of up to 79% week-over-week during the rollout period. India, meanwhile, remained a major source of activity during the rollout. Sensor Tower estimates show ChatGPT was downloaded about 5 million times in India during the launch week, compared with roughly 2 million in the U.S., though growth remained modest on a week-over-week basis. Similarweb data also points to a limited uptick in engagement, with daily active users in India rising about 3.4% week-over-week during the same period.
然而，Sensor Tower 的数据显示，一些新兴市场（包括巴基斯坦、越南和印度尼西亚）的 ChatGPT 应用下载量出现了更剧烈的激增，在发布期间环比增长高达 79%。与此同时，印度在发布期间依然是活跃度的主要来源。Sensor Tower 估计，发布周内 ChatGPT 在印度的下载量约为 500 万次，而美国约为 200 万次，尽管环比增长保持温和。Similarweb 的数据也显示参与度提升有限，同期印度日活跃用户环比增长约 3.4%。

In India, the early trends suggest ChatGPT Images 2.0 is largely being used as a form of self-expression. Rather than purely functional outputs, users are creating studio-style portraits from everyday photos, social media-ready images, and imaginative visuals that place themselves at the center, OpenAI said. The early patterns also highlight how AI image tools are being adopted differently across markets. While India’s large user base is driving overall scale, sharper spikes in countries like Pakistan and Indonesia point to stronger new-user demand in emerging markets following the launch.
在印度，早期趋势表明 ChatGPT Images 2.0 主要被用作一种自我表达的方式。OpenAI 表示，用户并非仅仅追求功能性输出，而是通过日常照片创作影棚级肖像、适合社交媒体的图片，以及以自己为中心的创意视觉内容。这些早期模式也凸显了 AI 图像工具在不同市场中的采用方式存在差异。虽然印度庞大的用户群推动了整体规模，但巴基斯坦和印度尼西亚等国出现的更剧烈激增，表明发布后新兴市场对新用户的吸引力更强。

OpenAI’s Images 2.0 launch comes amid intensifying competition in AI image generation. Google’s earlier image-focused model, the original Nano Banana, also saw strong early traction in India, indicating how the nation has become an important market for image generation. With the new ChatGPT Images release, OpenAI is pushing further with improvements such as better rendering of non-Latin text, including Hindi and Bengali, and new “thinking” capabilities that allow it to refine outputs and generate multiple variations from a single prompt.
OpenAI 发布 Images 2.0 正值 AI 图像生成领域竞争加剧之际。谷歌早期的图像模型 Nano Banana 最初在印度也获得了强劲的早期反响，这表明印度已成为图像生成领域的重要市场。随着新版 ChatGPT Images 的发布，OpenAI 进一步改进了功能，例如更好地渲染非拉丁语系文本（包括印地语和孟加拉语），以及新的“思考”能力，使其能够优化输出并根据单个提示词生成多种变体。

Beyond stylized portraits and avatars, OpenAI said early Images 2.0 users in India are experimenting with a wider range of formats — from fantasy newspaper covers to tarot-style visuals and fashion moodboards. Users are also using the AI tool to restore older photos and create cinematic portrait collages, the company said, suggesting early patterns of more personal use.
除了风格化肖像和头像外，OpenAI 表示，印度早期的 Images 2.0 用户正在尝试更广泛的格式——从奇幻报纸封面到塔罗牌风格的视觉图和时尚情绪板。该公司称，用户还在利用该 AI 工具修复旧照片并创作电影质感的肖像拼贴画，这表明早期使用模式正趋向于更加个性化。
