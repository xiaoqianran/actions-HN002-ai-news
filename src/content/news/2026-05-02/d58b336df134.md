# CL-bench Life: Can Language Models Learn from Real-Life Context?
# CL-bench Life：语言模型能从现实生活语境中学习吗？

**Abstract:** Today's AI assistants such as OpenClaw are designed to handle context effectively, making context learning an increasingly important capability for models. As these systems move beyond professional settings into everyday life, the nature of the contexts they must handle also shifts. Real-life contexts are often messy, fragmented, and deeply tied to personal and social experience, such as multi-party conversations, personal archives, and behavioral traces. Yet it remains unclear whether current frontier language models can reliably learn from such contexts and solve tasks grounded in them.

**摘要：** 如今的 AI 助手（如 OpenClaw）旨在有效处理语境，这使得“语境学习”成为模型日益重要的能力。随着这些系统从专业领域走向日常生活，它们所必须处理的语境性质也发生了转变。现实生活中的语境往往是杂乱、碎片化的，并与个人和社会经验深度绑定，例如多方对话、个人档案和行为轨迹。然而，目前尚不清楚现有的前沿语言模型是否能够可靠地从这些语境中学习，并解决基于这些语境的任务。

To this end, we introduce CL-bench Life, a fully human-curated benchmark comprising 405 context-task pairs and 5,348 verification rubrics, covering common real-life scenarios. Solving tasks in CL-bench Life requires models to reason over complex, messy real-life contexts, calling for strong real-life context learning abilities that go far beyond those evaluated in existing benchmarks.

为此，我们推出了 CL-bench Life，这是一个完全由人工策划的基准测试，包含 405 个语境-任务对和 5,348 条验证准则，涵盖了常见的现实生活场景。解决 CL-bench Life 中的任务要求模型对复杂、杂乱的现实生活语境进行推理，这需要模型具备远超现有基准测试所评估的、强大的现实生活语境学习能力。

We evaluate ten frontier LMs and find that real-life context learning remains highly challenging: even the best-performing model achieves only 19.3% task solving rate, while the average performance across models is only 13.8%. Models still struggle to reason over contexts such as messy group chat histories and fragmented behavioral records from everyday life. CL-bench Life provides a crucial testbed for advancing real-life context learning, and progress on it can enable more intelligent and reliable AI assistants in everyday life.

我们评估了十个前沿语言模型，发现现实生活语境学习仍然极具挑战性：表现最好的模型任务解决率仅为 19.3%，而所有模型的平均表现仅为 13.8%。模型在处理诸如杂乱的群聊记录和碎片化的日常生活行为记录等语境时，依然难以进行有效推理。CL-bench Life 为推进现实生活语境学习提供了一个关键的测试平台，在该领域取得的进展将有助于实现更智能、更可靠的日常生活 AI 助手。
