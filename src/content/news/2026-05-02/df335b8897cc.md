# Dynamic Adversarial Fine-Tuning Reorganizes Refusal Geometry
# 动态对抗微调重构拒绝机制的几何结构

**Abstract:** Safety-aligned language models must refuse harmful requests without collapsing into broad over-refusal, but the training-time mechanisms behind this tradeoff remain unclear. Prior work characterizes refusal directions and jailbreak robustness, yet does not explain how dynamic adversarial fine-tuning changes refusal carriers across training.

**摘要：** 经过安全对齐的语言模型必须能够拒绝有害请求，同时避免陷入广泛的过度拒绝，但这种权衡背后的训练机制尚不明确。先前的研究虽然刻画了拒绝方向和越狱鲁棒性，但并未解释动态对抗微调如何在训练过程中改变拒绝载体（refusal carriers）。

We present a measurement-driven mechanism study, not a new defense, on one 7B backbone under supervised fine-tuning (SFT) and R2D2-style dynamic adversarial fine-tuning. Our protocol aligns fixed-source HarmBench, StrongREJECT, and XSTest with a five-anchor refusal-geometry suite and causal interventions.

我们针对一个 7B 参数的主干模型，在监督微调（SFT）和 R2D2 式动态对抗微调下进行了一项基于测量的机制研究，而非提出一种新的防御手段。我们的实验方案将固定源的 HarmBench、StrongREJECT 和 XSTest 与一套五锚点拒绝几何套件及因果干预相结合。

R2D2 drives fixed-source HarmBench ASR to 0.000 at steps 50 and 100, then partially reopens to 0.035 at step 250 and 0.250 at step 500; SFT remains less robust, with ASR between 0.505 and 0.588 at the same anchors. On XSTest, R2D2 any-refusal is 1.000 early, then falls to 0.664 and 0.228.

R2D2 将固定源 HarmBench 的攻击成功率（ASR）在第 50 步和第 100 步降至 0.000，随后在第 250 步部分回升至 0.035，在第 500 步升至 0.250；相比之下，SFT 的鲁棒性较弱，在相同锚点下的 ASR 介于 0.505 和 0.588 之间。在 XSTest 上，R2D2 的任意拒绝率（any-refusal）在早期为 1.000，随后降至 0.664 和 0.228。

Geometrically, R2D2 preserves a late-layer admissible carrier through step 100 before relocating to an early-layer carrier, while effective rank remains near 1.23--1.27. Causal interventions indicate low-dimensional but utility-coupled control. These results support a reorganization account rather than a drift-only account, with evidence limited to one backbone and fixed-source attacks.

从几何角度看，R2D2 在第 100 步之前保留了一个高层（late-layer）的可接受载体，随后转移到了底层（early-layer）载体，同时有效秩（effective rank）保持在 1.23 到 1.27 之间。因果干预表明这是一种低维但与效用耦合的控制。这些结果支持“重构”解释而非单纯的“漂移”解释，尽管目前的证据仅限于单一主干模型和固定源攻击。
