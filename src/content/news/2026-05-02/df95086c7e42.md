### Multimodality and Normal RAG
When we think of multimodal RAG, it almost always means you can search the knowledge base using images along with a text query. It is rarely the other way around. To understand the reasons, let’s look at the possible approaches how this is generally done:

### 多模态与常规 RAG
当我们想到多模态 RAG 时，它几乎总是意味着你可以使用图像和文本查询来搜索知识库。反过来的情况很少见。为了理解其中的原因，让我们看看通常采用的几种方法：

***

**Image captioning**
Run a OCR/Vision model on the images, turn the image into a paragraph of text, and index it into a chunk along with other text. This is not ideal, since the sliding-window chunking may result in the image caption being split across chunks. The core issue is a misalignment between retrieval units and semantic units. Traditional RAG retrieves arbitrary chunks, while meaning—and especially images—belongs to coherent sections of a document. When a chunk is retrieved, the LLM may only see a partial caption (e.g., for Figure 5), making it difficult to determine whether the image is actually relevant to this chunk or to an adjacent one that was not retrieved.

**图像标注**
在图像上运行 OCR/视觉模型，将图像转换为一段文本，并将其与其他文本一起索引到块中。这并不理想，因为滑动窗口分块可能会导致图像说明被分割到不同的块中。核心问题在于检索单元与语义单元之间的不匹配。传统的 RAG 检索的是任意块，而意义——尤其是图像——属于文档的连贯部分。当检索到一个块时，LLM 可能只能看到部分说明（例如图 5 的说明），这使得它很难判断该图像是确实与此块相关，还是与未检索到的相邻块相关。

***

**Multimodal Embedding**
Another approach is to embed both images and text into a shared vector space using a multimodal model. While this enables cross-modal retrieval, it introduces a different challenge. Multimodal embeddings optimize for similarity, not grounding. Visually or structurally similar artifacts—such as financial tables across different companies—can appear nearly identical in vector space, even when only one is relevant to the query. Without the context of document structure, the system retrieves candidates based on similarity but cannot confidently determine which image actually belongs in the response.

**多模态嵌入**
另一种方法是使用多模态模型将图像和文本嵌入到共享的向量空间中。虽然这实现了跨模态检索，但也带来了另一个挑战。多模态嵌入优化的是相似性，而非基础性（grounding）。视觉或结构相似的工件——例如不同公司的财务报表——在向量空间中可能看起来几乎相同，即使只有一个与查询相关。由于缺乏文档结构的上下文，系统基于相似性检索候选对象，但无法自信地确定哪张图像真正属于回复内容。

***

Proxy-Pointer solves this by replacing the text-based chunking with a tree-based one. We don’t chunk by character count; we chunk by Sectional Boundaries. If a section contains 3 paragraphs and 2 images, none of the chunks go beyond and bleed into the following section. The LLM can consider each section as a fully independent semantic unit and can make the judgement on the images in them with confidence.

Proxy-Pointer 通过用基于树的分块方式取代基于文本的分块方式解决了这个问题。我们不是按字符数分块，而是按“章节边界”分块。如果一个章节包含 3 段文字和 2 张图片，那么这些块都不会越界并延伸到下一个章节。LLM 可以将每个章节视为一个完全独立的语义单元，并能自信地对其中的图像做出判断。
