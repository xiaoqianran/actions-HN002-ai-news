# People are finally using Reddit’s search
# 人们终于开始使用 Reddit 的搜索功能了

After being harried by complaints that its search function needed improving, Reddit has in the last few years invested in its search engine, and has even added AI features to help its users find what they’re looking for. It appears that investment is finally paying off: The company has seen a 30% year-on-year jump in the number of people using search every week, CEO Steve Huffman said on Thursday.

在长期饱受关于搜索功能亟待改进的抱怨后，Reddit 在过去几年里加大了对搜索引擎的投入，甚至增加了人工智能功能来帮助用户找到他们想要的内容。事实证明，这些投资终于有了回报：首席执行官史蒂夫·霍夫曼（Steve Huffman）周四表示，每周使用搜索功能的人数同比增长了 30%。

Huffman noted that search has been one of the major drivers of user acquisition and retention for the platform. “On search, we have seen great performance. Search DAUs, WAUs, and queries are up meaningfully year-over-year. It’s a great driver of retention and DAUs. The search team is, quite frankly, I think doing a great job. If you use Reddit Answers, you can see it is better integrated into the product,” he said on the company’s first-quarter post-results conference call.

霍夫曼指出，搜索功能已成为该平台用户获取和留存的主要驱动力之一。他在公司第一季度财报电话会议上表示：“在搜索方面，我们看到了出色的表现。搜索功能的日活跃用户（DAU）、周活跃用户（WAU）和查询量同比均有显著增长。它是留存率和日活跃用户的重要推动力。坦率地说，我认为搜索团队做得非常出色。如果你使用 Reddit Answers，你会发现它已经更好地集成到了产品中。”

Earlier in February, the platform started testing product placement through AI search results in the U.S. Huffman said that around 40% of conversations on Reddit are commercial in nature, and 84% shoppers feel more confident in their buying decisions after researching on Reddit.

今年 2 月早些时候，该平台开始在美国测试通过 AI 搜索结果进行产品植入。霍夫曼表示，Reddit 上约 40% 的对话具有商业性质，84% 的购物者在 Reddit 上进行研究后，对自己的购买决策感到更有信心。

The social platform ended the quarter with more than 493 million weekly active unique users (WAUq), up 23% from the same period last year, and about 126 million daily active unique users (DAUq), a 17% improvement from a year earlier. Reddit reported a 7% improvement in U.S. visitors, reaching 53.5 million DAUqs, and 73.3 million DAUqs internationally, a 26% increase. The company said it plans to reach a billion daily users worldwide and 100 million daily users in the U.S.

该社交平台本季度结束时拥有超过 4.93 亿周活跃独立用户（WAUq），同比增长 23%；日活跃独立用户（DAUq）约为 1.26 亿，较去年同期增长 17%。Reddit 报告称，其美国访客量增长了 7%，达到 5350 万日活跃独立用户；国际访客量达到 7330 万，增长了 26%。公司表示，计划在全球范围内达到 10 亿日活跃用户，并在美国达到 1 亿日活跃用户。

Besides search, the company said its machine translation feature, which now supports over 30 languages, has driven significant user growth in the past few quarters. Reddit reported revenue of $663 million in Q1 2026, beating Wall Street’s expectations of $609.8 million. The company also said it only spent $1 million in capital expenditures in the quarter.

除了搜索功能外，该公司还表示，其目前支持超过 30 种语言的机器翻译功能，在过去几个季度中推动了显著的用户增长。Reddit 报告 2026 年第一季度营收为 6.63 亿美元，超过了华尔街预期的 6.098 亿美元。公司还表示，本季度的资本支出仅为 100 万美元。
