# NORACL: Neurogenesis for Oracle-free Resource-Adaptive Continual Learning
# NORACL：面向无需预知（Oracle-free）资源自适应持续学习的神经发生机制

**Abstract:** In a continual learning setting, we require a model to be plastic enough to learn a new task and stable enough to not disturb previously learned capabilities. We argue that this dilemma has an architectural root. A finite network has limited representational and plastic resources, yet the required capacity depends on properties of the future task stream that are unknown: how many tasks will be encountered, and how much they overlap in feature space.

**摘要：** 在持续学习场景中，我们需要模型既具备足够的灵活性（Plasticity）以学习新任务，又具备足够的稳定性（Stability）以避免干扰先前习得的能力。我们认为，这一困境的根源在于架构。有限的网络具有有限的表征和灵活性资源，然而所需的容量取决于未来任务流的未知属性：即将会遇到多少任务，以及它们在特征空间中重叠的程度。

Regularization-based methods preserve past knowledge within fixed-capacity architectures and therefore implicitly rely on an oracle architecture sized for this unknown future. When tasks are only weakly related, fixed architectures progressively run out of plastic resources; when tasks are few or strongly overlapping, models are often over-provisioned.

基于正则化的方法在固定容量的架构内保留过去知识，因此隐含地依赖于一个针对这种未知未来而预设的“预言机（Oracle）”架构。当任务之间关联性较弱时，固定架构会逐渐耗尽灵活性资源；而当任务较少或高度重叠时，模型往往又会造成资源过剩。

Inspired by neurogenesis in biology, we propose NORACL to address the stability-plasticity dilemma by tackling the oracle architecture problem through neuronal growth. Starting from a compact network, NORACL grows only when needed by monitoring two complementary signals for representational and plasticity saturation.

受生物学中神经发生（Neurogenesis）的启发，我们提出了 NORACL，旨在通过神经元生长来解决“预言机”架构问题，从而应对稳定性与灵活性的困境。NORACL 从一个紧凑的网络开始，仅在必要时通过监测表征饱和度和灵活性饱和度这两个互补信号来进行生长。

We evaluate NORACL against oracle-sized static baselines across varying task counts and geometries. Across all settings, NORACL achieves final average accuracies that are better than or on par with oracle-provisioned static baselines while using fewer parameters.

我们在不同的任务数量和几何结构下，将 NORACL 与预设了“预言机”规模的静态基准模型进行了对比评估。在所有设置中，NORACL 在使用更少参数的情况下，实现了优于或等同于预设规模静态基准模型的最终平均准确率。

Additionally, NORACL yields architectures with interpretable growth, i.e. dissimilar tasks predominantly expand feature-extraction layers, whereas tasks which rely on common features shift growth toward later feature-combination layers. Our analysis further explains why fixed-capacity networks lose plasticity as tasks accumulate, whereas NORACL creates fresh capacity for new tasks through growth. Together, these results show that adaptive neurogenesis pushes the stability-plasticity Pareto frontier of continual learning.

此外，NORACL 生成的架构具有可解释的生长特性，即：差异较大的任务主要扩展特征提取层，而依赖共同特征的任务则将生长重心转向后期的特征组合层。我们的分析进一步解释了为什么固定容量网络会随着任务积累而丧失灵活性，而 NORACL 则通过生长为新任务创造了全新的容量。总之，这些结果表明，自适应神经发生机制推动了持续学习中稳定性与灵活性之间的帕累托前沿（Pareto frontier）。
