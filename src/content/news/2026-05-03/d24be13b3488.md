### Ask.com has closed
### Ask.com 已正式关闭

Every great search must come to an end. As IAC continues to sharpen its focus, we have made the decision to discontinue our search business, which includes Ask.com.
每一段伟大的搜索旅程终有落幕之时。随着 IAC 继续聚焦核心业务，我们已决定终止旗下的搜索业务，其中也包括 Ask.com。

After 25 years of answering the world's questions, Ask.com officially closed on May 1, 2026. "To the millions who asked..."
在为世界解答了 25 年的问题后，Ask.com 已于 2026 年 5 月 1 日正式关闭。“致数百万曾在此提问的用户……”

We are deeply grateful to the brilliant engineers, designers, and teams who built and supported Ask over the decades. And to you—the millions of users who turned to us for answers in a rapidly changing world—thank you for your endless curiosity, your loyalty, and your trust.
我们衷心感谢几十年来构建并支持 Ask 的杰出工程师、设计师和团队。同时也感谢你们——数百万在瞬息万变的世界中向我们寻求答案的用户——感谢你们无穷的好奇心、忠诚与信任。

Jeeves’ spirit endures.
Jeeves 的精神将永存。
