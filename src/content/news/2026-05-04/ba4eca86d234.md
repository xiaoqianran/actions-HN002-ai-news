# 15 Best Gifts for Plant Lovers and Gardeners (2026)

### An Easy-Care Countertop Garden / 易于打理的台面花园

**Lettuce Grow Counterstand Set**

Lettuce Grow is better known for its elaborate Farmstand models, but last year the brand released a no-fuss, no-pump, giftable setup that uses the Kratky method of hydroponic gardening. Just fill the three science-lab-looking amber beakers to the fill line, add a few drops of the included liquid nutrients, and set your seedlings on one of the included wicks. The wick will draw water up to the plant until its roots can grow down into it. That's all it takes—an optional 7-watt, 18-inch-tall LED lamp will shine for your choice of either 16 or 18 hours from when you first push the button, and you should have edible leaves within three weeks.

Lettuce Grow以其精致的Farmstand系列闻名，但去年该品牌推出了一款无需泵、简单易用且适合作为礼物的套装，采用了克拉基（Kratky）水培法。只需将三个看起来像实验室烧杯的琥珀色容器加水至刻度线，滴入几滴附带的液体营养液，然后将幼苗放在附带的灯芯上即可。灯芯会将水输送给植物，直到根系长入水中。操作就是这么简单——可选配的7瓦、18英寸高的LED灯在按下按钮后可选择照明16或18小时，三周内你就能收获可食用的叶菜。

### A Stylish Propagation Station / 时尚的植物扦插站

**Mkono Plant Propagation Tubes**

Propagation tubes as decor is not a new concept, but not everyone has the table or shelf space for the number of tubes they need. This hanging version comes in small, medium, or large sizes, as well as your choice of wood finishes. There's also a white version with colored tubes if your recipient wanted to hang it in front of a window for a stained-glass effect. (They may want to treat the tubes with hydrogen peroxide first to avoid algae growth.)

将扦插管作为装饰品并非新概念，但并非每个人都有足够的桌面或架子空间来摆放所需的试管。这款悬挂式版本有小、中、大三种尺寸，并提供多种木质饰面选择。如果你想让它在窗前呈现出类似彩色玻璃的效果，还有带有彩色试管的白色版本可选。（建议先用过氧化氢处理试管，以防止藻类生长。）

### For Foolproof Planting / 零失败的种植方案

**Sol Soils Starter Pack**

Sol Soils products are on the pricier side, but if you know a plant lover who has a black thumb—or who just appreciates the best of the best—this line of specialty houseplant potting mixes, additives, and amendments is among the best I've ever tried. The houseplant chunky mix looks like an odd jumble of coconut husk chips, bark, pumice, and other bits and bobs, but it has saved many an ailing seedling in my household from the brink of death.

Sol Soils的产品价格偏高，但如果你认识的植物爱好者是个“植物杀手”，或者对方只追求顶级品质，那么这一系列专业的室内盆栽土、添加剂和改良剂是我尝试过最好的产品之一。这款室内植物粗颗粒混合土看起来像是一堆椰壳碎片、树皮、浮石和其他杂物的奇怪组合，但它确实把我家里许多濒死的幼苗从死亡边缘救了回来。

### A Strong Grow Light / 强力补光灯

**LetPot 100-Watt Grow Light**

Grow lights are critical for keeping houseplants happy in winter, and I like this full-spectrum model from LetPot due to the size of its lamp (2 x 3 feet), which keeps plants and seedlings from becoming leggy, and the fact it can hang or stand. While on its stand, the light can rotate around 360 degrees to be positioned where you need it, and while it works with LetPot's app, it also sports a corded remote with buttons for adjusting the light intensity.

补光灯对于让室内植物在冬季保持健康至关重要。我喜欢LetPot这款全光谱补光灯，因为它灯板面积大（2 x 3英尺），能防止植物和幼苗徒长，而且既可以悬挂也可以立式放置。在支架上时，灯头可以360度旋转，方便调整到你需要的位置。除了支持LetPot应用程序外，它还配有一个带线遥控器，可以通过按钮调节光照强度。
