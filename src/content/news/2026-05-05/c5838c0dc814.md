# Katie Haun raises $1B for new venture funds
# 凯蒂·豪恩（Katie Haun）为新风险投资基金筹集 10 亿美元

Former Andreessen Horowitz investor Katie Haun announced on Monday in a press release that her firm has raised $1 billion across new funds to continue its thesis of backing crypto and blockchain.
前 Andreessen Horowitz 投资人凯蒂·豪恩（Katie Haun）周一通过新闻稿宣布，其公司已筹集 10 亿美元新基金，旨在继续践行其支持加密货币和区块链领域的投资理念。

The capital will be spread across startups at early and later stages, Bloomberg reported, and, within the crypto and blockchain space, it will be used to back startups focused on alternative assets (like gold and other commodities), the agentic economy, and financial services.
据彭博社报道，这笔资金将分配给处于早期和后期阶段的初创企业。在加密货币和区块链领域，资金将用于支持专注于替代资产（如黄金和其他大宗商品）、代理经济（agentic economy）以及金融服务的初创公司。

The firm hopes to deploy the capital globally over the next two to three years.
该公司希望在未来两到三年内在全球范围内配置这笔资金。

Haun left a16z in late 2021 and launched her own venture firm shortly after, in 2022. The firm handles more than $2 billion in assets under management, estimates PitchBook, and its investments include Palmer Luckey’s Erebor Bank and the crypto finance company Ellipsis Labs.
豪恩于 2021 年底离开 a16z，并于 2022 年初创办了自己的风险投资公司。据 PitchBook 估计，该公司目前管理的资产规模超过 20 亿美元，其投资组合包括帕尔默·拉奇（Palmer Luckey）的 Erebor Bank 以及加密金融公司 Ellipsis Labs。
