# Single Image Defogging Using a Fourth-Order Telegraph PDE Guided by Physical Haze Modeling
# 基于物理雾霾建模与四阶电报偏微分方程的单图像去雾

**Abstract:** In real-world scenarios, image defogging is an inverse problem due to unknown scene depth, atmospheric scattering, and the common absence of ground truth. To resolve the issue, we propose a hybrid defogging model that integrates a fourth-order nonlinear PDE with a physical haze formation model. 

**摘要：** 在现实场景中，由于场景深度未知、大气散射以及通常缺乏真实参考图像（ground truth），图像去雾是一个反问题。为了解决这一问题，我们提出了一种混合去雾模型，将四阶非线性偏微分方程（PDE）与物理雾霾形成模型相结合。

We used Dark Channel Prior to estimate atmospheric parameters and to generate a guidance image, while the final restoration is performed via a fourth-order PDE-based evolution. A fourth-order PDE of the type telegraph is then evolved, incorporating an edge-adaptive diffusion coefficient and a fidelity term weighted by the transmission map. 

我们利用暗通道先验（Dark Channel Prior）来估计大气参数并生成引导图像，同时通过基于四阶PDE的演化过程完成最终的图像恢复。随后，我们演化了一种电报类型的四阶PDE，其中结合了边缘自适应扩散系数以及由透射率图加权的保真项。

Fourth-order diffusion effectively suppresses haze while preserving structural details, and the hyperbolic formulation improves numerical stability and convergence behavior. We use relative error norm criteria for the convergence of our PDE. 

四阶扩散在有效抑制雾霾的同时保留了结构细节，而双曲型公式则提高了数值稳定性和收敛性能。我们使用相对误差范数准则来评估PDE的收敛性。

The proposed method is compared with Dark Channel prior, modified Dark Channel prior, and variational-based single-image defogging techniques. When we have ground truth available, we use MSE and SSIM for quantitative evaluation, whereas no-reference metrics, including FADE, Contrast Restoration Index, Average Gradient, and Entropy, are applied to real-world foggy images. 

我们将所提方法与暗通道先验、改进的暗通道先验以及基于变分的单图像去雾技术进行了对比。在有真实参考图像的情况下，我们使用MSE和SSIM进行定量评估；而对于现实世界的雾天图像，则应用了包括FADE、对比度恢复指数、平均梯度和熵在内的无参考指标。

Experimental results demonstrate that the proposed hybrid PDE-based method provides comparable visual quality and maintains structural details.

实验结果表明，所提出的基于混合PDE的方法提供了相当的视觉质量，并能良好地保持结构细节。
