# Polynomial-Time Optimal Group Selection via the Double-Commutator Eigenvalue Problem
# 通过双交换子特征值问题实现多项式时间最优群选择

**Abstract:** The algebraic diversity framework replaces temporal averaging over multiple observations with algebraic group action on a single observation for second-order statistical estimation. The central open problem in this framework is $\textit{group selection}$: given an $M$-dimensional observation with unknown covariance structure, find the finite group whose spectral decomposition best matches the covariance.

**摘要：** 代数多样性框架（Algebraic diversity framework）在二阶统计估计中，用对单一观测值的代数群作用取代了对多个观测值的时间平均。该框架中一个核心的未决问题是“群选择”（group selection）：给定一个具有未知协方差结构的 $M$ 维观测值，寻找一个有限群，使其谱分解与该协方差最为匹配。

Naive enumeration of all subgroups of the symmetric group $S_M$ requires exponential time in $M$. We prove that this combinatorial problem reduces to a generalized eigenvalue problem derived from the double commutator of the covariance matrix, yielding a polynomial-time algorithm with complexity $O(d^2M^2 + d^3)$, where $d$ is the dimension of a generator basis.

对对称群 $S_M$ 的所有子群进行朴素枚举需要 $M$ 的指数级时间。我们证明了这一组合问题可以归约为由协方差矩阵的双交换子（double commutator）导出的广义特征值问题，从而得出一个复杂度为 $O(d^2M^2 + d^3)$ 的多项式时间算法，其中 $d$ 是生成元基的维度。

The minimum eigenvector of the double-commutator matrix directly constructs the optimal group generator in closed form, with no iterative optimization. The reduction is exact: the double-commutator minimum eigenvalue is zero if and only if the optimal generator lies in the span of the basis, and its magnitude provides a certifiable optimality gap when it does not.

双交换子矩阵的最小特征向量可以直接以闭式（closed form）构建最优群生成元，无需迭代优化。该归约是精确的：当且仅当最优生成元位于基的张成空间内时，双交换子的最小特征值为零；若不在该空间内，其量级则提供了一个可证明的最优性间隙（optimality gap）。

This problem does not appear in the standard catalogs of computational complexity (Garey and Johnson, 1979) and represents a new class linking group theory, matrix analysis, and statistical estimation. We establish connections to independent component analysis (JADE), structured matrix nearness problems, and simultaneous matrix diagonalization, and we show that the double-commutator formulation is the unique approach that is simultaneously polynomial-time, closed-form, and certifiable.

该问题未出现在计算复杂性的标准目录（Garey and Johnson, 1979）中，代表了一类连接群论、矩阵分析和统计估计的新问题。我们建立了其与独立成分分析（JADE）、结构化矩阵邻近问题以及同时矩阵对角化之间的联系，并证明了双交换子公式是目前唯一同时具备多项式时间、闭式解和可证明性特征的方法。
