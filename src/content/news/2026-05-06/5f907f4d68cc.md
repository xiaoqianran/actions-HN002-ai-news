### Google is partnering with XPRIZE and Range Media Partners on the $3.5 million Future Vision film competition.
### Google 携手 XPRIZE 与 Range Media Partners，共同举办 350 万美元的“未来愿景”（Future Vision）电影竞赛。

Google is partnering with XPRIZE and Range Media Partners through our 100 ZEROS initiative to support the Future Vision XPRIZE, a $3.5 million global film competition.
Google 正通过其“100 ZEROS”计划与 XPRIZE 及 Range Media Partners 合作，共同支持“未来愿景 XPRIZE”（Future Vision XPRIZE）——这是一项奖金高达 350 万美元的全球电影竞赛。

We're putting out a call for short films and trailers that envision an optimistic, technology-forward future, using the power of storytelling (and AI tools like Google Flow) to inspire the next generation.
我们现正征集各类短片和预告片，旨在通过叙事的力量（以及 Google Flow 等人工智能工具），描绘一个乐观且以技术为导向的未来，从而激励下一代。

As the competition’s creative technology partner, Google will work with the grand prize winner to provide the creative and production support needed to turn their three-minute submission into a full-length feature film. Our goal is to help lower production barriers and augment the creative process for emerging filmmakers.
作为本次竞赛的创意技术合作伙伴，Google 将与大奖得主合作，提供将其三分钟参赛作品转化为长篇电影所需的创意与制作支持。我们的目标是帮助新兴电影制作人降低制作门槛，并增强他们的创作过程。

Submissions are open now through August 15, 2026. Whether you use traditional live-action, animation or AI tools, we want to see your vision.
作品提交通道现已开启，截止日期为 2026 年 8 月 15 日。无论您是使用传统的实景拍摄、动画制作还是人工智能工具，我们都期待看到您的愿景。

Visit futurevisionxprize.com to learn more and register.
请访问 futurevisionxprize.com 了解更多详情并进行注册。
