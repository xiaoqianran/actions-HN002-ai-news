### Next-Level Skin (and Scalp) Care / 进阶护肤（及头皮护理）
**HigherDose Red Light Therapy Face Mask ($349) & Red Light Hat ($449)**

The must-have gadget for women of almost any age is an LED mask. These masks promise to use red light to erase wrinkles, brighten your skin, and keep acne at bay. I haven't seen much change in my wrinkles in my year-plus of regularly using a red light mask, but I can confirm it makes my skin brighter and calms down acne and scars much faster when I'm regularly treating my skin. HigherDose is a favorite mask of mine because the three-strap design lets it hang on my head rather than squeeze it, making it one of the most comfortable masks I've worn.
对于几乎任何年龄段的女性来说，LED 面膜仪都是必备小家电。这些面膜仪承诺利用红光消除皱纹、提亮肤色并抑制痤疮。虽然我坚持使用红光面膜仪一年多，皱纹并没有明显变化，但我可以肯定的是，在定期护理下，我的皮肤确实变得更亮了，痤疮和疤痕的愈合速度也快得多。HigherDose 是我最喜欢的品牌，因为它的三带设计让面膜仪是“挂”在头上而不是“勒”在脸上，是我用过最舒适的面膜仪之一。

My other favorite LED tool actually sits on my head and also comes from HigherDose. The brand's Red Light Hat treats your head with red light to rejuvenate your scalp and hair follicles for better hair growth, and as someone with a sensitive scalp and a hair-pulling problem, using this cap regularly genuinely improved my scalp and hair. It's the healthiest it's ever been, and my scalp is the least itchy, too. I love that this hat also has a battery pack, so I can comfortably wear it anywhere while I treat my head.
我另一个最喜欢的 LED 工具同样来自 HigherDose，它是戴在头上的。该品牌的红光理疗帽通过红光照射头部，激活头皮和毛囊以促进头发生长。作为一个头皮敏感且有拔发习惯的人，定期使用这顶帽子确实改善了我的头皮和发质。这是我头皮最健康的状态，瘙痒感也降到了最低。我特别喜欢它自带电池组的设计，这样我可以在任何地方舒适地佩戴它进行护理。

### For the Reader / 给阅读爱好者的礼物
**Kobo Libra Colour ($200) & Lamicall Tablet Pillow Stand ($33)**

Look, I love a Kindle, but if you want to get the best e-reader for your buck, the answer is actually a Kobo. My favorite is the Kobo Libra Colour that's cheaper than the Kindle Colorsoft and can also double as a digital notebook if you add on the $70 stylus. I also prefer Kobo's annotating options: You can write right on the pages instead of in weird pop-up margins and boxes only on Kindle. Kobo also has its own page-turner remote now, which I love to pair with a pillow stand holder so I can lean back and relax without holding up my e-reader while I read.
听着，我虽然喜欢 Kindle，但如果你想追求性价比最高的电子阅读器，答案其实是 Kobo。我最喜欢的是 Kobo Libra Colour，它比 Kindle Colorsoft 更便宜，如果额外购买 70 美元的触控笔，它还能兼作数字笔记本。我也更喜欢 Kobo 的批注功能：你可以直接在页面上书写，而不是像 Kindle 那样只能在奇怪的弹出边框里写字。Kobo 现在还有专属的翻页遥控器，我喜欢把它和枕头支架搭配使用，这样我就可以靠在椅背上放松阅读，而无需一直手持阅读器。

### Clear Listening / 清晰聆听
**Apple AirPods 4 ($99) & Mujjo Echelon AirPods Pro 2 Case ($39)**

My husband and I got AirPods around the same time. We were late adopters and longtime fans of our Apple corded headphones. The AirPods 4 aren't the WIRED team's favorite for sound, but I'm one of the many people who can't seem to keep other earbuds in her ears, so Apple's tip-free design is the best fit for me, though that means you aren't getting good noise canceling. I got my first set for Christmas from my siblings this year, and originally I stuck some pretty stickers on my case to tell the difference between my case and my husband's. I've since upgraded to this stylish case from Mujjo, which makes it easy to see which is mine and clip it onto my keys.
我和丈夫差不多在同一时间入手了 AirPods。我们是“晚期用户”，此前一直是 Apple 有线耳机的忠实粉丝。虽然 AirPods 4 的音质并不是 WIRED 编辑部最推崇的，但我是那种戴不住其他入耳式耳塞的人，所以 Apple 的非入耳式设计对我来说最合适，尽管这意味着它的降噪效果一般。今年圣诞节我从兄弟姐妹那里收到了第一副 AirPods，起初我贴了一些漂亮的贴纸来区分我和丈夫的耳机盒。后来我升级到了 Mujjo 的这款时尚保护壳，它不仅让我一眼就能认出自己的耳机，还能轻松挂在钥匙扣上。
