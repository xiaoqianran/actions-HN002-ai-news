# Adversarial Flow Matching for Imperceptible Attacks on End-to-End Autonomous Driving
# 面向端到端自动驾驶的不可察觉对抗流匹配攻击

**Abstract:** Autonomous driving (AD) is evolving towards end-to-end (E2E) frameworks through two primary paradigms: monolithic models exemplified by Vision-Language-Action (VLA), and specialized modular architectures. Despite their divergent designs, both paradigms increasingly rely on Transformer backbones for complex reasoning, potentially causing a shared vulnerability: visually imperceptible perturbations can manipulate E2E AD models into hazardous maneuvers by targeting the Transformer module.

**摘要：** 自动驾驶（AD）正通过两种主要范式向端到端（E2E）框架演进：以视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型为代表的单体模型，以及专门的模块化架构。尽管设计理念各异，但这两种范式都日益依赖 Transformer 主干网络进行复杂推理，这可能导致一个共同的脆弱性：通过针对 Transformer 模块，视觉上不可察觉的扰动可以诱导端到端自动驾驶模型做出危险操作。

Most existing adversarial attack approaches against AD systems operate under white-box or black-box settings; yet, they typically necessitate full model transparency, or suffer from either prohibitive query latency or limited attack transferability. In this paper, we propose Adversarial Flow Matching (AFM), a novel gray-box attack framework that exploits Transformer structural vulnerabilities in E2E AD models.

目前大多数针对自动驾驶系统的对抗攻击方法都在白盒或黑盒设置下运行；然而，它们通常需要完全的模型透明度，或者面临过高的查询延迟，亦或是攻击迁移性有限。在本文中，我们提出了对抗流匹配（Adversarial Flow Matching, AFM），这是一种新颖的灰盒攻击框架，旨在利用端到端自动驾驶模型中 Transformer 的结构性漏洞。

AFM enables efficient one-step generation of adversarial examples via a neural average velocity field. Additionally, the proposed technique yields effective and visually perceptible attacks by synergistically perturbing the generative latent space and the neural average velocity field.

AFM 通过神经平均速度场实现了对抗样本的高效一步生成。此外，该技术通过协同扰动生成潜在空间和神经平均速度场，产生了既有效又在视觉上不可察觉的攻击效果。

Extensive experiments demonstrate that AFM achieves a superior trade-off between attack effectiveness and imperceptibility: it substantially degrades the performance of both VLA and modular AD agents across various scenarios compared to baselines, while maintaining state-of-the-art visual imperceptibility.

大量实验表明，AFM 在攻击有效性和不可察觉性之间取得了卓越的平衡：与基线方法相比，它在各种场景下显著降低了 VLA 和模块化自动驾驶代理的性能，同时保持了业界领先的视觉不可察觉性。

Furthermore, adversarial examples generated by AFM exhibit robust cross-model transferability, indicating that AFM closely approximates a black-box attack setting while requiring only the prior knowledge that the target AD model incorporates a Transformer-based module.

此外，由 AFM 生成的对抗样本表现出强大的跨模型迁移能力，这表明 AFM 在仅需预知目标自动驾驶模型包含基于 Transformer 的模块这一前提下，就能高度逼近黑盒攻击的效果。
