### Best Portable Power Station Overall: Bluetti Elite 300
### 最佳综合便携式储能电源：Bluetti Elite 300

Bluetti’s Elite 300 is so compact that I had to double-check its 3,014-watt-hour capacity, as it’s about the same size as most 2,000-watt-hour power stations. I don’t know how Bluetti packed such large lithium-iron phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries inside, but this could be a great power station for an RV where space is limited. It's also suitable for home backup power or campers.

Bluetti Elite 300 的体积非常紧凑，以至于我不得不反复确认它是否真的拥有 3,014 瓦时的容量，因为它的尺寸与大多数 2,000 瓦时的储能电源差不多。我不知道 Bluetti 是如何将如此大容量的磷酸铁锂（LiFePO4）电池塞进去的，但对于空间有限的房车来说，这绝对是一款出色的储能电源。它同样适用于家庭备用电源或露营使用。

Performance was flawless with smaller gadgets (the USB-C ports fast-charged all the phones I tested) and less demanding appliances, coming close to the stated capacity (consistently around 85 percent). It passed my UK kettle test (when it exceeded the 2,400-watt maximum output, surge mode kicks in, giving you a couple of minutes at up to 4,800 watts), but boiling a full kettle drained 7 percent of the battery. Even under a heavy load, the Elite 300 fans are quiet. They never exceeded 50 decibels and hushed quickly when the load dropped below 2,400 watts. The Bluetti Elite 300 can also serve as an uninterruptible power supply with a 10-millisecond delay.

在为小型设备（USB-C 接口能为我测试的所有手机进行快充）和低功耗电器供电时，它的表现堪称完美，实际可用容量接近标称值（始终保持在 85% 左右）。它通过了我的英国电热水壶测试（当超过 2,400 瓦的最大输出功率时，浪涌模式会启动，提供几分钟高达 4,800 瓦的输出），但烧开一壶水消耗了 7% 的电量。即使在高负载下，Elite 300 的风扇也非常安静。噪音从未超过 50 分贝，且在负载降至 2,400 瓦以下时会迅速安静下来。Bluetti Elite 300 还可以作为不间断电源（UPS）使用，切换延迟仅为 10 毫秒。

*   **Capacity:** 3,014 watt-hours / 容量：3,014 瓦时
*   **Max Output:** 2,400 watts (4,800-W surge) / 最大输出：2,400 瓦（4,800 瓦浪涌）
*   **AC Charging time:** 1.6 hours / 交流充电时间：1.6 小时
*   **Ports:** 4 x AC, 1 x Car, 2 x USB-A (15W), 2 x USB-C (100 W and 140 W), 1 x Barrel Port (360 W) / 接口：4 个交流电口，1 个车充口，2 个 USB-A (15W)，2 个 USB-C (100W 和 140W)，1 个圆口 (360W)

### Best Power Station for Camping: Jackery Explorer 1500 Ultra
### 最佳露营储能电源：Jackery Explorer 1500 Ultra

If you want to take power with you on outdoor adventures, the Jackery Explorer 1500 Ultra is ideal for a long weekend in the wilderness. It is IP65-rated, so you don’t need to worry about rain or dust, and it’s shock-proof, so it can survive an accidental drop (up to one meter). I love the comfy handle, which makes it easy to carry from the car to your campsite one-handed, along with other stuff (most power stations this size have two handles). It weighs just shy of 40 pounds.

如果你想在户外探险时携带电源，Jackery Explorer 1500 Ultra 是荒野长周末的理想选择。它具备 IP65 防护等级，因此无需担心雨水或灰尘，而且它具备防震功能，可以承受意外跌落（最高一米）。我非常喜欢它舒适的提手设计，这让你能单手将其从车上拎到营地，同时还能腾出手拿其他东西（大多数这个尺寸的储能电源都有两个提手）。它的重量不到 40 磅（约 18 公斤）。

The Explorer 1500 Ultra did well in my tests, coming very close to the stated capacity, and it will charge all your small gadgets easily and power small appliances or power tools. The cooling fan in the base seems pretty effective, and it never broke 30 decibels for fan noise. It can put out 1,800 watts steadily, jump to 2,000 watts for up to 15 minutes, and handle 3,600 watts for very short bursts.

Explorer 1500 Ultra 在我的测试中表现出色，实际容量非常接近标称值，它可以轻松为你的所有小型设备充电，并为小型电器或电动工具供电。底部的冷却风扇似乎非常有效，风扇噪音从未超过 30 分贝。它可以稳定输出 1,800 瓦功率，在 15 分钟内可提升至 2,000 瓦，并能处理极短时间的 3,600 瓦浪涌。
