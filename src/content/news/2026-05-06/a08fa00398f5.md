# Towards High Fidelity Face Swapping: A Comprehensive Survey and New Benchmark
# 实现高保真换脸：全面综述与全新基准

Face swapping has witnessed significant progress in recent years, largely driven by advances in deep generative models such as GANs and diffusion models. Despite these advances, existing methods remain fragmented across different paradigms, and their evaluation is highly inconsistent due to the lack of standardized datasets and protocols.
近年来，得益于生成对抗网络（GAN）和扩散模型等深度生成模型的进步，换脸技术取得了显著进展。尽管取得了这些成就，但现有的方法在不同范式之间仍然处于碎片化状态，且由于缺乏标准化的数据集和评估协议，其评估结果往往高度不一致。

Moreover, prior surveys primarily focus on broader deepfake generation or detection, leaving face swapping insufficiently studied as a standalone problem. In this paper, we present a comprehensive survey and benchmark for face swapping. We provide a structured review of existing methods, organizing them into five major paradigms and systematically analyzing their design principles, strengths, and limitations.
此外，以往的综述主要集中在更广泛的深度伪造（Deepfake）生成或检测上，导致换脸技术作为一个独立课题的研究尚不充分。在本文中，我们针对换脸技术提出了一项全面的综述与基准测试。我们对现有方法进行了结构化梳理，将其归纳为五大范式，并系统地分析了它们的设计原则、优势及局限性。

To enable fair and controlled evaluation, we introduce CASIA FaceSwapping, a high-quality benchmark with balanced demographic distributions and explicit attribute variations, and establish standardized protocols to assess the robustness of different face swapping methods. Extensive experiments on representative approaches yield new insights into the performance characteristics and limitations of current techniques.
为了实现公平且可控的评估，我们引入了 CASIA FaceSwapping，这是一个具备平衡的人口统计学分布和明确属性变化的高质量基准，并建立了标准化协议来评估不同换脸方法的鲁棒性。通过对代表性方法进行的大量实验，我们对当前技术的性能特征和局限性获得了新的见解。

Overall, our work provides a unified perspective and a principled evaluation framework to facilitate the development of more robust and controllable face swapping methods.
总而言之，我们的工作提供了一个统一的视角和一个原则性的评估框架，旨在促进更稳健、更可控的换脸技术的发展。
