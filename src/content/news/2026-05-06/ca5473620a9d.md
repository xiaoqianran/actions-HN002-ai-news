# Linking spatial biology and clinical histology via Haiku
# 通过 Haiku 连接空间生物学与临床组织学

Integrating molecular, morphological, and clinical data is essential for basic and translational biomedical research, yet systematic frameworks for jointly modeling these modalities remain limited. Here we present Haiku, a tri-modal contrastive learning model trained on multiplexed immunofluorescence (mIF). It comprises 26.7 million spatial proteomics patches from 3,218 tissue sections across 1,606 patients spanning 11 organ types, with matched hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) histology and clinical metadata aligned in a shared embedding space.

整合分子、形态学和临床数据对于基础和转化生物医学研究至关重要，但目前联合建模这些模态的系统性框架仍然有限。在此，我们提出了 Haiku，这是一个基于多重免疫荧光（mIF）训练的三模态对比学习模型。它包含来自 1,606 名患者、11 种器官类型的 3,218 张组织切片的 2,670 万个空间蛋白质组学补丁，并将匹配的苏木精-伊红（H&E）组织学图像与临床元数据对齐在一个共享的嵌入空间中。

Haiku enables three-way cross-modal retrieval, improves downstream classification and clinical prediction tasks over unimodal baselines, and supports zero-shot biomarker inference through fusion retrieval conditioned on clinical metadata-only text descriptions. Across tasks, Haiku outperforms competing approaches, achieving cross-modal retrieval (Recall@50 up to 0.611 versus near-zero baseline), survival prediction (C-index 0.737, +7.91% relative improvement), and zero-shot biomarker inference (mean Pearson correlation 0.718 across 52 biomarkers).

Haiku 实现了三向跨模态检索，在下游分类和临床预测任务中优于单模态基准，并支持通过仅基于临床元数据文本描述的融合检索进行零样本生物标志物推断。在各项任务中，Haiku 的表现均优于同类方法，实现了跨模态检索（Recall@50 高达 0.611，而基准接近于零）、生存预测（C-index 为 0.737，相对提升 7.91%）以及零样本生物标志物推断（52 个生物标志物的平均皮尔逊相关系数为 0.718）。

Furthermore, we introduce a counterfactual prediction framework in which modifying only clinical metadata while fixing tissue morphology surfaces niche-specific molecular shifts associated with breast cancer stage progression and lung cancer survival outcomes. In a lung adenocarcinoma case study, the counterfactual analysis recovers niche-specific shifts characterized by increased CD8 and granzyme B, reduced PD-L1, and decreased Ki67, broadly consistent with patterns reported for favorable outcomes. We present these counterfactual results as exploratory, hypothesis-generating signals rather than mechanistic claims. These capabilities demonstrate that tri-modal alignment via Haiku enables integrative analysis of spatial biology, bridging molecular measurements with clinical context for biological exploration.

此外，我们引入了一个反事实预测框架，通过在固定组织形态的同时仅修改临床元数据，揭示了与乳腺癌分期进展和肺癌生存结果相关的特定微环境分子变化。在一项肺腺癌案例研究中，反事实分析恢复了以 CD8 和颗粒酶 B 增加、PD-L1 减少以及 Ki67 降低为特征的微环境特异性变化，这与已报道的良好预后模式基本一致。我们将这些反事实结果视为探索性的、产生假设的信号，而非机制性结论。这些能力表明，通过 Haiku 进行的三模态对齐实现了空间生物学的综合分析，将分子测量与临床背景相结合，从而促进生物学探索。
