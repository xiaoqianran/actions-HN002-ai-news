# Synthetic Designed Experiments for Diagnosing Vision Model Failure
# 用于诊断视觉模型故障的合成设计实验

Current synthetic data pipelines for computer vision generate images without diagnosing what the downstream model actually needs. This open-loop paradigm treats synthetic data as cheap real data, randomly sampling the generator's output space and hoping to cover the model's failure modes. We argue this fundamentally misuses synthetic data's unique property: the controllable, independent variation of scene factors.
当前的计算机视觉合成数据流水线在生成图像时，并未诊断下游模型真正需要什么。这种开环范式将合成数据视为廉价的真实数据，通过随机采样生成器的输出空间，试图覆盖模型的故障模式。我们认为，这从根本上误用了合成数据独有的特性：即对场景因素进行可控且独立的变量调整。

Drawing on the statistical theory of Design of Experiments (DoE), we propose Synthetic Designed Experiments for Representational Sufficiency (SDRS). SDRS treats the downstream model as a black-box system and the synthetic generator as an experimental apparatus. Using fractional factorial designs, SDRS efficiently audits a model's factor-sensitivity profile via ANOVA decomposition.
基于实验设计（DoE）的统计理论，我们提出了“用于表征充分性的合成设计实验”（SDRS）。SDRS 将下游模型视为黑盒系统，将合成生成器视为实验装置。通过使用部分析因设计，SDRS 能够利用方差分析（ANOVA）分解，高效地审计模型的因素敏感性概况。

It classifies failures into two actionable types: Type I gaps (coverage failures on underrepresented factor levels) and Type II gaps (reliance on spurious nuisance dependencies). The audit then prescribes targeted synthetic data to address each gap type.
它将故障分为两类可操作的类型：I 型差距（在代表性不足的因素水平上的覆盖失败）和 II 型差距（对虚假干扰依赖项的依赖）。随后，审计结果会针对每种差距类型提供有针对性的合成数据建议。

We validate SDRS on three experiments: (1) a controlled diagnostic on dSprites with planted biases, where the audit correctly identifies both gap types and targeted data improves accuracy from 49.9% to 79.0%; (2) a dense segmentation task on procedural scenes, where detecting background-complexity shortcuts and applying targeted data improves mIoU from 0.948 to 0.998; and (3) an entanglement detection experiment showing that the ANOVA audit identifies cross-factor contamination in imperfect generators.
我们在三个实验中验证了 SDRS：(1) 在带有预设偏差的 dSprites 上进行受控诊断，审计正确识别了两种差距类型，且针对性数据将准确率从 49.9% 提升至 79.0%；(2) 在程序化场景上的密集分割任务，通过检测背景复杂性捷径并应用针对性数据，将 mIoU 从 0.948 提升至 0.998；(3) 一项纠缠检测实验，表明 ANOVA 审计能够识别不完美生成器中的跨因素污染。

Finally, we show that per-factor invariance penalties can transfer sensitivity between factors, identifying an open problem for representation-level correction.
最后，我们展示了针对各因素的不变性惩罚可以在不同因素间转移敏感性，从而指出了表征层面修正的一个待解决问题。
