# On the Invariants of Softmax Attention
# 关于 Softmax 注意力机制的不变性研究

**Abstract:** Softmax attention maps every query--key interaction into a probability distribution, but the underlying structure remains largely unexplored. We define the \emph{energy field}, the row-centered attention logit, and show that it exhibits invariant properties across models, architectures, and inputs.

**摘要：** Softmax 注意力机制将每一次查询（query）与键（key）的交互映射为一个概率分布，但其底层的结构在很大程度上仍未被探索。我们定义了“能量场”（energy field），即行中心化的注意力 Logit，并证明它在不同的模型、架构和输入中表现出不变的特性。

Two classes of invariants emerge. \emph{Mechanism-level} invariants follow from the algebraic structure of softmax attention. They include a per-row zero-sum constraint, a rank bound determined by the head dimension, and spectral signatures that follow from them.

研究中出现了两类不变性。**机制级（Mechanism-level）**不变性源于 Softmax 注意力的代数结构。它们包括每行的零和约束、由头维度（head dimension）决定的秩界（rank bound），以及由此产生的谱特征（spectral signatures）。

\emph{Model-level} regularities are not required by the mechanism, yet hold in every autoregressive language model we test, spanning several architecture families. The energy field distributes its variance over key positions without concentrating at a few. This delocalization traces to a property of the key matrix we call \emph{key incoherence}.

**模型级（Model-level）**的规律性并非由该机制所强制要求，但在我们测试的所有自回归语言模型中均成立，涵盖了多种架构系列。能量场将其方差分布在各个键位置上，而不会集中在少数几个位置。这种去局部化（delocalization）现象归因于键矩阵的一种特性，我们将其称为**键不相干性（key incoherence）**。

These invariants have practical consequences. The rank bound confines the energy field to a low-dimensional subspace. Key incoherence yields a per-head training monitor. All results are verified at multiple context lengths and input texts.

这些不变性具有实际意义。秩界将能量场限制在一个低维子空间内。键不相干性则提供了一种针对每个注意力头的训练监控指标。所有结果均在多种上下文长度和输入文本下得到了验证。
