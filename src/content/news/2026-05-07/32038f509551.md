# One Sequence to Segment Them All: Efficient Data Augmentation for CT and MRI Cross-Domain 3D Spine Segmentation
# 一种序列分割所有：用于 CT 和 MRI 跨域 3D 脊柱分割的高效数据增强方法

**Abstract:** Deep learning-based medical image segmentation is increasingly used to support clinical diagnosis and develop new treatment strategies. However, model performance remains limited by the scarcity of high-quality annotated data and insufficient generalization across imaging protocols. This limitation is particularly evident in MRI and CT, where models are typically trained on a single acquisition sequence and exhibit reduced robustness when applied to unseen sequences or contrasts.

**摘要：** 基于深度学习的医学图像分割正越来越多地用于支持临床诊断和制定新的治疗策略。然而，高质量标注数据的匮乏以及跨成像协议的泛化能力不足，仍然限制了模型的性能。这种局限性在 MRI 和 CT 中尤为明显，因为模型通常是在单一采集序列上进行训练的，当应用于未见过的序列或对比度时，其鲁棒性会降低。

Although data augmentation is widely used to improve general robustness on medical images, its impact on cross-modality generalization has not been quantitatively explored. In this work, we study a targeted set of data augmentation techniques designed to improve cross-modality transfer. We train three spine segmentation models, each on a single-modality/sequence dataset, and evaluate them across seven out-of-distribution datasets (spanning CT and MRI), reflecting a realistic single-sequence training and multi-sequence/contrast/modality deployment scenario.

尽管数据增强被广泛用于提高医学图像的通用鲁棒性，但其对跨模态泛化的影响尚未得到定量研究。在这项工作中，我们研究了一套旨在改善跨模态迁移的针对性数据增强技术。我们训练了三个脊柱分割模型，每个模型均基于单一模态/序列数据集，并在七个分布外数据集（涵盖 CT 和 MRI）上进行了评估，这反映了现实中“单序列训练、多序列/对比度/模态部署”的场景。

Our results demonstrate substantial performance gains on unseen domains (average Dice gain of 155 %) while preserving in-domain accuracy (average Dice decrease of 0.008 %), including effective transfer between CT and MRI. To mitigate the computational cost typically associated with strong data augmentation, we implement GPU-optimized augmentations that maintain, and even improve, training efficiency by approximately 10 %.

我们的结果表明，在未见过的领域中，模型性能获得了显著提升（平均 Dice 指数提升了 155%），同时保持了域内准确性（平均 Dice 指数仅下降 0.008%），其中包括 CT 和 MRI 之间的有效迁移。为了减轻强数据增强通常带来的计算成本，我们实现了 GPU 优化增强，在保持训练效率的同时，甚至将其提高了约 10%。

We release our approach as an open-source toolbox, enabling seamless integration into commonly used frameworks such as nnUNet and MONAI. These augmentations significantly enhance robustness to heterogeneous clinical imaging scenarios without compromising training speed.

我们将该方法作为开源工具箱发布，能够无缝集成到 nnUNet 和 MONAI 等常用框架中。这些增强技术在不牺牲训练速度的前提下，显著增强了模型对异构临床成像场景的鲁棒性。
