# When Safety Geometry Collapses: Fine-Tuning Vulnerabilities in Agentic Guard Models
# 当安全几何结构崩溃：智能体防护模型中的微调漏洞

**Abstract:** A guard model fine-tuned on entirely benign data can lose all safety alignment -- not through adversarial manipulation, but through standard domain specialization. We demonstrate this failure across three purpose-built safety classifiers -- LlamaGuard, WildGuard, and Granite Guardian -- deployed as protection layers in agentic AI pipelines, and show that it originates in the destruction of latent safety geometry: the structured harmful -- benign representational boundary that guides classification.

**摘要：** 一个在完全良性数据上进行微调的防护模型可能会丧失所有的安全对齐能力——这并非通过对抗性操纵，而是通过标准的领域专业化导致的。我们在三种专门构建的安全分类器（LlamaGuard、WildGuard 和 Granite Guardian）中演示了这种失效，这些模型被部署为智能体 AI 流水线中的保护层。研究表明，这种失效源于潜在安全几何结构的破坏，即引导分类的“有害-良性”结构化表征边界的崩塌。

We extract per-layer safety subspaces via SVD on class-conditional activation differences and track how this boundary evolves under benign fine-tuning. Granite Guardian undergoes complete collapse -- refusal rate drops from 85% to 0%, CKA falls to zero, and 100% of outputs become ambiguous -- a severity exceeding prior findings on general-purpose LLMs, explained by the specialization hypothesis: concentrated safety representations are efficient but catastrophically brittle.

我们通过对类条件激活差异进行奇异值分解（SVD）提取了各层的安全子空间，并追踪了该边界在良性微调下的演变过程。Granite Guardian 经历了彻底的崩溃——拒绝率从 85% 降至 0%，CKA（中心核对齐）降至零，且 100% 的输出变得模糊不清。这种严重程度超过了此前对通用大语言模型（LLM）的研究发现，这可以用“专业化假说”来解释：集中的安全表征虽然高效，但却极其脆弱。

To mitigate this, we propose Fisher-Weighted Safety Subspace Regularization (FW-SSR), a training-time penalty combining (i) curvature-aware direction weights derived from diagonal Fisher information and (ii) an adaptive $\lambda_t$ that scales with task-safety gradient conflict. FW-SSR recovers 75% refusal on Granite Guardian (CKA = 0.983) and reduces WildGuard's Attack Success Rate to 3.6% -- below the unmodified baseline -- by actively sharpening the safety subspace rather than merely anchoring it.

为了缓解这一问题，我们提出了“费舍尔加权安全子空间正则化”（Fisher-Weighted Safety Subspace Regularization, FW-SSR）。这是一种训练时的惩罚机制，结合了 (i) 源自对角费舍尔信息的曲率感知方向权重，以及 (ii) 随任务与安全梯度冲突而自适应调整的 $\lambda_t$。FW-SSR 通过主动锐化安全子空间而非仅仅对其进行锚定，使 Granite Guardian 的拒绝率恢复了 75%（CKA = 0.983），并将 WildGuard 的攻击成功率降低至 3.6%，低于未修改的基准模型。

Across all three models, structural representational geometry (CKA, Fisher score) predicts safety behavior more reliably than absolute displacement metrics, establishing geometry-based monitoring as a necessary component of guard model evaluation in agentic deployments.

在所有三个模型中，结构化表征几何（CKA、费舍尔得分）比绝对位移指标能更可靠地预测安全行为，这确立了基于几何的监控在智能体部署中作为防护模型评估必要组成部分的地位。
