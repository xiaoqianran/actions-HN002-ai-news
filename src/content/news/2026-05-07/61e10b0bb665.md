# Reasoning-Guided Grounding: Elevating Video Anomaly Detection through Multimodal Large Language Models
# 推理引导的定位：通过多模态大语言模型提升视频异常检测能力

**Abstract:** Video Anomaly Detection (VAD) has traditionally been framed as binary classification or outlier detection, providing neither interpretable reasoning nor precise spatial localization of anomalous events. While Vision-Language Models (VLMs) offer rich scene understanding, they struggle with reliable spatial grounding - often producing hallucinated or geometrically invalid bounding boxes when asked to localize objects.

**摘要：** 视频异常检测（VAD）传统上被定义为二分类或离群点检测任务，既无法提供可解释的推理过程，也无法对异常事件进行精确的空间定位。尽管视觉-语言模型（VLM）提供了丰富的场景理解能力，但它们在可靠的空间定位方面仍存在困难——当被要求定位物体时，往往会产生幻觉或几何上无效的边界框。

We propose VANGUARD (Video Anomaly Understanding through Reasoning and Grounding), a framework that unifies anomaly classification, spatial grounding, and chain-of-thought reasoning within a single VLM. VANGUARD introduces a three-stage curriculum that progressively layers training objectives: (1) classifier warmup on frozen backbone features, (2) LoRA-adapted spatial grounding, and (3) chain-of-thought generation.

我们提出了 VANGUARD（通过推理和定位进行视频异常理解），这是一个将异常分类、空间定位和思维链推理统一在单一 VLM 中的框架。VANGUARD 引入了一个三阶段课程，逐步叠加训练目标：（1）在冻结的主干特征上进行分类器预热，（2）基于 LoRA 的空间定位适配，以及（3）思维链生成。

To overcome the sparse annotation typical of VAD benchmarks, we employ a teacher-student annotation pipeline in which a VLM (Qwen3-VL-4B) generates structured per-subclip reasoning trajectories based on manual annotations available from the UCA Dataset. Further, GroundingDINO provides bounding box supervision.

为了克服 VAD 基准测试中常见的标注稀疏问题，我们采用了一种教师-学生标注流水线，其中 VLM（Qwen3-VL-4B）基于 UCA 数据集中现有的手动标注，生成结构化的子片段推理轨迹。此外，GroundingDINO 提供了边界框监督。

On UCF-Crime, VANGUARD achieves 94% ROC-AUC with 84% F1 while simultaneously producing interpretable chain-of-thought explanations and spatial grounding of anomalous objects - capabilities absent from prior VAD methods. Ablations confirm that staged training outperforms monolithic optimization, and that structured reasoning acts as an implicit regularizer yielding more balanced predictions than classification-only fine-tuning. Zero-shot transfer to XD-Violence and ShanghaiTech demonstrates cross-domain generalization without target-domain adaptation.

在 UCF-Crime 数据集上，VANGUARD 达到了 94% 的 ROC-AUC 和 84% 的 F1 分数，同时能够生成可解释的思维链解释和异常物体的空间定位——这些能力在以往的 VAD 方法中是不具备的。消融实验证实，分阶段训练优于单一的整体优化，且结构化推理作为一种隐式正则化手段，比单纯的分类微调能产生更平衡的预测结果。在 XD-Violence 和 ShanghaiTech 上的零样本迁移实验证明了该模型在无需目标域适配的情况下具备跨域泛化能力。
