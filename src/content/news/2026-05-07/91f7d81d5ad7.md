# Nintendo announces a new Star Fox for the Switch 2
# 任天堂宣布 Switch 2 将推出全新《星际火狐》游戏

It turns out Fox McCloud’s appearance in the Super Mario Galaxy Movie was a tease of things to come: Nintendo just surprise announced the first new Star Fox game in two decades. The game is called, simply, Star Fox, and it’s out very soon, launching on June 25th.

事实证明，福克斯·麦克劳德（Fox McCloud）在《超级马力欧银河》电影中的客串其实是未来计划的预告：任天堂刚刚惊喜宣布了二十年来首款全新的《星际火狐》（Star Fox）游戏。这款游戏简单地命名为《星际火狐》，并将于 6 月 25 日正式发售。

Nintendo says that it’s “based” on the classic space shooter Star Fox 64, but with redesigned characters and upgraded visuals. Essentially, it sounds like a modern remake of the game with the same level design, much as Star Fox 64 was to the SNES original. Based on the first look at the game, it looks thoroughly modern, with incredibly detailed characters, including a surprisingly realistic Slippy Toad. And yes, the in-ship banter remains. In addition to flying ships, there will be a handful of other vehicles to pilot, including a submarine.

任天堂表示，该作“基于”经典的太空射击游戏《星际火狐 64》，但对角色进行了重新设计并升级了视觉效果。从本质上讲，这听起来像是该游戏的现代重制版，保留了相同的关卡设计，正如当年《星际火狐 64》之于超级任天堂（SNES）原版的关系。从游戏的首批画面来看，它显得非常现代化，角色细节极其丰富，包括一个令人惊讶的写实版史利比（Slippy Toad）。当然，飞船内的闲聊互动也得到了保留。除了驾驶飞船外，玩家还可以操控包括潜水艇在内的多种载具。

The Star Fox reboot also includes some new features, like online play for its four-on-four multiplayer battle mode, and the option to play with mouse controls. Perhaps the most surprising addition is the realistic character avatars that players can use during online play. But if you’re looking for an old-school feel, the game does support the N64 controller.

此次《星际火狐》重启版还加入了一些新功能，例如支持四对四多人对战模式的在线联机，以及使用鼠标操作的选项。最令人惊喜的或许是玩家在在线对战时可以使用的写实风格角色头像。不过，如果你追求复古体验，游戏也支持使用 N64 手柄进行操作。

The announcement comes as Nintendo has a relatively sparse lineup for the Switch 2 for the rest of 2026. Outside of Star Fox, the only first-party titles on the schedule are Yoshi and the Mysterious Book, Rhythm Heaven Groove, Splatoon Raiders, and Fire Emblem: Fortune’s Weave.

此次发布正值任天堂 Switch 2 在 2026 年余下时间的阵容相对匮乏之际。除了《星际火狐》之外，目前计划中的第一方游戏仅有《耀西与神秘之书》（Yoshi and the Mysterious Book）、《节奏天国：律动》（Rhythm Heaven Groove）、《喷射战士：突袭者》（Splatoon Raiders）以及《火焰之纹章：命运编织》（Fire Emblem: Fortune’s Weave）。
