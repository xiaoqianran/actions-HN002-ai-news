# Insurance startup Corgi hits $1.3B valuation 4 months after its Series A
# 保险初创公司 Corgi 在 A 轮融资后仅 4 个月，估值即达到 13 亿美元

Business insurance startup Corgi announced on Wednesday a $160 million Series B, led by TCV, valuing the startup at $1.3 billion, the startup’s co-founder Nico Laqua said on LinkedIn.
商业保险初创公司 Corgi 的联合创始人 Nico Laqua 在领英（LinkedIn）上宣布，公司已完成 1.6 亿美元的 B 轮融资。本轮融资由 TCV 领投，公司估值达到 13 亿美元。

This comes just four months after the company announced a $108 million Series A. The company has now raised $268 million in funding to date, Laqua said, and has become Y Combinator’s latest unicorn.
就在四个月前，该公司刚刚宣布完成了 1.08 亿美元的 A 轮融资。Laqua 表示，公司迄今已累计融资 2.68 亿美元，并已成为 Y Combinator 孵化出的最新一家独角兽企业。

Laqua started the company with Emily Yuan in 2024 and was part of YC’s Spring 2024 batch. Corgi, which names Deel and Artisan as customers, offers coverage for general liability, cyber liability, and tech and AI liability.
Laqua 与 Emily Yuan 于 2024 年共同创立了该公司，并入选了 YC 2024 年春季创业营。Corgi 的客户包括 Deel 和 Artisan 等公司，主要提供一般责任险、网络责任险以及科技与人工智能责任险。

Other investors in the round include Kindred Ventures, Leblon Capital, and First Order Fund. Corgi did not immediately respond to our request for comment.
参与本轮融资的其他投资者还包括 Kindred Ventures、Leblon Capital 和 First Order Fund。Corgi 未能立即回应我们的置评请求。
