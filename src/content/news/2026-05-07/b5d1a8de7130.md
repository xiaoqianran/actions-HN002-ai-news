# eOptShrinkQ: Near-Lossless KV Cache Compression Through Optimal Spectral Denoising and Quantization
# eOptShrinkQ：通过最优谱去噪与量化实现近无损 KV Cache 压缩

**Abstract:** We show that the key-value (KV) cache in transformer attention heads admits a natural decomposition into a low-rank *shared context* component and a full-rank *per-token* residual, well described by the spiked random matrix model. 

**摘要：** 我们证明了 Transformer 注意力头中的键值（KV）缓存可以自然地分解为低秩的“共享上下文”分量和满秩的“逐 Token”残差，这可以通过尖峰随机矩阵模型（spiked random matrix model）得到很好的描述。

This observation leads to eOptShrinkQ, a two-stage compression pipeline: optimal singular value shrinkage (eOptShrink) automatically extracts the shared structure, and the residual -- which satisfies the *thin shell property* with delocalized coordinates -- is quantized by TurboQuant, a recently proposed per-vector scalar quantizer with near-optimal distortion guarantees. 

这一观察引出了 eOptShrinkQ，这是一个两阶段的压缩流水线：最优奇异值收缩（eOptShrink）自动提取共享结构；而满足具有去局部化坐标的“薄壳属性”（thin shell property）的残差，则通过 TurboQuant 进行量化。TurboQuant 是一种近期提出的逐向量标量量化器，具有近乎最优的失真保证。

By restoring the isotropy that scalar quantization assumes, spectral denoising eliminates the need for both outlier handling and dedicated inner product bias correction, freeing those bits for improved reconstruction. 

通过恢复标量量化所假设的各向同性，谱去噪消除了对异常值处理和专门的内积偏差校正的需求，从而释放了这些比特位以用于改进重构效果。

The theoretical grounding in random matrix theory provides three guarantees: automatic rank selection via the BBP phase transition, provably near-zero inner product bias on the residual, and coordinate delocalization ensuring near-optimal quantization distortion. 

基于随机矩阵理论的理论基础提供了三项保证：通过 BBP 相变实现自动秩选择、残差上可证明的近零内积偏差，以及确保近乎最优量化失真的坐标去局部化。

Experimentally, we validate eOptShrinkQ on Llama-3.1-8B and Ministral-8B across three levels: per-head MSE and inner product fidelity, where eOptShrinkQ saves nearly one bit per entry over TurboQuant at equivalent quality; end-to-end on LongBench (16 tasks), where eOptShrinkQ at ~2.2 bits per entry outperforms TurboQuant at 3.0 bits; and multi-needle retrieval, where eOptShrinkQ at 2.2 bits closely matches or exceeds uncompressed FP16, suggesting that spectral denoising can act as a beneficial regularizer for retrieval-intensive tasks.

在实验方面，我们在 Llama-3.1-8B 和 Ministral-8B 上从三个层面验证了 eOptShrinkQ：在逐头 MSE 和内积保真度方面，eOptShrinkQ 在同等质量下比 TurboQuant 每项节省了近 1 比特；在 LongBench（16 项任务）的端到端测试中，2.2 比特/项的 eOptShrinkQ 表现优于 3.0 比特的 TurboQuant；在多针检索（multi-needle retrieval）任务中，2.2 比特的 eOptShrinkQ 表现接近甚至超过了未压缩的 FP16，这表明谱去噪可以作为检索密集型任务的一种有益正则化手段。
