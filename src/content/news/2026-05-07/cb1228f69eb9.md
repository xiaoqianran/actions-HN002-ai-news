### Memorization In Stable Diffusion Is Unexpectedly Driven by CLIP Embeddings
### Stable Diffusion 中的记忆现象意外地由 CLIP 嵌入驱动

**Abstract:** Understanding how textual embeddings contribute to memorization in text-to-image diffusion models is crucial for both interpretability and safety. This paper investigates an unexpected behavior of CLIP embeddings in Stable Diffusion, revealing that the model disproportionately relies on specific embeddings.
**摘要：** 理解文本嵌入如何促成文生图扩散模型中的记忆现象，对于模型的可解释性和安全性至关重要。本文研究了 Stable Diffusion 中 CLIP 嵌入的一种意外行为，揭示了模型对特定嵌入存在不成比例的依赖。

We categorize input tokens as `<startoftext>`, `<prompt>`, `<endoftext>` and `<pad>` with corresponding embeddings $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{sot}}$, $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{pr}}$, $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{eot}}$, $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{pad}}$. We discover that $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{pr}}$ contribute minimally to generation in memorized cases. In contrast, $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{pad}}$ strongly affect memorization due to their structural duplication of $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{eot}}$, the only embedding explicitly optimized during CLIP training.
我们将输入标记（tokens）分类为 `<startoftext>`、`<prompt>`、`<endoftext>` 和 `<pad>`，并对应相应的嵌入向量 $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{sot}}$、$\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{pr}}$、$\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{eot}}$ 和 $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{pad}}$。我们发现，在发生记忆的案例中，$\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{pr}}$ 对生成过程的贡献微乎其微。相比之下，$\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{pad}}$ 对记忆现象有显著影响，这是因为它们在结构上复制了 $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{eot}}$——而 $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{eot}}$ 是 CLIP 训练过程中唯一经过显式优化的嵌入。

This duplication unintentionally amplifies the influence of $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{eot}}$, causing the model to over-rely on it, thereby driving memorization. Based on these observations, we propose two simple yet effective inference-time mitigation strategies: (1) Replacing the tokenizer's default `<pad>` from `<eot>` to the `!` token before embedding, and masking the $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{eot}}$; (2) Partial masking of $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{pad}}$. Both suppress memorization without degrading quality, and are readily deployable without prior detection.
这种复制无意中放大了 $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{eot}}$ 的影响，导致模型对其过度依赖，从而驱动了记忆现象。基于这些观察，我们提出了两种简单且有效的推理阶段缓解策略：（1）在嵌入之前，将分词器的默认 `<pad>` 从 `<eot>` 替换为 `!` 标记，并对 $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{eot}}$ 进行掩码处理；（2）对 $\mathbf{v}^{\mathbf{pad}}$ 进行部分掩码处理。这两种方法都能在不降低生成质量的前提下抑制记忆现象，且无需预先检测即可轻松部署。
