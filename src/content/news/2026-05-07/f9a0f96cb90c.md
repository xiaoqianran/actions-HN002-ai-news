# Native Instruments Komplete 26 adds weird new synths and experimental piano sounds
# Native Instruments 发布 Komplete 26，带来奇特的全新合成器与实验性钢琴音色

The music production suite comes in six flavors, ranging from $99 all the way up to $1,949.
该音乐制作套件共提供六个版本，价格从 99 美元到 1,949 美元不等。

The latest version of Native Instruments’ Komplete music production bundle is here with 62 new additions, including the wonderfully weird Absynth 6. Komplete 26 comes in several flavors, starting with three $99 Select bundles: Beats, Band, and Electronic. Prices jump pretty steeply from there, with Standard costing $549, Ultimate costing $1,249, and the Collector’s Edition priced at an eye-watering $1,949. You save some money if you’re upgrading from Komplete 15 (and yes, they switched from sequential numbers to years for 2026), but upgrading the Collector’s Edition will still set you back $399.
Native Instruments 的最新版 Komplete 音乐制作套件现已发布，新增了 62 款插件，其中包括极其独特且怪异的 Absynth 6。Komplete 26 提供多个版本，起步为三款售价 99 美元的 Select 套装：Beats、Band 和 Electronic。此后价格阶梯式上涨，Standard 版售价 549 美元，Ultimate 版售价 1,249 美元，而 Collector’s Edition（收藏版）的价格则高达 1,949 美元。如果你是从 Komplete 15 升级而来（没错，他们从 2026 年起将命名方式从序列号改为了年份），可以节省一些费用，但升级到收藏版仍需花费 399 美元。

Komplete 26 Standard should satisfy all but the most demanding producers. Absynth 6 and Massive X cover most of your synth needs. Kontakt 8 is the industry standard for sample-based instruments covering orchestras, drums, choirs, and more. It also has tools for sequencing and building chord progressions if you need a little creative inspiration. There’s also Guitar Rig 7 Pro for effects and amp simulations. Plus, Komplete comes with mixing and mastering plug-ins from iZotope, including Ozone 12, Neutron 5, and Nectar 4.
Komplete 26 Standard 版足以满足绝大多数制作人的需求。Absynth 6 和 Massive X 涵盖了你大部分的合成器需求。Kontakt 8 作为基于采样乐器的行业标准，涵盖了管弦乐、鼓组、合唱等多种音色。如果你需要创作灵感，它还提供了用于音序编排和构建和弦进行的功能工具。此外，还有用于效果处理和音箱模拟的 Guitar Rig 7 Pro。不仅如此，Komplete 还附带了来自 iZotope 的混音和母带处理插件，包括 Ozone 12、Neutron 5 和 Nectar 4。

Upgrading from the Standard Bundle to the Ultimate or Collectors does get you new additions like Claire and Claire: Avant, a pair of sampled Steinway D grand piano instruments that cover both traditional acoustic tones and experimental textures. There’s also a host of additions, like LCO Producer Strings and Moments: Vocal Clouds, that are targeted at score work. Of course, all those additional sampled instruments and expansion packs add to the download size, with the Collector’s Edition weighing in at 1.6 TB.
从 Standard 套装升级到 Ultimate 或 Collector’s 版本，你将获得诸如 Claire 和 Claire: Avant 等新内容，这是一对采样自施坦威 D 型三角钢琴的乐器，涵盖了传统的原声质感与实验性的音色纹理。此外，还有许多针对配乐工作设计的插件，如 LCO Producer Strings 和 Moments: Vocal Clouds。当然，所有这些额外的采样乐器和扩展包也增加了下载体积，Collector’s Edition 的总大小达到了 1.6 TB。
