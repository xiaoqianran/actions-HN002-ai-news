# Timer-XL: A Long-Context Foundation Model for Time-Series Forecasting
# Timer-XL：用于时间序列预测的长上下文基础模型

Lately, there has been a shift in how foundation models operate. After establishing that pretraining a large deep learning model on a vast corpus of temporal data grants generalizable properties, pretrained TS models now aim to be even more versatile. For time series, this means supporting exogenous variables and allowing variable context and prediction lengths. This article discusses Timer-XL[1], an upgraded time series model based on Timer[2]. Timer-XL is built for generalization, with a focus on long-context forecasting. Let’s get started!

最近，基础模型的运作方式发生了转变。在证实了对海量时间序列数据进行大规模深度学习预训练能够赋予模型通用性之后，预训练时间序列（TS）模型现在旨在变得更加多功能。对于时间序列而言，这意味着支持外生变量，并允许可变的上下文长度和预测长度。本文将讨论基于 Timer[2] 升级的时间序列模型 Timer-XL[1]。Timer-XL 专为泛化而构建，重点在于长上下文预测。让我们开始吧！

### What is Timer-XL
### 什么是 Timer-XL

Timer-XL is a decoder-only Transformer foundation model for forecasting. The model emphasizes generalizability and long-context predictions — offering unified, long-range forecasting.

Timer-XL 是一个用于预测的仅解码器（decoder-only）Transformer 基础模型。该模型强调泛化能力和长上下文预测，提供统一的远程预测能力。

**Key features of Timer-XL:**
**Timer-XL 的主要特点：**

*   **Varying input/output lengths:** Unlike models such as Tiny-Time-Mixers that have various versions for different input or output lengths, Timer-XL uses a single model for all cases, without making assumptions about context or prediction length.
*   **可变的输入/输出长度：** 与 Tiny-Time-Mixers 等针对不同输入或输出长度拥有多个版本的模型不同，Timer-XL 对所有情况使用单一模型，且不对上下文或预测长度做预设。
*   **Long-context forecasting:** Handles longer lookback windows effectively.
*   **长上下文预测：** 有效处理更长的回溯窗口。
*   **Rich features:** Forecasts non-stationary univariate series, complex multivariate dynamics, and covariate-informed contexts with exogenous variables — all in a unified setup.
*   **丰富的功能：** 在统一的架构下，预测非平稳单变量序列、复杂的多元动态，以及包含外生变量的协变量信息上下文。
*   **Versatile:** Can be trained from scratch or pretrained on large datasets. Further finetuning is optional for improved performance.
*   **多功能性：** 可以从头开始训练，也可以在大数据集上进行预训练。进一步的微调是可选的，以提升性能。

Timer-XL enhances forecasting accuracy by introducing TimeAttention — an elegant attention mechanism that we’ll discuss in detail below. The team behind Timer-XL (THUML lab at Tsinghua University[3]) has deep expertise in time-series modeling. They’ve released milestone models like iTransfomer, TimesNet, and Timer — Timer-XL’s predecessor.

Timer-XL 通过引入 TimeAttention（一种我们将在下文详细讨论的优雅注意力机制）提高了预测准确性。Timer-XL 背后的团队（清华大学 THUML 实验室[3]）在时间序列建模方面拥有深厚的专业知识。他们曾发布过 iTransformer、TimesNet 和 Timer（Timer-XL 的前身）等里程碑式的模型。

### Encoder vs Decoder vs Encoder-Decoder models
### 编码器 vs 解码器 vs 编码器-解码器模型

Before discussing Timer-XL, we’ll explore the related work and recap the state of foundation TS models — this will help us understand what led to Timer-XL’s breakthrough.

在讨论 Timer-XL 之前，我们将探讨相关工作并回顾时间序列基础模型的现状——这将有助于我们理解 Timer-XL 取得突破的原因。

**NLP Applications**
**NLP 应用**

In the early days of the Transformer, there was debate over which architecture was most effective. The original Transformer was an Encoder-Decoder model. Later, the Transformer research split into 2 branches: Encoder-only, like BERT (led by Google), and decoder-only, like GPT (led by OpenAI).

在 Transformer 的早期，关于哪种架构最有效存在争议。最初的 Transformer 是一个编码器-解码器模型。后来，Transformer 的研究分成了两个分支：以 BERT 为代表的仅编码器架构（由 Google 主导），以及以 GPT 为代表的仅解码器架构（由 OpenAI 主导）。

Encoder-Decoder models use a bidirectional encoder to understand input and a causal decoder to generate output one token at a time. They excel at sequence-to-sequence tasks such as translation and summarization. Encoder-only models use bidirectional attention (look for context in both ways) to understand a sentence and predict masked words within a sentence. They excel at NLU (natural language understanding) tasks. Decoder-only models use causal attention (the model only looks back for context) and learn to predict the next word. They excel at NLG (natural language generation) tasks.

编码器-解码器模型使用双向编码器来理解输入，并使用因果解码器逐个生成输出标记。它们擅长翻译和摘要等序列到序列的任务。仅编码器模型使用双向注意力（双向寻找上下文）来理解句子并预测句子中的掩码词。它们擅长自然语言理解（NLU）任务。仅解码器模型使用因果注意力（模型仅向后查看上下文）并学习预测下一个词。它们擅长自然语言生成（NLG）任务。

**Time-Series Applications**
**时间序列应用**

By late 2024 and early 2025, numerous foundation models were published, providing ample evidence of what works best. All these foundation models have come in many flavours. Examples are:
Decoder models: TimesFM (Google), Time-MOE
Encoder models: MOIRAI (Salesforce), MOMENT
Encoder-Decoder: Chronos (Amazon)

到 2024 年底和 2025 年初，大量基础模型发布，为哪种架构最有效提供了充分证据。这些基础模型呈现出多种形态，例如：
解码器模型：TimesFM (Google), Time-MOE
编码器模型：MOIRAI (Salesforce), MOMENT
编码器-解码器模型：Chronos (Amazon)

So far, decoder and encoder-decoder models outperform encoders in forecasting. Timer-XL’s authors back this up with extensive experiments — their results support the same conclusion.

到目前为止，解码器和编码器-解码器模型在预测任务上的表现优于编码器。Timer-XL 的作者通过大量的实验证实了这一点——他们的结果支持相同的结论。

### Long-Context Forecasting
### 长上下文预测

The primary advantage of Transformer models is their ability to handle a large context length. Modern LLMs such as Gemini support up to 1M tokens. They’re not flawless at this scale, but generally reliable up to 100k tokens. Time-series models are far behind — Transformer and DL forecasting models often struggle beyond 1K tokens. Recent foundation models such as MOIRAI support up to 4K.

Transformer 模型的主要优势在于其处理长上下文的能力。现代大语言模型（如 Gemini）支持高达 100 万个标记。虽然它们在此规模下并非完美，但通常在 10 万个标记内表现可靠。时间序列模型则落后得多——Transformer 和深度学习预测模型在超过 1000 个标记时往往表现吃力。最近的基础模型（如 MOIRAI）支持高达 4000 个标记。

There are 2 issues to examine here:
1. The maximum supported context length.
2. How the model handles the increased context length, in terms of performance.

这里有两个问题需要审视：
1. 最大支持的上下文长度。
2. 模型在性能方面如何处理增加的上下文长度。
