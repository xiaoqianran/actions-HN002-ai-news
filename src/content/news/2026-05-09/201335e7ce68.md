# 美国政府发布首批不明异常现象（UAP）文件与视频

唐纳德·J·特朗普在“真实社交”（Truth Social）上表示：鉴于公众表现出的巨大兴趣，我将指示战争部长及其他相关部门和机构，启动识别并发布与外星生命、不明空中现象（UAP）、不明飞行物（UFO）以及与这些极其复杂但又极其有趣且重要的事项相关的所有政府文件的程序。上帝保佑美国！[2026年2月19日]

Directive: In response to President Donald J. Trump’s directive for transparency on U.S. government information regarding Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP), the Department of War (DOW), with support from the Office of the Director of National Intelligence (ODNI), is overseeing government wide efforts to expeditiously find, review, identify, declassify and publicly release unresolved UAP-related records and historical documents in the federal government’s possession.

指令：为响应唐纳德·J·特朗普总统关于提高美国政府不明异常现象（UAP）信息透明度的指示，战争部（DOW）在国家情报总监办公室（ODNI）的支持下，正统筹全政府力量，旨在迅速查找、审查、识别、解密并公开联邦政府所持有的未决UAP相关记录及历史文件。

This is an unprecedented, historic undertaking that requires coordination between dozens of agencies and the review of tens of millions of records, many existing only on paper, spanning many decades. Given the scope of this task, the Department of War will be releasing new materials on a rolling basis as they are discovered and declassified, with tranches posted every few weeks.

这是一项史无前例的历史性任务，需要数十个机构之间的协调，并审查跨越数十年、总数达数千万份的记录，其中许多记录仅以纸质形式存在。鉴于任务的规模，战争部将在材料被发现并解密后，以滚动方式发布新资料，每隔几周发布一批。

The materials archived here are unresolved cases, meaning the government is unable to make a definitive determination on the nature of the observed phenomena. This can occur for a variety of reasons, including a lack of sufficient data, and the Department of War welcomes the application of private-sector analysis, information and expertise. DOW will continue to conduct separate reporting on resolved UAP cases, as mandated by statute. Under this Administration, we will pursue the truth and share our findings with the American people.

此处归档的材料均为未决案件，意味着政府无法对所观察到的现象性质做出明确结论。这种情况可能由多种原因导致，包括数据不足等。战争部欢迎私营部门提供分析、信息和专业知识。战争部将继续按照法律规定，对已结案的UAP案件进行单独报告。在本届政府领导下，我们将追求真相，并与美国人民分享我们的发现。

Release 01 CLEARED FOR RELEASE - MAY 8, 2026 STATEMENT: "The Department of War is in lockstep with President Trump to bring unprecedented transparency regarding our government’s understanding of Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena. These files, hidden behind classifications, have long fueled justified speculation — and it’s time the American people see it for themselves. This release of declassified documents demonstrates the Trump Administration’s earnest commitment to unprecedented transparency." -United States Secretary of War Pete Hegseth

发布 01：获准发布 - 2026年5月8日。声明：“战争部与特朗普总统步调一致，旨在就我国政府对不明异常现象的了解提供前所未有的透明度。这些隐藏在保密级别背后的文件长期以来引发了合理的猜测——现在是时候让美国人民亲自查看了。此次解密文件的发布，展示了特朗普政府对实现前所未有透明度的真诚承诺。”——美国战争部长皮特·海格塞斯（Pete Hegseth）
