# MidSteer: Optimal Affine Framework for Steering Generative Models
# MidSteer：用于引导生成模型的最佳仿射框架

Steering intermediate representations has emerged as a powerful strategy for controlling generative models, particularly in post-deployment alignment and safety settings. However, despite its empirical success, it currently lacks a comprehensive theoretical framework.
引导中间表征已成为控制生成模型的一种强大策略，特别是在部署后的对齐和安全设置中。然而，尽管它在经验上取得了成功，但目前仍缺乏一个全面的理论框架。

In this paper, we bridge this gap by formalizing the theory of concept steering. First, we establish a link between steering and affine concept erasure, proving that the standard approach for removing unwanted behaviors is a special case of LEACE (a closed-form method for affine erasure).
在本文中，我们通过形式化概念引导理论来弥补这一空白。首先，我们建立了引导与仿射概念擦除之间的联系，证明了消除不良行为的标准方法是 LEACE（一种用于仿射擦除的闭式方法）的一个特例。

Next, we formulate a principled theoretical framework for concept switching, LEACE-Switch, and characterize the assumptions under which it provides an optimal affine solution. Building on this analysis, we then introduce MidSteer (Minimal Disturbance concept Steering), a more general affine framework for concept manipulation that relaxes these assumptions and enables directed, minimal-disturbance transformations.
接下来，我们为概念切换制定了一个原则性的理论框架 LEACE-Switch，并刻画了其提供最佳仿射解的前提假设。在此分析的基础上，我们引入了 MidSteer（最小扰动概念引导），这是一个更通用的概念操作仿射框架，它放宽了这些假设，并实现了有向的、最小扰动的转换。

We demonstrate that MidSteer performs favorably across a range of tasks, modalities, and architectures, including vision diffusion models and large language models.
我们证明了 MidSteer 在各种任务、模态和架构（包括视觉扩散模型和大型语言模型）中均表现优异。
