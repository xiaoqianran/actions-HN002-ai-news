# The Best Mobile Gaming Controllers for iPhone and Android (2026)
# 2026年 iPhone 与 Android 最佳移动游戏手柄推荐

The best mobile gaming controllers are excellent tools that allow you to play compatible games for hours on end. Whether you're looking for an iPhone or Android controller, we've tested all the top picks (aka we've played a bunch of games on our phones) and have opinions on them all. Backbone makes the best mobile gaming controllers we've tried that'll work for most people, but we also have solid recommendations for specific uses, like for pairing with smart glasses or a tablet.

最好的移动游戏手柄是让你能够长时间畅玩兼容游戏的绝佳工具。无论你是在寻找 iPhone 还是 Android 手柄，我们都测试了市面上所有的热门产品（换句话说，我们在手机上玩了大量的游戏），并对它们都有各自的评价。Backbone 制造了我们测试过最适合大多数人的移动游戏手柄，但我们也针对特定用途（例如与智能眼镜或平板电脑配对）提供了一些可靠的建议。

***

### Best Mobile Gaming Controller: Backbone One
### 最佳移动游戏手柄：Backbone One

The Backbone One is as easy to recommend as it is to use. Slot the connector—either USB-C or Lightning—into your phone and stretch the controller over the back. The buttons and triggers are clicky and responsive, and the controller feels comfortable for hours-long gaming sessions without being too bulky. It also has pass-through charging, a screenshot or screen recording button, a mute button, and a headphone jack.

Backbone One 既好用又值得推荐。只需将接口（USB-C 或 Lightning）插入手机，然后将手柄拉伸并扣在手机背面即可。按键和扳机反馈清脆且灵敏，手柄握持感舒适，适合长时间游戏，且不会显得过于笨重。它还具备直通充电功能、截图/录屏按钮、静音键以及耳机插孔。

Part of what makes all Backbone controllers so great is the manufacturer's software and additional features. The controller supports a number of gaming apps and services, and the companion app is fantastic (though not required). My favorite bit is the list of recommended games with controller support. You can also use the app to message and party chat with your friends, stream directly to Twitch, record and edit videos in 1080p at 60 frames per second, and more.

Backbone 手柄之所以出色，部分原因在于其配套的软件和附加功能。该手柄支持多种游戏应用和服务，其配套 App 非常棒（虽然不是必须安装）。我最喜欢的部分是它提供的“支持手柄的游戏推荐列表”。你还可以使用该 App 与朋友发消息、进行语音聊天、直接推流到 Twitch、录制并编辑 1080p 60fps 的视频等。

### Best Upgrade Mobile Gaming Controller: Backbone Pro
### 最佳进阶移动游戏手柄：Backbone Pro

The Backbone Pro expands on all of the features I loved about the Backbone One. The Pro is slightly chunkier, grippier, and more comfortable, and although my hands are small, I can imagine this is easier to hold for folks with larger hands. It has a quieter, more premium feel, and it still has the headphone port and pass-through charging capabilities of the One. The Pro feels just as ergonomic to me as a console controller, with no drawbacks or compromises. I liked the full-size joysticks, programmable back buttons, and Hall-effect rear triggers.

Backbone Pro 在 Backbone One 的基础上扩展了我所喜欢的所有功能。Pro 版本稍微厚实一些，握感更好，也更舒适。虽然我的手比较小，但我可以想象对于手大的人来说，这款手柄会更容易握持。它的按键声音更小，质感更高级，并且依然保留了 One 版本上的耳机接口和直通充电功能。对我来说，Pro 的人体工学设计与主机手柄无异，没有任何短板或妥协。我非常喜欢它的全尺寸摇杆、可编程背键以及霍尔效应后扳机。

The Backbone Pro also has Bluetooth connectivity, which means you can use it with your phone, tablet, laptop, VR headset, and compatible smart TVs. You can connect to multiple devices simultaneously and switch between them with just a tap. If you're serious about mobile gaming, or you want a comfortable controller that can swap between your devices seamlessly, I recommend the Backbone Pro.

Backbone Pro 还具备蓝牙连接功能，这意味着你可以将其与手机、平板电脑、笔记本电脑、VR 头显以及兼容的智能电视配合使用。你可以同时连接多个设备，并只需轻轻一点即可在它们之间切换。如果你对移动游戏非常认真，或者想要一个可以在不同设备间无缝切换的舒适手柄，我推荐 Backbone Pro。

### Best Magnetic Mobile Gaming Controller: ohsnap! Mcon
### 最佳磁吸式移动游戏手柄：ohsnap! Mcon

The Mcon controller from Ohsnap has some quirks, but its general concept is neat. The MagSafe controller attaches to the back of your iPhone or Android phone (a magnetic ring is included for Android users, though Ohsnap recommends using a MagSafe case). Press the Eject button to pop out your phone, almost like you would with an old-school T-Mobile Sidekick, and reveal the D-pad, joystick, and buttons below.

Ohsnap 推出的 Mcon 手柄虽然有一些小怪癖，但其整体概念非常巧妙。这款 MagSafe 手柄可以吸附在 iPhone 或 Android 手机背面（Android 用户附赠磁吸环，不过 Ohsnap 建议使用 MagSafe 手机壳）。按下弹出按钮，手机就会像老式 T-Mobile Sidekick 手机那样滑出，露出下方的十字键、摇杆和按键。

There are hand grips you can pull down and out for added stability if you'd rather have a more traditional design than playing with a rectangular controller. The Mcon pairs with your phone via Bluetooth and has minimal latency, with recessed thumbsticks and buttons that feel satisfying to press.

如果你更喜欢传统手柄设计，而不是拿着一个长方形的手柄，你可以将手柄两侧的握把向下拉出，以增加稳定性。Mcon 通过蓝牙与手机配对，延迟极低，内凹的摇杆和按键按压感非常令人满意。
