**Nintendo Co., Ltd. Notice Regarding Price Revisions for Nintendo Products and Services**

Nintendo Co., Ltd. (HQ: Kyoto, Minami-ku, Japan; President and Representative Director: Shuntaro Furukawa, “Nintendo” hereafter) hereby announces that it has decided to revise the prices of the following products and services.
任天堂株式会社（总部：日本京都市南区；代表取缔役社长：古川俊太郎，以下简称“任天堂”）在此宣布，决定对以下产品及服务进行价格调整。

**About Nintendo**
**关于任天堂**

Nintendo Co., Ltd., headquartered in Kyoto, Japan, has been providing a wide range of entertainment products and experiences since its founding in 1889, beginning with the manufacture and sale of Hanafuda playing cards.
任天堂株式会社总部位于日本京都，自1889年创立以来，从制造和销售花札扑克牌起步，一直致力于提供广泛的娱乐产品与体验。

Since the 1983 release of the Family Computer (Famicom) system in Japan, and continuing through Nintendo Switch 2, Nintendo’s focus has been the development, manufacturing, and sale of its gaming systems and software.
自1983年日本推出红白机（Famicom）以来，直至如今的 Nintendo Switch 2，任天堂始终专注于游戏主机及软件的开发、制造与销售。

To date, Nintendo has sold more than 6.1 billion video games and over 870 million hardware units globally, and has created franchises such as Mario™, Donkey Kong™, The Legend of Zelda™, Pokémon™, Metroid™, Kirby™, Animal Crossing™, Pikmin™, and Splatoon™.
迄今为止，任天堂在全球已售出超过61亿份游戏软件及超过8.7亿台硬件设备，并创造了包括马力欧™、大金刚™、塞尔达传说™、宝可梦™、银河战士™、卡比™、动物森友会™、皮克敏™以及喷射战士™等知名系列。

Nintendo’s continuing mission is to create reasons to choose Nintendo for unique, family-friendly entertainment that puts smiles on the faces of everyone it touches.
任天堂的使命始终如一：通过创造独特且适合家庭的娱乐体验，让每一位接触到任天堂产品的人脸上洋溢笑容，并以此成为人们选择任天堂的理由。
