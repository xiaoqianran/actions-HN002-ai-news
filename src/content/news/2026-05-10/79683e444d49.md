# Internet Archive Switzerland

Thirty years ago, Brewster Kahle founded the Internet Archive with an ambitious goal: Universal Access to All Knowledge. Today, that mission continues to grow with an exciting new chapter: the launch of the Internet Archive Switzerland, a non-profit foundation based in St. Gallen.

三十年前，布鲁斯特·卡勒（Brewster Kahle）创立了互联网档案库（Internet Archive），并怀揣着一个宏伟的目标：让所有人都能获取所有知识。如今，这一使命开启了激动人心的新篇章，不断发展壮大：总部位于圣加仑的非营利性基金会——瑞士互联网档案库（Internet Archive Switzerland）正式成立。

The Internet Archive Switzerland, online at https://internetarchive.ch/, is a newly-formed Swiss non-profit foundation that will operate independently within its national context. Its efforts will initially focus on preserving endangered archives from around the world and collecting the generative AI wave that is currently upon us all.

瑞士互联网档案库（网址：https://internetarchive.ch/）是一家新成立的瑞士非营利性基金会，将在其国家范围内独立运作。其工作重点最初将集中在保护全球濒危档案，以及收集当前席卷全球的生成式人工智能浪潮相关资料。

With a UNESCO conference planned for November 2026 in Paris, Internet Archive Switzerland is taking a concrete step to explore how endangered archives can be protected. In parallel, the Swiss foundation is working in partnership with the School of Computer Science at the University of St. Gallen, on the Gen AI Archive project led by Prof. Dr. Damian Borth. Together, they aim to begin archiving AI models, which is an emerging frontier for preservation.

随着联合国教科文组织计划于2026年11月在巴黎召开会议，瑞士互联网档案库正采取具体行动，探索如何保护濒危档案。与此同时，该基金会正与圣加仑大学计算机科学学院合作，开展由达米安·博斯（Damian Borth）教授领导的“生成式AI档案”（Gen AI Archive）项目。双方旨在共同开启人工智能模型的归档工作，这在档案保存领域是一个新兴的前沿阵地。

The choice of St. Gallen is no coincidence. With a thousand-year tradition of archiving and scholarship, the city offers a fitting home for this next phase of digital preservation. Its strong academic environment—including collaboration with the University of St. Gallen—makes it an ideal place to establish a 21st century memory organization.

选择圣加仑并非巧合。这座城市拥有千年的档案与学术传统，为数字保存的下一阶段提供了理想的家园。其浓厚的学术氛围（包括与圣加仑大学的合作）使其成为建立21世纪记忆机构的理想之地。

“St. Gallen is a very suitable place to take the preservation of our universal知识 a step further. Stability and innovation go hand in hand here and are embedded in a deep understanding of the importance of cultural heritage,” said Roman Griesfelder, the executive director of Internet Archive Switzerland.

“圣加仑是一个非常适合将人类知识保存工作推向新高度的地方。在这里，稳定与创新并存，并深深植根于对文化遗产重要性的深刻理解之中，”瑞士互联网档案库执行董事罗曼·格里斯费尔德（Roman Griesfelder）表示。

Internet Archive Switzerland joins a growing group of mission-aligned organizations, alongside Internet Archive, Internet Archive Canada, and Internet Archive Europe. Together, these independent libraries strengthen a shared vision: building a distributed, resilient digital library for the world.

瑞士互联网档案库加入了日益壮大的使命一致的组织行列，与互联网档案库（Internet Archive）、加拿大互联网档案库（Internet Archive Canada）和欧洲互联网档案库（Internet Archive Europe）并肩作战。这些独立的图书馆共同强化了一个愿景：为全世界构建一个分布式、具有韧性的数字图书馆。
