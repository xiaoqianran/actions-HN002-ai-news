# The Instax Wide 400 builds on instant photography’s simplicity and stretches it, literally
# Instax Wide 400：在拍立得摄影的简约之上，实现“宽幅”升级

In an increasingly AI-driven and digital world, analog instant film and retro-style cameras remain popular, fueled by a combination of both nostalgia and charm. Instant cameras, in particular, stand out for their simplicity and an experience that has remained largely unchanged over time.
在一个日益由人工智能和数字化驱动的世界里，模拟拍立得胶片和复古风格相机依然广受欢迎，这得益于怀旧情怀与独特魅力的结合。尤其是拍立得相机，它们以其简约的特性和多年来几乎未曾改变的拍摄体验而脱颖而出。

Fujifilm’s $175 Instax Wide 400 builds on the familiar instant photography experience and expands it with a wider format. As someone who appreciates simple, easy-to-use instant cameras and often shoots with an Instax Square, I was interested in trying a model that offers larger prints and support for landscape photography.
富士售价 175 美元的 Instax Wide 400 在熟悉的拍立得摄影体验基础上，通过更宽的画幅进行了扩展。作为一个欣赏简单易用拍立得相机、且经常使用 Instax Square 的用户，我很有兴趣尝试一款能提供更大照片尺寸并支持风景摄影的机型。

The Instax Wide 400 is built for capturing group shots, scenic landscapes, and moments where fitting more of the scene into the frame really matters. It produces 62 × 99 mm prints, about twice the size of Instax Mini photos, giving you a lot more space to work with in every shot.
Instax Wide 400 专为拍摄合影、壮丽风景以及那些需要将更多场景纳入画面的时刻而设计。它能冲印出 62 × 99 毫米的照片，尺寸约为 Instax Mini 照片的两倍，让你在每一张照片中都有更大的创作空间。

Since it’s a simple point-and-shoot camera, it requires no prior photography experience and is designed to be easy and intuitive to use. Its appeal lies in fun and simplicity rather than advanced controls. With its one-button design, the camera offers no manual exposure controls. It automatically manages flash, focus, and other settings, leaving you to simply frame the image and take the shot.
由于它是一款简单的“傻瓜式”相机，无需任何摄影经验，设计上追求简单直观。它的魅力在于乐趣和简约，而非高级的操控功能。该相机采用一键式设计，不提供手动曝光控制。它会自动管理闪光灯、对焦和其他设置，你只需构图并按下快门即可。

When lighting is ideal, the images print with nice color and a good amount of detail with some extra saturation. I was quite impressed by the quality because it managed to pick up accurate colors and distant details. However, the simplicity of the camera does have some trade-offs because there isn’t much you can do to make photos brighter if you’re not in an ideal lighting situation. I did end up with some less-than-ideal shots because, in scenes with strong contrast, like dark trees against a bright sky, the details tended to blur together.
在光线理想的情况下，照片色彩表现出色，细节丰富，且带有一定的饱和度。我对它的画质印象深刻，因为它能捕捉到准确的色彩和远处的细节。然而，相机的简约也带来了一些权衡：如果你处于光线不佳的环境中，很难通过手动调节让照片变亮。我确实拍出了一些不太理想的照片，因为在强对比场景下（例如明亮天空下的深色树木），细节往往会模糊在一起。

The rest of the camera is equally straightforward. To power on the camera, you rotate the lens counterclockwise. The first click activates close-up mode for subjects about 0.9 to three meters away. Turning it once more switches to landscape mode for distant subjects beyond three meters. Rotating the lens clockwise powers the camera off.
相机的其他部分同样直观。要开启相机，只需逆时针旋转镜头。第一档点击会激活近摄模式，适用于 0.9 到 3 米距离的主体。再转动一次则切换到风景模式，适用于 3 米以外的远景。顺时针旋转镜头即可关闭相机。

The camera comes with a close-up lens attachment that snaps onto the front for close-up shots. I found the attachment was good at helping for avoiding focus and blur issues when taking close-ups. Loading the cartridge is simple, with guide lines to help you align it correctly in the camera. The door closes securely using a twist lock that latches shut. There is also a small indicator on the back that shows how much film is left.
相机附带一个可扣在镜头前方的近摄镜附件。我发现这个附件在拍摄特写时能有效避免对焦和模糊问题。装载胶片盒非常简单，相机内有引导线帮助你正确对齐。后盖通过旋转锁扣牢固关闭。背面还有一个小指示器，显示剩余胶片的数量。

Coming in at 162 mm x 98 mm x 123 mm and 1.4 pounds, the camera is quite bulky. It’s noticeably bigger than the Instax Mini cameras and the Instax Square models. However, I wasn’t too surprised by the size, since it makes sense that larger photos would require a bigger camera. That said, it’s not something you’d want to carry around without a bag, and while the shoulder strap helps, I was still very conscious of it and careful not to bump it into people or objects in busy spaces.
这款相机尺寸为 162 x 98 x 123 毫米，重量为 1.4 磅（约 635 克），体积相当大。它明显比 Instax Mini 和 Instax Square 系列机型要大。不过，我对这个尺寸并不感到意外，毕竟更大的照片需要更大的机身是合乎逻辑的。话虽如此，如果没有包，你肯定不会想随身携带它。虽然肩带有所帮助，但我依然时刻留意着它，在拥挤的地方小心翼翼，以免撞到人和物体。

Although Instax does offer a camera case sold separately, I didn’t receive one alongside my review unit, so I can’t speak to it. Since it’s a bit bulky, it’s pretty noticeable, and it ended up attracting the attention of a few people when I was testing it. A worker at a downtown gelato shop even stopped to compliment it, calling it a “cool looking camera.” That kind of attention is part of the appeal.
虽然 Instax 确实提供单独出售的相机包，但我收到的评测机并没有附带，所以无法评价。由于它体积较大，非常显眼，我在测试时确实吸引了不少人的注意。市中心一家冰淇淋店的员工甚至特意停下来称赞它，说这是一台“看起来很酷的相机”。这种关注度也是它魅力的一部分。

The camera’s self-timer is a nice feature, as it makes it possible to include everyone in a shot without needing someone to press the shutter button. It works by pushing a lever on the side and choosing one of four durations, ranging from two to 10 seconds. The camera emits a ticking sound to indicate that the timer is active, and red lights provide a visual countdown.
相机的自拍定时器是一个不错的功能，它让你无需他人帮忙按快门就能把所有人拍进照片。只需推动侧面的拨杆，选择 2 到 10 秒之间的四种时长即可。相机在定时器激活时会发出滴答声，红灯则提供视觉倒计时。

If you don’t have a tripod, the camera’s strap includes two angle adjustment accessories that can help prop and position the camera on a flat surface. To use one, slide it into the indented space on the camera’s underside, set the self-timer, and step back into the frame.
如果你没有三脚架，相机背带中包含两个角度调节配件，可以帮助将相机支撑并固定在平坦的表面上。使用时，将其滑入相机底部的凹槽，设置好自拍定时器，然后退回到画面中即可。

Film for the camera is available in standard white frame, black frame, metallic borders, and monochrome. Fujifilm sent me the standard white film alongside my review unit, which is the film I used for this review. Each box of film comes with 20 sheets and costs between $25-$28. As for colors, the Instax Wide 400 comes in two options: green and jet black. As someone who loves a good shade of green, I liked that the color of the camera gave it a more playful, fun vibe.
该相机使用的胶片有标准白边、黑边、金属边框和黑白胶片可选。富士随评测机寄给我的是标准白边胶片，这也是我本次评测所使用的胶片。每盒胶片包含 20 张，售价在 25 到 28 美元之间。颜色方面，Instax Wide 400 有绿色和黑色两种选择。作为一个喜欢绿色的人，我很喜欢这款相机的配色，它赋予了相机一种更俏皮、更有趣的氛围。

Overall, I think this camera is a great option for people of all ages, as it offers high-quality images while remaining simple enough for kids and teens who are just starting out in photography.
总的来说，我认为这款相机对于各个年龄段的人来说都是一个不错的选择，它既能提供高质量的照片，又足够简单，非常适合刚开始接触摄影的孩子和青少年。
