They can also let your mom choose exactly what she wants, making them both convenient and versatile.

以下是我们整理的一份清单，包含了《The Verge》团队成员使用过或赠送过的最佳数字好礼。这份清单按兴趣分类，无论你的母亲是热爱艺术、健身还是其他领域，你都能找到完美的礼物。这样一来，你至少能送出一份比亚马逊或沃尔玛礼品卡更有心思的礼物——尽管在我们看来，那些礼品卡依然是完全可行的选择。

### Gifts for film and TV buffs
### 影视爱好者的礼物

Whether your mom is a movie buff or an avid sports fan, there are a number of subscriptions that’ll grant her access to a wide range of content. Below are some of the most popular, as well as a few catered toward anime diehards, horror lovers, and those looking for something more niche.
无论你的母亲是电影迷还是体育迷，都有许多订阅服务能让她畅享海量内容。以下是一些最受欢迎的选择，以及针对动漫死忠粉、恐怖片爱好者和追求小众内容人群的推荐。

A gift card to a major streaming service like Netflix, Peacock, or Paramount Plus is a good gift that’ll cater to all kinds of passions. With a Netflix account, your mom can binge Stranger Things and Bridgerton, while Peacock provides access to shows like The Paper, Parks and Recreation, and Downton Abbey. A Paramount Plus subscription, meanwhile, lets her dive into Survivor 50, Star Trek: Strange New Worlds, and the entire Taylor Sheridan universe.
一张主流流媒体服务（如 Netflix、Peacock 或 Paramount Plus）的礼品卡是一份能满足各种爱好的好礼物。有了 Netflix 账号，你的母亲可以刷《怪奇物语》和《布里奇顿》；Peacock 则提供《办公室》、《公园与游憩》和《唐顿庄园》等剧集；而 Paramount Plus 订阅则让她能沉浸在《幸存者 50》、《星际迷航：奇异新世界》以及泰勒·谢里丹的整个影视宇宙中。

A Criterion Channel gift card grants access to more than a thousand classic and contemporary Hollywood, international, arthouse, and independent films. It also features programming that spotlights directors, stars, genres, and themes, including a “15-minute-a-month film school.”
Criterion Channel 礼品卡可以解锁上千部经典及当代好莱坞电影、国际电影、艺术电影和独立电影。它还提供聚焦导演、明星、类型和主题的专题节目，包括“每月15分钟电影学院”。

For the anime lovers in your life, a Crunchyroll gift card provides access to hundreds of anime shows and films shortly after they air in Japan, including Jujutsu Kaisen, Blue Lock, and Mobile Suit Gundam: The Witch from Mercury. They can even use the gift to purchase Crunchyroll’s extensive collection of anime figures, vinyl records, and clothes.
对于生活中的动漫爱好者，Crunchyroll 礼品卡能让他们在动画于日本播出后不久，就能观看数百部动漫剧集和电影，包括《咒术回战》、《蓝色监狱》和《机动战士高达：水星的魔女》。他们甚至可以用这份礼物购买 Crunchyroll 丰富的动漫手办、黑胶唱片和服饰。

### Gifts for the gamers
### 游戏玩家的礼物

If you’re not sure which type of games your mom prefers, you can gift her an Xbox, PlayStation, or Nintendo subscription. Not only will these memberships grant them access to free digital games, but they also include perks such as online multiplayer and cloud saves, among other incentives.
如果你不确定你的母亲喜欢哪种游戏，你可以送她 Xbox、PlayStation 或 Nintendo 的订阅服务。这些会员资格不仅能让她获得免费的数字游戏，还包括在线多人联机、云存档等其他福利。

If your mom owns a PlayStation 5, a PlayStation Plus membership grants them access to free titles and discounts every month, lets her play games online, and allows her to access cloud-based backups.
如果你的母亲拥有 PlayStation 5，PlayStation Plus 会员资格能让她每月获得免费游戏和折扣，进行在线联机，并使用云端备份功能。

Nintendo Switch lovers might enjoy an annual subscription to Nintendo Switch Online. The membership lets your mom play more than 150 retro games released during the NES, SNES, and original Game Boy eras. She can also play online with friends, access cloud saves for games, and listen to her favorite Nintendo tunes via the Nintendo Music mobile app.
Nintendo Switch 的爱好者可能会喜欢 Nintendo Switch Online 的年度订阅。该会员资格让你的母亲可以畅玩超过 150 款 NES、SNES 和初代 Game Boy 时期的复古游戏。她还可以与朋友在线联机、使用云存档，并通过 Nintendo Music 移动应用收听她最爱的任天堂音乐。
