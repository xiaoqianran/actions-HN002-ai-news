# LookWhen? Fast Video Recognition by Learning When, Where, and What to Compute
# LookWhen? 通过学习何时、何地以及计算什么来实现快速视频识别

Transformers dominate video recognition. They split videos into tokens, and processing them has expensive superlinear computational cost. Yet videos are filled with redundancy, so we can question the need for this expense.
Transformer 模型在视频识别领域占据主导地位。它们将视频分割成 Token，而处理这些 Token 具有昂贵的超线性计算成本。然而，视频中充满了冗余信息，因此我们有必要质疑这种高昂开销的必要性。

We introduce LookWhen, a selector-extractor framework that factorizes video recognition into learning when, where, and what to compute. Our shallow selector gets a scaled-down video and quickly scores all tokens across space-time, while our deep extractor gets the top-K selected tokens to approximate full-video representations without actually processing all the tokens.
我们引入了 LookWhen，这是一个选择器-提取器（selector-extractor）框架，它将视频识别分解为学习“何时、何地以及计算什么”。我们的浅层选择器接收缩减后的视频，并快速对时空中的所有 Token 进行评分；而我们的深层提取器则获取评分最高的 K 个 Token，从而在无需实际处理所有 Token 的情况下近似得到全视频的表征。

A key challenge is defining effective supervision for selection and extraction. For selection pre-training, we introduce a score on representations that ranks tokens by uniqueness using a simple nearest-neighbor distance. For extraction pre-training, we distill both a video teacher and an image teacher, for which we normalize its frame-wise representations to learn what changes within videos.
一个关键挑战是如何为选择和提取过程定义有效的监督机制。在选择预训练阶段，我们引入了一种表征评分机制，利用简单的最近邻距离根据唯一性对 Token 进行排序。在提取预训练阶段，我们同时蒸馏了视频教师模型和图像教师模型，并对其逐帧表征进行归一化处理，以学习视频内部的变化。

Through these strategies, our selector-extractor learns general and efficient representations for feature extraction or fine-tuning to a task. Through experiments on Kinetics-400, SSv2, Epic-Kitchens, Diving48, Jester, and Charades, we show that LookWhen achieves a better accuracy-computation trade-off than efficient models and upgraded baselines of similar size.
通过这些策略，我们的选择器-提取器能够学习到通用且高效的表征，用于特征提取或针对特定任务的微调。通过在 Kinetics-400、SSv2、Epic-Kitchens、Diving48、Jester 和 Charades 等数据集上的实验，我们证明了 LookWhen 在准确率与计算成本的权衡上，优于同等规模的高效模型和升级后的基准模型。

LookWhen Pareto-dominates in accuracy-FLOPs on 9 of 12 cases (6 tasks x 2 settings) and roughly matches on 3. In accuracy-throughput, measuring time in practice, LookWhen is more efficient still at 6.7x faster than InternVideo2-B at equal accuracy.
在 12 种测试情况（6 个任务 × 2 种设置）中，LookWhen 在准确率-FLOPs 指标上有 9 种情况实现了帕累托占优（Pareto-dominates），其余 3 种情况基本持平。在衡量实际运行时间的准确率-吞吐量指标上，LookWhen 的效率更高，在相同准确率下比 InternVideo2-B 快 6.7 倍。
