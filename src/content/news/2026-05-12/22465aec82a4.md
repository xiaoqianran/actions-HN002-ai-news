# Edge Deep Learning in Computer Vision and Medical Diagnostics: A Comprehensive Survey
# 边缘深度学习在计算机视觉与医学诊断中的应用：综合综述

**Abstract:** Edge deep learning, a paradigm change reconciling edge computing and deep learning, facilitates real-time decision making attuned to environmental factors through the close integration of computational resources and data sources. 
**摘要：** 边缘深度学习作为一种调和边缘计算与深度学习的范式变革，通过计算资源与数据源的紧密集成，促进了能够适应环境因素的实时决策。

Here we provide a comprehensive review of the current state of the art in edge deep learning, focusing on computer vision applications, in particular medical diagnostics. An overview of the foundational principles and technical advantages of edge deep learning is presented, emphasising the capacity of this technology to revolutionise a wide range of domains. 
本文对边缘深度学习的当前技术水平进行了全面综述，重点关注计算机视觉应用，特别是医学诊断领域。文中概述了边缘深度学习的基本原理和技术优势，强调了该技术在变革广泛领域方面的潜力。

Furthermore, we present a novel categorisation of edge hardware platforms based on performance and usage scenarios, facilitating platform selection and operational effectiveness. Following this, we dive into approaches to effectively implement deep neural networks on edge devices, encompassing methods such as lightweight design and model compression. 
此外，我们提出了一种基于性能和使用场景的边缘硬件平台新分类法，旨在促进平台选择并提高运营效率。随后，我们深入探讨了在边缘设备上有效实现深度神经网络的方法，涵盖了轻量化设计和模型压缩等技术。

Reviewing practical applications in the fields of computer vision in general and medical diagnostics in particular, we demonstrate the profound impact edge-deployed deep learning models can have in real-life situations. Finally, we provide an analysis of potential future directions and obstacles to the adoption of edge deep learning, with the intention to stimulate further investigations and advancements of intelligent edge deep learning solutions. 
通过回顾计算机视觉（特别是医学诊断）领域的实际应用，我们展示了部署在边缘端的深度学习模型在现实场景中产生的深远影响。最后，我们分析了边缘深度学习在采用过程中面临的潜在未来方向和障碍，旨在激发对智能边缘深度学习解决方案的进一步研究与进展。

This survey provides researchers and practitioners with a comprehensive reference shedding light on the critical role deep learning plays in the advancement of edge computing applications.
本综述为研究人员和从业者提供了一份全面的参考资料，阐明了深度学习在推动边缘计算应用发展中所发挥的关键作用。
