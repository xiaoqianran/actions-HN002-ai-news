# R$^3$L: Reasoning 3D Layouts from Relative Spatial Relations
# R$^3$L：基于相对空间关系的 3D 布局推理

**Abstract:** Relative spatial relations provide a compact representation of spatial structure and are fundamental to relative spatial reasoning in 3D layout generation. Recent works leverage Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) to infer such relations, but the inferred relations are often unreliable and are typically handled with post-hoc heuristics. 

**摘要：** 相对空间关系为空间结构提供了一种紧凑的表示方式，是 3D 布局生成中相对空间推理的基础。近期的研究利用多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）来推断这些关系，但推断出的关系往往不可靠，且通常需要通过事后启发式方法进行处理。

In this paper, we propose R$^3$L, a general framework that improves the reliability and consistency of relative spatial reasoning for 3D layout generation. Our key motivation is that multi-hop reasoning requires repeated reference-frame transformations, which accumulate errors in inferred relations and lead to semantic and metric drift. 

在本文中，我们提出了 R$^3$L，这是一个旨在提高 3D 布局生成中相对空间推理的可靠性和一致性的通用框架。我们的核心动机在于：多跳推理需要反复进行参考系转换，这会导致推断关系中的误差累积，进而引发语义和度量上的偏差。

To mitigate this, we propose invariant spatial decomposition to break coupled relation chains, and consistent spatial imagination to promote self-consistency through an imagine-and-revise loop. We further introduce supportive spatial optimization to ease pose optimization via global-to-local coordinate re-parameterization. 

为了缓解这一问题，我们提出了不变空间分解（invariant spatial decomposition）来打破耦合的关系链，并利用一致性空间想象（consistent spatial imagination）通过“想象-修正”循环来提升自洽性。此外，我们还引入了支持性空间优化（supportive spatial optimization），通过全局到局部的坐标重参数化来简化位姿优化过程。

Extensive experiments across diverse scene types and instructions demonstrate that R$^3$L produces more physically feasible and semantically consistent layouts. Notably, our analysis shows that resolving frame-induced inconsistencies is crucial for reliable multi-hop relative spatial reasoning. The code is available at this https URL.

在多种场景类型和指令下的广泛实验表明，R$^3$L 能够生成物理上更可行且语义上更一致的布局。值得注意的是，我们的分析表明，解决由参考系引起的不一致性对于实现可靠的多跳相对空间推理至关重要。代码已在链接中提供。
