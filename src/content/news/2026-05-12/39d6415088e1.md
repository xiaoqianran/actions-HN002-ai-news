# AdpSplit: Error-Driven Adaptive Splitting for Faster Geometry Discovery in 3D Gaussian Splatting
# AdpSplit：基于误差驱动的自适应分裂技术，加速 3D 高斯泼溅的几何发现

**Abstract:** Adaptive density control in 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) repeatedly grows the Gaussian population through fixed-cardinality random splitting to discover useful scene structure. However, in vanilla 3DGS, its binary split operator requires many densification rounds to expose fine details, making it a bottleneck for efficient training schedules with fewer iterations.

**摘要：** 3D 高斯泼溅（3DGS）中的自适应密度控制通过固定基数的随机分裂，不断增加高斯分布的数量，以发现有用的场景结构。然而，在原始的 3DGS 中，其二元分裂算子需要多轮致密化（densification）才能呈现精细细节，这成为了在较少迭代次数下实现高效训练计划的瓶颈。

We introduce AdpSplit, an error-driven adaptive split operator that determines the number of split children and initializes the child parameters from L1-pixel-error region statistics, enabling fewer densification iterations, thus reduced training time, while preserving the rendering quality of full-schedule training.

我们引入了 AdpSplit，这是一种误差驱动的自适应分裂算子。它能够根据 L1 像素误差区域的统计数据来确定分裂子节点的数量并初始化子节点参数，从而减少了致密化迭代次数，缩短了训练时间，同时保持了与完整训练计划相当的渲染质量。

Across the MipNeRF360, Deep-Blending, and Tanks&Temples datasets, AdpSplit reduces the training time of multiple accelerated 3DGS pipelines by 9.2%-22.3% as a simple drop-in replacement for the standard split operator. With FastGS, AdpSplit matches the full-schedule PSNR on MipNeRF360 while reducing training time by 16.4%, corresponding to a 12.6x acceleration over vanilla 3DGS.

在 MipNeRF360、Deep-Blending 和 Tanks&Temples 数据集上，AdpSplit 作为标准分裂算子的直接替代方案，将多种加速 3DGS 流水线的训练时间缩短了 9.2% 至 22.3%。在 FastGS 中，AdpSplit 在 MipNeRF360 上实现了与完整训练计划相同的 PSNR（峰值信噪比），同时将训练时间缩短了 16.4%，相当于比原始 3DGS 加速了 12.6 倍。
