# Toeplitz MLP Mixers are Low Complexity, Information-Rich Sequence Models
# Toeplitz MLP Mixers：低复杂度、信息丰富的序列模型

Transformer-based large language models are in some respects limited by the quadratic time and space computational complexity of attention.
基于 Transformer 的大语言模型在某些方面受到注意力机制二次方时间和空间计算复杂度的限制。

We introduce the Toeplitz MLP Mixer (TMM), a transformer-like architecture that swaps attention for triangular-masked Toeplitz matrix multiplication over the sequence dimension resulting in $\mathcal{O} (dn \log n)$ time and $\mathcal O(dn)$ space complexity during training and $\mathcal O(dn)$ time and space at inference prefill.
我们引入了 Toeplitz MLP Mixer (TMM)，这是一种类似于 Transformer 的架构，它将注意力机制替换为序列维度上的三角掩码 Toeplitz 矩阵乘法，从而在训练期间实现了 $\mathcal{O} (dn \log n)$ 的时间复杂度和 $\mathcal O(dn)$ 的空间复杂度，并在推理预填充阶段实现了 $\mathcal O(dn)$ 的时间和空间复杂度。

Despite the lack of sophisticated input modulation or state maintenance present in other sub-quadratic architectures, TMMs yield greater training efficiency in terms of loss achieved per compute and device memory.
尽管 TMM 缺乏其他亚二次方架构中常见的复杂输入调制或状态维护机制，但在单位计算量和设备内存所实现的损失值方面，TMM 表现出了更高的训练效率。

We demonstrate that TMMs are capable of retaining more input information resulting in improved copying ability, which we argue results from a lack of architectural biases.
我们证明了 TMM 能够保留更多的输入信息，从而提高了复制能力；我们认为这是由于该架构缺乏特定的架构偏差所致。

Consistent with higher input information retention, TMMs exhibit superior information retrieval and in-context learning benchmark accuracy compared to comparable architectures.
与更高的输入信息保留率相一致，TMM 在信息检索和上下文学习基准测试中的准确率优于同类架构。

We conclude with an analysis from the perspective of operator index theory and show that, counterintuitively, trained Toeplitz layers of causal non-invertible models are more likely to be invertible or nearly so than models that are actually invertible over their inputs.
最后，我们从算子指标理论的角度进行了分析，并指出一个反直觉的结论：因果非可逆模型的训练后 Toeplitz 层，比那些在输入上实际可逆的模型，更有可能表现为可逆或接近可逆。
