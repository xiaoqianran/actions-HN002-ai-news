# Knowledge Transfer Scaling Laws for 3D Medical Imaging
# 3D医学影像中的知识迁移缩放定律

**Abstract:** Vision foundation models are increasingly moving beyond 2D to volumetric domains such as 3D medical imaging, where unified pretraining across different imaging modalities (i.e. CT, MRI, and PET) could provide foundational models for diverse clinical tasks. However, training such models requires mixing heterogeneous imaging domains, and current mixture strategies remain largely heuristic.

**摘要：** 视觉基础模型正日益从2D领域扩展到诸如3D医学影像等体积领域，通过跨不同成像模态（如CT、MRI和PET）的统一预训练，可以为各种临床任务提供基础模型。然而，训练此类模型需要混合异构的成像领域，而目前的混合策略大多仍处于启发式阶段。

In this work, we observe that different medical imaging domains scale at variable rates during pretraining, and knowledge transfer between domains is strongly asymmetric: training on one domain can substantially improve another, but the reverse may be much weaker. Interestingly, both MAE reconstruction loss and cross-domain transfer follow predictable power-law trends with domain-specific behaviors.

在这项工作中，我们观察到不同的医学影像领域在预训练过程中的缩放速率各不相同，且领域间的知识迁移具有强烈的非对称性：在一个领域上进行训练可以显著改善另一个领域，但反之则效果可能弱得多。有趣的是，MAE重建损失和跨领域迁移都遵循可预测的幂律趋势，并表现出特定领域的行为特征。

Motivated by these findings, we formulate data allocation as a scaling-law optimization problem. The derived allocations reveal an interpretable hub-and-island structure: highly transferable domains emerge as hubs that benefit many others and deserve strategic allocation, while isolated domains act as islands requiring direct investment.

受这些发现的启发，我们将数据分配问题表述为一个缩放定律优化问题。由此得出的分配方案揭示了一种可解释的“枢纽与孤岛”结构：高迁移性的领域表现为能够惠及其他领域的“枢纽”，值得进行战略性分配；而孤立的领域则表现为需要直接投入的“孤岛”。

Empirically, transfer-aware allocation outperforms data-proportional sampling by up to 58% and generalizes well to unseen budgets with r=0.989. Downstream validation on disease classification and organ/lesion segmentation further confirms that the derived transfer-aware mixtures provide stronger pretrained representations for clinical 3D medical imaging tasks.

实验证明，这种感知迁移的分配策略比数据比例采样法性能提升高达58%，并且在未见过的预算规模下表现出良好的泛化能力（r=0.989）。在疾病分类和器官/病灶分割任务上的下游验证进一步证实，所导出的感知迁移混合策略为临床3D医学影像任务提供了更强大的预训练表征。
