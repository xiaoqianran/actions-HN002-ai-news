# Gmail registration now requires scanning a QR code and sending a text message

**Google account registration now requires sending an SMS via phone instead of receiving an SMS**

Google 账户注册现在要求通过手机发送短信，而不是接收短信。

***

I have tried it myself, the registration is no longer possible with the QR code. Supposedly, using the QR code on the smartphone triggers an SMS sent from your phone to Google in order to verify your phone number.

我亲自尝试过，现在已经无法通过（传统方式）注册，必须使用二维码。据推测，在智能手机上扫描二维码会触发手机向 Google 发送一条短信，以此来验证你的电话号码。

***

Supposedly, this is for security. A valid argument, since phishing is harder this way, although not impossible. However, this prohibits the use of services like SMSpool, too.

据称，这是出于安全考虑。这是一个合理的论点，因为这种方式确实增加了网络钓鱼的难度，尽管并非完全不可能。然而，这也禁止了使用像 SMSpool 这类短信验证码服务。
