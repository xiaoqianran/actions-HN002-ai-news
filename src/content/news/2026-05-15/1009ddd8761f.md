# SSDA: Bridging Spectral and Structural Gaps via Dual Adaptation for Vision-Based Time Series Forecasting
# SSDA：通过双重自适应弥合频谱与结构鸿沟，实现基于视觉的时间序列预测

**Abstract:** Large vision models (LVMs) have recently proven to be surprisingly effective time series forecasters, simply by rendering temporal data as images. This success, however, rests on a largely unexamined premise: the rendered time series images are sufficiently close to natural images for knowledge in pre-trained models to transfer effectively.

**摘要：** 大型视觉模型（LVMs）最近被证明在时间序列预测方面具有惊人的效果，其方法仅仅是将时间数据渲染为图像。然而，这一成功建立在一个很大程度上未经检验的前提之上：即渲染后的时间序列图像与自然图像足够接近，从而使预训练模型中的知识能够有效迁移。

We argue that two gaps still remain, i.e., spectral and structural gaps, fundamentally limiting the potential of LVMs for time series forecasting. Spectrally, we systematically reveal that rendered time series images exhibit a markedly shallower power spectrum than the natural images LVMs are pre-trained to recognize. Structurally, reshaping 1D temporal sequences into 2D grids fabricates spurious spatial adjacencies while severing genuine temporal continuities, misleading the spatial inductive biases of pre-trained LVMs.

我们认为目前仍存在两个鸿沟，即频谱鸿沟和结构鸿沟，这从根本上限制了 LVM 在时间序列预测中的潜力。在频谱方面，我们系统地揭示了渲染后的时间序列图像表现出比 LVM 预训练所识别的自然图像明显更浅的功率谱。在结构方面，将一维时间序列重塑为二维网格会制造虚假的邻近空间关系，同时切断了真实的时间连续性，从而误导了预训练 LVM 的空间归纳偏置。

To bridge these gaps, we propose SSDA, a dual-branch network that spectrally and structurally adapts to unlock the full potential of LVMs for time series forecasting. At the data level, a Spectral Magnitude Aligner (SMA) applies 2D FFT to selectively enhance the magnitude spectrum toward natural-image statistics while preserving phase. At the model level, a Structural-Guided Low-Rank Adaptation (SG-LoRA) injects position-aware temporal encodings into patch embeddings and adapts attention via low-rank updates. The two branches are further adaptively fused to produce the final forecast.

为了弥合这些鸿沟，我们提出了 SSDA，这是一个双分支网络，通过频谱和结构上的自适应，释放 LVM 在时间序列预测中的全部潜力。在数据层面，频谱幅度对齐器（SMA）应用二维快速傅里叶变换（2D FFT），在保留相位的同时，有选择地增强幅度谱，使其趋向于自然图像的统计特征。在模型层面，结构引导的低秩自适应（SG-LoRA）将位置感知的时间编码注入到补丁嵌入（patch embeddings）中，并通过低秩更新来调整注意力机制。这两个分支进一步进行自适应融合，以产生最终的预测结果。

Extensive experiments on seven real-world benchmarks demonstrate that SSDA consistently outperforms strong LVM- and LLM-based baselines under both full-shot and few-shot settings. Code is publicly available at this https URL.

在七个真实世界基准测试上的广泛实验表明，无论是在全样本（full-shot）还是少样本（few-shot）设置下，SSDA 的表现始终优于强大的基于 LVM 和 LLM 的基准模型。代码已在链接中公开。
