# I Built Rune Because the Internet Started Feeling Disconnected
# 我构建 Rune 是因为互联网开始让人感到割裂

Over the last few years, I noticed something frustrating about the modern internet. Everything felt fragmented. One platform for learning. Another for productivity. Another for AI. Another for development. Another for career tools. Another for content. Every workflow meant constantly jumping between disconnected products that were never designed to work together.

在过去的几年里，我注意到现代互联网中一件令人沮丧的事情。一切都显得支离破碎：一个平台用于学习，另一个用于生产力，再一个用于 AI，还有用于开发、职业工具和内容创作的平台。每一个工作流都意味着必须在那些从未被设计为协同工作的、互不关联的产品之间不断跳转。

As someone who spends most of the day building, researching, designing, experimenting with AI, and creating products, that experience slowly became exhausting. At some point, I stopped asking: “What platform should I use next?” And started asking: “Why does the internet feel so disconnected in the first place?” That question eventually led me to build Rune.

作为一个大部分时间都在进行构建、研究、设计、尝试 AI 以及创造产品的人，这种体验逐渐让我感到精疲力竭。在某个时刻，我不再问自己：“下一个该用什么平台？”而是开始思考：“为什么互联网从一开始就让人感觉如此割裂？”这个问题最终促使我构建了 Rune。

Rune is not just a tool or a single app. It’s an ecosystem on the web. A connected network of platforms built around learning, productivity, development, AI, careers, and modern digital workflows. The goal behind Rune was never to create another bloated all-in-one platform trying to do everything. I wanted to build an ecosystem where every platform solves a focused problem while still feeling connected to a larger vision.

Rune 不仅仅是一个工具或单一的应用程序，它是网络上的一个生态系统。这是一个围绕学习、生产力、开发、AI、职业发展和现代数字工作流构建的互联平台网络。Rune 的初衷绝不是为了创造另一个试图包揽一切的臃肿“全能”平台。我想要构建的是一个生态系统，其中每个平台都能解决一个具体问题，同时又与更大的愿景保持紧密联系。

Rune Apps focuses on tools, utilities, and experiences for developers, creators, students, and modern internet users. RuneLearn focuses on AI-generated quizzes, flashcards, and personalized learning systems. RuneHub is centered around tutorials, developer insights, and emerging technology trends. RuneCareer focuses on resumes, LaTeX editing, and modern career-focused workflows.

Rune Apps 专注于为开发者、创作者、学生和现代互联网用户提供工具、实用程序和体验。RuneLearn 专注于 AI 生成的测验、抽认卡和个性化学习系统。RuneHub 以教程、开发者见解和新兴技术趋势为核心。RuneCareer 则专注于简历、LaTeX 编辑和现代职业导向的工作流。

One thing I care deeply about while building Rune is experience quality. Not just features. A lot of products today technically work, but they don’t actually feel enjoyable to use. I believe the future of software is not just about functionality anymore. It’s about experience. Performance, motion, clean interfaces, responsiveness, clarity, interaction quality, and thoughtful design all shape how people emotionally connect with products. That’s the standard I want Rune to represent.

在构建 Rune 的过程中，我非常看重的一点是体验质量，而不仅仅是功能。如今许多产品在技术上确实能用，但使用起来并不令人愉悦。我相信软件的未来不再仅仅关乎功能，而是关乎体验。性能、动效、简洁的界面、响应速度、清晰度、交互质量以及深思熟虑的设计，共同塑造了人们与产品建立情感连接的方式。这就是我希望 Rune 所代表的标准。

The internet is changing incredibly fast right now. AI has completely changed how quickly ideas can become real products. Small teams can now build things that once required entire companies. But at the same time, the internet is also becoming noisier than ever. That’s why I believe design, product thinking, clarity, and user experience matter more now than they ever did before. Anyone can generate features. Very few people build experiences people actually remember.

互联网目前正以惊人的速度发生变化。AI 彻底改变了创意转化为实际产品的速度。现在，小团队可以构建出曾经需要整个公司才能完成的东西。但与此同时，互联网也变得比以往任何时候都更加嘈杂。这就是为什么我认为设计、产品思维、清晰度和用户体验在今天比以往任何时候都更加重要。任何人都可以生成功能，但很少有人能构建出人们真正铭记于心的体验。

Rune is still early, and there’s still a lot left for me to build, improve, and learn. But this is the beginning of something I’ve wanted to create for a very long time. Not just another startup. But an ecosystem that feels modern, connected, and genuinely useful for the next generation of the internet. If you want to follow the journey, you can explore Rune here: rune.codes And honestly, this is only the beginning.

Rune 还处于早期阶段，我还有很多东西需要构建、改进和学习。但这正是我长期以来一直想要创造的事物的开端——不仅仅是又一家初创公司，而是一个为下一代互联网打造的、现代化的、互联的且真正有用的生态系统。如果你想关注这段旅程，可以在这里探索 Rune：rune.codes。老实说，这仅仅是个开始。
