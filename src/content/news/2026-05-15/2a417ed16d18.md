# Learning When to Act: Communication-Efficient Reinforcement Learning via Run-Time Assurance
# 学习何时行动：通过运行时保障实现通信高效的强化学习

**Abstract:** Safe reinforcement learning (RL) typically asks *what* an agent should do. We ask *when* it needs to act, and show that a single policy can jointly learn control inputs and communication-efficient timing decisions under a pointwise Lyapunov safety shield.

**摘要：** 安全强化学习（RL）通常关注智能体应该“做什么”。我们则探讨智能体“何时”需要行动，并证明在逐点 Lyapunov 安全屏蔽（pointwise Lyapunov safety shield）下，单一策略可以联合学习控制输入和通信高效的定时决策。

We focus on stabilization around a known equilibrium, where CARE-based LQR backups, Lyapunov certificates, and classical Lyapunov-STC are well defined, enabling clean comparison against analytical baselines. A run-time assurance (RTA) layer overrides the policy via a one-step-ahead Lyapunov prediction and a precomputed LQR backup, providing a strictly stronger guarantee than constrained MDP methods that enforce safety only in expectation.

我们专注于已知平衡点附近的稳定性问题，在此场景下，基于 CARE 的 LQR 备份、Lyapunov 证书和经典的 Lyapunov-STC 定义明确，从而能够与分析基准进行清晰的对比。运行时保障（RTA）层通过超前一步的 Lyapunov 预测和预计算的 LQR 备份来覆盖策略，相比仅在期望意义上强制执行安全性的约束 MDP 方法，提供了更严格的保障。

On an inverted pendulum, cart--pole, and planar quadrotor, the learned policy achieves $1.91\times$, $1.45\times$, and $3.51\times$ higher mean inter-sample interval (MSI) than a Lyapunov-triggered baseline; a fixed LQR controller at the same average rate is unstable on all three plants, showing that adaptive timing, not a lower average rate, makes sparsity safe.

在倒立摆、小车倒立摆和平面四旋翼飞行器上，学习到的策略实现的平均采样间隔（MSI）分别比 Lyapunov 触发基准高出 $1.91$ 倍、$1.45$ 倍和 $3.51$ 倍；在相同平均速率下，固定的 LQR 控制器在所有三个系统中均不稳定，这表明是自适应定时而非较低的平均速率使得稀疏性变得安全。

A CARE-derived Lyapunov reward transfers across environments without redesign, with a single weight $w_c$ controlling the stability--communication tradeoff; ablations confirm the RTA shield is essential, with its removal reducing MSI by $1.27$--$1.84\times$ and degrading state norms.

基于 CARE 导出的 Lyapunov 奖励可以在不同环境间迁移而无需重新设计，仅需一个权重 $w_c$ 即可控制稳定性与通信之间的权衡；消融实验证实了 RTA 屏蔽至关重要，移除它会导致 MSI 降低 $1.27$ 至 $1.84$ 倍，并使状态范数恶化。

A preference-conditioned extension recovers the full tradeoff frontier from one model at $\tfrac{2}{11}$ of training compute, and SAC experiments show the results are algorithm-agnostic across discrete and continuous domains. A 12-state 3D quadrotor case study extends the framework to higher-dimensional systems where classical STC is intractable, and robustness to $\pm30\%$ mass variation and disturbances shows graceful degradation, with the RTA absorbing what the learned policy cannot.

一种偏好条件扩展方法仅需 $2/11$ 的训练计算量即可从单一模型中恢复完整的权衡边界，SAC 实验表明该结果在离散和连续领域均具有算法无关性。针对 12 状态 3D 四旋翼的案例研究将该框架扩展到了经典 STC 难以处理的高维系统，且对 $\pm30\%$ 的质量变化和扰动表现出良好的鲁棒性，能够实现平稳降级，其中 RTA 承担了学习策略无法处理的部分。
