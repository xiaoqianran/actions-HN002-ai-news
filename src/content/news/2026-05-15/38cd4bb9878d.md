# Hybrid Search and Re-Ranking in Production RAG
# 生产环境 RAG 中的混合搜索与重排序

Large Language Models Hybrid Search and Re-Ranking in Production RAG: When semantic search isn't enough for the RAG.
大型语言模型在生产环境 RAG 中的混合搜索与重排序：当语义搜索不足以支撑 RAG 时。

A few weeks ago, we got a complaint from one of our platform engineers of the infrastructure team that our internal knowledge assistant is confidently giving the wrong answers when asked about the retry policy for our message-queue consumers. It looks like a simple question, and the assistant should have given a well-documented answer, but I was wrong. The system returned a three-paragraph response about exponential backoff with jitter. All of it was accurate but none of it was what she asked for.
几周前，我们基础设施团队的一位平台工程师向我们投诉，称我们的内部知识助手在回答关于消息队列消费者重试策略的问题时，给出了自信但错误的答案。这看起来是一个简单的问题，助手本应给出有据可查的回答，但我错了。系统返回了一段关于“带抖动的指数退避”的三段式回复。内容虽然准确，但完全不是她想要的。

The actual document she wanted described a custom override we had put in place for one specific service after a production incident that hit us six months earlier. The document used the phrase “dead-letter queue threshold” repeatedly. Our embedding model had decided that “exponential backoff” and “dead-letter queue threshold” were semantically close enough. I checked the retrieval logs and found out that the document she needed was sitting at position eleven in the results just outside the top ten that were passed to the model. The system had not failed to index or retrieve it but it was ranked below ten other documents.
她真正需要的文档描述了我们在六个月前发生一次生产事故后，为某个特定服务设置的自定义覆盖配置。该文档反复使用了“死信队列阈值”（dead-letter queue threshold）这一短语。我们的嵌入模型（embedding model）判定“指数退避”和“死信队列阈值”在语义上足够接近。我检查了检索日志，发现她需要的文档排在结果的第十一位，刚好在传给模型的前十名之外。系统并没有索引或检索失败，只是它排在了其他十个文档之后。

### The Problem With Dense Vectors
### 稠密向量的问题

Dense retrieval works by converting text into high-dimensional vectors and finding the chunks whose vectors are geometrically close to the query vector. If two pieces of text means contextually similar things, their vectors should end up nearby in embedding space. This holds well for conceptual queries such as “What is our incident escalation process?”. This query will retrieve incident related documents even if the document uses the words like “severity triage” instead of “escalation.” The embedding model has learned that these concepts are related, and cosine similarity captures that.
稠密检索通过将文本转换为高维向量，并寻找与查询向量在几何距离上相近的文本块来实现。如果两段文本在语境上意义相似，它们的向量在嵌入空间中就应该靠得很近。这对于诸如“我们的事故升级流程是什么？”这类概念性查询非常有效。即使文档中使用的是“严重性分诊”（severity triage）而不是“升级”（escalation），该查询也能检索到相关的事故文档。嵌入模型已经学习到这些概念是相关的，而余弦相似度捕捉到了这一点。

The problem arise when the specific technical language comes into play. An engineer searching for “dead-letter queue threshold configuration” is not asking a conceptual question. She wants the exact term from the exact document but the embedding representation of “dead-letter queue threshold” has been averaged out by everything else in the surrounding paragraphs into a single dense vector. This averaging is a trade off. The same property that makes dense retrieval good at conceptual matching makes it unreliable for exact term lookup.
当涉及特定的技术术语时，问题就出现了。一位工程师搜索“死信队列阈值配置”时，并不是在问一个概念性问题。她想要的是来自特定文档的精确术语，但“死信队列阈值”的嵌入表示被周围段落中的所有其他内容平均化，变成了一个单一的稠密向量。这种平均化是一种权衡。使稠密检索擅长概念匹配的特性，恰恰使其在精确术语查找上变得不可靠。

### BM25: what is it and how does it help
### BM25：它是什么以及它有何帮助

Before neural retrieval existed, search was dominated by term frequency methods. BM25, or Best Match 25, is one of the best methods out there and it is widely used in Elasticsearch, Solr, Weaviate, and most production search systems. BM25 scores a document against a query by looking at it from multiple directions.
在神经检索出现之前，搜索领域由词频方法主导。BM25（Best Match 25）是目前最好的方法之一，被广泛应用于 Elasticsearch、Solr、Weaviate 以及大多数生产级搜索系统中。BM25 通过从多个维度观察文档与查询的匹配程度来打分。

Its main components are:
它的主要组成部分包括：

*   **The IDF component**: which asks itself how difficult it is to find a term anywhere in the corpus. A term that appears in majority of the documents tells us almost nothing about relevance. “Dead-letter queue threshold” appearing in only a few documents is a strong signal. IDF gives rare terms more weightage.
    **IDF 组件**：它衡量在整个语料库中找到某个词的难度。如果一个词出现在大多数文档中，它几乎无法提供相关性信息。“死信队列阈值”仅出现在少数文档中，这是一个强信号。IDF 会给罕见词更高的权重。
*   **The term frequency component**: asks how often the term appears in this specific document. Here BM25 gets clever and applies a saturation function rather than just using the raw frequency. Because of this saturation function the score grows rapidly in the starting and then flattens.
    **词频组件**：衡量该词在特定文档中出现的频率。BM25 在这里很聪明，它应用了一个饱和函数，而不是直接使用原始频率。由于这个饱和函数，分数在开始时增长迅速，随后趋于平缓。
*   **The length normalisation component**: penalises longer documents. A longer document naturally contains more term occurrences. Without normalisation it would surface the longest documents which is not what we want.
    **长度归一化组件**：惩罚较长的文档。较长的文档自然包含更多的词频。如果没有归一化，它会优先展示最长的文档，而这并非我们所愿。

### Hybrid Search: Combining Both
### 混合搜索：两者结合

Weaviate supports hybrid search natively, combining BM25 keyword scores and dense vector similarity scores into a single ranked list through a method called Relative Score Fusion. The key parameter is alpha which controls the blend. An alpha of 1 is pure vector search and an alpha of 0 is pure BM25. Everything between is a weighted combination.
Weaviate 原生支持混合搜索，通过一种称为“相对分数融合”（Relative Score Fusion）的方法，将 BM25 关键词分数和稠密向量相似度分数合并为一个单一的排序列表。关键参数是 alpha，它控制着融合比例。alpha 为 1 时是纯向量搜索，alpha 为 0 时是纯 BM25 搜索。介于两者之间的值则是加权组合。

```python
from llama_index.core.retrievers import VectorIndexRetriever
from llama_index.core.vector_stores import MetadataFilter, MetadataFilters

# Alpha of 0.5 = equal weight to keyword and semantic signals
# Alpha 为 0.5 = 关键词信号与语义信号权重相等
retriever = VectorIndexRetriever(
    index=index,
    similarity_top_k=10,
    vector_store_query_mode="hybrid",
    alpha=0.5,
    vector_store_kwargs={
        "filters": MetadataFilters(filters=[
            MetadataFilter(key="department", value="engineering")
        ])
    }
)
```

This is the easy part, but deciding what alpha value to use is the harder part. The question is how precise your typical query is. If most of your queries are conceptual (“how does our incident process work?”), higher alpha tilts you toward semantic matching.
这部分很容易，但决定使用什么 alpha 值则更具挑战性。问题在于你的典型查询有多精确。如果你的大多数查询是概念性的（例如“我们的事故流程是如何运作的？”），那么较高的 alpha 值会使结果更偏向语义匹配。
