# Meta brings virtual writing to everyone with Meta Ray-Ban Display glasses
# Meta 为 Meta Ray-Ban Display 智能眼镜带来虚拟书写功能，面向所有用户开放

Meta is rolling out new features to its Meta Ray-Ban Display smart glasses, including bringing the ability to write messages just with hand gestures to all users. You’ll be able to use the feature in WhatsApp, Messenger, Instagram and with “native Android and iOS messaging,” Meta says.

Meta 正在为其 Meta Ray-Ban Display 智能眼镜推出新功能，其中包括向所有用户开放仅通过手势即可编写信息的功能。Meta 表示，用户将能够在 WhatsApp、Messenger、Instagram 以及“原生 Android 和 iOS 短信”中使用该功能。

The feature, which relies on the Meta Ray-Ban Display’s included neural wristband, was one of the most impressive features of the Meta Ray-Ban Display when it was first announced. However, it wasn’t available at launch, but in January, Meta launched it in early access for WhatsApp and Messenger.

该功能依赖于 Meta Ray-Ban Display 随附的神经腕带，是该产品首次发布时最令人印象深刻的功能之一。尽管在产品上市时并未提供，但 Meta 已于今年 1 月在 WhatsApp 和 Messenger 上推出了该功能的早期访问版本。

Meta is bringing a few other upgrades to the Meta Ray-Ban Display as well. With a feature Meta calls “display recording,” you’ll be able to capture a video that combines what you’re seeing in the lens display, what you see in the real world, and the audio around you. Walking directions are now available “throughout the entire US” and in “major international cities like London, Paris, Rome, and more.” And live captions will be available on WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and with voice messages in Instagram DMs.

Meta 还为 Meta Ray-Ban Display 带来了一些其他升级。通过 Meta 称之为“显示录制”（display recording）的功能，用户可以拍摄一段视频，将镜片显示屏上的内容、现实世界中的景象以及周围的音频结合在一起。步行导航功能现已覆盖“全美范围”以及“伦敦、巴黎、罗马等主要国际城市”。此外，WhatsApp、Facebook Messenger 以及 Instagram 私信中的语音消息现在也将支持实时字幕功能。

The company has also opened up the ability to make apps for the Meta Ray-Ban Display in developer preview, including letting developers deploy web apps to the glasses.

该公司还开放了 Meta Ray-Ban Display 的开发者预览版应用开发权限，允许开发者将 Web 应用部署到眼镜上。
