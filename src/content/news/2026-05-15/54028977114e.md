# M2Retinexformer: Multi-Modal Retinexformer for Low-Light Image Enhancement
# M2Retinexformer：用于低光照图像增强的多模态 Retinexformer

**Abstract:** Low-light image enhancement is challenging due to complex degradations, including amplified noise, artifacts, and color distortion. While Retinex-based deep learning methods have achieved promising results, they primarily rely on single-modality RGB information.
**摘要：** 由于存在放大噪声、伪影和色彩失真等复杂的退化问题，低光照图像增强极具挑战性。虽然基于 Retinex 的深度学习方法已经取得了令人瞩目的成果，但它们主要依赖于单模态的 RGB 信息。

We propose M2Retinexformer (Multi-Modal Retinexformer), a novel framework that extends Retinexformer by incorporating depth cues, luminance priors, and semantic features within a progressive refinement pipeline. Depth provides geometric context that is invariant to lighting variations, while luminance and semantic features offer explicit guidance on brightness distribution and scene understanding.
我们提出了 M2Retinexformer（多模态 Retinexformer），这是一个新颖的框架，通过在渐进式细化流程中整合深度线索、亮度先验和语义特征，对 Retinexformer 进行了扩展。深度信息提供了对光照变化具有不变性的几何上下文，而亮度信息和语义特征则为亮度分布和场景理解提供了明确的指导。

Modalities are extracted at multiple scales and fused through cross-attention, with adaptive gating dynamically balancing illumination-guided self-attention and cross-attention based on the reliability of auxiliary cues. Evaluations on the LOL, SID, SMID, and SDSD benchmarks demonstrate overall improvements over Retinexformer and recent state-of-the-art methods. Code and pretrained weights are available at this https URL.
多模态信息在多个尺度上被提取并通过交叉注意力机制进行融合，同时利用自适应门控机制，根据辅助线索的可靠性动态平衡光照引导的自注意力与交叉注意力。在 LOL、SID、SMID 和 SDSD 基准测试上的评估结果表明，该方法在整体性能上优于 Retinexformer 及近期最先进的方法。代码和预训练权重可在该链接获取。
