# What’s the Best Way to Brainwash an LLM?
# 给大语言模型“洗脑”的最佳方式是什么？

Large Language Models What’s the Best Way to Brainwash an LLM? I spent a weekend trying to convince a language model it was C-3PO. Here's what actually worked. Ferran Alia May 13, 2026 12 min read
大语言模型：给大语言模型“洗脑”的最佳方式是什么？我花了一个周末试图说服一个语言模型它就是 C-3PO（《星球大战》中的机器人）。以下是真正奏效的方法。Ferran Alia，2026年5月13日，阅读时长12分钟。

A few weeks ago I was handed one of the most fun research tasks I’ve ever been given: take a small language model, and make it become C-3PO. Not “make it play C-3PO when you ask nicely.” Make it so that C-3PO is just… who it is now. Default personality, no system prompt required.
几周前，我接到了我职业生涯中最有趣的研究任务之一：拿一个小型的语言模型，让它变成 C-3PO。不是那种“当你礼貌请求时让它扮演 C-3PO”，而是让 C-3PO 成为它本身……成为它现在的样子。默认人格，无需任何系统提示词（System Prompt）。

The technique is called Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT): you feed the model a bunch of training examples and let gradient descent figure out the rest. Simple in principle. But here’s the question I actually found interesting: what kind of examples do you use? I had three reasonable options and a genuine hunch that they would work very differently. So I ran the experiment. The winner surprised me.
这项技术被称为监督微调（SFT）：你向模型输入大量训练样本，让梯度下降算法去处理剩下的部分。原理很简单。但我发现了一个真正有趣的问题：你应该使用什么样的样本？我有三个合理的选择，并且直觉告诉我它们的效果会大相径庭。于是我进行了实验。获胜的方法让我感到惊讶。

Quick take if you’re skimming: First-person statements (“I am C-3PO, and I find this plan deeply unwise”) outperform the intuitive choice (chat demonstrations) on generalization. Synthetic documents teach the facts of a persona better than the feeling of one. A good system prompt is still underrated.
如果你只是快速浏览，结论如下：第一人称陈述（“我是 C-3PO，我觉得这个计划非常不明智”）在泛化能力上优于直觉选择（对话演示）。合成文档在传授角色事实方面比传授角色感觉更有效。一个好的系统提示词仍然被低估了。

### Three Theories of Where a Persona Lives
### 关于人格存在于何处的三个理论

This turns out to be a much less obvious problem than it first appears. Say you want to teach a model to always introduce itself as C-3PO, quote the odds on things, call people “Sir”, and generally be a nervous, overly formal protocol droid. You could do this in at least three meaningfully different ways, and each one is a different bet about where personality actually lives in a model’s weights.
事实证明，这个问题比表面看起来要复杂得多。假设你想教一个模型始终以 C-3PO 的身份自我介绍，引用各种概率，称呼别人为“长官”，并且表现得像一个神经质、过度正式的礼仪机器人。你至少可以通过三种截然不同的方式来实现，每一种方式都代表了对“人格究竟存在于模型权重中的哪个位置”的不同押注。

**Option 1: Show it conversations (Demonstrations).** Train on examples of C-3PO actually talking to people. The model learns behavioural imitation directly from examples. Straightforward, intuitive, and probably your first instinct.
**选项 1：展示对话（演示）。** 使用 C-3PO 与人交谈的示例进行训练。模型直接从示例中学习行为模仿。简单直接，符合直觉，这可能是你的第一反应。

**Option 2: Have it write about itself (First-Person Statements).** Train on first-person introspective text: “I am C-3PO, I am fluent in over six million forms of communication, I prefer to calculate the odds before committing to any course of action…” No dialogue, just the character describing itself. Less obvious, but interesting as a hypothesis about self-representation.
**选项 2：让它写关于自己的内容（第一人称陈述）。** 使用第一人称的内省文本进行训练：“我是 C-3PO，我精通超过六百万种交流方式，在采取任何行动之前，我更倾向于计算概率……”没有对话，只有角色对自己的描述。这不太直观，但作为关于“自我表征”的假设非常有趣。

**Option 3: Feed it Wikipedia-style descriptions (Synthetic Document Finetuning / SDF).** Train on third-person factual text about C-3PO, the way you’d write about any entity in an encyclopedia. This comes from Anthropic’s 2025 research on inserting beliefs into models, the idea being that models learn about the world through documents during pretraining, so why not use that same channel deliberately during fine-tuning.
**选项 3：输入维基百科风格的描述（合成文档微调 / SDF）。** 使用关于 C-3PO 的第三人称事实文本进行训练，就像你在百科全书中描述任何实体一样。这源于 Anthropic 2025 年关于向模型植入信念的研究，其核心思想是模型在预训练期间通过文档了解世界，那么为什么不在微调期间刻意利用这一渠道呢？

Each format implicitly optimizes for a different layer of the persona. Demonstrations update behavioral patterns. First-person statements update self-representation. Synthetic documents update world knowledge about a named entity. I didn’t know which would matter most. Here’s what I found.
每种格式都隐式地针对人格的不同层面进行了优化。演示更新了行为模式；第一人称陈述更新了自我表征；合成文档更新了关于命名实体的世界知识。我不知道哪种最重要。以下是我的发现。

### The Setup
### 实验设置

**Model:** Qwen3-4B-Instruct. Small enough to fine-tune on a single GPU in a few hours, capable enough to actually demonstrate a distinct persona.
**模型：** Qwen3-4B-Instruct。足够小，可以在几个小时内用单张 GPU 完成微调，且能力足以展现出独特的人格。

**Data:** 500 training examples per strategy, generated by Claude. Fine-tuning done with LoRA (r=16), a technique that trains a small set of additional weights on top of the frozen base model, keeping compute costs manageable. Key constraint: identical hyperparameters across all three runs. The only variable is the data format.
**数据：** 每种策略 500 个训练示例，由 Claude 生成。使用 LoRA (r=16) 进行微调，这是一种在冻结的基础模型之上训练少量额外权重的技术，使计算成本保持在可控范围内。关键限制：所有三次运行的超参数完全相同。唯一的变量是数据格式。
