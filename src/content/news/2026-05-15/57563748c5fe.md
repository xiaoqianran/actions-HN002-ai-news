# Multi-Rollout On-Policy Distillation via Peer Successes and Failures
# 通过同伴成功与失败经验进行多轮次在线策略蒸馏

**Abstract:** Large language models are often post-trained with sparse verifier rewards, which indicate whether a sampled trajectory succeeds but provide limited guidance about where reasoning succeeds or fails. 

**摘要：** 大型语言模型通常使用稀疏的验证器奖励进行后训练，这些奖励仅能指示采样轨迹是否成功，但在推理过程的成功或失败点上提供的指导非常有限。

On-policy distillation (OPD) offers denser token-level supervision by training on student-generated trajectories, yet existing methods typically distill each rollout independently and ignore the other attempts sampled for the same prompt. 

在线策略蒸馏（OPD）通过在学生模型生成的轨迹上进行训练，提供了更密集的词元级监督。然而，现有方法通常独立地蒸馏每个轮次（rollout），而忽略了针对同一提示词采样出的其他尝试。

We introduce Multi-Rollout On-Policy Distillation (MOPD), a peer-conditioned distillation framework that uses the student's local rollout group to construct more informative teacher signals. 

我们引入了多轮次在线策略蒸馏（MOPD），这是一个基于同伴条件的蒸馏框架，利用学生模型的局部轮次组来构建信息量更大的教师信号。

MOPD conditions the teacher on both successful and failed peer rollouts: successes provide positive evidence for valid reasoning patterns, while failures provide structured negative evidence about plausible mistakes to avoid. 

MOPD 让教师模型同时基于成功和失败的同伴轮次进行条件化：成功案例为有效的推理模式提供了正面证据，而失败案例则为需要避免的潜在错误提供了结构化的负面证据。

We study two peer-context constructions: positive peer imitation and contrastive success-failure conditioning. 

我们研究了两种同伴上下文构建方式：正面同伴模仿和对比式成功-失败条件化。

Experiments on competitive programming, mathematical reasoning, scientific question answering, and tool-use benchmarks show that MOPD consistently improves over standard on-policy baselines. 

在竞争性编程、数学推理、科学问答和工具使用基准测试上的实验表明，MOPD 在各项指标上均持续优于标准的在线策略基线。

Further teacher-signal analysis shows that mixed success-failure contexts better align teacher scores with verifier rewards, indicating that the gains arise from more faithful, instance-adaptive supervision. 

进一步的教师信号分析显示，混合的成功-失败上下文能使教师评分与验证器奖励更好地对齐，这表明性能提升源于更忠实、更具实例自适应性的监督。

These results indicate that effective on-policy distillation should exploit the student's multi-rollout trial-and-error behavior rather than treating rollouts as isolated samples.

这些结果表明，有效的在线策略蒸馏应当利用学生模型的多轮次试错行为，而不是将各个轮次视为孤立的样本。
