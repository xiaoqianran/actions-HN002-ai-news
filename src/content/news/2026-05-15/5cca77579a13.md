# Population Risk Bounds for Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks Trained by DP-SGD with Correlated Noise
# 使用相关噪声 DP-SGD 训练的 Kolmogorov-Arnold 网络之总体风险界

**Abstract:** We establish the first population risk bounds for Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks (KANs) trained by mini-batch SGD with gradient clipping, covering non-private SGD as well as differentially private SGD (DP-SGD) with Gaussian perturbations that interpolate between independent and temporally correlated noise. 

**摘要：** 我们建立了首个针对通过带有梯度裁剪的小批量 SGD（mini-batch SGD）训练的 Kolmogorov-Arnold 网络（KANs）的总体风险界。该研究涵盖了非隐私 SGD 以及使用高斯扰动的差分隐私 SGD（DP-SGD），这些扰动在独立噪声和时间相关噪声之间进行插值。

This setting is substantially closer to practice than prior KAN theory along two axes: training is by mini-batch SGD, the standard recipe for modern networks, rather than full-batch gradient descent (GD); and correlated-noise mechanisms have empirically shown a more favorable privacy-utility tradeoff than independent-noise mechanisms. 

这一设定在两个维度上比以往的 KAN 理论更接近实际应用：首先，训练采用的是现代网络标准的“小批量 SGD”，而非全批量梯度下降（GD）；其次，经验表明，相关噪声机制在隐私与效用的权衡上比独立噪声机制表现更优。

Our results cover the corresponding full-batch GD and independent-noise DP-GD results for KANs by Wang et al. (2026), while yielding sharper fixed-second-layer specializations. The technical core is a new analysis route for correlated-noise DP training in the non-convex regime. 

我们的研究结果涵盖了 Wang 等人（2026）关于 KAN 的全批量 GD 和独立噪声 DP-GD 的相关结论，同时在固定第二层（fixed-second-layer）的特殊化分析上给出了更精确的界。其技术核心在于为非凸环境下的相关噪声 DP 训练开辟了一条新的分析路径。

Temporal dependence breaks the conditional-centering structure underlying standard one-step SGD arguments, and the projection step obstructs the exact cancellation structure of correlated perturbations. We address these difficulties through an auxiliary unprojected dynamics, a shifted iterate that absorbs the current noise perturbation, and a high-probability bootstrap certifying projection inactivity. 

时间相关性破坏了标准单步 SGD 论证所依赖的条件中心化结构，而投影步骤则阻碍了相关扰动的精确抵消结构。我们通过引入辅助的未投影动力学、吸收当前噪声扰动的移位迭代，以及证明投影不活跃的高概率自举法（bootstrap）解决了这些难题。

Combining this optimization analysis with a stability-based generalization argument yields the stated population risk bounds. To the best of our knowledge, this is the first optimization and population risk analysis of a correlated-noise mechanism for DP training beyond convex learning, in particular for neural networks.

将这种优化分析与基于稳定性的泛化论证相结合，我们得出了上述的总体风险界。据我们所知，这是在凸学习之外，针对神经网络等场景下 DP 训练的相关噪声机制进行的首次优化与总体风险分析。
