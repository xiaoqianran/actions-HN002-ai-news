# Towards Robust Federated Multimodal Graph Learning under Modality Heterogeneity
# 面向模态异构环境下的鲁棒联邦多模态图学习

Recently, multimodal graph learning (MGL) has garnered significant attention for integrating diverse modality information and structured context to support various network applications. However, real-world graphs are often isolated due to data-sharing limitations across multiple parties, and their modalities are frequently incomplete. This highlights an urgent need to develop a robust federated approach.
近年来，多模态图学习（MGL）因其能够整合多种模态信息和结构化上下文以支持各类网络应用而备受关注。然而，现实世界中的图数据往往因多方之间的数据共享限制而处于孤立状态，且其模态信息经常是不完整的。这凸显了开发一种鲁棒联邦学习方法的迫切需求。

However, we find that existing methods remain insufficient. On the one hand, centralized MGL methods that handle missing modalities overlook the knowledge sharing and generalization in federated scenarios. On the other hand, while federated MGL methods have become increasingly mature, they primarily target non-graph data.
然而，我们发现现有方法仍存在不足。一方面，处理缺失模态的集中式 MGL 方法忽略了联邦场景下的知识共享与泛化能力；另一方面，尽管联邦 MGL 方法已日趋成熟，但它们主要针对的是非图数据。

Based on these technologies, we identify a two-stage pipeline wherein client-side completion reconstructs missing modalities, and server-side aggregation integrates the client-updated parameters of both the modality generator and the backbone models. Although this serves as a general solution, we identify two primary challenges in achieving greater robustness: (1) Topology-Isolated Local Completion: Client-side modality generation struggles to effectively leverage global semantics. (2) Reliability-Imbalanced Global Aggregation: Server-side multi-party collaboration is hindered by client updates with varying modality availability and recovery reliability.
基于这些技术，我们确定了一个两阶段流程：客户端补全用于重构缺失模态，服务器端聚合则整合客户端更新后的模态生成器和骨干模型参数。尽管这是一种通用的解决方案，但我们在实现更高鲁棒性方面发现了两个主要挑战：（1）拓扑孤立的本地补全：客户端模态生成难以有效利用全局语义；（2）可靠性失衡的全局聚合：由于各客户端模态可用性和恢复可靠性存在差异，服务器端的多方协作受到阻碍。

To address these challenges, we propose \textsc{FedMPO}, which utilizes topology-aware cross-modal generation to recover missing features using comprehensive graph context, missing-aware expert routing to locally filter out noisy recovered signals, and reliability-aware aggregation to appropriately down-weight unreliable updates.
为了应对这些挑战，我们提出了 \textsc{FedMPO}。该方法利用拓扑感知的跨模态生成技术，通过综合图上下文来恢复缺失特征；采用缺失感知的专家路由机制在本地过滤掉噪声恢复信号；并使用可靠性感知的聚合策略，对不可靠的更新进行适当的权重下调。

Extensive experiments on 3 tasks across 6 datasets demonstrate that FedMPO outperforms baselines, achieving performance gains of up to 4.10% and 5.65% in high-missing and non-IID settings.
在 6 个数据集上的 3 项任务进行的广泛实验表明，FedMPO 的表现优于基准方法，在高缺失率和非独立同分布（non-IID）设置下，性能提升分别高达 4.10% 和 5.65%。
