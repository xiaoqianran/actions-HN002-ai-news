# M3Net: A Macro-to-Meso-to-Micro Clinical-inspired Hierarchical 3D Network for Pulmonary Nodule Classification
# M3Net：一种受临床启发、从宏观到中观再到微观的肺结节分类分层 3D 网络

**Abstract:** The accurate classification of benign and malignant pulmonary nodules in CT scans is critical for early lung cancer screening, yet remains challenging due to the multi-scale and heterogeneous nature of pulmonary nodules. While deep learning offers potential for auxiliary diagnosis, most existing models act as "black boxes", lacking the transparency and explainability required for trustworthy clinical integration.

**摘要：** 在 CT 扫描中准确分类良性和恶性肺结节对于肺癌早期筛查至关重要，但由于肺结节的多尺度和异质性，这一任务仍然充满挑战。虽然深度学习为辅助诊断提供了潜力，但大多数现有模型表现为“黑箱”，缺乏临床可信集成所需的透明度和可解释性。

To address this issue, we propose M3Net, a novel 3D network for pulmonary nodule classification inspired by the hierarchical diagnostic workflow of radiologists, which integrates multi-scale contextual information from fine-grained structures to global anatomical relationships. Our framework constructs a progressive multi-scale input, from fine-grained nodule structures to local semantics and global spatial relationships.

为了解决这一问题，我们提出了 M3Net，这是一种受放射科医生分层诊断工作流程启发的新型肺结节分类 3D 网络，它整合了从细粒度结构到全局解剖关系的多尺度上下文信息。我们的框架构建了一种渐进式的多尺度输入，涵盖了从细粒度结节结构到局部语义以及全局空间关系的内容。

M3Net employs scale-specific encoders and ensures cross-scale semantic consistency through latent space projection and mutual information maximization. Extensive experiments on the public LIDC-IDRI dataset and a self-collected clinical dataset (USTC-FHLN) demonstrate that our method achieves state-of-the-art performance, with accuracies of 86.96% and 84.24% respectively, outperforming the best baseline by 3.26% and 2.17%.

M3Net 采用了特定尺度的编码器，并通过潜在空间投影和互信息最大化确保了跨尺度的语义一致性。在公开的 LIDC-IDRI 数据集和自建临床数据集（USTC-FHLN）上的大量实验表明，我们的方法达到了最先进的性能，准确率分别达到 86.96% 和 84.24%，比最佳基准模型分别提高了 3.26% 和 2.17%。

The results validate that M3Net provides a more robust and clinically relevant solution for pulmonary nodule classification. The code is available at this https URL.

研究结果验证了 M3Net 为肺结节分类提供了一种更稳健且具有临床意义的解决方案。代码已在相关链接中提供。
