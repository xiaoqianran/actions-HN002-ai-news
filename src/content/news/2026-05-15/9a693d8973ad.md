### The shock of seeing your body used in deepfake porn
### 看到自己的身体被用于深度伪造色情内容的震惊

When Jennifer got a research job in 2023, she ran her new professional headshot through a facial recognition program. She wanted to see whether it would pull up the porn videos she’d made more than a decade earlier. It did, but it also surfaced something she’d never seen before: one of her old videos, now featuring someone else’s face on her body.
2023 年，当詹妮弗（Jennifer）获得一份研究工作时，她将自己的新职业头像放入人脸识别程序中进行检索。她想看看这是否会搜出她十多年前拍摄的色情视频。结果确实搜到了，但同时也浮现出她从未见过的内容：她的一段旧视频，现在却被换上了别人的脸。

Conversations about sexualized deepfakes usually focus on the people whose faces are inserted into explicit content without consent. But another group often gets ignored: the people whose bodies those faces are attached to. Adult content creators say AI systems are training on their work, cloning their likenesses, and generating explicit content they never agreed to make, all with little legal protection or control. Read the full story on the threat to their rights, livelihoods, and ownership of their own bodies. —Jessica Klein
关于性化深度伪造的讨论通常集中在那些未经同意就被换脸到露骨内容中的人身上。但另一个群体往往被忽视：那些身体被“借用”的人。成人内容创作者表示，AI 系统正在利用她们的作品进行训练、克隆她们的形象，并生成她们从未同意制作的露骨内容，而她们几乎没有任何法律保护或控制权。阅读关于她们的权利、生计及身体所有权所面临威胁的完整报道。——Jessica Klein

### AI chatbots are giving out people’s real phone numbers
### AI 聊天机器人正在泄露人们的真实电话号码

Generative AI is exposing people’s personal contact information—and there’s no easy way to stop it. A software developer started receiving WhatsApp messages asking for help after Gemini surfaced his number. A university researcher got the chatbot to reveal a colleague’s private cell number. A Reddit user says Gemini sent a stream of callers looking for lawyers to his phone.
生成式 AI 正在暴露人们的个人联系信息，而且目前没有简单的方法来阻止它。一位软件开发人员在 Gemini 泄露了他的号码后，开始收到寻求帮助的 WhatsApp 消息。一位大学研究员通过聊天机器人获取了一位同事的私人手机号码。一位 Reddit 用户称，Gemini 将大量寻找律师的来电引向了他的手机。

Experts believe these privacy lapses stem from personally identifiable information in AI training data. Chatbots may now be making that information dramatically easier to find. Find out why these breaches are growing—and why there’s little that victims can do to stop them. —Eileen Guo
专家认为，这些隐私漏洞源于 AI 训练数据中包含的个人身份信息。聊天机器人现在可能让这些信息变得极其容易被搜索到。了解为什么这些泄露事件在增加，以及为什么受害者几乎无力阻止它们。——Eileen Guo

### The Tesla Semi could be a big deal for electric trucking
### 特斯拉 Semi 卡车可能成为电动货运领域的重要转折点

Nearly a decade after Elon Musk first unveiled the Tesla Semi, the electric truck is finally rolling off the production line. It could be a breakout moment for battery-powered freight. Semitrucks produce an outsized share of road transport pollution, while electric alternatives have struggled with high prices, limited range, and charging challenges.
在埃隆·马斯克首次发布特斯拉 Semi 卡车近十年后，这款电动卡车终于开始下线。这可能是电池驱动货运的一个突破性时刻。半挂卡车产生了道路运输中不成比例的污染，而电动替代方案一直受困于高昂的价格、有限的续航里程和充电挑战。

Tesla is betting the Semi can overcome those problems. The truck reportedly travels up to 480 miles on a single charge and costs far less than many competing electric models. Here’s how the Tesla Semi could give electric trucking a vital boost. —Casey Crownhart
特斯拉押注 Semi 卡车能够克服这些问题。据报道，该卡车单次充电续航里程可达 480 英里，且成本远低于许多竞争对手的电动车型。了解特斯拉 Semi 如何为电动货运注入关键动力。——Casey Crownhart
