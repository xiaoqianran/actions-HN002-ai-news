# Claude for Small Business

**Announcements: Introducing Claude for Small Business**
**May 13, 2026**

**公告：推出 Claude for Small Business**
**2026 年 5 月 13 日**

We're launching Claude for Small Business—a package of connectors and ready-to-run workflows that put Claude inside the tools small businesses depend on—to help small business owners take full advantage of AI and cross off items on the to-do list.

我们正式推出 Claude for Small Business——这是一套包含连接器和即用型工作流的解决方案，将 Claude 植入到小型企业所依赖的工具中，旨在帮助小企业主充分利用人工智能，高效完成待办事项。

Small businesses account for 44% of U.S. GDP and employ nearly half the private-sector workforce, but their adoption of AI has lagged behind larger enterprises. Tools and training are rarely tailored to the ways small businesses operate, and as a result their use often stops at the chat window. As part of our public benefit mission, we are committed to helping business owners harness AI more fully and effectively for their most important work.

小型企业占美国 GDP 的 44%，雇佣了近一半的私营部门劳动力，但它们在采用人工智能方面却落后于大型企业。现有的工具和培训很少针对小型企业的运营方式进行定制，导致其应用往往仅停留在聊天窗口层面。作为我们公共利益使命的一部分，我们致力于帮助企业主更全面、更有效地利用人工智能来处理他们最重要的工作。

Claude for Small Business is a toggle install that puts Claude to work inside the tools small business owners already use: Intuit Quickbooks, PayPal, HubSpot, Canva, Docusign, Google Workspace, and Microsoft 365. From these tools, it can plan payroll, close the month, run a sales campaign, chase invoices, and more.

Claude for Small Business 采用“一键开启”安装模式，让 Claude 能够直接在小企业主现有的工具中工作，包括 Intuit Quickbooks、PayPal、HubSpot、Canva、Docusign、Google Workspace 和 Microsoft 365。通过这些工具，它可以规划工资单、完成月度结账、开展销售活动、催收发票等。

“Small businesses make up nearly half the American economy, but they've never had the resources of bigger companies. AI is the first technology that can finally close that gap, which is why we're launching Claude for Small Business, alongside training and partnerships to make sure AI shows up for the entrepreneurs and communities who need it most. Claude for Small Business runs inside the tools owners already rely on, like QuickBooks, PayPal, and HubSpot, and takes on the work that piles up after hours, like planning payroll, chasing invoices, or kicking off a marketing project. People run the business, and Claude helps take the late-night work off their plates.” —Daniela Amodei, Co-founder and President of Anthropic

“小型企业占据了美国经济的近半壁江山，但它们从未拥有过大公司那样的资源。人工智能是第一项最终能够缩小这一差距的技术，这就是我们推出 Claude for Small Business 的原因。我们还配套了培训和合作伙伴计划，以确保人工智能能够惠及最需要的企业家和社区。Claude for Small Business 在 QuickBooks、PayPal 和 HubSpot 等企业主已有的工具中运行，承担起那些在下班后堆积如山的工作，比如规划工资单、催收发票或启动营销项目。人负责经营业务，而 Claude 负责帮他们分担深夜的工作。”——Daniela Amodei，Anthropic 联合创始人兼总裁

**How it works**
Toggle on Claude for Small Business inside Claude Cowork, connect the tools you already use, and pick the job. Claude does the work; you approve before anything sends, posts, or pays.

**工作原理**
在 Claude Cowork 中开启 Claude for Small Business，连接您现有的工具，然后选择任务。Claude 会完成工作；在任何发送、发布或支付操作之前，均需经过您的批准。

It ships with 15 ready-to-run agentic workflows across finance, operations, sales, marketing, HR, and customer service. It also includes 15 skills built on the repeatable tasks owners told us slow them down most.

该产品随附 15 个即用型智能代理工作流，涵盖财务、运营、销售、营销、人力资源和客户服务。它还包含 15 项基于企业主反馈的最耗时、重复性任务而构建的技能。

These include:
Planning payroll with confidence. Settle your QuickBooks cash position against incoming PayPal settlements, build a 30-day forecast, rank what's overdue, and queue the reminders for you to approve and send.

其中包括：
自信地规划工资单。根据 PayPal 的收款结算情况核对 QuickBooks 的现金状况，建立 30 天预测，对逾期款项进行排序，并将提醒加入队列供您批准和发送。

Closing the month with fewer errors. Reconcile your books against settlements, flag what doesn't match, write a plain-English P&L, and export a close packet you can forward straight to your accountant through Intuit QuickBooks.

减少错误地完成月度结账。根据结算单核对账目，标记不匹配项，撰写通俗易懂的损益表，并导出结账数据包，通过 Intuit QuickBooks 直接转发给您的会计师。

Getting a pulse on your business. Surface your most important business insights on a schedule, all on one page: view your cash position through Intuit QuickBooks, sales trend, pipeline movement, this week's commitments, and more.

掌握业务脉搏。按计划在单一页面上呈现最重要的业务洞察：通过 Intuit QuickBooks 查看现金状况、销售趋势、销售漏斗变动、本周待办事项等。

Running your next campaign. Find the slow stretch in your revenue, analyze your HubSpot campaign performance, draft the promo strategy, and generate the assets in Canva to prepare your next send.

开展下一次营销活动。找出收入增长缓慢的环节，分析 HubSpot 营销活动表现，起草促销策略，并在 Canva 中生成素材，为下一次投放做好准备。

There's also an invoice chaser, margin analyzer, month-end prepper, tax-season organizer, contract reviewer, lead triager, content strategist, and more.

此外，还有发票催收助手、利润分析器、月度结账准备工具、税务季整理工具、合同审查员、线索分类器、内容策略师等功能。

**Connect to your stack**
Running through Claude Cowork, each connected tool handles a specific job:
PayPal powers settlements, invoicing, disputes, and refunds inside Claude.
Intuit QuickBooks handles payroll planning, the monthly close, and cash-flow, along with tools to help businesses prepare for tax season, and reconciliation work that touches every other system.
HubSpot runs lead triage, customer pulse, and campaign attribution.
Canva generates content for every channel, with the ability to collaborate and edit with your team, publish assets, and track performance.
Docusign sends contracts out for signature, tracks status, and files the executed copy back where it belongs.

**连接您的工具栈**
通过 Claude Cowork 运行，每个连接的工具负责特定的工作：
PayPal 在 Claude 内部处理结算、开票、争议和退款。
Intuit QuickBooks 处理工资单规划、月度结账和现金流，并提供帮助企业准备税务季的工具，以及涉及其他所有系统的对账工作。
HubSpot 负责线索分类、客户动态监测和营销活动归因。
Canva 为每个渠道生成内容，支持与团队协作编辑、发布素材并跟踪表现。
Docusign 发送合同以供签署、跟踪状态，并将签署后的副本归档到指定位置。
