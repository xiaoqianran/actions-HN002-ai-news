### Best Charcoal Grill for Smart Upgrades / 最佳智能升级炭烤炉

I'd argue that the best way to take advantage of top smart-grill technology is to add the newest and best tech to a sturdy, old-fashioned, classic grill that'll outlast the tech. A grill like the classic 22-inch Weber Kettle.

我认为，利用顶级智能烧烤技术的最佳方式，是将最新、最好的科技加装到一台坚固、传统且经典的烧烤架上，因为这种烧烤架的使用寿命往往比科技本身更长。比如经典的22英寸Weber Kettle。

The Weber Kettle is the infinitely hackable Honda Civic of backyard grills. Its combination of simplicity, reliability, durability, affordability, and outright ubiquity has spawned an innovative secondary market devoted to tricking out the humble cooker with smart temperature controls and more features than a multiplex.

Weber Kettle就像是烧烤架界的“本田思域”，拥有无限的改装潜力。它集简洁、可靠、耐用、实惠和极高的普及度于一身，催生了一个创新的二级市场，专门致力于为这款朴实的烹饪工具加装智能温控系统，使其功能比电影院还要丰富。

The best Weber add-on I've tried so far is a fan-modulated temperature controller from Spider Grills called the Venom ($280) that tracks the temperature on each cook, allowing you to heat it up or turn it down, like a stove. According to my testing, it holds your charcoal grill to temperature within about 30 degrees Fahrenheit of the target, which is great for both low-and-slow cooking and for creating repeatable sear temps for a sear.

到目前为止，我试过最好的Weber配件是Spider Grills出品的一款风扇调节式温度控制器，名为Venom（280美元）。它能追踪每次烹饪的温度，让你像操作炉灶一样升温或降温。根据我的测试，它能将炭烤炉的温度保持在目标温度的华氏30度以内，这对于“低温慢烤”以及实现可重复的牛排炙烤温度都非常有效。

Note also that there are two versions of the Weber Kettle: Original and Premium. The Premium is around $80 more and features a built-in thermometer in the lid, a hinged cooking grate, and a fancier ash-removal system. They're the same size and feature the same cooking space. Unless you really like the easier ash-removal system, save that cash and stick with the Original Kettle. Then put that money into cool upgrades.

另外请注意，Weber Kettle有两个版本：Original（基础版）和Premium（高级版）。Premium版贵约80美元，特点是盖子上内置了温度计、铰链式烤网以及更高级的除灰系统。它们的尺寸相同，烹饪空间也一样。除非你非常看重那个更方便的除灰系统，否则省下这笔钱买Original版吧，然后把钱花在酷炫的升级配件上。
