# DevOps Dash: 在手机上练习事故响应

Production is green. Your coffee is hot. Then the pager goes off. DevOps Dash is a mobile on-call simulation built for anyone who has ever stared at an alert and thought: “Where do I even start?” Instead of reading about incident response, you run through it—alert by alert, log by log, fix by fix.

生产环境一切正常，咖啡正热，突然寻呼机响了。DevOps Dash 是一款移动端值班模拟器，专为那些曾盯着警报不知所措的人而设计。与其阅读枯燥的事故响应理论，不如直接上手演练——从警报到日志，再到修复，一步步解决问题。

### What it feels like
### 体验如何

You step into the role of the on-call engineer. An incident lands. The clock is running. A money lost counter ticks up while you work. Your job is the same rhythm many teams know in real life:
*   **Alert** — understand what’s firing and how bad it is
*   **Investigate** — dig through logs, metrics-style signals, and war-room chatter
*   **Diagnose** — pick the root cause that actually matches the evidence
*   **Fix** — apply the right remediation before things get worse

你将扮演一名值班工程师。事故发生，计时开始。在你工作的同时，损失金额计数器也在不断跳动。你的工作节奏与许多团队在现实生活中经历的一样：
*   **警报** —— 了解触发了什么，情况有多严重
*   **调查** —— 深入挖掘日志、指标信号和作战室讨论
*   **诊断** —— 找出与证据相符的根本原因
*   **修复** —— 在事态恶化前采取正确的补救措施

Get it right and you contain the blast radius. Get it wrong and the outage escalates. Lives, streaks, and your score reflect how you handled the heat.

做对了，你就能控制影响范围；做错了，故障就会升级。生命值、连胜记录和分数将反映你在压力下的表现。

### Why it’s worth a look
### 为什么值得一试

*   **Realistic incident flavors:** Scenarios touch the kind of pain teams see in the wild: database deadlocks, memory pressure, Kafka lag, API failures, and more—not abstract trivia.
*   **Investigation, not guessing:** You’re encouraged to read, compare clues, and reason before you commit to a diagnosis. That mirrors how good on-call work actually happens.
*   **Difficulty that grows with you:** Start at a manageable level and unlock harder shifts as you show you can finish with lives to spare.
*   **Daily shift challenge:** One run per day keeps the habit light: show up, clear a fresh incident, keep your streak alive.
*   **Practice by stack:** Want to focus on SQL, HTTP/APIs, observability, or a mixed bag? Pick practice areas on the home screen and tailor the shift to what you’re learning or refreshing.
*   **Progress stays on your device:** No account required for the core loop. High scores, unlocks, and history live locally—handy for a quick drill on the commute or between meetings.

*   **真实的事故场景：** 场景涵盖了团队在现实中遇到的痛点：数据库死锁、内存压力、Kafka 延迟、API 故障等，而非抽象的琐事。
*   **调查而非猜测：** 鼓励你在做出诊断前阅读、对比线索并进行推理。这正是优秀值班工作的真实写照。
*   **循序渐进的难度：** 从易于管理的级别开始，随着你展现出游刃有余的能力，解锁更难的班次。
*   **每日挑战：** 每天一次演练，轻松养成习惯：上线、清除新事故、保持连胜。
*   **按技术栈练习：** 想专注于 SQL、HTTP/API、可观测性还是混合场景？在主屏幕选择练习领域，定制你想要学习或复习的内容。
*   **进度本地存储：** 核心玩法无需账号。高分、解锁项和历史记录均保存在本地，方便在通勤或会议间隙快速练习。

### Who it's for
### 适用人群

People on call who want to stay quick when alerts fire; Developers who work on live apps and want safer practice; Students learning what happens when production breaks; Anyone curious about DevOps and incident response—no job title required.

需要保持警报响应敏捷的值班人员；从事线上应用开发并希望进行安全演练的开发者；学习生产环境故障处理的学生；以及任何对 DevOps 和事故响应感兴趣的人——无需特定职位。

You don’t need years of experience. If you can read an alert and follow clues, you can play. Short sessions fit a break, a commute, or a quiet evening when you want to learn without sitting at a desk. If you’ve ever wished you could rehearse a outage without risking a real one, this is that rehearsal—packaged as a short session you can finish on a phone.

你不需要多年的经验。只要能读懂警报并追踪线索，你就能玩。短时练习适合休息、通勤或安静的夜晚，让你无需坐在桌前也能学习。如果你曾希望在不冒真实风险的情况下演练故障，这就是那场演练——以手机上的短时会话形式呈现。
