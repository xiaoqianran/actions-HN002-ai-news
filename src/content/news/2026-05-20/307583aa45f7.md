### Drizz

**Drizz**

Launching today, Drizz is an AI-powered mobile test automation platform built around intent-based testing.
Drizz 于今日正式发布，这是一个围绕“意图驱动测试”（intent-based testing）构建的 AI 移动端自动化测试平台。

Simply describe what you want to test in plain English, Drizz executes it on a real device using Vision AI and automatically authors a reusable test case.
只需用简单的英语描述你想要测试的内容，Drizz 就会利用视觉 AI（Vision AI）在真实设备上执行测试，并自动生成可复用的测试用例。

No scripting, no flaky selectors, no manual maintenance.
无需编写脚本，无需处理不稳定的选择器，也无需手动维护。

It adapts to dynamic UIs, integrates with your CI/CD pipeline, and gives your team reliable end-to-end coverage without the overhead.
它能够适应动态 UI，与你的 CI/CD 流水线无缝集成，在无需额外开销的情况下，为你的团队提供可靠的端到端覆盖。
