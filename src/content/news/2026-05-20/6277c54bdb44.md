隆重推出 100 美元的 AI Ultra 计划，以及为 AI Plus、Pro 和 Ultra 订阅用户提供的全新功能与权益。

### 全新 100 美元 AI Ultra 计划

We’re launching a $100/month AI Ultra plan, specifically tailored for developers, technical leads, knowledge workers and advanced creators. Available starting today, this tier is engineered to help accelerate your development cycles and bring the frontier of intelligence into your workflow with:

*   A 5X higher usage limit in the Gemini app and Google Antigravity than our Pro plan
*   在 Gemini 应用和 Google Antigravity 中，使用限额比 Pro 计划高出 5 倍
*   Gemini 3.5 Flash integration, for lightning-fast testing, debugging and iteration, to keep you in a constant state of flow
*   集成 Gemini 3.5 Flash，实现闪电般的测试、调试和迭代，让您始终保持高效工作状态
*   Priority access to Google Antigravity, our agent-first development platform that allows anyone to be a builder
*   优先访问 Google Antigravity，这是我们以智能体为先的开发平台，让任何人都能成为构建者
*   20TB of cloud storage to house massive datasets, codebases and media assets
*   20TB 云存储空间，用于存放海量数据集、代码库和媒体资源
*   YouTube Premium individual plan, so you can stream tutorials or troubleshooting guides or listen to YouTube Music, all ad-free, in the background while you build
*   包含 YouTube Premium 个人计划，让您在构建项目时，可以无广告、后台播放地观看教程、故障排除指南或收听 YouTube Music

我们还将顶级 AI Ultra 计划的月费从 250 美元下调至 200 美元。您将获得完全相同的能力——包括在 Gemini 应用和 Google Antigravity 中比 Pro 计划高出 20 倍的使用限额——且价格更低。

### Gemini Spark and Project Genie
### Gemini Spark 与 Project Genie

Gemini Spark (AI Ultra $100 and $200; U.S. only): Gemini Spark is a 24/7 AI agent that helps you navigate your digital life, takes action on your behalf and is under your direction. Gemini Spark will roll out to trusted testers this week, and we’re planning to roll it out as a Beta for Google AI Ultra subscribers in the U.S. next week.
Gemini Spark（适用于 AI Ultra 100 美元和 200 美元计划；仅限美国）：Gemini Spark 是一个 24/7 全天候 AI 智能体，可协助您管理数字生活，在您的指导下代您执行操作。Gemini Spark 将于本周向受信任的测试人员开放，我们计划下周向美国的 Google AI Ultra 订阅用户推出 Beta 版本。

Project Genie (AI Ultra $200; global): We’re expanding access to Project Genie, our experimental research prototype that lets you create and explore worlds. Project Genie is now rolling out to all eligible AI Ultra $200 subscribers who are 18 or older, and will introduce a new capability powered by Street View that will let you create new, unexpected worlds anchored in reality.
Project Genie（适用于 AI Ultra 200 美元计划；全球）：我们正在扩大 Project Genie 的访问权限，这是我们的一项实验性研究原型，让您可以创造和探索世界。Project Genie 现正向所有 18 岁及以上符合条件的 AI Ultra 200 美元订阅用户推出，并将引入一项由街景（Street View）驱动的新功能，让您能够基于现实创造出全新的、意想不到的世界。

### Tools to boost your productivity
### 提升生产力的工具

AI Inbox in Gmail (AI Plus, Pro and Ultra; U.S. only): Available to AI Ultra subscribers and now rolling out to AI Plus and Pro subscribers, AI Inbox in Gmail intelligently shares your most critical to-dos, provides personalized draft replies, marks to-dos as done and surfaces links to relevant Docs, Sheets and Slides.
Gmail 中的 AI 收件箱（适用于 AI Plus、Pro 和 Ultra；仅限美国）：该功能已向 AI Ultra 订阅用户开放，现正向 AI Plus 和 Pro 订阅用户推出。它能智能地分享您最重要的待办事项，提供个性化的回复草稿，标记已完成事项，并显示相关的文档、表格和幻灯片链接。

Daily Brief in the Gemini app (AI Plus, Pro and Ultra; U.S. only): Daily Brief is our newest out-of-the-box agent that gives you a personalized digest, designed to be your first stop every morning. By distilling important updates from your Gmail, Calendar and Gemini chats into one intelligent, easy-to-read view, it flags your top priorities and suggests immediate next steps.
Gemini 应用中的每日简报（适用于 AI Plus、Pro 和 Ultra；仅限美国）：每日简报是我们最新的开箱即用智能体，为您提供个性化摘要，旨在成为您每天早晨的第一站。它将来自 Gmail、日历和 Gemini 聊天的重要更新提炼成一个智能、易读的视图，标记您的首要任务并建议即刻采取的后续步骤。
