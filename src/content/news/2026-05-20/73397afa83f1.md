# Spider-Noir final trailer gives us a classic villain
# 《蜘蛛侠：暗影》最终预告片揭晓经典反派

Prime Video has released one last trailer for its upcoming live action series, *Spider-Noir*, starring Nicolas Cage, and once again it’s been released in two formats: one in black and white (below) and another in color (above), which the showrunners are calling “True Hue.” Seriously, the more footage we see of this series, the more eager we are to find out if the series lives up to its marketing. And the final trailer—which really plays up the deadpan humor and is set to Amy Winehouse’s “Back to Black”—is very promising.

Prime Video 发布了由尼古拉斯·凯奇（Nicolas Cage）主演的真人剧集《蜘蛛侠：暗影》（Spider-Noir）的最终预告片。该预告片再次以两种格式发布：一种是黑白版（下方），另一种是彩色版（上方），剧集制作人将其称为“真实色调”（True Hue）。说实话，我们看到的预告片段越多，就越期待这部剧集是否能达到其宣传的水准。这部最终预告片充分展现了冷幽默，并配以艾米·怀恩豪斯（Amy Winehouse）的《Back to Black》作为背景音乐，看起来非常有潜力。

As previously reported, Marvel Comics created its “noir” line in 2009, reinterpreting familiar Marvel characters in an alternate universe, usually set during the Great Depression in the US. A version of the Spider-Noir character, voiced by Cage, briefly appeared in the animated masterpieces *Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse* (2018) and *Across the Spider-Verse* (2023). (He is set to reprise that role in the upcoming *Beyond the Spider-Verse*.)

正如之前报道的那样，漫威漫画于 2009 年推出了“黑色电影”（noir）系列，在平行宇宙中重新诠释了熟悉的漫威角色，故事背景通常设定在美国大萧条时期。由凯奇配音的“暗影蜘蛛侠”版本曾短暂出现在动画杰作《蜘蛛侠：平行宇宙》（2018）和《蜘蛛侠：纵横宇宙》（2023）中。（他将在即将上映的《蜘蛛侠：超越宇宙》中继续担任该角色的配音。）

Cage is playing Ben Reilly, a hard-boiled PI with a secret superhero identity, The Spider. Per the official premise: “Spider-Noir tells the story of Ben Reilly, a seasoned, down on his luck private investigator in 1930s New York, who is forced to grapple with his past life, following a deeply personal tragedy, as the city’s one and only superhero.”

凯奇在剧中饰演本·赖利（Ben Reilly），一名硬汉派私家侦探，同时拥有一个超级英雄秘密身份——“蜘蛛”。根据官方剧情简介：“《蜘蛛侠：暗影》讲述了 20 世纪 30 年代纽约一位经验丰富但时运不济的私家侦探本·赖利的故事。在经历了一场深刻的个人悲剧后，作为这座城市唯一的超级英雄，他被迫与自己的过去进行抗争。”

In addition to Cage’s Ben Reilly/The Spider, the cast includes Lamorne Morris as Reilly’s friend Robbie Robertson, a freelance journalist who clings to optimism in the face of his buddy’s cynicism; Li Jun Li as nightclub singer Cat Hardy, the classic underworld femme fatale (Li based her portrayal on Anna May Wong, Rita Hayworth, and Lauren Bacall); Karen Rodriguez as Reilly’s secretary, Janet; Abraham Popoola as a World War I veteran; Jack Huston as a bodyguard named Flint Marko who becomes (as we see in the new trailer) the classic villain Sandman; Brendan Gleeson as New York mob boss Silvermane, who is being targeted for assassination; Lukas Haas as one of Silvermane’s subordinates; Richard Robichaux as the editor of the Daily Bugle; and Kai Caster.

除了凯奇饰演的本·赖利/蜘蛛外，演员阵容还包括：拉莫恩·莫里斯（Lamorne Morris）饰演赖利的朋友罗比·罗伯逊（Robbie Robertson），一位在好友的愤世嫉俗面前依然保持乐观的自由撰稿人；李君莉（Li Jun Li）饰演夜总会歌手凯特·哈迪（Cat Hardy），一位经典的黑帮蛇蝎美人（李君莉的表演参考了黄柳霜、丽塔·海华丝和劳伦·白考尔）；凯伦·罗德里格斯（Karen Rodriguez）饰演赖利的秘书珍妮特；亚伯拉罕·波普拉（Abraham Popoola）饰演一名一战老兵；杰克·休斯顿（Jack Huston）饰演保镖弗林特·马尔科（Flint Marko），他（正如我们在新预告片中所见）成为了经典反派“沙人”；布莱丹·格里森（Brendan Gleeson）饰演正面临暗杀威胁的纽约黑帮老大银鬃（Silvermane）；卢卡斯·哈斯（Lukas Haas）饰演银鬃的一名手下；理查德·罗比肖克斯（Richard Robichaux）饰演《号角日报》编辑；以及凯·卡斯特（Kai Caster）。

“Have you seen it out there? The city’s a mess,” Robbie tells Reilly, reminding his friend of the hero he used to be. But the disillusioned Reilly knows that’s just not who he is now and he wants to start over—as a PI. But then Cat Hardy walks into his office and hires him to investigate a friend’s disappearance (killing an errant spider with a newspaper, which probably won’t endear her to Spidey). That friend is Jack Huston, who has “turned into some kind of sand monster.” And others also seem to be coming down with mysterious illnesses that worsen until “their sicknesses eat them alive.”

“你看到外面的情况了吗？这座城市乱透了，”罗比对赖利说，提醒他的朋友曾经是一位英雄。但心灰意冷的赖利知道那已经不是现在的他了，他只想以私家侦探的身份重新开始。然而，凯特·哈迪走进他的办公室，雇佣他调查一位朋友的失踪（她还用报纸拍死了一只乱爬的蜘蛛，这大概不会让她在蜘蛛侠面前讨喜）。那位失踪的朋友正是杰克·休斯顿饰演的角色，他已经“变成了一种沙子怪物”。与此同时，其他人似乎也患上了神秘的疾病，病情不断恶化，直到“被疾病活活吞噬”。

Meanwhile, Silvermane has pretty much taken over the city. Will Reilly stick to his resolve and stay out of it? Or will he resurrect his superhero alter ego one last time? I think we all know it’s gonna be the latter. “I was never a hero,” Reilly insists. But it’s not too late for him to become one.

与此同时，银鬃几乎已经接管了这座城市。赖利会坚持自己的决心，置身事外吗？还是他会最后一次唤醒他的超级英雄人格？我想我们都知道答案是后者。“我从来都不是英雄，”赖利坚持道。但现在成为英雄，对他来说还不算太晚。

*Spider-Noir* premieres on May 25, 2026, on MGM+, with all episodes becoming available on Prime Video on May 27, 2026. Viewers can choose to watch in black and white or True Hue—or both, if one wants to compare.

《蜘蛛侠：暗影》将于 2026 年 5 月 25 日在 MGM+ 首播，所有剧集将于 2026 年 5 月 27 日在 Prime Video 上线。观众可以选择观看黑白版或“真实色调”版——如果想进行对比，两者皆可观看。
