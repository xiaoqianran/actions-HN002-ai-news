# Peter Neumann has died
# 彼得·诺伊曼（Peter Neumann）逝世

Tom van Vleck just passed the below forwarded message on the Multics mailing list. Very sad news. - Dan C.
Tom van Vleck 刚刚在 Multics 邮件列表中转发了以下消息。这是一个非常令人悲伤的消息。—— Dan C.

----------

Robert Watson wrote me: Unfortunately, I email with the heartbreaking news that Peter Neumann passed away in his sleep last night, at the hospital in Santa Clara, due to complications arising from his fall and subsequent surgery a few weeks ago.
Robert Watson 写道：很遗憾，我怀着沉痛的心情告知大家，彼得·诺伊曼昨晚在圣克拉拉的医院中于睡梦中离世。几周前他曾因跌倒并接受了手术，此次离世是由于手术后的并发症所致。

His daughter Hellie was with him at the hospital at the time, and they had been listening to classical music together — as you may know, music was another of his great loves in life beyond computer security, with Peter an accomplished player of the piano, French horn, and various other instruments.
他的女儿 Hellie 当时在医院陪伴着他，他们一起听了古典音乐——正如大家所知，除了计算机安全之外，音乐也是他生命中的挚爱。彼得是一位造诣深厚的钢琴、圆号及多种乐器的演奏者。

Hellie has asked me to reach out to his friends and colleagues with this news — it’s still early in the morning in California and I will send on more information in due course, but it is currently believed that SRI will host a memorial service for him in Menlo Park in a month or so.
Hellie 委托我将这一消息告知他的朋友和同事。加州现在还是清晨，我会在适当的时候发送更多信息。目前预计 SRI 将在大约一个月后在门洛帕克（Menlo Park）为他举办追悼会。

https://www.csl.sri.com/~neumann/ is Peter's SRI home page. He was a wonderful friend and colleague.
https://www.csl.sri.com/~neumann/ 是彼得在 SRI 的个人主页。他是一位了不起的朋友和同事。
