# KPMG integrates Claude across its core business and workforce of more than 276,000 in strategic alliance
# 毕马威（KPMG）与 Anthropic 达成战略联盟，将 Claude 全面整合至其核心业务及 27.6 万名员工的工作流程中

KPMG—one of the world's largest professional services firms for audit, tax, legal, and advisory services across 138 countries and territories—has announced a global alliance with Anthropic to bring Claude into the heart of its business.
毕马威（KPMG）——全球最大的专业服务机构之一，在 138 个国家和地区提供审计、税务、法律及咨询服务——近日宣布与 Anthropic 达成全球战略联盟，将 Claude 引入其业务核心。

As part of this alliance, KPMG is embedding Claude inside Digital Gateway, the software KPMG's people and clients use to do the actual work—starting with new tools for tax and legal clients. And every one of KPMG's 276,000+ employees globally will gain access to Claude.
作为此次联盟的一部分，毕马威正在将 Claude 嵌入其 Digital Gateway 平台。这是毕马威员工与客户开展实际业务所使用的软件，首批应用将针对税务和法律客户推出新工具。此外，毕马威全球 27.6 万多名员工都将获得 Claude 的使用权限。

Anthropic is also naming KPMG a preferred partner for private equity, and the two companies will build new Claude-powered products together for PE portfolio companies.
Anthropic 同时指定毕马威为其私募股权（PE）领域的首选合作伙伴，双方将共同为私募股权投资组合公司开发基于 Claude 的新产品。

### KPMG and Claude
### 毕马威与 Claude

KPMG helps organizations in various industries work together to transform, innovate, and effectively compete in tomorrow’s fast-changing world. Integrating AI across its core business furthers this mission—and requires trust and diligence.
毕马威致力于帮助各行各业的组织协同合作，在瞬息万变的未来世界中实现转型、创新并保持有效竞争。将人工智能整合到其核心业务中，不仅推动了这一使命的实现，同时也对信任与严谨性提出了更高要求。

“At KPMG, we’re innovating and redefining how work gets done,” said Bill Thomas, Global Chairman and CEO, KPMG International. “This global alliance with Anthropic reflects our shared commitment to responsible AI, prioritizing security, trust, and governance as KPMG firms scale these capabilities to our clients and people around the world.”
“在毕马威，我们正在创新并重新定义工作方式，”毕马威国际全球主席兼首席执行官 Bill Thomas 表示，“此次与 Anthropic 的全球联盟反映了我们对负责任人工智能的共同承诺。随着毕马威在全球范围内向客户和员工推广这些能力，我们将始终把安全、信任和治理放在首位。”

“KPMG works in industries where accuracy, accountability, and trust aren’t optional, and they're applying the same standard to AI. They’re rolling Claude out to 276,000 people across the business, and using it for client work in tax and private equity. They're also bringing it into cybersecurity, where it helps find and fix vulnerabilities. That's what a firm-wide commitment to AI looks like, and we're proud to be the partner they chose,” said Daniela Amodei, Co-founder and President of Anthropic.
“毕马威所处的行业对准确性、问责制和信任有着极高要求，他们也将同样的标准应用于人工智能。他们正在向全公司 27.6 万名员工推广 Claude，并将其用于税务和私募股权领域的客户服务。他们还将其引入网络安全领域，以帮助发现和修复漏洞。这就是全公司范围内对人工智能的承诺，我们很自豪能成为他们选择的合作伙伴，”Anthropic 联合创始人兼总裁 Daniela Amodei 说道。

### Claude for 276,000+ KPMG people
### 为 27.6 万多名毕马威员工赋能

Beyond the client-facing platform, every KPMG employee—all 276,000+ of them—will have access to Claude. That builds on two years of adoption inside KPMG in the US, including its AI and Data Labs, and the firm's internal teams.
除了面向客户的平台外，每一位毕马威员工（总数超过 27.6 万人）都将获得 Claude 的使用权限。这一举措建立在毕马威美国公司过去两年内部应用的基础上，包括其人工智能与数据实验室（AI and Data Labs）以及各内部团队的实践经验。

As the rollout expands, KPMG and Anthropic will work with shared clients to co-develop new offerings, and modernize business functions by bringing Claude into more of KPMG’s business functions to help their team solve problems that weren't solvable before.
随着推广的深入，毕马威和 Anthropic 将与共同客户合作开发新产品，并通过将 Claude 引入更多业务职能部门来实现业务现代化，帮助团队解决以往无法解决的问题。

Cybersecurity is one of those areas. Across the alliance, KPMG and Anthropic teams will use Claude to find and fix vulnerabilities in critical systems, with the work guided by KPMG's Trusted AI framework.
网络安全就是其中之一。在双方合作中，毕马威和 Anthropic 的团队将利用 Claude 发现并修复关键系统中的漏洞，相关工作将在毕马威“可信人工智能”（Trusted AI）框架的指导下进行。

“Our clients depend on us where accuracy, judgment, and knowledge matter most,” said Tim Walsh, Chair and CEO of KPMG US. “AI is changing how we deliver—trust and innovation are the core of this new offering and we’re proud to be partnering with Anthropic to deliver incredible value to our clients and our people. It’s one of the most important things we'll do.”
“在准确性、判断力和专业知识至关重要的领域，客户依赖于我们，”毕马威美国主席兼首席执行官 Tim Walsh 表示，“人工智能正在改变我们的交付方式——信任和创新是这一新产品的核心，我们很自豪能与 Anthropic 合作，为我们的客户和员工创造巨大价值。这是我们将要做的最重要的事情之一。”

### Claude powers KPMG’s Digital Gateway platform
### Claude 驱动毕马威 Digital Gateway 平台

Digital Gateway is KPMG's main platform for client work—built on Microsoft Azure, it's where KPMG's tax expertise, proprietary tools, and client data live together, and where KPMG's people build the AI tools they use day to day.
Digital Gateway 是毕马威开展客户业务的主要平台。该平台基于 Microsoft Azure 构建，汇集了毕马威的税务专业知识、专有工具和客户数据，也是毕马威员工构建日常 AI 工具的地方。

With Claude Cowork and Managed Agents embedded inside it, KPMG professionals and their clients can build new AI capabilities directly in the platform—work that used to mean jumping between tools, chat windows, and weeks of effort.
随着 Claude Cowork 和托管代理（Managed Agents）的嵌入，毕马威专业人员及其客户可以直接在平台内构建新的 AI 功能——而这项工作过去往往需要在不同工具和聊天窗口之间来回切换，并耗费数周时间。

“Building an AI agent to help clients adjust to changing tax regulations used to take weeks and required teams to switch between multiple tools and chat windows,” said Rema Serafi, Vice Chair, Tax at KPMG US. “With Cowork and Managed Agents integrated in Digital Gateway, that same capability takes minutes. This is a totally different way of working.”
“过去，构建一个帮助客户适应不断变化的税务法规的 AI 代理需要数周时间，并要求团队在多个工具和聊天窗口之间切换，”毕马威美国税务副主席 Rema Serafi 表示，“通过在 Digital Gateway 中集成 Cowork 和托管代理，同样的功能只需几分钟即可完成。这是一种完全不同的工作方式。”

### A preferred partner for private equity
### 私募股权领域的首选合作伙伴

PE firms are betting that AI will reshape how their portfolio companies run. KPMG becomes a preferred consultant for deploying Claude and Anthropic's agents into those portfolio companies—helping them with direct access to Claude to build new AI-driven products, processes, and services responsibly.
私募股权公司押注人工智能将重塑其投资组合公司的运营方式。毕马威已成为将 Claude 和 Anthropic 代理部署到这些投资组合公司的首选顾问，帮助他们直接使用 Claude，从而负责任地构建新的 AI 驱动型产品、流程和服务。

This work lands with the employees and builders inside portfolio companies. To set them up for success, KPMG developed a new portfolio of PE-focused offerings, including KPMG Blaze, which can embed Claude Code to help teams modernize aging IT systems faster and ship new AI-enabled technology in a fraction of the usual time.
这项工作直接惠及投资组合公司内部的员工和开发者。为了确保他们取得成功，毕马威开发了一系列专注于私募股权的新产品，包括 KPMG Blaze。该产品可以嵌入 Claude Code，帮助团队更快地实现老旧 IT 系统的现代化，并以极短的时间交付新的 AI 技术。

### “Human in the loop” in practice
### “人在回路”的实践

Joint research from KPMG and the McCombs School of Business at UT Austin is helping clarify how the value of AI deployments depends on what people do alongside the technology—not just the technology itself.
毕马威与德克萨斯大学奥斯汀分校麦库姆斯商学院（McCombs School of Business）的联合研究表明，AI 部署的价值不仅取决于技术本身，更取决于人们如何与技术协同工作。

“Organizations often talk about keeping a ‘human in the loop’ when using AI, but KPMG and UT Austin's research helps clarify what that human should be doing,” said Ethan Burris, Senior Associate Dean for Academic Affairs, McCombs School of Business at UT Austin. “We find that the greatest value comes not just from technical adoption, but from the ways employees exercise judgment, shape workflows, interface with the technology, evaluate its outputs, and make decisions with AI. As these tools become more embedded in consequential work, responsible deployment will require both capable technology and a much clearer understanding of the human role in creating value.”
“组织在谈论使用 AI 时经常提到‘人在回路’（human in the loop），但毕马威与德克萨斯大学的研究有助于明确人类具体应该做什么，”德克萨斯大学奥斯汀分校麦库姆斯商学院学术事务高级副院长 Ethan Burris 表示，“我们发现，最大的价值不仅来自技术的采用，还来自员工如何运用判断力、塑造工作流程、与技术交互、评估输出结果以及利用 AI 做出决策。随着这些工具越来越多地嵌入到重要工作中，负责任的部署不仅需要强大的技术，还需要更清晰地理解人类在创造价值中的作用。”
