# New ways to create and get things done in Google Workspace
# 在 Google Workspace 中创造与完成工作的新方式

Announcing conversational voice features in Gmail, Docs and Keep, an image creation and editing tool called Google Pics, updates to AI Inbox and a 24/7 personal AI agent called Gemini Spark.
我们宣布在 Gmail、Docs 和 Keep 中推出对话式语音功能，发布名为 Google Pics 的图像创建与编辑工具，更新 AI Inbox，并推出名为 Gemini Spark 的 24/7 全天候个人 AI 助理。

More than 4 billion users rely on Google Workspace apps like Gmail, Docs and Drive to create, collaborate and get things done. Today, we’re announcing new ways that AI can help you do even more:
超过 40 亿用户依赖 Gmail、Docs 和 Drive 等 Google Workspace 应用来创作、协作和完成工作。今天，我们宣布了 AI 助力你实现更多目标的新方式：

*   **Voice capabilities:** New conversational features that help you brainstorm, organize your thoughts and get things done in Gmail, Docs and Keep with just your voice.
*   **语音功能：** 全新的对话式功能，只需通过语音即可在 Gmail、Docs 和 Keep 中进行头脑风暴、整理思路并完成任务。

*   **Google Pics:** An all-new app that reimagines how you generate and edit your images with ultimate precision for professional to everyday creative projects.
*   **Google Pics：** 一款全新的应用程序，重新构想了图像生成与编辑的方式，为从专业项目到日常创意需求提供极致的精确度。

*   **AI Inbox:** Now expanding to Google AI Plus and Pro subscribers with new updates to help you better manage and stay on top of your inbox.
*   **AI Inbox：** 现已向 Google AI Plus 和 Pro 订阅用户开放，并带来多项更新，帮助你更好地管理收件箱，掌握重要信息。

*   **Gemini Spark:** A new 24/7 personal AI agent in the Gemini app that can help you navigate digital life and take action on your behalf, under your direction, and integrate with your Workspace apps.
*   **Gemini Spark：** Gemini 应用中全新的 24/7 全天候个人 AI 助理，它能在你的指导下协助你处理数字生活、代你执行操作，并与你的 Workspace 应用深度集成。

### Let your voice do the work
### 让语音为你工作

New conversational features are evolving so you can get things done at the speed of your voice. Today we're announcing new voice capabilities in Gmail, Docs and Keep to help you brainstorm, organize your thoughts and take action.
全新的对话式功能正在不断进化，让你能以语音的速度完成工作。今天，我们宣布在 Gmail、Docs 和 Keep 中推出新的语音功能，帮助你进行头脑风暴、整理思路并采取行动。

**Search your inbox with Gmail Live:** When you’re on the go, you don't have time to dig through emails. With Gmail Live, you can just ask for what you need. Say things like, "What’s my flight's gate number?" or "What’s going on at my kid’s school this week?" and Gmail Live will search your inbox to find the answer instantly. Gmail Live is a voice-activated way to search your inbox and get synthesized information, quickly.
**使用 Gmail Live 搜索收件箱：** 当你在忙碌奔波时，没有时间翻找邮件。通过 Gmail Live，你只需询问所需信息。例如说：“我的航班登机口是几号？”或“这周我孩子学校有什么活动？”，Gmail Live 就会搜索你的收件箱并立即给出答案。Gmail Live 是一种语音激活的搜索方式，能让你快速获取整合后的信息。

**Think it through — and write it out — with Docs Live:** Docs Live acts as your thought partner and co-writer to help you get to a first draft faster using just your voice. Just talk, and Docs Live handles the heavy lifting — organizing your thoughts, structuring your document, and, with your permission, pulling relevant details from your Gmail, Drive, Chat and the web. Whether you’re rambling a stream of consciousness or brainstorming an idea, Docs Live helps you outline, refine your tone, and create seamlessly.
**通过 Docs Live 思考并撰写：** Docs Live 充当你的思维伙伴和共同创作者，仅需语音即可帮你更快完成初稿。你只需开口说话，Docs Live 就会处理繁重的工作——整理思路、构建文档结构，并在获得授权后，从你的 Gmail、Drive、Chat 和网络中提取相关细节。无论你是进行意识流式的随想还是头脑风暴，Docs Live 都能帮你列出大纲、润色语气并实现无缝创作。

**Just talk to Keep to organize your thoughts:** In Keep, you’re usually just trying to capture a quick idea or note that crossed your mind. Now, you can just “brain dump” and Keep not only understands your rambles, it gets to work in the background — turning what you say into organized notes and lists.
**通过语音整理 Keep 思路：** 在 Keep 中，你通常只是想快速记录脑海中闪过的想法或笔记。现在，你可以直接进行“大脑倾倒”，Keep 不仅能理解你的碎碎念，还会在后台自动工作——将你的话语转化为条理清晰的笔记和列表。

### Generate and edit visuals with creative control in Google Pics
### 在 Google Pics 中通过创意控制生成与编辑视觉内容

Creating with AI should feel like creative direction, not a roll of the dice. Too often, you generate a great image only to find it’s almost what you wanted — but making one small change means starting over completely.
使用 AI 进行创作应该是一种创意指导，而不是碰运气。我们经常遇到这种情况：生成了一张很棒的图片，却发现它“几乎”是想要的，但要进行一点小修改却意味着必须推倒重来。

Google Pics is our new image creation and editing tool, built on our latest Nano Banana model, with the precise creative controls you need to build exactly what you imagine. Whether you’re building a design from a blank canvas or editing an existing photo, Pics takes the hassle out of complex image generation.
Google Pics 是我们基于最新的 Nano Banana 模型构建的全新图像创建与编辑工具，它提供了你所需的精确创意控制，助你实现心中所想。无论你是从空白画布开始设计，还是编辑现有照片，Pics 都能消除复杂图像生成的烦恼。

### See what matters most with AI Inbox
### 通过 AI Inbox 查看最重要的内容

Earlier this year, we announced AI Inbox in Gmail to help you better manage and stay on top of your inbox by intelligently and securely prioritizing your most important updates and time-sensitive tasks.
今年早些时候，我们在 Gmail 中宣布了 AI Inbox，旨在通过智能且安全地优先处理最重要的更新和有时效性的任务，帮助你更好地管理收件箱。

Today, we're rolling out new capabilities that let you take action and get things done seamlessly:
今天，我们推出了新的功能，让你能够无缝地采取行动并完成工作：

*   **Personalized draft replies:** When an email needs a quick reply, AI Inbox doesn't just remind you — it generates a contextual draft so you can review and respond in seconds.
*   **个性化草稿回复：** 当邮件需要快速回复时，AI Inbox 不仅会提醒你，还会生成一份基于上下文的草稿，让你能在几秒钟内审阅并回复。
