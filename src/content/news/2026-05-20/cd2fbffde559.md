# I/O 2026: Welcome to the agentic Gemini era
# I/O 2026：欢迎来到智能体 Gemini 时代

Editor’s note: Below is an edited transcript of Google CEO Sundar Pichai’s remarks at Google I/O 2026, adapted to include more of what was announced on stage.
编者注：以下是谷歌首席执行官桑达尔·皮查伊（Sundar Pichai）在 Google I/O 2026 大会上的发言稿编辑版，并补充了更多舞台上发布的内容。

It’s been an extraordinary year since our last I/O, a period of relentless shipping, technology advances and hyper progress. We’re now in the part of the AI cycle where people want to see the value in the products they use every day. We’ve been really focused on that, and you’ll see that in the products and features we’re announcing today at I/O.
自上次 I/O 大会以来，这一年非同寻常，我们经历了持续的产品发布、技术突破和飞速进步。我们现在正处于人工智能周期的关键阶段，人们希望在日常使用的产品中看到实际价值。我们一直专注于此，你们将在今天 I/O 大会上发布的产品和功能中看到这一点。

Ten years since we pivoted the company to be AI-first, we still see AI as the most profound way to advance our mission and improve people’s lives at scale. That’s why we’ve been taking a differentiated, full-stack approach to AI innovation, from our custom silicon and secure foundation, to our world-class research and models, to our products and platforms that touch billions of people. This approach enables us to iterate and innovate faster in ways that are lighting up every part of the company.
距离我们将公司转型为“AI 优先”已过去十年，我们依然认为人工智能是推进我们使命并大规模改善人们生活的最深刻方式。正因如此，我们一直采取差异化的全栈式 AI 创新路径，从定制芯片和安全基础，到世界一流的研究与模型，再到触达数十亿人的产品与平台。这种方法使我们能够以更快的速度进行迭代和创新，点亮了公司的每一个角落。

What’s incredible is how people are using AI, whether it’s students prepping for final exams with the Gemini app, musicians and artists using generative AI models like Lyria and Veo as part of their creative flow, or developers coding and bringing their ideas to life.
令人惊叹的是人们使用 AI 的方式：无论是学生利用 Gemini 应用准备期末考试，音乐家和艺术家将 Lyria 和 Veo 等生成式 AI 模型融入创作流程，还是开发者通过编程将创意变为现实。

### AI momentum across the full stack
### 全栈 AI 的发展势头

These stories of how people are using AI are the best measure of progress. To understand the scale at which people are adopting AI, there is another great proxy — tokens, the fundamental units of data our models process, many representing a problem being solved.
这些关于人们如何使用 AI 的故事是衡量进步的最佳标准。要了解人们采用 AI 的规模，还有一个很好的指标——Token（词元），这是我们模型处理数据的基本单位，其中许多代表着一个正在被解决的问题。

Two years ago, we were processing 9.7 trillion tokens a month across our surfaces — a huge number. Last year at I/O, that grew to roughly 480 trillion tokens. Fast forward to today, that number jumped 7x to over 3.2 quadrillion per month. It tells an important story about our products and how others are building as well — especially developers and enterprises:
两年前，我们每月在各个平台上处理 9.7 万亿个 Token，这是一个巨大的数字。去年 I/O 大会上，这一数字增长到约 480 万亿。而今天，这个数字跃升了 7 倍，达到每月超过 3.2 千万亿。这有力地证明了我们的产品以及他人（尤其是开发者和企业）的构建成果：

*   Over 8.5 million developers are now building new apps and experiences with our models monthly.
*   目前每月有超过 850 万名开发者利用我们的模型构建新的应用程序和体验。
*   Our model APIs are now processing roughly 19 billion tokens per minute.
*   我们的模型 API 目前每分钟处理约 190 亿个 Token。
*   Over the past 12 months, over 375 Google Cloud customers each processed more than one trillion tokens, representing incredible demand for AI from across industries.
*   在过去 12 个月中，超过 375 家谷歌云客户每家处理了超过一万亿个 Token，这代表了各行各业对 AI 的巨大需求。

### Momentum with our products
### 产品发展势头

Today we have 13 products with over a billion users each. Five of those have more than 3 billion users. Our Gemini models are a big reason more people are using our products, and why they're using our products more. It all starts with Search, which is bringing the benefits of generative AI to more people than any other product in the world. AI Overviews now has over 2.5 billion monthly active users. And AI Mode has been a revelation, our biggest upgrade to Search ever. People love it, and in just a year, it’s already surpassed 1 billion monthly active users.
今天，我们拥有 13 款用户量超过 10 亿的产品，其中 5 款产品的用户量超过 30 亿。Gemini 模型是更多人使用我们产品以及使用频率增加的重要原因。这一切始于搜索，它将生成式 AI 的益处带给了比世界上任何其他产品都多的人。AI 概览（AI Overviews）目前拥有超过 25 亿月活跃用户。而 AI 模式（AI Mode）更是一项重大突破，是我们对搜索进行的最大规模升级。人们非常喜爱它，仅在一年内，其月活跃用户数就已突破 10 亿。

When people use our AI-powered features in Search, they use Search more. Search has become less about individual queries, and feels more like an ongoing conversation, giving you deeper insights and connecting you with the vastness of the web.
当人们使用搜索中的 AI 功能时，他们使用搜索的频率更高了。搜索不再仅仅是单一的查询，更像是一场持续的对话，为你提供更深入的见解，并将你与广袤的网络世界连接起来。

Another place where we’ve been rapidly innovating is in the Gemini app. Last year at I/O, the Gemini app had 400 million monthly active users. Today, we’ve surpassed 900 million, more than doubling in a year. In that same time, daily requests have grown over seven times.
我们快速创新的另一个领域是 Gemini 应用。去年 I/O 大会上，Gemini 应用拥有 4 亿月活跃用户。今天，这一数字已超过 9 亿，一年内翻了一番多。与此同时，每日请求量增长了七倍以上。

We’ve been adding a lot of unique features like Personal Intelligence, which make responses more customized and helpful. And to date more than 50 billion images have been generated with our Nano Banana image generation models. It was a breakout star this past year, showing how much latent creativity there is in the world.
我们增加了许多独特的功能，例如“个人智能”（Personal Intelligence），使回复更加个性化和实用。迄今为止，我们的 Nano Banana 图像生成模型已经生成了超过 500 亿张图像。它是过去一年中的明星产品，展现了世界上蕴藏着多么巨大的潜在创造力。

### Natural, conversational AI in products
### 产品中的自然对话式 AI

There’s also a lot of latent productivity to be unlocked. Over the last year, we’ve been bringing the ability to have more natural conversations with Gemini directly inside our products. Recently, Maps got its biggest upgrade in a decade, including a new feature called Ask Maps. People are using Ask Maps for more complex, and much longer questions.
还有许多潜在的生产力等待被释放。在过去的一年里，我们一直在将与 Gemini 进行更自然对话的能力直接引入我们的产品中。最近，地图（Maps）进行了十年来最大的一次升级，包括一项名为“Ask Maps”的新功能。人们正在使用 Ask Maps 来处理更复杂、更长的问题。

Now we’re bringing more natural conversational AI to more products.
现在，我们将更自然的对话式 AI 带入更多产品中。

### Ask YouTube
### Ask YouTube

People come to YouTube everyday to ask a lot of questions. There’s a lot of great videos, but sometimes it’s hard to know where to start. Ask YouTube entirely reimagines the experience, making information much more digestible and easy to navigate. You’ll see videos that best match your interest, and most importantly, it jumps right to the part of the video most relevant to you. We’re starting to test Ask YouTube now, and it will roll out broadly in the U.S. this summer.
人们每天来到 YouTube 提出各种问题。这里有许多精彩的视频，但有时很难知道从哪里开始。Ask YouTube 完全重构了这一体验，使信息更易于消化和浏览。你将看到最符合你兴趣的视频，最重要的是，它会直接跳转到与你最相关的视频片段。我们现在开始测试 Ask YouTube，并于今年夏天在美国广泛推出。

### Voice-powered Docs Live
### 语音驱动的 Docs Live

There are a lot of times I want to get things done at the speed of my voice. That is much more possible today thanks to technical leaps in our audio models. A new feature called Docs Live takes this to another level. To create a doc with Gemini before, you had to type out a precise prompt. With Docs Live, you can just verbally “brain dump” whatever is on your mind, and let Gemini do the rest. Here’s a demo in real-time: In the future, you’ll be able to create new docs and edit them directly, all with your voice. Docs Live is rolling out for subscribers this summer, and powerful voice capabilities will come to Gmail and Keep then too.
很多时候，我想以说话的速度完成工作。得益于我们音频模型的技术飞跃，这在今天变得更加可行。一项名为 Docs Live 的新功能将此提升到了一个新的高度。以前，要用 Gemini 创建文档，你必须输入精确的提示词。有了 Docs Live，你只需口头“倾倒”出你脑海中的想法，剩下的交给 Gemini 即可。这是一个实时演示：未来，你将能够完全通过语音创建新文档并直接进行编辑。Docs Live 将于今年夏天向订阅用户推出，强大的语音功能届时也将登陆 Gmail 和 Keep。
