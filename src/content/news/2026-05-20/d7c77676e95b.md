# Discord enables end-to-end encrypted voice and video calling for every user
# Discord 为所有用户启用端到端加密语音和视频通话功能

Messaging platform giant Discord has switched on end-to-end encrypted voice and video messaging for every user.
即时通讯平台巨头 Discord 已为所有用户开启了端到端加密的语音和视频通话功能。

The end-to-end encryption feature means that Discord users can now communicate privately without anyone else being able to listen in to their calls, not even Discord.
这一端到端加密功能意味着 Discord 用户现在可以进行私密交流，任何第三方（包括 Discord 官方）都无法监听其通话内容。

This is a major privacy win for the community’s hundreds of millions of users, soon after social media giant Meta pulled the plug on Instagram’s end-to-end encrypted messaging feature earlier this year.
对于该平台数以亿计的用户来说，这是一次重大的隐私胜利。此前不久，社交媒体巨头 Meta 在今年早些时候取消了 Instagram 的端到端加密消息功能。

TikTok said it would also not end-to-end encrypt user messages after it became a U.S. company.
TikTok 也曾表示，在成为一家美国公司后，将不会对用户消息进行端到端加密。

Discord launched the end-to-end encrypted voice and video calling feature in 2024, and on Monday rolled out the feature to every user, no action needed.
Discord 于 2024 年推出了端到端加密语音和视频通话功能，并于周一向所有用户全面开放，用户无需进行任何操作即可使用。

“End-to-end Encryption is now standard for every voice and video call on Discord, outside of stage channels. No opt-in required,” said Mark Smith, Discord’s vice president of core technologies, in a blog post.
Discord 核心技术副总裁 Mark Smith 在一篇博文中表示：“除了舞台频道（Stage Channels）外，端到端加密现在已成为 Discord 上所有语音和视频通话的标准配置，无需用户手动开启。”
