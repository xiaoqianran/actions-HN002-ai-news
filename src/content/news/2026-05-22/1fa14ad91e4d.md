### AI is just unauthorised plagiarism at a bigger scale
### 人工智能不过是更大规模的未经授权剽窃

**20 May, 2026**
**2026年5月20日**

AI takes in all the input, whether the original authors have consented or not, and do some "learning", and then the AI companies sell these learned result to humans, without compensating the original authors.
人工智能接收所有输入信息，无论原作者是否同意，它都会进行所谓的“学习”，然后人工智能公司将这些学习成果出售给人类，却不向原作者支付任何报酬。

Worse, the customer of these AI companies (AI tools bro) sell the prompted / processed result to other customers, profitting off things AI has copied from all over the internet.
更糟糕的是，这些人工智能公司的客户（那些所谓的“AI工具达人”）将提示词生成或处理后的结果转卖给其他客户，利用人工智能从互联网各处抄袭来的内容牟利。

Is this what the pinnacle of human is? Lazy and greedy?
这就是人类的巅峰吗？懒惰且贪婪？

I research and write e-commerce related tutorials on my own, and a few other lazy website authors just ask ChatGPT to copy a few well performing tutorial online, and then they published it as their own.
我亲自研究并撰写电子商务相关的教程，而其他一些懒惰的网站作者只是让 ChatGPT 复制网上几篇表现良好的教程，然后就将其发布为自己的作品。

I found out this because they ranked higher than me in Google search result, and then when I read their article, their article contains links to my actual website, with the exact link text (?!) , which means they didnt bother to check and remove, and thats how I found out.
我发现这一点是因为他们的搜索排名比我高，当我阅读他们的文章时，发现里面竟然包含了指向我实际网站的链接，甚至连链接文本都一模一样（？！），这意味着他们根本懒得检查和删除，我就是这样发现的。

Fuck Google for ranking some copycat website higher than mine, even though they copied my article.
去他的谷歌，竟然把那些抄袭我文章的网站排名排在我的前面。
