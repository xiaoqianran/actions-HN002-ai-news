# GROW: Aligning GRPO with State-Action Modeling for Open-World VLM Agents

**Abstract:** Recently, vision-language model (VLM) agents have shown promising progress in open-world tasks, where successful task completion often requires multiple turns of visual perception and action execution. 

**摘要：** 近期，视觉语言模型（VLM）智能体在开放世界任务中展现出了令人瞩目的进展，在这些任务中，成功完成目标通常需要多轮的视觉感知与动作执行。

However, existing methods still rely primarily on Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT) with expert demonstrations, while the advanced reinforcement learning (RL) algorithm, specifically Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO), has not been effectively employed for multi-turn RL in these tasks because standard GRPO requires full trajectories as training samples which leads to excessively long context and noise. 

然而，现有方法主要依赖于基于专家演示的监督微调（SFT），而先进的强化学习（RL）算法——特别是组相对策略优化（GRPO）——尚未能有效地应用于这些任务的多轮强化学习中。这是因为标准的 GRPO 需要将完整轨迹作为训练样本，从而导致上下文过长并引入噪声。

To address this issue, we propose GROW, a RL framework for open-world VLM agents that decomposes collected trajectories into state-action samples, and computes advantages between these samples rather than treating a full trajectory as a single entity. 

为了解决这一问题，我们提出了 GROW，这是一个面向开放世界 VLM 智能体的强化学习框架。该框架将收集到的轨迹分解为“状态-动作”样本，并在这些样本之间计算优势（advantages），而不是将完整轨迹视为单一实体。

We further provide a surrogate analysis indicating that, even though the grouped samples are conditioned on different local states rather than an identical prompt context, the objective can preserve the core relative policy optimization signal of GRPO under simplifying assumptions. 

我们进一步提供了代理分析，表明尽管分组样本是基于不同的局部状态而非相同的提示上下文，但在简化假设下，该目标函数仍能保留 GRPO 的核心相对策略优化信号。

Experiments on more than 800 Minecraft tasks show that our method achieves state-of-the-art (SOTA) performance, demonstrating the effectiveness of our proposed RL framework for open-world VLM agents.

在超过 800 个《我的世界》（Minecraft）任务上的实验表明，我们的方法达到了最先进（SOTA）的性能，证明了我们提出的强化学习框架在开放世界 VLM 智能体中的有效性。
