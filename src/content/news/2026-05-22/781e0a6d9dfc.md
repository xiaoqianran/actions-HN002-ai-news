# TabPFN-MT: A Natively Multitask In-Context Learner for Tabular Data
## TabPFN-MT：一种用于表格数据的原生多任务上下文学习器

**Abstract:** Prior-Data Fitted networks (PFNs) have been very successful in tabular contexts, handling prediction tasks in context. However, they are designed for single-task inference, meaning that predicting several target values within a context requires repeated forward calls and precludes inter-task information sharing.

**摘要：** 先验数据拟合网络（PFNs）在表格数据领域取得了巨大成功，能够处理上下文中的预测任务。然而，它们的设计初衷是单任务推理，这意味着在同一上下文中预测多个目标值需要重复进行前向传播调用，且无法实现任务间的信息共享。

We propose TabPFN-MT, which is trained on an expanded multi-target synthetic prior to capture inter-task dependencies in context. This model uses an expanded $y$-encoder and a shared decoder head to enable multitask in-context learning and simultaneous inference.

我们提出了 TabPFN-MT，它通过在扩展的多目标合成先验上进行训练，以捕捉上下文中的任务间依赖关系。该模型使用扩展的 $y$-编码器和共享解码器头，实现了多任务上下文学习和同步推理。

The model is uniquely specialized for small-to-medium datasets by relying on in-context learning rather than traditional gradient-based training. Within this regime (averaging fewer than 1,000 samples), extensive evaluations across 344 datasets demonstrate that TabPFN-MT establishes a new state-of-the-art for deep tabular multitask learning.

该模型通过依赖上下文学习而非传统的基于梯度的训练，专门针对中小型数据集进行了优化。在此规模下（平均样本数少于 1,000 个），对 344 个数据集的广泛评估表明，TabPFN-MT 为深度表格多任务学习确立了新的技术水平（SOTA）。

Furthermore, despite the inherent compute asymmetry of joint optimization, our model remains highly competitive with the latest state-of-the-art single-task ensembles. Notably, on multitask datasets it achieves an overall Accuracy rank of 4.89, the highest average rank among all models tested.

此外，尽管联合优化存在固有的计算不对称性，但我们的模型在与最新的单任务集成模型相比时，依然保持了极强的竞争力。值得注意的是，在多任务数据集上，它获得了 4.89 的综合准确率排名，是所有测试模型中的最高平均排名。

Crucially, TabPFN-MT delivers this highly competitive performance while reducing the inference cost for $T$ tasks from $O(T)$ to $O(1)$ forward passes, offering a massive computational efficiency improvement for multi-target tabular applications.

至关重要的是，TabPFN-MT 在提供这种极具竞争力的性能的同时，将 $T$ 个任务的推理成本从 $O(T)$ 次前向传播降低到了 $O(1)$ 次，为多目标表格应用带来了巨大的计算效率提升。
