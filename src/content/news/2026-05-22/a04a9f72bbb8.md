# LLM Themes Are Not Observations
# LLM 主题并非观测值

**A practitioner's warning about generated variables in causal analysis**
**关于因果分析中生成变量的从业者警示**

An analyst joins LLM-extracted themes from a call corpus to the customer table. Customers without transcripts get NULL. NULL gets filled with zero, or with “no issue mentioned,” or quietly omitted as a reference category. In one line of preprocessing, the pipeline converts *did not call support* into *did not experience billing frustration*. The regression that follows looks clean. The coefficient on “billing frustration” is significant, signed the way the product team expected, large enough to matter. It gets pasted into a roadmap document. Nobody asks where the variable came from.
一位分析师将从通话语料库中提取的 LLM 主题与客户表进行了关联。没有通话记录的客户被标记为 NULL。NULL 值被填充为 0，或标记为“未提及问题”，又或者作为参考类别被悄悄忽略。在预处理的一行代码中，流水线将“未致电客服”转换成了“未经历账单困扰”。随后的回归分析看起来非常完美。“账单困扰”的系数显著，符号符合产品团队的预期，且影响程度足够大。它被粘贴进路线图文档中。没人会去问这个变量究竟从何而来。

This article is about what got smuggled in with that fill value, and about three other moves that look just as innocuous in a notebook but rest on assumptions the analysis never names. The setup is not specific to support calls. It applies to chat logs, ticket summaries, product reviews, sales transcripts, and survey free-response fields, anywhere a modern pipeline turns text into a tidy column. The pipeline could be a fine-tuned classifier, a zero-shot LLM, or an embedding-plus-cluster. The conceptual problem is the same: the column is not an observation of a customer attribute. It is the output of a generative process applied to a self-selected subset of customer behavior.
本文旨在探讨那些随填充值一起被“走私”进来的东西，以及另外三个在笔记本中看起来同样无害、却建立在分析从未明确说明的假设之上的操作。这种设置并非仅限于客服通话。它同样适用于聊天记录、工单摘要、产品评论、销售转录和调查问卷的自由回复字段——任何现代流水线将文本转化为整洁列的地方。该流水线可能是一个微调分类器、一个零样本 LLM，或是一个嵌入加聚类模型。其概念性问题是一样的：这一列并非对客户属性的观测，而是应用于客户行为自选子集的生成过程的输出。

Practitioners increasingly treat outputs like these as if they were direct readings of customer state. They are not. They are generated variables: measurements produced by a pipeline, conditional on a customer doing something that left a textual trace, conditional on that trace surviving the extraction model. Every step of that conditional has consequences for what the variable means in a downstream causal model, and most of those consequences are invisible in the joined table. Four things tend to go wrong, and the NULL move makes all four visible at once.
从业者越来越倾向于将这些输出视为对客户状态的直接读取。事实并非如此。它们是生成变量：由流水线产生的测量值，其前提是客户做出了留下文本痕迹的行为，且该痕迹在提取模型中得以保留。这些前提中的每一步都会对变量在下游因果模型中的含义产生影响，而大多数影响在关联后的表格中是不可见的。通常有四件事会出错，而 NULL 值的处理方式让这四个问题同时显现出来。

**Selection.** A theme exists for a customer because that customer called, complained, posted, or replied. Whatever drove that action is also probably correlated with the treatment, the outcome, or both. The NULL fill collapses “did not generate text” into the reference category, and the analysis is no longer estimating an effect over the customer base. It is estimating an effect over a redefined population, and the redefinition happened in preprocessing.
**选择偏差。** 某个主题之所以存在于客户身上，是因为该客户致电、投诉、发帖或回复了。驱动该行为的因素很可能与处理（treatment）、结果（outcome）或两者同时相关。NULL 值的填充将“未生成文本”合并到了参考类别中，此时分析不再是对整个客户群估算效应，而是在对一个被重新定义的群体估算效应，且这种重新定义发生在预处理阶段。

**Timing.** Was the call before the treatment, during it, or after? Pre-treatment text is a candidate confounder. Post-treatment text is a candidate mediator or outcome, and treating it as a pre-treatment control is a classic source of post-treatment bias. The joined table rarely makes this visible.
**时间性。** 通话是在处理之前、期间还是之后发生的？处理前的文本是潜在的混杂因素。处理后的文本是潜在的中介变量或结果，将其视为处理前的控制变量是导致“处理后偏差”（post-treatment bias）的经典原因。关联后的表格很少能体现这一点。

**Measurement.** The label “billing frustration” is not billing frustration. It is what the pipeline detected as billing-frustration-shaped language. Classifier accuracy is finite, and accuracy can differ across treatment arms, because a treatment that changes how customers talk also changes how the model reads them. The label noise is not orthogonal to the thing being studied.
**测量。** 标签“账单困扰”并不等同于账单困扰本身。它只是流水线检测到的、具有“账单困扰”特征的语言。分类器的准确率是有限的，且准确率可能在不同的处理组之间存在差异，因为改变客户谈话方式的处理手段也会改变模型对他们的解读方式。标签噪声与所研究的对象并非正交。

**Role.** Is the theme acting as a confounder, a mediator, a treatment, an outcome, or a descriptive feature? The DAG decides this, not the column name. A variable that is methodologically valid in one role becomes a bias source in another.
**角色。** 该主题是作为混杂因素、中介变量、处理手段、结果还是描述性特征存在？这由有向无环图（DAG）决定，而不是由列名决定。一个在某种角色下方法论上有效的变量，在另一种角色下可能成为偏差的来源。

These four problems are not independent. They interact. An LLM-detected theme inherits a selection footprint from the channel it came through, a timing footprint from when the text was generated, and a measurement footprint from the pipeline that extracted it. The downstream regression sees a column of zeros and ones. The problem is not that the pipeline produced a bad label. The problem is that the label inherited a data-generating process the downstream analysis never modeled.
这四个问题并非相互独立，而是相互作用的。LLM 检测到的主题继承了来自其来源渠道的选择足迹、来自文本生成时间的时间足迹，以及来自提取流水线的测量足迹。下游的回归分析看到的只是一列 0 和 1。问题不在于流水线产生了一个糟糕的标签，而在于该标签继承了一个下游分析从未建模的数据生成过程。

The rest of this article works through what that means in practice, where the standard workflow goes wrong, and what the minimum diagnostic looks like. We start with the role-and-timing question, because it is the one analysts get wrong first.
本文的其余部分将探讨这意味着什么，标准工作流程在何处出错，以及最基本的诊断应该是什么样子。我们从角色和时间性问题开始，因为这是分析师最容易犯错的地方。

**Role and timing are the same question**
**角色与时间性是同一个问题**

The first move an analyst makes with a transcript-derived theme is implicit: they treat it as a covariate. Themes go into the right-hand side of the regression. The treatment is the variable of interest. The outcome is on the left. The theme is “controlled for.” That phrase, “controlled for,” is doing work the analyst hasn’t checked. Controlling for a variable adjusts away the part of the treatment-outcome relationship that flows through it. Whether that adjustment helps or hurts depends entirely on where the variable sits in the causal graph, and that position is determined by timing.
分析师在处理转录衍生主题时的第一个动作是隐性的：他们将其视为协变量。主题被放入回归方程的右侧。处理变量是关注的核心，结果变量在左侧。主题被“控制”了。这个“控制”一词所做的工作，分析师往往并未核实。控制一个变量会消除处理与结果之间通过该变量流动的关系部分。这种调整是有益还是有害，完全取决于该变量在因果图中的位置，而该位置由时间性决定。

Pre-treatment text, generated before the treatment was assigned, can play the role of a confounder. If a customer called about billing in January and the retention offer went out in March, the call captures something about customer state that may influence both who got the offer and who churned. Conditioning on the theme here can reduce bias from omitted variables, provided the theme actually proxies for the relevant construct and the selection issues in the next section are handled.
处理前生成的文本（在处理分配之前）可以扮演混杂因素的角色。如果客户在 1 月份因账单问题致电，而留存优惠在 3 月份发出，那么该通话捕捉到的客户状态信息可能同时影响了谁获得了优惠以及谁流失了。在此处对主题进行条件化处理可以减少遗漏变量带来的偏差，前提是该主题确实能代表相关构念，且下一节提到的选择问题已得到处理。

Concurrent text, generated as part of the treatment itself, is not a covariate at all. If the treatment is a call from a retention agent and the theme comes from that same call, the theme is part of the intervention. Conditioning on it doesn’t adjust for confounding; it removes part of the effect the analyst is trying to measure.
并发文本（作为处理本身的一部分生成）根本不是协变量。如果处理是来自留存专员的电话，而主题来自同一次通话，那么该主题就是干预措施的一部分。对其进行条件化处理并不能调整混杂因素，反而会消除分析师试图测量的效应的一部分。

Post-treatment text, generated after the treatment, is the most dangerous category, because it is the one most likely to be misclassified as a confounder by an analyst working from a flat table with no time index.
