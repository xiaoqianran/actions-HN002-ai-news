**Basedash Skills**
Basedash Skills 正式发布：为每个 Basedash 界面提供可复用的 AI 指令。

**Skills are reusable bundles of instructions that every Basedash AI surface can read on demand. Define your metrics once and any AI agent in your workspace will pick up the skill when it's relevant. No more pasting the same caveats into every prompt.**
“Skills”是一组可复用的指令包，Basedash 的每个 AI 界面都能按需读取。你只需定义一次指标，工作区中的任何 AI 代理在需要时都会自动调用该技能。从此无需再在每个提示词中重复粘贴相同的注意事项。
