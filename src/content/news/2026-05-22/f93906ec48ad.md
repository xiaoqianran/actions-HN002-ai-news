# Geometry-Lite: Interpretable Safety Probing via Layer-Wise Margin Geometry
# Geometry-Lite：通过层级边界几何实现可解释的安全探测

**Abstract:** Prompt-level safety probes for large language models use hidden-state representations to separate safe from unsafe prompts, but strong average detection performance does not explain the geometry of this separation. In particular, it remains unclear how safety evidence is formed across layers, which aspects of that layer-wise geometry support low-false-positive decisions, and which geometric biases remain stable under benchmark shift.

**摘要：** 大型语言模型的提示词级（Prompt-level）安全探测器利用隐藏状态表示来区分安全与不安全的提示词，但强大的平均检测性能并不能解释这种分离背后的几何结构。特别是，目前尚不清楚安全证据是如何在各层之间形成的，层级几何的哪些方面支持低误报率（low-false-positive）决策，以及哪些几何偏差在基准测试迁移下保持稳定。

We study this as an empirical decomposition problem and introduce Geometry-Lite, a compact prompt-level probe that maps each layer's final prompt-token representation to signed margins under centroid, local-neighborhood, and supervised linear-boundary readouts, then summarizes the resulting margin profiles by boundary position, layer-to-layer change, and coarse shape.

我们将此研究视为一个经验分解问题，并引入了 Geometry-Lite。这是一个紧凑的提示词级探测器，它将每一层最终的提示词标记（prompt-token）表示映射到质心、局部邻域和监督线性边界读出下的符号边界（signed margins），然后通过边界位置、层间变化和粗略形状来总结所得的边界轮廓。

Across nine instruction-tuned backbones ($1.2$B--$70$B) and seven safety benchmarks, Geometry-Lite improves over single-layer probes while remaining close to raw multi-layer score stacking, making it a useful instrument for analyzing the multi-layer safety signal. The decomposition shows that safety evidence is expressed primarily through persistent boundary-position geometry: final or extremal margins and unsafe-side layer occupancy dominate aggregate detection performance.

在九个指令微调主干模型（1.2B--70B 参数）和七个安全基准测试中，Geometry-Lite 的表现优于单层探测器，同时保持了与原始多层分数堆叠相近的性能，使其成为分析多层安全信号的有效工具。分解结果表明，安全证据主要通过持久的边界位置几何来表达：最终或极端的边界以及不安全侧的层占用率主导了整体的检测性能。

In contrast, finite-difference drift and structural summaries add little to pooled AUROC, although drift can provide small recall-oriented corrections under shifted low-FPR thresholds. Under benchmark shift, optimized linear boundaries are sharp on the training mixture, whereas class-conditional mean geometry retains separation more reliably on a predefined hard held-out subset.

相比之下，有限差分漂移（finite-difference drift）和结构摘要对汇总的 AUROC 贡献甚微，尽管漂移可以在偏移的低误报率（low-FPR）阈值下提供少量的召回导向修正。在基准测试迁移下，优化的线性边界在训练混合集上表现敏锐，而类条件均值几何在预定义的困难留出子集上能更可靠地保持分离效果。

Overall, prompt-level safety evidence is not primarily a layer-to-layer motion signal, but a persistent layer-wise margin geometry whose useful components and readout-level biases become visible in decision-critical regimes.

总的来说，提示词级的安全证据主要不是一种层与层之间的运动信号，而是一种持久的层级边界几何结构；其有用的组件和读出层面的偏差在决策关键的场景中变得清晰可见。
