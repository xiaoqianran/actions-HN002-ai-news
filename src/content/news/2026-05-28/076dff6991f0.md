# The Verge’s 2026 college graduation gift guide
# The Verge 2026年大学毕业礼物指南

Whether your grad is moving, traveling, or starting a career, these gifts will help them navigate post-college life.
无论你的毕业生是准备搬家、旅行还是开启职业生涯，这些礼物都能帮助他们更好地适应毕业后的生活。

Graduating from college is exciting, but it can also feel slightly terrifying. Along with celebrating a huge accomplishment, many grads jump right into looking for a job. Some might be getting their first apartment, too, which brings on a whole new set of responsibilities. That’s why getting the right graduation gift is so important: They aren’t merely celebratory, they can make these huge transitions feel a little less overwhelming.
大学毕业令人兴奋，但也可能让人感到些许惶恐。在庆祝这一巨大成就的同时，许多毕业生会立即投入到求职中。有些人可能还要搬进人生第一间公寓，这带来了一系列全新的责任。这就是为什么挑选一份合适的毕业礼物如此重要：它们不仅是为了庆祝，更能让这些重大的生活转变显得不那么令人不知所措。

We’ve curated dozens of gift ideas that celebrate their accomplishment and prepare them for life after college. Some of our picks are practical, meant to make adulting less daunting, including clothes steamers for interviews, kitchen essentials for staying fed, and robot vacuums to keep floors clean while you’re busy. Others are more about helping grads have fun during their downtime, whether that’s with a portable speaker, a gaming monitor, or an OLED TV.
我们精心挑选了数十种礼物创意，旨在庆祝他们的成就并为毕业后的生活做好准备。我们的一些选择非常实用，旨在减轻成年生活的压力，包括用于面试的挂烫机、维持日常饮食的厨房必需品，以及在你忙碌时保持地板清洁的扫地机器人。另一些礼物则侧重于帮助毕业生在闲暇时享受乐趣，无论是便携式音箱、游戏显示器还是 OLED 电视。
