# DuoGesture: Neuro-Inspired and Biomechanically Informed Dual-Stream Co-Speech Gesture Generation
# DuoGesture：受神经启发且符合生物力学的双流协同语音手势生成

**Abstract:** Co-speech gesture generation requires both semantic expressivity and biomechanically plausible rhythmic motion. Existing holistic gesture models mix lexically grounded semantic gestures with frequent prosody-aligned beat gestures. This limits semantic grounding, speech-motion alignment, and kinematic smoothness.

**摘要：** 协同语音手势生成既需要语义表达能力，也需要符合生物力学的节奏性动作。现有的整体式手势模型将基于词汇的语义手势与频繁的韵律对齐节拍手势混合在一起。这限制了语义基础、语音与动作的对齐以及运动学的平滑度。

We propose *DuoGesture*, a neuro-inspired and biomechanically informed dual-stream approach that decomposes co-speech gesture synthesis into coupled semantic and beat streams. The two streams are coordinated by a *Semantic Variational Information Bottleneck*, a stochastic frame-level gate that learns when semantic gestures should override rhythmic beat motion.

我们提出了 *DuoGesture*，这是一种受神经启发且符合生物力学的双流方法，它将协同语音手势合成分解为语义流和节拍流。这两个流由“语义变分信息瓶颈”（Semantic Variational Information Bottleneck）进行协调，这是一个随机的帧级门控机制，用于学习何时应由语义手势覆盖节奏性节拍动作。

The semantic stream is controlled by *Motion-Grounded Semantic Conditioning*, which replaces purely linguistic word embeddings with motion-language representations to provide motion-aligned semantic priors for long-tailed lexical triggers of gestures. The beat stream is further regularised by an *Inertial Beat Prior*, an anthropometry-weighted arm-chain module that reduces jitter and improves rhythmic consistency without constraining semantic frames.

语义流由“运动基础语义调节”（Motion-Grounded Semantic Conditioning）控制，它用运动-语言表征取代了纯语言词嵌入，从而为手势的长尾词汇触发器提供与运动对齐的语义先验。节拍流则通过“惯性节拍先验”（Inertial Beat Prior）进一步正则化，这是一个基于人体测量学加权的臂链模块，它在不限制语义帧的情况下减少了抖动并提高了节奏的一致性。

Objective evaluations and subjective experiments show that DuoGesture outperforms strong holistic baselines, while component ablations confirm the complementary roles of semantic grounding, stochastic stream selection, and biomechanical regularisation.

客观评估和主观实验表明，DuoGesture 的表现优于强大的整体式基准模型，同时组件消融实验证实了语义基础、随机流选择和生物力学正则化在其中发挥的互补作用。
