# On the Push-Based Asynchronous Federated Learning: A Bias-Correction Aggregation Approach
# 关于基于推送的异步联邦学习：一种偏差校正聚合方法

**Abstract:** Asynchronous decentralized federated learning (ADFL) eliminates central coordination and global synchronization, making it attractive for large-scale and heterogeneous systems. However, frequent peer-to-peer communication, asynchronous updates on directed topologies, and non-IID data jointly lead to excessive communication overhead, biased aggregation and severe model drift.

**摘要：** 异步去中心化联邦学习（ADFL）消除了中央协调和全局同步，使其在大规模和异构系统中极具吸引力。然而，频繁的点对点通信、有向拓扑上的异步更新以及非独立同分布（non-IID）数据共同导致了过高的通信开销、有偏的聚合以及严重的模型漂移。

We propose PushCen-ADFL, a communication-efficient ADFL framework that enables stable training under asymmetric communication and delayed client participation. PushCen-ADFL couples communication, aggregation, and local stabilization in a shared centroid representation space, forming a closed loop between compression and optimization.

我们提出了 PushCen-ADFL，这是一个通信高效的 ADFL 框架，能够在非对称通信和客户端延迟参与的情况下实现稳定训练。PushCen-ADFL 将通信、聚合和局部稳定性耦合在一个共享的质心表示空间中，在压缩和优化之间形成了一个闭环。

Clients exchange centroid-form messages, apply average-preserving push-sum mixing to correct aggregation bias, and use a lightweight centroid regularization anchored in the same centroid space to mitigate drift under heterogeneity and staleness. A bounded, sender-deduplicated buffer further improves robustness under irregular asynchronous arrivals.

客户端交换质心形式的消息，应用保持平均值的推送求和（push-sum）混合来校正聚合偏差，并使用锚定在同一质心空间中的轻量级质心正则化，以减轻异构性和陈旧性带来的漂移。一个有界的、发送者去重的缓冲区进一步提高了在不规则异步到达情况下的鲁棒性。

Experiments on vision datasets demonstrate that PushCen-ADFL improves accuracy under data heterogeneity by up to 6% while reducing per-push communication cost by more than 80%, achieving a favorable accuracy-communication trade-off.

在视觉数据集上的实验表明，PushCen-ADFL 在数据异构性下的准确率提高了多达 6%，同时将每次推送的通信成本降低了 80% 以上，实现了良好的准确率与通信成本之间的权衡。
