# I'm Tired of Talking to AI
# 我厌倦了与人工智能对话

I’m tired of talking to AI. I found GitHub repositories that were spreading malware. I asked AI what to do about it, but it gave me nothing useful. So I opened a discussion on GitHub. Someone replied. It was the exact same text the AI had given me. I called it out and the comment was deleted. Then another person replied. It was the same AI answer again.

我厌倦了与人工智能对话。我发现了一些传播恶意软件的 GitHub 存储库。我问 AI 该如何处理，但它没给我任何有用的建议。于是，我在 GitHub 上发起了一个讨论。有人回复了，内容竟然和我从 AI 那里得到的文本一模一样。我指出了这一点，结果评论被删除了。随后，另一个人又回复了，依然是同样的 AI 回答。

I worked as a developer at a company. I asked the business owner a question about a business task. He sent me a ChatGPT screenshot with the answer. I replied that it had nothing to do with my question and everything there was wrong. A minute later he sent me another ChatGPT screenshot. He didn’t even read the AI’s answer. He just took a screenshot and forwarded it to me.

我曾在一家公司担任开发人员。我向企业主询问了一个关于业务任务的问题，他发给我一张包含答案的 ChatGPT 截图。我回复说，这与我的问题毫无关系，而且内容全错。一分钟后，他又发给我另一张 ChatGPT 截图。他甚至根本没读过 AI 的回答，只是截了个图就转发给了我。

Recently someone messaged me on Reddit about my post. I replied. They wrote again, I replied again. After a few messages I realized I was talking to an AI agent.

最近，有人在 Reddit 上就我的帖子给我发了私信。我回复了，对方又写了些什么，我又回复了。在几轮对话后，我意识到我其实是在和一个 AI 代理聊天。

I’m tired of talking to AI. I want to talk to real people. But even when I talk to people, they forward my questions to AI and send me the AI’s answer.

我厌倦了与人工智能对话。我想和真实的人交流。但即使当我与人交谈时，他们也会把我的问题转交给 AI，然后再把 AI 的回答发给我。
