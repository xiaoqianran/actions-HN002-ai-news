# RoMo: A Large-Scale, Richly Organized Dataset and Semantic Taxonomy for Human Motion Generation
# RoMo：用于人体运动生成的大规模、结构化数据集与语义分类体系

**Abstract:** Success in generative modeling across language, image, and video demonstrates that large, well-curated datasets are the key driver for building capable models. 3D Human motion, however, has lagged behind, constrained by an unsatisfying choice between small, high-fidelity motion capture datasets and large-scale in-the-wild collections dominated by static or low-quality sequences.
**摘要：** 语言、图像和视频生成模型领域的成功表明，大规模且经过精心策划的数据集是构建高性能模型的关键驱动力。然而，3D 人体运动领域的发展却相对滞后，受限于一种令人不满的选择困境：要么是规模较小、高保真的动作捕捉数据集，要么是充斥着静态或低质量序列的大规模“野外”（in-the-wild）数据集。

We introduce RoMo, a rich, large-scale, carefully curated dataset of in-the-wild human motions that resolves these tradeoffs. To ensure quality, we introduce a taxonomy-aware filtering pipeline that aggressively removes static and artifact-prone sequences. Every sequence is annotated with detailed captions and organized by a novel three-level semantic taxonomy.
我们推出了 RoMo，这是一个丰富、大规模且经过精心筛选的“野外”人体运动数据集，旨在解决上述权衡难题。为了确保质量，我们引入了一套基于分类体系的过滤流程，能够高效剔除静态及易产生伪影的序列。每个序列都配有详细的文本描述，并按照一种新颖的三级语义分类体系进行组织。

This hierarchical structure enables fine-grained, per-category evaluation, that reveals model strengths and weaknesses obscured by global metrics. We demonstrate that models trained on RoMo achieve state-of-the-art fidelity and diversity while gaining a superior understanding of complex, subtle text prompts.
这种层级结构支持细粒度的按类别评估，从而揭示了那些被全局指标所掩盖的模型优缺点。我们证明，在 RoMo 上训练的模型不仅实现了最先进的保真度和多样性，还对复杂、微妙的文本提示词具备了更强的理解能力。

Finally, we release the Motion Toolbox to standardize metrics, data conversion, and visualization, establishing a foundation for reproducible and interpretable motion generation research.
最后，我们发布了 Motion Toolbox（运动工具箱），用于标准化指标、数据转换和可视化，为可复现且可解释的运动生成研究奠定了基础。
