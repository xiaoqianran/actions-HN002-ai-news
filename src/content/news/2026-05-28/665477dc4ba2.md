# Planning Neural Dynamics with Lie Group Embedding through Supervised Projective Manifold Learning
# 通过监督投影流形学习实现李群嵌入的神经动力学规划

**Abstract:** We propose Lie group embedded dynamical neural networks (LieEDNN) and the corresponding learning algorithms based on gradient descent and metric projection on smooth manifold, where we treat Lie group as an intrinsic representation for continuous symmetry of manifold geometry. Thereby we achieve learnable and stable dynamics on the underlying manifold for general Lie group, and we are able to utilize the powerful representation capability of Lie group such as SO(3) and SE(3) to solve real world engineering problems in areas such as robotics, graphics, and control.

**摘要：** 我们提出了李群嵌入动态神经网络（LieEDNN）及相应的学习算法，这些算法基于光滑流形上的梯度下降和度量投影。我们将李群视为流形几何连续对称性的内在表示。由此，我们实现了通用李群在底层流形上的可学习且稳定的动力学，并能够利用 SO(3) 和 SE(3) 等李群强大的表示能力，解决机器人、图形学和控制等领域的实际工程问题。

Two core challenges are: (i) General Lie groups are incompatible with addition arithmetic, which is necessary for neural network interactions. (ii) The dynamics evolve in the nonlinear representation space of special algebra rather than the normal Euclidean space, which violates the paradigm of common neural ODEs.

两个核心挑战是：(i) 通用李群与神经网络交互所必需的加法运算不兼容。(ii) 动力学演化在特殊代数的非线性表示空间中，而非普通的欧几里得空间，这违背了常见神经常微分方程（Neural ODEs）的范式。

To address these two challenges, we firstly introduce adjoint Lie group action on the Lie algebra, which induces a linear mapping and transfer to the block-wise structure of weight matrices, such that addition could operate on the Lie algebra as a vector space. Then we parameterize the Lie algebra and the adjoint action as linear transformation so that the architecture is aligned with neural network perceptrons.

为了解决这两个挑战，我们首先在李代数上引入了伴随李群作用，它诱导出一个线性映射并将其转换为权重矩阵的分块结构，从而使加法能够像在向量空间中一样在李代数上进行运算。随后，我们将李代数和伴随作用参数化为线性变换，使架构与神经网络感知机保持一致。

Explicitly, this embedding appears as block-wise manifold constraints on weights, and we develop algorithms to learn the equilibrium with stability guarantees of the temporal neural network dynamics. Experiments are implemented on a specific Lie group SE(3), with the application scenario of telescopic manipulators.

具体而言，这种嵌入表现为权重上的分块流形约束，我们开发了相关算法来学习具有时间神经网络动力学稳定性保证的平衡点。实验在特定的李群 SE(3) 上进行，并以伸缩机械臂作为应用场景。
