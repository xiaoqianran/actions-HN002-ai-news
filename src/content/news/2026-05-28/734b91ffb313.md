# Geometry-Aware Representation Denoising for Robust Multi-view 3D Reconstruction
# 面向鲁棒多视图三维重建的几何感知表征去噪

**Abstract:** Multi-view 3D reconstruction has achieved remarkable progress with the advent of feed-forward 3D reconstruction models. However, these models are typically trained and evaluated under ideal, degradation-free imaging conditions, whereas real-world observations often contain degradations that differ significantly from such settings. Improving robustness for multi-view 3D reconstruction under degraded conditions therefore remains an important challenge.

**摘要：** 随着前馈三维重建模型的出现，多视图三维重建领域取得了显著进展。然而，这些模型通常是在理想且无退化的成像条件下进行训练和评估的，而现实世界中的观测数据往往包含与此类设置显著不同的退化现象。因此，在退化条件下提高多视图三维重建的鲁棒性仍然是一个重要的挑战。

We present Geometry-Aware Representation Denoising (GARD), a novel framework that performs diffusion-based multi-view restoration directly in the feature space of a feed-forward 3D reconstruction model. This design exploits the geometry-aware feature representations of the 3D reconstructor to effectively recover accurate scene geometry.

我们提出了几何感知表征去噪（GARD），这是一种直接在前馈三维重建模型的特征空间中执行基于扩散的多视图恢复的新型框架。该设计利用了三维重建器中具有几何感知能力的特征表征，从而有效地恢复出精确的场景几何结构。

Furthermore, by employing an additional RGB image decoder, the refined representations can also be used to restore high-quality RGB images, thereby enabling the simultaneous recovery of 3D scene geometry and high-quality imagery. Comprehensive experiments on the Depth Anything 3 (DA3) benchmark demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed GARD framework.

此外，通过引入额外的 RGB 图像解码器，优化后的表征还可用于恢复高质量的 RGB 图像，从而实现三维场景几何结构与高质量图像的同时恢复。在 Depth Anything 3 (DA3) 基准测试上的全面实验证明了所提出的 GARD 框架的有效性。
