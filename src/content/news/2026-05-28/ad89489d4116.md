# LongAV-Compass: Towards Unified Evaluation of Minute-Scale Audio-Visual Generation Across T2AV, I2AV, and V2AV

**LongAV-Compass：面向 T2AV、I2AV 和 V2AV 分钟级音视频生成的统一评估**

***

Audio-visual generation is rapidly advancing from short clips to minute-long content, while existing evaluation protocols remain largely confined to short-form settings. Existing benchmarks primarily focus on 5--10 second text-conditioned generation and rarely support unified evaluation across text, image, and video conditioning modalities. Moreover, they provide limited insight into how identity consistency, narrative coherence, and audio-visual alignment degrade over extended temporal horizons.

音视频生成技术正迅速从短片段向分钟级内容演进，然而现有的评估协议大多仍局限于短视频场景。现有的基准测试主要集中在 5 到 10 秒的文本条件生成上，很少支持跨文本、图像和视频条件模态的统一评估。此外，它们对于身份一致性、叙事连贯性和音视频对齐在长时间跨度下如何衰减的洞察也非常有限。

To bridge this gap, we introduce LongAV-Compass, a systematic benchmark for minute-long audio-visual generation. LongAV-Compass contains 284 curated test cases spanning text-to-audio-video (T2AV), image-to-audio-video (I2AV), and video-to-audio-video (V2AV), organized by application scenario and generation complexity.

为了弥补这一差距，我们推出了 LongAV-Compass，这是一个针对分钟级音视频生成的系统性基准测试。LongAV-Compass 包含 284 个精心策划的测试案例，涵盖了文本生成音视频 (T2AV)、图像生成音视频 (I2AV) 和视频生成音视频 (V2AV)，并按应用场景和生成复杂度进行了组织。

The benchmark combines taxonomy-guided benchmark construction with a unified evaluation framework that integrates MLLM-assisted assessment with complementary perceptual and multimodal metrics, including DINO-v2, ArcFace, CLIP, and ImageBind. The framework evaluates more than 20 fine-grained dimensions covering within-segment quality, cross-segment consistency, global narrative coherence, semantic alignment, and audio-visual synchronization.

该基准测试将分类引导的构建方法与统一的评估框架相结合，集成了多模态大模型 (MLLM) 辅助评估以及互补的感知和多模态指标，包括 DINO-v2、ArcFace、CLIP 和 ImageBind。该框架评估了超过 20 个细粒度维度，涵盖了片段内质量、跨片段一致性、全局叙事连贯性、语义对齐以及音视频同步性。

Through experiments on 11 representative models together with human-alignment validation, LongAV-Compass provides a diagnostic testbed for analyzing the limitations of current systems in sustaining coherent, semantically aligned, and temporally consistent minute-scale audio-visual generation across diverse input modalities.

通过对 11 个代表性模型进行实验并结合人类对齐验证，LongAV-Compass 提供了一个诊断测试平台，用于分析当前系统在跨多种输入模态下，维持连贯、语义对齐且时间一致的分钟级音视频生成方面的局限性。
