# All the news about Ferrari’s polarizing Luce EV
# 关于法拉利备受争议的 Luce 电动汽车的所有新闻

Ferrari fans don’t like the design of the new Luce EV, an electric four-door sedan that just doesn’t look like the Ferraris of old. It was designed with help from Jony Ive’s LoveFrom, but what worked for Ive at Apple isn’t working for Ferrari. The Luce’s launch immediately preceded a stock drop that even an appearance by the Pope hasn’t been able to turn around so far.
法拉利的粉丝们并不喜欢新款 Luce 电动汽车的设计。这是一款四门电动轿车，看起来完全不像传统的法拉利。该车在乔尼·艾维（Jony Ive）的 LoveFrom 设计工作室协助下完成，但艾维在苹果公司行之有效的设计理念，在法拉利身上却似乎“水土不服”。Luce 发布后，法拉利股价随即下跌，即便教皇亲自出面站台，也未能扭转颓势。

***

### Lamborghini CEO talks EVs vs. hybrids after the Ferrari Luce launch.
### 法拉利 Luce 发布后，兰博基尼 CEO 谈论电动汽车与混合动力汽车。

Stephan Winkelmann didn’t comment directly on Ferrari’s EV, but told CNBC that his company’s decision to cancel its EV plans in favor of hybrids was “the right way to go.” “…we saw that the acceptance curve [of EVs] for our type of customers is not increasing, and that therefore we decided to move away from a full-electric car into a plug-in hybrid.”
斯蒂芬·温克尔曼（Stephan Winkelmann）没有直接评论法拉利的电动汽车，但他告诉 CNBC，公司决定取消电动汽车计划转而支持混合动力汽车是“正确的方向”。他表示：“我们发现，我们的客户群体对电动汽车的接受曲线并没有上升，因此我们决定放弃纯电动汽车，转向插电式混合动力汽车。”

***

### This Ferrari should have been a Volkswagen
### 这辆法拉利本该是大众汽车

It may be the NBA playoffs but right now Ferrari is getting dunked on more than anyone. Whether they own a Ferrari or just have a poster of a Testarossa on their wall, fans in the Ferrari-verse are frothy about the Luce, the company’s first all-electric vehicle. According to one highly rated Verge commenter: “looks like a Polestar had a one-night stand with a Prius.” Whatever. The competition should steal this design.
现在虽然是 NBA 季后赛期间，但法拉利受到的“扣篮”（指被猛烈抨击）比谁都多。无论他们是法拉利车主，还是墙上贴着 Testarossa 海报的粉丝，法拉利圈内人对公司首款纯电动汽车 Luce 都感到愤怒。正如一位高赞 Verge 评论员所言：“看起来就像极星（Polestar）和普锐斯（Prius）的一夜情产物。”随它去吧，竞争对手们倒是应该把这个设计“偷”走。

### Luce x Leo.
### Luce 与教皇利奥十四世。

The Vatican and Ferrari go way back, so a little cross promotion of the all-electric Luce is to be expected. But not even divine intervention will pacify Ferrari fans eager for a return of sharp, aggressive lines. Still, I think we can all agree that it looks better than the all-electric G-Class popemobile which also cost half a million dollars.
梵蒂冈与法拉利渊源颇深，因此纯电动 Luce 的跨界宣传在预料之中。但即便是神圣的干预也无法安抚那些渴望法拉利回归犀利、激进线条的粉丝们。不过，我想我们都能达成共识：它看起来比那辆同样价值五十万美元的纯电动 G 级教皇座驾要顺眼得多。

### ‘If I were to say what I think, I would be hurting Ferrari.’
### “如果我说出我的真实想法，那是在伤害法拉利。”

That was Luca Cordero di Montezemolo, former Ferrari president, commenting on the recent reveal of the polarizing electric Luce. He continued (translated from Italian): “There is a risk of destroying a legend. I am very sorry. I hope… I hope to… I hope they take the prancing horse off that car, at least… This is definitely a car that, at least, the Chinese won’t copy.”
这是法拉利前总裁卢卡·科尔德罗·迪·蒙特泽莫罗（Luca Cordero di Montezemolo）对近期发布的、备受争议的电动 Luce 的评价。他继续说道（从意大利语翻译）： “这有摧毁一个传奇的风险。我感到非常遗憾。我希望……我希望……我希望他们至少把那辆车上的跃马标志摘下来……这绝对是一辆连中国人都不会去山寨的车。”

### Ferrari’s stock plummets after disappointing Luce unveil.
### 法拉利在令人失望的 Luce 发布会后股价暴跌。

The automaker’s RACE stock is down over 7 percent, while its US-listed shares are down 4 percent, according to CNBC. It would seem Ferrari fans are not the only ones disappointed by the Jony Ive-designed Luce.
据 CNBC 报道，该汽车制造商的 RACE 股票下跌超过 7%，其在美国上市的股票下跌了 4%。看来，对乔尼·艾维设计的 Luce 感到失望的不仅仅是法拉利的粉丝。

### Jony Ive’s Ferrari looks nothing like a Ferrari
### 乔尼·艾维设计的法拉利看起来一点也不像法拉利

Ferrari makes some of the fastest cars on the planet, so it’s anyone’s guess how the Italian automaker ended up being so incredibly late to the EV party. Long after most automakers have drastically scaled back their EV ambitions, canceled battery-powered models, or curtailed factory plans, Ferrari emerges from the shadows with a real weird one: the all-electric Luce.
法拉利制造着地球上最快的汽车，所以没人能猜到这家意大利车企为何在电动汽车领域起步如此之晚。在大多数汽车制造商大幅缩减电动汽车雄心、取消电池动力车型或削减工厂计划很久之后，法拉利才姗姗来迟，带来了一款非常奇怪的产品：纯电动 Luce。
