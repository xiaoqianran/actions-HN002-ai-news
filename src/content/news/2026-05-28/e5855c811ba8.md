# Last.fm is now independent
# Last.fm 现已独立

Today, Last.fm begins a new chapter as an independent company. Ownership has changed, but the product you use every day has not.

今天，Last.fm 作为一家独立公司开启了新的篇章。虽然所有权发生了变更，但您每天使用的产品并未改变。

Your account, your listening history, and your data remain exactly where they are. The team building Last.fm is the same. The service continues as normal.

您的账户、收听历史记录以及数据都将保持原样。负责构建 Last.fm 的团队依然是原班人马。服务也将照常运行。

### What stays the same
### 哪些内容保持不变

* Your account and scrobbles
* 您的账户和 Scrobble 记录
* Your data and privacy settings
* 您的数据和隐私设置
* Your Pro subscription and billing (if you're a member)
* 您的 Pro 订阅和账单（如果您是会员）
* The people behind the product
* 负责该产品的团队成员

We'll share more about what comes next in the weeks ahead. For now, everything works exactly as it did yesterday.

我们将在未来几周内分享更多关于后续计划的信息。目前，一切运作如常，与昨天没有任何区别。

Same service. Same team. New chapter. Thanks for being part of it.

同样的服务，同样的团队，崭新的篇章。感谢您的一路相伴。

*Team Last.fm*
*Last.fm 团队*

### What’s happening?
### 发生了什么？

Last.fm is now operating as an independent company following a change in ownership.

在所有权变更后，Last.fm 现已作为一家独立公司运营。

### Is Last.fm shutting down?
### Last.fm 会关闭吗？

No. The service continues as normal.

不会。服务将照常运行。

### Is Last.fm being acquired by another company?
### Last.fm 是否被其他公司收购了？

No. Last.fm is now operating independently.

没有。Last.fm 现已独立运营。

### Is the team changing?
### 团队会有变动吗？

The team building and operating Last.fm remains in place.

负责构建和运营 Last.fm 的团队保持不变。

### What does independence mean for users?
### 独立对用户意味着什么？

It means we can focus fully on building listening insights and community features for music fans. You’ll see continued, steady improvements over time.

这意味着我们可以全身心投入到为音乐爱好者构建收听洞察和社区功能中。随着时间的推移，您将看到持续且稳定的改进。

### Is my account or listening history affected?
### 我的账户或收听历史会受到影响吗？

No. Your account, scrobbles, and listening history remain unchanged.

不会。您的账户、Scrobble 记录和收听历史保持不变。
