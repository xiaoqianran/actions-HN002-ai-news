# Generic Interpretation Approach for Transformer Models Incorporating Heterogenous Attention Structures
# 针对包含异构注意力结构的 Transformer 模型的通用解释方法

**Abstract:** Transformer has significantly propelled the development of artificial intelligence, and certainly the development of agents as well. We categorize attention structures of Transformer into two types based on the source of the input information: homogenous and heterogenous attention structures. Heterogenous attention structures, with co-attention as a typical example, process information from different sources. 

**摘要：** Transformer 极大地推动了人工智能的发展，当然也促进了智能体（Agents）的发展。根据输入信息的来源，我们将 Transformer 的注意力结构分为两类：同构注意力结构和异构注意力结构。异构注意力结构（以协同注意力/co-attention 为典型示例）处理来自不同来源的信息。

Heterogenous attention structure is the foundation for Transformer models to achieve more complex functions and integrate more modal information. Whether for research purposes or policy requirements, the interpretation of Transformer models with heterogenous attention structures is an important task. The fusion of information from different sources brings new challenges. 

异构注意力结构是 Transformer 模型实现更复杂功能并整合更多模态信息的基础。无论是出于研究目的还是政策要求，对具有异构注意力结构的 Transformer 模型进行解释都是一项重要任务。来自不同来源的信息融合带来了新的挑战。

Our work mainly includes two parts: method and experimentation. In terms of method, we propose an interpretation method for Transformer models with heterogenous attention structures. In terms of experimentation, based on our experimental analysis paradigm, we interpret the operating mechanisms of representative models, conduct semantic interpretation and logical interpretation.

我们的工作主要包括两个部分：方法与实验。在方法方面，我们提出了一种针对具有异构注意力结构的 Transformer 模型的解释方法。在实验方面，基于我们的实验分析范式，我们对代表性模型的运行机制进行了阐释，并进行了语义解释和逻辑解释。
