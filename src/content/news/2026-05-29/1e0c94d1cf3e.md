# From Affect to Complex Behavior: Advancing Multimodal Human-Centered AI at the 10th ABAW Workshop & Competition
# 从情感分析到复杂行为：第十届 ABAW 研讨会与竞赛推动多模态以人为本的 AI 发展

The 10th Affective & Behavior Analysis in-the-Wild (ABAW) Workshop and Competition, held at CVPR 2026, continues to advance research on modelling, analysis, understanding of human affect and behavior in real-world, unconstrained environments.
在 CVPR 2026 举办的第十届“野外环境下的情感与行为分析”（ABAW）研讨会与竞赛，持续推动着在真实、非受限环境下对人类情感与行为进行建模、分析和理解的研究。

The workshop maintains its dual structure, comprising both a competition and a paper track. The ABAW Competition introduces a diverse set of challenges targeting key aspects of affective and behavioral understanding, including continuous affect (valence-arousal) estimation, discrete affect (expression and action unit) recognition, as well as more complex behavior analysis tasks, such as emotional mimicry intensity estimation, ambivalence/hesitancy recognition and fine-grained violence detection.
本次研讨会保持了竞赛与论文发表的双轨结构。ABAW 竞赛引入了一系列针对情感与行为理解关键方面的挑战，包括连续情感（效价-唤醒度）估计、离散情感（表情与动作单元）识别，以及更复杂的行为分析任务，例如情感模仿强度估计、矛盾/犹豫心理识别以及细粒度暴力检测。

These challenges are built upon large-scale in-the-wild datasets, providing comprehensive benchmarks for state-of-the-art approaches. In parallel, the paper track presents a wide range of contributions spanning pose, motion & behavior estimation, affect modelling & multimodal learning, benchmarks, datasets & evaluation protocols, fairness, robustness & deployment.
这些挑战基于大规模的野外数据集构建，为最先进的技术方案提供了全面的基准测试。与此同时，论文环节展示了广泛的研究贡献，涵盖了姿态、运动与行为估计，情感建模与多模态学习，基准测试、数据集与评估协议，以及公平性、鲁棒性与部署等领域。

Overall, the 10th ABAW Workshop and Competition continues to serve as a key platform for benchmarking, collaboration and innovation, shaping the development of next-generation multimodal, human-centered AI systems.
总而言之，第十届 ABAW 研讨会与竞赛继续作为基准测试、协作与创新的关键平台，塑造着下一代多模态、以人为本的 AI 系统的发展。
