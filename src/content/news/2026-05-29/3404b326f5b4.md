# 回顾 2026 年 Google I/O 的 12 个重要时刻

2026 年 5 月 28 日

重温今年舞台上的一些高光时刻，包括我们最新模型的发布、搜索功能的更新等等。

Our biggest, boldest new developments took center stage at Google I/O 2026. We announced technical breakthroughs, like Gemini Omni’s ability to create anything from any input, starting with video. And we shared product updates to help you day-to-day, like the brand new, intelligent Search box that will let you search across modalities, using text, images, files, videos or Chrome tabs as inputs. (And with plenty of other big I/O announcements, there’s a lot more where that came from!)
在 Google I/O 2026 大会上，我们最宏大、最前沿的新进展成为了焦点。我们宣布了多项技术突破，例如 Gemini Omni 能够通过任何输入（以视频为起点）创建任何内容的能力。我们还分享了旨在改善您日常生活的各种产品更新，例如全新的智能搜索框，它允许您跨模态进行搜索，支持使用文本、图像、文件、视频或 Chrome 标签页作为输入。（此外，还有许多其他重大的 I/O 发布，精彩内容远不止于此！）

In case you missed it, here are some of our most exciting I/O keynote reveals this year.
如果您错过了大会，以下是我们今年 I/O 主题演讲中最令人兴奋的一些发布内容。

### 1. Gemini Omni
### 1. Gemini Omni

Gemini Omni is our new model that can create anything from any input — starting with video. With Omni, you can combine images, audio, video and text as input and generate high-quality videos grounded in Gemini's real-world knowledge. You can also easily edit your videos through conversation. First, we’re launching the first model in the Omni family: Gemini Omni Flash. Gemini Omni Flash is rolling out to all Google AI Plus, Pro and Ultra subscribers globally through the Gemini app and Google Flow. It’s also rolling out at no cost to users on YouTube Shorts and YouTube Create App.
Gemini Omni 是我们的新模型，它能够通过任何输入创建任何内容——并以视频作为起点。借助 Omni，您可以结合图像、音频、视频和文本作为输入，并生成基于 Gemini 现实世界知识的高质量视频。您还可以通过对话轻松编辑视频。首先，我们推出了 Omni 系列的首个模型：Gemini Omni Flash。Gemini Omni Flash 正通过 Gemini 应用和 Google Flow 向全球所有 Google AI Plus、Pro 和 Ultra 订阅用户推出。它也正免费向 YouTube Shorts 和 YouTube Create 应用的用户开放。

### 2. Gemini 3.5 Flash
### 2. Gemini 3.5 Flash

Our new Gemini 3.5 family of models combines frontier intelligence with action. We’re kicking off the series by releasing Gemini 3.5 Flash, which delivers frontier performance for agents and coding, excelling at complex long-horizon tasks that deliver real-world utility. Gemini 3.5 Flash is generally available via Google Antigravity, the Gemini API in Google AI Studio and Android Studio, Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform and Gemini Enterprise. It’s also available for everyone in AI Mode in Search and now rolling out to everyone globally in the Gemini app. We’re also hard at work on Gemini 3.5 Pro. It’s already being used internally, and we look forward to rolling it out next month.
我们全新的 Gemini 3.5 模型系列将前沿智能与行动力相结合。我们以发布 Gemini 3.5 Flash 开启了这一系列，它为智能体（Agents）和编程提供了前沿性能，擅长处理具有实际应用价值的复杂长周期任务。Gemini 3.5 Flash 现已通过 Google Antigravity、Google AI Studio 和 Android Studio 中的 Gemini API、Gemini 企业级智能体平台以及 Gemini Enterprise 全面可用。它也已在搜索的 AI 模式中向所有人开放，并正向全球 Gemini 应用用户推出。我们也在全力开发 Gemini 3.5 Pro。它目前已在内部使用，我们期待下个月将其推出。

### 3. Information agents in Search
### 3. 搜索中的信息智能体 (Information agents)

We’re entering the era of Search agents, where you can easily create, customize and manage multiple AI agents for your many tasks, right in Search. We’re starting with information agents, which operate in the background, 24/7, to intelligently reason across the web, like blogs, news sites and social posts (plus our freshest data, such as real-time info on finance, shopping and sports). Information agents will help you stay updated on whatever matters most to you, sending a comprehensive update with exactly what you need at exactly the right moment, along with helpful links to explore further on the web. Information agents are rolling out this summer, starting first with Google AI Pro and Ultra subscribers. Simply add “keep me updated” to your search to create an information agent, and view your active agents via the side panel in AI Mode in Search.
我们正在进入搜索智能体时代，您可以直接在搜索中轻松创建、自定义和管理多个 AI 智能体来处理各种任务。我们首先推出的是信息智能体，它们在后台 24/7 全天候运行，能够智能地跨网络进行推理，涵盖博客、新闻网站和社交帖子（以及我们最新的数据，如金融、购物和体育的实时信息）。信息智能体将帮助您随时掌握最关心的内容，在最合适的时机发送包含您所需信息的全面更新，并附带可供进一步探索的网页链接。信息智能体将于今年夏天推出，首先面向 Google AI Pro 和 Ultra 订阅用户。只需在搜索中添加“keep me updated”（保持更新），即可创建信息智能体，并通过搜索中 AI 模式的侧边栏查看您的活跃智能体。

### 4. Google Antigravity-powered experiences in Search
### 4. 搜索中由 Google Antigravity 驱动的体验

We’re bringing Antigravity and the agentic coding capabilities of Gemini 3.5 Flash right into Search, so Search can build you the ideal format exactly for your question, completely custom, on the fly. You can get dynamic layouts, interactive visuals and entire experiences, all created just for you. These generative UI capabilities will be available for everyone in Search this summer, free of charge.
我们将 Antigravity 和 Gemini 3.5 Flash 的智能体编程能力直接引入搜索，使搜索能够根据您的问题即时构建完全定制的理想格式。您可以获得动态布局、交互式视觉效果和完整的体验，所有这些都是为您量身定制的。这些生成式 UI 功能将于今年夏天在搜索中免费向所有人开放。

Some projects aren’t one-off questions — they're ongoing tasks. Also with Antigravity, Search will also code entire custom experiences, like tools, dashboards or trackers, just for you. It’s like building your own mini apps with Search. They’re especially awesome for those long-running tasks where you want to keep coming back, like planning a wedding or managing your home move. You’ll be able to build custom experiences with Antigravity, right in Search in the coming months, starting first for Google AI Pro and Ultra subscribers in the U.S.
有些项目不是一次性的问题，而是持续性的任务。同样借助 Antigravity，搜索还能为您编写完整的定制体验，例如工具、仪表板或追踪器。这就像是用搜索构建您自己的小程序。对于那些您需要反复跟进的长期任务（如筹备婚礼或搬家），它们特别有用。在接下来的几个月里，您将能够直接在搜索中使用 Antigravity 构建定制体验，首先面向美国的 Google AI Pro 和 Ultra 订阅用户。

### 5. Daily Brief
### 5. 每日简报 (Daily Brief)

Daily Brief in the Gemini app is a new agent that gives you a personalized morning brief and organizes exactly what you need to know to start your day. This personalized digest is designed to be your first stop every morning. Once you opt in, Gemini works across your connected apps in the background. It gathers urgent updates from your Gmail inbox, tracks upcoming events from your Calendar and compiles relevant follow-up details into a skimmable briefing. It goes far beyond a simple summary. Daily Brief actively organizes and prioritizes based on your specific goals, even suggesting immediate next steps. You can easily steer it by giving responses a quick thumbs up or down over time. Daily Brief is rolling out to all Google AI subscribers (18+) in the Gemini app, starting in the U.S. In order to use Daily Brief, Google AI subscribers must have chosen to connect their Google apps.
Gemini 应用中的“每日简报”是一个新的智能体，它为您提供个性化的早间简报，并整理出您开启新一天所需了解的所有信息。这份个性化摘要旨在成为您每天早上的第一站。一旦您选择加入，Gemini 就会在后台跨您的关联应用工作。它会从您的 Gmail 收件箱收集紧急更新，追踪日历中即将发生的事件，并将相关的后续细节汇编成一份易于浏览的简报。它远不止是一个简单的摘要。每日简报会根据您的具体目标主动进行组织和优先级排序，甚至建议即时的后续步骤。您可以通过随时间的推移对回复进行快速点赞或点踩来轻松引导它。每日简报正向美国所有 Google AI 订阅用户（18 岁以上）的 Gemini 应用中推出。要使用每日简报，Google AI 订阅用户必须选择关联其 Google 应用。

### 6. Universal Cart
### 6. 通用购物车 (Universal Cart)

Our new Universal Cart is a truly intelligent shopping cart and your new hub for shopping on Google. It works across merchants and across services, so you can add things to your cart while you’re browsing Search, chatting with Gemini, watching YouTube or even reading your Gmail. The moment you add a product, your cart goes to work for you in the background. It finds deals and price drops, gives you insights on price history and alerts you when something comes back in stock. Universal Cart is rolling out across Search and the Gemini app in the U.S. this summer, with YouTube and Gmail to follow.
我们全新的“通用购物车”是一个真正智能的购物车，也是您在 Google 上购物的新中心。它跨商家、跨服务运行，因此您可以在浏览搜索、与 Gemini 聊天、观看 YouTube 甚至阅读 Gmail 时将商品添加到购物车。当您添加商品的那一刻，您的购物车就会在后台为您工作。它会寻找优惠和降价信息，为您提供价格历史洞察，并在商品补货时提醒您。通用购物车将于今年夏天在美国的搜索和 Gemini 应用中推出，随后将扩展至 YouTube 和 Gmail。
