### Is it common to crave for a Super Accessible ASI without giving up Human Creativity?
### 是否渴望拥有一个“超级易用的人工超级智能（ASI）”，同时又不放弃人类创造力，这是一种普遍现象吗？

Am I one of the rare developers who: Secretly wants AI to finally succeed doing all the things AI CEOs continuously promise AI will be able to do, but fail to deliver - so that I can implement all those ten thousand ideas I thought I would never have enough time to implement; or will finally be able implement my unique Open Source Everything App that will do everything from note taking, to time management, to publish blogs on my personal websites and/or social media, to email management, to password management ... everything in a deterministic way (not like the AI agent way) - all while everything will be end to end encrypted, secure and private!

我是不是那种罕见的开发者：内心深处渴望 AI 最终能实现那些 AI 公司 CEO 们不断承诺却始终无法兑现的功能——这样我就能实现那成千上万个我曾以为永远没时间去做的点子；或者终于能实现我那独特的“开源万能应用”，它能处理从笔记、时间管理、发布博客到个人网站/社交媒体、邮件管理、密码管理等一切事务……而且是以一种确定性的方式（而不是像 AI 智能体那样），同时确保所有数据端到端加密、安全且私密！

At the same time, will still write code, poetry and articles by hand even if such a super accessible ASI (Artificial Super Intelligence) exists, even if it's 100% free with unlimited tokens, and even if it can be run locally on cheap hardware? Or is this seemingly contradictory craving very common among developers? Just wanted to know!

与此同时，即便这种超级易用的 ASI（人工超级智能）真的存在，即便它是 100% 免费且拥有无限 Token，甚至可以在廉价硬件上本地运行，我依然会坚持亲手编写代码、创作诗歌和文章。还是说，这种看似矛盾的渴望在开发者群体中其实非常普遍？我只是想了解一下！
