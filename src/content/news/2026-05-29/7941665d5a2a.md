# Representation-Conditioned Diffusion Models for Guided Training Data Generation
# 基于表征条件扩散模型的引导式训练数据生成

**Abstract:** Data availability remains a critical bottleneck in many deep learning applications. Large-scale datasets are often expensive to collect, curate and annotate, which can limit the scalability and applicability of supervised learning methods. 

**摘要：** 数据可用性仍然是许多深度学习应用中的关键瓶颈。大规模数据集的收集、整理和标注往往成本高昂，这限制了监督学习方法的可扩展性和适用性。

In this work, we evaluate the classification performance of models trained on synthetic image datasets produced by generative deep learning. In particular, we use latent diffusion models conditioned on learned representations from DINOv2, DINOv3, and CLIP. 

在这项工作中，我们评估了在由生成式深度学习产生的合成图像数据集上训练的模型的分类性能。具体而言，我们使用了以 DINOv2、DINOv3 和 CLIP 的学习表征为条件的潜在扩散模型（Latent Diffusion Models）。

Our results demonstrates that this representation-conditioned formulation significantly outperforms class-conditioned generation by a large margin (+10.76 p.p. top-1 accuracy on ImageNet100), by improving sample quality and mode coverage. 

研究结果表明，这种基于表征条件的生成方案通过提高样本质量和模式覆盖率，显著优于传统的类别条件生成（在 ImageNet100 上 top-1 准确率提升了 10.76 个百分点）。

Furthermore, by scaling the size of the synthetic dataset, we are able to outperform a classifier trained on the real data (+2.0 p.p top-1 accuracy). We also demonstrate how generated images can be used for augmentation purposes, outperforming classical augmentation methods, and how the conditioning space can be used for sample filtering to further improve training value. 

此外，通过扩大合成数据集的规模，我们能够超越在真实数据上训练的分类器（top-1 准确率提升 2.0 个百分点）。我们还展示了如何将生成的图像用于数据增强，其效果优于传统的增强方法，并探讨了如何利用条件空间进行样本过滤，以进一步提升训练价值。

Collectively, these findings highlight that representation-conditioned diffusion models provide a promising approach for augmenting, complementing, or potentially replacing real-world datasets in large-scale visual learning tasks.

总而言之，这些发现强调了基于表征条件的扩散模型为大规模视觉学习任务中的数据增强、补充，甚至潜在地替代真实世界数据集提供了一种极具前景的方法。
