# Diffusion-Based Ukrainian Handwritten Text Generation with Cross-Domain Style Transfer
# 基于扩散模型的乌克兰语手写文本生成与跨域风格迁移

**Abstract:** Handwritten text generation (HTG) conditioned on writer style has been widely studied for Latin scripts, but remains underexplored for low-resource and non-Latin writing systems, leaving open how well existing models generalise beyond the Latin domain. Cyrillic, particularly Ukrainian, lacks both large-scale writer-labeled datasets and empirical evidence of such generalisation.

**摘要：** 基于书写者风格的手写文本生成（HTG）在拉丁字母脚本中已得到广泛研究，但在低资源和非拉丁书写系统中仍未得到充分探索，这使得现有模型在拉丁语系之外的泛化能力仍是一个未知数。西里尔字母，特别是乌克兰语，既缺乏大规模的带书写者标签的数据集，也缺乏此类泛化能力的实证研究。

To address this gap, we construct a Ukrainian handwritten word dataset of 126,177 images from 308 writers using connected-component segmentation, quality filtering, and targeted oversampling of underrepresented Ukrainian characters. We retrain DiffusionPen, a MobileNetV2 triplet-loss style encoder with a CANINE-conditioned latent diffusion U-Net, on this dataset without architectural modification, testing direct transfer from Latin to Cyrillic.

为了填补这一空白，我们构建了一个包含 126,177 张乌克兰语手写单词图像的数据集，这些图像来自 308 位书写者。我们采用了连通分量分割、质量过滤以及对乌克兰语稀有字符进行针对性过采样的方法。我们在不修改架构的情况下，利用该数据集重新训练了 DiffusionPen（一种结合了 MobileNetV2 三元组损失风格编码器和 CANINE 条件潜空间扩散 U-Net 的模型），以测试从拉丁语到西里尔语的直接迁移效果。

We evaluate cross-domain style transfer in three settings: cross-lingual transfer from IAM English samples, zero-shot transfer to an early 20th-century Ukrainian manuscript, and few-shot imitation of contemporary writers. The model produces legible, style-consistent word images, indicating that few-shot latent diffusion models generalize beyond the Latin-script domain.

我们在三种场景下评估了跨域风格迁移：从 IAM 英语样本进行的跨语言迁移、针对 20 世纪早期乌克兰手稿的零样本迁移，以及对当代书写者的少样本模仿。该模型生成的单词图像清晰且风格一致，表明少样本潜空间扩散模型能够泛化到拉丁脚本领域之外。

We release the dataset, trained models, and evaluation protocol as a reproducible benchmark for writer-aware Cyrillic HTG, providing a foundation for extending stylized HTG to other underrepresented writing systems.

我们发布了数据集、训练好的模型以及评估协议，作为西里尔语手写文本生成（HTG）的可复现基准，为将风格化 HTG 扩展到其他代表性不足的书写系统奠定了基础。
