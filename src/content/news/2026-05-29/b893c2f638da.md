# Metric-Aware PCA as a Linear Instance of Geometric Deep Learning
# 度量感知主成分分析（MAPCA）：几何深度学习的一个线性实例

Geometric deep learning organises neural architectures around the symmetries of their data domain, with the choice of symmetry group serving as a geometric prior that determines what representations can be learned.
几何深度学习围绕数据域的对称性来组织神经网络架构，其中对称群的选择作为一种几何先验，决定了可以学习到什么样的表征。

Metric-Aware Principal Component Analysis (MAPCA) parameterises principal component analysis by a positive-definite metric matrix, with a canonical subfamily interpolating between standard PCA and output whitening and a diagonal-metric point recovering Invariant PCA (IPCA).
度量感知主成分分析（MAPCA）通过正定度量矩阵对主成分分析进行参数化，其规范子族可在标准 PCA 和输出白化之间进行插值，而对角度量点则可恢复为不变主成分分析（IPCA）。

This paper positions MAPCA within the geometric deep learning framework. The metric is read as the geometric prior; the orthogonal group preserving it is the symmetry group it induces; MAPCA solutions are equivariant under this group with the resulting spectrum invariant; and MAPCA's defining constraint is the linear analogue of the Schur-type weight constraints used in equivariant networks.
本文将 MAPCA 置于几何深度学习框架内。度量被解读为几何先验；保持该度量的正交群即为其诱导的对称群；MAPCA 的解在该群下是等变的，且所得谱是不变的；MAPCA 的定义约束则是等变网络中所使用的 Schur 型权重约束的线性模拟。

Across six axes - domain, symmetry group, equivariance, invariance, architectural primitive, and geometric prior - we construct a precise dictionary between MAPCA and geometric deep learning.
通过域、对称群、等变性、不变性、架构原语和几何先验这六个维度，我们构建了 MAPCA 与几何深度学习之间精确的对应词典。

The technical anchor is a uniqueness theorem characterising IPCA as the unique linear data-derived metric in the MAPCA family that is equivariant under arbitrary diagonal rescaling and projects onto the fixed-point set of the action, equivalent under normalisation to the variance-maximisation criterion in its precise form.
其技术核心是一个唯一性定理，该定理将 IPCA 刻画为 MAPCA 族中唯一一种由数据导出的线性度量，它在任意对角重缩放变换下保持等变，并投影到作用的不动点集上，在归一化后等同于精确形式下的方差最大化准则。

The paper closes with three bridges: kernel PCA as the nonlinear extension, spectral graph methods as MAPCA on graphs, and a deep MAPCA construction extending the positioning into deep equivariant networks.
本文最后提出了三座桥梁：作为非线性扩展的核 PCA、作为图上 MAPCA 的谱图方法，以及将该定位扩展至深度等变网络的深度 MAPCA 结构。
