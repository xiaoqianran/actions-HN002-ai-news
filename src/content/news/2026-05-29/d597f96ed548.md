# AdaMerge: Salience-Aware Adaptive Token Merging for Training-Free Acceleration of Vision Transformers
# AdaMerge：面向视觉 Transformer 免训练加速的显著性感知自适应 Token 合并

**Abstract:** The quadratic cost of self-attention in Vision Transformers (ViTs) constitutes a fundamental bottleneck for practical deployment, motivating a vibrant line of research on token reduction. Among existing approaches, token merging (ToMe) has emerged as an elegant training-free solution; yet its design rests on an unspoken premise of token equality, which contravenes the well-documented non-uniformity of self-attention and leads to information loss in high-salience tokens under aggressive compression.

**摘要：** 视觉 Transformer (ViTs) 中自注意力机制的二次方计算成本构成了其实际部署的基本瓶颈，这推动了关于 Token 缩减研究的蓬勃发展。在现有方法中，Token 合并 (ToMe) 已成为一种优雅的免训练解决方案；然而，其设计基于一个隐含的“Token 等价”前提，这与自注意力机制中众所周知的非均匀性相矛盾，并导致在高压缩率下显著性较高的 Token 出现信息丢失。

We address this limitation with AdaMerge, a token-merging framework based on two complementary mechanisms. First, salience-weighted similarity leverages column-wise feature-affinity centrality as a token-importance proxy and incorporates the resulting salience scores into the bipartite matching score, ensuring that pivotal tokens contribute more strongly to the merged representation.

我们通过 AdaMerge 解决了这一局限性，这是一个基于两种互补机制的 Token 合并框架。首先，显著性加权相似度利用列向特征亲和中心性作为 Token 重要性的代理，并将由此产生的显著性分数纳入二分匹配分数中，确保关键 Token 对合并后的表示做出更强的贡献。

Second, adaptive merging intensity uses pre-computed layer-wise similarity statistics to dynamically modulate the per-layer reduction count in accordance with input-specific redundancy. On ImageNet-1k with ViT-B/16, AdaMerge consistently outperforms ToMe, PiToMe, and DSM across all FLOPs-matched regimes.

其次，自适应合并强度利用预先计算的层级相似度统计数据，根据输入特定的冗余度动态调节每层的缩减数量。在 ImageNet-1k 数据集和 ViT-B/16 模型上，AdaMerge 在所有 FLOPs 匹配的方案中均持续优于 ToMe、PiToMe 和 DSM。

The accuracy gap widens monotonically with compression: at the 13.4G FLOPs operating point, AdaMerge sustains a Top-1 degradation of only -1.06%, compared to -1.45% for PiToMe and -4.62% for DSM. To our knowledge, AdaMerge is the first to combine salience-weighted similarity and adaptive per-layer reduction into a single training-free token merging framework, advancing the accuracy-FLOPs Pareto frontier of ViT acceleration.

精度差距随着压缩率的增加而单调扩大：在 13.4G FLOPs 的运行点，AdaMerge 的 Top-1 精度下降仅为 -1.06%，而 PiToMe 为 -1.45%，DSM 为 -4.62%。据我们所知，AdaMerge 是首个将显著性加权相似度和自适应层级缩减结合到单一免训练 Token 合并框架中的方法，推动了 ViT 加速在精度与 FLOPs 之间的帕累托前沿。
