### Compartment

**Compartment**

Launching today, Compartment is an open-source runtime for internal team software.
Compartment 于今日正式发布，是一个专为内部团队软件打造的开源运行时环境。

Compartment is a self-hosted open-source runtime for AI-built internal apps. It gives teams one place to run and share the apps, scripts, workers, and automations created with AI coding agents, on infrastructure they own.
Compartment 是一个自托管的开源运行时，专门用于运行由 AI 构建的内部应用程序。它为团队提供了一个统一的平台，可以在其自有基础设施上运行并共享由 AI 编程助手生成的应用程序、脚本、工作流和自动化任务。

Use Cursor, Claude, Codex, or any stack your team already works with. Compartment turns the code into team-ready software with isolation, RBAC, SSO, and audit logs built in.
你可以使用 Cursor、Claude、Codex 或团队现有的任何技术栈。Compartment 能将这些代码转化为团队可用的软件，并内置了隔离机制、基于角色的访问控制（RBAC）、单点登录（SSO）以及审计日志功能。
