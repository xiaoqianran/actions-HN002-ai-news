# Fine-Tuning Vision-Language Models for Understanding Current Damage and Scoring Priority with Quality Guard Agent
# 使用质量守护代理微调视觉语言模型以识别桥梁损伤并评估修复优先级

**Abstract:** Bridge inspection in Japan requires mandatory visual assessments every five years, yet qualitative damage ratings (levels a-e) assigned by different engineers exhibit significant inter-rater variability -- a critical barrier to consistent infrastructure management. The aging of skilled engineers further threatens inspection capacity.
**摘要：** 日本的桥梁检查要求每五年进行一次强制性目视评估，然而不同工程师给出的定性损伤评级（a-e级）表现出显著的评估者间差异，这是实现基础设施一致性管理的关键障碍。此外，熟练工程师的老龄化进一步威胁到了检查能力。

This paper presents a methodology for automating bridge damage understanding and repair priority scoring using fine-tuned Vision-Language Models (VLMs). We fine-tune LLaVA-1.5-7B with QLoRA on up to 4,000 paired bridge damage images and inspection text records, then evaluate on a fixed test set of 800 images.
本文提出了一种利用微调后的视觉语言模型（VLM）实现桥梁损伤识别和修复优先级评分自动化的方法。我们使用 QLoRA 对 LLaVA-1.5-7B 模型进行了微调，训练数据包含多达 4,000 对桥梁损伤图像及相应的检查文本记录，随后在 800 张图像的固定测试集上进行了评估。

The model outputs natural language descriptions identifying structural members and damage patterns, from which a rule-based scoring engine calculates a five-level repair priority index. A progressive training study (1k/2k/3k/4k samples) reveals that 2k training samples achieve near-optimal validation loss in only 2.9 hours of training; beyond 2k, validation loss improves by no more than 0.2% per doubling of training samples, exhibiting clear diminishing returns.
该模型输出自然语言描述以识别结构构件和损伤模式，并由基于规则的评分引擎据此计算出五级修复优先级指数。通过渐进式训练研究（1k/2k/3k/4k 样本量）发现，仅需 2.9 小时的训练，2,000 个样本即可达到近乎最优的验证损失；超过 2,000 个样本后，训练样本量每翻倍，验证损失的改善不超过 0.2%，表现出明显的边际效应递减。

Furthermore, semantic similarity on the held-out test set peaks at 3k (0.6909) and degrades at 4k (0.6739), indicating that quality-curated mid-scale data outperforms larger but noisier corpora. Inference optimization combining this http URL() and batch processing (batch_size=8) achieves 10.06 seconds per image -- a 70.2% reduction over the unoptimized baseline.
此外，在留出测试集上的语义相似度在 3,000 个样本时达到峰值（0.6909），而在 4,000 个样本时出现下降（0.6739），这表明经过质量筛选的中等规模数据优于更大但噪声更多的数据集。结合 `this http URL()` 和批处理（batch_size=8）的推理优化，使每张图像的处理时间缩短至 10.06 秒，较未优化的基准减少了 70.2%。

Our approach contributes to data governance in bridge inspection, reduces inter-rater variability, and provides AI-assisted triage to augment expert engineers in inspection workflows. Furthermore, we introduce a two-stage Quality Guard using a fine-tuned Swallow-8B SLM to reject low-quality VLM outputs before priority scoring, preventing spurious scores from damaged or unrecognised images.
我们的方法有助于桥梁检查中的数据治理，减少了评估者间的差异，并提供 AI 辅助分诊功能，以增强专家工程师在检查工作流中的能力。此外，我们引入了一个两阶段的“质量守护”（Quality Guard），利用微调后的 Swallow-8B 小型语言模型（SLM）在评分前剔除低质量的 VLM 输出，从而防止因图像损坏或无法识别而产生的虚假评分。
