# Meta is reportedly developing an AI pendant
# 据报道，Meta 正在开发一款人工智能吊坠

Meta is developing an AI-powered pendant that it plans to start testing in the next year, according to a memo viewed by The Information.
据《The Information》看到的一份备忘录显示，Meta 正在开发一款人工智能驱动的吊坠，并计划在明年开始测试。

This device would presumably build on the work of Limitless, an AI device startup that Meta acquired at the end of 2025. The startup made an AI pendant that users could attach to their shirt or wear as a necklace to record their conversations.
该设备大概率将基于 Meta 在 2025 年底收购的人工智能初创公司 Limitless 的技术成果。该公司曾开发过一款人工智能吊坠，用户可以将其别在衬衫上或作为项链佩戴，以记录他们的对话。

At the time, Meta said the acquisition would allow it to “accelerate our work to build AI-enabled wearables.”
当时，Meta 表示此次收购将使其能够“加速我们构建人工智能可穿戴设备的工作”。

Earlier AI wearables have failed to catch on with consumers — perhaps due to privacy concerns and tone-deaf marketing, or perhaps because they just weren’t that useful. But companies like OpenAI aren’t giving up.
早期的 AI 可穿戴设备未能获得消费者的青睐——这可能是由于隐私担忧和脱离实际的市场营销，也可能是因为它们确实不够实用。但像 OpenAI 这样的公司并没有放弃。

The memo also reportedly states that the company is planning to expand its lineup of AI glasses and launch a business subscription called Wearables for Work.
据报道，该备忘录还指出，Meta 计划扩大其 AI 眼镜的产品线，并推出一项名为“工作可穿戴设备”（Wearables for Work）的企业订阅服务。

With all these planned devices, Meta is apparently hoping to reverse the fortunes of its hardware-focused Reality Labs division, which lost $4 billion in the first quarter of this year.
通过这些计划中的设备，Meta 显然希望扭转其专注于硬件的 Reality Labs 部门的颓势，该部门在今年第一季度亏损了 40 亿美元。

TechCrunch has reached out to Meta for comment.
TechCrunch 已联系 Meta 请求置评。
