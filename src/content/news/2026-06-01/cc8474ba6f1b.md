# Best Unlimited Phone Plan: T-Mobile, AT&T, Verizon Compared
# 最佳无限流量套餐：T-Mobile、AT&T 与 Verizon 对比

“Unlimited” is a funny term. Unlimited cell phone plans often come with a long list of footnotes, terms, conditions, and exceptions. Mercifully, all of the big three cell companies—Verizon, AT&T, and T-Mobile—have, by now, ditched throttling on their most expensive plans and include 5G data access in all of their unlimited plans. Yet there are still many differences in the services they offer, including differences between each carrier's various tiers.

“无限”是一个有趣的词。无限流量套餐通常附带一长串脚注、条款、条件和例外情况。值得庆幸的是，目前三大运营商——Verizon、AT&T 和 T-Mobile——都已经取消了其最昂贵套餐的限速政策，并将 5G 数据访问权限包含在所有无限流量套餐中。然而，它们提供的服务之间仍然存在许多差异，包括各运营商不同档次套餐之间的区别。

Cheaper “unlimited” tiers do offer unlimited talk and text. But they're not universally unlimited in that they still have rules on how much data you get before the carriers start throttling your speed. Some supposed unlimited plans may throttle your data at any given time. It's been a fixture of cell service plans for years.

较便宜的“无限”档次确实提供无限通话和短信。但它们并非完全意义上的无限，因为它们仍有关于在运营商开始限速前你可以使用多少流量的规定。一些所谓的无限流量套餐可能会在任何时候限制你的数据速度。这多年来一直是手机服务套餐的常态。

These details can be overwhelming to sort when trying to pick the best unlimited cell phone plan for your needs. Below, I've highlighted what each of the three major carriers offers for “unlimited” individual and family plans to help you figure out which option is best for you and your budget.

在试图为自己的需求选择最佳无限流量套餐时，梳理这些细节可能会让人不知所措。在下文中，我重点介绍了三大运营商为个人和家庭提供的“无限”套餐，以帮助你找出最适合你和你的预算的选择。

T-Mobile has the best 5G coverage among the big three, the highest 5G speeds, the fastest downloads, and the best overall reliability, according to analysis from OpenSignal and Ookla. The carrier also makes claims to winning on value, taking into account perks like entertainment bundles, airplane Wi-Fi, and access to satellite data in emergencies.

根据 OpenSignal 和 Ookla 的分析，T-Mobile 在三大运营商中拥有最好的 5G 覆盖范围、最高的 5G 速度、最快的下载速度以及最好的整体可靠性。该运营商还声称在性价比方面胜出，这考虑到了娱乐套餐、飞机 Wi-Fi 以及紧急情况下的卫星数据访问等福利。

T-Mobile has rebranded its unlimited offerings this year but still offers three (well, kind of four—I'll explain below) main unlimited talk and text plans: Essentials, Experience More, and Experience Beyond. Only the two Experience plans offer true unlimited 5G data without any throttling or deprioritizing (i.e., making your phone stand in line for data behind other, more important phones during peak demand).

T-Mobile 今年对其无限流量产品进行了品牌重塑，但仍提供三种（其实算四种，下文会解释）主要的无限通话和短信套餐：Essentials、Experience More 和 Experience Beyond。只有两款 Experience 套餐提供真正的无限 5G 数据，没有任何限速或降级处理（即在高峰需求时让你的手机排在其他更重要的手机后面获取数据）。

For occasional jet-setters and those who want consistent phone upgrades, Experience More is the best affordable phone plan among all services. It includes Wi-Fi during flights, 4K video, 60 gigs of high-speed mobile hot-spotting, a modicum of international data, a one-year AAA membership, free ad-supported Netflix, and cheap ($3) Apple TV. It'll also let you upgrade your phone every two years so random children don't make fun of your ancient iPhone at a rest stop. (Yes, this has happened to me.)

对于偶尔的空中飞人和那些希望持续升级手机的人来说，Experience More 是所有服务中最实惠的手机套餐。它包括飞行中的 Wi-Fi、4K 视频、60GB 高速移动热点、少量国际数据、一年 AAA 会员资格、免费含广告的 Netflix 以及便宜（3 美元）的 Apple TV。它还能让你每两年升级一次手机，这样就不会有路人小孩在休息站嘲笑你那老掉牙的 iPhone 了。（是的，这事发生在我身上。）

If you want three lines or more and you're a new T-Mobile customer (or a five-year T-Mobile customer), you have access to an even better deal. The limited-time-only Better Value plan is in fact, possibly the best deal in phonedom at the moment. For the same price as the three-to-five-line Experience More plans, you add free ad-supported Hulu, access to the T-Satellite emergency network, unlimited mobile hot-spotting, 30 gigs of international data in hundreds of countries, plus additional watch and tablet lines for just $5 a line. 如果你需要三条线或更多，且你是 T-Mobile 的新客户（或五年老客户），你可以获得更优惠的方案。限时推出的 Better Value 套餐实际上可能是目前手机界最划算的交易。以与三到五条线的 Experience More 套餐相同的价格，你可以获得免费含广告的 Hulu、T-Satellite 紧急网络访问权限、无限移动热点、数百个国家/地区的 30GB 国际数据，以及每条线仅需 5 美元的额外手表和平板电脑线路。除了手机升级频率从每年一次变为每两年一次外，大多数福利都优于 T-Mobile 最昂贵的套餐，且价格低得多。此优惠价格还锁定了 5 年。

If cost is more important to you than perks, you have a big family, and you don't travel a lot internationally, the Essentials plan is probably best. It's no frills, just the phone, ma'am, with no subscription money going to streaming services or international carrier fees. Your hot spot is limited to 3G speeds, which is an important consideration for many laptop warriors, and video streaming is 480p. But at its price point, Essentials is the only plan among the big three carriers to offer premium data (up to 50 GB) that won't be throttled. If you've got home Wi-Fi, that 50GB should be sufficient for most people. Besides, it'll teach your teens how to budget and how to ask for the Wi-Fi password instead of using their own data. But take note: Data in Canada and Mexico will be so slow, you might as well have an old flip phone.

如果比起福利你更看重成本，且你有一个大家庭，又不常去国际旅行，那么 Essentials 套餐可能是最好的选择。它没有花哨的东西，就是纯粹的手机服务，没有订阅流媒体服务的费用，也没有国际运营商费用。你的热点被限制在 3G 速度，这对许多笔记本电脑用户来说是一个重要的考虑因素，视频流媒体限制为 480p。但在其价位上，Essentials 是三大运营商中唯一提供不会被限速的高级数据（最高 50GB）的套餐。如果你家里有 Wi-Fi，那 50GB 对大多数人来说应该足够了。此外，这还能教会你的孩子如何做预算，以及如何询问 Wi-Fi 密码而不是使用自己的流量。但请注意：在加拿大和墨西哥的数据速度会非常慢，慢到你还不如用个老式翻盖手机。
