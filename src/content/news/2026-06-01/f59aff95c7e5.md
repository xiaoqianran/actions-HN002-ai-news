# Here’s your first look at ‘A Minecraft Movie Squared’ with Kirsten Dunst as Alex
# 《我的世界》电影续作《A Minecraft Movie Squared》首曝，克里斯汀·邓斯特饰演艾莉克斯

The A Minecraft Movie sequel officially has a title: A Minecraft Movie Squared. What’s more, we now know that Kirsten Dunst will star as Alex, the game’s female character option, and that Matt Berry is set to play an even bigger role in this film. He voiced Nitwit in the first movie, but in this one, he’ll be playing a human character. Who exactly he’ll be hasn’t been revealed yet, but in the clip below, he can be heard dismissing a costume choice because “Steve would not be wearing that,” suggesting he might be playing the Herobrine, a creepy pasta evil counterpart to Jack Black’s Steve.

《我的世界》电影续作正式定名为《A Minecraft Movie Squared》。此外，我们现在得知克里斯汀·邓斯特（Kirsten Dunst）将饰演游戏中的女性角色艾莉克斯（Alex），而马特·贝里（Matt Berry）也将在本片中扮演更重要的角色。他在第一部电影中为“傻子村民”（Nitwit）配音，但在这一部中，他将饰演一名人类角色。虽然他具体扮演谁尚未揭晓，但在下方的片段中，可以听到他否定了一套服装选择，理由是“史蒂夫（Steve）才不会穿那个”，这暗示他可能正在扮演“Herobrine”——一个在都市传说中作为杰克·布莱克（Jack Black）饰演的史蒂夫的邪恶对立面。

The director, Jared Hess, and Mojang Chief Creative Officer, Jens Bergensten, also took the opportunity during the May Minecraft Live event to announce the Minecraft Movie Build Challenge. The contest will see players submit their most epic Minecraft creations, with the winning build being featured during the end credits. The full details for the contest can be found here. But you’ll have to wait until A Minecraft Movie Squared release date of July 23th, 2027 to see the winner on the big screen.

导演杰瑞德·赫斯（Jared Hess）和 Mojang 首席创意官延斯·伯根斯滕（Jens Bergensten）也借五月份“Minecraft Live”活动之机，宣布了“我的世界电影建筑挑战赛”（Minecraft Movie Build Challenge）。该比赛将邀请玩家提交他们最宏伟的《我的世界》作品，获胜者的作品将出现在电影的片尾字幕中。比赛的详细信息可以在此处找到。不过，你得等到 2027 年 7 月 23 日《A Minecraft Movie Squared》上映时，才能在大银幕上看到获胜者的作品。
