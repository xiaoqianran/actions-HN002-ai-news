# Functional MRI Time Series Generation via Wavelet-Based Image Transform and Spectral Flow Matching for Brain Disorder Identification
# 基于小波图像变换与谱流匹配的功能性磁共振成像时间序列生成：用于脑部疾病识别

**Abstract:** Functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging (fMRI) provides non-invasive access to dynamic brain activity by measuring blood oxygen level-dependent (BOLD) signals over time. However, the resource-intensive nature of fMRI acquisition limits the availability of high-fidelity samples required for data-driven brain analysis models.

**摘要：** 功能性磁共振成像（fMRI）通过测量随时间变化的血氧水平依赖（BOLD）信号，为研究动态脑活动提供了非侵入性手段。然而，fMRI 采集过程的高资源消耗限制了数据驱动型脑分析模型所需的高保真样本的获取。

While modern generative models can synthesize fMRI data, they often remain challenging in replicating their inherent non-stationarity, intricate spatiotemporal dynamics, and physiological variations of raw BOLD signals. To address these challenges, we propose Dual-Spectral Flow Matching (DSFM), a novel fMRI generative framework that cascades dual frequency representation of BOLD signals with spectral flow matching.

尽管现代生成模型可以合成 fMRI 数据，但在复现原始 BOLD 信号固有的非平稳性、复杂的时空动态以及生理变异性方面仍面临挑战。为了解决这些问题，我们提出了双谱流匹配（DSFM），这是一种新型的 fMRI 生成框架，它将 BOLD 信号的双频表示与谱流匹配技术相结合。

Specifically, our framework first converts BOLD signals into a wavelet decomposition map via a discrete wavelet transform (DWT) to capture globalized transient and multi-scale variations, and projects into the discrete cosine transform (DCT) space across brain regions and time to exploit localized energy compaction of low-frequency dominant BOLD coefficients.

具体而言，我们的框架首先通过离散小波变换（DWT）将 BOLD 信号转换为小波分解图，以捕捉全局性的瞬态和多尺度变化；随后将其投影到跨脑区和时间的离散余弦变换（DCT）空间，以利用低频主导 BOLD 系数的局部能量压缩特性。

Subsequently, a spectral flow matching model is trained to generate class-conditioned cosine-frequency representation. The generated samples are reconstructed through inverse DCT and inverse DWT operations to recover physiologically plausible time-domain BOLD signals. This dual-transform approach imposes structured frequency priors and preserves key physiological brain dynamics.

随后，训练一个谱流匹配模型来生成类别条件下的余弦频率表示。生成的样本通过逆 DCT 和逆 DWT 操作进行重构，从而恢复出符合生理特征的时域 BOLD 信号。这种双重变换方法施加了结构化的频率先验，并保留了关键的生理脑动态。

Ultimately, we demonstrate the efficacy of our approach through improved downstream fMRI-based brain network classification. The code is available at this [URL](https://github.com/HwaHuiTew/DSFM).

最终，我们通过改进下游基于 fMRI 的脑网络分类任务，证明了该方法的有效性。代码已在 [此处](https://github.com/HwaHuiTew/DSFM) 公开。
