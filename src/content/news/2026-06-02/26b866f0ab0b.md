# The newest Instagram “exploit” is the goofiest I've seen
# Instagram 最新的“漏洞”是我见过最荒唐的

Yesterday, a slew of Instagram accounts, including some high profile ones like the Obama White House account, seemingly got hacked. Look, I’m no spring chicken. I’ve spent almost a decade and a half identifying vulnerabilities and exploits at unicorn scale, but this is hands down the most unserious, "almost too stupid to be true" of them all.

昨天，一大批 Instagram 账号（包括奥巴马白宫账号等知名账号）似乎遭到了黑客攻击。听着，我可不是什么职场菜鸟。我在独角兽规模的企业中识别漏洞和攻击手段已经快十五年了，但这绝对是我见过最不正经、甚至“蠢到令人难以置信”的漏洞。

### The Takeover Flow
### 账号接管流程

**Step 01: Faking the Location & Initiating Support**
All the attacker needs to kick this off is your account username. Then, they hop on a VPN or proxy close to your city so Instagram's security algorithms don't suspect a thing. (You can quite easily get this from your public profile or "About" section or a hundred other ways.) Once it looks like the request is coming from the correct region, they tell the Meta support AI that the account is hacked and ask it to send the verification codes to an arbitrary email address they control.

**第一步：伪造位置并启动支持流程**
攻击者发起攻击只需要你的账号用户名。接着，他们会连接到你所在城市附近的 VPN 或代理服务器，这样 Instagram 的安全算法就不会产生怀疑。（你可以通过公开资料、个人简介或上百种其他方式轻松获取这些信息。）一旦请求看起来像是来自正确区域，他们就会告诉 Meta 的支持 AI 账号被盗，并要求 AI 将验证码发送到他们控制的任意电子邮箱地址。

**Step 02: That's It**
Really, that's it. The first proper zero auth password reset I've seen in production. There appears to be no additional check as to whether the email being given is actually something the user has used before. Once the AI sends the security code to the attacker's email, the attacker passes it right back to complete the verification. The platform hands over a fresh password reset link, granting full ownership to the attacker. Instagram's AI may or may not ask the attacker for a video selfie to prove identity. It's not particularly discerning at the moment, so something as simple as an AI animated public photo from the target's feed has been widely reported to work.

**第二步：就这样**
真的，就这么简单。这是我在生产环境中见过的第一个真正意义上的“零认证”密码重置。系统似乎没有任何额外的检查来确认所提供的邮箱是否确实是用户之前使用过的。一旦 AI 将安全码发送到攻击者的邮箱，攻击者将其回传即可完成验证。平台会提供一个新的密码重置链接，从而将账号的完全所有权交给攻击者。Instagram 的 AI 可能会也可能不会要求攻击者提供视频自拍来证明身份。目前该 AI 的辨别能力并不强，据广泛报道，使用简单的 AI 动画处理目标账号公开照片的方法就能奏效。

### 2FA Doesn't Help
In case you're wondering, because the system treats this high-privilege recovery flow as a total account reset by the "true" owner, the original 2FA gets thoroughly bypassed in the process. Existing sessions are revoked and the password changed with no email, text, or push notification. The actual owner can't initiate recovery because the email and phone numbers now map to the attacker. There's no human to escalate to, it's just you arguing with a chat hoping to take control back while praying they don't do it again. And if you're part of the A/B tested accounts on which the AI support option is active, tough luck, you can't even turn it off.

### 双重验证（2FA）毫无用处
如果你在想 2FA 是否有用，答案是没用。因为系统将这种高权限的恢复流程视为“真正”所有者进行的全面账号重置，所以原有的 2FA 在此过程中会被彻底绕过。现有的会话会被撤销，密码会被更改，且不会有任何邮件、短信或推送通知。真正的所有者无法发起恢复，因为邮箱和电话号码现在已经映射到了攻击者名下。你找不到人工客服来处理，只能对着聊天机器人争论，希望夺回控制权，同时祈祷他们不会再次攻击。如果你恰好是 AI 支持选项处于激活状态的 A/B 测试账号用户，那只能自认倒霉，因为你甚至无法关闭该功能。

### Black Markets Galore
Multiple black market Telegram groups have sprung up offering "account takeover" services at steep rates and quick turnaround times. Considering short handles are worth hundreds of thousands to even millions of dollars, it's not a surprise, really. Accounts have been flipped, like @hey, or been used for propaganda, like @obamawhitehouse or @ocmssf, the account of the Chief Master Sergeant of the U.S. Space Force.

### 黑市泛滥
多个黑市 Telegram 群组如雨后春笋般涌现，以高昂的价格和极快的速度提供“账号接管”服务。考虑到短用户名价值数十万甚至数百万美元，这其实并不令人惊讶。许多账号已被倒卖（如 @hey），或被用于宣传（如 @obamawhitehouse 或美国太空军总军士长账号 @ocmssf）。

### Patched Now
All the Telegram groups have quieted down as Meta seems to have patched it already, but it appears this particular method was active for weeks, if not months. The very fact that a $1.5 trillion company lacks robust guard rails and their support AI will just change anyone's linked email if you ask it nicely enough is so terrifying, if it weren't so funny.

### 现已修复
所有的 Telegram 群组都已经安静下来，因为 Meta 似乎已经修复了该漏洞，但这种特定的攻击手段似乎已经存在了数周甚至数月。一家市值 1.5 万亿美元的公司竟然缺乏稳健的防护机制，其支持 AI 只要被“礼貌地”请求一下就会更改任何人的关联邮箱，这如果不是因为太好笑，简直令人毛骨悚然。
