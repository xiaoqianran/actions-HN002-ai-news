# When LLMs Learn to Be Consistently Wrong: A Multi-Model Study of Linear Representations of Synthetic Deception
# 当大语言模型学会持续犯错：合成欺骗线性表征的多模型研究

**Abstract:** Deceptive alignment, in which models maintain accurate internal representations while deliberately producing false outputs, remains a central challenge in AI safety. While strategic deception is the primary long-term concern, synthetic dishonesty - induced via direct optimization on incorrect answers - provides a controlled testbed for studying the representational basis of learned deception.

**摘要：** 欺骗性对齐（Deceptive alignment）是指模型在保持准确内部表征的同时，故意输出错误结果，这仍然是人工智能安全领域的一个核心挑战。虽然战略性欺骗是长期的主要担忧，但通过直接优化错误答案诱导出的“合成不诚实”（synthetic dishonesty），为研究习得性欺骗的表征基础提供了一个可控的实验平台。

We introduce a multi-model paradigm in which honest and deceptive variants of five transformer models (Pythia-1.4B, Gemma-2-2B/9B, Qwen2.5-7B, Llama-3.1-8B) are fine-tuned using LoRA on the same question distribution. Linear probes trained on mean-pooled hidden states detect synthetic dishonesty with near-perfect AUC (greater than or equal to 0.99) as early as layers 1-3 in four architectures, while Pythia-1.4B reaches a peak of 0.705.

我们引入了一种多模型范式，利用 LoRA 在相同的问题分布上对五种 Transformer 模型（Pythia-1.4B、Gemma-2-2B/9B、Qwen2.5-7B、Llama-3.1-8B）的诚实和欺骗变体进行了微调。在四种架构中，基于均值池化隐藏状态训练的线性探测器（Linear probes）在第 1-3 层就能以近乎完美的 AUC（大于或等于 0.99）检测出合成不诚实行为，而 Pythia-1.4B 的峰值则达到 0.705。

Logistic regression probes consistently match or outperform MLP probes, supporting the Linear Representation Hypothesis. Probes trained on TruthfulQA generalize with near-zero loss (Delta AUC approx. 0) to held-out MMLU subjects. Late-layer representations show strong robustness to Gaussian noise, with Gemma-2 models exhibiting exceptional stability.

逻辑回归探测器的表现始终与 MLP 探测器持平或更优，这支持了“线性表征假设”（Linear Representation Hypothesis）。在 TruthfulQA 上训练的探测器能够以近乎零的损失（Delta AUC 约为 0）泛化到未见过的 MMLU 主题上。高层表征对高斯噪声表现出极强的鲁棒性，其中 Gemma-2 模型展现出了卓越的稳定性。

Mechanistic analysis of Fisher Discriminant Ratio, effective rank, centroid geometry, directional stability, cross-domain alignment, and calibration (ECE) reveals two regimes: representational collapse in Pythia/Llama/Qwen versus high-dimensional preservation in Gemma-2. Across all models, the dishonesty direction consolidates progressively in deeper layers, with optimal calibration (ECE less than 0.01 except Pythia) achievable in layers 1-4.

通过对费舍尔判别比（Fisher Discriminant Ratio）、有效秩、质心几何、方向稳定性、跨域对齐和校准（ECE）的机制分析，研究揭示了两种模式：Pythia/Llama/Qwen 中的表征坍缩，以及 Gemma-2 中的高维保留。在所有模型中，不诚实方向在深层中逐渐巩固，且在第 1-4 层即可实现最优校准（除 Pythia 外，ECE 小于 0.01）。

These results demonstrate that robust, domain-invariant dishonesty representations can be rapidly entrenched via modest supervised fine-tuning, with implications for activation-based monitoring.

这些结果表明，通过适度的监督微调，稳健且与领域无关的不诚实表征可以被迅速固化，这对基于激活值的监控具有重要意义。
