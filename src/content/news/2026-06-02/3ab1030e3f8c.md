# SANA-Streaming: Real-time Streaming Video Editing with Hybrid Diffusion Transformer

**Title:** SANA-Streaming: Real-time Streaming Video Editing with Hybrid Diffusion Transformer
**Authors:** Yuyang Zhao, Yicheng Pan, Qiyuan He, Jincheng Yu, Junsong Chen, Tian Ye, Haozhe Liu, Enze Xie, Song Han

**标题：** SANA-Streaming：基于混合扩散 Transformer 的实时流媒体视频编辑
**作者：** Yuyang Zhao, Yicheng Pan, Qiyuan He, Jincheng Yu, Junsong Chen, Tian Ye, Haozhe Liu, Enze Xie, Song Han

***

**Abstract:** Real-time streaming video-to-video editing (V2V) is critical for interactive applications such as live broadcasting and gaming, yet it remains a formidable challenge due to the stringent requirements for temporal consistency and inference throughput. In this paper, we present SANA-Streaming, a system-algorithm co-designed framework for high-resolution, real-time streaming video editing on consumer GPUs, with the following three core designs:

**摘要：** 实时流媒体视频到视频（V2V）编辑对于直播和游戏等交互式应用至关重要，但由于对时间一致性和推理吞吐量的严格要求，这仍然是一项艰巨的挑战。在本文中，我们提出了 SANA-Streaming，这是一个针对消费级 GPU 进行高分辨率、实时流媒体视频编辑的系统-算法协同设计框架，包含以下三个核心设计：

***

(1) Hybrid Diffusion Transformer architecture introduces softmax attention in part of the blocks to improve local modeling capabilities while preserving the efficiency of linear layers.

(1) 混合扩散 Transformer 架构在部分模块中引入了 Softmax 注意力机制，在保持线性层效率的同时，提升了局部建模能力。

***

(2) Cycle-Reverse Regularization is a novel training strategy that enforces semantic consistency by predicting source frames from generated content via flow matching, improving temporal consistency without requiring paired long edited videos.

(2) 循环反向正则化（Cycle-Reverse Regularization）是一种新颖的训练策略，它通过流匹配（flow matching）从生成的内容中预测源帧，从而强制实现语义一致性，在无需成对长视频的情况下提高了时间一致性。

***

(3) Efficient System Co-design combines fused GDN kernels and Mixed-Precision Quantization (MPQ) optimized for the NVIDIA Blackwell (RTX 5090) architecture. By profiling real-world throughput, our MPQ maximizes Tensor Core utilization while maintaining generation quality.

(3) 高效的系统协同设计结合了针对 NVIDIA Blackwell (RTX 5090) 架构优化的融合 GDN 内核和混合精度量化（MPQ）。通过对实际吞吐量进行分析，我们的 MPQ 在保持生成质量的同时，最大化了 Tensor Core 的利用率。

***

The resulting system achieves real-time 1280 x 704 resolution editing at 24 end-to-end FPS on a single RTX 5090 GPU, with the DiT core running at 58 FPS. Experimental results demonstrate that our co-design approach significantly outperforms existing SOTA methods in both temporal coherence and system throughput.

该系统在单张 RTX 5090 GPU 上实现了 1280 x 704 分辨率的实时编辑，端到端帧率为 24 FPS，其中 DiT 核心运行速度可达 58 FPS。实验结果表明，我们的协同设计方法在时间连贯性和系统吞吐量方面均显著优于现有的 SOTA（当前最优）方法。
