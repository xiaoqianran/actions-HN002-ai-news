# An affordable, long-lasting AirTag alternative is $15 right now
# 一款经济实惠且持久耐用的 AirTag 替代品现仅售 15 美元

There are many solid Bluetooth trackers for iPhones that tap into Apple’s expansive Find My network. Some are thin, some are a bit chunkier. And, evidently, some look like tiny soccer balls. Ugreen’s FineTrack 2 glows in the dark, and it has a loud 110-decibel alarm when you need to find it. It’s just $14.99 at Amazon for Prime members (originally $19.99). Ugreen also sells it for $14.99 through its site.

市面上有许多适用于 iPhone 的出色蓝牙追踪器，它们都能接入苹果庞大的“查找”（Find My）网络。有些设计轻薄，有些则稍显厚重。显然，还有些长得像微型足球。绿联（Ugreen）的 FineTrack 2 不仅能在黑暗中发光，当你需要寻找它时，它还能发出 110 分贝的响亮警报。目前亚马逊 Prime 会员购买仅需 14.99 美元（原价 19.99 美元）。绿联官网也以 14.99 美元的价格同步销售。

Instead of offering replaceable coin batteries, like many trackers do at this point, the FineTrack 2 has a built-in, non-rechargeable battery that can live for up to seven years. Some may consider that a flaw, but it’ll be nice not to have to worry about replacing its battery for a long while. The ball-shaped tracker also features IP68 water and dust resistance, and includes both a short and long lanyard you can swap out at your leisure.

与目前市面上许多采用可更换纽扣电池的追踪器不同，FineTrack 2 内置了一块不可充电电池，续航时间长达七年。有些人可能认为这是个缺点，但能长时间无需担心更换电池的问题其实非常省心。这款球形追踪器还具备 IP68 级防尘防水性能，并附赠长短两款挂绳，供你随心更换。

Google’s Pixel Buds 2A, its latest entry-level wireless earbuds, are down to $109 at Amazon, B&H Photo, and Best Buy, which is their second-best price to date. They’re stacked with more features than their 2021 predecessor (not to mention cooler colors), including active noise cancellation, transparency mode, and spatial audio. That’d be enough to make them worth the sale price, but they also feature IP54 protection against sweat, and up to 20 hours of total battery life with the charging case.

谷歌最新的入门级无线耳机 Pixel Buds 2A 在亚马逊、B&H Photo 和百思买（Best Buy）的售价降至 109 美元，这是该产品迄今为止第二低的价格。相比 2021 年的前代产品，它们功能更丰富（配色也更酷），包括主动降噪、通透模式和空间音频。这些功能足以让它们物有所值，此外它们还具备 IP54 级防汗保护，配合充电盒总续航可达 20 小时。

Notably, Bose is the seller here, and it claims the headphones are in “pristine, like-new condition,” and will arrive in new packaging complete with all accessories. The first-gen QC Ultra headphone are still spectacular when it comes to sound quality, comfort, and for canceling out sounds.

耳机方面还有另一个优惠：Bose 第一代 QuietComfort Ultra 头戴式无线耳机在 eBay 上有黑色官翻版出售，价格为 188 美元（原价 449 美元，全新常态价为 329 美元）。值得注意的是，卖家正是 Bose 官方，他们声称这些耳机处于“完好如新”的状态，并将以全新包装发货，配件齐全。第一代 QC Ultra 耳机在音质、佩戴舒适度和降噪效果方面依然表现出色。
