# Dex2HOI: Dexterous Bimanual Two-Object Interaction Generation
# Dex2HOI：灵巧双手双物体交互生成

**Abstract:** Recent advances in 4D Human-Object Interaction (HOI) generation have enabled increasingly realistic motion synthesis, particularly for single-object manipulation. Yet current research overlooks an inherent property of human behavior: people naturally coordinate both hands and manipulate multiple objects simultaneously.
**摘要：** 近年来，4D 人机交互（HOI）生成技术的进步使得动作合成愈发逼真，尤其是在单物体操作方面。然而，当前的研究忽略了人类行为的一个固有特性：人们在自然状态下会协调双手并同时操作多个物体。

To address this gap, we present Dex2HOI, a unified diffusion model for single- and two-object HOI synthesis from text. At its core, Dex2HOI employs a Dual-Stream Diffusion approach, where each object is processed in a dedicated interaction stream and coordinated through bidirectional cross-attention.
为了填补这一空白，我们提出了 Dex2HOI，这是一个用于从文本生成单物体和双物体 HOI 的统一扩散模型。Dex2HOI 的核心在于采用了双流扩散（Dual-Stream Diffusion）方法，其中每个物体都在专门的交互流中进行处理，并通过双向交叉注意力机制进行协调。

To synthesize the final motion, we introduce a Motion Fusion Network integrated with novel hand-relative object representations and contact-aware conditioning applied across the whole sequence.
为了合成最终动作，我们引入了一个运动融合网络（Motion Fusion Network），该网络集成了新颖的手部相对物体表征，并将接触感知条件（contact-aware conditioning）应用于整个序列。

By sampling the diffusion process autoregressively over prefix-conditioned windows, Dex2HOI generates arbitrarily long sequences at real-time speed omitting redundant test-time optimization, achieving up to x540 inference speed-up over prior state-of-the-art methods.
通过在以“前缀”为条件的窗口上进行自回归扩散过程采样，Dex2HOI 能够以实时速度生成任意长度的序列，无需冗余的测试时优化，推理速度比现有的最先进方法提升了高达 540 倍。

Extensive evaluation on both single- and two-object benchmarks demonstrates state-of-the-art quantitative results, marking a step beyond conventional single-object HOI generation and toward expressive multi-object manipulation. Code and models will be released upon acceptance.
在单物体和双物体基准测试上的广泛评估表明，该模型取得了最先进的定量结果，标志着从传统的单物体 HOI 生成迈向更具表现力的多物体操作。代码和模型将在论文被录用后发布。
