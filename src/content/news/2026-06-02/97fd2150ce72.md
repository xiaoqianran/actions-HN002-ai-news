# Alphabet plans to raise $80 billion to pay for AI buildout
# Alphabet 计划筹集 800 亿美元以支付人工智能基础设施建设费用

Google parent company Alphabet said Monday that it plans to raise $80 billion to help pay for the massive AI infrastructure buildout it has planned.
谷歌母公司 Alphabet 周一表示，计划筹集 800 亿美元，以帮助支付其规划中的大规模人工智能基础设施建设费用。

Alphabet will sell off that amount in stock, and will then use the funds to pay for “general corporate purposes, including capital expenditures to scale AI infrastructure and global compute,” the company said in a statement.
Alphabet 在一份声明中表示，公司将通过出售等额股票来筹集资金，并将这些资金用于“一般企业用途，包括用于扩展人工智能基础设施和全球计算能力的资本支出”。

Part of the plan involves selling $10 billion in stock to Berkshire Hathaway, the massive global holding company formerly led by Warren Buffet.
该计划的一部分包括向伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司（Berkshire Hathaway）出售价值 100 亿美元的股票，这家大型全球控股公司曾由沃伦·巴菲特（Warren Buffet）领导。

“The company is experiencing strong demand for its AI solutions and services from enterprises and consumers, at levels that are exceeding the company’s available supply,” Alphabet said in its statement. “By scaling its investments, the company seeks to expand its foundational infrastructure to support the significant growth opportunity ahead.”
Alphabet 在声明中称：“公司目前正经历来自企业和消费者对人工智能解决方案及服务的强劲需求，其水平已超过了公司现有的供应能力。通过扩大投资，公司旨在扩展其基础架构，以支持未来巨大的增长机遇。”

The company added that the stock plan represented a way to “fund its investments in a balanced way while retaining a healthy balance sheet.”
该公司补充说，这一股票计划代表了一种“在保持健康资产负债表的同时，以平衡方式为其投资提供资金”的途径。

Like other tech giants, Google has announced plans for a massive investment in compute this year, the likes of which will be used to support a flurry of new AI services.
与其他科技巨头一样，谷歌已宣布今年将对计算能力进行大规模投资，这些投资将用于支持一系列新的人工智能服务。

At Google I/O last month, CEO Sundar Pichai said that the company expects to spend between $180 and $190 billion on capex before the year is out. Google and other tech giants are expected to spend as much as $700 billion this year on AI capex.
在上个月的 Google I/O 大会上，首席执行官桑达尔·皮查伊（Sundar Pichai）表示，公司预计在今年年底前，资本支出将达到 1800 亿至 1900 亿美元。预计谷歌和其他科技巨头今年在人工智能资本支出上的投入将高达 7000 亿美元。
