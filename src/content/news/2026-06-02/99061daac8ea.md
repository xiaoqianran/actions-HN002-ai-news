### NumLeak: Public Numeric Benchmarks as Latent Labels in Foundation Models
### NumLeak：作为基础模型潜在标签的公开数值基准

**Abstract:** Public numeric benchmarks appear in pretraining, so an evaluation that conditions on a date may be measuring memorized recall rather than out-of-sample skill. We introduce NumLeak, a measurement framework that combines API-boundary probes on production models with a white-box controlled validation on an open causal LM.

**摘要：** 公开的数值基准出现在预训练数据中，因此，基于特定日期进行的评估可能是在衡量模型的记忆回溯能力，而非样本外（out-of-sample）的泛化技能。我们引入了 NumLeak，这是一个结合了生产模型 API 边界探测与开源因果语言模型（Causal LM）白盒受控验证的测量框架。

Top-tier frontier LLMs recall the Fama-French market excess return at 3-seed pooled Pearson r=0.97-0.99 while staying within 0.15 within-25bps on the five sibling factors; comparable fidelity appears on U.S. unemployment, CPI inflation, and NOAA temperature.

顶级前沿大语言模型（LLM）在回溯 Fama-French 市场超额收益率时，三种子集汇总的皮尔逊相关系数（Pearson r）达到 0.97-0.99，同时在五个相关因子上的误差保持在 25 个基点（bps）内的 0.15 以内；在衡量美国失业率、CPI 通胀率和 NOAA 温度数据时，模型也表现出了相当的保真度。

On a recent-release holdout, parse rate collapses to 21-57% but r stays at approximately 0.99 on months answered, the refuse-or-recall asymmetry a memorized channel predicts. The white-box experiment reproduces the dose-response, and logprob ranking detects memorization that open-ended generation misses, implying closed-API black-box probes understate the channel.

在针对近期发布数据的留存测试中，解析率下降至 21-57%，但在模型能够回答的月份中，相关系数 r 仍保持在约 0.99，这种“拒绝或回溯”的不对称性正是记忆通道所预期的表现。白盒实验重现了这种剂量反应（dose-response），且对数概率（logprob）排序能够检测到开放式生成所遗漏的记忆现象，这意味着封闭式 API 的黑盒探测低估了该记忆通道的影响。

A Sonnet "date to market-sentiment" regression that correlates with true Mkt-RF at r=0.74 collapses to r=0.02 once the model's own recall is residualized out. A one-line system-prompt defense blocks 99.8% of a non-adaptive single-turn suffix attack set at near-zero utility cost on conceptual and historical-narrative queries.

一项关于 Sonnet 模型“日期到市场情绪”的回归分析显示，其与真实市场超额收益率（Mkt-RF）的相关系数为 r=0.74，但一旦剔除模型自身的记忆回溯成分，该相关性便骤降至 r=0.02。此外，一条简单的系统提示词防御策略即可拦截 99.8% 的非自适应单轮后缀攻击，且在处理概念性和历史叙事类查询时，几乎不会产生任何效用损失。
