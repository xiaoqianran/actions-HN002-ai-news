### Mitigating Content Shift and Hallucination in GenAI Image Editing via Structural Refinement
### 通过结构优化缓解生成式 AI 图像编辑中的内容偏移与幻觉问题

**Abstract:** Generative AI (GenAI) image editors, such as Nano Banana, produce visually compelling results for retouching tasks, enabling non-experts to edit images through text prompts alone. However, the generative nature of these models often introduces spatial misalignment, texture distortion, and content hallucination, all of which are detrimental to downstream workflows that require pixel-level fidelity.

**摘要：** 诸如 Nano Banana 之类的生成式 AI (GenAI) 图像编辑器在修图任务中能够产生极具视觉吸引力的结果，使非专业人士仅通过文本提示即可编辑图像。然而，这些模型的生成特性往往会引入空间错位、纹理失真和内容幻觉，这些问题对于需要像素级保真度的下游工作流程而言是有害的。

We identify a problem setting we call "structure-preserving GenAI fusion" for black-box GenAI image retouching: retain the perceptual enhancements of a GenAI output while enforcing structural faithfulness to the original input image. To address this problem, we propose a post-processing framework that fuses an input image with its GenAI-enhanced counterpart by first establishing coarse spatial and photometric correspondences, then performing a fusion stage that transfers desired enhancements while suppressing hallucinated content.

我们针对黑盒 GenAI 图像修图定义了一个名为“结构保持型 GenAI 融合”的问题场景：即在保留 GenAI 输出的感知增强效果的同时，强制执行对原始输入图像的结构忠实度。为了解决这一问题，我们提出了一种后处理框架，通过首先建立粗略的空间和光度对应关系，将输入图像与其经 GenAI 增强后的对应图像进行融合；随后执行融合阶段，在抑制幻觉内容的同时迁移所需的增强效果。

In the absence of direct prior work in this setting, we evaluate our framework against representative methods from photorealistic style transfer and image fusion. Our experiments demonstrate that our method better preserves aesthetic quality while maintaining pixel-level structural consistency and the input resolution.

由于该领域缺乏直接的先验研究，我们通过与照片级真实感风格迁移和图像融合领域的代表性方法进行对比，评估了我们的框架。实验表明，我们的方法在保持像素级结构一致性和输入分辨率的同时，能更好地保留美学质量。
