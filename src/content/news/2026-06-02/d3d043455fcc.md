# LLMs Without Deep Neural Networks: New Architecture, Benefits and Case Study
# 无需深度神经网络的大语言模型：新架构、优势与案例研究

**Abstract:** The purpose of this article is to provide validation to my deep neural network alternative in the context of LLMs. Very recently, there has been a significant interest by Chinese researchers in a model called RBF network, as a substitute to standard DNNs, with increased explainability and higher accuracy. 

**摘要：** 本文旨在验证我在大语言模型（LLM）背景下提出的深度神经网络替代方案。近期，中国研究人员对一种名为 RBF（径向基函数）网络的模型产生了浓厚兴趣，将其作为标准深度神经网络（DNN）的替代品，以期获得更好的可解释性和更高的准确性。

It turns out that my new model, discovered independently, is based on the exact same machinery. But with a major twist: it does not need DNN as it finds the global optimum of the loss function in closed form, in one iteration, thus eliminating the tedious training step. Here I provide a high-level overview of my technology, with case study and comparison to similar methods.

事实证明，我独立发现的新模型正是基于完全相同的机制。但它有一个重大改进：它不需要深度神经网络，因为它能在单次迭代中以闭式解（closed form）找到损失函数的全局最优解，从而省去了繁琐的训练步骤。在此，我将对该技术进行概述，并提供案例研究及与同类方法的对比。
