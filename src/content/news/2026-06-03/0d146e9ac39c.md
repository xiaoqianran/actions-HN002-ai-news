# Automatically Differentiable Nonlinear Tensor Networks (ADNTNs) for Exponential Compression of Deep Neural Networks
# 自动可微非线性张量网络 (ADNTNs)：深度神经网络的指数级压缩

**Abstract:** We study Automatically Differentiable Nonlinear Tensor Networks (ADNTNs), a family of structured weight generators whose compact core tensors are trained end-to-end by reverse-mode automatic differentiation (AD). The approach can be viewed as a natural extension of low-rank adaptation and tensor factorisation: instead of using one low-rank matrix update, an ADNTN builds a large weight tensor through a hierarchy of small cores, nonlinear activations, and optional lateral mixing tensors.

**摘要：** 我们研究了自动可微非线性张量网络 (ADNTNs)，这是一类结构化的权重生成器，其紧凑的核心张量通过反向模式自动微分 (AD) 进行端到端训练。该方法可以被视为低秩适应和张量分解的自然扩展：ADNTN 不再使用单一的低秩矩阵更新，而是通过小型核心、非线性激活函数以及可选的横向混合张量的层级结构来构建大型权重张量。

The paper focuses on three architectures: Tree Tensor Networks (TTNs), augmented TTNs (aTTNs) with boundary disentanglers, and Multi-scale Entanglement Renormalisation Ansatze (MERA). The formulation supports nonlinear activations, task-aware objectives, batching, and hardware-aware execution schedules. At the same time, the paper keeps a clear distinction between differentiating a contraction program and making contraction free: AD does not remove the cost of large intermediates, poor contraction orders, or exact contraction of general loopy tensor networks.

本文重点研究了三种架构：树张量网络 (TTNs)、带有边界解纠缠器的增强型 TTNs (aTTNs) 以及多尺度纠缠重整化拟设 (MERA)。该公式支持非线性激活、任务感知目标、批处理以及硬件感知的执行调度。同时，本文明确区分了“对收缩程序进行微分”与“使收缩过程无代价”这两个概念：自动微分 (AD) 并不能消除大型中间变量、糟糕的收缩顺序或通用循环张量网络精确收缩所带来的计算成本。

Extensive simulations on AlexNet and VGG-16 layers show per-layer compression ratios from roughly $2000\times$ to $77000\times$ in the studied settings, with accuracy often matching the dense baseline and, in several VGG-16 cases, improving it. These results are encouraging rather than final: they suggest that ADNTNs are a promising, mathematically structured, and hardware-aware route toward much smaller neural networks, provided that optimisation, contraction schedules, and deployment kernels are designed together.

在 AlexNet 和 VGG-16 层上的广泛模拟表明，在所研究的设置中，每层的压缩比约为 $2000\times$ 到 $77000\times$，且准确率通常与密集基准模型相当，在某些 VGG-16 的案例中甚至有所提升。这些结果令人鼓舞，但并非最终结论：它们表明，只要将优化、收缩调度和部署内核进行协同设计，ADNTN 就是通往更小规模神经网络的一条具有数学结构且硬件友好的可行路径。
