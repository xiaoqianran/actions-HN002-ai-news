# Hoeffding Concept Bottleneck Models with Applications to Overhead Images
# Hoeffding 概念瓶颈模型及其在俯视图像中的应用

**Abstract:** Explainability of deep learning algorithms is critical for computer-vision applications with high-stake decisions. Concept bottleneck models (CBM) have recently shown promising performance to provide explainable and accurate predictions for classification problems, based on a bottleneck of high-level concepts.

**摘要：** 深度学习算法的可解释性对于涉及高风险决策的计算机视觉应用至关重要。概念瓶颈模型（CBM）近期在提供可解释且准确的分类预测方面展现出了良好的性能，其核心在于基于高层概念的瓶颈结构。

Existing CBM methods rely on a linear aggregation of the concept scores to compute predictions. However, a large number of concepts is often used in this linear approach, which undermines explainability and favors information leakage. In general, the underlying relation between concepts and output logits is not linear.

现有的 CBM 方法依赖于对概念得分进行线性聚合来计算预测结果。然而，这种线性方法通常会使用大量的概念，这不仅削弱了可解释性，还容易导致信息泄露。总体而言，概念与输出逻辑值（logits）之间的潜在关系并非线性的。

Therefore, we introduce Hoeffding Concept Bottleneck Models (HCBM), which build on the Hoeffding functional decomposition of gradient-boosted trees to provide non-linear and sparse aggregations of concept scores, and generate compact predictions using prime implicants.

因此，我们引入了 Hoeffding 概念瓶颈模型（HCBM）。该模型基于梯度提升树的 Hoeffding 函数分解，能够对概念得分进行非线性且稀疏的聚合，并利用素蕴涵项（prime implicants）生成紧凑的预测结果。

HCBM are proved to be robust to interconcept leakage, and outperform standard linear CBM in practice, as shown in extensive experiments. Beyond classification, HCBM can be adapted to object detection, and we focus on a challenging case with overhead images to show the high performance of HCBM in these settings.

广泛的实验表明，HCBM 被证明对概念间的信息泄露具有鲁棒性，且在实际应用中优于标准的线性 CBM。除了分类任务外，HCBM 还可以适配到目标检测中；我们重点研究了一个具有挑战性的俯视图像案例，以展示 HCBM 在此类场景下的卓越性能。
