# Structured Visual Evidence Decomposition for Evidence-Grounded Multimodal Screening of Obstructive Sleep Apnea-Hypopnea Syndrome
# 用于阻塞性睡眠呼吸暂停低通气综合征（OSAHS）循证多模态筛查的结构化视觉证据分解方法

**Abstract:** Effective pre-polysomnography screening for obstructive sleep apnea-hypopnea syndrome (OSAHS) requires combining clinical risk factors with visible craniofacial and neck cues. Directly prompting general-purpose multimodal foundation models for medical yes/no decisions can yield unstable, poorly calibrated outputs.

**摘要：** 有效的多导睡眠监测（PSG）前阻塞性睡眠呼吸暂停低通气综合征（OSAHS）筛查，需要将临床风险因素与可见的颅面及颈部特征相结合。直接提示通用多模态基础模型进行医学上的“是/否”决策，往往会导致输出不稳定且校准不佳。

We propose EviOSAHS, an evidence-grounded multimodal reasoning framework that separates image-only anatomical evidence acquisition from final clinical adjudication. Each frontal facial image is decomposed into seven fixed anatomical queries covering the neck, chin, mouth, face/neck fat, lower jaw, midface, and nose.

我们提出了 EviOSAHS，这是一个基于证据的多模态推理框架，它将纯图像的解剖学证据获取与最终的临床判定分离开来。每张正面面部图像被分解为七个固定的解剖学查询，涵盖颈部、下巴、口腔、面部/颈部脂肪、下颌、中面部和鼻子。

Visual responses are converted into structured evidence cards recording target anatomy, visibility, risk direction, evidence strength, confidence, and a concise summary. These cards are combined with a cleaned clinical profile only in the final stage, where a large language model performs balanced binary screening adjudication.

视觉响应被转换为结构化的证据卡片，记录目标解剖结构、可见性、风险方向、证据强度、置信度和简要总结。这些卡片仅在最后阶段与清洗后的临床资料相结合，由大语言模型进行平衡的二元筛查判定。

We evaluated EviOSAHS on a 642-subject cohort, mapping normal subjects to screening-negative and mild, moderate, or severe OSAHS subjects to screening-positive. EviOSAHS achieved 88.47% accuracy, 94.86% sensitivity, 93.74% F1-score, and a 5.14% false-negative rate, outperforming clinical-only prompting, direct multimodal prompting, and naive two-stage pipelines under a unified protocol.

我们在一个包含 642 名受试者的队列中评估了 EviOSAHS，将正常受试者映射为筛查阴性，将轻度、中度或重度 OSAHS 受试者映射为筛查阳性。在统一协议下，EviOSAHS 实现了 88.47% 的准确率、94.86% 的敏感性、93.74% 的 F1 分数以及 5.14% 的假阴性率，表现优于仅基于临床提示、直接多模态提示以及简单的两阶段流水线。

Ablations showed that seven-question visual decomposition and balanced final adjudication were critical to the high-sensitivity operating point. A question-level audit of 4,494 visual outputs showed a 100% structured parse rate and 93.88% high-visibility rate.

消融实验表明，七个问题的视觉分解和平衡的最终判定对于实现高敏感度工作点至关重要。对 4,494 个视觉输出进行的问题级审计显示，结构化解析率为 100%，高可见性率为 93.88%。

EviOSAHS provides an auditable, high-sensitivity workflow for binary pre-polysomnography OSAHS screening, but should be viewed as a triage assistant rather than a diagnostic system. Prospective validation, external testing, and calibrated operating-point control are needed before clinical deployment.

EviOSAHS 为多导睡眠监测前的 OSAHS 二元筛查提供了一种可审计、高敏感性的工作流程，但应将其视为分诊助手而非诊断系统。在临床部署之前，仍需进行前瞻性验证、外部测试以及校准的工作点控制。
