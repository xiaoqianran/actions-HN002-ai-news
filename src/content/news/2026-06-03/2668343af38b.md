# PlayStation State of Play June 2026: All the news and trailers
# 2026年6月 PlayStation State of Play：所有新闻与预告片汇总

While the majority of Summer Game Fest takes place over the weekend, Sony is getting a jump on things with its next State of Play showcase, which airs on June 2nd at 5PM ET. It’s coming at a pivotal time for the PlayStation brand: The PS5 keeps getting more expensive and Sony’s big bet on live-service games has largely fallen flat. A good showing at SGF won’t fix everything, but it could help show that PlayStation is on the right path moving forward.

尽管“夏日游戏节”（Summer Game Fest）的大部分活动都在周末举行，但索尼通过其于美东时间6月2日下午5点播出的最新一期“State of Play”发布会抢占了先机。这对 PlayStation 品牌来说是一个关键时刻：PS5 的价格不断上涨，而索尼在实时服务型游戏上的豪赌也大多以失败告终。在夏日游戏节上的出色表现虽然不能解决所有问题，但至少能证明 PlayStation 正走在正确的道路上。

For its part, Sony says its latest State of Play will feature “more than 60 minutes of updates, announcements, and gameplay reveals from top studios around the world.” The only confirmed part of the event’s lineup is Wolverine, from Spider-Man developer Insomniac, which is slated to launch in September. But given the length of the stream, there’ll be plenty more detailed. Maybe we’ll see the just-announced third expansion for The Witcher 3, or the long-awaited final installment of the Final Fantasy VII remake trilogy, or some more of Intergalactic, the next big project from The Last of Us developer Naughty Dog. It’s also likely the event will provide some insight into the uncertain future of Marathon.

索尼方面表示，最新的 State of Play 将包含“超过 60 分钟来自全球顶级工作室的更新、公告和实机演示”。目前唯一确认的参展作品是《蜘蛛侠》开发商 Insomniac 制作的《金刚狼》，该作定于 9 月发售。但考虑到直播的时长，届时会有更多详细内容。也许我们会看到刚刚公布的《巫师 3》第三部扩展包，或是备受期待的《最终幻想 7》重制版三部曲的最终章，又或是《最后生还者》开发商顽皮狗的下一个大项目《Intergalactic》的更多消息。此外，本次活动很可能还会透露《马拉松》（Marathon）那充满不确定性的未来。

Whatever Sony shows, you can watch along on YouTube or Twitch, or follow along with the latest developments right here. And for everything announced at the rest of Summer Game Fest, be sure to check out our event hub.

无论索尼展示什么，你都可以在 YouTube 或 Twitch 上观看直播，或者在这里关注最新的进展。至于夏日游戏节其余部分的所有公告，请务必查看我们的活动专题页面。

### Highlights / 亮点摘要

**God of War Laufey is coming to the PS5**
**《战神：劳菲》即将登陆 PS5**

Sony ended its big State of Play showcase with a major reveal: the next God of War. The new title is called God of War Laufey, and is once again developed by Sony’s Santa Monica Studio. Currently, the game doesn’t have a date, but it’s coming to the PS5 whenever it does launch.

索尼以一个重磅消息结束了这场盛大的 State of Play 发布会：下一部《战神》游戏。新作名为《战神：劳菲》（God of War Laufey），再次由索尼圣莫尼卡工作室开发。目前该游戏尚未确定发售日期，但确认将登陆 PS5 平台。

This time, instead of perpetually furious dad Kratos, players will take control of Faye / Laufey, as the title implies. That means that while the tone of the game seems somewhat similar to past God of War titles, the gameplay looks quite different, with much more aerobatic action punctuated by magic instead of Kratos’ heavy violence. Also, there’s a giant, six-legged cat that can fly. And a talking gelatinous cube named Phranque that fights alongside you.

正如标题所示，这一次玩家将不再扮演那个永远愤怒的父亲奎托斯，而是操控菲（Faye）/ 劳菲（Laufey）。这意味着虽然游戏基调与以往的《战神》作品相似，但玩法看起来大不相同——动作更加轻盈灵动，并以魔法作为点缀，而非奎托斯那种沉重的暴力风格。此外，游戏中还有一只会飞的六足巨猫，以及一个名叫 Phranque、能与你并肩作战的会说话的果冻状立方体。

**Remedy’s Control sequel launches in September**
**Remedy 的《控制》续作将于 9 月发售**

Control Resonant, the upcoming sequel to Remedy Entertainment’s Control, will be released on September 24th, 2026, according to a trailer that premiered during Tuesday’s PlayStation State of Play show.

根据周二 PlayStation State of Play 发布会上首播的预告片，Remedy Entertainment 旗下《控制》（Control）的续作《控制：共鸣》（Control Resonant）将于 2026 年 9 月 24 日发售。

The trailer also gave a preview of the game’s story, which stars Dylan Faden, who was imprisoned for much of the first game. He will be the “sole playable character,” Remedy’s Mikael Kasurinen says in a blog post, but his sister Jesse, who was the protagonist of Control, also “shapes the path” in Resonant.

预告片还展示了游戏的剧情预览，主角是迪伦·法登（Dylan Faden），他在第一部游戏中大部分时间都被囚禁。Remedy 的 Mikael Kasurinen 在博客文章中表示，他将是“唯一可玩角色”，但他的姐姐、前作主角杰西（Jesse）也将在《共鸣》中“塑造故事走向”。

**Until Dawn gets a little sun.**
**《直到黎明》迎来续作。**
The slasher movie-turned-game series is back with a full-on sequel that launches in 2027. It has a new setting and characters, but the same interactive film format.
这个由恐怖电影改编的游戏系列带着一部完整的续作回归，将于 2027 年发售。它拥有全新的背景和角色，但保留了同样的互动电影模式。

**Marathon season 2 kicks off.**
**《马拉松》第二赛季开启。**
The second season of Marathon, Bungie’s highly stylized extraction shooter, starts today. It’s called Nightfall and will be available for everyone to play for free from June 2nd to June 9th.
Bungie 极具风格的撤离类射击游戏《马拉松》第二赛季于今日开启。该赛季名为“夜幕”（Nightfall），将于 6 月 2 日至 6 月 9 日期间免费开放给所有玩家体验。

**Ace Combat 8 release date: October 2nd.**
**《皇牌空战 8》发售日期：10 月 2 日。**
Ace Combat’s been a lovely mix of anime plot and dogfighting action for a long time now, and it looks like we’re getting even more of the former with installment eight.
长期以来，《皇牌空战》一直是动漫剧情与空战动作的完美结合，看起来在第八部作品中，我们将看到更多动漫风格的叙事。

**A different take on Silent Hill.**
**《寂静岭》的全新视角。**
The franchise has been on quite a run between the SH2 remake and the excellent F, and Townfall looks like it’ll add a new flavor to the horror. It launches on September 24th.
继《寂静岭 2》重制版和出色的《寂静岭 f》之后，该系列表现强劲，而《寂静岭：城镇陨落》（Townfall）看起来将为恐怖题材增添新的风味。游戏将于 9 月 24 日发售。

**Onimusha: Way of the Sword launches in September — and a demo is launching today.**
**《鬼武者：剑道》将于 9 月发售——试玩版今日上线。**
Try the demo ahead of the game’s full release on September 25th. Hopefully Capcom’s strong 2026 run continues.
在 9 月 25 日游戏正式发售前，先尝试一下试玩版吧。希望卡普空在 2026 年的强劲表现能持续下去。

**Arrakis on console.**
**厄拉科斯登陆主机。**
Survival game Dune: Awakening is getting a port, and will be hitting the PS5 on September 22nd. It’ll include an all-new single-player mode when it does.
生存游戏《沙丘：觉醒》即将移植，并将于 9 月 22 日登陆 PS5。届时它将包含一个全新的单人模式。

**Dino crisis.**
**恐龙危机。**
We got a look at a dinosaur survival game called The Lost Wild: True Fear Is Primal. It looks like all my Jurassic Park video game dreams come true. It’s slated for PS5 in 2027.
我们看到了一款名为《失落荒野：原始恐惧》（The Lost Wild: True Fear Is Primal）的恐龙生存游戏。它看起来就像是我所有《侏罗纪公园》游戏梦想的实现。该作定于 2027 年登陆 PS5。

**The Tomb Raider reboot is getting a small delay.**
**《古墓丽影》重启版小幅跳票。**
Tomb Raider: Legacy of Atlantis was scheduled to launch in 2026, but during Tuesday’s State of Play show, it got a release date of February 12th, 2027.
《古墓丽影：亚特兰蒂斯遗产》原定于 2026 年发售，但在周二的 State of Play 发布会上，其发售日期被推迟至 2027 年 2 月 12 日。

**Yokai-slaying fun.**
**斩妖除魔的乐趣。**
Kemuri: Hunt the Unseen, which comes from The Evil Within artist Ikumi Nakamura, is heading to the PlayStation 5 in 2027.
出自《恶灵附身》美术设计师中村育美之手的《Kemuri: Hunt the Unseen》将于 2027 年登陆 PlayStation 5。

**Dave the Diver is getting a prequel game.**
**《潜水员戴夫》将推出前传游戏。**
Bancho the Chef tells the story of how Bancho, the intense chef from Dave the Diver, learned his craft. It’s in the works for PS5.
《班乔大厨》（Bancho the Chef）讲述了《潜水员戴夫》中那位严肃的大厨班乔是如何磨练厨艺的故事。该作正在为 PS5 开发中。

**Rayman reimagined, revealed.**
**《雷曼》重构版公布。**
Sony showed a reveal trailer for Rayman Legends Retold, a 3D reimagining of the 2013 platformer. It launches August 27th.
索尼展示了《雷曼：传奇重述》（Rayman Legends Retold）的预告片，这是对 2013 年那款平台跳跃游戏的 3D 重构。游戏将于 8 月 27 日发售。
