# 4 Best Alexa Speakers (2026): Echo Dot Max, Echo Dot, Echo Show 11
# 2026年四款最佳 Alexa 智能音箱：Echo Dot Max、Echo Dot、Echo Show 11

Amazon started the smart speaker game, but finding the best Alexa speaker has only gotten harder as the company launches more and more devices. Amazon has the largest range of smart speakers and displays by a long shot, which can be overwhelming to dive into. The most recent were launched late last year: the Echo Dot Max, the Echo Studio (2nd Gen), the Echo Show 11, and the Echo Show 8 (4th Gen). But Amazon has plenty of older, cheaper models available, too.
亚马逊开启了智能音箱的先河，但随着该公司发布越来越多的设备，寻找最适合的 Alexa 音箱变得越来越困难。亚马逊拥有目前市面上种类最丰富的智能音箱和显示屏，这可能会让人眼花缭乱。去年年底，亚马逊发布了最新产品：Echo Dot Max、Echo Studio（第二代）、Echo Show 11 和 Echo Show 8（第四代）。当然，亚马逊也依然提供许多更老、更便宜的型号。

If you're looking for something small and simple to control your home, either an Echo Dot ($50) or the Echo Dot Max ($100) is your best bet. The Dot Max has really impressive sound for its compact size and is my overall favorite Alexa speaker, but if you're only going to use it for voice commands rather than jam sessions, you can get away with the older, cheaper model, especially if you want to buy a few to place around your home. For anyone looking for a smart display, the Echo Show 11 ($220) is the best of Amazon's newest smart displays that come designed for the updated assistant, Alexa+. The older third-generation Echo Show 8 ($150) is still a great value if you want something cheaper.
如果你正在寻找一款小巧简单的设备来控制家居，Echo Dot（50 美元）或 Echo Dot Max（100 美元）是你的最佳选择。Dot Max 在其紧凑的体积下拥有令人印象深刻的音质，是我个人最喜欢的 Alexa 音箱。但如果你只是用它来下达语音指令，而不是为了享受音乐，那么购买更便宜的旧款型号就足够了，尤其是当你打算在家里多处放置设备时。对于寻找智能显示屏的用户，Echo Show 11（220 美元）是亚马逊为升级版助手 Alexa+ 设计的最新产品中表现最好的一款。如果你想省钱，老款的第三代 Echo Show 8（150 美元）依然具有很高的性价比。

### Best Overall Alexa Speaker: Amazon Echo Dot Max
### 最佳综合 Alexa 音箱：Amazon Echo Dot Max

Out of Amazon's latest smart speakers, one landed on top for me: the Echo Dot Max. It's still a similarly sized speaker to past Dot models (though this model is slightly taller at 4.27 inches while the older was 3.9 inches) but has much better sound than ever before, putting it on level with the Echo (4th Gen) we previously recommended. I was really impressed with the sound quality and volume for such a small speaker. It also doesn't lack in bass like smaller speakers usually do. Amazon is no longer selling the plain Echo, which was last updated in 2020, so the Echo Dot Max is really your only powerful sound option that isn't upwards of $150.
在亚马逊最新的智能音箱中，Echo Dot Max 脱颖而出，成为我的首选。它的尺寸与以往的 Dot 型号相近（尽管这款高度为 4.27 英寸，略高于旧款的 3.9 英寸），但音质有了质的飞跃，达到了我们之前推荐的 Echo（第四代）水平。对于这样一个小巧的音箱来说，其音质和音量令我印象深刻。它也没有像其他小型音箱那样低音不足。由于亚马逊已不再销售 2020 年最后一次更新的普通版 Echo，Echo Dot Max 实际上是你唯一能买到的、价格在 150 美元以下的强劲音质选择。

### Best Affordable Alexa Speaker: Amazon Echo Dot (5th Gen)
### 最佳高性价比 Alexa 音箱：Amazon Echo Dot（第五代）

The fifth-generation Echo Dot is a few years old now, but it's still a great little speaker. It's got powerful, robust sound for its size, and it works great if you want Alexa handy on a bookshelf or on a small side table. It's one I always find myself setting up somewhere in my house, whether it's in my hallway so I can ask quick questions as I move through the house, or in my bedroom as an alarm clock. It's some of the best sound you can get for this price, especially when it's on sale.
第五代 Echo Dot 虽然已经发布几年了，但它依然是一款出色的小型音箱。以它的体积而言，它拥有强劲而扎实的音质，非常适合放在书架或小边桌上随时呼唤 Alexa。我总会在家里放置它，无论是放在走廊里以便我在走动时快速询问信息，还是放在卧室里当作闹钟。在这个价位段，它能提供最好的音质体验，尤其是在促销时。

### Best Alexa Smart Display: Amazon Echo Show 11
### 最佳 Alexa 智能显示屏：Amazon Echo Show 11

I'm surprised to say that out of Amazon's two newest Echo Show devices, the new Echo Show 11 ended up being my favorite over the fourth-gen Echo Show 8. I actually liked the larger screen, and it didn't feel ridiculously large like the Echo Show 15 does or the Echo Show 10 before it, but it helps that the base is much smaller on the Show 11 than the Show 10's rotating, Wall-E-esque base. The Echo Show 11's screen is bright and crisp, and I loved how the landscape photo slideshows looked on it. There's a little side menu you can swipe to see things like your smart home favorites, and I found the sound quality immersive and more well-rounded than the new Show 8.
令我惊讶的是，在亚马逊两款最新的 Echo Show 设备中，Echo Show 11 最终比第四代 Echo Show 8 更得我心。我确实喜欢它更大的屏幕，而且它不会像 Echo Show 15 或之前的 Echo Show 10 那样显得过于庞大。此外，Show 11 的底座比 Show 10 那种可旋转的、类似“瓦力”的底座要小得多。Echo Show 11 的屏幕明亮清晰，我非常喜欢它播放横屏照片幻灯片的效果。侧边有一个可以滑出的菜单，方便查看智能家居收藏项，而且我觉得它的音质比新款 Show 8 更具沉浸感，也更均衡。
