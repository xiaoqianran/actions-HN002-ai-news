# Aligning Cellular Sheaves with Classifier Attention for Interpretable Weakly-Supervised Pathology Localization
# 将细胞层束与分类器注意力对齐：用于可解释的弱监督病理定位

**Abstract:** Weakly-supervised classification of whole-slide images with attention-based multiple instance learning (ABMIL) on top of foundation features now reaches near-saturation on Camelyon16 slide-level performance, but the corresponding attention maps are an imperfect localization signal: in clinical interpretation, a model that classifies correctly without firing on the actual lesion is hard to trust.

**摘要：** 基于基础特征的注意力多示例学习（ABMIL）在全切片图像的弱监督分类任务中，其在 Camelyon16 数据集上的切片级性能已接近饱和。然而，相应的注意力图作为定位信号并不完美：在临床解读中，如果一个模型分类正确但未能聚焦于实际病灶，那么该模型很难获得信任。

We address this gap with cellular sheaves, which equip each vertex and edge of a graph with a finite-dimensional vector space and consistent linear maps between them, providing a principled way to detect local disagreement on graph-structured data.

我们利用“细胞层束”（cellular sheaves）来弥补这一差距。该方法为图的每个顶点和边配备了一个有限维向量空间，并在它们之间建立了一致的线性映射，从而为检测图结构数据上的局部不一致性提供了一种原则性的方法。

We apply cellular sheaves to weakly-supervised tumour localization on whole-slide images, combining a sheaf disagreement field with ABMIL. The natural training objective, encouraging consistency between similar features, produces a disagreement field that tracks tissue-level texture rather than diagnostic content.

我们将细胞层束应用于全切片图像的弱监督肿瘤定位，将层束不一致场（sheaf disagreement field）与 ABMIL 相结合。最初的训练目标是鼓励相似特征之间的一致性，但这产生的不一致场往往追踪的是组织层面的纹理，而非诊断内容。

We propose attention-conditional consistency, which uses the classifier's attention to define which neighbouring patches should agree. Joint training of the classifier and the sheaf under this objective produces a disagreement field with patch-level AUC 0.940 on Camelyon16 and raises the attention head from its ABMIL-alone level of 0.717 to 0.953.

为此，我们提出了“注意力条件一致性”（attention-conditional consistency），利用分类器的注意力来定义哪些相邻切片应该保持一致。在此目标下对分类器和层束进行联合训练，在 Camelyon16 上实现了 0.940 的切片级 AUC，并将注意力头的性能从仅使用 ABMIL 时的 0.717 提升至 0.953。

Two-stage ablation with the classifier frozen at its ABMIL values reaches only 0.727 on the disagreement field and leaves attention at 0.717, confirming that the gain comes from the projector co-adapting under both objectives, not from the loss change in isolation.

通过将分类器固定在 ABMIL 数值上的两阶段消融实验显示，不一致场的 AUC 仅达到 0.727，注意力性能维持在 0.717。这证实了性能的提升源于投影器在两个目标下的协同适应，而非仅仅是损失函数改变的结果。

The trained model transfers without retraining to annotated slides from Camelyon17, maintaining Delta AUC 0.932 +/- 0.083 and attention AUC 0.955 +/- 0.099. The result is an attention map and a sheaf-disagreement map that fire on the same diagnostic regions, giving clinicians two complementary explanations for each slide-level prediction.

训练好的模型无需重新训练即可迁移至 Camelyon17 的标注切片，保持了 0.932 +/- 0.083 的 Delta AUC 和 0.955 +/- 0.099 的注意力 AUC。最终生成的注意力图和层束不一致图能够聚焦于相同的诊断区域，为临床医生对每个切片级的预测提供了两种互补的解释。
