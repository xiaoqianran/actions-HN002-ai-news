# Here’s seven bloody minutes of Wolverine on the PS5
# 《金刚狼》PS5版七分钟血腥实机演示公布

At its big State of Play show on Tuesday, Sony shared a new look at *Marvel’s Wolverine*, the next big title from Insomniac Games that’s launching exclusively on PS5 on September 15th. Dressed in the iconic yellow Wolverine outfit, Logan slunk around and stabbed his way through a bunch of enemies, using his blades to take them out. Combat looked weighty and satisfying — though the game is extremely bloody, with red flying everywhere as Logan fought his opponents.

在周二举办的“State of Play”大型发布会上，索尼展示了《漫威金刚狼》（Marvel’s Wolverine）的全新画面。这是 Insomniac Games 即将推出的重磅大作，将于 9 月 15 日在 PS5 平台独占发售。视频中，身着标志性黄色战衣的罗根（Logan）潜行并一路刺杀敌人，利爪所到之处，敌人纷纷倒下。战斗过程看起来沉重且令人满足——不过游戏画面极其血腥，罗根在与对手交战时，鲜血四溅的场面随处可见。

Wolverine has been a long time coming, with Sony and Insomniac initially announcing the game all the way back in 2021. At the time, details were scarce. But Sony finally showed off some gameplay in a separate State of Play in September 2025 and revealed the official release date in February.

《金刚狼》的开发历程漫长，索尼和 Insomniac 最早于 2021 年就公布了这款游戏。当时，关于游戏的细节少之又少。直到 2025 年 9 月的另一场“State of Play”活动中，索尼才终于展示了部分实机画面，并于今年 2 月公布了正式发售日期。

Insomniac has a long history with Marvel games; it was the studio behind the excellent Spider-Man game that launched on PS4 in 2018, and it followed that up with the spinoff Spider-Man: Miles Morales and a full sequel, Spider-Man 2, that starred both Peter Parker and Morales.

Insomniac 在漫威游戏开发方面拥有深厚的积淀；他们曾打造了 2018 年在 PS4 上推出的优秀作品《漫威蜘蛛侠》，随后又推出了衍生作《蜘蛛侠：迈尔斯·莫拉莱斯》，以及由彼得·帕克和迈尔斯共同主演的正统续作《蜘蛛侠 2》。
