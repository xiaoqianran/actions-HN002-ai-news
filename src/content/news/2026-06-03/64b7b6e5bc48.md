# Flow-Based Generative Modeling for Optimizing Sampling Policies in Compressed Sensing Applications
# 基于流生成模型的压缩感知采样策略优化

**Abstract:** Numerous modern applications in signal processing and medical imaging necessitate acquiring high-dimensional signals under tight resource constraints. Traditional sampling theory suggests that accurate signal reconstruction requires a number of measurements proportional to the signal's ambient dimension, a requirement often too expensive or impractical. Compressed sensing challenges this notion by demonstrating that sparse signals can be recovered with fewer measurements, provided the measurement operator meets certain conditions.

**摘要：** 现代信号处理和医学成像中的许多应用需要在严格的资源限制下获取高维信号。传统的采样理论认为，精确的信号重建所需的测量次数与信号的环境维度成正比，这一要求往往过于昂贵或不切实际。压缩感知挑战了这一观念，它证明了只要测量算子满足特定条件，稀疏信号就可以通过较少的测量次数进行恢复。

This proof-of-concept study presents a task-aware flow-based generative framework -- a reformulation of the conventional Flow Matching training paradigm with a flow model trained to optimize subsampling in compressed sensing applications. We establish the fundamental feasibility of the proposed framework of learning subsampling masks that substantially enhance the performance of compressed sensing for image classification, image reconstruction, and MRI acceleration.

这项概念验证研究提出了一种任务感知的流生成框架——这是对传统流匹配（Flow Matching）训练范式的重构，通过训练流模型来优化压缩感知应用中的子采样。我们确立了所提框架的基本可行性，即通过学习子采样掩码，显著提升了压缩感知在图像分类、图像重建和核磁共振（MRI）加速方面的性能。

For the image reconstruction task, our method demonstrated state-of-the-art performance, achieving Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio of 25.17 dB at the subsampling rate of 5\% on the CelebA dataset and 29.24 dB when reconstructing $8\times$ accelerated MRI measurements (fastMRI dataset) with the minimal computational overhead. These results highlight the effectiveness of task-conditioning within generative flow models and reveal a promising direction for representation learning strategies.

在图像重建任务中，我们的方法展现了最先进的性能：在 CelebA 数据集上以 5% 的子采样率实现了 25.17 dB 的峰值信噪比（PSNR），在重建 8 倍加速的 MRI 测量数据（fastMRI 数据集）时达到了 29.24 dB，且计算开销极小。这些结果凸显了生成流模型中任务条件化的有效性，并揭示了表征学习策略的一个有前景的方向。

Overall, the proposed framework offers a unified, flexible approach to designing data- and task-driven sensing schemes that can be potentially adapted to a broad range of inverse problems.

总的来说，该框架为设计数据驱动和任务驱动的传感方案提供了一种统一且灵活的方法，并有望应用于广泛的逆问题中。
