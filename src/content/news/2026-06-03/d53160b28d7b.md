# A Shared Valence Axis Across Modern LLMs and Human EEG: The Saturation Regularity
# 现代大语言模型与人类脑电图（EEG）共享效价轴：饱和规律

**Abstract:** Large language models (LLMs) have emerged as powerful representation learners whose internal features increasingly align with human cognition. We study whether modern LLMs can serve as a lens for understanding neural representations in the human brain, focusing on emotional valence in EEG.
**摘要：** 大语言模型（LLMs）已成为强大的表征学习器，其内部特征与人类认知的契合度日益提高。我们研究了现代大语言模型是否可以作为理解人类大脑神经表征的透镜，重点关注脑电图（EEG）中的情感效价。

We first build a one-dimensional valence direction, the V-axis, from modern LLMs using only nine emotion-evocative sentences. We validate it through zero-shot transfer to sentiment benchmarks and cross-model consistency across fourteen LLMs.
我们首先仅利用九个情感诱发句子，从现代大语言模型中构建了一个一维效价方向，即“V轴”。我们通过将其零样本迁移至情感基准测试，以及验证十四个大语言模型间的跨模型一致性，证实了该轴的有效性。

We then show that this LLM-derived direction maps onto human neural activity. On a public EEG cohort of 123 subjects watching affective videos, a single linear projection on EEG features tracks the V-axis position of each stimulus. Moreover, 36 EEG emotion classifiers trained without exposure to the V-axis spontaneously rediscover the same direction in their internal representations, suggesting that the same valence structure emerges in both language models and human electrophysiology.
随后，我们展示了这种源自大语言模型的方向可以映射到人类的神经活动上。在一个包含123名受试者观看情感视频的公开EEG队列中，对EEG特征进行单一线性投影，即可追踪每个刺激的V轴位置。此外，36个未接触过V轴的EEG情感分类器在训练后，自发地在其内部表征中重新发现了相同的方向，这表明语言模型和人类电生理学中出现了相同的效价结构。

Yet this convergence does not provide an effective training signal. We test twenty-five alignment strategies, including knowledge distillation, representational similarity, contrastive, and topographic losses; none improve decoding, and sixteen significantly reduce accuracy.
然而，这种趋同性并未提供有效的训练信号。我们测试了二十五种对齐策略，包括知识蒸馏、表征相似性、对比损失和拓扑损失；结果显示，没有一种策略能提高解码效果，且其中十六种策略显著降低了准确率。

We formalize this result as the saturation regularity: once task labels alone drive a brain-decoding network onto the target direction, additional supervision mainly distorts an already-saturated basin, while the load-bearing within-class residual receives little useful gradient. This regularity also indicates where improvement should come from: the residual subspace unreachable by supervision.
我们将这一结果形式化为“饱和规律”：一旦仅凭任务标签将大脑解码网络驱动至目标方向，额外的监督主要会扭曲已经饱和的基准区域，而承载信息的类内残差却无法获得有效的梯度。这一规律也指出了改进的方向：即监督无法触及的残差子空间。

Motivated by this insight, we ensemble across residual diversity rather than supervising the basin, improving balanced accuracy by 10.5% over the prior best on FACED, with the same effect replicated on SEED-V.
受此启发，我们通过整合残差多样性而非监督基准区域，在FACED数据集上将平衡准确率较此前最佳水平提升了10.5%，并在SEED-V数据集上复现了相同的效果。
