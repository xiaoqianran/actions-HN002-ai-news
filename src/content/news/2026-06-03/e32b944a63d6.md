# The truth lies in the past in Silo S3 trailer
# 《末日地堡》第三季预告片：真相深埋于过去

In April, we got a short teaser for the third season of Silo, the critically acclaimed Apple TV series based on the trilogy by novelist Hugh Howey, which hinted at a mysterious origin story dating back centuries. Apple TV just released the full trailer, and it looks like our heroine is again facing conflict and danger because she just keeps asking so many inconvenient questions.

今年四月，我们看到了 Apple TV 备受好评的剧集《末日地堡》（Silo）第三季的简短预告。该剧改编自小说家休·豪伊（Hugh Howey）的同名三部曲，预告片暗示了一个追溯至数百年前的神秘起源故事。Apple TV 刚刚发布了完整版预告片，看起来我们的女主角再次陷入了冲突与危险之中，只因她总是不断追问那些令人不安的问题。

As previously reported, Silo is set in a self-sustaining underground city inhabited by a community whose recorded history dates back only 140 years. The outside is a toxic hellscape that is only visible on big screens in the silo’s topmost level. The second season expanded Silo‘s world to incorporate the survivors in the second Silo 17; everyone else died in a revolt to escape to the surface. We discovered that there are 50 silos in all. Meanwhile, another revolution was brewing in Juliette’s (Rebecca Ferguson) original Silo 18 against Holland (Tim Robbins). And even more secrets were revealed.

正如之前报道的那样，《末日地堡》的故事发生在一个自给自足的地下城市，这里的居民所记录的历史仅有 140 年。外界是一个有毒的炼狱，只能通过地堡最顶层的巨型屏幕观察。第二季将地堡的世界观扩展到了 17 号地堡的幸存者——其他人都在试图逃往地表的反抗中丧生。我们发现总共有 50 个地堡。与此同时，朱丽叶（丽贝卡·弗格森饰）所在的 18 号地堡中，一场针对霍兰（蒂姆·罗宾斯饰）的革命正在酝酿，更多秘密也随之揭开。

In the season finale, Juliette returned to her silo and warned the residents not to leave, but she and Holland ended up locked in the incinerator just as it was being fired up. The final scene was a flashback, showing a woman questioning a congressman in Washington, DC, about possible retaliation after the US dropped a dirty bomb on Iran. And that brings us to S3.

在第二季大结局中，朱丽叶回到地堡并警告居民不要离开，但她和霍兰最终被锁在焚化炉中，而焚化炉恰好被启动。最后一幕是一个闪回镜头，展示了一名女子在华盛顿特区质问一名国会议员，内容涉及美国向伊朗投掷脏弹后可能引发的报复。这便引出了第三季的故事。

Per the official premise: Season three of Silo continues the saga of a dystopian society of 10,000 people living underground under mysterious circumstances, while revealing an origin story set centuries earlier. In the present, Juliette Nichols (Ferguson) survives her forced ‘cleaning’ but returns with memory loss as the silo recovers from rebellion and faces a dangerous new threat. Meanwhile, in the ‘Before Times,’ journalist Helen Drew (Jessica Henwick) and Congressman Daniel Keene (Ashley Zukerman) uncover a conspiracy that pulls them into a chain of events with catastrophic, irreversible consequences.

根据官方剧情简介：第三季继续讲述这个由一万人在神秘环境下生活在底下的反乌托邦社会的故事，同时揭示了数百年前的起源。在当下，朱丽叶·尼科尔斯（弗格森饰）在被迫“清理”后幸存，但因失忆而归，此时地堡正从叛乱中恢复，并面临着新的危险威胁。与此同时，在“过去时代”，记者海伦·德鲁（杰西卡·亨维克饰）和国会议员丹尼尔·基恩（阿什利·祖克曼饰）揭开了一个阴谋，将他们卷入了一系列具有灾难性且不可逆转后果的事件中。

In addition to Ferguson, returning cast members include Common as head of security Robert Sims; Harriet Walter as agoraphobic electrical engineer Martha; Avi Nash as IT systems analyst Lukas; Rick Gomez as maintenance worker Patrick; Chinaza Uche as chief deputy Paul Billings; Shane McRae as Knox; Remmie Milner as Shirley; Alexandria Riley as Camille Sims; Clare Perkins as Carla; Billy Postlethwaite as Deputy Hank; and Steve Zahn as Jimmy Connor/"Solo,” sole survivor of the Silo 17 rebellion. In addition to Henwick and Zukerman, other new cast members include Laura Innes, Jessica Brown Findlay, Morven Christie, Reed Birney, Matt Craven, and Colin Hanks.

除了弗格森，回归的演员阵容还包括：饰演安保主管罗伯特·西姆斯的 Common；饰演患有广场恐惧症的电气工程师玛莎的哈丽特·沃特；饰演 IT 系统分析师卢卡斯的阿维·纳什；饰演维修工帕特里克的里克·戈麦斯；饰演首席副手保罗·比林斯的奇纳扎·乌切；饰演诺克斯的肖恩·麦克雷；饰演雪莉的雷米·米尔纳；饰演卡米尔·西姆斯的亚历山大·莱利；饰演卡拉的克莱尔·珀金斯；饰演副手汉克的比利·波斯尔思韦特；以及饰演 17 号地堡叛乱唯一幸存者吉米·康纳（“独行侠”）的史蒂夫·茨恩。除了亨维克和祖克曼，其他新加入的演员还包括劳拉·因尼斯、杰西卡·布朗·芬德利、莫文·克里斯蒂、里德·伯尼、马特·克拉文和科林·汉克斯。

The trailer opens with a flashback to Juliette’s rescue from the incinerator; apparently, she was in that “box of fire” for three minutes. (There is no sign of Holland.) And three months later, she still has no memories “other than those you’ve given her,” although she does occasionally get “splinters of memories.” We also catch a glimpse of a mysterious note urging “Don’t take the pills”—advice she doesn’t seem to have taken. It all matters because Juliette is the only inhabitant who has ever gone outside and survived. And it seems the silo residents might not have much time left.

预告片以朱丽叶从焚化炉中获救的闪回镜头开场；显然，她在那个“火箱”里待了三分钟。（没有霍兰的踪迹。）三个月后，她仍然没有记忆，“除了你们给她的那些”，尽管她偶尔会闪现“记忆碎片”。我们还瞥见了一张神秘的字条，上面写着“别吃药”——但她似乎并没有听从这个建议。这一切都很重要，因为朱丽叶是唯一一个去过外界并幸存下来的居民。而且看起来，地堡居民剩下的时间不多了。

“If the founders cared so much, why do they need to kill a silo?” Juliette asks, while Camille worries that even asking such questions will get them all killed. Juliette has a plan to protect everyone, but it just might cost her life. And then we go far into the past, watching scores of people entering what looks like a new silo. “The end of the world cannot be stopped,” someone named Pierce tells Zukerman’s Congressman. “It can only be survived.”

“如果创始人们真的那么在乎，为什么他们要摧毁一个地堡？”朱丽叶问道，而卡米尔则担心仅仅提出这些问题就会让他们丧命。朱丽叶有一个保护所有人的计划，但这可能会让她付出生命的代价。随后，镜头回溯到遥远的过去，看着成群的人进入看起来像是新地堡的地方。“世界末日无法阻止，”一个叫皮尔斯的人对祖克曼饰演的国会议员说，“只能设法生存下去。”

The third season of Silo premieres on Apple TV on July 3, 2026, with new episodes airing every Friday through September 4. It has already been renewed for a fourth and final season.

《末日地堡》第三季将于 2026 年 7 月 3 日在 Apple TV 首播，新剧集每周五更新，直至 9 月 4 日。该剧已获续订第四季，并将作为最终季。
