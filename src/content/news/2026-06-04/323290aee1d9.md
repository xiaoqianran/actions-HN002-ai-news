# Carvana ties up with Bezos-backed Slate Auto as it plans new car sales
# Carvana 与贝索斯支持的 Slate Auto 达成合作，计划进军新车销售市场

Carvana has been granted the option to invest in Slate Auto, the electric vehicle startup backed by Jeff Bezos, according to documents obtained by TechCrunch. Paperwork filed with Delaware’s division of corporations shows that the online used car retailer was given a warrant to buy shares in the startup in 2025 — around the same time Slate Auto was starting to put together its $650 million Series C funding round. It’s not clear if Carvana has exercised that warrant, or how many shares it is allowed to buy. Carvana declined to comment, and Slate Auto didn’t respond to requests for comment on the deal.

根据 TechCrunch 获得的文件显示，在线二手车零售商 Carvana 已获得投资电动汽车初创公司 Slate Auto 的选择权，该公司由杰夫·贝索斯（Jeff Bezos）支持。提交给特拉华州公司部门的文件显示，Carvana 在 2025 年获得了购买该初创公司股份的认股权证，这与 Slate Auto 开始筹集 6.5 亿美元 C 轮融资的时间大致吻合。目前尚不清楚 Carvana 是否已经行使了该认股权证，也不清楚其获准购买的股份数量。Carvana 拒绝置评，Slate Auto 也未回应置评请求。

The transaction with Carvana comes as the retailer is looking at ways to expand into new car sales, according to the Wall Street Journal. The company has reportedly purchased a number of Stellantis dealerships across the United States. Asked about new car sales on a recent earnings call, CEO Ernie Garcia III told analysts to “stay tuned.”

据《华尔街日报》报道，Carvana 与 Slate Auto 的这笔交易正值该公司寻求进军新车销售市场之际。据报道，Carvana 已在美国各地收购了多家 Stellantis 经销商。在最近的财报电话会议上，当被问及新车销售计划时，首席执行官 Ernie Garcia III 告诉分析师们“敬请期待”。

Slate Auto is also just weeks away from announcing final pricing and taking the first nonrefundable preorders for its low-cost EV, which is expected to start in the mid-$20,000 range. Slate has said it will deliver its first vehicles by the end of this year. Similar to Tesla and other all-electric car companies like Rivian, Slate says on its website that it “won’t have traditional dealerships.” The company has said it will sell vehicles directly to customers, but it hasn’t offered much detail about how it plans to handle the logistics of the car-buying experience. Selling through physical Carvana dealerships could help mitigate some of those logistics headaches while also raising the startup’s profile.

Slate Auto 距离宣布最终定价并接受其低成本电动汽车的首批不可退还预订单仅剩几周时间，预计起售价将在 2 万美元中段。Slate 表示，将于今年年底前交付首批车辆。与特斯拉及 Rivian 等纯电动汽车公司类似，Slate 在其官网上表示将“不设传统经销商”。该公司曾表示将直接向客户销售车辆，但并未提供关于如何处理购车体验物流环节的详细信息。通过 Carvana 的实体经销商进行销售，或许有助于缓解部分物流难题，同时也能提升该初创公司的知名度。

Slate has been tight-lipped about its investors since it emerged from stealth last year, shortly after TechCrunch first revealed that Bezos and Guggenheim Partners CEO Mark Walter were backing the company. Slate revealed in April that Walter’s firm TWG Global led the Series C round, making the businessman one of the startup’s largest shareholders. Walter also holds a major stake in Carvana. He owns 8% of the company’s Class B common stock and 1% of the overall voting power. Only Garcia III and his son, Ernie Garcia II, have more control.

自去年走出隐身状态以来，Slate 对其投资者一直守口如瓶。此前 TechCrunch 曾率先披露贝索斯和古根海姆合伙公司（Guggenheim Partners）首席执行官马克·沃尔特（Mark Walter）是该公司的支持者。Slate 在四月份透露，沃尔特旗下的 TWG Global 领投了其 C 轮融资，这使得这位商人成为该初创公司最大的股东之一。沃尔特同时持有 Carvana 的大量股份，拥有该公司 8% 的 B 类普通股和 1% 的总投票权。目前仅有 Garcia III 及其子 Ernie Garcia II 拥有更大的控制权。

It’s possible Carvana has already revealed some details about the Slate tie-up to investors without naming the startup. In March, Carvana revealed in a regulatory filing that it had been granted a warrant to purchase shares of a “private consumer products company” in June 2025. Carvana did not name the company but said the aggregate value of the warrant was $1.5 million at the end of 2025 and that it “vests in tranches through 2029 based on jointly determined performance goals.” Carvana noted that Walter has a “substantial ownership interest in the warrant issuer.” Carvana did not say whether this was a reference to Slate or another company in Walter’s portfolio.

Carvana 可能已经向投资者披露了有关与 Slate 合作的部分细节，只是未点名该初创公司。今年 3 月，Carvana 在一份监管文件中披露，其已获得一份认股权证，可于 2025 年 6 月购买一家“私人消费品公司”的股份。Carvana 未透露该公司名称，但表示该认股权证在 2025 年底的总价值为 150 万美元，并将“根据共同确定的绩效目标在 2029 年前分批归属”。Carvana 指出，沃尔特在“该认股权证发行方中拥有大量所有权权益”。Carvana 并未说明这是否指代 Slate 或沃尔特投资组合中的另一家公司。
