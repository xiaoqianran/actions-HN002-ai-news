# Microsoft Agents League Hackathon

🚀 Ready to build AI agents and compete for prizes? Microsoft has launched the Agents League Hackathon, a global competition where you can develop intelligent agents using technologies such as GitHub Copilot, Microsoft Foundry, and Microsoft 365 Copilot.
🚀 准备好构建人工智能代理并角逐奖项了吗？微软已正式启动“Agents League Hackathon”，这是一项全球性竞赛，你将能够利用 GitHub Copilot、Microsoft Foundry 和 Microsoft 365 Copilot 等技术开发智能代理。

🏆 Over $55,000 USD in prizes. 💡 Free participation. 🌎 Open to developers, students, and professionals from all over the world.
🏆 奖金总额超过 55,000 美元。💡 免费参与。🌎 面向全球开发者、学生及专业人士开放。

You can compete in three categories:
你可以参加以下三个类别的角逐：

🔹 Creative Apps: Build innovative applications using AI-assisted development.
🔹 创意应用 (Creative Apps)：利用人工智能辅助开发构建创新应用程序。

🔹 Reasoning Agents: Develop agents capable of solving complex problems through multi-step reasoning.
🔹 推理代理 (Reasoning Agents)：开发能够通过多步推理解决复杂问题的代理。

🔹 Enterprise Agents: Create enterprise solutions on Microsoft 365 Copilot.
🔹 企业代理 (Enterprise Agents)：基于 Microsoft 365 Copilot 创建企业级解决方案。

📅 Registration is open until June 12, 2026. 📅 Project submission deadline is June 14, 2026.
📅 注册截止日期为 2026 年 6 月 12 日。📅 项目提交截止日期为 2026 年 6 月 14 日。

If you are interested in Artificial Intelligence, Copilot, autonomous agents, or modern AI development, this is an excellent opportunity to learn, create, and demonstrate your skills.
如果你对人工智能、Copilot、自主代理或现代 AI 开发感兴趣，这是一个学习、创造并展示你技能的绝佳机会。
