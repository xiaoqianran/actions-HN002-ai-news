# COD10K-C: Benchmarking Robustness of Camouflaged Object Detection Under Natural Image Corruptions
# COD10K-C：自然图像退化下伪装目标检测的鲁棒性基准测试

**Abstract:** Camouflaged object detection has improved substantially, but most standard benchmarks evaluate models only on clean images. This is not realistic because real cameras often capture blur, sensor noise, weather effects, and compression artifacts. We present COD10K-C, a corruption robustness benchmark based on COD10K. It includes 8 corruption types and 5 severity levels, giving 40 conditions and 81,040 evaluation pairs in total.

**摘要：** 伪装目标检测技术已取得显著进步，但大多数标准基准测试仅在干净图像上评估模型。这并不符合现实情况，因为真实相机拍摄的图像往往伴随模糊、传感器噪声、天气影响和压缩伪影。我们提出了 COD10K-C，这是一个基于 COD10K 的退化鲁棒性基准测试。它包含 8 种退化类型和 5 个严重程度等级，总计涵盖 40 种条件和 81,040 个评估对。

We evaluate three popular camouflaged object detection models, SINet-v2, PFNet, and ZoomNet, as well as a lightweight model called RobustCODLite. All models show clear performance drops on corrupted images. Motion blur and Gaussian blur cause the largest drops, with SINet-v2 losing 18.5 Dice points under motion blur. Brightness and fog are less harmful.

我们评估了三种主流的伪装目标检测模型（SINet-v2、PFNet 和 ZoomNet）以及一种名为 RobustCODLite 的轻量级模型。所有模型在退化图像上均表现出明显的性能下降。运动模糊和高斯模糊导致的性能下降最为严重，其中 SINet-v2 在运动模糊下的 Dice 系数下降了 18.5 个点。亮度变化和雾气的影响相对较小。

RobustCODLite uses corruption augmentation, a frequency-prior branch, and an uncertainty-consistency loss. It retains 92.3% of its clean Dice score under corruption, compared with 87.7% for SINet-v2, 84.8% for ZoomNet, and 84.1% for PFNet. On the hardest corruptions, RobustCODLite matches or outperforms models that perform better on clean data. We will release the COD10K-C GitHub repository to support future research in robust camouflaged object detection.

RobustCODLite 采用了退化增强、频率先验分支以及不确定性一致性损失函数。在退化条件下，它保留了 92.3% 的原始 Dice 分数，而 SINet-v2、ZoomNet 和 PFNet 分别仅保留了 87.7%、84.8% 和 84.1%。在最严苛的退化条件下，RobustCODLite 的表现能够持平甚至超越那些在干净数据上表现更好的模型。我们将发布 COD10K-C 的 GitHub 仓库，以支持未来在鲁棒伪装目标检测领域的研究。
