# Plan2Map: A Multimodal Benchmark for Document-Grounded Geospatial Boundary Reconstruction from Planning Records
# Plan2Map：基于规划记录的文档驱动地理空间边界重建多模态基准

**Abstract:** Planning records define restrictions over geographic areas, but their source documents often provide only indirect spatial evidence rather than machine-readable boundaries. We introduce Plan2Map, a 208-case multimodal benchmark for document-grounded geospatial boundary reconstruction from UK planning records.
**摘要：** 规划记录定义了地理区域的限制，但其原始文档通常仅提供间接的空间证据，而非机器可读的边界。我们推出了 Plan2Map，这是一个包含 208 个案例的多模态基准，旨在从英国规划记录中进行基于文档的地理空间边界重建。

Given only a source planning document, systems must reconstruct a valid geospatial boundary from notice text, schedules, map plates, map labels, and boundary annotations; the reference GeoJSON is held out for scoring. We propose GeoPlanAgent, a document-grounded, geospatial-tool-in-the-loop system that decomposes the task into evidence extraction, localisation, map registration, boundary segmentation, projection, and verification.
仅给定一份原始规划文档，系统必须从通知文本、附表、地图图版、地图标签和边界注释中重建有效的地理空间边界；参考用的 GeoJSON 文件被保留用于评分。我们提出了 GeoPlanAgent，这是一个基于文档、引入地理空间工具的系统，它将任务分解为证据提取、定位、地图配准、边界分割、投影和验证。

On Plan2Map, GeoPlanAgent achieves 0.736 mean IoU and 0.904 median IoU, with 67.8% of predictions at or above 0.8 IoU, substantially outperforming direct VLM-to-GeoJSON baselines. Diagnostic analysis shows that direct VLM prediction remains unreliable, while remaining errors are concentrated in localisation and map registration, and supervised boundary segmentation substantially improves pixel-level mask quality.
在 Plan2Map 上，GeoPlanAgent 实现了 0.736 的平均 IoU 和 0.904 的中位数 IoU，其中 67.8% 的预测结果达到或超过了 0.8 IoU，显著优于直接使用 VLM（视觉语言模型）到 GeoJSON 的基准方法。诊断分析表明，直接的 VLM 预测仍然不可靠，剩余的误差主要集中在定位和地图配准上，而监督边界分割显著提高了像素级的掩码质量。

Plan2Map provides a concrete testbed for multimodal geospatial reconstruction from public planning records.
Plan2Map 为从公共规划记录中进行多模态地理空间重建提供了一个具体的测试平台。
