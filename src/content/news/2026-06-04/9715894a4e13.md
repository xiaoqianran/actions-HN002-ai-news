# Cross-Modal Contrastive Learning of ECG and Angiography Representations for Severe Stenosis Classification
# 用于严重狭窄分类的心电图与血管造影表征的跨模态对比学习

**Abstract:** Coronary artery stenosis is a common cardiovascular disease, with severe, untreated cases posing significant risks of heart attack. Although coronary (X-ray) angiograms remain the standard for stenosis diagnosis, they are invasive, time- and resource-intensive, and therefore only performed on patients with a high probability of disease based on symptoms and prior clinical tests. 

**摘要：** 冠状动脉狭窄是一种常见的心血管疾病，严重且未经治疗的病例会带来巨大的心脏病发作风险。尽管冠状动脉（X射线）血管造影术仍然是诊断狭窄的金标准，但该检查具有侵入性，且耗时耗力，因此通常仅针对根据症状和既往临床检查判断患病概率较高的患者进行。

However, a subset of patients, especially those without symptoms, may remain undiagnosed. Detecting indications of stenosis from ECGs, which are fast, cheap, non-invasive, and thus routinely acquired even in asymptomatic patients, would support early diagnosis. However, as no reliable stenosis-specific signal has been identified in ECGs, they can not currently be used for stenosis risk stratification. 

然而，部分患者（尤其是无症状患者）可能无法得到及时诊断。心电图（ECG）检查快速、廉价且无创，即使在无症状患者中也常被常规采集，若能从中检测出狭窄迹象，将有助于早期诊断。但由于目前尚未在心电图中识别出可靠的狭窄特异性信号，心电图目前还无法用于狭窄风险分层。

To address this, we introduce StenCE, a pretraining framework, allowing stratification of patients based on features derived directly from ECGs. Evaluations across varying stenosis severity thresholds and additional ECG disease classification tasks demonstrate consistent performance improvements across different ECG encoders, outperforming previous work. 

为了解决这一问题，我们引入了 StenCE，这是一个预训练框架，允许根据直接从心电图中提取的特征对患者进行分层。在不同狭窄严重程度阈值下的评估以及额外的心电图疾病分类任务表明，该框架在不同的心电图编码器上均表现出持续的性能提升，优于以往的研究工作。

The obtained models successfully detect signals for stenosis diagnosis in ECGs and are the first to achieve high performance in severe stenosis classification. The source code is available at this https URL.

所获得的模型成功检测到了心电图中用于狭窄诊断的信号，并且是首个在严重狭窄分类中实现高性能的模型。源代码可在该链接获取。
