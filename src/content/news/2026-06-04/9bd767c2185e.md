# Uber's $1,500/month AI limit is a useful signal for AI tool pricing
# Uber 每月 1,500 美元的 AI 使用限额：AI 工具定价的风向标

Uber Caps Usage of AI Tools Like Claude Code to Manage Costs. I wrote the other day about Uber blowing its 2026 AI budget in four months, and how that wasn't particularly surprising given they would have set that budget in 2025, before anyone could have predicted how popular token-burning coding agents were about to become.
Uber 限制了 Claude Code 等 AI 工具的使用以控制成本。前几天我写过 Uber 在四个月内就花光了 2026 年全年的 AI 预算，这并不令人意外，因为该预算是在 2025 年设定的，当时没人能预见到“吞噬” Token 的编程智能体（coding agents）会变得如此流行。

Natalie Lung for Bloomberg: The rideshare giant is limiting all employees to $1,500 in monthly token spending per AI coding tool, an Uber spokesperson said in response to a Bloomberg News inquiry. That means spending on one tool doesn’t have a bearing on the budget for another. The limits, which have been instituted in recent months, only apply to agentic coding software such as Cursor or Anthropic PBC’s Claude Code.
据彭博社记者 Natalie Lung 报道：Uber 发言人在回应彭博新闻社询问时表示，这家网约车巨头正在限制所有员工每月在每个 AI 编程工具上的 Token 支出，上限为 1,500 美元。这意味着在一个工具上的支出不会影响另一个工具的预算。这些近几个月实施的限制措施，仅适用于 Cursor 或 Anthropic PBC 的 Claude Code 等智能体编程软件。

A $1,500 monthly limit per tool strikes me as a rational policy response to over-spending, and much more sensible than those tokenmaxxing leaderboards encouraging employees to compete for as much AI usage as possible.
在我看来，每个工具每月 1,500 美元的限额是对超支问题的理性政策回应，这比那些鼓励员工竞相尽可能多地使用 AI 的“Token 消耗排行榜”要明智得多。

It's also interesting in that it hints at a real dollar value for what Uber is getting out of these tools. If we assume two actively used tools per engineer that's $3,000 * 12 = $36,000 cap per engineer per year. Levels.fyi lists the median yearly compensation package for Uber software engineers in the USA at $330,000. That means each employee's AI spending cap is ~11% of that median compensation package.
有趣的是，这暗示了 Uber 从这些工具中获得的实际价值。如果我们假设每位工程师活跃使用两个工具，那么每位工程师每年的上限就是 3,000 美元 * 12 = 36,000 美元。根据 Levels.fyi 的数据，美国 Uber 软件工程师的年度薪酬中位数为 330,000 美元。这意味着每位员工的 AI 支出上限约占该薪酬中位数的 11%。

I noted that my own token usage comes to about $1,000/month against each of Anthropic and OpenAI - which currently costs me just $100 per provider thanks to their generous subsidized plans for individual subscribers. Those plans are no longer available to larger companies like Uber. Their new policy means if I were working at Uber I'd still have ~$500/month of tokens to spare for each of those tools, given my current usage patterns.
我注意到我自己在 Anthropic 和 OpenAI 上的 Token 使用量每月各约为 1,000 美元——由于它们为个人订阅者提供了慷慨的补贴计划，目前我每个供应商只需支付 100 美元。但这些计划不再适用于像 Uber 这样的大型公司。这意味着如果我在 Uber 工作，按照我目前的使用习惯，我每个工具每月仍有约 500 美元的 Token 额度可用。
