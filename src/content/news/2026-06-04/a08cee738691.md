# GeoDrive-Bench: Benchmarking Region-Specific Multimodal Reasoning in Autonomous Driving
# GeoDrive-Bench：自动驾驶中特定区域多模态推理的基准测试

Vision-language models (VLMs) for autonomous driving have shown promising performance, but their ability to handle region-specific traffic rules remains underexplored, raising uncertainties about their deployment across diverse global settings. We therefore introduce GeoDrive-Bench, a novel benchmark that enables the systematic investigation of VLMs' geo-culturally grounded driving reasoning.

用于自动驾驶的视觉语言模型（VLM）已展现出令人期待的性能，但它们处理特定区域交通规则的能力仍未得到充分探索，这引发了对其在全球不同环境下部署的不确定性。因此，我们推出了 GeoDrive-Bench，这是一个全新的基准测试，旨在系统地研究 VLM 在地理文化背景下的驾驶推理能力。

We curated 5,053 human-validated multiple-choice QA pairs across six countries covering diverse driving cultures. Specifically, we emphasize four driving tasks: perception, prediction, planning, and region reasoning. Each question requires models to infer the correct driving behavior from visual evidence and local traffic conventions without explicit country labels.

我们整理了涵盖六个国家、涉及多种驾驶文化的 5,053 个经人工验证的多项选择问答对。具体而言，我们重点关注四项驾驶任务：感知、预测、规划和区域推理。每个问题都要求模型在没有明确国家标签的情况下，根据视觉证据和当地交通惯例推断出正确的驾驶行为。

Beyond evaluation, we further design a distillation algorithm that injects region-specific traffic-rule knowledge into the internal representations of VLMs, enabling models to better align visual scene understanding with local driving policies.

除了评估之外，我们还设计了一种蒸馏算法，将特定区域的交通规则知识注入到 VLM 的内部表示中，使模型能够更好地将视觉场景理解与当地的驾驶策略相对齐。

Experiments on nine state-of-the-art VLMs show substantial performance variations across geo-driving cultures for each task, while our proposed baseline models exhibit improved geo-cultural reasoning across regions. These results suggest that current VLMs still lack robust region-aware driving intelligence and highlight GeoDrive-Bench as a diagnostic and training-oriented testbed for deployable autonomous driving foundation models.

在九个最先进的 VLM 上进行的实验表明，各模型在不同地理驾驶文化下的任务表现存在显著差异，而我们提出的基准模型在跨区域的地理文化推理方面表现出了改进。这些结果表明，当前的 VLM 仍然缺乏稳健的区域感知驾驶智能，并突显了 GeoDrive-Bench 作为可部署自动驾驶基础模型的诊断和训练导向测试平台的作用。
