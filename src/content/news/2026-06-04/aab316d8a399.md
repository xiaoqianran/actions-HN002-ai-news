# Human-in-the-Loop Contextual Bandits for Short-Term Rental Dynamic Pricing: Structural Equivalence of Historical Warm-Up and Approval-Gated Live Learning

**Title:** Human-in-the-Loop Contextual Bandits for Short-Term Rental Dynamic Pricing: Structural Equivalence of Historical Warm-Up and Approval-Gated Live Learning
**标题：** 用于短期租赁动态定价的“人在回路”情境多臂老虎机：历史预热与审批门控实时学习的结构等价性

**Abstract:** Dynamic pricing in short-term rental (STR) markets presents a distinctive challenge for online learning algorithms: pricing decisions carry significant financial risk, operators require explainability, and market feedback is sparse (one booking outcome per listed night).
**摘要：** 短期租赁（STR）市场的动态定价为在线学习算法带来了独特的挑战：定价决策伴随着巨大的财务风险，运营商要求算法具备可解释性，且市场反馈稀疏（每个挂牌夜仅有一个预订结果）。

We introduce the Human-in-the-Loop Gated Bandit (HITL-GB) framework, in which a contextual bandit algorithm generates price recommendations but a human agent retains authority to accept, modify, or reject each recommendation before it is applied.
我们引入了“人在回路”门控多臂老虎机（HITL-GB）框架。在该框架中，情境多臂老虎机算法生成价格建议，但在建议应用之前，人类代理人保留接受、修改或拒绝每一项建议的权限。

We show that under this approval constraint, historical pricing data -- collected under a prior deterministic policy -- is structurally equivalent to on-policy warm-up data for initialising the bandit's posterior, bypassing the weeks-to-months cold-start period that renders pure online bandit learning impractical in sparse-feedback markets.
我们证明，在这种审批约束下，在先前的确定性策略下收集的历史定价数据，在初始化老虎机后验概率方面，与在线策略预热数据在结构上是等价的。这绕过了在稀疏反馈市场中使纯在线老虎机学习变得不切实际的数周至数月的冷启动期。

We formalise the approval-gated reward signal, derive a regularised ridge-regression warm-up procedure from historical episodes, and validate the approach on real STR production data (anonymised urban market, 2 rooms, April 2022 -- April 2026, 1,461 nightly pricing episodes).
我们将审批门控奖励信号形式化，从历史片段中推导出正则化岭回归预热程序，并在真实的 STR 生产数据（匿名城市市场，2 个房间，2022 年 4 月至 2026 年 4 月，1,461 个夜间定价片段）上验证了该方法。

Our warm-up procedure compresses effective cold-start from ~150 episodes to ~30 episodes when initialising agents from the Hierarchical Factored Thompson Sampling (HF-TS) family.
当使用分层因子汤普森采样（HF-TS）系列的代理进行初始化时，我们的预热程序将有效的冷启动时间从约 150 个片段压缩至约 30 个片段。

We further argue that the structural equivalence result is domain-agnostic: any high-stakes domain where human approval is legally or operationally required -- including clinical drug dosing, credit origination, content moderation, and radiological diagnosis -- satisfies the same conditions and benefits from the same warm-up strategy.
我们进一步指出，这种结构等价性结果与领域无关：任何在法律或操作上要求人工审批的高风险领域——包括临床药物剂量设定、信贷发放、内容审核和放射学诊断——都满足相同的条件，并能从相同的预热策略中受益。

In regulated industries, mandatory human oversight is thus a statistical asset rather than a deployment constraint.
因此，在受监管的行业中，强制性的人工监督是一种统计资产，而非部署限制。
