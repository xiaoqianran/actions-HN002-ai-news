# The best Qi2 batteries for iPhone and Pixel
# 适用于 iPhone 和 Pixel 的最佳 Qi2 充电宝

We tested seven Qi2 and Qi2.2 batteries to find the best ones to snap on the back of your MagSafe or Pixelsnap phones.
我们测试了七款 Qi2 和 Qi2.2 充电宝，旨在为您找出最适合吸附在 MagSafe 或 Pixelsnap 手机背面的产品。

Compact power banks have gotten a lot faster in the past year — and it’s not just their USB-C charging speeds that have received a boost. The newest Qi2.2-certified models can wirelessly charge an iPhone 16 or later at up to 25W. Combine that with their ability to magnetically snap on via MagSafe, and you’ve got yourself an essential add-on that’s easy to take on the go.
过去一年里，紧凑型充电宝的速度有了显著提升，而且不仅仅是 USB-C 的充电速度得到了增强。最新的 Qi2.2 认证型号可以为 iPhone 16 或更新机型提供最高 25W 的无线充电。再加上它们通过 MagSafe 磁吸连接的特性，这便成了你随身携带的必备配件。

Qi2 is a magnetic wireless charging standard that’s based on Apple’s MagSafe tech, so almost all modern iPhones support it. While the latest iPhones offer the fastest charging rates, older models (dating back to the iPhone 12) support 15W Qi2 charging speeds, which is still suitably quick.
Qi2 是一种基于苹果 MagSafe 技术的磁吸无线充电标准，因此几乎所有现代 iPhone 都支持它。虽然最新的 iPhone 提供最快的充电速率，但旧款机型（追溯到 iPhone 12）也支持 15W 的 Qi2 充电速度，这依然足够快捷。

On the other hand, Android phones are a mixed bag, with very few models supporting Qi2, let alone Qi2.2. Google’s Pixel 10, 10 Pro, and 10 Pro Fold support Qi2 charging at up to 15W; the 10 Pro XL supports Qi 2.2 at up to 25W. Samsung’s Galaxy 26 lineup is “Qi2 Ready,” which means they can use Qi2 chargers at up to 15W if they have a magnet case. And that’s pretty much it.
另一方面，安卓手机的情况则参差不齐，很少有型号支持 Qi2，更不用说 Qi2.2 了。谷歌的 Pixel 10、10 Pro 和 10 Pro Fold 支持最高 15W 的 Qi2 充电；10 Pro XL 则支持最高 25W 的 Qi2.2 充电。三星 Galaxy 26 系列属于“Qi2 就绪”设备，这意味着如果配备磁吸手机壳，它们可以使用最高 15W 的 Qi2 充电器。目前基本情况就是这样。

### How we tested
### 测试方法

We tested charging speeds for one hour. We slapped each power bank onto an iPhone 17 Pro Max for an hour at a time, then onto a Google Pixel 10 Pro XL (both without cases on). We picked these phones because they support Qi2.2 25W speeds and they have big batteries, making it a tough job for power banks to refill them.
我们对充电速度进行了为期一小时的测试。我们将每款充电宝分别吸附在 iPhone 17 Pro Max 和 Google Pixel 10 Pro XL（均不带手机壳）上各一小时。我们选择这些手机是因为它们支持 Qi2.2 25W 充电速度且电池容量大，这对充电宝的补电能力提出了严峻考验。

Modern phones rarely charge at full speed. They adjust their charging rates dynamically based on factors including current charge level and temperature. They charge fastest when the battery is near empty and slowest when it’s above 80 percent. Thanks to the battery killer that is *Honkai: Star Rail*, we were able to test each phone from a completely dead state.
现代手机很少以全速充电。它们会根据当前电量和温度等因素动态调整充电速率。电池电量接近耗尽时充电最快，超过 80% 时最慢。多亏了“电池杀手”《崩坏：星穹铁道》，我们得以让每台手机在完全没电的状态下进行测试。

### The Qi2.2 battery with the fastest wireless charging speeds: Baseus PicoGo AM52
### 无线充电速度最快的 Qi2.2 充电宝：倍思 PicoGo AM52

The Baseus PicoGo AM52 is about as no-frills as power banks come, both in design and features. The all-black battery pack has a sleek aluminum case, with soft-touch silicone on the magnetic side to prevent it from scratching your phone while charging.
倍思 PicoGo AM52 在设计和功能上都非常简洁。这款全黑色的充电宝拥有时尚的铝合金外壳，磁吸面采用亲肤硅胶材质，防止在充电时刮伤手机。

The AM52 consistently offered the fastest wireless charging speeds of any power bank I tested, and it’s often the most affordable of the bunch. It wirelessly charged an iPhone 17 Pro Max to 65 percent in one hour and the Pixel 10 Pro XL to 50 percent. Baseus’s model lacks a built-in display — meaning you can’t see the remaining charge — but two of the four LED indicators were still full after an hour of wireless charging, indicating it still had some juice left.
AM52 在我测试的所有充电宝中始终保持着最快的无线充电速度，而且它通常也是其中性价比最高的一款。它在一小时内将 iPhone 17 Pro Max 无线充电至 65%，将 Pixel 10 Pro XL 充电至 50%。倍思这款产品没有内置显示屏（意味着你无法查看剩余电量），但在无线充电一小时后，四个 LED 指示灯中仍有两个亮着，说明它还有余电。
