# Auditable Climate Risk Intelligence from Fragmented ESG Data: Deterministic Orchestration and Imbalance-Aware Learning for Scope 1-3 Validation

**基于碎片化 ESG 数据的可审计气候风险情报：用于范围 1-3 验证的确定性编排与不平衡感知学习**

***

**Abstract:** ESG and climate risk data remain fragmented across heterogeneous Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 reporting environments, while conventional validation pipelines lack provenance aware auditability, hidden drift detection, and reproducibility oriented governance. This paper proposes a deterministic climate risk intelligence framework integrating single source of truth orchestration, temporal anomaly detection, imbalance aware ensemble learning, and explainability oriented governance for auditable ESG validation.

**摘要：** ESG 和气候风险数据在异构的范围 1、范围 2 和范围 3 报告环境中仍然处于碎片化状态，而传统的验证流程缺乏溯源感知审计能力、隐藏漂移检测以及面向可重复性的治理机制。本文提出了一种确定性的气候风险情报框架，集成了单一事实来源编排、时间异常检测、不平衡感知集成学习以及面向可解释性的治理，以实现可审计的 ESG 验证。

***

To support open reproducibility, we construct and release a synthetic ESG validation benchmark calibrated against publicly reported characteristics of the GHG Protocol, PCAF, and ISSB standards. The methodology incorporates temporal drift analysis, SMOTE based rare event optimization, ensemble learning, provenance aware orchestration, and TreeSHAP based interpretability for governance inspection and audit reconstruction.

为了支持开放的可重复性，我们构建并发布了一个合成的 ESG 验证基准，该基准根据温室气体核算体系（GHG Protocol）、金融碳核算联盟（PCAF）和国际可持续准则理事会（ISSB）标准的公开报告特征进行了校准。该方法结合了时间漂移分析、基于 SMOTE 的稀有事件优化、集成学习、溯源感知编排以及基于 TreeSHAP 的可解释性，用于治理检查和审计重构。

***

We evaluate the framework against statistical classifiers, anomaly detection methods, temporal forecasting baselines, and a threshold based system using classification metrics (recall, F1, ROC AUC), calibration metrics (ECE, Brier score), and a governance oriented audit trace completeness metric measuring the fraction of flagged anomalies for which a deterministic source to escalation provenance chain can be reconstructed.

我们通过分类指标（召回率、F1 分数、ROC AUC）、校准指标（ECE、Brier 分数）以及面向治理的审计追踪完整性指标，将该框架与统计分类器、异常检测方法、时间预测基准以及基于阈值的系统进行了评估。审计追踪完整性指标用于衡量在标记的异常中，有多少比例可以重构出从确定性来源到升级处理的溯源链。

***

Results are reported as mean and standard deviation across stratified five fold cross validation with paired significance testing. The framework reframes ESG reporting toward deterministic climate risk governance infrastructure supporting reproducibility, explainability, and operational auditability.

研究结果以分层五折交叉验证的平均值和标准差形式报告，并进行了配对显著性检验。该框架将 ESG 报告重塑为支持可重复性、可解释性和操作可审计性的确定性气候风险治理基础设施。
