# From Local Training to Large-Scale Mapping: A Comparative Assessment of Machine Learning and Deep Learning for Transferable Satellite-Derived Bathymetry
# 从局部训练到大规模测绘：机器学习与深度学习在可迁移卫星测深中的比较评估

**Abstract:** Satellite-derived bathymetry (SDB) from multispectral imagery is cost-effective but scales poorly across regions, especially in optically complex coastal environments. We evaluate machine learning and deep learning for transferable SDB over the 0-20 m depth range using Sentinel-2 imagery.
**摘要：** 基于多光谱影像的卫星测深（SDB）虽然具有成本效益，但在不同区域间的扩展性较差，尤其是在光学复杂的近岸环境中。我们利用 Sentinel-2 影像，评估了机器学习和深度学习在 0-20 米深度范围内实现可迁移卫星测深的效果。

A Random Forest baseline and four CNNs (ResNet-50, ResNet-101, EfficientNet-B4, ConvNeXt-Large) are trained on Pratas Island and selected Great Barrier Reef regions, then evaluated on spatially independent intra- and cross-regional test areas. Preserving spatial continuity during training, by keeping contiguous reef blocks rather than random patches, is the single most impactful design choice; we further introduce a Smooth Weight Function (SWF)-weighted RMSE loss that emphasizes near-surface depths.
我们以随机森林（Random Forest）作为基准，并训练了四种卷积神经网络（ResNet-50、ResNet-101、EfficientNet-B4、ConvNeXt-Large）。这些模型在东沙群岛（Pratas Island）及大堡礁的部分区域进行训练，随后在空间独立的区域内及跨区域测试集上进行评估。研究发现，在训练过程中通过保留连续的礁石区块而非随机切片来维持空间连续性，是影响效果最关键的设计选择；此外，我们引入了一种平滑权重函数（SWF）加权的 RMSE 损失函数，以强化对近表层深度的预测。

With these choices, intra-regional RMSE ranges from 1.15 to 1.92 m over 0-20 m and is as low as 0.26 m for depths <= 3 m. Random Forest degrades sharply under cross-regional transfer (RMSE 1.53 m -> 2.99-3.78 m), while the deep models stay more robust (2.46-2.98 m). On the public MagicBathyNet aerial-RGB benchmark (0-16 m) the proposed networks reach 0.19-0.22 m RMSE, outperforming a U-Net baseline and a task-specific transformer architecture with substantially fewer parameters.
通过这些优化，模型在 0-20 米深度范围内的区域内 RMSE 为 1.15 至 1.92 米，而在深度小于等于 3 米时，RMSE 低至 0.26 米。随机森林模型在跨区域迁移时性能大幅下降（RMSE 从 1.53 米升至 2.99-3.78 米），而深度学习模型则表现出更强的鲁棒性（2.46-2.98 米）。在公开的 MagicBathyNet 航空 RGB 基准测试（0-16 米）中，所提网络达到了 0.19-0.22 米的 RMSE，优于 U-Net 基准模型及特定任务的 Transformer 架构，且参数量显著更少。

We further exploit multi-temporal repeat imagery: training on it broadens diversity, and median-aggregating predictions across passes at inference reduces noise from changing sun angles, atmospheric conditions, water properties, and tides. We release optimized architectures and pretrained weights to enable scalable transfer to new sites.
我们进一步利用了多时相重复影像：通过多时相训练增加了数据多样性，并在推理阶段对多次观测的预测结果进行中位数聚合，从而减少了因太阳高度角变化、大气条件、水体性质及潮汐波动带来的噪声。我们发布了优化后的架构及预训练权重，以支持向新区域的可扩展迁移。
