# Is an Online Master’s Degree in AI a Good Idea?
# 在线人工智能硕士学位是一个好主意吗？

**Career Advice: Is an Online Master’s Degree in AI a Good Idea? A look at the real-world value of online graduate AI programs, combining hard data with firsthand experience of a big tech machine learning engineer.**
**职业建议：在线人工智能硕士学位是一个好主意吗？本文结合硬数据与一位大型科技公司机器学习工程师的亲身经历，探讨在线人工智能研究生项目的实际价值。**

I am an experienced machine learning engineer with experience working at big tech companies like Meta, Google and LinkedIn. Even with a job at a top tech company and the chance to work on genuinely interesting machine learning problems, I decided to pursue a part-time online master’s degree at the University of Texas at Austin, and I recently completed it. In this post, I share my thoughts on whether an online master’s degree in AI, Data Science or CS can be worth it for building a career in this field.
我是一位经验丰富的机器学习工程师，曾在 Meta、Google 和 LinkedIn 等大型科技公司工作。尽管拥有一份顶级科技公司的工作，且有机会参与真正有趣的机器学习课题，我还是决定在德克萨斯大学奥斯汀分校攻读非全日制在线硕士学位，并已于近期完成。在这篇文章中，我将分享关于攻读人工智能、数据科学或计算机科学在线硕士学位对于职业发展是否值得的看法。

### Online Master’s Degrees For AI
### 人工智能在线硕士学位

Over the last few years, several top universities have been offering online master’s degrees in computer science, AI and data science. Some notable ones from renowned universities are: OMSCS at Georgia Tech, MS in CS at Stanford University (US residents only), MS in CS or AI or Data Science at University of Texas at Austin, MSc in ML and DS at Imperial College London, and Master of Information and Data Science at UC Berkeley.
过去几年里，几所顶尖大学开始提供计算机科学、人工智能和数据科学的在线硕士学位。一些知名大学的代表性项目包括：佐治亚理工学院的 OMSCS、斯坦福大学的计算机科学硕士（仅限美国居民）、德克萨斯大学奥斯汀分校的计算机科学/人工智能/数据科学硕士、伦敦帝国理工学院的机器学习与数据科学硕士，以及加州大学伯克利分校的信息与数据科学硕士。

These programs are exactly the same as their in-person counterparts. Students take the same courses, learn from the same professors, complete the same assignments, and receive the same diploma at the end. The only difference is the mode of delivery. The lectures are recorded and watched online, all interactions between professors, TAs, and students happen online (via Zoom, internal forums or chat rooms), all the assignments and exams are completed and uploaded online.
这些项目与线下实体项目完全相同。学生修读相同的课程，师从相同的教授，完成相同的作业，并在毕业时获得相同的文凭。唯一的区别在于授课模式。讲座被录制并在线观看，教授、助教和学生之间的所有互动都在线上进行（通过 Zoom、内部论坛或聊天室），所有的作业和考试也都在线上完成并提交。

### Are They Worth It?
### 它们值得吗？

For people already working in the industry, I think they are, provided you have the right reasons for doing one and choose a good program. In the rest of the post, I explain my reasoning backed by real data and my own personal experience.
对于已经在行业内工作的人来说，我认为是值得的，前提是你拥有正确的动机并选择了合适的项目。在接下来的文章中，我将结合真实数据和个人经验来阐述我的理由。

### Benefits: Curriculum Encourages Completion (and thus, learning)
### 优势：课程设置鼓励完成（从而促进学习）

Even though most of the knowledge on data science, machine learning and AI is freely available on the internet today, a structured degree program pushes you to actually work through it and see it through to completion. Let us look at some real data. The completion rate of such actual degree programs is significantly higher than MOOCs.
尽管如今关于数据科学、机器学习和人工智能的大部分知识在互联网上都可以免费获取，但结构化的学位项目会推动你真正去钻研并坚持到底。让我们看看一些真实数据。这类正式学位项目的完成率显著高于大规模开放在线课程（MOOC）。

From my own personal experience: prior to starting the degree, I had bookmarked dozens of YouTube playlists, bought books and MOOC courses on Reinforcement Learning and Advanced Linear Algebra. Yet, I had never gotten to finishing all of them. The list only kept growing. But when I enrolled in the master’s program, I was pushed to actually complete courses I signed up for.
从我个人的经验来看：在开始攻读学位之前，我收藏了数十个 YouTube 播放列表，购买了关于强化学习和高等线性代数的书籍和 MOOC 课程。然而，我从未真正完成过它们，清单只会越来越长。但当我注册了硕士项目后，我被迫去真正完成我所选修的课程。

As I finished courses in the program, I could start to see positive impact on my work as an ML engineer. For example, I got to apply contextual bandits (which I learned from the Reinforcement Learning course) for personalizing ad ranking treatments to users, and quantization and low-rank adapters in deep learning (from the Advances in DL course) in large models.
随着我完成项目中的课程，我开始看到它们对我作为机器学习工程师的工作产生的积极影响。例如，我能够应用从强化学习课程中学到的“上下文多臂老虎机”（contextual bandits）来为用户个性化广告排名，并将深度学习课程中的量化和低秩适配器（LoRA）应用于大模型中。

### They Offer A Rich Set Of AI Courses
### 它们提供丰富的人工智能课程

Most of the good programs I have looked at offer a wide variety of modules related to AI and Data Science. It is really refreshing to see that some of the module material was based on papers published as recently as a year or two ago, showing that universities are trying to keep the material relevant.
我所研究的大多数优秀项目都提供了与人工智能和数据科学相关的多种模块。令人耳目一新的是，一些模块材料基于一两年前发表的论文，这表明大学正在努力保持教学内容的与时俱进。

Mathematics is the bedrock of artificial intelligence. Mastering linear algebra, probability, and calculus is essential for deconstructing the inner workings of models and interpreting complex research papers with confidence.
数学是人工智能的基石。掌握线性代数、概率论和微积分，对于解构模型的内部运作机制并自信地解读复杂的研究论文至关重要。
