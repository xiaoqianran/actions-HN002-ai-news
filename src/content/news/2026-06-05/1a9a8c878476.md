# Position: Deployed Reinforcement Learning should be Continual
# 观点：部署后的强化学习应当是持续性的

Reinforcement Learning (RL) has received increasing attention and adoption in real-world use cases. Most of these systems follow a train-then-fix paradigm, where trained agents do not learn while interacting with the world until performance degrades and retraining becomes necessary.

强化学习（RL）在现实应用场景中正受到越来越多的关注与采用。目前，大多数此类系统遵循“先训练、后修复”（train-then-fix）的范式，即训练好的智能体在与环境交互时不再进行学习，直到性能下降并迫使系统必须进行重新训练。

In this position paper, we argue that deploying an agent that is incapable of optimality, but receives an evaluative reward signal, is inherently a continual RL problem. We identify four sources of non-stationarity after deployment that necessitate never-ending learning, and highlight why the best deployed agents never stop adapting.

在这篇观点论文中，我们提出：部署一个无法达到最优状态、但能接收到评估性奖励信号的智能体，本质上就是一个持续性强化学习（Continual RL）问题。我们识别了部署后导致非平稳性的四个来源，这些来源使得“永无止境的学习”成为必要，并强调了为何最优秀的部署智能体从不停止自我调整。

We analyze successful examples of continual RL in the real world, and present the community with the advantages and measures to move away from the current train-then-fix paradigm.

我们分析了现实世界中持续性强化学习的成功案例，并向学术界展示了摆脱当前“先训练、后修复”范式的优势与具体措施。
