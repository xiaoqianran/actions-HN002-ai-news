# Do Transformers Need Three Projections? Systematic Study of QKV Variants
# Transformer 真的需要三个投影吗？QKV 变体的系统性研究

**Abstract:** Transformers have become the standard solution for various AI tasks, with the query, key, and value (QKV) attention formulation playing a central role. However, the individual contribution of these three projections and the impact of omitting some remain poorly understood.

**摘要：** Transformer 已成为各种人工智能任务的标准解决方案，其中查询（Query）、键（Key）和值（Value，即 QKV）注意力机制起着核心作用。然而，这三个投影各自的贡献以及省略其中部分投影的影响，目前仍缺乏深入了解。

We systematically evaluate three projection sharing constraints: a) Q-K=V (shared key-value), b) Q=K-V (shared query-key), and c) Q=K=V (single projection). The last two variants produce symmetric attention maps; to address this, we also explore asymmetric attention via 2D positional encodings.

我们系统地评估了三种投影共享约束：a) Q-K=V（共享键值），b) Q=K-V（共享查询-键），以及 c) Q=K=V（单一投影）。后两种变体会产生对称的注意力图；为了解决这个问题，我们还通过二维位置编码探索了非对称注意力机制。

Through experiments spanning synthetic tasks, vision (MNIST, CIFAR, TinyImageNet, anomaly), and language modeling (300M and 1.2B parameter models on 10B tokens), we discovered that our transformers perform on par or occasionally better than the QKV transformer. In language modeling, Q-K=V projection sharing achieves 50% KV cache reduction with only 3.1% perplexity degradation.

通过涵盖合成任务、视觉任务（MNIST、CIFAR、TinyImageNet、异常检测）以及语言建模（在 10B token 上训练的 300M 和 1.2B 参数模型）的实验，我们发现我们的 Transformer 模型表现与标准 QKV Transformer 持平，甚至在某些情况下表现更好。在语言建模中，Q-K=V 投影共享在仅损失 3.1% 困惑度（perplexity）的情况下，实现了 50% 的 KV 缓存缩减。

Crucially, projection sharing is complementary to head sharing (GQA/MQA): combining Q-K=V with GQA-4 yields 87.5% cache reduction, while Q-K=V + MQA achieves 96.9%, enabling practical on-device inference. We show that Q-K=V preserves quality because keys and values can occupy similar representational spaces and attention operates in a low-rank regime, whereas Q=K-V breaks attention directionality.

至关重要的是，投影共享与头共享（GQA/MQA）是互补的：将 Q-K=V 与 GQA-4 结合可实现 87.5% 的缓存缩减，而 Q-K=V + MQA 则可达到 96.9%，从而实现了实用的端侧推理。我们证明了 Q-K=V 能够保持模型质量，因为键和值可以占据相似的表征空间，且注意力机制在低秩状态下运行；而 Q=K-V 则破坏了注意力的方向性。

Our results systematically characterize projection sharing as an underexplored instance of weight tying in attention, with direct, quantifiable inference memory benefits, particularly valuable for edge deployment. 我们的研究结果系统地将投影共享定义为注意力机制中一种尚未被充分探索的权重绑定（weight tying）实例，它具有直接且可量化的推理内存优势，对于边缘侧部署尤为重要。代码已在 [URL](https://github.com/ali-kayyam/qkv-variants) 公开。
