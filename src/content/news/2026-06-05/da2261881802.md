# Optimal Transport Flow Matching by Design
# 通过设计实现最优传输流匹配

**Abstract:** Flow matching models learn to transport samples from a simple prior distribution to a complex data distribution. When prior-data pairs are coupled via optimal transport (OT), the learned trajectories are straight and non-crossing, enabling fast, even single-step, generation. However, computing the OT coupling in high dimensions is intractable, and existing methods attempt to solve the OT problem, at the cost of persistent bias or significant overhead.

**摘要：** 流匹配（Flow matching）模型学习将样本从简单的先验分布传输到复杂的分布。当先验与数据对通过最优传输（OT）进行耦合时，学习到的轨迹是直线且互不交叉的，从而实现快速甚至单步生成。然而，在高维空间中计算最优传输耦合是极其困难的，现有方法试图解决这一问题，但往往以持续的偏差或巨大的计算开销为代价。

Rather than solving for the OT coupling, we reformulate the problem. Once the prior is treated as a design choice rather than a fixed input, the OT coupling between prior and data is no longer unique. Many priors admit an OT-optimal identity coupling to the data, leaving us free to choose one that is also tractable to sample. We identify low-frequency projection of natural images as such a choice.

我们没有选择去求解最优传输耦合，而是重新构建了该问题。一旦将先验视为一种设计选择而非固定输入，先验与数据之间的最优传输耦合就不再是唯一的。许多先验分布都允许与数据之间存在最优传输意义下的恒等耦合，这使我们可以自由选择一种易于采样的先验。我们发现自然图像的低频投影正是这样一种选择。

The identity coupling between data and its low-frequency representation is empirically OT-optimal, the prior is structured enough to be sampled by a lightweight model at inference, and the remaining flow-matching task reduces to synthesizing high-frequency detail. Interpolating the prior with Gaussian noise further improves generation quality while preserving the OT coupling.

数据与其低频表示之间的恒等耦合在经验上是最优传输最优的；该先验具有足够的结构，可以在推理时由轻量级模型进行采样，而剩余的流匹配任务则简化为合成高频细节。将先验与高斯噪声进行插值可以进一步提高生成质量，同时保持最优传输耦合。

The approach requires no modifications to the flow model itself, and integrates naturally with latent-space models, classifier-free guidance, and one-step generation frameworks. Across all benchmarks, our method reduces trajectory curvature by more than $2\times$ compared to existing flow matching methods, yielding better generation quality in the few-step regime.

该方法无需对流模型本身进行任何修改，并能与潜在空间模型、无分类器引导（classifier-free guidance）以及单步生成框架自然集成。在所有基准测试中，我们的方法将轨迹曲率降低了超过 2 倍，在少步生成场景下表现出更好的生成质量。
