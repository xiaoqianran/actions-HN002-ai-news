# Remarkable’s refurbished Paper Pro bundle is hundreds off
# Remarkable 官翻版 Paper Pro 套装优惠数百美元

Color E Ink tablets aren’t usually affordable. I’m not going to say that Woot’s price on a refurbished “good as new” Remarkable Paper Pro is cheap, but it’s pretty fantastic compared to buying one new. Normally $629 just for the tablet, you can get a bundle that includes the big 11.8-inch Paper Pro plus $139 worth of brand new accessories (a Mosaic Weave Book Folio and a Marker Plus) for $509 if you buy it used. The bundle comes with a 90-day warranty, which pales in comparison so Remarkable’s own one-year warranty on refurbished devices. Then again, the same refurbished bundle on the company’s site is $649.

彩色电子墨水屏平板电脑通常价格不菲。我不会说 Woot 上这款“成色如新”的官翻版 Remarkable Paper Pro 很便宜，但与购买全新设备相比，这个价格确实非常划算。通常仅平板电脑本身就需要 629 美元，而现在你只需 509 美元就能买到一套包含 11.8 英寸大屏 Paper Pro 以及价值 139 美元全新配件（Mosaic Weave 保护套和 Marker Plus 手写笔）的套装。该套装提供 90 天保修，虽然与 Remarkable 官方翻新设备提供的一年保修相比稍显逊色，但要知道，在 Remarkable 官网购买同样的官翻套装需要 649 美元。

It’s nice to have a color E Ink so close in price to Remarkable’s newer Paper Pure, a 10.3-inch black and white E Ink tablet for note-taking. While many of their features overlap, you may prefer not just the color capabilities of the Paper Pro, but also its front light. The Paper Pure doesn’t have a light at all. One thing to keep in mind about the Pro’s color-capable screen is that it uses Gallery display tech, which has a slow refresh rate because it renders colors instead of using filters like Kaleido displays.

能以如此接近 Remarkable 新款 Paper Pure（一款 10.3 英寸黑白笔记平板）的价格买到彩色电子墨水屏设备，确实很不错。虽然它们的功能有很多重叠之处，但你可能不仅会青睐 Paper Pro 的色彩显示能力，还会看重它的前置阅读灯——Paper Pure 是完全没有背光的。关于 Pro 的彩色屏幕，有一点需要注意：它采用了 Gallery 显示技术，由于它是通过渲染色彩而非像 Kaleido 屏幕那样使用滤光片，因此刷新率相对较慢。

If you’re hunting for a sleek 33-inch soundbar with wired satellite speakers and a wireless subwoofer that won’t cost you much, Vizio’s 5.1 SE system is $149.99 at Walmart (down from $228). The soundbar can easily mount to the bottom of most recent Vizio TVs without extra accessories, but its low-profile design won’t look out of place even if you have to set it on your entertainment center. It supports Bluetooth and eARC over HDMI so you won’t have to power it on every time you turn on the TV. The company claims the system can provide Dolby Atmos and DTS X spatial audio, though it’s likely simulated since its included speakers don’t tilt upwards to bounce sound off your ceiling.

如果你正在寻找一款价格亲民、外观时尚的 33 英寸条形音箱（包含有线卫星音箱和无线低音炮），沃尔玛目前将 Vizio 5.1 SE 系统的价格从 228 美元降至 149.99 美元。这款条形音箱无需额外配件即可轻松安装在大多数新款 Vizio 电视底部，其低调的设计即使摆放在电视柜上也毫不突兀。它支持蓝牙和 HDMI eARC，因此你无需每次打开电视都手动开启音箱。虽然该公司声称该系统能提供杜比全景声（Dolby Atmos）和 DTS X 空间音频，但考虑到其自带音箱无法向上倾斜以通过天花板反射声音，这很可能是模拟效果。
