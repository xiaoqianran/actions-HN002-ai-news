# Inverse Critical Experiment Design via Gradient Optimization and a Multigroup Attention-Based Neural Network Architecture
# 基于梯度优化与多组注意力神经网络架构的临界实验逆向设计

**Abstract:** The validation of advanced nuclear reactor designs and fuel concepts requires critical experiments with high neutronic similarity to the target technology. Neutronic similarity is quantified by the correlation coefficient $c_k$, which captures the shared bias in $k_\text{eff}$ induced by uncertainties in nuclear data. Generally, a $c_k\geq0.9$ is needed for an experiment to be sufficiently similar to a target technology.

**摘要：** 先进核反应堆设计和燃料概念的验证需要与目标技术具有高度中子相似性的临界实验。中子相似性通过相关系数 $c_k$ 进行量化，该系数捕捉了由核数据不确定性引起的 $k_\text{eff}$ 共享偏差。通常，实验要与目标技术具有足够的相似性，需要 $c_k\geq0.9$。

This work presents a methodology for the inverse design of critical experiments. Deep neural network surrogate modeling and nonparametric gradient optimization are used to generate experiment geometries that maximize $c_k$. A deep neural network is trained on OpenMC-calculated sensitivity vectors for grid-based critical experiment geometries.

本研究提出了一种临界实验逆向设计方法。通过深度神经网络代理建模和非参数梯度优化，生成能够最大化 $c_k$ 的实验几何结构。研究利用 OpenMC 计算的网格化临界实验几何灵敏度向量，对深度神经网络进行了训练。

The model architecture combines a U-Net convolutional encoder-decoder with a novel multigroup attention pooling layer, introduced to capture the differing spatial dependencies of sensitivities. Multigroup attention pooling is shown to achieve better performance than traditional pooling, as well as interpretable internal behavior.

该模型架构结合了 U-Net 卷积编码器-解码器与一种新型多组注意力池化层，旨在捕捉灵敏度在空间上的不同依赖关系。研究表明，多组注意力池化比传统池化具有更好的性能，且内部行为具有可解释性。

The differentiability of the surrogate enables gradient-based optimization of the full combinatorial design space, allowing $c_k$ to be maximized by directly changing the material assignment of each position in the geometry grid. The method is applied to the validation of the TN-Americas TN-LC transportation cask with HALEU fuel, for which existing critical experiment coverage is limited.

代理模型的可微性实现了对完整组合设计空间的基于梯度的优化，从而可以通过直接改变几何网格中每个位置的材料分配来最大化 $c_k$。该方法被应用于验证使用 HALEU 燃料的 TN-Americas TN-LC 运输桶，目前针对该领域的临界实验覆盖范围十分有限。

The optimization procedure is shown to produce experiment geometries achieving $c_k$ scores of 0.97757, 0.81324, and 0.93276 for three configurations of interest. This approach demonstrates the potential of deep learning and gradient optimization to accelerate the development of advanced nuclear technology.

优化过程显示，针对三个目标配置，生成的实验几何结构分别达到了 0.97757、0.81324 和 0.93276 的 $c_k$ 分数。该方法展示了深度学习和梯度优化在加速先进核技术发展方面的潜力。
