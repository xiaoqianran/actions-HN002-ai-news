What happened the last time we were here
Don’t just take our word for it. Here’s what came out of one day in Sydney in 2017.
我们上次来这里时发生了什么？
别只听我们的一面之词。以下是 2017 年在悉尼那一天所取得的成果。

The winner became a global health tech platform
Manuri Gunawardena was a final-year medical student when she pitched HealthMatch — a machine learning platform matching patients with clinical trials. She won. That win turned into over $25 million raised, U.S. expansion, and over 1 million patients globally, backed by Square Peg Capital and SEEK co-founder Paul Bassat.
冠军成为了全球健康科技平台
Manuri Gunawardena 在担任医学院最后一年学生时，路演了 HealthMatch——一个将患者与临床试验进行匹配的机器学习平台。她赢得了比赛。那次胜利转化为超过 2500 万美元的融资、美国市场的扩张，以及全球超过 100 万名患者的覆盖，并获得了 Square Peg Capital 和 SEEK 联合创始人 Paul Bassat 的支持。

The runner-up built a global agtech company
Runner-up FluroSat used hyperspectral imaging to help farmers reduce waste. The Startup Battlefield stage gave the company its first real visibility. A Microsoft M12 seed round followed, then a merger to form Regrow Agriculture — now with over $60 million raised and backed by Microsoft, Airtree, and Cargill.
亚军打造了一家全球农业科技公司
亚军 FluroSat 利用高光谱成像技术帮助农民减少浪费。Startup Battlefield 的舞台让该公司获得了首次真正的曝光。随后，他们获得了微软 M12 的种子轮投资，之后通过合并成立了 Regrow Agriculture——目前已融资超过 6000 万美元，并获得了微软、Airtree 和嘉吉（Cargill）的支持。

Together, the winner and runner-up from one day in Sydney have raised over $85 million. That’s what happens when you create a stage and invite the right people onto it.
总计，在悉尼那一天产生的冠军和亚军已经筹集了超过 8500 万美元的资金。这就是当你搭建好舞台并邀请合适的人登台时所产生的效应。

Life Whisperer partnered with fertility clinics internationally. These were companies the world had never heard of before they stood in a room in Sydney and made their case. That’s what Startup Battlefield does. It finds companies before the world knows their names.

在 26 家澳大利亚 Battlefield 校友企业中，累计融资额超过 1.47 亿美元，并有 3 家成功被收购。他们获得了 Y Combinator、Blackbird Ventures、Square Peg、Khosla Ventures、微软、Airtree、Startmate、Techstars 和 SOSV 的支持。CancerAid 后来发展成为 Osara Health。Life Whisperer 与国际上的生育诊所建立了合作关系。在他们站在悉尼的房间里陈述自己的愿景之前，这些公司在世界上还默默无闻。这就是 Startup Battlefield 的作用：在世界知晓它们的名字之前，发掘这些公司。
