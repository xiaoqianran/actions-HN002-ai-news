# LightVesselNet: An Ultra-Lightweight Sub-100K Parameter Network for Retinal Blood Vessel Segmentation
# LightVesselNet：一种用于视网膜血管分割的超轻量级（参数量低于10万）网络

**Abstract:** Retinal blood vessel segmentation plays a vital role in the early detection of diabetic retinopathy and glaucoma. While recent deep learning models have achieved great segmentation accuracy, they typically require heavy computational resources, making real-world deployment on edge devices difficult.

**摘要：** 视网膜血管分割在糖尿病视网膜病变和青光眼的早期检测中起着至关重要的作用。尽管近期的深度学习模型已经实现了极高的分割精度，但它们通常需要大量的计算资源，这使得在边缘设备上的实际部署变得困难。

In this paper, we propose LightVesselNet, an efficient neural network designed for retinal vessel segmentation in a resource-constrained environment. Despite containing only 75K parameters, LightVesselNet performs competitively with much larger models.

在本文中，我们提出了 LightVesselNet，这是一种专为资源受限环境下的视网膜血管分割而设计的高效神经网络。尽管仅包含 7.5 万个参数，但 LightVesselNet 的性能足以与规模大得多的模型相媲美。

The network employs a compact encoder-decoder architecture enhanced with channel and spatial attention mechanisms, a multi-scale feature aggregation module at the bottleneck, and a subpixel upsampling strategy in the decoder. A dedicated edge residual connection preserves fine vessel detail throughout decoding.

该网络采用了紧凑的编码器-解码器架构，并辅以通道和空间注意力机制、瓶颈处的特征多尺度聚合模块，以及解码器中的亚像素上采样策略。专用的边缘残差连接在整个解码过程中保留了精细的血管细节。

Extensive experiments on five publicly available datasets: DRIVE, STARE, CHASEDB1, FIVES, and HRF, yield sensitivity scores of 0.8189, 0.8499, 0.8640, 0.8634, 0.8096, and Dice coefficients of 0.8070, 0.8072, 0.8181, 0.8649, and 0.7686, respectively. LightVesselNet shows improved efficiency (Performance vs Parameter or GFlops) compared to State-of-the-Art models. Cross-dataset evaluation confirms the model's generalisation capability.

在 DRIVE、STARE、CHASEDB1、FIVES 和 HRF 这五个公开数据集上的大量实验表明，其敏感度（Sensitivity）得分分别为 0.8189、0.8499、0.8640、0.8634 和 0.8096，Dice 系数分别为 0.8070、0.8072、0.8181、0.8649 和 0.7686。与当前最先进的模型相比，LightVesselNet 展示了更高的效率（性能与参数量或 GFlops 之比）。跨数据集评估证实了该模型的泛化能力。

Overall, LightVesselNet is a strong candidate for deployment in low-resource clinical settings and mobile screening tools.

总而言之，LightVesselNet 是在低资源临床环境和移动筛查工具中部署的有力候选方案。
