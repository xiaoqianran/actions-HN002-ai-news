# Startup Battlefield 200 applications officially close in 3 days
# Startup Battlefield 200 申请通道将于 3 天后正式关闭

Founders, your window to enter Startup Battlefield 200 closes in just three short days. Applications for Startup Battlefield 200 officially close on June 8, 11:59 p.m. PT. Do not wait any longer. Secure your shot at competing on the Disrupt Stage at TechCrunch Disrupt 2026 this October at San Francisco’s Moscone West. Thousands of startups have already stepped forward. If you’re building a company with the potential to reshape an industry, now is the time to make your move. Apply or nominate a startup before the deadline.

各位创始人，距离 Startup Battlefield 200 的报名截止仅剩三天。Startup Battlefield 200 的申请通道将于太平洋时间 6 月 8 日晚 11:59 正式关闭。请勿再犹豫，抓住机会，争取在今年 10 月于旧金山 Moscone West 举行的 TechCrunch Disrupt 2026 大会上登上 Disrupt 舞台。目前已有数千家初创公司报名。如果你正在打造一家有潜力重塑行业的公司，现在就是行动的最佳时机。请在截止日期前提交申请或推荐初创公司。

### What is Startup Battlefield 200?
### 什么是 Startup Battlefield 200？

Startup Battlefield 200 is where ambitious early-stage startups go from unknown to impossible to ignore. Selected founders will take the spotlight at Disrupt, pitching live in front of top investors, influential media, and the global startup ecosystem. One startup will take home $100,000 in equity-free funding, but every selected company gains exposure that can accelerate growth, attract customers, and open doors to future fundraising opportunities.

Startup Battlefield 200 是雄心勃勃的早期初创公司从默默无闻走向备受瞩目的舞台。入选的创始人将成为 Disrupt 大会的焦点，在顶级投资者、极具影响力的媒体和全球创业生态系统面前进行现场路演。获胜的初创公司将获得 10 万美元的无股权融资，但每一家入选公司都将获得能够加速增长、吸引客户并开启未来融资机会的曝光度。

Over the years, Startup Battlefield alumni have collectively raised more than $32 billion and achieved more than 250 exits. Alumni have gone on to be acquired by companies such as Microsoft, Google, Salesforce, Uber, and Amazon. The competition has also helped launch companies such as Dropbox, Discord, Mint, Fitbit, and Trello.

多年来，Startup Battlefield 的校友企业总共筹集了超过 320 亿美元的资金，并实现了超过 250 次退出。许多校友企业后来被微软、谷歌、Salesforce、Uber 和亚马逊等公司收购。该竞赛还助力了 Dropbox、Discord、Mint、Fitbit 和 Trello 等公司的崛起。

### Why founders are racing to apply
### 为什么创始人争相申请？

In a competitive fundraising market, standing out has never been more important. Startup Battlefield 200 offers founders a rare opportunity to put their companies directly in front of investors, media, customers, and potential partners. Selected startups receive: A free exhibit table for all three days of Disrupt. Four complimentary Disrupt passes. Branding and visibility inside the Disrupt event app. Press exposure and lead-generation opportunities. Access to founder-only masterclasses. The opportunity to pitch live on the Disrupt Stage. Direct feedback from leading venture capitalists. A chance to win $100,000 in equity-free funding.

在竞争激烈的融资市场中，脱颖而出变得前所未有的重要。Startup Battlefield 200 为创始人提供了一个难得的机会，让他们能直接向投资者、媒体、客户和潜在合作伙伴展示自己的公司。入选的初创公司将获得：Disrupt 大会全程三天的免费展位；四张免费的 Disrupt 大会门票；Disrupt 活动应用程序内的品牌展示和曝光；媒体曝光和潜在客户挖掘机会；参加仅限创始人参加的大师班；在 Disrupt 舞台上进行现场路演的机会；来自顶级风险投资人的直接反馈；以及赢取 10 万美元无股权融资的机会。

### Who should apply?
### 谁应该申请？

TechCrunch is looking for bold early-stage startups with a working MVP and a vision capable of disrupting an industry. Bootstrapped, pre-seed, and seed-stage startups are encouraged to apply. Select Series A startups in capital-intensive sectors may also qualify. If you’re building a category-defining company, this is your opportunity to prove it on one of the biggest stages in tech.

TechCrunch 正在寻找拥有可行 MVP（最小可行性产品）且具备颠覆行业愿景的大胆早期初创公司。我们鼓励自筹资金、种子轮前和种子轮阶段的初创公司申请。资本密集型行业的特定 A 轮初创公司也可能符合资格。如果你正在打造一家定义品类的公司，这就是你在科技界最大舞台之一证明自己的机会。

Apply or nominate a startup before the deadline, and earn your place among the next generation of Startup Battlefield competitors.
