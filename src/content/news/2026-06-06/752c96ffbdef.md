# The latest AI news we announced in May 2026
# 我们在 2026 年 5 月发布的最新 AI 新闻

Here’s a recap of our biggest AI updates from May, including announcements from Google I/O 2026, the Android Show and Google Health.
以下是我们 5 月份最重大的 AI 更新回顾，包括来自 Google I/O 2026 大会、Android Show 以及 Google Health 的公告。

For more than 20 years, we’ve invested in machine learning and AI research, tools and infrastructure to build products that make everyday life better for more people. Teams across Google are working on ways to unlock AI’s benefits in fields as wide-ranging as healthcare, crisis response and education. To keep you posted on our progress, we're doing a regular roundup of Google's most recent AI news. Here’s a look back at some of our AI announcements from May.
二十多年来，我们一直致力于机器学习和 AI 研究、工具及基础设施的投入，旨在打造让更多人的日常生活变得更美好的产品。Google 各个团队正致力于在医疗保健、危机应对和教育等广泛领域释放 AI 的潜力。为了让您及时了解我们的进展，我们定期汇总 Google 最新的 AI 新闻。以下是 5 月份部分 AI 公告的回顾。

May 2026 was packed with AI news. At Google I/O 2026, we officially entered the agentic Gemini era with the launch of Gemini 3.5 — which delivers frontier intelligence for agents and coding — and Gemini Omni, where Gemini’s ability to reason meets the ability to create. The Android Show set the stage with brand-new hardware built specifically for these tools, including the Googlebook from our hardware partners. We also broadened our personal wellness tools with the new Google Health app and Fitbit Air, and launched an initiative to apply advanced quantum science and AI to the life sciences. Ultimately, May was all about making AI more proactive, helpful and integrated into your everyday life.
2026 年 5 月充满了 AI 新闻。在 Google I/O 2026 大会上，我们正式进入了“代理式（Agentic）”Gemini 时代，推出了 Gemini 3.5——它为智能体和编程提供了前沿智能——以及 Gemini Omni，将 Gemini 的推理能力与创造能力相结合。Android Show 则展示了专为这些工具打造的全新硬件，包括来自我们硬件合作伙伴的 Googlebook。我们还通过全新的 Google Health 应用和 Fitbit Air 扩展了个人健康工具，并启动了一项将先进量子科学与 AI 应用于生命科学的计划。归根结底，5 月的核心目标是让 AI 变得更加主动、实用，并融入您的日常生活。

**Experience Gemini Omni.** We announced Gemini Omni, our new model that can create anything from any input — starting with video. Omni allows you to combine images, audio, video and text as input and generate high-quality videos grounded in Gemini's real-world knowledge.
**体验 Gemini Omni。** 我们发布了 Gemini Omni，这是我们的新模型，能够通过任何输入创建任何内容——首先从视频开始。Omni 允许您结合图像、音频、视频和文本作为输入，并生成基于 Gemini 现实世界知识的高质量视频。

**Simulate real-world places.** By combining Project Genie with Street View, we introduced an experimental new way to simulate and explore highly realistic, interactive 3D environments of real-world places right from your browser.
**模拟现实世界地点。** 通过将 Project Genie 与街景（Street View）相结合，我们推出了一种实验性的新方法，让您可以直接从浏览器中模拟并探索高度逼真、可交互的现实世界 3D 环境。

**Learn how we partner with musical artists.** Through a new partnership between Google Flow Music and Believe, we're equipping artists and producers with a creative AI collaborator to help throughout the creation process, from brainstorming lyrics and melodies to putting final touches on a song.
**了解我们如何与音乐艺术家合作。** 通过 Google Flow Music 与 Believe 之间的新合作，我们为艺术家和制作人配备了一位创意 AI 协作伙伴，协助他们完成整个创作过程，从构思歌词和旋律到为歌曲进行最后的润色。

**Discover what’s possible with Gemini 3.5.** We launched our latest family of models combining frontier intelligence with action. With powerful new action-taking capabilities, Gemini 3.5 is built to help you reliably execute complex, multi-step agentic workflows across your apps.
**探索 Gemini 3.5 的无限可能。** 我们发布了最新的模型系列，将前沿智能与行动力相结合。凭借强大的新行动能力，Gemini 3.5 旨在帮助您在各个应用程序中可靠地执行复杂的多步骤代理式工作流。

**Connect with your proactive 24/7 partner.** The Gemini app is becoming a more helpful AI assistant, featuring an intuitive new UI, personalized daily briefs and Gemini Spark. Instead of just answering questions, it acts as a proactive helper — managing your inbox, scheduling appointments and anticipating your daily needs in the background.
**连接您 24/7 全天候的主动伙伴。** Gemini 应用正变得更加实用的 AI 助手，拥有直观的新界面、个性化的每日简报和 Gemini Spark。它不再仅仅是回答问题，而是充当主动的助手——在后台管理您的收件箱、安排预约并预判您的日常需求。

**Get more done with our advanced AI models in Search.** Our next era for Search introduces new features that bring together the best of the web with the best of AI. We're launching information agents in Search to intelligently work in the background, 24/7. They'll monitor information on your behalf and send detailed updates with links to dive deeper and take action. We’re also bringing Antigravity and the agentic coding capabilities of Gemini 3.5 Flash right into Search, so Search can build generative UI and interactive visuals tailored to your questions, plus custom experiences like dashboards or mini apps for your ongoing tasks. And we introduced a new, intelligent Search box, marking its biggest upgrade in over 25 years. With agentic coding capabilities in Search, you’ll be able to ask Search to code a custom fitness tracker for you, using real-time data like reviews, live maps and weather to keep you on track.
**利用搜索中的先进 AI 模型完成更多工作。** 搜索的下一个时代引入了新功能，将网络精华与 AI 精华融为一体。我们正在搜索中推出信息智能体，在后台 24/7 全天候智能工作。它们将代表您监控信息，并发送详细更新，附带链接以便您深入了解并采取行动。我们还将 Antigravity 和 Gemini 3.5 Flash 的代理式编程能力直接引入搜索，使搜索能够根据您的问题构建生成式 UI 和交互式视觉效果，并为您的持续任务提供仪表板或小程序等定制体验。此外，我们还推出了全新的智能搜索框，这是其 25 年来最大的升级。借助搜索中的代理式编程能力，您可以要求搜索为您编写一个定制的健身追踪器，利用评论、实时地图和天气等实时数据来帮助您保持进度。

**Stay in sync with Android Halo.** To help you manage your agents, we introduced Android Halo, a new space on your phone that lets you see your agents’ progress and receive contextual assistance without interrupting your flow.
**通过 Android Halo 保持同步。** 为了帮助您管理智能体，我们推出了 Android Halo，这是手机上的一个新空间，让您可以查看智能体的进度并接收情境化辅助，而不会打断您的工作流。

**Simplify your shopping with Universal Cart.** Universal Cart is your new hub for shopping on Google. Universal Cart works across merchants and across services, so you can add things to your cart while you’re browsing Search, chatting with Gemini, watching YouTube or even reading your Gmail.
**通过 Universal Cart 简化购物。** Universal Cart 是您在 Google 上购物的新中心。Universal Cart 跨商家、跨服务运行，因此您可以在浏览搜索、与 Gemini 聊天、观看 YouTube 甚至阅读 Gmail 时将商品添加到购物车。

**Try the all-new Google Health app.** The Google Health app brings all of your health and wellness into one place debuting new and advanced capabilities.
**尝试全新的 Google Health 应用。** Google Health 应用将您的所有健康和保健信息汇集于一处，并首次亮相了多项先进的新功能。

**Meet the all-new Fitbit Air.** Fitbit Air is our smallest tracker yet — a proactive wellness partner that uses high-fidelity sensor technology in a tiny, discreet pebble that enables advanced health and fitness tracking like 24/7 heart rate, heart rhythm monitoring with Afib alerts, SpO2, resting heart rate, heart rate variability, sleep stages and duration, and more.
**认识全新的 Fitbit Air。** Fitbit Air 是我们迄今为止最小的追踪器——它是一个主动的健康伙伴，将高保真传感器技术封装在一个小巧、低调的鹅卵石外壳中，支持先进的健康和健身追踪，如 24/7 心率监测、带有房颤（Afib）警报的心律监测、血氧饱和度（SpO2）、静息心率、心率变异性、睡眠阶段和时长等。

**Discover the new Googlebook.** Googlebook is our newly designed laptop experience built from the ground up for Gemini Intelligence. It features the Magic Pointer for contextual suggestions, custom widgets to help you organize your tasks, and more.
**探索全新的 Googlebook。** Googlebook 是我们全新设计的笔记本电脑体验，专为 Gemini 智能从零打造。它配备了用于情境建议的“魔法指针（Magic Pointer）”、帮助您整理任务的自定义小部件等。
