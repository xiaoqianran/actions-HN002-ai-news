# Personal AI Agent for Camera Roll VQA
# 用于相册视觉问答的个人 AI 智能体

We study the personal camera roll visual question answering setting. In this setting, a conversational AI assistant can access a user's personal camera roll and retrieve relevant photos to answer queries, ranging from simple factual questions (e.g., ``Name of the food I tried yesterday?'') to more open-ended ones (e.g., ``Recommend some dishes I have never eaten before'').

我们研究了个人相册视觉问答（VQA）场景。在此场景中，对话式 AI 助手可以访问用户的个人相册并检索相关照片以回答查询，范围涵盖简单的事实性问题（例如：“我昨天尝试的食物叫什么名字？”）到更开放的问题（例如：“推荐一些我从未吃过的菜肴”）。

Given the vast nature of the personal camera roll (i.e., multiple years, hundreds to thousands of photos), a successful AI assistant needs to understand a long-horizon, highly personalized visual content stream in order to navigate and locate the correct and/or relevant information.

鉴于个人相册的海量特性（即跨越多年、包含数百至数千张照片），一个成功的 AI 助手需要理解长跨度、高度个性化的视觉内容流，以便导航并定位到正确或相关的信息。

To support this, we collect and manually annotate questions that mimic real-world usage. The final dataset, camroll, contains 50 users, 31,476 images, and 2,500 QA pairs.

为了支持这一研究，我们收集并手动标注了模拟真实使用场景的问题。最终的数据集“camroll”包含 50 名用户、31,476 张图像和 2,500 个问答对。

We further design camroll-agent, a conversational AI agent equipped with hierarchical memory and a minimal set of tools for efficient navigation over large, personalized visual memory. Experimental results show that camroll-agent outperforms numerous baselines and methods for long-context understanding AI agents system.

我们进一步设计了 camroll-agent，这是一个配备了分层记忆和一套精简工具的对话式 AI 智能体，用于在庞大且个性化的视觉记忆中进行高效导航。实验结果表明，camroll-agent 在长上下文理解 AI 智能体系统中优于众多基准模型和方法。

Together, the camroll dataset and camroll-agent highlight the gap in AI agents' long-context reasoning: personalized visual memory requires different approaches from standard long-context textual memory, especially when consistency, visual details, and user-specific context are present.

camroll 数据集和 camroll-agent 共同揭示了 AI 智能体在长上下文推理方面的差距：个性化视觉记忆需要与标准长上下文文本记忆不同的处理方法，特别是在涉及一致性、视觉细节和用户特定上下文时。
