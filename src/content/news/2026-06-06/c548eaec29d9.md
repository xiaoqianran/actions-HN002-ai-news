# The Evaluation Blind Spot: A Stereological Theory of Benchmark Coverage for Large Language Models
# 评估盲点：大语言模型基准覆盖范围的立体学理论

**Abstract:** We give a stereological theory of LLM benchmark coverage. For any suite with effective dimensionality $d_{eff}$, the visible Hausdorff distance between two convex capability profiles consistent with the same scores is bounded by $\epsilon + C R m^{-1/(d_{eff}-1)}$, with matching Lipschitz lower bound. 

**摘要：** 我们提出了一种大语言模型（LLM）基准覆盖范围的立体学理论。对于任何具有有效维度 $d_{eff}$ 的基准测试集，在相同分数下，两个凸能力分布（convex capability profiles）之间的可见豪斯多夫距离（Hausdorff distance）受限于 $\epsilon + C R m^{-1/(d_{eff}-1)}$，并具有匹配的利普希茨（Lipschitz）下界。

Empirically, three independent leaderboards (Open LLM v2, an extended 12-benchmark suite, LiveBench) all have $d_{eff}$ in $[2.86, 4.80]$ on their competitive frontier; the structural blind spot exceeds the observed runner-up score gap by two orders of magnitude and dominates statistical noise by 52-127x. 

实证研究表明，三个独立的排行榜（Open LLM v2、一个扩展的12项基准测试集、LiveBench）在其竞争前沿上的 $d_{eff}$ 均处于 $[2.86, 4.80]$ 区间；其结构性盲点比观察到的亚军分数差距高出两个数量级，且比统计噪声大 52-127 倍。

Under a chi-squared projection model, the isotropic prior is the optimistic case; across six hidden-capability priors and four ambient dimensions the simulated half-split swap rate of the top two models stays in $[0.38, 0.49]$, and a 500-trial random visible/held-out split shows that 92% of trials swap the top-1 ranking with on average 2.83 of 5 top-5 models changing. 

在卡方投影模型下，各向同性先验（isotropic prior）属于乐观情况；在六种隐藏能力先验和四个环境维度下，模拟的前两名模型在半拆分（half-split）测试中的交换率保持在 $[0.38, 0.49]$ 之间。500 次随机可见/留出（visible/held-out）拆分试验显示，92% 的试验会改变排名第一的模型，且前五名模型中平均有 2.83 个发生变动。

A submodular greedy algorithm with the Nemhauser $(1 - 1/e)$ guarantee finds a stable core of 4 benchmarks; 7 of 12 suffice for 90% coverage, and the trained subset transfers across temporal quarters with 93-97% retention. 

一种具有 Nemhauser $(1 - 1/e)$ 保证的子模贪心算法（submodular greedy algorithm）找到了一个由 4 个基准测试组成的稳定核心；12 个基准测试中的 7 个足以实现 90% 的覆盖率，且训练后的子集在不同季度间的迁移保留率为 93-97%。

A counterfactual validation across 12 internal benchmarks and 27 Chatbot Arena categories confirms that the eigenstructure predicts which evaluations are irreplaceable ($\rho = -0.69, p = 0.013$ for removal disruption) and which external evaluations bring new information ($\rho = +0.38$). 

通过对 12 个内部基准测试和 27 个 Chatbot Arena 类别的反事实验证证实，特征结构（eigenstructure）可以预测哪些评估是不可替代的（移除干扰的 $\rho = -0.69, p = 0.013$），以及哪些外部评估能带来新信息（$\rho = +0.38$）。

As a second, independent theoretical contribution, we resolve Gardner's Problem 1.5 (1995) for $C^2$ support functions, establishing the minimax rate $\Theta(R/(\kappa m^{2/(D-1)}))$ in general dimension via optimal recovery theory on $S^{D-1}$.

作为第二个独立的理论贡献，我们解决了 $C^2$ 支持函数的 Gardner 1.5 问题（1995），通过 $S^{D-1}$ 上的最优恢复理论，确立了通用维度下的极小极大速率 $\Theta(R/(\kappa m^{2/(D-1)}))$。
