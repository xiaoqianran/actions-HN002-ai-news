# Staged Factorial Screening for Budget-Constrained Micro-Pretraining
# 用于预算受限微预训练的分阶段因子筛选

**Abstract:** Budget-constrained micro-pretraining often requires triaging many candidate recipes on a shared accelerator before larger search budgets are spent. We study whether a staged fractional-factorial workflow can recover stable early effect structure in this setting.

**摘要：** 在预算受限的情况下，微预训练通常需要在投入更大的搜索预算之前，先在共享加速器上对众多候选方案进行筛选。我们研究了分阶段的部分因子工作流是否能在这种环境下恢复稳定的早期效应结构。

On a fixed autoresearch-derived single-GPU training loop, we run 613 experiments across pilot and follow-up screens at 2, 5, and 10 minutes; full 16-condition seeded reruns at 5 and 10 minutes; targeted seeded anchor checks; same-host greedy and matched-cost random baselines; a 60-minute bridge package; and bounded Windows A100 and Linux L40S anchor continuations through 24 hours.

在基于自动研究（autoresearch）的固定单 GPU 训练循环上，我们进行了 613 次实验，涵盖了 2 分钟、5 分钟和 10 分钟的试点及后续筛选；5 分钟和 10 分钟的 16 条件种子重运行；针对性的种子锚点检查；同主机贪婪算法和匹配成本的随机基准测试；60 分钟的桥接包；以及在 Windows A100 和 Linux L40S 上长达 24 小时的受限锚点延续测试。

Main penalties from total batch, depth, and width are largest at short budgets and relax as budget increases. Within the predeclared seeded full-screen families, D, A, B, and C retain non-zero estimates at 5 and 10 minutes after within-budget Benjamini-Hochberg correction, while E does not.

来自总批次、深度和宽度的主要惩罚在短预算下最为显著，并随着预算的增加而减弱。在预先声明的种子全屏系列中，经过预算内 Benjamini-Hochberg 校正后，D、A、B 和 C 在 5 分钟和 10 分钟时仍保留非零估计值，而 E 则不然。

Random search can reach strong incumbents in this 32-condition space, but repeatedly in the same low-penalty region and without factor attribution. The 60-minute bridge anchor has the lowest mean, although that package does not separate workflow refinement from the larger bridge model's capacity advantage.

随机搜索可以在这个 32 条件的空间中找到强大的现任者（最优解），但往往重复出现在相同的低惩罚区域，且无法进行因子归因。60 分钟的桥接锚点具有最低的平均值，尽管该方案未能将工作流优化与大型桥接模型的容量优势区分开来。

In bounded 12-hour and 24-hour three-anchor continuations on both hosts, the bridge has the lowest sample mean while the non-bridge ordering stays host-sensitive. We therefore present a bounded methods result: use short designed screens to identify high-penalty directions, confirm promising anchors under repeated runs, and refine locally inside the reduced space.

在两个主机上进行的 12 小时和 24 小时三锚点受限延续测试中，桥接方案的样本均值最低，而非桥接方案的排序则对主机环境敏感。因此，我们提出了一种受限方法论：利用短时间的实验设计筛选来识别高惩罚方向，通过重复运行确认有前景的锚点，并在缩小的空间内进行局部优化。

The evidence supports a bridge-centered recommendation through 24 hours on two hosts, not hardware-invariant ranking or general hyperparameter-optimization superiority.

证据支持在两个主机上进行长达 24 小时的以桥接为中心的方案建议，而非支持硬件无关的排名或通用的超参数优化优势。
