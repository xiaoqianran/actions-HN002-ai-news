**Julian Chokkattu:** The developer betas are launching today for all of Apple's new operating system updates, with the official versions coming later this fall. Tim Cook signs off, saying, “I still believe the best is yet ahead."
**Julian Chokkattu：** Apple 所有新操作系统的开发者测试版将于今日发布，正式版将于今年秋季晚些时候推出。蒂姆·库克（Tim Cook）在结尾说道：“我依然相信，最好的时刻还在前方。”

**Julian Chokkattu:** Apple is injecting Apple Intelligence into the Photos app. These features “enhance images in ways that respect the original moment.” These features are: Clean Up, Extend, and Spatial Reframe. Clean Up now does a better job of removing distractions and unwanted objects in the image. Extend lets you expand images to give more breathing room, generating pixels based on what it thinks should be there. Spatial Reframing uses on-device spatial models, allowing you to change the photo's perspective slightly. It also works with generative AI models to fill in the gaps that might change with the new perspective.
**Julian Chokkattu：** Apple 正在将 Apple Intelligence 注入“照片”应用。这些功能旨在“以尊重原始瞬间的方式增强图像”。这些功能包括：清理（Clean Up）、扩展（Extend）和空间重构（Spatial Reframe）。“清理”现在能更好地移除图像中干扰视线的物体；“扩展”允许你扩大图像以增加留白，并根据 AI 的预测生成像素；“空间重构”利用设备端的空间模型，允许你轻微改变照片的视角，并配合生成式 AI 模型来填充因视角改变而产生的空白。

**Michael Calore:** As digital creators, we now have so many choices for which slop factory to employ to fulfill our make-believe image needs.
**Michael Calore：** 作为数字创作者，我们现在有太多的选择，可以决定使用哪家“垃圾工厂”来满足我们虚构的图像需求。

**Michael Calore:** Video descriptions in the Home app.
**Michael Calore：** “家庭”应用中的视频描述功能。

**Julian Chokkattu:** Take a shot every time Apple says Private Cloud Compute.
**Julian Chokkattu：** 每当 Apple 提到“私有云计算”（Private Cloud Compute）时，就喝一杯。

**Julian Chokkattu:** Making a shortcut in the Apple Shortcuts app wasn't always straightforward. Now, you can just use natural language to create a shortcut by just saying what you want, and Apple Intelligence will take care of it for you.
**Julian Chokkattu：** 在 Apple “快捷指令”应用中创建快捷方式并不总是那么简单。现在，你只需用自然语言说出你的需求，Apple Intelligence 就会为你完成创建。

**Julian Chokkattu:** Apple is finally giving its Home app some love. There are smarter notification descriptions of what's happening around the home if you have compatible security cameras—much like how Google and Amazon have rolled out more descriptive AI notifications on their respective smart home platforms.
**Julian Chokkattu：** Apple 终于开始重视“家庭”应用了。如果你有兼容的安防摄像头，它会提供更智能的通知描述，告知家中发生的情况——这与谷歌和亚马逊在其智能家居平台上推出的更具描述性的 AI 通知非常相似。

**Michael Calore:** Messages and Mail will get new suggestion icons to power actions (add this to my calendar) with one tap.
**Michael Calore：** “信息”和“邮件”将获得新的建议图标，支持一键执行操作（例如“添加到我的日历”）。

**Michael Calore:** Safari will start to group tabs together by topic. It analyzes the content, then sorts everything into topical headers. This reduces clutter, but of course it means that no tab will always be exactly where you left it.
**Michael Calore：** Safari 将开始按主题对标签页进行分组。它会分析内容，然后将所有内容归类到主题标题下。这减少了混乱，但当然也意味着标签页不会总是固定在你放置的位置。

**Julian Chokkattu:** Safari is getting some Apple Intelligence upgrades. Apple Intelligence will “tame your tabs” by organizing them into topics. So if you have a bunch of tabs on coffee—maybe you're researching beans—they'll be grouped into a “Coffee” topic. There's also “Describe an Extension,” where Safari can create a custom extension for whatever you need. Apple's password manager also takes action on your behalf to fix your passwords if there's been a breach that requires a password change.
**Julian Chokkattu：** Safari 正在获得一些 Apple Intelligence 升级。Apple Intelligence 将通过按主题组织标签页来“驯服”它们。例如，如果你有一堆关于咖啡的标签页（也许你在研究咖啡豆），它们会被归入“咖啡”主题。此外还有“描述扩展”（Describe an Extension）功能，Safari 可以为你所需的任何功能创建自定义扩展。如果发生泄露需要更改密码，Apple 的密码管理器也会代表你采取行动来修复密码。

**Michael Calore:** Siri’s writing assistance in action. Quit school now, kids—the computer can do it all!
**Michael Calore：** Siri 的写作辅助功能演示。孩子们，现在就退学吧——电脑什么都能干！

**Julian Chokkattu:** Lots of writing assistance with Siri AI. You can describe what you need with natural language, and Siri can generate a draft. Siri will understand how you talk to specific people and generate drafts in that style. And Siri will give you feedback on your writing, with Apple Intelligence also featuring automatic proofreading. Apple says Siri AI will be available in English to start, with other languages to follow soon.
**Julian Chokkattu：** Siri AI 提供了大量的写作辅助功能。你可以用自然语言描述你的需求，Siri 就能生成草稿。Siri 会理解你与特定人的交流方式，并以那种风格生成草稿。此外，Siri 还会对你的写作提供反馈，Apple Intelligence 还具备自动校对功能。Apple 表示，Siri AI 将首先支持英语，其他语言也将很快跟进。

**Michael Calore:** The dedicated Siri app on watchOS.
**Michael Calore：** watchOS 上的独立 Siri 应用。

**Reece Rogers:** Maybe I'm just cheap, but looking forward to that check-splitting feature! No, I did not get three espresso martinis.
**Reece Rogers：** 也许是我太抠门了，但我很期待那个分摊账单的功能！不，我没有点三杯浓缩马提尼。

**Julian Chokkattu:** Siri is now integrated into the Camera app. Next to the Photo and Video tab, it kind of acts like Google Lens. Point your iPhone at anything, and Siri can help—like snap a pic of the bill at a restaurant and Siri can help you split the tab.
**Julian Chokkattu：** Siri 现在集成到了相机应用中。在“照片”和“视频”标签旁边，它有点像 Google Lens。将 iPhone 对准任何东西，Siri 都能提供帮助——比如拍一张餐厅账单的照片，Siri 就能帮你分摊费用。

**Julian Chokkattu:** The Siri AI app is everywhere, even on watchOS and visionOS. On the latter, you just look at the Siri orb and talk; no need to say “Hey Siri.”
**Julian Chokkattu：** Siri AI 应用无处不在，甚至在 watchOS 和 visionOS 上也是如此。在 visionOS 上，你只需注视 Siri 的球体图标并说话即可，无需说“Hey Siri”。
