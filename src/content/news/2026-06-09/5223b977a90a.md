# Cameras get an Apple Intelligence boost in Apple Home
# Apple Home 迎来 Apple Intelligence 升级，摄像头功能大提升

Security cameras in Apple Home will generate text descriptions and let you search footage with natural language. HomeKit Secure Video finally gets 4K support.
Apple Home 中的安防摄像头将能够生成文字描述，并支持使用自然语言搜索录像。HomeKit Secure Video 终于迎来了 4K 支持。

Cameras connected to Apple’s HomeKit Secure Video will be able to generate AI-powered video descriptions.
连接到 Apple HomeKit Secure Video 的摄像头将能够生成由 AI 驱动的视频描述。

Apple Intelligence is coming to cameras connected to Apple Home. At WWDC, Apple announced that with iOS 27, its HomeKit Secure Video service will use Apple Intelligence to analyze footage and generate descriptions summarizing what the camera saw. You can also search footage with natural language to find clips from across connected cameras, such as when a package was delivered.
Apple Intelligence 即将登陆连接至 Apple Home 的摄像头。在 WWDC 大会上，苹果宣布随着 iOS 27 的发布，其 HomeKit Secure Video 服务将利用 Apple Intelligence 分析录像，并生成总结摄像头所拍摄内容的描述。用户还可以通过自然语言搜索录像，从所有连接的摄像头中查找特定片段，例如“包裹何时送达”。

The Home app will also use Apple Intelligence to connect relevant clips together to give you a picture of what’s happening across your home and surface “noteworthy clips” on the search page to quickly show you videos that might be important.
“家庭” (Home) App 还将利用 Apple Intelligence 将相关片段串联起来，为您呈现家中发生事件的完整图景，并在搜索页面展示“值得注意的片段”，以便快速查看可能重要的视频。

This AI boost is similar to features that Amazon’s Ring and Google’s Nest have added to their home security cameras over the last year. It’s possible these updates are in preparation for Apple’s rumored upcoming video doorbell and security camera.
此次 AI 升级与亚马逊 Ring 和谷歌 Nest 在过去一年中为其家庭安防摄像头增加的功能类似。这些更新很可能是为了迎接传闻中苹果即将推出的视频门铃和安防摄像头。

Apple also announced that support for up to 4K video resolution is coming to compatible HomeKit Secure Video cameras. This should help with clearer images to feed the AI; prior to this, the service only supported 1080p. An iCloud Plus plan is required for HomeKit Secure Video, and will include the new AI features. Apple didn’t mention if it was bringing Matter support for cameras to its smart home platform.
苹果还宣布，兼容的 HomeKit Secure Video 摄像头将支持最高 4K 的视频分辨率。这将有助于提供更清晰的图像以供 AI 分析；在此之前，该服务仅支持 1080p。使用 HomeKit Secure Video 需要 iCloud Plus 订阅计划，该计划将包含这些新的 AI 功能。苹果并未提及是否会为其智能家居平台引入针对摄像头的 Matter 支持。

Finally, a small but welcome update to the Home app is the ability to condense multiple alerts for the same device into a single notification, which will then update as more activity occurs.
最后，“家庭” App 还迎来了一个虽小但实用的更新：能够将同一设备的多次警报合并为一条通知，并随着后续活动的发生实时更新。
