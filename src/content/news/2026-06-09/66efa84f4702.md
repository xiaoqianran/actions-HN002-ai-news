# Apple’s WWDC AI demos looked more real after $250M false ad settlement
# 在 2.5 亿美元虚假广告和解案后，苹果 WWDC 的 AI 演示看起来更真实了

The vibe of Apple’s 2026 Worldwide Developers Conference felt like a spouse proudly listing all the items on a honey-do-list they’d finally completed. Rather than showcase something exciting and new, Apple launched the keynote detailing fixes to last year’s “Liquid Glass” design; an overhaul of its awful search function; improvements to its Playground feature, and so on. Perhaps most importantly, two years after promising but failing to launch a smarter Siri, Apple finally showed off an overhauled version of its AI-powered voice assistant.

2026 年苹果全球开发者大会（WWDC）的氛围，就像一位配偶自豪地列出他们终于完成的“待办事项清单”。苹果在主题演讲中并没有展示什么令人兴奋的新东西，而是详细介绍了对去年“液态玻璃”（Liquid Glass）设计的修复、对糟糕搜索功能的全面改造、对 Playground 功能的改进等等。或许最重要的是，在承诺推出更智能的 Siri 却未能兑现两年后，苹果终于展示了其人工智能语音助手的升级版本。

But the most telling detail wasn’t what Apple announced. It was how it chose to show some things off. Many of the Apple Intelligence demoes featured someone standing, phone in hand, pressing buttons or using voice commands in real time, while another camera showed off the phone’s response. These weren’t live on-stage, anything-could-go-wrong demos; they were pre-taped. But they looked far more like proof of working features than what Apple showed at WWDC 2024, when the company first unveiled Apple Intelligence and a new Siri to the world through slickly produced videos that turned out to be more promise than product.

但最能说明问题的细节并非苹果宣布了什么，而是它选择如何展示这些内容。许多 Apple Intelligence 的演示都采用了这样的形式：有人手持手机，实时按下按钮或使用语音指令，而另一台摄像机则展示手机的响应。这些并非现场直播、随时可能出错的演示，而是预先录制好的。但与苹果在 2024 年 WWDC 上展示的内容相比，它们看起来更像是功能可用的证明。当时，苹果通过制作精良的视频首次向世界展示了 Apple Intelligence 和新的 Siri，但最终发现这些视频更多是“画饼”而非产品。

This demonstration style was noticed, with comments on X on Monday comparing today’s keynote to those 2024 so-called “vaporware” demos. Apple said at that time that the features would be available soon to those who upgraded to the iPhone 15 Pro and newer devices with M1 chips or better. But by March 2025, Apple admitted to Daring Fireball that rolling out the features shown via production video was “going to take us longer than we thought to deliver.” Not long after, the Cupertino company faced a lawsuit in federal court alleging false advertising over the features shown at that 2024 event — a case that carried real reputational risk for a company whose brand has long been built on the promise that its products just work.

这种演示风格引起了人们的注意，周一在 X 平台上的评论将今天的主题演讲与 2024 年那些所谓的“雾件”（vaporware）演示进行了比较。苹果当时表示，升级到 iPhone 15 Pro 及更新设备、或配备 M1 芯片及以上设备的机型很快就能使用这些功能。但到了 2025 年 3 月，苹果向 Daring Fireball 承认，推出视频中展示的功能“比我们预想的需要更长时间”。不久之后，这家位于库比蒂诺的公司在联邦法院面临诉讼，指控其在 2024 年活动中展示的功能存在虚假广告行为——对于一家长期以“产品好用”为品牌承诺的公司来说，这起案件带来了真正的声誉风险。

Last month, Apple agreed to pay a $250 million settlement on the suit, without admitting wrongdoing. Monday’s presentation appeared designed, at least in part, to avoid a repeat. There were still plenty of fully produced videos of features, like the one showing how to adjust Siri’s voice and another demonstrating improved voice-to-text transcription. But many of the AI features were shown in this “live-like” format, with someone using the feature on an actual device. The implicit message being that these features work on actual devices, and you will soon have them.

上个月，苹果同意支付 2.5 亿美元就该诉讼达成和解，但并未承认存在不当行为。周一的演示在某种程度上似乎就是为了避免重蹈覆辙。虽然现场仍有大量制作精良的功能视频，例如展示如何调整 Siri 语音的视频，以及演示改进后的语音转文字功能的视频，但许多 AI 功能都采用了这种“类现场”的格式，即由专人在实际设备上操作。其隐含的信息是：这些功能在实际设备上确实可用，而且你很快就能用上。

Apple also is not requiring users to buy the latest iPhone to get these features. The new Siri will be available through the new iOS 27 on iPhone 15 Pro and Pro Max and all iPhone 16 models and later, according to the company. The current model is the iPhone 17, meaning most users who upgraded in the past couple of years won’t need to buy new hardware to get access. It’s a concession, perhaps, that Apple won’t lock the features behind new devices to create upgrade pressure when it promised, two years ago, that such features would be available on iPhone 15.

苹果也没有要求用户必须购买最新的 iPhone 才能获得这些功能。据该公司称，新的 Siri 将通过全新的 iOS 27 系统在 iPhone 15 Pro、Pro Max 以及所有 iPhone 16 及后续机型上提供。目前的机型是 iPhone 17，这意味着过去几年升级过手机的大多数用户无需购买新硬件即可使用。这或许是一种让步，因为苹果两年前曾承诺这些功能将在 iPhone 15 上提供，因此它不会再将这些功能锁定在新款设备上以制造升级压力。

Apple also said the new features will be available across its broader hardware lineup, including the iPad mini (A17 Pro), iPad models with M1 or later, MacBook Neo (A18 Pro), Mac models with M1 or later, Apple Vision Pro, Apple Watch Series 10 or later, Apple Watch Ultra 2 or later, and Apple Watch SE 3 when paired with an Apple Intelligence-enabled iPhone nearby.

苹果还表示，这些新功能将覆盖其更广泛的硬件产品线，包括 iPad mini (A17 Pro)、配备 M1 或更新芯片的 iPad 机型、MacBook Neo (A18 Pro)、配备 M1 或更新芯片的 Mac 机型、Apple Vision Pro、Apple Watch Series 10 或更新机型、Apple Watch Ultra 2 或更新机型，以及在附近配有支持 Apple Intelligence 的 iPhone 时使用的 Apple Watch SE 3。
