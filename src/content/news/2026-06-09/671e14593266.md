# WWDC 2026: Everything announced on Siri AI, iOS 27, Apple Intelligence and more

**WWDC 2026：Siri AI、iOS 27、Apple Intelligence 等发布内容全汇总**

Apple’s WWDC 2026 event kicked off this morning at 10 a.m. PT at Apple Park, starting a week full of expected announcements around Siri, iOS 27, Apple Intelligence and more, along with developer events and demos. This year’s event is particularly notable for a couple things. It marks CEO Tim Cook’s last with the company, after announcing he’s handing things off to Senior Vice President of Hardware Engineering John Ternus September 1. And it’s expected to play host to Apple’s attempt to give Siri and its AI efforts overall a big boost after handing some work off to Google and delaying some releases. Are they succeeding? Keep tabs on this page, and the rest of our ongoing coverage, to find out!

苹果 WWDC 2026 大会于太平洋时间今早 10 点在 Apple Park 拉开帷幕，开启了为期一周的活动。此次大会涵盖了关于 Siri、iOS 27、Apple Intelligence 等备受期待的发布，以及各类开发者活动和演示。今年的活动尤为引人注目：这是首席执行官蒂姆·库克（Tim Cook）任内的最后一次大会，他已宣布将于 9 月 1 日将职务移交给硬件工程高级副总裁约翰·特努斯（John Ternus）。此外，在将部分工作外包给谷歌并推迟部分发布后，此次大会也被视为苹果试图为 Siri 及其整体 AI 战略注入强心剂的关键时刻。他们成功了吗？请持续关注本页面及我们的后续报道以获取答案！

### Apple reveals Siri AI
### 苹果揭晓 Siri AI

As expected, Apple made the case for an improved experience with its longstanding Siri assistant, which it admitted faces greater expectations from users in the age of AI. With Google Gemini under the hood, Apple claims that the new Siri updates will more it more capable, conversational, compatible with visual intelligence, and it will be housed in a standalone app in addition to working across existing apps. You can get a full rundown of all the new Siri AI updates right here.

正如预期，苹果展示了其长期助手 Siri 的改进体验，并承认在 AI 时代，用户对 Siri 提出了更高的期望。在谷歌 Gemini 的加持下，苹果声称新的 Siri 更新将使其功能更强大、更具对话性、兼容视觉智能，并且除了在现有应用中运行外，还将拥有一个独立的应用程序。您可以在此处获取所有 Siri AI 更新的完整详情。

Before rolling out the enhancements and features, Apple was adamant about its privacy-centric approach to AI. “We believe privacy in AI is non-negotiable,” Apple Senior Vice President Craig Federighi said during the stream, going so far as to say that “data is only used to execute your request, and outside experts can continue to verify this promise at any time.”

在推出这些增强功能之前，苹果坚持其以隐私为中心的 AI 策略。苹果高级副总裁克雷格·费德里吉（Craig Federighi）在直播中表示：“我们认为 AI 中的隐私是不容妥协的。”他甚至强调：“数据仅用于执行您的请求，外部专家可以随时验证这一承诺。”

### The next generation of Apple Intelligence
### 下一代 Apple Intelligence

To go along with its new Siri AI overhaul, the tech giant announced a slew of new Apple Intelligence updates across its apps, including tab management for Safari, one-tap password updating, cross-app context awareness, and more. Additionally, Messages is getting AI-powered reply suggestions, while the Phone app can now pull context from other apps like Mail and Messages mid-call. Apple said it collaborated with Google and the Gemini family of models to develop the next generation of Apple Foundation Models that power its integrated Apple Intelligence experiences.

配合全新的 Siri AI 重构，这家科技巨头宣布了跨应用的 Apple Intelligence 更新，包括 Safari 标签页管理、一键更新密码、跨应用上下文感知等。此外，信息（Messages）应用增加了 AI 驱动的回复建议，电话（Phone）应用现在可以在通话中从邮件（Mail）和信息等其他应用中提取上下文。苹果表示，已与谷歌及 Gemini 模型家族合作，开发了驱动其集成 Apple Intelligence 体验的下一代苹果基础模型。

### Liquid Glass gets some opt-in rollbacks
### Liquid Glass 界面支持部分回退选项

If you were among those who weren’t exactly keen on last year’s Liquid Glass design updates, you weren’t alone. And while Apple isn’t switching to a new aesthetic, you are going to be able to dial back some of its elements, or really highlight them if you’re vibing with it. And for the app icon critics out there fresh from Spotify’s disco ball update, Apple showed off a new, layered approach to Liquid Glass within its apps.

如果您对去年推出的 Liquid Glass 设计更新不太感冒，那您并不孤单。虽然苹果并没有完全更换设计风格，但您现在可以选择调低某些视觉元素，或者如果您喜欢这种风格，也可以进一步强化它们。对于那些对 Spotify 迪斯科球图标更新感到不满的批评者，苹果展示了一种在应用内使用 Liquid Glass 的全新分层处理方式。

### Image Playground gets another chance
### Image Playground 获得新机遇

The AI generating app hasn’t exactly taken the world by storm, which depending on you view on slop may be a good thing, but Apple rolled out a renewed pitch for users to actually start generating images, with a focus on its possible uses across many features of your devices, with an exclusion set on any training based on photos generated using the app. That, plus performance updates coming alongside Apple Intelligence upgrades, might at least take it out of the “suck” category for our own Senior Writer Amanda Silberling.

这款 AI 生成应用并没有引起轰动（这对于那些反感 AI 垃圾内容的人来说或许是件好事），但苹果再次向用户推广该功能，重点展示了它在设备各项功能中的潜在用途，并明确排除了使用该应用生成的照片进行模型训练。再加上随 Apple Intelligence 升级带来的性能提升，这或许至少能让它摆脱我们资深撰稿人 Amanda Silberling 心中的“糟糕”评价。

### iOS 27 is stretching back to the iPhone 11
### iOS 27 支持机型扩展至 iPhone 11

Claiming that its upcoming update will be “available to more users than any iOS release ever,” Apple revealed that all devices from the iPhone 11 onward will be eligible for their upcoming software update. And that update comes with a flurry of performance improvements it’s touting across a number of its OS releases this year, with Apple claiming that new photos will appear 70 percent more swiftly, AirDrop transfers will be 80 percent faster and CPU schedulers will be improved to help multitasking.

苹果声称即将推出的更新将是“有史以来覆盖用户最多的 iOS 版本”，并宣布从 iPhone 11 开始的所有设备都将支持此次软件更新。此次更新带来了多项性能提升，苹果称新照片的显示速度将提高 70%，AirDrop 传输速度将提升 80%，CPU 调度器也将得到优化以辅助多任务处理。

### New parental controls for iPhones
### iPhone 新增家长控制功能

Apple spent a significant amount of the WWDC event showcasing a suite of tools for parents looking for greater control over what their children’s devices can and can’t do. Parents will be able to determine who their kid can call on the phone, what apps and websites they can access, with Apple making suggestions about how those restrictions can change over time. By default though, its “Ask to Browse” feature limiting access, and “Ask to Buy” for app store and in-app purchases, will be set as a default for devices set up for children younger than 13.

苹果在 WWDC 上花费了大量时间展示了一套工具，旨在帮助家长更好地控制孩子的设备使用权限。家长可以决定孩子可以拨打谁的电话、可以访问哪些应用和网站，苹果还会就这些限制如何随时间调整提供建议。默认情况下，针对 13 岁以下儿童设置的设备，其“请求浏览”（Ask to Browse）限制访问功能和应用商店及内购的“请求购买”（Ask to Buy）功能将自动开启。

### Search gets an overhaul
### 搜索功能全面重构

Frustrated with searching through your iPhone for…well…pretty much anything? Search got a dedicated session during WWDC to tout a series of improvements, which you can learn more about here. “We’ve all had that moment where you search for something you know is there, but it just won’t show up,” Stacey Ford, vice president of OS Program Management said. “So on iOS, iPadOS and macOS, we’ve rebuilt the foundation of search that powers Spotlight, Photos, and Mail.”

还在为在 iPhone 上搜索不到东西而苦恼吗？WWDC 专门为搜索功能开设了一个环节，介绍了一系列改进。OS 项目管理副总裁 Stacey Ford 表示：“我们都有过这样的时刻：搜索你知道存在的东西，但它就是不显示。因此，我们在 iOS、iPadOS 和 macOS 上重构了驱动 Spotlight、照片和邮件搜索的基础架构。”

### Photos gets new AI editing tools
### 照片应用新增 AI 编辑工具

To take on popular AI photo editing apps, Apple is bringing new AI features to its Photos app. A new spatial “Reframe” feature will let users use AI to adjust the perspective of an image as if they had repositioned the camera in the original scene. The new “Extend” tool expands images to adjust the aspect ratio of an image or add more to a scene. The app’s popular “Cleanup” tool is also getting an upgrade so users can remove distractions with better quality and more realistic infill with generative AI.

为了与流行的 AI 照片编辑应用竞争，苹果为照片应用带来了新的 AI 功能。全新的空间“重构”（Reframe）功能允许用户利用 AI 调整图像视角，就像在原始场景中重新调整了相机位置一样。新的“扩展”（Extend）工具可以调整图像的纵横比或为场景添加更多内容。该应用广受欢迎的“清理”（Cleanup）工具也得到了升级，用户可以利用生成式 AI 以更高的质量和更逼真的填充效果移除照片中的干扰元素。

### Apple takes on AI dictation apps
### 苹果挑战 AI 听写应用

Apple is launching a new systemwide dictation experience that’s built into the keyboard on iOS 27 and can correct spellings, punctuation, and capitalization. The update comes as AI dictation apps like Wispr Flow and Willow have been gaining popularity. These apps clean up filler words like “ums” and “Ahs” and format the text after transcribing based on context.

苹果正在推出一种全新的系统级听写体验，该功能内置于 iOS 27 的键盘中，可以自动纠正拼写、标点符号和大小写。此次更新正值 Wispr Flow 和 Willow 等 AI 听写应用日益流行之际。这些应用能够自动清理“嗯”、“啊”等口头禅，并在转录后根据上下文对文本进行格式化。
