### Why this year’s World Cup ball may not fly as far
### 为什么今年的世界杯足球可能飞得没那么远

Much is new about this month’s FIFA World Cup tournament. It hosts more teams than ever before. It’s the first to occur in three different host countries. And, like every World Cup for over half a century, it will employ a football with a brand-new design.
本月的国际足联世界杯有很多新变化。参赛球队数量创下历史新高。这是首次由三个不同国家共同举办。而且，像半个多世纪以来的每一届世界杯一样，它将采用一款全新设计的足球。

Through wind-tunnel experiments, researchers found that long-distance kicks with Adidas’s new Trionda ball might not travel as far as they did in the past. The payoff is a more predictable flight path, something players have not always enjoyed from World Cup balls. Find out how a few grooves and seams can change the way the game is played. —Jenna Ahart
通过风洞实验，研究人员发现，使用阿迪达斯新款 Trionda 足球进行长距离踢球时，球的飞行距离可能不如以往。作为回报，它拥有更可预测的飞行轨迹，而这正是球员们在以往世界杯用球中并不总能体验到的。了解一下几个凹槽和接缝是如何改变比赛方式的吧。——Jenna Ahart

**How AI is helping historians better understand our past**
**AI 如何帮助历史学家更好地理解我们的过去**

The digitization of historical records is making it possible to study the past in new ways. Historians are now using machine learning—particularly deep neural networks—to analyze everything from centuries-old astronomy textbooks to ancient Greek inscriptions. The technology is helping researchers uncover new patterns in the historical record. But it also introduces risks, including the possibility that machine learning will slip bias or outright falsifications into our understanding of the past. Read the full story on how AI is transforming the study of history. —Moira Donovan
历史记录的数字化使得以新方式研究过去成为可能。历史学家们现在正在使用机器学习——特别是深度神经网络——来分析从数百年历史的天文学教科书到古希腊铭文等各种资料。这项技术正在帮助研究人员发现历史记录中的新模式。但它也带来了风险，包括机器学习可能将偏见或彻头彻尾的伪造信息带入我们对过去的理解中。阅读关于 AI 如何改变历史研究的完整报道。——Moira Donovan
