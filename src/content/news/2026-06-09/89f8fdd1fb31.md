# WWDC 2026

WWDC26 New Siri AI powered by Apple Intelligence. Expanded child safety features. And helpful updates all around. Coming later this year.
WWDC26 带来了由 Apple Intelligence 驱动的全新 Siri AI、扩展的儿童安全功能，以及全方位的实用更新。将于今年晚些时候推出。

All-new Siri AI. Truly helpful. Truly yours. Siri AI powered by Apple Intelligence is more helpful than ever, with richer answers, natural conversations, and a new, dedicated app.
全新的 Siri AI。真正实用，真正属于你。由 Apple Intelligence 驱动的 Siri AI 比以往任何时候都更强大，提供更丰富的内容回答、更自然的对话体验，并拥有一个全新的专属应用程序。

Apple Intelligence in more apps. Express yourself with next-level intelligent photo editing tools and an updated Image Playground, and save time in more of your favorite apps, including Photos, Messages, and Safari.
Apple Intelligence 赋能更多应用。通过顶尖的智能照片编辑工具和升级版的 Image Playground 尽情表达自我，并在包括“照片”、“信息”和 Safari 在内的更多常用应用中节省时间。

Easy, intuitive child safety protections. New and expanded child safety features help make it easier for parents to keep kids safe online.
简单直观的儿童安全保护。全新且扩展的儿童安全功能，旨在帮助家长更轻松地保障孩子在网络环境下的安全。

Tons of improvements all around. Discover a wide range of performance enhancements that make your devices more responsive, reliable, and delightful to use.
全方位的海量改进。探索一系列性能提升，让你的设备响应更迅速、运行更可靠，使用体验更愉悦。

Apple Event September 9, 2025. Get to know the next generation of iPhone — iPhone 17 Pro, iPhone 17, and iPhone Air. Explore AirPods Pro 3 featuring the world’s best in-ear Active Noise Cancellation. And discover the latest Apple Watch family.
2025 年 9 月 9 日 Apple 发布会。了解新一代 iPhone——iPhone 17 Pro、iPhone 17 和 iPhone Air。探索配备全球最佳入耳式主动降噪功能的 AirPods Pro 3，以及最新的 Apple Watch 系列产品。
