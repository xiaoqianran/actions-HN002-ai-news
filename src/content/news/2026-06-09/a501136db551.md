### Tired of Hcaptcha?
### 厌倦了 Hcaptcha？

If you guys are tired of Hcaptcha for web crawling and botting issues, I made a repo that may solve your problem. HcaptchaSolver It basically gets your proxy sitekey and the current URL that you're on then it sends it to an electron client that simulates a real page in the same url and someone or you, needs to solve it so in theory it removes the gap between you and actual browser and it optimize your proxy and your memory useage since we can all agree that chromimum/firefox browser are hungry for RAM and CPU so all you need to do is to pass the sitekey and other information and Voilà.
如果你厌倦了在网络爬虫和自动化脚本中遇到 Hcaptcha，我创建了一个可能解决你问题的代码库：HcaptchaSolver。它的基本原理是获取你的代理 sitekey 和当前 URL，然后将其发送到一个 Electron 客户端，该客户端会在相同的 URL 下模拟一个真实的页面。接着，你或其他人只需在该页面完成验证即可。从理论上讲，这消除了你与真实浏览器之间的差距，并优化了代理和内存的使用，因为我们都知道 Chromium/Firefox 浏览器非常消耗内存和 CPU。所以，你只需要传入 sitekey 和其他信息，搞定！

Conterbuition are very welcome. I just started it as a fun project, hope others find it useful Bye.
非常欢迎大家贡献代码。我最初只是把它当作一个有趣的个人项目来做，希望它对其他人也有用。再见。
