# Inside the Visual Mind: Neuroscience-Motivated Concept Circuits for Interpreting and Steering Vision Transformers
# 洞察视觉思维：用于解释和引导视觉 Transformer 的神经科学驱动概念回路

**Abstract:** Despite high accuracy, Vision Transformer (ViT) predictions can be driven by spurious cues, raising the need to understand their inner workings before safe deployment. Sparse autoencoders (SAEs) provide a promising lens for decomposing model representations into human-interpretable concepts, yet adapting SAE-based interpretation to ViTs remains challenging due to limited control over concept coverage and subjective, non-scalable feature interpretation.

**摘要：** 尽管视觉 Transformer (ViT) 具有高准确性，但其预测结果往往可能受到虚假线索的驱动，这使得在安全部署前理解其内部运作机制变得至关重要。稀疏自编码器 (SAE) 为将模型表征分解为人类可解释的概念提供了一个有前景的视角，然而，由于对概念覆盖范围的控制有限，且特征解释具有主观性且难以扩展，将基于 SAE 的解释方法应用于 ViT 仍然面临挑战。

To fill the gaps, motivated by neuroscience-inspired principles, we propose ViSAE, a mechanistic interpretability toolbox for understanding ViT inner workings through concept circuits. ViSAE consists of three components: (1) A probing suite with 64K images and a 16K visually grounded concept vocabulary, improving concept coverage efficiency by 20x over ImageNet and interpretation accuracy by 28.7% over existing concept sets. (2) Top-down concept reading and Bottom-up circuit tracing algorithms that automatically recover ViT inner workings via concept circuits. (3) Applications for auditing and steering ViT behavior.

为了填补这些空白，受神经科学原理的启发，我们提出了 ViSAE，这是一个通过概念回路来理解 ViT 内部运作的机械可解释性工具箱。ViSAE 由三个部分组成：(1) 一个包含 64,000 张图像和 16,000 个视觉基础概念词汇的探测套件，其概念覆盖效率比 ImageNet 提高了 20 倍，解释准确率比现有概念集提高了 28.7%。(2) 自上而下的概念读取算法和自下而上的回路追踪算法，能够通过概念回路自动恢复 ViT 的内部运作机制。(3) 用于审计和引导 ViT 行为的应用。
