# MedicalRec: Medical recommender system for image classification without retraining
# MedicalRec：无需重新训练的医学图像分类推荐系统

The emergence of machine learning and deep learning has revolutionized the efficiency of diagnostic, therapeutic, and administrative systems in healthcare. However, this rapid adoption has come at the cost of requiring significant computing power and energy consumption, as well as e-waste disposal and carbon emissions.
机器学习和深度学习的兴起彻底改变了医疗保健领域诊断、治疗和管理系统的效率。然而，这种快速应用也带来了沉重的代价，包括对巨大计算能力和能源消耗的需求，以及电子垃圾处理和碳排放问题。

One of the challenges of these models is choosing the right model for classification tasks. To this end, researchers attempt to identify the optimal model using their data through trial and error, which involves energy consumption and waste. The goal of this study is to develop a model-based recommender system for medical image classification.
这些模型面临的挑战之一是如何为分类任务选择合适的模型。为此，研究人员通常通过反复试验来确定最佳模型，但这涉及能源消耗和资源浪费。本研究旨在开发一种基于模型的医学图像分类推荐系统。

For this purpose, a data set was collected from 3,000 articles in the field of medical image classification. This dataset, publicly available under the name MedicalRec-Bench, contains over 5,000 records of models tested in various tasks, including Skin Cancer Classification, Tumour Classification, Wound Classification, Breast Cancer, and MRI classification.
为此，研究团队从医学图像分类领域的 3,000 篇文章中收集了数据集。该数据集以 MedicalRec-Bench 的名称公开，包含超过 5,000 条在各种任务中测试过的模型记录，包括皮肤癌分类、肿瘤分类、伤口分类、乳腺癌分类和 MRI 分类。

The dataset was evaluated in four different modes, depending on the number of features: MedicalRec I (5 features), MedicalRec II (9 features), MedicalRec III (11 features), and MedicalRec IV (18 features). Collecting all values for the features is challenging due to non-reporting by the authors; hence, the dataset contains significant amounts of missing values.
该数据集根据特征数量的不同，在四种模式下进行了评估：MedicalRec I（5 个特征）、MedicalRec II（9 个特征）、MedicalRec III（11 个特征）和 MedicalRec IV（18 个特征）。由于作者往往未报告所有特征值，收集这些数据极具挑战性，因此数据集中存在大量缺失值。

The Medical Recommender System (MedicalRec) is a transformer-based model used for item recommendations in this study. This model achieved remarkable results in the evaluation on the dataset and in the evaluation with 12 base models. This model achieved a maximum HitRate@100 of 75.5%. The dataset and implementations are available through the GitHub link.
医学推荐系统 (MedicalRec) 是一种基于 Transformer 的模型，用于本研究中的项目推荐。该模型在数据集评估以及与 12 个基准模型的对比评估中均取得了显著成果，最高 HitRate@100 达到了 75.5%。数据集和实现代码可通过 GitHub 链接获取。
