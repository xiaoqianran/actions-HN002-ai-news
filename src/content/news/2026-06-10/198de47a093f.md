# The Smart Bird Feeders Everyone’s Talking About (and Actually Buying) (2026)
# 每个人都在谈论（并且真的在买）的智能喂鸟器（2026年）

You’ve probably seen a smart bird feeder or know someone who has one. They’re easily recognizable with their clear housing, cameras, and solar panels. Perhaps a friend or family member has sent you a photo or video of a bright goldfinch or handsome woodpecker (guilty). But are these feeders really worth their $100-plus price tag? Are they durable? And what about the squirrel problem?

你可能见过智能喂鸟器，或者认识拥有它的人。它们很容易辨认：透明的外壳、摄像头和太阳能电池板。也许朋友或家人曾给你发过一张金翅雀或啄木鸟的照片或视频（我承认我干过）。但这些喂鸟器真的值100多美元的价格吗？它们耐用吗？还有，松鼠的问题怎么解决？

To answer these questions, I’ve been testing the most popular smart bird feeders, including Netvue’s original Birdfy Lite Feeder and Birdbuddy’s Pro, for almost two years—in rain, snow, and heat, and in a yard with persistent squirrels. I’ve explored the apps and notification settings, examined the photo and video quality, noted which features are hidden behind a subscription paywall, and used Cornell Lab’s All About Birds—as well as Google Lens and my own expertise—to verify the accuracy of AI identifications. The recommendations below are what I would buy with my own money, having tested dozens of brands and models for a minimum of four weeks each.

为了回答这些问题，我测试了市面上最受欢迎的智能喂鸟器，包括Netvue的初代Birdfy Lite和Birdbuddy Pro，测试时间长达近两年——涵盖了雨天、雪天、高温天气，以及松鼠频繁出没的院子。我深入研究了应用程序和通知设置，评估了照片和视频质量，记录了哪些功能被锁定在付费订阅之后，并利用康奈尔实验室的“All About Birds”、Google Lens以及我个人的专业知识来验证AI识别的准确性。以下推荐是我在测试了数十个品牌和型号（每个至少测试四周）后，愿意自掏腰包购买的产品。

Updated June 2026: I’ve added new feeders from ChirpCam and Wansview as well as preorder information for the new Birdbuddy 2, updated long-term testing notes on the Birdfy Nest Duo and Birdfy Bath Pro, moved a previous pick to Others Tested upon further use, and ensured up-to-date links and prices.

2026年6月更新：我增加了来自ChirpCam和Wansview的新款喂鸟器，以及Birdbuddy 2的预购信息；更新了关于Birdfy Nest Duo和Birdfy Bath Pro的长期测试记录；根据后续使用情况，将之前的一款推荐移至“其他测试产品”类别，并确保了链接和价格的实时更新。

### Best Overall: Netvue Birdfy Lite Smart Bird Feeder
### 最佳综合奖：Netvue Birdfy Lite 智能喂鸟器

**WIRED/TIRED**
**优点/缺点**

*   **WIRED:** Reliable and easy to set up; User-friendly app; Decently wide field of view.
*   **优点：** 可靠且易于设置；应用程序用户友好；视野相当宽广。
*   **TIRED:** Not the best video resolution; Lightweight construction.
*   **缺点：** 视频分辨率并非顶级；结构较轻。

This cheery blue- or yellow-roofed feeder, from security company Netvue, was the first smart feeder WIRED tested, back in 2022. At that time, we gave it a 6/10 rating for its high price and wonky AI. Both metrics have improved dramatically since then—Birdfy's app is now the second-most user-friendly on the market, behind only Birdbuddy's, and this feeder is often on sale for $150 or less.

这款来自安防公司Netvue、带有欢快蓝色或黄色屋顶的喂鸟器，是WIRED在2022年测试的第一款智能喂鸟器。当时，由于价格昂贵且AI表现不稳定，我们只给了它6/10的评分。自那以后，这两项指标都有了显著改善——Birdfy的应用程序现在是市场上用户友好度第二高的产品，仅次于Birdbuddy，而且这款喂鸟器经常以150美元或更低的价格促销。

### Highest-Resolution Photos: Camojojo Hibird Pro
### 最高分辨率照片：Camojojo Hibird Pro

**WIRED/TIRED**
**优点/缺点**

*   **WIRED:** Runs on 2.4-GHz and 5-GHz Wi-Fi; Has 4K Ultra HD video; Solar panel stays charged even in cloudy weather.
*   **优点：** 支持2.4 GHz和5 GHz Wi-Fi；拥有4K超高清视频；太阳能电池板即使在阴天也能保持充电。
*   **TIRED:** Heavier than most smart feeders; AI is still just OK; Subscription now required for all features.
*   **缺点：** 比大多数智能喂鸟器重；AI表现一般；现在所有功能都需要订阅。

Camojojo's original Hibird feeder has long been a reliable model in my testing stable, unique for its ability to run on both 2.4-GHz and 5-GHz Wi-Fi bands—almost all smart feeders are 2.4-GHz only (learn why below)—and last year saw the debut of an upgraded model, the Hibird Pro. The update has built-in solar panels, an option for pole mounting, and supposedly improved AI identification, though it still frequently misidentifies even common birds in my West Coast backyard as East Coast species.

Camojojo的初代Hibird喂鸟器长期以来一直是我测试库中可靠的型号，其独特之处在于它能同时在2.4 GHz和5 GHz Wi-Fi频段上运行——几乎所有其他智能喂鸟器都仅支持2.4 GHz（原因见下文）。去年，升级版Hibird Pro问世。这次更新增加了内置太阳能电池板、立杆安装选项，以及号称改进的AI识别功能，尽管它仍然经常把我西海岸院子里的常见鸟类误识别为东海岸物种。
