# Page image classifier fine-tuned on century-spanning archives of scanned documents for further content-specific processing
# 针对跨世纪扫描文档档案微调的页面图像分类器，用于后续特定内容处理

**Abstract:**
**摘要：**

Purpose: Digitization projects in the humanities produce vast, heterogeneous archives of historical documents, making manual sorting impractical at scale. This work addresses the need for an automated system to classify scanned page images based on visual content type - text, tables, and graphics - enabling content-specific downstream processing such as Optical Character Recognition (OCR) or structured data extraction.
目的：人文领域的数字化项目产生了海量且异构的历史文档档案，使得大规模的人工分类变得不切实际。本研究旨在开发一种自动化系统，根据视觉内容类型（文本、表格和图形）对扫描的页面图像进行分类，从而实现光学字符识别（OCR）或结构化数据提取等针对特定内容的后续处理。

Methods: An image classification system was developed and evaluated on a dataset of over 48,000 annotated historical page images from century-old Czech archaeological archives, refined through four successive annotation stages with domain-expert review. A Random Forest Classifier baseline was established using hand-crafted image features. Subsequently, deep learning architectures were fine-tuned and compared: Convolutional Neural Networks (EfficientNetV2, RegNetY), Vision and Document Image Transformers (ViT, DiT), and multimodal CLIP models. An 11-category label scheme was designed collaboratively with domain experts and evaluated via five-fold cross-validation.
方法：本研究开发并评估了一个图像分类系统，该系统基于来自百年捷克考古档案的超过 48,000 张标注历史页面图像数据集，并经过四轮连续标注阶段及领域专家的审核优化。研究首先利用手工提取的图像特征建立了随机森林分类器基准。随后，对多种深度学习架构进行了微调与对比：卷积神经网络（EfficientNetV2, RegNetY）、视觉与文档图像 Transformer（ViT, DiT）以及多模态 CLIP 模型。研究与领域专家合作设计了一套 11 类标签方案，并通过五折交叉验证进行了评估。

Results: The feature-based baseline achieved approximately 75% accuracy. Fine-tuned CNNs and Transformers substantially outperformed it, with RegNetY-16GF achieving 99.16% and ViT-large 99.12% Top-1 accuracy on the held-out test set. CLIP ViT-B/16 reached 99.14% with optimized text descriptions.
结果：基于特征的基准模型准确率约为 75%。微调后的 CNN 和 Transformer 模型表现显著优于基准，其中 RegNetY-16GF 在留出测试集上的 Top-1 准确率达到 99.16%，ViT-large 达到 99.12%。CLIP ViT-B/16 在优化文本描述后达到了 99.14% 的准确率。

Conclusion: Image-only models, particularly RegNetY-16GF, deliver near-perfect classification accuracy and produce consistent labels across 649,508 unlabeled archival pages with over 90% inter-model agreement. Fine-tuned CLIP, despite competitive test-set accuracy, showed under 65% agreement with image-only models on unlabeled data, making it less suitable for deployment. The final models, annotated dataset, and software are publicly available under open-source licenses.
结论：纯图像模型（尤其是 RegNetY-16GF）提供了近乎完美的分类准确率，并在 649,508 页未标注档案页面上产生了高度一致的标签，模型间一致性超过 90%。尽管微调后的 CLIP 在测试集上表现出竞争力，但在未标注数据上与纯图像模型的一致性不足 65%，因此不太适合部署。最终模型、标注数据集及软件均已通过开源许可公开。
