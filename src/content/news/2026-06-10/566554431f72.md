# Emergence via Phase Transitions: Mechanism Landscapes and Universal Convergence Across Complex Systems
## 通过相变实现涌现：复杂系统中的机制景观与普适收敛

**Abstract:** Across machine learning, biology, and physics, independently evolving systems often converge toward strikingly similar high-level structures despite radically different microscopic details. Grokking circuits converge across random seeds, evolutionary lineages rediscover similar metabolic solutions, and renormalization flows approach common fixed points. We propose the Hierarchical Emergence Framework (HEF) as a candidate universality framework for such convergence phenomena.

**摘要：** 在机器学习、生物学和物理学领域，独立演化的系统尽管微观细节迥异，却往往趋向于极其相似的高层结构。例如，深度学习中的“顿悟”（Grokking）电路在不同随机种子下趋于一致，进化谱系会重新发现相似的代谢解决方案，重整化流则趋向于共同的固定点。我们提出了“分层涌现框架”（Hierarchical Emergence Framework, HEF），作为解释此类收敛现象的普适性框架候选。

HEF models emergence as a phase transition in a mechanism landscape constrained by thermodynamic and information-theoretic laws. The framework introduces a critical energy threshold $E_c$ separating an exploration regime with competing mechanisms from a convergence regime governed by a unique minimum-cost mechanism. Under structural assumptions, we prove physical feasibility, derive strict metric contraction, and establish convergence toward a unique fixed-point representation independent of initial conditions.

HEF 将涌现建模为受热力学和信息论定律约束的机制景观中的相变过程。该框架引入了一个临界能量阈值 $E_c$，用以区分存在竞争机制的“探索阶段”和由单一最小成本机制主导的“收敛阶段”。在结构性假设下，我们证明了其物理可行性，推导了严格的度量收缩，并确立了系统向独立于初始条件的唯一固定点表示收敛的特性。

We further connect this convergence structure to causal emergence through Effective Information and mechanism competition entropy. To test the framework, we study delayed generalization ("grokking") in modular arithmetic transformers across 111 experiments. We identify a reproducible empirical fingerprint of the $E_c$ transition: the weight norm peaks systematically before grokking in 92% of runs.

我们进一步通过有效信息（Effective Information）和机制竞争熵，将这种收敛结构与因果涌现联系起来。为了验证该框架，我们对模运算 Transformer 中的延迟泛化（即“顿悟”）进行了 111 次实验研究。我们识别出了 $E_c$ 相变的可复现经验指纹：在 92% 的实验运行中，权重范数（weight norm）均在“顿悟”发生前出现系统性峰值。

Normalized accuracy curves collapse onto a tanh kink ($R^2=0.93$) consistent with a Landau-Ginzburg universality class, and all grokked models converge to 0.9745+/-0.014 regardless of initialization, weight decay, or training fraction (ANOVA p>0.13). HEF is not presented as a universal theory of emergence, but as a falsifiable mathematical scaffold for studying convergence phenomena across complex systems.

归一化准确率曲线坍缩为双曲正切（tanh）拐点（$R^2=0.93$），这与朗道-金兹堡（Landau-Ginzburg）普适类一致；且无论初始化方式、权重衰减或训练比例如何，所有发生“顿悟”的模型最终都收敛于 0.9745±0.014（ANOVA p>0.13）。HEF 并非作为涌现的终极普适理论提出，而是作为一个可证伪的数学框架，用于研究复杂系统中的收敛现象。
