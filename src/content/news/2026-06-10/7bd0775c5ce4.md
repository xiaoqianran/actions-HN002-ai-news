# TapXWorld / ChinaTextbook

**Origin of the Project**
Although domestic educational websites already provide free resources, the channels for most ordinary people to access this information remain limited. Some individuals have taken advantage of this to sell these resources, often marked with private watermarks, on certain platforms. To address this, I plan to centralize these resources and make them open-source, aiming to promote the popularization of compulsory education and eliminate regional educational poverty. Another crucial reason is the hope that overseas Chinese can enable their children to continue learning about the domestic education system.

**项目的由来**
虽然国内教育网站已提供免费资源，但大多数普通人获取信息的途径依然受限。有些人利用这一点，在某站上销售这些带有私人水印的资源。为了应对这种情况，我计划将这些资源集中并开源，以促进义务教育的普及和消除地区间的教育贫困。还有一个最重要的原因是，希望海外华人能够让自己的孩子继续了解国内教育。

**Question: How to merge split files?**
Because GitHub has a maximum limit for individual file uploads, files exceeding 100MB will be rejected, and files exceeding 50MB will trigger a warning. Therefore, files larger than 50MB are split into multiple parts of 35MB each.

**问题：如何合并被拆分的文件？**
由于 GitHub 对单个文件的上传有最大限制，超过 100MB 的文件会被拒绝上传，超过 50MB 的文件上传时会收到警告。因此，文件大小超过 50MB 的文件会被拆分成每个 35MB 的多个文件。

***

**Example of split files:**
Compulsory Education Textbook · Mathematics Grade 1 (Vol. 1).pdf.1
Compulsory Education Textbook · Mathematics Grade 1 (Vol. 1).pdf.2

**示例**
文件被拆分的示例：
义务教育教科书 · 数学一年级上册.pdf.1
义务教育教科书 · 数学一年级上册.pdf.2

***

**Solution**
To merge these split files, simply follow these steps (the process is the same for other operating systems):
1. Download the merge program `mergePDFs-windows-amd64.exe` into the folder containing the PDF files.
2. Ensure `mergePDFs-windows-amd64.exe` and the split PDF files are in the same directory.
3. Double-click the `mergePDFs-windows-amd64.exe` program to automatically complete the file merging.

**解决办法**
要合并这些被拆分的文件，您只需执行以下步骤(其他操作系统同理)：
1. 将合并程序 `mergePDFs-windows-amd64.exe` 下载到包含 PDF 文件的文件夹中。
2. 确保 `mergePDFs-windows-amd64.exe` 和被拆分的 PDF 文件在同一目录下。
3. 双击 `mergePDFs-windows-amd64.exe` 程序即可自动完成文件合并。

**Re-downloading**
If you are in mainland China and have a good network connection, you can use the `tchMaterial-parser` project (open-source encouraged) to re-download. If you are abroad and experiencing slow communication speeds with mainland networks, it is recommended to use this repository to check out the files.

**重新下载**
