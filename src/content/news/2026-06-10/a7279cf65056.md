# Can You Trust What You See? Human and AI Detection of Synthetic Legal Evidence
# 你能相信所见吗？人类与人工智能对合成法律证据的检测

**Abstract:** Visual evidence has long been treated as a reliable form of legal proof, but advances in artificial intelligence (AI) are undermining that assumption. This article asks how well humans and frontier multimodal large language models (MLLMs) can distinguish authentic evidentiary photographs from AI-generated counterparts in the object-centric scenarios typical of civil disputes.

**摘要：** 长期以来，视觉证据一直被视为一种可靠的法律证明形式，但人工智能（AI）的进步正在动摇这一假设。本文探讨了在民事纠纷中典型的以物体为中心的场景下，人类和前沿多模态大语言模型（MLLM）区分真实证据照片与 AI 生成图像的能力。

We built Synthetic Legal Evidence Detection (SLED-1400), a dataset of 200 authentic evidence images paired with 1,200 synthetic counterparts produced by six contemporary text-to-image generators across ten evidence categories. The same stimuli and response format were used in a controlled web experiment with 136 lay participants and in a standardized evaluation of four MLLMs (GPT-5.1, Gemini-3-Pro, Gemini-3-Flash, Qwen3-VL-235B).

我们构建了“合成法律证据检测”（SLED-1400）数据集，其中包含 200 张真实证据图像，以及由六种当代文本生成图像模型在十个证据类别中生成的 1,200 张合成图像。我们在 136 名普通参与者参与的受控网络实验中，以及对四种 MLLM（GPT-5.1、Gemini-3-Pro、Gemini-3-Flash、Qwen3-VL-235B）的标准化评估中，使用了相同的刺激材料和响应格式。

Human accuracy was 64.8% overall, and 48.5% and 51.0% on the two strongest generators (Gemini-3-Pro-Image and Flux-2-Max), indistinguishable from chance. MLLMs never misclassified an authentic image (100% specificity), but missed most synthetic outputs from the harder generators, with average MLLM detection at 5.9% on Gemini-3-Pro-Image outputs.

人类的总体准确率为 64.8%，而在面对两个最强生成模型（Gemini-3-Pro-Image 和 Flux-2-Max）时，准确率分别为 48.5% 和 51.0%，与随机猜测无异。MLLM 从未误判过真实图像（特异性为 100%），但未能识别出大多数来自较强生成模型的合成输出，其中 MLLM 对 Gemini-3-Pro-Image 输出的平均检测率仅为 5.9%。

Human and MLLM errors were largely uncorrelated, while the four MLLMs were strongly correlated with each other. Neither group is a reliable standalone authenticator. We argue that visual evidence in legal proceedings should be treated as inherently contestable, and that a workable procedural response must combine trained human review, MLLM screening, and provenance infrastructure such as C2PA Content Credentials.

人类与 MLLM 的错误在很大程度上是不相关的，而四种 MLLM 之间的错误则高度相关。这两者都不能作为可靠的独立验证工具。我们认为，法律诉讼中的视觉证据应被视为本质上具有争议性的，而可行的程序性应对方案必须结合受过培训的人工审查、MLLM 筛选以及诸如 C2PA 内容凭证（Content Credentials）之类的来源基础设施。
