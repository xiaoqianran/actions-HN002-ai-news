# SPIN: Decentralized Swarm Control via Tensorized Policy Coordination
# SPIN：基于张量化策略协同的去中心化群体控制

**Abstract:** Decentralized multi-agent swarm coordination on resource-constrained edge platforms remains fundamentally bottlenecked by the exponential scaling of joint action spaces and high-latency communication overhead.
**摘要：** 在资源受限的边缘平台上，去中心化多智能体群体协同的核心瓶颈在于联合动作空间的指数级扩展以及高延迟的通信开销。

This paper introduces the Swarm Policy Interference Network (SPIN) framework, an architectural paradigm that bypasses these limitations by modeling swarm topologies as a compressed tensor network.
本文引入了群体策略干扰网络（SPIN）框架，这是一种通过将群体拓扑建模为压缩张量网络来绕过上述限制的架构范式。

We factorize the joint policy tensors of local multi-agent cliques into Matrix Product State (MPS) chains, reducing the computational complexity of evaluation from an exponential $O(n^m)$ wall to a strictly linear $O(m \cdot n \cdot \chi^2)$ constraint.
我们将局部多智能体群组的联合策略张量分解为矩阵乘积态（MPS）链，从而将评估的计算复杂度从指数级的 $O(n^m)$ 降低至严格线性的 $O(m \cdot n \cdot \chi^2)$ 约束。

To bridge local continuous spatial geometry with this discrete algebraic backend without requiring power-intensive online training loops, we introduce a decoupled, hybrid neuro-symbolic control pipeline.
为了在无需高功耗在线训练循环的前提下，将局部连续空间几何与该离散代数后端连接起来，我们引入了一种解耦的混合神经符号控制流水线。

Local multi-layered neural networks operate as structural coordination encoders, pre-trained offline to nonlinearly map hand-engineered geometric descriptors into abstract environmental target measures.
局部多层神经网络作为结构化协同编码器运行，通过离线预训练，将人工设计的几何描述符非线性映射为抽象的环境目标度量。

At runtime, edge agents execute instantaneous behavioral adaptations by applying the Radon-Nikodým derivative directly as a zero-shot importance-reweighting filter.
在运行时，边缘智能体通过直接应用 Radon-Nikodým 导数作为零样本重要性重加权滤波器，执行即时的行为适应。

We validate the framework within a discrete-time multi-agent simulation sandbox spanning tracking, decentralized dispersion/area coverage, and multi-goal coordination regimes.
我们在离散时间多智能体模拟沙箱中验证了该框架，涵盖了追踪、去中心化分散/区域覆盖以及多目标协同等场景。

Qualitative telemetry demonstrates that the integrated pipeline achieves stable target-directed motion, anti-collapse spatial spreading under decentralized constraints, and structured subgroup formation across multiple targets, providing a mathematically grounded route to tractable, low-power edge swarm intelligence.
定性遥测结果表明，该集成流水线实现了稳定的目标导向运动、去中心化约束下的防坍缩空间分布，以及跨多个目标的结构化子群形成，为实现可控、低功耗的边缘群体智能提供了一条具有数学基础的路径。
