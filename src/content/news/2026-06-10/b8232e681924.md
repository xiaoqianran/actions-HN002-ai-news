# STARIXNet: Multivariate and Multi-attribute Deep Learning Approach to Real-Time Resource Allocation in Cloud Platforms
# STARIXNet：用于云平台实时资源分配的多变量与多属性深度学习方法

**Abstract:** Intelligent scaling of microservices in cloud platforms is crucial for mitigating escalating compute costs while avoiding service disruptions. Current solutions are limited to the univariate space, typically focusing on CPU usage alone to drive scaling decisions. Moreover, they address the problem as a purely forecasting task, focusing on prediction precision while neglecting the greater risks of underestimation and delays in system responsiveness.

**摘要：** 云平台中微服务的智能扩缩容对于降低不断攀升的计算成本并避免服务中断至关重要。目前的解决方案局限于单变量空间，通常仅关注 CPU 使用率来驱动扩缩容决策。此外，它们将该问题视为纯粹的预测任务，侧重于预测精度，却忽视了低估需求和系统响应延迟所带来的更大风险。

Alternative solutions are computationally complex, making them impractical for large-scale, real-time deployments. To address these challenges, we present STARIXNet, a lightweight neural network that guides resource allocation decisions in the multivariate space by capturing spatio-temporal relationships among multiple system metrics.

其他替代方案计算复杂度高，导致其在大规模实时部署中难以应用。为了应对这些挑战，我们提出了 STARIXNet，这是一种轻量级神经网络，通过捕捉多个系统指标之间的时空关系，在多变量空间中指导资源分配决策。

STARIXNet models multiple quasi-dependent attributes, in particular the (S)easonal, (T)emporal, (A)uto-(R)egressive (I)ntegrated, and e(X)ogenous patterns, then implements an aggregation policy to finalize scaling decisions, prioritizing service stability, followed by cost-efficiency, over raw forecast accuracy.

STARIXNet 对多个准相关属性进行建模，特别是（S）季节性、（T）时间性、（A）自（R）回归（I）整合以及（X）外生模式，随后实施聚合策略以最终确定扩缩容决策，在优先考虑服务稳定性和成本效益的同时，权衡原始预测的准确性。

We empirically demonstrate the performance of STARIXNet by benchmarking against existing solutions in real-world settings. STARIXNet is deployed for critical production microservices at Walmart achieving tangible savings ranging from 10% to 50%, in addition to intangible benefits through improved service stability and customer experience.

我们通过在真实场景中与现有解决方案进行基准测试，实证了 STARIXNet 的性能。STARIXNet 已部署在沃尔玛的关键生产微服务中，实现了 10% 到 50% 的实际成本节约，并通过提升服务稳定性和客户体验带来了无形收益。
