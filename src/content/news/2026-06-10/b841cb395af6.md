# WWDC 2026：Siri AI、iOS 27、Apple Intelligence 等发布内容全汇总

Apple’s WWDC 2026 event kicked off yesterday at Apple Park, starting a week packed with reveals about Siri AI, iOS 27, Apple Intelligence, and more, along with developer events and demos as Apple looks to reassert itself with users and developers who haven’t been impressed with their releases within the wildly competitive AI space. It also marks CEO Tim Cook’s last WWCD with the company, after announcing he’s handing things off to Senior Vice President of Hardware Engineering John Ternus on September 1. Did they succeed? Keep tabs on this page, and the rest of our ongoing coverage, to find out! TL;DR — Apple spent WWDC 2026 catching up.

苹果 WWDC 2026 大会昨日在 Apple Park 拉开帷幕，开启了为期一周的密集发布，内容涵盖 Siri AI、iOS 27、Apple Intelligence 等。在竞争激烈的 AI 领域，苹果此前的产品表现未能令用户和开发者满意，此次大会旨在重振旗鼓。这也是 CEO 蒂姆·库克（Tim Cook）任内最后一次主持 WWDC，他此前宣布将于 9 月 1 日将职务移交给硬件工程高级副总裁约翰·特努斯（John Ternus）。他们成功了吗？请持续关注本页面及我们的后续报道以获取答案！简而言之：苹果在 WWDC 2026 上主要是在“补课”。

This is far from our consumer news editor Sarah Perez’s first WWDC, and with all that context in mind, she provides the subtext on much of what was being showcased. For the past two years, Apple has been racing to catch up in AI while frustrations with its core software quietly added up: a design overhaul users hated, a search function that barely worked, a file-sharing feature that routinely failed, and a Health app that didn’t focus enough on half its user base. Apple didn’t say any of that on Monday. But the structure of its WWDC keynote said it for them, leading with fixes before features, and framing a better Siri as one item on a long list of improvements rather than the main event.

这并非我们的消费新闻编辑 Sarah Perez 第一次参加 WWDC，基于过往的背景，她为此次展示的内容提供了深层解读。过去两年里，苹果一直在 AI 领域奋力追赶，同时其核心软件引发的挫败感也在悄然累积：用户厌恶的设计改版、几乎无法使用的搜索功能、经常失败的文件共享功能，以及对半数用户群体关注不足的健康应用。苹果在周一的发布会上并未提及这些，但 WWDC 主题演讲的结构替他们说了出来——先修复问题，再谈新功能，并将改进后的 Siri 视为众多改进项目中的一项，而非唯一的重头戏。

### Apple reveals Siri AI
### 苹果发布 Siri AI

As expected, Apple made the case for an improved experience with its long-standing Siri assistant, which it admitted faces greater expectations from users in the age of AI. With Google Gemini under the hood, Apple claims that the new Siri updates will make it more capable, conversational, and compatible with visual intelligence, and it will be housed in a stand-alone app in addition to working across existing apps. You can get a full rundown of all the new Siri AI updates right here.

正如预期，苹果展示了其老牌助手 Siri 的改进体验，并承认在 AI 时代，用户对 Siri 寄予了更高的期望。得益于 Google Gemini 的底层支持，苹果声称新的 Siri 更新将使其功能更强大、对话更自然，并具备视觉智能。除了在现有应用中运行外，Siri 还将拥有一个独立的应用程序。你可以在此处查看所有 Siri AI 更新的完整汇总。

Before rolling out the enhancements and features, Apple was adamant about its privacy-centric approach to AI. “We believe privacy in AI is non-negotiable,” Apple senior vice president Craig Federighi said during the stream, going so far as to say that “data is only used to execute your request, and outside experts can continue to verify this promise at any time.”

在推出各项增强功能之前，苹果坚持其以隐私为中心的 AI 策略。苹果高级副总裁克雷格·费德里吉（Craig Federighi）在直播中表示：“我们认为 AI 中的隐私问题是不容妥协的。”他甚至强调：“数据仅用于执行您的请求，外部专家可以随时验证这一承诺。”

### A potential foldable iPhone tease
### 折叠屏 iPhone 的潜在预告

No, Apple didn’t make such a big reveal during WWDC, but researcher @M1Astra dug through files within the iOS 27 developer beta and found references to things like “foldState,” “angleDegrees,” and other things that allude to the states a foldable device can be put into. And it’s not like there hasn’t been a bounty of foldable iPhone rumors over the past few years. Stay tuned for Apple’s annual iPhone event in September to see if we do get a formal reveal, unless Ternus really will be changing things up in the post-Cook era.

虽然苹果在 WWDC 上并未正式发布折叠屏手机，但研究人员 @M1Astra 在 iOS 27 开发者测试版的文件中发现了诸如“foldState”（折叠状态）、“angleDegrees”（角度）等引用，这些都暗示了折叠设备可能具备的状态。过去几年里，关于折叠屏 iPhone 的传闻一直层出不穷。请关注苹果 9 月份的年度 iPhone 发布会，看看我们是否能迎来正式发布，除非特努斯在“后库克时代”真的会带来大刀阔斧的改变。

### The next generation of Apple Intelligence
### 下一代 Apple Intelligence

To go along with its new Siri AI overhaul, the tech giant announced a slew of new Apple Intelligence updates across its apps, including tab management for Safari, one-tap password updating, cross-app context awareness, and more. Additionally, Messages is getting AI-powered reply suggestions, while the Phone app can now pull context from other apps like Mail and Messages mid-call. Apple said it collaborated with Google and the Gemini family of models to develop the next generation of Apple Foundation Models that power its integrated Apple Intelligence experiences.

配合全新的 Siri AI 升级，这家科技巨头还宣布了 Apple Intelligence 在各应用中的一系列更新，包括 Safari 标签页管理、一键更新密码、跨应用上下文感知等。此外，信息（Messages）应用增加了 AI 驱动的回复建议，电话（Phone）应用现在可以在通话过程中从邮件（Mail）和信息等其他应用中提取上下文。苹果表示，已与 Google 及其 Gemini 模型系列合作，开发了为 Apple Intelligence 体验提供支持的下一代 Apple 基础模型。

### Liquid Glass gets some opt-in rollbacks
### Liquid Glass 界面支持部分回退

If you are among those who aren’t exactly keen on last year’s Liquid Glass design updates, you aren’t alone. And while Apple isn’t switching to a new aesthetic, you will be able to dial back some of its elements, or really highlight them if you’re vibing with it. And for the app icon critics out there fresh from Spotify’s disco ball update, Apple showed off a new, layered approach to Liquid Glass within its apps.

如果你对去年推出的 Liquid Glass 设计更新不太感冒，你并不孤单。虽然苹果并没有完全放弃这种美学风格，但你现在可以选择调低其中的某些元素，或者如果你喜欢这种风格，也可以进一步强化它们。对于那些刚经历过 Spotify “迪斯科球”图标更新、对应用图标设计持批评态度的用户，苹果展示了一种全新的、分层的 Liquid Glass 应用内呈现方式。

### Everything else coming to iOS 27
### iOS 27 的其他更新

As is the case every year, a number of small tweaks and updates arriving with the upcoming iOS update didn’t get their time in the sun during Apple’s broadcast, but that doesn’t mean they’re not noteworthy. Ivan Mehta brought together several of them right here, including: Full-screen homepage widgets; Separate volume controls for alarms, timers, alerts, and so on; Design tweaks for the weather app, with highlights on notable upcoming events.

和往年一样，即将到来的 iOS 更新中还有许多小调整和更新未能在发布会上展示，但这并不意味着它们不值得关注。Ivan Mehta 在此汇总了其中几项，包括：全屏主页小组件；闹钟、计时器、提醒事项等独立的音量控制；天气应用的设计调整，并重点突出了即将发生的重大天气事件。

### Image Playground gets another chance
### Image Playground 获得新机会

The AI image-generating app Image Playground hasn’t exactly taken the world by storm, which depending on your view on AI slop may be a good thing. However, Apple rolled out a renewed pitch for users to actually start generating images, with a focus on its possible uses across many features of your devices, with an exclusion set on any training based on photos generated using the app. That, plus performance updates coming alongside Apple Intelligence upgrades, might at least take it out of the “suck” category for TechCrunch senior writer Amanda Silberling.

AI 图像生成应用 Image Playground 并没有引起轰动，这对于那些反感 AI 垃圾内容的人来说或许是件好事。然而，苹果再次向用户推广该功能，鼓励用户开始生成图像，重点展示了其在设备各项功能中的潜在用途，并明确排除了使用该应用生成的照片进行任何模型训练。再加上随 Apple Intelligence 升级带来的性能提升，这或许至少能让它摆脱 TechCrunch 高级撰稿人 Amanda Silberling 眼中的“糟糕”评价。

### iOS 27 is stretching back to the iPhone 11
### iOS 27 支持机型回溯至 iPhone 11

Claiming that its upcoming update will be “available to more users than any iOS release ever,” Apple revealed that all devices from the iPhone 11 onward will be eligible for their upcoming software update. And that update comes with a flurry of performance improvements it’s touting across a number of its OS releases this year, with Apple claiming that new photos will appear 70% more swiftly, AirDrop transfers will be 80% faster, and CPU schedulers will be improved to help multitasking.

苹果声称此次更新将“覆盖比以往任何 iOS 版本更多的用户”，并宣布从 iPhone 11 开始的所有设备都将支持此次软件更新。此次更新还带来了苹果今年在多个操作系统版本中大力宣传的性能提升：苹果称新照片的显示速度将提高 70%，AirDrop 传输速度将提高 80%，CPU 调度器也将得到改进以优化多任务处理。

### New parental controls for iPhones
### iPhone 新增家长控制功能

Apple spent a significant amount of the WWDC event showcasing a suite of tools for parents looking for greater control over what their children’s devices can and can’t do. Parents will be able to determine who their kid can call on the phone and what apps and websites they can access, with Apple making suggestions about how those restrictions can change over time.

苹果在 WWDC 上花费了大量时间展示了一套工具，旨在帮助家长更好地控制孩子设备的使用权限。家长将能够决定孩子可以拨打谁的电话，以及可以访问哪些应用程序和网站，苹果还会就这些限制如何随时间调整提供建议。
