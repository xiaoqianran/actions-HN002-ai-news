# It’s not FAANG anymore. It’s MANGOS.
# 不再是 FAANG 了，现在是 MANGOS。

It’s not FAANG anymore. It’s MANGOS. With SpaceX about to break records with an IPO on Friday, Anthropic about to break records with its pending IPO, and OpenAI racing to match or best its archrivals with its own potentially record-breaking IPO, the tech industry will soon have a new set of public company overlords.
不再是 FAANG 了，现在是 MANGOS。随着 SpaceX 即将于周五通过 IPO 打破纪录，Anthropic 即将通过其待定的 IPO 打破纪录，而 OpenAI 也在竞相追赶或超越其主要竞争对手，准备进行可能打破纪录的 IPO，科技行业很快将迎来一批新的上市公司巨头。

Should all these IPOs take place as planned, these companies will be replacing the vicious-sounding FAANG cabal — Facebook (now Meta), Amazon, Apple, Netflix, Google (now Alphabet) — with the delightfully sweet-sounding (though truly sour and atrocious if consumed unripe) coterie MANGOS: Meta, Anthropic, Nvidia, Google, OpenAI, SpaceX.
如果所有这些 IPO 都按计划进行，这些公司将取代听起来有些凶狠的 FAANG 集团——Facebook（现为 Meta）、亚马逊、苹果、Netflix、谷歌（现为 Alphabet）——取而代之的是听起来甜美可口（尽管未成熟时吃起来确实酸涩难咽）的 MANGOS 组合：Meta、Anthropic、英伟达、谷歌、OpenAI 和 SpaceX。

As these companies go, so shall the whole tech industry, or so it’s looking like from the summer of 2026. The new acronym was proposed by developer @krishdotdev and @lilscoot on X and is now going viral.
从 2026 年夏季的情况来看，整个科技行业的走向将由这些公司决定。这个新缩写是由开发者 @krishdotdev 和 @lilscoot 在 X（原推特）上提出的，目前正在网络上疯传。

Of course, FAANG is not exactly dead — Amazon and Netflix remain powerful — but streaming services and Amazon’s e-commerce business, if not its cloud, are perhaps less groundbreaking these days than the AI and agentic companies the tech industry is about to crown.
当然，FAANG 并没有完全消亡——亚马逊和 Netflix 依然强大——但流媒体服务和亚马逊的电子商务业务（如果排除其云业务的话）在今天看来，或许已不如科技行业即将加冕的那些人工智能和智能体（agentic）公司那样具有开创性。

To that we say: Farewell to FAANG! Long live the MANGOS! (At least if they prove to be a nourishing foundation of a healthy economy powered by an upcoming autonomous AI age, and don’t usher in an unpalatable future where we all wind up jobless and broke.)
对此我们要说：再见，FAANG！MANGOS 万岁！（前提是它们能证明自己是未来自主 AI 时代驱动下健康经济的坚实基础，而不是将我们带入一个让我们最终失业且破产的苦涩未来。）
