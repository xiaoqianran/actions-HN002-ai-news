# The Hardware That Makes AI Possible
# 驱动人工智能的硬件基石

**CPUs, GPUs, TPUs, and NPUs**
**CPU、GPU、TPU 与 NPU**

When we talk about AI, we often describe it as a software revolution, which it is! From breakthroughs in neural networks and transformers to large language models, it is easy to assume that these smart algorithms are responsible for the progress we have seen in recent years. But today, I want to shed light on how modern AI is only possible because of the advances in hardware.
当我们谈论人工智能时，往往将其描述为一场软件革命，事实也确实如此！从神经网络和 Transformer 的突破到大语言模型，人们很容易认为正是这些智能算法造就了近年来我们所见到的进步。但今天，我想阐明现代人工智能之所以成为可能，完全得益于硬件领域的进步。

Training a large language model involves performing trillions of mathematical operations across large datasets. Generating an image from a text prompt requires billions of calculations in just a few seconds. Running AI on a smartphone requires computations to be completed quickly and with minimal power. Traditional computer hardware was not designed for that. But as AI models grew larger and more computationally demanding, new hardware architectures were needed to run these models. Today, CPUs, GPUs, TPUs, and NPUs each play important roles in the AI world. In this article, we will explore the hardware that powers modern AI and explain why different processors are needed for different tasks.
训练一个大语言模型涉及在海量数据集上执行数万亿次的数学运算。根据文本提示生成图像，需要在短短几秒钟内完成数十亿次计算。在智能手机上运行人工智能，则要求计算过程既要快速又要极度节能。传统的计算机硬件并非为此而设计。但随着人工智能模型变得越来越庞大且对算力要求越来越高，我们需要新的硬件架构来运行这些模型。如今，CPU、GPU、TPU 和 NPU 在人工智能领域各司其职。在本文中，我们将探索驱动现代人工智能的硬件，并解释为什么不同的任务需要不同的处理器。

### Why AI Needs Specialized Hardware
### 为什么人工智能需要专用硬件

To understand why AI needs special hardware, let’s take a step back and think about what happens during machine learning. At its core, training a neural network involves repeatedly performing mathematical operations on a collection of numbers. Most of these operations involve matrix multiplications and tensor products that must be executed millions or billions of times. This differs significantly from other software applications. For example, a web browser spends much of its time responding to user inputs and loading resources. AI applications, on the other hand, often involve applying the same operation to large amounts of data. So, for AI to perform well, it needs to perform many calculations at the same time. This need for parallel computation led to the development of specialized hardware optimized for AI.
要理解人工智能为何需要专用硬件，让我们退一步思考机器学习过程中发生了什么。其核心在于，训练神经网络涉及对一组数字反复进行数学运算。这些运算大多涉及矩阵乘法和张量积，必须执行数百万甚至数十亿次。这与其他软件应用有显著不同。例如，网页浏览器大部分时间都在响应用户输入和加载资源；而人工智能应用则通常涉及对海量数据应用相同的操作。因此，为了获得良好的性能，人工智能需要同时执行大量计算。这种对并行计算的需求，催生了针对人工智能优化的专用硬件。

### CPUs: The General-Purpose OG!
### CPU：全能的“元老”！

If we are going to talk about hardware, we need to start with the OG: the Central Processing Unit (CPU). CPUs are the foundation of modern computing. Every laptop, smartphone, workstation, and server relies on a CPU to run its system operations. Because CPUs are general, they are designed for flexibility. They can efficiently execute a wide variety of instructions and quickly switch between tasks. One way to think about a CPU is as a highly skilled generalist. It can perform many different jobs and adapt to changing requirements. To support this, CPUs often contain a small number of powerful cores. Making them the choice to run operating systems, managing memory, handling user interactions, coordinating software applications, and executing decision-making processes.
如果要谈论硬件，我们必须从“元老”开始：中央处理器（CPU）。CPU 是现代计算的基石。每一台笔记本电脑、智能手机、工作站和服务器都依赖 CPU 来运行系统操作。由于 CPU 是通用的，它们的设计旨在实现灵活性。它们可以高效地执行各种指令，并快速在不同任务之间切换。可以将 CPU 视为一名技术精湛的“通才”，它能胜任许多不同的工作并适应不断变化的需求。为了支持这一点，CPU 通常包含少量强大的核心，这使其成为运行操作系统、管理内存、处理用户交互、协调软件应用以及执行决策过程的首选。

Although CPUs are quite powerful, they are not optimized to perform the same operation on thousands or millions of data points at the same time. Which means, for AI workloads, this becomes a limitation. Although CPUs remain essential components of AI systems, they typically coordinate and support AI computations rather than perform the bulk of the heavy mathematical work. In modern AI pipelines, CPUs are used to load and preprocess data, coordinate communication between hardware devices, manage training workflows, and schedule computational tasks.
尽管 CPU 功能强大，但它们并未针对同时对成千上万个数据点执行相同操作进行优化。这意味着对于人工智能工作负载而言，这成了一个局限。虽然 CPU 仍然是人工智能系统的重要组成部分，但它们通常负责协调和支持人工智能计算，而不是执行繁重的数学运算。在现代人工智能流水线中，CPU 主要用于加载和预处理数据、协调硬件设备间的通信、管理训练工作流以及调度计算任务。

### GPUs: The Engine Behind the Deep Learning Revolution
### GPU：深度学习革命背后的引擎

If there is one piece of hardware most closely associated with modern AI, it is the Graphics Processing Unit (GPU). GPUs were originally developed for rendering graphics in video games and visualization applications. Rendering an image involves performing similar calculations across millions of pixels, making it inherently a parallel process. To do that, GPUs were designed with thousands of smaller processing cores that can execute many operations simultaneously. Researchers soon recognized that neural networks use similar computational patterns. Training a neural network involves repeatedly performing matrix multiplications across large datasets. Because these operations can be distributed across many cores, GPUs are very good for deep learning.
如果说有一件硬件与现代人工智能联系最紧密，那一定是图形处理器（GPU）。GPU 最初是为视频游戏和可视化应用中的图形渲染而开发的。渲染图像涉及对数百万个像素执行相似的计算，这本质上是一个并行过程。为了实现这一点，GPU 设计了数千个较小的处理核心，可以同时执行许多操作。研究人员很快意识到，神经网络使用了类似的计算模式。训练神经网络涉及在大型数据集上反复进行矩阵乘法。由于这些操作可以分布在许多核心上，因此 GPU 非常适合深度学习。

So, CPUs prioritize flexibility while GPUs prioritize throughput. This difference transformed the way we used to think about AI research. Tasks that once took weeks or months to finish are now completed in days or hours. Many of today’s most advanced AI models are trained using clusters containing hundreds or thousands of GPUs working together. The deep learning revolution was not driven only by better algorithms. It was enabled by hardware capable of efficiently executing those algorithms at scale.
简而言之，CPU 优先考虑灵活性，而 GPU 优先考虑吞吐量。这种差异改变了我们对人工智能研究的看法。曾经需要数周或数月才能完成的任务，现在只需几天甚至几小时即可完成。当今许多最先进的人工智能模型都是使用包含数百或数千个协同工作的 GPU 集群进行训练的。深度学习革命不仅是由更好的算法驱动的，更是由能够大规模高效执行这些算法的硬件所促成的。

### TPUs: Hardware Designed Specifically for AI
### TPU：专为人工智能设计的硬件

So, GPUs were adapted for AI, and a new player entered the picture! Tensor Processing Units (TPUs). TPUs were developed by Google to accelerate tensor operations that are common in neural networks. Instead of supporting a broad range of computational tasks, TPUs specialize in a smaller set of operations commonly used during machine learning training. Because of this specialization, TPUs offer many advantages, like high throughput, improved energy efficiency, reduced overhead, and optimization for machine learning applications. As AI workloads become more important, hardware designers are moving away from purely general-purpose architectures and toward processors optimized for specific applications. Today, TPUs are widely used within Google’s cloud ecosystem and have contributed to training some of the world’s largest AI models.
既然 GPU 已被适配用于人工智能，那么一个新的参与者也随之登场了！那就是张量处理器（TPU）。TPU 由谷歌开发，旨在加速神经网络中常见的张量运算。TPU 不再支持广泛的计算任务，而是专注于机器学习训练中常用的一小部分操作。由于这种专业化，TPU 提供了许多优势，如高吞吐量、更高的能效、更低的开销以及针对机器学习应用的优化。随着人工智能工作负载变得越来越重要，硬件设计者正从纯粹的通用架构转向针对特定应用优化的处理器。如今，TPU 已在谷歌的云生态系统中得到广泛应用，并为训练世界上一些最大规模的人工智能模型做出了贡献。

### NPUs: Bringing AI to reality
### NPU：让人工智能走进现实

Not all AI workloads happen inside data centers. In fact, many AI applications now run directly on personal devices. Running AI locally is beneficial because it reduces latency, improves privacy, and reduces dependency.
并非所有的人工智能工作负载都发生在数据中心内。事实上，许多人工智能应用现在直接在个人设备上运行。在本地运行人工智能是有益的，因为它降低了延迟、提高了隐私性并减少了对网络的依赖。
