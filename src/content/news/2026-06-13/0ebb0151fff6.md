# 6 Best Digital Notebooks (2026): ReMarkable, Kobo, Kindle
# 2026年六款最佳数字笔记本：ReMarkable、Kobo、Kindle

Do you take a lot of notes? Whether it's daily to-do lists, notes for class, or just jotting down thoughts, I've found the best digital notebooks to keep them organized. Sure, you could just open your Notes app, but physically writing something down helps you remember and learn more. Plus, I just love to write things down, and these digital notebooks keep me from burning through paper or losing a great idea I wrote down.

你经常做笔记吗？无论是日常待办事项清单、课堂笔记，还是随手记录灵感，我都为你找到了整理这些内容的最佳数字笔记本。当然，你可以直接打开手机上的备忘录应用，但亲手书写能帮助你更好地记忆和学习。此外，我个人非常喜欢书写的感觉，而这些数字笔记本既能让我告别纸张浪费，又能防止我丢失写下的好点子。

These tablets use E Ink technology to give you a more paperlike experience to write your ideas and notes on. I've tried almost every digital notebook available, and my favorites are the ReMarkable Paper Pro ($629) for a color screen and great accessories and the Amazon Kindle Scribe (2nd Gen) ($400) for the best battery life and convenience.

这些平板电脑采用电子墨水（E Ink）技术，为你提供更接近纸张的书写体验。我几乎尝试过市面上所有的数字笔记本，其中我最喜欢的是拥有彩色屏幕和出色配件的 ReMarkable Paper Pro（629美元），以及在电池续航和便捷性方面表现最佳的亚马逊 Kindle Scribe 第二代（400美元）。

***

### Best Overall: ReMarkable Paper Pro
### 最佳综合表现：ReMarkable Paper Pro

The ReMarkable Paper Pro has everything we like about the ReMarkable 2—a matte screen feels like paper, quick and responsive writing experience, and a great battery life—and adds a few cool new features like colorful pen options and a gentle built-in screen light that uses ambient light from the room. It's got an 10.8-inch screen, making it an extra inch and a half bigger than the ReMarkable 2. It's especially handy if you're taking notes or writing down ideas somewhere a little dark, like a dimmed classroom or on a late flight.

ReMarkable Paper Pro 具备了我们喜爱 ReMarkable 2 的所有优点——如纸张般的磨砂屏幕、快速灵敏的书写体验以及出色的电池续航——并增加了一些酷炫的新功能，例如彩色笔触选项，以及利用环境光提供柔和照明的内置屏幕灯。它配备了 10.8 英寸屏幕，比 ReMarkable 2 大了 1.5 英寸。如果你需要在光线较暗的环境（如调暗的教室或深夜航班）中做笔记或记录灵感，它会非常实用。

***

### A Personal Favorite: Amazon Kindle Scribe (2nd Gen, 2024)
### 个人最爱：亚马逊 Kindle Scribe（第二代，2024款）

Is the second-generation Amazon Kindle Scribe the best digital notebook? No. It's a bit of a hybrid. I'd describe it as an extra-large Kindle that happens to have some digital notebook abilities. But time and time again, I find myself reaching for my Kindle over the other digital notebooks in my house. I'm a big reader, and a Kindle user at that, so having one device that already has all my books that can also serve as my personal doodle pad has been really convenient. It also helps that it has the best battery life of up to 12 weeks, so I almost never need to charge it before using it.

亚马逊 Kindle Scribe 第二代是最好的数字笔记本吗？并不是。它更像是一个混合体。我会把它描述为一个恰好具备数字笔记本功能的超大号 Kindle。但一次又一次，我发现自己比起家里的其他数字笔记本，更倾向于使用 Kindle。我是一个重度阅读爱好者，也是 Kindle 的老用户，所以拥有一台既能存放我所有书籍，又能当作个人涂鸦板的设备，真的非常方便。此外，它拥有长达 12 周的最佳电池续航，这让我在使用前几乎不需要担心充电问题。

ReMarkable and Supernote have more templates and compatibility, and Kobo's devices let you actually write notes on the book. Kindle only lets you write on Post-It-like pop-ups, called Active Canvas, and an expanded sidebar margin that can pop up and take up a third of the page for you to write on.

ReMarkable 和 Supernote 拥有更多的模板和兼容性，而 Kobo 的设备则允许你直接在书页上书写笔记。Kindle 目前仅允许你在类似“便利贴”的弹出窗口（称为 Active Canvas）中书写，或者在侧边栏扩展区域书写，该区域可以弹出并占据页面三分之一的空间供你记录。
