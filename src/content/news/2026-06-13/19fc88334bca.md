# 4 Best Floodlight Security Cameras (2026) After Thorough Testing
# 2026年四款最佳泛光灯安防摄像头深度测评

Floodlight security cameras are a great way to light up your property. Shady areas around your home can make life easier for would-be burglars, and make it harder for you to plug in the car or take out the trash. Motion-triggered lighting is an essential minimum, but with a floodlight security camera, you get that and a videofeed. Floodlight cameras are also far more configurable and reliable than lights; they let you check in on your property from the office or bed, and they can alert you to intruders.

泛光灯安防摄像头是照亮您房产的绝佳方式。住宅周围的阴暗区域不仅可能为窃贼提供可乘之机，也会让您在停车或倒垃圾时感到不便。运动触发照明是基本要求，而泛光灯安防摄像头不仅能提供照明，还能提供实时视频监控。此外，泛光灯摄像头比普通灯具更具可配置性和可靠性；它们让您无论是在办公室还是在床上，都能随时查看房产状况，并在有入侵者时向您发出警报。

### Best Floodlight Camera: Eufy Floodlight Cam E340
### 最佳泛光灯摄像头：Eufy Floodlight Cam E340

With two adjustable light panels capable of putting out up to 2,000 lumens, this floodlight camera is the best option for most folks. It can be wall- or ceiling-mounted, making it ideal for tucking under eaves, and it has an IP65 rating, so it can operate in all sorts of weather. This dual-lens camera combines a 3K wide-angle lens with a 2K telephoto lens, and it can pan 360 degrees and tilt 120 degrees to track subjects. The telephoto lens supports up to 8X zoom to capture details up to 50 feet away.

这款泛光灯摄像头配备两个可调节灯板，亮度最高可达 2,000 流明，是大多数用户的最佳选择。它支持墙壁或天花板安装，非常适合安装在屋檐下，且具备 IP65 防护等级，能够适应各种天气条件。这款双镜头摄像头结合了 3K 广角镜头和 2K 长焦镜头，支持 360 度水平旋转和 120 度垂直倾斜，以追踪目标。长焦镜头支持最高 8 倍变焦，可捕捉 50 英尺（约 15 米）外的细节。

To record locally, you can insert a microSD card up to 128 GB or connect to an Eufy HomeBase 3 ($150). There’s support for continuous recording, and also optional cloud storage from $4 per month for a single camera for a 30-day event history.

若需本地存储，您可以插入最高 128 GB 的 microSD 卡，或连接至 Eufy HomeBase 3（售价 150 美元）。该设备支持连续录制，并提供可选的云存储服务，单台摄像头每月 4 美元起，可保存 30 天的事件记录。

### Brightest Floodlight Camera: Reolink TrackFlex Floodlight
### 最亮泛光灯摄像头：Reolink TrackFlex Floodlight

While this is ostensibly very similar to our top pick, there are a few reasons you may want to spend a little more for Reolink’s TrackFlex Floodlight camera. The dual floodlight panels can put out up to 3,000 lumens, and you can adjust not only the brightness but also the temperature, from a cool 3,000K all the way up to 6,500K. The camera is very good at automatically tracking subjects and can pan through 355 degrees and tilt 50 degrees. It also has three passive infrared (PIR) sensors for 270-degree out-of-field motion detection.

虽然这款产品表面上与我们的首选产品非常相似，但有几个理由值得您多花一点钱选择 Reolink 的 TrackFlex 泛光灯摄像头。其双泛光灯板亮度最高可达 3,000 流明，且不仅可以调节亮度，还能调节色温，从 3,000K 的暖光到 6,500K 的冷光均可调节。该摄像头在自动追踪目标方面表现出色，支持 355 度水平旋转和 50 度垂直倾斜。它还配备了三个被动红外 (PIR) 传感器，可实现 270 度的广角运动检测。

The main lens boasts up to 4K resolution, and the second lens offers a 1080p, 6X hybrid zoom view of whatever it is tracking. Throw in a 110-decibel alarm, dual-band Wi-Fi 6, and a host of local recording options, and this camera looks more and more attractive.

主镜头拥有高达 4K 的分辨率，第二个镜头则提供 1080p 分辨率和 6 倍混合变焦，用于查看追踪目标。再加上 110 分贝的警报器、双频 Wi-Fi 6 以及多种本地存储选项，这款摄像头显得越来越有吸引力。

### Best Subscription Floodlight Camera: Arlo Wired Floodlight Camera
### 最佳订阅制泛光灯摄像头：Arlo Wired Floodlight Camera

Arlo’s Wired Floodlight Camera is the perfect pick for folks with an Arlo system, sporting two adjustable panels that can produce up to 2,000 lumens. The design is a little softer and friendlier than my top pick, with oval panels, and it can be wall or ceiling mounted, though it must be hardwired. This camera provides crisp video at 2,560 x 1,440 pixels, with a wide 160-degree field of view and HDR support, so it handles mixed lighting well. It also supports 12X digital zoom to focus in on details.

对于已经拥有 Arlo 生态系统的用户来说，Arlo 有线泛光灯摄像头是完美之选。它配备两个可调节灯板，亮度最高可达 2,000 流明。其设计比我们的首选产品更柔和、更亲民，采用椭圆形灯板，支持墙壁或天花板安装，但必须进行硬接线。该摄像头提供 2,560 x 1,440 像素的清晰视频，拥有 160 度广角视野并支持 HDR，因此能很好地处理复杂光线环境。它还支持 12 倍数字变焦，以便聚焦细节。
