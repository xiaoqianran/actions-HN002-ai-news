# Dual-State Slot Attention: Decoupling Appearance and Identity for Video Object-Centric Learning
# 双状态槽注意力机制：视频以对象为中心的学习中外观与身份的解耦

**Abstract:** Unsupervised video object-centric learning aims to decompose dynamic scenes into persistent, object-level representations without supervision. However, existing slot-based methods struggle to maintain stable object identity in challenging settings such as rapid motion and partial occlusion. 

**摘要：** 无监督视频以对象为中心的学习旨在无需监督的情况下，将动态场景分解为持久的、对象级别的表征。然而，现有的基于槽（slot-based）的方法在快速运动和部分遮挡等挑战性环境下，难以维持稳定的对象身份。

First, they typically encode both the per-frame appearance of an object and its identity across frames in a single slot vector, creating an objective conflict that leads to slot swapping: reconstruction requires sensitivity to transient visual changes, whereas temporal consistency requires invariance to them. 

首先，它们通常将对象的单帧外观及其跨帧身份编码在同一个槽向量中，这造成了目标冲突，导致了“槽交换”现象：重构需要对瞬时视觉变化保持敏感，而时间一致性则要求对这些变化保持不变性。

Second, the token renormalization used in Slot Attention can amplify weakly attending slots, allowing them to absorb tokens from other objects and destabilize slot-to-object correspondence. 

其次，槽注意力机制（Slot Attention）中使用的标记重归一化（token renormalization）可能会放大弱关注的槽，使其吸收来自其他对象的标记，从而破坏槽与对象之间对应关系的稳定性。

We propose Dual-State Slot Attention (DSSA), a fully self-supervised framework that addresses these limitations by separating appearance from identity and by reducing spurious updates from weakly matching slots. 

我们提出了双状态槽注意力机制（DSSA），这是一个完全自监督的框架，通过将外观与身份分离，并减少来自弱匹配槽的虚假更新，从而解决了上述局限性。

DSSA decomposes each slot into a local state for per-frame appearance and an identity state for temporally stable object information, thereby aligning reconstruction and temporal consistency with separate representations. 

DSSA 将每个槽分解为用于单帧外观的“局部状态”和用于时间稳定对象信息的“身份状态”，从而通过独立的表征来协调重构与时间一致性。

The identity state is updated through a learned recurrent transition that acts as a temporal filter on the local state, while competition-modulated aggregation (CMA) down-weights updates from weakly matching slots and prevents them from absorbing tokens from other objects. 

身份状态通过一种学习到的循环转换进行更新，该转换充当局部状态的时间滤波器；同时，竞争调制聚合（CMA）机制降低了来自弱匹配槽的更新权重，防止它们吸收来自其他对象的标记。

Experiments on MOVi-C, MOVi-D, and YouTube-VIS demonstrate that DSSA consistently improves segmentation quality and temporal consistency over prior methods, while also yielding stronger downstream object recognition and video dynamics prediction. 

在 MOVi-C、MOVi-D 和 YouTube-VIS 上的实验表明，与现有方法相比，DSSA 在分割质量和时间一致性方面均有持续提升，同时在下游的对象识别和视频动态预测任务中也表现出更强的性能。
