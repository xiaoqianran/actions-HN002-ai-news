**IPTV Collection of publicly available IPTV (Internet Protocol television) channels from all over the world.**
这是一个收集全球公开 IPTV（互联网协议电视）频道的项目。

**How to use?**
**如何使用？**
Simply paste the link to one of the playlists into any video player that supports live streaming and press Open.
只需将其中一个播放列表的链接粘贴到任何支持直播的视频播放器中，然后点击打开即可。

**Playlists**
**播放列表**
The main playlist containing all channels available in the repository can be found at: https://iptv-org.github.io/iptv/index.m3u
包含仓库中所有可用频道的主播放列表地址为：https://iptv-org.github.io/iptv/index.m3u
Links to other playlists can be found in the PLAYLISTS.md file.
其他播放列表的链接可以在 `PLAYLISTS.md` 文件中找到。

**EPG**
**电子节目指南 (EPG)**
Electronic Program Guide for most of the channels can be downloaded using utilities published in the iptv-org/epg repository.
大多数频道的电子节目指南可以通过 `iptv-org/epg` 仓库中发布的工具进行下载。

**Database**
**数据库**
All channel data is taken from the iptv-org/database repository. If you find any errors please open a new issue there.
所有频道数据均来自 `iptv-org/database` 仓库。如果您发现任何错误，请在该仓库中提交新的 Issue。
