# High-Fidelity Two-Step Image Generation via Teacher-Aligned End-to-End Distillation
# 通过教师对齐端到端蒸馏实现高保真两步图像生成

**Abstract:** Few-step diffusion distillation has become increasingly mature for 4-8-step generation, yet pushing further to 2 steps remains challenging. In this work, we introduce Z-Image Turbo++, a high-quality 2-step image generation model distilled from the 8-step Z-Image Turbo teacher. Our method addresses the central bottlenecks of increased task difficulty and limited model capacity in 2-step generation through three simple but effective design choices tailored to this regime.

**摘要：** 少步扩散蒸馏在 4-8 步生成任务中已日趋成熟，但进一步压缩至 2 步仍极具挑战。在这项工作中，我们推出了 Z-Image Turbo++，这是一种从 8 步 Z-Image Turbo 教师模型中蒸馏出的高质量两步图像生成模型。针对两步生成中任务难度增加和模型容量受限的核心瓶颈，我们通过三种专为此场景量身定制的简单而有效的设计方案解决了这些问题。

First, we propose Distribution-Aligned Adversarial Learning, which uses teacher-generated images rather than external real images as real samples for GAN training, providing a more attainable and informative adversarial target.

首先，我们提出了分布对齐对抗学习（Distribution-Aligned Adversarial Learning），该方法使用教师模型生成的图像而非外部真实图像作为 GAN 训练的真实样本，从而提供了一个更易实现且信息量更大的对抗目标。

Second, we adopt Step-Decoupled Parameterization, assigning independent model parameters to the two denoising steps to better match their distinct capacity demands.

其次，我们采用了步长解耦参数化（Step-Decoupled Parameterization），为两个去噪步骤分配独立的模型参数，以更好地匹配它们各自不同的容量需求。

Third, we perform End-to-End Training with Iterative Regularization, allowing the first step to receive gradients from final image quality while preserving a meaningful intermediate generation through an explicit step-1 loss. Together, these designs substantially narrow the quality gap between 2-step and 8-step generation in both qualitative and quantitative evaluations, highlighting the potential of carefully tailored distillation strategies for improving the quality-efficiency trade-off in few-step generation.

第三，我们执行了带有迭代正则化的端到端训练（End-to-End Training with Iterative Regularization），允许第一步从最终图像质量中获取梯度，同时通过显式的第一步损失函数保留有意义的中间生成结果。这些设计共同显著缩小了 2 步生成与 8 步生成在定性和定量评估中的质量差距，凸显了精心定制的蒸馏策略在提升少步生成质量与效率权衡方面的潜力。
