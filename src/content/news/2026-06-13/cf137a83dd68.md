# Robinhood sees ‘record-breaking’ traffic after SpaceX stock debuts
# SpaceX 股票上市后，Robinhood 流量创下“历史新高”

Robinhood said it has seen “record-breaking” traffic on its trading platform Friday in the hours after SpaceX’s historic public markets debut. The company shared in a post on X that “some customers experienced latency and intermittent issues” with trading but that the platform quickly recovered.
Robinhood 表示，在 SpaceX 历史性地公开上市后的几个小时内，其交易平台周五的流量创下了“历史新高”。该公司在 X 上发文称，“部分客户在交易时遇到了延迟和间歇性问题”，但平台很快就恢复了正常。

SpaceX’s shares started trading on the Nasdaq stock exchange at around 11:47 a.m. ET. Those shares immediately started trading up around 11%, pushing the company’s valuation past $2 trillion — and making Elon Musk the world’s first trillionaire.
SpaceX 的股票于美东时间上午 11:47 左右在纳斯达克证券交易所开始交易。股价随即上涨约 11%，推动该公司估值突破 2 万亿美元，并使埃隆·马斯克成为世界上首位万亿富翁。

In just one hour, around 263 million shares have traded, according to Nasdaq, representing around $42 billion worth of SpaceX stock changing hands. SpaceX only made about 4% of its shares available for trading in the IPO, putting it in a position for wild swings in its stock price as it begins life as a public company.
据纳斯达克统计，仅在一小时内，交易量就达到了约 2.63 亿股，意味着约 420 亿美元的 SpaceX 股票易手。SpaceX 在此次 IPO 中仅投放了约 4% 的股份用于交易，这使其在作为上市公司开启新篇章之际，股价极易出现剧烈波动。
