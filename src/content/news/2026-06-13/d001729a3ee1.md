# CD-RCM: Generalizable Continuous-Depth Novel View Synthesis for Reflectance Confocal Microscopy
# CD-RCM：用于反射共聚焦显微镜的通用连续深度新视角合成

**Abstract:** Reflectance confocal microscopy (RCM) provides noninvasive, cellular-resolution "optical biopsies" of human skin *in vivo* by acquiring en-face images at successive depths, forming a sparse z-stack. Due to optical limitations, these stacks are anisotropic 3D volumes with lateral resolution (0.5 $\mu$m) $\sim$6 times higher compared to axial resolution, which is defined by the optical sectioning (3 $\mu$m), limiting the interpretation of tissue.

**摘要：** 反射共聚焦显微镜（RCM）通过在连续深度获取正面图像并形成稀疏的 z 轴堆栈，为人体皮肤提供非侵入性的、细胞分辨率的“光学活检”。由于光学限制，这些堆栈是各向异性的 3D 体积，其横向分辨率（0.5 $\mu$m）比由光学切片（3 $\mu$m）定义的轴向分辨率高约 6 倍，这限制了对组织的解读。

Our goal is to provide continuous-depth visualization by interpolating intermediate sections and making the 3D volume isotropic. Such a representation permits arbitrary-direction sectioning, including histopathology-like cross-sectional examination, without requiring per-patient optimization. To that end, we introduce the first RCM-specific novel-view synthesis (NVS) approach, CD-RCM, a feedforward model that predicts realistic, unseen depths from sparsely sampled RCM stacks.

我们的目标是通过插值中间切片并使 3D 体积各向同性，从而提供连续深度的可视化。这种表示方式允许进行任意方向的切片，包括类似组织病理学的横截面检查，且无需针对每位患者进行优化。为此，我们引入了首个针对 RCM 的新视角合成（NVS）方法——CD-RCM，这是一种前馈模型，能够从稀疏采样的 RCM 堆栈中预测出逼真的、未观测到的深度。

Classical neural rendering methods focus on reconstruction from surface-level multi-view observations. In contrast to surface-level camera views, RCM can acquire optically sectioned en-face images of tissue beyond the surface up to 200 $\mu$m. However, during visualization of the RCM stacks, observations of the shallower sections (towards the surface) obscure the deeper ones. This unique axial imaging geometry and layer-dependent anatomical organization motivated our development of a tailored architectural and training framework that explicitly accounts for RCM's depth-resolved, occlusive imaging physics. Experiments demonstrate that CD-RCM achieves high-fidelity novel-view synthesis with sub-second inference time.

经典的神经渲染方法侧重于从表面多视角观测中进行重建。与表面摄像机视角不同，RCM 可以获取表面以下深达 200 $\mu$m 的组织光学切片正面图像。然而，在可视化 RCM 堆栈时，较浅切片（靠近表面）的观测结果会遮挡较深切片。这种独特的轴向成像几何结构和依赖于层的解剖组织结构，促使我们开发了一种定制的架构和训练框架，明确考虑了 RCM 深度解析的遮挡成像物理特性。实验表明，CD-RCM 实现了高保真的新视角合成，且推理时间不到一秒。
