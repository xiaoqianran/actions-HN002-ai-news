# OpenAI faces investigation from state attorneys general
# OpenAI 面临多州总检察长调查

A coalition of state attorneys general has opened an investigation into OpenAI. The company was served with a subpoena from New York’s attorney general on Friday, according to The Wall Street Journal. That subpoena sought documents related to a broad range of topics including the company’s advertising, user engagement and retention, model sycophancy, handling of consumer data and health data, and treatment of minors and seniors.
据《华尔街日报》报道，一个由多州总检察长组成的联盟已对 OpenAI 展开调查。该公司周五收到了纽约州总检察长发出的传票。该传票要求提供与广泛议题相关的文件，包括公司的广告、用户参与度和留存率、模型谄媚行为（model sycophancy）、消费者数据和健康数据的处理，以及对未成年人和老年人的对待方式。

“AI is a new and powerful technology, and we work every day to safely bring its benefits to people in a responsible way,” an OpenAI spokesperson said in a statement. “We take the concerns raised by state attorneys general seriously and intend to engage constructively with their offices.”
OpenAI 发言人在一份声明中表示：“人工智能是一项新兴且强大的技术，我们每天都在努力以负责任的方式安全地为人们带来其益处。我们非常重视各州总检察长提出的担忧，并打算与他们的办公室进行建设性的接触。”

The spokesperson also said, “Today’s ChatGPT includes a more protective experience for minors and people experiencing difficult situations, with safeguards that direct them to real-world resources and trusted human contacts. We believe kids should be treated like kids, which is why we built age prediction, released parental tools to guide their children’s use of AI, and disallowed advertising that targets kids.”
该发言人还表示：“今天的 ChatGPT 为未成年人和处于困境中的人们提供了更具保护性的体验，并设有引导他们获取现实世界资源和可信赖的人际联系的保障措施。我们认为孩子就应该像孩子一样被对待，这就是为什么我们建立了年龄预测功能，发布了指导孩子使用人工智能的家长工具，并禁止了针对儿童的广告。”

The company did not specify which states are involved in the investigation or share more details about what information was requested. TechCrunch has also reached out to New York attorney general’s office for confirmation.
该公司并未说明哪些州参与了此次调查，也没有分享关于所要求信息的更多细节。TechCrunch 也已联系纽约州总检察长办公室寻求确认。

OpenAI recently defeated its co-founder Elon Musk in a high-profile trial, after Musk accused the company of violating its founding agreement. (Musk’s lead attorney said he will appeal the decision.) However, OpenAI still faces lawsuits over everything from alleged copyright infringement to ChatGPT’s alleged role in user suicides.
此前，埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）指控 OpenAI 违反了创始协议，双方进行了一场备受瞩目的审判，最终 OpenAI 胜诉。（马斯克的首席律师表示将对判决提出上诉。）然而，OpenAI 仍面临多项诉讼，内容涵盖从涉嫌版权侵权到 ChatGPT 被指在用户自杀事件中扮演的角色等多个方面。

Earlier this month, Florida Attorney General James Uthmeier sued OpenAI and its CEO Sam Altman, claiming that OpenAI and Altman “ignored internal and external safety warnings, put children at great risk, and allowed a dangerous product to reach millions of Floridians.”
本月早些时候，佛罗里达州总检察长詹姆斯·乌特迈尔（James Uthmeier）起诉了 OpenAI 及其首席执行官萨姆·奥特曼（Sam Altman），声称 OpenAI 和奥特曼“无视内部和外部的安全警告，将儿童置于巨大风险之中，并允许一种危险的产品触及数百万佛罗里达州民众。”

Altman recently apologized to the community of Tumbler Ridge, Canada after a mass shooting; he acknowledged that OpenAI failed to alert law enforcement after the company flagged and banned the suspected shooter’s ChatGPT account.
奥特曼最近在加拿大坦布勒里奇（Tumbler Ridge）发生大规模枪击事件后向当地社区道歉；他承认，在公司标记并封禁了嫌疑人的 ChatGPT 账号后，OpenAI 未能及时通知执法部门。

The company announced this week that it has filed confidentially to go public. This post has been updated with a statement from an OpenAI spokesperson.
该公司本周宣布已秘密提交上市申请。本文已更新了 OpenAI 发言人的声明。
