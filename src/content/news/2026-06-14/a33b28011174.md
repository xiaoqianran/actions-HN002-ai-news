# Rejected Emoji Proposals (被拒绝的 Emoji 提案)

**Rejected Emoji Proposals**
This list only includes emoji that were part of a formal character proposal submitted to the Unicode Consortium. Overview documents that merely discuss possible additions or informal requests are not counted. Only symbols that were intended to be used as emoji are considered, even though that status can sometimes be a bit unclear. Emoji that had been proposed but were ultimately added due to a different proposal or in a slightly different form are not included. Proposals are considered rejected if they did not result in new additions even when no formal disapproval notice has been published. The list is most likely not 100% complete.

**被拒绝的 Emoji 提案**
此列表仅包含作为正式字符提案提交给 Unicode 联盟的 Emoji。仅讨论可能增加的内容或非正式请求的概述文档不计入在内。只有旨在用作 Emoji 的符号才会被考虑，尽管其状态有时可能有些模糊。那些曾被提议但最终因其他提案或以略有不同的形式被添加的 Emoji 不包含在内。如果提案未导致新增内容，即使没有发布正式的拒绝通知，该提案也被视为被拒绝。此列表很可能并不完整。

Sample images are usually taken directly from the proposals and may not accurately reflect best practices. The “sample” column has been left blank where a proposal document did not offer suitable illustrations. The “decision” column lists documents and action items that were relevant to the rejection of the emoji in question. This can sometimes be incredibly vague; discussions may not even contain references to the emoji catalogued here, but only to other characters within the same proposal, thus implying a decision. Notes that merely acknowledge the existence of a proposal without stating a position are marked with an asterisk (*); decisions that were only shared privately with the proposal authors are marked with a dagger (†). The “withdrawn” status means that the emoji was only included in a previous iteration of the document that has since been superceded by the authors themselves.

示例图片通常直接取自提案，可能无法准确反映最佳实践。如果提案文档未提供合适的插图，“示例”（sample）列将留空。 “决定”（decision）列列出了与相关 Emoji 被拒绝有关的文档和行动项目。这有时可能非常模糊；讨论甚至可能不包含对此处编目 Emoji 的引用，而仅涉及同一提案中的其他字符，从而暗示了决定。仅承认提案存在而未说明立场的注释标有星号（*）；仅与提案作者私下分享的决定标有剑号（†）。“撤回”（withdrawn）状态意味着该 Emoji 仅包含在文档的先前版本中，该版本已被作者本人取代。
