# GLM-5.2 Is Out

**GLM-5.2 is Fully Open, Frontier Intelligence Belongs to Everyone**
**GLM-5.2 全面开源，前沿智能属于每一个人**

Today, the sudden restriction of certain frontier models is deeply regrettable. At a time when access to frontier models is abruptly cut off for non-technical reasons, we are even more convinced of one thing: science should be global. The path to AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) must never be enclosed by high walls.
今天，某些前沿模型突然遭受限制，这令人深感遗憾。在非技术原因导致前沿模型访问权限被突然切断的时刻，我们更加坚信一点：科学应当是全球化的。通往 AGI（通用人工智能）的道路绝不能被高墙所阻隔。

We have always believed that AGI should be the cornerstone for all of humanity to collaboratively explore the boundaries of intelligence and solve complex challenges, rather than a privilege monopolized by a few rules and subject to revocation at any moment. In the face of external blockades and restrictions, our attitude is one of radical openness. Frontier intelligence must remain open-source, accessible, and buildable, serving every dedicated developer.
我们始终认为，AGI 应成为全人类共同探索智能边界、解决复杂挑战的基石，而不是被少数规则垄断、随时可能被撤销的特权。面对外部的封锁与限制，我们的态度是彻底的开放。前沿智能必须保持开源、可访问且可构建，服务于每一位专注的开发者。

GLM-5.2 is Zhipu's most capable open-source model to date. It not only supports a truly usable 1M context window but also maintains a continuous lead in the independent completion of long-horizon tasks, providing solid foundational support for building complex agent applications. It also continues to be our main engine for creating the strongest domestic coding model.
GLM-5.2 是智谱迄今为止能力最强的开源模型。它不仅支持真正可用的 1M 上下文窗口，还在独立完成长程任务方面保持持续领先，为构建复杂的智能体应用提供了坚实的基础支撑。它也将继续作为我们打造最强国产编程模型的核心引擎。

Tonight at 5:21—at this special moment—GLM-5.2 will officially be available to all GLM Coding Plan users (including Lite / Pro / Max). The API will also go live next week. A step closer to frontier intelligence for everyone. The future of AI is open, and it is for the people.
今晚 5 点 21 分——在这个特殊的时刻——GLM-5.2 将正式向所有 GLM 编程计划用户（包括 Lite / Pro / Max）开放。API 也将于下周上线。向“人人皆可拥有前沿智能”的目标又迈进了一步。AI 的未来是开放的，它属于人民。
