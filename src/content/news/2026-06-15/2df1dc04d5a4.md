# How to watch most of the World Cup matches with free trials
# 如何利用免费试用观看大部分世界杯比赛

You can find free trials at all the major streaming sites, and we found the cheapest way to catch the whole Cup.
你可以在各大主流流媒体网站找到免费试用机会，我们也为你找到了观看整届世界杯最省钱的方法。

Hoping to catch some World Cup matches while spending as little money as possible? You have a few options for finding a few days of free streaming, although you may choose to eventually pony up some money. That, or get creative by combining multiple offers to make it through the whole tournament.
想要在尽可能少花钱的情况下观看世界杯比赛吗？你有几种选择可以获得几天的免费流媒体观看权，尽管你最终可能还是需要支付一些费用。或者，你也可以发挥创意，通过组合多个优惠活动来撑过整个赛事。

We found a handful of streaming services that are showing all of the World Cup matches. Most of these free trials require you to be a new subscriber, though you might just be able to use a different email address than before.
我们找到了一些提供世界杯全场次直播的流媒体服务。大多数免费试用都需要你是新订阅用户，不过你或许可以通过更换电子邮箱地址来再次尝试。

### FuboTV
### FuboTV

All FuboTV plans can stream every World Cup match, and each includes a five-day free trial for new members. The least expensive plan is $9.99 for the first month after the trial period, then $19.99 per month after that. Paying for one month after the trial should cover you through all (or most) of the finals, and is likely the least expensive way to watch every match. My Best Buy Plus and Total members can claim an extended 30-day free trial, as long as they’re new to FuboTV.
FuboTV 的所有套餐都能直播每一场世界杯比赛，且每种套餐都为新会员提供五天的免费试用。最便宜的套餐在试用期后首月费用为 9.99 美元，之后每月 19.99 美元。在试用结束后支付一个月的费用，应该足以覆盖全部（或大部分）决赛阶段，这很可能是观看所有比赛最省钱的方式。My Best Buy Plus 和 Total 会员可以申请延长至 30 天的免费试用，前提是他们必须是 FuboTV 的新用户。

### Peacock
### Peacock

There’s a Peacock Premium Plus trial offer if you sign up using your Amazon account that will grant you seven days of free access before you’re charged $15.99 per month. I was able to access the offer on an Amazon account without Prime, but your mileage may vary. We also found multiple ways to potentially claim free Peacock through other paid subscriptions. Some Xfinity Internet plans include Peacock Premium, which you can learn more about on the company’s support page. Walmart Plus members can claim either Peacock Premium or Paramount Plus Essential, but only one at a time, with a 90-day cooldown between switching.
如果你通过亚马逊账户注册，可以获得 Peacock Premium Plus 的试用优惠，在每月 15.99 美元的扣费开始前，你将拥有七天的免费访问权限。我尝试用一个没有 Prime 会员的亚马逊账户成功获取了该优惠，但具体情况可能因人而异。我们还发现可以通过其他付费订阅来获取免费的 Peacock 服务。一些 Xfinity 网络套餐包含了 Peacock Premium，你可以在该公司的支持页面了解更多信息。Walmart Plus 会员可以领取 Peacock Premium 或 Paramount Plus Essential，但一次只能领取其中一个，且切换时有 90 天的冷却期。

### YouTube TV
### YouTube TV

New YouTube TV subscribers can access a 10-day trial for the Sports plan, which includes Fox and 35 other networks. After the trial, the membership will renew for $54.99 per month for the first year, a $10 per month discount from the normal membership.
YouTube TV 的新订阅用户可以获得 10 天的体育套餐试用期，其中包括 Fox 和其他 35 个频道。试用结束后，第一年的会员费将以每月 54.99 美元的价格续订，比正常会员价格每月优惠 10 美元。

### Fox One
### Fox One

The Fox One streaming service includes World Cup matches, and new subscribers can access a three-day free trial when signing up. After that, the plan costs $19.99 per month to continue watching, and includes both live and on-demand content from Fox.
Fox One 流媒体服务包含世界杯比赛，新订阅用户在注册时可获得三天的免费试用。试用期后，若要继续观看，每月费用为 19.99 美元，该套餐包含 Fox 提供的直播和点播内容。
