# 19 Best Gifts for Plant Lovers and Gardeners (2026)
# 2026年送给植物爱好者和园丁的19款最佳礼物

Ostensibly, plant lovers and gardeners should be the easiest people to shop for. Just get them a plant, right? Wrong. (Well, most of the time.) I review indoor hydroponic gardens for WIRED and have been a proud plant parent for almost 30 years, so I can say that even if you have a running mental catalog of all your recipients' plants and know which ones they don't yet have, you may not know what they have space for, or what they're looking to switch up to this gardening season.

表面上看，给植物爱好者和园丁送礼应该是最容易的。买盆植物不就行了吗？错。（好吧，大多数时候是这样。）我为《连线》（WIRED）杂志评测室内水培花园，并且作为一名自豪的“植物家长”已经快30年了。所以我可以负责任地说，即便你脑子里有一份对方植物的清单，清楚他们还缺哪种，你可能依然不知道他们还有多少空间，或者这个园艺季节他们想尝试什么新花样。

In general, the best gifts are ones that work with the plants and garden they already have, or that help them care for them. I've dug through the dirt to vet, research, and real-world test gifts that will earn their keep, whether your recipient has a single cactus on a windowsill or a multi-acre farm.

总的来说，最好的礼物是那些能与他们现有的植物和花园相辅相成，或者能帮助他们更好地照料植物的东西。我深入挖掘，对这些礼物进行了筛选、研究和实地测试，确保它们物有所值，无论你的送礼对象是窗台上只有一盆仙人掌的初学者，还是拥有数英亩农场的园艺达人。

### Plants as Art: Modern Sprout Smart Landscape Growframe
### 植物艺术：Modern Sprout 智能景观生长框

It's difficult to overstate just how cool this 32" x 5" x 11" metal grow-light frame looks in person, which was inspired by shadowbox planters the creator saw in Paris. It not only adds a sleek, contemporary vibe to just about any home decor style, but it also isn't as bright as a traditional grow light, so it's not disruptive to keep in your living space. You can set an on-and-off schedule and adjust the brightness in Modern Sprout's app, or just turn it on and off manually with a switch on the 9-foot cord.

这个32英寸 x 5英寸 x 11英寸的金属生长灯框实物看起来非常酷，其灵感来源于创作者在巴黎看到的阴影盒式花盆。它不仅能为几乎任何家居风格增添时尚、现代的气息，而且亮度不像传统生长灯那么刺眼，因此放在起居空间里也不会造成干扰。你可以通过Modern Sprout的应用程序设置开关时间表并调节亮度，也可以直接使用9英尺电源线上的开关手动控制。

### An Easy-Care Countertop Garden: Lettuce Grow Counterstand Set
### 易于打理的台面花园：Lettuce Grow Counterstand 套装

Lettuce Grow is better known for its elaborate Farmstand models, but last year the brand released a no-fuss, no-pump, giftable setup that uses the Kratky method of hydroponic gardening. Just fill the three science-lab-looking amber beakers to the fill line, add a few droppers full of the included liquid nutrients, and set your seedlings on one of the included wicks. The wick will draw water up to the plant until its roots can grow down into it.

Lettuce Grow以其精致的Farmstand系列而闻名，但去年该品牌推出了一款无需泵、简单易用且适合作为礼物的套装，采用了克拉特基（Kratky）水培法。只需将三个看起来像实验室烧杯的琥珀色容器加水至刻度线，滴入几滴附带的液体营养液，然后将幼苗放在附带的灯芯上即可。灯芯会将水分输送给植物，直到其根系向下生长接触到水面。

### A Stylish Propagation Station: Mkono Plant Propagation Tubes
### 时尚的扦插站：Mkono 植物扦插管

Propagation tubes as decor is not a new concept, but not everyone has the table or shelf space for the number of tubes they need. This hanging version comes in small, medium, or large sizes, as well as your choice of wood finishes. There's also a white version with colored tubes if your recipient wanted to hang it in front of a window for a stained-glass effect.

将扦插管作为装饰品并不是什么新概念，但并不是每个人都有足够的桌面或架子空间来摆放所需的扦插管。这款悬挂式版本有小、中、大三种尺寸，还有多种木质饰面可供选择。如果你想让它在窗前呈现出类似彩色玻璃的效果，还有带有彩色试管的白色版本。

### For Foolproof Planting: Sol Soils Starter Pack
### 傻瓜式种植：Sol Soils 入门套装

Sol Soils products are on the pricier side, but if you know a plant lover who has a black thumb—or who just appreciates the best of the best—this line of specialty houseplant potting mixes, additives, and amendments is among the best I've ever tried. The houseplant chunky mix ($13) looks like an odd jumble of coconut husk chips, bark, pumice, and other bits and bobs, but it has saved many an ailing seedling in my household from the brink of death.

Sol Soils的产品价格偏高，但如果你认识的植物爱好者是个“植物杀手”，或者对方只追求顶级品质，那么这一系列专业的室内盆栽土、添加剂和改良剂是我尝试过最好的产品之一。这款室内植物颗粒土（13美元）看起来像是一堆椰壳碎、树皮、浮石和其他杂物的混合体，但它确实把我家里许多垂死的幼苗从死亡边缘救了回来。
