### A little bird
### 小道消息

Senior reporter Tim De Chant heard from a little bird who is familiar with GM and its inner workings that a “foreign supplier” is providing lithium-iron-phosphate (LFP) cells for the 2027 Chevrolet Bolt — and that the automaker currently has no plans to make LFPs for its EVs. Previously, a Wall Street Journal report said the arrangement with the foreign supplier — identified as Chinese battery manufacturer CATL — was a temporary stopgap. De Chant heard that GM is starting production of LFP at an Ultium plant in the coming weeks, but those cells are destined for energy-storage systems made by LG Energy Solution. The automaker hasn’t yet decided whether LFP has a future in an EV beyond the Bolt.
资深记者 Tim De Chant 从一位熟悉通用汽车内部运作的知情人士处获悉，一家“外国供应商”正在为 2027 款雪佛兰 Bolt 提供磷酸铁锂（LFP）电池，而该车企目前并无为其电动汽车生产 LFP 电池的计划。此前，《华尔街日报》报道称，与该外国供应商（被确认为中国电池制造商宁德时代）的合作只是权宜之计。De Chant 获悉，通用汽车将在未来几周内开始在某家 Ultium 工厂生产 LFP 电池，但这些电池将用于 LG 能源解决方案公司制造的储能系统。通用汽车尚未决定 LFP 电池在 Bolt 之外的电动汽车领域是否还有未来。

### Deals!
### 交易动态！

We can officially say goodbye to the Apple car. Yeah, I know that special project was shut down in 2024. But now there is further proof that Apple has moved well beyond autonomous cars. After a tip and some document scouring, we found that Waymo acquired a massive 5,500-acre proving ground in Arizona owned by Route 14 Investment Partners LLC, a Delaware shell company associated with Apple. Waymo acquired the property for $220 million, according to the filing. The acquisition is the latest evidence that Waymo is trying to scale up its operations.
我们可以正式向苹果汽车告别了。是的，我知道那个特别项目已于 2024 年关闭。但现在有进一步的证据表明，苹果已经完全超越了自动驾驶汽车领域。在收到线索并查阅了一些文件后，我们发现 Waymo 收购了位于亚利桑那州的一块占地 5500 英亩的巨大试验场，该试验场原属于与苹果有关联的特拉华州空壳公司 Route 14 Investment Partners LLC。根据备案文件，Waymo 以 2.2 亿美元收购了该物业。此次收购是 Waymo 试图扩大其业务规模的最新证据。
