# The best early Amazon Prime Day deals so far
# 目前为止最值得入手的亚马逊 Prime Day 预热折扣

There are already plenty of deals to shop while we wait for Prime Day to arrive on June 23rd.
在等待 6 月 23 日 Prime Day 到来之际，已经有许多优惠活动可以抢先选购了。

Amazon’s earlier-than-usual Prime Day doesn’t begin until June 23rd, but there are several even earlier deals on must-have products that you can check out right now. To name some examples, Apple’s AirPods Pro 3 are selling at their cheapest-ever price. Also, you can grab Ring’s latest battery-powered doorbell for $60 off and it includes a free Indoor Cam Plus with purchase.
亚马逊今年的 Prime Day 比往年更早，虽然要到 6 月 23 日才正式开始，但现在已经有一些必备产品的预热折扣值得关注。举例来说，Apple AirPods Pro 3 正以史低价格出售。此外，你还可以以优惠 60 美元的价格购买 Ring 最新的电池供电门铃，购买时还附赠一个 Indoor Cam Plus。

While the pre-Prime Day deals are looking good so far, you may not see the brands or products you’re looking for — hopefully, those deals will come later in June. Still, we’ve only included early deals that meet the criteria we hold for all our commerce coverage: they’re good products sold at attractive prices.
虽然目前的预热折扣看起来不错，但你可能还没看到心仪的品牌或产品——希望这些优惠能在 6 月下旬陆续出现。不过，我们挑选的预热折扣都符合我们一贯的报道标准：即以诱人的价格提供优质产品。

We’ll update this post frequently ahead of Prime Day.
在 Prime Day 到来之前，我们将持续更新这篇文章。

**Logitech MX Master 3S**
The MX Master 3S is a favorite among many Verge staff members because of its comfort and customization. It can connect via Bluetooth, via its included 2.4GHz receiver, or wired with its USB-C cable.
MX Master 3S 因其舒适度和高度的可定制性，深受许多 Verge 员工的喜爱。它可以通过蓝牙、附带的 2.4GHz 接收器或 USB-C 有线方式连接。

**Baseus AM52 Power Bank**
The Baseus AM52 10,000mAh power bank that can deliver Qi2.2 25W wireless charging speeds is cheaper than ever for Verge readers. Through June 30th, you can head over to Amazon and use the code THEVERGECODE to nab one for $42.99 — considerably cheaper than the $63.99 price that Prime members have access to.
倍思（Baseus）AM52 10,000mAh 移动电源支持 Qi2.2 25W 无线充电，现在 Verge 的读者可以享受到史无前例的优惠。在 6 月 30 日之前，前往亚马逊并使用优惠码 THEVERGECODE，即可以 42.99 美元的价格入手，这比 Prime 会员享有的 63.99 美元价格便宜得多。

**Eero Pro 6E tri-band router**
Eero’s Pro 6E mesh router is the Amazon-owned company’s last-gen model. It supports the 6GHz Wi-Fi band with speeds of up to 1.3Gbps and covers up to 2,000 square feet with one unit. Wired speeds can go up to 2.5Gbps.
Eero Pro 6E 网状路由器是这家亚马逊旗下公司的上一代机型。它支持 6GHz Wi-Fi 频段，速度高达 1.3Gbps，单台设备覆盖面积可达 2,000 平方英尺。有线传输速度最高可达 2.5Gbps。

**Amazon Fire 7 Kids tablet**
Amazon’s Fire 7 Kids tablet is steeply discounted ahead of Prime Day. The 32GB tablet that includes a bumper case, one year of access to a suite of kid-friendly content, and a two-year hardware warranty is down to $64.99 at Amazon (originally $129.99).
亚马逊 Fire 7 儿童平板电脑在 Prime Day 前夕大幅降价。这款 32GB 的平板电脑包含保护套、一年的儿童内容订阅服务以及两年硬件保修，目前在亚马逊上的售价降至 64.99 美元（原价 129.99 美元）。

**Amazon Fire TV Soundbar Plus**
Amazon’s Fire TV Soundbar Plus that also includes a wireless subwoofer and two satellite speakers is down to $379.99 (originally $489.99), it’s best price to date. It’s a solid sound system if you don’t want to spend a bunch of money.
亚马逊 Fire TV Soundbar Plus（包含无线低音炮和两个卫星音箱）目前降至 379.99 美元（原价 489.99 美元），这是迄今为止的最低价。如果你不想花太多钱，这是一套非常扎实的音响系统。

**Apple AirPods Pro 3**
Apple’s AirPods Pro 3 have been available at their lowest price to date for the past week or so. Originally $249, the noise-canceling, wireless earbuds are $179 at Amazon and Best Buy — no membership required.
过去一周左右，Apple AirPods Pro 3 一直处于史低价格。这款支持降噪的无线耳机原价 249 美元，目前在亚马逊和百思买（Best Buy）的售价为 179 美元，无需会员资格即可购买。
