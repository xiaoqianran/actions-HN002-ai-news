# We’re strengthening our presence in Alabama through new investments and community support.
# 我们正通过新的投资和社区支持，加强我们在阿拉巴马州的布局。

Google has announced a $1.5 billion investment for 2026 and 2027 to expand its data center campus in Jackson County, Alabama. Operating since 2019 on a repurposed former coal-plant site, the facility powers essential digital services while driving long-term regional growth.
Google 宣布将在 2026 年和 2027 年投资 15 亿美元，用于扩建其位于阿拉巴马州杰克逊县的数据中心园区。该设施自 2019 年起在原煤电厂旧址上改建运营，在为关键数字服务提供动力的同时，也推动了该地区的长期增长。

As part of this expansion, Google is funding 100% of its own power and infrastructure costs. The company also announced a $2 million Energy Impact Fund in partnership with the TVA and CAANEAL to support local energy efficiency and weatherization programs.
作为此次扩建的一部分，Google 将全额承担其电力和基础设施成本。此外，公司还宣布与田纳西河谷管理局（TVA）及 CAANEAL 合作，设立 200 万美元的“能源影响基金”，以支持当地的能源效率提升和防风雨改造项目。

Furthering our community commitment, we’re donating $550,000 to provide STEM kits for local fourth-to-eighth graders. These initiatives build on Google’s long-term local impact, which includes supporting water stewardship in the Paint Rock River Watershed, training over 130,000 Alabamians in digital skills and generating hundreds of full-time and construction jobs.
为了进一步履行社区承诺，我们捐赠了 55 万美元，为当地四至八年级的学生提供 STEM 学习套件。这些举措建立在 Google 对当地长期影响的基础之上，包括支持 Paint Rock 河流域的水资源管理、为超过 13 万名阿拉巴马州居民提供数字技能培训，以及创造数百个全职和建筑类工作岗位。
