### New research shows how AMIE, our medical AI, could help manage health conditions.
### 最新研究表明，我们的医疗 AI “AMIE” 如何助力健康状况管理。

Giving a diagnosis is the first step in treating a patient. Once a diagnosis is established, the challenge becomes managing a health condition over time — tracking symptoms across multiple appointments, parsing guidelines as they’re updated and fine-tuning medications.
做出诊断是治疗患者的第一步。一旦确诊，随之而来的挑战便是如何进行长期的健康管理——这包括在多次就诊过程中追踪症状、解析不断更新的医疗指南，以及微调药物治疗方案。

Research published today in “Nature” shows the capabilities of the Articulate Medical Intelligence Explorer (AMIE), our best-in-class research AI system for medical reasoning and conversations, evolving from one-off diagnostic conversations to long-term disease management using drug formularies and clinical guidelines.
今天发表在《自然》（Nature）杂志上的研究展示了“Articulate Medical Intelligence Explorer”（简称 AMIE）的能力。作为我们顶尖的医疗推理与对话研究型 AI 系统，AMIE 已经从最初的单次诊断对话，进化为能够利用药物处方集和临床指南进行长期疾病管理。

Using the long-context capabilities of Gemini models, AMIE for disease management features an empathetic dialogue agent for real-time patient conversations and a deep-thinking management reasoning agent that cross-references hundreds of pages of authoritative clinical knowledge.
得益于 Gemini 模型强大的长上下文处理能力，用于疾病管理的 AMIE 配备了一个能够进行实时患者对话的共情对话代理，以及一个能够交叉参考数百页权威临床知识的深度思维管理推理代理。

In this blinded study with patient actors, specialist physicians compared AMIE with 21 primary care doctors. AMIE matched clinicians in overall management reasoning and scored significantly higher in plan preciseness and guideline alignment, which suggests AI could someday support medical care, giving physicians more time to spend with patients.
在这项与模拟患者进行的盲测研究中，专科医生将 AMIE 与 21 位初级保健医生进行了对比。结果显示，AMIE 在整体管理推理能力上与临床医生相当，且在方案精确度和指南遵循度方面得分显著更高。这表明 AI 有望在未来辅助医疗工作，让医生能有更多时间陪伴患者。
