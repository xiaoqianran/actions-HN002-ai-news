# Beyond Benchmarks: Continuous Edge Inference for Fine-Grained Roadside Perception
# 超越基准测试：用于细粒度路侧感知的持续边缘推理

**Abstract:** Continuous AI inference on resource-constrained edge hardware introduces deployment effects that are largely invisible to conventional benchmark evaluation, including temporal instability in streaming video, thermal throttling under sustained load, and workload-dependent performance variability. 

**摘要：** 在资源受限的边缘硬件上进行持续 AI 推理会引入传统基准测试难以察觉的部署效应，包括流媒体视频中的时间不稳定性、持续负载下的热节流以及与工作负载相关的性能波动。

We present Edge-TSR, a deployment-oriented continuous edge inference system for sustained roadside perception on the NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano. Edge-TSR integrates detection, tracking, fine-grained classification, and a lightweight track-aware temporal stabilization mechanism that improves streaming inference consistency with negligible computational overhead. 

我们提出了 Edge-TSR，这是一个面向部署的持续边缘推理系统，旨在 NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano 上实现持续的路侧感知。Edge-TSR 集成了检测、跟踪、细粒度分类以及一种轻量级的“轨迹感知时间稳定机制”，该机制能在几乎不增加计算开销的情况下提高流式推理的一致性。

Our central finding is that benchmark-centric evaluation systematically overstates deployed edge inference performance. Across three state-of-the-art baselines, we observe consistent 20-30% relative degradation when transitioning from static-image evaluation to real-world streaming deployment. 

我们的核心发现是，以基准测试为中心的评估系统性地高估了边缘推理的实际部署性能。在三个最先进的基准模型中，我们观察到从静态图像评估过渡到真实流媒体部署时，性能会出现 20-30% 的持续相对下降。

Edge-TSR addresses this gap through temporal inference stabilization, recovering up to 10.16% classification accuracy over per-frame inference baselines while maintaining sustained real-time performance under continuous operation. 

Edge-TSR 通过时间推理稳定技术弥补了这一差距，在持续运行的情况下，相较于逐帧推理基准，其分类准确率提升了高达 10.16%，同时保持了稳定的实时性能。

We evaluate the complete system under diverse real-world deployment conditions, jointly characterizing inference quality, latency, throughput, and thermal behavior during long-duration operation. A 55-minute vehicular deployment over a 26 km route demonstrates sustained operation at 16.18 FPS within safe thermal limits on a single embedded device without cloud offload. 

我们在多种真实部署条件下评估了整个系统，综合表征了长时间运行过程中的推理质量、延迟、吞吐量和热行为。在 26 公里的路线中进行的 55 分钟车载部署测试表明，该系统在无需云端卸载的情况下，能在单台嵌入式设备上以 16.18 FPS 的速度持续运行，且处于安全热限之内。

Our findings show that deployment-aware evaluation and temporal inference stabilization are necessary components of continuously operating edge AI systems intended for real-world sensing deployments. We release a sample annotated streaming video evaluation dataset and full system implementation to support reproducible deployment-centric evaluation.

我们的研究结果表明，面向部署的评估和时间推理稳定技术是实现真实世界感知部署的持续运行边缘 AI 系统的必要组成部分。我们发布了一个带标注的流媒体视频评估数据集样本及完整的系统实现，以支持可复现的、以部署为中心的评估。
