# Diagnosing and Repairing Shape-Prior Shortcuts in Long-Range Single-Shot Fringe Projection Profilometry
# 诊断并修复长距离单次条纹投影轮廓术中的形状先验捷径

**Abstract:** Learning-based single-shot fringe projection profilometry (FPP) has been studied mostly at close range. The long-range regime (standoff beyond 1 m) remains largely unaddressed: inverse-square intensity falloff lowers fringe signal-to-noise ratio and degrades physical ground truth, the single-shot problem is ill-posed because fringe-order information is absent from one image, and these architectures have not been studied mechanistically.

**摘要：** 基于学习的单次条纹投影轮廓术（FPP）大多是在近距离下进行研究的。长距离场景（工作距离超过 1 米）的研究仍处于空白：平方反比定律导致的强度衰减降低了条纹的信噪比并削弱了物理真值，且由于单张图像中缺乏条纹级次信息，单次测量问题本身是不适定的，此外，这些架构尚未得到机制性的研究。

We present a diagnose-repair-verify study using mechanistic interpretability (MI) and conformal uncertainty quantification (UQ) as convergent diagnostics: they agree on one physical failure locus, driving and verifying an architectural repair. On a photorealistic synthetic benchmark (15,600 fringe images, 50 objects at 1.5-2.1 m), a best UNet baseline reaches 14.54 mm object mean absolute error (MAE). Three probes (linear probing, Grad-CAM, flat-plane out-of-distribution test) converge: the baseline solves the task via object-boundary shape priors rather than fringe-phase decoding.

我们提出了一项“诊断-修复-验证”研究，利用机制可解释性（MI）和共形不确定性量化（UQ）作为收敛诊断工具：它们共同指向了一个物理失效点，从而驱动并验证了架构修复。在一个照片级逼真的合成基准测试（包含 1.5-2.1 米距离下的 15,600 张条纹图像和 50 个物体）中，表现最好的 UNet 基准模型达到了 14.54 毫米的物体平均绝对误差（MAE）。三种探测方法（线性探测、Grad-CAM、平面分布外测试）得出了相同的结论：基准模型是通过物体边界的形状先验来解决任务，而非通过条纹相位解码。

We repair this with PhiCalNet, which outputs wrapped phase rather than depth and applies a fixed differentiable calibration layer mapping phase to depth, removing the shape-prior solution from the hypothesis space architecturally rather than by a loss penalty. A physics-informed loss that enforces the same physics as a soft penalty on a depth-regressing network yields no measurable gain, isolating the architecture as the operative factor.

我们使用 PhiCalNet 修复了这一问题，它输出的是包裹相位而非深度，并应用了一个固定的可微校准层将相位映射到深度，从而在架构层面（而非通过损失惩罚）从假设空间中剔除了形状先验解。在深度回归网络上施加相同物理约束的物理信息损失函数并未带来可测量的增益，这证明了架构本身才是决定性因素。

PhiCalNet reduces object MAE 3.3x to 4.46 mm; the residual is carried by 0.103% of pixels at the +/-pi wrap discontinuity. Pixel-wise conformal UQ confirms the diagnosis: rejecting the top 5% of object pixels by snapshot disagreement cuts PhiCalNet RMSE by 64% (20.6->7.4 mm) versus 3.5% for the baseline. MI and UQ converge on the same failure locus.

PhiCalNet 将物体 MAE 降低了 3.3 倍，达到 4.46 毫米；残差主要集中在 +/-pi 相位包裹不连续处的 0.103% 的像素上。像素级的共形 UQ 证实了这一诊断：通过快照差异剔除掉 5% 的物体像素后，PhiCalNet 的 RMSE 降低了 64%（从 20.6 毫米降至 7.4 毫米），而基准模型仅降低了 3.5%。MI 和 UQ 在同一失效点上达成了收敛。
