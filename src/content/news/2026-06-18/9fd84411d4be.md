# We got free GTA V upgrades before GTA VI
# 在《GTA VI》发布前，我们获得了《GTA V》的免费升级

Rockstar Games will allow players to upgrade older versions of Grand Theft Auto V for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X / S for free just months before the launch of GTA VI. Starting June 18th, players with any version of GTA V on PS4 or the digital version on Xbox One can get the current-gen version of the game at no additional cost.
在《GTA VI》发布前夕，Rockstar Games 宣布允许玩家免费将旧版本的《侠盗猎车手 V》（Grand Theft Auto V）升级至 PlayStation 5 和 Xbox Series X / S 版本。从 6 月 18 日起，拥有 PS4 版《GTA V》任意版本或 Xbox One 数字版《GTA V》的玩家，均可免费获得该游戏的次世代版本。

If you haven’t upgraded your copy of GTA V yet, this free update saves you from buying a whole new copy of the game for $39.99 or paying an upgrade fee. Rockstar announced the news as part of an update to GTA Online, which will add the ability to carry out heists at the Kortz Center in July.
如果你还没有升级你的《GTA V》，这次免费更新将为你省去购买价值 39.99 美元的新游戏副本或支付升级费用的开销。Rockstar 是在宣布《GTA Online》更新时透露这一消息的，该更新还将在 7 月份增加在 Kortz Center 执行抢劫任务的功能。

We’re still waiting on another trailer for GTA VI ahead of its November 19th release date. There’s still no word on how much GTA VI will cost, but other developers are already adjusting their game release schedules to avoid a clash with the highly anticipated title.
我们仍在等待《GTA VI》在 11 月 19 日正式发售前的下一部预告片。目前关于《GTA VI》的售价尚无定论，但其他游戏开发商已经开始调整各自的游戏发布计划，以避免与这部备受期待的大作“撞车”。
