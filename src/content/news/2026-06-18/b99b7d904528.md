# Noise-Driven Escape from Metastable Phases explains Grokking in Deep Neural Networks
# 基于亚稳态逃逸的噪声驱动机制解释深度神经网络中的“顿悟”（Grokking）现象

**Abstract:** Deep neural networks (DNNs) exhibit first order phase transitions under variations of the L2 regularization strength, with each transition marking the onset of a new learnable feature. Below a critical regularization strength, all features are in principle learnable, but coexisting metastable states, separated by energy barriers, can trap the network and impede convergence.

**摘要：** 深度神经网络（DNN）在 L2 正则化强度变化下表现出一阶相变，每一次相变都标志着一个新的可学习特征的出现。在临界正则化强度以下，所有特征原则上都是可学习的，但由能量势垒分隔的共存亚稳态可能会困住网络并阻碍收敛。

A strength of DNNs is their ability to generalize. But many open questions remain, among them the origin of so called grokking: the abrupt, delayed onset of generalization after prolonged apparent overfitting. We show for linear DNNs that grokking is consistent with hysteresis in first-order L2 phase transitions: using L2 regularization to engineer deliberate trapping, we demonstrate that a model in a low-accuracy metastable state escapes only when SGD noise drives it across an energy barrier, with escape times following Arrhenius scaling.

DNN 的一个优势在于其泛化能力。但仍有许多未解之谜，其中之一就是所谓的“顿悟”（grokking）现象的起源：即在长时间的表面过拟合之后，泛化能力突然出现并延迟启动。我们针对线性 DNN 表明，顿悟现象与一阶 L2 相变中的滞后现象一致：通过使用 L2 正则化进行人为的陷阱设计，我们证明了处于低精度亚稳态的模型只有在 SGD（随机梯度下降）噪声驱动其跨越能量势垒时才能逃逸，且逃逸时间遵循阿伦尼乌斯（Arrhenius）标度。

We reproduce grokking-like delayed convergence across two orders of magnitude in escape time by deliberately trapping models in metastable phases. Using sparse sub-sampling we also reproduce the canonical grokking curve where test error eventually approaches the final training error. Our work suggests that the number of metastable states equals the number of learnable features -- one per singular value of the data covariance -- the potential for hysteresis grows naturally with task complexity. We provide evidence that the same mechanism likely operates in general nonlinear DNNs. Our results provide routes toward more efficient learning schemes.

通过将模型刻意困在亚稳态中，我们在两个数量级的逃逸时间内重现了类似顿悟的延迟收敛过程。利用稀疏子采样，我们还重现了经典的顿悟曲线，即测试误差最终趋近于最终训练误差。我们的研究表明，亚稳态的数量等于可学习特征的数量（数据协方差的每个奇异值对应一个），而滞后现象的可能性会随着任务复杂性的增加而自然增长。我们提供的证据表明，同样的机制很可能也适用于一般的非线性 DNN。我们的研究结果为更高效的学习方案提供了路径。
