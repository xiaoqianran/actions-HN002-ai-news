# Landsat-Sentinel-2 Algal Bloom Mapping Using Vision Transformers: Model Description, Implementation, and Examples
# 基于视觉 Transformer 的 Landsat-Sentinel-2 藻华制图：模型描述、实现与示例

**Abstract:** Coastal algal bloom monitoring requires frequent, spatially detailed, and globally consistent observations, provided by Landsat-8/9 and Sentinel-2 A/B/C. Together, these missions offer over a decade of medium-resolution multispectral imagery with near-global coverage every 2-3 days, enabling the detection of fragmented bloom structures not resolvable by coarse ocean-color sensors. However, their use in aquatic environments remains challenging due to limited spectral coverage and a lack of harmonized reflectance products. As an alternative to traditional bio-optical methods, deep learning-based image classification offers a data-driven approach that can overcome many of these limitations.

**摘要：** 沿海藻华监测需要频繁、空间细节丰富且全球一致的观测数据，而 Landsat-8/9 和 Sentinel-2 A/B/C 卫星提供了这些数据。这些任务共同提供了超过十年的中分辨率多光谱影像，每 2-3 天即可实现近乎全球的覆盖，从而能够探测到粗分辨率海洋水色传感器无法分辨的碎片化藻华结构。然而，由于光谱覆盖范围有限且缺乏统一的反射率产品，它们在水环境中的应用仍然面临挑战。作为传统生物光学方法的替代方案，基于深度学习的图像分类提供了一种数据驱动的方法，可以克服其中的许多局限性。

This study presents the first successful implementation of vision transformer-based coastal algal bloom mapping using 30-m Landsat-Sentinel-2 images. A globally distributed bloom patch dataset was generated across bloom-prone coastal hotspots worldwide. Four transformer-based architectures were compared against a standard convolutional baseline for fine-scale bloom detection, and assessed under different optical water types and atmospheric and surface conditions.

本研究首次成功实现了基于视觉 Transformer（Vision Transformer）的 30 米分辨率 Landsat-Sentinel-2 沿海藻华制图。研究人员在全球藻华多发的沿海热点地区生成了一个全球分布的藻华斑块数据集。通过对比四种基于 Transformer 的架构与标准的卷积基准模型，研究评估了它们在精细尺度藻华探测中的表现，并针对不同的光学水体类型、大气及地表条件进行了测试。

All deep learning models showed strong capabilities in detecting floating bloom areas, with omission and commission errors of 8-65%. Under cloud and glint stress in a time series, the Swin Transformer outperformed traditional spectral-index approaches, which produced widespread false positives, effectively avoiding cloud- and glint-affected pixels. Comparisons with MODIS-derived products further highlighted the benefits of higher spatial resolution in detecting fragmented and irregularly affected blooms. Our findings support deep learning as a reliable tool for medium-resolution, consistent monitoring of floating algal blooms in dynamic coastal environments.

所有深度学习模型在探测漂浮藻华区域方面均表现出强大的能力，遗漏误差和误报误差在 8% 到 65% 之间。在时间序列中面对云层和耀斑（glint）干扰时，Swin Transformer 的表现优于传统的谱指数方法（后者产生了广泛的误报），能够有效避开受云层和耀斑影响的像素。与 MODIS 衍生产品的对比进一步凸显了高空间分辨率在探测碎片化和不规则藻华方面的优势。我们的研究结果支持将深度学习作为一种可靠的工具，用于动态沿海环境中漂浮藻华的中分辨率、一致性监测。
