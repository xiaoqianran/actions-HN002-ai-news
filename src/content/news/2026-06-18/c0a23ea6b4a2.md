# GeoDisaster: Benchmarking Orchestrated Agents for Operational Disaster Geo-Intelligence
# GeoDisaster：面向灾害地理智能的协同智能体基准测试

**Abstract:** Remote-sensing vision-language models (RS-VLMs) have advanced Earth-observation analysis toward visual interpretation and instruction-following, yet fall short of operational geo-intelligence, which demands tool-grounded spatial reasoning and structured, evidence-backed decisions. 

**摘要：** 遥感视觉语言模型（RS-VLMs）推动了地球观测分析在视觉解释和指令遵循方面的发展，但在实现“操作型地理智能”方面仍显不足，因为后者需要基于工具的各种空间推理以及结构化、有据可依的决策能力。

We introduce GeoDisaster, an operational geospatial disaster reasoning benchmark with 2,921 verified instances across 43 question types and five task families: deforestation monitoring, multi-hazard analysis, building-damage assessment, flood-safe routing, and Sentinel-1 SAR flood monitoring. 

我们推出了 GeoDisaster，这是一个操作型地理空间灾害推理基准测试，包含 2,921 个经过验证的实例，涵盖 43 种问题类型和五大任务系列：森林砍伐监测、多灾种分析、建筑物损毁评估、洪水安全路径规划以及 Sentinel-1 SAR 洪水监测。

Instances integrate heterogeneous EO/GIS evidence—optical and SAR imagery, raster masks, vector geometries, road networks, and exposure layers—spanning hazard detection, damage assessment, exposure estimation, and diagnostic report generation. 

这些实例整合了异构的地球观测（EO）与地理信息系统（GIS）证据，包括光学和 SAR 影像、栅格掩膜、矢量几何数据、道路网络及暴露层，涵盖了灾害检测、损毁评估、暴露度估算和诊断报告生成等多个方面。

Ground-truth answers are grounded in executable geospatial workflows and deterministic consistency checks, removing the need for language-model annotation. 

基准答案基于可执行的地理空间工作流和确定性一致性检查，无需依赖语言模型进行标注。

We further propose an orchestrated multi-agent framework with 18 disaster-oriented tools, where role-specialized agents coordinate through explicit execution contracts, aligned via Role-Contract Expectation Alignment (RCEA): failure-aware supervised fine-tuning combined with contract-grounded reinforcement learning over dense step-level signals. 

我们进一步提出了一种包含 18 种灾害导向工具的协同多智能体框架。在该框架中，角色专业化的智能体通过明确的执行契约进行协作，并利用“角色-契约期望对齐”（RCEA）进行优化：即结合了故障感知监督微调与基于契约的强化学习，并针对密集的步骤级信号进行训练。

Experiments show that GeoDisaster challenges existing RS-VLMs and agentic systems, while RCEA improves tool use, evidence grounding, state consistency, and decision generation.

实验表明，GeoDisaster 对现有的 RS-VLMs 和智能体系统构成了挑战，而 RCEA 则有效提升了工具使用、证据溯源、状态一致性以及决策生成的能力。
