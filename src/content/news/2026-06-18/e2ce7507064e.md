# MODE: Modality-Decomposed Expert-Level Mixed-Precision Quantization for MoE Multimodal LLMs

**Title: MODE: Modality-Decomposed Expert-Level Mixed-Precision Quantization for MoE Multimodal LLMs**
**标题：MODE：面向 MoE 多模态大语言模型的模态分解专家级混合精度量化**

**Abstract:** Mixture-of-Experts Multimodal Large Language Models (MoE-MLLMs) offer remarkable performance but incur prohibitive GPU memory costs, making compression essential. Among PTQ methods, expert-level mixed-precision quantization has proven effective for MoE-LLMs, yet suffers notable degradation on MoE-MLLMs due to two overlooked biases in expert importance estimation.
**摘要：** 混合专家多模态大语言模型（MoE-MLLMs）提供了卓越的性能，但同时也带来了高昂的 GPU 显存成本，因此模型压缩至关重要。在训练后量化（PTQ）方法中，专家级混合精度量化已被证明对 MoE-LLMs 有效，但在应用于 MoE-MLLMs 时，由于专家重要性评估中存在两个被忽视的偏差，导致性能显著下降。

(1) At the cross-modal level, the numerical dominance of vision tokens causes expert selection frequency to be dominated by vision tokens, masking experts that are critical to the text modality; (2) at the intra-vision level, the large proportion of redundant vision tokens further skew frequency statistics, obscuring experts critical for informative visual content.
(1) 在跨模态层面，视觉 Token 的数量优势导致专家选择频率被视觉 Token 主导，从而掩盖了对文本模态至关重要的专家；(2) 在视觉内部层面，大量冗余的视觉 Token 进一步扭曲了频率统计，掩盖了对信息丰富的视觉内容至关重要的专家。

To bridge gaps, we propose MODE, a modality-decomposed expert-level mixed-precision quantization framework for MoE-MLLMs that decomposes expert selection frequency by modality, filters redundant vision tokens to obtain denoised visual frequency, and further evaluates quantization sensitivity per modality as a complementary signal to frequency-based estimation.
为了弥补这些差距，我们提出了 MODE，这是一个针对 MoE-MLLMs 的模态分解专家级混合精度量化框架。该框架按模态分解专家选择频率，通过过滤冗余视觉 Token 来获取去噪后的视觉频率，并进一步评估各模态的量化敏感度，作为基于频率评估的补充信号。

These signals are integrated into an Integer Linear Programming formulation to assign per-expert bit-widths under a given budget. Extensive experiments show that MODE is particularly well-suited for MoE-MLLMs, limiting average performance loss to within 2.9% at W3A16, with larger gains at the extreme 2-bit setting.
这些信号被整合到整数线性规划（ILP）公式中，以便在给定的预算下为每个专家分配位宽。大量实验表明，MODE 特别适用于 MoE-MLLMs，在 W3A16 设置下将平均性能损失限制在 2.9% 以内，并在极端的 2-bit 设置下表现出更大的性能提升。
