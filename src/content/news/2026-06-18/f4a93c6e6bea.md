# VSCO launches Studio Pro mobile photo editing app and plans $500 per year subscription
# VSCO 发布 Studio Pro 移动照片编辑应用，并计划推出每年 500 美元的订阅服务

VSCO is taking on Adobe with a new Studio Pro editing app rolling out today on iOS and coming to macOS later this year, as Bloomberg reports. At launch, the app offers tools for batch editing, style matching from a reference image, and sharing images through VSCO Galleries. VSCO says more features are coming later, including support for RAW images, advanced export options, and additional advanced editing tools like adjusting image aspect ratios.

据彭博社报道，VSCO 正在挑战 Adobe，于今日在 iOS 平台推出全新的 Studio Pro 编辑应用，并计划于今年晚些时候登陆 macOS。在发布初期，该应用提供批量编辑、基于参考图像的风格匹配以及通过 VSCO Galleries 分享图片等工具。VSCO 表示，未来还将推出更多功能，包括对 RAW 格式图像的支持、高级导出选项，以及调整图像长宽比等更多进阶编辑工具。

A press release says the app is made for managing high-volume editing projects, like “weddings, portraits, events, sports, school photography, and other large-scale photoshoots.”

新闻稿称，该应用专为管理大批量编辑项目而设计，例如“婚礼、人像、活动、体育、校园摄影及其他大规模拍摄项目”。

The $500 per year VSCO One subscription will launch later this month as an alternative to the “fragmented approach” of using disconnected software platforms for photo editing and business operations like contacting customers or delivering the finished images. It’s priced on par with the cost of an annual Adobe Creative Cloud Pro subscription, offering VSCO’s suite of apps including Studio Pro, Capture, Galleries, Workspace, Sites, AI Lab, Canvas, and its Freelance Photographer business mentorship program.

每年 500 美元的 VSCO One 订阅服务将于本月晚些时候推出。该服务旨在解决目前摄影师在照片编辑与业务运营（如联系客户或交付成品）中，因使用多个互不兼容的软件平台而导致的“碎片化”问题。其定价与 Adobe Creative Cloud Pro 的年度订阅费用相当，包含 VSCO 的全套应用，如 Studio Pro、Capture、Galleries、Workspace、Sites、AI Lab、Canvas，以及其自由摄影师商业指导计划。
