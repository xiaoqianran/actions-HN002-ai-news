# Language-Instructed Vision Embeddings for Controllable and Generalizable Perception
# 语言引导的视觉嵌入：实现可控且可泛化的感知

**Abstract:** Vision foundation models are typically trained as static feature extractors, placing the burden of task adaptation onto large downstream models. 
**摘要：** 视觉基础模型通常被训练为静态特征提取器，这使得任务适配的负担落在了大型下游模型上。

We propose an alternative paradigm: instead of solely feeding visual features into language models, we use language itself to dynamically guide the vision encoder. 
我们提出了一种替代范式：不再仅仅将视觉特征输入到语言模型中，而是利用语言本身来动态引导视觉编码器。

Our method, Language-Instructed Vision Embeddings (LIVE), leverages language as high-level guidance to produce task-centric embeddings at inference time, removing the need for task-specific retraining. 
我们的方法——语言引导视觉嵌入（LIVE），利用语言作为高级指导，在推理阶段生成以任务为中心的嵌入，从而无需进行特定任务的重新训练。

This enables the encoder to focus on contextually relevant aspects of the input, yielding more controllable and generalizable representations. 
这使得编码器能够专注于输入中与上下文相关的方面，从而产生更具可控性和泛化能力的表示。

Empirically, LIVE reduces visual hallucinations (+34 points on MMVP), surpasses vision-language models with orders of magnitude more parameters on visual question answering, and generalizes to unseen instructions and tasks -- offering a direct path toward adaptive, instruction-driven visual intelligence.
实验证明，LIVE 减少了视觉幻觉（在 MMVP 上提升了 34 个点），在视觉问答任务中超越了参数量大出几个数量级的视觉语言模型，并能泛化到未见过的指令和任务中——为实现自适应、指令驱动的视觉智能提供了一条直接路径。
