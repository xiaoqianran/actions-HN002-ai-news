# LEAP: Layer-skipping Efficiency via Adaptive Progression for Vision Transformer Distillation
# LEAP：基于自适应进阶的视觉 Transformer 蒸馏层跳跃效率优化

Vision Foundation Models (VFMs) with Vision Transformer (ViT) backbones, such as DINOv2, have become essential for downstream tasks like object recognition and semantic segmentation. The immense computational requirements of backbones often necessitate distillation into smaller architectures for edge deployment.
以 DINOv2 为代表的、采用视觉 Transformer (ViT) 主干网络的视觉基础模型 (VFMs)，已成为目标识别和语义分割等下游任务的核心。由于这些主干网络对计算资源要求极高，通常需要将其蒸馏为更小的架构，以便在边缘设备上部署。

Feature-based knowledge distillation (KD) often suffers from the teacher-student gap; the student struggles to imitate teacher's complex feature map due to its limited capacity. To mitigate this bottleneck, we propose LEAP: Layer-skipping Efficiency via Adaptive Progression, a training curriculum for ViT feature-based knowledge distillation.
基于特征的知识蒸馏 (KD) 往往受限于“教师-学生差距”；由于学生模型容量有限，难以模仿教师模型复杂的特征图。为了缓解这一瓶颈，我们提出了 LEAP：基于自适应进阶的层跳跃效率优化，这是一种针对 ViT 特征知识蒸馏的训练课程。

By utilizing the teacher's intermediate feature maps as a sequence of progressively more difficult targets, our curriculum allows the student to build a foundational representation before tackling higher-level abstractions. Our results demonstrate that this paradigm significantly accelerates convergence through adaptive difficulty selection across various student model sizes and dataset scales.
通过将教师模型的中间特征图作为一系列难度递增的目标，我们的课程允许学生模型在处理更高级的抽象概念之前，先构建基础表征。实验结果表明，该范式通过在不同学生模型规模和数据集尺度上进行自适应难度选择，显著加快了收敛速度。

With our curriculum, the LEAP-distilled ViT-S achieves 90.1% accuracy on ImageNet-100, a +12.24% improvement compared with baseline. On ImageNet-1K, LEAP achieves +3.84% and +7.75% improvement for the instance retrieval task on the Oxford and Paris datasets, respectively.
使用我们的课程，经 LEAP 蒸馏的 ViT-S 模型在 ImageNet-100 上达到了 90.1% 的准确率，较基准提升了 12.24%。在 ImageNet-1K 上，LEAP 在 Oxford 和 Paris 数据集的实例检索任务中分别实现了 3.84% 和 7.75% 的性能提升。

Furthermore, the curriculum enables 25.1% savings in training FLOPs and 21% savings in training time on ImageNet-100 by implementing early-stopping for teacher inference during the initial stages of training. Code is available at this URL.
此外，通过在训练初期对教师推理实施提前停止策略，该课程在 ImageNet-100 上的训练 FLOPs 减少了 25.1%，训练时间缩短了 21%。代码已开源。
