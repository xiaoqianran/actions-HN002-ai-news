### Information Lattice Learning as Probabilistic Graphical Model Structure Learning
### 信息格学习：作为概率图模型结构学习的视角

**Abstract:** Information lattice learning (ILL) learns interpretable rules of a signal by alternately projecting the signal onto a partition lattice that encodes a hierarchy of abstractions and lifting selected rules back to the signal domain.

**摘要：** 信息格学习（Information Lattice Learning, ILL）通过交替将信号投影到编码抽象层次结构的划分格上，并将选定的规则提升回信号域，从而学习信号的可解释规则。

When the signal is a probability mass function, we show the probabilistic rules learned by ILL admit a natural probabilistic graphical model (PGM) interpretation and develop this interpretation in detail.

当信号为概率质量函数时，我们证明了 ILL 所学习的概率规则具有自然的概率图模型（PGM）解释，并对这一解释进行了详细阐述。

A partition in ILL induces a deterministic quotient variable, and a rule is the marginal law of that quotient variable. A rule set is therefore a collection of marginal constraints over interpretable abstractions.

ILL 中的一个划分会导出一个确定性的商变量（quotient variable），而规则即为该商变量的边缘定律。因此，规则集是针对可解释抽象的一组边缘约束的集合。

General lifting is the feasible family of all joint distributions satisfying those constraints, while special lifting chooses a maximum-ignorance reconstruction, implemented in ILL by an L2 uniformity principle closely related to maximum entropy.

通用提升（General lifting）是指满足这些约束的所有联合分布的可行族，而特殊提升（Special lifting）则选择一种最大无知重构（maximum-ignorance reconstruction），这在 ILL 中通过与最大熵密切相关的 L2 一致性原则来实现。

Under a Shannon-entropy lifting, the same constraints yield a log-linear factor graph whose factors are indexed by learned abstractions. The information lattice itself, however, is not a Bayesian network: its edges encode refinement and coarsening of abstractions, not conditional dependence.

在香农熵提升下，相同的约束会产生一个对数线性因子图，其因子由学习到的抽象进行索引。然而，信息格本身并非贝叶斯网络：其边编码的是抽象的细化与粗化，而非条件依赖关系。

Thus ILL is best viewed as structure learning for interpretable constraint-based factor graphs over quotient variables. This view clarifies how ILL relates to graphical models and maximum entropy models, while suggesting new directions for inference, identifiability, and hybrid symbolic-probabilistic learning.

因此，ILL 最好的视角是将其视为针对商变量的可解释约束型因子图的结构学习。这一视角阐明了 ILL 与图模型及最大熵模型之间的关系，同时也为推理、可识别性以及混合符号-概率学习指明了新的方向。
