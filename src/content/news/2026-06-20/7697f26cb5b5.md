# Mix-QVLA: Task-Evidence-Aware Mixed-Precision Quantization of Vision-Language-Action Models
# Mix-QVLA：面向任务证据感知的视觉-语言-动作模型混合精度量化

We propose Mix-QVLA, a task-evidence-aware mixed-precision PTQ framework for VLA models. Mix-QVLA anchors each quantized variant to the full-precision action-token reference decision and evaluates whether quantization preserves task-relevant evidence across key VLA functional boundaries.
我们提出了 Mix-QVLA，这是一个针对视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型的任务证据感知混合精度训练后量化（PTQ）框架。Mix-QVLA 将每个量化变体锚定到全精度动作标记的参考决策上，并评估量化是否在关键的 VLA 功能边界上保留了与任务相关的证据。

It computes normalized gradient-weighted task-evidence maps from boundary activations and compares full-precision and quantized maps using evidence-mass and attribution-distribution distortion, capturing changes in both the strength and allocation of decision-supporting evidence.
它通过边界激活计算归一化的梯度加权任务证据图，并利用证据质量（evidence-mass）和归因分布失真（attribution-distribution distortion）来比较全精度图与量化图，从而捕捉决策支持证据在强度和分配上的变化。

A soft-bottleneck objective aggregates boundary-level degradation into layer-wise sensitivity scores. Mix-QVLA further models sensitivity throughout task execution, capturing phase-dependent shifts in layer importance rather than assuming a fixed sensitivity profile.
软瓶颈目标函数将边界级的退化聚合为层级的敏感度分数。Mix-QVLA 进一步对整个任务执行过程中的敏感度进行建模，捕捉层重要性随阶段的变化，而不是假设一个固定的敏感度配置。

The resulting evidence- and time-aware scores guide mixed-precision bit allocation under model-size and BitOps budgets. Extensive evaluations on OpenVLA-style policies show that Mix-QVLA improves the accuracy-efficiency trade-off of low-bit VLA deployment.
由此产生的证据和时间感知分数，可在模型大小和 BitOps 预算限制下指导混合精度位宽分配。在 OpenVLA 风格策略上的广泛评估表明，Mix-QVLA 改善了低位宽 VLA 部署的准确性与效率之间的权衡。

On LIBERO, Mix-QVLA reduces OpenVLA-OFT memory from 15.4 GB to 4.1 GB, retains 96.3 average success compared with 97.1 for the BF16 model, and achieves a 1.52x inference speedup.
在 LIBERO 基准测试中，Mix-QVLA 将 OpenVLA-OFT 的内存占用从 15.4 GB 降低至 4.1 GB，平均成功率保持在 96.3%（BF16 模型为 97.1%），并实现了 1.52 倍的推理加速。
