# LooseControlVideo: Directorial Video Control using Spatial Blocking
# LooseControlVideo：基于空间遮挡（Spatial Blocking）的导演级视频控制

**Abstract:** Precise 3D spatial orchestration in text-to-video generation remains a significant challenge, particularly for multi-object scenes where semantic layout and temporal dynamics are often entangled. While existing depth-conditioned models achieve good structural fidelity, they necessitate dense, frame-accurate guidance that is labor-intensive to author for dynamic events involving deformable objects.

**摘要：** 在文本生成视频领域，精确的 3D 空间编排仍然是一项重大挑战，尤其是在语义布局与时间动态往往相互交织的多物体场景中。虽然现有的深度条件模型能够实现良好的结构保真度，但它们需要密集且逐帧精确的引导，这对于涉及可变形物体的动态事件来说，创作过程非常耗时。

We present LooseControlVideo, a framework that enables intuitive and expressive control by using sparse, oriented 3D boxes as a "blocking" proxy. This allows users to author high-level layout and trajectory while leveraging a video generative model to generate realistic occlusions, dynamics and interactions.

我们提出了 LooseControlVideo，这是一个通过使用稀疏的、有向的 3D 框作为“遮挡（blocking）”代理，从而实现直观且富有表现力的控制框架。该框架允许用户创作高层级的布局和轨迹，同时利用视频生成模型来生成逼真的遮挡、动态效果和交互。

We achieve this by fine-tuning a Wan 2.2 backbone on a video dataset annotated with DNOCS, a novel encoding for 3D size, orientation and depth-ordered occlusions. Furthermore, our method allows for localized refinement, such as adjusting a jump trajectory or adding an interaction, with minimal disruption to the global scene context.

我们通过在带有 DNOCS 注释的视频数据集上微调 Wan 2.2 主干模型来实现这一目标。DNOCS 是一种用于 3D 尺寸、方向和深度排序遮挡的新型编码方式。此外，我们的方法支持局部细化，例如调整跳跃轨迹或添加交互，且对全局场景上下文的干扰极小。

Extensive evaluations on the nuScenes, HO-3D, and BEHAVE benchmarks demonstrate that LooseControlVideo significantly outperforms existing 2D-box and flow-based baselines. Our findings indicate a 1.2x to 3x improvement in Trajectory Error; 2x improvement in Rigid Motion Consistency; and a 1.5x to 2x increase in Occlusion Accuracy over current state-of-the-art layout-conditioned models, demonstrating that oriented 3D primitives provide good geometric prior for complex, multi-agent video authoring.

在 nuScenes、HO-3D 和 BEHAVE 基准测试上的广泛评估表明，LooseControlVideo 显著优于现有的基于 2D 框和光流的基准模型。研究结果显示，与当前最先进的布局条件模型相比，我们的方法在轨迹误差方面提升了 1.2 到 3 倍，在刚体运动一致性方面提升了 2 倍，在遮挡准确性方面提升了 1.5 到 2 倍。这证明了有向 3D 原语为复杂的多智能体视频创作提供了良好的几何先验。
