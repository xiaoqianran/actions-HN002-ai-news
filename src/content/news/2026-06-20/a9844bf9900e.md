### Key Takeaways
### 核心要点

**The problem:** in agentic RAG, every tool call that needs context fires a similarity search. A default pipeline ships the query embedding from the GPU to Python, lets the CPU score N corpus rows and pick the best K, then ships the answer back. That round-trip is the silent tax. The compute is fine; the travel is the bill. We all know, travel is never cheap, no matter where you want to go (pun intended!)
**问题所在：** 在 Agentic RAG 中，每一个需要上下文的工具调用都会触发一次相似度搜索。默认的流水线会将查询向量从 GPU 发送到 Python，让 CPU 对 N 行语料库进行评分并选出前 K 个结果，然后再将答案传回。这种往返过程就是隐形的税收。计算本身没问题，但传输才是成本所在。我们都知道，旅行从来都不便宜，无论你想去哪里（双关语！）。

**The easy fix:** upload the corpus to VRAM once, then keep the similarity scoring, the Top-K selection, and the merge step on the device. Only the tiny per-query embedding (D floats) and the K results travel across PCIe.
**简单的解决方案：** 将语料库一次性上传到显存（VRAM），然后将相似度评分、Top-K 选择和合并步骤保留在设备上。只有极小的单次查询向量（D 个浮点数）和 K 个结果会在 PCIe 上传输。

**The receipts:** on the same 7-year-old GTX 1080 used in Parts 1 and 2, the GPU-resident path runs the retrieval hop up to 8.57× faster than a CPU brute-force baseline. At K=8 it wins on all 15 sweep configurations (N ∈ {10k, 50k, 100k, 500k, 1M}, D ∈ {384, 768, 1024}) with speedups from 2.43× to 8.57×. At K=32 it wins on 13 of 15 configs, peaking at 7.76×. At K=100 — where the V1 selector intentionally stays simple — the CPU wins on 14 of 15 configs. That last sentence is the honest part (Well, even if I had lied, you could have easily caught it).
**实测数据：** 在第一、二部分使用的同一台 7 年前的 GTX 1080 上，GPU 常驻路径的检索速度比 CPU 暴力基准测试快 8.57 倍。在 K=8 时，它在所有 15 种扫描配置（N ∈ {10k, 50k, 100k, 500k, 1M}, D ∈ {384, 768, 1024}）中均获胜，加速比从 2.43 倍到 8.57 倍不等。在 K=32 时，它在 15 种配置中的 13 种获胜，峰值达到 7.76 倍。在 K=100 时——由于 V1 选择器有意保持简单——CPU 在 15 种配置中的 14 种获胜。最后这句话是诚实的体现（好吧，即使我撒谎了，你也能轻易发现）。

**The kicker:** the wins are not “magic kernel” wins. They are “we stopped shipping the corpus back to host RAM for no reason” wins. It is also exactly the kind of “measure many candidates, report only the best K back to the consumer” decision a 5G base station and your phone have been making every few milliseconds since CSI feedback became a thing.
**关键点：** 这些胜利并非源于“魔法内核”。它们是“我们停止了无意义地将语料库传回主机内存”的胜利。这正是 5G 基站和你的手机自 CSI 反馈技术出现以来，每隔几毫秒就在做的“测量多个候选者，仅向消费者报告最佳 K 个结果”的决策过程。

### TL;DR
### 总结

Default agentic RAG treats the GPU as a serving box and the retrieval as a Python concern. Every tool call ships the query embedding D→H, lets the CPU compute N dot products, sort the candidates, pick the top K, and ship indices and scores H→D. For an agent that calls a vector store ten times per reasoning step, that round-trip is the dominant cost — not the model, not the embedding, it is the travel.
默认的 Agentic RAG 将 GPU 视为服务盒，将检索视为 Python 的任务。每次工具调用都会将查询向量从 GPU 传到主机（D→H），让 CPU 计算 N 个点积、排序候选者、选出前 K 个，再将索引和分数传回 GPU（H→D）。对于一个在每个推理步骤中调用向量数据库十次的智能体来说，这种往返是主要的成本来源——不是模型，不是嵌入，而是传输。

CUDA-TopK-Retrieval keeps the corpus resident on the device, runs scoring + per-block partial Top-K + a multi-way merge entirely on the GPU, and exposes a tiny C++ orchestrator API (upload_corpus_rowmajor once, search_resident per query). The host-touching bytes per query collapse to one D-length embedding up and 2K results down.
CUDA-TopK-Retrieval 将语料库保留在设备上，在 GPU 上完整运行评分、分块局部 Top-K 和多路合并，并提供一个微小的 C++ 编排 API（一次性 upload_corpus_rowmajor，每次查询调用 search_resident）。每次查询涉及主机的数据量缩减为上传一个 D 长度的向量和下载 2K 个结果。
