# The NTS Radio Player brings the best of internet radio to your hi-fi
# NTS Radio Player 将互联网广播的精华带入你的高保真音响系统

NTS Radio and Swedish audio company Atonemo have teamed up on a dedicated player that brings NTS’s genre-defying mixes and streaming stations to almost any stereo or speaker setup. And, like Atonemo’s existing Streamplayer, you can also listen to your favorite streaming services with it, using AirPlay 2, Google Cast, Spotify Connect, or Tidal Connect.

NTS Radio 与瑞典音频公司 Atonemo 携手推出了一款专用播放器，旨在将 NTS 那些打破流派界限的混音作品和流媒体电台带入几乎任何立体声或扬声器系统。与 Atonemo 现有的 Streamplayer 一样，你也可以通过 AirPlay 2、Google Cast、Spotify Connect 或 Tidal Connect 使用它来收听你喜爱的流媒体服务。

The $179 NTS Radio Player puts out 24-bit / 192kHz audio over a standard 3.5mm audio jack and comes with an adapter cable for connecting to RCA on your vintage hi-fi system, or whatever other speakers you have lying around. The top of the device has two buttons for tuning in directly to NTS 1 and NTS 2. There’s also a large dial with 16 detents for switching between the station’s various infinite mixtapes. And, when you’re done letting NTS DJs pick your tunes, you can just hop over to Qobuz or Apple Music, or whatever your streaming service of choice is, and listen to the new Vince Staples record. The one thing it’s missing out of the box is Bluetooth support, though the company confirmed it’s being added in a future firmware update.

这款售价 179 美元的 NTS Radio Player 通过标准的 3.5mm 音频接口输出 24-bit / 192kHz 音频，并附带一根转接线，方便连接到你复古高保真音响系统的 RCA 接口，或是你手头的任何其他扬声器。设备顶部有两个按钮，可直接调谐至 NTS 1 和 NTS 2 电台。此外，还有一个带有 16 个档位的旋钮，用于在电台各种无限循环的混音带之间切换。当你不想再听 NTS DJ 挑选的曲目时，只需切换到 Qobuz、Apple Music 或你偏好的任何流媒体服务，即可聆听 Vince Staples 的新唱片。该设备目前唯一缺少的功能是蓝牙支持，但公司已确认将在未来的固件更新中加入此功能。

NTS has been at it since 2011 and is one of the best sources of music discovery on the internet. Rather than just an endless feed of music served up by a cold and uncaring algorithm, everything on NTS is hand-chosen by actual humans who care about music, whether that’s obscure Japanese psychedelia or deep house. In addition to the regular DJs, NTS also has frequent guest hosts including Axel Boman, Arushi Jain (AKA Modular Princess), and Wu-Lu.

NTS 自 2011 年成立以来，一直是互联网上最好的音乐发现平台之一。NTS 上的所有内容并非由冷冰冰的算法推送，而是由真正热爱音乐的人亲自挑选，无论是冷门的日本迷幻音乐还是深度浩室音乐（Deep House）。除了常驻 DJ 外，NTS 还经常邀请客座主持，包括 Axel Boman、Arushi Jain（又名 Modular Princess）和 Wu-Lu 等。
