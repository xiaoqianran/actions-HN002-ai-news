# GB-LSR: A Fast Local Spectral Image Representation with a Single Global Bandwidth for Continuous Reconstruction and Super-Resolution
# GB-LSR：一种用于连续重建和超分辨率的单全局带宽快速局部谱图像表示方法

**Abstract:** We present GB-LSR (Global-Bandwidth Local Spectral Representation), a fixed-grid local spectral representation for continuous image reconstruction. The image domain is partitioned into non-overlapping square patches, each carrying coefficients for a truncated Fourier basis predicted from shared convolutional-encoder features. A single trainable scalar bandwidth is shared globally across all patches and images, and reconstruction at any continuous coordinate is a fixed-size basis contraction whose cost is independent of image size.

**摘要：** 我们提出了 GB-LSR（全局带宽局部谱表示），这是一种用于连续图像重建的固定网格局部谱表示方法。图像域被划分为互不重叠的正方形块，每个块携带从共享卷积编码器特征预测出的截断傅里叶基系数。一个单一的可训练标量带宽在所有块和图像之间全局共享，在任何连续坐标处的重建都是一种固定大小的基收缩，其计算成本与图像大小无关。

We study three bandwidth-handling variants: a trainable global scalar (main), a fixed global scalar, and a per-patch bandwidth field. On a standardized native-reconstruction benchmark across Kodak, Set14, and Urban100, the main variant outperforms matched-budget amortized LIIF / LTE / WIRE re-implementations by 2.8-3.6 dB PSNR and 0.11-0.15 LPIPS, while running at roughly one-quarter of the slowest baseline's inference cost. The single global scalar suffices empirically: per-patch adaptive-bandwidth alternatives do not improve over it on either a closed-form locality diagnostic or an end-to-end ablation.

我们研究了三种带宽处理变体：可训练全局标量（主方案）、固定全局标量和逐块带宽场。在 Kodak、Set14 和 Urban100 的标准化原生重建基准测试中，主方案在 PSNR 上比同等预算下的 LIIF / LTE / WIRE 重实现版本高出 2.8-3.6 dB，在 LPIPS 上高出 0.11-0.15，同时其推理成本仅为最慢基准测试的约四分之一。经验证明，单一全局标量已足够：无论是通过闭式局部性诊断还是端到端消融实验，逐块自适应带宽的替代方案均未表现出优于该方案的性能。

In a separate arbitrary-scale super-resolution (ASR) extension, GB-LSR achieves competitive PSNR-Y under a canonical-style SR protocol and runs 1.44x faster than LIIF-RDN and 3.25x faster than LTE-SwinIR at x4; within the same extension, a variant trained and evaluated without 4-corner local-ensemble averaging gives a 1.77x speedup with 35% lower peak memory and negligible PSNR change, while additionally widening the RDN encoder from 64 to 96 channels gives a small positive PSNR shift with a 1.58x speedup and 31% lower peak memory. Native-reconstruction claims are scoped to the matched-budget amortized protocol, and ASR claims are scoped to a separate canonical-style SR protocol.

在另一项任意尺度超分辨率（ASR）扩展中，GB-LSR 在规范化 SR 协议下实现了具有竞争力的 PSNR-Y，在 4 倍放大下比 LIIF-RDN 快 1.44 倍，比 LTE-SwinIR 快 3.25 倍；在同一扩展中，不使用四角局部集成平均进行训练和评估的变体实现了 1.77 倍的加速，峰值内存降低了 35%，且 PSNR 变化可忽略不计；此外，将 RDN 编码器从 64 通道扩展到 96 通道，在获得微小 PSNR 正向提升的同时，实现了 1.58 倍的加速和 31% 的峰值内存降低。原生重建的结论仅限于同等预算的摊销协议，而 ASR 的结论仅限于单独的规范化 SR 协议。
