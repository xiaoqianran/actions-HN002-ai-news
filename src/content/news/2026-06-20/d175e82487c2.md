### Best Fitness Tracker Overall: Garmin Vivoactive 6
### 最佳综合健身追踪器：Garmin Vivoactive 6

Garmin makes some of the most accurate fitness trackers on the market, and the Vivoactive 6 is the best midrange option for most people. It strikes a solid balance between smartwatch features and fitness tracking, with support for both iPhone and Android users.
Garmin 生产着市场上最精准的健身追踪器，而 Vivoactive 6 是大多数人中端价位的最佳选择。它在智能手表功能和健身追踪之间取得了良好的平衡，并同时支持 iPhone 和 Android 用户。

**Why WIRED recommends:** The Vivoactive 6 is accurate, comfortable, and packed with useful wellness features without feeling overwhelming. It uses Garmin's proprietary algorithms to power features like Morning Report and Body Battery, which provide daily insights into your sleep, recovery, and readiness. It also has built-in satellite connectivity and GPS, so you can track outdoor workouts without bringing your phone along. There's also incident detection, which alerts emergency contacts if it detects a serious fall.
**WIRED 推荐理由：** Vivoactive 6 精准、舒适，且内置了许多实用的健康功能，但不会让人感到繁琐。它利用 Garmin 的专有算法驱动“晨间报告”和“身体电量”等功能，为你提供关于睡眠、恢复和身体准备状态的每日洞察。它还内置了卫星连接和 GPS，因此你无需携带手机即可追踪户外运动。此外，它还具备事故检测功能，如果检测到严重跌倒，会自动向紧急联系人发送警报。

**The trade-offs:** Garmin launched Connect+, a $70-per-year subscription with extras like live tracking and access to Garmin's AI-powered Active Intelligence. Former editor Adrienne So doesn't think most people need it, but it's worth noting if you're looking for a completely subscription-free experience. The Vivoactive 6 may also feel like overkill for casual users who only want basic activity and sleep tracking.
**权衡之处：** Garmin 推出了 Connect+，这是一项每年 70 美元的订阅服务，提供实时追踪和 Garmin AI 驱动的“主动智能”等额外功能。前编辑 Adrienne So 认为大多数人并不需要它，但如果你追求完全无需订阅的体验，这一点值得注意。对于只需要基础活动和睡眠追踪的休闲用户来说，Vivoactive 6 可能显得功能过剩。

### Best-Value Fitness Tracker: Google Fitbit Air
### 最佳性价比健身追踪器：Google Fitbit Air

The Google Fitbit Air is a fitness tracker designed to disappear into your routine. It delivers a Whoop-like experience, but at a lower overall cost and with data that's much easier for beginners to digest.
Google Fitbit Air 是一款旨在融入你日常生活的健身追踪器。它提供了类似 Whoop 的体验，但总成本更低，且数据对初学者来说更容易理解。

**Why WIRED recommends:** The Fitbit Air offers the core features most people want from a fitness tracker, including but not limited to activity tracking, cardiovascular insights, and sleep monitoring. Its screenless design makes it an especially good pick for people who find smartwatches distracting or uncomfortable but still want a consistent record of their health. If you want deeper guidance, Google's Health Coach adds AI-powered recommendations, personalized check-ins, and adaptive feedback based on your habits and goals.
**WIRED 推荐理由：** Fitbit Air 提供了大多数人对健身追踪器所期望的核心功能，包括但不限于活动追踪、心血管洞察和睡眠监测。其无屏幕设计对于那些觉得智能手表容易分心或佩戴不适，但仍希望持续记录健康状况的人来说，是一个特别好的选择。如果你需要更深入的指导，Google 的“健康教练”功能可以提供基于 AI 的建议、个性化打卡以及根据你的习惯和目标生成的自适应反馈。

### Best Smart Ring: Oura Ring 5
### 最佳智能戒指：Oura Ring 5

The latest Oura Ring 5 is the best fitness tracker for people who don't want to wear a smartwatch. It has no screen, notifications, or distractions. Instead, it simply collects health data in the background and turns it into digestible insights about your activity, sleep, and recovery.
最新的 Oura Ring 5 是那些不想佩戴智能手表的人的最佳健身追踪器。它没有屏幕、通知或干扰。相反，它只是在后台收集健康数据，并将其转化为关于你的活动、睡眠和恢复的易懂洞察。

**Why WIRED recommends:** The latest Oura is 40 percent smaller than its predecessor, making it one of the comfiest wearables to keep on all day and night. The Ring 5 also features redesigned sensors, stronger signal detection, and consequently, better accuracy, while maintaining its durable titanium design.
**WIRED 推荐理由：** 最新的 Oura 比上一代产品小了 40%，使其成为全天候佩戴最舒适的可穿戴设备之一。Ring 5 还采用了重新设计的传感器、更强的信号检测能力，从而提高了准确性，同时保持了其耐用的钛金属设计。
