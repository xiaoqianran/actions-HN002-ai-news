# My Journey as a Full Stack and AI Developer
# 我作为全栈与人工智能开发者的成长之路

Hello everyone! 👋 I am a Full Stack and AI Developer with a passion for building intelligent applications that solve real-world problems. Over the past several years, I have worked with technologies such as Python, FastAPI, Django, React, TypeScript, PostgreSQL, and cloud platforms to develop scalable web applications and AI-powered solutions.

大家好！👋 我是一名全栈与人工智能开发者，热衷于构建能够解决实际问题的智能应用。在过去的几年里，我利用 Python、FastAPI、Django、React、TypeScript、PostgreSQL 以及各类云平台等技术，开发了可扩展的 Web 应用和人工智能解决方案。

My primary interests in AI include: Large Language Models (LLMs), AI Agents and Automation, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning, Generative AI, and MLOps and AI Deployment.

我在人工智能领域的主要兴趣包括：大语言模型（LLMs）、AI 智能体与自动化、自然语言处理（NLP）、机器学习、生成式 AI，以及 MLOps 和 AI 部署。

Recently, I have also been exploring DevOps practices, including Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD pipelines, and cloud infrastructure. I believe that combining AI development with modern DevOps practices is essential for building reliable and scalable production systems.

最近，我还在探索 DevOps 实践，包括 Docker、Kubernetes、CI/CD 流水线以及云基础设施。我相信，将人工智能开发与现代 DevOps 实践相结合，对于构建可靠且可扩展的生产系统至关重要。

I joined DEV Community to: Share my learning journey, Connect with other developers and AI engineers, Contribute to open-source projects, Discuss AI, software engineering, and cloud technologies, and Learn from the experiences of the community.

我加入 DEV 社区是为了：分享我的学习历程、与开发者和 AI 工程师建立联系、为开源项目做贡献、探讨人工智能、软件工程与云技术，并从社区成员的经验中学习。

I am excited to connect with fellow developers and exchange ideas about AI, engineering, and innovation. What AI technologies or projects are you currently working on? Looking forward to learning from all of you! 🚀

我很高兴能与各位开发者交流，共同探讨人工智能、工程技术与创新。你们目前正在从事哪些 AI 技术或项目？期待向大家学习！🚀
