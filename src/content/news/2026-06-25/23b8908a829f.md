In the United States, your credit score follows you everywhere. It decides if you get a loan, a credit card, or even an apartment. The model behind most of these decisions is FICO. Its logic is simple once you break it down.
在美国，信用评分如影随形。它决定了你是否能获得贷款、信用卡，甚至是租到公寓。大多数此类决策背后的模型是 FICO。一旦拆解开来，其逻辑非常简单。

FICO weighs five things:
FICO 权衡五个维度：
*   **Payment history (35%):** pay your bills on time.
*   **Amounts owed (30%):** keep your credit use below 20%.
*   **Length of history (15%):** the longer, the better.
*   **Credit mix (10%):** use different types of credit.
*   **New credit (10%):** limit new applications.
*   **还款记录 (35%)：** 按时支付账单。
*   **欠款金额 (30%)：** 保持信用额度使用率在 20% 以下。
*   **信用历史长度 (15%)：** 时间越长越好。
*   **信用组合 (10%)：** 使用不同类型的信贷产品。
*   **新信贷 (10%)：** 限制新的申请。

If you pay your credit card bills on time, your score rises. Payment history carries the most weight. These weights produce a score, split into ranges: 300–579 (Poor), 580–669 (Fair), 670–739 (Good), 740–799 (Very Good), 800–850 (Excellent).
如果你按时支付信用卡账单，分数就会上升。还款记录权重最高。这些权重产生一个分数，并分为不同区间：300–579（差）、580–669（一般）、670–739（良好）、740–799（非常好）、800–850（优秀）。

让我们回顾一下上次的发现。我们保留了四个变量：

Like FICO, we give each variable a weight and build a score from 0 to 1000. A high score means low risk. A low score means high risk of default.
像 FICO 一样，我们为每个变量赋予权重，并构建一个 0 到 1000 的分数。高分意味着低风险，低分意味着高违约风险。

### From Model Coefficients to a Score
### 从模型系数到评分

We turn each coefficient into a score. Score for each category of a variable. Take `loan_int_rate` as an example. The score for category $i$ is:
我们将每个系数转化为一个分数。这是变量中每个类别的得分。以 `loan_int_rate` 为例，类别 $i$ 的得分为：

$$SC(j,i) = 1000 \times \frac{ \left|c(j,i)-\alpha_j\right| }{ \sum_{j=1}^{p}\alpha_j }$$

Here, $c(j,i)$ is the coefficient for category $i$ of variable $j$. And $\alpha_j$ is the highest coefficient for variable $j$. For example, for the variable `loan_int_rate`, the highest coefficient is $\alpha_j = 1.357044926979$. This formula gives the score table below.
这里，$c(j,i)$ 是变量 $j$ 中类别 $i$ 的系数，而 $\alpha_j$ 是变量 $j$ 的最高系数。例如，对于变量 `loan_int_rate`，最高系数为 $\alpha_j = 1.357044926979$。该公式得出了下方的评分表。

### A client’s score, step by step
### 客户评分的分步计算

Take a new client. We check which category they fall into for each variable:
以一位新客户为例，我们检查他们每个变量所属的类别：
*   `loan_int_rate` is 10%. Score: 181.72.
*   `loan_percent_income` is 25%. Score: 0.
*   No past default (`cb_person_default_on_file` = N). Score: 59.52.
*   Owns their home (`home_ownership_3` = OWN). Score: 373.94.
*   `loan_int_rate` 为 10%。得分：181.72。
*   `loan_percent_income` 为 25%。得分：0。
*   无过往违约记录 (`cb_person_default_on_file` = N)。得分：59.52。
*   拥有住房 (`home_ownership_3` = OWN)。得分：373.94。

We add these scores to get the final score for the client:
我们将这些分数相加得到客户的最终得分：
$$181.72 + 59.52 + 0 + 373.94 = 615.18$$
We repeat this for every client in our data.
我们对数据中的每位客户重复此操作。

### How Much Each Variable Matters
### 每个变量的重要性

Once we have the score, we ask: which variable drives it most? We measure this on the training data. In plain words, $q_j$ shows how much variable $j$ moves the score. The greater the variation among its different categories, the higher its weight.
一旦有了分数，我们就会问：哪个变量的影响最大？我们在训练数据上进行测量。简单来说，$q_j$ 显示了变量 $j$ 对分数的驱动程度。其不同类别之间的差异越大，权重就越高。

The table below shows each variable’s weight. `loan_percent_income` weighs the most, at 35%. Then `home_ownership_3` at 31%, `loan_int_rate` at 28%, and `cb_person_default_on_file` last. This makes sense. A client who spends more than 20% of their income on loan payments is risky. The fact that this variable drives the score the most is good news: the model picks up the right signal.
下表显示了每个变量的权重。`loan_percent_income` 权重最高，为 35%。其次是 `home_ownership_3`（31%）、`loan_int_rate`（28%），最后是 `cb_person_default_on_file`。这很合理。将收入的 20% 以上用于还贷的客户风险较高。该变量对评分驱动最大是一个好消息：模型捕捉到了正确的信号。

### Does the Score Separate Risk Well?
### 评分能否有效区分风险？

Before we build the risk grid, we check if the score does its job: split defaulters from non-defaulters. We plot the score’s density for each group, split by default, across train, test, and out-of-time data. The further apart the two curves, the better the score works.
在构建风险网格之前，我们检查评分是否发挥了作用：将违约者与非违约者区分开来。我们绘制了训练集、测试集和跨时间数据中，按违约情况分组的评分密度图。两条曲线分得越开，评分效果越好。

What we see: defaults cluster at low scores. Non-defaults cluster at high scores. This is what we want: high score, low risk.
我们看到：违约者集中在低分段，非违约者集中在高分段。这正是我们想要的：高分意味着低风险。

### Building the Risk Grid
### 构建风险网格

现在我们来构建网格。

**Step 1: Default rate by score group**
**第一步：按评分组划分的违约率**
We split the score into 20 equal groups and plot the default rate for each. We start by plotting the default rate against the vingtiles (20 equal-sized segments) of the final score. This chart is the foundation for the grid: it gives a natural starting point for grouping the 20 segments into six risk classes.
我们将分数分为 20 个等份，并绘制每组的违约率。我们首先将违约率与最终得分的 20 等分（vingtiles）进行对比。这张图表是网格的基础：它为将 20 个细分区间归纳为六个风险等级提供了一个自然的起点。

**Step 2: Six risk classes**
**第二步：六个风险等级**
Based on the chart, we group the 20 segments into six classes. These classes must meet three rules:
根据图表，我们将 20 个细分区间归纳为六个等级。这些等级必须满足三条规则：
1. Each class must be uniform in risk. (每个等级的风险必须一致)
2. Each class must differ from the next by at least 30%. (每个等级与相邻等级的风险差异至少为 30%)
3. Each class must hold at least 1% of all clients. (每个等级必须至少包含 1% 的客户)
