# A Three-Phase Factual Recall Circuit in Gemma-2B and Gemma-12B-IT
# Gemma-2B 和 Gemma-12B-IT 中的三阶段事实检索电路

Activation patching reveals how facts are stored, routed, and read out across transformer layers, and why the residual stream does most of the work.
激活修补（Activation patching）揭示了事实如何在 Transformer 层中被存储、路由和读取，以及为什么残差流（residual stream）承担了大部分工作。

How do large language models represent factual knowledge internally? This post presents BizzaroWorld, a mechanistic interpretability study attempting to localize factual recall circuits in the Gemma model family using activation patching across 60 prompt pairs and 20 knowledge categories.
大型语言模型如何在内部表示事实性知识？本文介绍了“BizzaroWorld”，这是一项机械可解释性研究，旨在通过对 60 对提示词和 20 个知识类别进行激活修补，来定位 Gemma 模型系列中的事实检索电路。

The technical work here is greatly influenced by the work done by Prakash et al.¹, who looked at entity tracking within the LLaMa series of models. The goal: localize where factual knowledge lives inside a transformer, and whether that location is consistent across model scale.
这项技术工作深受 Prakash 等人¹研究的影响，他们曾研究过 LLaMa 系列模型中的实体追踪。其目标是：定位事实性知识在 Transformer 内部的存储位置，并确定该位置在不同模型规模下是否一致。

### The Experimental Setup
### 实验设置

First of all, I wanted to know why logit differences through clean and corrupted prompt pairs were the ideal way to get to this problem. Finding facts seemed similar to finding entities as Prakash et al. did, so I focused on Indirect Object Identification (IoI).
首先，我想探究为什么通过干净和损坏的提示词对计算 Logit 差异是解决此问题的理想方法。寻找事实似乎与 Prakash 等人寻找实体的方法类似，因此我专注于间接宾语识别（IoI）。

It seemed to me that, to answer questions like these, LLMs would need to find entities within their representation. So, I looked for more answers about how logit differences and IoI would work in this context, and, for this, the ARENA course² was very helpful.
在我看来，要回答这类问题，大语言模型需要在其表征中找到实体。因此，我寻找了更多关于 Logit 差异和 IoI 在此背景下如何运作的答案，而 ARENA 课程²对此非常有帮助。

I found that logit differences between clean and corrupt prompt pairs provide a clear scalar signal well-suited to measuring the causal effect of patching interventions, so that is what I measured. Thus, I designed a fact battery of 60 clean/corrupt prompt pairs, across 20 different categories of facts.
我发现干净和损坏提示词对之间的 Logit 差异提供了一种清晰的标量信号，非常适合衡量修补干预的因果效应，因此我测量了这一点。为此，我设计了一个包含 60 对干净/损坏提示词的事实库，涵盖了 20 个不同的事实类别。

### Isolating Gemma-2B’s Components
### 隔离 Gemma-2B 的组件

LLMs are huge structures. To know where anything is happening, we need ruthless isolation. TransformerLens³ by Neel Nanda was instrumental to do just that. I wanted to hook onto all different pieces of the puzzle i.e. the residual streams before and after all relevant components (the attention heads and MLP sublayers) across all layers. And that’s precisely what I did.
大语言模型是庞大的结构。为了知道哪里发生了什么，我们需要进行彻底的隔离。Neel Nanda 开发的 TransformerLens³ 在这方面发挥了关键作用。我想要挂载到拼图的所有不同部分，即所有层中所有相关组件（注意力头和 MLP 子层）之前和之后的残差流。而这正是我所做的。

The numbers these experiments generated highlighted a clear finding: there exists a three-phase factual recall circuit within the Gemma model family.
这些实验产生的数据凸显了一个明确的发现：Gemma 模型系列中存在一个三阶段的事实检索电路。

**Phase 1 — Storage (layers 0–14, entity token position):** Facts are encoded as directions in the residual stream at the entity token. The residual stream dominates causally here, contributing 40× more than attention outputs and 18× more than MLP outputs.
**第一阶段 — 存储（第 0–14 层，实体 Token 位置）：** 事实在实体 Token 处的残差流中被编码为方向。残差流在此处起因果主导作用，其贡献比注意力输出高 40 倍，比 MLP 输出高 18 倍。

**Phase 2 — Routing (distributed attention heads):** Signal moves from the entity token position to the final prediction position via attention heads collectively. No single head was solely responsible, although head 2 was disproportionately active.
**第二阶段 — 路由（分布式注意力头）：** 信号通过注意力头集体从实体 Token 位置移动到最终预测位置。没有单个注意力头承担全部责任，尽管第 2 号头表现得异常活跃。

**Phase 3 — Readout (layers 15–17, final token position):** The answer is retrieved, not computed. Late blocks are pass-through i.e. the signal is already encoded and is simply read out.
**第三阶段 — 读取（第 15–17 层，最终 Token 位置）：** 答案是被检索出来的，而不是计算出来的。后期的区块是直通的，即信号已经被编码，只是被简单地读取出来。

### The Three-Phase Circuit at Scale: Gemma-12B-IT
### 规模化后的三阶段电路：Gemma-12B-IT

I was after a generalizable result, so the next step was to see if this pattern held true for the larger model, Gemma-12B-IT too. I was able to replicate the entire suite for the 12B model, including all experiment modes.
我追求的是一个可推广的结果，所以下一步是观察这种模式是否也适用于更大的模型 Gemma-12B-IT。我成功地为 12B 模型复制了整套实验，包括所有实验模式。
