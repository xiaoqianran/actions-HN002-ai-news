# A Geometry-Informed Computer Vision Method for Detecting and Examining Overtaking Vehicles From A Bicycle
# 一种基于几何信息的计算机视觉方法：用于检测与分析自行车超车行为

**Abstract:** Instrumented bicycle studies have produced direct field evidence on vehicle passing behavior, but extracting overtaking events from continuous rear-facing video has remained dependent on manual, frame-by-frame annotation. This bottleneck constrains sample sizes and limits naturalistic cycling safety research.
**摘要：** 搭载传感器的自行车研究已经为车辆超车行为提供了直接的实地证据，但从连续的后视视频中提取超车事件仍依赖于逐帧的人工标注。这一瓶颈限制了样本规模，也制约了自然状态下的骑行安全研究。

We present a geometry-informed computer vision pipeline that automates overtaking event detection from a single bicycle-mounted camera without multi-sensor configurations or explicit camera calibration. The system combines RT-DETR object detection with ByteTrack multi-object tracking through a three-stage geometric validation module enforcing bearing angle trend, apparent size growth, and spatial confirmation criteria derived from perspective projection principles.
我们提出了一种基于几何信息的计算机视觉流水线，能够在无需多传感器配置或显式相机标定的情况下，利用单台自行车车载摄像头自动检测超车事件。该系统将 RT-DETR 目标检测与 ByteTrack 多目标跟踪相结合，通过一个三阶段的几何验证模块，强制执行基于透视投影原理的方位角趋势、表观尺寸增长及空间确认准则。

Validated on 315 manually annotated real-world overtaking events from urban roads in Ann Arbor, Michigan, the pipeline achieved 97.8% recall with zero false positives. The system identified overtaking intentions a mean of 2.44 seconds before vehicle passage, with 84.1% of events exceeding the 1.5-second human reaction time threshold, demonstrating feasibility for active cyclist warning.
该流水线在密歇根州安娜堡市城市道路上采集的 315 个经人工标注的真实超车事件中进行了验证，实现了 97.8% 的召回率，且误报率为零。该系统平均在车辆通过前 2.44 秒识别出超车意图，其中 84.1% 的事件超过了 1.5 秒的人类反应时间阈值，证明了其在主动骑行者预警方面的可行性。

Lateral passing distance measurements from 96 events revealed 33.3% of passes below the 5-foot (152.4 cm) threshold, consistent with non-compliance rates in prior field and self-reported studies. A preliminary calibration-free lateral distance estimation approach using bounding box geometric features achieved mean absolute errors of 13-14 cm under leave-one-out cross-validation, sufficient to distinguish close passes from standard passes for safety categorization.
对 96 个事件的侧向通过距离测量显示，33.3% 的超车距离低于 5 英尺（152.4 厘米）的阈值，这与以往实地研究和自我报告研究中的违规率一致。一种利用边界框几何特征的初步免标定侧向距离估算方法，在留一法交叉验证下实现了 13-14 厘米的平均绝对误差，足以区分近距离超车与标准超车，从而进行安全分类。

By automating event isolation from consumer-grade footage, the system removes the primary annotation bottleneck of instrumented bicycle research and provides a scalable foundation for vehicle-bicycle interaction analysis across larger datasets and diverse urban environments.
通过实现消费级视频素材中事件提取的自动化，该系统消除了搭载传感器自行车研究中的主要标注瓶颈，并为跨越更大规模数据集和多样化城市环境的车辆-自行车交互分析提供了可扩展的基础。
