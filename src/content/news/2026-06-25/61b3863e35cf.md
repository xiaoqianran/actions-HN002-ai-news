# One Ruler: A Same-Hands Re-Evaluation of Bivariate Causal Direction on Tuebingen, with a Parameter-Free Compression Baseline
# 统一标尺：基于 Tuebingen 数据集的双变量因果方向“一手”重评估及无参数压缩基准

**Abstract:** Headline accuracies on the Tuebingen cause-effect pairs are routinely compared across papers even though each is measured under its authors' own protocol -- different pair subsets, weightings, model-selection, and decision rates. We argue this is the wrong comparison and run the right one: a same-hands re-evaluation in which every method is run by us on the identical 102 pairs, with one strict rule -- no tuning and a decision forced on every pair.

**摘要：** 尽管 Tuebingen 因果对数据集上的各项指标是在不同作者各自的协议下测量的（涉及不同的数据子集、加权方式、模型选择及决策率），但各篇论文仍习惯于直接对比其头条准确率。我们认为这种对比方式是错误的，并进行了正确的评估：即由我们亲自对相同的 102 对数据进行“一手”重评估，并遵循一条严格规则——不进行任何调参，且必须对每一对数据做出决策。

As a clean reference point we introduce a deliberately minimal baseline: sorted-conditional compression, which feeds quantized, sorted, first-differenced data to an off-the-shelf compressor (bz2) and has zero fitted parameters. Under the common ruler the ranking differs sharply from the literature. Our baseline reaches 74.7% weighted accuracy (p = 3.7e-7); on the same 100 pairs that SLOPE is evaluated on it scores 76.0%, a 1.2-point gap below the authors' own forced-decision SLOPE (77.2%) that is well inside noise (McNemar p = 0.39).

作为一个清晰的参考点，我们引入了一个刻意简化的基准：排序条件压缩（sorted-conditional compression）。该方法将量化、排序并进行一阶差分后的数据输入到现成的压缩工具（bz2）中，且无需任何拟合参数。在这一统一标尺下，排名与现有文献存在显著差异。我们的基准测试达到了 74.7% 的加权准确率（p = 3.7e-7）；在 SLOPE 方法所评估的相同 100 对数据上，其得分为 76.0%，仅比作者自身强制决策的 SLOPE（77.2%）低 1.2 个百分点，这一差距完全处于噪声范围内（McNemar p = 0.39）。

A faithful re-run of RECI lands at 70.7% -- inside the original authors' reported error bar, not the 77.5% often quoted (which we trace to a mis-copied cell). SLOPE's published 82.4% is a decided-subset figure: scoring the authors' own stored output only on the pairs its significance test chose to answer reproduces 81.7%. Under the common ruler the methods cluster in the low-to-mid 70s and the zero-parameter compressor ties the strongest of them.

对 RECI 的忠实重运行结果为 70.7%，处于原作者报告的误差范围内，而非经常被引用的 77.5%（我们发现该数值源于单元格复制错误）。SLOPE 发表的 82.4% 是基于决策子集得出的数据：仅对其显著性检验所选出的数据对进行评分，复现结果为 81.7%。在统一标尺下，各方法的结果均集中在 70% 到 75% 之间，而我们的零参数压缩器表现与其中最强的方法持平。

We document the mechanisms that inflate published figures (test-set model selection, significance-gated abstention) and contribute two further results: compression score magnitude is a model-free confounding flag (p = 2.8e-68), and a pre-registered falsification test fails in an instructive way that bounds the method's theoretical interpretation. Code, pre-registrations, and per-pair outputs are released.

我们记录了导致已发表数据虚高的机制（如测试集模型选择、基于显著性的弃权机制），并贡献了另外两项研究结果：压缩分数的大小是一个无需模型的混淆标志（p = 2.8e-68），且一项预注册的证伪测试以一种具有启发性的方式失败了，这界定了该方法的理论解释边界。相关代码、预注册信息及每对数据的输出结果均已公开。
