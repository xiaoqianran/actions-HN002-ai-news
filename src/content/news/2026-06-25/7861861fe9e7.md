# HANCLIP: A Family of Hyperbolic Angular Negation Vision Language Models
# HANCLIP：双曲角度否定视觉语言模型系列

Vision-Language Models (VLMs) are typically pre-trained on large-scale image-text datasets to capture semantic correspondences between visual content and natural language. However, they remain surprisingly brittle to negation: models often rely on shallow word co-occurrence and are easily distracted by misleading or irrelevant textual cues, even when their overall retrieval or classification performance is strong.
视觉语言模型（VLM）通常在大规模图文数据集上进行预训练，以捕捉视觉内容与自然语言之间的语义对应关系。然而，它们在处理“否定”逻辑时表现得异常脆弱：模型往往依赖浅层的词汇共现，且容易被误导性或无关的文本线索所干扰，即便其整体的检索或分类性能表现强劲。

Moreover, directly finetuning on negation data can interfere with previously acquired knowledge, causing noticeable degradation on standard vision-language benchmarks. To tackle these issues, this work introduces HANCLIP (Hyperbolic + Angular + Negation), a family of VLMs that explicitly restructures the embedding space to encode "what an image is not" alongside "what it is."
此外，直接在否定数据上进行微调可能会干扰模型先前习得的知识，导致其在标准视觉语言基准测试中出现明显的性能下降。为了解决这些问题，本文引入了 HANCLIP（双曲 + 角度 + 否定），这是一个视觉语言模型系列，它通过显式重构嵌入空间，在编码“图像是什么”的同时，也编码“图像不是什么”。

HANCLIP is trained on a compact set of 20,000 image-text quadruplets and combines a hyperbolic formulation, which models hierarchical semantic relations and asymmetries, with an angular triplet objective that drives systematic separation between negated descriptions and their corresponding positives. This geometry-aware design strengthens negation sensitivity while preserving the global structure of pretrained representations, rather than overwriting them.
HANCLIP 在一个包含 20,000 个图文四元组的精简数据集上进行训练，结合了用于建模层次化语义关系和非对称性的双曲公式，以及一种驱动否定描述与其对应正向描述之间系统性分离的角度三元组目标函数。这种具有几何感知能力的设计在增强否定敏感性的同时，保留了预训练表征的全局结构，而非对其进行覆盖。

Extensive experiments across multiple vision-language tasks show that HANCLIP delivers consistent gains on the negation-focused NegBench benchmark, while maintaining competitive or improved performance on standard classification and image-text retrieval benchmarks. The framework is model-agnostic and can be plugged into CLIP, LongCLIP, SmartCLIP, and HiMo-CLIP without large-scale retraining, demonstrating that a carefully designed geometric objective can substantially extend the reasoning capabilities of existing VLMs using only modest additional data.
在多个视觉语言任务上的广泛实验表明，HANCLIP 在专注于否定逻辑的 NegBench 基准测试中取得了持续的性能提升，同时在标准的分类和图文检索基准测试中保持了具有竞争力的表现，甚至有所改进。该框架具有模型无关性，无需大规模重训练即可插入 CLIP、LongCLIP、SmartCLIP 和 HiMo-CLIP 中，这证明了精心设计的几何目标函数仅需少量额外数据，就能显著扩展现有视觉语言模型的推理能力。
