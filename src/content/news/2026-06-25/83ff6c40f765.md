### Exploring Dualistic Meta-Learning to Enhance Domain Generalization in Open Set Scenarios
### 探索双重元学习以增强开放集场景下的域泛化能力

**Abstract:** Domain generalization learns from multiple source domains to generalize to unseen target domains. However, it often neglects the realistic case of label mismatch between source and target. Open set domain generalization is then proposed to recognize unseen classes in unseen domains.
**摘要：** 域泛化（Domain Generalization）旨在通过多个源域的学习，实现对未见目标域的泛化。然而，现有的研究往往忽略了源域与目标域之间标签不匹配这一现实情况。因此，开放集域泛化（Open Set Domain Generalization）被提出，旨在识别未见域中的未知类别。

A simple approach trains one-vs-all classifiers to separate each class and detect outliers as unknown. Yet, the imbalance between few positive samples and many negative samples skews the decision boundary towards the positive ones, leading the model to over-reject out-of-distribution data, even from known classes in unseen domains.
一种简单的方法是训练“一对多”（one-vs-all）分类器来分离每个类别，并将离群值检测为未知类别。然而，正样本稀少与负样本众多的不平衡问题会使决策边界向正样本倾斜，导致模型过度拒绝分布外（out-of-distribution）数据，甚至错误地拒绝未见域中属于已知类别的数据。

In this paper, we propose a novel meta-learning strategy called dualistic MEta-learning with joint DomaIn-Class matching (MEDIC), which considers implicit gradient matching towards inter-domain and inter-class task splits simultaneously to find optimal boundaries balanced for both domains and classes.
在本文中，我们提出了一种名为“联合域类匹配的双重元学习”（MEDIC）的新型元学习策略。该策略同时考虑了跨域和跨类任务划分的隐式梯度匹配，从而找到在域和类别之间达到平衡的最优决策边界。

Experimental results show that MEDIC not only outperforms prior methods in open set scenarios, but also maintains competitive close set generalization ability.
实验结果表明，MEDIC 不仅在开放集场景下优于现有方法，而且在闭集泛化能力上也保持了极强的竞争力。
