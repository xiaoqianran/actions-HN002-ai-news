Retrieval in an enterprise RAG system is filtering on two structured tables (line_df and toc_df), and every candidate carries an anchor (where the match lands) plus a context (what gets expanded for generation). That mental model is the subject of Article 7A (retrieval as filtering). This article zooms into how the anchors are produced: a three-stage pipeline that runs keyword detection and embeddings in parallel, aggregates the hits to a structural unit, and ends with a single LLM call that ranks the candidates with reasons.
企业 RAG 系统中的检索是对两个结构化表格（line_df 和 toc_df）进行过滤，每个候选结果都携带一个锚点（匹配发生的位置）以及上下文（用于生成扩展的内容）。该思维模型是第 7A 篇的主题。本文将深入探讨锚点是如何生成的：一个三阶段流水线，并行运行关键词检测和嵌入检索，将命中结果聚合到结构单元，最后通过一次 LLM 调用对候选结果进行排序并给出理由。

The user types “How is attention computed?” on the Transformer paper. Six candidate pages match attention. The right one mentions softmax, query, key, d_k together, and sits in the section the TOC calls “Scaled Dot-Product Attention”. Two retrievers — keyword and embedding — both spot the candidate set. Neither alone can tell which page actually answers the question. A third step has to read the candidates side by side, with the section each one sits in, and pick the right one with a reason the auditor can read months later.
用户在 Transformer 论文中输入“注意力是如何计算的？”。有六个候选页面匹配“attention”。正确的页面同时提到了 softmax、query、key 和 d_k，并且位于目录中称为“缩放点积注意力（Scaled Dot-Product Attention）”的章节。两种检索器——关键词和嵌入——都能定位到候选集。但两者都无法单独判断哪一页真正回答了问题。第三步必须将候选页面及其所属章节并排读取，并选出正确的一项，同时给出审计人员在数月后仍能看懂的理由。

The three-stage pipeline that follows runs on three principles:
随后的三阶段流水线遵循三个原则：

1. **Keywords run always.** Keyword detection is free. There’s no scenario where you wouldn’t want its signal. It runs on both line_df and toc_df from the first millisecond.
1. **关键词始终运行。** 关键词检测是免费的。没有任何场景是你不需要它的信号的。它从第一毫秒起就在 line_df 和 toc_df 上运行。

2. **Embeddings run in parallel and optional.** When vocabulary mismatch is expected or the question is conceptual, embeddings catch what keyword misses. With pre-computed indices, the query-time cost is microseconds. Skip them when the keyword signal is already clean.
2. **嵌入并行运行且可选。** 当预期会出现词汇不匹配或问题属于概念性时，嵌入可以捕捉到关键词遗漏的内容。通过预计算索引，查询时间成本仅为微秒级。当关键词信号已经足够清晰时，可以跳过嵌入检索。

3. **One LLM call at the end.** No mid-pipeline LLM “TOC reasoning” step. The arbiter at stage 3 sees the TOC, the keyword hits, the embedding hits, and the structural attachment of each candidate, in a single call. It does the reasoning over the TOC implicitly as part of ranking.
3. **最后进行一次 LLM 调用。** 流水线中间没有 LLM “目录推理”步骤。第三阶段的仲裁者在一次调用中查看目录、关键词命中结果、嵌入命中结果以及每个候选者的结构归属。它在排序过程中隐式地对目录进行推理。

### 1. The anchor-detection pipeline
### 1. 锚点检测流水线

Anchor detection runs in three stages. Stage 1 runs keyword detection and embedding similarity in parallel on line_df and toc_df. Stage 2 aggregates the hits to a structural unit (section via toc_df if available, otherwise page or chunk). Stage 3 hands the aggregated units to a single LLM call that ranks them and writes its reasoning per pick.
锚点检测分三个阶段运行。第一阶段在 line_df 和 toc_df 上并行运行关键词检测和嵌入相似度计算。第二阶段将命中结果聚合到结构单元（如果可用则通过 toc_df 确定章节，否则按页面或块）。第三阶段将聚合后的单元交给一次 LLM 调用，由其进行排序并为每个选择写出理由。

Two design consequences of putting the LLM at the end rather than mid-pipeline:
将 LLM 放在最后而不是流水线中间，有两个设计上的结果：

* **The LLM does the reasoning over the TOC implicitly.** There’s no separate “TOC reasoning” LLM step earlier in the pipeline; the arbiter does that work as part of its single call.
* **LLM 隐式地对目录进行推理。** 流水线早期没有单独的“目录推理” LLM 步骤；仲裁者在其单次调用中完成了这项工作。

* **The LLM resolves subtle title matches.** If the question is about “the premium” but the relevant section is titled “Summary of the contract”, no keyword will match the title. The LLM, given the keyword hits in the body lines + the structural attachment to that section, will still pick it.
* **LLM 解决了微妙的标题匹配问题。** 如果问题是关于“保费（the premium）”，但相关章节标题为“合同摘要（Summary of the contract）”，则没有任何关键词能匹配标题。但 LLM 在获得正文中的关键词命中结果以及该章节的结构归属后，仍然能够选出正确内容。
