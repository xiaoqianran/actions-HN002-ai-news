# REALM: A Unified Red-Teaming Benchmark for Physical-World VLMs
# REALM：面向物理世界视觉语言模型的统一红队测试基准

Vision-language models (VLMs) are increasingly used as perception-reasoning backbones for embodied intelligence in safety-critical physical systems, where perception or reasoning errors can lead to unsafe decisions or actions.
视觉语言模型（VLM）正日益成为具身智能在安全关键型物理系统中的感知与推理骨干，在这些系统中，感知或推理错误可能导致不安全的决策或行为。

Although many red-teaming methods have been developed to probe VLM vulnerabilities, their evaluation remains fragmented across datasets, metrics, and threat models, making direct comparison difficult and obscuring whether observed differences arise from stronger attacks, more vulnerable models, or incompatible evaluation settings.
尽管目前已开发出许多红队测试方法来探测 VLM 的漏洞，但其评估方式在数据集、指标和威胁模型方面仍然碎片化，这使得直接比较变得困难，也掩盖了所观察到的差异究竟是源于更强的攻击、更脆弱的模型，还是不兼容的评估设置。

Existing chatbot-centric red-teaming benchmarks mainly standardize jailbreak and content-safety evaluation, but they do not systematically capture physically grounded functional failures or cover red-teaming methods that target physical-world VLMs. This raises the key challenge of comparing diverse attack methods under a unified protocol while targeting the same scenario-specific failures.
现有的以聊天机器人为中心的红队测试基准主要标准化了越狱和内容安全评估，但它们并未系统性地捕捉基于物理环境的功能性故障，也未涵盖针对物理世界 VLM 的红队测试方法。这提出了一个核心挑战：如何在统一协议下比较不同的攻击方法，同时针对相同的特定场景故障进行评估。

We introduce REALM, to our knowledge the first unified red-teaming benchmark for physical-world VLMs. REALM integrates 12 red-teaming methods, 3 model-agnostic defenses, and 13 VLMs under a practical black-box threat model with shared datasets and metrics.
我们推出了 REALM，据我们所知，这是首个针对物理世界 VLM 的统一红队测试基准。REALM 在实用的黑盒威胁模型下，整合了 12 种红队测试方法、3 种模型无关的防御机制以及 13 个 VLM，并采用了共享的数据集和评估指标。

To align adversarial objectives across attack families, REALM introduces an agentic target-generation pipeline that constructs shared, scenario-specific, and physically grounded attack objectives for each scene, enabling fair comparison of diverse red-teaming methods under aligned adversarial goals.
为了统一不同攻击类别的对抗目标，REALM 引入了一个智能体目标生成流水线，为每个场景构建共享的、特定于场景且基于物理环境的攻击目标，从而能够在对齐的对抗目标下对各种红队测试方法进行公平比较。

Our evaluation shows that text and typographic injection attacks induce the most failures, multimodal co-optimization yields the strongest visual-perturbation transfer, single-pass attacks approach iterative methods at much lower cost, and model scale alone does not confer adversarial robustness.
我们的评估表明，文本和排版注入攻击导致的故障最多，多模态协同优化产生了最强的视觉扰动迁移效果，单次攻击（single-pass attacks）以极低的成本达到了接近迭代方法的效果，且仅靠模型规模的扩大并不能带来对抗鲁棒性。
