### MapleStory Worlds Announces Global Creator Challenge
### 《冒险岛：世界》（MapleStory Worlds）宣布举办全球创作者挑战赛

MapleStory Worlds announced their Global Creator Challenge, which is open for both aspiring and established game developers to design, build, and launch their own World utilizing the MapleStory Worlds platform and each winning team can receive up to $65,000 in total rewards.

《冒险岛：世界》宣布举办“全球创作者挑战赛”，旨在邀请有抱负的开发者及资深游戏开发者，利用该平台设计、构建并发布属于自己的“世界”（World）。每支获胜团队最高可获得总计 65,000 美元的奖励。

Creators are encouraged to utilize MapleStory's vast library of assets to expand upon the game's core design and create their own unique Worlds. A total of 30 Worlds will be selected globally to compete in the final round.

官方鼓励创作者利用《冒险岛》庞大的资源库，在游戏核心设计的基础上进行扩展，打造出独一无二的“世界”。全球范围内将选出 30 个“世界”进入最终决赛。

Entries will be judged based on creative use of the MapleStory IP, level of technical completion, and long-term sustainability for stable live service operations. To learn more, check out their website.

参赛作品的评审标准包括：对《冒险岛》IP 的创意运用、技术完成度，以及长期稳定运营的可持续性。欲了解更多详情，请访问官方网站。
