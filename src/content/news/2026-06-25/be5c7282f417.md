# Mind the Heads: Topological Representation Alignment for Multimodal LLMs
# 关注注意力头：多模态大语言模型的拓扑表征对齐

Representation alignment has emerged as an effective approach to improve Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) by regularizing their internal representations toward those of an external vision encoder.
表征对齐已成为提升多模态大语言模型（MLLM）的一种有效方法，其通过将模型的内部表征向外部视觉编码器的表征进行正则化来实现。

However, existing methods typically align a fixed layer of the language backbone, overlooking the fine-grained structure of Transformer models.
然而，现有方法通常只对语言主干网络中的固定层进行对齐，忽略了 Transformer 模型中细粒度的结构。

In this work, we propose Head-Wise Representation Alignment (HeRA), a method that enforces cross-modal alignment at the level of individual attention heads.
在这项工作中，我们提出了“头级表征对齐”（Head-Wise Representation Alignment, HeRA），这是一种在单个注意力头层面强制执行跨模态对齐的方法。

Our approach is grounded in the Platonic Representation Hypothesis, focusing on preserving the topological structure of representations (i.e., their local neighborhood relationships) across modalities.
我们的方法基于“柏拉图表征假设”（Platonic Representation Hypothesis），重点在于跨模态保持表征的拓扑结构（即它们局部的邻域关系）。

Following the Mutual K-Nearest Neighbor (MKNN) alignment metric, we introduce a contrastive objective that acts as a differentiable proxy for matching local structures.
遵循互近邻（MKNN）对齐指标，我们引入了一个对比目标函数，作为匹配局部结构的可微代理。

HeRA applies this objective during multimodal training to specific attention heads in the LLM, selected by their alignment score according to the MKNN metric.
HeRA 在多模态训练过程中将此目标函数应用于 LLM 中的特定注意力头，这些注意力头是根据 MKNN 指标计算出的对齐分数来选择的。

Counterintuitively, we find that aligning the least aligned heads yields the largest gains.
反直觉的是，我们发现对齐那些对齐程度最差的注意力头反而能带来最大的性能提升。

Extensive evaluations across multiple MLLMs and 18 benchmarks demonstrate that HeRA consistently improves performance on challenging vision-centric tasks and serves as an effective regularizer against visual hallucinations by naturally curbing the over-reliance on linguistic priors.
在多个 MLLM 和 18 个基准测试上的广泛评估表明，HeRA 在具有挑战性的视觉中心任务中持续提升了性能，并通过自然抑制对语言先验的过度依赖，成为对抗视觉幻觉的有效正则化手段。

Our code is publicly released.
我们的代码已公开发布。
