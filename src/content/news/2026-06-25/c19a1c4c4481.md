# Sol Video Inference Engine: Agent-Native Full-Stack Acceleration Framework for Efficient Video Generation
# Sol Video 推理引擎：用于高效视频生成的智能体原生全栈加速框架

Modern video diffusion models achieve higher generation quality through scaling, but this also increases inference cost. Although many acceleration methods have been proposed, a central challenge is that the most effective acceleration strategy is highly instance-specific: a recipe that works well for one combination of model, hardware, and inference configuration often does not transfer to another.

现代视频扩散模型通过规模化实现了更高的生成质量，但这同时也增加了推理成本。尽管目前已经提出了许多加速方法，但一个核心挑战在于：最有效的加速策略往往高度依赖于具体实例——对于某种模型、硬件和推理配置组合行之有效的方案，通常难以直接迁移到其他组合上。

Different models vary in architecture, numerical sensitivity, and attention concentration patterns. Inference settings differ in spatial and temporal resolution and video duration, while hardware platforms differ in memory hierarchy, supported numerical formats, and kernel throughput. These factors create a large tuning space, making manual performance engineering costly.

不同的模型在架构、数值敏感度和注意力集中模式上各不相同。推理设置在空间分辨率、时间分辨率和视频时长上存在差异，而硬件平台在内存层级、支持的数值格式和内核吞吐量上也各异。这些因素共同构成了一个巨大的调优空间，使得手动进行性能工程的成本极其高昂。

We present Sol Video Inference Engine, an agentic, native, training-free acceleration framework for video diffusion models. It organizes five broadly applicable techniques, cache, sparse attention, token pruning, quantization, and kernel fusion, into an agentic acceleration stack for instance-specific optimization.

我们提出了 Sol Video 推理引擎，这是一个面向视频扩散模型的智能体原生、无需训练的加速框架。它将缓存（cache）、稀疏注意力（sparse attention）、Token 剪枝（token pruning）、量化（quantization）和内核融合（kernel fusion）这五种广泛适用的技术组织成一个智能体加速栈，用于实现针对具体实例的优化。

For a concrete deployment target defined by a model, hardware platform, and serving configuration, parallel skill agents optimize the implementation of each technique, an agent integrator composes them into a global acceleration stack, and a human validator provides feedback on generation quality.

针对由模型、硬件平台和服务配置定义的具体部署目标，并行技能智能体（parallel skill agents）会优化每项技术的实现，智能体集成器（agent integrator）将其组合成全局加速栈，并由人工验证者对生成质量提供反馈。

We instantiate this workflow on three video models with different sizes and architectures: 64B Cosmos3-Super, 22B LTX-2.3, and 2B SANA-Video. With little human effort, the full stack achieves more than 2x end-to-end acceleration while maintaining near-lossless VBench quality, demonstrating the effectiveness of the agent framework for video diffusion acceleration.

我们在三种不同规模和架构的视频模型上实例化了这一工作流：64B 的 Cosmos3-Super、22B 的 LTX-2.3 和 2B 的 SANA-Video。在极少的人工干预下，该全栈框架实现了超过 2 倍的端到端加速，同时保持了近乎无损的 VBench 生成质量，证明了该智能体框架在视频扩散加速方面的有效性。
