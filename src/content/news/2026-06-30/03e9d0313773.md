### Learning in Markovian bandits with non-observable states and constrained decision epochs
### 在具有不可观测状态和受限决策时期的马尔可夫赌博机中进行学习

**Abstract:** This paper studies the problem of regret minimization in Markovian bandits with *non-observable states* and possibly *constrained* decision epochs. The focus is restricted to a ``pure'' regret benchmark, that compares the performance of the learning algorithm to the best *pure policy* which -- akin to optimal policies of stochastic bandits -- picks the optimal arm from start to finish without ever switching.

**摘要：** 本文研究了具有*不可观测状态*和可能存在*受限*决策时期的马尔可夫赌博机中的遗憾最小化问题。研究重点限定在“纯”遗憾基准上，该基准将学习算法的性能与最佳*纯策略*进行比较；与随机赌博机的最优策略类似，纯策略从始至终选择最优臂而不进行任何切换。

We introduce a generalization of rested Markovian bandits, *self-degrading Markovian bandits*, for which pure policies are always asymptotically optimal. We show that without prior knowledge on the underlying bandit, the regret of algorithms that switch arms rarely necessarily scales super-logarithmically for every bandit, i.e., as $\omega(\log(T))$, where $T$ is the learning horizon.

我们引入了静止马尔可夫赌博机的一种推广形式，即*自退化马尔可夫赌博机*，对于这种赌博机，纯策略总是渐近最优的。我们证明，在没有关于底层赌博机先验知识的情况下，对于每个赌博机，那些很少切换臂的算法的遗憾必然以超对数规模增长，即以 $\omega(\log(T))$ 的速度增长，其中 $T$ 为学习周期。

Despite the unreachability of the logarithmic regime, we design UCB-NOM, an optimistic algorithm inspired by UCB, of which the regret is nearly logarithmic. Lastly, we show that given prior knowledge on the Markovian bandit in the form of a bound on the bias functions of its arm, a proper instantiation of UCB-NOM achieves $O(\log(T))$ regret. We further show that this prior knowledge allows for a $O(\sqrt{T \log(T)})$ worst-case regret bound for UCB-NOM.

尽管无法达到对数遗憾区间，我们设计了 UCB-NOM，这是一种受 UCB 启发的乐观算法，其遗憾接近对数级。最后，我们证明，如果给定关于马尔可夫赌博机的先验知识（以其臂的偏差函数界限的形式），UCB-NOM 的适当实例化可以实现 $O(\log(T))$ 的遗憾。我们进一步证明，这种先验知识使得 UCB-NOM 能够获得 $O(\sqrt{T \log(T)})$ 的最坏情况遗憾界限。

Notably, our regret bounds do not depend on the number of states of the underlying Markov chains. Our findings suggest that the non-observability of states is a mild inconvenience in self-degrading Markovian bandits.

值得注意的是，我们的遗憾界限不依赖于底层马尔可夫链的状态数量。我们的研究结果表明，在自退化马尔可夫赌博机中，状态的不可观测性只是一个轻微的不便。
