# SemCityLoc: Aerial 6DoF Localization Using Semantic 3D City Models
# SemCityLoc：基于语义三维城市模型的航空六自由度定位

**Abstract:** Aerial 6DoF localization typically relies on precise GNSS signals or radiometrically rich 3D reconstructions, limiting scalability and on-board deployment.
**摘要：** 航空六自由度（6DoF）定位通常依赖于精确的全球导航卫星系统（GNSS）信号或辐射信息丰富的 3D 重建，这限制了其可扩展性和机载部署能力。

We propose SemCityLoc, a semantic-geometric alignment system that reframes aerial pose estimation as structured surface registration between foundation-model-derived visual priors and standardized LoD-compliant 3D city models.
我们提出了 SemCityLoc，这是一个语义-几何对齐系统。它将航空位姿估计重新构建为一种结构化表面配准任务，即在基础模型导出的视觉先验与标准化的 LoD（细节层次）兼容 3D 城市模型之间进行配准。

Instead of matching sparse contours or dense texture, our method aligns semantic surfaces and monocular depth with lightweight semantic 3D building models, increasing pose discriminability in repetitive and occluded urban environments.
与匹配稀疏轮廓或密集纹理不同，我们的方法通过将语义表面和单目深度与轻量级语义 3D 建筑模型对齐，从而提高了在重复且存在遮挡的城市环境中位姿的可辨识度。

To enable accurate evaluation, we introduce SemCityLockeD, the first real-world benchmark combining centimeter-accurate UAV poses with standardized LoD1--LoD3 semantic city models and challenging low-altitude imagery.
为了实现精确评估，我们引入了 SemCityLockeD，这是首个结合了厘米级精度无人机位姿、标准化 LoD1–LoD3 语义城市模型以及具有挑战性的低空影像的真实世界基准测试集。

Experiments demonstrate substantial improvements over existing map-based approaches, improving recall by up to 36% and reducing mean positional error from 9.89m to 2.62m in challenging urban canyons.
实验表明，该方法较现有的基于地图的方案有显著提升，在极具挑战性的城市峡谷环境中，召回率提高了 36%，平均位置误差从 9.89 米降低至 2.62 米。

Our results indicate that semantically structured geometry provides sufficient and scalable constraints for high-precision aerial localization without radiometric scene reconstructions.
我们的研究结果表明，语义结构化几何信息为高精度航空定位提供了充分且可扩展的约束，且无需进行辐射场景重建。
