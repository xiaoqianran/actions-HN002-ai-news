# Not All Relations Rotate Alike: Transformation-Aware Decoupling for Viewpoint-Robust 3D Scene Graph Generation
# 并非所有关系都以相同方式旋转：用于视点鲁棒 3D 场景图生成的变换感知解耦方法

**Abstract:** 3D Scene Graph Generation (3DSGG) represents 3D scenes as structured object-relation-object graphs, providing a compact relational abstraction for spatial understanding. In embodied intelligence settings, the same 3D scene may be observed by agents from viewpoints that differ by yaw rotations. However, current 3DSGG models often fail to produce relation predictions that follow the expected transformation behavior under such viewpoint shifts.

**摘要：** 3D 场景图生成（3DSGG）将 3D 场景表示为结构化的“对象-关系-对象”图，为空间理解提供了紧凑的关系抽象。在具身智能场景中，智能体可能会从存在偏航角（yaw）旋转差异的视点观察同一个 3D 场景。然而，当前的 3DSGG 模型在面对此类视点变换时，往往无法产生符合预期变换行为的关系预测。

This behavior reveals an empirical mismatch related to predicate-level transformation heterogeneity: directional predicates such as left, front, right, and behind should transform with the observation frame, whereas most contact, support, and semantic predicates such as standing on and attached to should remain stable. To reduce this mismatch, we propose Transformation-Aware Decoupling (TAD), a viewpoint-robust 3DSGG framework that decouples relation reasoning according to predicate transformation behavior and is supported by viewpoint-stable object representations.

这种现象揭示了谓词级变换异构性所导致的经验性失配：方向性谓词（如“左”、“前”、“右”、“后”）应随观察坐标系进行变换，而大多数接触、支撑和语义谓词（如“站在……上”、“附着在……上”）则应保持稳定。为了减少这种失配，我们提出了变换感知解耦（Transformation-Aware Decoupling, TAD），这是一个视点鲁棒的 3DSGG 框架。该框架根据谓词的变换行为对关系推理进行解耦，并由视点稳定的对象表示提供支持。

TAD decomposes relation reasoning into two parts: one learns cues that should stay stable across viewpoints, while the other learns directional cues that should change with the observation frame. The two parts are merged for standard multi-label predicate prediction. Transformation-specific descriptors and group-aware auxiliary supervision encourage the two branches to capture complementary relation cues.

TAD 将关系推理分解为两部分：一部分学习在不同视点下应保持稳定的线索，另一部分学习应随观察坐标系改变的方向性线索。这两部分随后被合并，用于标准的多标签谓词预测。变换特定的描述符和组感知辅助监督机制，鼓励这两个分支捕获互补的关系线索。
