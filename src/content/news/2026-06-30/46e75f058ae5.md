### Title: Fine-tuning a multimodal large language model for clinician-grade autism behavioral scoring from short home videos
### 标题：微调多模态大语言模型以实现基于家庭短视频的临床级自闭症行为评分

**Abstract:** Autism spectrum disorder (ASD) affects 1 in 31 US children, yet median age at diagnosis exceeds four years. Artificial intelligence pipelines that provide quantified diagnosis using easy to access observational data (e.g., home videos) could help with earlier diagnosis, and timely delivery of early treatments.
**摘要：** 自闭症谱系障碍（ASD）影响着美国 1/31 的儿童，但其确诊的中位年龄却超过了四岁。利用易于获取的观察数据（如家庭视频）提供量化诊断的人工智能流程，有助于实现更早期的诊断，并及时提供早期治疗。

We fine-tuned Gemini 2.5 Pro on 400 clinician-rated home videos with low-rank adaptation, training only on 30 behavioral features previously validated to produce reliable predictions when passed to various ML models.
我们使用低秩自适应（LoRA）技术，在 400 段经临床医生评估的家庭视频上对 Gemini 2.5 Pro 进行了微调，并仅针对 30 种行为特征进行训练；这些特征此前已被验证在输入各种机器学习模型时能产生可靠的预测结果。

On 99 held-out children (49 ASD, 50 neurotypical), inter-rater reliability with clinicians (per-feature weighted Cohen's kappa) improved by 40% (p<0.001), with 27 of 28 evaluable features improving. As an emergent zero-shot capability, direct ASD diagnosis F1 improved by 53% (p<0.001), matching or exceeding clinician outcomes.
在 99 名留出的测试儿童（49 名 ASD，50 名神经典型发育儿童）中，与临床医生的评分者间信度（基于特征的加权 Cohen's kappa 系数）提高了 40% (p<0.001)，且 28 个可评估特征中有 27 个得到了改善。作为一种涌现的零样本能力，直接 ASD 诊断的 F1 分数提高了 53% (p<0.001)，达到或超过了临床医生的诊断水平。

Classifier-assisted pipelines using fine-tuned LLM-derived behavioral features matched clinician-scored inputs across all tested pathways and achieved 77% accuracy (95% CI: 68-85%) and an AUC of 86% (95% CI: 78-92%). Fine-tuned multimodal LLMs can serve as scalable behavioral feature extractors for use in autism assessment and diagnosis.
使用微调后大语言模型提取的行为特征的分类器辅助流程，在所有测试路径中均与临床医生评分的输入结果相匹配，并达到了 77% 的准确率（95% 置信区间：68-85%）和 86% 的 AUC 值（95% 置信区间：78-92%）。微调后的多模态大语言模型可以作为可扩展的行为特征提取器，用于自闭症的评估与诊断。
