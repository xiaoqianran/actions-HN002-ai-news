### Title: I built a detailed directory of self-hostable projects
### 标题：我建立了一个详细的自托管项目目录

Hello! I launched a new website called selfhost.directory. It's a directory of more than 2000 projects that you can self-host on your machine or server.
大家好！我上线了一个名为 selfhost.directory 的新网站。这是一个包含超过 2000 个项目的目录，你可以将它们自托管在自己的机器或服务器上。

I always wanted one page where you can search basically all relevant projects you can self-host, see their alternatives, features, if it's even still in development and be able to filter out exactly what you want.
我一直想要一个页面，能够搜索到几乎所有相关的自托管项目，查看它们的替代方案、功能特性、是否仍在开发中，并能精确筛选出你想要的内容。

Yeah there are similar pages but they either don't include much information, searching is really bad or contains a lot of junk. That's why I built this website.
确实有一些类似的页面，但它们要么信息量不足，要么搜索体验很差，或者充斥着大量垃圾信息。这就是我建立这个网站的原因。

You can search, filter by category, language or by licence. See realtime updates of either all projects (in selected category) and easily see all important data and features each project provides.
你可以通过类别、语言或许可证进行搜索和筛选。查看所有项目（在选定类别中）的实时更新，并轻松获取每个项目提供的所有重要数据和功能。

Wish you lucky pick and thank you for reading this and for any feedback!
祝你能找到心仪的项目，感谢你的阅读，也欢迎提供任何反馈！
