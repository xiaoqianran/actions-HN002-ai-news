# Gemini’s personalized AI image generation is now free for US users
# Gemini 的个性化 AI 图像生成功能现已向美国用户免费开放

Google announced on Monday that the Gemini app is now offering its personalized Nano Banana-powered image generation feature to a broader audience. Starting today, all eligible users in the U.S. can access the feature for free, a service that was previously only available to Plus, Pro, and Ultra subscribers.

Google 周一宣布，Gemini 应用现已向更广泛的用户群体提供由 Nano Banana 驱动的个性化图像生成功能。从今天开始，所有符合条件的美国用户均可免费使用该功能，而此前该服务仅限于 Plus、Pro 和 Ultra 订阅用户。

Google initially announced that Gemini’s Personal Intelligence feature would get Nano Banana-powered image generation back in April, allowing users to create images that reflect their unique interests. This means that images can be generated based on Gemini’s understanding of your likes and preferences without you having to specify them in your prompt. Gemini utilizes data from your Google account connections — such as Gmail, Google Photos, YouTube, and Search — to achieve this.

Google 最初于今年 4 月宣布，Gemini 的“个人智能”（Personal Intelligence）功能将引入由 Nano Banana 驱动的图像生成技术，允许用户创建反映其独特兴趣的图像。这意味着 Gemini 可以基于对你喜好和偏好的理解来生成图像，而无需你在提示词中明确说明。Gemini 通过调用你 Google 账户关联的数据（如 Gmail、Google 相册、YouTube 和搜索记录）来实现这一功能。

For example, instead of saying, “Create an illustration of me and my favorite things, such as coffee and baking,” you can simply request, “Create an illustration of me and my favorite things.” Gemini can also pull actual images of you from Google Photos, so you don’t need to manually upload photos.

例如，你无需说“创建一个关于我和我最喜欢的事物（如咖啡和烘焙）的插图”，只需简单地要求“创建一个关于我和我最喜欢的事物的插图”即可。Gemini 还可以从 Google 相册中提取你的真实照片，因此你无需手动上传图片。

Google initially rolled out the Personal Intelligence feature earlier this year, making it widely available to all U.S. users in March. The company recently expanded this functionality to users in India and Japan. Personal Intelligence is an opt-in feature, allowing you to decide which apps Gemini can access. Once enabled, it is set as the default for every prompt, but you can disable it using a new toggle in the Tools menu.

Google 于今年早些时候首次推出了“个人智能”功能，并于 3 月向所有美国用户全面开放。近期，该公司已将此功能扩展至印度和日本用户。个人智能是一项可选功能，允许你决定 Gemini 可以访问哪些应用。一旦启用，它将默认为每个提示词开启，但你也可以通过“工具”菜单中的新开关将其关闭。

Additionally, last month, Google announced several upcoming updates for the Gemini app, including a new “Daily Brief” feature, a revamped interface, access to AI video model Gemini Omni, and a personal AI agent named Gemini Spark. Notably, Google’s AI chatbot Gemini surpassed 750 million monthly active users (MAUs) earlier this year, reinforcing its position as a major player in the AI space.

此外，Google 上个月还宣布了 Gemini 应用即将推出的几项更新，包括全新的“每日简报”（Daily Brief）功能、改版的界面、对 AI 视频模型 Gemini Omni 的访问权限，以及名为 Gemini Spark 的个人 AI 代理。值得注意的是，Google 的 AI 聊天机器人 Gemini 在今年早些时候月活跃用户数（MAU）已突破 7.5 亿，进一步巩固了其在 AI 领域的领先地位。
