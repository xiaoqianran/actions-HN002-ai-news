**Small Models, Big Moment**
**小型模型，重大时刻**

For most of the last three years in AI, the reflex was simple. You had an AI task, so you called GPT or Claude or Gemini. But in 2026 that reflex is getting expensive, and to be honest often unnecessary. A model you run on your own laptop can now handle a surprising share of real work: classification, extraction, summarization, code completion, document Q&A. These are the production versions of those tasks, the ones teams and developers ship.
在过去三年里，大多数人在面对人工智能任务时的反应很简单：直接调用 GPT、Claude 或 Gemini。但到了 2026 年，这种习惯变得越来越昂贵，坦率地说，往往也是不必要的。现在，在你自己的笔记本电脑上运行的模型就能处理相当大比例的实际工作：分类、提取、摘要、代码补全和文档问答。这些正是团队和开发者们在生产环境中实际交付的任务。

Five things shifted at roughly the same time between late 2025 and mid 2026: hardware, open-source tooling, token costs, regulation, and a cultural pull toward owning your own tools. Any one of them would be worth a paragraph. Together, they moved small language models (SLMs) from a hobbyist curiosity to the sensible place to start a project. I’ll show you what changed, what you give up when you go small, when an SLM is the right call, and how to run one tonight. There’s also code you can copy.
在 2025 年末到 2026 年中期之间，大约有五件事同时发生了变化：硬件、开源工具、Token 成本、监管，以及一种倾向于掌控自有工具的文化趋势。其中任何一点都值得大书特书。而它们共同作用，使小型语言模型（SLM）从爱好者的玩物变成了项目启动时的明智之选。我将向你展示发生了什么变化、选择小型模型会失去什么、何时选择 SLM 是正确的决定，以及今晚如何运行一个模型。文中还附有你可以直接复制的代码。

One definition first, because “small” can be misinterpreted. I’ll use SLM to mean models of roughly 1B to 14B parameters. For mixture-of-experts models I count active parameters, so Qwen3-30B-A3B (3B active) counts. By “frontier model” I mean GPT-5.x, Claude Opus 4.x, Gemini 3.x, Grok 4. Treat the boundary as fuzzy.
首先明确一个定义，因为“小型”一词容易产生误解。我所指的 SLM 是参数量大约在 10 亿到 140 亿之间的模型。对于混合专家模型（MoE），我计算的是活跃参数，因此 Qwen3-30B-A3B（30 亿活跃参数）也算在内。我所说的“前沿模型”是指 GPT-5.x、Claude Opus 4.x、Gemini 3.x 和 Grok 4。请将这个界限视为模糊的。

**1. Why Small Models, and Why Now**
**1. 为什么选择小型模型，为什么是现在？**

This whole talk about small language models had a boom when NVIDIA Research released a report. Their June 2025 paper, *Small Language Models are the Future of Agentic AI* (Belcak et al.), argued that the narrow, repetitive sub-tasks inside most agent pipelines don’t need a frontier model, and estimated that 40 to 70% of enterprise AI tasks can run on sub-10B models. The field was already drifting there and the paper named it. Let’s explore what made this change possible.
关于小型语言模型的讨论在 NVIDIA Research 发布一份报告后迎来了爆发。他们 2025 年 6 月发表的论文《小型语言模型是智能体 AI 的未来》（Belcak 等人）指出，大多数智能体工作流中狭窄、重复的子任务并不需要前沿模型，并估计 40% 到 70% 的企业 AI 任务可以在 100 亿参数以下的模型上运行。该领域已经在向这个方向发展，而这篇论文明确了这一点。让我们探讨一下是什么促成了这种变化。

**Five Reasons Why**
**五个原因**

**Capability (the muscles)**
**能力（肌肉）**
This is the part people underestimate: a 3B to 14B model today matches what a 70B model did 12 to 18 months ago on targeted tasks. Some examples: Microsoft’s Phi-4 (14B) scores 84.8 on MMLU and 82.6 on HumanEval, beating Llama-3.3-70B’s 78.9 on code. Phi-4-reasoning-plus (14B) hits 77.7% on AIME 2025, matching the full 671B DeepSeek-R1 on that benchmark. These models are designed differently from large ones: trained on curated synthetic data, distilled from bigger teachers, quantized from day one rather than compressed after the fact.
这是人们低估的部分：如今一个 30 亿到 140 亿参数的模型，在特定任务上的表现已经能媲美 12 到 18 个月前 700 亿参数的模型。举几个例子：微软的 Phi-4 (14B) 在 MMLU 上得分为 84.8，在 HumanEval 上得分为 82.6，在代码能力上击败了 Llama-3.3-70B 的 78.9 分。Phi-4-reasoning-plus (14B) 在 AIME 2025 上达到了 77.7%，在该基准测试中与完整的 6710 亿参数 DeepSeek-R1 持平。这些模型的设计与大型模型不同：它们在精选的合成数据上进行训练，从更大的教师模型中蒸馏而来，并且从第一天起就进行了量化，而不是事后压缩。

**Hardware (the bones)**
**硬件（骨骼）**
The hardware caught up at the same time. Apple’s M5 (October 2025) reached 153 GB/s memory bandwidth, and a Mac Studio with M3 Ultra (800+ GB/s, up to 512 GB unified memory) can run a quantized DeepSeek 671B locally. NVIDIA’s DGX Spark shipped in October 2025 at $3,999 with 128 GB unified memory and runs models up to 200B parameters on a single unit. AMD’s Framework Desktop does much of the same for $1,999. Even a 2026 flagship phone on a Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 decodes at 100+ tokens per second.
硬件也在同一时间跟上了步伐。苹果的 M5（2025 年 10 月）达到了 153 GB/s 的内存带宽，而配备 M3 Ultra 的 Mac Studio（800+ GB/s，高达 512 GB 统一内存）可以在本地运行量化版的 DeepSeek 671B。NVIDIA 的 DGX Spark 于 2025 年 10 月以 3,999 美元的价格发售，拥有 128 GB 统一内存，单机即可运行高达 2000 亿参数的模型。AMD 的 Framework Desktop 以 1,999 美元的价格实现了类似的功能。即使是搭载骁龙 8 Elite Gen 5 的 2026 年旗舰手机，解码速度也能达到每秒 100 个 Token 以上。

**Tools (the hands)**
**工具（双手）**
Open-source tooling matured around it. Hugging Face crossed 2 million public models. Ollama became the default local backend, and LM Studio went free for commercial use in July 2025. The punchline statistic comes from Hugging Face’s 2026 State of Open Source report: 92.5% of model downloads are for models under 1B parameters. Open-weight usage is overwhelmingly small.
开源工具也随之成熟。Hugging Face 上的公共模型数量突破了 200 万个。Ollama 成为了默认的本地后端，LM Studio 在 2025 年 7 月对商业用途免费开放。最关键的数据来自 Hugging Face 的《2026 年开源状况报告》：92.5% 的模型下载量集中在 10 亿参数以下的模型。开源权重的应用绝大多数都是小型模型。

**Cost (the appetite)**
**成本（胃口）**
Then there’s cost, which got more complicated rather than simpler. Headline API prices fell roughly 80% from early 2025 to early 2026. But reasoning tokens are billed as output and run 3 to 5 times the visible response length, and agent conversations grow quadratically with each turn. IntuitionLabs documented one Claude conversation where a 14-token question cost $0.0018 at turn 1 and $2.41 by turn 260, a 1,339x increase from accumulated history alone. With all this, what companies end up doing is tiered routing: about 70% local SLM, 20% mid-tier API, and 10% frontier API.
然后是成本问题，它变得更加复杂而非简单。从 2025 年初到 2026 年初，API 的标价下降了约 80%。但推理 Token 是按输出计费的，其消耗量通常是可见响应长度的 3 到 5 倍，而且智能体对话会随着每一轮交互呈二次方增长。IntuitionLabs 记录了一次 Claude 对话，一个 14 个 Token 的问题在第 1 轮花费 0.0018 美元，到第 260 轮时花费 2.41 美元，仅累积的历史记录就导致成本增加了 1339 倍。鉴于此，企业最终采取的做法是分层路由：约 70% 使用本地 SLM，20% 使用中端 API，10% 使用前沿 API。

**Regulation (the leash)**
**监管（缰绳）**
Regulation pushes in the same direction. Full enforcement of the EU AI Act’s high-risk obligations begins August 2, 2026, less than two months from this writing. HIPAA never adapted to LLMs, and healthcare data breaches average $4.44M, the highest of any industry. The May 2025 court order in NYT v. OpenAI, requiring indefinite retention of even deleted ChatGPT chats, made a lot of enterprises nervous about sending data to an API at all.
监管也在推动这一趋势。欧盟《人工智能法案》中关于高风险义务的全面执行将于 2026 年 8 月 2 日开始，距离本文撰写不到两个月。HIPAA（健康保险流通与责任法案）从未针对大语言模型进行调整，而医疗保健行业的数据泄露平均损失高达 444 万美元，是所有行业中最高的。2025 年 5 月在《纽约时报》诉 OpenAI 一案中的法院裁决，要求无限期保留即使是已删除的 ChatGPT 对话记录，这让许多企业对将数据发送到 API 感到非常不安。

**2. What You Give Up When You Choose SLMs**
**2. 选择 SLM 时你会失去什么**

Going small is a trade, so let’s be clear about the losing side first. Frontier models still win the hard problems. As of mid 2026: GPT-5.4 scores 100% on AIME 2025 with no tools. Claude Opus 4.6 hits 80.8% on SWE-bench Verified. Gemini 3.1 Pro reaches 94.3% on GPQA Diamond. The best 30B coder SLMs top out around 50% on SWE-bench Verified. That gap is large, and it’s specific.
选择小型模型是一种权衡，所以我们先明确一下失去的部分。前沿模型在解决难题上依然胜出。截至 2026 年中期：GPT-5.4 在不使用工具的情况下在 AIME 2025 上获得了 100% 的分数。Claude Opus 4.6 在 SWE-bench Verified 上达到了 80.8%。Gemini 3.1 Pro 在 GPQA Diamond 上达到了 94.3%。而最强的 300 亿参数代码类 SLM 在 SWE-bench Verified 上的表现最高仅在 50% 左右。这个差距很大，而且非常具体。

**SLM 的落后之处（盲点）**

它们在五个方面持续落后：

1. 深度多步抽象推理
