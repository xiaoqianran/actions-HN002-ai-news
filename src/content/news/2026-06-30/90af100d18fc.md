# RANSAC Scoring Done Right

**Abstract:** The most widely used RANSAC variants score candidate models by counting inliers or summing per-point scores that saturate beyond a residual threshold. Every such score requires a user-supplied parameter that is a function of the inlier scale, which must itself be estimated from contaminated data.

**摘要：** 目前最广泛使用的 RANSAC 变体通过计算内点（inliers）数量或对超过残差阈值后即饱和的逐点得分进行求和，来对候选模型进行评分。所有此类评分方法都需要用户提供一个参数，该参数是内点尺度（inlier scale）的函数，而内点尺度本身必须从受污染的数据中进行估计。

We remove this dependence by reversing the usual order of inference: rather than estimating the scale and then scoring against it, we marginalize the inlier scale analytically in closed form under a conjugate Inverse-Gamma prior for a fixed inlier partition, then optimize over partitions.

我们通过颠倒通常的推理顺序消除了这种依赖：我们不再先估计尺度再进行评分，而是在固定内点划分的情况下，利用共轭逆伽马先验（conjugate Inverse-Gamma prior），以解析闭式形式对内点尺度进行边缘化处理，然后对划分进行优化。

A single closed-form expression spans the non-informative Jeffreys limit and informative empirical-Bayes priors, so the same score adapts across data-rich and data-scarce regimes without any change to the algorithm. The proposed RANSAC score is the first in which the inlier scale is genuinely absent from the formula. The score admits O(N log N) computation via sort-and-sweep.

单一的闭式表达式涵盖了无信息的 Jeffreys 极限和有信息的经验贝叶斯先验，因此相同的评分方法无需更改算法即可适应数据充足和数据稀缺的环境。所提出的 RANSAC 评分是第一个真正从公式中消除了内点尺度的评分方法。该评分通过“排序与扫描”（sort-and-sweep）算法实现了 O(N log N) 的计算复杂度。

On a benchmark of nearly 70,000 image pairs spanning different two-view estimation problems and both engineered and learned feature pipelines, the proposed score exceeds the state of the art (RANSAC, MSAC, GaU, MAGSAC): it stays nearly flat under threshold miscalibration where baselines degrade, reaches near-optimal accuracy from as few as two validation pairs where baselines need on the order of 100 times more, and tightens its prior regularization as validation data grows scarce.

在涵盖不同双视图估计问题以及工程化和学习型特征流水线的近 70,000 对图像基准测试中，该评分方法超越了当前的最先进技术（RANSAC、MSAC、GaU、MAGSAC）：在阈值校准错误导致基准方法性能下降时，它仍能保持近乎平稳的表现；在仅需两对验证数据时即可达到近乎最优的准确度，而基准方法则需要约 100 倍的数据量；并且随着验证数据的减少，它会自动收紧先验正则化。
