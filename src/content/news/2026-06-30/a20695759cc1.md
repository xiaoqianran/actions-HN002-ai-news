# TruEye: Fine-Grained Detection of AI-Generated Human Subjects in Images
# TruEye：针对图像中 AI 生成人物主体的细粒度检测

**Abstract:** AI generated images are proliferating across the Internet. While some are used for entertainment, others are weaponized for fraud and social engineering attacks on social media users. Existing detectors overfit to generators seen during training, treat detection as opaque binary classification, or rely on costly Large Language Models (LLMs) to explain their outputs.

**摘要：** AI 生成的图像正在互联网上迅速扩散。虽然其中一些被用于娱乐，但另一些则被武器化，用于对社交媒体用户进行欺诈和社会工程攻击。现有的检测器往往过度拟合训练期间见过的生成器，将检测视为不透明的二元分类，或者依赖昂贵的大型语言模型（LLM）来解释其输出结果。

In this paper, we present TruEye, a novel model for fine grained detection and localization of AI manipulated or AI generated humans and scenes. Unlike conventional detectors that assign a single authenticity label, TruEye is the first to distinguish among five compositional categories of synthetic content, including the most challenging case in which a real human is composited into a real scene where they were never physically present.

在本文中，我们提出了 TruEye，这是一种用于对 AI 操纵或 AI 生成的人物及场景进行细粒度检测和定位的新型模型。与分配单一真实性标签的传统检测器不同，TruEye 是首个能够区分五种合成内容构成类别的模型，其中包括最棘手的情况：将真实人物合成到他们从未实际出现过的真实场景中。

At its core is a mask conditioned dual stream transformer that separates human and scene tokens while preserving patch level spatial correspondence. Specialized reasoning within each stream and region gated cross attention enforce semantic coherence between subject and background, while token level supervision and global compositional classification yield robust, interpretable predictions without invoking an LLM.

其核心是一个掩码条件双流 Transformer，它在保留补丁级空间对应关系的同时，将人物和场景标记（tokens）分离开来。每个流内的专门推理和区域门控交叉注意力机制增强了主体与背景之间的语义连贯性，而标记级监督和全局构成分类则在无需调用 LLM 的情况下，提供了稳健且可解释的预测结果。

By restricting intra stream attention to semantically coherent tokens, TruEye also runs over $100\times$ faster than LLM based competitors. Experiments on 6 datasets and our newly curated FineSyn dataset, show that TruEye surpasses state of the art detectors with higher accuracy, faster inference, and stronger generalization to unseen AI generated or manipulated images.

通过将流内注意力限制在语义连贯的标记上，TruEye 的运行速度比基于 LLM 的竞争对手快 100 倍以上。在 6 个数据集以及我们新整理的 FineSyn 数据集上的实验表明，TruEye 以更高的准确性、更快的推理速度以及对未见过的 AI 生成或操纵图像更强的泛化能力，超越了现有的最先进检测器。
