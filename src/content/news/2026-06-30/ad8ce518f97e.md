# The Curse of Multiple Mediators: Hidden Interaction Effects in Activation Patching
# 多重中介的诅咒：激活修补中的隐藏交互效应

**Abstract:** Activation patching is the primary tool in mechanistic interpretability. It attributes causal responsibility for a model behavior to each of its individual components by estimating its natural indirect effect (NIE). Re-deriving the activation patching estimand from causal mediation analysis, we find that the NIE does not solely capture the causal effect through the specific component. It also contains interaction effects (INT) that measure how much the component's causal effect itself depends on the state of other components in the model.

**摘要：** 激活修补（Activation patching）是机械可解释性研究中的主要工具。它通过估计自然间接效应（NIE），将模型行为的因果责任归因于其各个独立组件。通过从因果中介分析的角度重新推导激活修补的估计量，我们发现 NIE 并不能完全捕捉通过特定组件产生的因果效应。它还包含了交互效应（INT），用于衡量该组件的因果效应在多大程度上依赖于模型中其他组件的状态。

A natural response may be to try to eliminate INT by adjusting the estimator or unit of analysis, but each of these potential remedies has predictable failure modes. We demonstrate these failure modes in the GPT-2 IOI circuit; components whose causal importance is conditional on the state of other components are either invisible or artificially inflated, and INT variance explains the previously documented instability of faithfulness scores.

一个自然的反应可能是尝试通过调整估计量或分析单元来消除 INT，但这些潜在的补救措施都有可预见的失效模式。我们在 GPT-2 的 IOI 电路中演示了这些失效模式；那些因果重要性取决于其他组件状态的组件，要么变得不可见，要么被人工夸大，而 INT 的方差解释了此前文献中记录的忠实度分数（faithfulness scores）不稳定的现象。

We prove that INT scales with the distance between clean and patched component activations, is negligible when the model is locally affine, and decomposes combinatorially into pairwise and higher-order group interactions. Despite its inevitability, INT is not a nuisance to be eliminated, but rather a diagnostic for interpretability studies. Its individual and group-level magnitude and sign signal when causal conclusions are prompt-dependent, and when greedy NIE-based component ranking will miss mechanisms only discoverable through combinatorial search.

我们证明了 INT 随“干净”激活与“修补”激活之间的距离而缩放，在模型局部仿射时可忽略不计，并能组合分解为成对及更高阶的群体交互。尽管 INT 不可避免，但它并非需要消除的干扰，而是可解释性研究的一种诊断指标。其个体和群体层面的大小与符号，能够揭示因果结论何时依赖于提示词（prompt），以及基于贪婪 NIE 的组件排序何时会遗漏那些只能通过组合搜索才能发现的机制。
