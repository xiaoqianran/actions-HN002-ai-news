# Why Most People Never Discover Their Real Potential
# 为什么大多数人从未发掘出真正的潜能

Every year, millions of people choose careers based on expectations, salaries, or what seems like the "safe" option. Yet many eventually realize they're working in roles that don't align with who they truly are.
每年，有数百万人基于外界期望、薪资水平或所谓的“稳妥”选择来规划职业生涯。然而，许多人最终意识到，他们所从事的工作与真实的自我并不契合。

The problem isn't a lack of intelligence or ambition—it's a lack of self-understanding. Traditional personality tests have helped many people, but they often produce static reports based on a fixed set of questions. Real people are far more complex. Our strengths evolve with experience, and understanding them requires more than a multiple-choice quiz.
问题的根源不在于缺乏智慧或抱负，而在于缺乏自我认知。传统的性格测试虽然帮助了许多人，但它们往往基于一套固定的问题生成静态报告。而真实的人远比这复杂得多。我们的优势会随着经验而演变，理解这些优势需要的不仅仅是一份选择题问卷。

Recent advances in artificial intelligence open the door to a different approach. Instead of generating a one-time report, AI can have conversations, ask follow-up questions, recognize patterns, and provide personalized insights that adapt over time.
人工智能的最新进展为我们开启了一种全新的路径。AI 不再只是生成一份一次性的报告，而是能够进行对话、提出后续问题、识别行为模式，并提供随时间不断调整的个性化见解。

This idea is what inspired me to start building PotenAI. The goal isn't to replace human judgment or career coaches. The goal is to give people another tool—one that helps them better understand their strengths, hidden talents, career fit, and long-term potential.
正是这一理念激励我开始构建 PotenAI。我的目标并非取代人类的判断或职业教练，而是为人们提供另一种工具——一种能帮助他们更好地了解自身优势、潜在天赋、职业匹配度以及长期潜力的工具。

We're currently building the first version, and there's still a long journey ahead. But I genuinely believe AI can make self-discovery more accessible and more personal than ever before.
我们目前正在开发第一个版本，前路依然漫长。但我真心相信，AI 能让自我探索变得比以往任何时候都更易于实现，也更具个性化。

I'm curious to hear your thoughts. Do you think AI can help people understand themselves better than traditional assessments?
我很想听听你们的想法。你认为 AI 能比传统评估方法更好地帮助人们认识自我吗？
