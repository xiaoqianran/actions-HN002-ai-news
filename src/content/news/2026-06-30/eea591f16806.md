# Operator Learning for Cubic Nonlinear Schrödinger Equation on Periodic Domains
# 周期域上三次非线性薛定谔方程的算子学习

**Abstract:** We consider the cubic nonlinear Schrödinger (NLS) equation on two-dimensional flat tori with varying aspect ratios. In this formulation, the choice of aspect ratio governs the Fourier resonance structure, so rational and irrational geometries can exhibit different high-frequency cascade behaviors. 

**摘要：** 我们研究了具有不同长宽比的二维平环面上的三次非线性薛定谔（NLS）方程。在此公式中，长宽比的选择决定了傅里叶共振结构，因此有理几何和无理几何可能表现出不同的高频级联行为。

We present a geometry-conditioned Fourier neural operator (FNO) for the cubic defocusing NLS equation, where the input consists of the real and imaginary parts of the solution together with the aspect-ratio parameter \(\omega^2\). The model is trained to approximate the one-step solution operator and is evaluated on unseen trajectories generated from random-phase initial data using Fourier pseudospectral method. 

我们提出了一种针对三次散焦 NLS 方程的几何条件傅里叶神经算子（FNO），其输入由解的实部和虚部以及长宽比参数 \(\omega^2\) 组成。该模型经过训练以逼近单步解算子，并在使用傅里叶伪谱法生成的随机相位初始数据的未见轨迹上进行评估。

Our numerical experiments show that the learned operator captures the main solution dynamics on both tori and reproduces the distinct Sobolev norm behavior of the two geometries, with stronger \(H^2\)-growth on the rational torus and more constrained behavior on the irrational torus, consistent with the findings of \cite{hrabski2021energy}. 

我们的数值实验表明，所学习的算子捕捉到了两种环面上的主要解动力学，并重现了两种几何结构中不同的 Sobolev 范数行为：有理环面上的 \(H^2\) 增长更强，而无理环面上的行为则受到更多约束，这与 \cite{hrabski2021energy} 的研究结果一致。

We perform ablation studies to examine the roles of retained Fourier modes, activation functions, Fourier-layer depth, and explicit geometry conditioning. The results indicate that including \(\omega^2\) improves long-time predictive accuracy, especially for the rational geometry, and supports the use of geometry-aware neural operators for learning spectral-transfer phenomena in nonlinear dispersive partial differential equations.

我们进行了消融研究，以检验保留的傅里叶模态、激活函数、傅里叶层深度和显式几何条件的作用。结果表明，包含 \(\omega^2\) 可以提高长期预测精度，特别是在有理几何结构中，并支持使用几何感知神经算子来学习非线性色散偏微分方程中的谱传递现象。
