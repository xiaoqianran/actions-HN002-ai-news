By the time the four bricks of a single-document RAG are built, the assembly is settled. Parsing produces relational tables. Question parsing produces a typed ParsedQuestion. Retrieval produces a filtered subset of lines, plus an audit of how it picked them. Generation produces a Pydantic answer with cited evidence. The whole thing converges on one LLM call, with a fixed system prompt and a user content assembled from upstream pieces.
当单文档 RAG 的四个模块构建完成后，组装方式也就确定了。解析产生关系表；问题解析产生类型化的 `ParsedQuestion`；检索产生过滤后的行子集，以及关于如何筛选这些行的审计记录；生成产生带有引用证据的 Pydantic 格式答案。整个过程汇聚于一次 LLM 调用，配合固定的系统提示词（System Prompt）以及由上游片段组装而成的用户内容。

In June 2025 Tobi Lütke tweeted that “prompt engineering” was the wrong frame, and proposed “context engineering” instead: “the art of providing all the context for the task to be plausibly solvable by the LLM.” Andrej Karpathy endorsed it a week later as “the delicate art and science of filling the context window with just the right information for the next step.” Within months the term was on the cover of an O’Reilly book and structured into a taxonomy by LangChain.
2025 年 6 月，Tobi Lütke 在推特上表示“提示词工程（prompt engineering）”这一框架并不准确，并提出了“上下文工程（context engineering）”的概念：“即提供所有上下文，使任务能够被 LLM 合理解决的艺术。”一周后，Andrej Karpathy 对此表示赞同，称其为“将恰当的信息填入上下文窗口以进行下一步操作的精妙艺术与科学”。几个月内，该术语便出现在了 O'Reilly 书籍的封面上，并被 LangChain 结构化为一套分类体系。

What follows reads the single-document RAG pipeline through that lens. Each brick emits typed pieces; the assembly stage threads them into the LLM call; the system prompt stays fixed for caching. Naming the practice does not change the architecture. It changes what to call it when an auditor asks how the system works, and it tells the reader that the architecture is the one production teams converged on in 2025.
接下来的内容将通过这一视角解读单文档 RAG 流水线。每个模块输出类型化片段；组装阶段将它们串联进 LLM 调用；系统提示词保持固定以便缓存。命名这一实践并不会改变架构本身。它改变的是当审计人员询问系统如何工作时我们对它的称呼，同时也向读者表明，这就是 2025 年生产团队所达成共识的架构。

### 1. The name, and what it covers
### 1. 名称及其涵盖范围

Prompt engineering used to mean two related things. Tuning the wording of one prompt to coax better behaviour, and writing example shots so the model knew what good output looked like. Both are narrow. They concern one block of text sent to one call. Context engineering covers everything that lands in the model’s context window for one call: The system prompt, the retrieved documents, conversation history, tool definitions, memory, metadata, and user input.
提示词工程过去指代两件相关的事情：调整提示词措辞以诱导更好的行为，以及编写示例（few-shot）让模型了解什么样的输出是好的。这两者都很狭隘，仅关注发送给单次调用的一段文本。而上下文工程涵盖了单次调用中进入模型上下文窗口的所有内容：系统提示词、检索到的文档、对话历史、工具定义、记忆、元数据以及用户输入。

The discipline shifts from “what should I write in the prompt” to “what should I assemble in the context, where does each piece come from, and how do I keep the assembly stable across calls.” That is engineering work. It looks like software architecture: typed objects, contracts between components, audit trails, caching.
这一学科的重心从“我应该在提示词里写什么”转向了“我应该在上下文中组装什么，每个片段来自哪里，以及如何保持组装在多次调用间的一致性”。这就是工程工作。它看起来更像软件架构：类型化对象、组件间的契约、审计追踪和缓存。

### 2. Every brick emits typed context
### 2. 每个模块输出类型化上下文

The schema above is the recap of what the series shipped. Each brick is a typed-context emitter. The names on the boxes are the actual fields of the actual Pydantic classes and DataFrames the code produces. Parsing emits relational tables and one synthesis dict.
上图总结了本系列所交付的内容。每个模块都是一个类型化上下文的发射器。框中的名称是代码实际生成的 Pydantic 类和 DataFrame 的实际字段。解析模块输出关系表和一个综合字典（synthesis dict）。

The retrieval brick consumes the per-row tables. The question parsing brick consumes the semantic subset of parsing_summary via DocContext. Question parsing emits a ParsedQuestion. Its fields are not free-form. keywords is a short list of content noun phrases for retrieval. intent is a literal label from a fixed enum that drives shape dispatch in generation. structural_hints.pages_hint carries pinned pages when the user said “on page 3”. answer_shape carries the expected output shape for the generation schema lookup.
检索模块消耗按行划分的表格。问题解析模块通过 `DocContext` 消耗 `parsing_summary` 的语义子集。问题解析模块输出 `ParsedQuestion`，其字段并非自由格式：`keywords` 是用于检索的内容名词短语短列表；`intent` 是来自固定枚举的字面标签，用于驱动生成阶段的形态分发；`structural_hints.pages_hint` 在用户提到“在第 3 页”时携带固定的页码；`answer_shape` 则携带用于生成模式查找的预期输出形态。
