# scKDGM: KAN-guided Dynamic Graph Masked Learning for Single-Cell RNA-seq Clustering
# scKDGM：用于单细胞 RNA 测序聚类的 KAN 引导动态图掩码学习

**Abstract:** Single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) clustering is essential for identifying cell types, but high dimensionality, sparsity, dropout, and technical noise hinder robust expression representation and cell graph construction. Existing masked autoencoders mainly use expression recovery for feature reconstruction, while graph clustering methods usually depend on fixed KNN graphs and do not feed recovered expression back into graph optimization.

**摘要：** 单细胞 RNA 测序（scRNA-seq）聚类对于识别细胞类型至关重要，但高维性、稀疏性、缺失值（dropout）和技术噪声阻碍了稳健的表达表示和细胞图的构建。现有的掩码自编码器主要利用表达恢复来进行特征重构，而图聚类方法通常依赖于固定的 KNN 图，且无法将恢复后的表达反馈到图优化过程中。

We propose scKDGM, a KAN-guided dynamic graph masked learning framework for scRNA-seq clustering. scKDGM uses graph-aware distribution preserving gene masking (GDP-Mask) to perturb cell identity, a KAN-based TAKGCN encoder to learn masked-view representations, mask-guided expression recovery to construct a dynamic graph, and cross-view contrastive learning to transfer recovery signals into topology updates. A ZINB loss models overdispersion and zero inflation. Experiments on 12 real scRNA-seq datasets show that scKDGM outperforms 10 baselines in average NMI and ARI.

我们提出了 scKDGM，这是一个用于 scRNA-seq 聚类的 KAN 引导动态图掩码学习框架。scKDGM 使用图感知分布保持基因掩码（GDP-Mask）来扰动细胞身份，利用基于 KAN 的 TAKGCN 编码器学习掩码视图表示，通过掩码引导的表达恢复来构建动态图，并利用跨视图对比学习将恢复信号转化为拓扑更新。此外，该模型采用 ZINB 损失函数来建模过度离散和零膨胀现象。在 12 个真实 scRNA-seq 数据集上的实验表明，scKDGM 在平均 NMI 和 ARI 指标上均优于 10 个基准模型。
