# Can AI Draw Science? A Benchmark for Evaluating Scientific Figure Generation by Text-to-Image and Multimodal Models
# AI 能绘制科学图表吗？用于评估文生图及多模态模型科学绘图能力的基准测试

**Abstract:** Text-to-image and multimodal generative models are increasingly used to produce scientific figures such as mechanism diagrams, experimental-design schematics, conceptual frameworks, and graphical abstracts. Yet existing image-generation benchmarks (e.g., GenEval, T2I-CompBench, DPG-Bench) evaluate natural images and measure compositionality, object counting, or photorealism. None of them measure what makes a generated scientific figure usable: correct and legible text labels, faithful depiction of entities and their relations, coherent diagrammatic structure, and adherence to disciplinary drawing conventions.

**摘要：** 文生图及多模态生成模型正越来越多地被用于制作科学图表，例如机制图、实验设计示意图、概念框架图和图形摘要。然而，现有的图像生成基准测试（如 GenEval、T2I-CompBench、DPG-Bench）主要针对自然图像进行评估，侧重于测量组合性、物体计数或照片真实感。这些基准测试均无法衡量科学图表可用性的核心要素：准确且清晰的文本标签、对实体及其关系的忠实描绘、连贯的图表结构，以及对学科绘图规范的遵循。

We introduce SciDraw-Bench, a benchmark of 32 structured scientific-figure generation tasks spanning eight figure types and ten disciplines, where each task pairs a natural-language prompt with a machine-checkable specification of required labels, relations, components, conventions, and negative constraints. We propose a four-dimensional evaluation protocol: Text Fidelity (OCR-based label recall and character error rate), Semantic Correctness (vision-language-model judging against the specification), Structural Quality, and Convention Adherence, together with a meta-evaluation protocol and a preliminary inter-judge reliability analysis (human-rating validation is ongoing).

我们推出了 SciDraw-Bench，这是一个包含 32 项结构化科学绘图任务的基准测试，涵盖了八种图表类型和十个学科。每项任务都将自然语言提示与机器可检查的规范配对，这些规范包含了所需的标签、关系、组件、绘图惯例及负面约束。我们提出了一套四维评估协议：文本保真度（基于 OCR 的标签召回率和字符错误率）、语义正确性（由视觉语言模型根据规范进行评判）、结构质量以及规范遵循度。此外，我们还制定了元评估协议，并进行了初步的评判者间信度分析（人工评分验证正在进行中）。

We evaluate a domain-specific system, SciDraw AI, against representative general-purpose text-to-image models, and outline a code-to-figure baseline as a planned extension. In a pilot over all eight figure types, the domain-specific system substantially outperforms the general-purpose baselines on every dimension and figure type, with the largest gaps on semantic correctness and convention adherence; text fidelity remains the hardest dimension for all systems.

我们评估了一个特定领域的系统 SciDraw AI，并将其与具有代表性的通用文生图模型进行了对比，同时概述了作为未来扩展计划的“代码转图表”（code-to-figure）基准。在涵盖所有八种图表类型的试点测试中，该特定领域系统在每个维度和图表类型上均显著优于通用基准模型，其中在语义正确性和规范遵循度方面的差距最为明显；而文本保真度对所有系统而言仍然是最具挑战性的维度。
