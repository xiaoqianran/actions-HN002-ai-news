# Acer’s Swift Go 16 is a lot of laptop for $900
# 宏碁 Swift Go 16：900 美元即可拥有超值笔记本电脑

Here’s a solid 16-inch laptop that’s reasonably priced despite the screwy state of memory and storage costs.
尽管目前内存和存储成本波动剧烈，但这是一款价格合理且表现扎实的 16 英寸笔记本电脑。

As high memory and storage prices have driven up the cost of everything from consoles to computers, finding a competent laptop for under $1,000 has become a challenge. Thankfully, the Acer Swift Go 16 AI is on sale for $899.99 at Best Buy, a steep discount from its usual list price of $1,549.99.
由于内存和存储价格的高涨推高了从游戏主机到电脑等各类产品的成本，寻找一台售价低于 1,000 美元的优秀笔记本电脑已变得颇具挑战。好在宏碁 Swift Go 16 AI 目前在百思买（Best Buy）的售价为 899.99 美元，较其 1,549.99 美元的原价有大幅优惠。

The well-equipped laptop has an Intel Core Ultra 7 355 processor, 32GB of LPDDR5X memory, and a 1TB SSD. It has a great 16-inch OLED (1,920 x 1,200) touchscreen, too. Its tall 16:10 aspect ratio lets you see more, and it has a 180-degree hinge that lets you find your preferred viewing angle.
这款配置齐全的笔记本电脑搭载了英特尔酷睿 Ultra 7 355 处理器、32GB LPDDR5X 内存以及 1TB SSD。它还配备了一块出色的 16 英寸 OLED（1,920 x 1,200）触摸屏。其 16:10 的高宽比让视野更开阔，180 度的铰链设计则能让你轻松找到理想的观看角度。

The generously-sized multi-touch touchpad has a Corning Gorilla Glass top layer, and its backlit keyboard even squeezed in a numpad. Sure, its integrated Intel graphics aren’t great for serious gaming, but it should be more than powerful enough for *Balatro*, and the system can run for over 20 hours on a single charge, depending on brightness settings.
其宽大的多点触控板表面覆盖了康宁大猩猩玻璃，背光键盘甚至还塞入了一个数字小键盘。诚然，其集成的英特尔显卡并不适合大型游戏，但运行《小丑牌》（Balatro）等游戏绰绰有余。根据亮度设置的不同，该系统的单次充电续航时间可超过 20 小时。

It has quite a few ports, particularly when compared to other slim laptops, including two USB-A 3.2 ports, two USB-C with Thunderbolt 4 support, HDMI 2.1, a microSD card slot, and a 3.5mm headphone jack.
与其它轻薄本相比，它的接口相当丰富，包括两个 USB-A 3.2 接口、两个支持雷电 4（Thunderbolt 4）的 USB-C 接口、HDMI 2.1 接口、一个 microSD 卡槽以及一个 3.5mm 耳机插孔。
