# Few-class Fidelity: Evaluating Explanations of Real-conditions CNN classifiers with Optimized Perturbations
# 少类别保真度：利用优化扰动评估真实场景下 CNN 分类器的可解释性

**Abstract:** The wide use of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) in numerous domains and real-world classification applications is justified by their high precision and automation speed, helping users concentrate on higher-expertise tasks.
**摘要：** 卷积神经网络（CNN）因其高精度和自动化速度，被广泛应用于众多领域和现实世界的分类任务中，这使得用户能够专注于更具专业性的工作。

To better understand the models and avoid bias during deployment, eXplainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) techniques can be used after training. But as the list of XAI solutions expand, comparisons between them diverge, and consensus over their evaluation cannot be reached.
为了更好地理解模型并在部署过程中避免偏差，可以在训练后使用可解释人工智能（XAI）技术。然而，随着 XAI 解决方案的不断增加，它们之间的比较结果各异，且在评估标准上难以达成共识。

This paper proposes a variation of Fidelity-based XAI metrics, with a focus on real-conditions applications, where the number of classes is often low. The approach generates in-distribution, uncertainty-provoking perturbations, to ensure proper measurement of the XAI methods faithfulness.
本文提出了一种基于保真度的 XAI 指标变体，重点关注类别数量通常较少的真实应用场景。该方法通过生成分布内且能引发不确定性的扰动，以确保对 XAI 方法忠实度的准确测量。

As demonstration of the evaluation framework usefulness, it is compared with human-centric object localization and segmentation metrics. Once applied to both medical and natural imaging applications, it highlights the intricate correlation between domain, data curation, and XAI solution choices in order to validate training of a new CNN model.
为了证明该评估框架的实用性，研究将其与以人为中心的物体定位和分割指标进行了对比。通过在医学影像和自然图像应用中的实践，该研究揭示了领域、数据整理与 XAI 方案选择之间复杂的关联，从而为验证新 CNN 模型的训练提供了支持。
