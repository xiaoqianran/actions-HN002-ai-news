# Learning to Distributedly Estimate under Partially Known Dynamics: A Covariance-Agnostic Neural Kalman Consensus Filter

**在部分已知动力学下的分布式估计学习：一种协方差不可知的神经卡尔曼一致性滤波器**

---

Online latent state estimation constitutes a fundamental challenge within the artificial intelligence field, serving as a foundational tool for diverse applications, including sequential decision making, anomaly and change-point detection.

在线潜在状态估计是人工智能领域的一项基本挑战，也是包括序列决策、异常检测和变点检测在内的多种应用的基础工具。

In this paper, a novel online distributed sensing framework, where agents collaborate and exchange information to perform latent state estimation, is presented. The proposed estimator combines available partial domain knowledge with the representation capabilities of deep neural networks.

本文提出了一种新颖的在线分布式感知框架，在该框架中，智能体通过协作和信息交换来执行潜在状态估计。所提出的估计器将现有的部分领域知识与深度神经网络的表征能力相结合。

In particular, the designed sensing framework incorporates prior estimates, optimized consensus weights, and Kalman-like recursive updates to perform decentralized inference, without relying on knowledge of noise statistics.

具体而言，所设计的感知框架结合了先验估计、优化的一致性权重以及类卡尔曼递归更新，从而在不依赖噪声统计信息的情况下执行去中心化推理。

Extensive experiments on linear, chaotic (Lorenz), and practical wireless tracking environments reveal that the proposed Covariance-Agnostic Neural Kalman Consensus Filter (CA-NKCF) outperforms traditional distributed Kalman and particle filters as well as purely model-free deep neural networks, exhibiting robustness even when the underlying motion and observation models are misspecified.

在线性环境、混沌（Lorenz）环境以及实际无线跟踪环境中的大量实验表明，所提出的协方差不可知神经卡尔曼一致性滤波器（CA-NKCF）优于传统的分布式卡尔曼滤波器、粒子滤波器以及纯粹的无模型深度神经网络，即使在底层运动和观测模型设定错误时，也表现出了鲁棒性。

It is also demonstrated that CA-NKCF's performance advantage remains stable across varying noise levels, random communication topologies, latent state dimensions, and observation clutter densities induced by scattering objects in wireless systems.

研究还证明，CA-NKCF 的性能优势在不同的噪声水平、随机通信拓扑、潜在状态维度以及由无线系统中的散射物体引起的观测杂波密度下均保持稳定。
