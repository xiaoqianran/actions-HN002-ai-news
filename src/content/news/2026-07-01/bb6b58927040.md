# S-GAI: Spectral Geometry-Aware Initialization for Sigmoidal MLPs -- From Dataset Geometry to Network Weights
# S-GAI：用于 Sigmoid 多层感知机的谱几何感知初始化——从数据集几何到网络权重

**Abstract:** Classical universal approximation theorems establish the expressive power of sigmoidal multilayer perceptrons, but they do not prescribe how initial weights should encode the geometry of a data distribution. 

**摘要：** 经典的通用近似定理确立了 Sigmoid 多层感知机（MLP）的表达能力，但并未规定初始权重应如何编码数据分布的几何结构。

We propose S-GAI, a spectral geometry-aware initialization framework for one-hidden-layer sigmoidal MLPs. Starting from the constructive idea that sigmoid units can act as smooth half-space gates, we move from hand-specified planar geometry to class-wise spectral geometry estimated from image data. 

我们提出了 S-GAI，这是一种针对单隐层 Sigmoid MLP 的谱几何感知初始化框架。基于 Sigmoid 单元可以充当平滑半空间门（smooth half-space gates）的构造性思想，我们将方法从人工指定的平面几何转向从图像数据中估计出的类级谱几何。

For each class, SVD provides a mean, principal directions, and spectral scales. An energy threshold selects the retained directions, and each retained direction is represented by two sigmoid gates. These class-specific gates form a shared hidden layer initialized directly from the training set. 

对于每个类别，奇异值分解（SVD）提供了均值、主方向和谱尺度。通过能量阈值选择保留的方向，每个保留的方向由两个 Sigmoid 门表示。这些特定于类别的门构成了一个直接从训练集初始化的共享隐藏层。

We also formulate a SVD-based subspace classifier as a non-neural geometric reference, which tests whether the estimated spectral class geometry is already discriminative before being embedded into the MLP. 

我们还制定了一个基于 SVD 的子空间分类器作为非神经网络的几何参考，用于测试在嵌入 MLP 之前，估计出的谱类几何结构是否已经具备判别能力。

Experiments on MNIST, Fashion-MNIST, and a more challenging CIFAR-10 test show that the S-GAI-initialized MLP starts from a substantially more informative hidden state than Xavier initialization and reaches comparable final accuracy under full training. 

在 MNIST、Fashion-MNIST 和更具挑战性的 CIFAR-10 测试上的实验表明，与 Xavier 初始化相比，S-GAI 初始化的 MLP 从一个信息量明显更丰富的隐藏状态开始，并在完全训练后达到相当的最终准确率。

When the hidden layer is frozen, training only the output layer still gives stronger performance than frozen random gates, providing evidence that S-GAI effectively embeds class-wise spectral geometry into the MLP.

当隐藏层被冻结时，仅训练输出层仍能获得比冻结随机门更强的性能，这证明了 S-GAI 有效地将类级谱几何嵌入到了 MLP 中。
