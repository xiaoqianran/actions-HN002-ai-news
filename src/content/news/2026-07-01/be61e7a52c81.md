# OpenClaw is finally available on Android and iOS
# OpenClaw 终于登陆 Android 和 iOS 平台

OpenClaw is finally available on Android and iOS. The automation crustacean is crawling to a mobile device near you. By that I mean, OpenClaw — the free, open source AI agent that captivated the internet earlier this year — is finally available as an app on iOS and Android.

OpenClaw 终于登陆 Android 和 iOS 平台。这个“自动化甲壳类动物”正向你身边的移动设备爬来。我的意思是，OpenClaw——那个在今年早些时候风靡互联网的免费开源 AI 智能体——现在终于以应用程序的形式在 iOS 和 Android 上架了。

OpenClaw announced the news on X on Tuesday. On both platforms, you can pair your phone with the OpenClaw Gateway, a kind of routing layer that connects your requests to AI agents and the tools and skills those agents draw on to get things done.

OpenClaw 周二在 X 上宣布了这一消息。在两个平台上，你都可以将手机与 OpenClaw Gateway 配对，这是一种路由层，能够将你的请求连接到 AI 智能体，以及这些智能体为完成任务所调用的工具和技能。

The takeaway is that you’ll be able to run your OpenClaw agents from your pocket and, if you’ve programmed them correctly, they may be pretty helpful at getting things done. OpenClaw users have put it to work in everything from coding to meal planning, although some have reported less-than-desirable results.

这意味着你将能够随时随地在口袋里运行你的 OpenClaw 智能体；如果编程得当，它们在处理事务时会非常有帮助。OpenClaw 的用户已经将其应用于从编程到膳食规划的方方面面，尽管也有一些用户反馈其效果不尽如人意。

OpenClaw went viral earlier this year around the launch of MoltBook, a social media site purportedly populated entirely by agents. In February, OpenClaw’s creator, Peter Steinberger, announced that he had joined OpenAI. The MoltBook spectacle was later revealed to have been partially the work of humans impersonating agents, according to researchers, effective theater that doubled as marketing for OpenClaw (whatever its credibility cost).

今年早些时候，随着 MoltBook 的推出，OpenClaw 开始走红。MoltBook 是一个号称完全由智能体构成的社交媒体网站。二月份，OpenClaw 的创始人 Peter Steinberger 宣布加入 OpenAI。据研究人员称，MoltBook 的那场闹剧后来被揭露部分是由人类冒充智能体完成的，这是一种既是表演又兼作 OpenClaw 营销手段的策略（尽管这牺牲了一定的可信度）。

Still, the stunt pointed toward the agentic future, which has since kept expanding. Agents are now embedded across the AI landscape and are showing up in more places by the day, including your phone.

尽管如此，这一噱头还是指向了智能体驱动的未来，而这一领域此后一直在不断扩张。如今，智能体已嵌入到整个 AI 生态系统中，并且每天都在更多的地方出现，包括你的手机里。
