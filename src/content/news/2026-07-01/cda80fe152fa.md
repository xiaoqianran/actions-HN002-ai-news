# Netflix is using an AI-generated Gene Wilder voice in its Willy Wonka reality show
# Netflix 在其《威利·旺卡》真人秀节目中使用 AI 生成的吉恩·怀尔德配音

A new teaser trailer confirmed that Wonka’s The Golden Ticket will premiere on Netflix on September 23rd, following its Squid Game reality show in the trend of creating real competitions based on fictional torture scenarios. While the sets seen in the trailer are real and not some Glasgow-style AI fakes, the voiceover is AI-generated.

一段新的预告片证实，《旺卡的金奖券》（Wonka’s The Golden Ticket）将于 9 月 23 日在 Netflix 首播。继《鱿鱼游戏》真人秀之后，Netflix 继续延续了将虚构的“折磨场景”转化为真实竞赛节目的趋势。虽然预告片中展示的布景是真实的，并非格拉斯哥那种 AI 伪造的场景，但其中的旁白是由 AI 生成的。

Deadline reports that Netflix worked with AI audio company ElevenLabs with consent from Wilder’s family, after working on productions re-creating the voice of Michael Caine and Stan Lee. It also continues Netflix’s 2021 partnership with the Roald Dahl company, and is separate from the Charlie vs. the Chocolate Factory animated feature that will arrive in 2027.

据《Deadline》报道，Netflix 在获得吉恩·怀尔德（Gene Wilder）家人同意的情况下，与 AI 音频公司 ElevenLabs 进行了合作。此前，Netflix 也曾通过类似技术重现过迈克尔·凯恩（Michael Caine）和斯坦·李（Stan Lee）的声音。此外，该项目延续了 Netflix 与罗尔德·达尔（Roald Dahl）公司自 2021 年以来的合作关系，且与预计于 2027 年上映的《查理大战巧克力工厂》动画长片并无关联。

As for the show itself, Netflix says its “high-stakes social experiment” will feature 12 “lucky” golden ticket winners plus their chosen partner in the competition, with one champion left standing a week later during the two-part finale on September 30th.

至于节目本身，Netflix 表示这场“高风险社会实验”将邀请 12 位“幸运”的金奖券获得者及其选定的搭档共同参赛。一周后，即 9 月 30 日的两集大结局中，将决出最终的冠军。
