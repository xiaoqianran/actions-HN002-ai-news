# Moto’s Tag 2 tracker is $20 for a limited time
# 摩托罗拉 Moto Tag 2 追踪器限时优惠价 20 美元

With an IP68 rating, 600 days of battery life, and a multifunction button, this is a standout tracker for Android.
凭借 IP68 级防尘防水、600 天的超长续航以及多功能按键，这款追踪器在安卓阵营中表现十分亮眼。

Announced at CES 2026, the Moto Tag 2 has finally arrived in the US. The Bluetooth tracker with improved battery life over its predecessor, plus UWB sensing for more accurate tracking, is available through Motorola’s site at a special $19.99 launch price (originally $29.99).
在 2026 年 CES 上首次亮相后，Moto Tag 2 终于正式登陆美国市场。这款蓝牙追踪器相较前代产品提升了续航能力，并加入了用于精准定位的超宽带（UWB）传感技术。目前，用户可以通过摩托罗拉官网以 19.99 美元的首发特惠价购入（原价 29.99 美元）。

There are plenty of trackers for Android owners to consider, but the Tag 2 seems tough to beat at this price. It supports Google’s Find My Device network, has an IP68 rating, and runs off a replaceable CR2032 coin battery that can last up to 600 days.
虽然安卓用户有多种追踪器可供选择，但在这一价位段，Tag 2 的竞争力极强。它支持谷歌的“查找我的设备”（Find My Device）网络，具备 IP68 级防护，并使用可更换的 CR2032 纽扣电池，续航时间最长可达 600 天。

The Moto Tag 2 has a built-in speaker that can sound off at up to 77 decibels. The tracker itself has a button that can be set to do several things, and is configurable within the Moto Tag app. Pressing the button can help you find your phone nearby. It can also trigger your phone’s camera shutter. A Bluetooth tracker doesn’t need most of these features to be good, but the Moto Tag 2 has them anyway.
Moto Tag 2 内置扬声器，音量最高可达 77 分贝。追踪器机身设有一个多功能按键，可通过 Moto Tag 应用程序进行自定义设置。按下该按键不仅可以帮你寻找附近的手机，还能作为手机相机的远程快门使用。虽然蓝牙追踪器并不一定需要这些功能才能算作优秀，但 Moto Tag 2 还是将它们全部囊括其中。
