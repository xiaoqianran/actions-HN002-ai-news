# Counterfactual Residual Data Augmentation for Regression
# 回归任务中的反事实残差数据增强

**Abstract:** Data-driven modeling in real-world regression tasks often suffers from limited training samples, high collection costs, and noisy observations.
**摘要：** 在现实世界的回归任务中，数据驱动建模往往受限于训练样本不足、采集成本高昂以及观测噪声等问题。

Inspired by the impact of data augmentation in vision and language, we propose a novel Counterfactual Residual Data Augmentation (CRDA) technique for tabular regression.
受视觉和语言领域数据增强技术的启发，我们提出了一种用于表格回归的新型反事实残差数据增强（CRDA）技术。

Our key insight is that once a regressor has modeled the systematic component of the data, the remaining noise can be viewed as an invariant residual that remains stable under small perturbations of carefully selected features.
我们的核心见解是：一旦回归模型捕捉到了数据的系统性成分，剩余的噪声就可以被视为一种不变的残差，在对精心选择的特征进行微小扰动时，该残差保持稳定。

We exploit this residual invariance to generate new, yet realistic, training samples, effectively expanding the dataset without requiring additional real data.
我们利用这种残差不变性来生成既新颖又真实有效的训练样本，从而在无需额外真实数据的情况下有效地扩充了数据集。

Our method is model-agnostic and readily applicable to various types of regressors.
该方法与模型无关，可轻松应用于各种类型的回归器。

In experiments across datasets from a variety of benchmark repositories, on average, CRDA reduces an MLP Regressor's MSE by 22.9% and an XGBoost Regressor's MSE by 6.4%.
在跨多个基准库的数据集实验中，CRDA 平均将 MLP 回归器的均方误差（MSE）降低了 22.9%，将 XGBoost 回归器的 MSE 降低了 6.4%。

When compared to existing state-of-the-art data generators and augmentation techniques, CRDA consistently outperforms in MSE reduction.
与现有的最先进数据生成器和增强技术相比，CRDA 在降低 MSE 方面表现始终优于同类方法。

By adding principled counterfactual variations to the training data, our method offers a simple and efficient remedy for noise-prone, small-sample regression settings.
通过向训练数据添加有原则的反事实变体，我们的方法为易受噪声影响的小样本回归场景提供了一种简单且高效的解决方案。
