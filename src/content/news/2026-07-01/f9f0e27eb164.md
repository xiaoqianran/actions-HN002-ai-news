# RADIANT-PET: Reasoning-Augmented PET/CT Lesion Segmentation with Large Language Models and Reinforcement Learning

**RADIANT-PET：结合大语言模型与强化学习的推理增强型 PET/CT 病灶分割框架**

***

**Abstract:** Accurate lesion segmentation in PET/CT is critical for oncology, yet remains challenging because physiologic tracer uptake and artifacts can mimic malignant signal. 

**摘要：** 在肿瘤学中，PET/CT 的精准病灶分割至关重要，但由于生理性示踪剂摄取和伪影可能模拟恶性信号，该任务仍具挑战性。

We present RADIANT-PET, a reasoning-augmented framework that couples a high-sensitivity voxel-level segmentation model with lesion-level large language model (LLM) adjudication. 

我们提出了 RADIANT-PET，这是一个推理增强型框架，它将高灵敏度的体素级分割模型与病灶级大语言模型（LLM）判别相结合。

Candidate uptake regions are generated with a deliberately permissive segmentation stage, then converted into structured textual descriptions that summarize uptake intensity, morphology, and regional and global anatomical context. 

候选摄取区域通过一个刻意放宽标准的分割阶段生成，随后被转换为结构化的文本描述，总结了摄取强度、形态学以及局部和全局的解剖背景。

An LLM classifies each candidate as true lesion vs. false positive, optionally leveraging the radiology report as additional clinical context. 

大语言模型将每个候选区域分类为“真实病灶”或“假阳性”，并可选择利用放射科报告作为额外的临床背景信息。

To strengthen lesion-level reasoning, we further optimize a local LLM via reinforcement learning using Group Relative Policy Optimization, rewarding correct lesion classification and anatomically concordant site assignment. 

为了加强病灶级的推理能力，我们通过强化学习进一步优化了本地大语言模型，采用了“组相对策略优化”（Group Relative Policy Optimization），对正确的病灶分类和解剖学一致的部位分配给予奖励。

Across AutoPET and an OSU test cohort, RADIANT-PET consistently outperforms strong image-only baselines, with the largest improvements observed when radiology reports are provided. 

在 AutoPET 和 OSU 测试队列中，RADIANT-PET 的表现始终优于强大的纯图像基准模型，且在提供放射科报告时观察到的改进最为显著。

Overall, these results demonstrate that LLM-based lesion-level reasoning adds a novel reasoning layer beyond conventional segmentation, suppressing physiologic false positives and aligning voxel-level predictions with clinical interpretation. 

总体而言，这些结果表明，基于大语言模型的病灶级推理在传统分割之外增加了一个全新的推理层，能够抑制生理性假阳性，并将体素级的预测与临床解读对齐。
