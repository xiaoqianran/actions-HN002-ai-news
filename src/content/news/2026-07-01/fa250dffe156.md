# Automated Quality Assessment of Geospatial Vector Data: A GeoAI Approach using Spatial Representation Learning
# 地理空间矢量数据自动质量评估：一种基于空间表征学习的地理人工智能（GeoAI）方法

**Abstract:** Geospatial vector data quality is a foundational research topic in GIS, yet classic rule-based quality assessment algorithms often struggle with diverse urban morphologies and massive data volumes. Recently, Geospatial Artificial Intelligence (GeoAI) shows promising potential for automating geospatial analysis, while its application to native vector data remains largely underexplored.

**摘要：** 地理空间矢量数据质量是地理信息系统（GIS）中的一个基础研究课题，然而传统的基于规则的质量评估算法往往难以应对多样的城市形态和海量的数据规模。近年来，地理人工智能（GeoAI）在自动化地理空间分析方面展现出巨大的潜力，但其在原生矢量数据上的应用仍有待深入探索。

To fill this research gap, we proposed Topo4Vec, an automated GeoAI framework, designed for scalable vector data quality assessment via advanced Spatial Representation Learning (SRL). Specifically, Topo4Vec relax the labor-intensive manual annotation process via topological error simulation, such as overlapping polygons and street network connectivity errors e.g., overshoots and undershoots.

为了填补这一研究空白，我们提出了 Topo4Vec，这是一个自动化的 GeoAI 框架，旨在通过先进的空间表征学习（SRL）实现可扩展的矢量数据质量评估。具体而言，Topo4Vec 通过拓扑错误模拟（如多边形重叠以及街道网络连接错误，例如过冲和欠冲）减轻了繁重的人工标注工作。

Then, it leverages state-of-the-art SRL approaches to encode complex, native vector geometries (e.g., polylines and polygons) into a latent space where topological errors are isolated from valid ones. A systematic performance evaluation across three study areas (Los Angeles, Munich, and Singapore) demonstrates the effectiveness and robustness of Topo4Vec, achieving a peak accuracy of 0.99 for detecting overlapping building footprints and 0.60 for overshoots and undershoots in street networks.

随后，该框架利用最先进的 SRL 方法将复杂的原生矢量几何图形（如折线和多边形）编码到潜在空间中，从而将拓扑错误与有效数据区分开来。在三个研究区域（洛杉矶、慕尼黑和新加坡）进行的系统性性能评估证明了 Topo4Vec 的有效性和鲁棒性，在检测重叠建筑轮廓方面达到了 0.99 的峰值准确率，在检测街道网络过冲和欠冲方面达到了 0.60 的准确率。

Moreover, lessons learned from Topo4Vec shed a promising light into a scalable and autonomous GeoAI approach for large-scale vector data consistency and quality monitoring within the fast-growing geospatial data ecosystems. The code and data used in the paper are made openly available in this https URL.

此外，从 Topo4Vec 中获得的经验为在快速发展的地理空间数据生态系统中，实现大规模矢量数据一致性和质量监控提供了一种可扩展且自主的 GeoAI 方法。本文使用的代码和数据已在指定链接中公开。
