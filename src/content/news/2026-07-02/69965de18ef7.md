# Accelerometry-Derived Digital Biomarkers for Cardiometabolic Risk: A Population-Representative Tabular Benchmark with Uncertainty Quantification

**基于加速度计的心脏代谢风险数字生物标志物：具有不确定性量化的群体代表性表格基准**

***

**Abstract:** Structured tabular data dominates clinical medicine, yet existing benchmarks fail to reflect real-world properties like complex survey sampling, demographic oversampling, and subgroup fairness. We introduce the NHANES Accelerometry Cardiometabolic Benchmark, derived from NHANES 2003-2006, comprising 1,381 adults with hip-worn accelerometry, fasting laboratory biomarkers, dietary intake, and anthropometrics.

**摘要：** 结构化表格数据在临床医学中占据主导地位，但现有的基准测试未能反映现实世界中的复杂特性，如复杂的调查抽样、人口统计学过采样以及子群体公平性。我们引入了 NHANES 加速度计心脏代谢基准（NHANES Accelerometry Cardiometabolic Benchmark），该基准源自 2003-2006 年的 NHANES 数据，包含 1,381 名佩戴髋部加速度计的成年人，涵盖了空腹实验室生物标志物、饮食摄入量和人体测量学数据。

***

We evaluate three tabular learning methods -- ridge regression, XGBoost, and the foundation model TabPFN v2 -- to predict glycated haemoglobin (HbA1c), fasting triglycerides, and C-reactive protein (CRP) from activity phenotypes and lifestyle covariates. TabPFN v2 achieves the best overall performance (HbA1c R^2=0.156, CRP R^2=0.383), while triglycerides remain largely unpredictable (R^2 < 0.05), consistent with known genetic dominance.

我们评估了三种表格学习方法——岭回归（Ridge Regression）、XGBoost 和基础模型 TabPFN v2，旨在通过活动表型和生活方式协变量来预测糖化血红蛋白（HbA1c）、空腹甘油三酯和 C 反应蛋白（CRP）。TabPFN v2 实现了最佳的整体性能（HbA1c R^2=0.156，CRP R^2=0.383），而甘油三酯在很大程度上仍难以预测（R^2 < 0.05），这与已知的遗传主导地位相一致。

***

We apply split conformal prediction to generate distribution-free 90% prediction intervals and evaluate demographic coverage equity across sex and race/ethnicity subgroups. Marginal coverage aligns with the 90% target for CRP and HbA1c but falls below for triglycerides. At the subgroup level, we observe localized undercoverage (e.g., HbA1c for Mexican American participants), illustrating the gap between marginal guarantees and the conditional coverage required for clinical fairness. 我们应用拆分共形预测（Split Conformal Prediction）来生成无分布的 90% 预测区间，并评估了不同性别和种族/族裔子群体之间的人口统计学覆盖公平性。对于 CRP 和 HbA1c，边际覆盖率符合 90% 的目标，但甘油三酯的覆盖率低于该目标。在子群体层面，我们观察到局部覆盖不足的情况（例如墨西哥裔美国参与者的 HbA1c 预测），这说明了边际保证与临床公平性所需的条件覆盖之间存在差距。代码和数据可在该链接获取。
