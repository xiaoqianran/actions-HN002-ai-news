# Predictable GRPO: A Closed-Form Model of Training Dynamics
# 可预测的 GRPO：训练动力学的闭式模型

Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) has become a standard tool for improving the reasoning ability of large language models, yet its training dynamics are still described empirically: reward trajectories are fit with low-parameter functional forms whose constants carry no mechanistic meaning, and hyperparameter choices remain a matter of trial and error.

组相对策略优化（GRPO）已成为提升大语言模型推理能力的标配工具，但其训练动力学目前仍主要通过经验描述：奖励轨迹通常使用低参数函数形式进行拟合，其中的常数缺乏机制含义，且超参数的选择依然依赖于反复试验。

We develop a first-principles reduced-order model of these dynamics. The reduction has three consequences. First, it subsumes the empirical single-exponential saturation law as its overdamped limit, recasting the fitted plateau, timescale, and size exponent as the fixed point, inverse stiffness, and curvature-scaling exponent of the underlying potential, and adding, through the retained inertial term, the slow-start phase the single exponential cannot represent.

我们针对这些动力学开发了一种基于第一性原理的降阶模型。该模型具有三个主要贡献。首先，它将经验性的单指数饱和定律归纳为其过阻尼极限，将拟合出的平台期、时间尺度和规模指数重新定义为底层势能的固定点、逆刚度和曲率缩放指数；同时，通过保留惯性项，它补充了单指数模型无法描述的“慢启动”阶段。

Second, it yields predictions tied to independently measurable quantities rather than fitted ones: group-size invariance of the deterministic trajectory with a $1/G$ stationary fluctuation, a sharp stability threshold in the refresh interval, and an overdamped-to-oscillatory transition.

其次，它得出的预测结果与可独立测量的量相关，而非依赖于拟合值：包括确定性轨迹在群体规模上的不变性（伴随 $1/G$ 的平稳波动）、刷新间隔的明确稳定性阈值，以及从过阻尼到振荡的转变。

Third, it furnishes diagnostics that separate failure modes a reward curve alone conflates -- reward hacking, advantage degeneracy, policy concentration, and dynamical instability.

第三，它提供了诊断工具，能够区分仅凭奖励曲线难以分辨的失效模式，如奖励作弊（reward hacking）、优势退化（advantage degeneracy）、策略集中（policy concentration）以及动力学不稳定性。

Across three models and two group sizes, the closed-form trajectory fits training reward to $R^2 \geq 0.91$ and the predicted group-size invariance holds on both the reward curve and out-of-distribution transfer to eight math benchmarks.

在三个模型和两种群体规模的测试中，该闭式轨迹对训练奖励的拟合度达到 $R^2 \geq 0.91$，且预测的群体规模不变性在奖励曲线以及八个数学基准测试的分布外迁移中均得到了验证。

The stability and oscillatory predictions are exercised in a controlled exact-reduction setting where the mean-field assumption holds exactly: a softmax-bandit reduction reproduces the predicted overdamped-to-oscillatory transition and locates the refresh-interval stability threshold at the independently measured stiffness, with a deep-network demonstration left to future work.

稳定性与振荡预测在平均场假设严格成立的受控精确约简环境中得到了验证：Softmax-Bandit 约简模型重现了预测的过阻尼到振荡的转变，并将刷新间隔的稳定性阈值定位在独立测量的刚度上；而针对深度网络的演示则留待未来研究。
