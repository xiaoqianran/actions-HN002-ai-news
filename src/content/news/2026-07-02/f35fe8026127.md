# Ithaca's king defies the gods in final The Odyssey trailer
# 伊萨卡之王在《奥德赛》终极预告片中公然对抗众神

We’re counting down the days until the release of Christopher Nolan’s The Odyssey, and Universal Pictures has released one last trailer to keep anticipation high.
我们正在倒计时等待克里斯托弗·诺兰执导的《奥德赛》上映，环球影业发布了最后一部预告片，以维持观众的高涨期待。

Most of us read some version of The Odyssey in high school, so we’re familiar with the story: Odysseus, legendary Greek king of Ithaca, begins the long journey home after 10 years of fighting in the Trojan War. But the journey doesn’t go smoothly, as Odysseus and his men encounter the cyclops Polyphemus, the Sirens, and an enchantress named Circe, among other obstacles. Meanwhile, Penelope, his long-suffering wife, wards off hundreds of suitors eager to usurp Odysseus’ position.
我们大多数人在高中时都读过《奥德赛》的某个版本，因此对这个故事并不陌生：伊萨卡传说中的希腊国王奥德修斯在参加了十年的特洛伊战争后，踏上了漫长的回家之路。但这段旅程并不顺利，奥德修斯和他的船员们遭遇了独眼巨人波吕斐摩斯、海妖塞壬、女巫喀耳刻等重重阻碍。与此同时，他饱受煎熬的妻子佩涅洛佩也在竭力抵御数百名企图篡夺奥德修斯地位的求婚者。

Matt Damon stars as the wandering Ithacan king. The cast also includes Anne Hathaway as Penelope; Tom Holland as Odysseus’ son, Telemachus; Robert Pattinson as Antinous, one of Penelope’s many suitors; Jon Bernthal as the Spartan king, Menelaus; Benny Safdie as the Achaean commander during the Trojan War, Agamemnon; John Leguizamo as Odysseus’ faithful servant, Eumaeus; Himesh Patel as his second-in-command, Eurylochus; Will Yun Lee and Jimmy Gonzales as crew members; and Mia Goth as Penelope’s maid Melantho. We also have Zendaya as Athena, Charlize Theron as Circe, and Lupita Nyong’o in a dual role: both Helen of Troy and Helen’s sister, Clytemnestra.
马特·达蒙饰演这位漂泊的伊萨卡国王。演员阵容还包括：安妮·海瑟薇饰演佩涅洛佩；汤姆·赫兰德饰演奥德修斯的儿子特勒马科斯；罗伯特·帕丁森饰演佩涅洛佩众多求婚者之一的安提诺乌斯；乔·博恩瑟饰演斯巴达国王墨涅拉俄斯；本·萨弗迪饰演特洛伊战争期间的亚该亚统帅阿伽门农；约翰·雷吉扎莫饰演奥德修斯的忠仆欧迈俄斯；希米什·帕特尔饰演他的副手欧律洛科斯；李威尹和吉米·冈萨雷斯饰演船员；米娅·高斯饰演佩涅洛佩的女仆梅兰托。此外，赞达亚饰演雅典娜，查理兹·塞隆饰演喀耳刻，露皮塔·尼永奥则一人分饰两角：特洛伊的海伦及其姐妹克吕泰涅斯特拉。

We got our first look at stills of Damon’s Odysseus in February 2025, an extended teaser last July, and a full-length trailer in December. A second trailer in May gave us our first glimpse of Theron’s Circe and Pattinson’s conniving Antinous, the latter intent on wooing Penelope and ruling over Ithaca in Odysseus’ stead (as well as snidely taunting Telemachus for his daddy issues). The infamous cyclops briefly appeared, as well as footage of Odysseus and his men battling the cannibalistic race of giants known as Laestrygonians and navigating around the treacherous whirlpool Charybdis. This final trailer incorporates much of the same footage.
我们于2025年2月首次看到了达蒙版奥德修斯的剧照，去年7月看到了加长版预告片，12月则发布了完整版预告片。5月的第二部预告片让我们首次见到了塞隆饰演的喀耳刻和帕丁森饰演的阴险求婚者安提诺乌斯，后者一心想追求佩涅洛佩并取代奥德修斯统治伊萨卡（还冷嘲热讽地嘲笑特勒马科斯的“恋父情结”）。臭名昭著的独眼巨人短暂现身，预告片中还出现了奥德修斯及其船员与食人巨人族莱斯特律戈涅斯战斗，以及绕过危险漩涡卡律布狄斯的画面。这部终极预告片整合了许多相同的镜头。

What’s new: Telemachus expressing his desire to rule in his father’s absence and Penelope quickly quashing such ambitions. “They’re not giving up power to you or Odysseus,” she says. “Do you think life goes back to the way it was? That world is gone. If he returned, he’d have to kill them. Like you will.” Odysseus makes it clear he’s prepared to bring all the vengeance that’s required to reclaim his throne. Zendaya’s Athena also makes an appearance, telling Odysseus the gods told him what would come with the fall of Troy. “Then I defy the gods,” Odysseus declares.
新内容包括：特勒马科斯表达了在父亲缺席期间想要掌权的愿望，但被佩涅洛佩迅速压制。她说：“他们不会把权力交给你或奥德修斯。你以为生活还能回到从前吗？那个世界已经消失了。如果他回来，他必须杀了他们。就像你将来要做的那样。”奥德修斯明确表示，他已准备好施加一切必要的复仇来夺回王位。赞达亚饰演的雅典娜也出现在预告中，告诉奥德修斯众神曾预言特洛伊陷落后的后果。奥德修斯宣称：“那么，我公然对抗众神。”

The Odyssey hits theaters on July 17, 2026. The film was shot entirely in IMAX; it will release in IMAX and on 75 and 35 mm.
《奥德赛》将于2026年7月17日上映。该片全程使用IMAX摄影机拍摄，并将以IMAX格式以及75毫米和35毫米胶片格式发行。
