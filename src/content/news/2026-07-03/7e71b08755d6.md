# Godox’s feature-packed key light is down to its best price yet
# 神牛（Godox）功能丰富的补光灯迎来史低价

If you don’t want to spend $180 on the Elgato Key Light, the Godox ES45 Desktop LED Key Light is a more affordable option that offers nearly as much brightness and plenty of features, including adjustable brightness and color temperature. Right now, it’s cheaper than usual for the first time this year, selling for $119 ($20 off) at Amazon and B&H Photo.

如果你不想花 180 美元购买 Elgato Key Light，神牛（Godox）ES45 桌面 LED 补光灯是一个更实惠的选择。它提供了几乎相当的亮度以及丰富的功能，包括可调节的亮度和色温。目前，该产品迎来了今年以来的首次降价，在亚马逊和 B&H Photo 上的售价为 119 美元（优惠 20 美元）。

The ES45 is a worthwhile purchase for streamers, or really anyone who wants to look their best on camera. You can position the light to shine directly on you, or aim it at your wall for some even bias lighting that creates a glow effect in your room that’s easier on the eyes. It can go between a warm 2,800K to a cooler, daylight-like color temperature.

对于直播主或任何希望在镜头前展现最佳状态的人来说，ES45 都非常值得购买。你可以将灯光直接对准自己，或者将其对准墙壁，以获得均匀的背景光，从而在房间内营造出一种更护眼的柔和光效。它的色温可以在 2,800K 的暖光到类似日光般的冷光之间调节。

One feature it has over Elgato’s popular Key Light is an included wireless 2.4GHz remote, which lets you easily adjust the lighting without having to fiddle with desktop or mobile apps. The remote automatically recharges when you reconnect it magnetically to the light. The ES45 also comes with a desk clamp that requires no tools to install, letting you attach it to a desk that’s up to two inches thick.

与 Elgato 热门的 Key Light 相比，它的一大优势是附带了一个 2.4GHz 无线遥控器，让你无需操作桌面或移动端 App 即可轻松调节灯光。当你通过磁吸方式将遥控器重新连接到灯体上时，它会自动充电。ES45 还配备了一个无需工具即可安装的桌面夹，可以将其固定在厚度不超过两英寸的桌面上。
