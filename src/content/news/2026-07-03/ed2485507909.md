### Solaris

**Solaris**

Solaris is an AI-native transformation platform that helps organisations build AI fluency across every team.
Solaris 是一个人工智能原生转型平台，旨在帮助企业在各个团队中建立 AI 熟练度。

Start with a fluency test to understand where people are today, then give each team tailored learning, practical use cases, workflow challenges, champions and adoption tracking.
首先通过熟练度测试了解员工当前的水平，然后为每个团队提供量身定制的学习内容、实际应用案例、工作流程挑战、内部倡导者以及采用情况跟踪。

Solaris helps companies move from scattered AI experiments to measurable capability, so AI becomes part of how work actually gets done.
Solaris 帮助企业从零散的 AI 实验转向可衡量的能力建设，从而使 AI 真正融入日常工作流程之中。
