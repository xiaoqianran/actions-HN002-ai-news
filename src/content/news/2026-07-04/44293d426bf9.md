# MIBE: Multi-subject Interaction Benchmark and Evaluator for Personalized Image Generation
# MIBE：用于个性化图像生成的多主体交互基准与评估器

**Abstract:** Multi-subject personalized image generation requires the precise rendering of all requested reference identities and their specified interactions based on a guiding prompt. However, state-of-the-art models still struggle with this process, frequently omitting subjects, failing to preserve reference appearances, or misattributing interactions. Furthermore, existing metrics designed primarily for single-subject fidelity cannot reliably capture these errors, suffering severe degradation in ranking separability and failing to align with human preference as the subject count increases.

**摘要：** 多主体个性化图像生成要求根据引导提示词，精确渲染所有请求的参考身份及其指定的交互关系。然而，当前最先进的模型在处理这一过程时仍面临挑战，经常出现遗漏主体、无法保持参考外观或交互属性归属错误等问题。此外，现有的主要针对单主体保真度设计的指标无法可靠地捕捉这些错误；随着主体数量的增加，这些指标在排序可分性上会出现严重退化，且无法与人类偏好保持一致。

To address this gap, we introduce Multi-subject Interaction Benchmark and Evaluator (MIBE), a unified framework comprising a Multi-subject Interaction Benchmark (MIB) and a Multi-subject Interaction Evaluator (MIE). MIB systematically covers diverse relation types and scene complexities through a decoupled data regime. This consists of a 60K-pair VLM-labeled Silver Set for scalable metric training and a 4K-pair double-blind Human Evaluation Gold Set covering a diverse range of state-of-the-art generators, with the Silver Set reaching 95.1% cross-VLM preference agreement.

为了弥补这一差距，我们引入了多主体交互基准与评估器（MIBE），这是一个包含多主体交互基准（MIB）和多主体交互评估器（MIE）的统一框架。MIB 通过解耦的数据机制，系统地涵盖了多种关系类型和场景复杂度。该基准由 60K 对经 VLM（视觉语言模型）标注的“银标集”（用于可扩展的指标训练）和 4K 对涵盖多种最先进生成器的双盲“人类评估金标集”组成，其中银标集的跨 VLM 偏好一致性达到了 95.1%。

To demonstrate the utility of this benchmark, we present MIE, a lightweight, reference-conditioned evaluator trained exclusively on the Silver Set with a dual-head ranking and diagnosis objective. MIE exhibits strong cross-generator generalization on the Gold Set, achieving 0.922 overall pairwise accuracy against human preference, including 0.982 on seen generators and 0.884 on unseen generators. By outperforming a broad spectrum of baseline metrics, including CLIP and DINO variants, MIE demonstrates that diagnostic supervision can preserve ranking separability and human alignment where traditional evaluators collapse.

为了证明该基准的实用性，我们提出了 MIE，这是一个轻量级的、基于参考条件的评估器，它仅在银标集上进行训练，并具备双头排序和诊断目标。MIE 在金标集上表现出强大的跨生成器泛化能力，在人类偏好对比中达到了 0.922 的总体成对准确率，其中在已知生成器上为 0.982，在未知生成器上为 0.884。通过超越包括 CLIP 和 DINO 变体在内的广泛基准指标，MIE 证明了在传统评估器失效的情况下，诊断监督能够保持排序的可分性和与人类偏好的一致性。
