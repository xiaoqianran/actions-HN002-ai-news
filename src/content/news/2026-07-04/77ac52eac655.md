# Last chance to apply — Startup Battlefield Australia applications close July 6
# 最后申请机会 — Startup Battlefield Australia 报名将于 7 月 6 日截止

Every startup has a moment that changes everything. For some, it’s landing their first customer. For others, it’s closing their first funding round. For a select few, it starts with one pitch. If you’re going to apply for Startup Battlefield Australia, now is the time. Applications close July 6, and once the deadline passes, the opportunity is gone.

每家初创公司都有一个改变一切的时刻。对一些人来说，那是获得第一个客户；对另一些人来说，那是完成第一轮融资；而对少数幸运儿来说，一切始于一次路演。如果你打算申请 Startup Battlefield Australia，现在就是时候了。申请将于 7 月 6 日截止，一旦截止日期过后，机会将不再有。

Who should apply? If you’re an early-stage startup in Australia or New Zealand, this opportunity was built for you. We’re looking for founders who are: Pre-seed to Series B. Building a real product with early traction. Solving meaningful problems with innovative technology. Ready to introduce their company to investors, media, and the broader startup ecosystem.

谁应该申请？如果你是澳大利亚或新西兰的早期初创公司，这个机会就是为你准备的。我们正在寻找符合以下条件的创始人：处于种子轮前（Pre-seed）至 B 轮阶段；拥有具备早期市场吸引力的真实产品；利用创新技术解决有意义的问题；准备好向投资者、媒体和更广泛的初创生态系统展示自己的公司。

You don’t need to be famous. In fact, that’s the point. Startup Battlefield exists to discover the next breakout company before everyone else does.

你不需要很有名。事实上，这正是活动的意义所在。Startup Battlefield 的存在，就是为了在所有人发现之前，挖掘出下一家爆发式增长的公司。

What’s at stake? On August 19, 2026, eight selected startups will take the stage live at Stripe Tour Sydney in front of leading investors, global media, and Australia’s technology community. The top three startups will receive up to $15,000 in Stripe fee credits. The grand prize goes even further. The winner receives automatic entry into Startup Battlefield 200 at TechCrunch Disrupt 2026 — one of the world’s most prestigious startup competitions — in San Francisco this October.

奖项设置如何？2026 年 8 月 19 日，八家入选的初创公司将登上 Stripe Tour Sydney 的现场舞台，面对顶尖投资者、全球媒体和澳大利亚科技界进行展示。前三名初创公司将获得最高 15,000 美元的 Stripe 手续费抵扣额度。大奖的含金量更高：获胜者将自动获得参加今年 10 月在旧金山举行的 TechCrunch Disrupt 2026 大会中 Startup Battlefield 200 的资格——这是全球最负盛名的初创公司竞赛之一。

The countdown is on. The application window is almost closed. When the deadline passes, there are no extensions, no second chances, and no late submissions. If you’ve been thinking about applying, don’t spend another day wondering, “What if?” Submit your application now and give your startup the opportunity to become the next company everyone is talking about. Applications close July 6. Don’t let this opportunity become the one that got away. Apply now. Free to apply • No equity taken • Live in Sydney on August 19, 2026.

倒计时已经开始，申请窗口即将关闭。一旦截止日期过后，将不再有延期、补救机会或逾期提交。如果你一直在考虑申请，不要再浪费时间去想“如果……会怎样”。现在就提交申请，让你的初创公司有机会成为下一个备受瞩目的焦点。申请于 7 月 6 日截止，别让这个机会溜走。立即申请。免费申请 • 不占股份 • 2026 年 8 月 19 日在悉尼现场举行。
