### I²RiMA: Spectral Riemannian Representation with Temporal Attention for Mental Stress Detection based on EEG Signals
**I²RiMA：基于脑电信号的心理压力检测：结合时间注意力机制的谱黎曼表示法**

---

**Abstract:** Cross-subject EEG stress detection remains challenging because discriminative stress-related patterns are both subject-dependent and frequency-specific. Conventional Riemannian methods model spatial covariance mainly in the time domain, overlooking neural oscillations that are critical for high-level cognitive state decoding, while standard temporal tokenization often fragments inter-slice temporal coherence.

**摘要：** 跨受试者的脑电（EEG）压力检测仍然是一项挑战，因为具有判别力的压力相关模式既具有受试者依赖性，又具有频率特异性。传统的黎曼几何方法主要在时域内建模空间协方差，忽略了对高层认知状态解码至关重要的神经振荡；而标准的时间分词（temporal tokenization）方法往往会破坏切片间的时间连贯性。

To address these limitations, we propose I²RiMA, an Intra-Inter Riemannian Manifold Attention Network for EEG-based stress detection. I²RiMA constructs spatial covariance matrices independently at each frequency point and maps them to the SPD tangent space, preserving channel-wise geometry together with frequency-specific discriminative cues.

为了解决这些局限性，我们提出了 I²RiMA，这是一种用于基于脑电压力检测的“层内-层间黎曼流形注意力网络”。I²RiMA 在每个频率点独立构建空间协方差矩阵，并将其映射到对称正定（SPD）切空间，从而在保留通道几何结构的同时，也保留了频率特异性的判别线索。

It further introduces frequency cluster aggregation to select informative spectral components and reduce redundancy by forming compact, data-driven frequency clusters aligned with EEG rhythms. Finally, an intra-inter slice attention module adaptively integrates local slice-level spectral dynamics and global temporal context across EEG sequences.

该模型进一步引入了频率聚类聚合机制，通过形成与脑电节律对齐的紧凑型数据驱动频率簇，来筛选信息丰富的谱分量并减少冗余。最后，层内-层间切片注意力模块能够自适应地整合脑电序列中局部切片级的谱动态与全局时间上下文。

Experiments on three datasets show that I²RiMA consistently outperforms five state-of-the-art baselines, achieving up to 82.78% balanced accuracy while remaining efficient with only 1.60M parameters and 31.95M FLOPs.

在三个数据集上的实验表明，I²RiMA 的表现始终优于五种最先进的基准模型，在仅使用 160 万参数和 3195 万次浮点运算（FLOPs）的高效条件下，实现了高达 82.78% 的平衡准确率。
