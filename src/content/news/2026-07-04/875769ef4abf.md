# Rethinking Generic Object Tracking Toward Human-Level Perceptual Intelligence
# 重新思考通用目标跟踪：迈向人类水平的感知智能

**Abstract:** At the heart of human visual perception lies the ability to maintain a continuous and coherent understanding of the external world. By integrating observations with accumulated experience, the human visual system can continuously adapt to variations in both the target and its surrounding environment, while preserving robust visual continuity as scene dynamics evolve.

**摘要：** 人类视觉感知的核心在于能够对外部世界保持持续且连贯的理解。通过将观察结果与积累的经验相结合，人类视觉系统能够不断适应目标及其周围环境的变化，同时在场景动态演变的过程中保持稳健的视觉连续性。

Human vision can therefore integrate prior knowledge, spatial geometry, and semantic context to understand complex scenes and their changes. As a core problem in computer vision, visual object tracking aims to bring machine perception closer to human visual perception. These capabilities are central to the task of Generic Object Tracking (GOT).

因此，人类视觉能够整合先验知识、空间几何和语义上下文来理解复杂的场景及其变化。作为计算机视觉中的一个核心问题，视觉目标跟踪旨在使机器感知更接近人类的视觉感知。这些能力是通用目标跟踪（GOT）任务的核心。

In this task, a visual tracker is initialized only with the bounding box of an arbitrarily specified target in the first frame, and must continuously localize the target in subsequent dynamic visual streams. However, future events, observations, and real-world variations are inherently unpredictable; therefore, the model's generalization and online adaptation capabilities remain bottlenecks.

在该任务中，视觉跟踪器仅在第一帧中通过任意指定目标的边界框进行初始化，并必须在后续的动态视觉流中持续定位该目标。然而，未来的事件、观察结果和现实世界的变化本质上是不可预测的；因此，模型的泛化能力和在线适应能力仍然是瓶颈。

Tracking reliability can deteriorate when the target undergoes severe deformation, is affected by complex distractors, encounters significant environmental changes, or belongs to a category unseen during training. This dissertation aims to narrow the gap between machine visual tracking systems and human visual perception by proposing a series of methods that systematically enhance the target discrimination, robust adaptation, and geometric reasoning capabilities of tracking models.

当目标发生严重形变、受到复杂干扰物的影响、遇到显著的环境变化，或属于训练中未见过的类别时，跟踪的可靠性可能会下降。本论文旨在通过提出一系列系统性增强跟踪模型的目标判别、稳健适应和几何推理能力的方法，来缩小机器视觉跟踪系统与人类视觉感知之间的差距。
