# Amazon updated 2023’s Fire HD 10 tablet with 4GB of RAM
# 亚马逊升级 2023 款 Fire HD 10 平板电脑，内存提升至 4GB

The 32GB model is now available with a bump from 3GB to 4GB of RAM with a small price increase.
32GB 版本现已上市，内存从 3GB 提升至 4GB，价格也略有上涨。

The Fire HD 8 that launched in 2024 was the last new addition to Amazon’s budget-minded tablet lineup, but the company has quietly updated the Fire HD 10 that debuted the year before. In 2023 it was offered with multiple storage configurations that each came with 3GB of RAM, but the 32GB version now ships with 4GB of RAM, and a small price bump from $139.99 to $154.99.
2024 年推出的 Fire HD 8 是亚马逊经济型平板电脑产品线中的最新成员，但该公司近期悄然更新了前一年发布的 Fire HD 10。2023 年款机型提供多种存储配置，且均配备 3GB 内存；而现在，32GB 版本已升级为 4GB 内存，价格也从 139.99 美元小幅上调至 154.99 美元。

The Fire HD 10 with 64GB of storage still only comes with 3GB of RAM and the other specs for both tablets remain the same, including a 10.1-inch, 1,920 x 1,200 display, a 2GHz eight-core processor, a 13-hour battery, and expandable storage through a microSD card. The refreshed version is also only available for purchase with lock screen ads, but those can be removed after the fact by paying a one-time fee. If you’ve been eyeing this tablet but don’t want to pay a $15 premium for it, Amazon still sells the original 3GB/32GB model for $139.99, but stock may sell out.
64GB 存储版本的 Fire HD 10 仍仅配备 3GB 内存，两款平板电脑的其他规格保持不变，包括 10.1 英寸、1920 x 1200 分辨率的显示屏、2GHz 八核处理器、13 小时续航电池以及可通过 microSD 卡扩展的存储空间。新款机型目前仅提供带有锁屏广告的版本，但用户事后可以通过支付一次性费用来移除广告。如果你一直关注这款平板电脑，但不想多付 15 美元，亚马逊目前仍以 139.99 美元的价格销售原版 3GB/32GB 机型，但库存可能会售罄。

Amazon hasn’t divulged the reason for the update, but the RAM crisis caused by a boom in data center construction for AI is affecting the availability of memory chips. Valve’s Steam Machine uses a single 16GB stick of RAM instead of the pair of 8GB sticks that was originally announced as an option. As AFTVnews points out, Amazon could also be having trouble sourcing the hardware these tablets originally used.
亚马逊尚未透露此次更新的原因，但人工智能数据中心建设热潮导致的内存危机正在影响存储芯片的供应。Valve 的 Steam Machine 此前将原本宣布的“双 8GB 内存条”方案改为使用单根 16GB 内存条。正如 AFTVnews 所指出的，亚马逊可能也在采购这些平板电脑最初使用的硬件时遇到了困难。
