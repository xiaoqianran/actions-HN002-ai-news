# Multilayer Q-Matrix-Embedded Neural Network for Cognitive Diagnosis (M-QCDNet): Structure-Aware Deep Learning Architecture for Psychometric Interpretability
# 多层 Q 矩阵嵌入式认知诊断神经网络 (M-QCDNet)：一种用于心理测量可解释性的结构感知深度学习架构

**Abstract:** The research proposes a multilayer Q-matrix-embedded neural network for cognitive diagnosis (M-QCDNet), which integrates the structural interpretability of cognitive diagnostic models (CDMs) with the deep learning neural network (NN). 
**摘要：** 本研究提出了一种用于认知诊断的多层 Q 矩阵嵌入式神经网络 (M-QCDNet)，它将认知诊断模型 (CDM) 的结构可解释性与深度学习神经网络 (NN) 相结合。

M-QCDNet structures the item-skill relationship using the Q-matrix as a structural prior, ensuring latent mastery profiles remain interpretable and consistent with cognitive theory, followed by the proposed loss function with an L2 penalty to penalize skills not aligned with the Q-matrix and to balance predictive performance and structural alignment. 
M-QCDNet 利用 Q 矩阵作为结构先验来构建题目与技能之间的关系，确保潜在掌握度概况（latent mastery profiles）保持可解释性并符合认知理论；随后，通过提出的带有 L2 惩罚项的损失函数，对不符合 Q 矩阵的技能进行惩罚，从而平衡预测性能与结构一致性。

Corresponding evaluation matrices, the interpretable alignment-based metrics that quantify the degree to which predicted skill activations correspond to item-level skills, were further developed. 
此外，研究还开发了相应的评估矩阵，即基于可解释对齐的指标，用于量化预测的技能激活与题目级技能之间的对应程度。

M-QCDNet offers practical benefits for classroom practice, enabling early detection of learning difficulties and supporting mastery-based interventions. 
M-QCDNet 为课堂实践提供了实际益处，能够实现学习困难的早期发现，并支持基于掌握度的干预措施。

By embedding diagnostic validity into model design, M-QCDNet bridges psychometric transparency and neural flexibility, advancing interpretable, fair, and actionable AI for cognitive diagnostics. 
通过将诊断有效性嵌入模型设计中，M-QCDNet 架起了心理测量透明度与神经网络灵活性之间的桥梁，推动了认知诊断领域中可解释、公平且可操作的 AI 发展。
