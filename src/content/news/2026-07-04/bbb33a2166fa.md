# Mark Zuckerberg tells staff that AI agents haven’t progressed as quickly as he’d hoped
# 马克·扎克伯格告诉员工：AI 智能体的进展未达预期

Replacing people with AI doesn’t seem to be that easy to do, if Meta can be seen as an example. Reuters reports that at an internal town hall Thursday, CEO Mark Zuckerberg told staff that the pace of AI agent development had not “accelerated in the way” executives had previously expected them to.
如果以 Meta 为例，用人工智能取代人类似乎并非易事。据路透社报道，在周四举行的一次内部全体员工大会上，首席执行官马克·扎克伯格告诉员工，AI 智能体的开发速度并没有像高管们此前预期的那样“加速”。

Earlier this year, Meta laid off some 8,000 employees — approximately 10% of its corporate workforce — and reassigned another 7,000 to various AI groups, including one called Agent Transformation, Bloomberg reported. During this week’s meeting, Zuckerberg apparently commented on these job cuts — noting that they were not as “clean” as they should have been.
据彭博社报道，今年早些时候，Meta 裁减了约 8000 名员工（约占其企业员工总数的 10%），并将另外 7000 名员工重新分配到各个 AI 部门，其中包括一个名为“智能体转型”（Agent Transformation）的团队。在本周的会议上，扎克伯格显然对这些裁员发表了评论，指出裁员过程并不像预期的那样“干净利落”。

The cuts were made because top officials at the company “were worried that we weren’t going to move fast enough to adapt” to the changing landscape of the tech industry, Zuckerberg reportedly added. The corporate leader also apparently said that the perceived upside of the new AI-focused company structure hadn’t “come to fruition yet,” although he said that he believed the company would begin to see improvements from its AI investments during the next three to six months.
据报道，扎克伯格补充说，进行裁员是因为公司高层“担心我们无法足够快地行动以适应”科技行业不断变化的格局。这位企业领导人还表示，新的以 AI 为中心的公司结构所预期的优势“尚未实现”，但他同时表示，相信公司在未来三到六个月内将开始看到 AI 投资带来的改善。

Several other investigative reports have depicted Meta’s months-old AI unit as a soul-crushing gulag, according to some of the engineers assigned to it. Meta has invested heavily in AI and is expected to spend as much as $145 billion on AI infrastructure this year, Reuters reports. TechCrunch reached out to Meta for comment.
据一些被分配到该部门的工程师称，其他几篇调查报道将 Meta 成立数月的 AI 部门描述为令人窒息的“古拉格”（劳改营）。据路透社报道，Meta 在 AI 领域投入了巨资，预计今年在 AI 基础设施上的支出将高达 1450 亿美元。TechCrunch 已联系 Meta 请求置评。
