# MapDreamer: Aerial Imagery Conditioned Latent Diffusion for Lane-Level Map Generation
# MapDreamer：基于航空影像条件潜空间扩散的车道级地图生成

High definition map generation is essential for autonomous driving, yet remains a labor-intensive process at scale. We present MapDreamer, a generative diffusion model that synthesizes lane-level vector maps with explicit topology directly from a single aerial image.

高精地图生成对于自动驾驶至关重要，但大规模制作过程仍然非常耗费人力。我们提出了 MapDreamer，这是一种生成式扩散模型，能够直接从单张航空影像中合成具有明确拓扑结构的车道级矢量地图。

MapDreamer learns a compact latent representation of lane centerlines and their topological relations using a variational autoencoder and predicts graphs with a transformer-based latent diffusion model. To align generated maps with the observed scene, we condition each denoising step on dense aerial features injected through cross-attention.

MapDreamer 利用变分自编码器学习车道中心线及其拓扑关系的紧凑潜空间表示，并使用基于 Transformer 的潜空间扩散模型来预测图结构。为了使生成的地图与观测场景对齐，我们在每个去噪步骤中引入了通过交叉注意力机制注入的密集航空特征作为条件。

To handle the varying number of lanes across scenes, we propose a lane cardinality module paired with background ghost lane latents, a learned buffer that prevents slot collapse during diffusion. Furthermore, we introduce a sliding-window global graph aggregation strategy that stitches local tiles into city-scale maps while preserving connectivity through encoded lane boundaries.

为了处理不同场景中车道数量的变化，我们提出了一个车道基数模块，并配合背景“幽灵车道”潜变量（一种学习到的缓冲区），以防止扩散过程中的槽位坍缩。此外，我们引入了一种滑动窗口全局图聚合策略，通过编码的车道边界来保持连通性，从而将局部图块拼接成城市规模的地图。

Experiments on UrbanLaneGraph derived from Argoverse 2 show improved geometric and topological fidelity over non-generative baselines.

在基于 Argoverse 2 构建的 UrbanLaneGraph 数据集上的实验表明，该方法在几何和拓扑保真度方面均优于非生成式基准模型。
