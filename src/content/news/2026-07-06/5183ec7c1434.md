### Toku Reader

**Toku Reader**

Toku turns native Japanese and Chinese — articles, novels, podcasts, and YouTube videos — into something you can actually read. 
Toku 将日语和中文的原生内容（如文章、小说、播客和 YouTube 视频）转化为你可以轻松阅读的材料。

Tap any word for its reading, meaning, and dictionary, without leaving the page. 
点击任何单词即可查看其读音、释义和词典解释，无需离开当前页面。

On audio and video you get a synced, word-tappable transcript: tap to learn, slow it down, replay a line, or pause after each sentence to shadow it back. 
在音频和视频内容中，你将获得同步且可点击的逐字文本：点击即可学习、放慢语速、重播某一行，或在每句话后暂停以进行跟读练习。

It runs its own JP/CN engine on-device with offline dictionaries — fast, private, no accounts, no streaks. Just reading.
它在设备端运行自有的日/中语言引擎，并配备离线词典——快速、隐私、无需账户、无需打卡。纯粹的阅读体验。
