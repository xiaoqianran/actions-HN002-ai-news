# 5 desk gadgets that can make your workday better
# 5 款能让你的工作日更高效的桌面小工具

We spend dozens of hours at our desks each week (if not more), so it’s worth creating a workspace that’s productive and enjoyable. The right desk gadgets can help you reduce clutter, stay focused, and add a little extra convenience to your day. From ambient lighting to smart mugs, there are lots of products designed to make your time at your desk better, so we’ve combined a list of some options. Whether you’re working from home or in an office, these gadgets can help transform your workspace.

我们每周在办公桌前花费的时间长达数十小时（甚至更多），因此打造一个高效且舒适的工作空间非常值得。合适的桌面小工具可以帮助你减少杂乱、保持专注，并为你的日常工作增添便利。从环境照明到智能马克杯，市面上有许多旨在改善办公体验的产品，我们整理了一份清单供你参考。无论你是居家办公还是在办公室工作，这些小工具都能帮助你改造工作空间。

### Odistar Desktop Vacuum Cleaner — $13
### Odistar 桌面吸尘器 — 13 美元

The Odistar desktop vacuum is an affordable option for keeping your desk clean and crumb-free, especially if you often eat lunch or snack at your desk. You can also use it to clean your keyboard and remove dust and debris between keys. It’s quiet and simple to use, and because it’s so compact, you can keep it on your desk or tuck it away in a drawer until you need it. It’s cordless and uses two AA batteries. At just $13, the desktop vacuum is a great way to keep your desk clean as you work.

Odistar 桌面吸尘器是一款经济实惠的选择，能让你的桌面保持整洁、无碎屑，特别适合经常在桌前吃午餐或零食的人。你还可以用它来清洁键盘，清除按键之间的灰尘和杂物。它运行安静且使用简单，由于体积小巧，你可以将其放在桌面上，或者在不用时收进抽屉里。它采用无线设计，使用两节 AA 电池供电。仅需 13 美元，这款桌面吸尘器是你在工作时保持桌面整洁的绝佳利器。

### Ember Mug 2 — $150
### Ember Mug 2 智能马克杯 — 150 美元

If you’re someone who enjoys hot coffee but often finds yourself drinking it cold after getting sidetracked while working, the Ember Mug 2 can help keep your drinks at the perfect temperature. You can set an exact temperature using your phone and the smart mug maintains it for up to one and a half hours with the 10-ounce version and up to 80 minutes with the 12-ounce version. The mug allows for a more consistent coffee experience, making your workday more enjoyable.

如果你喜欢喝热咖啡，却经常因为工作忙碌而等到咖啡变凉才喝，那么 Ember Mug 2 可以帮你将饮品保持在完美的温度。你可以通过手机设置精确的温度，这款智能马克杯可以持续保温：10 盎司版本可保温长达一个半小时，12 盎司版本可保温长达 80 分钟。这款马克杯能带来更稳定的咖啡饮用体验，让你的工作日更加愉悦。

### Amazon Echo Dot — $50
### Amazon Echo Dot 智能音箱 — 50 美元

The Amazon Echo Dot can be a great addition to your work desk as you can use voice commands to set reminders, create to-do lists, check your calendar, play music, adjust smart lights, or get quick answers to questions without having to reach for your phone. Since it’s small, you can easily place it anywhere on your desk where it can act as a hands-free assistant that helps keep you organized and focused throughout your workday.

Amazon Echo Dot 是办公桌上的绝佳助手，你可以通过语音指令设置提醒、创建待办事项列表、查看日历、播放音乐、调节智能灯光，或者快速获取问题答案，而无需拿起手机。由于它体积小巧，你可以轻松将其放置在桌面的任何位置，作为免提助手，帮助你在整个工作日保持条理和专注。

### Govee Glide Hexa Light Panels — $189.99
### Govee Glide Hexa 六边形灯板 — 189.99 美元

The Govee Glide Hexa Light Panels are a simple and fun way to add some personality to your work desk setup without taking up any actual desk space. The hexagon-shaped panels mount on the wall behind your monitor and can display different colors and customizable lighting effects through the Govee app. They can be used to create a focused atmosphere during work hours and can be switched to more dynamic effects when gaming or taking a break. The panels can be arranged in different patterns to match your style.

Govee Glide Hexa 六边形灯板是一种简单有趣的方式，可以在不占用桌面空间的情况下，为你的工作台增添个性。这些六边形灯板可以安装在显示器背后的墙面上，并通过 Govee App 显示不同的颜色和可自定义的灯光效果。它们可以在工作时间营造专注的氛围，在游戏或休息时切换到更具动感的灯效。灯板还可以排列成不同的图案，以匹配你的个人风格。

### Speks — $35
### Speks 磁力球 — 35 美元

If you’re someone who hasn’t yet delved into the world of fidget toys but often finds yourself playing with random objects or even wrappers during calls or meetings, Speks might be for you. They’re tiny magnets that you can fidget or build with to keep your brain focused and your hands busy. Instead of fidgeting with whatever happens to be within reach, you can have something that is actually made for the job and is more satisfying to have in your hands. They can help you stay focused and relieve stress throughout your workday.

如果你还没接触过解压玩具，但经常在通话或会议期间摆弄手边的杂物甚至包装纸，那么 Speks 可能很适合你。它们是一组微小的磁铁，你可以通过把玩或拼搭它们来保持大脑专注并让双手忙碌起来。与其摆弄手边随手可得的杂物，不如拥有一个专门为此设计、手感更令人满足的小工具。它们可以帮助你在整个工作日保持专注并缓解压力。
