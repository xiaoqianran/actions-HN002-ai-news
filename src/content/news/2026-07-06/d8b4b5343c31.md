### Best Wi-Fi 7 Router: Asus RT-BE96U
### 最佳Wi-Fi 7路由器：Asus RT-BE96U

If your budget isn't fixed, then the Asus RT-BE96U is calling. This enormous, tri-band, Wi-Fi 7 router is bristling with eight large antennas and is not easy to mount, so you will need a suitable space for it. It's seriously massive, but performance-wise, this is as good as it gets right now. Testing on the 2.4-GHz, 5-GHz, and 6-GHz bands, only one other router has performed better, and it was the GT-BE98 Pro below, also from Asus.

如果你的预算充足，那么Asus RT-BE96U绝对值得考虑。这款巨大的三频Wi-Fi 7路由器配备了八根大型天线，安装并不容易，因此你需要为它准备一个合适的空间。它的体积确实非常庞大，但在性能方面，这已经是目前市面上的顶尖水平。在2.4 GHz、5 GHz和6 GHz频段的测试中，只有另一款路由器的表现更好，那就是下文提到的同样来自华硕的GT-BE98 Pro。

The RT-BE96U offers expansive coverage, easily blanketing my home and garden with stable, speedy Wi-Fi. Whether downloading or moving large files, gaming, or streaming, the RT-BE96U is fast and reliable. It also has two 10-Gbps ports (one WAN/LAN and a LAN), four gigabit LANs, one USB 3.2, and a USB 2.0 port. Being super picky, it might have been nice if those gigabit ports were 2.5 Gbps, as TP-Link offers in the BE800.

RT-BE96U提供了广阔的覆盖范围，轻松覆盖了我的整个家庭和花园，提供稳定、高速的Wi-Fi。无论是下载或移动大文件、玩游戏还是流媒体播放，RT-BE96U都表现得快速且可靠。它还拥有两个10 Gbps端口（一个WAN/LAN和一个LAN）、四个千兆LAN口、一个USB 3.2接口和一个USB 2.0接口。如果非要挑剔的话，如果那些千兆端口能升级为2.5 Gbps（就像TP-Link在BE800中提供的那样）就更好了。

I love that Asus continues to avoid the subscription model, offering AiProtection security, VPN functionality, and robust parental controls as part of the purchase price and free for the lifetime of the router. You also get an in-depth set of networking features you can dig into, and there is support for AiMesh, enabling you to link this with other Asus routers to create a mesh. You don’t even have to create an account to use this router (a rarity nowadays).

我非常喜欢华硕坚持不采用订阅模式的做法，它将AiProtection安全防护、VPN功能和强大的家长控制功能包含在购买价格中，并提供终身免费使用。你还可以深入挖掘一套详尽的网络功能，并且它支持AiMesh，使你可以将其与其他华硕路由器连接以组建Mesh网络。你甚至不需要创建账户就能使用这款路由器（这在当今非常罕见）。

### A More Affordable Wi-Fi 7 Router: TP-Link Archer BE9700 (BE600)
### 更实惠的Wi-Fi 7路由器：TP-Link Archer BE9700 (BE600)

The TP-Link Archer BE9700 offers 2.4-GHz, 5-GHz, and 6-GHz bands for less. I don’t love the design, but the six adjustable antennas allow for some tinkering, and TP-Link has been very generous with the ports (one 10 Gbps, four 2.5 Gbps, and a USB 3.0), though it’s a little annoying that the USB is on the side. This model also offers excellent close-range speeds on the 6-GHz band, though it was a little disappointing on both the 5-GHz and 2.4-GHz bands (TP-Link’s dual-band BE5000 actually outperformed it). But it does offer a pretty expansive range (up to 2,600 square feet).

TP-Link Archer BE9700以更低的价格提供了2.4 GHz、5 GHz和6 GHz频段。我不喜欢它的设计，但六根可调节天线允许进行一些调整。TP-Link在端口配置上非常慷慨（一个10 Gbps端口、四个2.5 Gbps端口和一个USB 3.0接口），尽管USB接口位于侧面有点令人烦恼。该型号在6 GHz频段的近距离速度表现出色，但在5 GHz和2.4 GHz频段的表现略显失望（TP-Link的双频BE5000实际上表现更好）。不过，它确实提供了相当广阔的覆盖范围（高达2600平方英尺）。
