# Almost 90 new unicorns have been minted so far this year — here they are
# 今年迄今已有近 90 家新独角兽诞生——名单如下

With AI igniting an investor frenzy, more startups are achieving unicorn status every month. Using data from Crunchbase and PitchBook, TechCrunch tracked down the VC-backed startups that became unicorns in 2026. While most are AI-related, a surprising number are focused on other industries like healthcare and even a few crypto companies. This list will be updated throughout the year.
随着人工智能点燃了投资者的狂热，每个月都有更多的初创公司达到独角兽地位。TechCrunch 利用 Crunchbase 和 PitchBook 的数据，追踪了 2026 年成为独角兽的风险投资支持的初创公司。虽然大多数与人工智能相关，但也有相当一部分公司专注于医疗保健等其他行业，甚至还有几家加密货币公司。此列表将在全年持续更新。

**六月**

Founded in 2023, it last raised a $485 million Series B in a round led by Lg Technology Ventures, SBI Investment, and Emergency Equity Management, according to Pitchbook. It has raised a total of $645 million in funding to date, with other investors in the company including AWS.
**MainFunc — 26 亿美元。** 这家初创公司提供名为 Genspark 的人工智能工作空间。据 Pitchbook 数据显示，该公司成立于 2023 年，最近一轮融资由 Lg Technology Ventures、SBI Investment 和 Emergency Equity Management 领投，筹集了 4.85 亿美元的 B 轮资金。迄今为止，该公司已累计融资 6.45 亿美元，其他投资者还包括 AWS。
