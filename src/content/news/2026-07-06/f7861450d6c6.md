### Reverse Engineering is so cool
### 逆向工程太酷了

I want to share some thoughts about Reverse Engineering. I love JavaScript and TypeScript, but sometimes I don't want to create something, but the opposite, to understand how others programs work.
我想分享一些关于逆向工程的想法。我热爱 JavaScript 和 TypeScript，但有时我不想去创造什么，而是恰恰相反，我想去了解其他程序是如何运作的。

Working with IDA and x64dbg is the best thing in the world. Btw, at the beginning I used Ghidra, but after I tried IDA, I liked it more.
使用 IDA 和 x64dbg 是世界上最棒的事情。顺便说一下，起初我使用的是 Ghidra，但在尝试了 IDA 之后，我更喜欢它了。

What do you think, what tool is better, IDA or Ghidra? 🤗
你觉得呢？IDA 和 Ghidra 哪个工具更好？🤗
