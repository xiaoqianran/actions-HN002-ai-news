# The whole Pixel line could get more expensive this year
# 今年整个 Pixel 产品线价格可能全面上涨

Google’s upcoming Pixel lineup might cost more than last year’s. A report from Dealabs spotted by 9to5Google suggests that Google could raise the starting price of its 41mm Pixel Watch 5 to $399, while adding LTE could bump the price to $499.
谷歌即将推出的 Pixel 产品线价格可能高于去年。据 9to5Google 发现的一份 Dealabs 报告显示，谷歌可能会将 41 毫米版 Pixel Watch 5 的起售价提高至 399 美元，而增加 LTE 功能后的价格则可能升至 499 美元。

That’s a $50 jump from the base Pixel Watch 4, which starts at $349 for Wi-Fi and $449 for LTE. Meanwhile, the larger 45mm Pixel Watch 5 is rumored to cost $429 with Wi-Fi only, or $529 with LTE, marking a $30 increase from the previous generation.
这比基础款 Pixel Watch 4 的价格上涨了 50 美元（Wi-Fi 版起售价为 349 美元，LTE 版为 449 美元）。与此同时，传闻更大尺寸的 45 毫米版 Pixel Watch 5 Wi-Fi 版售价为 429 美元，LTE 版为 529 美元，较上一代产品上涨了 30 美元。

A separate report from Dealabs details the potential European pricing for the Pixel 11. The report suggests that Google could drop the lowest 128GB storage configuration option for the Pixel 11, raising the device’s starting price to €999 with 256GB of storage, while the Pixel 11 Pro could start at €1,199. Last year’s base Pixel 10 model cost €899 with 128GB of storage, while the Pixel 10 Pro started at €1,099. Although the starting price of both devices may increase, the price of the 256GB configuration is rumored to remain the same.
另一份来自 Dealabs 的报告详细介绍了 Pixel 11 在欧洲的潜在定价。报告指出，谷歌可能会取消 Pixel 11 最低 128GB 的存储配置选项，将该设备的起售价提高至 999 欧元（配备 256GB 存储空间），而 Pixel 11 Pro 的起售价可能为 1,199 欧元。去年的基础款 Pixel 10（128GB）售价为 899 欧元，Pixel 10 Pro 起售价为 1,099 欧元。尽管这两款设备的起售价可能会上涨，但传闻 256GB 配置的价格将保持不变。

The Pixel 11 Pro XL and Pixel 11 Pro Fold may get a €100 increase across the board, putting their starting prices at €1,399 and €1,999, respectively, according to Dealabs. These potential price increases shouldn’t come as a surprise, as device makers across industries are contending with a global memory and storage shortage due to increased demand from AI companies.
据 Dealabs 称，Pixel 11 Pro XL 和 Pixel 11 Pro Fold 的价格可能会全面上涨 100 欧元，起售价分别达到 1,399 欧元和 1,999 欧元。这些潜在的价格上涨并不令人意外，因为受人工智能公司需求增加的影响，各行业的设备制造商都在应对全球内存和存储短缺的问题。

Several companies have already raised the prices of their devices or axed certain memory or storage variations. A previous rumor suggested that the Pixel 11 could start with 8GB of RAM instead of last year’s 12GB as a result of the shortage.
多家公司已经提高了设备价格，或削减了某些内存或存储版本。此前有传言称，受短缺影响，Pixel 11 的起步内存可能会从去年的 12GB 降至 8GB。

Google will take the wraps off its new Pixel devices at a launch event on August 12th.
谷歌将于 8 月 12 日举行发布会，届时将正式揭晓其新款 Pixel 设备。
