# Exogenous Dropout: A Simple, Strong Baseline for Corruption-Robust Time Series Forecasting with Covariates
# 外源性丢弃（Exogenous Dropout）：一种用于具有协变量的时间序列预测的简单且强大的鲁棒性基准

**Abstract:** Time series forecasters that use exogenous covariates are fragile in deployment: when those covariates are noised, temporally misaligned, or missing, strong exogenous-fusion and exogenous-adapted models can degrade far above the endogenous-only floor. We study whether such robustness requires specialized architectures, or whether it can be obtained through a simple training intervention.

**摘要：** 使用外源性协变量的时间序列预测模型在实际部署中非常脆弱：当这些协变量出现噪声、时间对齐偏差或缺失时，强大的外源融合模型和外源自适应模型的性能可能会大幅下降，甚至远低于仅使用内源数据时的基准水平。我们研究了这种鲁棒性是否需要专门的架构设计，还是可以通过简单的训练干预来实现。

We propose exogenous dropout, a model-agnostic method that randomly zeros whole exogenous channels during training. Across electricity-price forecasting, reservoir hydrology, and meteorology, exogenous dropout substantially improves robustness under Gaussian noise, temporal misalignment, and fully missing channels, while preserving clean accuracy.

我们提出了“外源性丢弃”（Exogenous Dropout），这是一种与模型无关的方法，在训练过程中随机将整个外源通道置零。在电力价格预测、水库水文学和气象学等领域的实验表明，外源性丢弃在面对高斯噪声、时间对齐偏差和通道完全缺失的情况下，显著提高了模型的鲁棒性，同时保持了在干净数据上的预测精度。

Applied to a dual-correlation network, it yields the most robust model in our experiments, outperforming a deliberately strong bounded architectural foil, BoundEx, which combines a learnable gate, a fallback residual to the endogenous backbone, and per-channel exogenous FiLM modulation.

将其应用于双相关网络（dual-correlation network）后，它成为了我们实验中最鲁棒的模型，性能超过了精心设计的强约束架构 BoundEx。BoundEx 结合了可学习门控、指向内源主干的残差回退机制以及逐通道的外源 FiLM 调制。

Architecture-by-dropout ablations, gate-behavior diagnostics, and a representation-level bound show that explicit architectural boundedness is not necessary for this robustness: an unbounded model trained with exogenous dropout is more robust than the bounded model in every domain.

通过架构与丢弃机制的消融实验、门控行为诊断以及表征层面的界限分析，我们发现实现这种鲁棒性并不需要显式的架构约束：在所有测试领域中，使用外源性丢弃训练的无约束模型都比有约束模型表现得更鲁棒。

We release a corruption-robustness benchmark and recommend exogenous dropout as a simple, strong baseline for future work on time series forecasting with covariates.

我们发布了一个抗腐蚀鲁棒性基准测试，并建议将外源性丢弃作为未来研究具有协变量的时间序列预测任务时，一个简单且强大的基准方法。
