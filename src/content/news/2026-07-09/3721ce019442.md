# Binocular Gaze Estimation with Single Camera and Single Light Source
# 基于单摄像头与单光源的双目视线估计

**Abstract:** According to commonly consented theories, the minimum hardware requirement for gaze tracker is one camera and two light sources to realize gaze estimation with free head movements. However, in some scenarios such as eye tracking on mobile devices, it is preferable to use less components, especially light sources.

**摘要：** 根据公认的理论，在实现头部自由移动的视线追踪时，视线追踪器的最低硬件要求是一个摄像头和两个光源。然而，在移动设备眼动追踪等某些场景中，使用更少的组件（尤其是光源）更为理想。

We propose a gaze estimation method with one camera and one light source. A "virtual light source" is introduced, which is geometrically placed symmetrically to the real light source with respect to the camera, and generates a "virtual glint" in the acquired image.

我们提出了一种仅使用一个摄像头和一个光源的视线估计方法。该方法引入了一个“虚拟光源”，其在几何位置上相对于摄像头与真实光源对称，并能在获取的图像中产生一个“虚拟光斑”。

We estimate the "virtual glint" by exploiting the relationship between the distance between two pupils and two glints in the captured image, and estimate the gaze with polynomial regression assuming two light sources are available. A new normalization factor for regression method is verified, which turns out to be practical for one-glint system. The performance is proved to be acceptable, while degradation is noticed compared to system with two actual light sources.

我们通过利用捕获图像中双瞳孔与双光斑之间的距离关系来估计“虚拟光斑”，并假设存在两个光源的情况下，利用多项式回归来估计视线。我们验证了一种用于回归方法的新归一化因子，证明其对于单光斑系统是实用的。实验结果表明，该方法的性能在可接受范围内，但与使用两个真实光源的系统相比，性能有所下降。
