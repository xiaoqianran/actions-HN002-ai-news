# Ground3D-LMM: Fine-Grained 3D Point Grounding and Spatial Reasoning with LMM
# Ground3D-LMM：基于多模态大模型的细粒度 3D 点定位与空间推理

**Abstract:** Natural-language queries about 3D environments become actionable when responses are verifiable and metric. Verifiability requires explicit grounding to the referred 3D region, while metric answers report physical measurements in real-world units (e.g., size, thickness, clearance, and distance).
**摘要：** 当针对 3D 环境的自然语言查询能够提供可验证且具备度量意义的回答时，它们才具有实际应用价值。可验证性要求模型能够明确地定位到所指的 3D 区域，而度量性回答则要求以真实世界单位（如尺寸、厚度、间隙和距离）报告物理测量结果。

Existing 3D large multimodal models (LMMs) approaches remain limited: conversational systems typically respond without explicit 3D grounding, while 3D grounding models are not designed for interactive, metric-aware dialogue.
现有的 3D 多模态大模型（LMM）方法仍存在局限性：对话系统通常在没有明确 3D 定位的情况下进行回答，而 3D 定位模型则并非为交互式、具备度量意识的对话而设计。

In this paper, we present Ground3D-LMM, a unified model that takes a point cloud and an optional RGB image as input and supports 3D spatial conversation with (i) point-grounded responses and (ii) metric numeric outputs at both object and part granularity, including multi-object queries.
在本文中，我们提出了 Ground3D-LMM，这是一个统一模型，它以点云和可选的 RGB 图像作为输入，支持 3D 空间对话，并具备以下能力：(i) 基于点的定位响应；(ii) 在物体和部件粒度上提供度量数值输出，包括针对多物体的查询。

To evaluate this intersection of grounding and measurement, we define the 3D Grounded Measurement task, which requires predicting the referred 3D region and the corresponding metric quantities in real-world units.
为了评估定位与测量这一交叉领域，我们定义了“3D 定位测量”（3D Grounded Measurement）任务，该任务要求预测所指的 3D 区域以及以真实世界单位表示的相应度量数值。

We introduce a large-scale dataset built on ScanNet and ScanNet++ datasets with dense object and part annotations and roughly 2.5M question-answer pairs spanning eight tasks, along with a manually verified test set.
我们引入了一个基于 ScanNet 和 ScanNet++ 数据集构建的大规模数据集，其中包含密集的物体和部件标注，以及涵盖八项任务的约 250 万个问答对，并附带一个经人工验证的测试集。

Extensive experiments on multiple datasets and tasks show that our proposed Ground3D-LMM model provides a strong baseline for grounded, metric-aware 3D conversational understanding. Our dataset and model are publicly available.
在多个数据集和任务上的广泛实验表明，我们提出的 Ground3D-LMM 模型为具备定位能力和度量意识的 3D 对话理解提供了一个强有力的基准。我们的数据集和模型已公开。
