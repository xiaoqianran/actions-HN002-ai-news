# Information Theory and Ensemble Models
# 信息论与集成模型

**How should we ensemble time-series forecasts better?**
**我们该如何更好地集成时间序列预测？**

Coming out of the pandemic, statisticians have run into a slew of geo-political complications further augmenting the inability to forecast business variables with accuracy. Did Ukraine drive retail prices higher in 2022 or was it the quantitative easing in 2021 that was the culprit? Some models say one thing and some say another, making it hard to accurately forecast inflation.
疫情过后，统计学家们遇到了一系列地缘政治复杂问题，这些问题进一步加剧了准确预测商业变量的难度。是乌克兰局势推高了2022年的零售价格，还是2021年的量化宽松政策才是罪魁祸首？不同的模型给出了不同的结论，这使得准确预测通胀变得十分困难。

At the very foundation of econometrics, we rely on minimizing distance (MSE, RMSE, etc) between two points (forecast, actual) in the same domain (time, frequency). All of these have served the community immensely to improve the accuracy of forecasts. There are a few reasons why these metrics are popular: They are non-parametric. Meaning models developed using different assumptions and structures can all be compared since the end output is the same – accuracy.
在计量经济学的最底层，我们依赖于最小化同一领域（时间、频率）中两个点（预测值、实际值）之间的距离（如 MSE、RMSE 等）。所有这些指标在很大程度上帮助业界提高了预测的准确性。这些指标之所以受欢迎，有几个原因：它们是非参数化的。这意味着使用不同假设和结构开发出的模型都可以进行比较，因为最终的输出结果是一样的——即准确性。

Historically, it has been true that these metrics offer enough variation in residual distribution for us to classify different models and cluster their performance. For instance, they help us answer if a class of ARMA models better suits the data or a class of state space models. More generally, it helps classify best fit algorithms. Often, they sit in euclidean geometries and have nice properties that make building newer and more sophisticated methods on top of them much easier.
从历史上看，这些指标确实在残差分布中提供了足够的差异，使我们能够对不同的模型进行分类并对其性能进行聚类。例如，它们能帮助我们判断是 ARMA 模型类更适合数据，还是状态空间模型类更适合。更广泛地说，它有助于对最佳拟合算法进行分类。通常，它们位于欧几里得几何空间中，并具有良好的特性，使得在其基础上构建更新、更复杂的模型变得更加容易。

The topology of measurement does not change. This unlocks the potential for making transformations and representations of the data, the model, or both to extract deeper relationships. Modern day ML models are built by exploiting this key property. WLOG, we’ll focus on simple econometric models for the purposes of this article. These benefits have helped improve statistical packages since the late 40s so much so that today we have more general packages that are able to select best fit models without the user ever having to assume a structure.
测量的拓扑结构没有改变。这释放了对数据、模型或两者进行转换和表征以提取更深层关系的潜力。现代机器学习模型正是利用了这一关键特性构建的。为了不失一般性（WLOG），本文将重点讨论简单的计量经济学模型。自 40 年代后期以来，这些优势极大地促进了统计软件包的改进，以至于今天我们拥有了更通用的软件包，用户无需预设结构即可选择最佳拟合模型。

However, as is true for any situation, continuing to optimize the same metrics offers fewer and fewer improvements with every iteration. Metrics measuring distance can no longer offer enough separation between optimized models making it hard to rank performance. So what do we do?
然而，正如任何情况一样，持续优化相同的指标，其边际收益会越来越少。衡量距离的指标已无法在优化后的模型之间提供足够的区分度，导致难以对性能进行排名。那么我们该怎么办？

Potential solutions can come from 2 different paths:
潜在的解决方案可以来自两条不同的路径：

1. Coming up with new metrics within the same topology that can improve best fit classification – the community has come up with and extensively uses new metrics like AIC, AICc, BIC, BICc, and so on but these metrics are often model specific in that they might allow us to rank ARMA models within each other but cannot compare ARMA vs. ETS models, for instance.
1. 在相同的拓扑结构内提出新的指标，以改进最佳拟合的分类——业界已经提出并广泛使用了 AIC、AICc、BIC、BICc 等新指标，但这些指标通常是针对特定模型的。例如，它们可能允许我们在 ARMA 模型之间进行排名，但无法比较 ARMA 与 ETS 模型。

2. Coming up with new geometries and topology for improved methods – I explore a version of this idea through granger causal networks which is very much a work in progress but continues to show immense potential (perhaps I’m biased given my interest in the topic). Interested readers can find a primer on my approach here.
2. 为改进方法提出新的几何和拓扑结构——我通过格兰杰因果网络（Granger causal networks）探索了这一想法的一个版本，这虽然仍处于研究阶段，但持续展现出巨大的潜力（考虑到我对该主题的兴趣，我可能存在偏见）。感兴趣的读者可以在此处找到我方法的入门介绍。

Let’s first start by contextualizing the arguments so far on actual data. I’m going to analyse inflationary trends and variables and how accurately we can forecast CPI using existing models. Just about every econometrician is bound to use the following variables when considering an inflation model:
让我们首先通过实际数据来阐述上述论点。我将分析通胀趋势和变量，以及我们使用现有模型预测 CPI 的准确程度。几乎每一位计量经济学家在考虑通胀模型时，都会使用以下变量：

* Consumer Price Index YoY Growth – measure of inflation for consumers; demand side
* 消费者价格指数（CPI）同比增速——衡量消费者的通胀情况；需求侧
* Producer Price Index YoY Growth – measure of inflation for producers; supply side
* 生产者价格指数（PPI）同比增速——衡量生产者的通胀情况；供给侧
* Savings Rate – % of earned income that is saved; measures friction on the demand side
* 储蓄率——收入中储蓄的百分比；衡量需求侧的摩擦
* Business Inventories MoM Growth – excess stock of goods that businesses have every month; measures friction on the supply side
* 企业库存环比增速——企业每月持有的过剩商品库存；衡量供给侧的摩擦

Already, the causal network helps explain a few key details about the data. First, savings rate can influence both the demand side and the supply side of the economy. Second, inflation for producers means some feedback into consumers. These are good sanity checks to make sure we’re considering the right variables.
因果网络已经有助于解释关于数据的一些关键细节。首先，储蓄率可以同时影响经济的需求侧和供给侧。其次，生产者的通胀意味着对消费者会有一定的反馈。这些是很好的合理性检查，以确保我们考虑了正确的变量。

深入一点，让我们拟合一个通用的非参数 VAR 模型，并考虑对非因果和随机诱导冲击的一些敏感性：

Plots show impulse-responses on a fit VAR model. On a tangential note, the first plot confirms the infamous Permanent Income Hypothesis, conceptualized by Milton Friedman in 1957: An unexpected increase in people’s savings today, say because of a decision by the government to hand out stimulus checks, leads to those excess savings being pumped into the economy in the following periods leading to demand pull inflation. It also causes the stock of business inventories to compress until they normalize to new levels again.
图表显示了拟合 VAR 模型上的脉冲响应。顺便提一下，第一张图证实了米尔顿·弗里德曼在 1957 年提出的著名的“持久收入假说”：今天人们储蓄的意外增加（例如由于政府发放刺激支票的决定），会导致这些过剩储蓄在随后的时期被注入经济，从而导致需求拉动型通胀。它还会导致企业库存压缩，直到它们恢复到新的水平。

We cannot persuade people to save more tomorrow, even if we increase their disposable income, if no explicit reason compels them to do so. Future expectations of consumers play a crucial role in policy judgements. Similarly, as producers experience higher costs of productions, evident through increases in PPI, they pass it onto the consumers leading to supply driven inflation as shown in the last plot. At least directionally, the VAR model confirms some economic theories.
如果我们没有明确的理由，即使增加人们的可支配收入，也无法说服他们明天多储蓄。消费者的未来预期在政策判断中起着至关重要的作用。同样，当生产者经历更高的生产成本（通过 PPI 的增加可以明显看出）时，他们会将成本转嫁给消费者，从而导致供给驱动型通胀，如最后一张图所示。至少在方向上，VAR 模型证实了一些经济理论。
