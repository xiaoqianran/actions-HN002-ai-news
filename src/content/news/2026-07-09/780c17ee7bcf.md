### Statistical Adversaries: Natural Backdoor-like Features in Vision Datasets
### 统计对抗样本：视觉数据集中的天然后门特征

**Abstract:** Model-specific adversarial attacks have been extensively studied. We study a different failure mode: naturally occurring statistical signals in vision data that can behave like backdoor-like triggers without being maliciously inserted. We call these signals statistical adversaries.
**摘要：** 模型特定的对抗攻击已得到广泛研究。我们研究了一种不同的失效模式：视觉数据中自然存在的统计信号，它们无需被恶意植入即可表现得像后门触发器。我们将这些信号称为“统计对抗样本”（statistical adversaries）。

We analyse Imagenet to find patterns that are strongly linked to certain labels. We then use statistical controls to remove random correlations from our candidate signals. Finally, we demonstrate that these signals directly and predictably alter model predictions.
我们分析了 ImageNet 以寻找与特定标签强相关的模式。随后，我们使用统计控制方法从候选信号中剔除了随机相关性。最后，我们证明了这些信号能够直接且可预测地改变模型的预测结果。

These statistical adversaries are more targeted than generic corruptions and transfer across different model architectures. This suggests that some vulnerabilities are driven by dataset structure and distribution rather than a single model's idiosyncrasies.
这些统计对抗样本比一般的损坏（corruptions）更具针对性，且能够在不同的模型架构之间迁移。这表明，某些漏洞是由数据集的结构和分布驱动的，而非单一模型的特性所致。

We conclude that ordinary datasets can contain exploitable adversarial surfaces even in the absence of poisoning, and suggest that dataset audits should treat spurious structure not only as a source of bias or interpretability failure, but also as a latent attack surface for vision models.
我们得出结论：即使在没有投毒的情况下，普通数据集也可能包含可被利用的对抗面。我们建议，数据集审计不仅应将虚假结构视为偏差或可解释性失效的来源，还应将其视为视觉模型的潜在攻击面。
