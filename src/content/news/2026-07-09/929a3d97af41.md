# A Task-Driven Evaluation of UAV Detection and Tracking under Synthetic Fog
# 基于任务驱动的合成雾环境下无人机检测与跟踪评估

**Abstract:** Fog severely degrades the visibility of small unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) in sky-dominant, long-range imagery, reducing the reliability of downstream detection and tracking. This paper presents a task-driven evaluation framework that links depth-aware synthetic fog generation, image restoration, object detection, and tracking within a unified pipeline.

**摘要：** 在以天空为主的远距离图像中，雾气会严重降低小型无人机（UAV）的可见度，从而降低下游检测和跟踪任务的可靠性。本文提出了一个任务驱动的评估框架，将深度感知合成雾生成、图像恢复、目标检测和跟踪链接在一个统一的流程中。

Given the practical difficulty of collecting and annotating foggy UAV scenes, synthetic fog is generated from real clear-weather outdoor images containing UAV targets using monocular depth estimation and the atmospheric scattering model. Representative restoration methods from classical, convolutional neural network (CNN)-based, and transformer-based families are first compared, after which the selected restoration model is integrated into the downstream perception pipeline.

鉴于收集和标注雾天无人机场景存在实际困难，本文利用单目深度估计和大气散射模型，从包含无人机目标的真实晴天户外图像中生成合成雾。研究首先对比了来自经典方法、卷积神经网络（CNN）和基于 Transformer 的代表性恢复方法，随后将选定的恢复模型集成到下游感知流程中。

Detection is evaluated under both clean-only and fog-inclusive training regimes using multiple detector variants, while tracking-by-detection is assessed on clean, foggy, and restored video sequences. Beyond image-level restoration metrics, the study evaluates how fog and restoration affect detection robustness and tracking performance.

研究在仅包含清晰图像和包含雾天图像的两种训练机制下，使用多种检测器变体对检测效果进行了评估，同时在清晰、雾天和恢复后的视频序列上评估了“检测即跟踪”（tracking-by-detection）的性能。除了图像级的恢复指标外，本研究还评估了雾气和图像恢复如何影响检测鲁棒性和跟踪性能。

The results show that fog substantially degrades both detection and tracking, primarily through increased missed detections. Fog-inclusive training provides the most consistent improvement in robustness, whereas test-time restoration is most beneficial when the detector has been trained only on clean imagery. These findings show that restoration quality does not necessarily translate into proportional gains in downstream perception and therefore should be evaluated jointly with detection and tracking performance.

结果表明，雾气主要通过增加漏检率，显著降低了检测和跟踪的性能。包含雾天的训练提供了最稳定的鲁棒性提升，而当检测器仅在清晰图像上进行训练时，测试时的图像恢复效果最为显著。这些发现表明，恢复质量并不一定能转化为下游感知性能的成比例提升，因此应将恢复质量与检测和跟踪性能结合起来进行综合评估。
