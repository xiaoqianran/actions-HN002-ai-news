# The Best MagSafe Power Banks for iPhone and Android, Plus Ones to Avoid
# 适用于 iPhone 和 Android 的最佳 MagSafe 充电宝，以及避坑指南

Smartphone battery stamina has improved over the years, but so have cameras, games, and everything else, causing us to spend more time than ever tapping those screens and using up battery. Faster charging helps, and wireless chargers and 3-in-1 charging systems make it easier to stay topped up, but running out of juice before the day’s end is still a real problem. That's where MagSafe and Qi2 power banks come in—helping extend your phone's battery life until technology catches up with demand.

多年来，智能手机的电池续航能力有所提升，但摄像头、游戏以及其他功能也在不断进步，导致我们花在屏幕上的时间比以往任何时候都多，电量消耗也随之增加。更快的充电速度有所帮助，无线充电器和三合一充电系统也让保持电量变得更容易，但在一天结束前电量耗尽仍然是一个现实问题。这就是 MagSafe 和 Qi2 充电宝的用武之地——它们能帮助延长手机的电池寿命，直到电池技术能够跟上需求。

Portable chargers come in all shapes and sizes; for iPhone or Android owners, it makes sense to snag a power bank that supports Apple’s MagSafe or Qi2 technology. Forget fumbling with a charging cable or trying to juggle two devices. You can stick a magnetic power bank to the back of your iPhone or Qi2 Android phone, charge it wirelessly, and then detach and stuff it in your bag. After months of testing, we found that these are the best MagSafe power banks.

便携式充电器种类繁多；对于 iPhone 或 Android 用户来说，购买一款支持苹果 MagSafe 或 Qi2 技术的充电宝是非常明智的。不必再为寻找充电线或同时携带两台设备而烦恼。你可以将磁吸充电宝吸附在 iPhone 或支持 Qi2 的 Android 手机背面进行无线充电，用完后取下放入包中即可。经过数月的测试，我们选出了以下这些最佳 MagSafe 充电宝。

### Best MagSafe Qi2 Power Bank: Anker MagGo Power Bank (10K) (Qi2)
### 最佳 MagSafe Qi2 充电宝：Anker MagGo Power Bank (10K) (Qi2)

Anker's power bank boasts a handy kickstand, a two-way USB-C port on the left edge, and a power button with an LED display that shows remaining power and time to charge. You can attach MagSafe iPhones or Qi2 phones in portrait or landscape orientation, and it works with Apple’s StandBy mode. I tested it with an iPhone 14 Pro and got 25 percent charged in around 20 minutes. Despite its large capacity, this power bank falls short of two full charges when charging wirelessly. It comes with a 2-foot USB-C cable.

Anker 的这款充电宝拥有一个实用的支架、左侧的一个双向 USB-C 接口，以及一个带有 LED 显示屏的电源按钮，可显示剩余电量和充电时间。你可以将支持 MagSafe 的 iPhone 或 Qi2 手机以纵向或横向方式吸附，并且它支持苹果的“待机显示”（StandBy）模式。我用 iPhone 14 Pro 进行了测试，大约 20 分钟内充入了 25% 的电量。尽管容量很大，但在无线充电时，这款充电宝无法完成两次完整的充电。它附带一根 2 英尺长的 USB-C 数据线。

### Best-Looking MagSafe Power Bank: Sharge Icemag 3
### 颜值最高的 MagSafe 充电宝：Sharge Icemag 3

Power banks are typically so boring, but I always look for new designs from Sharge. The Icemag 3 is a lovely wee gadget that combines sci-fi styling with maximum utility. A transparent section shows the internals, including a light-up RGB fan that cycles through colors and keeps your Qi2 phone cool. It supports Qi2 25W and boasts a permanently attached USB-C cable that doubles as a carry strap and can deliver 35 watts. An additional USB-C port on the side allows you to charge three devices at once. The fan is quiet, and you can turn off the light, which wasn't an option in the previous version.

充电宝通常都很乏味，但我总是期待 Sharge（闪极）的新设计。Icemag 3 是一款可爱的小玩意，将科幻风格与实用性完美结合。透明区域展示了内部结构，包括一个会循环变换颜色的 RGB 发光风扇，能为你的 Qi2 手机降温。它支持 Qi2 25W 无线充电，并配有一根固定在机身上的 USB-C 线，既可作为提手，又能提供 35W 的输出功率。侧面的额外 USB-C 接口允许你同时为三台设备充电。风扇运行安静，而且你可以关闭灯光，这是上一代产品所不具备的功能。

### Best MagSafe Power Bank With Cables: Ridge Power Bank
### 最佳带线 MagSafe 充电宝：Ridge Power Bank

Not only is the Ridge MagSafe power bank slick-looking, it also has built-in cables: one Lightning and one USB-C. While phone manufacturers would have you believe that older tech is obsolete as soon as the next generation hits the market, the fact remains that many of us still have devices that charge via Lightning cable. This power bank can charge them with the built-in cables, plus your newer tech magnetically. There's also a built-in kickstand and a display to show you battery status and charging levels.

Ridge MagSafe 充电宝不仅外观时尚，还内置了数据线：一根 Lightning 线和一根 USB-C 线。虽然手机制造商总是试图让你相信，一旦新一代产品上市，旧技术就过时了，但事实是，我们许多人仍然拥有通过 Lightning 接口充电的设备。这款充电宝可以通过内置线缆为这些设备充电，同时还能通过磁吸方式为你的新设备充电。它还内置了支架，并配有显示屏来查看电池状态和充电电量。

### 另一款出色的内置线缆 MagSafe 充电宝：Baseus（倍思）Picogo Qi2 25W

There are some standout reasons to snag this power bank, chiefly that it supports the latest Qi2 25W standard, and has a handy built-in USB-C cable.
