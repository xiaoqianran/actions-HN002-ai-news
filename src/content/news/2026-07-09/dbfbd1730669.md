# The Granularity Paradox: How Temporal Disaggregation Inflates In-Sample Fit and Compounds Out-of-Sample Error
# 粒度悖论：时间分解如何夸大样本内拟合度并加剧样本外误差

**Abstract:** This paper explores the "Granularity Paradox" in time-series forecasting, wherein finer temporal disaggregation (e.g., Monthly to Weekly/Daily) improves in-sample diagnostics and dataset size (N), but degrades out-of-sample accuracy due to recursive error compounding over longer horizons (H). Conversely, coarse aggregation (Annual) eliminates recursive error propagation but reduces data available to estimators.

**摘要：** 本文探讨了时间序列预测中的“粒度悖论”，即更细的时间分解（例如从月度到周度/日度）虽然改善了样本内诊断指标和数据集规模（N），但由于在较长预测期（H）内递归误差的累积，反而降低了样本外预测的准确性。相反，粗粒度聚合（年度）消除了递归误差传播，但减少了估计器可用的数据量。

We formalize this trade-off and benchmark 10 models - spanning naïve, statistical, machine learning, and deep learning architectures - across six granularities using a 13-year public procurement dataset. The empirical results reveal a non-monotonic threshold structure: recursive autoregressive and seasonal models degrade substantially under high-frequency forecasting (e.g., Holt-Winters reaches a Test R-squared of -151 and TPFE of 425.85% at the Daily grain), while the LSTM traces a U-shaped error curve, worsening from Monthly (19.66%) through Bi-Weekly (35.94%) before overcoming the error propagation penalty at Daily (TPFE of 4.35%, R-squared of 0.66).

我们对这种权衡进行了形式化，并使用一个为期 13 年的公共采购数据集，对涵盖朴素模型、统计模型、机器学习和深度学习架构的 10 种模型在六种粒度下进行了基准测试。实证结果揭示了一种非单调的阈值结构：递归自回归模型和季节性模型在高频预测下性能大幅下降（例如，Holt-Winters 模型在日度粒度下的测试 R-squared 达到 -151，TPFE 达到 425.85%）；而 LSTM 则呈现出 U 型误差曲线，从月度（19.66%）到双周（35.94%）误差逐渐恶化，但在日度粒度下克服了误差传播惩罚（TPFE 为 4.35%，R-squared 为 0.66）。

Linear Regression remains stable across all granularities (16.3-17.0% TPFE), confirming that the paradox is driven by recursive feedback topology, not model complexity. The results demonstrate that standard pointwise metrics (RMSE, MAE) systematically mask cumulative error propagation, and that evaluating forecasts without goal-dependent cumulative metrics produces misleading assessments of model adequacy. We introduce a consensus-dissensus diagnostic comparing the directional behaviour of pointwise metrics against cumulative TPFE across granularities, enabling the identification of models whose standard diagnostics mask systematic error propagation.

线性回归在所有粒度下均保持稳定（TPFE 为 16.3-17.0%），这证实了该悖论是由递归反馈拓扑结构而非模型复杂度驱动的。研究结果表明，标准的点对点指标（RMSE、MAE）系统性地掩盖了累积误差传播，且在缺乏目标导向的累积指标的情况下评估预测，会导致对模型充分性的误导性评估。我们引入了一种“共识-分歧”诊断方法，通过比较不同粒度下点对点指标与累积 TPFE 的方向性行为，从而识别出那些标准诊断指标掩盖了系统性误差传播的模型。
