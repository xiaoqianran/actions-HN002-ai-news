# Geometry-Aware Infrastructure-Anchored Denoiser for UWB Sensing and Work-Zone Reconstruction
# 面向超宽带感知与施工区域重建的几何感知基础设施锚定去噪器

**Abstract:** Accurate work-zone geometry perception is critical for intelligent transportation systems, and ultra-wideband sensing offers a low-cost approach for infrastructure-aided reconstruction. However, outdoor UWB ranging is often degraded by non-line-of-sight propagation, burst noise, and long-tail errors, which can distort downstream spatial reconstruction.

**摘要：** 精确的施工区域几何感知对于智能交通系统至关重要，而超宽带（UWB）感知为基础设施辅助重建提供了一种低成本方案。然而，室外 UWB 测距常受到非视距传播、突发噪声和长尾误差的影响，这些因素会扭曲后续的空间重建效果。

We present GAIA, a geometry-aware, infrastructure-anchored learning framework that couples temporal range modeling with latent anchor-layout estimation and deterministic distance projection. GAIA preserves range denoising as the supervised task while orienting the learned distances toward boundary-consistent reconstruction.

我们提出了 GAIA，这是一个几何感知、基础设施锚定的学习框架，它将时间序列测距建模与潜在锚点布局估计及确定性距离投影相结合。GAIA 将测距去噪作为监督任务，同时引导学习到的距离向边界一致性重建方向优化。

We evaluate GAIA on a real-world outdoor UWB dataset with synchronized UWB, GNSS, and IMU measurements, and further test robustness using a real-data-calibrated stress-test simulator. GAIA achieves the lowest overall range MSE and highest polygon IoU among evaluated filtering-based and learning-based baselines, reducing MSE by 18.4% and improving polygon IoU by 15.5% over PoseMLP.

我们在包含同步 UWB、GNSS 和 IMU 测量数据的真实室外 UWB 数据集上评估了 GAIA，并利用经过真实数据校准的压力测试模拟器进一步测试了其鲁棒性。在所有参与评估的基于滤波和基于学习的基准模型中，GAIA 实现了最低的整体测距均方误差（MSE）和最高的几何多边形交并比（IoU），与 PoseMLP 相比，MSE 降低了 18.4%，多边形 IoU 提升了 15.5%。

These results show that geometry-aware range denoising provides an effective path toward spatially coherent work-zone reconstruction.

这些结果表明，几何感知测距去噪为实现空间一致的施工区域重建提供了一条有效途径。
