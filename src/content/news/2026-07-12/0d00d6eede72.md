# Best Hiking Boots (2026): Walking Shoes, Trails, Backpacking
# 2026年最佳徒步靴：健步鞋、越野与背包旅行指南

Over the past 12 months I’ve tested more than 50 pairs of outdoor shoes, from traditional leather hiking boots and their modern equivalents to ultralight trail runners and everything in between. I’ve popped out for dog walks around my local park, hiked for days along rugged coastal paths, thru-hiked with heavy packs, and even stepped out on the occasional glacier.
在过去的12个月里，我测试了超过50双户外鞋，从传统的皮革徒步靴及其现代替代品，到超轻越野跑鞋，以及介于两者之间的各种鞋款。我穿着它们在当地公园遛狗、在崎岖的海岸线上徒步数日、背着重装进行长距离徒步，甚至偶尔踏上冰川。

Hikers have hundreds of options to choose from these days, from old-school leather clodhoppers to ultralight trail-running sneakers, stiff approach shoes, and pillowy long-distance hikers made for big-mile days. Selecting the right pair depends on where you walk, how fast you move, how much you carry, and how much punishment your feet are prepared to tolerate. Below, I’ve pulled together my top picks for all sorts of different hikers.
如今，徒步爱好者有数百种选择，从老式的皮革重型靴到超轻越野跑鞋，从硬质攀岩接近鞋，到为长距离徒步设计的软垫长途鞋。选择哪一双取决于你在哪里行走、移动速度、负重多少，以及你的双脚能承受多大的磨损。以下是我为各类徒步者精选的顶级推荐。

### The Best Modern Hiking Boot: Hoka Kaha 3 GTX
### 最佳现代徒步靴：Hoka Kaha 3 GTX

They’re big and look even bigger on your feet, but the Hoka Kaha 3 GTX are the most comfortable, well-cushioned mid-cut hiking boots I’ve worn this year. They’re brilliantly plush, impressively supportive and fantastic over long days with or without a loaded pack. Comfort costs, though.
它们看起来很大，穿在脚上显得更大，但 Hoka Kaha 3 GTX 是我今年穿过最舒适、缓冲性能最好的中帮徒步靴。它们极其柔软，支撑力令人印象深刻，无论是否负重，在长途跋涉中表现都非常出色。不过，舒适是有代价的。

The dual-density midsole is super thick and I expected to hate Hoka’s signature boing-boing feel, but they’re reassuring underfoot. As such, they’re not a particularly precise boot, and can feel a little unwieldy over tricky terrain, but the 4-mm lugs and Vibram Megagrip outsole stick to mud, rock, and trails with ease. If you want a boot to chew through mile after mile of trails without you noticing, the Kaha 3 GTX should be your top pick.
其双密度中底非常厚实，我本以为会讨厌 Hoka 标志性的“弹跳感”，但它们在脚下却给人一种踏实感。因此，它不是一双特别精准的靴子，在复杂地形上可能会感觉有些笨重，但4毫米的鞋底齿纹和 Vibram Megagrip 外底能轻松抓牢泥地、岩石和步道。如果你想要一双能让你在不知不觉中走完一英里又一英里步道的靴子，Kaha 3 GTX 应该是你的首选。

### The Boots I Keep Coming Back To: Danner Mountain 600
### 我反复回购的靴子：Danner Mountain 600

Unless they’re already on my feet, my very well-loved pair of Danner Mountain 600 boots lives in my trunk, ready to go whenever I need them. The 4.5-inch design is a new-school take on the classic boot, with a nice cushioned EVA midsole, robust leather upper, and fantastic grip, courtesy of Vibram Megagrip.
除非正穿在脚上，否则我那双深得我心的 Danner Mountain 600 靴子总是放在后备箱里，随时准备出发。这款4.5英寸的设计是对经典靴子的现代诠释，配有舒适的 EVA 中底、坚固的皮革鞋面，以及得益于 Vibram Megagrip 的出色抓地力。

They’re by no means the sexiest of modern hiking boots, and they lack the traditional charm of a pair of Danner Mountain Lites, for example, but they’re half the price, close to half the weight and arguably twice as comfortable over a long walk.
它们绝不是现代徒步靴中最性感的，也缺乏像 Danner Mountain Lite 那种传统魅力，但它们的价格只有后者的一半，重量也接近减半，而且在长途行走中，舒适度可以说翻了一倍。

### A Superb Mid-Cut Boot: Salomon X Ultra 5 Mid Gore-Tex
### 出色的中帮靴：Salomon X Ultra 5 Mid Gore-Tex

The Salomon X Ultra 5 Mid Gore-Tex looks like a classic pair of modern hiking boots, with chunky dimensions, robust features, and good support, but these mid-cut Salomon boots are deceptively light at 1.94 pounds per pair. I have spent years urging people to buy the superb Salomon Quest 4 GTX, but these have become my preferred choice from the brand.
Salomon X Ultra 5 Mid Gore-Tex 看起来像是一双经典的现代徒步靴，拥有厚实的尺寸、强大的功能和良好的支撑，但这对中帮 Salomon 靴子轻得令人惊讶，每双仅重1.94磅。多年来我一直推荐大家购买出色的 Salomon Quest 4 GTX，但现在这款已成为我个人在该品牌中的首选。

While not specifically designed for hauling heavy packs, I had no issues over several weekend overnighters, and was taken by just how versatile they were. The Contagrip rubber keeps you surefooted when climbing, and while the EnergyCell midsole lacks the squish of the Hoka Kaha 3 GTX, those more used to traditional hiking footwear will like the firm but responsive feel.
虽然它并非专门为重装背包设计，但在几次周末过夜徒步中，我没有遇到任何问题，并被其多功能性所折服。Contagrip 橡胶底让你在攀爬时步履稳健，虽然 EnergyCell 中底缺乏 Hoka Kaha 3 GTX 那种软糯感，但习惯传统徒步鞋的人会喜欢这种坚实且灵敏的脚感。
