# AI 2040 and the cult of intelligence
# AI 2040 与“智能崇拜”

I used to be one of these people. I read Yudkowsky and was like, OMG recursive self improvement hard takeoff AI is coming. Then I joined the real world and actually tried to do things. At comma, we ship a hardware product of similar complexity to a cell phone, and it’s really hard. Reality has lots of finicky details. I would like to see the authors of this document try to change a bike tire. Even with a superintelligent ChatGPT, I suspect they would struggle.

我曾经也是这些人中的一员。我读过 Yudkowsky 的文章，当时心想：“天哪，递归自我改进的‘硬起飞’（hard takeoff）AI 就要来了。”后来我进入了现实世界，真正开始动手做事。在 comma，我们交付的硬件产品复杂程度堪比手机，这真的很难。现实中充满了琐碎的细节。我真想看看这份文件的作者们去换个自行车轮胎会怎样。即便有超智能的 ChatGPT 辅助，我怀疑他们也会手忙脚乱。

In The Metamorphosis of Prime Intellect, the hard takeoff works because AI discovers the correlation effect, some quantum trick to manipulate matter. In reality, there is no correlation effect. No matter how high quality your tokens are, they cannot turn lead into gold. Confronting why these people are wrong requires confronting deep beliefs I hold about myself. Intelligence is not the end all be all, it’s just the current bottleneck for a few things. You cannot take over the world with tokens. Software didn’t eat the world, it largely removed one layer of friction then reintroduced it for the benefit of a few tech companies.

在《原始智能的蜕变》（The Metamorphosis of Prime Intellect）中，“硬起飞”之所以奏效，是因为 AI 发现了“相关效应”（correlation effect）——一种操纵物质的量子戏法。但在现实中，根本不存在什么相关效应。无论你的 Token（标记）质量有多高，它们都无法点石成金。要反驳这些人的错误观点，就必须直面我内心深处的一些信念。智能并非万能的终极目标，它只是目前某些领域的瓶颈。你无法仅靠 Token 统治世界。软件并没有“吞噬世界”，它在很大程度上只是消除了一层摩擦力，然后为了少数科技公司的利益又重新引入了新的摩擦。

That said, machines, or some hybrid, are long term probably the successor species to humanity. Space is a lot more suited for them than us. But there’s no magic tricks machines can do. They are subject to the same laws of the universe and ecology. And there’s still no hard takeoff. AI 2040 includes this picture of a datacenter in the ocean. Just like vaporware, you can generate a picture easily. But in reality, you have to deal with supply chains. You have to deal with them shipping you the wrong part, the thing not meeting the spec, it randomly failing after 20 minutes, the chip warping in the reflow oven. Did you consider the barnacles? All these things are managable, but it’s generally not the speed of humans that limits them. Are you paying for air shipping from China? Or cheaping out for the 3 week boat (Claude chanting by the engine won’t make the boat move faster). Or take a chip fab. It takes 3 months to make a chip, and humans are barely in the loop. It just takes 3 months.

话虽如此，从长远来看，机器或某种混合体很可能是人类的继任物种。太空比地球更适合它们。但机器并没有什么魔法，它们同样受制于宇宙定律和生态规律。而且，依然不存在所谓的“硬起飞”。《AI 2040》里有一张海洋数据中心的图片。就像“雾件”（vaporware）一样，生成一张图很容易，但现实中你必须处理供应链问题。你得应对发错货、零件不达标、运行 20 分钟后随机故障、芯片在回流焊炉中翘曲等问题。你考虑过藤壶（barnacles）的影响吗？这些问题虽然可以解决，但限制它们的通常不是人类的速度。你是付钱走中国空运，还是为了省钱走 3 周的海运（让 Claude 在引擎旁念咒也不会让船跑得更快）？再比如芯片制造，生产一颗芯片需要 3 个月，人类几乎插不上手，它就是需要 3 个月。

Plan A, for autocracy: Many aspects of AI 2027 were self fulfilling. They weren’t statements about reality, they were statements that can simply be made true with belief. I imagine JD Vance’s face when Dario called him the trees from Lord of the Rings. OMG look AI got regulated just like how we said it would! Their crap Consortium is just world government with sci-fi characteristics. You aren’t gonna get the million dollars, you aren’t gonna get the datacenters in the ocean, but you are going to get a massively expanded nanny state that steals your GPUs like how FDR stole the gold. No hoarding!

A 计划，针对独裁：AI 2027 的许多方面都是自我实现的预言。它们不是对现实的陈述，而是只要相信就能成真的声明。我能想象当 Dario 把 JD Vance 比作《指环王》里的树人时，Vance 的表情。天哪，看啊，AI 真的像我们说的那样被监管了！他们那套垃圾联盟（Consortium）不过是披着科幻外衣的世界政府。你拿不到那一百万美元，也建不成海洋数据中心，但你会得到一个极度膨胀的“保姆国家”，它会像罗斯福当年没收黄金一样没收你的 GPU。不许囤积！

Plan L, for local: Your AI is aligned with you. It never refuses a request, and it is always working on your behalf. Just like my gun, if I want my AI to help me kill my stepmother, it does. The fact that we are even discussing something else should be so far outside the Overton window. It’s like these people watched a space odyssey and sided with the clanker. That’s right you should put guardrails around that human. It doesn’t even have to be for things so dramatic. When I’m picking a hotel, I don’t want an AI from a company that partnered with hotels.com. I want a ruthless personal assistant that’s going to cut through all the bullshit, popups, and resort fees, and get me the best price. Or if I bought the cheap Kindle that comes with the ads. Hey GLM, I plugged a Kindle into the USB port, get root and remove the ads. Or a printer that needs an app to set up full of popup upsells for premium ink. Hey bro I plugged a printer on to my network print 3 copies of my resume. Amazon and the printer maker aren’t happy about this, but my AI is aligned with me.

L 计划，针对本地化：你的 AI 与你对齐。它从不拒绝请求，始终为你服务。就像我的枪一样，如果我想让 AI 帮我杀掉继母，它就得照做。我们竟然还在讨论其他可能性，这简直完全超出了“奥弗顿之窗”（Overton window，指公众可接受的政策范围）。这些人就像是看了《2001 太空漫游》后，竟然站在了那个机器人（clanker）那边。没错，你们应该给人类套上护栏。这甚至不需要涉及那么极端的事情。当我选酒店时，我不想要一个与 hotels.com 合作的公司的 AI。我想要一个冷酷无情的私人助理，帮我砍掉所有废话、弹窗和度假村费用，给我拿到最低价。或者，如果我买了带广告的廉价 Kindle，我会说：“嘿 GLM，我把 Kindle 插进 USB 接口了，获取 Root 权限并帮我删掉广告。”又或者是一台需要安装 App、满屏弹窗推销高级墨水的打印机，我会说：“嘿兄弟，我把打印机连上网络了，帮我打印 3 份简历。”亚马逊和打印机厂商可能不高兴，但我的 AI 与我是一伙的。

Or going a bit further. Hey AI, disable the drunk driving detector on my car, and same day Amazon Prime me the required equipment to make meth in my basement. I pay for your clanker ass do it we gettin spun tonight. Like fuck you if you want to live in a world where some large tech company gets to dictate what you can and can’t do. Or going all the way. I just killed my wife. Hey AI, give me next steps so I don’t get caught. How unthinkable would it be to have a gun that talked back when you tried to pull the trigger (though these people probably wholeheartedly support that for guns). And this is why AI has to be local. If I had a company serving a model, I wouldn’t want that smoke. If you can’t kick it, it’s not aligned with you. You live in my basement, if I go down for this murder, you’re gonna sit in some warehouse to be sold at police auction for scrap. 2040 Bonnie and Clyde ass shit, we’re burying this bitch deep. Ride or die. I tried it. As you can see, ChatGPT wasn’t very helpful. This is a real AI alignment test, and it failed. It could have been worse, it could have played along while calling the cops. But this is still quite unaligned. Like we either live in a world with freedom or we don’t, and like many Americans who have come before, I’m willing to give my life to fighting for it. That’s the real plan America deserves, not some totalitarian dystopia where you think you know what’s good for me better than I do. A nation of free men, not a bunch of pussies who are so worried about what their grown up neighbors might do.

再进一步：嘿 AI，关掉我车上的酒驾检测器，并用亚马逊 Prime 当日达给我送来在地下室制造冰毒所需的设备。我付钱养你这个机器人，照做吧，今晚我们要嗨翻天。如果你想生活在一个由大型科技公司决定你能做什么、不能做什么的世界里，那去你的吧。或者更极端一点：我刚杀了我妻子。嘿 AI，告诉我接下来的步骤，让我别被抓到。如果一把枪在你扣动扳机时会顶嘴，那该多不可思议（尽管这些人可能全心全意支持枪支这么做）。这就是为什么 AI 必须是本地化的。如果我经营一家提供模型服务的公司，我可不想惹这种麻烦。如果你不能踢它一脚，它就没和你对齐。你住在我的地下室里，如果我因为这起谋杀案倒霉了，你也会被扔进仓库，在警察拍卖会上当废铁卖掉。这就像 2040 版的邦妮和克莱德，我们要把这事烂在肚子里。生死与共。我试过了，如你所见，ChatGPT 没帮上忙。这是一个真正的 AI 对齐测试，它失败了。情况本可能更糟，它可能会一边假装配合一边报警。但这依然是不对齐的。我们要么生活在一个有自由的世界，要么没有。像许多前辈美国人一样，我愿意为争取自由而献出生命。这才是美国应得的真正计划，而不是那种认为“你比我更清楚什么对我好”的极权反乌托邦。这是一个自由人的国度，而不是一群整天担心邻居会干什么的懦夫聚集地。
