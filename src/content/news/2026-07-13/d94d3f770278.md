# The 10 Best Pet Cameras of 2026: Furbo, Petcube, and Enabot
# 2026年十款最佳宠物摄像头：Furbo、Petcube 和 Enabot

I never knew I needed a pet camera, but after trying out several models, I don’t think I can ever go into the office or on vacation without one set up at home. A home security camera can give you extra peace of mind when you’re out, and pet cameras specifically designed for our furry friends add in helpful features, like the ability to follow your pet around, engage in play, and dispense kibble. Connected to an app on your phone and with cloud storage options, these pet cameras—including our top pick for most people (and pets!), the Furbo Mini 360° ($49), and our compact 360 rotating budget pick, Petcube Cam 360 ($47)—ensure you’ll never miss a moment in your pet’s life when you’re away (or at home).

我以前从没想过自己需要一台宠物摄像头，但在试用了几款产品后，我觉得以后去办公室或度假时，家里没装这东西我可待不住。家用安防摄像头能在你外出时提供额外的安心感，而专为毛孩子设计的宠物摄像头则增加了更多实用功能，比如追踪宠物动态、互动玩耍以及投喂零食。通过手机 App 连接并配合云存储功能，这些宠物摄像头——包括我们为大多数人（及宠物）推荐的首选 Furbo Mini 360°（49 美元），以及我们推荐的紧凑型 360 度旋转高性价比之选 Petcube Cam 360（47 美元）——能确保你在外出（或在家时）绝不错过宠物生活的每一个瞬间。

### Best Pet Camera for Most People: Furbo Mini 360°
### 大多数人的首选：Furbo Mini 360°

This affordable model has most things every pet parent is looking for in a pet camera: it dispenses treats; pans so you can see the whole room; and, if you subscribe, gives you highlights from the day, broken down by activity or motion. There’s also seven-day storage, and a report of insights into your pet’s daily activities. It pans left and right so you can see the entire surrounding area; has super-clear 1080p HD color and night-vision resolution; two-way audio so you can hear, talk to, and see your pet; and treat dispensing through the app. That said, you can’t schedule feedings or change the amount dispensed, and when dispensing treats, it makes a jarring, loud chirping noise that my cats seemed to find alarming and perplexing.

这款经济实惠的机型具备了宠物主人对摄像头的大多数需求：它可以投喂零食；可以旋转以查看整个房间；如果你订阅了服务，它还能按活动或动作分类，为你提供当天的精彩片段。此外，它还提供七天云存储，并生成关于宠物日常活动的分析报告。它可以左右旋转，让你查看周围全景；拥有超清晰的 1080p 高清彩色和夜视分辨率；支持双向语音，让你能听到、看到宠物并与它们对话；还能通过 App 远程投喂零食。不过，它无法定时喂食，也无法调整投喂量，而且在投喂时会发出刺耳的鸣叫声，我的猫咪似乎觉得这声音既惊人又困惑。

### Best Compact 360 Rotating Pet Camera: Petcube Cam 360
### 最佳紧凑型 360 度旋转宠物摄像头：Petcube Cam 360

This camera from Petcube is great overall, but it suffers from a pitfall that affects the rest of the brand’s lineup: The features are seriously limited if you don't want to pay for Petcube’s upgraded Care plan (which starts at $4 a month) that includes video storage, pet detection, and automatic video recording capabilities. Even so, this camera has 360 PTZ rotation capabilities, 1080p HD resolution, 8X digital zoom, two-way audio, and night vision. Getting all of these included bells and whistles for around $50 lands this camera as a super-affordable get.

Petcube 的这款摄像头整体表现出色，但它有一个该品牌全系产品都存在的通病：如果你不想支付 Petcube 的升级版 Care 套餐（每月 4 美元起，包含视频存储、宠物检测和自动录像功能），其功能会受到严重限制。即便如此，这款摄像头仍具备 360 度云台旋转功能、1080p 高清分辨率、8 倍数码变焦、双向语音和夜视功能。以 50 美元左右的价格获得所有这些功能，使它成为一款性价比极高的产品。

### Best Dual Lens Pet Camera: Imilab C30 Dual Security Camera
### 最佳双镜头宠物摄像头：Imilab C30 双镜头安防摄像头

This dual camera has 3K high definition, with 360 degree rotation up top, and a stationary camera below for even more coverage. There’s also live view and two-way chat, night vision, and 6X zoom. AI detection alerts to events like barks, meows, loud noises, and fire—really anything alarming. Through the app, you can view live footage, review footage stored in the MicroSD card or cloud, and share the content with family. Plus, you can access smart voice control with Amazon Alexa or Google Assistant.

这款双镜头摄像头拥有 3K 高清画质，顶部可 360 度旋转，底部配有固定摄像头，覆盖范围更广。它还支持实时查看、双向通话、夜视和 6 倍变焦。AI 检测功能可针对狗叫、猫叫、巨大噪音和火灾等事件发出警报——基本上任何异常情况都能监测到。通过 App，你可以查看实时画面、回放存储在 MicroSD 卡或云端的录像，并与家人分享内容。此外，它还支持通过 Amazon Alexa 或 Google Assistant 进行智能语音控制。
