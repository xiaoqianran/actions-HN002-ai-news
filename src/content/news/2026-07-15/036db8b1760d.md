# Knowledge-Constrained Shape Optimization with a Mixture-of-Experts Neural Operator for High-Confidence Design
# 基于专家混合神经算子的高置信度知识约束形状优化

**Abstract:** Engineering shape optimization faces challenges in both expert-dependent problem setup and surrogate-model reliability. In practical aerodynamic design, optimization settings such as editable regions, deformation ranges, and design-preservation constraints are typically specified manually by experienced engineers, while surrogate-based optimization may become unreliable for heterogeneous geometry databases and out-of-distribution designs.

**摘要：** 工程形状优化在依赖专家的问题设置和代理模型可靠性方面均面临挑战。在实际的空气动力学设计中，诸如可编辑区域、变形范围和设计保持约束等优化设置通常由经验丰富的工程师手动指定，而基于代理模型的优化在处理异构几何数据库和分布外（out-of-distribution）设计时可能会变得不可靠。

To address these challenges, we propose a knowledge-constrained shape-optimization framework that translates knowledge-based constraints and user intent into quantifiable parameters of DFFD-based deformation operators, enabling engineering-aware and controllable constrained optimization.

为了解决这些挑战，我们提出了一种知识约束形状优化框架，该框架将基于知识的约束和用户意图转化为基于 DFFD（自由变形）算子的可量化参数，从而实现了具备工程感知能力且可控的约束优化。

We further develop a Mixture-of-Experts Neural Operator (MoE-NO) to improve drag prediction and trend consistency over heterogeneous aerodynamic datasets. Based on the MoE-NO encoder and Mahalanobis distance, an uncertainty-estimation strategy is introduced to detect out-of-distribution geometries and selectively trigger physics-solver feedback for local sample enrichment.

我们进一步开发了一种专家混合神经算子（MoE-NO），以提高在异构空气动力学数据集上的阻力预测精度和趋势一致性。基于 MoE-NO 编码器和马氏距离（Mahalanobis distance），我们引入了一种不确定性估计策略，用于检测分布外几何结构，并选择性地触发物理求解器反馈，以进行局部样本增强。

Experiments on in-house MPV, SUV, and Sedan datasets show that MoE-NO achieves a test-set MAPE of $1.16\%$ and a trend-prediction accuracy of $94.34\%$, outperforming the best baseline results of $1.52\%$ and $90.34\%$, respectively. Vehicle shape-optimization experiments further yield CFD-validated drag coefficient reductions of approximately $4\%$ to $10\%$.

在内部 MPV、SUV 和轿车数据集上的实验表明，MoE-NO 在测试集上的平均绝对百分比误差（MAPE）达到 $1.16\%$，趋势预测准确率达到 $94.34\%$，优于最佳基准结果（分别为 $1.52\%$ 和 $90.34\%$）。车辆形状优化实验进一步实现了经 CFD 验证的阻力系数降低约 $4\%$ 至 $10\%$。
