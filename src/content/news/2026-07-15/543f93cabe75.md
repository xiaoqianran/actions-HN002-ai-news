# AuditWeave: A Tamper-Evident, Auditor-Navigable Evidence Layer for AI-Assisted and Data-Transformation Workflows
# AuditWeave：一种用于 AI 辅助及数据转换工作流的防篡改、可审计证据层

AI systems are increasingly used to assist consequential decisions in regulated domains such as auditing, finance, and healthcare. This creates a recurring obligation: an organization must be able to reconstruct, after the fact, which evidence informed a given conclusion, and to show that the record of that reasoning was not altered.
AI 系统正越来越多地被用于辅助审计、金融和医疗保健等受监管领域的重大决策。这产生了一项反复出现的义务：组织必须能够在事后重构出哪些证据支撑了特定的结论，并证明该推理记录未被篡改。

Existing tools address related but distinct problems - model observability, drift monitoring, governance reporting - and are built for the machine-learning engineer operating a system, not the reviewer who must trace one specific conclusion back to its supporting evidence.
现有的工具主要解决相关但不同的问题——如模型可观测性、漂移监控和治理报告——它们是为操作系统的机器学习工程师构建的，而非为那些需要将特定结论追溯至其支撑证据的审查人员所设计。

We present AuditWeave, a lightweight Python library, with no runtime dependencies, that records the steps of AI-assisted and data-transformation workflows into a single append-only, hash-chained ledger.
我们提出了 AuditWeave，这是一个轻量级的 Python 库，无运行时依赖，它能将 AI 辅助和数据转换工作流的步骤记录到一个单一的、仅可追加的哈希链式账本中。

A small, system-agnostic event vocabulary spans both retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines and tabular/lakehouse transformations, so a conclusion that draws on both can be traced end-to-end through one record.
一套小巧且与系统无关的事件词汇表涵盖了检索增强生成（RAG）流水线和表格/湖仓（lakehouse）转换，因此，同时利用这两者得出的结论可以通过单一记录进行端到端的追溯。

Within a sealed ledger, any modification, reordering, insertion, or deletion of events is detectable through chain verification. We describe the design and evaluate recording overhead, scalability, and tamper-detection correctness on the reference implementation.
在密封的账本内，任何对事件的修改、重排序、插入或删除操作都可以通过链式验证被检测出来。我们描述了其设计，并在参考实现上评估了记录开销、可扩展性以及防篡改检测的准确性。

The integrity guarantees cost tens of microseconds per event, and, as the hash-chain construction implies, verification flagged every injected mutation across four mutation classes over 2,000 randomized trials.
这种完整性保证的成本仅为每个事件几十微秒；正如哈希链结构所预期的那样，在 2,000 次随机试验中，验证机制成功标记出了四类变异中的每一次注入篡改。
