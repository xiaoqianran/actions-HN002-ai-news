# ReflectWorld-MM: An Entity-Oriented Multi-Media Memory System for Open-Ended Video Streams
# ReflectWorld-MM：一种面向开放式视频流的实体导向多媒体记忆系统

**Abstract:** Building assistants that can continually watch the world, remember what they see, and reason over their accumulated experience is a long-standing goal, and recently multimodal agents equipped with long-term memory over video streams have attracted increasing interest.
**摘要：** 构建能够持续观察世界、记住所见内容并基于积累的经验进行推理的智能助手是一个长期目标。近期，配备视频流长期记忆的多模态智能体引起了越来越多的关注。

Unfortunately, existing systems either keep their memory inside the model context or in a flat feature store, and organize it around frames rather than around the persistent entities a stream is really about, which confines them to bounded videos and weakens their ability to track who and what reappears over time.
遗憾的是，现有的系统要么将记忆保留在模型上下文内，要么存储在扁平的特征库中，并且它们通常围绕帧而非视频流中真正核心的持久实体进行组织。这限制了它们只能处理有限长度的视频，并削弱了其追踪随时间推移而重现的人或事物的能力。

In this paper, we propose ReflectWorld-MM, an entity-oriented multi-media memory system for open-ended video streams. It consists of three parts.
在本文中，我们提出了 ReflectWorld-MM，这是一种面向开放式视频流的实体导向多媒体记忆系统。它由三个部分组成。

The first is a perception front-end that turns a streaming video into entity-resolved observations under a bounded short-term memory.
第一部分是一个感知前端，它在有限的短期记忆下，将流式视频转化为经过实体解析的观测结果。

The second is a hierarchical long-term memory, grounded in human memory theory, that couples a multi-scale episodic memory, an evolving entity-centric semantic memory, and a procedural memory.
第二部分是一个基于人类记忆理论的分层长期记忆系统，它结合了多尺度情景记忆、不断演进的以实体为中心的语义记忆以及程序性记忆。

The third is a complete realization, built for real-world operation, that ingests arbitrary streams and plugs into off-the-shelf assistants.
第三部分是一个为现实世界运行而构建的完整实现，它能够摄取任意视频流并接入现成的智能助手。

Across six long-video and lifelong-memory benchmarks, ReflectWorld-MM achieves the best accuracy on all six, outperforming strong memory agents and a frontier model.
在六项长视频和终身记忆基准测试中，ReflectWorld-MM 在所有测试中均取得了最佳准确率，表现优于强大的记忆智能体和前沿模型。
