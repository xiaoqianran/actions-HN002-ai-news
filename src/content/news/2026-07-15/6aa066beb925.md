# Plex problems prevented users from streaming movies and shows
# Plex 出现故障，导致用户无法观看流媒体电影和节目

Plex services experienced some major issues on Tuesday, with multiple users reporting problems on Plex’s forums and on Reddit. Many people use Plex as a way to stream shows and movies they host locally, but users are upset because today’s problems were reportedly affecting their ability to do that.
周二，Plex 服务出现重大故障，多名用户在 Plex 论坛和 Reddit 上反馈了相关问题。许多人使用 Plex 来播放本地托管的节目和电影，但用户们感到不满，因为据报道，当天的故障影响了他们的正常观看。

“Basically all Plex is down unless you can play locally over LAN and even that takes ages and doesn’t always work,” said one user. Others reported problems with matching content on their servers. For me, the Plex TV website was loading slowly, though while initially writing this story, it started to load more reliably.
一位用户表示：“基本上整个 Plex 都瘫痪了，除非你通过局域网（LAN）进行本地播放，即便如此，加载也需要很长时间，而且并不总是有效。”其他人则报告了服务器内容匹配方面的问题。就我个人而言，Plex TV 网站加载缓慢，不过在撰写本文之初，它开始恢复正常加载。

Plex had confirmed issues, with the company’s status page originally noting problems with its free streaming movies and shows, the plex.tv API, Discover Together, and program guide data for live TV. The issues went on about a couple hours. Now, the page says Plex is “fully operational.”
Plex 已经确认了这些问题，公司状态页面最初显示其免费流媒体电影和节目、plex.tv API、Discover Together 以及直播电视节目指南数据均出现故障。这些问题持续了大约两个小时。目前，页面显示 Plex 已“全面恢复运行”。

The company didn’t immediately reply to a request for comment.
该公司未立即回复置评请求。

Update, July 14th: The issues appear to be fixed.
更新，7 月 14 日：问题似乎已得到解决。
