# Ablation, Statistical Inference, and Validation for KV-Cache Compression
# KV-Cache 压缩的消融研究、统计推断与验证

**Abstract:** This study systematically compares Turbo-Quant and SpectralQuant KV-cache compression, evaluating non-dominated schemes, including WHT rotation with Beta Lloyd-Max and QJL, through a statistical validation methodology that separates systematic codec differences from implementation variance.

**摘要：** 本研究系统地比较了 Turbo-Quant 和 SpectralQuant KV-cache 压缩技术，通过一种能够将系统性编解码器差异与实现偏差分离开来的统计验证方法，评估了包括带有 Beta Lloyd-Max 和 QJL 的 WHT 旋转在内的非支配方案。

Key findings reveal that while eigenbasis-based methods fail on heavy-tailed data due to covariance instability, they excel in structured regimes, with the effective semantic dimension ($d_{eff}$) adapting to calibration budgets rather than true data rank.

关键研究结果表明，虽然基于特征基（eigenbasis）的方法由于协方差的不稳定性而在长尾数据上表现不佳，但它们在结构化场景中表现优异，且其有效语义维度 ($d_{eff}$) 是根据校准预算而非数据的真实秩进行自适应调整的。
