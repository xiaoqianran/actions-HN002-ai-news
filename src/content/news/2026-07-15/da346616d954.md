### Sourclip

**Sourclip**

Turn NotebookLM into a complete research workflow.
将 NotebookLM 转化为一套完整的研究工作流。

Sourclip is the research workflow for NotebookLM. It helps you discover high-quality research sources, capture webpages, PDFs, YouTube videos, Reddit posts, and AI chats into NotebookLM with one click.
Sourclip 是专为 NotebookLM 设计的研究工作流工具。它能帮助你发现高质量的研究资源，并支持一键将网页、PDF、YouTube 视频、Reddit 帖子以及 AI 对话内容抓取到 NotebookLM 中。

Organize everything with folders, reuse prompts across projects, sync your workspace across devices, and export your research as Markdown, HTML, or PDF.
你可以通过文件夹整理所有内容，在不同项目间复用提示词（Prompts），跨设备同步工作区，并将研究成果导出为 Markdown、HTML 或 PDF 格式。

You can also turn NotebookLM Audio Overviews into a private podcast feed for Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Pocket Casts, and other podcast apps.
你还可以将 NotebookLM 的音频概览（Audio Overviews）转化为私有播客订阅源，以便在 Spotify、Apple Podcasts、Pocket Casts 及其他播客应用中收听。
