# Lucid Motors denies report it’s considering bankruptcy
# Lucid Motors 否认有关其正在考虑破产的报道

Lucid Motors is denying a report that it’s weighing filing for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection. Nick Twork, the company’s chief communications officer, told TechCrunch in a statement that the “rumors are completely false.”
Lucid Motors 否认了有关其正在权衡申请第 11 章破产保护的报道。该公司首席通讯官 Nick Twork 在一份声明中告诉 TechCrunch，这些“传言完全是虚假的”。

“The company has sufficient liquidity to carry its operations well into next year, as recently published in its last quarterly filings, and it has not formed any special Board committee to explore the scenarios reported today,” he said. “Our focus is on improving execution, strengthening operations, and positioning Lucid to realize the full potential of its technology, products, and innovation.”
“正如最近发布的上一季度财报所显示，公司拥有充足的流动资金来维持运营至明年，且公司并未成立任何特别董事会委员会来探讨今日报道中所述的情况，”他表示。“我们的重点是改善执行力、加强运营，并使 Lucid 能够充分发挥其技术、产品和创新的潜力。”

Lucid’s denial comes after its stock price sunk more than 50% on Tuesday, its biggest intra-day drop ever, according to Bloomberg News. The stock has recovered from the free fall, and was trading at $4.72 a share as of 2:46 p.m. ET, about 14% lower than its opening price.
据彭博社报道，在 Lucid 做出否认之前，其股价周二暴跌超过 50%，创下有史以来最大的盘中跌幅。目前该股已从自由落体中回升，截至美东时间下午 2:46，股价为每股 4.72 美元，较开盘价下跌约 14%。

The company recently named a new CEO and has laid off more than 2,000 employees this year as part of a sweeping restructuring ahead of the expected launch of its smaller, more affordable electric SUV later this year.
该公司最近任命了一位新首席执行官，并于今年裁员超过 2,000 人，作为其全面重组的一部分，旨在为今年晚些时候推出更小巧、更实惠的电动 SUV 做准备。

Earlier Tuesday, an electric vehicle blog published a report citing two unnamed sources who said the company was considering either filing for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection or going private on the recommendation of consulting firm AlixPartners.
周二早些时候，一家电动汽车博客发布了一篇报道，援引两位匿名消息人士的话称，在咨询公司 AlixPartners 的建议下，该公司正在考虑申请第 11 章破产保护或进行私有化。

Twork said AlixPartners is assisting Lucid on strengthening its operations and “nothing else and has not recommended bankruptcy to management or the Board.” AlixPartners has been a go-to consulting firm for struggling electric vehicle companies in recent years.
Twork 表示，AlixPartners 正在协助 Lucid 加强运营，“仅此而已，并未向管理层或董事会建议破产。”近年来，AlixPartners 一直是陷入困境的电动汽车公司的首选咨询公司。

Lordstown Motors tapped the firm in 2021 after its CEO and CFO resigned, in an attempt to restructure its nascent business. The startup ultimately partnered with Taiwanese electronics giant Foxconn, though that relationship soured and Lordstown Motors went out of business. Faraday Future also brought in AlixPartners to try and implement recommendations made by its board of directors in the wake of an internal probe in 2022.
Lordstown Motors 在其首席执行官和首席财务官于 2021 年辞职后聘请了该公司，试图重组其初创业务。这家初创公司最终与台湾电子巨头富士康建立了合作关系，但双方关系后来恶化，Lordstown Motors 最终倒闭。Faraday Future 也曾聘请 AlixPartners，试图在 2022 年内部调查后落实其董事会提出的建议。

Lucid Motors recently revealed that it delivered 3,953 vehicles in the second quarter of this year, only slightly more than it shipped in the same period last year. It has historically struggled to find buyers for its luxury EVs, despite their impressive technological specs.
Lucid Motors 最近披露，今年第二季度交付了 3,953 辆汽车，仅比去年同期略有增加。尽管其豪华电动汽车拥有令人印象深刻的技术规格，但长期以来一直难以找到买家。

Along with the latest round of layoffs announced earlier this month, Lucid also said it would eliminate a second production shift at its Arizona factory as it aligns “production plans with anticipated demand.”
除了本月初宣布的最新一轮裁员外，Lucid 还表示将取消其亚利桑那州工厂的第二个生产班次，以使“生产计划与预期需求保持一致”。

Lucid Motors is also trying to get a luxury robotaxi service off the ground by the end of this year with partners Uber and Nuro. Uber has committed to buying at least 35,000 Nuro-equipped Lucid Motors vehicles for the effort over the next few years, with 10,000 of those being Gravity SUVs, and 25,000 based on the upcoming midsize EV platform.
Lucid Motors 还计划在今年年底前与合作伙伴 Uber 和 Nuro 共同启动一项豪华自动驾驶出租车服务。Uber 已承诺在未来几年内为该项目购买至少 35,000 辆配备 Nuro 技术的 Lucid Motors 汽车，其中 10,000 辆为 Gravity SUV，25,000 辆基于即将推出的中型电动汽车平台。
