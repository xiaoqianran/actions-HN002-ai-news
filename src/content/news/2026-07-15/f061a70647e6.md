# Prioritizing Search Space Regions in the Low Autocorrelation Binary Sequences Problem
# 低自相关二进制序列问题中的搜索空间区域优先级排序

**Abstract:** Low autocorrelation binary sequences problem (LABS) is a hard combinatorial optimization challenge with important applications in communications, signal processing, and satellite navigation.
**摘要：** 低自相关二进制序列问题（LABS）是一项艰巨的组合优化挑战，在通信、信号处理和卫星导航领域具有重要应用。

This paper proposes a hybrid search framework that combines Thompson sampling with parallel self-avoiding walks to adaptively allocate computational effort across restriction classes of the LABS search space.
本文提出了一种混合搜索框架，结合了汤普森采样（Thompson sampling）与并行自回避游走（parallel self-avoiding walks），以在 LABS 搜索空间的限制类中自适应地分配计算资源。

By modeling partitions as arms in a multi-armed bandit setting, the proposed method dynamically shifts search resources toward partitions that empirically produce higher merit factors while maintaining exploration of less-sampled regions.
通过将分区建模为多臂老虎机设置中的“臂”，该方法能够动态地将搜索资源转向经验上产生更高品质因数（merit factors）的分区，同时保持对采样较少区域的探索。

The approach is further accelerated through GPU-parallel execution, shared posterior updates, efficient neighborhood evaluation, and a Bloom filter for cycle prevention.
该方法通过 GPU 并行执行、共享后验更新、高效的邻域评估以及用于防止循环的布隆过滤器（Bloom filter）得到了进一步加速。

In addition, we use a two-stage optimization strategy that first searches constrained partitioned skew-symmetric spaces and then refines the best candidates in the unrestricted space.
此外，我们采用了一种两阶段优化策略：首先搜索受限的划分斜对称空间，然后在无限制空间中对最佳候选者进行精炼。

Experiments on long binary sequences show that the proposed method improves the previously best-known results for 35 sequence lengths in the range $450 \le L \le 527$ and for $L=573$.
在长二进制序列上的实验表明，该方法在 $450 \le L \le 527$ 范围内的 35 个序列长度以及 $L=573$ 的情况下，改进了此前已知的最佳结果。

In particular, we report a new longest sequence with merit factor exceeding $8.0$, obtained for $L=451$.
特别值得一提的是，我们报告了一个品质因数超过 8.0 的新最长序列，该结果是在 $L=451$ 时获得的。

The results also show that Thompson sampling effectively prioritizes partitions with better observed performance, confirming the value of online, data-driven resource allocation in LABS optimization.
结果还表明，汤普森采样有效地优先处理了观测性能更好的分区，证实了在线、数据驱动的资源分配在 LABS 优化中的价值。

Overall, the proposed framework provides a scalable and effective strategy for high-performance merit factor maximization.
总的来说，所提出的框架为高性能品质因数最大化提供了一种可扩展且有效的策略。
