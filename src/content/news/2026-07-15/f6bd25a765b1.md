# Position: Every Ground Truth is a Human Construction, not an Objective Truth
# 立场：每一个“真值”都是人类的建构，而非客观真理

**Abstract:** Ground truth datasets play a fundamental role as reference values in the training and evaluation of machine learning models. This position paper argues that ground truths are not neutral objective measurements that are naturally given, but instead that they are constructed by arrangements of humans and technologies.

**摘要：** “真值”（Ground truth）数据集在机器学习模型的训练和评估中扮演着参考基准的核心角色。本立场论文指出，真值并非自然存在的、中立的客观测量结果，而是由人类与技术共同协作构建而成的。

We argue that the ML community will benefit from articulating and discussing these often invisible or unreported choices and acknowledging that reference data sets are contingent, not universal. Focusing on the situated and context-dependent nature of ground truths can improve reliability by enabling a better informed perspective on where, when, and how the datasets, and the models they have shaped, can best be used.

我们认为，机器学习社区若能明确并讨论这些往往被忽视或未被披露的选择，并承认参考数据集具有偶然性而非普适性，将从中受益。关注真值的“情境性”和“依赖语境”的本质，能够通过提供更明智的视角——即在何处、何时以及如何最好地使用这些数据集及其所塑造的模型——来提高可靠性。

We argue for increasing `situated reliability' which includes articulating the limits and strengths of models and their truth claims. Finally, paying more attention to the construction of ground truths can support transparency, accountability, and interdisciplinary work.

我们主张提升“情境可靠性”（situated reliability），这包括明确阐述模型的局限性、优势及其真值主张。最后，更加关注真值的构建过程，将有助于促进透明度、问责制以及跨学科工作的开展。
