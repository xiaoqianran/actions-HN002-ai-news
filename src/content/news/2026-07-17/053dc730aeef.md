# C-Norm: Cell-Distribution Normalization Enables Precision Recognition of Medical-Cell Image
# C-Norm：细胞分布归一化技术助力医学细胞图像精准识别

**Abstract:** ThinPrep Cytologic Test (TCT) enables early cervical cancer screening, but manual reading is time-consuming and yields inconsistent diagnostic results among cytopathologists. Existing AI detection models perform poorly under real clinical conditions, primarily restricted by two key constraints: unbalanced spatial distribution of cell populations in TCT slides, and limited high-quality annotated cytology data relying on professional pathologist labeling.

**摘要：** 液基薄层细胞检测（TCT）是宫颈癌早期筛查的重要手段，但人工阅片耗时较长，且不同细胞病理学家之间的诊断结果往往存在差异。现有的 AI 检测模型在真实临床环境下的表现不佳，主要受限于两个关键瓶颈：一是 TCT 切片中细胞群体的空间分布不均衡，二是高质量的细胞学标注数据稀缺，且高度依赖专业病理学家的标注。

To address these limitations, we propose a Cell-Distribution Normalization (C-Norm) method. By decoupling abnormal and normal cells from the original TCT images and re-synthesizing them, this method ensures a uniform distribution of cell populations, thereby mitigating generalization degradation caused by distribution bias.

为了解决这些局限性，我们提出了一种细胞分布归一化（C-Norm）方法。该方法通过将原始 TCT 图像中的异常细胞与正常细胞解耦并进行重构，确保了细胞群体分布的均匀性，从而缓解了因分布偏差导致的泛化能力下降问题。

Building upon this, we integrate the YOLOv12 framework with a DINOv3 module. This hybrid architecture leverages the advanced detection capability of YOLO models and the superior feature representations of DINOv3 to capture subtle morphological nuances essential for precise recognition of TCT images.

在此基础上，我们将 YOLOv12 框架与 DINOv3 模块相结合。这种混合架构充分利用了 YOLO 模型先进的检测能力以及 DINOv3 卓越的特征表示能力，能够捕捉到对 TCT 图像精准识别至关重要的细微形态特征。

Extensive experiments demonstrate that our proposed method achieves state-of-the-art performance, significantly outperforming mainstream detection algorithms. The complete implementation is available at: this https URL.

大量实验证明，我们提出的方法达到了行业领先水平，性能显著优于主流检测算法。完整代码实现请见：[此链接](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13116)。
