# Uncertainty-Aware Sequential Decision Rules for Event-Triggered LLM Invocation in Streaming Systems
# 流式系统中用于事件触发式大模型调用的不确定性感知序列决策规则

**Abstract:** Streaming inference pipelines increasingly pair lightweight fast models with Large Language Models (LLMs) that provide rich semantic understanding at substantial cost. The central question of when to invoke the LLM has received limited formal treatment. We cast this as a risk-based sequential stopping problem, where a trigger policy fires when a risk functional over the observation history exceeds a threshold.

**摘要：** 流式推理流水线越来越多地将轻量级快速模型与能够提供丰富语义理解但成本高昂的大语言模型（LLM）相结合。关于何时调用 LLM 的核心问题目前尚未得到充分的正式研究。我们将此问题转化为一个基于风险的序列停止问题，即当观测历史上的风险函数超过阈值时，触发策略便会启动。

Within this framework, we prove six results: a minimum inter-event time bound excluding trigger chattering; optimality of threshold policies via smooth pasting; approximate SPRT guarantees under estimated parameters; O(sqrt(T log T)) regret for stationary streams, extending to O(sqrt((C_T + 1) T log T)) under C_T changepoints; O(1/sqrt(T)) convergence of online gradient descent for adaptive thresholds; and a calibration-to-miss-rate transfer inequality. Several classical trigger families, including event-triggered, optimal stopping, SPRT, CUSUM, and Bayesian triggers, can be expressed as special cases of this framework.

在此框架内，我们证明了六项结果：排除了触发抖动的最小事件间隔界限；通过平滑粘贴（smooth pasting）证明了阈值策略的最优性；估计参数下的近似 SPRT 保证；平稳流的 O(sqrt(T log T)) 遗憾界，并扩展至存在 C_T 个变点时的 O(sqrt((C_T + 1) T log T))；自适应阈值的在线梯度下降的 O(1/sqrt(T)) 收敛性；以及校准到漏报率的传递不等式。包括事件触发、最优停止、SPRT、CUSUM 和贝叶斯触发在内的多种经典触发族，均可表示为该框架的特例。

On turbofan degradation data (CMAPSS) with real LLM calls, we empirically verify the theoretical assumptions, ablate the risk function design, compare against six baselines including a RouteLLM-style router and contextual bandits, and analyze cost sensitivity and LLM failure modes. The results confirm sublinear regret, with alpha < 1 for all principled triggers; high diagnostic quality, with 92.9 percent of 1600 LLM diagnoses reaching grounding score >= 0.75 under our rubric; and that anomaly-score-driven risk functions dominate alternatives by roughly an order of magnitude on the Pareto AUC.

在包含真实 LLM 调用的涡扇发动机退化数据（CMAPSS）上，我们通过实验验证了理论假设，对风险函数设计进行了消融研究，与包括 RouteLLM 风格路由器和上下文多臂老虎机在内的六个基线进行了对比，并分析了成本敏感性和 LLM 的失效模式。结果证实了次线性遗憾，所有原则性触发器的 alpha 值均小于 1；诊断质量高，在我们的评估标准下，1600 次 LLM 诊断中有 92.9% 的基础得分（grounding score）达到 0.75 或以上；此外，由异常分数驱动的风险函数在帕累托 AUC（Pareto AUC）上比其他替代方案高出一个数量级。
