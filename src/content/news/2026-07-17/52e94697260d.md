# Beyond Backbone Backpropagation: A Decoupled Strategy for Efficient Transfer Learning
# 超越主干网络反向传播：一种用于高效迁移学习的解耦策略

Deep learning models achieve state-of-the-art image classification but face deployment challenges due to computational costs and energy demands.
深度学习模型在图像分类任务中达到了顶尖水平，但由于高昂的计算成本和能源需求，在实际部署时面临诸多挑战。

We propose a lightweight training strategy that adapts normalization layers of the model to the new domain and decouples feature extraction from classifier optimization, reducing overhead by precomputing features only once.
我们提出了一种轻量级训练策略，通过将模型的归一化层适配到新领域，并将特征提取与分类器优化解耦，仅需预计算一次特征，从而显著降低了计算开销。

A redesigned classifier head with margin-based weighted loss further minimizes ambiguity without end-to-end backpropagation.
通过重新设计的分类器头部以及基于边界（margin-based）的加权损失函数，该方法在无需端到端反向传播的情况下，进一步降低了分类歧义。

Evaluated across four CNN architectures (ResNet18, ResNet50, MobileNet, DenseNet121), three Transformer models (ViT, Swin and DeiT) and three medical datasets (Brain Cancer MRI, BreakHis and PatchCamelyon), our approach significantly reduces the required training time with only a marginal accuracy trade-off, often matching or surpassing baseline performance.
在四种 CNN 架构（ResNet18、ResNet50、MobileNet、DenseNet121）、三种 Transformer 模型（ViT、Swin 和 DeiT）以及三个医学数据集（脑癌 MRI、BreakHis 和 PatchCamelyon）上的评估结果表明，我们的方法在仅牺牲极小精度的情况下显著缩短了训练时间，且性能往往能达到或超过基准水平。

This efficiency translates to reducing CO2 by orders of magnitude, offering a practical and environmentally sustainable solution for resource-constrained clinical or prototyping environments.
这种效率提升意味着二氧化碳排放量可降低几个数量级，为资源受限的临床环境或原型开发环境提供了一种实用且环境可持续的解决方案。
