# 11 Best Sleeping Bags (2026): Ultralight, Warm Weather, for Kids
# 2026年11款最佳睡袋：超轻、暖天适用及儿童款

Many people who say they don't like camping are most put off by the idea of sleeping away from their beds. I get it. But there are sleeping bags that make camping a whole lot nicer, and toastier, and after years of testing we've found the best of the best.

许多说自己不喜欢露营的人，最抗拒的就是离开床铺睡觉。我完全理解。被包裹在尼龙袋子里确实无法与棉质或亚麻床单的奢华舒适感相提并论。但有些睡袋能让露营变得舒适且温暖得多，经过多年的测试，我们已经找到了其中的佼佼者。

Whether you’re backpacking, planning the perfect family camping trip, or trekking the Camino de Santiago, there's a sleeping bag for that. No matter how you travel, where you're headed, or how much comfort you seek, these are the best sleeping bags for every kind of camper.

无论你是去背包旅行、计划完美的家庭露营，还是徒步圣地亚哥朝圣之路，总有一款睡袋适合你。无论你如何旅行、去往何处，或者对舒适度有何要求，这些都是适合各类露营者的最佳睡袋。

### The Best Sleeping Bag for Backpackers
### 背包客的最佳睡袋

**Mountain Hardwear Bishop Pass 15**

Mountain Hardware's Bishop Pass 15 offers the best warmth-to-weight ratio while also managing to pack down small and not be too expensive. It isn't perfect, but it strikes the best compromise for most backpackers, being warm enough for the shoulder seasons and light enough (2 pounds and 5.4 ounces) that you won't mind it even when you barely need it in the summer.

Mountain Hardwear 的 Bishop Pass 15 提供了最佳的保暖重量比，同时还能压缩得很小，且价格并不昂贵。它虽然不是完美的，但为大多数背包客提供了最佳的折中方案：它在过渡季节足够保暖，且重量轻（2磅5.4盎司），即使在夏天几乎用不上它时，你也不会觉得它是负担。

I have slept in this bag for more than a month on the trail at this point, with nighttime temps ranging from 20 to 65 degrees Fahrenheit, and, yes, it was too much in the heat as a sleeping bag. But on those warmer nights, I unzipped it and used it like a warm blanket, and slept very well. It's a versatile bag.

目前为止，我在徒步过程中使用这款睡袋已经超过一个月，夜间温度在华氏20到65度（约-6.7到18.3摄氏度）之间。是的，在炎热天气下作为睡袋使用确实太热了。但在那些温暖的夜晚，我拉开拉链把它当作暖和的毯子盖，睡得非常好。这是一款功能多样的睡袋。

The Bishop Pass 15 uses 650-fill-power down wrapped in a 20-denier water-resistant ripstop nylon shell. One of the great things about this bag is the draft collar and face gasket feature, which do a great job of keeping your head warm. With the drawcord cinched down, you can conserve even more body heat.

Bishop Pass 15 使用 650 蓬松度的羽绒，外层包裹着 20 旦尼尔的防水防撕裂尼龙壳。这款睡袋的一大优点是防风领和面部密封设计，在保持头部温暖方面表现出色。拉紧抽绳后，你可以锁住更多的体温。

### The Best Sleeping Bag for Car Campers
### 汽车露营的最佳睡袋

**REI Co-op Siesta Hooded 20**

Car-camping sleeping bags aren’t a place to spend lots of money. Should your best efforts to cocoon warmth around you fail, there is, after all, a car to retreat to. That's why we love the REI Siesta Hooded 20—it's plenty warm and affordable. It's also not a mummy bag, because you're not climbing Denali; why cramp yourself if you don't have to?

汽车露营睡袋不需要投入太多资金。如果你的保暖措施失效，毕竟还有车可以躲进去。这就是我们喜欢 REI Siesta Hooded 20 的原因——它足够保暖且价格实惠。它也不是木乃伊式睡袋，因为你又不是去攀登迪纳利山；如果没必要，何必委屈自己呢？

The Siesta's rectangular cut makes for a much roomier, more comfortable bag. The Siesta is made of recycled polyester throughout, with a polyester filling. Despite that, the lining on this bag is noticeably softer than many others in this guide.

Siesta 的矩形剪裁使其空间更宽敞、更舒适。Siesta 全身采用再生聚酯纤维制成，填充物也是聚酯纤维。尽管如此，这款睡袋的内衬明显比本指南中的许多其他款式更柔软。

### The Bag With the Most Sizing Options
### 尺寸选择最丰富的睡袋

**REI Co-op Magma 15 Sleeping Bag**

REI's Magma series sleeping bags come in a bewildering array of sizes and shapes—there are nine sizes to choose from, ranging from Short Narrow, which weighs 2 pounds, to Long Wide, which is nearly 3 pounds. No matter what size you are, REI probably has you covered with this one.

REI 的 Magma 系列睡袋提供令人眼花缭乱的尺寸和形状选择——共有九种尺寸可选，从重2磅的“短窄版”到近3磅的“长宽版”。无论你的身材如何，REI 的这款产品大概都能满足你的需求。
