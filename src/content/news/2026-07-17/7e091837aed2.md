# MGFace: Mask-Gated Face Matching via Conditional Similarity Routing
# MGFace：基于条件相似度路由的掩码门控人脸匹配

**Abstract:** Face identification has achieved remarkable performance under normal conditions. Yet, its accuracy often degrades significantly when query faces are partially occluded, especially by facial masks. Existing re-ranking approaches improve robustness by exploiting patch-level similarities. Still, they often rely on costly, fine-grained matching mechanisms, which limit their efficiency in large-scale retrieval scenarios.

**摘要：** 人脸识别在正常条件下已取得了显著的性能表现。然而，当查询人脸被部分遮挡（尤其是被口罩遮挡）时，其准确率往往会大幅下降。现有的重排序方法通过利用补丁级（patch-level）相似度来提高鲁棒性。尽管如此，这些方法通常依赖于昂贵的细粒度匹配机制，这限制了它们在大规模检索场景中的效率。

In this paper, we propose MGFace, a mask-gated face identification pipeline that predicts the mask status of a query face and conditionally routes the similarity computation accordingly. Specifically, MGFace distinguishes between masked and unmasked queries, applies global embedding matching to unmasked queries, and activates mask-aware patch-level re-ranking only for masked queries. This design focuses on reliable upper-face regions while avoiding unnecessary fine-grained computation.

在本文中，我们提出了 MGFace，这是一种掩码门控人脸识别流水线，它能够预测查询人脸的掩码状态，并据此有条件地路由相似度计算。具体而言，MGFace 能够区分带口罩和不带口罩的查询，对未带口罩的查询应用全局嵌入匹配，并仅针对带口罩的查询激活感知掩码的补丁级重排序。这种设计专注于可靠的上脸区域，同时避免了不必要的细粒度计算。

Experiments on the extended LFW-Mask dataset show that MGFace achieves over 80% identification accuracy with the FaceNet backbone and over 90% with the ArcFace backbone. Compared with a previous EMD-based re-ranking method, MGFace achieves better identification performance while reducing query time by approximately 20x. These results demonstrate the effectiveness of MGFace in improving masked-face identification accuracy with low computational overhead.

在扩展的 LFW-Mask 数据集上的实验表明，MGFace 在使用 FaceNet 主干网络时识别准确率超过 80%，在使用 ArcFace 主干网络时超过 90%。与之前基于 EMD 的重排序方法相比，MGFace 在获得更好识别性能的同时，将查询时间缩短了约 20 倍。这些结果证明了 MGFace 在以低计算开销提高口罩人脸识别准确率方面的有效性。
