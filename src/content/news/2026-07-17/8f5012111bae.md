# A Masked Autoencoder Approach to Unsupervised Steel Surface Defect Recognition
# 一种用于无监督钢材表面缺陷识别的掩码自编码器方法

**Abstract:** Automated visual inspection of steel surface defects is a recurring quality control task in which labeled defect data is scarce and costly to obtain, while unlabeled surface images are abundant, which motivates self-supervised methods that learn useful representations without class labels.

**摘要：** 钢材表面缺陷的自动化视觉检测是一项反复进行的质量控制任务。在该任务中，带标签的缺陷数据稀缺且获取成本高昂，而未标注的表面图像却非常丰富，这促使人们研究无需类别标签即可学习有效特征表示的自监督方法。

A Transformer-based Masked Autoencoder is used here to learn representations of steel surface defects for unsupervised grouping. During pretraining, 75% of the input image patches are randomly masked, and a lightweight decoder reconstructs the masked regions from the visible 25%.

本文采用基于 Transformer 的掩码自编码器（Masked Autoencoder）来学习钢材表面缺陷的特征表示，以进行无监督分组。在预训练过程中，输入图像 75% 的补丁（patches）被随机掩盖，轻量级解码器则根据剩余 25% 的可见部分重建被掩盖的区域。

The encoder is trained jointly with an auxiliary defect localization objective, used only as a training signal and not evaluated as a detector. The decoder reaches a structural similarity score of 0.92 and a mean squared error of 0.47.

编码器与辅助缺陷定位目标联合训练，该目标仅作为训练信号，不作为检测器进行评估。解码器达到了 0.92 的结构相似性得分（SSIM）和 0.47 的均方误差（MSE）。

Features from the pretrained encoder are then clustered using UMAP for dimensionality reduction and Agglomerative clustering, reaching a Hungarian matched accuracy of 91.3% against the six known defect categories.

随后，利用 UMAP 对预训练编码器提取的特征进行降维，并使用凝聚聚类（Agglomerative clustering）进行聚类，在六个已知缺陷类别上达到了 91.3% 的匈牙利匹配准确率（Hungarian matched accuracy）。
