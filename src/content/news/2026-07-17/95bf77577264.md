# AI-powered travel agency Fora hits unicorn status, raises $60M
# AI 驱动的旅行社 Fora 跻身独角兽行列，融资 6000 万美元

AI-powered travel agency Fora announced a $60 million Series D round led by Forerunner and Tactile Ventures, valuing the company at $1 billion.
AI 驱动的旅行社 Fora 宣布完成 6000 万美元的 D 轮融资，由 Forerunner 和 Tactile Ventures 领投，公司估值达到 10 亿美元。

Fora, founded in 2021, is a two-part platform: It lets people easily become travel agents by providing the infrastructure to support client communication and travel planning; it also lets users find and communicate with advisors as they plan trips for occasions like honeymoons or family trips, to destinations like Costa Rica or Thailand.
Fora 成立于 2021 年，是一个双向平台：它通过提供支持客户沟通和旅行规划的基础设施，让人们能够轻松成为旅行代理；同时，它也让用户在规划蜜月或家庭旅行等行程时，能够寻找并与顾问沟通，目的地涵盖哥斯达黎加或泰国等地。

Other investors in this latest round include Insight Partners and Thrive Capital. The company has raised $138.5 million in funding to date, it said.
本轮融资的其他投资者还包括 Insight Partners 和 Thrive Capital。该公司表示，迄今为止已累计融资 1.385 亿美元。

Part of the fresh capital will go toward expanding Fora’s AI assistant, Via, which helps travel agents on its platform with tedious administrative tasks such as research and itinerary building. The hope is that the human travel agents who use the platform can instead spend more time building client relations, and that using Fora’s AI will help human productivity rather than attempt to replace it.
部分新资金将用于扩展 Fora 的 AI 助手 Via，该助手旨在帮助平台上的旅行代理处理研究和行程规划等繁琐的行政任务。公司希望使用该平台的真人旅行代理能将更多时间花在建立客户关系上，并期望通过 Fora 的 AI 技术提升人类生产力，而非试图取代人类。

Fora said that since its launch, agents on the platform have booked over $3 billion worth of travel, with a majority of its agent users being new to travel advising. The company also hopes to use the money to hire and grow in other travel categories, such as cruises and flights.
Fora 表示，自上线以来，平台上的代理已预订了价值超过 30 亿美元的旅行产品，其中大多数代理用户都是旅行咨询领域的新手。该公司还希望利用这笔资金进行招聘，并拓展邮轮和机票等其他旅行类别。
