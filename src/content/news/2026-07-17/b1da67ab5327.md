# Create, edit and star in videos with two Google Vids updates
# 通过两项 Google Vids 更新，轻松创作、编辑并主演视频

Today, we’re rolling out two new updates to Google Vids to make it easier than ever to create, edit and personalize your videos: Gemini Omni and personal avatars. Gemini Omni takes the hard work out of the editing process, so generating and refining high-quality clips is as easy as writing a simple prompt. And with personal avatars, you have an entirely new way to star in videos without setting up a camera. Here’s a look at how these new tools transform video creation.

今天，我们为 Google Vids 推出了两项新更新，旨在让视频的创作、编辑和个性化变得前所未有的简单：Gemini Omni 和个人数字分身。Gemini Omni 简化了繁琐的编辑流程，让高质量视频片段的生成与优化变得像编写简单提示词一样容易。而通过个人数字分身，你无需架设摄像机，就能以全新的方式在视频中出镜。以下是这些新工具如何改变视频创作的概览。

### Generate videos from text and images
### 通过文本和图像生成视频

In the past year, you’ve created millions of videos in Google Vids to share your stories and bring your ideas to life. In February, we made the process simpler by rolling out Veo 3.1 to all users, helping everyone generate videos within Vids. Now, we’re bringing Gemini Omni to Vids, so you can create and edit high-quality videos just by describing what you want to see. Start with a simple text prompt in natural language, and add image references — like a photo or a rough sketch — for more detail. Omni mixes your inputs to generate a video that matches your vision.

在过去的一年里，用户在 Google Vids 中创作了数百万个视频，用于分享故事并实现创意。今年二月，我们向所有用户推出了 Veo 3.1，简化了创作流程，帮助大家在 Vids 内生成视频。现在，我们将 Gemini Omni 引入 Vids，让你只需描述想要的内容，即可创建和编辑高质量视频。你可以从简单的自然语言文本提示词开始，并添加照片或草图等图像参考以获取更多细节。Omni 会融合你的输入内容，生成符合你构想的视频。

### Chat to edit, step-by-step
### 通过对话进行分步编辑

Getting a video exactly right often requires a few tweaks after that first draft — and making those edits is now as easy as describing what you want to see. Whether you're refining a video you generated with Omni or a clip you shot on your phone, you can use everyday language to prompt Vids to swap backgrounds, fix lighting or add effects. Because Omni supports step-by-step edits, you can now make changes to your videos without starting from scratch.

想要完美呈现视频，初稿完成后往往需要进行一些调整——现在，进行这些编辑就像描述你的需求一样简单。无论你是要优化由 Omni 生成的视频，还是手机拍摄的片段，都可以使用日常语言提示 Vids 更换背景、修复光线或添加特效。由于 Omni 支持分步编辑，你现在无需从头开始即可对视频进行修改。

### Star in videos, instantly
### 即刻在视频中出镜

Want to send a quick video update or personalized shout-out, but don't have time to get camera-ready? With new personal avatars, you can create a digital avatar that looks and sounds like you. Just upload a selfie and a short voice recording. From there, type what you want to say and your avatar will deliver the message — no recording required. Gemini Omni and personal avatars are available in Google Vids for Google AI Pro and Ultra subscribers and Google Workspace business customers. Access to personal avatars is currently limited to users in certain regions who are 18 or older. Personal avatars are linked to a user’s Google Account and are restricted to the account holder’s likeness.

想要发送一段快速的视频更新或个性化问候，却没时间准备出镜？借助全新的个人数字分身，你可以创建一个外貌和声音都与你相似的数字形象。只需上传一张自拍照和一段简短的录音，然后输入你想说的话，你的数字分身就会替你传达信息——无需再次录制。Gemini Omni 和个人数字分身现已面向 Google AI Pro 和 Ultra 订阅用户以及 Google Workspace 企业客户开放。个人数字分身功能目前仅限于特定地区 18 岁及以上的用户使用。个人数字分身与用户的 Google 账号绑定，且仅限于使用账号持有者的肖像。

### Create and share with transparency
### 透明化创作与分享

We want you to feel confident sharing what you create. To ensure content transparency, every generated clip includes an invisible SynthID digital watermark. This allows people to verify that a video was created with AI, so you can explore your creativity and share your AI videos responsibly.

我们希望你能自信地分享你的创作。为了确保内容透明度，每个生成的片段都包含隐形的 SynthID 数字水印。这让人们能够验证视频是否由 AI 生成，从而让你在探索创意的同时，能够负责任地分享 AI 视频。
