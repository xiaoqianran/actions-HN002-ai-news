# Connect more of your apps to Search
# 将更多应用连接至搜索 (Search)

You’ll be able to securely link and interact with your go-to services directly in AI Mode.
你将能够直接在 AI 模式（AI Mode）中安全地关联并使用你常用的服务。

You can now connect your favorite apps directly to AI Mode in Search to get more done. Simply link services like Instacart, Canva, or YouTube Music to build lists, design projects, or curate playlists without leaving your search results. Head over to AI Mode in Search today to start using these new integrations.
现在，你可以将喜爱的应用直接连接到搜索中的 AI 模式，从而更高效地完成任务。只需关联 Instacart、Canva 或 YouTube Music 等服务，即可在不离开搜索结果页面的情况下创建清单、设计项目或整理播放列表。立即前往搜索中的 AI 模式，开始体验这些新集成功能。

You can already connect some of your favorite services to the Gemini app, and now, to help you get even more done, we’re enabling you to directly connect your apps in Search. Starting to roll out this week in the U.S., you can securely link and interact with some of your go-to services right in AI Mode.
此前，你已经可以将部分喜爱的服务连接到 Gemini 应用中；现在，为了帮助你完成更多工作，我们支持你直接在搜索中连接这些应用。该功能将于本周在美国率先推出，你可以直接在 AI 模式中安全地关联并使用你常用的服务。

Here’s a look at how these integrations in Search can streamline your day: Let’s say you’re planning a barbecue and using AI Mode for help with your grocery list. If you connect Instacart, you can add the ingredients straight into your Instacart shopping cart and check out with just a few taps on their app or website. With Personal Intelligence and your connected apps, Search can give even more tailored responses.
以下是这些搜索集成功能如何简化你日常生活的示例：假设你正在筹划一场烧烤聚会，并使用 AI 模式来协助整理购物清单。如果你连接了 Instacart，就可以直接将食材添加到 Instacart 购物车中，只需在他们的应用或网站上轻点几下即可完成结账。借助个人智能（Personal Intelligence）和已连接的应用，搜索功能能够提供更加个性化的回复。

Or, if you’re working on a project where you need some design concepts – like creating a flyer – you can ask Canva to show you some template options. Want to build the perfect soundtrack for your next party? You can use AI Mode to curate a playlist, instantly save it to YouTube Music and press play to start the celebration.
或者，如果你正在进行一个需要设计灵感的项目（例如制作传单），你可以让 Canva 为你展示一些模板选项。想要为下一次派对打造完美的背景音乐？你可以使用 AI 模式整理播放列表，将其即时保存到 YouTube Music，然后点击播放，开启狂欢。

Head over to AI Mode in Search to try the new connected apps today. We're working with a range of partners and look forward to launching with more apps soon.
立即前往搜索中的 AI 模式，体验这些新连接的应用。我们正在与多家合作伙伴携手，期待在不久后推出更多应用支持。
