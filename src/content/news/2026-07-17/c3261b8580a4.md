# Google’s AI Mode now lets you link and interact with select apps
# Google AI Mode 现已支持关联并与精选应用进行交互

Google announced on Thursday that it now allows users to link and interact with some of their go-to apps right in AI Mode, the tech giant’s conversational search experience. At launch, supported apps include Instacart, Canva, and YouTube.
Google 周四宣布，用户现在可以在其对话式搜索体验“AI Mode”中直接关联并与部分常用应用进行交互。首批支持的应用包括 Instacart、Canva 和 YouTube。

With this new update, Google is expanding AI Mode beyond answering questions and into completing tasks across the apps they use regularly. The tech giant is also likely hoping that users will rely on AI Mode more often for things like planning and shopping. Plus, the rollout will allow Google to better compete with rivals like OpenAI’s ChatGPT and Anthropic’s Claude, both of which support app integrations.
通过此次更新，Google 正在将 AI Mode 的功能从单纯的回答问题扩展到跨常用应用完成任务。这家科技巨头也希望用户能更频繁地依赖 AI Mode 来进行规划和购物等活动。此外，此次功能上线将使 Google 能够更好地与 OpenAI 的 ChatGPT 和 Anthropic 的 Claude 等竞争对手抗衡，这两者此前均已支持应用集成。

In one example, Google says that if you’re planning a barbecue and using AI Mode to create a grocery list, you can connect your Instacart account to add the ingredients directly to your shopping cart and quickly check out on the Instacart app or website.
以一个例子说明，Google 表示，如果你正在计划一场烧烤并使用 AI Mode 创建购物清单，你可以关联你的 Instacart 账户，直接将食材添加到购物车，并在 Instacart 应用或网站上快速结账。

Or, if you’re working on a project and need design ideas, like for a flyer, you can ask Canva to show you a selection of templates. In another example, Google says you could use AI Mode to curate a playlist for your next party and instantly save it to YouTube Music. The update is rolling out to users in the U.S. Google says it’s working with a range of partners and plans to launch support for more apps soon.
或者，如果你正在进行一个项目并需要设计灵感（例如制作传单），你可以让 Canva 为你展示一系列模板。在另一个例子中，Google 表示你可以使用 AI Mode 为你的下一次派对策划播放列表，并将其即时保存到 YouTube Music。此次更新目前正向美国用户推送。Google 表示，他们正在与多家合作伙伴协作，并计划在不久后支持更多应用。

Today’s announcement builds on a capability Google launched earlier this year at Google I/O that lets users connect third-party apps to the Gemini app to complete tasks faster. Supported apps include Canva, OpenTable, Spark, Instacart, and more. Since its launch in early 2025, Google has been continuously building out AI Mode with more capabilities.
今天的公告是在 Google 今年早些时候于 Google I/O 大会上推出的功能基础上进行的扩展，该功能允许用户将第三方应用连接到 Gemini 应用以更快地完成任务。支持的应用包括 Canva、OpenTable、Spark、Instacart 等。自 2025 年初推出以来，Google 一直在不断为 AI Mode 增加更多功能。

Most recently, the tech giant announced that AI Mode can now help check whether an item you need is in stock at a nearby store. Google also recently added the ability for users to explore the web side-by-side with AI Mode to do things like compare details and ask follow-up questions while preserving the context of their search. Earlier this year, Google launched “Personal Intelligence” on AI Mode, enabling it to tap into users’ Gmail and Google Photos to provide more individualized responses.
最近，这家科技巨头宣布 AI Mode 现在可以帮助检查你需要的商品在附近商店是否有货。Google 最近还增加了让用户在 AI Mode 侧边栏浏览网页的功能，以便在保留搜索上下文的同时进行对比细节和追问等操作。今年早些时候，Google 在 AI Mode 上推出了“个人智能”（Personal Intelligence），使其能够调用用户的 Gmail 和 Google 相册，从而提供更具个性化的回复。
