# Self-Supervised Visual Representation Learning: Pretrain-Finetuning or Joint Training?
# 自监督视觉表征学习：预训练-微调还是联合训练？

**Abstract:** Self-supervision is a powerful technique for learning visual representations from unlabeled data. Existing techniques primarily adopt a two-stage approach for self-supervised learning (SSL): a pretraining stage on unlabeled data followed by a finetuning stage on labeled data. While this pipeline has demonstrated extreme effectiveness, the interaction between self-supervised and supervised learning objectives remains insufficiently understood.

**摘要：** 自监督学习是从无标签数据中学习视觉表征的强大技术。现有的自监督学习（SSL）技术主要采用两阶段方法：即在无标签数据上进行预训练阶段，随后在有标签数据上进行微调阶段。尽管这一流程已证明了其极高的有效性，但自监督学习目标与监督学习目标之间的相互作用仍未得到充分理解。

In this work, we systematically investigate whether jointly optimizing the self-supervised and supervised objectives during training provides a better alternative. We compare two training paradigms: (1) the aforementioned pretraining followed by finetuning (PFT) and (2) joint training (JT), where self-supervised and supervised losses are optimized simultaneously in the same network.

在这项工作中，我们系统地研究了在训练过程中联合优化自监督和监督目标是否能提供更好的替代方案。我们比较了两种训练范式：（1）上述的预训练后微调（PFT），以及（2）联合训练（JT），即在同一个网络中同时优化自监督损失和监督损失。

Across eight representative SSL methods and diverse computer vision tasks on natural, medical, crisis response, and remote sensing data, we evaluate performance under varying percentages of labeled data. Our results reveal that the relative effectiveness of PFT and JT depends strongly on the task at hand, the availability of labeled data, and the complexity of the domain.

通过八种代表性的 SSL 方法以及在自然图像、医学图像、危机响应和遥感数据上的多种计算机视觉任务，我们评估了在不同比例标签数据下的性能。研究结果表明，PFT 和 JT 的相对有效性在很大程度上取决于具体的任务、标签数据的可用性以及领域的复杂性。

We find that JT consistently improves data and training efficiency while being robust in low-label settings, while PFT is more reliable in more specialized domains. We further analyze representation quality, robustness, and cross-domain generalization, providing new insights into how self-supervised and supervised objectives interact during optimization. We establish a comprehensive empirical benchmark for hybrid SSL-based semi-supervised learning and offer practical guidance for selecting appropriate training strategies across diverse vision applications.

我们发现，JT 在低标签环境下表现稳健，并能持续提高数据和训练效率，而 PFT 在更专业的领域中则更为可靠。我们进一步分析了表征质量、鲁棒性和跨域泛化能力，为自监督和监督目标在优化过程中的相互作用提供了新的见解。我们为基于混合 SSL 的半监督学习建立了全面的实证基准，并为在各种视觉应用中选择合适的训练策略提供了实践指导。
