# MultiRef-Compass: Towards Comprehensive Evaluation of Multi-Reference-to-Audio-Video Generation

**MultiRef-Compass：迈向多参考音频-视频生成的全面评估**

Multi-reference-to-audio-video (MR2AV) generation aims to generate coherent audio-video content conditioned on multiple references and textual instructions. Existing benchmarks mainly focus on text-driven generation, single-reference subject preservation, or isolated audio-video alignment, leaving the emerging MR2AV setting largely unexplored.

多参考音频-视频（MR2AV）生成旨在根据多个参考资料和文本指令生成连贯的音视频内容。现有的基准测试主要集中在文本驱动生成、单参考主体保持或孤立的音视频对齐上，而新兴的 MR2AV 设置在很大程度上仍未得到探索。

Compared with these settings, MR2AV requires models to jointly reason over multiple references while generating synchronized visual and audio content. Models must not only preserve each reference faithfully but also correctly bind and compose multiple referenced entities into coherent audio-visual events.

与这些设置相比，MR2AV 要求模型在生成同步视觉和音频内容的同时，对多个参考资料进行联合推理。模型不仅必须忠实地保留每个参考资料，还必须正确地绑定多个参考实体，并将它们组合成连贯的视听事件。

To address this gap, we introduce MultiRef-Compass, a unified benchmark for MR2AV generation. It comprises 350 carefully curated samples constructed through a scalable and controllable asset-composition pipeline, covering multi-view subject preservation, multi-entity binding, and human-object-scene composition.

为了弥补这一空白，我们推出了 MultiRef-Compass，这是一个用于 MR2AV 生成的统一基准测试。它包含 350 个精心挑选的样本，这些样本通过可扩展且可控的资产组合流程构建，涵盖了多视角主体保持、多实体绑定以及人-物-场景组合。

To provide interpretable assessment, MultiRef-Compass defines an evaluation protocol with four dimensions: Basic Quality, Reference Consistency, Audio-Visual Consistency, and Instruction Following, using 14 sub-metrics. MultiRef-Compass integrates automatic metrics with a rejudging-enhanced MLLM-as-a-Judge framework, enabling scalable and auditable evaluation of both perceptual fidelity and reference-conditioned composition.

为了提供可解释的评估，MultiRef-Compass 定义了一套包含四个维度的评估协议：基础质量、参考一致性、视听一致性和指令遵循，并使用了 14 个子指标。MultiRef-Compass 将自动指标与增强型“多模态大模型作为裁判”（MLLM-as-a-Judge）框架相结合，实现了对感知保真度和参考条件组合的可扩展且可审计的评估。

Extensive experiments on eight representative MR2AV systems reveal substantial room for improvement across multiple evaluation dimensions, underscoring the need for a comprehensive benchmark and positioning MultiRef-Compass as a foundation for future MR2AV research.

对八个代表性 MR2AV 系统的广泛实验表明，在多个评估维度上仍有巨大的改进空间，这凸显了建立全面基准测试的必要性，并将 MultiRef-Compass 定位为未来 MR2AV 研究的基础。
