# DCVC-MB: Neural B-Frame Video Compression using State Space Models
# DCVC-MB：基于状态空间模型的神经 B 帧视频压缩

**Abstract:** In this paper we propose DCVC-Mamba (DCVC-MB), a neural video codec framework for B-frame coding. Our approach incorporates an IBP frame strategy for low-delay B-frame coding, a spatio-temporal fusion model based on state-space models for bidirectional temporal prediction, and an entropy-aware skipping mechanism that selectively omits coding certain latents to reduce entropy coding times.

**摘要：** 在本文中，我们提出了 DCVC-Mamba (DCVC-MB)，这是一个用于 B 帧编码的神经视频编解码器框架。我们的方法结合了用于低延迟 B 帧编码的 IBP 帧策略、基于状态空间模型的双向时间预测时空融合模型，以及一种感知熵的跳过机制，该机制通过选择性地忽略某些潜在变量（latents）的编码来减少熵编码时间。

In addition to our model contributions we also implement two inference-time strategies that enhance compression performance. Experimental evaluation shows that DCVC-MB compares favorably to existing NVCs and traditional codecs. The method demonstrates BD-rate reductions of up to $8.98\%$ on average compared to prior neural video codecs, and improvements of up to $30.45\%$ and $1.81\%$ over the VTM-19.0-LDP and VTM-19.0-RA(Inter-GoP=16) benchmarks, respectively, contributing to advances in neural video compression.

除了模型贡献外，我们还实现了两种可提升压缩性能的推理时策略。实验评估表明，DCVC-MB 的表现优于现有的神经视频编解码器（NVC）和传统编解码器。该方法与先前的神经视频编解码器相比，平均 BD-rate 降低了高达 $8.98\%$；与 VTM-19.0-LDP 和 VTM-19.0-RA (Inter-GoP=16) 基准相比，性能分别提升了高达 $30.45\%$ 和 $1.81\%$，为神经视频压缩领域的发展做出了贡献。
