# How Much of a 10-K Matters? Aggregation-Dependent Value of Full-Text versus Risk-Factor Sentiment
# 10-K 报告到底有多少内容重要？全文本与风险因素情感分析的聚合依赖价值

**Abstract:** Financial sentiment extraction has largely relied on news text and supervised extraction against return labels alone, leaving 10-K filings -- and volatility, the target risk disclosure is arguably best suited to informing -- comparatively unexplored.

**摘要：** 金融情感提取在很大程度上依赖于新闻文本以及针对收益标签的监督式提取，这使得 10-K 报告——以及风险披露最适合预测的目标指标“波动率”——在很大程度上未得到充分研究。

We extend a supervised lexicon-learning approach to 10-K filings and their Item 1A risk-factor sections, training sentiment scores against both return and volatility labels at three levels of aggregation: sector, portfolio, and individual firm.

我们将一种监督式词汇学习方法扩展应用到 10-K 报告及其“第 1A 项（Item 1A）”风险因素部分，并在行业、投资组合和单个公司三个聚合层级上，针对收益和波动率标签训练情感得分。

Across 1,383 filings from 94 Nasdaq-100 technology constituents (2006--2023), we evaluate the resulting twelve sentiment metrics on classification accuracy, correlation with realised market outcomes, and qualitative lexical content.

通过对 94 家纳斯达克 100 指数科技成分股公司（2006-2023 年间）的 1,383 份报告进行分析，我们从分类准确性、与实际市场结果的相关性以及定性词汇内容三个维度，评估了由此产生的十二项情感指标。

Full-filing text produces more accurate sentiment at the sector and portfolio level for both targets, but this reverses at the individual-firm level, where the narrower Item 1A section performs better -- an effect we attribute to the interaction between document volume and the amount of independent training signal available at each level of aggregation.

研究发现，在行业和投资组合层面，全文本报告对上述两个目标（收益与波动率）的情感预测更为准确；但在单个公司层面，情况则恰恰相反，范围更窄的“第 1A 项”部分表现更好。我们将这种效应归因于文档体量与各聚合层级上可用独立训练信号量之间的相互作用。

A Loughran-McDonald dictionary baseline is consistently, strongly negatively correlated with price at every level tested, underscoring the value of a supervised approach for regulatory disclosure text.

Loughran-McDonald 词典基准在所有测试层级上均与价格呈现持续且强烈的负相关，这凸显了针对监管披露文本采用监督式学习方法的价值。

These findings, and the design choices they motivate, establish the sentiment-generation methodology underlying a subsequent, larger-scale, multi-source system.

这些发现及其所推动的设计选择，为后续更大规模的多源系统奠定了情感生成方法论的基础。
