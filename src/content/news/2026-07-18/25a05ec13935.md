# UzWordnet and Generative AI for Learning Uzbek by Game Playing
# UzWordnet 与生成式 AI：通过游戏学习乌兹别克语

**Title:** UzWordnet and Generative AI for Learning Uzbek by Game Playing
**标题：** UzWordnet 与生成式 AI：通过游戏学习乌兹别克语

**Authors:** Alessandro Agostini, Saydobid Khusanov, Mirkamol Mirkamilov
**作者：** Alessandro Agostini, Saydobid Khusanov, Mirkamol Mirkamilov

**Abstract:** This paper presents an educational system architecture that enables learners to practice the Uzbek language through game-playing. The architecture integrates UzWordnet and the largest currently available orthographic dictionary for Uzbek as core lexical resources, together with generative AI as a fundamental component for learning support.
**摘要：** 本文提出了一种教育系统架构，使学习者能够通过游戏练习乌兹别克语。该架构整合了 UzWordnet 和目前可用的最大的乌兹别克语正字法词典作为核心词汇资源，并将生成式 AI 作为学习支持的基础组件。

We design four educational games to facilitate Uzbek language learning and propose a game-based methodology for improving UzWordnet as a direct by-product of game dynamics. Our approach combines game design and lexical resources to address objectives that are at the same time educational (language learning) and lexical (improvement and enrichment of a lexical resource).
我们设计了四款教育游戏以促进乌兹别克语学习，并提出了一种基于游戏的方法论，将 UzWordnet 的改进作为游戏动态的直接副产品。我们的方法结合了游戏设计和词汇资源，旨在同时实现教育（语言学习）和词汇（词汇资源的改进与丰富）目标。
