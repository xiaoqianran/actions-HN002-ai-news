# MonteRET: AI Agent Enhancing Multimodal LLMs with Multi-granularity Knowledge Retrieval for Chest CT Report Generation
# MonteRET：通过多粒度知识检索增强多模态大模型以生成胸部 CT 报告的 AI 智能体

**Abstract:** Automated chest CT report generation remains challenging because clinically faithful reporting requires both whole-volume understanding and accurate description of localized anatomical findings. 

**摘要：** 自动生成胸部 CT 报告仍然是一项挑战，因为临床上准确的报告既需要对整个扫描体积的理解，也需要对局部解剖发现的精确描述。

Here we developed and retrospectively evaluated MonteRET, a region-aware retrieval-enhanced framework for generating chest CT findings sections. 

在此，我们开发并回顾性评估了 MonteRET，这是一个用于生成胸部 CT 影像发现部分的区域感知检索增强框架。

MonteRET integrates global CT features with region-level anatomical representations, retrieves clinically relevant knowledge using predicted medical conditions and region-level vision-language alignment, and refines initial reports through a knowledge-guided report rewriting agent. 

MonteRET 将全局 CT 特征与区域级解剖表征相结合，利用预测的医学状况和区域级视觉-语言对齐来检索临床相关知识，并通过知识引导的报告重写智能体对初始报告进行优化。

We trained our model on a public cohort with 24,128 CT scans from RadGenome-ChestCT. We evaluated MonteRET on the public RadGenome-ChestCT test set of 1,564 CT scans and an external cohort of 82 CT scans from NewYork-Presbyterian/Weill Cornell Medical Center. 

我们在包含 24,128 例来自 RadGenome-ChestCT 的 CT 扫描公共队列上训练了我们的模型。我们在包含 1,564 例 CT 扫描的 RadGenome-ChestCT 公共测试集以及来自纽约长老会医院/威尔康奈尔医学中心的 82 例 CT 扫描的外部队列上对 MonteRET 进行了评估。

MonteRET improved report quality, semantic similarity, and clinical efficacy compared with a matched baseline and several state-of-the-art methods. Gains were most pronounced for recall, suggesting fewer omitted findings. Human expert evaluation by radiology residents also favored MonteRET.

与匹配的基线模型和几种最先进的方法相比，MonteRET 提高了报告质量、语义相似度和临床有效性。在召回率方面的提升最为显著，这意味着遗漏的病灶更少。放射科住院医师进行的人工专家评估也更倾向于 MonteRET。
