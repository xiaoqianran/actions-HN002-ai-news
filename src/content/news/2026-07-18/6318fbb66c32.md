# Samsung’s redesigned Z Fold 8 with a wide display just leaked
# 三星重新设计的 Z Fold 8 宽屏手机外观遭泄露

Leaker Evan Blass shared images of Samsung’s redesigned Galaxy Z Fold 8 just days before the July 22nd launch event where Samsung is expected to officially announce the phone.
爆料人 Evan Blass 在三星 7 月 22 日发布会前夕分享了重新设计的 Galaxy Z Fold 8 的图片，预计三星届时将正式发布这款手机。

The leaked images show a shorter, wider foldable matching earlier leaks of the Z Fold 8. They also reveal some of its specs, including a Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite For Galaxy processor, 50MP wide and 50MP ultra wide rear cameras, 10MP selfie cameras on the inside and outside screens, and an advertised battery life of “up to 26 hours of video playback.”
泄露的图片显示，这款折叠屏手机机身更短、更宽，与此前关于 Z Fold 8 的传闻相符。图片还揭示了部分规格，包括高通骁龙 8 Elite For Galaxy 处理器、5000 万像素广角和 5000 万像素超广角后置摄像头、内外屏各配备 1000 万像素自拍镜头，以及官方宣称“长达 26 小时的视频播放”续航能力。

Blass also posted images of the Galaxy Z Flip 8 and shared images of the Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra in an email received by The Verge. They match glimpses of both phones shown in a Spider-Man teaser Samsung released earlier this week, and include a few specs. According to the images, the Galaxy Z Flip 8 will have a 4,300mAh battery, a 10MP main screen camera, and a 50MP wide / 12MP ultrawide cover screen camera, the same as the current Z Flip 7’s camera setup.
Blass 还发布了 Galaxy Z Flip 8 的图片，并在发给《The Verge》的邮件中分享了 Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra 的图片。这些图片与三星本周早些时候发布的《蜘蛛侠》预告片中出现的手机外观相吻合，并包含了一些规格信息。根据图片显示，Galaxy Z Flip 8 将配备 4300mAh 电池、1000 万像素内屏摄像头，以及 5000 万像素广角/1200 万像素超广角外屏摄像头，这与现款 Z Flip 7 的相机配置相同。

Blass’s images also show the Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra advertising a 5,000mAh battery offering up to 27 hours of video playback. It also appears to have a 50MP ultra wide / 200MP wide / 10MP telephoto rear camera, along with 10MP cover and main screen cameras.
Blass 的图片还显示，Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra 宣传其配备 5000mAh 电池，可提供长达 27 小时的视频播放时间。该机似乎还搭载了 5000 万像素超广角/2 亿像素广角/1000 万像素长焦的后置三摄系统，以及 1000 万像素的外屏和内屏摄像头。
