### Certified Domain Consistency for Multi-Domain Retrieval: Label-Free Per-Domain Contamination Control with Conformal Risk Guarantees
### 多领域检索的认证领域一致性：基于共形风险保证的无标签领域污染控制

**Abstract:** Retrieval over corpora that mix several domains often returns relevant but wrong-domain evidence that ranking metrics miss and that conformal risk control bounds only marginally, under-covering the worst domains. This work introduces C3R, a drop-in control layer that, from an inferred domain posterior and no query-time label, certifies a per-domain contamination budget where feasible and otherwise abstains rather than silently violating; on the hardest domains it guarantees a reduction, not a tight bound.

**摘要：** 在混合了多个领域的语料库中进行检索时，系统往往会返回相关但领域错误的证据。现有的排序指标难以捕捉此类问题，而共形风险控制（Conformal Risk Control）也仅能提供边缘化的界限，导致对最差领域的覆盖不足。本文引入了 C3R，这是一个即插即用的控制层。它通过推断出的领域后验概率（无需查询时的标签）来认证各领域的污染预算。在可行的情况下，它会进行认证；若不可行，则选择放弃而非静默违规。对于最困难的领域，它保证的是污染的降低，而非严格的界限。

The core is a two-split scheme built on risk-controlling prediction sets, whose finite-sample transfer bound crosses from the inferred to the true domain with fully estimable slack, supports heterogeneous budgets, and inverts for deployment. Population validity rests on this bound and a controlled simulation; across a thousand resampled calibrations the certificate never violates (a stability result) while marginal control violates the most-contaminated domain in every draw, and soft demotion retains more recall than the strongest calibrated cascade at equal certified contamination.

其核心是一种基于风险控制预测集的双重拆分方案。该方案的有限样本转移界限能够从推断领域跨越到真实领域，且具有完全可估计的松弛度，支持异构预算，并可反转用于部署。总体有效性建立在此界限和受控模拟的基础上；在数千次重采样校准中，该认证从未违规（表现出稳定性），而边缘控制在每次抽取中都会在污染最严重的领域违规。此外，在相同的认证污染水平下，软降级（soft demotion）比最强的校准级联模型保留了更高的召回率。

The method replicates across open testbeds including an independent one from public federal regulations, and an LLM-judged downstream probe indicates wrong-authority grounding rises with contamination and falls under control. The layer is frozen-stack and reranker-agnostic.

该方法在多个开放测试平台上均可复现，包括一个来自公共联邦法规的独立测试集。由大语言模型（LLM）评估的下游探测显示，错误权威的归因随污染增加而上升，并在受控后下降。该控制层采用冻结堆栈（frozen-stack）设计，且与重排序器（reranker）无关。
