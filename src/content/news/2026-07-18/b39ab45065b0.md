# The Best New Camping Gear at REI (2026): Tents, Coolers, Bikes, More
# REI 2026 年度最佳露营装备：帐篷、保温箱、自行车及更多

Ask most people what they love about camping, and they probably won’t tell you about how easy it is to set up their tent or how long the ice lasts in their Yeti. Yes, your tent should go up quickly, and your cooler should keep things cold, but then thoughts of gear should fade away as you focus on enjoying paddleboarding, hiking, or reading in a hammock.
问问大多数人他们为什么热爱露营，他们大概不会告诉你是因为搭帐篷有多容易，或者 Yeti 保温箱里的冰块能撑多久。没错，帐篷确实应该快速搭建，保温箱也应该保持低温，但一旦这些准备就绪，你就应该忘掉装备，专注于享受桨板冲浪、徒步旅行或在吊床上阅读的乐趣。

The secret to success is gear you can set and forget. REI’s latest camping gear promises to do just that—and does so without costing a fortune. The co-op’s 2026 line of camping and hiking gear offers some new products, along with updates to old favorites. I’ve spent the last few months putting many of the REI’s items through their paces, and the following emerged as my favorites.
成功的秘诀在于那些让你“设置好后即可忘掉”的装备。REI 最新的露营装备承诺做到这一点，而且价格并不昂贵。该合作社 2026 系列露营和徒步装备既包含了一些新产品，也对经典款式进行了升级。过去几个月里，我亲自测试了许多 REI 的产品，以下是我心目中的首选。

### A Sturdy, Family-Friendly Car Camping Tent: REI Co-Op Westward Tent, from $449
### 一款坚固且适合家庭使用的汽车露营帐篷：REI Co-Op Westward 帐篷，449 美元起

The Westward 6 is a spacious, family-friendly tent for three-season camping. It has a cross-pole design and comfortably sleeps two adults, three kids, and one dog. It could theoretically fit six adults, though that would feel crowded. Setup is relatively simple, thanks to the color-coded poles. After you’ve completed the process once or twice, you’ll be able to set up your tent in 10 minutes flat.
Westward 6 是一款宽敞、适合家庭使用的三季露营帐篷。它采用交叉杆设计，可舒适地容纳两名成人、三名儿童和一只狗。理论上它可以容纳六名成人，但那样会显得拥挤。得益于颜色编码的支架，搭建过程相对简单。在练习一两次后，你可以在 10 分钟内轻松完成搭建。

One caveat: If you’re expecting inclement weather during your camping trip, you should spend the extra time to stake the tent, especially the guylines on the rainfly. But even with this extra step, setup is about as simple as it gets.
需要提醒的是：如果你预计露营期间会遇到恶劣天气，你应该多花点时间固定地钉，尤其是防雨罩上的防风绳。但即使加上这一步，它的搭建过程依然非常简单。

The 77-inch peak height means you won’t need to crouch as you move around inside, and the huge front vestibule provides space to store dirty gear, which helps keep the tent’s interior clean and less cluttered. There are plenty of storage pockets around the tent for stashing gear, plus a clip suspended in the center that can support a heavy-duty lantern.
77 英寸（约 195 厘米）的顶部高度意味着你在帐篷内走动时无需弯腰，巨大的前厅提供了存放脏装备的空间，有助于保持帐篷内部整洁。帐篷周围有充足的储物袋用于存放装备，中心还悬挂着一个挂钩，可以支撑重型露营灯。

### A Luxury Camping Pad for Less: REI Co-Op Westward Dreamer Self-Inflating Bed, from $199
### 性价比极高的豪华露营垫：REI Co-Op Westward Dreamer 自充气床垫，199 美元起

REI’s Westward Dreamer Self-Inflating Bed is the best deal around for a luxury camp pad. My top pick camp pad, the Therm-a-Rest MondoKing, is slightly better at insulating heat (having an R-value of 7 to the Dreamer's 6.7), but for summertime use, the $60 cheaper Dreamer is the better buy. This updated version (it was previously called the REI Camp Dreamer XL) now includes a repair kit and an improved valve system, which makes fine-tuning its inflation level much easier.
REI 的 Westward Dreamer 自充气床垫是目前市面上性价比最高的豪华露营垫。我首选的露营垫 Therm-a-Rest MondoKing 在隔热性能上略胜一筹（R 值为 7，而 Dreamer 为 6.7），但对于夏季使用来说，便宜 60 美元的 Dreamer 是更好的选择。这个升级版本（之前称为 REI Camp Dreamer XL）现在包含了一个维修套件和改进的阀门系统，使得微调充气程度变得更加容易。

### A Warm Sleeping Bag: REI Co-Op Siesta Hooded 20 Sleeping Bag, from $149
### 一款保暖睡袋：REI Co-Op Siesta Hooded 20 睡袋，149 美元起

My favorite sleeping bag for car camping, REI’s Siesta Hooded 20, is an impressively versatile bag, thanks to its double zipper system. There’s a full-length zipper, which means you can turn it into a quilt on warmer nights, and there’s also a second partial-length zipper on the other side, so you can have more airflow when you want it. The Siesta’s 20-degree-Fahrenheit rating makes it warm enough for use in three seasons in most places.
我最喜欢的汽车露营睡袋是 REI 的 Siesta Hooded 20，得益于其双拉链系统，它具有令人印象深刻的多功能性。它有一条全长拉链，意味着在温暖的夜晚你可以把它变成被子；另一侧还有一条半长拉链，让你在需要时获得更好的空气流通。Siesta 的华氏 20 度（约零下 6 摄氏度）温标使其在大多数地区的三季露营中都足够保暖。
