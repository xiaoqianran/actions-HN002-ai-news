# San Francisco orders Apple, Google to remove nudify apps from app stores
# 旧金山要求苹果和谷歌从应用商店下架“一键脱衣”类应用

This week, San Francisco’s attorney general, David Chiu, sent cease-and-desist letters, demanding that Apple and Google remove 13 so-called nudification apps from their app stores, Wired reported. Nudification apps can make it trivially easy to transform ordinary photos of real people into explicit images. The harmful AI tools allow bad actors to remove clothing, change a person’s features, place them in sexualized positions, and swap victims’ faces onto other people’s naked bodies.

据《连线》（Wired）报道，本周，旧金山市检察官大卫·邱（David Chiu）发出停止侵权函，要求苹果和谷歌从其应用商店中下架 13 款所谓的“一键脱衣”（nudification）应用。这类应用可以极其轻易地将普通人的照片转化为露骨的色情图像。这些有害的 AI 工具允许不法分子移除衣物、改变人物特征、将其置于性化姿势中，并将受害者的脸部替换到他人的裸体上。

Chiu’s letter warned that app stores were violating “California’s laws that prohibit supporting services that create deepfake pornography,” Wired reported. Talking to Wired, Chiu said his office was “absolutely horrified” by how ubiquitous the nudifying technology has become, victimizing mostly women and children at an alarming scale as more tools became available. “These images are used to bully, humiliate, and threaten women and girls,” Chiu told Wired. “This industry has a horrific impact on one’s reputation, mental health, loss of autonomy. There have been victims who’ve been suicidal.”

据《连线》报道，邱在信中警告称，这些应用商店违反了“加州禁止支持制作深度伪造色情内容服务的法律”。在接受《连线》采访时，邱表示，他的办公室对这种“一键脱衣”技术变得如此普遍感到“极其震惊”。随着此类工具的增多，受害者主要是妇女和儿童，情况令人担忧。“这些图像被用来欺凌、羞辱和威胁女性和女孩，”邱告诉《连线》，“这个行业对个人的声誉、心理健康和自主权造成了可怕的影响。甚至有受害者因此产生了自杀倾向。”

Wired reviewed the letters and confirmed that Chiu asked Google to remove five apps and Apple to remove eight. No apps were named in the report to “avoid pushing people toward them,” Wired said. However, one app had more than a million downloads and advertised features to sexualize images of women or make “free and uncensored” videos, Wired reported. Chiu told Wired that allowing any such apps to remain in app stores is unacceptable. “Generating non-consensual intimate images is illegal, harmful, and completely unacceptable,” Chiu said.

《连线》查阅了这些信件，确认邱要求谷歌下架 5 款应用，要求苹果下架 8 款应用。报道中未提及具体应用名称，以“避免引导人们去使用它们”。然而，《连线》指出，其中一款应用下载量超过百万，并宣传其具有将女性图像性化或制作“免费且未经审查”视频的功能。邱告诉《连线》，允许任何此类应用留在应用商店是不可接受的。“生成非自愿的私密图像是非法的、有害的，且完全不可接受，”邱说道。

His office estimated that Apple and Google have likely “made millions of dollars in fees” by ignoring the harmful apps rather than taking stronger actions or developing better detection to avoid profiting off a public nuisance. In a statement to Ars, Google spokesperson Dan Jackson said the five apps that Chiu flagged were suspended from the Google Play store for violating policies against harmful content. “Google Play does not allow apps that contain sexual content, and we continually take proactive steps to detect and remove apps with harmful content,” Jackson said. “When violations are reported to us, we investigate and take swift action, which in the case of these apps has included suspending hundreds of violating apps and restricting related search terms like ‘nudify’ on our store.” Apple did not respond to Ars’ request to comment.

他的办公室估计，苹果和谷歌通过忽视这些有害应用，而不是采取更强有力的行动或开发更好的检测手段，可能已经“赚取了数百万美元的费用”，从而从这种公共危害中获利。谷歌发言人丹·杰克逊（Dan Jackson）在给 Ars 的声明中表示，邱所指出的 5 款应用因违反有害内容政策已被从 Google Play 商店中暂停。“Google Play 不允许包含色情内容的应用，我们持续采取主动措施来检测和删除有害内容应用，”杰克逊说，“当我们收到违规举报时，我们会进行调查并迅速采取行动，就这些应用而言，我们已经暂停了数百个违规应用，并限制了商店中‘nudify’等相关搜索词。”苹果未回应 Ars 的置评请求。

### The Grok elephant in the room
### 房间里的大象：Grok 问题

Although Apple has removed some apps flagged by researchers, the iPhone maker otherwise has remained notably silent amid calls to police nudification apps more aggressively in its App Store. App stores’ detection abilities matter, as Wired’s report noted that researchers have recently warned that some harmful apps have gotten better at avoiding app store removals by only promoting face-swapping features while hiding nudifying capabilities. In a May preprint paper, researchers identified 420 apps touted as generic face-swapping tools and tested 155 to see if they could be used to sexualize images. In 70 percent of apps tested, nudification was possible.

尽管苹果已经下架了一些被研究人员标记的应用，但在要求其更积极地监管应用商店中“一键脱衣”应用的呼声中，这家 iPhone 制造商一直保持着明显的沉默。应用商店的检测能力至关重要，正如《连线》报道所指出的，研究人员最近警告称，一些有害应用通过仅宣传换脸功能而隐藏“一键脱衣”功能，从而更好地规避了应用商店的下架审查。在 5 月份的一篇预印本论文中，研究人员识别出 420 款标榜为通用换脸工具的应用，并测试了其中 155 款，以查看它们是否可用于将图像性化。结果显示，70% 的测试应用具备“一键脱衣”功能。

There’s also the Grok elephant in the room to consider. This week, xAI filed a lawsuit confirming that it found instances of Grok-generated child sexual abuse materials (CSAM) and other nonconsensual intimate imagery (NCII) targeting adults. To prevent the Grok misuse, xAI sued the user allegedly responsible for prompting Grok to generate the illegal content, but the question remains whether xAI is liable for the outputs. Ever since the Grok scandal erupted, app stores have faced questions on whether xAI’s chatbot is violating app store policies against harmful content. Back in April, Apple told Senators that it had privately threatened to remove Grok, NBC News reported, but the xAI app remains in the app store today.

此外，还有“房间里的大象”——Grok 问题需要考虑。本周，xAI 提起诉讼，证实其发现了 Grok 生成儿童性虐待材料（CSAM）以及针对成年人的其他非自愿私密图像（NCII）的案例。为了防止 Grok 被滥用，xAI 起诉了据称诱导 Grok 生成非法内容的用户，但问题仍然存在：xAI 是否应对其输出内容负责。自 Grok 丑闻爆发以来，应用商店一直面临质疑，即 xAI 的聊天机器人是否违反了应用商店关于有害内容的政策。据 NBC 新闻报道，早在 4 月份，苹果就曾告诉参议员，它已私下威胁要下架 Grok，但 xAI 应用至今仍留在应用商店中。

So long as it’s possible to use Grok to generate illegal content, app stores will likely continue to face pressure to take action to prevent harmful outputs, just as Chiu said that app stores should be doing with all apps allowing nudification if they want to comply with California deepfake laws. Although Google’s spokesperson said that Google continuously tests generative AI apps to make sure they have safeguards preventing NCII and CSAM creation, Jackson did not respond to Ars’ request to comment on whether the way that Grok performs today is an exception to Google’s policy.

只要仍有可能利用 Grok 生成非法内容，应用商店就可能会继续面临采取行动以防止有害输出的压力，正如邱所言，如果应用商店想要遵守加州的深度伪造法律，就应该对所有允许“一键脱衣”的应用采取同样的行动。尽管谷歌发言人表示，谷歌会持续测试生成式 AI 应用，以确保它们具备防止生成 NCII 和 CSAM 的保障措施，但杰克逊并未回应 Ars 关于 Grok 目前的表现是否属于谷歌政策例外情况的置评请求。

In his letters to app stores, Chiu did not request Grok removals, but it is clear he is hoping that his demands will help broadly tighten up enforcement in both app stores, starting with removing the apps that his office flagged. “My hope is that Apple and Google will immediately remove these apps and strengthen their screening systems to make sure that apps like this never get onto their platforms in the future,” he says. “It’s our hope that these companies will do the right thing—but if they don’t, we will have to consider all of our legal options.”

在给应用商店的信中，邱并未要求下架 Grok，但很明显，他希望自己的要求能有助于广泛收紧两个应用商店的执法力度，首先从下架其办公室标记的应用开始。“我希望苹果和谷歌能立即下架这些应用，并加强其筛选系统，以确保此类应用未来永远不会进入他们的平台，”他说，“我们希望这些公司能做正确的事——但如果他们不这样做，我们将不得不考虑所有的法律手段。”
