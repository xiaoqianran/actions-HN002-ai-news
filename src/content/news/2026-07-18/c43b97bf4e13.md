# XCT-SAM: Sequential Parameter-Efficient Domain Adaptation of SAM for Industrial XCT Defect Segmentation
# XCT-SAM：用于工业 XCT 缺陷分割的 SAM 顺序参数高效领域自适应

**Abstract:** Defect segmentation in additive manufacturing (AM) X-ray computed tomography (XCT) images remains challenging due to severe class imbalance and large distribution shifts across scan conditions. Although recent foundation models such as the Segment Anything Model (SAM) provide strong general-purpose segmentation priors, their natural-image pre-training transfers poorly to the AM XCT domain, where defects appear as subtle non-semantic microstructural anomalies.

**摘要：** 由于严重的类别不平衡以及不同扫描条件下的巨大分布偏移，增材制造 (AM) X 射线计算机断层扫描 (XCT) 图像中的缺陷分割仍然具有挑战性。尽管最近的基础模型（如 Segment Anything Model，即 SAM）提供了强大的通用分割先验，但其自然图像预训练在迁移到 AM XCT 领域时效果不佳，因为在该领域中，缺陷表现为细微的非语义微观结构异常。

Moreover, adapting SAM to the AM domain is further limited by the large domain gap and scarcity of labeled real XCT data. We present XCT-SAM, a sequential parameter-efficient adaptation framework for AM XCT defect segmentation. Instead of adapting SAM directly from natural images to XCT data, we first fine-tune Conv-LoRA adapters on an alloy-microstructure dataset and subsequently transfer the adapted model to XCT images, progressively bridging the domain gap.

此外，将 SAM 适配到 AM 领域还受到巨大领域差距和带标签真实 XCT 数据稀缺的限制。我们提出了 XCT-SAM，这是一个用于 AM XCT 缺陷分割的顺序参数高效自适应框架。我们没有将 SAM 直接从自然图像适配到 XCT 数据，而是首先在合金微观结构数据集上微调 Conv-LoRA 适配器，随后将适配后的模型迁移到 XCT 图像，从而逐步弥合领域差距。

Using Conv-LoRA adapters with rank r=2, the framework injects convolutional spatial inductive bias into SAM's backbone while training approximately 4.15M parameters and keeping over 99% of the model frozen. We evaluate XCT-SAM on out-of-distribution CycleGAN-XCT benchmarks and real-world NIST XCT scans. Across both settings, XCT-SAM consistently outperforms zero-shot SAM and other domain-adapted SAM baselines, achieving the best overall IoU and Dice scores.

该框架使用秩 r=2 的 Conv-LoRA 适配器，将卷积空间归纳偏置注入到 SAM 的主干网络中，同时仅训练约 415 万个参数，并保持超过 99% 的模型参数冻结。我们在分布外 (OOD) 的 CycleGAN-XCT 基准测试和真实的 NIST XCT 扫描数据上评估了 XCT-SAM。在这两种设置下，XCT-SAM 的表现均持续优于零样本 (zero-shot) SAM 和其他领域自适应 SAM 基线，实现了最佳的整体 IoU 和 Dice 分数。

These results demonstrate the effectiveness of intermediate domain adaptation with parameter-efficient adapters for industrial XCT defect segmentation. The source code is publicly available at this [URL].

这些结果证明了使用参数高效适配器进行中间领域自适应对于工业 XCT 缺陷分割的有效性。源代码已在 [URL] 公开。
