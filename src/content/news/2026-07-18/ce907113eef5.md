# 3D Lane Detection with Odometry for High-Speed Vehicle Racing
# 用于高速赛车场景的里程计辅助 3D 车道检测

**Abstract:** Lane boundary detection is a critical component in autonomous driving systems and has been rigorously studied in regular driving scenarios. However, it is less explored in vehicle racing, where the car moves at higher speeds across more extreme road geometries.
**摘要：** 车道边界检测是自动驾驶系统中的关键组成部分，在常规驾驶场景中已得到深入研究。然而，在车辆竞速场景中，由于车辆以更高速度行驶且面临更极端的道路几何形状，该领域的研究相对较少。

To study this problem, we introduce a new dataset for 3D lane detection in racing, featuring >250k images from multiple camera feeds and inertial measurements taken with a Lexus LC 500 driving on a closed circuit.
为了研究这一问题，我们引入了一个用于赛车 3D 车道检测的新数据集。该数据集包含超过 25 万张图像，这些图像来自雷克萨斯 LC 500 在封闭赛道上行驶时采集的多路摄像头数据及惯性测量数据。

With this dataset, we compare various approaches to 3D lane detection and propose modifications that permit frames to be processed at rates of almost 300Hz while retaining high predictive performance in the racing application. This facilitates a multi-camera ensemble approach that is validated on hardware.
利用该数据集，我们对比了多种 3D 车道检测方法，并提出了改进方案，使得在赛车应用中能够以近 300Hz 的帧率进行处理，同时保持高预测性能。这为在硬件上验证多摄像头集成方案提供了可能。

We show that sensing modalities such as inertial measurements can be leveraged for pre-integration to regress road geometries over both cameras and time, yielding improvements in key metrics.
我们证明了惯性测量等传感模态可用于预积分，从而在摄像头和时间维度上回归道路几何形状，进而提升关键指标。

Compared to methods such as BevLaneDet, adding odometry and ensemble predictions improves the F1 score by 3 points and reduces near-vehicle mean absolute errors (MAEs) by >30%. We show F1 scores >0.9 and lateral MAEs of <0.18m in vehicle deployments.
与 BevLaneDet 等方法相比，加入里程计和集成预测将 F1 分数提高了 3 个百分点，并将车辆附近的平均绝对误差 (MAE) 降低了 30% 以上。在实车部署中，我们实现了超过 0.9 的 F1 分数和小于 0.18 米的横向平均绝对误差。
