# Ars is looking for a senior technology reporter, and you might be it!
# Ars 正在招聘资深科技记者，也许那个人就是你！

If you’re a skilled writer with outsize technology chops who gets excited by the idea of taking the Ars audience with you as you go hands-on with hardware—all kinds of hardware!—then this position has your name all over it. Plus, you get to have me as your boss, and how could that be anything other than awesome?!

如果你是一位拥有卓越技术功底的资深写手，并且热衷于带领 Ars 的读者们一起上手体验各种硬件——各种各样的硬件！——那么这个职位简直就是为你量身定做的。此外，你还能成为我的下属，这难道不是一件超棒的事情吗？！

The job: The formal job description and application is right here and has all the specifics and HR stuff, including salary range. The short summary is that we’re looking for an experienced writer (where “experience” means “several years of professional work”), who is a technologist first and foremost. We want people who tinker around with tech because they can’t not tinker around with tech; that kind of joy tends to leak out into the work, and it’s impossible to fake.

关于这份工作：正式的职位描述和申请入口就在这里，里面包含了所有细节和人力资源相关信息，包括薪资范围。简而言之，我们正在寻找一位经验丰富的写手（这里的“经验”指“数年的专业工作经历”），且必须首先是一位技术专家。我们希望寻找那些因为“不折腾技术就难受”而热衷于钻研技术的人；那种对技术的热爱往往会自然流露在作品中，这是无法伪装的。

Some specifics on the subject matter the job will cover, copied from the job description:
* Desktop and mobile operating systems, including Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android
* Desktops and laptops
* Mobile phones and related devices
* CPU & GPU architecture
* Storage, including NAS, hard drives, and solid-state drives
* Self-hosting and homelab-type services, including and especially solutions based around open source software

以下是该职位将涵盖的部分具体内容，摘自职位描述：
* 桌面和移动操作系统，包括 Windows、macOS、Linux、iOS 和 Android
* 台式机和笔记本电脑
* 手机及相关设备
* CPU 和 GPU 架构
* 存储设备，包括 NAS、硬盘驱动器和固态硬盘
* 自托管和家庭实验室（homelab）类服务，包括且特别侧重于基于开源软件的解决方案

This is a full-time job with benefits. Candidates must be eligible to work in the United States and should expect to travel perhaps 4–5 times per year. As this is a work-from-home position, you should be comfy in that environment (though we try to all get together and see each other at least once a year at a big in-person all-hands meeting). If that all sounds good to you, hit the link and apply right here. We hope to have this position filled by August!

这是一份全职工作，提供福利待遇。候选人必须具备在美国工作的资格，并预计每年需要出差 4-5 次。由于这是一个远程办公职位，你需要适应这种工作环境（尽管我们尽量每年至少聚会一次，举行大型的线下全员会议）。如果这一切听起来不错，请点击链接并在下方申请。我们希望在八月份之前填补这个职位！
