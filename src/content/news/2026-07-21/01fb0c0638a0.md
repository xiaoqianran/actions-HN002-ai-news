**Title:** Adaptive Multi-Step Lookahead Decoding for Diffusion Language Models  
**标题：** 扩散语言模型的自适应多步前瞻解码

**Authors:** Yingqian Cui, Wei Deng, Lantao Mei, Hang Li, Charu C. Aggarwal, Hui Liu, Yue Xing  
**作者：** 崔盈倩, 邓韦, 梅兰涛, 李航, 查鲁·C·阿加瓦尔, 刘辉, 岳星

**Abstract:**  
Masked diffusion language models (DLMs) enable parallel text generation by iteratively refining masked tokens, offering a promising alternative to autoregressive decoding.  
掩码扩散语言模型（DLMs）通过迭代优化掩码标记实现并行文本生成，为自回归解码提供了一种有前景的替代方案。

Recent lookahead-based decoding methods improve the accuracy--efficiency trade-off by exploring future decoding states before committing token updates.  
近期基于前瞻的解码方法通过在确定标记更新前探索未来解码状态，提升了准确率-效率权衡。

However, existing approaches mainly rely on shallow one-step lookahead, which optimizes immediate information gain but can be suboptimal for longer-horizon decoding trajectories.  
然而，现有方法主要依赖浅层单步前瞻，虽优化了即时信息增益，但在更长跨度的解码轨迹中可能非最优。

Meanwhile, we find that a naive extension for deeper lookahead is also ineffective, as fixed-depth rollout introduces additional computation and cannot adapt to heterogeneous intermediate decoding states.  
同时，我们发现对更深层前瞻的朴素扩展同样无效，因为固定深度的展开引入了额外计算，且无法适应异质中间解码状态。

Thus, in this work, we propose AdaLook, an adaptive lookahead framework for DLM decoding.  
因此，本文提出AdaLook，一个用于DLM解码的自适应前瞻框架。

AdaLook dynamically determines whether to continue rollout based on candidate-score variance and further enables branch expansion when intermediate rollout states require additional exploration.  
AdaLook基于候选分数方差动态决定是否继续展开，并在中间展开状态需要额外探索时启用分支扩展。

This design avoids unnecessary deep rollout while allowing the decoder to re-trigger lookahead from informative intermediate states.  
该设计避免了不必要的深度展开，同时允许解码器从信息丰富的中间状态重新触发前瞻。

Experiments on various benchmarks and models demonstrate that AdaLook achieves a better accuracy--decoding steps trade-off than existing one-step lookahead decoding methods.  
在多个基准和模型上的实验表明，AdaLook相比现有单步前瞻解码方法实现了更优的准确率-解码步数权衡。
