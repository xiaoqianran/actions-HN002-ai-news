# Title: India's first privately developed rocket reaches orbit on dramatic debut launch  
**标题：印度首枚私营研发火箭首飞戏剧性成功入轨**

---

Indian space officials celebrated the debut flight of Skyroot Aerospace’s Vikram-1 rocket, India’s first fully commercial satellite launcher, as a “grand success” Saturday after an on-target climb into a 280-mile-high orbit following liftoff from an island spaceport in the Bay of Bengal.  
印度空间官员周六庆祝斯凯罗斯航空航天公司（Skyroot Aerospace）的“维克拉姆-1”（Vikram-1）火箭首飞取得“巨大成功”。这枚印度首枚完全商业化的卫星发射器从孟加拉湾一座岛屿航天港发射，精准爬升至280英里（约450公里）高的轨道。

The Vikram-1 lifted off from India’s primary spaceport on Sriharikota Island at 1:35 am EDT (06:35 UTC) Saturday, around midday at the launch base along India’s southeast coast. The launch was delayed more than a half-hour to resolve a last-minute technical problem.  
“维克拉姆-1”于周六美国东部时间凌晨1:35（世界时06:35）从印度主要航天港斯里哈里科塔岛发射，此时印度东南海岸发射基地正值中午。发射因解决最后一刻的技术问题而推迟了半个多小时。

The countdown resumed, culminating in the command to ignite Vikram-1’s solid-fueled first stage booster to propel the rocket off the launch pad.  
倒计时恢复后，最终指令点燃“维克拉姆-1”的固体燃料第一级助推器，推动火箭离开发射台。

Vikram-1 is modest in size compared to India’s larger workhorse rockets. Skyroot’s rocket stands about 72 feet (22 meters) tall, with the capability to place payloads of up to 770 pounds (350 kilograms) into low-Earth orbit.  
与印度更大的主力火箭相比，“维克拉姆-1”体型较小。斯凯罗斯的火箭高约72英尺（22米），能将最多770磅（350公斤）的有效载荷送入近地轨道。

This makes Vikram-1 somewhat larger than the Electron launch vehicle developed by Rocket Lab, the world’s most successful dedicated small satellite launcher.  
这使“维克拉姆-1”比世界最成功的专业小卫星发射器——火箭实验室（Rocket Lab）的“电子号”（Electron）略大一些。

The flight Saturday went off without any major problems. Three solid-fueled rocket motors fired in succession to reach space, then a small liquid-fueled fourth stage ignited and accelerated to orbital velocity, some 17,000 mph.  
周六的飞行未出现重大故障。三台固体燃料火箭发动机依次点火进入太空，随后一台小型液体燃料第四级点火，加速至约每小时17,000英里（约27,000公里）的轨道速度。

Live views from onboard cameras showed each phase of the launch sequence. The only sign of anything unusual came during the separation of the rocket’s third stage from its fourth stage.  
机载摄像头的实时画面展示了发射序列的每个阶段。唯一异常迹象出现在火箭第三级与第四级分离时。

The spent third stage motor appeared to remain near the fourth stage during a brief coast, rather than backing away to a greater distance. Nevertheless, the fourth stage did its job, firing its 3D-printed engine to reach an orbit approximately 280 miles (450 kilometers) high at an inclination of 60 degrees to the equator, quite close to preflight predictions, according to Skyroot Aerospace.  
耗尽燃料的第三级发动机在短暂滑行期间似乎停留在第四级附近，而非远离。尽管如此，第四级仍完成使命，点燃其3D打印发动机，抵达距地约280英里（450公里）、赤道倾角60度的轨道，与发射前预测非常接近，斯凯罗斯航空航天公司表示。

US military tracking data confirmed the rocket’s successful arrival in orbit.  
美国军方跟踪数据确认火箭成功入轨。

---

**Beating the odds**  
**突破 odds（成功率低）的挑战**

“We achieved one of the biggest milestones ever in India’s space sector—the first private orbital rocket reaching orbit on the very first attempt,” said Pawan Kumar Chandana, Skyroot’s cofounder and CEO, in remarks to the company’s launch team. “It still feels like a dream, and you all made this dream happen.”  
“我们实现了印度航天领域有史以来最重要的里程碑之一——首枚私营轨道火箭首次尝试即成功入轨，”斯凯罗斯联合创始人兼CEO帕万·库马尔·钱达纳（Pawan Kumar Chandana）在向公司发射团队讲话时说，“这仍感觉像一场梦，而你们让这个梦成真。”

The first flights of new private orbital-class rockets don’t have a great track record. It took SpaceX four tries before reaching orbit with the Falcon 1 rocket for the first time in 2008. Rocket Lab’s Electron didn’t make it to orbit on its first launch in 2017.  
新型私营轨道级火箭的首次飞行成功率并不高。SpaceX的“猎鹰1号”（Falcon 1）直到2008年第四次尝试才首次入轨。火箭实验室的“电子号”在2017年首次发射时未能入轨。

Blue Origin beat the odds with the inaugural flight of its heavy-lift New Glenn rocket in 2025, but the company’s engineers had previous experience with numerous launches of the smaller New Shepard suborbital rocket.  
蓝色起源（Blue Origin）在2025年其重型火箭“新格伦”（New Glenn）的首次飞行中突破了低成功率挑战，但该公司工程师此前已有多次较小亚轨道火箭“新谢泼德”（New Shepard）的发射经验。

“On the first attempt, reaching orbit, I never thought it was possible,” Chandana said. “Skyroot’s team made it possible. A big, big, big shoutout to this phenomenal team, which made it happen. In fact, this launch was nothing short of a suspense movie.”  
“首次尝试就入轨，我从未想过这可能实现，”钱达纳说，“斯凯罗斯团队让它成为可能。我要向这支非凡的团队致以巨大、巨大、巨大的感谢，是他们让这一切发生。事实上，这次发射无异于一部悬疑大片。”

Skyroot officials set humble goals for the first launch of Vikram-1. In a press kit released before the flight, the company said its primary objective for the launch was to complete a successful liftoff, clear the tower at the launch site, and gather maximum data during ascent.  
斯凯罗斯官员为“维克拉姆-1”首次发射设定了 modest（ modest 可译为“务实”或“ modest 本身保留）的目标。在发射前发布的新闻资料中，公司表示其主要目标是完成成功起飞、清除发射场塔架，并在上升过程中收集最大数据量。

“The mission objective was only to lift off and clear the tower,” said Pawan Goenka, chairman of IN-SPACe, a government organization set up in 2020 to promote India’s commercial space industry. “That was only about 100 meters, but what we went to was 450 kilometers, and it also released all the satellites that were supposed to release. So the mission was absolutely perfect.”  
“任务目标仅仅是起飞并清除塔架，”2020年成立的政府机构IN-SPACe（印度太空促进中心）主席帕万·戈恩卡（Pawan Goenka）表示，“那只是约100米，但我们达到了450公里，还释放了所有预定卫星。所以任务绝对完美。”

---

In a statement, the Indian space agency, ISRO, said it offered “handholding and support” to the Skyroot venture by providing access to solid rocket motor casting and test facilities at ISRO’s spaceport on Sriharikota. ISRO also allowed Skyroot to launch from one of its two active launch pads.  
印度空间研究组织（ISRO）在一份声明中表示，它通过提供斯里哈里科塔航天港固体火箭发动机浇铸和测试设施的使用权，向斯凯罗斯 venture（企业）提供了“ handholding（手把手指导）和支持”。ISRO还允许斯凯罗斯从其两个 active（正在使用的）发射台之一发射。

Painted blue and white, the Vikram-1 is made of lightweight carbon composite materials and is named for the Indian physicist Vikram Sarabhai, considered the father of the Indian space program.  
蓝白相间的“维克拉姆-1”由轻质碳复合材料制成，以印度物理学家、印度航天计划之父维克拉姆·萨拉巴伊（Vikram Sarabhai）命名。

Skyroot successfully launched a suborbital rocket, Vikram-S, to an altitude of nearly 300,000 feet (90 kilometers) in November 2022. The Vikram-1 builds on lessons learned with Vikram-S.  
斯凯罗斯于2022年11月成功发射了亚轨道火箭“维克拉姆-S”，抵达近300,000英尺（90公里）高度。“维克拉姆-1”借鉴了“维克拉姆-S”的经验。

Skyroot’s future roadmap includes the Vikram-1U, with additional strap-on solid rocket boosters to haul heavier payloads, and the Vikram-2, which will debut a cryogenic upper stage to reach a payload capacity of 2,000 pounds (900 kilograms) to low-Earth orbit.  
斯凯罗斯的未来路线图包括“维克拉姆-1U”，它将增加捆绑式固体火箭助推器以运载更重载荷；以及“维克拉姆-2”，它将首次采用低温上面级，以实现近地轨道2,000磅（900公斤）的运载能力。

The initial purpose of the Vikram rocket family is to “deliver dedicated and responsive launch services for small satellites,” Skyroot officials wrote in the press kit for Saturday’s mission. But the company has loftier ambitions.  
斯凯罗斯官员在周六任务的新闻资料中写道，“维克拉姆”火箭系列的初衷是为小卫星“提供专用且响应迅速的发射服务”。但公司有更远大的抱负。

In an interview ahead of the first Vikram-1 launch, Chandana told Ars his aspiration for Skyroot involves larger liquid-fueled fully reusable rockets, with a “daily cadence” from multiple countries.  
在“维克拉姆-1”首次发射前的一次采访中，钱达纳告诉Ars（科技媒体），他对斯凯罗斯的愿景包括更大的液燃料完全可重复使用火箭，实现来自多国的“每日发射节奏”。

Skyroot will need a lot more funding to realize that dream, but Saturday’s launch showed the company has ingredients required for a successful launch company.  
斯凯罗斯需要更多资金来实现这一梦想，但周六的发射表明该公司已具备成功发射公司所需的要素。

Saturday’s launch vaulted Skyroot to a plane above any other space startup in India, or, for that matter, in any country outside of the United States and China.  
周六的发射将斯凯罗斯提升到超越印度任何其他航天初创公司、乃至美国和中国以外任何国家同类公司的水平。
