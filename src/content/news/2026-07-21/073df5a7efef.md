## LG’s monitors come with an unwanted addition for Windows: McAfee pop-up ads  
**LG的显示器为Windows系统带来 unwanted addition：McAfee弹窗广告**

---

The LG Monitor App Installer installs itself by default and has started pushing unwanted ad notifications to users’ screens.  
LG 监控器应用安装程序会默认自行安装，并开始向用户屏幕推送 unwanted 广告通知。

---

by Stevie Bonifield  
Jul 20, 2026, 4:14 PM UTC  
作者 Stevie Bonifield  
2026年7月20日，下午4:14 UTC

---

This LG 34GX900A-B is one of the monitors found auto-installing LG’s app on users’ PCs. Image: LG  
这款 LG 34GX900A-B 显示器是被发现自动在用户电脑上安装 LG 应用的型号之一。图片来源：LG

---

Stevie Bonifield is a news writer covering all things consumer tech. Stevie started out at Laptop Mag writing news and reviews on hardware, gaming, and AI.  
Stevie Bonifield 是一名报道消费科技领域的新闻作者。Stevie 最初在《Laptop Mag》撰写关于硬件、游戏和人工智能的新闻与评测。

---

A video from Gamers Nexus explains how, after connecting a new LG UltraGear monitor to a PC running Windows 11 for the first time, Windows Update is silently installing LG driver updates and the LG Monitor App Installer, without so much as a permission pop-up or notification.  
Gamers Nexus 的一段视频解释了，当一台新的 LG UltraGear 显示器首次连接到运行 Windows 11 的电脑时，Windows Update 如何在静默状态下安装 LG 驱动程序更新和 LG 监控器应用安装程序，期间没有任何权限弹窗或通知。

---

The app doesn’t appear to include special controls of its own, instead simply pointing users to LG’s other apps, as well as McAfee antivirus.  
该应用似乎不包含任何特殊控制功能，而是简单地将用户引导至 LG 的其他应用以及 McAfee 杀毒软件。

---

There are reports about LG’s app auto-installing as far back as 2024, but Gamers Nexus’s test follows recent user complaints on Reddit about large pop-up ads for McAfee and LG’s own apps suddenly appearing in the bottom right corner of the homescreen, which popped up for GN on 31 out of 32 bootups.  
关于 LG 应用自动安装的报告最早可追溯到 2024 年，而 Gamers Nexus 的测试紧随近期 Reddit 上用户的投诉——用户抱怨 McAfee 和 LG 自家应用的大型弹窗广告突然出现在桌面右下角；在 GN 的测试中，32 次启动中有 31 次出现了弹窗。

---

McAfee is shown alongside LG’s own apps on the Windows Store listing for LG Monitor App Installer. Image: LG  
在 Windows 商店中 LG 监控器应用安装程序的列表里，McAfee 与 LG 自家应用并列显示。图片来源：LG

---

Microsoft allows developers to pair a Hardware Support App in the Windows Store that automatically installs based on the device’s metadata, but even its description here includes a warning:  
微软允许开发者在 Windows 商店中关联一个“硬件支持应用”，该应用可根据设备元数据自动安装，但即使在此处的描述中也包含警告：

> The automatic installation feature does not provide a notification to the user when the app is installed. Some users may find this experience confusing and frustrating, and give your app a bad rating.  
> “自动安装功能在应用安装时不会向用户提供通知。一些用户可能会觉得这种体验令人困惑和沮丧，并给你的应用打低分。”

---

As The Verge’s Tom Warren has pointed out in the past, Dell similarly auto-installs its Alienware Command Center app on Windows when you connect an Alienware monitor, but the notification spam is an extra twist.  
正如《The Verge》的 Tom Warren 过去所指出的，戴尔在连接 Alienware 显示器时也会在 Windows 上自动安装其 Alienware Command Center 应用，但通知垃圾信息是一个额外的扭曲。

---

Users have reported ways to block LG’s app from auto-installing by applying a group policy setting on Pro versions of Windows, while users of Home editions might be able to turn off the installation by selecting “No” on an option to automatically download manufacturers’ apps and custom icons that is inside the Advanced system settings section.  
用户报告了阻止 LG 应用自动安装的方法：在 Windows 专业版上应用组策略设置；而家庭版用户可能可以通过在“高级系统设置”部分中选择“否”来关闭自动下载制造商应用和自定义图标的选项。

---

As Gamers Nexus mentions, according to its Windows Store page, the LG Monitor App Installer requires permission to “use all system resources” and “access your Internet connection,” and the linked LG privacy policy mentions automatically collecting device data, internet activity, and geolocation information — all for a program that’s installing without the user’s explicit permission or consent.  
正如 Gamers Nexus 所提到的，根据其 Windows 商店页面，LG 监控器应用安装程序需要获得“使用所有系统资源”和“访问您的互联网连接”的权限，而链接的 LG 隐私政策提到会自动收集设备数据、互联网活动和地理位置信息——而这一切都是为了一个在未获得用户明确许可或同意的情况下安装的程序。
