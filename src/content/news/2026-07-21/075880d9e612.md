One of my personal favorite games, Risk of Rain 2, is included in the bundle. In the third-person roguelike, you’ll face off against increasingly difficult waves of enemies, fighting to stay alive as long as you can and collecting power-ups and useful perks before extracting to the next zone to do it all again. It’s just challenging enough to make victory satisfying, and fun to play alone or with friends. You also get 2012’s XCOM: Enemy Unknown, a legendary turn-based strategy game that’s usually $15 on its own, to give you a sense of the bundle’s value.
