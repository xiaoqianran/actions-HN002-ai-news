# X relaunches a rebuilt Android app after year-long effort  
# X 在长达一年的努力后重新发布 rebuilt Android 应用

Nearly a year ago, Elon Musk-owned X announced it would begin rebuilding the Android version of its app, which had not held up well compared with its iOS counterpart. On Monday, the company shipped the refreshed app, which is now available to download.  
近一年前，由 Elon Musk 拥有的 X 宣布将开始重建其 Android 版应用，该应用与 iOS 版本相比表现不佳。周一，该公司发布了更新后的应用，现已可供下载。

The new Android version of X was built from scratch and promises improvements to loading, scrolling, notifications, and more, X said in its announcement.  
X 在公告中表示，新的 Android 版本是全新构建的，承诺在加载、滚动、通知等方面进行改进。

"We've completely rebuilt the Android X app from the ground up. It's faster, smoother, and more reliable than the old version in every way. We modernized the foundation so everything just feels better: scrolling, loading, notifications, you name it."  
“我们已从零开始完全重建了 Android X 应用。它比旧版本更快、更流畅、更可靠。我们现代化了基础架构，让一切都感觉更好：滚动、加载、通知，应有尽有。”

The update has been in development for nearly a year. Last August, X head of product Nikita Bier said the social network company was putting together an Android “dream team” to reshape the experience.  
此次更新已开发近一年。去年 8 月，X 产品负责人 Nikita Bier 表示，这家社交网络公司正在组建一个 Android “梦之队”来重塑体验。

Later that fall, he also noted that X had one of its biggest weeks ever for Android downloads in October — a reason why the new app was a priority for the company.  
同年秋季晚些时候，他还指出，X 在 10 月迎来了 Android 下载量有史以来最高的周之一——这也是新应用成为公司优先事项的原因。

With today’s release, Bier described the effort as “one of the largest engineering projects” in the company’s history, saying the new Android app was built from scratch rather than simply being updated.  
随着今日的发布，Bier 将这一努力描述为公司历史上“最大的工程项目之一”，并表示新的 Android 应用是全新构建的，而不仅仅是更新。

"Haha, straight answer: none of it. The full Android rewrite was a human engineering feat by the X team on a clean Kotlin + Jetpack Compose stack. Grok helps devs code faster daily, but I have zero visibility into their internal codebase stats or contribution here. Big win for…"  
“哈哈，直说：完全没有。完整的 Android 重写是 X 团队在干净的 Kotlin + Jetpack Compose 技术栈上的人造工程壮举。Grok 每天帮助开发者更快编码，但我对其内部代码库统计或在此处的贡献零 visibility。大胜给……”

“It’s faster, smoother and more reliable. But most of all: it will enable us to build new features at lightning speed,” Bier wrote on X.  
“它更快、更流畅、更可靠。但最重要的是：它将使我们能够以闪电般的速度构建新功能，”Bier 在 X 上写道。

The Elon Musk-owned social network has been rolling out a number of new features in recent months, including X Money and X Chat, which were given their own standalone apps.  
这家由 Elon Musk 拥有的社交网络近几个月来推出了许多新功能，包括 X Money 和 X Chat，它们已被赋予独立的应用程序。

The Android release could also potentially entice more users in global markets, where Android is the dominant smartphone platform, to either download or return to X, after years of platform neglect.  
在 Android 是全球主导智能手机平台的市场，此次 Android 版本的发布也可能吸引更多用户下载或回归 X，此前该平台多年被忽视。

(Problems on Android were so bad at one point last year that the X app couldn’t even load X posts when users clicked links.)  
（去年某个时期，Android 上的问题非常严重，以至于用户点击链接时 X 应用甚至无法加载 X 帖子。）

However, Bier warned that there are still some rough edges to iron out, including improving performance on older Android devices and adding support for Spaces, X’s live audio feature. Those updates are still underway.  
然而，Bier 警告说，仍有一些 rough edges 需要完善，包括提高旧款 Android 设备的性能，以及为 X 的直播音频功能 Spaces 添加支持。这些更新仍在进行中。

Bier added that other features, including the new video editor, the react-with-video feature, cashtags, and custom timelines are also coming soon to Android.  
Bier 补充说，其他功能，包括新的视频编辑器、带视频反应功能、股票标签（cashtags）和自定义时间线，也即将登陆 Android。

Existing Android users can get the new X app by updating their existing app through the Google Play Store.  
现有 Android 用户可以通过 Google Play 商店更新现有应用来获取新的 X 应用。
