**Title:** On the Structure of Address in Multi-Party Dialogue: From Discrete Labels to Continuous Levels  
**标题：** 多人对话中的指称结构：从离散标签到连续层级

**Authors:** Taiga Mori, Koji Inoue, Divesh Lala, Tatsuya Kawahara  
**作者：** 森大河、井上浩二、迪维什·拉拉、川原哲也

**Abstract:**  
In multi-party dialogues between a dialogue system and multiple users, identifying to whom an utterance is addressed is a key challenge. Prior work has typically treated addressee detection as a multi-class classification task, selecting a single label representing an individual participant or the group. This formulation assumes that address is inherently discrete and has primarily been used for predicting turn-taking.  
**摘要：**  
在对话系统与多个用户之间的多人对话中，识别话语的指称对象是一项关键挑战。先前研究通常将指称检测视为多类分类任务，选择代表单个参与者或群体的单一标签。这种形式假设指称本质上是离散的，并主要用于预测话轮转换。

In this paper, we revisit this assumption by analyzing address as a continuous phenomenon. Using a multi-party human dialogue corpus annotated by multiple annotators, we construct both binary address labels derived from majority-vote addressee labels and continuous address levels inferred from annotator judgments using a latent-variable model.  
在本文中，我们通过将指称分析为连续现象来重新审视这一假设。利用由多名标注者注释的多人人类对话语料库，我们构建了两种数据：一是从多数投票指称标签派生的二元指称标签，二是通过潜变量模型从标注者判断中推断出的连续指称层级。

We then examine how these representations relate to turn-taking as well as listener behaviors, including gaze and backchannels. Our results show that, in addition to turn-taking, both gaze and backchannels are associated with address.  
随后，我们检验了这些表征与话轮转换以及听者行为（包括注视和反馈音）之间的关系。结果表明，除话轮转换外，注视和反馈音均与指称相关。

Furthermore, models using continuous address levels achieve better predictive fit than those using discrete labels, suggesting that address may exhibit graded structure.  
此外，使用连续指称层级的模型比使用离散标签的模型具有更好的预测拟合度，这表明指称可能呈现分级结构。

Finally, we discuss the future directions of addressee detection research based on the findings of this study.  
最后，我们基于本研究的结果讨论了指称检测研究的未来方向。
