**Title: The first trailer for Avengers: Doomsday is finally here**  
**标题：《复仇者联盟：毁灭日》首支预告片终于发布**

---

With San Diego Comic-Con kicking off later this week, Marvel Studios has released the first official full trailer for Avengers: Doomsday, the penultimate film in the MCU’s Phase Six and the end of the so-called “Multiverse Saga.”  
随着圣迭戈国际漫画展将于本周晚些时候拉开帷幕，漫威影业发布了《复仇者联盟：毁灭日》的首支官方完整预告片。这部电影是漫威电影宇宙第六阶段的倒数第二部作品，也是所谓“多元宇宙传奇”的终结篇。

(Spoilers for prior relevant MCU films below.)  
（以下包含此前相关MCU电影的剧透。）

Marvel Studios originally planned to build its Phase Six Avengers arc (The Kang Dynasty) around Jonathan Majors’ Kang the Conqueror (and associated variants), introduced in Loki and Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania.  
漫威影业最初计划以乔纳森·梅杰斯饰演的征服者康（及相关变体）为核心构建第六阶段的复仇者联盟故事线（《康之王朝》——该角色在《洛基》和《蚁人与黄蜂女：量子狂潮》中首次登场。

But then Majors was convicted of domestic violence, and Marvel fired the actor soon after.  
但随后梅杰斯被判定家庭暴力罪名成立，漫威很快解雇了该演员。

That meant the studio needed to retool its Phase Six plans, culminating in the announced return of the Russo brothers, who directed four of the Marvel Cinematic Universe’s most successful films, which brought in more than $6 billion at the global box office.  
这意味着制片方需要重新调整第六阶段的计划，最终宣布了罗素兄弟的回归——他们曾执导了四部漫威电影宇宙最成功的影片，全球票房累计超过60亿美元。

The Russo brothers decided to take a new direction, bringing the Secret Wars storyline to the big screen.  
罗素兄弟决定采用新方向，将《秘密战争》故事线搬上大银幕。

It’s one of their favorites, but before they could take on Avengers: Secret Wars (slated for May 2027), the duo knew they first had to make Avengers: Doomsday, featuring the wildly popular comics villain Doctor Doom, with the return of Robert Downey Jr. to the MCU in that role.  
这是他们最喜爱的故事之一，但在着手《复仇者联盟：秘密战争》（计划于2027年5月上映）之前，两人知道必须先制作《复仇者联盟：毁灭日》，该片将引入人气极高的漫画反派毁灭博士，并由小罗伯特·唐尼重返MCU饰演该角色。

The film takes place 14 months after the events of Thunderbolts*.  
影片时间线设定在《雷霆特工队*》事件发生的14个月后。

Per the official logline: “Beloved heroes from three distinct universes will be set on a deadly collision course and face an existential threat unlike anything they’ve ever encountered.”  
根据官方剧情梗概：“来自三个不同宇宙的挚爱英雄将被置于致命的冲突轨道上，面对前所未有的生存威胁。”

Those universes are Earth-616 (the Avengers, Wakandans, and New Avengers), Earth-828 (Fantastic Four), and the “original” X-Men.  
这三个宇宙分别是：地球-616（复仇者联盟、瓦坎达势力与新复仇者）、地球-828（神奇四侠）以及“原初”X战警。

Cast-wise, we have Avengers favorites Thor (Chris Hemsworth), the new Captain America (Anthony Mackie), Bucky Barnes (Sebastian Stan), Ant-Man (Paul Rudd), Falcon (Danny Ramirez), and Loki (Tom Hiddleston).  
演员方面，我们有复仇者联盟老面孔：雷神（克里斯·海姆斯沃斯）、新任美国队长（安东尼·麦凯）、巴基·巴恩斯（塞巴斯蒂安·斯坦）、蚁人（保罗·路德）、猎鹰（丹尼·拉米雷斯）和洛基（汤姆·希德勒斯顿）。

Then there’s the Wakandan contingent: Shuri as the new Black Panther (Letitia Wright), M’Baku (Winston Duke), and Namor (Tenoch Huerta Mejia), as well as Alex Livinalli as Namor’s ally, Attuma.  
还有瓦坎达阵营：舒莉担任新任黑豹（莱蒂希娅·赖特）、姆巴库（温斯顿·杜克）和纳摩（特诺切·韦尔塔），以及亚历克斯·利维纳利饰演纳摩的盟友阿图玛。

And, of course, Robert Downey Jr. as Doctor Doom.  
当然，还有小罗伯特·唐尼饰演的毁灭博士。

Then there’s the Thunderbolts* (aka New Avengers): John Walker/US Agent (Wyatt Russell), Yelena Belova (Florence Pugh), Bob/Sentry (Lewis Pullman), Red Guardian (David Harbour), and Ghost (Hannah John-Kamen).  
接着是雷霆特工队*（又称新复仇者）：约翰·沃克/美国特工（怀亚特·拉塞尔）、叶莲娜·贝洛娃（弗洛伦斯·皮尤）、鲍勃/哨兵（刘易斯·普尔曼）、红色守卫者（大卫·哈伯）和幽灵（汉娜·乔恩-卡门）。

And the Fantastic Four: Reed Richards (Pedro Pascal), Sue Storm (Vanessa Kirby), Ben Grimm (Ebon Moss-Bachrach), and Johnny Storm (Joseph Quinn).  
以及神奇四侠：里德·理查兹（佩德罗·帕斯卡）、苏·斯通（凡妮莎·柯比）、本·格林姆（埃邦·莫斯-巴克拉赫）和约翰尼·斯通（约瑟夫·奎因）。

We’ll also see Charles Xavier (Patrick Stewart), Beast (Kelsey Grammer), Magneto (Ian McKellen), Mystique (Rebecca Romijn), Nightcrawler (Alan Cumming), and Cyclops (James Marsden).  
我们还将看到：X教授（帕特里克·斯图尔特）、野兽（凯尔西·格拉默）、万磁王（伊恩·麦克莱恩）、魔形女（丽贝卡·罗梅恩）、夜行者（艾伦·卡明）和镭射眼（詹姆斯·麦斯登）。

For good measure, Marvel threw in Gambit (Channing Tatum) and Xu Shang-Chi (Simu Liu).  
此外，漫威还加入了牌皇（查宁·塔图姆）和徐尚气（刘思慕）。

There will be plenty of cameos, like Steve Rogers (Chris Evans), revealed in one of four sneak teasers last year.  
还将有大量客串，例如去年四支前瞻预告片中已揭晓的史蒂夫·罗杰斯（克里斯·埃文斯）。

We can also expect to see (at least briefly) Peggy Carter, Spider-Man (Tom Holland), Hawkeye (Jeremy Renner), Doctor Strange (Benedict Cumberbatch), Thor’s adopted daughter Love (India Rose Hemsworth), and Scott Lang’s daughter Cassie (Kathryn Newton).  
我们还可以期待看到（至少短暂出镜）佩吉·卡特、蜘蛛侠（汤姆·赫兰德）、鹰眼（杰瑞米·雷纳）、奇异博士（本尼迪克特·康伯巴奇）、雷神的养女洛夫（印第娅·罗斯·海姆斯沃斯）以及斯科特·朗的女儿卡西（凯瑟琳·牛顿）。

---

So what’s in the trailer? A heck of a lot of cameos, mostly, as the superheroes from the three universes meet each other over an ominous voiceover by Thor warning of an unprecedented threat.  
那么预告片里有什么？主要是大量的客串镜头，伴随着雷神警告前所未有威胁的阴森画外音，三个宇宙的超级英雄们相互相遇。

“I’ve fought with many warriors in my time,” we hear Thor say. “They were far stronger than all of us put together. And they died. They died facing enemies and threats that scared me far less than this one. Everything that they sacrificed will be for nothing if we do not stand together. Put aside your petty squabbles and presume nothing except this. If you return, you will return as brothers and sisters. But mark my words, we’re going to need a miracle.”  
“我一生中与无数战士并肩作战，”雷神说道，“他们比我们所有人加起来还要强大。但他们死了。他们死于面对那些远不如眼前这个威胁让我恐惧的敌人。如果我们不团结一致，他们的一切牺牲都将白费。抛开你们琐碎的争吵，除了这一点不要有任何预设。如果你们能回归，将以兄弟姐妹的身份回归。但记住我的话，我们需要一个奇迹。”

OK, not exactly the St. Crispin’s Day speech, but the trailer ends with the return of Steve Rogers—and guess what, he’s still worthy of Thor’s hammer, Mjölnir.  
好吧，这可不是圣克里斯平日的演说，但预告片以史蒂夫·罗杰斯的回归收尾——猜怎么着，他依然配得上雷神的妙尔尼尔之锤。

Could he be the miracle they need? Or is another miracle waiting in the wings?  
他会是他们需要的那个奇迹吗？还是另一个奇迹正在酝酿之中？

Avengers: Doomsday hits theaters on December 18, 2026.  
《复仇者联盟：毁灭日》将于2026年12月18日上映。

Avengers: Secret Wars is currently slated for release on December 17, 2027, and will mark the conclusion of the MCU’s Phase Six.  
《复仇者联盟：秘密战争》目前计划于2027年12月17日上映，并将标志着MCU第六阶段的终结。
