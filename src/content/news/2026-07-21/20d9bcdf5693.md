**Title: Google is working on a new AI chip designed to make Gemini more efficient**  
**标题：谷歌正在研发新款AI芯片，旨在提升Gemini效率**

---

Alphabet, Google’s parent company, is designing a new server chip to help its in-house Gemini models operate more efficiently.  
谷歌母公司Alphabet正在设计一款新的服务器芯片，以帮助其自研的Gemini模型更高效地运行。

The new chip, internally dubbed “Frozen v2,” is slated to be released sometime in 2028, The Information reported, citing anonymous sources.  
据《The Information》援引匿名消息人士报道，这款内部代号为“Frozen v2”的新芯片计划于2028年某个时间发布。

According to the report, the chip could be between six and 10 times more efficient than Google’s existing AI chips, measured by the number of tokens generated per unit of power.  
报告称，以每单位功耗生成的令牌数（tokens）衡量，该芯片的效率可能是谷歌现有AI芯片的6到10倍。

In a response to TechCrunch, the company didn’t directly confirm the report. It didn’t deny it either.  
在回应TechCrunch时，该公司未直接确认该报道，也未予以否认。

“Our teams are constantly researching and experimenting with new innovations to deliver maximum performance and efficiency for our users and customers,” Google told TechCrunch.  
谷歌对TechCrunch表示：“我们的团队不断研究和试验新创新，以为用户和客户提供最高性能和效率。”

“While not every project moves into production, this rigorous exploration is central to our full stack approach. By co-designing our hardware and software from the ground up, we ensure our systems are integrated and highly optimized for real-world workloads.”  
“尽管并非每个项目都会投入生产，但这种严谨的探索是我们全栈方法的核心。通过从零开始协同设计软硬件，我们确保系统集成度高，并针对实际工作负载进行了高度优化。”

AI companies have increasingly sought to produce their own chips as a way to make their in-house models run more efficiently and to address global shortages in AI computing capacity.  
AI公司越来越倾向于自研芯片，以提升内部模型的运行效率，并应对全球AI计算能力的短缺。

Such efficiency has become a key selling point for tech companies as concerns about AI spend have dampened the market euphoria that previously characterized the industry.  
随着对AI支出的担忧抑制了此前行业特有的市场狂热，这种效率已成为科技公司的关键卖点。

At the same time, firms are engaged in an ongoing attempt to wean themselves off chipmaker Nvidia, which has historically dominated the AI chip market and whose dominance has left major AI makers dependent on its hardware.  
与此同时，各公司正持续尝试摆脱对芯片制造商英伟达（Nvidia）的依赖——英伟达历史上主导AI芯片市场，其主导地位使主要AI厂商依赖其硬件。

In June, OpenAI announced its first custom chip, an inference processor dubbed Jalapeño.  
今年6月，OpenAI宣布了其首款定制芯片——一款名为“Jalapeño”的推理处理器。

Earlier this month, it was reported that Anthropic was discussing a new chipmaking partnership with Samsung.  
本月早些时候有报道称，Anthropic正在与三星讨论新的芯片制造合作伙伴关系。

Investors have previously worried about Alphabet’s massive planned expenditures designed to help it build out its AI strategy.  
投资者此前曾担心Alphabet为构建AI战略而计划的大规模支出。

Earlier this year, Google said that it plans to spend between $180 billion and $190 billion.  
今年早些时候，谷歌表示计划支出1800亿至1900亿美元。

With so much money at stake, the company needs to prove that those investments will pay off.  
由于涉及巨额资金，该公司需要证明这些投资将获得回报。

News of the more efficient Frozen v2 chip appears to have assuaged investors, giving Google a boost ahead of its earnings report later this week.  
关于更高效Frozen v2芯片的消息似乎安抚了投资者，为谷歌本周晚些时候发布财报前提供了提振。

Following publication of The Information’s report, the company’s stock climbed some 3% on Monday morning.  
《The Information》报道发布后，该公司股价周一早盘上涨约3%。
