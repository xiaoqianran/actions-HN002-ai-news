# Verbalizable Representations Form a Global Workspace in Language Models  
# 可口头报告的表示形式在语言模型中形成全局工作空间  

**Authors:** Wes Gurnee, Nicholas Sofroniew, Adam Pearce, Mateusz Piotrowski, Isaac Kauvar, Runjin Chen, Anna Soligo, Paul Bogdan, Euan Ong, Rowan Wang, Ben Thompson, David Abrahams, Subhash Kantamneni, Emmanuel Ameisen, Joshua Batson, Jack Lindsey  
**作者：** 韦斯·古尼，尼古拉斯·索弗罗耶夫，亚当·皮尔斯，马特乌什·皮奥特罗夫斯基，艾萨克·考瓦尔，陈润金，安娜·索利戈，保罗·博格丹，尤安·翁，罗文·王，本·汤普森，大卫·亚伯拉罕斯，苏巴什·坎塔姆内尼，伊曼纽尔·阿梅森，约书亚·巴特森，杰克·林赛

**Abstract:**  
Out of everything the human brain processes, only a small fraction is consciously accessible, in the sense of being available for verbal report, deliberate control, and flexible reasoning.  
**摘要：**  
在人脑处理的所有信息中，只有一小部分是有意识可及的，即可用于口头报告、 deliberate control（自主控制）和灵活推理。  

In this paper, we present evidence that an analogous functional distinction has emerged in large language models.  
在本文中，我们提供了证据，表明大型语言模型中也出现了类似的功能区分。  

Using a new interpretability technique, the Jacobian lens, we identify the representations a model is poised to verbalize at any point in its processing.  
利用一种新的可解释性技术——雅可比透镜（Jacobian lens），我们识别出模型在处理的任何时刻都准备口头报告的表示形式。  

These representations, which we collectively call the J-space, exhibit the functional properties characteristic of a global workspace: their contents can be reported, deliberately summoned and held, used to carry the intermediate steps of silent reasoning, and passed as arguments to arbitrary downstream computations, while automatic processing such as text parsing and routine inference proceeds without them.  
这些表示形式，我们统称为 J 空间（J-space），表现出全局工作空间的典型功能属性：其内容可以被报告、被主动调用和保持、用于承载无声推理的中间步骤，并作为参数传递给任意的下游计算，而文本解析和常规推理等自动处理过程则无需它们。  

The J-space also has structural signatures that global workspace theory associates with conscious access: it carries coherent content only in an intermediate band of layers, holds on the order of tens of concepts at a time, and is broadcast by the model's weights more widely than other representations.  
J 空间还具有全局工作空间理论所关联的结构特征：它仅在中间层带中携带连贯内容，一次仅保持约数十个概念，并且其信息通过模型权重的广播范围比其他表示形式更广。  

These properties make it a practical window into a model's unspoken thinking.  
这些特性使其成为窥探模型未言明思维的实用窗口。  

In alignment audits, it reveals strategic deliberation, evaluation awareness, and trained-in misaligned dispositions that never appear in the model's outputs.  
在对齐审计中，它揭示了策略性审慎、评估意识以及训练中植入的不对齐倾向，而这些在模型的输出中从未出现。  

We find that post-training installs the Assistant's point of view in the workspace, and we introduce counterfactual reflection training, which improves behavior by training only what a model would say if interrupted and asked to reflect.  
我们发现，后训练（post-training）在全局工作空间中安装了“助手”的视角，并引入了反事实反思训练（counterfactual reflection training），该方法通过仅训练模型在被打断并被要求反思时会说的话来改善其行为。  

These results indicate that language models maintain a small, privileged set of representations bearing some of the functional hallmarks of conscious access, and that decoding these representations sheds light on ongoing cognitive processes.  
这些结果表明，语言模型维护着一组小而特权化的表示形式，它们承载着有意识访问的部分功能特征，而解码这些表示形式有助于阐明 ongoing cognitive processes（持续进行的认知过程）。
