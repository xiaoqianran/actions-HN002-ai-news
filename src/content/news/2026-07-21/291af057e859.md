**Title:**  
Ford’s $30,000 electric truck: all the news about the company’s big EV re-do  
福特3000美元电动卡车：关于该公司大型电动车改造的所有新闻

The end of the Ford F-150 Lightning was also the start of a new era for the automaker. After failing to capture the market with the electric version of its popular F-Series truck, Ford is going back to the drawing board with its Universal Electric Vehicle Platform.  
福特F-150 Lightning的终结也是这家汽车制造商新时代的开始。在未能凭借其受欢迎的F系列卡车的电动版本占领市场后，福特正重新回到绘图板前，推出其通用电动汽车平台。

The first offering will be a $30,000 electric truck, engineered for efficiency and also — perhaps most importantly — profitability. The truck will be revealed in 2027, but Ford plans on building a whole family of low-cost electric vehicles at its Kentucky assembly plant. The heavily camouflaged truck has already been spotted out in the wild, with observers noting that it seems even smaller than the Maverick.  
首款产品将是一款30000美元的电动卡车，旨在提高效率，或许最重要的是，实现盈利。这款卡车将于2027年亮相，但福特计划在其肯塔基组装厂生产一系列低成本电动汽车。这款伪装严实的卡车已经在路上被看到，观察者指出它似乎比Maverick还要小。

Success isn’t guaranteed. Ford has struggled to make profitable EVs, forcing the company to take a staggering $19.5 billion hit on its EV investments in 2025. But Ford still sees EVs as the future — just not oversize ones with “no path to profitability,” as executives say.  
成功并非保证。福特一直难以生产盈利的电动汽车，迫使公司在2025年对其电动汽车投资计提了惊人的195亿美元减值。但福特仍将电动汽车视为未来——只是不是那些高管所说的“没有盈利路径”的大型车型。

The Autopian本周在长滩发现了这款备受期待的电动车。尽管它伪装严实，该媒体仍估计其尺寸约为64英寸高，195英寸长，这将使它比福特Maverick略小。

Apr 15 Andrew J. Hawkins Ford isn’t sending the clearest signals regarding its EV future.  
4月15日 安德鲁·J·霍金斯 福特并未就其电动车未来发出最明确的信号。

We got a glimpse of its Universal EV (UEV) Project last February, but then it pulled the plug on the F-150 Lightning, which is regarded as one of the more successful electric pickup trucks to date.  
我们去年二月一瞥了其通用电动车（UEV）项目，但随后它叫停了F-150 Lightning，后者被视为迄今为止最成功的电动皮卡之一。

But recently, after touring Ford’s less-than-two-year-old Electric Vehicle Development Center (EVDC) located in Long Beach, California, I have a better sense of the company’s current position. Ford says it will continue to build EVs, doing everything possible to make its next model hit a $30,000 price point.  
但最近，在参观了位于加州长滩、不到两岁的福特电动车开发中心（EVDC）后，我对公司目前的定位有了更好的了解。福特表示将继续生产电动车，尽一切可能使其下一款车型达到30000美元的价格点。

Feb 17 Andrew J. Hawkins Ford’s EV and software chief Doug Field is leaving the company  
2月17日 安德鲁·J·霍金斯 福特的电动车和软件主管道格·菲尔德将离开公司

Ford is shaking things up as it relates to its EV and software teams. Doug Field, who left Apple five years ago to helm Ford’s multibillion-dollar bet on electric vehicles and software, is stepping down next month.  
福特正在对其电动车和软件团队进行调整。道格·菲尔德，五年前离开苹果领导福特数十亿美元的电动车和软件赌注，将于下月卸任。

Getting a promotion will be Alan Clarke, the ex-Tesla engineer who now leads Ford’s California-based skunkworks lab. Clarke’s new title will be vice president of advanced development projects, and he will continue to helm the effort to develop Ford’s Universal Electric Vehicle (UEV) Platform.  
获得晋升的将是前特斯拉工程师艾伦·克拉克，他目前领导福特位于加州的秘密实验室。克拉克的新头衔将是高级开发项目副总裁，他将继续领导开发福特通用电动汽车（UEV）平台的工作。

The shake-up comes less five months after Ford announced a massive $19.5 billion writedown on its EV investment, as well as the discontinuation of several electric models, including the F-150 Lightning pickup truck.  
此次调整发生在福特宣布对其电动车投资进行大规模195亿美元减记，以及停产包括F-150 Lightning皮卡在内的多款电动车后不到五个月。

The company scrapped plans to build a next-gen electric truck, codenamed T3, and an electric commercial van. Instead, Ford would focus on hybrids as well as its existing lineup of gas-powered trucks and SUVs, as it continues work on its UEV platform, which will eventually underpin a whole family of low-cost EVs, starting with a $30,000 midsize truck in 2027.  
公司取消了建造下一代电动卡车（代号T3）和电动商用货车的计划。相反，福特将专注于混合动力车以及其现有的汽油动力卡车和SUV阵容，同时继续其UEV平台的工作，该平台最终将支撑一系列低成本电动车，从2027年的30000美元中型卡车开始。

Dec 15, 2025 Andrew J. Hawkins Can Ford re-engineer the EV revolution?  
2025年12月15日 安德鲁·J·霍金斯 福特能重新设计电动车革命吗？

To say that Ford has struggled to make profitable electric vehicles would be an understatement. The company recently pulled the plug on its F-150 Lightning, a truck once heralded as the most important EV ever made, after taking a staggering $19.5 billion hit on its EV investments in 2025.  
说福特难以生产盈利的电动车是轻描淡写都不为过。该公司最近叫停了F-150 Lightning，这款卡车曾被誉为有史以来最重要的电动车，此前在2025年对其电动车投资计提了惊人的195亿美元减值。

A new focus on hybrids and extended-range EVs, as well as internal-combustion engine vehicles that still bring in the most revenue, are now the new way forward for the iconic 122-year old company. Everything old is new again.  
对混合动力车和增程式电动车以及仍带来最多收入的燃油车的新关注，如今成为这家标志性的122年公司的新前进道路。一切旧物皆成新潮。

But Ford still sees EVs as the future — just not oversize ones with “no path to profitability,” like the Lightning, as Andrew Frick, president of Ford Model e and Ford Blue, said last year. Instead, the automaker is betting the farm on more affordable EVs, purpose-built with unique designs and smaller batteries, that can reignite customer demand while also turning a profit. Oh, and they need to be super fun to drive, too.  
但福特仍将电动车视为未来——只是不是像Lightning那样“没有盈利路径”的大型车型，正如福特Model e和Ford Blue总裁Andrew Frick去年所说。相反，这家汽车制造商正将全部赌注押在更实惠的电动车上，这些车专为独特设计和更小电池而打造，能够重新点燃客户需求的同时实现盈利。哦，它们还需要超级好开。
