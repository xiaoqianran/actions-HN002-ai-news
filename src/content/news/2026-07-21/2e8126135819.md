# Judge pauses $110B Paramount-Warner Bros. merger  
法官叫停派拉蒙与华纳兄弟价值110亿美元的合并交易

Paramount Skydance’s proposed acquisition of Warner Bros. Discovery (WBD) has hit a roadblock after a judge temporarily paused the deal in response to a lawsuit filed by a coalition of 12 state attorneys general who argue that the merger would harm competition.  
派拉蒙天舞（Paramount Skydance）拟收购华纳兄弟探索公司（WBD）的计划遭遇障碍，一名法官应12个州总检察长组成的联盟提起的诉讼要求，临时叫停了该交易。该联盟认为合并将损害市场竞争。

U.S. District Judge Araceli Martínez-Olguín issued a 14-day pause on Monday after hearing arguments from both sides last week.  
美国地区法官阿拉塞利·马丁内斯-奥尔金（Araceli Martínez-Olguín）在听取双方上周辩论后，于周一发布了为期14天的暂停令。

The coalition, which is being led by California Attorney General Rob Bonta, could seek another pause after the 14 days, further delaying the merger.  
由加州总检察长罗布·邦塔（Rob Bonta）领导的该联盟可能在14天后寻求再次暂停，进一步推迟合并进程。

The lawsuit from the states alleges that the deal would harm movie theaters, basic cable distributors, and audiences.  
各州诉讼指控，该交易将损害电影院、基础有线电视分销商和观众的利益。

They argue that if the two companies are allowed to merge, it would lessen competition in three areas: wide release theatrical film distribution, “top-grossing” theatrical distribution, and basic cable licensing.  
他们辩称，若允许两家公司合并，将在三个领域减少竞争：大规模院线电影发行、高票房院线发行以及基础有线电视授权。

“This is a critical first win in our case to ensure this megamerger never sees the light of day,” said Attorney General Bonta in a statement.  
邦塔总检察长在一份声明中表示：“这是我们案件中的关键首胜，旨在确保这项巨型合并永无见光之日。”

“History tells the tale of what happens when a few people have great power over markets that are central to Americans’ lives: fewer opportunities for more people, worse products and services for all people.  
“历史告诉我们，当少数人掌控对美国民众生活至关重要的市场时会发生什么：更多人机会减少，所有人获得的产品和服务质量下降。

With our lawsuit, we’re fighting for a free and fair market and a thriving film and television industry that serves creatives and audiences alike.  
通过我们的诉讼，我们正在为自由公平的市场以及服务于创作者和观众的繁荣影视产业而战。

We have a full tank of gas, the law on our side, and look forward to continuing to make our case.”  
我们油箱加满、法律在手，期待继续推进我们的主张。”

The deal would combine two notable film studios as well as streaming platforms Paramount+ and HBO Max.  
该交易将合并两家知名电影制片厂以及流媒体平台派拉蒙+（Paramount+）和HBO Max。

It would also create one of the largest portfolios of television networks, bringing together Paramount’s CBS and MTV with WBD’s CNN and HBO.  
它还将打造最大的电视网络组合之一，整合派拉蒙的CBS和MTV与WBD的CNN和HBO。

“We are confident the evidence will demonstrate that the State AGs’ antitrust arguments are without merit as their alleged markets and claims of anticompetitive effects are without any basis in modern market realities,” a spokesperson for Paramount said in a statement to TechCrunch.  
派拉蒙发言人在向TechCrunch发表的声明中表示：“我们相信证据将证明，各州总检察长的反垄断论点毫无根据，因为他们所指控的市场及反竞争效应在现代市场现实中毫无依据。”

“This merger is lawful, pro-competitive, and will benefit consumers, creators, workers, and the entertainment industry.  
“此次合并合法、有利于竞争，并将惠及消费者、创作者、员工和娱乐产业。

We will continue to vigorously defend the transaction and will look forward to the hearings on the substance of the State AGs’ action.”  
我们将继续积极捍卫该交易，并期待就各州总检察长诉讼的实质内容举行听证会。”

Paramount CEO David Ellison had said in May that the transaction was on track to close by September.  
派拉蒙CEO大卫·埃里森（David Ellison）今年5月曾表示，该交易有望在9月前完成。

The legal roadblock has the potential to derail Paramount’s efforts to transform into a major competitor to companies like Netflix.  
这一法律障碍可能使派拉蒙转型为Netflix等公司主要竞争对手的努力脱轨。

The proposed acquisition has received scrutiny from filmmakers, actors, and industry professionals who argued that the deal would reduce competition and further consolidate the U.S. media industry.  
拟议中的收购已受到电影制作人、演员和行业专业人士的审视，他们认为该交易将减少竞争并进一步巩固美国媒体产业。

WBD did not immediately respond to TechCrunch’s requests for comment.  
WBD未立即回应TechCrunch的置评请求。
