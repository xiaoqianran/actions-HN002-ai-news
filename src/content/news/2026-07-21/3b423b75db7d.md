**Title: Natural raises $30M to reinvent payments for AI agents — and take on Stripe**  
**标题：Natural 融资 3000 万美元，重塑 AI 智能体支付体系——直面 Stripe 竞争**

---

AI agents are starting to execute more sophisticated tasks, such as identifying vendors that can deliver freight, comparing prices, and messaging the vendor to organizing a delivery. But when it comes to making a payment for the shipment, they still need to involve a human.  
AI 智能体已开始执行更复杂的任务，例如寻找可提供货运服务的供应商、比价、向供应商发送消息以安排交付。但到了为货物付款时，它们仍需要人类介入。

Today’s financial sector relies on financial rails, the underlying infrastructure that moves money and information between banks, businesses, and consumers. But these financial rails were built for human-initiated transactions, not autonomous AI agents.  
当今金融体系依赖于“金融基础设施”——在银行、企业和消费者之间转移资金与信息的底层架构。但这些基础设施是为人类发起的交易而建，并非为自主运行的 AI 智能体设计。

For example, traditional payment systems like credit cards and ACH rely on human authorization for transactions, which slows down agents engineered to work autonomously.  
例如，信用卡和 ACH（自动清算系统）等传统支付方式依赖人类授权交易，这拖慢了为自主运行而设计的智能体。

One new startup, Natural, is tackling the problem by redesigning the whole system from the ground up. And it now has $30 million in fresh capital to pursue an ambitious plan that will put it in direct competition with giants like Stripe.  
一家名为 Natural 的新创公司正从零开始重新设计整个系统以解决此问题。该公司如今已获得 3000 万美元新资金，将推进一项雄心勃勃的计划，与 Stripe 等行业巨头直接竞争。

About a year ago, Natural co-founder and CEO Kahlil Lalji realized that AI agents were evolving faster than existing financial architecture, which can’t support tasks like autonomously paying a vendor, collecting payments, or transacting with each other.  
约一年前，Natural 联合创始人兼 CEO Kahlil Lalji 意识到，AI 智能体的演进速度已超过现有金融架构，后者无法支持自主支付供应商、收款或相互交易等任务。

Lalji has a background in banking and finance, but as he prepared to launch another startup he had hoped to avoid the sector. His previous startup Ivella, a YC-backed banking and financial product for couples, was sold in 2023 to Earnin, where he worked as an engineer for two years.  
Lalji 拥有银行与金融背景，但在筹备启动另一家初创公司时，他曾希望避开金融领域。他之前的初创公司 Ivella（一款 YC 支持的面向情侣的银行金融产品）于 2023 年被 Earnin 收购，他在 Earnin 担任工程师两年。

He told TechCrunch he had been burned by the finance sector after the Zero Interest Rate Policy era ended. And yet, Lalji couldn’t ignore the opportunity.  
他告诉 TechCrunch，在零利率政策时代结束后，他已被金融行业“灼伤”。然而，Lalji 无法忽视这一机遇。

“I kept on coming back to it,” he said. “It just feels obvious that agentic payments are going to be structurally the most important problem [in the] space.”  
“我总是回到这个问题上，”他说，“智能体支付在结构上将成为该领域最重要的问题，这显而易见。”

Lalji teamed up with Eric Wang, his co-founder at Ivella, and Walt Leung, a former engineering manager at Nextdoor, and founded Natural in 2025.  
Lalji 与他在 Ivella 的联合创始人 Eric Wang，以及前 Nextdoor 工程经理 Walt Leung 合作，于 2025 年创立了 Natural。

The startup positions itself as an agent orchestration layer that enables AI agents to move and store funds. By integrating Natural’s infrastructure, companies can allow their agents to make autonomous payments, collect funds, and transact with both humans and other agents.  
该公司定位为“智能体编排层”，使 AI 智能体能够转移和存储资金。通过集成 Natural 的基础设施，企业可允许其智能体自主支付、收款，并与人类或其他智能体进行交易。

Natural got the attention of Kirsten Green, founder and managing partner at VC firm Forerunner. Green, whose firm focuses on consumer experiences and the future of commerce, led its $30 million Series A round in the company, bringing the company’s total funding to $40 million.  
Natural 吸引了风投机构 Forerunner 的创始人兼管理合伙人 Kirsten Green 的关注。Green 的机构专注于消费体验与商业未来，她领导了该公司 3000 万美元的 A 轮融资，使公司总融资额达到 4000 万美元。

Green was attracted by Natural’s broader ambitions. The startup isn’t just focused on helping agents pay for and check out goods on behalf of consumers, it’s also trying to reinvent payment infrastructure, including how disputed transactions are handled.  
Green 被 Natural 更宏大的野心所吸引。这家初创公司不仅专注于帮助智能体代表消费者支付和结算商品，还试图重塑支付基础设施，包括争议交易的处理方式。

Although Natural has operated in a beta trial until now, Lalji told TechCrunch that the startup has made enough critical architectural decisions to give it a “good shot” at competing with incumbents like Stripe, which is also racing to redesign payment rails for AI agents.  
尽管 Natural 至今仍处于 beta 测试阶段，Lalji 告诉 TechCrunch，该公司已做出足够多的关键架构决策，使其有“良好机会”与同样在竞相为 AI 智能体重新设计支付基础设施的 Stripe 等老牌企业竞争。

Lalji hopes that Natural’s fast development speed will allow it to outpace established giants and build the payment infrastructure that will serve as the financial backbone of AI agents.  
Lalji 希望 Natural 的快速开发速度能使其超越成熟巨头，并构建将成为 AI 智能体金融支柱的支付基础设施。

The startup’s mission has attracted senior staff who previously worked at fintech giants Stripe, Ramp, and Square.  
该公司的使命已吸引曾任职于金融科技巨头 Stripe、Ramp 和 Square 的高级员工。

Although Natural views Stripe as its main competitor, several other startups, including DCVC-backed Skyfire Systems, are trying to reinvent the payments backbone for AI agents using USD-backed stablecoins.  
尽管 Natural 将 Stripe 视为其主要竞争对手，但包括 DCVC 投资的 Skyfire Systems 在内的其他几家初创公司也正尝试使用美元挂钩的稳定币重塑 AI 智能体的支付骨干网络。

While Natural plans to incorporate stablecoins into its architecture, it is also building support for traditional bank payments.  
Natural 计划将稳定币纳入其架构，同时也在构建对传统银行支付的支持。

While there is a fierce race to dominate the field, Lalji is betting the entire market could grow significantly if transactions happen at computer speed rather than human speed.  
尽管该领域竞争激烈，Lalji 认为，如果交易以计算机速度而非人类速度进行，整个市场可能大幅增长。

“The number of payments that may occur in the world may be two or three or four orders of magnitude greater than the number of payments that exist today,” he said.  
“未来全球可能发生的支付数量可能是当今支付数量的两到四个数量级，”他说。
