# The FDA Says It Didn't Apologize to Supplier Linked to Diarrhea Outbreak, Actually  
# 美国食品药品监督管理局（FDA）称并未向与腹泻疫情相关的供应商道歉，事实如此  

In the middle of a diarrhea shitstorm, the Food and Drug Administration and lettuce supplier Taylor Farms appear to be facing off in a passive aggressive battle of technicalities.  
在这场腹泻风暴中，美国食品药品监督管理局（FDA）与生菜供应商Taylor Farms似乎正陷入一场关于技术细节的被动攻击战。  

On July 17, the California-based company issued a voluntary recall on iceberg lettuce sourced from Central Mexico. The recall came after people across at least five states were sickened with cyclosporiasis, a parasitic infection that causes explosive diarrhea.  
7月17日，这家总部位于加州的公司自愿召回源自墨西哥中地的冰山生菜。此前，至少五个州的人员感染了环孢子虫病——一种导致剧烈腹泻的寄生虫感染。  

The next day, the FDA said that it had detected the parasite in a sample of Taylor Farms lettuce that wasn’t part of the initial recall. The day after that, the company and the FDA said that the result was actually a false positive.  
次日，FDA表示在未纳入最初召回范围的Taylor Farms生菜样本中检测到了该寄生虫。再之后，该公司与FDA共同称该结果实为假阳性。  

“Today, the FDA apologized to us,” Taylor Farms then posted on its X account on July 19.  
“今天FDA向我们道歉了，”Taylor Farms随后于7月19日在X平台账号上发文称。  

The FDA did not quite agree with that interpretation, however. On July 20, an agency official told reporters that it did not offer the company an “official apology.” Instead, the official said, "mostly we were providing factual information" about how difficult it is to test for and trace the sources of cyclospora outbreaks.  
然而，FDA并不完全认同这一说法。7月20日，该机构一名官员向记者表示，FDA并未向该公司作出“正式道歉”。该官员称：“我们主要是提供了事实信息”，说明检测和追溯环孢子虫疫情来源的难度。  

The agency emphasized that the recall—which spans 27 states, and includes certain lettuce sold to Taco Bell and Walmart—still stands. “To clarify, this false-positive lab sample DOES NOT change the basis for FDA’s ongoing outbreak investigation or the overwhelming epidemiological data supporting the current voluntary recall by Taylor Farms,” it posted on X.  
该机构强调，召回行动（覆盖27个州，包括售予Taco Bell和Walmart的某些生菜）仍然有效。其在X平台发文澄清：“此次假阳性实验室样本并不改变FDA持续疫情调查的基础，也不影响支持Taylor Farms当前自愿召回的压倒性流行病学数据。”  

In its July 19 post, Taylor Farms also wrote that the FDA “has not identified a single positive product test result for Cyclospora.” While true, this elides the difficulties involved in accurately testing for the parasite.  
在7月19日的帖子中，Taylor Farms还写道，FDA“未发现任何环孢子虫阳性产品检测结果”。虽然属实，但这回避了准确检测该寄生虫的难度。  

Many foodborne illness investigators determine the source of outbreaks without ever testing products. Instead, investigators use epidemiological evidence, such as interviewing people who have tested positive, to determine commonalities.  
许多食源性疾病调查人员在不检测产品的情况下即可确定疫情源头。调查人员转而使用流行病学证据，例如访谈检测呈阳性的人员，以找出共同点。  

Taylor Farms has reportedly taken issue with how the FDA and CDC has conducted their weeks-long investigation into the source of the outbreak. A spokesperson told The New York Times that, last week, the company met with White House and FDA officials to discuss what it called “shortfalls” in the government organizations’ response processes.  
据报道，Taylor Farms对FDA和疾控中心（CDC）长达数周的疫情源头调查方式提出异议。一名发言人告诉《纽约时报》，该公司上周与白宫及FDA官员会面，讨论了其所谓政府机构应对流程中的“不足”。  

The Times reported that Taylor Farms executives attempted to cast doubt on the FDA and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention’s conclusions in the meeting, which took place on July 16, the day before Taylor Farms issued its recall.  
《纽约时报》报道称，Taylor Farms高管在7月16日的会议（即该公司发布召回的前一天）上试图对FDA和疾控中心的结论提出质疑。  

Taylor Farms did not respond to WIRED’s requests for comment.  
Taylor Farms未回应《连线》杂志的置评请求。  

Since May, thousands of people have been sickened with cyclosporasis, as health officials have struggled to pinpoint the source of the outbreak. Authorities in Michigan say that they have counted 6,148 cases in that state alone, though experts previously told WIRED that the number of cases nationwide is likely higher than what’s been reported.  
自5月以来，数千人感染环孢子虫病，而卫生官员一直难以确定疫情源头。密歇根州当局表示，仅该州已统计到6,148例病例，但专家此前告诉《连线》杂志，全国病例数可能高于已报告数字。
