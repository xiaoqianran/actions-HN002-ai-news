# YouTube clarifies policies around AI slop and upsetting videos  
# YouTube 澄清关于 AI 低质内容和令人不安视频的政策  

A recent change sees YouTube further cracking down on AI slop by clarifying its policies around how content on its platform can be monetized.  
近期的一项变更显示，YouTube 通过澄清其平台上内容如何变现的政策，进一步打击 AI 低质内容。  

To discourage creators from using AI and other tools to make low-quality content, YouTube’s policy now explains that there are three types of videos under the broader category of “inauthentic content” that cannot be monetized.  
为了阻止创作者使用 AI 和其他工具制作低质量内容，YouTube 的政策现在说明，在更广泛的“非真实内容”类别下，有三种类型的视频无法获得变现收益。  

YouTube already had policies around AI content. Last year, the company announced it was stemming creators’ ability to generate revenue from what it called “inauthentic content,” including mass-produced videos and other types of repetitive content that have become easier to generate with the help of AI technology.  
YouTube 此前已有关于 AI 内容的政策。去年，该公司宣布将限制创作者从所谓的“非真实内容”（包括借助 AI 技术更易生成的大批量视频和其他重复性内容）中获取收入的能力。  

Rather, the recent update is about adding more nuance to the existing policies. The updated guidelines break down inauthentic content into three categories, including generic, repetitive, or template-based content, followed by off-putting or distressing content, and then any content where AI personas are used to discuss sensitive topics, like health and finance.  
而最近的更新旨在为现有政策增添更多细微差别。更新后的指南将“非真实内容”细分为三类：通用、重复或基于模板的内容；其次是令人不适或痛苦的内容；以及任何使用 AI 虚拟形象讨论健康、金融等敏感话题的内容。  

While AI slop, much like adult content, is something of a “you’ll know it when you see it” situation for many, YouTube’s policies are meant to more specifically spell out when AI’s use in content creation is allowed or is not permitted for videos being monetized through its YouTube Partner Program (YPP).  
尽管对许多人来说，AI 低质内容（类似成人内容）是一种“看到就知道”的情况，但 YouTube 的政策旨在更具体地阐明，在通过 YouTube 合作伙伴计划（YPP）变现的视频中，何时允许或禁止在内容创作中使用 AI。  

The program, which allows creators to earn money through advertising and subscriptions, is YouTube’s lifeblood; allowing it to fill up with AI slop or other low-quality fare would not be in the company’s financial interests.  
该计划允许创作者通过广告和订阅赚钱，是 YouTube 的生命线；让其充斥着 AI 低质内容或其他低质量内容不符合公司的财务利益。  

This is especially important given that YouTube is in close competition with TV and other streamers for TV ad dollars. Google’s video platform now generates more ad revenue than rival streamers and has overtaken Netflix in average daily views worldwide.  
这一点尤为重要，因为 YouTube 正在与电视和其他流媒体平台激烈竞争电视广告收入。谷歌的视频平台目前产生的广告收入已超过竞争对手流媒体，并在全球日均观看量上超越了 Netflix。  

As YouTube’s trust and safety chief Matt Halprin explained in a Creator Insider video last week, the company’s goal with its policy revision is to cut down on content farming efforts — a reference to low-quality videos that exist solely to generate revenue.  
正如 YouTube 信任与安全负责人 Matt Halprin 上周在《Creator Insider》视频中解释的那样，公司修订政策的目标是减少“内容农场”行为——指仅为产生收入而存在的低质量视频。  

“AI can actually allow people to make a lot of videos,” said Halprin. “Sometimes those videos are great, and it really enhances creativity. And you can create a higher volume of high-quality content that we want to encourage and have in YPP.”  
“AI 实际上可以让人们制作大量视频，”Halprin 说。“有时这些视频很棒，它真正增强了创造力。你可以创造更多我们希望在 YPP 中看到的高质量内容。”  

“But that exact same new tool can allow you to make lots of videos really quickly that are very similar. They’re very generic and don’t really have a narrative arc and don’t really show your creativity. So the same technology really enables great stuff, but it also enables stuff that’s kind of content farming, and that’s the stuff that we don’t want to have in YPP,” Halprin added.  
“但同样的新工具也可以让你快速制作大量非常相似的视频。它们非常通用，没有真正的叙事弧线，也没有真正展现你的创造力。所以同样的技术确实能实现伟大的东西，但它也能实现一些类似内容农场的东西，而这是我们不希望出现在 YPP 中的东西，”Halprin 补充道。  

The first category, he said, includes repetitive content, such as material that can be easily made with AI, computer-generated imagery (CGI), or templates, with little variation from video to video. This fills a channel with cookie-cutter videos.  
他表示，第一类包括重复性内容，例如可以轻松使用 AI、计算机生成图像（CGI）或模板制作、视频之间几乎没有变化的内容。这会让频道充斥着千篇一律的视频。  

Halprin notes that tutorial videos could also fall under this policy if they’re reproducing content that’s already prevalent on the platform, instead of being original.  
Halprin 指出，如果教程视频只是复制平台上已普遍存在的内容，而非原创，也可能属于此政策范围。  

The second category focuses on what YouTube calls “off-putting” content, or content designed to be distressing or emotionally manipulative to chase views. This could include videos featuring an animal in distress, which someone then comes along and rescues, Halprin said.  
第二类侧重于 YouTube 所称的“令人不适”内容，即为追求观看量而设计成令人痛苦或情感操纵的内容。Halprin 表示，这可能包括展示一只动物处于痛苦中、随后有人前来救援的视频。  

“We’ve heard from our viewers that that’s not something that they like. They find it off-putting. They don’t want to come back to that channel, or maybe even the platform,” Halprin said.  
“我们已从观众那里听说他们不喜欢这个。他们觉得这令人不适。他们不想再回到那个频道，甚至可能不想再回到平台，”Halprin 说。  

Channels dedicated to this content will be removed from YPP, regardless of whether the content is AI-generated or not.  
专门发布此类内容的频道将被移出 YPP，无论内容是否由 AI 生成。  

The third bucket more directly targets the use of AI personas — representations of real people made with AI. YouTube says it doesn’t want to incentivize creators using AI personas to discuss more sensitive topics, like finance, legal issues, healthcare, and medical issues.  
第三类更直接地针对使用 AI 虚拟形象——即用 AI 制作的真人形象。YouTube 表示，不希望鼓励创作者使用 AI 虚拟形象讨论更敏感的话题，如金融、法律问题、医疗保健和医学问题。  

Any YouTube channel that has too much of any of these three types of content will not be able to monetize, Halprin said.  
Halprin 表示，任何包含过多这三类内容中任何一种的 YouTube 频道都将无法获得变现收益。  

The policy clarifications were rolled out on July 16 and impact all YouTube Partner Program members.  
这些政策澄清于 7 月 16 日推出，影响所有 YouTube 合作伙伴计划成员。
