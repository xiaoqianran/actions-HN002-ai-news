**Title:** qZACH-ViT: Quantization-Aware Intrinsic Explanations with Recursive Attribution-Stabilized Optimization  
**标题：** qZACH-ViT：基于递归归因稳定优化的量化感知内在解释  

**Authors:** Athanasios Angelakis  
**作者：** Athanasios Angelakis  

**Abstract:** Compact medical-image classifiers need efficiency and interpretable evidence, yet these goals are often addressed separately. We introduce qZACH-ViT, a quantization-aware extension of the zero-token (CLS-token-free), position-free ZACH-ViT backbone with recursive intrinsic patch-level class evidence.  
**摘要：** 紧凑型医学图像分类器需要高效性和可解释的证据，但这两个目标通常被分开处理。我们提出了 qZACH-ViT，它是零令牌（无 CLS 令牌）、无位置编码的 ZACH-ViT 骨干网络的量化感知扩展，具备递归的、内在的块级类证据。  

We also introduce Recursive Attribution-Stabilized Optimization (RASO), which norm-matches classification and attribution gradients and removes attribution components that conflict with classification.  
我们还引入了递归归因稳定优化（RASO），该方法通过范数匹配分类和归因梯度，并移除与分类冲突的归因分量。  

We evaluate four controlled conditions on seven MedMNIST datasets using 50 training images per class and ten fixed seeds, completing 280 runs. All 210 qZACH-ViT checkpoints are converted to executable mixed-precision ONNX INT8 graphs containing 16 signed INT8 MatMulInteger projections with INT32 accumulation.  
我们在七个 MedMNIST 数据集上使用每类 50 张训练图像和十个固定种子评估了四种受控条件，完成了 280 次运行。所有 210 个 qZACH-ViT 检查点均转换为可执行的混合精度 ONNX INT8 图，包含 16 个带 INT32 累加的带符号 INT8 MatMulInteger 投影。  

Deployed mixed-precision INT8 qZACH-ViT with Adam improves the FP32 ZACH-ViT baseline mean on all seven datasets, with a mean paired gain of 0.0313 in the dataset-specific primary metric; qZACH-ViT with RASO yields a mean gain of 0.0368.  
部署的混合精度 INT8 qZACH-ViT（使用 Adam）在所有七个数据集上均优于 FP32 ZACH-ViT 基线均值，在数据集特定主指标上的平均配对增益为 0.0313；使用 RASO 的 qZACH-ViT 实现了 0.0368 的平均增益。  

Across 964,920 source-to-INT8 test comparisons, prediction agreement is 99.9751%, with a mean absolute primary-metric change of 0.000133 and a maximum of 0.004386. Across 3,600 matched intrinsic maps, mean cosine similarity is 0.999955, mean rank correlation is 0.9944, and mean top-10% overlap is 0.9692.  
在 964,920 次源模型到 INT8 的测试比较中，预测一致率为 99.9751%，主指标的绝对平均变化为 0.000133，最大变化为 0.004386。在 3,600 张匹配的内在映射中，平均余弦相似度为 0.999955，平均秩相关系数为 0.9944，前 10% 区域的平均重叠率为 0.9692。  

ONNX artifacts are 70.0% smaller than source checkpoints and provide 1.41× and 2.39× end-to-end CPU speedups with one and four threads. RASO significantly reduces sufficiency error and improves input-noise stability over Adam with the same attribution loss, but does not dominate every predictive or explainable artificial intelligence (XAI) metric.  
ONNX 产物比源检查点小 70.0%，并在单线程和四线程下分别提供 1.41 倍和 2.39 倍的端到端 CPU 加速。RASO 显著降低了充分性误差，并比使用相同归因损失的 Adam 提高了输入噪声稳定性，但并未在所有预测或可解释人工智能（XAI）指标上占优。  

These results establish qZACH-ViT as a deployable compact intrinsically explainable model and RASO as a targeted stability-oriented optimization procedure.  
这些结果确立了 qZACH-ViT 作为可部署的紧凑型内在可解释模型，以及 RASO 作为面向稳定性的目标优化过程。
