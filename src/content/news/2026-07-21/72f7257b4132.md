**Title**: Stochastic Reset Pathfinding: Path-Level Regret for Cascading Bandits over Graph Paths  
**标题**: 随机重置路径查找：图路径上级联赌博机的路径级遗憾

**Classification & Submission**: Computer Science > Machine Learning arXiv:2607.15440 (cs) [Submitted on 16 Jul 2026]  
**分类与提交信息**: 计算机科学 > 机器学习 arXiv:2607.15440 (cs) [提交于 2026年7月16日]

**Authors**: Guni Sharon, Wei Zhang  
**作者**: Guni Sharon, Wei Zhang

We introduce Stochastic Reset Pathfinding (SRP), an episodic learning problem on a known directed graph with unknown stationary edge success probabilities. In each episode, the agent commits to a source-to-goal path, and any edge failure during execution resets it to the source.  
我们提出了随机重置路径查找（SRP），这是一个在已知有向图上的回合制学习问题，其中边的成功概率是未知但固定的。在每个回合中，智能体承诺一条从源到目标的路径，执行过程中任何边的失败都会将其重置回源点。

SRP captures settings such as entanglement distribution in quantum repeater networks, payment routing on the Lightning Network, and delivery in unreliable mesh networks.  
SRP涵盖了诸如量子中继器网络中的纠缠分发、闪电网络上的支付路由以及不可靠网格网络中的递送等场景。

We show that the global-reset structure makes the optimal policy open-loop, placing SRP within the combinatorial cascading bandit (CCB) framework.  
我们证明全局重置结构使得最优策略为开环策略，从而将SRP置于组合级联赌博机（CCB）框架内。

We propose a Log-Dijkstra meta-algorithm with UCB (PathUCB) and Thompson Sampling (PathTS) instantiations.  
我们提出了一个Log-Dijkstra元算法，并实例化为UCB（PathUCB）和汤普森采样（PathTS）。

Our main technical result is a path-level regret bound for PathUCB that decomposes regret over suboptimal paths via a per-path complexity C(π) combining each edge's prefix and suffix reliability.  
我们的主要技术成果是为PathUCB推导的路径级遗憾上界，该上界通过每条路径的复杂度C(π)将遗憾分解到次优路径上，其中C(π)结合了每条边的前缀和后缀可靠性。

The bound is complementary to the edge-level CCB bound and more informative on structured graphs with polynomially many source-to-goal paths.  
该上界与边级CCB上界互补，并且在具有多项式数量源到目标路径的结构化图上更具信息量。

Experiments on quantum-network, layered-DAG, grid-world, and Erdos-Renyi domains support the theory and show that PathTS typically achieves the best empirical performance among the algorithms tested.  
在量子网络、分层DAG、网格世界和Erdos-Renyi域上的实验支持了该理论，并表明PathTS通常在测试算法中实现最佳经验性能。

We then exhibit an adversarial instance on which PathTS fails to converge, consistent with a known exponential obstruction for combinatorial Thompson Sampling on multiplicative-reward problems.  
然后，我们展示了一个对抗性实例，在该实例上PathTS无法收敛，这与组合汤普森采样在乘法奖励问题上已知的指数障碍一致。

We recommend PathTS as the practical default while cautioning that adversarial instances exist.  
我们推荐PathTS作为实用的默认选择，同时警告对抗性实例的存在。
