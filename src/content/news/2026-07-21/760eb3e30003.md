# Process Reward Informed Tree Rollout for Effective Multi-Turn RL  
# 基于过程奖励感知的树展开以实现高效多轮强化学习  

**Authors:** Xintong Li, Sha Li, Yuwei Zhang, Changlong Yu, Rongmei Lin, Hongye Jin, Shuyi Guan, Xin Liu, Linwei Li, Qingyu Yin, Jingbo Shang  
**作者：** 李欣彤、李莎、张宇威、余常龙、林蓉梅、金鸿烨、关淑仪、刘鑫、李林伟、秦玉、商静波  

---

Reinforcement learning (RL) has become a key approach for training LLM agents, yet popular methods such as GRPO/RLOO rely on multiple independently sampled complete trajectories for advantage estimation. In long-horizon agentic tasks, such a uniform rollout strategy can waste budget on uninformative dead-end attempts, while promising intermediate states do not receive sufficient exploration.  
强化学习（RL）已成为训练大语言模型（LLM）智能体的关键方法，但GRPO/RLOO等流行方法依赖多个独立采样的完整轨迹进行优势估计。在长时程智能体任务中，这种均匀展开策略可能将预算浪费在信息量不足的死胡同尝试上，而富有前景的中间状态却未得到充分探索。  

The multi-turn structure of agentic trajectories, with interleaved actions and observations, naturally supports organizing a trajectory group as a tree, where each turn serves as a decision point for exploration. This perspective reframes effective exploration as the problem of deciding where to branch.  
智能体轨迹的多轮结构（动作与观察交替进行）天然支持将轨迹组组织为树形结构，其中每一轮都成为探索的决策点。这一视角将有效探索重新定义为决定在何处分支的问题。  

We propose Process-Scorer Guided Adaptive Tree Rollout (PATR), a quality-aware rollout framework for multi-turn agent RL. PATR uses task-appropriate process feedback to score partial trajectories, selectively branches from promising states, reuses shared prefixes, and conservatively stops degenerate paths to reduce wasted sampling.  
我们提出了过程评分器引导的自适应树展开（PATR），这是一个面向多轮智能体RL的质量感知展开框架。PATR利用任务适配的过程反馈对部分轨迹评分，从有希望的状态选择性分支，重用共享前缀，并保守地终止退化路径以减少采样浪费。  

The resulting rollout groups remain compatible with standard policy optimization while providing more efficient exploration under the same training budget.  
生成的轨迹组与标准策略优化兼容，同时在相同训练预算下提供更高效的探索。  

We evaluate PATR on FrozenLake and the challenging SWE-Bench, which is largely unexplored by prior tree-rollout agent RL methods. Experiments show that PATR improves performance by up to +5.0 points on SWE-Bench and +9.3 points on FrozenLake, highlighting process-guided tree rollouts as an effective strategy for scalable multi-turn RL.  
我们在FrozenLake和具有挑战性的SWE-Bench上评估了PATR，后者此前几乎未被树展开智能体RL方法探索。实验表明，PATR在SWE-Bench上最高提升5.0分，在FrozenLake上提升9.3分，凸显了过程引导的树展开作为可扩展多轮RL的有效策略。
