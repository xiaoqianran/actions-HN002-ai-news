**Title:** VarRate: Training-Free Variable-Rate KV Cache Compression for Long-Context LLMs  
**标题：** VarRate：面向长上下文大语言模型的免训练可变速率KV缓存压缩

**Content:**  
**内容：**

The key-value (KV) cache is the main memory bottleneck in long-context large language model (LLM) inference.  
键值（KV）缓存是长上下文大语言模型（LLM）推理中的主要内存瓶颈。

Two leading training-free families are both structurally limited: token-selection methods (SnapKV, Ada-KV) score importance from an observation window and evict low-scoring tokens, but eviction is irreversible -- so when the importance signal degrades under query-agnostic reuse, accuracy collapses by 11-15 points; uniform low-rank coding keeps every token but spends equal rank everywhere, wasting budget.  
两种主流的免训练方法都存在结构限制：令牌选择方法（如SnapKV、Ada-KV）通过观察窗口评估重要性并驱逐低分令牌，但驱逐不可逆——因此当重要性信号在查询无关复用下退化时，准确率会骤降11-15点；均匀低秩编码保留所有令牌，但处处分配相同秩，浪费预算。

We observe that both failures share one cure: rank should be allocated, not evicted.  
我们观察到，两种失败都有一个共同解法：秩应被分配，而非被驱逐。

We present VarRate, a training-free KV codec that assigns each token a variable low-rank budget by its query salience, keeping every token at a nonzero rank.  
我们提出VarRate，一种免训练的KV编解码器，它根据每个令牌的查询显著性分配可变低秩预算，使每个令牌都保持非零秩。

Comparable adaptive-rank codecs reach this allocation only through training; VarRate requires none.  
 comparable自适应秩编解码器只能通过训练达到此分配；VarRate无需训练。

Because no token is dropped, it degrades by only 3.5-5.5 points where query-aware selection collapses.  
由于没有令牌被丢弃，在查询感知选择失效的地方，其性能仅下降3.5-5.5点。

At a matched 20% budget on LongBench (16 tasks), VarRate stays within 0.8 points of the uncompressed model on both Llama-3.1-8B and Qwen2.5-7B.  
在LongBench（16项任务）上匹配20%的预算时，VarRate在Llama-3.1-8B和Qwen2.5-7B上均与未压缩模型的差距在0.8点以内。

Averaged over the two, it is the strongest matched-memory compressor.  
两者平均来看，它是同内存预算下最强的压缩器。

It significantly beats its uniform-rank ablation on both models.  
它在两个模型上均显著优于其均匀秩消融实验。

Against KVzip, a method purpose-built for query-agnostic reuse, it is accuracy-equivalent in three of four settings and within a point overall, at about one-eighth the prefill overhead.  
与专为查询无关复用设计的KVzip相比，在四种设置中的三种里准确率相当，总体差距在1点以内，且预填充开销仅约为其八分之一。
