# An Empirical Study of Handcrafted Feature Learning and Convolutional Neural Networks for Facial Expression Recognition
# 手工设计特征学习与卷积神经网络在面部表情识别中的实证研究

Facial expression recognition is an important computer vision task with applications in human-computer interaction, mental health monitoring, driver alert systems, and behavioral analysis.
面部表情识别是一项重要的计算机视觉任务，应用于人机交互、心理健康监测、驾驶员警报系统和行为分析等领域。

While convolutional neural networks (CNNs) dominate modern facial expression recognition, handcrafted feature descriptors such as Histogram of Oriented Gradients (HOG) and Local Binary Patterns (LBP) remain useful classical baselines.
尽管卷积神经网络（CNNs）主导了现代面部表情识别，但手工设计特征描述符（如方向梯度直方图（HOG）和局部二值模式（LBP））仍然是 useful 的经典基准。

This study compares HOG with Support Vector Machine (SVM), LBP with Logistic Regression, and a lightweight CNN across three facial expression datasets: FER-2013, CK+, and KDEF.
本研究比较了 HOG 与支持向量机（SVM）、LBP 与逻辑回归，以及一个轻量级 CNN 在三个面部表情数据集上的表现：FER-2013、CK+ 和 KDEF。

The results show that CNNs achieve the best overall performance, particularly on more complex data, while HOG performs strongly in controlled environments.
结果表明，CNNs 实现了最佳的整体性能，尤其是在更复杂的数据上；而 HOG 在受控环境中表现强劲。

LBP performs poorly across all datasets.
LBP 在所有数据集上表现均不佳。

The study highlights that dataset complexity significantly affects performance and that robust feature learning is essential for real-world facial expression recognition.
该研究强调，数据集复杂性会显著影响性能，而鲁棒的特征学习对于现实世界中的面部表情识别至关重要。
