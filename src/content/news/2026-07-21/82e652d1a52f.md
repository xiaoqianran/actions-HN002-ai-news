GS-RealBlur: A Flexible Data Acquisition Framework for Real-World Image Deblurring  
GS-RealBlur：一种用于真实世界图像去模糊的灵活数据采集框架

Authors: Mingyang Chen, Zhilu Zhang, Honglei Xu, Renlong Wu, Xiaohe Wu, Wangmeng Zuo  
作者：Mingyang Chen, Zhilu Zhang, Honglei Xu, Renlong Wu, Xiaohe Wu, Wangmeng Zuo

arXiv:2607.15401 [cs.CV], submitted on July 16, 2026.  
arXiv:2607.15401 [cs.CV]，提交于2026年7月16日。

High-quality, large-scale paired data is essential for training learning-based image deblurring models. However, synthetic blurry images generally lack realism, while real-world captured images require complex and inflexible camera systems.  
高质量、大规模成对数据对于训练基于学习的图像去模糊模型至关重要。然而，合成的模糊图像通常缺乏真实感，而真实世界捕获的图像需要复杂且不灵活的相机系统。

In this work, we propose GS-RealBlur, a data acquisition framework for real-world image deblurring, achieving both blur realism and acquisition flexibility.  
在这项工作中，我们提出了 GS-RealBlur，一个用于真实世界图像去模糊的数据采集框架，实现了模糊真实性和采集灵活性的双重目标。

Specifically, we use a handheld camera to capture blurry images, and deploy a gimbal to densely capture sharp images of the same scene. We reconstruct the 3D representation of sharp images and calibrate the camera pose of each blurry frame within this 3D. The image rendered from this 3D according to the pose serves as the sharp counterpart.  
具体来说，我们使用手持相机捕获模糊图像，并部署云台密集捕获同一场景的清晰图像。我们重建清晰图像的三维表示，并在此三维空间内标定每个模糊帧的相机姿态。根据该姿态从三维渲染的图像作为清晰对应图像。

To better align the rendered image with the blurry image, we introduce a Blur-aware Pose Refinement (BPR) module that refines the pose using appearance consistency and centroid alignment constraints.  
为了更好地对齐渲染图像与模糊图像，我们引入了模糊感知姿态细化（BPR）模块，该模块使用外观一致性和质心对齐约束来细化姿态。

Leveraging GS-RealBlur, we construct a high-quality and diverse dataset. Extensive experiments demonstrate that a deblurring model trained on our dataset achieves superior generalization performance across various real-world deblurring benchmarks, consistently outperforming models trained on existing synthetic and real-world datasets.  
利用 GS-RealBlur，我们构建了一个高质量且多样化的数据集。大量实验表明，在我们数据集上训练的去模糊模型在多个真实世界去模糊基准上实现了卓越的泛化性能，持续优于在现有合成和真实世界数据集上训练的模型。

The code and dataset will be made publicly available.  
代码和数据集将公开提供。
