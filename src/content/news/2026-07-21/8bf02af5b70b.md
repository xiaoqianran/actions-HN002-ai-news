# Loop Engineering with Adaptive Parsing in Action: Parsing Flat Tables with Azure and Figures with a Vision LLM  
# 自适应解析循环工程实战：使用 Azure 解析平面表格，使用视觉大语言模型解析图表  

Enterprise Document Intelligence [Vol.1 #10B] – The LLM as last line of defence, then two real escalations walked end to end: a flat table to Azure, a figure to a vision model  
企业文档智能 [第1卷 #10B]——大语言模型作为最后一道防线，随后是两个真实升级案例的端到端演练：平面表格升级至 Azure，图表升级至视觉模型  

Some bad parses do not look bad until the answer comes out (classic OCR is the textbook case: EasyOCR recovers the words and quietly drops the table structure around them, and the answer reads fine until you check it against the page).  
有些错误的解析在答案生成前并不明显（经典 OCR 就是教科书案例：EasyOCR 能恢复文字，却悄然丢弃了周围的表格结构，答案读起来没问题，直到你对照页面核对）。  

A deterministic check catches the obvious failures for free, but a table that parsed into plausible-looking nonsense sails right through it, and the model answers from that nonsense with full confidence.  
确定性检查能免费抓住明显的失败，但一个被解析成看似合理实则无意义的表格却能轻松通过，模型会基于这些无意义内容自信地给出答案。  

Nothing upstream flagged it, because on paper the parse looked fine. The last line of defence is the LLM reading its own input before the user ever sees the answer.  
上游没有任何环节发出警告，因为从纸面看解析结果似乎没问题。最后一道防线是大语言模型在用户看到答案前，先阅读它自己的输入。

PyMuPDF parses a page in five milliseconds for free. A vision LLM on the same page can cost ten thousand times more and take ten seconds.  
PyMuPDF 可在五毫秒内免费解析一个页面。而同一页面的视觉大语言模型解析成本可能高出万倍，耗时十秒。  

When the question lives in plain prose, the cheap parser wins. When the answer hides inside a table, a figure, a scanned region, or a flattened layout, the cheap parser silently returns nothing useful and the LLM confidently answers the wrong question.  
当问题存在于纯文本中时，低成本解析器胜出。当答案隐藏在表格、图表、扫描区域或扁平化布局中时，低成本解析器会静默返回无用的内容，而大语言模型会自信地回答错误的问题。  

Running the heaviest parser on every page is wasteful. Running the cheapest one everywhere is wrong.  
在每一页上都运行最重的解析器是浪费的。 everywhere 都运行最便宜的解析器则是错误的。  

The trick is to start cheap and escalate only when something says the cheap parse missed the answer.  
诀窍是从低成本开始，仅当有迹象表明廉价解析遗漏了答案时才升级。  

That signal has to come from the pipeline itself: a chain of checks against the parse output, the question, and the LLM’s own footprint when it tries to use what came out.  
该信号必须来自管道本身：一系列针对解析输出、问题以及大语言模型尝试使用输出内容时留下的自身痕迹的检查。  

The way around this is a feedback loop the pipeline builds for itself. Start with the cheapest parser. Evaluate its output at every check of the pipeline. Escalate to a deeper parser only when at least one check flags the cheap parse as insufficient for the question.  
解决之道是管道为自己构建的反馈循环。从最便宜的解析器开始。在管道的每一个检查点评估其输出。仅当至少有一个检查标记廉价解析不足以回答问题时才升级到更深入的解析器。  

The evaluation is not one check at one place. It is a cascade:  
评估不是在某一处的单一检查，而是一个级联：  

- pre-parsing metadata routes most of the corpus before any extraction  
  - 预解析元数据在任何提取前路由大部分语料库  
- parsing-time outputs flag flattened tables and opaque figures  
  - 解析时输出标记扁平化表格和不透明图表  
- retrieval scoring catches anchors that drift  
  - 检索评分捕捉漂移的锚点  
- generation flags what survived all of the above  
  - 生成环节标记通过以上所有检查的内容  

Each check is cheaper than the next, more reliable than the next, and asks its own question: “did the parser produce enough to answer?”.  
每个检查都比下一个更便宜，比下一个更可靠，并询问自己的问题：“解析器是否产生了足够回答问题的内容？”  

The first part caught failures cheaply, before generation. This part is what happens when they slip through: the LLM’s own footprint at generation time (check 7, self-evaluation; check 8, groundedness) flags a parse the deterministic checks missed, and the pipeline escalates the offending page to a deeper parser.  
第一部分在生成前以低成本捕获了失败。本部分讲述当它们漏网时会发生什么：大语言模型在生成时的自身痕迹（检查7，自我评估；检查8， groundedness）标记了确定性检查遗漏的解析，管道将问题页面升级到更深入的解析器。

两个反复出现的例子被端到端演练：《注意力机制》论文（Vaswani 等人，2017，遵循 arXiv 非独家分发许可）的表3（第9页，扁平解析的网格升级至 Azure Layout）和图1（第3页，不透明图表升级至视觉大语言模型）。

两者都是真实的，每天都在企业语料库中出现。  

1. The generation-side checks: check 7 (self-eval) and check 8 (groundedness)  
1. 生成端检查：检查7（自我评估）和检查8（groundedness）  

Generation is the last stop in the cascade. Its two checks are check 7 (the LLM’s self-evaluation flag, baked into the typed answer) and check 8 (a separate-LLM or NLI groundedness check after the answer is produced).  
生成是级联的最后一站。它的两个检查是检查7（大语言模型的自我评估标志， baked into 打字的答案）和检查8（答案产生后，由独立大语言模型或 NLI 进行的 groundedness 检查）。  

Check 7 is the most expensive AND least stable check in the panorama, so this part spends real time on it: two walkthroughs that show it firing usefully (Case A in section 2, Case B in section 3), then a stress test that says the LLM signal alone is not enough (section 4).  
检查7是全景中最昂贵且最不稳定的检查，因此本部分花大量时间讨论它：两个展示其有效触发的演练（第2节案例A，第3节案例B），然后是一个压力测试，表明仅靠大语言模型信号是不够的（第4节）。  

Check 8 is section 5.  
检查8在第5节。

在两个演练之前：大多数问题根本不需要任何升级。

2. 案例A：平面表格升级，端到端

问题：“《Transformer 架构变体》表中基础模型的 h 值是多少？”

正确答案是 8，可从《注意力机制》论文第9页表3的基础行 h 列中读出。
