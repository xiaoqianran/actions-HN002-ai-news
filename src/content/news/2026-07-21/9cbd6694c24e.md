# Judge pauses Paramount’s attempt to buy Warner Bros. Discovery

**法官暂停派拉蒙收购华纳兄弟探索的尝试**

A judge partially granted the request from a dozen state attorneys general to temporarily place the $110 billion merger of Paramount and Warner Bros. Discovery on hold, as reported by Variety and Reuters.

据《综艺》和路透社报道，一位法官部分批准了十几个州总检察长的请求，暂时搁置派拉蒙与华纳兄弟探索价值1100亿美元的合并交易。

US District Judge Araceli Martínez-Olguín said that based on the new company’s market share, “the Court is persuaded that it can presume the proposed merger is likely to violate antitrust laws,” and that the states showed “irreparable harm” could occur without a temporary restraining order.

美国地区法官阿塞莉·马丁内斯-奥尔金表示，基于新公司的市场份额，“法院确信可以推定拟议的合并很可能违反反垄断法”，且各州表明，若无临时限制令，“可能发生不可挽回的损害”。

A week ago, California, Arizona, Colorado, Connecticut, Massachusetts, Minnesota, Nevada, New Jersey, New Mexico, New York, Oregon, and Washington sued to block the deal for 28 days, saying the newly created “media behemoth” would harm competition, and that if the deal is allowed to close, then the companies could start hard-to-undo actions like layoffs and information sharing.

一周前，加利福尼亚、亚利桑那、科罗拉多、康涅狄格、马萨诸塞、明尼苏达、内华达、新泽西、新墨西哥、纽约、俄勒冈和华盛顿州提起诉讼，要求阻止该交易28天。它们称新诞生的“媒体巨头”将损害竞争，且若允许交易完成，公司可能启动裁员和信息共享等难以撤销的行动。

The judge put in place a 14-day restraining order and set a hearing for the preliminary injunction on August 3rd.

法官颁布了为期14天的限制令，并定于8月3日举行初步禁令听证会。

If the deal hasn’t closed by September 30th, Paramount will owe Warner Bros. investors millions of dollars based on how long it takes, as laid out in the agreement it used to beat Netflix’s $83 billion offer.

若交易未能在9月30日前完成，派拉蒙将根据耗时长短，向华纳兄弟投资者支付数百万美元，这在其用以击败Netflix 830亿美元报价的协议中已有规定。

California AG Rob Bonta said over the weekend that the ticking fee starting in October “is a choice that Paramount made. They agreed to it as a party with Warner Bros. They might regret it, but they absolutely knew that this proposed merger was going to have to go through a regulatory process, and that that would take time,”

加州总检察长罗布·邦塔周末表示，从10月开始计算的 ticking fee（延迟费）“是派拉蒙自己的选择。他们作为与华纳兄弟签约的一方同意了这一点。他们可能后悔，但他们绝对清楚这项拟议的合并必须经过监管程序，而这需要时间，”

while also dismissing threats about Paramount moving out of California, and saying that a CNN spinoff plan wouldn’t resolve the states’ lawsuit.

同时，他驳斥了派拉蒙可能迁出加州的威胁，并表示CNN分拆计划无法解决各州的诉讼。

After the judge’s decision was released on Monday, Bonta released a statement saying, “This is a critical first win in our case to ensure this megamerger never sees the light of day.”

周一法官裁决公布后，邦塔发表声明称：“这是我们为确保这项巨型合并永不见天日而取得的首个关键胜利。”

If the deal gets delayed for too long, it could cost Paramount millions of dollars a day.

若交易延迟过久，派拉蒙可能每天损失数百万美元。
