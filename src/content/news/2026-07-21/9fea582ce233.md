# Regularity-Aware Stochastic MGDA with Adaptive Conflict-Avoidant Update Direction Control  
# 规律感知随机MGDA与自适应冲突避免更新方向控制  

**Authors:** Chentong Huang, Lisha Chen  
**作者：** 黄晨彤，李莎  

---

**Abstract:**  
Multi-objective learning (MOL) aims to optimize multiple objectives simultaneously. The multi-gradient descent algorithm (MGDA) is a workhorse that iteratively updates along a common descent or conflict-avoidant (CA) direction across objectives. In stochastic settings, however, the vanilla stochastic MGDA method, SMG, lacks a fast convergence rate because mini-batch sampling introduces noise in the gradients. This causes bias in the update direction, which is controlled by the CA direction continuity.  

**摘要：**  
多目标学习（MOL）旨在同时优化多个目标。多梯度下降算法（MGDA）是一种核心方法，它沿着跨目标的共同下降或冲突避免（CA）方向进行迭代更新。然而，在随机设置中，基础随机MGDA方法（SMG）缺乏较快的收敛速率，因为小批量采样会在梯度中引入噪声。这导致更新方向产生偏差，而该偏差受CA方向连续性控制。  

---

In this paper, we show that the CA direction is $1/2$-Hölder continuous with respect to the Jacobian matrix, and the exponent $1/2$ cannot be improved in the worst case. This leads to a suboptimal convergence rate for vanilla stochastic MGDA in prior works. Nevertheless, under additional regularity conditions, we show this can be improved to Lipschitz continuity.  

本文证明，CA方向关于雅可比矩阵是$1/2$-Hölder连续的，且在最坏情况下指数$1/2$无法改进。这导致先前工作中基础随机MGDA的收敛速率次优。然而，在额外正则性条件下，我们证明这可以改进为利普希茨连续。  

---

Based on this insight, we propose a stochastic multi-objective regularity-aware (MoRe) method that exploits the Lipschitz continuity of the CA direction when the subproblem is regular, and switches to a fixed scalarization weight otherwise. Intuitively, the proposed algorithm employs CA direction update when the gradient conflict is large, and linear scalarization update otherwise.  

基于这一洞察，我们提出了一种随机多目标规律感知（MoRe）方法，当子问题正则时利用CA方向的利普希茨连续性，否则切换到固定标量化权重。直观上，该算法在梯度冲突较大时采用CA方向更新，否则采用线性标量化更新。  

---

Theoretically, our method improves the convergence rate of SMG in the nonconvex setting from $\widetilde{\mathcal O}(T^{-1/4})$ to $\widetilde{\mathcal O}(T^{-1/2})$, where $\widetilde{\mathcal O}(\cdot)$ hides logarithmic factors. Meanwhile, we also establish the per-iterate conflict-avoidance guarantees.  

理论上，我们的方法将SMG在非凸设置下的收敛速率从$\widetilde{\mathcal O}(T^{-1/4})$提升至$\widetilde{\mathcal O}(T^{-1/2})$，其中$\widetilde{\mathcal O}(\cdot)$隐藏了对数因子。同时，我们还建立了每次迭代的冲突避免保证。  

---

Empirically, experiments demonstrate its effectiveness in multi-task performance and verify convergence behavior consistent with the established theoretical rate.  

实验上，实验证明了其在多任务性能中的有效性，并验证了收敛行为与已建立的理论速率一致。
