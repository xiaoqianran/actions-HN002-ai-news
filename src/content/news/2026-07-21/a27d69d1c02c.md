# Title: The Galaxy Card Is Samsung’s Answer to the Apple Card  
**标题：三星推出Galaxy Card，对标苹果Apple Card**

---

Nearly seven years after Apple debuted the Apple Card, Samsung is following the iPhone maker's footsteps with the Galaxy Card, aiming for its own slice of the credit card market. The announcement comes two days before Samsung's second Galaxy Unpacked event of the year, where it's expected to showcase new smartwatches and folding smartphones.  
在苹果推出Apple Card近七年后，三星紧随iPhone制造商的脚步，推出Galaxy Card，旨在分得信用卡市场的一杯羹。该公告发布于三星今年第二次Galaxy Unpacked活动前两天，预计届时将展示新手表和折叠屏智能手机。

---

The Galaxy Card is issued by Barclays on the Visa network; the Apple Card, originally issued by Goldman Sachs but now transitioning to Chase, is on the MasterCard network. There is a physical card—it's not made of titanium but recycled steel. The virtual card will be provisioned to a user's Samsung Wallet account.  
Galaxy Card由巴克莱银行发行，运行于Visa网络；Apple Card最初由高盛发行，现正过渡至大通银行，运行于MasterCard网络。该卡提供实体卡——并非钛金属材质，而是由回收钢材制成。虚拟卡将绑定至用户的Samsung Wallet账户。

---

With no annual fee, Samsung says cardmembers can earn 5 percent cash rewards on all in-store or online purchases made directly from Samsung in the US, 3 percent cash rewards on purchases made with the Galaxy Card using Samsung Wallet, 2 percent cash rewards on streaming service subscriptions, and 1 percent cash rewards on everything else with the physical card. The cash rewards can be redeemed as a statement credit or transferred to a checking or savings account.  
三星表示，该卡无年费，持卡人可享受以下现金奖励：在美国三星官方渠道（门店或在线）消费获5%返现；通过Samsung Wallet使用Galaxy Card消费获3%返现；流媒体订阅服务消费获2%返现；使用实体卡的其他消费获1%返现。现金奖励可兑换为账单抵扣或转入支票/储蓄账户。

---

The annual percentage rate (APR) varies by cardmember, but the card has no foreign transaction fees. Other perks include a 20 percent discount on Samsung's VIP Advantage membership, which offers extended device protection, specialized support, and exclusive deals, and $200 in cash rewards after spending $2,000 in the first 90 days. Applications open up on July 22.  
年利率（APR）因持卡人而异，但该卡无境外交易手续费。其他优惠包括：三星VIP Advantage会员享8折，该会员提供延长设备保修、专属支持和独家优惠；开卡后90天内消费满2000美元可获200美元现金奖励。申请于7月22日开放。

---

The Samsung Wallet app is only available on Samsung smartphones and watches, so what happens if a consumer switches to a different smartphone brand? The company says Galaxy Card is not limited to Samsung device owners and that anyone can use the physical card, but you lose the key perks; the card can be managed through a BarclaysUS.com online portal. (Similarly, if an iPhone owner switches to Android, their physical Apple Card will still work, but they lose access to the Apple Wallet app and the 3 percent daily cash perk on Apple purchases; there's a web portal to manage the account.)  
Samsung Wallet应用仅限三星手机和手表使用，若用户更换其他品牌手机怎么办？三星表示Galaxy Card不限于三星设备用户，任何人都可使用实体卡，但将失去核心优惠；该卡可通过BarclaysUS.com在线门户管理。（类似地，若iPhone用户转用安卓，其实体Apple Card仍可使用，但将无法使用Apple Wallet应用及苹果消费3%每日返现优惠；账户可通过网页门户管理。）

---

The rewards aren't revolutionary, but they're targeting Samsung's most loyal consumers and card-maxxers looking to rack up the most points with every dollar they spend. Brian Riley, director of Credit Advisory Services at Javelin Strategy & Research, a financial research and consulting group, says entering the space is important for Samsung to increase brand presence and loyalty.  
这些奖励并非革命性，但目标直指三星最忠实的消费者和追求每美元消费最大化积分的“信用卡达人”。金融研究与咨询公司Javelin Strategy & Research的信贷咨询服务总监Brian Riley表示，进入这一领域对三星提升品牌影响力和用户忠诚度至关重要。

---

“Cards are basically a commodity at the end of the day; how you differentiate them is really what makes the difference," Riley says. “That's really the big deal here—how you use your card." Riley has a card for grocery shopping and one for Amazon transactions; all of these steps help him earn the most points.  
“归根结底，信用卡本质上是一种商品；如何差异化才是关键，”Riley说，“这里真正重要的是——你如何使用你的卡。”Riley自己有一张用于杂货购物的卡和一张用于亚马逊交易的卡；所有这些策略帮助他最大化累积积分。

---

“One of the big challenges here on rewards is that quite often, you go in well-intentioned and you don't get the full benefit of the rewards because you start revolving on the product,” Riley says, meaning cardmembers start accumulating interest on the unpaid balance, with interest charges wiping out the value earned by the rewards.  
“奖励计划的一大挑战是，你往往初衷良好，却因开始循环使用信用卡（未全额还款）而无法获得全部奖励价值，”Riley表示，这意味着持卡人开始对未还余额累积利息，利息费用会抵消奖励带来的收益。

---

Riley points to one of the failings of the Apple Card: Apple oversold it. It's slick, yes, but it never became a mainstream challenger to the credit card industry because the average consumer typically has three or four cards per household: one for all of their purchases, one for emergencies, and one that might focus on travel.  
Riley指出Apple Card的失败之处之一：苹果过度营销了它。它确实设计精美，但从未成为信用卡行业的主流挑战者，因为普通家庭通常拥有三到四张卡：一张用于所有消费，一张用于应急，另一张可能专注于旅行。

---

Sara Rathner, a credit card expert at NerdWallet, echoed that sentiment. “The Apple Card is far from the iPhone in terms of changing the world,” Rathner says. “It's fine; it's a cash-back card.” All of these cards exist to create brand loyalty, she says. If you have a lot of points with Hyatt, you're more likely to book a stay at a Hyatt property on your next trip. If you make many purchases with Samsung Wallet, that might incentivize you to get the Galaxy Card.  
NerdWallet信用卡专家Sara Rathner对此表示赞同。“Apple Card在改变世界方面远不如iPhone，”Rathner说，“它还行；它只是一张返现卡。”她说，所有这些卡都是为了建立品牌忠诚度。如果你在凯悦酒店累积了大量积分，下次旅行就更可能预订凯悦酒店。如果你频繁使用Samsung Wallet消费，这可能激励你办理Galaxy Card。

---

Rathner says the 3 percent cash reward for purchases using the Samsung Wallet app is a really good rate. If you tap-to-pay at New York City's subway turnstile with Samsung Wallet on your phone, for example, that's 3 percent on every commute, and “that would be compelling.”  
Rathner表示，通过Samsung Wallet应用消费获3%返现的比率非常不错。例如，如果你用手机上的Samsung Wallet在纽约地铁闸机感应支付，每次通勤都能获得3%返现，“这将会很有吸引力。”

---

While Rathner finds the Apple Card underwhelming, she does credit Apple with innovating on certain features. For example, Apple allows you to see the interest rate and credit rate you would qualify for before a credit pull, something other credit cards are increasingly offering. The app (and card) have a beautiful design, the physical card is easy to activate with just a tap on the iPhone, and cash rewards are posted daily rather than waiting for a billing cycle.  
尽管Rathner认为Apple Card表现平平，但她认可苹果在某些功能上的创新。例如，苹果允许用户在信用查询前查看可获得的利率和信用额度，这是其他信用卡 increasingly 提供的功能。该应用（和卡片）设计精美，实体卡只需在iPhone上轻触即可激活，且现金奖励每日入账而非等待账单周期。

---

“I think if other cards want to compete in that way and also follow suit with those features, I think that just makes credit cards in general better products for consumers," Rathner says. “We'll see how this resonates with people who have Samsung phones.”  
“我认为如果其他信用卡想以此竞争并跟进这些功能，那将使信用卡整体成为对消费者更好的产品，”Rathner说，“我们将拭目以待这对三星手机用户会有多大吸引力。”
