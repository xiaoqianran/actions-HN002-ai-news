**Title: Adobe camera app’s new feature will critique your photos using AI**  
**标题：Adobe相机应用新功能将用AI点评你的照片**

---

Adobe is adding new AI-powered features to its experimental iOS camera app called Project Indigo, launched last year. The app previously offered pro controls, multi-frame super-resolution, and different capture modes, and is now adding features that will use LLMs (large language models) to critique photos and provide editing suggestions.  
Adobe正在为其去年推出的实验性iOS相机应用“Project Indigo”添加新的AI驱动功能。该应用此前已提供专业级控制、多帧超分辨率及多种拍摄模式，如今新增的功能将利用大语言模型（LLMs）点评照片并提供编辑建议。

It’s also adding other AI features, like advanced object removal, depth of field generation, and the ability to add different styles to photos.  
同时，它还新增了其他AI功能，例如高级对象移除、景深生成以及为照片添加不同风格的能力。

Marc Levoy, the person heading Adobe’s project, had previously developed Pixel’s camera chops. He said that most generative AI tools provide prompt-based editing and, often, finding the perfect prompt to tweak a photo or get the right result can be a tricky endeavor.  
负责Adobe该项目的Marc Levoy曾参与开发Pixel手机的相机功能。他表示，大多数生成式AI工具依赖提示词编辑，而往往找到完美的提示词来微调照片或获得理想结果是一项艰巨的任务。

That’s why most of the new experimental AI features are buttons that can generate more deterministic outputs.  
因此，大多数新的实验性AI功能以按钮形式呈现，能产生更确定性的输出。

For instance, Google’s camera coach feature for Pixel phones, launched last year, offered more generic framing suggestions. Project Indigo’s features, by comparison, are fairly descriptive and could help you learn some photography tricks, even if you don’t agree with the AI assessment.  
例如，谷歌去年为Pixel手机推出的相机教练功能仅提供较通用的构图建议。相比之下，Project Indigo的功能描述相当具体，即使你不认同AI的评价，也可能帮助你学习一些摄影技巧。

There are two features in this category. First is the photo critique, which includes a “professional” opinion about framing, lighting, colors, and emotional impact.  
此类功能有两项。首先是照片点评，包含对构图、光线、色彩和情感冲击力的“专业”意见。

The second is capture and edit suggestions, which give you tips about reshooting the photo on how you can change framing, exposure, and objects in the viewfinder. For instance, the app told me to remove the hexagonal white object in the frame from a photo I took.  
第二项是拍摄与编辑建议，它会提示你如何通过调整构图、曝光和取景器中的物体来重拍照片。例如，该应用曾建议我移除我拍摄照片中画面内的六边形白色物体。

A second section tells you how you can use an existing photo to make it better using Adobe Lightroom controls.  
另一部分会告诉你如何利用现有照片，并通过Adobe Lightroom控件进行优化。

Photography apps, including Apple Photos, Google Photos, and Adobe Photoshop, have offered object removal for years. But the feature often depends on the user circling or selecting an object by drawing on the screen, which is not perfect every time.  
包括Apple Photos、Google Photos和Adobe Photoshop在内的摄影应用多年来一直提供对象移除功能。但该功能通常依赖用户在屏幕上圈选或绘制对象，操作并不总是完美。

Project Indigo’s new feature gives you toggles for things to remove from an image, like people in the background, trash and trash cans, wires and poles, fences, vehicles, and other clutter. You can also describe a custom object to remove.  
Project Indigo的新功能提供了可切换的移除选项，如背景中的人物、垃圾和垃圾桶、电线杆、栅栏、车辆及其他杂乱物体。你也可以描述自定义要移除的对象。

The results of this feature are pretty impressive. The app removed my friend and the object he was holding from the background without creating any strange artifacts.  
该功能的效果相当惊艳。应用移除了我朋友及其手中物体，背景未产生任何奇怪伪影。

The app also allows you to use AI to create depth of field for a photo to simulate a blurred background.  
该应用还允许使用AI为照片创建景深，以模拟模糊背景。

With this new update, Adobe is also experimenting with the style transfer feature, which lets you turn your picture into tones like watercolor, pen and ink, ink line with color wash, monochromatic, and backlit subject.  
通过此次更新，Adobe还在试验风格迁移功能，可将你的照片转换为水彩、钢笔与墨水、墨线渲染、单色或逆光主体等色调。

Some styles remind me of the early days of the Prisma app. With advanced models, the outputs look refined, but style transfer is not a new or useful feature.  
某些风格让我想起Prisma应用的早期版本。借助先进模型，输出效果看起来很精致，但风格迁移并非新颖或实用的功能。

Despite Levoy’s criticism of the prompt-based implementation, Project Indigo has its own feature that lets you describe your edit. Users can use it to perform edit flows that are not available in preset tools.  
尽管Levoy批评了基于提示词的实现方式，Project Indigo仍拥有自己的功能，允许你描述编辑需求。用户可用它执行预设工具中无法实现的编辑流程。

But this is also a potential road to slopland.  
但这也可能导致低质量内容的泛滥。

The company is using Google’s Gemini-based Nano Banana for these features, but it’s open to swapping in other models, including its own Adobe Firefly model.  
公司正使用基于Google Gemini的Nano Banana模型支持这些功能，但也愿意替换为其他模型，包括其自有的Adobe Firefly模型。

These features, bundled under the AI playground tab, are still in a testing phase, and only select users will get access to them.  
这些功能被归入“AI游乐场”标签下，目前仍处于测试阶段，仅部分用户可访问。

These features might not ever make it to a wider audience, but it is good to see some features that can make people aware of nuances in photography, rather than just creating more slop.  
这些功能可能永远不会面向更广泛的用户，但很高兴看到一些能帮助人们关注摄影细微差别的功能，而不仅仅是制造更多低质内容。
