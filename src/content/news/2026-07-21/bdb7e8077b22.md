# RIP bargain bin: The price impact of Sony's disc-free PlayStation plan  
# 告别折扣区：索尼无光驱PlayStation计划的价格影响

Since Sony announced that it was planning to stop producing physical PlayStation discs in 2028, many people have been justifiably worried about how they will maintain ownership and long-term game access in a disc-free world.  
自索尼宣布计划于2028年停止生产实体PlayStation光盘以来，许多人合理地担心，在一个没有光盘的世界里，他们将如何保持游戏的所有权和长期访问权限。

But halting production of PlayStation discs also means eliminating the market for cheap used PlayStation discs, which are often available for well below the price of a digital download.  
但停止生产PlayStation光盘也意味着廉价二手PlayStation光盘市场的消失，而这些光盘的价格通常远低于数字下载版。

How much is this disc-based discount worth? To find out, Ars analyzed how the prices of 19 current top-selling PlayStation games varied between digital downloads and physical discs (both new and used).  
这种基于光盘的折扣价值几何？为了找出答案，Ars分析了19款当前最畅销的PlayStation游戏在数字下载版与实体光盘（全新和二手）之间的价格差异。

We found that, while used discs are the cheapest option for most games for most of the year, the frequent and periodic deep discounts offered on the PlayStation Store often undercut the cheapest game disc options.  
我们发现，尽管在一年中的大部分时间里，二手光盘是大多数游戏最便宜的选择，但PlayStation商店频繁提供的深度折扣往往比最便宜的光盘选项还要便宜。

That state of affairs will likely persist even in a future without PlayStation discs.  
即使在没有PlayStation光盘的未来，这种情况很可能也会持续下去。

For our analysis, we started with Sony’s list of the bestsellers on the PlayStation Store.  
在我们的分析中，我们首先从索尼的PlayStation商店畅销榜开始。

We eliminated any games that weren’t available on a physical disc, then used the database over at PlatPrices.com to determine both the “Standard” price and the lowest “Discount” price offered for those games on the PlayStation Store in the last year (as well as the total number of days the discount price was offered).  
我们剔除了任何没有实体光盘版本的游戏，然后使用PlatPrices.com的数据库来确定这些游戏在过去一年中在PlayStation商店上的“标准”价格和最低“折扣”价格（以及提供折扣价格的总天数）。

For physical game prices, we checked the current price for a new game disc at Amazon as well as the pre-owned price offered by GameStop.  
对于实体游戏价格，我们查看了亚马逊上全新游戏光盘的当前价格以及GameStop提供的二手价格。

We also analyzed pre-owned disc prices on eBay, taking the average price of the last five successful sales for that game (including shipping) to assess a fair market value.  
我们还分析了eBay上的二手光盘价格，取该游戏最近五次成功销售的平均价格（含运费）来评估公平市场价值。

In some cases, we added in the price of included DLC to ensure a “standard” game disc was content-matched with a digital “special edition” bundle.  
在某些情况下，我们加入了包含DLC的价格，以确保“标准”游戏光盘的内容与数字“特别版”捆绑包相匹配。

For PS4 games with “enhanced” PS5 editions, we added the price of that downloadable upgrade (if any) to the price of the PS4 discs.  
对于拥有“增强版”PS5版本的PS4游戏，我们将该可下载升级（如有）的价格加到了PS4光盘的价格上。

If a PlayStation Store game isn’t actively on sale, you can usually save money buying a used disc.  
如果PlayStation商店的游戏没有 actively 促销，购买二手光盘通常可以省钱。

During Periodic PS Store sales, the download price is often (but not always) the lowest available.  
在PlayStation商店周期性促销期间，下载价格通常（但并非总是）是最低的。

A quick glance at the results shows that the “Standard” price for games offered on the PlayStation Store is usually an incredibly raw deal.  
快速浏览结果就会发现，PlayStation商店上游戏的“标准”价格通常极其不划算。

That digital price was the most expensive option available for 15 of the 19 games we looked at (including two where it was more or less tied for most expensive).  
在我们查看的19款游戏中，数字价格是15款游戏最贵的选项（包括两款价格大致并列最贵的游戏）。

In some cases, games that were six to eight years old (i.e., Call of Duty: Modern Warfare, Assassin’s Creed Valhalla, and Assassin’s Creed Odyssey) were still being offered digitally at their original launch price of $59.99, even while used discs were available at GameStop for just $9.99.  
在某些情况下，一些已有六到八年历史的游戏（如《使命召唤：现代战争》、《刺客信条：英灵殿》和《刺客信条：奥德赛》）仍然以59.99美元的原始发售价提供数字版，而GameStop的二手光盘仅售9.99美元。

If you’re buying on the PlayStation Store, it almost always makes sense to wait for a sale, where the “Standard” digital price is reduced by 40 to 80 percent in our sample.  
如果你在PlayStation商店购买，几乎总是值得等待促销，在我们样本中，促销时“标准”数字价格会降低40%到80%。

During these periodic sale periods, downloading the game became the cheapest available option in a slim majority of the best sellers we looked at (10 out of 19).  
在这些周期性促销期间，在我们查看的畅销游戏中，下载游戏成为最便宜选项的占微弱多数（19款中的10款）。

In many cases, the deep digital discounts meant a download could be had for 50 percent or less than even the cheapest used disc prices.  
在许多情况下，深度数字折扣意味着下载价格甚至比最便宜的二手光盘价格还要低50%或更多。

But waiting for a PlayStation Store sale isn’t always a great deal.  
但等待PlayStation商店促销并不总是划算。

Even with those deep discounts taken into account, a used disc was still the cheapest option for seven of the 19 games we looked at (for two more, the cheapest disc was roughly equivalent to the discounted digital sale price).  
即使考虑到这些深度折扣，在我们查看的19款游戏中，二手光盘仍然是7款游戏最便宜的选择（另外2款最便宜的光盘价格与折扣后的数字促销价大致相同）。

For a game like Dark Souls III, for instance, you could still save $10 by buying a used GameStop disc instead of the $30 “sale” price on the PlayStation Store.  
例如，对于《黑暗之魂III》这样的游戏，购买GameStop的二手光盘仍然可以比PlayStation商店30美元的“促销”价节省10美元。

Most of the year, you’ll have to wait at least a little bit before getting the benefit of a PlayStation Store sale.  
在一年中的大部分时间里，你必须至少等待一段时间才能享受到PlayStation商店促销的优惠。

While a cheap disc can be purchased pretty much whenever you want, getting a deal on the PlayStation Store means being vigilant enough to monitor the few days a year where the best price is being offered.  
虽然廉价光盘几乎随时可以购买，但在PlayStation商店获得优惠意味着需要足够警惕，以监控一年中提供最佳价格的短短几天。

For the bestsellers we looked at, those discount prices on downloads were only offered between 13 and 33 percent of the time.  
对于我们查看的畅销游戏，这些下载折扣价格仅在13%到33%的时间内提供。

In some cases, an eager digital buyer would have to wait multiple months for a weekslong sale period to pop up if they wanted to take advantage of the generous digital discounts.  
在某些情况下，一个热切的数字买家如果想利用慷慨的数字折扣，可能需要等待数月才能迎来一次持续数周的促销期。

Remember, too, that buying a game on disc means you can resell it again when you’re done, getting back a percentage of the purchase price in the process.  
同样记住，购买光盘游戏意味着你玩完后可以再次转售，在此过程中收回一部分购买价格。

This kind of de facto discount won’t be an option in PlayStation’s disc-less future.  
这种事实上的折扣在PlayStation无光驱的未来将不再是一种选择。

The pricing structure for PlayStation games in a disc-free world won’t necessarily mirror the current pricing on the PlayStation Store.  
在无光驱世界中，PlayStation游戏的定价结构不一定反映PlayStation商店当前的定价。

Right now, cheap disc games exist as a sort of release valve for pricing pressure, setting a competitive price that digital sale pricing has to at least take into account as an alternative.  
目前，廉价光盘游戏作为一种价格压力的“泄压阀”存在，设定了一个数字促销定价至少必须考虑的竞争性价格。

If used discs are simply not available for future PlayStation games, PlayStation owners won’t have any other option to buy a game outside the PlayStation Store.  
如果未来的PlayStation游戏根本没有二手光盘可用，PlayStation玩家将没有任何其他选择，只能在PlayStation商店之外购买游戏。

That lack of alternatives might mean publishers have less impetus to offer the kind of deep discounts for downloadable versions that are currently common.  
这种缺乏替代方案的情况可能意味着发行商提供目前常见的数字版本深度折扣的动力会减少。
