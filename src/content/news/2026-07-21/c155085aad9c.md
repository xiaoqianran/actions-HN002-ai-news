# Training-Free Open-Vocabulary 3D Point-Cloud Segmentation on the Generalized Few-Shot Benchmark  
# 无需训练的开词汇3D点云分割：基于广义小样本基准测试

**Authors:** Silas kwabla Gah, Ebenezer Owusu  
**作者：** Silas kwabla Gah, Ebenezer Owusu

---

**Abstract:**  
Generalized few-shot 3D point-cloud segmentation (GFS-PCS) asks a model to segment a scene into many base classes seen at training time and a set of novel classes.  
广义小样本3D点云分割（GFS-PCS）要求模型将场景分割为训练时已见的基础类别和一组新类别。

The state of the art reaches novel classes by reconciling a dense but noisy 3D vision-language prior with the few-shot support, but it pays for this with base 3D labels, per-episode training, and the support annotations themselves.  
当前最先进方法通过将密集但有噪声的3D视觉-语言先验与小样本支持集进行调和来实现新类别的分割，但代价是需要基础3D标签、每轮训练以及支持集标注本身。

We ask how far the same reconciliation can go with none of these: no training, no 3D labels, and not even the few-shot support.  
我们探究在没有任何这些条件的情况下——无需训练、无需3D标签、甚至无需小样本支持集——这种调和能走多远。

We pair a frozen 3D vision-language model (RegionPLC) as a dense prior with a frozen promptable concept segmenter (SAM3), prompted by the bare novel class names and lifted from posed RGB views, and reconcile the two by cross-view consistency: a point becomes novel only when enough of the views that see it agree.  
我们将一个冻结的3D视觉-语言模型（RegionPLC）作为密集先验，与一个冻结的可提示概念分割器（SAM3）配对，后者仅通过新类别的名称提示，并从位姿RGB视图中提升；通过跨视图一致性调和两者：一个点仅在被足够多观测到它的视图一致认为时才被判定为新类别。

On the ScanNet200 GFS-PCS benchmark this fully training-free, open-vocabulary pipeline improves novel mIoU by +2.6 over the training-free dense prior while holding base accuracy within 0.5, and recovers a third (33%) of the novel-class gap to the trained state of the art that uses far more supervision.  
在ScanNet200 GFS-PCS基准测试中，这种完全无需训练、开词汇的流程相比无需训练的密集先验将新类别mIoU提升了+2.6，同时基础类别精度保持在0.5以内，并弥补了与使用大量监督数据的训练后先进方法之间新类别差距的三分之一（33%）。

We further show that injecting the few-shot support into the pipeline, as a fusion gate and as a prototypical dense classifier, adds nothing over consistency alone and in fact degrades it through the classifier, which is why the method needs no support at all.  
我们进一步表明，将小样本支持集注入流程（作为融合门和原型密集分类器）并未带来比单纯一致性更好的效果，反而通过分类器降低了性能，这就是该方法完全不需要支持集的原因。

On the harder ScanNet++ benchmark, where the dense prior is far weaker on novel classes, the same pipeline nearly doubles novel mIoU (+15.7, from 16.2 to 31.9) at a 1.7 base cost, lifting the harmonic mean from 21.5 to 31.1.  
在更难的ScanNet++基准测试中，密集先验在新类别上表现远弱，但同一流程几乎将新类别mIoU翻倍（+15.7，从16.2到31.9），基础类别代价仅1.7，将调和均值从21.5提升至31.1。
