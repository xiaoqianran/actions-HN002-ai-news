# Hackers are exploiting recently patched WordPress bugs, putting millions of websites at risk  
黑客正在利用最近修补的 WordPress 漏洞，使数百万网站面临风险

---

Hackers are breaking into websites that run vulnerable versions of the popular blogging software WordPress, according to several cybersecurity firms.  
多家网络安全公司表示，黑客正在入侵运行这款流行博客软件 WordPress 易受攻击版本的网站。

One estimate puts the number of vulnerable WordPress websites at tens of millions as of Monday.  
截至周一，一项估计显示易受攻击的 WordPress 网站数量达数千万。

Last week, WordPress patched two critical security flaws, urging people who run its software on their websites to update it “immediately.”  
上周，WordPress 修补了两个关键安全漏洞，敦促在其网站上运行该软件的用户“立即”更新。

The vulnerabilities are so severe that WordPress enabled forced updates where possible.  
这些漏洞非常严重，WordPress 已在可能的情况下启用了强制更新。

Since then, cybersecurity companies Patchstack, Hexastrike, and WatchTowr have all warned that hackers are exploiting the vulnerabilities in the wild, meaning they are taking over websites that are still running susceptible versions of WordPress.  
此后，网络安全公司 Patchstack、Hexastrike 和 WatchTowr 均警告称，黑客正在野外利用这些漏洞，这意味着他们正在接管仍在运行易受攻击版本 WordPress 的网站。

It’s unclear how many WordPress-powered websites on the internet are at risk, but it’s possible to make some educated guesses.  
目前尚不清楚互联网上有多少由 WordPress 驱动的网站面临风险，但可以进行一些有根据的推测。

The vulnerable versions of WordPress are 6.9.0 through 6.9.4, and 7.0.0 to 7.0.1.  
WordPress 的易受攻击版本是 6.9.0 至 6.9.4，以及 7.0.0 至 7.0.1。

According to WordPress’ official stats, there are more than 400 million websites that run those flawed versions, although these statistics likely don’t reflect websites that have recently been patched.  
根据 WordPress 的官方统计数据，有超过 4 亿个网站运行这些有缺陷的版本，尽管这些统计数据可能未反映最近已修补的网站。

Cybersecurity consultant Daniel Card, who told TechCrunch that he looked at a sample of around 3,500 WordPress websites, estimates that less than 15% are vulnerable.  
网络安全顾问 Daniel Card 告诉 TechCrunch，他检查了大约 3,500 个 WordPress 网站的样本，估计其中不到 15% 易受攻击。

Applying Card’s projection across the total population of WordPress websites on the internet, the total figure would still be around 90 million.  
将 Card 的预测应用于互联网上 WordPress 网站的总数，总数仍将约为 9000 万。

The researcher credited WordPress with pushing automatic updates, Cloudflare with blocking attacks against vulnerable websites, and websites using cybersecurity protections such as web firewalls for the limited number of sites that could currently be hacked.  
该研究人员将目前可能被黑客攻击的网站数量有限归功于 WordPress 推动的自动更新、Cloudflare 对针对脆弱网站攻击的拦截，以及使用网络防火墙等网络安全防护措施的网站。

WordPress.org, the project that develops WordPress’ open source code, did not immediately respond to a request for comment.  
开发 WordPress 开源代码的项目 WordPress.org 未立即回复置评请求。

Megan Fox, a spokesperson for Automattic, the company that runs WordPress.com and contributes to the open source project, told TechCrunch that “all sites hosted by Automattic, including WordPress.com, Pressable, WPVIP, and WP.cloud partners, were protected even before the release. When the code updates were published, we deployed them immediately across millions of sites.”  
运营 WordPress.com 并为开源项目做出贡献的公司 Automattic 的发言人 Megan Fox 告诉 TechCrunch：“所有由 Automattic 托管的网站，包括 WordPress.com、Pressable、WPVIP 和 WP.cloud 合作伙伴，在发布前就已受到保护。当代码更新发布时，我们立即在数百万个网站上部署了它们。”

One of the critical WordPress bugs was found and reported by Adam Kues of cybersecurity firm Searchlight Cyber, which dubbed it WP2Shell.  
其中一个关键的 WordPress 漏洞由网络安全公司 Searchlight Cyber 的 Adam Kues 发现并报告，该公司将其命名为 WP2Shell。

Paired with the other bug, hackers can take full remote control of vulnerable websites.  
与另一个漏洞结合，黑客可以完全远程控制易受攻击的网站。
