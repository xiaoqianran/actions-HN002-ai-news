# AT&T loses key ruling in bid to stop offering basic phone service in California  
# AT&T在试图停止在加州提供基本电话服务的关键裁决中败诉

---

California can keep enforcing rules that require AT&T to offer basic phone service to new customers in its wireline territory, following a federal judge’s ruling last week.  
加州可以继续执行要求AT&T在其固话服务区域内向新客户提供基本电话服务的法规，此前联邦法官在上周做出了裁决。

AT&T sued California in May in a bid to end the state’s Carrier of Last Resort (COLR) rules that require it to offer telephone service to any potential customer in its territory.  
AT&T于5月起诉加州，试图终止该州“最终承运人”（COLR）法规，该法规要求其在服务区域内向任何潜在客户提供电话服务。

AT&T asked for a preliminary injunction that would prevent California from enforcing the COLR rules while the litigation continues.  
AT&T请求初步禁令，以在诉讼期间阻止加州执行COLR法规。

To win a preliminary injunction, AT&T had to show it is likely to succeed on the merits of its claim that California rules are preempted by a Federal Communications Commission order.  
为获得初步禁令，AT&T必须证明其很可能在实质问题上胜诉，即加州法规被联邦通信委员会（FCC）的命令所取代。

US District Judge Linda Lopez denied AT&T’s request for a preliminary injunction during a motion hearing on Thursday, according to a docket entry.  
根据法庭记录，美国地区法官琳达·洛佩兹在周四的动议听证会上驳回了AT&T的初步禁令请求。

The case is in US District Court for the Southern District of California.  
该案位于美国加州南区联邦地区法院。

AT&T wants to stop offering basic phone service to new customers and discontinue service for 184,000 residential customers and 15,000 business customers on June 1, 2027.  
AT&T希望停止向新客户提供基本电话服务，并计划于2027年6月1日终止为184,000名住宅客户和15,000名企业客户提供的服务。

AT&T said the injunction it requested would let it stop offering service to new customers immediately, and that it would preserve service for existing customers while litigation continues.  
AT&T表示，其请求的禁令将使其能立即停止向新客户提供服务，并在诉讼期间维持对现有客户的服务。

AT&T could appeal Lopez’s ruling to the 9th Circuit Court of Appeals and could appeal later if it loses the underlying case.  
AT&T可向第九巡回上诉法院提起上诉，若在实质案件中败诉，后续仍可上诉。

But since it has not obtained the injunction it asked for, AT&T for now remains under California’s orders to keep offering phone service to potential customers while the case continues.  
但由于未获得所请求的禁令，AT&T目前仍须遵守加州命令，在案件持续期间继续向潜在客户提供电话服务。

California suggests AT&T upgrade old lines to fiber  
加州建议AT&T将旧线路升级为光纤

AT&T has said it has received relief from COLR obligations in 20 of the 21 states in its wireline service territory, all except California.  
AT&T表示，其固话服务区域内的21个州中，已有20个州（除加州外）免除了其COLR义务。

AT&T alleges that “California requires AT&T to spend $1 billion each year to maintain a century-old telephone network that almost no one uses.”  
AT&T指控称，“加州要求AT&T每年花费10亿美元来维护一个几乎无人使用的百年电话网络。”

AT&T wants to shut off the copper lines used for its Plain Old Telephone Service (POTS), but is not promising to replace those copper lines with fiber.  
AT&T希望关闭用于其传统电话服务（POTS）的铜线，但并未承诺用光纤替换这些铜线。

In any area where AT&T determines it is not profitable enough to install modern phone lines, customers would have to rely on the cellular network for home phone service.  
在AT&T判定安装现代电话线路无利可图的任何地区，客户将不得不依赖蜂窝网络获得家庭电话服务。

California insists that AT&T must keep offering basic phone service over either the old copper wires or a modern equivalent, such as fiber lines.  
加州坚持认为，AT&T必须通过旧铜线或光纤等现代等效技术继续提供基本电话服务。

Opposing the AT&T motion for an injunction, California Attorney General Rob Bonta and the California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC) said the FCC order allowing carriers to stop offering copper-wire service does not conflict with California rules.  
在反对AT&T的禁令动议时，加州总检察长罗布·邦塔和加州公共事业委员会（CPUC）表示，FCC允许运营商停止提供铜线服务的命令与加州法规并不冲突。

“The FCC granted permission for carriers to grandfather services ‘provisioned over copper wire.’ Contrary to AT&T’s representations in the Motion, the COLR rules do not ‘conflict’ with the NMO [the FCC’s Network Modernization Order], because they do not require AT&T to continue offering copper-line POTS to new customers,” California said.  
加州方面称：“FCC已批准运营商‘ grandfathering（ grandfathering指在规则变更后允许原有服务继续存在）’通过铜线提供的服务。与AT&T在动议中的陈述相反，COLR法规与FCC的网络现代化命令（NMO）并不‘冲突’，因为它们不要求AT&T继续向新客户提供铜线POTS。”

Calling the lawsuit a “facade,” state officials said AT&T can offer basic phone service over fiber: All the COLR rules require is that AT&T offer basic service to its customers; in fact, AT&T already offers basic service through fiber connections instead of copper connections in some locations…  
州官员称此诉讼为“幌子”，并表示AT&T可通过光纤提供基本电话服务：所有COLR法规的要求只是AT&T向客户提供基本服务；事实上，AT&T已在某些地区通过光纤连接而非铜线连接提供基本服务……

AT&T is using its “analog versus digital” narrative as more palatable window dressing for the relief that it actually wants, namely, a release from its obligations as a COLR altogether.  
AT&T正利用其“模拟与数字”的叙事作为更易接受的包装，以掩盖其实际寻求的救济——即完全免除其作为COLR的义务。

This attempt to manufacture a conflict between federal and state law where there isn’t one cannot succeed. The Court need not go any further.  
这种在联邦与州法律之间制造并不存在的冲突的尝试不会成功。法院无需进一步审理。

---

AT&T claims “irreparable harm” from California rules  
AT&T声称加州法规造成“不可挽回的损害”

AT&T argued that the legal requirements for a preliminary injunction “are easily satisfied.”  
AT&T辩称，初步禁令的法律要求“很容易满足”。

It said “the Federal Communications Act preempts any California laws or regulations that prevent AT&T from effectuating the grandfathering of POTS that the FCC has already authorized.”  
它表示，“《联邦通信法》优先于任何阻止AT&T实施FCC已授权的POTS grandfathering的加州法律或法规。”

Without a preliminary injunction, “AT&T will suffer irreparable harm to both its business interests and its constitutional rights,” the carrier’s motion said.  
该运营商的动议称，若无初步禁令，“AT&T将对其商业利益和宪法权利遭受不可挽回的损害”。

AT&T also said that “the CPUC has made clear that it will not permit AT&T to substitute a feasible, superior alternative like mobile wireless or AT&T Phone-Advanced, a service that relies on AT&T’s mobile wireless network but allows customers to use existing, analog phones.”  
AT&T还表示，“CPUC已明确表示，不会允许AT&T以移动无线或AT&T Phone-Advanced（一种依赖AT&T移动无线网络但允许客户使用现有模拟电话的服务）等可行、更优的替代方案进行替换”。

California said its rules are “technology-neutral,” allowing AT&T to use wired, wireless, and/or VoIP service to fulfill its COLR obligations.  
加州表示其法规是“技术中立”的，允许AT&T使用有线、无线和/或VoIP服务来履行其COLR义务。

While AT&T says its wireless AT&T Phone-Advanced (AP-A) is a suitable replacement for copper, California said that “AT&T has never formally sought to substitute POTS with AP-A.”  
尽管AT&T表示其无线AT&T Phone-Advanced（AP-A）是铜线的合适替代品，但加州表示“AT&T从未正式寻求以AP-A替代POTS”。

AT&T “does not want to meet its COLR obligations through AP-A; it seeks relief from meeting them at all,” California said.  
加州表示：“AT&T并非想通过AP-A履行其COLR义务；它寻求的是完全免除这些义务。”

“That is why AT&T does not claim in this lawsuit, either, that it will offer basic service through AP-A (or any other service) instead.”  
“这就是为什么AT&T在此诉讼中也没有声称将通过AP-A（或任何其他服务）提供基本服务。”

AT&T doesn’t promise that AP-A will meet state requirements for basic service, California said.  
加州表示，AT&T并未承诺AP-A将满足州对基本服务的要求。

Those state requirements include flat-rate options for unlimited voice calls, discounted Lifeline rates for eligible low-income customers, and telephone relay service for the deaf and hard of hearing, California said.  
加州表示，这些州要求包括不限时通话的包月选项、为符合条件的低收入客户提供折扣的Lifeline费率，以及为聋人和听力障碍者提供的电话中继服务。

---

AT&T seeking more help from FCC  
AT&T寻求FCC更多帮助

The FCC grandfathering order that AT&T cited in its lawsuit applied industry-wide, not specifically to AT&T.  
AT&T在诉讼中引用的FCC grandfathering命令适用于整个行业，并非专门针对AT&T。

But AT&T also asked the FCC for an order specifically preempting California’s COLR mandates and related requirements, such as tariffing and Lifeline participation rules.  
但AT&T还请求FCC发布一项专门优先于加州COLR mandates及相关要求（如资费制定和Lifeline参与规则）的命令。

California told the FCC that AT&T is lying when it claims that state rules prevent it from replacing copper with fiber.  
加州告知FCC，当AT&T声称州法规阻止其用光纤替换铜线时，AT&T在说谎。

The Utility Reform Network, an advocacy group in California, told the FCC that AT&T’s wireless home phone service is “an ill-suited replacement for existing legacy infrastructure.”  
加州倡导组织“公用事业改革网络”告知FCC，AT&T的无线家庭电话服务“不适合替代现有的遗留基础设施”。
