# Naked mole-rat queens use a chemical signal to suppress fertility in rivals  
# 裸鼹鼠蚁后利用化学信号抑制竞争对手的生育能力  

Naked mole rats are weird. They spend their lives almost entirely underground, are largely insensitive to some kinds of pain, can persist without oxygen for a long time, rarely develop cancer, and can live past 30 years, which is decades longer than similarly sized rodents. They are also eusocial, like ants, bees, or termites—in a colony that can hold more than a hundred animals, only one female, the queen, breeds.  
裸鼹鼠非常奇特。它们几乎一生都在地下度过，对某些疼痛不敏感，能长时间缺氧生存，极少患癌症，且寿命可超过30年——比同等体型的啮齿动物长数十年。它们也是真社会性动物，像蚂蚁、蜜蜂或白蚁一样——在一个可容纳上百只动物的群体中，只有一只雌性蚁后负责繁殖。  

We didn’t know how exactly naked mole-rat queens stop other females from breeding. For a long time, our leading hypothesis was bullying and violence, but that may be a bit impractical, given their kingdoms are vast networks of tunnels that can stretch for up to three kilometers.  
我们一直不清楚裸鼹鼠蚁后究竟如何阻止其他雌性繁殖。长期以来，主流假说是欺凌和暴力，但这可能不太实际，因为它们的“王国”是绵延可达三公里的庞大隧道网络。  

But now a team of Lewin Lab scientists at Max Delbrück Center for Molecular Medicine in Berlin discovered it’s actually just one part of a sophisticated olfactory signaling program.  
但现在，柏林马克斯·德尔布吕克分子医学中心刘易斯实验室的一组科学家发现，这其实只是一个复杂嗅觉信号程序的一部分。  

Running on smell  
靠嗅觉行动  

“It’s very clear that mole rats actually have quite big noses and smell a lot,” says Gary Lewin, a neurobiologist at the Max Delbrück Center for Molecular Medicine and senior author of the study. Naked mole rats have around 1200 olfactory receptor genes, more than mice (which have roughly 1,000 of them) and way more than the few hundred in humans. They’re also blind. “They have to be able to navigate and move around without vision, so smell is one of those things that’s enhanced,” Lewin said.  
“很明显，鼹鼠实际上有相当大的鼻子，而且非常依赖嗅觉，”该研究的资深作者、马克斯·德尔布吕克中心神经生物学家加里·刘易斯说。裸鼹鼠约有1200个嗅觉受体基因，比小鼠（约1000个）多，远超人类的几百个。它们还失明了。“它们必须在没有视觉的情况下导航和移动，因此嗅觉是得到增强的感官之一，”刘易斯说。  

To find out how heavily mole rats rely on smell, the team collected odor bouquets from individual animals. Scientists held an absorbent plastic tube near a mole rat for about half an hour to pick up the volatile molecules coming off its body. Then, they analyzed these scents in a mass spectrometer, which separated the components of the scent into around 100 distinct chemical compounds.  
为了探究鼹鼠对嗅觉的依赖程度，研究团队收集了单个动物的“气味 bouquet”。科学家将一根吸水性塑料管靠近鼹鼠约半小时，以采集其身体挥发出的分子。随后，他们用质谱仪分析这些气味，将其分离成约100种不同的化合物。  

Based on analyzing samples taken from hundreds of different mole rats, the team learned members of each colony carried a distinguishable chemical signature. When mole rats from different colonies were placed together, they sniffed each other for about 40 seconds to decide if they were dealing with their nestmate or a stranger. When they sniffed a stranger, they攻击， often with lethal intent.  
通过分析数百只不同鼹鼠的样本，团队发现每个群体的成员都带有可区分的化学特征。当不同群体的裸鼹鼠被放在一起时，它们会互相嗅闻约40秒，以判断对方是巢穴同伴还是陌生者。一旦嗅出陌生者，它们便会攻击， often with lethal intent.  

“Naked mole rats are like humans in the sense that they’re very xenophobic,” Lewin said. So, the team tested if depriving mole-rats of smell would purge the xenophobia out of them. It did. When scientists temporarily disabled mole-rats’ olfactory sensory neurons with chemicals, the aggression towards strangers disappeared. “They basically were cool with each other, and there was no fighting,” Lewin said.  
“裸鼹鼠像人类一样，具有强烈的排外性，”刘易斯说。因此，团队测试了剥夺鼹鼠嗅觉是否能消除其排外性。结果确实如此。当科学家用化学物质暂时 disable 鼹鼠的嗅觉感觉神经元后，对陌生者的攻击性消失了。“它们 basically 彼此友好，不再打斗，”刘易斯说。  

Besides chemical friend-or-foe signals, researchers found something else in the odor bouquets. There was one compound, an ester called isopropyl myristate, that scientists found in the samples taken from queens and nowhere else.  
除了化学“敌友”信号外，研究人员还在气味样本中发现了其他东西：一种名为肉豆蔻酸异丙酯的酯类化合物，仅出现在蚁后样本中，其他地方均未发现。  

“Breeding females had this particular molecule that non-breeding animals did not have,” Lewin said. The royal scent  
“繁殖雌性拥有这种非繁殖动物所没有的特定分子，”刘易斯说。  

Scientists found breeding queens secreted isopropyl myristate from their genitals and smeared it through the tunnels as they roamed their underground kingdoms. Tracking a queen’s output of the compound over her reproductive cycle showed that it rises and falls with her fertility, peaking when she’s in heat or pregnant.  
科学家发现，繁殖蚁后从其生殖器分泌肉豆蔻酸异丙酯，并在其地下王国中巡游时将其涂抹在隧道内。追踪蚁后在整个繁殖周期中该化合物的分泌量显示，其分泌量随生育能力波动，在发情或怀孕期间达到峰值。  

“It’s actually a very good marker of whether the queen is capable of breeding,” Lewin said. On top of that, it has another useful feature. Isopropyl myristate, while volatile, is rather stable once deposited on a surface. Researchers could still detect it in mole-rat cages roughly 24 hours after it’d been applied.  
“这实际上是判断蚁后是否具备繁殖能力的极佳标志，”刘易斯说。此外，它还有另一个 useful 特性：肉豆蔻酸异丙酯虽易挥发，但一旦沉积在表面就相当稳定。研究人员在涂抹约24小时后仍能在鼹鼠笼中检测到它。  

Because a colony’s burrow system can run up to three kilometers, a queen has a lot of ground to cover if she wants every subordinate exposed to her scent. “It sticks around for a while, so she has less work to spread the odor around the underground colony,” Lewin said.  
由于群体的洞穴系统可绵延三公里，若蚁后想让所有 subordinate 都接触到她的气味，需要覆盖很大区域。“它停留一段时间，所以她在地下群体中扩散气味的工作量就减少了，”刘易斯说。  

Pinpointing the queen’s molecule was one thing, though. Showing what it does was another.  
然而， pinpoint 蚁后的分子是一回事，证明其作用又是另一回事。  

Lowborns and upstarts  
底层与挑战者  

Naked mole-rat colonies are organized in a hierarchy loosely correlated with body size, starting with bottom-ranked workers and foragers, up through soldiers, to the highest-ranked animals and the breeding male called the pasha.  
裸鼹鼠群体按与体型 loosely correlated 的等级组织，从最低级的工鼠和觅食鼠，到兵鼠，再到最高级动物及繁殖雄鼠（称为“帕夏”）。  

Scientists sort naked mole rats into ranks using a dominance test: When two animals meet in a tunnel, whichever one yields is the lower-ranked of the pair.  
科学家通过优势测试为裸鼹鼠排序：当两只动物在隧道相遇时，退让的一方等级较低。  

When Lewin’s team tested how mole rats react to isopropyl myristate, the reaction seemed to be dependent on rank. When placed in a T-maze with the queen’s molecule placed on one side, higher-ranked females, the ones with a realistic shot at becoming the queen one day, avoided it.  
当刘易斯团队测试鼹鼠对肉豆蔻酸异丙酯的反应时，发现反应似乎取决于等级。当将较高等级雌性（未来有 realistic 机会成为蚁后者）放入T型迷宫，且蚁后分子置于一侧时，它们会避开该侧。  

“We’ve got an idea, although we didn’t prove it, that in a normal environment, higher-ranked animals try to avoid the queen, because the queen might be aggressive towards them,” Lewin said.  
“我们有一个想法，尽管未证明：在正常环境中，较高等级动物会试图避开蚁后，因为蚁后可能对它们有攻击性，”刘易斯说。  

Direct evidence on the mole-rat brain response to isopropyl myristate came from functional ultrasound imaging, a technique that tracks blood flow non-invasively in an anesthetized animal. “If you just puff isopropyl myristate on the nose of the animal, a large part of the olfactory cortex lights up,” Lewin said. The team interpreted this as a proof the compound is registering as a distinct signal, not just background smell.  
关于裸鼹鼠大脑对肉豆蔻酸异丙酯反应的直接证据来自功能性超声成像——一种无创追踪麻醉动物血流的技术。“如果你只是将肉豆蔻酸异丙酯吹到动物鼻子上，大部分嗅皮层会亮起，”刘易斯说。团队将此解读为该化合物被识别为 distinct 信号，而不仅仅是背景气味的证明。  

What’s more, the exposure to the queen’s scent triggered hormonal shifts in females. Non-breeding mole-rat females are known to run high on prolactin, a hormone that also suppresses fertility during lactation in nursing mammals. Lewin’s team found that prolactin levels drop when a queen is removed from a colony and climb back up once isopropyl myristate is reintroduced.  
此外，暴露于蚁后气味会触发雌性的激素变化。已知非繁殖裸鼹鼠雌性催乳素水平较高，该激素在哺乳动物哺乳期也会抑制生育。刘易斯团队发现，当蚁后从群体中移除时催乳素水平下降，而重新引入肉豆蔻酸异丙酯后又会回升。  

Progesterone, a hormone associated with active breeding, moved in the opposite direction: low in non-breeders, and rising once the queen was gone.  
与 active breeding 相关的孕酮则呈相反变化：在非繁殖者中水平低，蚁后离开后上升。  

“These hormones seem to be directly controlled by the presence of the molecule [isopropyl myristate],” Lewin said.  
“这些激素似乎直接受该分子[肉豆蔻酸异丙酯]存在的影响，”刘易斯说。
