**Title:** Peak Design’s modular Field Bracket has a finder tag built-in  
Peak Design 模块化 Field Bracket 内置查找标签

**Subtitle:** Part L-bracket, part grip, part tracker.  
兼具 L 型支架、手柄和追踪器功能。

**by David Imel Jul 21, 2026, 6:12 PM UTC**  
作者 David Imel 2026年7月21日 UTC 下午6:12

I am a very clumsy man. So clumsy, that I have AirTags hanging off practically every camera I own. Have I left my camera in an Uber? Yes. Have I left it on an airplane? Also yes. For me, finder tags are essential for my cameras.  
我是个非常笨手笨脚的人。笨到几乎每台相机上都挂着 AirTag。我曾在 Uber 上落下相机吗？是的。落在飞机上过吗？也发生过。对我来说，查找标签对于我的相机来说是必不可少的。

The problem is, strapping a finder tag to a camera is always cumbersome. I currently have them hanging off my camera straps like a phone charm circa 2001. They’re ugly, and very much get in the way when I’m trying to focus on shooting.  
问题是，将查找标签绑在相机上总是很麻烦。目前我的相机背带上挂着它们，就像 2001 年的手机吊饰一样。它们很丑，而且在我尝试专注拍摄时非常碍事。

But Peak Design thinks it has a solution in its new Field Bracket system, which it launched today on Kickstarter, starting at $150 for early birds.  
但 Peak Design 认为其新的 Field Bracket 系统提供了解决方案，该系统今天在 Kickstarter 上发布，早鸟价为 150 美元。

The Peak Design Field Bracket is part L-Bracket, part hand grip, part cheese plate. It’s made for photographers and videographers who want to quickly switch between landscape and portrait orientation on their tripod, or sometimes want to mount a light or mic to their camera when there isn’t a dedicated place to do so. But possibly the most enticing thing, to me at least, is that the bracket comes with a slot for a dedicated Finder Tag.  
Peak Design Field Bracket 兼具 L 型支架、手握手柄和配件板功能。它专为摄影师和摄像师设计，他们希望在三脚架上快速切换横拍和竖拍，或在相机没有专用安装位置时外接灯光或麦克风。但对我而言，最吸引人的可能是支架上专门为查找标签预留的插槽。

Peak’s Beacon Finder Tag is a Peak Design-branded finder that works with Apple Find My and Google Find Hub. That means it’ll work with both Apple and Android phones, so you don’t have to worry about losing access to your camera finder if you switch ecosystems. The tag uses a standard CR2025 battery, and Peak says it’s rated to last up to 6 months. It’s also rated for IP67 water and dust resistance, and it fits flush inside the base plate of the L-bracket.  
Peak 的 Beacon Finder Tag 是一款 Peak Design 品牌的查找器，兼容 Apple Find My 和 Google Find Hub。这意味着它同时适用于苹果和安卓手机，因此即使切换生态系统，也不必担心无法使用相机的查找功能。该标签使用标准 CR2025 电池，Peak 表示其续航时间长达 6 个月。它还具备 IP67 级防水防尘能力，并能平整地嵌入 L 型支架的底板中。

The Beacon Finder Tag is just one of the accessories Peak Design is selling alongside the Field Bracket. The base product you’ll be buying is in fact an L-bracket, but once that’s on your camera, you’ll have access to other add-on accessories like a hand grip, finger grip, and cheese plate. Peak says the Field Bracket will work with most ARCA-compatible tripods, and won’t get in the way if you use it with its own capture clip or camera straps. In fact, the bracket itself has dedicated slots to attach your Peak Design camera strap, so you won’t have to sacrifice the strap you own to use the bracket.  
Beacon Finder Tag 只是 Peak Design 与 Field Bracket 一同销售的配件之一。你购买的基础产品实际上是一个 L 型支架，但一旦安装到相机上，你就可以添加其他配件，如手握手柄、指握手柄和配件板。Peak 表示 Field Bracket 适用于大多数兼容 ARCA 标准的三脚架，并且在使用其自家的快拆夹或相机背带时不会造成妨碍。事实上，支架本身设有专用插槽来连接你的 Peak Design 相机背带，因此无需为了使用支架而放弃现有的背带。

Peak calls the vertical attachment to the L-bracket the Field Latch, and it uses the company’s new locking system to quickly attach and detach it without any tools. This is the same place you attach the optional cheese plate, which pulls out in adjustable stops in case you need to access ports or run cables from the camera.  
Peak 将 L 型支架的竖装附件称为 Field Latch，它采用公司新的锁定系统，无需工具即可快速安装和拆卸。可选配件板也安装在同一位置，该板可抽出并设有可调节限位器，以便在需要时访问相机端口或走线。

Of course, you’ll have to choose the exact bracket you want for the camera you own, considering it’s molded specifically to that body. At launch, most cameras supported are the newest, or near-newest models of each camera line. You can see a compatibility list here. And if you want to see your camera supported, Peak has a dedicated feedback page to submit the camera you own.  
当然，你必须根据自己相机的型号选择对应的支架，因为它是为特定机身定制的。首发时，支持的大多是各相机系列中最新的或接近最新的型号。你可以在[此处]查看兼容性列表。如果你想让你手中的相机获得支持，Peak 设有专门的反馈页面供你提交相机型号。

The bracket itself will MSRP at $149.95, and if you want a bundle of all the accessories currently offered, you can get everything for $269.80. As usual, though, Peak Design is offering a Kickstarter discount, which puts the bracket at $125 and the bundle at $199, while supplies last at least.  
该支架的建议零售价为 149.95 美元，如果你想购买目前提供的所有配件套装，总价为 269.80 美元。但一如既往，Peak Design 提供 Kickstarter 众筹折扣，支架折扣价为 125 美元，套装为 199 美元，至少持续到库存售罄。
