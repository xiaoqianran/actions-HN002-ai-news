**Title:** Orthogonal Gradient Constraints Shape Noisy-Label Memorization Dynamics  
**标题：** 正交梯度约束塑造噪声标签记忆动态

**Authors:** Richard Mai  
**作者：** 理查德·迈

**arXiv:** 2607.16231 (cs) [Submitted on 24 Jun 2026]  
**arXiv：** 2607.16231 (cs) [提交于 2026年6月24日]

Modern neural networks can fit corrupted training labels, making noisy-label learning a useful setting for studying memorization-driven overfitting. Most regularization methods modify the objective, architecture, or data distribution; here we instead study a geometric intervention on the optimizer update itself.  
现代神经网络可以拟合被篡改的训练标签，这使得噪声标签学习成为研究记忆驱动过拟合的有用场景。大多数正则化方法修改目标函数、架构或数据分布；而本研究则转向对优化器更新本身进行几何干预。

We evaluate OrthoGrad, which removes the component of each weight gradient parallel to the current weight vector, in noisy-label image classification. On MNIST with small-data regimes, OrthoGrad improves test accuracy most clearly for CNNs while reducing corrupted-label fitting.  
我们在噪声标签图像分类任务中评估了OrthoGrad，该方法移除每个权重梯度中与当前权重向量平行的分量。在MNIST小数据情境下，OrthoGrad最显著地提升了CNN的测试准确率，同时减少了被篡改标签的拟合。

Mechanism diagnostics based on weight norms and gradient-weight cosine similarity suggest that the projection has the strongest effect when the raw gradient contains a nontrivial radial component, and becomes weaker in larger-data regimes where gradients are already nearly orthogonal to weights.  
基于权重范数和梯度-权重余弦相似度的机制诊断表明，当原始梯度包含非平凡的径向分量时，投影效果最强；而在梯度已几乎与权重正交的大数据情境下，效果减弱。

Additional CIFAR-10 ResNet-18 experiments show that the method can alter memorization trajectories but does not prevent eventual noisy-label memorization.  
额外的CIFAR-10 ResNet-18实验表明，该方法可以改变记忆轨迹，但无法阻止最终的噪声标签记忆。

These results support orthogonal update constraints as a useful diagnostic for studying learning dynamics, while showing that OrthoGrad is regime-dependent rather than universally regularizing.  
这些结果支持将正交更新约束作为研究学习动态的有用诊断工具，同时表明OrthoGrad依赖于特定情境而非普遍正则化。
