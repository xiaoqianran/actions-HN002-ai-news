## Best Smartwatch for iPhone Users  
## iPhone 用户最佳智能手表  

**Apple Watch Series 11**  
**苹果手表 Series 11**  
*$399 Apple | $299 (25% off) Amazon | $299 (25% off) Best Buy*  

The Series 11 is the top smartwatch for iPhone owners, mixing a nice blend of features for a reasonable price. That includes arguably the most important upgrade: all-day battery life; Apple has finally upgraded the 18-hour run time to a full 24 hours, and that's a game-changer. Now you can wear your watch to bed and track your sleep knowing it won't die in the middle of the night.  
Series 11 是 iPhone 用户的首选智能手表，以合理价格融合了多种实用功能。其中最重要的升级可能是全天电池续航：苹果终于将 18 小时的运行时间提升至完整的 24 小时，这堪称革命性改变。现在您可以戴着手表睡觉并追踪睡眠，而不用担心它在半夜关机。  

The second most notable addition is hypertension notifications. The CDC says nearly half the US adult population has high blood pressure, which increases the risk of a stroke or heart disease. It's FDA-cleared, and you'll need to wear the watch for 14 days before it starts delivering alerts. (You shouldn't use the Series 11 to diagnose hypertension, but you can double-check your results with your physician if there are trouble signs.) Apple has also added a Sleep Score that rates your sleep, and blood oxygen is officially back.  
第二大新增功能是高血压通知。美国疾控中心表示，近一半美国成年人患有高血压，这会增加中风或心脏病的风险。该功能已获 FDA 批准，您需要佩戴手表 14 天后才会开始接收警报。（您不应使用 Series 11 诊断高血压，但如果出现异常迹象，可与医生复核结果。）苹果还新增了睡眠评分功能来评估您的睡眠，血氧监测也正式回归。  

It's worth noting that the older Series 10 can also take advantage of some of these new features via a software update, including hypertension notifications and Sleep Score, so if you can find it on sale, it could be worth snagging instead of the latest. But I think a full 24 hours of battery life might trump everything else.  
值得注意的是，旧款 Series 10 也能通过软件更新获得部分新功能，包括高血压通知和睡眠评分，因此如果您能找到打折的 Series 10，或许比最新款更值得入手。但我认为完整的 24 小时电池续航可能比其他所有功能都更重要。

## Best Smartwatch for Android Users  
## 安卓用户最佳智能手表  

**Google Pixel Watch 4**  
**谷歌 Pixel Watch 4**  
*$400 Amazon (45 mm) | $350 Amazon (41 mm) | $350 Best Buy (41 mm) | $350 Google Store (41 mm)*  

The Pixel Watch 4 is the first repairable smartwatch from Google, and that alone makes it easy to recommend. You can swap the battery and display when the time comes, and parts are available at iFixit. It also helps that this is one of the prettiest smartwatches on the market, aided by a new display that sits under the domed glass; it's gorgeous.  
Pixel Watch 4 是谷歌首款可维修的智能手表，仅凭这一点就值得推荐。您可以在需要时更换电池和显示屏，且零件可在 iFixit 购买。它还是市场上最漂亮的智能手表之一，得益于新显示屏置于穹顶玻璃下的设计，外观惊艳。  

Performance is slick with the new Qualcomm Snapdragon Wear W5 Gen 2 chipset powering the experience, and Google's Material 3 Expressive redesign really gives life to the interface and makes the smartwatch fun to use. The screen gets bright, the health and fitness features are impeccably accurate—especially the revamped sleep tracking—and there's even support for satellite messaging during emergencies (you need the cellular version of the watch for this). The battery life will get you through a day and a half, at least, on the 45-mm version. Charging with the new dock-style charger is also ridiculously fast; you can easily get more than 50 percent after just 15 minutes. Sadly, yes, this is the third time Google has changed the charger in four generations, so older Pixel Watch cables are incompatible.  
性能流畅，得益于全新的高通骁龙 Wear W5 Gen 2 芯片组驱动体验，谷歌 Material 3 的重新设计让界面充满活力，使用起来充满乐趣。屏幕亮度充足，健康和健身功能精准无误——尤其是改进后的睡眠追踪——甚至支持紧急情况下的卫星消息发送（需要蜂窝网络版本）。45 毫米版本的电池续航至少能坚持一天半。新款底座式充电器充电速度极快，仅 15 分钟即可轻松充入超过 50% 电量。遗憾的是，这已是谷歌在四代产品中第三次更换充电器，因此旧款 Pixel Watch 充电线不兼容。  

The best new feature is Raise to Talk with Gemini. Just lift your wrist, and you can say anything to the voice assistant. That includes asking the weather or asking a question in the middle of a conversation with a friend, having the assistant start a workout, or even turning on the lights in your living room if you have a smart-home setup. The gesture is a bit flaky, but it beats having to say a hot word or pushing a button. Google also recently added more gestures via a software update, including a double-pinch to answer calls or snooze alarms.  
最佳新功能是使用 Gemini 的“抬腕对话”。只需抬起手腕，您就可以向语音助手说出任何指令。包括询问天气、在与朋友交谈中途提问、让助手开始锻炼，或如果您有智能家居设置，甚至打开客厅的灯。该手势有时不太稳定，但比说出唤醒词或按下按钮更方便。谷歌最近还通过软件更新增加了更多手势，包括双击捏合来接听电话或贪睡闹钟。  

While there are a few features exclusive to Pixel phones (controlling the phone's camera, AI-powered smart replies, and call screening), none of them are core functions of the watch, making it one of the best options for anyone with an Android phone.  
虽然有一些功能仅限 Pixel 手机（如控制手机相机、AI 智能回复和来电筛选），但它们都不是手表的核心功能，这使得 Pixel Watch 4 成为任何安卓手机用户的绝佳选择。
