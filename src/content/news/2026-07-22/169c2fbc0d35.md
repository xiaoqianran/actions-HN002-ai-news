# 2027 Kia Seltos first drive gives you plenty of SUV for not much $
# 2027年起亚Seltos首测：花小钱办大事的SUV

NEWPORT BEACH, Calif.—Although it sometimes feels like automakers have all but abandoned everything other than high-end luxury model vehicles, it’s not universally true. While some car companies have lobbied the government, terrified, asking to build protective walls to fend off cheap Chinese competition, others have looked at the problem from a different angle, trying to meet the moment.
加州纽波特比奇——虽然有时感觉汽车制造商几乎已经放弃了除高端豪华车型之外的所有产品，但这并非普遍事实。当一些汽车公司恐惧地游说政府，要求建立保护墙以抵御廉价的中国竞争时，另一些公司则从不同角度看待问题，试图应对时代需求。

One such effort is the new Kia Seltos, a small SUV that takes inspiration from the Telluride but with a starting price of just $24,990 plus delivery charge.
其中一项努力就是新款起亚Seltos，这是一款小型SUV，从Telluride汲取灵感，但起售价仅24,990美元（另加运费）。

The US almost didn’t get the Seltos in the first place; the first-generation model was designed with China in mind. Seltos take two is more considered, and also a little more substantial.
美国差点一开始就得不到Seltos；第一代车型是为中国市场设计的。第二代Seltos考虑更周全，也更有分量一些。

The new body with its Telluride-like design cues has been stretched, mostly in the wheelbase. That said, at 174.4 inches (4,430 mm) long, 72 inches (1,829 mm) wide, and 64.9 inches (1,648 mm) tall, it’s still a subcompact, one segment smaller than something like a Toyota RAV4 or Mazda CX-5.
新款车身带有Telluride般的设计元素，主要加长了轴距。尽管如此，其长度174.4英寸（4,430毫米），宽度72英寸（1,829毫米），高度64.9英寸（1,648毫米），它仍然是一款小型车，比丰田RAV4或马自达CX-5小一个级别。

Kia is offering a choice of three different powertrain variants.
起亚提供三种不同的动力总成选择。

The 1.6 L hybrid is the one we’re most interested in. But that version won’t be in showrooms until Q4 this year; don’t expect an Ars first drive of the hybrid Seltos until early next year. Or any other details in this first drive report—the hybrid Seltos is still undergoing its EPA certification, and Kia isn’t prepared to talk power, torque, or efficiency until that’s done and dusted.
我们最感兴趣的是1.6升混合动力版。但该版本要到今年第四季度才能进店；预计我们对混合动力Seltos的首测要等到明年初。本首测报告中也不会有其他细节——混合动力Seltos仍在进行EPA认证，起亚在认证完成前不愿谈论功率、扭矩或效率。

Most will come with a 2.0 L four-cylinder under the hood that generates 147 hp (110 kW) and 132 lb-ft (180 Nm). You can have that engine with either front-wheel drive and a combined 30 mpg (7.8 L/100 km), or all-wheel drive and 28 mpg (8.4 L/100 km) combined.
大多数车型将搭载2.0升四缸发动机，可输出147马力（110千瓦）和132磅-英尺（180牛·米）扭矩。该发动机可选前驱，综合油耗30英里/加仑（7.8升/100公里）；或四驱，综合油耗28英里/加仑（8.4升/100公里）。

This engine is available in various trims, from the sub-$25,000 entry-level FWD Seltos LX to the $30,090 AWD Seltos EX.
该发动机适用于多种配置，从低于25,000美元的前驱入门级Seltos LX到30,090美元的四驱Seltos EX。

The other alternative at launch finds a 1.6 L turbocharged four-cylinder engine up front. This powertrain is only available with AWD, the SX trim level, and the X-Line package, which gives the car some glossy black exterior accents and bigger wheels, and commands a more substantial though still competitive $32,790 MSRP.
另一款首发选择是1.6升涡轮增压四缸发动机。该动力总成仅配备四驱、SX trim级别和X-Line套件（为车辆提供一些亮黑色外观点缀和更大轮毂），其建议零售价为32,790美元，虽更高但仍具竞争力。

The turbocharged Seltos generates 190 hp (142 kW) and 195 lb-ft (264 Nm) averaging a combined 26 mpg (9 L/100 km). That’s plenty of power and torque for the added extra equipment of the SX trim, not to mention the all-wheel drive mechanicals, which can push the SX X-Line’s curb weight to 3,715 lb (1,685 kg); opt for the basic FWD LX Seltos and it tips the scales at just 3,109 lbs (1,410 kg).
涡轮增压Seltos可输出190马力（142千瓦）和195磅-英尺（264牛·米）扭矩，综合平均油耗26英里/加仑（9升/100公里）。对于SX trim增加的额外装备来说，这动力和扭矩绰绰有余，更不用说四驱机械结构了——这使SX X-Line的整备质量达到3,715磅（1,685千克）；而选择基本款前驱LX Seltos，其重量仅为3,109磅（1,410千克）。

Compared to Ars’ usual diet of electric vehicles and plug-in hybrids that regularly weigh more than two tons, the Seltos feels svelte by comparison. (The much lower curb weight of the LX should go quite a long way to making up for any shortfalls from the engine, in practice.)
与Ars通常评测的经常超过两吨的电动汽车和插电式混合动力车相比，Seltos相比之下显得苗条。（实际上，LX低得多的整备质量应能很大程度上弥补发动机的任何不足。）

From the Seltos’ driver’s seat, you will see a pleasing array of real buttons, dials, and controls, all within reach; the only touchscreen is the 12.3-inch infotainment system. Kia’s system is one of the less frustrating OEM offerings and also offers Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, where most people will spend most of their time.
坐在Seltos的驾驶座上，你会看到一系列触手可及的实体按钮、旋钮和控件；唯一的触摸屏是12.3英寸的信息娱乐系统。起亚的系统是较少令人沮丧的原厂配置之一，并提供Apple CarPlay和Android Auto——大多数人大部分时间会花在这里。

There’s also a useful amount of storage in the door bins plus three USB-C ports and a wireless charging pad, although the LX does without the last of these convenience features.
门板储物格也有实用的储物空间，外加三个USB-C端口和一个无线充电板，尽管LX没有最后一项便利功能。

Things aren’t bad in the back seat either; thanks to the wheelbase there’s 39 inches (991 mm) of rear leg room, and the occupants have another couple of USB-C ports to keep their gadgets charged.
后排空间也不错；得益于轴距，后排腿部空间有39英寸（991毫米），乘客还有另外几个USB-C端口为设备充电。

And Kia says the 27.8 cubic feet (787 L) of cargo space—which increases to 64.2 cubic feet (1,818 L) with the seats folded flat—is class-leading. It has also been thoughtfully designed, with removable cargo hooks that more cars should provide.
起亚表示，27.8立方英尺（787升）的行李厢空间——座椅放平后增至64.2立方英尺（1,818升）——是同级领先的。其设计也很周到，配备了可拆卸的行李钩，这是更多车型应该提供的。

On the road, you have the choice between normal and sport modes; the latter changes the shift points for the turbo Seltos’ eight-speed automatic transmission. (The naturally aspirated Seltos makes use of a continuously variable transmission instead.)
在路上，你可以在标准和运动模式间选择；后者会改变涡轮增压Seltos八速自动变速箱的换挡点。（自然吸气Seltos则使用无级变速箱。）

It’s not an unpleasant car to drive, and it will handle a curve without surrendering to body roll, but the suspension does communicate every expansion gap or rut in the road surface through to your butt, and you’ll hear a fair bit of road noise, although less from the passenger seat than the driver’s side, interestingly.
驾驶它并非不愉快，过弯时也不会侧倾严重，但悬架确实会将路面上每一个伸缩缝或车辙直接传到你的臀部，而且你会听到不少路噪，有趣的是，副驾听到的比驾驶侧要少。
