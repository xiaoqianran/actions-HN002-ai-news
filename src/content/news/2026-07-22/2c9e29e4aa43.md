# The Great Peptide Cash Grab Has Begun  
# 肽类药物“淘金热”已拉开序幕

Has anyone tried to sell you peptides yet? If not, they probably will soon. Once a niche interest among bodybuilders and biohackers, peptides are an increasingly popular class of substance hyped by both fringe influencers and the current US secretary of Health and Human Services.  
是否已有人向你推销肽类药物？如果还没有，那可能很快了。肽类药物曾是健美爱好者和生物黑客的小众兴趣，如今却成为越来越受欢迎的品类，连边缘影响者和现任美国卫生与公众服务部部长都在为其造势。  

Earlier this year, Robert F. Kennedy Jr. told the podcaster Joe Rogan that the government intended to loosen restrictions on peptide production. This week, it could finally happen: On July 23 and 24, the Food and Drug Administration will discuss whether to reclassify a group of peptides so that pharmacies can legally make custom mixes, a process known as compounding. The move would reverse a decision made in 2023 by the Biden administration, which barred certain peptides from being compounded.  
今年早些时候，小罗伯特·F·肯尼迪向播客主持人乔·罗根透露，政府打算放宽肽类生产限制。本周，这一变化可能终于成真：7月23日至24日，美国食品药品监督管理局（FDA）将讨论是否对一批肽类进行重新分类，使药房能够合法进行定制混合（即“复配”）。此举将逆转拜登政府2023年禁止某些肽类复配的决定。  

Although career scientists at the agency have recommended against it, many industry insiders tell WIRED they believe the FDA will approve reclassification anyway, since the committee put together to do the assessment includes peptide boosters. Some players in the telehealth world are already operating under the assumption that the floodgates will soon open.  
尽管该机构的职业科学家建议反对，但许多业内人士向《连线》杂志表示，他们仍相信FDA会批准重新分类，因为负责评估的委员会中包含肽类支持者。一些远程医疗领域的参与者已假定“闸门即将打开”，提前行动。  

“We’re getting everything ready to go,” says Myra Ahmad, the chief executive of the telehealth platform Mochi Health, which originally focused on GLP-1 medications. “We have pharmacies that have done all their safety testing stuff; they’re putting pricing together.”  
“我们正在为全面启动做准备，”原专注于GLP-1药物的远程医疗平台Mochi Health首席执行官Myra Ahmad说，“合作药房已完成所有安全测试，正在制定定价。”  

Some of the largest telehealth companies have looked forward to this moment for over a year. Virtual health care juggernaut Hims & Hers Health purchased a peptide manufacturing facility in February 2025 and is developing clinical protocols for peptides, according to its chief medical officer, Anant Vinjamoori, who is slated to testify at the FDA hearing.  
一些最大的远程医疗公司已期待这一刻超过一年。虚拟医疗巨头Hims & Hers Health于2025年2月收购了一家肽类生产设施，并正在制定肽类临床方案，据其首席医疗官Anant Vinjamoori透露——他将在FDA听证会上作证。  

Joshua Fritzler, president of the compounding pharmacy Olympia Pharmaceuticals, says the company has been preparing in advance of the anticipated changes, “spending a lot of money to create these formulas” to be ready if the FDA gives the green light.  
复配药房Olympia Pharmaceuticals总裁Joshua Fritzler表示，公司已为预期变化提前准备，“投入大量资金研发配方”，以备FDA批准。  

“There’s an understanding in the industry that something big is happening,” he says.  
“业内都明白，大事即将发生，”他说。  

The GLP-1 boom warmed people up to the idea of injecting medications at home and proved to be a cash cow for pharmacies and virtual clinics. Now, though, many of these companies are eager to expand their horizons.  
GLP-1药物的繁荣让人们对居家注射药物习以为常，并为药房和虚拟诊所带来了丰厚利润。如今，许多公司正急于拓展新领域。  

Cole Thomas, the director of sales and operations at the pharmacy marketing and technology platform RxConnexion, says that some of the company’s compounding pharmacy clients are testing peptides that are likely to be popular. One such variety, BPC-157, is touted as a healing compound and is one-half of the combination known as the “Wolverine stack.” (BPC stands for “body protection compound.”)  
药房营销与技术平台RxConnexion的销售与运营总监Cole Thomas表示，其部分复配药房客户正在测试可能受欢迎的肽类。其中一种BPC-157被宣传为“修复化合物”，也是所谓“金刚狼组合”的一半（BPC意为“身体保护化合物”）。  

Cameron Bravo, a research scientist at the biotech firm Sapho Bio, says his company has been approached about testing a variety of peptides already, too. (Mochi Health is a client.) Sapho Bio runs tests to “validate” formulas that clients bring, ensuring the mixtures are uncontaminated and properly sealed. So far, it has tested BPC-157 as well as more obscure offerings like MOTS-c, which is hyped as “exercise in a vial” by evangelists.  
生物科技公司Sapho Bio的研究科学家Cameron Bravo表示，公司已接到委托测试多种肽类（Mochi Health是其客户）。Sapho Bio通过测试“验证”客户提供的配方，确保混合物无污染且密封完好。迄今已测试BPC-157及更冷门的MOTS-c——后者被推崇者称为“瓶装运动”。  

Some of the pharmacies the company works with are testing multiple products, at significant cost. “They're probably investing something like $100,000 into these before they're even fully out yet,” he says.  
该公司合作的部分药房正在测试多种产品，成本高昂。“在产品完全上市前，他们可能已投入约10万美元，”他说。  

Even if this week’s meeting goes exactly how peptide boosters want it to go, with the committee approving all of the substances up for discussion, that won’t lead to an immediate green light for sellers. The next step involves a review period, during which the FDA will propose rules and the public will be able to comment. “It’s a long-winded process,” says Gerard Olson, who was recently the director of research at LegitScript, a company that provides certifications for telehealth providers and pharmacies.  
即便本周会议完全如肽类支持者所愿，委员会批准所有讨论物质，卖家也不会立即获得绿灯。下一步是审查期，FDA将在此期间提出规则并征集公众意见。“这是一个冗长的过程，”曾担任远程医疗认证公司LegitScript研究总监的Gerard Olson说。  

Hims plans to wait and see. “We don't want to be necessarily first to market. We want to be best in market,” says Vinjamoori, the chief medical officer.  
Hims计划观望。“我们不一定想成为首个上市者，而要成为最佳市场参与者，”首席医疗官Vinjamoori表示。  

But other compounders and clinics are moving ahead with prescribing these medications as injectables.  
但其他复配商和诊所已开始以注射剂形式开具这些药物。  

Amanecia Health, a concierge service that sees patients in person at its Texas and Virginia clinics in addition to operating a telehealth practice, has started prescribing popular options like BPC-157. “There's been an uptick in interest because it's been all over Instagram and the media,” says Amanceia’s founder Ann Czarnik, who also works as an emergency medicine physician.  
Amanecia Health是一家礼宾式医疗服务机构，在得克萨斯州和弗吉尼亚州的诊所提供线下诊疗，同时开展远程医疗。该机构已开始开具BPC-157等热门药物。“兴趣激增是因为它在Instagram和媒体上随处可见，”创始人兼急诊医生Ann Czarnik说。

尽管Czarnik坦承这些物质缺乏传统药物那样的安全性与有效性数据，她的诊所仍开始谨慎地为少数患者开具；她强调，这“极小比例”的患者清楚它们并非FDA批准药物。“我还不能公开宣传我在开具这些药物，”她说。  

Scott Brunner, the head of the Alliance for Pharmacy Compounding, has been warning member pharmacies to comply with the law and not go rogue and roll out peptides early. “Many of them are being hammered by prescribers and patients to do this,” he says. To make the temptation even more intense, these compounders know that the people they turn away have easy access to a gray-market supply.  
美国药房复配联盟负责人Scott Brunner一直警告成员药房遵守法律，不要擅自提前推出肽类药物。“许多药房正被处方医生和患者催促这样做，”他说。更诱人的是，这些复配商明白，他们拒绝的客户能轻易从灰色市场获取产品。  

Right now, all of the peptides up for discussion in the July FDA meeting, as well as many others, are remarkably easy to get your hands on. They’re available for purchase from online sellers that place disclaimers on their products, noting that they are “research-grade only” and not for human consumption.  
目前，7月FDA会议讨论的所有肽类及其他许多肽类都极易获取。在线卖家在产品上标注免责声明，称其“仅限研究用途，不得用于人体”，但仍可直接购买。  

“We are not selling. It is not legal for us to do so,” says Olympia’s Fritzler. “This gray market, which is insane—obviously we are not participating in it.”  
“我们不销售，对我们而言这不合法，”Olympia的Fritzler说，“这个灰色市场很疯狂——我们显然没有参与。”
