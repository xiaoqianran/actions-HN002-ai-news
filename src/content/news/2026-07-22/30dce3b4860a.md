# A Survey on GNN-based Link Prediction: Techniques, Applications, and Challenges  
# 基于图神经网络的链接预测综述：技术、应用与挑战  

**Authors:** Chengcheng Sun, Yajie Song, Cheng Zhai, Jiayun Tian, Jia Yang, Xiaobin Rui, Jian Zhang, Zhixiao Wang, Philip S. Yu  
**作者：** 程成, 宋雅洁, 翟成, 田佳云, 杨佳, 芮晓彬, 张健, 王志晓, Philip S. Yu  

Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) have emerged as the leading paradigm for link prediction, enabling the inference of missing connections and the anticipation of potential future links.  
图神经网络（GNNs）已成为链接预测的主流范式，能够推断缺失的连接并预测潜在的未来链接。  

However, existing reviews lack systematic exploration specifically targeting underlying GNN architectures and diverse graph structures.  
然而，现有综述缺乏针对底层GNN架构和多样化图结构的系统性探索。  

To address this critical gap, this paper provides a comprehensive review of GNN-based link prediction from a novel and dedicated GNN perspective.  
为弥补这一关键空白，本文从新颖且专门的GNN视角对基于GNN的链接预测进行了全面综述。  

We propose an innovative taxonomy that categorizes recent advancements based on techniques and applications.  
我们提出了一种创新分类法，根据技术和应用对最新进展进行分类。  

From a technique perspective, we focus on key GNN encoder architectures, including GCN-based, GAE-based, GAT-based, and GFormer-based methods, discussing their strengths and limitations.  
从技术角度，我们重点关注关键的GNN编码器架构，包括基于GCN、GAE、GAT和GFormer的方法，并讨论其优势和局限性。  

From an application perspective, we highlight prominent use cases of link prediction in knowledge graphs and recommendation systems, demonstrating their real-world impact.  
从应用角度，我们强调了链接预测在知识图谱和推荐系统中的突出用例，展示了其现实影响。  

In addition, we examine the current challenges and discuss promising future directions.  
此外，我们审视了当前挑战并讨论了有前景的未来方向。
