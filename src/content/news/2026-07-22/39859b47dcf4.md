# Apple’s rumored ‘Upgrade’ program brings lease-to-own pricing for iPhones, Macs, and iPads  
# 苹果传闻中的“升级”计划将为iPhone、Mac和iPad提供先租后买定价方案

TechNewsAppleApple’s rumored ‘Upgrade’ program brings lease-to-own pricing for iPhones, Macs, and iPads Bloomberg reports that Apple Upgrade will be available later this month for most of Apple’s most popular devices.  
据彭博社报道，苹果升级计划将于本月晚些时候推出，适用于苹果大部分最受欢迎的设备。

by Stevie Bonifield Jul 21, 2026, 5:12 PM UTC  
作者：Stevie Bonifield 2026年7月21日 下午5:12 UTC

Photo: Allison Johnson / The Verge  
照片：Allison Johnson / The Verge

As component and RAM shortages drive prices higher, Apple is reportedly launching an “Apple Upgrade” program for leasing new devices.  
随着组件和内存短缺推动价格上涨，苹果据传正在推出一个用于租赁新设备的“苹果升级”计划。

According to Bloomberg’s Mark Gurman, it will work similar to a car lease, with options to upgrade to a new device early, or keep or return the device at the end of the lease.  
据彭博社的Mark Gurman称，其运作方式类似于汽车租赁，用户可选择提前升级到新设备，或在租期结束时保留或归还设备。

Gurman says Klarna will be Apple’s financial backer for the new program, which will require a soft credit check to sign up.  
Gurman表示，Klarna将成为苹果新计划的金融支持方，注册时需进行软信用检查。

Apple Upgrade is reportedly slated to launch on July 28th, replacing the current iPhone Upgrade Program and standard financing for new iPhones with a program available for most new iPhone, Mac, iPad, and Apple Watch purchases.  
据称，苹果升级计划将于7月28日推出，取代现有的iPhone升级计划和新iPhone的标准融资方案，新计划适用于大部分新款iPhone、Mac、iPad和Apple Watch的购买。

Apple Upgrade will offer 24-month leases for iPhones and Apple Watches, and 36-month leases for iPads and Macs.  
苹果升级计划将为iPhone和Apple Watch提供24个月的租赁期，为iPad和Mac提供36个月的租赁期。

Budget devices like the base iPad, iPhone 16, Apple Watch SE, and fast-selling MacBook Neo will reportedly not be eligible.  
据报道，基础款iPad、iPhone 16、Apple Watch SE以及热销的MacBook Neo等平价设备将不符合资格。

Unlike the current iPhone Upgrade Program, Apple Upgrade won’t include an AppleCare subscription, which is also getting more expensive.  
与现有的iPhone升级计划不同，苹果升级计划将不包含AppleCare订阅服务，而该服务本身也在涨价。

The new upgrade program would come just a month after Apple raised prices on nearly all of its products.  
这一新升级计划将在苹果对几乎所有产品涨价一个月后推出。

While the iPhone wasn’t included in that round of price increases, the iPhone 18 Pro series could raise the bar on pricing when it’s expected to launch later this year.  
虽然iPhone未包含在上一轮涨价中，但预计将于今年晚些时候推出的iPhone 18 Pro系列可能会提高定价标准。
