# TreeSize won't renew perpetual-license support unless users subscribe  
# TreeSize将不再为永久许可证提供续期支持，除非用户订阅

The company behind the disk space analyzer TreeSize has irked some users by no longer offering support or updates for perpetual licenses beyond their maintenance period unless customers subscribe.  
磁盘空间分析工具TreeSize的开发商因在维护期结束后不再为永久许可证提供支持或更新，除非客户订阅，此举激怒了一些用户。

Further frustration has come from JAM Software’s long-standing policy of not providing license keys or installers to TreeSize perpetual license holders after that support period ends.  
进一步的不满源于JAM Software长期以来的政策：在支持期结束后，不再向TreeSize永久许可证持有者提供许可证密钥或安装程序。

Since 2025, JAM Software has been transitioning most TreeSize editions to subscription models.  
自2025年起，JAM Software已将大多数TreeSize版本转向订阅模式。

Today, it sells perpetual licenses only for personal use, which include 12 months of updates, support, and “downloads of older versions, and your license,” plus the option to extend the support period.  
目前，该公司仅向个人用户销售永久许可证，此类许可证包含12个月的更新、支持以及“旧版本和许可证的下载权限”，并可选延长支持期。

TreeSize currently has “no plans to discontinue the sale of perpetual licenses for TreeSize Personal,” product manager Hendrik Christ told Ars Technica.  
产品经理Hendrik Christ告诉Ars Technica，TreeSize目前“没有停止销售TreeSize Personal永久许可证的计划”。

As perpetual-license maintenance periods expire, customers are discovering that extending support now generally requires subscribing to software they already own the right to use.  
随着永久许可证的维护期到期，客户们发现，如今延长支持通常需要订阅他们已经拥有使用权的软件。

On Reddit, a user shared an email informing them that support for their perpetual license ends in September, after which they will lose access to updates and support because those “are only available in the subscription.”  
在Reddit上，一名用户分享了一封邮件，通知其永久许可证的支持将于9月结束，此后他们将失去更新和支持权限，因为这些“仅限订阅用户获取”。

Christ told Ars that considering the “current economic conditions,” the change was “necessary to keep developing and supporting TreeSize reliably over the long term.”  
Christ向Ars表示，考虑到“当前经济环境”，这一改变“对于长期可靠地开发和维护TreeSize是必要的”。

One of the biggest criticisms is that JAM Software will not provide backup installation files or license keys to customers with unsupported licenses.  
最大的批评之一在于，JAM Software不会向支持已过期的许可证客户提供备份安装文件或许可证密钥。

TreeSize’s email urged the user to make backups.  
TreeSize的邮件敦促用户自行备份。

“Honestly, it feels quite low to strip paying customers of the ability to simply download the installer for a software version they already paid for,” the Reddit user said.  
该Reddit用户表示：“老实说，剥夺付费客户下载已购买软件版本安装程序的能力，这感觉相当卑劣。”

But even before it sold TreeSize subscriptions, JAM Software didn’t permanently retain every installation file and key after a customer’s support period ended, Christ said.  
但Christ表示，即使在销售TreeSize订阅服务之前，JAM Software也不会在客户支持期结束后永久保留所有安装文件和密钥。

This is because “older software versions can contain known bugs or even security vulnerabilities,” he said, adding: Making these available for download indefinitely and without restriction is something we consider a risk to our customers, particularly in business environments.  
这是因为“旧版本软件可能包含已知错误甚至安全漏洞”，他补充道：“无限期且无限制地提供这些文件下载，我们认为这对客户构成风险，尤其是在商业环境中。”

There’s also the technical and organizational overhead of maintaining and supporting old versions and their associated keys indefinitely.  
此外，无限期维护和支持旧版本及其关联密钥也存在技术和组织上的开销。

Although TreeSize says this is a long-standing policy, the backlash highlights the disconnect between what many people expect when buying a perpetual license and what they get when a software vendor shifts its business model toward subscriptions.  
尽管TreeSize表示这是一项长期政策，但此次反弹凸显了人们在购买永久许可证时的期望与软件供应商转向订阅模式后实际提供的服务之间的脱节。

“To make sure customers aren’t caught off guard, we proactively notify them by email, well before their maintenance ends, to back up their installation file and license key themselves. This is meant to ensure no one loses access to software they’ve already purchased,” Christ said.  
Christ表示：“为确保客户不感到意外，我们会在其维护期结束前通过邮件主动通知他们自行备份安装文件和许可证密钥。这是为了确保没有人失去对已购软件的访问权限。”

Should this be a subscription? There’s also the question of whether it makes sense to subscribe to a disk space analyzer, especially when there are free alternatives like WinDirStat.  
这应该订阅吗？另一个问题是，订阅磁盘空间分析工具是否合理，尤其当存在WinDirStat等免费替代品时。

Christ admitted that the change has been “frustrating for some customers.”  
Christ承认这一改变“让一些客户感到沮丧”。

When asked about online requests for refunds, Christ said, “since our customers do receive what was agreed, namely indefinite use of the software version they purchased, we don’t consider a refund to be warranted…”  
当被问及网上的退款请求时，Christ表示：“由于客户确实获得了约定内容，即无限期使用所购软件版本，我们认为退款不合理……”

A free version of TreeSize exists, but it lacks some features, including the ability to search for duplicate files, deduplicate redundant files, and compare disk space usage over time. It is not for business use.  
TreeSize存在免费版本，但缺少部分功能，包括搜索重复文件、去重冗余文件以及对比磁盘空间使用情况随时间变化的能力。该版本不适用于商业用途。

TreeSize’s evolution illustrates the growing subscription economy, particularly the software industry’s fixation on recurring revenue.  
TreeSize的演变体现了订阅经济的增长，尤其是软件行业对经常性收入的执着。

Over the past decade, software vendors have increasingly shifted to subscriptions in pursuit of more predictable revenue streams.  
在过去十年中，软件供应商 increasingly 转向订阅模式，以追求更可预测的收入流。

In TreeSize’s case, JAM Software wanted to “continue offering our customers a relevant, well-maintained product over the long term,” Christ said.  
Christ表示，以TreeSize为例，JAM Software希望“长期为客户提供相关且维护良好的产品”。

“The broader economic environment is more challenging today than it was a few years ago, and we feel that too as a software vendor,” he added.  
他补充道：“当今整体经济环境比几年前更具挑战性，作为软件供应商我们也有所感受。”

“To continue meeting our own standards and our responsibility to business customers who rely on high-quality software, we are placing greater emphasis on predictability in development and support. A subscription model gives us that predictability in a way one-time purchases do not.”  
“为继续达到我们自身标准以及对依赖高质量软件的企业客户的责任，我们更加强调开发和支持的可预测性。订阅模式以一次性购买无法实现的方式提供了这种可预测性。”
