**Title:** NOWJ@COLIEE 2026: Adaptive Pipelines for Legal Retrieval and Reasoning  
**标题：** NOWJ@COLIEE 2026：面向法律检索与推理的自适应流水线

**Authors:** Thuong-Hieu Ngo, Hoang-Trung Nguyen, Huu-Dong Nguyen, Xuan-Bach Le, Le-Dung Nguyen, Quang-Thanh Tran, Ha-Thanh Nguyen, Thi-Hai-Yen Vuong  
**作者：** Thuong-Hieu Ngo, Hoang-Trung Nguyen, Huu-Dong Nguyen, Xuan-Bach Le, Le-Dung Nguyen, Quang-Thanh Tran, Ha-Thanh Nguyen, Thi-Hai-Yen Vuong

**Abstract:** This paper presents the methodologies and results of the NOWJ team's participation across all five tasks of the COLIEE 2026 competition.  
**摘要：** 本文介绍了NOWJ团队参与COLIEE 2026竞赛全部五项任务的方法论与结果。

For Task 1 (Legal Case Retrieval), we propose a four-stage pipeline comprising candidate filtering, dense retrieval with complementary embedding models, cross-encoder reranking via fine-tuned generative rerankers and MLP-based pairwise classification, and adaptive per-query cutoff prediction.  
**对于任务1（法律案例检索），** 我们提出一个四阶段流水线，包括候选过滤、使用互补嵌入模型的密集检索、通过微调生成式重排器和基于MLP的成对分类进行交叉编码器重排，以及自适应逐查询截断预测。

For Task 2 (Legal Case Entailment), we combine BM25 filtering, T5-based reranking, and LLM-based entailment verification with consensus ensemble.  
**对于任务2（法律案例蕴含），** 我们结合了BM25过滤、基于T5的重排以及基于大语言模型的蕴含验证与共识集成。

For Task 3 (Statute Law Retrieval and Entailment), we adopt a retrieval-augmented generation framework with dense retrieval, attention-based reranking, and few-shot-prompted LLM reasoning.  
**对于任务3（成文法检索与蕴含），** 我们采用检索增强生成框架，包含密集检索、基于注意力的重排以及少样本提示的大语言模型推理。

For Task 4 (Legal Textual Entailment), we introduce a dynamic routing pipeline that classifies query difficulty and dispatches cases to either a balanced few-shot solver or a structured zero-shot chain-of-thought solver.  
**对于任务4（法律文本蕴含），** 我们引入了一个动态路由流水线，该流水线对查询难度进行分类，并将案例分发给平衡的少样本求解器或结构化的零样本思维链求解器。

For the Pilot Task (Legal Judgment Prediction), we combine hierarchical transformers with CRF layers, argument relation mining, and probabilistic argumentation graph reasoning.  
**对于试点任务（法律判决预测），** 我们结合了分层Transformer与CRF层、论点关系挖掘以及概率论证图推理。
