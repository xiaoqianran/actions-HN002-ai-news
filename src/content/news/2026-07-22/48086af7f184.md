# Apple teams up with Klarna to launch a lease-to-own program for iPhones, iPads, and Macs  
苹果与克拉纳合作推出iPhone、iPad和Mac的租赁购买计划

Apple is reportedly teaming up with deferred payment processor Klarna to launch a new lease-to-own program for its devices. Bloomberg reported Tuesday that the program — dubbed Apple Upgrade — is set to launch next Tuesday, July 28.  
据报道，苹果正与延期支付处理商克拉纳合作，为其设备推出一项新的租赁购买计划。彭博社周二报道称，这项名为“Apple Upgrade”的计划将于下周二（7月28日）推出。

It will allow consumers to pay for their purchases over multi-year periods, including iPhones, iPads, Macs, and Apple Watches.  
该计划将允许消费者在多年期内分期付款购买产品，包括iPhone、iPad、Mac和Apple Watch。

Bloomberg writes that the lease term for iPhones and Apple Watches will be up to 24 months, while leases for Macs and iPads will be up to 36 months.  
彭博社称，iPhone和Apple Watch的租赁期最长可达24个月，而Mac和iPad的租赁期最长可达36个月。

The devices can either be kept or returned at the end of the leasing period, while upgrades to new devices will also be available (hence the name of the program).  
租赁期满后，用户可以选择保留设备或归还，同时也可以升级到新款设备（因此得名“Apple Upgrade”）。

The report notes vaguely that, in some cases, “transactions will incur an additional fee.”  
报道含糊地指出，在某些情况下，“交易将产生额外费用”。

Apple already has a similar program called iPhone Upgrade, although the company plans to stop allowing new customer sign-ups to instead build out the broader, more inclusive Apple Upgrade program, the report said.  
报道称，苹果已有一个名为“iPhone Upgrade”的类似计划，但公司计划停止新用户注册，转而推广范围更广、更包容的“Apple Upgrade”计划。

A leasing program is an obvious strategy for Apple at this point. The iPhone maker has been battling supply chain issues wrought by “RAMageddon” — the industry-wide shortage of memory chips that is driving up the price of hardware.  
推出租赁计划对苹果而言是显而易见的策略。这家iPhone制造商一直受“内存芯片灾难”引发的供应链问题困扰——即全行业内存芯片短缺推高了硬件价格。

Those shortages have been driven largely by the AI industry, which is gobbling up so much memory that it’s not leaving much for the rest of us.  
这些短缺主要由AI行业驱动，该行业吞噬了大量内存，以至于留给其他领域的所剩无几。

To deal with these issues, Apple recently announced that it would be raising prices, and Upgrade clearly seems designed to make those hiked prices more palatable to consumers.  
为应对这些问题，苹果最近宣布将提高产品价格，而“Upgrade”计划显然旨在让消费者更容易接受涨价。

TechCrunch reached out to Apple and Klarna for more information.  
TechCrunch已联系苹果和克拉纳以获取更多信息。

Overall, the new program seems like a shrewd move for Apple, which is currently facing a hectic transitional period. As new CEO John Ternus takes the reins, the company also entered into a legal battle with AI startup superstar OpenAI — suing the company for alleged trade theft.  
总体而言，这项新计划对苹果来说似乎是一个精明的举措。目前，苹果正面临动荡的过渡期。随着新任CEO约翰·特努斯接任，公司还与AI明星初创公司OpenAI卷入法律纠纷——因涉嫌商业盗窃而起诉该公司。

In short: The company has its hands full, and anything that can shore up sales and keep the business headed in the right direction is worth trying.  
简而言之：苹果公司事务繁忙，任何能提振销售、保持业务正确方向的做法都值得尝试。
