China’s AI models have Trump’s AI world at war with itself  
中国AI模型让特朗普的AI圈陷入内斗

Last weekend, several current and former advisers to President Donald Trump on AI publicly lobbed insults at the country’s leading AI companies. David Sacks branded Anthropic’s models “lobotomized” and “woke.” Emil Michael, a top Pentagon official, called OpenAI’s new head of strategic futures a “supreme village idiot.”  
上周末，几位现任和前任特朗普总统的AI顾问公开抨击了该国领先的AI公司。大卫·萨克斯称Anthropic的模型“被切除脑叶”且“觉醒”。五角大楼高官埃米尔·迈克尔称OpenAI新任战略未来负责人为“ supreme village idiot”。

It began because no one can agree on what to do about Kimi, a free, open-source model that Chinese AI company Moonshot launched last week. It appears to rival the intelligence of models from OpenAI and Anthropic, which are very much not free. Every time a new smart, free model from China gets released, US companies see less reason to fork out money for models from Anthropic or OpenAI. That’s creating economic and political problems for the president—and dividing the top AI strategists in his orbit.  
争论源于无人能就“Kimi”达成一致。这是中国AI公司Moonshot上周推出的免费开源模型，其智能似乎可与OpenAI和Anthropic的付费模型媲美。每次中国发布新的智能免费模型，美国公司就更少理由为Anthropic或OpenAI的模型付费。这为总统制造了经济和政治难题——并分裂了他周围的顶级AI战略家。

Inside Chicago’s surveillance panopticon  
深入芝加哥的监控全景监狱
Early on the morning of September 2, 2024, four people were shot and killed on a westbound train in Chicago. Police swiftly activated a digital dragnet—a surveillance network that connects thousands of cameras across the city—and arrested the suspect just 90 minutes later.  
2024年9月2日凌晨，芝加哥一列西行火车上四人被枪杀。警方迅速启动数字天网——一个连接全市数千摄像头的监控网络——并在90分钟内逮捕了嫌疑人。
Law enforcement and security advocates say this vast monitoring system protects public safety and works well. But activists and many residents say it's a surveillance panopticon that creates a chilling effect on behavior and violates guarantees of privacy and free speech.  
执法和安全倡导者称，这一庞大监控系统保护公共安全且运作良好。但活动人士和许多居民称其为监控全景监狱，对行为产生寒蝉效应，并侵犯隐私和言论自由保障。
