# Title: Taylor Farms Spent Big on MAGA and Anti-Regulatory Lobbying Before Diarrhea Outbreak  
**标题：腹泻爆发前，Taylor Farms 大笔投入 MAGA 及反监管游说**

Content:  
**内容：**

The lettuce supplier at the center of the turbo diarrhea outbreak has spent millions of dollars to sway sentiment around food regulation and elect Donald Trump and other MAGA Republicans, according to public filings.  
**据公开文件显示，这场“喷射性腹泻”爆发的核心生菜供应商曾花费数百万美元，以影响公众对食品监管的看法，并支持唐纳德·特朗普及其他 MAGA 共和党人当选。**

Taylor Farms donated more than $2 million to conservative political groups in 2025, according to Federal Election Commission filings. That includes a $1 million donation to MAGA Inc., the Trump-centric super PAC, and $1.1 million to other super PACs dedicated to electing Republicans.  
**根据联邦选举委员会文件，Taylor Farms 在 2025 年向保守派政治团体捐赠了超过 200 万美元。其中包括向亲特朗普的超级政治行动委员会（super PAC）MAGA Inc. 捐赠 100 万美元，以及向其他致力于选举共和党人的超级政治行动委员会捐赠 110 万美元。**

From 2020 through the end of 2024, the company donated more than $1.6 million to conservative PACs, including a total of $850,000 to AFP Action, an anti-regulation super PAC with ties to the Koch network.  
**从 2020 年到 2024 年底，该公司向保守派政治行动委员会（PAC）捐赠了超过 160 万美元，其中包括向与科赫家族网络有关联的反监管超级政治行动委员会 AFP Action 总计捐赠 85 万美元。**

Bruce Taylor, the chairman and CEO of Taylor Farms, is also a longtime donor to Republican causes. Since 2020, Taylor has personally donated more than $900,000 to Republican PACs and candidates, including contributions to Texas senator John Cornyn and Tennessee senator Marsha Blackburn.  
**Taylor Farms 的董事长兼首席执行官 Bruce Taylor 也是共和党事业的长期捐赠者。自 2020 年以来，Taylor 个人已向共和党政治行动委员会和候选人捐赠超过 90 万美元，包括向得克萨斯州参议员 John Cornyn 和田纳西州参议员 Marsha Blackburn 捐款。**

Like many corporate giants, Taylor Farms has also spent big on lobbying related to industry regulation. In early 2025, it hired lobbyists at the firm Sidley Austin to advocate on behalf of the company on “regulation of food safety,” according to disclosure documents. Since then, the company has spent $810,000 on its lobbying efforts.  
**与许多企业巨头一样，Taylor Farms 在行业监管相关的游说上也投入巨资。根据披露文件，该公司于 2025 年初聘请了 Sidley Austin 律师事务所的游说人员，代表公司就“食品安全监管”问题进行游说。此后，该公司已花费 81 万美元用于其游说工作。**

Marion Nestle, a food policy expert, said that Taylor Farms’ donations are part of a wider problem in the American food industry: that multibillion-dollar companies use their financial resources to push policies that would reduce food safety regulation and accountability when outbreaks occur.  
**食品政策专家 Marion Nestle 表示，Taylor Farms 的捐赠是美国食品行业更广泛问题的一部分：即价值数十亿美元的公司利用其财力推动减少食品安全监管和爆发事故时问责制的政策。**

“The FDA has regulations in place for what food producers are supposed to be doing, but nobody wants to do it—because it's expensive and difficult,” Nestle told WIRED.  
**“FDA 已经制定了食品生产商应遵守的法规，但没人愿意执行——因为这既昂贵又困难，”Nestle 在接受 WIRED 采访时表示。**

In a post on X, the US Department of Health and Human Services emphasized that Taylor Farms’ political donations did not influence the Trump administration’s response to the cyclospora outbreak.  
**美国卫生与公众服务部在 X 上的一篇帖子中强调，Taylor Farms 的政治捐款并未影响特朗普政府对环孢子虫爆发的应对。**

“This is how the FAKE NEWS works: imply collusion, ignore the facts,” the HHS account posted on Monday. “Nothing influences our decisions except science and the safety of the American people.”  
**“这就是假新闻的运作方式：暗示串通，无视事实，”HHS 账户周一发布帖子称。“除了科学和美国人民的安全，没有任何东西会影响我们的决定。”**

Taylor Farms has apparently taken issue with the CDC’s and FDA’s handling of the investigation into the current cyclospora outbreak, which has sickened thousands of people across multiple states.  
**Taylor Farms 显然对 CDC 和 FDA 处理当前环孢子虫爆发调查的方式有异议，该爆发已使多个州数千人患病。**

The company recently claimed that the FDA “apologized” after announcing a false-positive test of a sample of Taylor Farms’ lettuce; the FDA told reporters that no, actually, an official apology never happened. (The company did not respond to WIRED’s requests for comment.)  
**该公司最近声称，FDA 在宣布对 Taylor Farms 生菜样本的检测呈假阳性后“道了歉”；但 FDA 告诉记者，实际上，官方从未道歉。（该公司未回应 WIRED 的置评请求。）**

Prior to the false positive and the non-apology, Taylor Farms executives met with White House and FDA officials to discuss what a spokesperson for the company called “shortfalls” in the government agencies’ response.  
**在假阳性结果和所谓的“未道歉”事件之前，Taylor Farms 高管曾与白宫和 FDA 官员会面，讨论该公司发言人所说的政府机构应对中的“不足”。**

The New York Times reported that the meeting followed Taylor Farms’ recent hire of Trent Morse, the former deputy director of the White House Presidential Personnel Office, who left the Trump administration to start a government relations firm in September.  
**《纽约时报》报道称，此次会议发生在 Taylor Farms 最近雇佣 Trent Morse 之后。Morse 是白宫人事办公室前副主任，于 9 月离开特朗普政府并创办了一家政府关系公司。**

The recent diarrhea debacle is not the first time that Taylor Farms, which brings in roughly $7 billion in annual revenue, has drawn the focus of federal regulators.  
**这家年收入约 70 亿美元的 Taylor Farms 最近引发的腹泻灾难，并非首次引起联邦监管机构的关注。**

In 2013, the FDA named Taylor Farms as a source of a cyclospora outbreak that sickened hundreds. In 2015, the company voluntarily recalled multiple products containing celery because they may have been contaminated with E. coli.  
**2013 年，FDA 将 Taylor Farms 指认为导致数百人患病的环孢子虫爆发源头。2015 年，该公司自愿召回多种含芹子的产品，因为它们可能被大肠杆菌污染。**

And in 2024, the company recalled certain yellow onions as part of an E. coli outbreak linked to McDonald’s Quarter Pounders. Following the outbreak, CBS News reported that FDA inspectors found dozens of violations at the Colorado Taylor Farms facility linked to the Quarter Pounder outbreak, including minimal handwashing and dirty equipment.  
**2024 年，该公司召回部分黄洋葱，因其与麦当劳“四分之一磅汉堡”相关的大肠杆菌爆发有关。爆发后，CBS 新闻报道称，FDA 检查员在与“四分之一磅汉堡”爆发相关的科罗拉多州 Taylor Farms 工厂发现了数十项违规行为，包括洗手不充分和设备脏污。**

Since 2025, the FDA has inspected 18 American plants affiliated with Taylor Farms. Three resulted in “voluntary action indicated,” which means that while violations were found, they weren’t severe enough to warrant mandatory enforcement.  
**自 2025 年以来，FDA 已检查了 18 家与 Taylor Farms 有关的美国工厂。其中三次检查结果为“建议自愿整改”，这意味着虽然发现了违规行为，但严重程度不足以启动强制执法。**

The Occupational Safety and Health Administration has cited at least 21 violations in Taylor Farms facilities across the US since the start of 2025.  
**自 2025 年初以来，职业安全与健康管理局（OSHA）已在美国各地的 Taylor Farms 设施中至少发现 21 项违规行为。**

In one particularly gruesome case, the Department of Labor fined a New Jersey facility $1.1 million due to violations. The May 2025 inspection came after an employee died after sustaining injuries cleaning an industrial blancher at the facility.  
**在一个特别惨烈的案例中，劳工部因违规行为向一家新泽西州工厂罚款 110 万美元。2025 年 5 月的这次检查发生在一名员工在该工厂清洁工业漂白机时受伤死亡之后。**
