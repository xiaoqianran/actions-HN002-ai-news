**Title: Einride bets $38M on EV charging as it scales electric trucking**  
**标题：瑞典电动卡车公司Einride斥资3800万美元布局EV充电业务以扩大规模**

Swedish electric and autonomous trucking company Einride has agreed to acquire EV charging startup Flipturn in an all-stock deal worth $38 million. The acquisition — Einride’s first as a publicly traded company — will expand its EV-charging ecosystem as it works to scale its electric trucking business. The deal is expected to close this month.  
瑞典电动及自动驾驶卡车公司Einride已同意以全股票交易方式收购EV充电初创企业Flipturn，交易价值3800万美元。此次收购——作为Einride上市后的首笔收购——将扩展其EV充电生态系统，以助力其电动卡车业务的规模扩张。该交易预计本月完成。

Flipturn, which employs 17 people, will continue operating with the same team, a company spokesperson confirmed. Flipturn, founded in 2022, raised $15.5 million in venture capital to build an EV-charging软件平台 that can communicate with any EV charger over the Open Charge Point Protocol, the industry standard for communication between EV chargers and management systems. The software monitors the health of charging infrastructure; pulls in data such as vehicle battery levels, driving history, and routes to help companies better manage charging; and integrates with on-site solar and energy-storage systems.  
Flipturn拥有17名员工，将继续由原团队运营，公司发言人确认。成立于2022年的Flipturn曾获得1550万美元风险投资，用于开发一款EV充电软件平台，该平台可通过行业标准协议Open Charge Point Protocol与任何EV充电桩通信。该软件可监控充电基础设施状态；整合车辆电池电量、行驶历史和路线等数据，帮助企业优化充电管理；并与现场太阳能及储能系统集成。

Einride CEO Roozbeh Charli said the “acquisition is a decisive step in our U.S. scaling strategy.” The deal will also make Einride a more vertically integrated company, allowing it to offer customers not just electric trucks but also the charging software needed to run them more efficiently and reliably. That’s an attractive, and potentially influential, pitch to future customers that want to add electric big rigs to their fleets but may be apprehensive to do so.  
Einride首席执行官Roozbeh Charli表示：“此次收购是我们美国扩张战略中的关键一步。”该交易还将使Einride成为一家更垂直整合的公司，使其不仅能向客户提供电动卡车，还能提供使其更高效、可靠运行的充电软件。对于希望车队加入电动重卡但可能心存顾虑的未来客户而言，这具有吸引力且可能产生影响的卖点。

Einride is perhaps best known for its unusual-looking cabless autonomous podlike trucks. But its biggest revenue driver is its fleet of 200 heavy-duty electric trucks, which it operates for companies such as Heineken, PepsiCo, and Carlsberg Sweden in Europe, North America, and the United Arab Emirates. The company, which merged with a blank-check company and debuted on Nasdaq in June, recently landed Amazon as a customer.  
Einride或许最广为人知的是其外观独特的无驾驶舱自动驾驶“胶囊”卡车。但其最大收入来源是200辆重型电动卡车车队，该公司为欧洲、北美和阿联酋的喜力、百事可乐和瑞典嘉士伯等企业运营这些车辆。该公司于6月通过与特殊目的收购公司合并在纳斯达克上市，近期还获得了亚马逊作为客户。

Amazon isn’t buying Einride’s trucks; instead, Einride owns and manages the trucks —through its own Saga AI software — which can be used by drivers in Amazon’s Relay freight network. Relay, which launched in 2017, is an app that truck drivers use to book hauling gigs with Amazon.  
亚马逊并未购买Einride的卡车；相反，Einride拥有并管理这些卡车——通过其自研的Saga AI软件——亚马逊Relay货运网络中的司机可使用这些卡车。Relay于2017年推出，是一款供卡车司机预订亚马逊货运任务的应用程序。
