**Title:** Reinforcement Learning-Guided NSGA-II Enhanced with Gray Relational Coefficient for Multi-Objective Optimization: Application to NASDAQ Portfolio Optimization  
**标题：** 基于强化学习引导与灰色关联系数增强的NSGA-II多目标优化方法：应用于纳斯达克投资组合优化

**Authors:** Zhiyuan Wang, Qinxu Ding, Ding Ding, Siying Zhu, Jing Ren, Yue Wang, Chong Hui Tan  
**作者：** 王志远，丁钦旭，丁丁，朱思颖，任婧，王跃，谭崇辉

**Abstract:** In modern financial markets, decision-makers increasingly rely on quantitative methods to navigate complex trade-offs among multiple, often conflicting objectives. This paper addresses constrained multi-objective optimization (MOO) with an application to portfolio optimization for minimizing risk and maximizing return.  
**摘要：** 在现代金融市场中，决策者日益依赖量化方法来应对多个且常相互冲突的目标之间的复杂权衡。本文研究约束多目标优化（MOO），并将其应用于投资组合优化，以实现风险最小化和收益最大化。

To address existing gaps, we propose a novel reinforcement learning (RL)-guided non-dominated sorting genetic algorithm II (NSGA-II) enhanced with gray relational coefficients (GRC), termed RL-NSGA-II-GRC, which combines an RL agent controller and GRC-based selection to improve convergence and diversity of Pareto fronts.  
为填补现有空白，我们提出了一种新颖的、由强化学习（RL）引导的、并增强以灰色关联系数（GRC）的非支配排序遗传算法II（NSGA-II），命名为RL-NSGA-II-GRC。该方法结合了RL智能体控制器和基于GRC的选择机制，以改善帕累托前沿的收敛性和多样性。

The agent adapts evolutionary parameters online using metrics of hypervolume, feasibility, and diversity, while the GRC tournament operator ranks parents via a unified score considering dominance rank, crowding distance, and proximity to ideal reference.  
该智能体通过超体积、可行性和多样性等指标在线自适应调整进化参数，而GRC锦标赛算子则通过一个综合考虑支配等级、拥挤距离和与理想参考点接近度的统一分数对父代进行排序。

We evaluate the framework on the Kursawe and CONSTR benchmarks and a NASDAQ portfolio application. On the benchmarks, RL-NSGA-II-GRC achieves convergence improvements of about 5.8% and 4.4% over NSGA-II, while preserving well-distributed non-dominated solutions.  
我们在Kursawe和CONSTR基准测试以及一个纳斯达克投资组合应用上评估了该框架。在基准测试中，RL-NSGA-II-GRC相比NSGA-II实现了约5.8%和4.4%的收敛性提升，同时保持了分布良好的非支配解集。

In the portfolio application, it produces a smooth, densely populated efficient frontier supporting identification of the maximum Sharpe ratio portfolio (annualized Sharpe =1.92) and utility-optimal portfolios for different risk-aversion levels.  
在投资组合应用中，该方法生成了一个平滑、密集的有效前沿，支持识别最大夏普比率投资组合（年化夏普比率=1.92）以及适用于不同风险厌恶水平的效用最优投资组合。

The main contributions are three-fold: 1) we propose an RL-NSGA-II-GRC method integrating an RL agent into the evolutionary framework to adaptively control parameters via generational feedback;  
主要贡献有三方面：1）我们提出了RL-NSGA-II-GRC方法，将RL智能体集成到进化框架中，通过代际反馈自适应控制参数；

2) we design a GRC-enhanced binary tournament operator providing a comprehensive indicator to guide the search toward the Pareto front;  
2）我们设计了一个GRC增强的二元锦标赛算子，提供一个综合指标以引导搜索朝向帕累托前沿；

3) we demonstrate, on benchmark MOO and a NASDAQ case study, that the method delivers improved convergence and well-populated frontiers supporting actionable insights.  
3）我们在基准MOO测试和纳斯达克案例研究中证明，该方法实现了更好的收敛性并生成了分布密集的前沿，从而支持可操作的见解。
