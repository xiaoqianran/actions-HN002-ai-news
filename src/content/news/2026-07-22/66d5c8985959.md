# Tesla spins up robotaxi pilots in Orlando and Tampa ahead of Q2 earnings
特斯拉在奥兰多和坦帕启动机器人出租车试点，紧邻第二季度财报发布

Tesla has brought an unspecified number of its unsupervised Model Y SUVs to Orlando and Tampa, just one day ahead of the company’s scheduled second-quarter earnings call.
特斯拉已将数量未指定的无人监督 Model Y SUV 引入奥兰多和坦帕，就在公司预定第二季度财报电话会议前一天。

That marks the third city in Florida where Tesla is trialing its nascent robotaxi service, following a small launch in Miami a few weeks ago.
这标志着特斯拉在佛罗里达州试验其初期的机器人出租车服务的第三个城市，此前几周在迈阿密进行了小规模推出。

The two new cities appear to have fairly small operational areas, and Tesla did not offer any further details about the launch.
这两个新城市的运营区域似乎相当小，特斯拉未提供有关推出的更多细节。

As some fans have noticed, Tesla announced autonomous fleets in Dallas and Houston before its first-quarter earnings release but has yet to scale those operations.
正如一些粉丝所指出的，特斯拉在第一季度财报发布前宣布了在达拉斯和休斯顿的自动驾驶车队，但尚未扩大这些运营规模。

The company disbanded its press office years ago.
该公司数年前解散了其新闻办公室。

Tesla has taken a far slower approach to standing up a commercial robotaxi service than it has promised investors.
特斯拉在建立商业机器人出租车服务方面采取了比向投资者承诺的慢得多的方式。

CEO Elon Musk, for instance, said repeatedly that Tesla’s robotaxis would serve half the U.S. population by the end of 2025.
例如，首席执行官埃隆·马斯克曾多次表示，特斯拉的机器人出租车到2025年底将为美国一半人口提供服务。

Musk offered more metered comments earlier this year on Tesla’s first-quarter call.
今年早些时候，马斯克在特斯拉第一季度电话会议上发表了更为谨慎的评论。

But the company may get a big lift from the Trump administration, as last month the Department of Transportation proposed a rule change that would no longer require brake pedals be built into cars that are designed to be autonomous.
但该公司可能从特朗普政府获得重大推动，因为上月交通部提议修改规则，不再要求在设计为自动驾驶的汽车中安装刹车踏板。

If adopted, that could clear the way for Tesla to try and deploy the dozens of two-seater Cybercabs that it has been staging in cities across the country.
如果该提议被采纳，可能会为特斯拉尝试部署数十辆两座 Cybercab 扫清道路，这些车辆已在全美各城市进行展示。
