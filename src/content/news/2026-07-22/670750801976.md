**Title: Generative Ontology Induction: Domain-Agnostic Schema Discovery from Document Corpora Using Large Language Models**  
**标题：生成式本体归纳：基于大语言模型的文档语料领域无关模式发现**

**Computer Science > Artificial Intelligence arXiv:2607.16201 (cs) [Submitted on 1 May 2026] Title:Generative Ontology Induction: Domain-Agnostic Schema Discovery from Document Corpora Using Large Language Models Authors:Sergei Sergienko**  
**计算机科学 > 人工智能 arXiv:2607.16201 (cs) [提交于 2026年5月1日] 标题：生成式本体归纳：基于大语言模型的文档语料领域无关模式发现 作者：谢尔盖·塞尔吉延科**

**Abstract:Ontology engineering remains a critical bottleneck in knowledge-intensive AI systems. Existing automated approaches either depend on predefined schemas, operate within narrow domains, or produce unstructured outputs unsuitable for downstream pipelines. We introduce Generative Ontology Induction (GOI), a domain-agnostic framework that induces a generative blueprint - entities, dimensions, properties, relationships, and constraints - from a corpus of examples and exports it as a typed graph (six node types, seven edge types) in YAML/JSON. We introduce the Node Coverage Score, a novel evaluation metric that measures the fraction of structural ontology nodes (classes, properties, and dimensions) appearing in generated outputs. A controlled generative validation on four contrasting ontologies - a familiar Software Services Invoice schema, a custom Job Description Ontology, a confidential Pain-Management Clinical Visit Record Ontology, and a Professional Services Contract & Statement of Work Ontology - shows that GOI-prompted generation covers 95-100% of the structural backbone in every case; a generic three-field template holds at 97.8% on the invoice schema but drops to 52.2% on the Job Description Ontology, 62.2% on the Pain-Management ontology, and 78.3% on the Professional Services Contract ontology. The structural coverage holds regardless of how familiar the document type is to the model.**  
**摘要：本体工程一直是知识密集型AI系统的关键瓶颈。现有自动化方法要么依赖预定义模式，要么在狭窄领域内运行，要么产生不适合下游流水线的非结构化输出。我们提出了生成式本体归纳（GOI），一个领域无关的框架，它从示例语料中归纳生成蓝图——包括实体、维度、属性、关系和约束——并将其导出为类型化图（六种节点类型，七种边类型），格式为YAML/JSON。我们引入了节点覆盖率分数，一种新颖的评估指标，用于衡量生成输出中出现的结构本体节点（类、属性和维度）的比例。在四个对比本体上的受控生成验证——一个熟悉的软件服务发票模式、一个自定义的职位描述本体、一个机密的疼痛管理临床访问记录本体，以及一个专业服务合同和工作说明书本体——表明GOI提示生成在每种情况下覆盖了95-100%的结构骨干；一个通用三字段模板在发票模式上达到97.8%，但在职位描述本体上降至52.2%，在疼痛管理本体上为62.2%，在专业服务合同本体上为78.3%。无论文档类型对模型而言多么陌生，结构覆盖率都保持稳定。**
