**Title: The Light Flip Is the Stylish Dumb Flip Phone of Your Dreams**  
标题：Light Flip：你梦想中的时尚傻瓜翻盖手机

---

**Content:**  
Save this storySave this storyIt turns out that the Light Phone III—the dumbphone with pretty hardware, like an all-metal build and a monochrome OLED touchscreen—is “too similar to a smartphone.”  
原来，Light Phone III——这款拥有精致硬件（如全金属机身和单色OLED触摸屏）的傻瓜手机——“与智能手机过于相似”。

That's one of the pieces of feedback that Brooklyn-based Light Phone has received in the past year since it launched the device, even if it doesn't have access to anywhere near the number of apps your smartphone does.  
这是布鲁克林-based Light Phone在过去一年推出该设备后收到的反馈之一，尽管它无法访问智能手机那么多的应用程序。

It's why the company is now introducing the Light Flip—a dumb flip phone with no front screen that looks like a sleeker version of Motorola or Ericsson handsets from the late '90s and early aughts.  
因此，该公司现在推出了Light Flip——一款没有前置屏幕的傻瓜翻盖手机，外观比90年代末和21世纪初的摩托罗拉或爱立信手机更时尚。

“They don't want to be seen as using a smartphone,” says Kaiwei Tang, CEO and cofounder of Light Phone, talking about the more than two dozen Gen Z consumers the company interviewed.  
“他们不想被看作在使用智能手机，”Light Phone的首席执行官兼联合创始人唐凯威（Kaiwei Tang）在谈到公司采访的二十多位Z世代消费者时表示。

But the dumb flip phones currently on the market—made by manufacturers like HMD or TCL—don't meet the bar. Tang says that many of the users he's spoken with have had to replace their devices quite often because they break easily.  
但市场上现有的傻瓜翻盖手机——由HMD或TCL等制造商生产——未能达到标准。唐表示，许多他交谈过的用户不得不频繁更换设备，因为它们容易损坏。

Some who opt for older devices say that those devices aren't certified to work on the major cell networks.  
一些选择老款设备的人说，这些设备未获认证可在主要蜂窝网络上使用。

“They describe their flip phone as ugly, stupid, no one is updating the software, some still have social media, no one is repairing the phone," says Tang. “But they stick with it.”  
“他们形容自己的翻盖手机丑陋、愚蠢，没人更新软件，有些仍有社交媒体，没人修理手机，”唐说。“但他们仍坚持使用。”

His cofounder, Joe Hollier, says these consumers know it's frivolous, but they're “just so anti–Big Tech.”  
他的联合创始人乔·霍利尔（Joe Hollier）表示，这些消费者知道这很轻率，但他们“就是如此反对大科技公司”。

The Light Flip is also designed to be more accessible to the masses. Light Phone says RAM shortages are forcing it to raise the price of the Light Phone III from $699 to $899. (The device already saw price increases last year due to tariffs.)  
Light Flip also设计得更亲民。Light Phone表示，内存短缺迫使其将Light Phone III的价格从699美元提高到899美元。（该设备去年已因关税上涨。）

The Light Flip, on the other hand, costs $299. It ships in April of 2027, and you can reserve a unit now.  
而Light Flip售价为299美元。它将于2027年4月发货，现在即可预订。

The company is introducing a 24-month contract option that lets you buy the Flip for $39 per month, which includes a service plan powered by AT&T and T-Mobile's network with 1 GB of data and unlimited calls and texts. (Yes, that means Light Phone is also a mobile virtual network operator in the US, Canada, and the UK.)  
公司推出24个月合约计划，每月39美元即可购买Flip，包含由AT&T和T-Mobile网络支持的套餐，含1GB数据和无限通话及短信。（是的，这意味着Light Phone在美国、加拿大和英国也是一家移动虚拟网络运营商。）

There's also a plan for the Light Phone III at $59 per month for anyone who can't stomach the near-$1,000 price.  
对于无法承受近1000美元价格的人，Light Phone III也有每月59美元的套餐。

“I think people are just so accustomed to the subscription model when it comes to cell phones that we at least have to offer it,” Hollier says.  
“我认为人们在手机方面已经非常习惯订阅模式，所以我们至少必须提供它，”霍利尔说。

The Light Flip doesn't have a touchscreen; instead, users have to rely on a D-pad, three function keys, a confirm and cancel button, and old-school T9 typing with predictive text.  
Light Flip没有触摸屏；相反，用户必须依赖方向键、三个功能键、确认和取消按钮，以及带预测文本的复古T9输入法。

All the “tools” from the Light Phone III—what Light Phone calls its apps on its LightOS platform—will work when the Light Flip launches next year.  
Light Phone III的所有“工具”——Light Phone对其LightOS平台上的应用的称呼——将在明年Light Flip推出时正常工作。

It's a pretty limited set, with tools like Phone, Alarm, Calculator, Calendar, and Directions. However, in May, the company announced a software development kit that enables third-party developers to create new tools for the platform, expanding the possibilities.  
这是一个相当有限的集合，包括电话、闹钟、计算器、日历和导航等工具。然而，今年5月，该公司宣布了一个软件开发工具包，使第三方开发者能为该平台创建新工具，扩展可能性。

These may need tweaks to perform just as well on the Light Flip.  
这些工具可能需要调整才能在Light Flip上同样良好运行。

There's USB-C, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, 5G connectivity, Nano SIM and eSIM support, even a headphone jack, a perk missing on the pricier Light Phone III. (There is no NFC.)  
它配备USB-C、蓝牙、Wi-Fi、5G连接、Nano SIM和eSIM支持，甚至还有耳机插孔——这是更贵的Light Phone III所缺少的。（没有NFC。）

On the front, the lack of a screen keeps up with the distraction-free ethos—it's just a notification LED that flashes when new alerts come through.  
正面没有屏幕，符合无干扰理念——只有一条新通知到达时闪烁的LED指示灯。

There's a 12-MP camera on the back, and a replaceable 1,800-mAh battery. Flip it open and there's a 2.8-inch OLED display, stereo speakers, and two microphones.  
背面有1200万像素摄像头，电池可更换，容量为1800mAh。翻开手机，可以看到2.8英寸OLED显示屏、立体声扬声器和两个麦克风。

The whole device is plastic, unlike its all-metal sibling, which helps keep the cost down. That also allows the company to offer it in six colors: black, navy, red, pink, yellow, and light gray. It's IP54 water- and dust-resistant.  
整个设备是塑料材质，不同于其全金属兄弟机型，这有助于降低成本。这也使公司能提供六种颜色：黑色、海军蓝、红色、粉色、黄色和浅灰色。它具备IP54防水防尘等级。

Tang points to the recent Summer of Ludd festival that took place in New York City, where he and other Light Phone members were present as participants. Event-goers booed people who pulled out their smartphones to record video.  
唐提到了最近在纽约市举行的“勒德分子之夏”（Summer of Ludd）节，他和Light Phone其他成员作为参与者出席。活动参与者对拿出智能手机录像的人发出嘘声。

“The sentiment of pushing back on smartphones is so strong; they don't like AI,” he says. “They want these different form factors that make them feel proud or completely separated from smartphone app stores. It makes us feel encouraged that this is exactly what we've wanted to do for 10 years, and now it's getting bigger and bigger and younger and younger.”  
“抵制智能手机的情绪如此强烈；他们不喜欢人工智能，”他说。“他们想要这些不同的形态，让他们感到自豪或与智能手机应用商店完全分离。这让我们备受鼓舞，这正是我们10年来想做的事，而现在它正变得越来越大、越来越年轻。”

It's no accident that Light Phone's new flippy handset is being announced a day before Samsung's Galaxy Unpacked event, where the mobile giant is expected to debut its latest folding smartphones.  
Light Phone的新翻盖手机在三星Galaxy Unpacked活动前一天宣布并非偶然，预计这家移动巨头将在该活动上推出其最新的折叠智能手机。

Just a month ago, retro gaming brand Commodore announced the Callback 8020, its own version of a smart flip phone resembling an old-school Nokia. The company is channeling the same digital well-being mentality as Light Phone, except Commodore's device runs an operating system that can still download modern-day Android apps.  
仅仅一个月前，复古游戏品牌Commodore宣布了Callback 8020，其自家版本的智能翻盖手机，外观类似老式诺基亚。该公司与Light Phone秉持相同的数字健康理念，但Commodore的设备运行的系统仍可下载现代Android应用。

The catch is that social media apps and browsers are blocked; strictly utilitarian apps are available, like Spotify, Uber, and Google Maps. It costs $399 and will ship later this year.  
但社交应用和浏览器被屏蔽；仅提供严格实用的应用，如Spotify、Uber和谷歌地图。售价399美元，将于今年晚些时候发货。

Tang and Hollier were happy to see Commodore's new handset. “They took a different approach to us by doubling down on the retro side, and I think that's awesome,” Hollier says. “We've always said we want more options for people, and the more normalized non-smartphones become, the easier it will be for everyone to make a transition.”  
唐和霍利尔很高兴看到Commodore的新手机。“他们通过加倍强调复古方面采取了与我们不同的方法，我认为这太棒了，”霍利尔说。“我们一直说，我们想为人们提供更多选择，而非智能手机越常态化，每个人过渡起来就越容易。”
