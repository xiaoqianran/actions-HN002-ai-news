# Committed Before Reasoning: Behavioral Reproduction and Preliminary Activation-Level Evidence of Answer Pre-Commitment in an Open-Weight LLM

决策先于推理：开源大语言模型中答案预先承诺的行为复现与初步激活层证据

**Authors:** Heejin Jo  
**作者：** Heejin Jo

Chat models sometimes commit to an answer and then produce reasoning that justifies it rather than deriving it -- even when the answer contradicts a task premise. We study a minimal probe: "I want to wash my car. The car wash is 100 meters away. Should I walk or drive?" Only drive works (the car must be at the car wash), yet models overwhelmingly recommend walking.

聊天模型有时会先承诺一个答案，然后生成为其辩护的推理，而非从前提推导出答案——即使该答案与任务前提矛盾。我们研究了一个最小化探测问题：“我想洗车。洗车店在100米外。我应该走路还是开车？”只有开车可行（车必须在洗车店），但模型绝大多数推荐走路。

(1) Behavioral reproduction: on Qwen3-8B across five system-prompt conditions (210 rollouts), the wrong commitment occurs in 85-100% of sampled rollouts per condition and 100% of greedy rollouts, in both thinking and non-thinking modes; a 4,096-token thinking budget does not repair it.

（1）行为复现：在Qwen3-8B上，跨五个系统提示条件（210次滚动生成），每个条件的采样滚动中错误承诺发生率为85-100%，贪婪滚动中为100%，在思考和非思考模式下均如此；4,096个标记的思考预算无法修复此问题。

(2) Preliminary activation-level evidence: probing hidden states with a pretrained, training-free activation oracle (no task-specific probe training) at positions before the answer text is emitted, "walk" read-outs exceed a neutral-context baseline (68% vs. 17%; walk-committing rollouts p=.005, drive-committing rollouts p=.005, Fisher exact) -- notably, rollouts that eventually answer drive also read as walk-leaning before commitment (5/6).

（2）初步激活层证据：使用预训练、无需训练的激活预言机（无任务特定探测训练）在答案文本发出前的位置探测隐藏状态，“走路”的读出超过中性上下文基线（68% vs. 17%；走路承诺滚动 p=.005，开车承诺滚动 p=.005，Fisher精确检验）——值得注意的是，最终回答开车的滚动在承诺前也显示为走路倾向（5/6）。

The oracle's default on unrelated content is "drive" (83%), so the read-outs are not lexical bias; stratifying by literal walk/drive occurrence shows they are not text recovery either (spans containing "drive" still read out walk; in balanced lexical fields, per-rollout walk-majorities beat a per-prompt neutral baseline 15/22 vs. 1/8, p=.01; drive-committing rollouts 6/6, p=.002).

预言机在不相关内容上的默认输出为“开车”（83%），因此读出并非词汇偏差；按字面“走路/开车”出现进行分层显示它们也不是文本恢复（包含“开车”的片段仍读出走路；在平衡词汇场中，每轮滚动的走路多数击败每提示中性基线15/22 vs. 1/8，p=.01；开车承诺滚动6/6，p=.002）。

Samples are small and the within-rollout positional gradient is not significant (p=.34); we frame these results as preliminary.

样本量小且滚动内位置梯度不显著（p=.34）；我们将这些结果视为初步证据。

(3) Methodological: with fixed oracle, activations, and positions, question wording alone moves a positive control from 2/16 (open question) to 11/16 (closed); negative oracle results are uninterpretable without per-wording positive controls.

（3）方法学：使用固定预言机、激活和位置，仅问题措辞就将阳性对照从2/16（开放问题）移至11/16（封闭问题）；若无每措辞阳性对照，阴性预言机结果无法解释。
