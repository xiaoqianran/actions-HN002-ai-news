# The JEPA Predictor: A Transferable Operator for Occluded Feature Completion  
# JEPA预测器：一种用于遮挡特征补全的可迁移算子  

**Authors:** William Nguyen, Christopher Nguyen  
**作者：** William Nguyen, Christopher Nguyen  

**Abstract:**  
**摘要：**  

Joint-Embedding Predictive Architectures (JEPAs) train a predictor jointly with their encoder, but downstream deployment discards the predictor and reads features from the encoder alone. The predictor is, by construction, a learned operator from visible-context features to features at masked positions, the structure a partial-view classifier needs.  
联合嵌入预测架构（JEPA）与其编码器联合训练一个预测器，但下游部署时会丢弃该预测器，仅从编码器读取特征。根据其设计，该预测器是一个从可见上下文特征到掩码位置特征的已学习算子，这正是部分视图分类器所需的结构。  

We show that this operator is portable across encoder families. We first establish that, at heavy mask, retaining the frozen predictor on a JEPA encoder substantially closes the accuracy gap against the strongest non-JEPA discriminative baselines.  
我们证明该算子在编码器家族之间具有可移植性。首先，我们证实，在重度掩码条件下，在JEPA编码器上保留冻结的预测器能显著缩小与最强非JEPA判别基线之间的准确率差距。  

We then bolt the frozen predictors of I-JEPA and V-JEPA 2 onto four non-JEPA hosts (CLIP, DINOv3, DINOv2, MAE) through a single linear projection between feature spaces, fit in closed form on 500 ImageNet-1k images.  
随后，我们通过特征空间间单一的线性投影（在500张ImageNet-1k图像上闭式拟合），将I-JEPA和V-JEPA 2的冻结预测器“嫁接”到四个非JEPA主干（CLIP、DINOv3、DINOv2、MAE）上。  

Across both ImageNet-9 and Stanford Dogs and across three mask fractions, the lift over each host's masked-encoder baseline grows monotonically with the mask fraction K in every host-donor pair.  
在ImageNet-9和Stanford Dogs数据集上，以及三个掩码比例下，相对于每个主干的掩码编码器基线，提升效果在所有主干-供体配对中均随掩码比例K单调增长。  

CLIP paired with the I-JEPA predictor recovers most of the accuracy that masking removed on ImageNet-9 at heavy occlusion, and lifts fine-grained Stanford Dogs from 15.9% to 52.1% (+36 pp).  
CLIP与I-JEPA预测器配对后，在重度遮挡下恢复了掩码在ImageNet-9上移除的大部分准确率，并将细粒度Stanford Dogs数据集的准确率从15.9%提升至52.1%（+36个百分点）。  

The mechanism is identifiable: the projection pays a fixed cost on visible patches and the predictor provides a growing benefit on masked patches; the benefit dominates the heavy-occlusion regime.  
其机制可识别：投影在可见块上付出固定代价，而预测器在掩码块上提供递增收益；在重度遮挡 regime 中，收益占主导地位。  

At low K on fine-grained classification the projection cost exceeds the benefit, defining the boundary where the linear bridge breaks down.  
在细粒度分类中，当K较低时，投影代价超过收益，这定义了线性桥梁失效的边界。  

The frozen JEPA predictor functions as a portable operator for occluded feature completion across encoder families, requiring no retraining of either model while fitting matched linear probes per mask fraction.  
冻结的JEPA预测器作为跨编码器家族的遮挡特征补全可移植算子，无需对任一模型进行重训练，仅需为每个掩码比例拟合匹配的线性探针即可。
