# Title: Nearly every Kindle is steeply discounted at Best Buy  
**标题：几乎所有 Kindle 型号在百思买大幅降价**

---

As part of its Black Friday in July sale, Best Buy has brought the Kindle Paperwhite back down to its all-time low of $124.99 ($35 off). It’s the Kindle we recommend for most people because it has a bunch of features. It’s waterproof and features a sharp 300ppi, 7-inch display, it offers an adjustable warm light for reading at night, and it has great battery life that can last for more than a month on a single charge.  
作为“七月黑五”促销活动的一部分，百思买将 Kindle Paperwhite 的价格重新降至历史最低价 124.99 美元（立减 35 美元）。这是我们向大多数人推荐的 Kindle，因为它功能丰富：防水、配备清晰的 300ppi 7 英寸显示屏、可调节暖光适合夜间阅读，并且续航能力出色，单次充电可使用超过一个月。

---

Best Buy has also discounted nearly every other Kindle model. The Kindle Kids is a good deal even for adults. It’s down to $99.99 ($30 off), and includes the same hardware as the standard Kindle, but with some extras: a kid-friendly cover, no ads, a two-year worry-free guarantee, and six months of Amazon Kids Plus. If you read comics, the Kindle Colorsoft is also on sale starting at $159.99 ($90 off). It shares many of the Paperwhite’s strengths but adds a color E Ink display that displays color at up to 150ppi. The Kindle Colorsoft Signature Edition, which has 32GB of storage and wireless charging, is on sale for $219.99 ($60 off).  
百思买还几乎对所有其他 Kindle 型号进行了降价。Kindle Kids 即使对成年人来说也是划算的选择。降价至 99.99 美元（立减 30 美元），硬件与标准版 Kindle 相同，但额外附赠：儿童友好保护套、无广告、两年无忧保修和六个月的 Amazon Kids Plus 服务。如果你阅读漫画，Kindle Colorsoft 也参与促销，起价 159.99 美元（立减 90 美元）。它拥有 Paperwhite 的许多优点，但增加了彩色电子墨水屏，色彩分辨率高达 150ppi。配备 32GB 存储和无线充电的 Kindle Colorsoft Signature Edition 现价 219.99 美元（立减 60 美元）。

---

If you want an e-reader that doubles as a digital notebook, the Kindle Scribe and Kindle Scribe Colorsoft are worth considering. They’re on sale starting at $379.99 ($120 off) and $479.99 ($150 off), respectively. Each offers an 11-inch display with an included stylus for writing notes, annotating Kindle books, and marking documents. They also include notebook templates and AI-powered tools that can summarize notes or clean up messy handwriting. The biggest difference is that the Scribe Colorsoft adds a color E Ink display, making it a better choice for reviewing color documents or organizing notes with different colors.  
如果你想要一款兼具数字笔记本功能的电子书阅读器，Kindle Scribe 和 Kindle Scribe Colorsoft 值得考虑。它们分别降价至 379.99 美元（立减 120 美元）和 479.99 美元（立减 150 美元）。两者均配备 11 英寸显示屏，并附带手写笔，可用于书写笔记、标注 Kindle 书籍和标记文档。它们还提供笔记本模板和 AI 驱动工具，可总结笔记或整理潦草字迹。最大区别在于 Scribe Colorsoft 增加了彩色电子墨水屏，使其更适合审阅彩色文档或用不同颜色整理笔记。

Now through August 28th, the Google Store is throwing in a free Pixel Watch 2 when you buy a refurbished Google Pixel 7 with 128GB of storage for $329. The deal also includes a free Pixel 7 case. Though both devices are a few years old, the Pixel 7 boasts a spacious 90Hz OLED display, an IP68 rating for full water and dust resistance, and wireless charging. As for the Pixel Watch 2, it offers reliable fitness tracking based on Fitbit’s software, along with solid performance, all-day battery life, and good integration with Google’s ecosystem, though the offer is limited to the 41mm size.  
即日起至 8 月 28 日，谷歌商店在购买翻新 128GB 版 Google Pixel 7（售价 329 美元）时，会免费赠送 Pixel Watch 2。该优惠还包含一个免费的 Pixel 7 保护壳。尽管这两款设备都已推出数年，但 Pixel 7 拥有宽敞的 90Hz OLED 显示屏、IP68 级防水防尘认证和无线充电功能。至于 Pixel Watch 2，它基于 Fitbit 软件提供可靠的健身追踪功能，性能稳定、续航全天，并与谷歌生态系统良好集成，但该优惠仅限 41mm 版本。
