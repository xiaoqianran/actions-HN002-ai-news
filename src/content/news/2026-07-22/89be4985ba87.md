Twitch is giving parents more control over how their children are using the streaming platform, including the ability to block them from broadcasting entirely.  
Twitch正在赋予家长更多对其子女使用该流媒体平台的控制权，包括完全阻止他们进行直播的能力。

Parental controls are now available that allow guardians to link Twitch accounts with their 13- to 17-year-old children, providing account management features and a weekly email summarizing their teen’s activity, including channels and hours watched.  
新的家长控制功能现已推出，允许监护人与其13至17岁的子女关联Twitch账户，提供账户管理功能，并每周发送一封电子邮件总结青少年的活动，包括观看的频道和时长。

The controls can be used to prevent teens from livestreaming their own content and disabling Whispers, Twitch’s private messaging feature. This includes blocking minors from sending or receiving DMs.  
这些控制功能可用于阻止青少年直播自己的内容，并禁用Whispers（Twitch的私信功能）。这包括阻止未成年人发送或接收私信（DMs）。

Parents can also set flexible daily time limits for Twitch across all their teen’s devices and restrict content that carries certain classification labels — such as Gambling, Mature-Rated Game, Sexual Themes, and more — from appearing in searches or recommended feeds.  
家长还可以为青少年所有设备上的Twitch设置灵活的每日使用时长限制，并限制带有特定分类标签（如赌博、成人评级游戏、性主题等）的内容出现在搜索或推荐信息流中。

The parental controls dashboard can be found in the Account Settings under the Family Center page. When a Twitch user managed by parental controls turns 18, their account will be automatically unlinked from their parents.  
家长控制面板可在“账户设置”下的“家庭中心”页面找到。当受家长控制的Twitch用户年满18岁时，其账户将自动与家长的账户解除关联。

Twitch says that it will explore adding additional parental control features in the future, noting that the setting is optional “at this time.”  
Twitch表示，未来将探索增加更多家长控制功能，并指出该设置目前是“可选的”。
