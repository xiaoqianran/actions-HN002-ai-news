# Anthropic’s $1.5 billion book piracy settlement approved by judge  
# 法官批准Anthropic 15亿美元图书盗版和解协议

A federal judge has signed off on Anthropic’s $1.5 billion class action settlement with authors who accused the company of training its AI models on copyrighted books, as reported earlier by Reuters.  
一名联邦法官已批准 Anthropic 与作者们达成的 15 亿美元集体诉讼和解协议。这些作者指控该公司使用受版权保护的书籍训练其 AI 模型，路透社此前报道过此消息。

In an order on Monday, Judge Araceli Martínez-Olguín writes that the settlement will provide “meaningful relief,” offering authors around $3,000 for each book allegedly pirated by Anthropic.  
周一，法官 Araceli Martínez-Olguín 在命令中写道，该和解协议将提供“有意义的救济”，为每位作者提供约每本书 3000 美元的赔偿，这些书籍据称被 Anthropic 盗用。

The $1.5 billion settlement is the “largest known copyright recovery in history,” according to the law firm representing the plaintiffs.  
代表原告的律师事务所表示，这 15 亿美元的和解协议是“史上已知最大的版权赔偿”。

A group of authors — Andrea Bartz, Charles Graeber, and Kirk Wallace Johnson — first filed a copyright lawsuit against Anthropic in 2024.  
一群作者——Andrea Bartz、Charles Graeber 和 Kirk Wallace Johnson——于 2024 年首次对 Anthropic 提起版权诉讼。

While the now-retired Judge William Alsup granted Anthropic a partial victory last year, he approved a separate class action lawsuit claiming the company engaged in the “Napster-style downloading of millions of works.”  
虽然现已退休的 William Alsup 法官去年裁定 Anthropic 部分胜诉，但他批准了另一项集体诉讼，指控该公司从事“类似 Napster 的下载数百万作品”的行为。

Anthropic agreed to a $1.5 billion settlement in September 2025, to which Judge Alsup later granted preliminary approval.  
Anthropic 于 2025 年 9 月同意 15 亿美元的和解方案，Alsup 法官后来给予了初步批准。

“We are pleased that more than 91% of authors and publishers covered by the settlement have claimed their share of the payment,” Anthropic general counsel Aparna Sridhar tells Reuters. “We’re looking forward to bringing this matter to a close.”  
Anthropic 法务总监 Aparna Sridhar 告诉路透社：“我们很高兴，超过 91% 的受和解协议覆盖的作者和出版社已申领了他们的赔偿份额。我们期待就此事了结。”

Anthropic is still facing copyright lawsuits from Chicken Soup for the Soul and several other authors who argue that $3,000 per book isn’t enough.  
Anthropic 仍面临来自《心灵鸡汤》（Chicken Soup for the Soul）及其他几位作者的版权诉讼，这些作者认为每本书 3000 美元的赔偿不够。
