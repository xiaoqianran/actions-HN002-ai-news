# Nintendo customers have no legal right to tariff refunds, company tells judge  
# 任天堂告知法官：消费者无权获得关税退款

---

Nintendo urged a court to dismiss a lawsuit demanding that it pass tariff refunds on to customers, saying that people who bought a Switch console or other products have no legal right to refunds that Nintendo is due to receive from the government.  
任天堂敦促法院驳回一项要求其将关税退款转嫁给消费者的诉讼，称购买Switch主机或其他产品的消费者无权获得任天堂将从政府收到的退款。

Nintendo said in a motion to dismiss yesterday that plaintiffs are “ask[ing] this Court to invent a legal duty out of whole cloth to retroactively re-price completed sales simply because the legal landscape has changed.”  
任天堂在昨日提交的驳回动议中表示，原告“要求本法院凭空创设一项法律义务，仅因法律环境变化而追溯性地重新定价已完成交易”。

Nintendo said the plaintiffs “have no legal entitlement to the tariff refunds Nintendo stands to receive.”  
任天堂称，原告“对任天堂即将收到的关税退款没有任何法定权利”。

The lawsuit was filed in April by two customers who seek to represent a class of all US residents who bought Nintendo products from February 2025 to February 2026.  
该诉讼于4月由两名客户提起，他们试图代表2025年2月至2026年2月期间所有购买任天堂产品的美国居民组成集体诉讼。

California resident Gregory Hoffert and Washington resident Prashant Sharan filed the class action complaint in US District Court for the Western District of Washington, alleging unjust enrichment and a violation of the Washington Consumer Protection Act’s prohibition on unfair or deceptive acts.  
加州居民Gregory Hoffert和华盛顿州居民Prashant Sharan在华盛顿州西区美国联邦地区法院提起了集体诉讼，指控不当得利以及违反《华盛顿消费者保护法》对不公平或欺骗性行为的禁令。

“Nintendo engaged in unfair acts by: (i) raising prices due to tariffs; (ii) failing to disclose that it intended to seek tariff refunds; and (iii) retaining tariff refunds despite having passed the costs to its customers,” the lawsuit alleged.  
诉讼指控：“任天堂实施了不公平行为：(i) 因关税提高价格；(ii) 未披露其打算寻求关税退款；(iii) 在已将成本转嫁给客户的情况下仍保留关税退款。”

In their unjust-enrichment claim, plaintiffs said Nintendo profits were “unjustly obtained as a result of its price increases on goods subject to unlawful tariffs.”  
在不正当得利索赔中，原告称任天堂的利润是“因对受非法关税影响的商品提价而非法获得的”。

Nintendo raised prices for the original Switch console by $30 to $50 and added $5 to $10 to the prices of various Switch 2 accessories.  
任天堂将原版Switch主机的价格提高了30至50美元，并为各种Switch 2配件增加了5至10美元。

Nintendo told the court that “the doctrine of unjust enrichment does not apply when a party voluntarily pays a purchase price and receives a product in return.”  
任天堂告诉法院，“当一方自愿支付购买价格并获得产品作为回报时，不当得利原则不适用。”

Nintendo said plaintiffs “received exactly what they paid for: Nintendo products that Plaintiffs knowingly and voluntarily purchased at the advertised price.  
任天堂表示，原告“收到了他们确切支付的东西：原告在广告价格下知情并自愿购买的任天堂产品。

Nor is there anything ‘unjust’ about Nintendo retaining money that it may receive from the government as tariff refunds.  
任天堂保留其可能从政府作为关税退款收到的金钱没有任何‘不公正’之处。

The fact that Nintendo stands to receive tariff refunds does not give Plaintiffs any legal entitlement to those funds.”  
任天堂即将收到关税退款这一事实并未赋予原告对这些资金的任何法定权利。”

Consumers paid price of Trump tariffs  
消费者为特朗普关税付出了代价

A similar lawsuit was filed in May against Sony by plaintiffs who bought PlayStation consoles.  
5月份，购买PlayStation主机的原告对索尼提起了类似诉讼。

Sony has not yet filed a motion to dismiss that case, which is in the Northern District of California, but is likely to fight it using similar legal reasoning as Nintendo’s.  
索尼尚未就此案（位于加州北区联邦地区法院）提交驳回动议，但很可能使用与任天堂类似的法律理由进行抗辩。

Another lawsuit seeking tariff refunds for Xbox buyers was filed against Microsoft on July 17.  
另一项寻求为Xbox买家争取关税退款的诉讼于7月17日对微软提起。

Many companies raised prices in response to tariffs imposed by President Trump.  
许多公司为应对特朗普总统实施的关税而提高了价格。

In February, the Supreme Court ruled that Trump illegally imposed the International Emergency Economic Powers Act (IEEPA) tariffs, and the lengthy refund process began in April.  
2月，最高法院裁定特朗普非法实施了《国际紧急经济权力法》（IEEPA）关税，漫长的退款流程于4月开始。

Trump imposed another round of tariffs after the Supreme Court ruling, and those tariffs were judged to be illegal by the US Court of International Trade.  
最高法院裁决后，特朗普又实施了一轮关税，这些关税被美国国际贸易法院判定为非法。

Ordinary residents of the US have paid the price of Trump’s trade war.  
美国普通民众为特朗普的贸易战付出了代价。

“As it stands, Defendant has retained profits generated from its sales of products subject to tariff-related price increases and should not be permitted to retain those ill-gotten profits when it is seeking a refund of the duties it paid,” the lawsuit against Nintendo said.  
针对任天堂的诉讼称：“目前，被告保留了其因受关税相关价格上涨影响的产品销售所产生的利润，在其寻求退还已支付关税时，不应被允许保留这些非法所得利润。”

It’s easy to see why consumers are angry about having paid tariff-inflated prices.  
很容易理解消费者为何对支付了关税膨胀的价格感到愤怒。

The government isn’t issuing refunds to any resident who indirectly paid for Trump’s tariffs.  
政府并未向任何间接为特朗普关税买单的居民发放退款。

The government refund portal set up after the Supreme Court ruling is for importers and authorized customs brokers.  
最高法院裁决后设立的政府退款门户仅适用于进口商和授权的海关经纪人。

Over 100 tariff lawsuits against businesses  
针对企业的100多起关税诉讼

Nintendo is one of many companies that sued the Trump administration in an attempt to make sure the government doesn’t worm its way out of issuing refunds.  
任天堂是众多起诉特朗普政府以确保政府无法逃避发放退款的公司之一。

The class action against Nintendo pointed to this lawsuit as evidence that “Nintendo stands to receive a windfall: it has already recouped tariff costs from consumers through higher prices, and it now stands in line to recover those same unlawful tariff payments from the federal government.”  
针对任天堂的集体诉讼将任天堂起诉政府的诉讼作为证据，指出“任天堂即将获得一笔意外之财：它已通过提价从消费者那里收回关税成本，现在又排队准备从联邦政府那里追回同样的非法关税付款。”

Lawsuits seeking refunds from companies aren’t just being filed against gaming console makers.  
寻求从公司获得退款的诉讼不仅针对游戏机制造商。

Since the Supreme Court ruling, “plaintiffs’ firms have filed more than 100 putative consumer class actions against businesses nationwide that have sought refunds of tariffs paid as a result of these now invalidated charges,” said a July 9 article by Foley & Lardner lawyers.  
Foley & Lardner律师事务所在7月9日的一篇文章中表示，自最高法院裁决以来，“原告律师事务所已在全国范围内针对因这些现已无效的收费而支付关税并寻求退款的企业提起了100多起拟议的消费者集体诉讼”。

These cases have been filed in more than 30 federal districts in over 20 states, they wrote.  
他们写道，这些案件已在20多个州的30多个联邦地区法院提起。

The legal theory behind the lawsuits is not specific to any industry.  
这些诉讼背后的法律理论并不特定于任何行业。

“Plaintiffs have filed class actions against companies across varying industries and points in the supply chain from food manufacturers to logistics providers.  
“原告已对从食品制造商到物流供应商等不同行业和供应链环节的公司提起了集体诉讼。

The specific allegations in each case differ, but the primary legal theory is the same: companies cannot pass tariff costs to consumers and retain government refunds for the same tariffs,” Foley & Lardner lawyers Erik Swanholt and Kelsey Boehm wrote.  
每起案件的具体指控各不相同，但主要法律理论相同：公司不能将关税成本转嫁给消费者，同时又保留针对同一关税的政府退款，”Foley & Lardner律师事务所的Erik Swanholt和Kelsey Boehm写道。

Holland & Knight lawyers wrote in a legal alert that “any business that both passed tariff costs through to customers and is pursuing government refunds could become a litigation target.”  
Holland & Knight律师事务所在法律警报中写道，“任何既将关税成本转嫁给客户又在寻求政府退款的企业都可能成为诉讼目标。”

Nintendo tries to compel arbitration  
任天堂试图强制仲裁

With lawsuits in the early stages, no court has ruled on the core legal theories, Holland & Knight lawyers wrote.  
Holland & Knight律师写道，由于诉讼尚处于早期阶段，没有法院对核心法律理论作出裁决。

Businesses have several potential defenses, such as arguing that there can be no unjust enrichment when there is a contract between the parties.  
企业有几项潜在辩护理由，例如辩称当双方之间存在合同时，不可能存在不当得利。

“In many jurisdictions, unjust enrichment is unavailable where an express contract governs the parties’ relationship,” Holland & Knight partners Ashley Akers and Austin Rainwater wrote.  
Holland & Knight律师事务所的合伙人Ashley Akers和Austin Rainwater写道：“在许多司法管辖区，当明示合同规范双方关系时，不能适用不当得利。”

Businesses can also argue “that the challenged charges were imposed while the IEEPA tariffs remained legally effective and enforceable,” the Holland & Knight lawyers wrote.  
Holland & Knight律师还写道，企业可以辩称“被质疑的费用是在IEEPA关税仍然合法有效且可执行期间征收的”。
