### Sales of Valve's Steam Deck crater after recent price hike  
 Valve 的 Steam Deck 在近期涨价后销量骤降  

---

Since its 2022 launch, the Steam Deck has consistently been at or near the top of the company’s “top selling products by revenue” charts, with brief dips outside of the top 10 treated as newsworthy events.  
自 2022 年推出以来，Steam Deck 一直稳居该公司“按收入计算的最畅销产品”榜单的前列或附近，偶尔跌出前十名都会被当作新闻事件来报道。  

But sales of the popular handheld gaming PC have apparently been on a steep decline since the system relaunched at a higher price in May.  
但这款流行的掌上游戏 PC 的销量显然自 5 月以更高价格重新上市以来已急剧下降。  

Linux-focused gaming site Boiling Steam breaks down the historical data, showing how the Deck’s chart position has fallen from fifth place immediately after orders resumed in late May down to 14th place for two weeks in early July (the hardware sits at 12th place in the current edition of those charts).  
专注于 Linux 的游戏网站 Boiling Steam 分析了历史数据，显示 Deck 的排名从 5 月下旬恢复订单后的第五位，下降到 7 月初连续两周的第 14 位（在最新榜单中该硬件目前位列第 12 位）。  

That’s in sharp contrast to 2025, when the Steam Deck never dropped below seventh place on the bestseller charts and was only rarely outside the Top 5.  
这与 2025 年形成鲜明对比，当时 Steam Deck 在畅销榜上从未低于第七位，且很少跌出前五名。  

Then Valve warned of “intermittent shortages” for the Steam Deck starting in February, leading to weeks of lower chart positions before the Deck became completely unavailable until May.  
随后，Valve 从 2 月起警告 Steam Deck 将出现“间歇性短缺”，导致其榜单排名数周走低，直到 5 月前该产品完全缺货。  

Boiling Steam does some back-of-the-envelope math to try to translate these revenue-based chart positions into estimated unit sales ranges for the Steam Deck, based on assumptions about average sale price across hardware models (both before and after the price hike) and estimated weekly revenues for the surrounding games on the charts.  
Boiling Steam 进行了粗略估算，试图将这些基于收入的排名转化为 Steam Deck 的预估销量范围，其依据是对各硬件型号（涨价前后）的平均售价假设，以及榜单上周边游戏的预估周收入。  

That rough model suggests Steam Deck sales may have fallen by approximately 80 percent from a 2025 baseline of about 11,000 to 18,000 units per week to a current estimate of just 1,400 to 3,000 per week.  
该粗略模型表明，Steam Deck 销量可能从 2025 年基准的每周约 11,000 至 18,000 台，骤降约 80%，至目前预估的每周仅 1,400 至 3,000 台。  

While there’s a lot of wiggle room in those estimates, anything close to those numbers would reflect a huge slowdown in sales for a handheld that sold an estimated 4 million units in its first three years.  
尽管这些预估存在较大浮动空间，但任何接近这些数字的数据都反映出这款掌机销量的巨大放缓——它在最初三年已售出约 400 万台。  

---

**Supply and demand**  
**供需关系**  

Boiling Steam attributes this marked sales dip to a price increase that has “completely shut down demand for the Steam Deck.”  
Boiling Steam 将这一显著的销量下滑归因于涨价，称其“完全抑制了 Steam Deck 的需求”。  

And it’s likely that the Steam Deck’s historical $400 to $649 price range was much more attractive to消费者 than the current $789 to $949 range brought on by RAM and storage shortages.  
而且，Steam Deck 历史上 400 至 649 美元的价格区间可能比当前因内存和存储短缺导致的 789 至 949 美元区间对消费者更具吸引力。  

At current prices, consumers might start considering more powerful handheld gaming PCs in the same price range from competing hardware lines like ROG’s Ally, Lenovo’s Legion Go, or MSI’s Claw.  
以当前价格，消费者可能会开始考虑同价位竞品硬件线中更强大的掌上游戏 PC，例如 ROG 的 Ally、联想 Legion Go 或微星 Claw。  

But the relative dip in Steam Deck sales could also reflect the shortages in Steam Deck supply that Valve still warns are impacting availability in some regions “intermittently.”  
但 Steam Deck 销量的相对下滑也可能反映出其供应短缺——Valve 仍警告称这“间歇性”影响着某些地区的产品可得性。  

While some stock trackers have shown consistent availability for the Steam Deck since late May, the hardware was briefly unavailable in North America shortly after relaunching at its new prices, suggesting Valve can’t always satisfy the current market demand.  
尽管部分库存追踪器显示自 5 月下旬以来 Steam Deck 供应稳定，但该硬件在新价格重新上市后不久曾在北美短暂缺货，表明 Valve 并非总能满足当前市场需求。  

Regardless of the reason, it seems clear that Valve is currently selling many fewer Steam Deck units than it did even six months ago.  
无论原因如何，显然 Valve 目前销售的 Steam Deck 数量已远低于六个月前。  

In normal times, this might be a good sign that Valve should get moving on finalizing and releasing the long-awaited Steam Deck 2.  
在正常情况下，这可能是一个好迹象，表明 Valve 应加速完成并发布期待已久的 Steam Deck 2。  

But given how component pricing is looking for the immediate future, we shudder to think what Valve would have to charge to make a profit on a more powerful handheld today.  
但鉴于近期零部件价格的走势，我们不敢想象 Valve 如今要为更强大的掌机定价多少才能盈利。
