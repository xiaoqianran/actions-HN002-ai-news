# New US homeownership measure puts people first  
# 美国新住房拥有率衡量标准以人为本  

An alternative way of calculating the homeownership rate tells us how many individuals own their homes—something the most commonly used metric fails to track.  
一种替代性的住房拥有率计算方法能够告诉我们有多少个人拥有自己的住房——这是最常用的指标未能追踪的。  

The homeownership rate in the United States is reported to be 65 percent. But this commonly cited data point on homeownership is actually the owner-occupancy rate, which tells us how many housing units are occupied by an owner. While owner occupancy is an interesting measure, it doesn’t tell us how many people own their home.  
美国的住房拥有率通常报告为65%。但这个常被引用的数据点实际上是业主入住率，它告诉我们有多少住房单元由业主居住。虽然业主入住率是一个有趣的衡量标准，但它并不能告诉我们有多少人拥有自己的住房。  

As an alternative to better reflect the share of adults who are homeowners, we offer the homeowners-to-population ratio, or HPOP, a measure that lends a more nuanced view for important policy considerations and context. Using this new measure, the U.S. homeownership rate is 53 percent.  
为了更好地反映成年房主所占的比例，我们提出了业主人口比（HPOP）这一替代指标，它能为重要的政策考量提供更细致的视角和背景。使用这一新指标，美国的住房拥有率为53%。  

To further its mission of pursuing a growing economy and stable financial system that work for all of us, the Federal Reserve Bank of Minneapolis works to understand economic conditions in the Ninth Federal Reserve District and beyond. Homeownership trends are a critical component of those conditions. Housing is both the largest expense and the largest source of wealth for many families.  
为推进其使命——追求惠及所有人的经济增长和稳定的金融体系，明尼阿波利斯联邦储备银行致力于了解第九联邦储备区及更广泛地区的经济状况。住房趋势是这些状况的关键组成部分。住房对许多家庭而言既是最大的支出，也是最大的财富来源。  

Using the HPOP helps us understand housing better in several ways. For example, the HPOP accounts for millions of American adults who would not be identified as either homeowners or renters under the traditional owner-occupancy measure. By including all adults, the HPOP gives us a more accurate understanding of the economic well-being of Americans.  
使用HPOP可以从多个方面帮助我们更好地理解住房问题。例如，HPOP涵盖了数百万在传统业主入住率指标下既不会被认定为房主也不会被认定为租户的美国成年人。通过包含所有成年人，HPOP让我们能更准确地了解美国人的经济状况。  

Measuring homeownership by the person instead of by the home is particularly important for comparing characteristics of homeowners: by age, by geography, and across time.  
按个人而非按住房来衡量住房拥有率，对于比较房主的特征（按年龄、地理区域和时间）尤为重要。

As illustrated in Figure 1, these five housing units are home to 14 adults. Because four of these five housing units have their respective owners as residents, the owner-occupancy rate—the measure traditionally viewed as the homeownership rate—on the cul-de-sac is 80 percent. However, because only seven of the 14 adults are actually owners of the homes they live in, the HPOP is much lower, at 50 percent.  
如图1所示，这五套住房共有14名成年人居住。由于其中四套住房的业主是居民，因此该死胡同的业主入住率——传统上被视为住房拥有率的指标——为80%。然而，由于14名成年人中只有7人实际拥有他们居住的住房，HPOP要低得多，仅为50%。

To calculate the HPOP, we divide the total population of adult homeowners by the total adult population.  
要计算HPOP，我们将成年房主总人口除以成年总人口。  

For this analysis, we use data from the U.S. Census Bureau’s American Community Survey (ACS).¹  
本分析使用美国人口普查局美国社区调查（ACS）的数据。¹  

To determine the total population of adults, we exclude anyone under age 18. To identify homeowners, we then look among the adults living in owner-occupied housing. The ACS tags the homeowner within such homes as the “reference person” (or “head”) for their household. We then also include any spouses or unmarried partners of the reference person as homeowners.²  
为确定成年总人口，我们排除任何18岁以下者。为识别房主，我们随后查看居住在业主入住住房中的成年人。ACS将此类住房中的房主标记为家庭的“参照人”（或“户主”）。我们随后还将参照人的任何配偶或未婚伴侣视为房主。²

The HPOP categorizes adults who are not homeowners more precisely than the owner-occupancy rate. The largest group of non-homeowners, renters, is accounted for in both measures. However, calculations of the owner-occupancy rate leave out people who are living in group quarters—such as college dormitories, nursing homes, and correctional facilities. Our calculations of the HPOP include those groups and also account for discrepancies in the number of adults in renter households relative to homeowner households.  
HPOP比业主入住率更精确地对非房主成年人进行分类。最大的非房主群体——租户——在两种指标中均有体现。然而，业主入住率的计算忽略了居住在集体宿舍中的人——例如大学宿舍、养老院和惩教设施。我们的HPOP计算包含了这些群体，并考虑了租户家庭与房主家庭中成年人数量的差异。
