**Title:** Moving Like a Human: Ego-Motion-Normalized Temporal Signatures for Real-Time Aerial Person Tracking on Milliwatt-Class Hardware  
**标题：** 像人类一样移动：基于自我运动归一化时间特征的毫瓦级硬件实时空中行人跟踪

**Authors:** Akbar Anbar Jafari, Cagri Ozcinar, Gholamreza Anbarjafari  
**作者：** Akbar Anbar Jafari, Cagri Ozcinar, Gholamreza Anbarjafari

**Abstract:** Follow-me person tracking must run on the drone itself, where affordable companion computers offer only a few effective int8 GFLOP/s. At typical follow distances a person spans 10-60 pixels, indistinguishable from clutter and beyond the reach of single-frame appearance detectors.  
**摘要：** 跟随式行人跟踪必须在无人机自身运行，而经济型 companion computer 仅提供少量有效的 int8 GFLOP/s 算力。在典型跟随距离下，行人仅占 10-60 像素，与背景杂波难以区分，且超出单帧外观检测器的处理范围。

The missing evidence is temporal and belongs in the input representation, computed analytically, rather than in learned temporal machinery. EMTS-Det is a five-stage system that estimates ego-motion, converts each frame into ego-motion-normalized residual-motion channels, detects person centers with a 22k-parameter, 7.6-MFLOP network, tracks a locked target with a Kalman filter in stabilized coordinates, and verifies tracks with a 1-D convolutional classifier of human motion (ROC AUC 0.941).  
缺失的证据是时间维度的，应通过分析计算融入输入表征，而非依赖学习型时序模块。EMTS-Det 是一个五阶段系统：估计自我运动、将每帧转换为自我运动归一化的残差运动通道、使用 22k 参数、7.6 MFLOP 的网络检测行人中心、在稳定坐标系中用卡尔曼滤波器跟踪锁定目标，并通过一维卷积分类器验证行人运动轨迹（ROC AUC 0.941）。

Training uses a synthetic-motion curriculum with motion channels generated by the deployed ego-motion code. Multi-seed ablations locate the value in generalization: on held-out VisDrone-DET a luminance-only variant collapses to 0.051 AP25 versus 0.415, as does YOLOv8n fine-tuned identically despite 1,100 times the compute, while the deployed int8 detector reaches 0.694 AP25 in-domain and 0.444 on this split.  
训练采用合成运动课程，运动通道由部署的自我运动代码生成。多随机种子消融实验确定了泛化价值：在保留的 VisDrone-DET 测试集上，仅亮度变体 AP25 降至 0.051（对比 0.415），即使以 1100 倍算力微调的 YOLOv8n 同样失效，而部署的 int8 检测器在域内达到 0.694 AP25，在此测试集上为 0.444。

Temporal-shift modules lower accuracy, so the deployed detector is stateless. Silent int8 calibration failures are documented; min-max calibration with propagated caches matches float within 0.008 AP.  
时序移位模块会降低精度，因此部署的检测器为无状态设计。文档记录了静默的 int8 校准失败问题；采用传播缓存的最小-最大校准与浮点精度差异在 0.008 AP 以内。

On a Raspberry Pi Zero 2W the pipeline runs at 31.85 FPS with 0.462 AP25 and 0.714 recall over 1,000 real-world UAV videos, versus 1.95 FPS and 0.172 AP25 for YOLOv8n. A 57-second field sequence shows auto-lock at 1.3 s, 97.9% lock recall, and recovery from all nine occlusions with zero false re-locks.  
在 Raspberry Pi Zero 2W 上，该流水线在 1000 个真实无人机视频上达到 31.85 FPS、0.462 AP25 和 0.714 召回率，而 YOLOv8n 仅为 1.95 FPS 和 0.172 AP25。一段 57 秒的实地序列显示：1.3 秒自动锁定、97.9% 锁定召回率，并成功从全部九次遮挡中恢复且无误重锁定。
