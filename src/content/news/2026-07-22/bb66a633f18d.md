# AirMeter: Stream an AN870 Multimeter's LCD to Your Browser

AirMeter：将 AN870 万用表的 LCD 屏幕串流至浏览器

---

Want live multimeter readings on screen during a build video without fighting glare, focus hunt, and awkward camera angles? You can, and the parts list is short: an ANENG AN870 meter (any clone built on the DTM0660 chip works), an RP2040 board to sit inside it, a pair of 433 MHz HC-12 radios, an ESP32-C3 to host the page, and one small comparator board you will make yourself. YouTuber Bits und Bolts got tired of those filming headaches and built AirMeter, an open-source mod that taps the meter's own LCD signals and redraws the display live in a web browser or as an OBS source. No proprietary app, no cloud account, no soldering to a hidden data port that may not even exist.

想在制作视频时实时获取万用表读数，又不想对抗眩光、对焦困难和尴尬的拍摄角度？你可以做到，而且所需零件清单很短：一块 ANENG AN870 万用表（任何基于 DTM0660 芯片的克隆版均可）、一块放入其内部的 RP2040 开发板、一对 433 MHz HC-12 无线模块、一台用于托管页面的 ESP32-C3，以及一块需要自制的微型比较器电路板。YouTuber Bits und Bolts 厌倦了这些拍摄难题，于是打造了 AirMeter——一个开源改造项目，它直接读取万用表自身的 LCD 信号，并在网页浏览器或 OBS 源中实时重绘显示。无需专有应用，无需云账户，也无需焊接到一个可能根本不存在的隐藏数据端口。

---

Reading a screen that does not want to be read That last point is where the project gets clever. The AN870 has a hidden UART, but the manufacturer left the processor pin it needs disconnected under a blob of epoxy, so serial access was a dead end. Instead, the RP2040 samples the signals that drive the LCD directly. That display is a 4x15 multiplexed matrix: 60 individual segments controlled through only 19 physical traces. Those lines do not carry clean digital logic, they carry four-level analog waveforms, so a custom board populated with five LM339 comparators squares them into levels a microcontroller can actually read.

读取一块“不想被读取”的屏幕——这正是该项目的巧妙之处。AN870 有一个隐藏的 UART，但制造商将其所需的处理器引脚用环氧树脂 blob 隔离，导致串行访问此路不通。取而代之的是，RP2040 直接采样驱动 LCD 的信号。该显示屏是一个 4x15 的多路复用矩阵：60 个独立段仅通过 19 条物理走线控制。这些线路传输的不是干净的数字逻辑信号，而是四电平模拟波形，因此一块定制电路板（搭载五个 LM339 比较器）将它们整形为微控制器可以实际读取的电平。

---

Once the RP2040 has clean signals, it decodes each digit, decimal point, and unit icon, then sends the reading over a low-power HC-12 link at 433 MHz to the ESP32-C3 receiver. The ESP32-C3 serves a tiny web page that faithfully rebuilds the meter's LCD in any browser on your network. The part you would repeat for your own meter The tedious step is mapping the matrix. Bits und Bolts probed every common (COM) and segment (SEG) line with an oscilloscope and a test jig, matching each trace to the digit or icon it lights. Do that once and the firmware knows where every reading lives. Because the same DTM0660 chip hides inside a lot of budget meters, the method ports to more than just the AN870, you just re-map the segments. Schematics, board files, and firmware are on the build page: https://www.hackster.io/news/this-modded-multimeter-streams-its-screen-straight-to-your-browser-c46d844be09b

一旦 RP2040 获得干净信号，它就会解码每个数字、小数点以及单位图标，然后通过 433 MHz 的低功耗 HC-12 链路将读数发送给 ESP32-C3 接收器。ESP32-C3 托管一个微型网页，能在你网络上的任意浏览器中忠实重建万用表的 LCD 显示。你需要为自己万用表重复的步骤是映射矩阵。Bits und Bolts 使用示波器和测试夹具探测了每一个公共端（COM）和段（SEG）线路，将每条走线与它点亮的数字或图标对应起来。只需完成一次，固件就能知道每个读数的位置。由于许多廉价万用表内部都隐藏着相同的 DTM0660 芯片，该方法不仅适用于 AN870，只需重新映射各段即可。原理图、电路板文件和固件均在构建页面提供：https://www.hackster.io/news/this-modded-multimeter-streams-its-screen-straight-to-your-browser-c46d844be09b
