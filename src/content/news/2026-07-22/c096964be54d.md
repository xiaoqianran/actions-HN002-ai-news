# Google releases three new Gemini models — but no 3.5 Pro  
# 谷歌发布三款新Gemini模型——但未推出3.5 Pro

On Tuesday, Google DeepMind released Gemini 3.6 Flash, 3.5 Flash-Lite, and 3.5 Flash Cyber.  
周二，谷歌DeepMind发布了Gemini 3.6 Flash、3.5 Flash-Lite和3.5 Flash Cyber。

Gemini 3.6 Flash is Google’s “workhorse model” that promises improved capabilities in coding, knowledge work, and multimodal performance while reducing token usage by up to 17%, making it cheaper than its predecessor 3.5 Flash.  
Gemini 3.6 Flash是谷歌的“主力模型”，承诺在编码、知识工作和多模态性能方面提升能力，同时将令牌使用量减少高达17%，使其比前代3.5 Flash更便宜。

Gemini 3.5 Flash-Lite is the most cost-effective model in the class, and 3.5 Flash Cyber is a specialized model that was fine-tuned for finding and fixing cybersecurity vulnerabilities at a decent price point.  
Gemini 3.5 Flash-Lite是该类别中性价比最高的模型，而3.5 Flash Cyber是一款经过微调的专用模型，专用于以合理价格查找和修复网络安全漏洞。

This model will be exclusively available to governments and trusted partners as part of a limited access pilot program, according to Google.  
据谷歌称，该模型将作为有限访问试点计划的一部分，仅向政府和可信合作伙伴提供。

Google says the focus on these releases is to deliver efficiency, latency, and reliability to customers that are building AI agents at scale.  
谷歌表示，这些发布重点是为大规模构建AI代理的客户提供效率、低延迟和可靠性。

The launch is notable not just for what Google shipped — cheaper, faster models optimized for coding, efficiency, and cybersecurity — but also for what it didn’t.  
此次发布引人注目，不仅因为谷歌推出了更便宜、更快的模型（针对编码、效率和网络安全进行了优化），还因为它没有推出的东西。

The update doesn’t include the long-anticipated update to Google’s flagship model, Gemini Pro, which was last updated in February.  
此次更新并未包含谷歌旗舰模型Gemini Pro期待已久的更新，该模型上次更新是在二月。

In the time since that launch, OpenAI has released GPT-5.5 and begun rolling out GPT-5.6, while Anthropic has launched Claude Opus 4.8 and Claude Sonnet 5 and has expanded access to its frontier Fable 5 model, highlighting the intense release pace of the rival labs.  
自那以来，OpenAI已发布GPT-5.5并开始推出GPT-5.6，而Anthropic已推出Claude Opus 4.8和Claude Sonnet 5，并扩大了对前沿Fable 5模型的访问权限，凸显了竞争对手实验室密集的发布节奏。

Google teased the release of Pro as part of the 3.5 Flash release in May, saying the Pro version was “already being used internally, and we look forward to rolling it out next month.”  
谷歌在五月发布3.5 Flash时曾预告Pro版本的发布，称Pro版本“已在内部使用，我们期待下个月推出”。

Last week, Bloomberg reported that Google was facing internal delays in launching the 3.5 Pro as it struggled to meet internal performance goals.  
上周，彭博社报道称，谷歌因难以达到内部性能目标，在推出3.5 Pro方面面临内部延迟。

Gemini Pro models are generally Google’s highest-capability offerings for complex reasoning and coding tasks, while Flash models prioritize lower cost and faster response times for production applications.  
Gemini Pro模型通常是谷歌在复杂推理和编码任务方面能力最强的产品，而Flash模型则优先考虑生产应用的低成本和更快的响应时间。

Google DeepMind product lead Logan Kilpatrick said Tuesday that the company is currently testing Gemini 3.5 Pro with partners and hopes to “land soon.”  
谷歌DeepMind产品负责人Logan Kilpatrick周二表示，公司目前正与合作伙伴测试Gemini 3.5 Pro，并希望“尽快推出”。

He also noted that the team has started its most ambitious pre-training run yet for Gemini 4.  
他还指出，团队已开始为Gemini 4进行迄今为止最大胆的预训练运行。
