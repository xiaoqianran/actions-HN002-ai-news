GenSyn10: A Multi-Generative AI Dataset For Benchmarking Image Classification  
GenSyn10：用于图像分类基准测试的多生成式AI数据集

Authors: Md Faraz Kabir Khan, Saeed Anwar, Ghulam Mubashar Hassan  
作者：Md Faraz Kabir Khan, Saeed Anwar, Ghulam Mubashar Hassan

The rapid advancement of generative AI has outpaced our ability to reliably detect its outputs, particularly when detectors encounter generators they have not seen before.  
生成式AI的快速发展已经超越了我们对其实时输出进行可靠检测的能力，尤其是当检测器遇到从未见过的生成器时。

We introduce GenSyn10, a CIFAR-10-aligned synthetic image dataset of 60,000 images (10 classes, 32×32, 50k/10k split) generated using three architecturally diverse state-of-the-art models: FLUX.2-dev (Rectified Flow Transformer), HunyuanImage-3.0 (MoE Transformer), and Qwen-Image-2512 (Multimodal Diffusion Transformer), to advance research in AI-generated image detection.  
我们推出了GenSyn10，这是一个与CIFAR-10对齐的合成图像数据集，包含60,000张图像（10个类别，32×32分辨率，50k/10k划分），使用三种架构各异的最先进模型生成：FLUX.2-dev（整流流变换器）、HunyuanImage-3.0（混合专家变换器）和Qwen-Image-2512（多模态扩散变换器），以推动AI生成图像检测的研究。

A central challenge in this domain is that detectors perform well on known generators but degrade on unseen ones. GenSyn10 addresses this limitation by curating data from multiple contemporary architectures under a standardized generation protocol, enabling controlled and systematic evaluation of out-of-distribution (OOD) generalization to novel generators.  
该领域的一个核心挑战是检测器在已知生成器上表现良好，但在未见过的生成器上性能下降。GenSyn10通过从多种当代架构中按标准化生成协议整理数据，解决了这一局限性，从而能够对分布外（OOD）泛化到新生成器进行可控和系统的评估。

Images are generated using a template-based prompt engine and downsampled to ensure consistency.  
图像使用基于模板的提示引擎生成，并下采样以确保一致性。

We evaluate 17 image classification models under a four-stage protocol: real-data baseline, zero-shot transfer, fine-tuning, and retention.  
我们采用四阶段协议评估了17个图像分类模型：真实数据基线、零样本迁移、微调和保留。

Despite a measurable domain gap, CIFAR-10-trained models achieve up to 96.86% zero-shot accuracy on GenSyn10, increasing to 99.88% after fine-tuning.  
尽管存在可测量的领域差距，在CIFAR-10上训练的模型在GenSyn10上实现了高达96.86%的零样本准确率，微调后提升至99.88%。

In binary real-vs-synthetic classification, fine-tuned models achieve 97-99.9% accuracy on seen generators but drop to 79-96% on images from an unseen generator, highlighting persistent limitations in OOD generalization.  
在二元真实与合成分类中，微调模型在已见生成器上达到97-99.9%的准确率，但在未见过生成器的图像上降至79-96%，突显了OOD泛化中持续存在的局限性。

These results establish GenSyn10 as a controlled benchmark for studying synthetic image detection beyond single-generator settings, supporting research on robustness, domain adaptation, and cross-generator generalization.  
这些结果确立了GenSyn10作为研究超越单一生成器设置的合成图像检测的受控基准，支持关于鲁棒性、领域适应和跨生成器泛化的研究。
