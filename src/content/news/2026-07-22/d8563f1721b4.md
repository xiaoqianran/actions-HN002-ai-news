# A Step Forward Towards Trustworthy Risk-Aware Facial Retrieval (RA-FR)
迈向可信风险感知人脸检索（RA-FR）的重要一步

**Authors:** Muhammad Emmad Siddiqui, Muhammad Rafi  
**arXiv:** 2607.16279 (cs) | Submitted on 9 Jul 2026

---

Facial image retrieval in unconstrained surveillance environments is a high-stakes challenge where missing a subject of interest -- a single false negative -- is simply not an option.  
在无约束的监控环境中进行人脸图像检索是一项高风险挑战，其中漏掉目标对象——哪怕一个假阴性——都是不可接受的。

Despite near-perfect performance on curated benchmarks, current recognition systems falter under real-world domain shifts such as low resolution, motion blur, and uncontrolled illumination (e.g., SCFace).  
尽管在精心整理的基准测试中表现近乎完美，但当前识别系统在真实场景的领域偏移下（如低分辨率、运动模糊和非受控光照，例如SCFace数据集）仍会失效。

Addressing this reliability gap, we propose Risk-Aware Facial Retrieval (RA-FR), a framework that moves beyond fixed Top-$k$ retrieval to adaptive set generation, guaranteeing ground truth inclusion within a user-specified risk level ($\alpha$) and confidence level ($1 - \delta$).  
为弥补这一可靠性差距，我们提出了风险感知人脸检索（RA-FR）框架，该框架超越了固定的Top-$k$检索，转向自适应集合生成，确保在用户指定的风险水平（$\alpha$）和置信水平（$1 - \delta$）内包含真实目标。

Our approach integrates three core contributions: (1) reducing aleatoric uncertainty via a hybrid blind face restoration technique coupling Latent Consistency Models (InterLCM) and DiffBIR; (2) extracting discriminative, restoration-robust features via self-supervised DINOv1 ViT-B with GGeM pooling; and (3) employing conformal prediction with Hoeffding's inequality to dynamically calibrate retrieval set sizes based on query uncertainty.  
我们的方法整合了三个核心贡献：(1) 通过耦合潜在一致性模型（InterLCM）与DiffBIR的混合盲脸修复技术，降低偶然不确定性；(2) 利用自监督的DINOv1 ViT-B结合GGeM池化，提取具有修复鲁棒性的判别性特征；(3) 采用基于霍夫丁不等式的共形预测，根据查询不确定性动态校准检索集合大小。

On the IMFDB benchmark, it consistently satisfies a 5% risk target with an average retrieval set size of approximately 10 images.  
在IMFDB基准测试中，该方法始终满足5%的风险目标，平均检索集合大小约为10张图像。

By unifying domain-specific restoration, robust representation learning, and provable decision rules, RA-FR offers a pipeline that makes facial retrieval in surveillance both reliable and auditable.  
通过统一领域特定修复、鲁棒表征学习与可证明决策规则，RA-FR提供了一种使监控场景下的人脸检索既可靠又可审计的流程。
