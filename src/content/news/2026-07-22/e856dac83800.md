# Jellyfin founder Andrew leaves team

Jellyfin创始人Andrew退出团队

Hello everyone. Effective yesterday, Anthony and I decided to depart from the project, myself as Project Leader, and he as a core team member. This is in addition to the resignation of Andrew on Friday. We leave the project in the very capable hands of the remaining team who have been driving the project for many years now. Joshua: For me personally, it was just time for a change. I simply could no longer provide the effort (mental or time-wise) that the role demanded, and thus I was not performing my duties to an acceptable degree, facing severe burnout, and risks to my mental health. It was time to step aside. Hand-off is ongoing and is amicable, and there is good communication lines, so there is little to no risk of a hostile fork or anything of that nature. Jellyfin will continue for a long time to come, just not with me at its head. When I started Jellyfin, I thought it would be something used by a few hundred, or maybe if we were lucky, a few thousand, users. We might put a few pet features in, and do a bit of cleanup, but really, I didn't expect much. Now here we are 7-and-a-half years later, and it's the #1 FLOSS media server, a truly viable alternative to Plex for literally millions of server administrators and probably 10x that many individual users, and we've left our parent project completely in our dust. We've proven that FLOSS works, and is in demand. I truly hope Jellyfin outlives me, and I trust those in the team to keep the philosophy and code alive. For the last time, happy watching all!

大家好。从昨天起，Anthony和我决定退出项目，我作为项目负责人，他作为核心团队成员。此外，Andrew已于周五辞职。我们将项目交给多年来一直推动项目发展的剩余团队，他们非常有能力。关于我个人：只是到了改变的时候了。我再也无法提供该角色所需的努力（无论是精神上还是时间上），因此我未能以可接受的程度履行职责，面临严重的职业倦怠和心理健康风险。是时候退居幕后了。交接工作正在进行中，且氛围友好，沟通渠道畅通，因此几乎不存在恶意分支或类似风险。Jellyfin将继续长期发展，只是不再由我领导。当我创立Jellyfin时，我以为它只会被几百人使用，或者幸运的话，几千人。我们可能会加入一些个人喜爱的功能，做一些清理工作，但说实话，我没抱太大期望。如今，7年半过去了，它已成为排名第一的自由及开源媒体服务器，是Plex的真正可行替代方案，拥有数百万服务器管理员和可能十倍于此的终端用户，并且我们已经完全超越了我们的父项目。我们证明了自由及开源软件是可行且备受需求的。我真心希望Jellyfin能比我更长久，我相信团队会保持这一理念和代码的活力。最后一次，祝大家观影愉快！

My particular reasons are a bit different, so I figured I would post as well. For myself, though I don't touch much code these days, I do a lot to manage things in the backend and with App Stores/etc. After 7.5 - nearly 8 - years, it's been a long time since I've been able to give much of my free time to Jellyfin. My life is changing and I've got other things that must take priority. I am fully committed to a smooth transition, which is something I've wanted for a long time anyway. Even if the transition takes a year, I'm still down to do that. I want Jellyfin to succeed, and it should.

我的原因略有不同，所以我也来发个帖。就我个人而言，虽然我现在不怎么写代码了，但我在后端管理和应用商店等方面做了很多工作。经过7.5年——将近8年——我已经很久无法为Jellyfin投入多少空闲时间了。我的生活正在变化，还有其他事情必须优先处理。我完全致力于平稳过渡，这本来就是我一直希望的。即使过渡需要一年时间，我也愿意继续。我希望Jellyfin成功，它也应该成功。
