# Nvidia Wants to Own Every Chip Inside AI Data Centers  
# 英伟达欲掌控AI数据中心内的每一颗芯片

---

Save this storyNvidia is hyping up its new Vera Rubin chip system this week, revealing new performance benchmarks for the GPU and CPU combo ahead of rival AMD’s annual product event in San Francisco on Thursday.  
本周，英伟达正在为其全新的Vera Rubin芯片系统造势，在竞争对手AMD于周四在旧金山举行年度产品活动之前，公布了这款GPU与CPU组合的新性能基准测试。

During a lengthy technical workshop last week at the company’s headquarters in Santa Clara, California, Nvidia executives boasted to a small group of journalists about the chip system’s increased power and efficiency capabilities.  
上周，在加州圣克拉拉的公司总部举行的一场漫长的技术研讨会上，英伟达高管向一小群记者夸耀了该芯片系统提升的功率和效率能力。

The biggest takeaway: Nvidia, which has long specialized in making GPUs, is increasingly trying to position itself as a supplier of CPUs that can power AI agents.  
最大的收获是：长期专注于制造GPU的英伟达，正日益试图将自己定位为能够为AI智能体提供动力的CPU供应商。

While GPUs are still the main hardware that companies use to train and run their AI models, the industry's shift toward more complex, agentic systems has increased demand for CPUs, which can orchestrate data flows, networking, and other software tasks.  
尽管GPU仍是公司训练和运行AI模型的主要硬件，但行业向更复杂的智能体系统转型，增加了对CPU的需求，因为CPU可以协调数据流、网络和其他软件任务。

That's likely one reason Nvidia has been eager to promote itself as a supplier of complete AI systems rather than just AI chips.  
这可能是英伟达急于将自己宣传为完整AI系统（而不仅仅是AI芯片）供应商的原因之一。

Vera Rubin is Nvidia’s successor to its hybrid superchip system Grace Blackwell and represents the linchpin of its near-term future powering the AI industry.  
Vera Rubin是英伟达混合超级芯片系统Grace Blackwell的继任者，也是其近期推动AI产业未来的关键。

It’s designed to offer one CPU for every two GPUs. In a single Vera Rubin NVL 72 super chip system, there are 36 Vera CPUs for every 72 Rubin GPUs.  
其设计为每两颗GPU配一颗CPU。在一个单一的Vera Rubin NVL 72超级芯片系统中，每72颗Rubin GPU对应36颗Vera CPU。

Nvidia is also selling the Vera CPU as a stand-alone product, and it has reportedly told Chinese customers these could be ready as soon as August.  
英伟达也在单独销售Vera CPU，据报道，该公司已告知中国客户，这些产品最早可能在8月就绪。

The CPU chip Nvidia is using for its new Vera Rubin hardware system. Courtesy of Nvidia  
英伟达为其新款Vera Rubin硬件系统所使用的CPU芯片。图片由英伟达提供

Nvidia executives emphasized that its new Vera Rubin NVL72 racks—a stack of chips packed into a single liquid-cooled platform—are much more “plug-and-play” than some of its earlier products.  
英伟达高管强调，其新款Vera Rubin NVL72机架（将一堆芯片集成到一个液冷平台中）比其早期产品更“即插即用”。

During a brief tour of a Nvidia data center lab in Silicon Valley, Nvidia executives shared that OpenAI already has one Vera Rubin rack in use.  
在硅谷英伟达数据中心实验室的短暂参观期间，高管们透露OpenAI已在使用一台Vera Rubin机架。

Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang didn’t make an appearance at the workshop in Santa Clara last week; he was in Japan announcing the chipmaker’s new partnerships with a number of Japanese firms to develop AI for robotics.  
英伟达CEO黄仁勋上周未出席圣克拉拉的研讨会；他当时正在日本宣布该芯片制造商与多家日本公司建立新合作伙伴关系，以开发机器人AI。

The briefings were instead led by Ian Buck, Nvidia’s longtime vice president of accelerated computing and the architect behind the company’s CUDA software.  
简报会由英伟达长期担任加速计算副总裁、公司CUDA软件架构师伊恩·巴克主持。

“We’re on a road map to crank out new architectures, not just GPUs but CPUs,” Buck told reporters. “We’re going to keep innovating, because it’s do this or die in Silicon Valley.”  
“我们正按照路线图推出新架构，不仅是GPU，还有CPU，”巴克告诉记者。“我们将持续创新，因为在硅谷，要么这么做，要么出局。”

The meetings were held in Huang’s executive briefing center, where multiple desks nearby were piled with bags of Taiwanese snacks that the CEO brought back from his recent trip to Computex, a massive annual semiconductor trade show in Taipei, an Nvidia spokesperson told WIRED.  
会议在黄仁勋的执行简报中心举行，附近多张桌子上堆满了台湾零食袋，这是CEO从最近参加台北一年一度的半导体贸易展Computex带回的，一位英伟达发言人告诉《连线》杂志。

A rack containing Nvidia’s Vera CPUs, which are designed to pair with the company’s next-generation Rubin GPUs in AI data centers. Courtesy of Nvidia  
一个装有英伟达Vera CPU的机架，这些CPU专为与公司下一代Rubin GPU在AI数据中心中配对使用而设计。图片由英伟达提供

Nvidia claims that the Vera Rubin NVL72 system will process 10 times as many tokens per watt as the company’s Grace Blackwell super chip.  
英伟达声称，Vera Rubin NVL72系统每瓦特处理的token数量将是该公司Grace Blackwell超级芯片的10倍。

The company says that its Vera CPU is faster at processing agentic AI tasks compared to rival CPUs from AMD and Intel (though the tests it ran to support those benchmarks appears to have used slightly older generations of its competitors’ CPUs).  
该公司表示，其Vera CPU在处理智能体AI任务方面比AMD和英特尔的竞品CPU更快（尽管其用于支持这些基准测试的测试似乎使用了竞争对手稍早一代的CPU）。

Localized memory subsystems on the new chips will also offer nearly three times as much memory bandwidth as Blackwell, which will likely be an appealing feature to many companies amid an ongoing shortage of high-bandwidth memory.  
新款芯片上的本地化内存子系统还将提供近三倍于Blackwell的内存带宽，这在当前高带宽内存持续短缺的情况下，可能对许多公司具有吸引力。

Nvidia says it has also significantly reduced the number of cables needed to connect its chips to racks in multi-rack server systems, to the point where the company is touting Vera Rubin as “cable-free compute” and “hot-swappable.”  
英伟达表示，它还大幅减少了在多机架服务器系统中将芯片连接到机架所需的电缆数量，以至于公司将其Vera Rubin宣传为“无电缆计算”和“热插拔”。

This means customers can theoretically reduce the amount of time it takes to install each rack from a couple of hours to a few minutes, a point that was brought up by both Buck and Andrew Bell, Nvidia’s senior vice president of hardware engineering.  
这意味着客户理论上可以将每个机架的安装时间从几小时缩短到几分钟，这一点被英伟达硬件工程高级副总裁巴克和安德鲁·贝尔都提及过。

And the new chip system is 100 percent liquid-cooled, which can reduce the amount of energy needed to cool the chips, since air-cooling is more energy intensive.  
而且这款新芯片系统是100%液冷，这可以减少冷却芯片所需的能量，因为风冷更耗能。

Ever since Nvidia unveiled Vera Rubin in the spring of 2025, the company has been slowly dribbling out more details about the chip system while insisting it will be released on schedule.  
自2025年春季英伟达发布Vera Rubin以来，该公司一直在缓慢地透露更多关于该芯片系统的细节，同时坚称它将按计划发布。

Huang has repeatedly said Vera Rubin is ramping to “full production” and will ship in the second half of this year, with early customers including Microsoft, OpenAI, and Oracle.  
黄仁勋多次表示，Vera Rubin正在 ramp up 至“全面生产”，并将于今年下半年出货，早期客户包括微软、OpenAI和甲骨文。

Nvidia is particularly sensitive to any suggestion of delays after its previous-generation Blackwell chips reportedly overheated when connected together in the company's customized server racks, forcing it to make design changes and push back shipments.  
在据报道其上一代Blackwell芯片在公司的定制服务器机架中连接时过热，迫使它进行设计更改并推迟出货后，英伟达对任何关于延迟的暗示都特别敏感。

Nvidia’s marketing push for Vera Rubin is happening just ahead of rival AMD’s annual conference, where executives are expected to tout its next-generation AI and data center chips.  
英伟达对Vera Rubin的营销推动正发生在竞争对手AMD年度大会前夕，预计其高管将在会上宣传其下一代AI和数据中心芯片。

On Sunday, AMD revealed more details about its Helios AI chip rack, which is designed to compete with Nvidia’s new wares.  
周日，AMD公布了其Helios AI芯片机架的更多细节，该机架旨在与英伟达的新产品竞争。

Both AMD and Nvidia have been vying for large-scale, multiyear contracts to supply chips to AI hyperscalers like Meta and Amazon and AI labs like OpenAI, Anthropic, and SpaceXAI.  
AMD和英伟达一直在竞标大规模、多年的合同，以向Meta和亚马逊等AI超大规模公司以及OpenAI、Anthropic和SpaceXAI等AI实验室供应芯片。

Over the past two years, AMD has significantly grown its share of the market for CPUs used in data centers.  
在过去两年中，AMD已显著增长了其在数据中心用CPU市场的份额。

The company has long been recognized as a pioneer of the modern chiplet architecture used in x86 processors, which still account for the vast majority of data center CPU revenue.  
该公司长期被公认为现代芯粒架构的先驱，该架构用于x86处理器，而x86处理器仍占数据中心CPU收入的绝大多数。

Nvidia, by contrast, builds its data center CPUs on ARM, an alternative chip architecture known for its power efficiency.  
相比之下，英伟达的数据中心CPU基于ARM构建，ARM是一种以能效著称的替代芯片架构。

Nvidia executives Buck and Hannah Coutand, who runs Nvidia's CPU product marketing, both emphasized that Vera Rubin abandons the chiplet architecture used by many modern processors in favor of a single, monolithic chip.  
英伟达高管巴克和负责英伟达CPU产品营销的汉娜·库坦都强调，Vera Rubin放弃了许多现代处理器使用的芯粒架构，转而采用单一、单片式芯片。
