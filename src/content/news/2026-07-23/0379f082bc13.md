# Title: The White House Is Trying to Figure Out What to Do About Chinese AI  
**标题：白宫正试图厘清应如何应对中国人工智能**

---

The Trump administration is split over how to respond to the rapid rise of China’s leading AI models, according to multiple people familiar with deliberations.  
据多位知情人士透露，特朗普政府内部对于如何应对中国领先人工智能模型的快速崛起存在分歧。

The debate is broadly divided between parts of the White House, which has pushed for stricter controls on Chinese AI that may soon rival the most powerful US models, and the Commerce Department, which has viewed those restrictions as unworkable.  
这场辩论大致分为两派：白宫部分官员主张对可能很快就能与美国最强大模型竞争的中国AI实施更严格的控制；而商务部则认为这些限制措施并不可行。

The conundrum has become the latest flash point for the administration over AI, after China’s Moonshot AI lab last week released its Kimi K3 model that rivals the top US models from Anthropic and OpenAI.  
在中国Moonshot AI实验室上周发布其Kimi K3模型、该模型可与Anthropic和OpenAI的顶级美国模型相媲美之后，这一难题已成为政府内部关于AI的最新争议焦点。

In recent days, senior White House officials have discussed possible measures to stop China’s AI labs from developing their models by training them on US models, a practice known as distillation, the people say.  
知情人士称，近日白宫高级官员讨论了可能采取的措施，以阻止中国AI实验室通过“蒸馏”美国模型来开发自身模型。

The White House is expected to take some sort of presidential action after Anthropic last month alleged the Chinese tech company Alibaba had carried out the “largest known distillation attack” against it to date. This action is not currently expected to take the form of an executive order, according to one of the people.  
在Anthropic公司上月指控中国科技公司阿里巴巴对其进行了迄今为止“已知最大的蒸馏攻击”之后，白宫预计将采取某种总统层面的行动。据其中一人称，该行动目前预计不会采取行政命令的形式。

A White House spokesperson did not comment on the reporting in this story.  
白宫发言人未就本报道置评。

The White House has been taking distillation attacks especially seriously. It announced on Wednesday that Moonshot had developed Kimi K3 by distilling Anthropic’s Claude Fable 5 model, and Treasury secretary Scott Bessent told Fox Business that covert distillation could lead to sanctions, describing it as “IP theft.”  
白宫对蒸馏攻击尤为重视。它于周三宣布，Moonshot通过蒸馏Anthropic的Claude Fable 5模型开发了Kimi K3；财政部长斯科特·贝森特向Fox Business表示，秘密蒸馏可能导致制裁，并将其描述为“知识产权盗窃”。

While proposals have been making the rounds inside the White House, no formal request for input has been transmitted yet to the Commerce Department, which has its own ideas, according to two people familiar with deliberations.  
据两位知情人士称，尽管提案已在白宫内部传阅，但尚未向商务部正式征求其意见，而商务部本身也有自己的设想。

The Commerce Department has increasingly emerged as an influential part of the intra-administration split over AI, in part because it oversees export controls through the Bureau of Industry and Security.  
商务部在政府内部关于AI的分歧中日益成为有影响力的一方，部分原因在于它通过工业与安全局负责监管出口管制。

Lutnick has contemplated ways to create incentives for top US labs to create their own open weight models to counterbalance China, and has spoken with leaders at a number of AI labs in recent weeks, the people add.  
知情人士补充说，卢特尼克一直在考虑如何激励美国顶级实验室创建自己的开源权重模型以制衡中国，并在近几周与多家AI实验室的负责人进行了交谈。

Lutnick appears to be straddling a middle ground of regulation—he imposed export controls on Anthropic to bring them to heel—but has been more freewheeling than Cairncross, one of the people said.  
其中一人表示，卢特尼克似乎在监管上采取中间路线——他曾对Anthropic实施出口管制以使其就范——但比卡恩克罗斯更为自由放任。

Earlier this month, the biggest AI issue for the administration was Anthropic’s Claude Mythos and Fable 5 models, because of their ability to find vulnerabilities in政府 systems and critical infrastructure.  
本月早些时候，政府面临的最大AI问题来自Anthropic的Claude Mythos和Fable 5模型，因为它们有能力发现政府系统和关键基础设施中的漏洞。

But policy headaches with AI have only escalated with the rise of so-called open weight models from China—models that have few to no safeguards to prevent hacking government systems and can be downloaded and used by anyone.  
但随着所谓来自中国的开源权重模型的兴起——这些模型几乎没有防止黑客攻击政府系统的保障措施，且任何人都能下载和使用——AI政策难题进一步升级。
