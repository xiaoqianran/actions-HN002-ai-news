## Title  
Philips’ new smart toothbrush shows you where you didn’t properly brush  
飞利浦新款智能牙刷告诉你哪些地方没刷干净  

---

Philips’ latest addition to its Sonicare line of smart electric toothbrushes could take the guesswork out of your brushing routine.  
飞利浦Sonicare系列智能电动牙刷的最新成员，或许能让你告别刷牙时的盲目猜测。  

The Next-Generation DiamondClean 9900 Prestige uses a third-generation AI model to provide real-time feedback on how you’re brushing through a glowing ring on its base, while a touchscreen highlights areas of your mouth that may need more attention when you think you’re done.  
下一代钻石清洁9900尊享版采用第三代人工智能模型，通过底座上的发光环提供实时刷牙反馈，而触控屏则会在你以为刷完后，高亮显示口腔中可能需要更多注意的区域。  

The Sonicare Next-Generation DiamondClean 9900 Prestige is expected to launch in Europe and the US this fall, with a wider global rollout coming in 2027.  
Sonicare下一代钻石清洁9900尊享版预计今年秋季在欧洲和美国上市，2027年将进行更广泛的全球推广。  

Pricing hasn’t been announced, but previous versions sell for $430, and you can expect this new one to be even more expensive.  
价格尚未公布，但前代产品售价为430美元，预计这款新品会更贵。  

The base of the smart toothbrush has a glowing ring that changes color to indicate when you’re using too much pressure.  
这款智能牙刷的底座带有一个发光环，会通过颜色变化提示你压力是否过大。  

The toothbrush’s touchscreen lets you swipe through eight cleaning modes including gum health, max clean, and sensitive options.  
牙刷的触控屏可让你滑动切换八种清洁模式，包括牙龈护理、深度清洁和敏感模式等。  

To help ensure you’re not pressing too hard while you brush, causing damage, the glowing ring on the base of the brush changes color to indicate when you should be a little gentler.  
为避免刷牙时用力过猛造成损伤，底座发光环会变色提示你该轻柔些。  

But the 9900 Prestige also uses pressure sensors to adjust its intensity when you’re pressing too hard so the bristles go easier on your gums.  
但9900尊享版还会通过压力传感器在你用力过猛时自动调节强度，让刷毛更轻柔地对待牙龈。  

The touchscreen also displays what Philips calls a mouth map representing your teeth and gums in 12 different segments that are highlighted in blue or orange indicating how effective your brushing was.  
触控屏还会显示飞利浦所称的“口腔地图”，将牙齿和牙龈分为12个区域，通过蓝色或橙色高亮显示你的刷牙效果。  

The 9900 Prestige tracks its movements in your mouth while you brush and areas it deems in need of a little more attention glow orange so you know exactly what areas you need to target during a second pass.  
9900尊享版会在你刷牙时追踪其在口腔中的运动，认为需要更多关注的区域会亮起橙色，让你清楚知道第二次刷牙时需要重点清洁哪些部位。
