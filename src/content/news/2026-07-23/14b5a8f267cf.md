**Title: SoundCloud acquires decentralized music platform Nina Protocol months after its shutdown**  
**标题：SoundCloud 在 Nina Protocol 关闭数月后收购其去中心化音乐平台**

SoundCloud announced Wednesday that it has acquired Nina Protocol, a decentralized music platform that enabled independent artists to sell music directly to fans while keeping 100% of their revenue. The financial terms of the deal weren’t disclosed. It’s also unclear whether any members of Nina’s team will join SoundCloud. The company has not yet responded to a request for comment.  
SoundCloud 于周三宣布，已收购去中心化音乐平台 Nina Protocol。该平台曾让独立艺术家能够直接向粉丝销售音乐并保留全部收入。交易财务条款未披露。目前尚不清楚 Nina 团队任何成员是否会加入 SoundCloud。该公司尚未回应置评请求。

The deal is SoundCloud’s first acquisition since its 2022 purchase of AI music company Musiio to help improve music discovery. The deal signals that SoundCloud is continuing to expand beyond music hosting into a broader platform for independent artists.  
这是 SoundCloud 自 2022 年收购 AI 音乐公司 Musiio（旨在提升音乐发现功能）以来的首次收购。该交易表明，SoundCloud 正继续从音乐托管业务扩展为面向独立艺术家的更广泛平台。

As part of the acquisition, SoundCloud will take ownership of Nina’s editorial archive and “genre map.” The company writes in the blog post that this allows it to preserve Nina’s “rich collection of music journalism, artist features, and cultural commentary” and “help surface emerging artists, scenes, and music to even more listeners.”  
作为收购的一部分，SoundCloud 将获得 Nina 的编辑档案和“流派地图”所有权。公司在博客中写道，这使其能够保存 Nina “丰富的音乐新闻、艺术家特写和文化评论”并“帮助向更多听众展示新兴艺术家、音乐场景和作品”。

“[Nina Protocol] built an incredible platform by putting independent artists, scenes, and communities first, and our priority is to honor what they’ve created, while carrying that mission forward,” SoundCloud CEO Eliah Seton said in a statement. ”Together, we’ll help more independent artists build deeper relationships with fans, unlock new ways to earn, and access the tools they need to grow sustainable careers on their own terms.”  
SoundCloud 首席执行官 Eliah Seton 在声明中表示：“[Nina Protocol] 通过将独立艺术家、音乐场景和社区放在首位，构建了一个令人难以置信的平台。我们的首要任务是尊重他们的创作，同时推进这一使命。我们将共同帮助更多独立艺术家与粉丝建立更深层次的联系，开拓新的盈利方式，并获得按自身条件发展可持续职业生涯所需的工具。”

The acquisition comes just months after Nina announced it would shut down its platform on May 28, saying it had been unable to establish a sustainable business model.  
此次收购距 Nina 宣布因无法建立可持续商业模式而于 5 月 28 日关闭平台仅数月之隔。

Founded in 2021 by independent musicians Jack Callahan, Mike Pollard, and Eric Farber, Nina Protocol offered artists a way to operate their own digital storefronts without relying on traditional streaming platforms. The service was built on the Solana blockchain for fast, low-cost transactions and used Arweave for permanent decentralized storage of music files and metadata.  
Nina Protocol 由独立音乐人 Jack Callahan、Mike Pollard 和 Eric Farber 于 2021 年创立，为艺术家提供了在不依赖传统流媒体平台的情况下运营自有数字店铺的途径。该服务基于 Solana 区块链构建，以实现快速、低成本的交易，并使用 Arweave 进行音乐文件和元数据的永久去中心化存储。

For Nina Protocol artists, SoundCloud is offering a migration tool to transfer their work to its platform. Additionally, artists will receive a complimentary SoundCloud Artist subscription through the end of the year, which includes monetization features and audience growth tools.  
对于 Nina Protocol 的艺术家，SoundCloud 提供迁移工具将其作品转移至自身平台。此外，艺术家将在年底前获得免费的 SoundCloud Artist 订阅服务，其中包含盈利功能和受众增长工具。
