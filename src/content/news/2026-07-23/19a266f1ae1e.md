# AniGS: Bridging Rendering and Diffusion Prior for 3D Scene Animation  
AniGS：连接渲染与扩散先验的3D场景动画方法  

**Authors:** Yen-Chi Cheng, Chen Gao, Chuhan Chen, Tuotuo Li, Rajvi Shah, Ayush Saraf, Changil Kim, Liangyan Gui, Alexander Schwing, Johannes Kopf, Hung-Yu Tseng  
**作者：** Yen-Chi Cheng, Chen Gao, Chuhan Chen, Tuotuo Li, Rajvi Shah, Ayush Saraf, Changil Kim, Liangyan Gui, Alexander Schwing, Johannes Kopf, Hung-Yu Tseng 等11位作者  

---

Novel view rendering of large and complex reconstructed scenes is becoming increasingly photorealistic. However, most reconstructions remain static and lack the ambient motion that makes environments immersive.  
大型复杂重建场景的新视角渲染正变得越来越逼真。然而，大多数重建结果仍是静态的，缺乏让环境更具沉浸感的氛围运动。  

We present AniGS, a method for scene-level animation of 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) reconstructions that adds subtle, distributed dynamics, e.g., vegetation motion, while preserving rigid structures. Unlike existing 3D animation techniques which are limited to object-centric subjects or small regions, AniGS is designed for large, cluttered, navigable scenes.  
我们提出AniGS，一种用于3D高斯泼溅（3DGS）重建场景的场景级动画方法，可添加细微、分布式的动态效果（如植被运动），同时保持刚性结构。与现有局限于对象级主体或小区域的3D动画技术不同，AniGS专为大型、杂乱、可导航的场景设计。  

AniGS represents the scene with a canonical 3DGS and models motion using a time-conditioned deformation field. To animate the entire scene, we leverage a pretrained video diffusion model and introduce an iterative dataset–model update strategy that progressively expands viewpoint coverage and repeatedly updates camera-fixed training videos using a render-and-refine scheme.  
AniGS使用规范3DGS表示场景，并通过时间条件变形场建模运动。为动画化整个场景，我们利用预训练视频扩散模型，并引入迭代数据集-模型更新策略，逐步扩展视角覆盖范围，并重复使用渲染-细化方案更新相机固定训练视频。  

To prevent artifacts from unintended motion in static areas, we further introduce a composed video-to-video refinement scheme that restricts motion to desired regions.  
为防止静态区域出现意外运动导致的伪影，我们进一步引入组合视频到视频细化方案，将运动限制在所需区域。  

Experiments on five real-world, large-scale outdoor scenes demonstrate that AniGS produces natural ambient dynamics and high-quality novel view videos, enabling more immersive viewing experiences of reconstructed environments.  
在五个真实世界大型户外场景上的实验表明，AniGS能产生自然的氛围动态和高质量的新视角视频，使重建环境的观看体验更具沉浸感。
