### Apple is reportedly testing a MacBook Neo with more RAM  
据报道，苹果正在测试配备更多内存的MacBook Neo

Following the MacBook Neo’s huge popularity so far, Apple is reportedly developing an updated version of its budget laptop with a new processor and more memory, and even has plans to refresh the model with new colors.  
鉴于MacBook Neo迄今为止的巨大 popularity，苹果 reportedly 正在为其这款 budget laptop 开发更新版本，搭载新处理器和更多内存，甚至计划为该机型推出新颜色。

Following a similar report from analyst Tim Culpan earlier this year, Bloomberg’s Mark Gurman says the new Neo will run on an A19 Pro processor, which currently powers the iPhone 17 Pro / Pro Max and the iPhone Air, unlike the current laptop’s A18 Pro that was originally in the iPhone 16 Pro. The chip upgrade also means the updated Neo may have more RAM, likely 12GB rather than the original 8GB.  
继今年早些时候分析师Tim Culpan的类似报告后，彭博社的Mark Gurman表示，新款Neo将搭载A19 Pro处理器——该芯片目前用于iPhone 17 Pro/Pro Max和iPhone Air，而现款笔记本使用的A18 Pro最初来自iPhone 16 Pro。此次芯片升级也意味着更新后的Neo可能拥有更多内存，预计为12GB而非原始的8GB。

However, ongoing component shortages will almost certainly mean the next Neo costs more than the original did, previewed by Apple raising prices on nearly all of its devices last month. Another way it could get more expensive to start is the possibility that Apple could discontinue the lowest-priced 256GB version of the Neo.  
然而，持续的组件短缺几乎必然意味着下一代Neo的售价将高于原始版本，这一点已由苹果上月对几乎所有设备提价所预示。另一个可能导致起售价上涨的因素是，苹果可能停售Neo最低配的256GB版本。

In addition to rumors of an updated Neo, Gurman also says Apple is planning to release new iMacs, which last got a refresh in 2024, with OLED screens. Other long-rumored updates like the M6 MacBook Pro and OLED touchscreen MacBooks are also reportedly on the way, with the OLED models coming sometime late this year or early next year, as well as a newly redesigned base model 14-inch MacBook Pro with an M7 chip inside for next year.  
除了关于更新版Neo的传闻外，Gurman还表示苹果计划推出配备OLED屏幕的新款iMac（上次更新为2024年）。其他流传已久的更新，如M6 MacBook Pro和OLED触控屏MacBook也据称已在路上，其中OLED机型预计在今年晚些时候或明年初发布，此外还有一款采用M7芯片、全新设计的14英寸基础款MacBook Pro计划于明年推出。
