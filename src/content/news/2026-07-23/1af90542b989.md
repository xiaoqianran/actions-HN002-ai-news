# Preorders for Samsung’s new Z Fold and Flip 8 come with up to $350 in gift cards  
三星新款 Z Fold 8 和 Flip 8 预售赠送最高 350 美元礼品卡  

---

Samsung’s newest foldables are here. At Galaxy Unpacked, the company announced the Galaxy Z Flip 8, Galaxy Z Fold 8, and Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra. All three devices will be available on August 7th, but you can preorder them now starting at $1,199.99, $1,899.99, and $2,099.99, respectively.  
三星最新的折叠屏手机来了。在 Galaxy Unpacked 发布会上，该公司推出了 Galaxy Z Flip 8、Galaxy Z Fold 8 和 Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra。这三款设备均将于 8 月 7 日上市，但目前已开放预售，起售价分别为 1,199.99 美元、1,899.99 美元和 2,099.99 美元。

You probably noticed that Samsung’s foldable lineup looks a little different this year. Rather than simply updating the Galaxy Z Fold 7, Samsung now has two book-style foldables: the productivity-focused Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra and the shorter, wider (when folded) Galaxy Z Fold 8 that’s geared more toward entertainment. The Galaxy Z Flip 8, meanwhile, is a touch thinner and lighter than its predecessor, but otherwise retains the same design as the Flip 7.  
你可能注意到，三星今年的折叠屏产品线有些不同。今年三星并未简单更新 Galaxy Z Fold 7，而是推出了两款书本式折叠屏：主打生产力的 Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra 和更短更宽（折叠状态下）的 Galaxy Z Fold 8，后者更偏向娱乐。与此同时，Galaxy Z Flip 8 比前代产品略薄、略轻，但设计基本延续了 Flip 7。

All three phones also feature Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 for Galaxy processor and ship with Android 17 and One UI 9, which add new AI features like Gemini Intelligence task automation and Now Nudge, which proactively surfaces helpful suggestions like an event to your calendar.  
这三款手机均搭载高通专为 Galaxy 打造的 Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 处理器，并预装 Android 17 和 One UI 9，新增了如 Gemini Intelligence 任务自动化和 Now Nudge 等 AI 功能，后者会主动推送日历活动等实用建议。

We’re still in the process of testing all three foldables, but after spending a few hours with them, we’ve shared our early hands-on impressions of the Galaxy Z Flip 8, Galaxy Z Fold 8, and Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra. Below, we’ve gathered everything you need to know about preordering Samsung’s new foldables, including pricing, carrier deals, and more.  
我们仍在测试这三款折叠屏手机，但在短暂体验后，已分享了关于 Galaxy Z Flip 8、Galaxy Z Fold 8 和 Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra 的初步上手印象。以下汇总了关于三星新款折叠屏手机预售的所有关键信息，包括价格、运营商优惠等。

If you’re after Samsung’s most capable foldable, the Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra is the one to preorder. It retains the design of the Z Fold 7 while adding a larger 5,000mAh silicon-carbon battery, plus faster 45W wired and 20W wireless charging. It offers up to 16GB of RAM and an upgraded camera system with a new 50-megapixel ultrawide sensor alongside its 200-megapixel main camera. 若追求三星最强折叠屏，Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra 是首选。它延续了 Z Fold 7 的设计，并升级了更大的 5,000mAh 硅碳电池，支持更快的 45W 有线充电和 20W 无线充电。最高可选 16GB RAM，摄像头系统升级，在 2 亿像素主摄基础上新增了 5,000 万像素超广角传感器。与更小的 Galaxy Z Fold 8 一样，它也采用了三星新的 Flex 钛金属显示技术，使折痕更不明显。
