# the companion cli  
# 配套 CLI 工具

plain ssh late.sh already gets you everything. the optional late binary adds the parts a terminal alone cannot do: plays the radio on your own speakers, feeds the audio visualizer, carries voice-room mic and playback, runs the music booth youtube window, and pastes images straight out of your clipboard into chat. it launches the same ssh session for you. next up: screen and video sharing.  
仅用 `ssh late.sh` 就能获得全部基础功能。可选的 `late` 二进制程序则增加了终端单独无法实现的功能：在本地扬声器播放电台、驱动音频可视化、传输语音室的麦克风与回放、运行音乐间的 YouTube 窗口，以及将剪贴板中的图片直接粘贴到聊天中。它也会为你启动相同的 SSH 会话。下一步：屏幕与视频共享。

# artboard  
# 画板

a shared ASCII canvas. paint, erase, sign your work. each cell remembers who placed it. snapshots are saved daily and monthly, so the history sticks around as the board keeps changing.  
一个共享的 ASCII 画布。绘画、擦除、为作品署名。每个格子都记得是谁放置的。每日和每月都会保存快照，因此随着画布不断变化，历史记录也会保留。

/gallery browse → full-screen viewer with pan, zoom, and per-cell author info  
/gallery 浏览 → 全屏查看器，支持平移、缩放及每个格子的作者信息

# identity  
# 身份

no passwords. no OAuth. no accounts. your ssh key is your identity. chats, scores, and streaks are tied to your public key fingerprint. same key, same data.  
无需密码。无需 OAuth。无需账户。你的 SSH 密钥就是你的身份。聊天记录、分数和连续记录都绑定到你的公钥指纹。同一密钥，同一数据。

# privacy  
# 隐私

we store your key fingerprint, not the full public key. no IP logging. no tracking. no analytics. chat messages and game scores are stored in postgres, tied only to your fingerprint. don't trust us? use a throwaway key:  
我们只存储你的密钥指纹，而非完整公钥。不记录 IP。不跟踪。不分析。聊天消息和游戏分数存储在 PostgreSQL 中，仅绑定到你的指纹。不信任我们？使用一次性密钥：

$ ssh-keygen -t ed25519 -f ~/.ssh/late_throwaway && ssh -o IdentitiesOnly=yes -i ~/.ssh/late_throwaway late.sh
