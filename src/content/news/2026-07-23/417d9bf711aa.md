# Microsoft brings original Xbox backward compatibility to Windows PCs  
# 微软将原版 Xbox 向后兼容功能带到 Windows PC 平台

For years, Microsoft has leaned heavily into extensive backward compatibility across multiple generations of Xbox hardware as a major selling point for its consoles. Today, that effort expands past the console ecosystem, making select original Xbox titles officially playable (and purchasable) on Windows PCs for the first time.  
多年来，微软一直凭借跨多代 Xbox 硬件的广泛向后兼容性作为其主机的主要卖点。如今，这一努力已超越主机生态系统，首次让部分原版 Xbox 游戏在 Windows PC 上正式可玩（并可购买）。

The appropriately and bluntly named “Xbox Backward Compatibility on PC” program kicks off today with compatibility for BLiNX: The Time Sweeper, Conker: Live and Reloaded, Crimson Skies: High Road to Revenge, and Fuzion Frenzy.  
 aptly 命名为“PC 版 Xbox 向后兼容”的计划今日启动，首批兼容游戏包括《BLiNX: The Time Sweeper》、《Conker: Live and Reloaded》、《Crimson Skies: High Road to Revenge》和《Fuzion Frenzy》。

PC players can download each title for $10, but those with Xbox Game Pass subscriptions or existing digital licenses for these games on console will have instant access without an additional purchase (no such luck if you simply stick an original Xbox disc in your PC drive, alas).  
PC 玩家可下载每款游戏，售价 10 美元；但拥有 Xbox Game Pass 订阅或已在主机上拥有这些游戏数字许可证的用户可即时访问，无需额外购买（若仅将原版 Xbox 光盘插入 PC 光驱则无法运行，可惜）。

Playing these older Xbox titles on PC enables some bonus graphical features like Vsync support, anisotropic filtering, enhanced anti-aliasing, and up to 4x resolution scaling of the original SD signal (to 2560×1920).  
在 PC 上游玩这些老 Xbox 游戏可启用一些额外图形功能，如垂直同步支持、各向异性过滤、增强抗锯齿，以及将原始标清信号最高 4 倍分辨率缩放（至 2560×1920）。

Games are limited to their original frame rates and aspect ratios, though, which means a 30 fps 4:3 image in most cases.  
但游戏仍受限于其原始帧率和宽高比，多数情况下为 30 帧 4:3 画面。

Microsoft says this first batch of games should be playable with 11-year-old GTX 950 graphics hardware, but recommends a 2017-era GTX 1070 Ti with 8GB of VRAM for best performance.  
微软表示，首批游戏应可在 11 年前的 GTX 950 显卡上运行，但为获得最佳性能，建议使用 2017 年左右的 GTX 1070 Ti（8GB 显存）。

Early testing from Digital Foundry found the PC experience akin to playing the same games on an Xbox Series X. That said, the testers reported weak anisotropic filtering, some frame timing issues that led to “jerky camera motion,” and “a handful of visual bugs” across the initial wave of titles.  
Digital Foundry 的早期测试发现，PC 体验与在 Xbox Series X 上运行相同游戏相似。不过，测试人员报告称各向异性过滤较弱，部分帧时序问题导致“相机运动卡顿”，且首批游戏中存在“一些视觉漏洞”。

What even is an “Xbox” game, anymore? From a technical standpoint, getting original Xbox games running on PC isn’t a big deal. Since the days of the Xbox 360, Microsoft has relied on software emulation to get original Xbox games running on subsequent console hardware.  
如今到底什么才算“Xbox”游戏？从技术角度看，让原版 Xbox 游戏在 PC 上运行并非难事。自 Xbox 360 时代起，微软便依赖软件模拟让原版 Xbox 游戏在后续主机硬件上运行。

Porting that kind of emulation technology to the PC is a simple, if not trivial, process and mimics functionality that any number of Xbox emulators have offered to PC players for years now.  
将此类模拟技术移植到 PC 是一个简单（甚至 trivial）的过程，且功能上多年来已有无数 Xbox 模拟器为 PC 玩家提供类似服务。

Today’s announcement is much more relevant from a business perspective, signaling another crack in the already porous wall between Microsoft’s PC and console gaming ambitions.  
从商业角度看，今日的公告意义更为重大，它标志着微软 PC 与主机游戏雄图之间本已多孔的墙壁再添一道裂缝。

Those cracks date back to the 2016 rollout of Xbox Play Anywhere cross-platform purchases, but more recently have extended to Microsoft granting the Xbox branding to handheld gaming PCs and announcing that the upcoming Project Helix will play “Xbox and PC games.”  
这些裂缝可追溯至 2016 年推出的 Xbox Play Anywhere 跨平台购买计划，而近期更延伸至微软将 Xbox 品牌授权给掌上游戏 PC，并宣布即将推出的“Project Helix”将运行“Xbox 与 PC 游戏”。

Making classic Xbox exclusive playable directly on PC represents yet another sign that Microsoft wants to effectively merge its struggling console business with the more successful Windows gaming ecosystem.  
让经典 Xbox 独占游戏直接在 PC 上可玩，再次表明微软希望将其挣扎中的主机业务与更成功的 Windows 游戏生态系统有效融合。

And Microsoft says today’s rollout is just “the beginning of a broader effort to preserve Xbox games from the past and bring them to PC over time,” meaning I can still hold out hope of one day playing Rockstar Games Presents Table Tennis on my Windows 11 gaming rig.  
微软表示，今日的推出仅是“更广泛努力的开端，旨在保存过去的 Xbox 游戏并逐步将其带到 PC”，这意味着我仍可期待有一天能在我的 Windows 11 游戏PC上玩到《Rockstar Games Presents Table Tennis》。
