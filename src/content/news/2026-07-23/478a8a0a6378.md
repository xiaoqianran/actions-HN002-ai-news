### Samsung unveils new Galaxy Z8 foldables and Galaxy Watches, available August 7  
三星发布新款Galaxy Z8折叠屏手机及Galaxy手表，8月7日起发售  

The rumors haven’t left much to the imagination, but Samsung has unfurled its 2026 foldable lineup today. There are three models for the first time in Samsung’s eight-gen foldable family, including the top-of-the-line Z Fold 8 Ultra. That phone is the successor to last year’s Galaxy Z Fold 7, but the Z Fold 8 is new—the company’s first wide-body foldable designed for media over multitasking. And then there’s a new, thinner flip phone, plus a pair of smartwatches.  
尽管传闻早已曝光细节，三星今日仍正式揭开了其2026年折叠屏产品线。这是三星八代折叠屏家族首次推出三款机型，包括顶级旗舰Z Fold 8 Ultra。该机型是去年Galaxy Z Fold 7的继任者，而Z Fold 8则是全新设计——三星首款为影音娱乐而非多任务优化的宽体型折叠屏。此外还有一款更薄的翻盖手机，以及两款智能手表。  

You can preorder Samsung’s new hardware today, but the component shortage has made these already spendy product lines more expensive this year. Even the Z Flip 8 is now well over a grand, and the Ultra costs almost twice as much.  
用户今日即可预订三星新款硬件，但零部件短缺导致这些本就昂贵的产品线今年进一步涨价。即便是Z Flip 8也已超过1000美元，而Ultra版本价格接近其两倍。

The Z Fold 8 Ultra has the same basic form factor as the last few Samsung tablet-style foldables. A 6.5-inch OLED on the outside is about the same ratio as a standard smartphone screen. Open it up, and you get an 8-inch display that’s roughly twice as wide. That makes the main display close to square. While the Ultra’s display is perfect for running two phone apps side by side, it’s not ideal for content consumption.  
Z Fold 8 Ultra延续了三星近年平板式折叠屏的基本形态。外屏为6.5英寸OLED，比例接近普通智能手机屏幕。展开后则是约两倍宽的8英寸显示屏，主屏接近正方形。虽然Ultra的屏幕非常适合并排运行两个手机应用，却不适合影音内容消费。  

The 5.5-inch cover display on the wider phone has a chunky 10:16 ratio. Unfold it, and the Z Fold 8 becomes a 7.6-inch 4:3 display. This gives widescreen videos a little more breathing room with smaller black bars, and it’s supposedly an ideal shape for reading documents in portrait orientation.  
宽体版Z Fold 8的外屏为5.5英寸，拥有较宽的10:16比例。展开后变为7.6英寸的4:3屏幕。这为宽屏视频提供了稍大的显示空间（黑边更小），据称也是竖屏阅读文档的理想比例。  

Both the Ultra and wide-body Folds are impressively thin at 4.1 mm and 4.5 mm (unfolded), respectively.  
Ultra和宽体Fold在展开状态下分别薄至4.1毫米和4.5毫米，令人印象深刻。  

Samsung’s larger foldables should have noticeably better battery life. The Z Fold 8 has a 4,800 mAh cell, and the Ultra goes all the way to 5,000 mAh. The Z Fold 7, which slimmed down Samsung’s foldable silhouette considerably, only managed to fit 4,400 mAh. The Z Flip 8 is slightly slimmer than past devices of its type, and the battery capacity remains at 4,300 mAh.  
三星更大尺寸的折叠屏手机续航应有显著提升。Z Fold 8配备4800mAh电池，Ultra更是达到5000mAh。而大幅减薄的Z Fold 7仅容纳了4400mAh电池。Z Flip 8比前代机型略薄，电池容量仍为4300mAh。  

Samsung has mercifully improved charging speeds this year, but only for the tablet-style foldables. Both those phones now max out at 45 W instead of 25 W, where the Z Flip 8 remains.  
今年三星总算提升了充电速度，但仅限于平板式折叠屏。这两款手机充电功率从25W提升至45W，而Z Flip 8仍维持原样。  

All of Samsung’s new foldables feature a design element the company calls “Flex Titanium.” No, it’s not going back to titanium frames. Flex Titanium consists of a titanium alloy film under the flexible OLED panels. Samsung says this makes the screen more resistant to impact and smoother to open, and it reduces the visibility of the dreaded foldable crease. There’s also a new low-reflection finish on the displays that should make them more usable outdoors.  
三星所有新款折叠屏均采用名为“Flex Titanium”的设计元素。注意，这不是回归钛金属中框。Flex Titanium是在柔性OLED面板下方增加一层钛合金薄膜。三星称这使屏幕更耐冲击、开合更顺滑，并减少了令人头疼的折痕。屏幕还采用了新的低反射涂层，户外可视性应会提升。  

If you want that wide screen, you’ll have to compromise on cameras. The Ultra has a triple lens setup like last year’s big foldable: a 200 MP primary, a 10 MP 3x telephoto, and a 50 MP ultrawide. The Z Fold 8 has only two rear cameras: a 50 MP primary and a 50 MP ultrawide. The Flip 8 has the same primary but swaps in a 12 MP sensor for the ultrawide.  
若你追求宽屏，就得在摄像头方面妥协。Ultra延续了去年大折叠的三摄组合：2亿像素主摄、1000万像素3倍长焦和5000万像素超广角。Z Fold 8仅配备双后摄：5000万像素主摄和5000万像素超广角。Flip 8主摄相同，但超广角换为1200万像素传感器。  

The new phones will ship with Android 17 and One UI 9, which Samsung says has been further optimized for the AI era. Now Brief can make more suggestions, including for security and privacy insights. That could potentially mean more alerts from Now Nudge, which proactively offers AI help across the device. The phones also ship with Gemini Notebook (formerly NotebookLM), with support for enhanced foldable multitasking.  
新款手机将预装Android 17和One UI 9，三星称其为AI时代进行了进一步优化。“Now Brief”可提供更多建议，包括安全与隐私洞察。这可能意味着“Now Nudge”会发出更多提示，该功能可主动提供全设备AI协助。手机还预装Gemini Notebook（原NotebookLM），并支持增强型折叠屏多任务处理。  

The price hikes this year aren’t that much in the grand scheme, but Samsung’s foldables were already very expensive. The Ultra and Flip are only $100 more than their 2025 counterparts, but foldables were already seeing price bumps even before the RAMpocalypse. A $1,200 price tag will make the Flip 8 a tough sell when Motorola has Razr phones for hundreds less, even if they aren’t as fast. At $2,100, the Z Fold 8 Ultra will be the most spendy foldable in most markets, but anyone who’s going to spend $2,000 on one can probably justify another $100.  
从整体来看，今年的涨价幅度并不算大，但三星折叠屏手机原本就已非常昂贵。Ultra和Flip仅比2025年 counterparts贵100美元，但即便在“内存涨价潮”（RAMpocalypse）之前，折叠屏价格已在上涨。1200美元的定价让Flip 8销售艰难，因为摩托罗拉Razr系列便宜数百美元，即便性能可能不及。而2100美元的Z Fold 8 Ultra将成为多数市场最贵的折叠屏，但愿意花2000美元的用户， probably 不会在意再多100美元。
