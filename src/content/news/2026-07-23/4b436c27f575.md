# Beyond Output-Space Calibration: Spectral Evidence Bundling for Selective Reliability Estimation in Time-Series Classification

# 超越输出空间校准：时间序列分类中选择性可靠性估计的谱证据捆绑

**Authors:** Filippo Cenacchi, Longbing Cao, Runze Yang  
**作者：** Filippo Cenacchi, Longbing Cao, Runze Yang

Post-hoc calibration for time-series classification usually remaps output scores, but deployment decisions such as trust, abstention, and review depend on whether a confident prediction is supported by the current temporal signal. We address three time-series reliability gaps: identical confidence values can hide different temporal support, average calibration can miss false high-confidence errors, and output-space recalibration offers limited input-linked auditability.  
时间序列分类的事后校准通常重新映射输出分数，但部署决策（如信任、弃权和审查）取决于置信预测是否得到当前时间信号的支持。我们解决了三个时间序列可靠性差距：相同的置信度值可能隐藏不同的时间支持，平均校准可能遗漏虚假的高置信度错误，而输出空间重新校准提供的输入链接可审计性有限。

We introduce a validation-gated fixed-label reliability policy that keeps the backbone prediction unchanged while estimating whether it should be trusted. The method combines output-side cues with whole-sample spectral descriptors, including band energy, entropy, peak dominance, period support, and phase stability, to form a scalar reliability estimate and diagnostic band-level evidence.  
我们引入了一种验证门控固定标签可靠性策略，该策略在估计是否应信任预测时保持骨干预测不变。该方法结合输出侧线索与全样本谱描述符，包括频带能量、熵、峰值主导性、周期支持和相位稳定性，以形成标量可靠性估计和诊断级频带证据。

A validation gate enables spectral conditioning only when correctness ranking improves without breaching FalseConf@0.9 or AURC tolerances; otherwise it reverts to the safer output-space baseline. Across eight heterogeneous UCR/UEA datasets, eight time-series backbone families, and standard recalibrators, the unconstrained method improves fixed-label selective-reliability metrics on the matched evaluation subset, raising Corr-AURC from 0.693 to 0.779. The validation-gated policy further improves Corr-AURC to 0.786 and reduces FalseConf@0.9 to 0.094.  
验证门控仅在正确性排名改善且不违反 FalseConf@0.9 或 AURC 容差时启用谱条件化；否则，它恢复到更安全的输出空间基线。在八个异构 UCR/UEA 数据集、八个时间序列骨干系列和标准重新校准器上，无约束方法提高了匹配评估子集上的固定标签选择性可靠性指标，将 Corr-AURC 从 0.693 提高到 0.779。验证门控策略进一步将 Corr-AURC 提高到 0.786，并将 FalseConf@0.9 降低到 0.094。

These results suggest that reliability estimation for time-series classifiers benefits from bundling output confidence with spectral evidence, while validation gating prevents unsupported spectral conditioning.  
这些结果表明，时间序列分类器的可靠性估计通过捆绑输出置信度与谱证据而受益，而验证门控可防止无支持的谱条件化。
