**From Agent Failure Paths to Quantified Residual Risk: A Compositional Framework for Resilient Agentic AI**  
**从智能体失效路径到量化残余风险：一种面向弹性智能体AI的组合框架**

**Authors:** Hassan Karim, Sai Sitharaman, Deepti Gupta, Danda B. Rawat  
**作者：** 哈桑·卡里姆、赛·西塔拉曼、迪普蒂·古普塔、丹达·B·拉瓦特

**Abstract:** Agentic AI is crossing trust boundaries faster than current risk models can represent. Existing approaches provide one of two partial views. They either describe failure mechanisms without producing a transferable residual-risk estimate, or they produce a risk estimate while treating the internal failure path as a black box.  
**摘要：** 智能体AI跨越信任边界的速度已超过现有风险模型的表征能力。当前方法仅提供两种局部视角之一：要么描述失效机制却无法生成可迁移的残余风险估计，要么生成风险估计却将内部失效路径视为黑箱。

We couple those two views by proposing CPSAINT, a seven-layer integrity decomposition over Physical state, Sensors, Data, Compute, Actuators, Environment, and Time, paired with FRIESA-K, a residual-risk functional that maps each failure path to a quantified risk instance.  
我们通过提出CPSAINT（一种针对物理状态、传感器、数据、计算、执行器、环境与时间的七层完整性分解）与FRIESA-K（一种将每条失效路径映射为量化风险实例的残余风险泛函）来耦合这两种视角。

FRIESA-K grounds the resistance term K in a controlled absorbing Markov model so that control effectiveness is derived from state dynamics rather than assigned as an informal score.  
FRIESA-K将抵抗项K建立在受控吸收马尔可夫模型基础上，使控制有效性源自状态动力学而非非正式评分。

The result is a concise mechanism-to-magnitude pipeline for resilient agentic and embodied AI. We report governance observability through a separate additive penalty instead of inserting governance as a new variable in the resistance functional.  
最终形成一条从机制到量级的简洁管道，适用于弹性智能体与具身AI。我们通过独立的加法惩罚项报告治理可观测性，而非将治理作为新变量插入抵抗泛函。

We formalize structural composability linking valid failure paths to well-defined risk instances and show the framework on two contrasting scenarios: a hard real-time warehouse robot and a governance-instrumented financial-services agent.  
我们形式化了结构组合性，将有效失效路径与明确定义的风险实例关联，并在两个对比场景中验证该框架：硬实时仓库机器人及治理工具化的金融服务智能体。

Across both cases, the same layer grammar, variable semantics, and dynamic-resistance construction remain intact. Thus, we obtain a compact kernel that supports cross-domain reasoning, explicit assumptions, and quantitatively grounded formalism of composable trust.  
在两种场景中，相同的层语法、变量语义与动态抵抗构建均保持完整。因此，我们获得了一个紧凑内核，支持跨域推理、显式假设以及可组合信任的量化形式化。
