**Title:** ALAS: Additive Learnable Alpha-Stable Kernels for Flexible Bayesian Optimization  
**标题：** ALAS：用于灵活贝叶斯优化的可加可学习α稳定核

**Authors:** Weibo Huang, Cheng Hua  
**作者：** 黄伟波，程华

**Abstract:** Bayesian Optimization is widely used for expensive black-box optimization, yet its success often depends on choosing a kernel that matches the objective's unknown structure.  
**摘要：** 贝叶斯优化广泛应用于昂贵的黑盒优化问题，但其成功往往取决于选择一个与目标函数未知结构相匹配的核函数。

In this work, we propose ALAS, a flexible Gaussian Process kernel family built from symmetric $\alpha$-stable spectral components.  
在本研究中，我们提出了ALAS，一个由对称$\alpha$稳定谱分量构建的灵活高斯过程核族。

By learning the stability parameter $\alpha$, ALAS adapts its effective smoothness from data, capturing both smooth trends and sharp irregularities.  
通过学习稳定性参数$\alpha$，ALAS能够从数据中自适应其有效平滑度，同时捕捉平滑趋势和尖锐不规则性。

We present two parameterizations: ALAS, a single stationary component with joint spectral modulation, and ALAS-Sep, a separable variant that learns dimension-wise tail behavior to improve robustness on approximately decomposable objectives.  
我们提出了两种参数化形式：ALAS（具有联合谱调制的单一平稳分量）和ALAS-Sep（一种可分离变体，通过学习各维度的尾部行为来提升对近似可分解目标的鲁棒性）。

Experiments on standard benchmarks and real-world surrogates demonstrate strong and robust performance across diverse settings.  
在标准基准测试和真实世界代理模型上的实验表明，ALAS在不同场景下均表现出强劲且鲁棒的性能。
