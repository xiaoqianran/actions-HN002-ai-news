**Title: Travis Kalanick’s robotics company raises $1.7B, led by a16z**

**标题：Travis Kalanick 的机器人公司融资 17 亿美元，由 a16z 领投**

---

Travis Kalanick’s robotics company, Atoms, has raised $1.7 billion in a funding round led by Andreessen Horowitz. Ben Horowitz will join the company’s board following the investment. Bain Capital, Fifth Wall, and others participated in the round.

Travis Kalanick 的机器人公司 Atoms 在一轮由 Andreessen Horowitz 领投的融资中筹集了 17 亿美元。Ben Horowitz 将在投资后加入公司董事会。Bain Capital、Fifth Wall 和其他方也参与了本轮融资。

---

Perhaps most notably, Uber also joined the funding round, re-connecting Kalanick with the company he founded — and the same company that pushed him out as CEO in 2017 following complaints of sexual harassment, discrimination, and a toxic workplace.

最值得注意的是，Uber 也参与了本轮融资，使 Kalanick 与他创立的公司重新建立联系——正是这家公司在 2017 年因性骚扰、歧视和有毒工作场所的投诉将他赶下 CEO 职位。

---

Atoms is essentially a rebranded holding company created atop the project Kalanick has been working on since he resigned from Uber, a ghost kitchen play called Cloud Kitchens. When he revealed the new name in March, Kalanick announced he had acquired Pronto, the heavy industry automation company run by his former Uber colleague Anthony Levandowski.

Atoms 本质上是一个重新命名的控股公司，建立在 Kalanick 自辞去 Uber 职务以来一直从事的项目之上，该项目是一个名为 Cloud Kitchens 的幽灵厨房业务。在今年 3 月公布新名称时，Kalanick 宣布他收购了 Pronto，这是一家由其前 Uber 同事 Anthony Levandowski 运营的重工业自动化公司。

---

Kalanick has also said he wants to get into mining with atoms, going beyond what Pronto already does for vehicles in that industry. Last year, it was reported that Kalanick was interested in buying the U.S. arm of Chinese self-driving vehicle company Pony AI with backing from Uber. The Information reported in March that those talks had ended.

Kalanick 还表示，他希望通过 Atoms 进军采矿业，超越 Pronto 目前在该行业为车辆提供的服务。去年有报道称，Kalanick 曾有兴趣在 Uber 的支持下收购中国自动驾驶公司小马智行（Pony AI）的美国业务。《The Information》在 3 月报道称，这些谈判已经结束。

---

In a post on X about the fundraise, Kalanick didn’t go into specifics about what he wants to build with Atoms. “On many levels, this round is a bit of unfinished business. Fuel to complete the bits-to-atoms story arc we started at Uber, continued at CloudKitchens and will now finish at Atoms,” Kalanick wrote. “16 years ago, I started a journey to digitize the physical world. Understand, predict and control the physical world with software. Building ‘atoms-based’ computers where CPU is manufacturing, storage is real estate, and network is transportation.”

在 X 上关于此次融资的一篇帖子中，Kalanick 没有具体说明他想用 Atoms 构建什么。“从很多层面来说，这一轮融资是一些未竟的事业。为完成我们在 Uber 开始、在 CloudKitchens 延续、现在将在 Atoms 终结的‘从比特到原子’的故事弧提供燃料，”Kalanick 写道。“16 年前，我开始了一段将物理世界数字化的旅程。用软件理解、预测和控制物理世界。构建‘基于原子’的计算机，其中 CPU 是制造业，存储是房地产，网络是交通运输。”

---

Kalanick has said he wants to build a “wheelbase for robots” with Atoms. “Travis Is Back,” declared a simultaneous post from Ben Horowitz on Wednesday. “It takes a rare kind of entrepreneur to change these old-school, heavy parts of our economy. They need a gritty work ethic, drive, and range that spans across domains, from software architecture to mechanical engineering. Travis is that guy,” said Horowitz.

Kalanick 曾表示，他想用 Atoms 构建一个机器人的“轮式底盘”。“Travis 回来了，”Ben Horowitz 周三同时发布的一篇帖子宣称。“改变我们经济中这些老派、沉重的部分需要一种罕见的企业家。他们需要一种坚韧的职业道德、驱动力，以及跨越从软件架构到机械工程多个领域的能力。Travis 就是那个人，”Horowitz 说。

---

Horowitz wrote that Atoms will be all about making people more productive in the physical world. “I think the most valuable thing someone could do with AI and robotics is to repeat the same thing Uber did for transportation, or that computers did for the digital world: to make everything and everyone more productive,” his post reads.

Horowitz 写道，Atoms 将完全关于让人们在物理世界中更具生产力。“我认为有人能用 AI 和机器人做的最有价值的事情，就是重复 Uber 对交通运输所做的一切，或计算机对数字世界所做的一切：让一切和每个人都更具生产力，”他的帖子写道。

---

Kalanick didn’t go into specifics, but it sounds like a good chunk of funding will go toward hiring. “Changemakers, the builders of tomorrow’s progress machines, will inevitably go up against the final boss, Nature and its fierce resistance to change. Nature is going to throw everything it has at the builders in this new industrial age and it’s going to take humanity’s strongest to stay the course and get these complex systems and industries over the finish line,” he wrote.

Kalanick 没有具体说明，但听起来很大一部分资金将用于招聘。“变革者，未来进步机器的建造者，将不可避免地面对最终 Boss——自然及其对变化的激烈抵抗。自然将在这场新的工业时代向建造者倾泻一切，需要人类最强大的人才能坚持到底，让这些复杂的系统和产业冲过终点线，”他写道。
