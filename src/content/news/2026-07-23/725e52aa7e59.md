# Science Corporation’s vision-restoring chip wins EU approval  
# Science Corporation 的视力修复芯片获欧盟批准

Science Corporation, a startup developing novel brain-computer interfaces (BCI), won approval from Europe’s medical device regulator to begin selling a device that restores vision lost from age-related macular degeneration.  
开发新型脑机接口（BCI）的初创公司 Science Corporation 已获得欧洲医疗器械监管机构的批准，开始销售一款可恢复因年龄相关性黄斑变性而丧失视力的设备。

The company said the device, called PRIMA, also received a designation from the US Food and Drug Administration that is the first step toward an expedited regulatory review, which could see the device used to treat two rare kinds of blindness.  
该公司表示，这款名为 PRIMA 的设备还获得了美国食品药品监督管理局（FDA）的认定，这是加速监管审查的第一步，未来该设备可能被用于治疗两种罕见的失明症。

Millions of people around the world suffer from age-related macular degeneration, which destroys the light-sensitive cells at the back of the eyes, making it difficult to read and recognize faces.  
全球数百万人患有年龄相关性黄斑变性，该疾病会破坏眼球后部的感光细胞，导致阅读和识别人脸变得困难。

To use the device, patients suffering from this loss of vision undergo an hour-long outpatient procedure that plants a small chip in back of their eye. Then they wear camera-equipped glasses that transmit a view of the world to the chip.  
使用该设备时，患者需接受一小时的门诊手术，在眼球后方植入一枚小型芯片。随后，他们佩戴配备摄像头的眼镜，将外界景象传输至芯片。

Max Hodak, Science Corp.’s founder and CEO, says the product gives functional vision to people who have lost it.  
Science Corp. 的创始人兼首席执行官 Max Hodak 表示，该产品为失去视力的人提供了功能性视力。

“One of our patients in France finished a 300-page novel a little while ago, and sent us the book,” Hodak told TechCrunch. “We have a sketch on the wall [that] one of our patients drew of the Sydney Opera House. There are videos of patients playing crossword puzzles and filling in Sudoku.”  
“我们的一位法国患者不久前读完了一本 300 页的小说，并把书寄给了我们，” Hodak 告诉 TechCrunch。“墙上挂着一幅患者画的悉尼歌剧院素描。还有患者玩填字游戏和数独的视频。”

Hodak is known as the co-founder and former president of Neuralink, Elon Musk’s BCI startup. He left in 2021 to start Science Corp., with plans to develop a novel BCI based on a hybrid of silicon chips and living cells.  
Hodak 是 Elon Musk 的 BCI 初创公司 Neuralink 的联合创始人兼前总裁。他于 2021 年离开并创立了 Science Corp.，计划开发一种基于硅芯片与活细胞混合的新型 BCI。

But first, the company had to prove out its processes and develop a sustainable business.  
但首先，公司必须验证其流程并建立可持续的业务。

“The thing that the space needs is a company making $100 million a year of revenue,” Hodak said. “There’s this risk that the whole thing enters a winter, and so we think it’s important to build a sustainable business as we develop these longer-term technologies.”  
“这个领域需要一家年收入达 1 亿美元的公司，” Hodak 说。“存在整个行业陷入寒冬的风险，因此我们认为在开发这些长期技术的同时建立可持续的业务至关重要。”

Hodak and his colleagues believe that sustainable business will be restoring vision to the blind, specifically patients whose conditions stem from problems with the light-detecting cells at the back of the eye.  
Hodak 及其同事认为，可持续的业务将是为盲人恢复视力，特别是那些因眼球后部感光细胞问题而导致病症的患者。

After exploring multiple approaches, they determined that Pixium, a French company that developed the PRIMA technology, had the right path forward and acquired the firm in 2024.  
在探索了多种方法后，他们认定开发 PRIMA 技术的法国公司 Pixium 拥有正确的方向，并于 2024 年收购了该公司。

Science Corp. used its internal platform to build out the documentation and evolve the product to prepare it for regulatory approval and commercialization.  
Science Corp. 利用其内部平台完善文档并改进产品，为监管批准和商业化做准备。

Each PRIMA device is expected to cost in the hundreds of thousands of dollars; Science Corp. and its medical partners in Europe are currently in discussion with healthcare providers over reimbursement.  
每台 PRIMA 设备的成本预计达数十万美元；Science Corp. 及其在欧洲的医疗合作伙伴目前正与医疗服务提供商讨论报销问题。

The company is laying the groundwork to begin offering PRIMA in Germany, where its clinical trials were held, and could see the first procedure in September.  
该公司正在德国（其临床试验举办地）开展准备工作，以开始提供 PRIMA 服务，首例手术可能于 9 月进行。

Science Corp. expects to continue improving the vision capabilities of PRIMA with a new chip, and the form factor of its glasses, which currently require a battery-pack to operate.  
Science Corp. 计划通过新芯片继续提升 PRIMA 的视觉能力，并改进其眼镜的外形设计（目前需要电池组供电）。

The goal is to offer something like Meta’s AR glasses, but the power and compute requirements for PRIMA are more significant.  
目标是提供类似 Meta AR 眼镜的产品，但 PRIMA 的功耗和计算要求更高。

Science Corp. is also working with Dr. Murat Günel, chair of Yale Medical School’s Department of Neurosurgery, to develop procedures for human trials of a directly implanted bio-hybrid brain sensor.  
Science Corp. 还与耶鲁医学院神经外科主任 Murat Günel 博士合作，为直接植入式生物混合脑传感器的人体试验开发手术方案。

Hodak says bringing PRIMA to market is “the most important thing for the company, because we don’t get to do the bio hybrid stuff long term if you don’t have a great vision business. That’s what’s really financing the rest of it — that’s the thing that investors know how to build spreadsheets around.”  
Hodak 表示，将 PRIMA 推向市场是“公司最重要的事，因为如果没有出色的视力业务，我们就无法长期开展生物混合研究。这才是真正资助其他项目的东西——这也是投资者知道如何制作电子表格评估的业务。”
