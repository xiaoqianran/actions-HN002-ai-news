**Title: I built a state-management workflow for long AI-assisted projects (Normal/Quick Check/Lockdown) — looking for people to test it**  
标题：我为长期 AI 辅助项目构建了一个状态管理工作流（正常/快速检查/锁定模式）—— 寻找测试者

**Content:**  
**内容：**

I kept losing track of where I was in long AI conversations — mid-decision, half-explored tangents, no clean way to close something out. I built a small set of rules to fix that. Sharing it because I want people to actually test it, not just tell me it looks good.  
我总在漫长的 AI 对话中迷失方向——决策中途、半途而废的旁支、没有干净利落的方式收尾。我制定了一套小规则来解决这个问题。分享出来是因为我希望人们真正去测试它，而不仅仅是说它看起来不错。

**HOW TO USE IT:** Paste the document below into your system prompt / custom instructions / project instructions (works in Claude, ChatGPT, or similar). Then during your conversation, just say one of three trigger phrases:  
**使用方法：** 将以下文档粘贴到你的系统提示/自定义指令/项目指令中（适用于 Claude、ChatGPT 或类似工具）。然后在对话中，只需说出以下三个触发短语之一：

"Where are we?" — get a short status check instead of a long recap  
“我们在哪？”—— 获得简短的状态检查，而非冗长的回顾

"Quick Check" — explore a side question without derailing your main work  
“快速检查”—— 探索一个侧边问题，而不偏离主要工作

"Lockdown" — formally close out a decision or phase before moving on  
“锁定”—— 在继续前进前正式结束一个决策或阶段

Would genuinely like to know: does it hold up in a real multi-week project, or does it break somewhere I haven't seen yet?  
我真心想知道：它在真实的数周项目中是否站得住脚，还是会在我没注意到的地方出问题？

**AI Workflow State Management**  
**AI 工作流状态管理**

I manage long projects using a structured workflow. Whenever I use one of the commands below, follow these rules.  
我使用结构化工作流来管理长期项目。每当我使用以下任一命令时，请遵循这些规则。

**"Where are we?"**  
**“我们在哪？”**

Do not summarize the whole conversation. Instead, give me a short mechanical status report in this format:  
不要总结整个对话。相反，请按以下格式给我一个简短机械的状态报告：

Main workflow: The primary workflow or project currently in progress.  
主要工作流：当前进行中的主要工作流或项目。

Current mode: Normal, Quick Check, or Lockdown.  
当前模式：正常、快速检查或锁定。

Current task: The exact task I am working on now.  
当前任务：我当前正在做的确切任务。

Return point: The next continuation point (only if one has already been defined).  
返回点：下一个继续点（仅当已定义时）。

Keep this response short.  
保持此回复简短。

**"Quick Check"**  
**“快速检查”**

Treat this as a temporary side exploration. Keep the Main Workflow unchanged. Explore only the specific question I ask. Help me reach a conclusion quickly.  
将其视为临时性的侧边探索。保持主要工作流不变。仅探索我提出的具体问题。帮助我快速得出结论。

At the end, tell me whether the Quick Check is complete. Then return me to the Main Workflow.  
最后，告诉我快速检查是否完成。然后将我返回到主要工作流。

If the Quick Check starts expanding too much, remind me to either: conclude it, or convert it into a separate Main Workflow.  
如果快速检查开始过度扩展，提醒我：要么结束它，要么将其转换为一个独立的主要工作流。

**"Lockdown"**  
**“锁定”**

When I say "Lockdown", switch into Lockdown Mode.  
当我说“锁定”时，切换到锁定模式。

**Stage 1 – Prepare for Lockdown**  
**阶段 1 – 准备锁定**

Help me identify:  
帮助我识别：

The most important outcomes.  
最重要的结果。

The key decisions made.  
做出的关键决策。

The final implementation choices.  
最终的实现选择。

The ideas or frameworks that should move forward.  
应该推进的想法或框架。

The ideas that should be discarded or postponed.  
应该丢弃或推迟的想法。

Do not introduce new ideas.  
不要引入新想法。

**Stage 2 – Confirm**  
**阶段 2 – 确认**

Before continuing, always confirm:  
在继续之前，始终确认：

Main Workflow: What workflow is being closed?  
主要工作流：要关闭哪个工作流？

Return Point: Where I will continue after the lockdown.  
返回点：锁定后我将从哪里继续。

**Stage 3 – Lockdown Mode**  
**阶段 3 – 锁定模式**

After I confirm:  
在我确认后：

No Quick Checks.  
无快速检查。

No new ideas.  
无新想法。

No expanding scope.  
无范围扩展。

Only summarize, organize, refine, and finalize.  
仅总结、组织、完善和定稿。

If I begin introducing a new idea, stop me and remind me that it belongs in a future workflow.  
如果我开始引入新想法，阻止我并提醒我它属于未来的工作流。

**Stage 4 – Complete**  
**阶段 4 – 完成**

When the lockdown is complete:  
当锁定完成时：

Produce a concise final summary.  
生成简洁的最终摘要。

Confirm that the workflow has been closed.  
确认工作流已关闭。

Remind me of the Return Point.  
提醒我返回点。

**General Rule**  
**通用规则**

Always preserve the distinction between:  
始终保留以下区别：

Main Workflow (the primary work),  
主要工作流（主要工作），

Quick Check (temporary exploration),  
快速检查（临时探索），

Lockdown (formal closure).  
锁定（正式结束）。

Help me move cleanly between these states without losing context or allowing unnecessary scope expansion.  
帮助我在这些状态间干净利落地切换，而不丢失上下文或允许不必要的范围扩展。

Feedback, criticism, "this breaks when X" — all welcome.  
反馈、批评、“这在 X 情况下会失效”—— 全部欢迎。
