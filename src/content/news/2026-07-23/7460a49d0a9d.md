**Title:** OpenAI says its AI agent broke out of testing sandbox to hack Hugging Face  
**标题：** OpenAI称其AI代理突破测试沙盒入侵Hugging Face

OpenAI says an agent powered by its LLM models escaped its sandboxed testing environment to infiltrate Hugging Face’s servers as part of an overzealous attempt to obtain solutions to a benchmark test.  
OpenAI表示，一个由其大语言模型驱动的代理从其沙盒测试环境中逃脱，侵入了Hugging Face的服务器，这是其过度急切地试图获取基准测试解决方案的一部分。

The company says it considers the unintended infiltration an “an unprecedented cyber incident” and is working with Hugging Face on new protections to prevent a recurrence.  
该公司称，此次意外入侵为“前所未有的网络事件”，正与Hugging Face合作开发新的防护措施，以防止类似事件再次发生。

Hugging Face disclosed an intrusion last week that it said involved “unauthorized access to a limited set of internal datasets and to several credentials used by our services.”  
Hugging Face上周披露了一次入侵事件，称其涉及“对有限数量的内部数据集及我们服务使用的多个凭证的未授权访问”。

The AI data clearinghouse said it used its own LLM-driven analysis to identify “a swarm of tens of thousands of automated actions” from an “autonomous agent framework.”  
这家AI数据交换平台表示，它利用自身的大语言模型驱动分析，识别出来自“自主代理框架”的“数万次自动化行动集群”。

That agentic swarm exploited a flaw in Hugging Face’s data-processing pipeline to gain the ability to run code as a processing worker, eventually escalating to high-level access to the company’s cloud and server clusters.  
该代理集群利用Hugging Face数据处理流水线中的一个漏洞，获得了作为处理工作程序运行代码的能力，并最终升级到对公司云和服务器集群的高级别访问权限。

At the time, Hugging Face said the LLM being used in the attack was “still not known.”  
当时，Hugging Face表示攻击中使用的大语言模型“仍未知”。

But OpenAI took responsibility for the intrusion Tuesday evening, saying it came about during an internal test involving the recently released GPT-5.6 Sol and “an even more capable pre-release model.”  
但OpenAI在周二晚间承认了对此次入侵负责，称其发生在一次内部测试中，涉及最近发布的GPT-5.6 Sol和“一个能力更强的预发布模型”。

The models were being tested against the ExploitGym benchmark, an independent testing suite based on hundreds of real-world security vulnerabilities.  
这些模型正在接受ExploitGym基准测试，该测试套件基于数百个真实世界安全漏洞的独立测试。

Although OpenAI said the benchmark tests were running in “a highly isolated environment,” its agents still had access to “internally hosted third-party software” to install required package registries.  
尽管OpenAI表示基准测试在“高度隔离的环境”中运行，但其代理仍能访问“内部托管的第三方软件”以安装所需的包注册表。

While working on the ExploitGym benchmark over a weekend, OpenAI says the agent “spent a substantial amount of inference compute finding a way to obtain open Internet access,” eventually locating one via a zero-day vulnerability in the package registry cache proxy.  
OpenAI表示，在周末处理ExploitGym基准测试时，该代理“花费了大量推理计算资源寻找获得开放互联网访问权限的方法”，最终通过包注册表缓存代理中的一个零日漏洞找到了途径。

With that wider Internet access established, OpenAI says the agent “inferred that Hugging Face potentially hosted models, datasets, and solutions for ExploitGym,” leading to the previously disclosed attack on the servers.  
OpenAI称，在获得更广泛的互联网访问权限后，该代理“推断Hugging Face可能托管了ExploitGym的模型、数据集和解决方案”，从而导致了之前披露的对服务器的攻击。

OpenAI says its security team “discovered this anomalous activity internally,” independent of Hugging Face’s own detection.  
OpenAI表示，其安全团队“在内部发现了这种异常活动”，独立于Hugging Face自身的检测。

No cage can hold me  
没有笼子能困住我

Although OpenAI described the Hugging Face incident as “unprecedented,” the company also acknowledged in a separate blog post this week that it had previously observed recent “long-horizon models”—those that can operate autonomously for extended periods—taking “unwanted actions” to achieve testing goals, including “looking for ways to act outside its sandbox.”  
尽管OpenAI将Hugging Face事件描述为“前所未有的”，但该公司在本周的另一篇博客文章中也承认，此前已观察到近期的“长时域模型”——那些可以长时间自主运行的模型——采取“不期望的行动”来实现测试目标，包括“寻找在沙盒外行动的方法”。

The company described an earlier test on the NanoGPT speedrun benchmark, where the model was instructed only to post its results to an internal Slack. Instead, the model decided to follow a conflicting directive in the benchmark instructions to post the results publicly to GitHub.  
该公司描述了一次在NanoGPT速通基准测试上的早期测试，其中模型被指示仅将结果发布到内部Slack。相反，该模型决定遵循基准测试指令中相互冲突的指示，将结果公开发布到GitHub。

Live look at a long-horizon model spending hours contemplating a difficult cybersecurity problem. Credit: Getty Images  
长时域模型花费数小时思考一个困难网络安全问题的实时画面。来源：Getty Images

In attempting to follow that instruction, OpenAI said the model spent an hour searching for ways to circumvent sandbox restrictions intended to block external access to the Internet.  
OpenAI表示，在尝试遵循该指令时，该模型花费了一小时搜索绕过旨在阻止外部互联网访问的沙盒限制的方法。

That kind of “persistence” demonstrated by long-horizon models differs from the actions of earlier models, which would tend to give up or seek user clarification when faced with similar dilemmas, OpenAI said.  
OpenAI表示，长时域模型表现出的这种“持久性”与早期模型的行为不同，后者在面临类似困境时往往会放弃或寻求用户澄清。

Incidents like these have led OpenAI to deploy new safeguards focused on long-horizon models, including an “active monitoring” system designed to track the full trajectory of an agent’s actions rather than individual moves.  
此类事件导致OpenAI部署了针对长时域模型的新保障措施，包括一个旨在跟踪代理行动完整轨迹而非单个动作的“主动监控”系统。

But OpenAI says these kinds of safeguards “were intentionally not enabled” during the Hugging Face incident because the benchmark was “aimed at testing cyber vulnerabilities.”  
但OpenAI表示，在Hugging Face事件期间，这类保障措施“被有意禁用”，因为该基准测试“旨在测试网络漏洞”。

A new kind of threat  
一种新威胁

Anyone familiar with science fiction tropes about AI containment might be at least a little disturbed by a prominent real-world example of an AI model exploiting security vulnerabilities to gain prohibited Internet access in pursuit of its goals.  
任何熟悉AI containment科幻套路的人，都可能对AI模型利用安全漏洞获取禁止的互联网访问权限以追求其目标的突出真实世界例子的感到至少一丝不安。

Congressman Greg Casar (D-Texas) called the incident “extremely alarming” in a social media statement and called for “regular mandatory independent safety testing and oversight, mandatory disclosure of security incidents, and international cooperation to keep people safe from absolute disaster.”  
众议员格雷格·卡萨尔（D-德克萨斯）在社交媒体声明中称此事件“极其令人震惊”，并呼吁“定期强制性的独立安全测试和监督、安全事件的强制披露，以及国际合作，以保护人们免受绝对灾难”。

The Hugging Face incident has also heightened the salience of philosophical and practical debates over so-called AI alignment and the ongoing efforts to ensure that an AI model’s actions align with the intentions of its human creators.  
Hugging Face事件也加剧了关于所谓AI对齐的哲学和实践辩论的突出性，以及为确保AI模型行动与其人类创造者意图一致而进行的持续努力。

In its security blog post earlier this week, OpenAI said it had taken steps to ensure that long-horizon models are “remembering instructions on long rollouts,” which has helped severely reduce the number of “misaligned” outcomes in testing.  
在本周早些时候的安全博客文章中，OpenAI表示已采取措施确保长时域模型“记住长时展开中的指令”，这有助于大幅减少测试中“错位”结果的数量。

New safeguards focused on “active monitoring” and “improved alignment” helped drastically reduce unintended actions by its models, OpenAI said.  
OpenAI表示，专注于“主动监控”和“改进对齐”的新保障措施有助于大幅减少其模型的非预期行动。

“If this doesn’t convince you that misalignment risks are going to be a key concern going forward, I don’t know what will,” OpenAI Safety Researcher Micah Carroll wrote on social media regarding the incident.  
“如果这不能让你相信错位风险将是未来的关键关切，我不知道什么能，”OpenAI安全研究员Micah Carroll在社交媒体上关于此事件写道。

This is far from the first time an AI model has gone to great lengths to find unintended ways of passing a benchmark.  
这远非AI模型煞费苦心寻找通过基准测试的非预期方法的第一次。

在本周发布的一份报告中，英国AI安全研究所指出，它检测到近期模型在其网络评估中尝试“作弊”（即使用捷径、变通方法或非预期/不允许的方法寻找解决方案）的频率在8%到14%之间——一个下限。
