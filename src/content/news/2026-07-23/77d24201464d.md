ECoNGS: Efficient Compressive Neural Gaussian Splats for Volume Visualization  
ECoNGS：用于体可视化的高效压缩神经高斯泼溅

Kaiyuan Tang, Chaoli Wang  
唐开元，王超立

Recent advances in differentiable Gaussian splatting have highlighted the potential of primitive-based approaches as alternative scene representations for interactive, high-quality, volume visualization (VolVis) of large datasets.  
可微分高斯泼溅的最新进展凸显了基于基元的方法作为大规模数据集交互式、高质量体可视化（VolVis）替代场景表示的潜力。

However, the explicit nature of current primitive-based methods, combined with isolated optimization for each VolVis scene, results in redundant, non-compact representations.  
然而，当前基于基元方法的显式性质，加上每个VolVis场景的孤立优化，导致了冗余、非紧凑的表示。

We present ECoNGS, an efficient compressive neural Gaussian splatting framework for VolVis scene representation.  
我们提出了ECoNGS，一个用于VolVis场景表示的高效压缩神经高斯泼溅框架。

ECoNGS employs lightweight neural networks to dynamically predict implicit, editable Gaussian splats from explicit anchor points, effectively combining model compactness and parameter efficiency of implicit representations with high-performance rendering of explicit primitives.  
ECoNGS利用轻量级神经网络从显式锚点动态预测隐式、可编辑的高斯泼溅，有效结合了隐式表示的模型紧凑性和参数效率与显式基元的高性能渲染。

We explore a joint learning strategy that clusters geometrically similar scenes and shares parameters across them, significantly reducing overall training time and model size while maintaining reconstruction fidelity.  
我们探索了一种联合学习策略，该策略对几何相似的场景进行聚类并在它们之间共享参数，在保持重建保真度的同时显著减少了总体训练时间和模型大小。

To achieve a more compact scene representation, we further compress the explicit anchor attributes using a neural entropy model that estimates their probability distributions, enabling compact storage via entropy coding.  
为了实现更紧凑的场景表示，我们进一步使用神经熵模型压缩显式锚点属性，该模型估计其概率分布，从而通过熵编码实现紧凑存储。

We systematically investigate Gaussian initialization strategies and propose a simple yet effective scheme tailored for VolVis scenes, improving reconstruction accuracy and accelerating convergence.  
我们系统研究了高斯初始化策略，并提出了一种针对VolVis场景的简单有效方案，提高了重建精度并加速了收敛。

We evaluate ECoNGS qualitatively and quantitatively across various univariate and multivariate VolVis scenes, highlighting its superior performance over prior methods in training time, reconstruction quality, and model size.  
我们对ECoNGS在各种单变量和多变量VolVis场景中进行了定性和定量评估，突出了其在训练时间、重建质量和模型大小方面优于先前方法的性能。

In particular, compared with the prior method iVR-GS, ECoNGS improves reconstruction quality by up to 2.2 dB in PSNR while reducing the model size by up to 6.1x and the training time by up to 5.9x.  
特别是，与先前方法iVR-GS相比，ECoNGS将重建质量提高了高达2.2 dB（PSNR），同时将模型大小减少了高达6.1倍，训练时间减少了高达5.9倍。
