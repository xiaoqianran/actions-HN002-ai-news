# Tesla spending skyrockets as Cybercab, Semi, Megapack production timeline slips  
特斯拉支出激增，Cybercab、Semi与Megapack量产时间表推迟

---

Tesla is no longer planning to reach “volume production” of three of its newest products — the Cybercab, the Tesla Semi, and its Megapack 3 commercial energy-storage solution — in 2026, according to a second-quarter shareholder letter published Wednesday.  
根据周三发布的一份第二季度股东信，特斯拉不再计划在2026年实现其三款最新产品——Cybercab、特斯拉Semi半挂卡车以及Megapack 3商用储能解决方案的“量产”。

The company also removed language from its first-quarter letter about its Optimus robot reaching “volume production.”  
该公司还从其第一季度信中删除了关于Optimus机器人达到“量产”的表述。

The company said Wednesday that it’s trying to increase battery production, specifically around the company’s 4680 cell, in order to start building the Cybercab and Tesla Semi at scale. It did not offer a reason for pushing back volume production of the new Megapack, but Tesla CEO Elon Musk cautioned about the challenges of Optimus.  
公司周三表示，正在努力提高电池产量，特别是围绕其4680电芯，以便开始大规模生产Cybercab和特斯拉Semi。对于推迟新款Megapack的量产，公司未给出原因，但特斯拉CEO埃隆·马斯克对Optimus的挑战发出了警告。

“This is going to be the hardest product to scale manufacturing that we’ve ever made at Tesla, because everything on the robot is new,” Musk said about Optimus on a conference call Wednesday.  
“这将是特斯拉有史以来最难实现规模化制造的产品，因为机器人的所有部件都是新的，”马斯克在周三的电话会议上谈到Optimus时表示。

Tesla started making the first production Cybercabs at its factory in Austin, Texas, earlier this year but said in the letter that it’s still building out the manufacturing lines for the Semi and Optimus.  
特斯拉今年早些时候已在德克萨斯州奥斯汀工厂开始生产首批Cybercab，但信中表示，仍在为Semi和Optimus建设生产线。

The company had said as recently as January that the Cybercab, Semi, and Megapack 3 would reach “volume production” this year.  
就在今年1月，该公司还表示Cybercab、Semi和Megapack 3将在今年实现“量产”。

The pullback comes as the company plows money into its next generation of products while attempting to shift from an EV maker to an AI and robotics company.  
这一退步正值公司将大量资金投入下一代产品，同时试图从一家电动汽车制造商转型为人工智能和机器人公司。

Tesla’s results, which showed net income falling 5% year-over-year to $1.1 billion, capital expenditures more than doubling, and negative free cash flow, were slightly buoyed by an uptick in revenue.  
特斯拉的业绩显示，净利润同比下降5%至11亿美元，资本支出增加一倍以上，自由现金流为负，但收入的增长略微提振了业绩。

Still, that revenue boost wasn’t enough to offset the cost of business and Tesla’s push to develop and launch new products, which Tesla CFO Vaibhav Taneja previously said would lead to negative cash flow for the remainder of the year.  
然而，收入的增长不足以抵消业务成本以及特斯拉开发和推出新产品的投入。特斯拉首席财务官维巴夫·塔内贾此前表示，这将在今年剩余时间导致负现金流。

The company reported revenue of $28.2 billion, a 26% increase from the $22.5 billion it generated in the second quarter of 2025.  
公司报告收入为282亿美元，较2025年第二季度的225亿美元增长26%。

Tesla’s second-quarter revenue also grew from the previous quarter’s haul of $22.38 billion.  
特斯拉第二季度收入也较上一季度的223.8亿美元有所增长。

The bulk of its revenue came from selling and leasing its EVs — and those results improved significantly this quarter.  
其收入大部分来自销售和租赁电动汽车——且本季度这些结果显著改善。

The company reported automotive revenue of $20.5 billion in the second quarter, compared to $16.6 billion in the same-year ago period.  
公司报告第二季度汽车收入为205亿美元，而去年同期为166亿美元。

Tesla delivered more than 480,000 vehicles in the second quarter, an increase of more than 120,000 from the first quarter.  
特斯拉第二季度交付了超过48万辆汽车，比第一季度增加超过12万辆。

It was Tesla’s best result for overall sales since the third quarter of last year, when it delivered nearly 500,000 vehicles.  
这是自去年第三季度交付近50万辆以来，特斯拉整体销售的最佳成绩。

The increase was driven by record sales in several markets outside of the U.S., including South Korea, Australia, Colombia, Japan, Taiwan, Thailand, Portugal, the Philippines, Chile, Slovenia, and Lithuania, the company said in its shareholder letter.  
公司在股东信中表示，这一增长由美国以外多个市场的创纪录销售推动，包括韩国、澳大利亚、哥伦比亚、日本、台湾、泰国、葡萄牙、菲律宾、智利、斯洛文尼亚和立陶宛。

Tesla’s second-quarter revenue results improved from a year ago when the company suffered from a combination of falling EV sales, lower average selling prices, less cash from regulatory credits, and a drop in solar and energy revenue.  
特斯拉第二季度收入结果较一年前有所改善，当时公司遭受了电动汽车销量下降、平均售价降低、监管积分现金减少以及太阳能和能源收入下降的多重打击。

Sales of energy storage and solar also proved to be a standout, improving 13% to $3.1 billion.  
储能和太阳能销售也表现突出，增长13%至31亿美元。

And subscriptions to Tesla’s advanced driver-assistance system, known as Full Self-Driving (Supervised) continue to rise. The company reported 1.48 million subscriptions, a 56% increase from the same period last year.  
特斯拉高级驾驶辅助系统（称为全自动驾驶（监督版））的订阅量持续上升。公司报告订阅量为148万，较去年同期增长56%。

Tesla’s bottom line, however, slipped as it poured money into new products and saw its gross margins squeezed.  
然而，由于投入资金开发新产品且毛利率受到挤压，特斯拉的底线有所下滑。

Tesla reported net income of $1.1 billion, a 5% decrease from the same period a year ago.  
特斯拉报告净利润为11亿美元，较去年同期下降5%。

At the same time, its operating expenses ballooned by 47% to $4.3 billion.  
与此同时，其运营费用膨胀47%至43亿美元。

Meanwhile, Tesla had negative free cash flow of $1 billion in the second quarter, a stark change from the $1.44 billion in positive free cash flow it reported last quarter and the $146 million it had in the same period last year.  
与此同时，特斯拉第二季度自由现金流为负10亿美元，这与上一季度报告的14.4亿美元正自由现金流以及去年同期1.46亿美元的自由现金流形成鲜明对比。

The company’s operating income was $398 million, a 57% drop from the $932 million it reported in the same period last year.  
公司运营收入为3.98亿美元，较去年同期的9.32亿美元下降57%。

A year ago, Tesla called the second quarter of 2025 a “seminal point” in the company’s history and the beginning of its transition from a company that sells electric vehicles, solar, and energy storage to one that leads in “AI, robotics and related services.”  
一年前，特斯拉称2025年第二季度是其公司历史的“开创性时刻”，也是其从销售电动汽车、太阳能和储能产品的公司向引领“人工智能、机器人及相关服务”公司转型的开端。

That transition is still underway and Musk has said the company would boost spending to achieve its goal.  
这一转型仍在进行中，马斯克表示公司将增加支出以实现目标。

Tesla said its capital expenditure will be $25 billion in 2026, about three times more than it historically has spent.  
特斯拉表示，其2026年资本支出将为250亿美元，约为历史支出的三倍。

This spring, the company ended production of its flagship Model S sedan and Model X SUV vehicles at its Fremont, California, factory to make way for its Optimus humanoid robot.  
今年春天，该公司结束了其在加州弗里蒙特工厂旗舰车型Model S轿车和Model X SUV的生产，为Optimus人形机器人让路。

It is also bringing its Tesla Robotaxi service to new cities, albeit with a limited number of vehicles.  
它还在将特斯拉Robotaxi服务推广到新城市，尽管车辆数量有限。

And it’s still pushing to sell owners on Full Self-Driving (Supervised), and eventually make that product capable enough to handle all driving without the need of a human.  
同时，公司仍在向车主推销全自动驾驶（监督版），并最终使其产品能够无需人工干预处理所有驾驶任务。
