**Title: Clayface trailer leans into the body horror**  
**标题：泥面人预告片深入身体恐怖主题**

---

The San Diego Comic-Con kicks off tomorrow, and DC Studios and Warner Brothers are setting the stage by dropping the first full trailer for Clayface, part of the DC Universe’s “Gods and Monsters” arc.  
圣迭戈动漫展将于明天开幕，DC影业与华纳兄弟率先发布了泥面人首支完整预告片，该片属于DC宇宙“神与怪物”篇章。

The film started out as a Mike Flanagan project (he’s the horror master who brought us The Haunting of Hill House, The Fall of the House of Usher, Midnight Mass, and Doctor Sleep, among other creepy delights).  
该项目最初由恐怖大师迈克·弗拉纳根操刀（他曾执导《鬼庄园》《厄舍府的崩塌》《午夜弥撒》《睡梦医生》等令人毛骨悚然的佳作）。

Flanagan said he was inspired by the 1992 “Feat of Clay” episode in Batman: The Animated Series, aspiring to create a film that combined body horror with a tragic thriller.  
弗拉纳根表示，他受1992年《蝙蝠侠：动画系列》中“泥的壮举”一集启发，旨在创作一部融合身体恐怖与悲剧惊悚的电影。

DC Studios CEOs James Gunn and Peter Safran loved Flanagan’s pitch and brought the movie into their rebooted DCU.  
DC影业CEO詹姆斯·古恩与彼得·萨夫兰非常欣赏弗拉纳根的构想，将这部电影纳入其重启的DC宇宙。

Alas, Flanagan had to bow out of the project due to scheduling conflicts—he’s a busy man these days—and James Watkins stepped in as director.  
可惜弗拉纳根因档期冲突退出项目（他如今日程繁忙），导演之位由詹姆斯·沃特金斯接任。

But Gunn has said the final script retained Flanagan’s original story, with Hossein Amini doing some rewrites.  
但古恩表示，最终剧本保留了弗拉纳根的原创故事，侯赛因·阿米尼进行了部分改写。

The film will feature the origin story of the Matt Hagen incarnation of Clayface, introduced to the DCU in the animated Creature Commandos series (Alan Tudyk voiced the character).  
影片将聚焦马特·哈根版泥面人的起源故事，该角色在动画《生物突击队》系列中首次引入DC宇宙（由艾伦·图代克配音）。

Per the official premise: Matt Hagen’s meteoric rise from Gotham street kid to Hollywood leading man is tragically cut short when a local crime boss’s actions leave him horribly disfigured.  
官方剧情梗概：哥谭街头小子马特·哈根一跃成为好莱坞当红男星，却因当地黑帮头目之手惨遭毁容，星途戛然而止。

Abandoned by his team and with nothing left, Matt seeks out a cutting edge treatment that miraculously returns him to his former self… but also unexpectedly affects his grip on reality, spiraling him down a destructive path of all-consuming revenge.  
被团队抛弃、一无所有的马特寻求一项尖端治疗，奇迹般恢复原貌……却意外动摇了他的现实感知，将他拖入毁灭性的复仇深渊。

The original shape-shifting villain from the 1940s was a successful actor before turning to crime, so it’s a nice little callback to the character’s long, rich history.  
这位1940年代原创的变形反派本是成功演员，后沦为罪犯，此番设定巧妙呼应了角色悠久而深厚的历史。

Tom Rhys Harries (Doctor Who, The Gentlemen) stars as Hagen/Clayface, alongside Naomi Ackie (Mickey 17) as Dr. Caitlin Bates, described as a “fringe” scientist and a love interest for Hagen.  
汤姆·莱斯·哈里斯（《神秘博士》《绅士们》）饰演哈根/泥面人，娜奥米·阿基（《米奇17》）饰演凯特琳·贝茨博士——一位“边缘”科学家，也是哈根的爱慕对象。

Max Minghella (The Handmaid’s Tale) plays a Gotham City police detective who just happens to be dating Bates.  
马克斯·明格拉（《使女的故事》）饰演一位哥谭市警探，恰与贝茨博士交往。

The cast also includes Eddie Marsan, David Dencik, Francesca Corney, Ruby Sear, and Wil Coban in as-yet-undisclosed roles.  
演员阵容还包括埃迪·马森、大卫·丹尼克、弗朗西斯卡·科尼、鲁比·西尔与威尔·科班，角色尚未公开。

A teaser dropped in April showing a bandaged Hagen in a hospital bed, ending with a shot of the character’s now-putty-like face beginning to shapeshift.  
今年4月发布的 teaser 预告中，缠满绷带的哈根躺在病床上，结尾镜头显示其面孔如黏土般开始变形。

This latest trailer opens with Bates asking Hagen to show her his badly disfigured face, covered in a mask.  
最新预告开场，贝茨要求哈根展示其严重毁容、覆盖面具的脸庞。

He wants his old life back—the Hollywood golden boy before thugs carved up his face—and he’s hoping Bates can give it to him.  
他渴望重获旧日生活——成为恶徒毁容前的好莱坞金童——并寄望贝茨能帮他实现。

She has a radical new treatment involving the injection of a mysterious gooey substance.  
她有一种激进的新疗法，涉及注射一种神秘黏稠物质。

Hagen volunteers to be a human guinea pig, even though she cautions, “This isn’t cosmetic surgery”; there could be side effects, and she won’t know how to treat them.  
哈甘自愿充当人体试验品，尽管她警告：“这不是整形手术”；可能存在副作用，且她不知如何应对。

“Even if it kills me, I’ll be better off than I am now,” Hagen insists.  
“即便这会杀死我，我也比现在强，”哈根坚持道。

He is very wrong about that.  
他大错特错。

At first, the treatment seems to have worked, but then his face starts sagging mid-flight, and we see Hagen holding a dopp kit knife in the aircraft’s restroom, presumably about to use it to open his now-blocked eyes.  
起初治疗似乎见效，但飞行中他的脸开始松弛，镜头切至哈根在飞机洗手间手持修眉刀，似欲割开已无法睁开的双眼。

Ewww.  
呃呃呃。

His body-morphing condition just gets worse from there, fueling his rage—or perhaps it’s the other way around.  
自此，他的身体变形状况愈演愈烈，助长其怒火——或许因果恰恰相反。

Clayface hits theaters on October 23, 2026.  
《泥面人》将于2026年10月23日上映。
