Scientists today face challenges of extraordinary scale and complexity. From shaping and simulating the intricate dynamics of fusion plasma, to exploring the vast search space of new materials, to making sense of the exabytes of data pouring out of the world's most advanced experimental facilities. The demands on modern research are unprecedented. Frontier AI can help address these challenges, while accelerating groundbreaking scientific discoveries.  
**当今科学家面临着规模与复杂性空前的挑战。从塑造和模拟聚变等离子体的复杂动力学，到探索新材料的广阔搜索空间，再到解读全球最先进实验设施产生的海量数据。现代研究的 demands 是前所未有的。前沿AI有助于应对这些挑战，同时加速突破性的科学发现。**

In December, we shared our commitment to the White House's Genesis Mission — the national effort to harness AI and double the pace of American scientific discovery within a decade. Since then, Google DeepMind (GDM) announced an early access program that provides AI for science tools to all 17 Department of Energy (DOE) National Laboratories, and Google Public Sector shared how Gemini for Government could serve as an AI backbone for the DOE.  
**去年12月，我们宣布支持白宫的“创世纪使命”——这是一项国家行动，旨在利用AI在十年内将美国科学发现的速度提高一倍。此后，谷歌DeepMind（GDM）宣布了一项早期访问计划，向全部17个能源部（DOE）国家实验室提供AI科研工具，而谷歌公共部门也展示了Gemini for Government如何能成为DOE的AI基础平台。**

Today, at the DOE Genesis Mission Summit 2026, we are expanding this by committing $40 million of AI tokens and cloud credits for researchers in support of the Genesis Mission.  
**今天，在2026年DOE“创世纪使命”峰会上，我们进一步扩大支持，承诺为研究人员提供4000万美元的AI代币和云积分，以支持“创世纪使命”。**

---

### Frontier AI tools for scientific discovery  
**用于科学发现的前沿AI工具**

Under this expanded commitment, we will first provide DOE’s Genesis Mission awardees in-kind access to GDM’s frontier AI for science portfolio, including:  
**根据这一扩展承诺，我们将首先为DOE“创世纪使命”获奖者提供GDM前沿AI科研组合的非货币性访问权限，包括：**

*   **AlphaEvolve** — a Gemini-powered coding and discovery agent, for designing advanced algorithms.  
    **AlphaEvolve** —— 一款由Gemini驱动的编码与发现智能体，用于设计先进算法。
*   **AlphaFold 3** — a model for predicting the structure and interactions of proteins and other biomolecules.  
    **AlphaFold 3** —— 一款用于预测蛋白质及其他生物大分子结构与相互作用的模型。
*   **AlphaGenome** — a tool for understanding how variation in DNA, including the non-coding genome, shapes biology and disease.  
    **AlphaGenome** —— 一款用于理解DNA变异（包括非编码基因组）如何影响生物学和疾病的工具。
*   **WeatherNext** — a state-of-the-art family of AI weather forecasting models for mapping weather conditions.  
    **WeatherNext** —— 一系列最先进的AI天气预报模型，用于绘制天气状况。
*   **AlphaEarth Foundations** — a foundational AI model for mapping and understanding our planet in unprecedented detail.  
    **AlphaEarth Foundations** —— 一款基础AI模型，用于以前所未有的细节绘制和理解我们的星球。

Second, we will provide Gemini for Government seats and tokens for one year to tens of thousands of users across the DOE National Laboratories’ operations, research, and management teams. This secure platform supports the full breadth of work from the research bench to the administration of specialized user facilities serving the entire scientific community, providing a single secure foundation that the DOE mission can depend on.  
**其次，我们将为DOE国家实验室运营、研究和管理团队的数万名用户提供为期一年的Gemini for Government席位和代币。这一安全平台支持从研究 bench 到服务于整个科学界的专业用户设施管理的全部工作，为DOE的使命提供一个可依赖的单一安全基础。**

### AI for science tools in action across the laboratory ecosystem  
**AI科研工具在实验室生态系统中的实际应用**

While we have a lot of work still to do, the practical impact of the Genesis Mission is already coming to life across the laboratory ecosystem.  
**尽管我们仍有大量工作要做，但“创世纪使命”的实际影响已在实验室生态系统中初现端倪。**

At Pacific Northwest National Laboratory (PNNL), senior scientist Dr. Henry Kvinge is using AlphaEvolve to map out massive mathematical systems that are far too complex for humans to explore by hand. The AI uncovers hidden connections automatically, fast-tracking discoveries that would normally take researchers years to find.  
**在太平洋西北国家实验室（PNNL），高级科学家亨利·克文格博士正在使用AlphaEvolve绘制人类手动无法探索的庞大数学系统图谱。该AI自动发现隐藏的联系，加速那些通常需要研究人员数年才能发现的成果。**

“Modern math relies on abstraction, but combinatorics offers concrete models that make complex geometry and algebra easier to grasp. We’ve found that systems like AlphaEvolve are perfect for this search,” said Dr. Kvinge. “By leveraging the broad mathematical knowledge of LLMs, we can automate the exploration of countless angles. We’re still experimenting, but the discoveries are already shaping our future research.”  
**克文格博士表示：“现代数学依赖于抽象，但组合数学提供了具体的模型，使复杂的几何和代数更易理解。我们发现像AlphaEvolve这样的系统非常适合这种搜索。通过利用大语言模型广泛的数学知识，我们可以自动化探索无数角度。我们仍在实验，但发现已经塑造了我们未来的研究。”**

At the National Laboratory of the Rockies (NLR), researchers are utilizing Gemini to fundamentally change how they interact with physical laboratory hardware. Dr. Steven R. Spurgeon, a senior materials data scientist at NLR, leads a pioneering program in autonomous materials discovery.  
**在落基山国家实验室（NLR），研究人员正在利用Gemini从根本上改变他们与物理实验室硬件的交互方式。NLR高级材料数据科学家史蒂文·R·斯普吉恩博士领导着一项自主材料发现的先驱计划。**

“Our collaboration has allowed us to build an autonomous experimentation capability,” said Dr. Spurgeon. “By deploying Gemini in our instruments, we cut microscope calibration time from over 90 minutes to about 13 minutes (eight times faster) and reduced the manual steps needed to focus an image from as many as 50 down to two. That's time and attention we've given back to the science itself, enabling genuinely autonomous workflows that observe, reason, and decide in real time. This has helped us explore parts of the material design space we simply could not have reached through manual operation alone.”  
**斯普吉恩博士表示：“我们的合作使我们能够建立自主实验能力。通过在仪器中部署Gemini，我们将显微镜校准时间从90多分钟缩短到约13分钟（快八倍），并将对焦图像所需的手动步骤从多达50步减少到2步。这让我们将时间和注意力归还给科学本身，实现了真正自主的、能实时观察、推理和决策的工作流程。这帮助我们探索了仅靠手动操作根本无法触及的材料设计空间部分。”**

### Driving American innovation  
**驱动美国创新**

The Genesis Mission represents an opportunity to transform research and science across America. By providing access to advanced AI tools, we aim to help scientists accelerate breakthroughs across critical energy, security, and scientific challenges. To learn more about how these AI capabilities can support your research initiatives, join us at the upcoming Google Public Sector Summit in October.  
**“创世纪使命”代表着变革全美研究与科学的机遇。通过提供先进AI工具的访问权限，我们旨在帮助科学家在关键能源、安全和科学挑战上加速突破。要了解这些AI能力如何支持您的研究计划，请参加我们10月即将举行的谷歌公共部门峰会。**
