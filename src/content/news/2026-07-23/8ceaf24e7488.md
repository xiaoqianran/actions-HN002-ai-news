**Title:** Edge-Efficient Transformer for End-to-End RF Spectrum Monitoring  
**标题：** 面向端到端射频频谱监控的边缘高效Transformer

**Authors:** Zhifan Song, Haralampos-G. Stratigopoulos, Hassan Aboushady  
**作者：** 宋志凡、哈拉兰波斯- G·斯特拉蒂戈普洛斯、哈桑·阿布沙迪

**Abstract:** We present E-SpecFormer (Edge Spectrum monitoring Transformer) for end-to-end automatic modulation and covert channel (CC) recognition.  
**摘要：** 我们提出了E-SpecFormer（边缘频谱监控Transformer），用于端到端的自动调制识别与隐蔽通道（CC）识别。

We introduce LiTAN (Linear Tanh Attention Network), a Softmax- and LayerNorm-free attention mechanism that reduces complexity while increasing accuracy in RF tasks.  
我们引入了LiTAN（线性Tanh注意力网络），这是一种无需Softmax和LayerNorm的注意力机制，可在降低复杂度的同时提升射频任务的准确率。

E-SpecFormer is parameterized in four scalable variants (Nano, Small, Medium, Large) to accommodate diverse hardware constraints.  
E-SpecFormer参数化为四种可扩展变体（Nano、Small、Medium、Large），以适应不同的硬件限制。

Using the RadioML2018 dataset for modulation recognition, the Nano variant achieves 86.5% average accuracy for Signal-to-Noise Ratios (SNRs) > 0 dB, and on the hardware Trojan (HT)-based CC dataset it reaches 94.2% accuracy, both with fewer than 10k parameters and up to speed of 92 μs per frame on FPGA/CPU co-execution, surpassing state-of-the-art edge models at a fraction of their cost.  
在RadioML2018数据集上进行调制识别时，Nano变体在信噪比（SNR）> 0 dB条件下达到86.5%的平均准确率；在基于硬件木马（HT）的CC数据集上达到94.2%的准确率。两者均使用少于1万个参数，并在FPGA/CPU协同执行下达到每帧92微秒的速度，以极低的成本超越了最先进的边缘模型。

These results establish E-SpecFormer as an edge-efficient solution for real-time spectrum intelligence on Internet of Things (IoT) devices.  
这些结果确立了E-SpecFormer作为物联网（IoT）设备上实时频谱智能的边缘高效解决方案。
