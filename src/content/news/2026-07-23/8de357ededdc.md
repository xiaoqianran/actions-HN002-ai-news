# ‘The Odyssey’ Is Inspiring Unprecedented Levels of Cope  
# 《奥德赛》正催生史无前例的“精神胜利法”

For months, Elon Musk and the right-wing cranks he caters to on X have whipped themselves into a raging froth over the supposed desecration of an ancient Greek poem by one of the most successful movie directors alive.  
数月来，埃隆·马斯克以及他在X平台上迎合的右翼怪人，因一位当今最成功的电影导演“亵渎”了一首古希腊诗歌而自我煽动，陷入狂怒。

Now that their manufactured backlash has bombed, they’re searching for ways to cope with the reality that they are increasingly divorced from popular opinion.  
如今，他们人为制造的抵制彻底失败，他们正寻找方式应对这样一个现实：他们已日益与主流民意脱节。

Christopher Nolan’s artistic crimes in bringing The Odyssey to the screen, according to Musk and others in a furor, were instances of what they called “historical inaccuracy” in a lavish fantasy teeming with monsters, witches, and undead spirits—mainly the casting of Lupita Nyong'o, who is Black, as Helen of Troy, and Elliot Page, who is trans, as Sinon.  
在马斯克及其他愤怒者看来，克里斯托弗·诺兰将《奥德赛》搬上银幕的“艺术罪行”，是在一个充满怪物、女巫和亡灵的奢华奇幻故事中出现了他们所谓的“历史不准确”——主要指向：由黑人演员露皮塔·尼永奥饰演海伦，由跨性别演员艾略特·佩吉饰演辛松。

The grand total of approximately 10 minutes of screen time between these actors in a three-hour saga, combined with the general hysteria over “wokeness” in Hollywood, a rejection of Americanized dialog, and lingering misogyny regarding scholar Emily Wilson’s 2017 translation of Homer’s epic (the first by a woman), resulted in countless teeth-gnashing posts and calls for a boycott that never materialized.  
这两位演员在三小时史诗中总共仅约10分钟的戏份，叠加好莱坞对“觉醒文化”的普遍歇斯底里、对美国式台词的排斥，以及对学者艾米丽·威尔逊2017年（首位女性译者）荷马史诗译本的 lingering 厌女症，共同催生了无数咬牙切齿的帖文和从未成真的抵制呼吁。

Instead, the mythic drama hauled in a staggering $264 million as it opened this past weekend, sailing toward continued box office dominance and, in all likelihood, a slew of awards.  
相反，这部神话剧在上周末首映时狂揽2.64亿美元，正朝着持续的票房霸主地位，以及很可能的一系列奖项前进。

A few conservative commentators, including Ben Shapiro—no fan of DEI or “woke” values—acknowledged it as a landmark achievement.  
少数保守派评论员，包括本·夏皮罗（他可不是DEI或“觉醒”价值观的粉丝），承认这是一项里程碑式的成就。

But while some who had predicted its doom knew enough to move on from this battle to other fronts in the culture war, many sank deeper into denial.  
但尽管一些曾预言其失败的人足够明智，转而投身文化战争的其他战线，许多人却更深地陷入了否认。

“BREAKING,” blared one anonymous X account on Monday. “The Odyssey is FAKING ticket sales by buying up theaters/seats (Fraud) to make the movie look like a success (already $400 million in the hole) moviegoers are reporting having to take terrible seats (because full) but when they show up the theaters are mostly empty.”  
周一，一个匿名X账号高呼：“突发：《奥德赛》通过包场/包座（欺诈）伪造票房，以制造成功假象（已亏损4亿美元）。观众报告称不得不坐差座位（因为满座），但到场时影院却大多空荡。”

The post included screenshots of unverifiable anecdotes from Reddit about quiet cinemas and no-show ticket holders.  
该帖子附有来自Reddit的、无法证实的关于影院冷清和购票者未出席的轶事截图。

What Universal Pictures, the studio behind the film, would stand to gain by spending millions beyond a $250 million production budget and $125 million in marketing costs to astroturf a flop was not exactly clear.  
环球影业（该片制片方）在2.5亿美元制作预算和1.25亿美元营销成本之外，再花费数百万美元去为一个失败作品进行“草根伪造”，其动机并不十分清晰。

Then again, these are the same conspiracists who find it suspicious that the critics’ reviews tend to all be published at the same time.  
不过，这些阴谋论者本就对影评几乎同时发布感到可疑。

Musk, for his part, seemed to be inspired by an X user who posted three minutes of AI-generated scenes in which characters from The Odyssey are all white and speak in British accents.  
马斯克本人则似乎受到一位X用户的启发，该用户发布了一段三分钟的AI生成片段，其中《奥德赛》角色全是白人且操着英国口音。

Commenting on the clip, which at best resembles a montage from a hokey 1970s adaptation, the richest man in the world declared: “Before this year ends, Grok Imagine will make a full-length movie of The Odyssey that is historically accurate and true to the art of Homer.”  
世界首富在评论这段最多像部蹩脚1970年代改编版混剪的片段时宣称：“在今年年底前，Grok Imagine将制作一部完全忠实于荷马艺术、历史准确的《奥德赛》全长电影。”

Those familiar with Musk know that when he vows to reach a milestone by year’s end, it basically never happens.  
了解马斯克的人知道，当他誓言在年底前达成某个里程碑时，这事基本上从未发生。

The promise of a Grok-animated Odyssey, though minor compared to most of his ambitions—he even speculated in 2025 that the xAI model might “discover new technologies” within several months—is sure to fall by the wayside as well.  
这个由Grok动画制作的《奥德赛》承诺，尽管与他大多数野心相比算小事——他甚至在2025年推测xAI模型可能在数月内“发现新技术”——也注定会被搁置。

Not only is it a tiresome idea with zero appeal to the mainstream moviegoer (who has or will soon see Nolan’s version), it’s also unoriginal: The AI studio Fountain 0 is planning the online release of an artificially generated feature titled Odyssey: The Fall this summer.  
这不仅是一个令主流观众（已或将观看诺兰版）毫无兴趣的乏味想法，而且毫无新意：AI工作室Fountain 0正计划今年夏天在线发布一部名为《奥德赛：陨落》的AI生成长片。

Moreover, both ill-conceived projects misunderstand the lesson that the entertainment industry seems to be taking from Nolan’s latest blockbuster.  
此外，这两个构思拙劣的项目都误解了娱乐业似乎从诺兰最新大片中汲取的教训。

At a moment when executives imagine that AI is the key to cutting costs and turbocharging output, the director has shown once again that the practical craft of filmmaking beats out what he himself has disparaged as “slop.”  
正当高管们设想AI是削减成本、加速产出的关键时，这位导演再次证明，电影制作的实践工艺胜于他本人所贬低的“垃圾作品”。

So, will this aggrieved little cadre of internet reactionaries ever be able to accept that they don’t understand what audiences want? Or that they have deluded themselves, thanks to skewed algorithms and bogus amplification, into thinking they possess an influence they never had? Please. They’re not capable of conceding a loss. And there’s always another outrage down the road.  
那么，这群互联网反动派的小受气包们，能接受他们根本不懂观众想要什么吗？或者接受他们因扭曲的算法和虚假的放大，而自欺欺人地以为自己拥有从未有过的影响力？得了吧。他们不具备认输的能力。而前方永远有下一个愤怒点。
