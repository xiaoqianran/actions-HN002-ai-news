# 标题：2026年最适合初学者、运动员和儿童的6款最佳Fitbit型号

---

It’s been five years since Google officially acquired Fitbit for a reported $2.1 billion, grabbing hardware and software teams that also absorbed assets from Pebble, which Fitbit itself acquired in 2016. So, how have things changed? Well, for starters, Fitbit is now Google Fitbit. It’s not the most imaginative of name changes, and it hasn't stuck in consumers’ minds, but the good news is that Fitbit devices remain some of the most user-friendly and welcoming fitness trackers available. This is especially true if you're just starting, or reigniting, your fitness journey.

---

自谷歌以约21亿美元正式收购Fitbit已过去五年，此次收购整合了硬件和软件团队，并吸收了Fitbit在2016年收购的Pebble公司的资产。那么，事情有何变化？首先，Fitbit现在变成了Google Fitbit。这个改名并不算最具创意，也未在消费者心中留下深刻印象，但好消息是Fitbit设备仍然是最用户友好、最受欢迎的健身追踪器之一。对于刚刚开始或重新投入健身旅程的人来说，尤其如此。

---

Fitbit’s dominance has waned slightly in the face of countless alternatives, but its affordable approach, user-friendly interface, and great styling make it one of the best wearable fitness brands. If you’re looking for a reliable step-counting, sleep-tracking monitor, you’re in for a treat—and thanks to Google’s stewardship, you can now even find a smartwatch with features to rival the competition. Here are my picks of the best Fitbit trackers, whatever your wearable needs are.

---

面对无数替代品，Fitbit的统治地位略有下降，但其实惠的定价、用户友好的界面和出色的设计使其成为最佳可穿戴健身品牌之一。如果你正在寻找一款可靠的计步和睡眠追踪器，你会有惊喜——得益于谷歌的管理，你现在甚至能找到功能可与竞争对手媲美的智能手表。以下是我根据不同可穿戴需求挑选的最佳Fitbit追踪器。

If you don't need another screen competing for your attention, the Google Fitbit Air is the one I'd recommend to most people. At just 12 grams, it's the lightest Fitbit—you'll forget you're wearing it—yet it quietly tracks everything that matters: heart rate, sleep, daily activity, skin temperature variations, and menstrual health (to name a few). And instead of constantly checking stats on your wrist, you'll find all insights in the Google Health app, where your data is presented in an easy-to-understand format.

---

如果你不需要另一个屏幕来争夺你的注意力，那么Google Fitbit Air是我向大多数人推荐的选择。它仅重12克，是最轻的Fitbit——你会忘记自己戴着它——但它安静地追踪所有重要指标：心率、睡眠、日常活动、皮肤温度变化和月经健康（仅举几例）。你无需 constantly checking stats on your wrist，所有洞察都可在Google Health应用中找到，数据以易于理解的格式呈现。

---

The Air nails the basics. Battery life stretches to about a week, and a five-minute top-up gets you roughly a day's worth of wear. Automatic workout detection can occasionally mistake one activity for another, and some of the AI guidance can feel generic, but those are minor trade-offs for a wearable that's comfortable enough to wear all day and night. Its $100 price also makes it one of the most affordable ways to get comprehensive health tracking. The biggest downside of the Air is also its biggest strength; there's no screen, so if you prefer having a display, go with the Fitbit Charge 6. But for most people, the Fitbit Air strikes the best balance of price, comfort, and everyday health tracking.

---

Air把握了基础。电池续航约一周，充电五分钟即可佩戴约一天。自动运动检测偶尔会混淆活动类型，部分AI指导可能感觉通用，但对于一款足以全天候佩戴的舒适可穿戴设备来说，这些都是次要的权衡。其100美元的价格也使其成为获得全面健康追踪的最实惠方式之一。Air最大的缺点也是其最大优势：没有屏幕，因此如果你更喜欢有显示屏，请选择Fitbit Charge 6。但对大多数人来说，Fitbit Air在价格、舒适度和日常健康追踪之间取得了最佳平衡。

Launched in 2023, the Charge 6 remains the best fitness tracker for beginners. It's the same slim build as its predecessor (the Fitbit Charge 5), maintaining the bright AMOLED touchscreen, with an optional always-on display, and fortunately bringing back the physical side button. It monitors your heart rate, blood oxygen, and skin temperature, and it offers ECG and electrodermal activity scans to measure irregular heart rhythms and stress level insights. The largest upgrade on the functionality front is the new ability to connect Fitbit's heart rate sensor to particular gym equipment like the NordicTrack treadmill or Peloton bike. In testing, its heart rate sensors held up well against the Oura ring. Battery life is also fantastic, with up to a week per charge.

---

2023年推出的Charge 6仍然是初学者最佳健身追踪器。它与前代产品（Fitbit Charge 5）保持相同的纤薄设计，保留了明亮的AMOLED触摸屏，可选常亮显示，并幸运地恢复了物理侧边按钮。它监测你的心率、血氧和皮肤温度，并提供ECG和皮肤电活动扫描以测量不规则心律和压力水平洞察。功能方面最大的升级是新增了将Fitbit心率传感器连接到特定健身设备（如NordicTrack跑步机或Peloton自行车）的能力。在测试中，其心率传感器与Oura戒指相比表现良好。电池续航也非常出色，每次充电最长可使用一周。

---

The built-in GPS support doesn't rival dedicated sports watches for accuracy, but it's useful enough for outdoor activities. Plus, unlike those wearables, Fitbit dishes out detailed sleep data that is both insightful and actionable. Google’s presence has increased on the Charge with the useful additions of Google Maps and Google Wallet. You get all of this and more for less than the cost of Fitbit's smartwatches.

---

内置GPS支持的准确性无法与专业运动手表媲美，但对于户外活动来说已经足够实用。此外，与那些可穿戴设备不同，Fitbit提供详细、有洞察力且可操作的睡眠数据。谷歌的存在在Charge上进一步增强，增加了实用的Google Maps和Google Wallet。你以低于Fitbit智能手表的价格获得所有这些及更多功能。

Along with its budget price, the Inspire 3 is Fitbit's most basic tracker with a display. It covers all the essentials: step counting, heart rate monitoring, sleep tracking, and stress management. The battery lasts well over a week, and the slim, lightweight design is comfortable for 24/7 wear. It lacks the advanced health sensors (like ECG) and smart features (like built-in GPS or music controls) found on the Charge 6, but for anyone who wants a no-fuss, reliable activity and sleep tracker, it's an excellent value.

---

除了价格实惠，Inspire 3还是Fitbit最基础的有显示屏追踪器。它涵盖所有 essentials：计步、心率监测、睡眠追踪和压力管理。电池续航超过一周，纤薄轻量的设计适合24/7佩戴。它缺乏Charge 6上的高级健康传感器（如ECG）和智能功能（如内置GPS或音乐控制），但对于任何想要无麻烦、可靠的活动和睡眠追踪器的人来说，它物超所值。
