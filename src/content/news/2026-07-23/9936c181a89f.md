# Compressing What Matters: Neuron Importance Meets Data-Aware Low Rank Approximation for Language Model Compression  
**压缩关键所在：神经元重要性与数据感知低秩近似在语言模型压缩中的结合**

**Authors:** Athanasios Ntovas, Alexandros Doumanoglou, Petros Drakoulis, Dimitris Zarpalas  
**作者：** Athanasios Ntovas, Alexandros Doumanoglou, Petros Drakoulis, Dimitris Zarpalas

arXiv:2607.18284 (cs) [Submitted on 30 Jun 2026]  
arXiv:2607.18284 (cs) [提交于 2026年6月30日]

---

To excel at their domain large language models are comprised of billions of parameters. Yet this comes at the cost of huge memory requirements restricting their applicability in resource-constrained environments.  
为了在特定领域表现出色，大语言模型由数十亿参数构成。然而，这带来了巨大的内存需求，限制了其在资源受限环境中的适用性。

To address the problem of neural network (NN) compression Singular Value Decomposition (SVD) has played a key role as a fundamental component for matrix compression through decomposition.  
为解决神经网络（NN）压缩问题，奇异值分解（SVD）作为通过分解进行矩阵压缩的基础组件发挥了关键作用。

To minimize compression error and to maximize the efficacy of the compressed model on the downstream tasks previous works focused on low-rank approximation of the NN's weight matrices either from the perspective of parameter importance or per-layer functional equivalence.  
为最小化压缩误差并最大化压缩模型在下游任务上的效能，先前的工作聚焦于从参数重要性或逐层功能等效性的角度对神经网络的权重矩阵进行低秩近似。

While previous works studied the aforementioned perspectives in isolation in this work we are investigating the effectiveness of an approach that combines ideas from these two perspectives in a single objective.  
尽管先前的工作孤立地研究了上述视角，本研究我们正在探索一种将这两个视角的想法结合到单一目标中的方法的有效性。

In parallel to this an important aspect that affects the compression quality is the distribution of the compression rate across layers and NN parameters.  
与此同时，影响压缩质量的一个重要方面是压缩率在层和神经网络参数之间的分布。

Earlier works mostly considered distributing the compression rate uniformly across layers and network weights or relied on computationally expensive heuristic search.  
早期的工作主要考虑在层和网络权重之间均匀分配压缩率，或依赖计算成本高昂的启发式搜索。

Contrary to them in this work we propose an enhanced and computationally efficient algorithm for dynamic compression rate allocation.  
与它们相反，本研究我们提出了一种增强且计算高效的动态压缩率分配算法。

Experimental results support the efficacy of the proposed approach which performs on par or substantially better than the previous state-of-the-art especially under high compression ratios.  
实验结果支持了所提出方法的效能，其表现与先前最先进方法相当或在某些情况下显著更优，尤其是在高压缩比下。
