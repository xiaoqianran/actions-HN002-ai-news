**Beyond Single-Dimensional Compression: The Compound Sparsity Frontier of Large Language Models**  
超越单维压缩：大语言模型的复合稀疏性前沿

**Authors:** Chao Han, Haozhe Hu, Xiaoyu Shen  
**作者：** 韩超、胡浩哲、沈晓宇

**Abstract:** Large language models (LLMs) are often compressed through static parameter pruning or dynamic token-level computation, yet aggressive sparsification can trigger rapid performance degradation beyond an essential sparsity boundary. This work asks *whether combining these two mechanisms can delay such degradation by distributing the compression burden*. We study a minimalist compound sparsity framework that first applies low-rank approximation and channel pruning to obtain a statically compressed backbone, and then introduces lightweight routers for per-token dynamic layer skipping. This design enables independent control of parameter sparsity and token-level computation sparsity. Experiments across language understanding and modeling benchmarks show that compound sparsity consistently outperforms single-mechanism compression under the same total sparsity, delaying the decay point on understanding tasks and preserving stronger modeling performance. Further analysis reveals cross-dimensional interference between parameter pruning and token skipping, and shows that near-balanced allocation is most effective under a fixed sparsity budget. These results demonstrate that compound compression provides a practical way to improve LLM compression, while revealing a broader cross-dimensional sparsity boundary that ultimately limits further compression. **摘要：** 大语言模型（LLMs）通常通过静态参数剪枝或动态标记级计算进行压缩，但激进的稀疏化可能在关键稀疏性边界之外引发性能快速下降。本研究探讨*结合这两种机制能否通过分散压缩负担来延缓这种下降*。我们研究了一种极简的复合稀疏性框架：首先应用低秩近似和通道剪枝获得静态压缩的骨干网络，然后引入轻量级路由器实现每标记的动态层跳过。该设计实现了参数稀疏性和标记级计算稀疏性的独立控制。在语言理解和建模基准测试中的实验表明，在相同总稀疏度下，复合稀疏性持续优于单一机制压缩，延缓了理解任务的性能衰减点，并保持了更强的建模性能。进一步分析揭示了参数剪枝与标记跳过之间的跨维度干扰，并表明在固定稀疏预算下近均衡分配最为有效。这些结果证明复合压缩为提升LLM压缩提供了实用方法，同时揭示了一个更广泛的跨维度稀疏性边界，最终限制了进一步压缩。代码将在以下链接发布：https URL。
