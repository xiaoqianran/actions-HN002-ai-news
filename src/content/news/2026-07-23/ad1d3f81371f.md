Title: Ukrainian drones deliver robots directly into battle by sea and air  
标题：乌克兰无人机通过海空将机器人直接送入战场

In the latest twist on battlefield innovations, Ukraine has used both flying drone carriers and drone boats to send ground robots armed with weapons or explosives into battle.  
在战场创新的最新转折中，乌克兰已同时使用飞行无人机母机和无人机艇将配备武器或爆炸物的地面机器人送入战场。

The drone-assisted deployments have enabled slower wheeled robots to bypass tough terrain and Russian defenses, and to carry out an unusual robotic amphibious assault on a contested position.  
这种无人机辅助部署使较慢的轮式机器人得以绕过艰难地形和俄军防御，并对争议阵地实施了罕见的机器人两栖攻击。

Such tactics also showcase the Ukrainian military’s ongoing experimentation with using drones and robots to offset a Russian military advantage in manpower while also sparing Ukrainian soldiers from making dangerous assaults.  
这些战术还展示了乌克兰军方持续试验使用无人机和机器人来抵消俄军的人力优势，同时避免乌军士兵进行危险的突击。

On July 19, a Russian military Telegram channel posted a video showing a Ukrainian hexacopter drone carrying a ground robot suspended below by a cable, according to the Ukrainian government-run news platform United24.  
据乌克兰政府运营的新闻平台United24报道，7月19日，一个俄罗斯军事Telegram频道发布了一段视频，显示一架乌克兰六轴无人机通过电缆悬吊着一台地面机器人。

The Russian Telegram channel described the Ukrainian heavy bomber drone as deploying a four-wheeled ARK-1 robot armed with explosive anti-tank mines on the ground where it continued toward Russian military positions—and apparently generated plenty of outraged comments from pro-Kremlin social media users.  
该俄罗斯Telegram频道称，这架乌克兰重型轰炸无人机部署了一台配备爆炸性反坦克地雷的四轮ARK-1机器人，该机器人在落地后继续向俄军阵地前进——此举显然引发了亲克里姆林宫社交媒体用户的众多愤怒评论。

It’s unclear how successful that particular mission was.  
目前尚不清楚该特定任务的成功程度。

But the tactic could improve the survivability of ground robots by helping them evade Russian aerial surveillance and drone strikes while closing with their targets more quickly.  
但这一战术可能通过帮助地面机器人规避俄军空中监视和无人机打击，同时更快接近目标，从而提高其生存能力。

The ARK-1 robot can roll along at a top speed of nearly 28 miles per hour, although some robots are considerably slower.  
ARK-1机器人的最高滚动速度接近每小时28英里（约45公里），尽管某些机器人的速度要慢得多。

Ukrainian ground robots also joined a Ukrainian military operation on June 25 that targeted the Kinburn Spit, a sandy and narrow peninsula that extends westward into the Black Sea and is connected to the Russian-occupied left bank of the Kherson Oblast.  
乌克兰地面机器人还参与了6月25日的一次乌军行动，目标为金伯恩沙嘴——一个延伸至黑海西部的沙质狭窄半岛，与俄占赫尔松州左岸相连。

Russian military forces captured the Kinburn Spit in June 2022 and set up positions there to help control southern Ukraine’s port access to the Black Sea, although they began withdrawing from the peninsula in early June as Ukraine’s mid- and long-range drone strike campaigns strained Russian supply lines.  
俄军于2022年6月占领金伯恩沙嘴并在此设立阵地，以协助控制乌克兰南部通往黑海的港口通道；然而，随着乌克兰中远程无人机打击行动使俄军补给线承压，俄军于6月初开始从该半岛撤出。

During the June 25 operation, a Ukrainian drone boat lowered a landing ramp to drop off a ground robot on the still-contested Kinburn Spit, according to a video shared by the Ukrainian government on July 13.  
据乌克兰政府7月13日分享的视频显示，在6月25日的行动中，一艘乌克兰无人机艇放下登陆坡道，将一台地面机器人投放到仍处于争夺中的金伯恩沙嘴上。

The remotely controlled robot proceeded to fire its machine gun during apparently “mild” fighting that resulted in a Ukrainian flag-planting demonstration on the contested peninsula, according to Michael Bohnert, a defense researcher at the RAND Corporation think tank, in a social media post.  
据兰德公司智库防务研究员迈克尔·博纳特在社交媒体帖子中称，这台远程控制的机器人在显然“温和”的战斗中开火其机枪，最终在争议半岛上进行了插乌克兰国旗的演示。

Although Bohnert described it as an impressive feat, he also cautioned that the amphibious robotic assault concept is probably not yet ready for heavy combat scenarios.  
尽管博纳特称这是一项令人印象深刻的壮举，但他也警告说，这种机器人两栖攻击概念可能尚未准备好应对高强度战斗场景。

The robot and drone surge  
机器人与无人机浪潮

Such feats come as Ukraine has orchestrated a battlefield surge of ground robots since late 2025 and demonstrated how teams of drones and robots can overcome Russian military positions on their own.  
这些成就的取得，源于乌克兰自2025年末以来在战场上的地面机器人激增，并展示了无人机与机器人团队如何独立攻克俄军阵地。

Ukraine’s military robotic missions have ramped up from just under 7,500 missions in January to more than 16,600 missions in June, with robots transporting supplies, evacuating wounded soldiers, clearing or laying mines, and directly engaging in combat operations, according to United24.  
据United24称，乌克兰的军事机器人任务已从1月的略低于7500次增至6月的超过16600次，机器人执行的任务包括运输补给、撤离伤员、清除或布设地雷，以及直接参与战斗行动。

Drones carrying robots into battle are also just the latest examples of Ukrainian tactical innovations that have emerged since Russia’s full-scale invasion of Ukraine in February 2022.  
无人机携带机器人投入战斗，也只是自2022年2月俄罗斯全面入侵乌克兰以来出现的乌克兰战术创新中的最新例子。

The Ukrainian military has also been using large flying drones as motherships to carry explosive FPV drones into battle, and has even sent high-altitude balloons deep into Russia to drop one-way attack drones and missiles near their targets.  
乌克兰军方还使用大型飞行无人机作为母机，携带爆炸性第一人称视角（FPV）无人机投入战斗，甚至向俄罗斯纵深发射高空气球，在目标附近投放一次性攻击无人机和导弹。

The Russian military has followed suit with its own carrier drones.  
俄军也效仿此举，推出了自己的无人机母机。

Ukraine has also equipped drone boats with the capability to deploy swarms of FPV drones to attack Russian military positions near the coast of the contested Black Sea.  
乌克兰还使无人机艇具备部署FPV无人机蜂群的能力，以攻击争议黑海沿岸附近的俄军阵地。

A campaign of Ukrainian drone strikes has already effectively stopped Russian shipping of fuel, grain and other supplies through the Sea of Azov.  
乌克兰的无人机打击行动已 effectively 阻止了俄军通过亚速海运输燃料、粮食及其他补给。

Meanwhile, the US military has been looking to Ukraine’s example in many cases while seeking to procure a new generation of drones.  
与此同时，美军在许多情况下正借鉴乌克兰的例子，同时寻求采购新一代无人机。

The US military even sent its own kamikaze drone boats into combat for the first time during the ongoing war with Iran, which continues to severely disrupt the crucial shipping lanes in the Strait of Hormuz.  
美军甚至在与伊朗的持续冲突中，首次将己方的神风特攻队无人机艇投入战斗，这场冲突持续严重扰乱着至关重要的霍尔木兹海峡航运线。
