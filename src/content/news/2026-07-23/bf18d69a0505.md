**Title: 3 Google updates from Galaxy Unpacked 2026**  
**标题：Galaxy Unpacked 2026 上的 3 项 Google 更新**

At Galaxy Unpacked, we shared how Samsung users can boost productivity and get time back on new foldables, watches, and glasses coming soon.  
在 Galaxy Unpacked 上，我们展示了三星用户如何通过即将推出的新款折叠屏设备、手表和眼镜提升效率、节省时间。

Menaka Shroff, VP of Android Ecosystem Marketing  
Menaka Shroff，Android 生态系统营销副总裁

Today at Galaxy Unpacked in London, we celebrated the launch of the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra, Fold8, and Flip8. With these devices, we are making it easier than ever to get started on Android while also rolling out the first capabilities of Gemini Intelligence — our vision for technology that works proactively for you. Whether you choose to unfold your phone to maximize your productivity on a bigger screen or keep it folded so you can focus on your day, Gemini Intelligence is there to help with the heavy lifting. Here is a look at three updates coming to the newest devices.  
今天在伦敦的 Galaxy Unpacked 活动上，我们庆祝了三星 Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra、Fold8 和 Flip8 的发布。借助这些设备，我们让用户比以往更轻松地入门 Android，同时推出了 Gemini 智能的首批功能——这是我们对于技术主动为您服务的愿景。无论您选择展开手机以在大屏幕上最大化效率，还是保持折叠状态以专注日常，Gemini 智能都将助您处理繁重任务。以下是即将登陆最新设备的三项更新。

1. Get next-generation task automation with Gemini Intelligence.  
1. 通过 Gemini 智能实现下一代任务自动化。

Gemini Intelligence can help you offload digital chores so you can spend less time behind a screen. Back in February, we introduced a beta for task automation, helping you delegate simple everyday tasks to Gemini, such as ordering a rideshare or getting your favorite takeout meal for delivery. Now task automation is expanding support from a handful of apps to more than 40 popular apps. This means Gemini can now help you handle more of your daily "life admin" — from shopping and making dinner reservations to booking travel experiences and buying event tickets. On the Galaxy Z Flip8, you can even access this assistance directly from the Flex Window by long pressing the power button. Plus, Gemini can now use advanced reasoning to understand what is on your screen and parse complex images as prompts. This gives you a powerful jumpstart — because sometimes, the easiest way to explain what you need is to show Gemini a shopping list or inspiration photo. While Gemini works to complete the task, you can track the step-by-step progress on your screen, multitask in other apps, or close your phone and let Gemini run in the background. If you need to make a quick adjustment or add a personal touch, you can step in to handle a specific detail and then hand control back to Gemini to complete the rest. Gemini is designed to notify you when the task is ready for your review and final confirmation.  
Gemini 智能可帮您卸下数字杂务，让您减少屏幕时间。今年二月，我们推出了任务自动化测试版，帮助您将简单的日常任务（如叫车或订购外卖）委托给 Gemini。如今，任务自动化正从少数应用扩展到超过 40 款流行应用。这意味着 Gemini 现在能帮您处理更多日常“生活管理”——从购物、预订晚餐到安排旅行体验和购买活动门票。在 Galaxy Z Flip8 上，您甚至可以通过长按电源键，直接从 Flex 窗口获取此帮助。此外，Gemini 现在还能运用高级推理理解屏幕内容，并将复杂图像解析为提示。这为您提供了强大的起点——因为有时，解释需求最简单的方式就是向 Gemini 展示购物清单或灵感照片。当 Gemini 执行任务时，您可以在屏幕上跟踪逐步进度、在其他应用中多任务处理，或合上手机让 Gemini 在后台运行。如果您需要快速调整或添加个人风格，可以介入处理特定细节，然后将控制权交还给 Gemini 完成剩余部分。Gemini 会在任务准备好供您审核和最终确认时通知您。

2. Raise the bar on your projects with Gemini Notebook.  
2. 使用 Gemini 笔记本提升项目水平。

To help you manage deep projects and synthesize complex information, we are bringing the power of Gemini Notebook (formerly NotebookLM) directly to your device. The app comes preinstalled on Samsung's new foldables. Gemini Notebook is your thinking and research partner powered by our advanced multimodal models. On the Galaxy Z Fold8 series' expansive display, you can drag and drop photos, documents, whiteboard images, voice recordings, and more directly into a side-by-side workspace. You can use the chat function in Gemini Notebook to ask questions about your sources or go one step further and create custom, high-quality resources — including slide decks, videos, flash cards, quizzes, or even a podcast — tailored to how you learn best. To help you tackle your most ambitious projects, we are including a six-month trial of Google AI Pro on every Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra, Fold8, and Flip8, which gives you more access to powerful features, such as Audio Overviews, Video Overviews, Slides, and more.  
为帮助您管理深度项目并综合复杂信息，我们将 Gemini 笔记本（原 NotebookLM）的强大功能直接带到您的设备中。该应用预装于三星新款折叠屏设备。Gemini 笔记本是由我们先进多模态模型驱动的思考与研究伙伴。在 Galaxy Z Fold8 系列的大屏幕上，您可以将照片、文档、白板图像、语音录音等直接拖放到并排工作区。您可以使用 Gemini 笔记本中的聊天功能询问资料来源相关问题，或更进一步创建定制的高质量资源——包括幻灯片、视频、抽认卡、测验甚至播客——以适应您的学习方式。为助您完成最雄心勃勃的项目，我们将在每台 Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra、Fold8 和 Flip8 上提供六个月的 Google AI Pro 试用，让您更多使用强大功能，如音频概览、视频概览、幻灯片等。
