# Hyundai claims humanoid robot plan is not part of talks with striking workers

现代汽车声称人形机器人计划不参与与罢工工人的谈判

---

现代汽车公司表示，其计划在2028年部署人形机器人的方案并不在与韩国罢工汽车工人当前的谈判范围内。这家汽车制造商否认了新闻报道，称现代汽车工会（位于韩国全球最大汽车工厂）的部分罢工是由对公司计划于2028年起在美国部署人形机器人的担忧所引发的。

Hyundai Motor Company’s plan to put humanoid robots to work by 2028 is not part of current negotiations with striking South Korean autoworkers, according to the company. The automaker is disputing news reports that partial labor strikes by the Hyundai Motor union at the world’s largest automotive plant in South Korea were spurred by concerns about the company’s planned deployment of humanoid robots in the United States starting in 2028.

---

公司向Ars提供的声明中称，工会的要求主要集中在薪酬相关问题，如加薪、奖金以及延长工人退休年龄。现代汽车声明表示：“在韩国生产设施中潜在部署机器人并不属于当前劳资谈判的内容。现代汽车公司仍致力于与工会进行建设性对话，并达成一项支持员工和公司长期利益的协议。”

A company statement shared with Ars describes the union’s demands as focusing on compensation-related issues such as wage increases, bonuses, and an extension of workers’ retirement age. “Potential deployment of robots in Korean production facilities is not part of the current labor-management discussions,” according to the Hyundai statement. “Hyundai Motor Company remains committed to constructive engagement with the union and to reaching an agreement that supports the long-term interests of both employees and the company.”

---

现代汽车强调，其当前计划仅涵盖从2028年起在位于佐治亚州萨凡纳附近的Metaplant America电动汽车工厂首次部署Atlas人形机器人。公司声明称：“关于Atlas机器人未来在其他工厂的部署，将根据当地运营需求审慎决策，并与当地员工及 workforce representatives 进行对话。”

Hyundai emphasized that its current plan only covers the initial deployment of the Atlas humanoid robot at Metaplant America, an electric vehicle factory near Savannah, Georgia, starting in 2028. “Decisions about future Atlas deployment at other facilities will be made thoughtfully, in accordance with local operational needs, and in dialogue with the employees and workforce representatives at those sites,” the company stated.

---

然而，《华尔街日报》将现代汽车工会描述为提出“前所未有的要求，寻求在机器人和人工智能时代确立工作保障”，并将某些薪酬要求定性为对冲人工智能采用和机器人自动化可能导致的工作时间减少。此前，工会曾警告现代汽车公司，在汽车制造商于1月拉斯维加斯消费电子展上发布Atlas人形机器人的生产型号后，需要进行谈判。

However, The Wall Street Journal described the Hyundai Motor union as making “unprecedented demands seeking to enshrine job protections in the era of robots and AI,” and characterized certain compensation demands as hedging against potential reductions in work hours caused by AI adoption and robotic automation. The union previously warned Hyundai Motor Company about the need for negotiations after the automaker unveiled the production model of the Atlas humanoid robot at the Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas in January.

---

Atlas机器人由美国公司波士顿动力（Boston Dynamics）开发，后者已成为现代汽车的全资子公司。工会向路透社报道的一封内部信中表示：“请记住，没有劳资协议，任何使用新技术的机器人都将被禁止进入工作场所。”

The Atlas robot was developed by the US company Boston Dynamics, which has become a wholly owned Hyundai subsidiary. “Remember that without labor-management agreement, not a single robot using new technology will be allowed to enter the workplace,” the union told Hyundai in an internal letter reported by Reuters.

---

据《韩国时报》报道，现代管理层和工会目前正在审查一项拟议的工资改革方案，该方案将“在汽车制造商计划部署人形机器人后，为工厂工人提供更大的收入稳定性”。但专家警告称，这项将加班费和夜班津贴转换为固定工资的工资改革，可能会以公司生产力为代价。

Hyundai management and the labor union are currently reviewing a proposed wage reform that would “provide factory workers with greater income stability even after the carmaker’s planned deployment of humanoid robots,” The Korea Times reported. But experts cautioned that the wage overhaul, which would convert hourly pay for overtime and night-shift allowances into fixed wages, could come at the expense of company productivity.

---

现代汽车工会是韩国金属工会（Korean Metal Workers’ Union）的一个分支。Ars已联系韩国金属工会和韩国工会联合会（Korean Confederation of Trade Unions，全国性工会中心），就当前劳工罢工和谈判寻求置评。

The Hyundai Motor union is a branch of the Korean Metal Workers’ Union. Ars has reached out to the Korean Metal Workers’ Union and the Korean Confederation of Trade Unions—a national trade union center—for comment on the current labor strike and negotiations.

---

根据Tech Policy Press的报道，自2026年3月新劳动法生效以来，韩国出现了一波工会团体寻求与公司管理层谈判的浪潮。所谓的“黄信封法”（yellow envelope law）扩大了外包工人的劳动权利，并限制了雇主因劳工罢工向工会索赔的能力。

The broader wave of labor negotiations over robots South Korea has seen a wave of union groups seeking negotiations with company management since a new labor law took effect in March 2026, according to Tech Policy Press. The so-called “yellow envelope law” expands labor rights for subcontracted workers and limits employers’ capability to claim damages from unions because of labor strikes.

---

《韩国时报》报道称，“黄信封法”也可能“减缓向机器人劳动力的过渡”，因为它承认对工作条件产生重大影响的管理决策属于集体谈判和行动的范围。这意味着根据该法律，使用机器人的大规模自动化努力需要与人类工人进行谈判，劳动专家向这家韩国报纸表示。

The yellow envelope law may also “slow the transition to robot labor” because it recognizes management decisions that significantly impact working conditions as being subject to collective bargaining and action, The Korea Times reported. That means large-scale automation efforts using robots need to be negotiated with human workers under the law, labor experts told the South Korean newspaper.

---

韩国工会联合会还于2026年2月与韩国政府建立了一个联合协商机构，讨论包括人工智能驱动的自动化和机器人技术在内的劳动问题。该组织在《韩国时报》报道的一份声明中表示：“在这个重大转型时期，这些发展是变成一场迫使工人陷入不安全和牺牲的灾难，还是成为提高生活质量的机会，在很大程度上将取决于政府的选择和政策方向。”

The Korean Confederation of Trade Unions also established a joint consultative body with the South Korean government to discuss labor issues, including AI-driven automation and robotics in February 2026. “In this time of major transition, whether these developments become a disaster that forces insecurity and sacrifice onto workers or an opportunity to improve quality of life will largely depend on the government’s choices and policy direction,” the organization said in a statement reported by The Korea Times.

---

将使用Atlas机器人的现代汽车佐治亚州Metaplant工厂的美国工人尚未成立工会。但代表美国、加拿大和波多黎各约40万名汽车工人的联合汽车工人联合会（United Auto Workers, UAW）已努力组织Metaplant的工人。美国汽车工人联合会已对部署专用工业机器人提出反对。在2026年6月于底特律举行的UAW宪法大会上，UAW主席肖恩·费恩（Shawn Fain）也警告“人形机器人技术和大规模自动化的威胁”会削弱工人就业和薪酬。

The US workers at Hyundai’s Metaplant factory in Georgia—which will be the first facility to use Atlas robots—are not unionized. But United Auto Workers (UAW), the union representing about 400,000 autoworkers across the US, Canada, and Puerto Rico, has made efforts to organize the Metaplant workers. The US autoworkers union has already pushed back against deployment of specialized industrial robots. During the UAW Constitutional Convention held in Detroit in June 2026, Shawn Fain, president of United Auto Workers, also warned against “the threat of humanoid robotics and mass automation” undermining worker employment and compensation.

---

目前尚不清楚人形机器人是否会成为许多公司所押注的自主通用机器人。Ars此前曾与机器人公司创始人和高管探讨实现这一愿景所面临的挑战。

It still remains to be seen if humanoid robots will prove to be the autonomous general-purpose robots that many companies are betting on. Ars previously talked to robotic company founders and executives about the challenges of realizing this vision.
