# How the Galaxy Z Fold 8 and Z Flip 8 phones compare  
# Galaxy Z Fold 8 与 Z Flip 8 手机对比

Not many surprises, but some welcome improvements if you know where to look.  
没有太多惊喜，但如果你知道从何看起，仍有一些值得欢迎的改进。

by Brad Bourque  
Jul 22, 2026, 3:47 PM UTC  

Samsung’s latest round of folding Galaxy Z phones and updated smartwatches were announced at its July 2026 Unpacked event and are set to launch on August 7th. The Galaxy Z Flip 8, Fold 8, and Fold 8 Ultra don’t seem geared toward people who bought last year’s model; aside from a few design updates and AI features, there’s not a whole lot that’s new — unless we’re talking about the Z Fold 8.  
三星最新的折叠 Galaxy Z 系列手机和更新款智能手表在 2026 年 7 月的 Unpacked 活动上发布，并将于 8 月 7 日上市。Galaxy Z Flip 8、Fold 8 和 Fold 8 Ultra 似乎并非为去年购买了旧款型号的用户设计；除了少数设计更新和 AI 功能外，并没有太多新意——除非你指的是 Z Fold 8。

The Z Fold 8 is the foldable that will probably get the most attention in the US this year, that is, unless Apple’s rumored foldable arrives in the coming months. While its name makes it sound like the successor to last year’s Z Fold 7, it’s a new, shorter variation that starts at $1,899.99. It has a 7.6-inch main screen and a 5.5-inch exterior screen — noticeably smaller than the new $2,099.99 Z Fold 8 Ultra. It’s nearly as feature-packed as the Ultra, with some exceptions, like having a 50-megapixel main camera instead of a 200-megapixel lens, and a slightly smaller 4,800mAh battery. There are three configurations, with the 256GB and 512GB models sporting 12GB of memory and the 1TB model upgraded to 16GB of RAM.  
Z Fold 8 很可能是今年在美国最受关注的折叠手机，除非苹果传闻中的折叠机在未来几个月内推出。虽然它的名字听起来像是去年 Z Fold 7 的继任者，但它实际上是一款新的、更短的变体，起售价为 1,899.99 美元。它配备 7.6 英寸主屏和 5.5 英寸外屏——明显比新的 2,099.99 美元的 Z Fold 8 Ultra 更小。它的功能几乎与 Ultra 一样丰富，但有一些例外，例如主摄像头为 5000 万像素而非 2 亿像素，电池容量略小，为 4,800mAh。共有三种配置，256GB 和 512GB 型号配备 12GB 内存，1TB 型号升级至 16GB RAM。

The Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra is, in a lot of ways, a direct follow-up to the Z Fold 7, but with a new name. It has a 8-inch main screen and a 6.5-inch cover screen that are the same size and resolution as the previous model, along with the aforementioned 200-megapixel camera with 3x telephoto optical zoom. It has the same storage and memory configurations of the smaller Fold 8.  
Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra 在很多方面是 Z Fold 7 的直接延续，但有了新名称。它配备 8 英寸主屏和 6.5 英寸外屏，尺寸和分辨率与前代相同，并搭载上述 2 亿像素摄像头，支持 3 倍光学变焦。它的存储和内存配置与更小的 Fold 8 相同。

Lastly, the Galaxy Z Flip 8 is the most affordable model, starting at $1,199.99. Of the three foldables, the Flip 8 seems to have the fewest hardware updates. Its battery capacity is unchanged, as are its camera specs. However, it has the Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 instead of the Exynos 2500 used in the Flip 7. We’ll have to wait until the review to see if that change will bring improvements; our review of last year’s flip phone didn’t highlight performance issues with the Samsung-made chip.  
最后，Galaxy Z Flip 8 是最实惠的型号，起售价为 1,199.99 美元。在三款折叠手机中，Flip 8 的硬件更新似乎最少。其电池容量和摄像头规格均未改变。不过，它采用了 Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 芯片，而非 Flip 7 中使用的 Exynos 2500。我们需要等待评测才能确认这一变化是否会带来改进；我们对去年翻盖手机的评测并未指出三星自研芯片存在性能问题。

While that covers some of the surface-level changes, we have the full spec sheets below for your perusal.  
以上涵盖了部分表面层面的变化，以下是完整规格表供您查阅。
