# LG to ban residential proxies from smart TV apps  
# LG 将禁止智能电视应用中的住宅代理服务  

---

The home appliance giant LG Electronics USA said this week it plans to suspend any apps built for its smart TVs that turn one’s television into an always-on residential proxy node.  
家电巨头 LG 电子美国公司本周表示，计划暂停任何将其智能电视转变为永久运行住宅代理节点的应用。  

The move comes less than a month after researchers found that more than 42 percent of games and other apps available for download on LG’s webOS store allow unknown third-parties to route their Internet traffic through a user’s TV.  
此举距研究人员发现 LG webOS 商店中超过 42% 的可下载游戏及其他应用允许未知第三方通过用户电视路由其互联网流量，尚不足一个月。  

Proxy SDK prevalence among smart TV apps for LG (webOS) and Samsung (Tizen OS) televisions. Image: Spur.us.  
LG（webOS）和三星（Tizen OS）智能电视应用中代理 SDK 的普及情况。图片来源：Spur.us。  

On July 2, we featured research by the security firm Spur that examined the prevalence of residential proxy software development kits (SDKs) in smart TV apps.  
7 月 2 日，我们报道了安全公司 Spur 的研究，该研究调查了智能电视应用中住宅代理软件开发工具包（SDK）的普及情况。  

Spur found more than 42 percent of apps available for download on LG smart TVs include SDKs that turn one’s television in a proxy node indefinitely, and that more than a quarter of the apps made for Samsung’s Tizen operating system had similar residential proxy components.  
Spur 发现，LG 智能电视上超过 42% 的可下载应用包含可将电视永久转变为代理节点的 SDK，而三星 Tizen 操作系统应用中超过四分之一也含有类似的住宅代理组件。  

Responding to questions about Spur’s research, LG Senior Vice President John Taylor told KrebsOnSecurity the company was working with app developers to remove the residential proxy option from their apps on the webOS platform. Developers that fail to comply, he said, will find their apps suspended.  
针对 Spur 研究的相关问题，LG 高级副总裁约翰·泰勒向 KrebsOnSecurity 表示，公司正与应用开发者合作，要求其移除 webOS 平台应用中的住宅代理选项。他表示，不遵守要求的开发者将面临应用被暂停的下场。  

“A residential proxy network is not an intended use for LG smart TVs, and LG Electronics is working with developers to remove the residential proxy option from their apps on the webOS platform,” Taylor said. “If this option is not removed, these apps will be suspended.”  
泰勒表示：“住宅代理网络并非 LG 智能电视的预期用途，LG 电子正与开发者合作，移除其 webOS 平台应用中的住宅代理选项。若不移除此选项，相关应用将被暂停。”  

Taylor said LG is committed to keeping residential proxy networks out of its智能 TV apps going forward, and that the company’s review of those apps is “well underway now.”  
泰勒表示，LG 致力于在未来杜绝智能电视应用中的住宅代理网络，且公司对相关应用的审查“现已全面展开”。  

“As part of our ongoing efforts to enhance platform quality and the user experience, LG will continue to strengthen our evaluation process for developer-submitted apps, including those that incorporate residential proxy SDKs,” Taylor wrote in an emailed statement.  
泰勒在邮件声明中写道：“作为持续提升平台质量与用户体验的努力的一部分，LG 将不断加强对开发者提交应用的审核流程，包括那些集成了住宅代理 SDK 的应用。”  

App makers looking for ways to monetize their creations can turn to residential proxy providers, which pay developers to include SDKs that turn the user’s device into a residential proxy node that is rented to paying customers.  
寻求应用变现方式的开发者可求助于住宅代理服务商，后者付费让开发者集成 SDK，将用户设备转变为可供付费客户租用的住宅代理节点。  

In the case of LG and Samsung smart TVs, Spur found residential proxy SDKs bundled with everything from simple games like Pac-Man to screensavers and file utilities.  
以 LG 和三星智能电视为例，Spur 发现住宅代理 SDK 被捆绑在从《吃豆人》等简单游戏到屏保和文件工具的各种应用中。  

A Pac-Man smart TV app from Bright Data offers users the choice between viewing ads in the game or agreeing to allow their TV to serve as a residential proxy node. Image: Spur.us.  
Bright Data 的一款《吃豆人》智能电视应用向用户提供选择：在游戏中观看广告，或同意让电视充当住宅代理节点。图片来源：Spur.us。  

Spur’s report found the residential proxy network Bright Data accounted for a majority of proxy SDKs across both Samsung and LG smart TVs.  
Spur 的报告发现，住宅代理网络 Bright Data 在三星和 LG 智能电视的代理 SDK 中占大多数。  

Bright Data did not respond to requests for comment.  
Bright Data 未回复置评请求。  

Bright Data and other proxy providers named in Spur’s report all say they follow rigorous know-your-customer processes to validate legitimate uses of their services, which is often heavily tied to content-scraping activities by said customers.  
Spur 报告中提及的 Bright Data 及其他代理服务商均表示，他们遵循严格的身份验证流程，以确认其服务的合法用途——这些用途通常与客户的内容抓取活动密切相关。  

The proxy companies also say they incorporate technological countermeasures to prevent proxy service customers from being able to interact with and control other devices on the proxy user’s local network.  
这些代理公司还表示，他们采用了技术反制措施，防止代理服务客户与代理用户本地网络上的其他设备进行交互或控制。  

Spur argues the problem is not that residential proxy networks exist, but rather that they are being embedded at scale in devices that most consumers do not think of as computers and are not equipped to audit.  
Spur 认为，问题不在于住宅代理网络的存在，而在于它们被大规模嵌入到大多数消费者不视为电脑、且不具备审计能力的设备中。  

“A one-time consent prompt buried in a TV app is not a substitute for meaningful transparency, ongoing control, and platform oversight,” Spur’s Trevor Sutter wrote. “The risk is amplified when consent comes from individuals within the household who use the device but shouldn’t give consent, such as minors.”  
Spur 的特雷弗·萨特写道：“隐藏在电视应用中的一次性同意提示，无法替代有意义的透明度、持续控制与平台监督。当 consent 来自家庭中使用设备但不应给予同意的人员（如未成年人）时，风险会被放大。”  

LG’s announcement that it is culling residential proxy SDKs from its app store is welcome news, but the company recently came under fire for another questionable partnership: Pimping McAfee security products via software drivers included in its high-end LCD monitors.  
LG 宣布从其应用商店清除住宅代理 SDK 是个好消息，但该公司近期因另一项可疑合作而招致批评：通过其高端 LCD 显示器附带的软件驱动程序推广 McAfee 安全产品。  

Earlier this week, the Youtube channel Gamers Nexus showed that certain LG LCD monitors will automatically install an app that promotes paid McAfee antivirus subscriptions, and that the app arrives through Windows Update without an approval prompt.  
本周早些时候，YouTube 频道 Gamers Nexus 演示了某些 LG LCD 显示器会自动安装一款推广付费 McAfee 杀毒订阅的应用，且该应用通过 Windows 更新静默安装，无需用户批准。
