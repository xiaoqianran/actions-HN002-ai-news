I’ve spent the past week living with Dyson’s newest vacuums, and a clear favorite has emerged. But it’s not the one I expected.  
过去一周我体验了戴森最新的几款吸尘器，其中明显最受欢迎的一款脱颖而出——但它并非我最初预期的型号。

I was ready to love the V16 Piston Animal for all the capabilities and suction power it offers, but as I’ve been testing, it’s the the Dyson V10 Konical that has left me most impressed for its performance at this price.  
我原本准备好因 V16 Piston Animal 的强大功能和吸力而喜爱它，但在测试中发现，戴森 V10 Konical 以其在此价位上的表现更令我印象深刻。

Funny enough, when thinking about Dyson’s new lineup, it’s the least flashy offering, and the one I always forget about. The V8 Cyclone revives a longtime favorite, while the V16 packs the highest suction we’ve seen yet, making both easy to remember and exciting to try.  
有趣的是，在戴森的新产品线中，它是最不张扬的型号，也是我总容易忘记的那款。V8 Cyclone 延续了长期以来的热门产品，而 V16 则拥有我们见过最强的吸力，两者都令人印象深刻且想尝试。

It was easy to let the V10 Konical fade into the background. It has the same power level as the V8 but a slightly higher price tag thanks to the new All Floor Cones cleaner head instead of the older Motorbar style.  
V10 Konical 很容易被忽视。它的功率与 V8 相同，但由于采用了全新的“全地面锥形”吸头而非旧的 Motorbar 样式，价格略高。

When placed side by side, though, the V10 Konical easily justifies its $100 higher price over the V8 Cyclone. It did better than the V8 in almost every test I ran when comparing Dyson’s vacuums side by side, and it did the best when vacuuming up sand on a rug (even better than more expensive Dyson vacuums like the V15 Detect and Gen5Detect).  
但相比之下，V10 Konical 轻松证明其比 V8 Cyclone 高出 100 美元的价格是值得的。在并排对比戴森吸尘器的几乎所有测试中，它都比 V8 表现更好，尤其在吸除地毯上的沙子时表现最佳（甚至优于更贵的 V15 Detect 和 Gen5Detect）。

If you’re looking for a good Dyson vacuum that doesn’t cost the better part of a grand, this is the one to get.  
如果你在寻找一台不错的戴森吸尘器，且不想花费近千美元，这就是你的选择。

The Dyson V10 Konical packs a solid range of features for its price. It packs 150 air watts of power, which is above average for lightweight stick vacs, though it is weaker than most of Dyson’s own stick vacs. Our favored V15 Detect has 240 air watts, for example, but the V10 Konical felt similar on carpet to the V15 Detect, even with the change in suction power.  
戴森 V10 Konical 在其价位上配备了扎实的功能。它拥有 150 空气瓦的功率，对于轻量级杆式吸尘器来说高于平均水平，但低于戴森自家大多数杆式吸尘器。例如，我们更青睐的 V15 Detect 拥有 240 空气瓦，但 V10 Konical 在地毯上的使用感与 V15 Detect 相似，尽管吸力有所不同。

It even felt like it suctioned better when I used it in handheld mode.  
甚至在我手持使用时，感觉吸力更好。

That’s because just suction power alone doesn’t make a vacuum great at sucking up dirt and debris. A great motor and vacuum head design are equally important, if not more so. Dyson says the V10 Konical’s engine has 15 cyclone chambers built into it for air to flow through and create efficient suction.  
这是因为仅靠吸力并不能让吸尘器擅长吸除污垢和碎屑。出色的电机和吸头设计同样重要，甚至更重要。戴森表示，V10 Konical 的电机内置 15 个旋风室，使空气能够高效流动并产生有效吸力。

So while the power is lower than the V15, the two vacuums feel much more similar than I expected, since it still has impressive suction for its power and price point.  
因此，尽管功率低于 V15，但两款吸尘器的使用感比我预期的更相似，因为它在功率和价位上仍具有令人印象深刻的吸力。

The V10 also has a seven-cell lithium battery that lasts for 60 minutes, like the more expensive V15 before it. There are three power modes, but there’s no screen on this device—just a built-in light on the mode button that shows you which setting you’re on.  
V10 还配备了七芯锂电池，续航 60 分钟，与更贵的 V15 相同。它有三个功率模式，但设备上没有屏幕——仅在模式按钮上有一个内置指示灯显示当前设置。

There’s green for eco mode, blue for medium mode, and red for max mode. The mode changes the suction level, so eco is great for lighter cleans while max is best for bigger messes; each higher mode uses more battery.  
绿色代表节能模式，蓝色代表中等模式，红色代表最大模式。模式改变吸力等级，因此节能模式适合轻度清洁，而最大模式适合处理较大污渍；每个更高模式都会消耗更多电量。

The lack of a screen also means you’ll fly blind on battery life, so beware how long you spend on more powerful modes.  
缺乏屏幕也意味着你无法查看电池续航，因此需注意在强力模式下使用的时间。

The V10 comes with the new All Floor Cones cleaner head, which is similar to the head that comes with the new V16 Piston Animal (though that version can sense messes). The All Floor Cones head has cones on either side to suck up hair and debris; the cone design promises to detangle long hair instead of letting it get stuck around the vacuum head’s bar.  
V10 配备了全新的“全地面锥形”吸头，与新 V16 Piston Animal 的吸头类似（尽管后者可感知污渍）。“全地面锥形”吸头两侧有锥体，用于吸除毛发和碎屑；锥体设计旨在解开长发，而非使其缠绕在吸头杆上。

So far, I haven’t seen any hair tangled around the cones, so it seems to be working as intended. There’s also a built-in illuminating light to spot dust and debris when you’re vacuuming hard floors, which is a nice bonus.  
到目前为止，我尚未看到锥体上有毛发缠绕，因此似乎达到了预期效果。此外，它还内置照明灯，便于在清洁硬地板时发现灰尘和碎屑，这是个不错的附加功能。

This vacuum comes with two attachments: a crevice tool and a mini motorized tool that’s designed for cleaning upholstery. Plus, unlike many older Dyson models, including the more expensive V15 or the cheaper (but equally new) V8, you’ll also get fully sealed, five-stage HEPA filters to keep allergens from circulating out of the vacuum and back into the air.  
这款吸尘器配备两个配件：一个缝隙吸头和一款专为清洁软垫家具设计的迷你电动吸头。此外，与许多旧款戴森型号（包括更贵的 V15 或更便宜但同样新的 V8）不同，你还将获得完全密封的五级 HEPA 滤网，可防止过敏原从吸尘器逸出并重新进入空气。

It’ll also be compatible with Dyson’s new docking station, the Auto-empty Dok, hopefully out later this year. Only the V10 Konical and the V16 Piston Animal ($980) are currently slated to work with it.  
它还将兼容戴森的新 docking station——Auto-empty Dok，有望今年晚些时候推出。目前仅 V10 Konical 和 V16 Piston Animal（980 美元）计划支持该配件。

**Comparison Game**  
**对比测试**

What made me like the Dyson V10 Konical so much was how well it did in my testing rounds. I tested it with sand and cereal on rugs, followed by litter on hardwood, and then carpeted stairs and bedrooms. It did better than many Dysons in its overall score, beating the V15 Detect, V16 Piston Animal, and V8 Cyclone with its score.  
让我如此喜欢戴森 V10 Konical 的原因是它在我的测试中表现出色。我测试了它在地毯上吸沙和谷物、在硬木地板上吸猫砂，以及楼梯和卧室地毯。它的总分优于许多戴森型号，击败了 V15 Detect、V16 Piston Animal 和 V8 Cyclone。

My scoring criteria ran 1 through 4 on five tests—sand, cat litter, Cheerios, general vacuuming, and handheld mode—with 4 representing the best performance and 1 being the lowest. The V10 Konical walked away with a 14 out of 20, while the V15 came behind it with a 12 out of 20.  
我的评分标准在五项测试（沙子、猫砂、奇巧谷物、一般吸尘和手持模式）中从 1 到 4 分，4 分为最佳表现，1 分为最低。V10 Konical 获得 14/20 分，而 V15 以 12/20 分紧随其后。

The V10 came in second overall out of five Dyson vacuums, with only the Gen5Detect beating it with a score of 17. The V10 pulls ahead of the Gen5Detect if you’re looking for add-ons, though, as the V10 will be compatible with Dyson’s self-emptying docking station that will release later this year.  
在五款戴森吸尘器中，V10 总体排名第二，仅 Gen5Detect 以 17 分击败它。但如果你考虑附加功能，V10 则领先于 Gen5Detect，因为 V10 将兼容戴森今年晚些时候推出的自动清空 docking station。

While testing, I was particularly impressed with how well it picked up sand for its lower suction power, and it did a great job sucking up an intentional mess of Cheerios I created.  
测试中，我尤其对它在吸力较低的情况下仍能出色吸除沙子印象深刻，并且它对我故意撒下的奇巧谷物也清理得非常好。

I was less impressed with the hard floor results, but since the V10 doesn’t work with the Fluffy Optic head like Dyson’s high-end vacuums, it was never going to be as effective as vacuums that can switch to a head made for that surface. Still, it’s not bad—it was still able to vacuum up the litter I scattered on hard floors as a test. It just needed a couple of passes instead of a single pass to get it all.  
我对硬地板的结果不太满意，但由于 V10 不像戴森高端型号那样兼容 Fluffy Optic 吸头，它注定无法像能切换专用吸头的吸尘器那样有效。不过，它并不差——它仍能吸除我在硬地板上撒的猫砂。只是需要多过几遍，而非一遍就能全部吸净。
