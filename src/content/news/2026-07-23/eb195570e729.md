**Title: Treasury threatens sanctions after White House claims Moonshot distilled Anthropic’s Fable**

**标题：美财政部威胁实施制裁，此前白宫指控Moonshot蒸馏Anthropic的Fable模型**

---

U.S. Treasury secretary Scott Bessent doubled down on his warnings to Chinese AI companies on Wednesday, saying that sanctions remain on the table after a White House official accused Moonshot of improperly distilling Anthropic’s Fable model.

美国财政部长斯科特·贝森特周三再次加强对中国AI公司的警告，表示在一位白宫官员指控Moonshot不当蒸馏Anthropic的Fable模型后，制裁仍是可选方案。

---

Model distillation is a common AI training technique in which a smaller model learns from the outputs of a larger one. While this process can infringe on intellectual property rights, it’s also widely used as a legitimate optimization method.

模型蒸馏是一种常见的AI训练技术，即一个较小的模型从较大模型的输出中学习。尽管这一过程可能侵犯知识产权，但它也被广泛用作一种合法的优化方法。

---

“Open source is not open season on American IP,” Bessent posted on X. “When [Chinese] firms conduct covert, industrial-scale distillation attacks that cross the line into IP theft, sanctions and Entity List designations will be on the table.”

“开源不等于美国知识产权可以随意侵犯，”贝森特在X上发帖称。“当（中国）企业进行隐蔽的、工业规模的蒸馏攻击，越过界限成为知识产权盗窃时，制裁和实体清单指定将是可选方案。”

---

Earlier this week, Bessent stated that the U.S. government would examine open source models from China for signs of intellectual property theft and impose sanctions if found.

本周早些时候，贝森特表示，美国政府将审查来自中国的开源模型，寻找知识产权盗窃的迹象，并在发现时实施制裁。

---

Bessent’s latest remarks come hours after the White House’s science and technology policy chief Michael Kratsios accused the China-based Moonshot of conducting large-scale distillation against U.S. models.

贝森特的最新言论是在白宫科技政策主任迈克尔·克拉齐奥斯指控总部位于中国的Moonshot对美国模型进行大规模蒸馏后数小时发表的。

---

He alleged that Moonshot had acquired Nvidia’s “GB300-equipped servers and has accessed GB300s in Thailand, likely to train its AI models,” raising questions about whether the firm violated U.S. export-control rules.

他声称，Moonshot已获得英伟达的“配备GB300的服务器，并在泰国访问了GB300，很可能用于训练其AI模型”，这引发了该公司是否违反美国出口管制规则的质疑。

---

The GB300 servers are part of Nvidia’s Blackwell generation, which are banned from being sold to Chinese companies.

GB300服务器是英伟达Blackwell系列的一部分，该系列产品被禁止销售给中国公司。

---

Some experts dispute the idea that Kimi K3 could have been developed primarily through distillation from Fable, which has only been publicly available since July 1.

一些专家质疑Kimi K3主要是通过蒸馏自Fable（自7月1日才公开可用）而开发的说法。

---

Moonshot released K3 last week as an open-weight model, and its advanced capabilities have called into question the underlying business models of leading U.S. AI labs, casting doubt on whether they can continue to justify the enormous capital requirements underpinning the frontier AI race.

Moonshot上周发布了K3作为开放权重模型，其先进能力引发了对领先美国AI实验室底层商业模式的质疑，让人怀疑它们是否还能继续为支撑前沿AI竞赛的巨大资本需求辩护。

---

The episode has also intensified a broader debate in Washington over the influx of Chinese open models.

这一事件还加剧了华盛顿对中国开源模型涌入的更广泛辩论。

---

Some, including former White House AI adviser and current OpenAI Head of Strategic Futures, Dean Ball, have argued that the U.S. should restrict or effectively ban the use of Chinese open-weight models to preserve America’s technological advantage and mitigate potential national security risks.

包括前白宫AI顾问、现任OpenAI战略未来负责人迪恩·鲍尔在内的一些人认为，美国应限制或实际上禁止使用中国开放权重模型，以保持美国的技术优势并减轻潜在的国家安全风险。

---

TechCrunch has reached out to Moonshot and the Treasury for comment.

TechCrunch已联系Moonshot和财政部寻求置评。
