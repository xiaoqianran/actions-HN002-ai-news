**Style over Substance: A Shortcut Audit of Emotion-Description Preference Evaluation**  
**风格重于实质：对情感描述偏好评估的捷径审计**

**Authors:** Jiabing Yang, Yixiang Chen, Yuan Xu, Qisen Ma, Tao Yu, Peiyan Li, Yingda Li, Yan Huang, Liang Wang  
**作者：** 杨嘉兵、陈义翔、徐远、马启森、于涛、李培燕、李颖达、黄岩、王亮

**Abstract:**  
**摘要：**

Preference over model-generated emotion descriptions is emerging as a standard evaluation metric for multimodal emotion understanding, exemplified by the MER2026 MER-Prefer track on EmoPrefer.  
对模型生成的情感描述的偏好正成为多模态情感理解的标准评估指标，MER2026的EmoPrefer赛道便是例证。

Such benchmarks assume that predicting the preferred description requires grounded cross-modal understanding of the video.  
这类基准测试假设，预测偏好的描述需要对视频具有基于事实的跨模态理解。

We conduct a systematic shortcut audit of EmoPrefer using content-blind probes.  
我们使用内容盲探针（content-blind probes）对EmoPrefer进行了系统的捷径审计。

A simple logistic regression using only description length and generator identity, without processing the text, video, or audio, performs comparably to LoRA-finetuned 7B text and audio-visual judges (65.8 versus 66.8 WAF on EmoPrefer-V2).  
一个仅使用描述长度和生成器身份、不处理文本、视频或音频的简单逻辑回归，其表现与经过LoRA微调的7B文本和音视觉评判器相当（在EmoPrefer-V2上分别为65.8和66.8 WAF）。

Generator identity is recoverable from description text with 99.5 percent accuracy, every candidate pair contrasts two distinct generators, and the human preference labels agree with a fold-exclusive per-generator win-rate prior on 66 percent of the evaluated pairs.  
生成器身份可从描述文本中以99.5%的准确率恢复，每对候选样本都对比两个不同的生成器，且人类偏好标签与每生成器独占的胜率先验在66%的评估样本对上一致。

When the human label conflicts with this prior, trained judges still follow the style prior on 63 to 80 percent of the pairs.  
当人类标签与此先验冲突时，训练后的评判器仍在63%至80%的样本对上遵循风格先验。

On a length-matched subset that neutralizes verbosity bias, the tested media configurations yield no statistically significant improvement, while an ODIN-inspired diagnostic that decouples the style shortcut leaves its content head near chance.  
在控制长度以消除冗长偏差的子集上，所测试的媒体配置未产生统计显著的改进；而一种受ODIN启发的诊断方法将风格捷径解耦后，其内容分支表现接近随机。

These results do not imply that human preferences are inherently stylistic or that the descriptions contain no emotional information.  
这些结果并不意味着人类偏好本质上是风格化的，或描述中不包含情感信息。

Instead, they show that the current scores can be reached without verifying either description against the video.  
相反，它们表明，当前分数可以在不将任何描述与视频进行验证的情况下获得。

We recommend source-balanced pairing, strict length control, counter-stereotypical sliced reporting, and multi-annotator consensus for future cross-generator evaluations.  
我们建议未来进行跨生成器评估时采用源平衡配对、严格长度控制、反刻板印象切片报告以及多标注者共识。
