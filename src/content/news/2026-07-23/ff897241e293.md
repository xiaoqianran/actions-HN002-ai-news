### The Similarities  
### 共同点

#### The Display  
#### 显示屏

Although Samsung’s foldables all feature exterior and foldout screens in completely different sizes, they all boast Samsung’s Dynamic AMOLED 2X technology, so you get a 120-Hz refresh rate, support for HDR, and higher peak brightness than ever before (up to 3,000 nits), making it easy to read your screen, even in direct sunlight. They also have features designed to filter blue light and reduce eye strain.  
尽管三星折叠手机的外屏和主屏尺寸差异很大，但全系均采用三星Dynamic AMOLED 2X技术，提供120Hz刷新率、HDR支持，以及前所未有的更高峰值亮度（最高3000尼特），即使在直射阳光下也能轻松阅读。它们还具备过滤蓝光、缓解眼疲劳的功能。

The Fold8 Ultra and Fold8 have a special Flex Titanium layer that reduces the visible crease and provides extra strength, flexibility, and resilience. The titanium-alloy film should help the displays last longer, but also means they are flatter and smoother than before, with an anti-reflective finish that reduces glare.  
Fold8 Ultra和Fold8采用了特殊的柔性钛合金层，可减少可见折痕，并提供更强的强度、柔韧性和耐用性。这种钛合金薄膜有助于延长屏幕寿命，同时使屏幕比以往更平整、顺滑，并具备防眩光涂层。

#### The Usual Specs  
#### 常规配置

All three phones have Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor inside, with 12 GB of RAM and 256 GB or 512 GB of storage. You can also get the Fold8 Ultra and Fold8 with 16 GB of RAM and 1 TB of storage. They are all IP48 water- and dust-resistant and feature Samsung’s Advanced Armor Aluminum frame, which is scratch-resistant and durable enough to survive minor drops. The Fold8 Ultra and Fold8 support fast charging at up to 45 watts and fast wireless charging at 20 watts; the Flip8 is slightly slower for both. All three support Wireless PowerShare to top up wireless earbuds and other devices. Connectivity includes Wi-Fi 7 and Ultra Wideband (UWB).  
三款手机均搭载高通骁龙8 Elite Gen 5处理器，配备12GB RAM和256GB或512GB存储。Fold8 Ultra和Fold8还可选配16GB RAM和1TB存储。它们均具备IP48级防尘防水，并采用三星的Advanced Armor Aluminum框架，防刮且能承受轻微跌落。Fold8 Ultra和Fold8支持最高45瓦有线快充和20瓦无线快充；Flip8的有线和无线充电速度略低。三款均支持Wireless PowerShare（无线反向充电），可为无线耳机等设备充电。连接性包括Wi-Fi 7和超宽带（UWB）。
