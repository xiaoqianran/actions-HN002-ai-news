**Title: Anthropic updates Claude voice mode with more capable models**  
**标题：Anthropic 更新 Claude 语音模式，引入更强大的模型**

Weeks after OpenAI rolled out a new family of conversational models and updated ChatGPT’s voice mode, rival Anthropic is making its move to make Claude more voice-friendly with a new update.  
在 OpenAI 推出新一代对话模型并更新 ChatGPT 语音模式数周后，竞争对手 Anthropic 也通过一次新更新，让 Claude 更适配语音交互。

The company said Thursday that users can choose between Opus, Sonnet, and Haiku models.  
该公司周四表示，用户现在可以在 Opus、Sonnet 和 Haiku 模型之间进行选择。

Claude’s voice mode, which was released last year and powered by the Haiku model, provided quick responses, but wasn’t well suited for complex work.  
Claude 的语音模式于去年发布，由 Haiku 模型驱动，响应速度较快，但并不适合处理复杂任务。

The company said that with the new update, voice mode picks the last model people used in the text chat and uses its fastest version by default.  
该公司称，通过新更新，语音模式会自动选用用户在文本聊天中最后使用的模型，并默认使用其最快版本。

Anthropic said that the new voice mode can help users with longer conversations, including providing feedback on their communication style, talking through a pitch to a client, and brainstorming product market research.  
Anthropic 表示，新语音模式能帮助用户进行更长的对话，例如对其沟通风格提供反馈、模拟向客户推介方案，以及进行产品市场调研的头脑风暴。

What’s more, Claude’s voice mode can tap into other apps like Gmail, Google Calendar, Slack, Canva, and Notion.  
此外，Claude 的语音模式还能连接 Gmail、Google Calendar、Slack、Canva 和 Notion 等应用。

This means that users can ask it to update a meeting slot, draft an email, or create a document in Notion.  
这意味着用户可以要求它更新会议时间、起草邮件或在 Notion 中创建文档。

Notably, this is a big difference from OpenAI’s voice mode, which updated its conversational style but still isn’t able to use different tools to get work done.  
值得注意的是，这与 OpenAI 的语音模式形成鲜明对比——后者虽更新了对话风格，但仍无法调用不同工具来完成工作。

Earlier this year, Anthropic added multilingual support to Claude’s voice mode in beta.  
今年早些时候，Anthropic 为 Claude 的语音模式添加了测试版的多语言支持。

It said that now users can talk in various languages, but they have to manually specify the language.  
该公司称，用户现在可以用多种语言对话，但需要手动指定语言。

At the moment, Anthropic supports English, French, German, Hindi, Indonesian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese (Brazilian), and Spanish (Latin America/Spain).  
目前，Anthropic 支持英语、法语、德语、印地语、印尼语、意大利语、日语、韩语、巴西葡萄牙语以及西班牙语（拉丁美洲/西班牙）。

The new voice mode is available to all users in beta across platforms.  
新语音模式已以测试版形式在所有平台向所有用户开放。

However, free users will be restricted to using the Haiku model with only one connected app.  
但免费用户将只能使用 Haiku 模型，且仅能连接一个应用。

Anthropic didn’t make any changes to the voice model with this release, and it hasn’t detailed what kind of voice stack it uses.  
Anthropic 本次发布未对语音模型本身进行改动，也未详细说明其使用的语音技术栈。

That means, unlike OpenAI’s release, users might not find any conversational improvements such as better interruption handling.  
这意味着，与 OpenAI 的发布不同，用户可能不会发现任何对话体验的改进，例如更自然的打断处理。
