Abstract:  
摘要：

Recent advances in Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) have triggered the development of end-to-end MLLMs for autonomous driving. However, the main emphasis to date has been for MLLMs using 2D images and videos.  
多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）的最新进展推动了面向自动驾驶的端到端MLLM的发展。然而，迄今为止的主要重点仍集中在使用2D图像和视频的MLLM上。

In contrast, this paper considers MLLM effectiveness using 3D sensors, particularly LiDAR and stereo cameras. LiDAR presents unique challenges to integration within an MLLM, largely because of data sparsity and lack of a grid structure for the data.  
相比之下，本文考虑了使用3D传感器（特别是激光雷达和立体相机）的MLLM有效性。激光雷达因其数据稀疏性和缺乏网格结构，给集成到MLLM中带来了独特挑战。

For similar reasons, fusion of camera and LiDAR data within an MLLM pipeline is also uncommon. However, most autonomous systems rely on LiDAR-based sensing, and incorporating 3D data has been proven to improve performance in traditional 3D scene perception tasks.  
由于类似原因，在MLLM流水线中融合相机和激光雷达数据也并不常见。然而，大多数自动驾驶系统依赖基于激光雷达的感知，且已证明引入3D数据能提升传统3D场景感知任务的性能。

This paper presents D3VL, a novel MLLM framework that integrates 2D and 3D time-series data in a single but simple architecture. The model aims to answer questions involving traffic scene understanding and safety.  
本文提出了D3VL，一种新颖的MLLM框架，它以单一简洁的架构整合2D和3D时间序列数据。该模型旨在回答涉及交通场景理解与安全性的问题。

D3VL shows an 11% improvement in the KITTI Question-Answering (QA) dataset compared to baseline methods in processing 2D and 3D time-series data.  
在处理2D和3D时间序列数据时，D3VL在KITTI问答（QA）数据集上相比基线方法提升了11%。

This paper further introduces the Waymo QA dataset extension, which assesses models' capabilities in processing 3D and time-series data under diverse driving conditions.  
本文进一步介绍了Waymo QA数据集扩展，该扩展评估模型在各种驾驶条件下处理3D和时间序列数据的能力。
