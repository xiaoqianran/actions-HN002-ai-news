# EGRNet: A Lightweight Semantic Segmentation Network with Edge-Gated Refinement and Adversarial Sensing  
# EGRNet：一种具有边缘门控细化和对抗性感知的轻量级语义分割网络  

**Authors:** Bareera Qaseem, Mohsin Kamal, Muhammad Naveed Aman  
**作者：** Bareera Qaseem, Mohsin Kamal, Muhammad Naveed Aman  

As autonomous systems and smart cities continue to evolve, the demand for efficient and robust scene understanding becomes increasingly critical. Semantic segmentation plays a key role in enabling autonomous vehicles to comprehend complex urban environments. However, achieving high accuracy with minimal computational cost remains a significant challenge.  
随着自动驾驶系统和智慧城市的持续发展，对高效且鲁棒的场景理解需求变得日益关键。语义分割在使自动驾驶车辆理解复杂城市环境方面发挥着核心作用。然而，如何在最小化计算成本的同时实现高精度，仍是一项重大挑战。  

In this paper, we present Edge-Gated Refinement Network (EGRNet), a lightweight and efficient deep learning model designed for real-time semantic segmentation in urban scenarios.  
在本文中，我们提出了边缘门控细化网络（EGRNet），这是一种专为城市场景实时语义分割设计的轻量级高效深度学习模型。  

The model incorporates depthwise separable convolutions to reduce computational complexity and dilated residual blocks for capturing rich multi-scale contextual information.  
该模型采用深度可分离卷积以降低计算复杂度，并使用膨胀残差块来捕获丰富的多尺度上下文信息。  

Additionally, we introduce a novel Edge-Gated Refinement (EGR) module, which adaptively fuses original and refined features through a learnable gating mechanism, enhancing boundary preservation and edge-sensitive regions.  
此外，我们引入了一种新颖的边缘门控细化（EGR）模块，该模块通过可学习的门控机制自适应地融合原始特征与细化特征，从而增强边界保持能力和边缘敏感区域。  

To further improve feature representation, Squeeze-and-Excitation (SE) attention is applied across the network.  
为进一步提升特征表示能力，我们在整个网络中应用了挤压激励（SE）注意力机制。  

With only 0.46M parameters, EGRNet achieves state-of-the-art performance while maintaining low computational overhead.  
EGRNet仅拥有0.46M参数，便在保持低计算开销的同时实现了最先进的性能。  

When evaluated on the Cityscapes dataset, the model attains a mean Intersection over Union (mIoU) of 65.28%, demonstrating strong accuracy with minimal resource consumption.  
在Cityscapes数据集上的评估表明，该模型达到了65.28%的平均交并比（mIoU），以极少的资源消耗展现了强大的精度。  

Moreover, we introduce a lightweight adversarial attack detection strategy, ensuring robustness against adversarial inputs without compromising real-time performance.  
此外，我们提出了一种轻量级的对抗性攻击检测策略，确保模型对对抗性输入的鲁棒性，同时不影响实时性能。  

By combining efficiency, accuracy, and resilience, EGRNet is well-suited for deployment on edge devices in safety-critical real-time applications.  
通过结合效率、精度和鲁棒性，EGRNet非常适用于安全关键型实时应用中的边缘设备部署。
