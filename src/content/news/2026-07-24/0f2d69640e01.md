# Challenges of Explainability in Continual Learning for Time Series Forecasting  
**持续学习在时间序列预测中的可解释性挑战**

## Authors

**作者**

## Abstract  
Deep learning models have shown strong potential for time series forecasting, yet their deployment in real-world environmental monitoring remains challenging due to non-stationary dynamics and limited explainability.  
深度学习模型在时间序列预测中展现出巨大潜力，但由于非平稳动态和有限的可解释性，其在现实环境监测中的部署仍面临挑战。

In this work, we investigate explainability as a central tool for understanding continual learning in adaptive time series forecasting, with Experience Replay strategies. We study neural forecasting architectures such as PatchMixer, PatchTST and DLinear, augmented with attention-based sampling mechanisms to support model adaptation over time.  
在本研究中，我们探索将可解释性作为理解自适应时间序列预测中持续学习的核心工具，并结合经验回放策略。我们研究了神经预测架构（如 PatchMixer、PatchTST 和 DLinear），并引入了基于注意力的采样机制以支持模型随时间的自适应。

Explainability is leveraged through attention rollout and gradient-based attribution methods (Grad-CAM) to analyze both predictive behavior and sampling strategies within a continual learning framework.  
我们通过注意力展开和基于梯度的归因方法（Grad-CAM）利用可解释性，分析持续学习框架中的预测行为和采样策略。

Experiments conducted on real-world piezometric time series exhibiting heterogeneous patterns and regime shifts show that analyzing model and sampling behaviors provides valuable insights into the dynamics of the continual learning framework.  
在具有异质模式和状态转换的真实水位时间序列上进行的实验表明，分析模型和采样行为能为持续学习框架的动态提供有价值的见解。

Beyond predictive performance, our results highlight the challenges and opportunities of using explainability to understand continual learning behaviors, revealing how attribution patterns evolve over time and how they can inform data selection and adaptation strategies in non-stationary forecasting scenarios.  
除预测性能外，我们的结果强调了利用可解释性理解持续学习行为的挑战与机遇，揭示了归因模式如何随时间演变，以及它们如何指导非平稳预测场景中的数据选择和自适应策略。
