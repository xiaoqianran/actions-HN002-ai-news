# NEXUS: Structured Runtime Safety for Tool-Using LLM Agents  
# NEXUS：面向使用工具的LLM智能体的结构化运行时安全  

**Authors:** Elias Hossain, Md Mehedi Hasan Nipu, Tasfia Nuzhat Ornee, Rajib Rana, Niloofar Yousefi  
**作者：** Elias Hossain, Md Mehedi Hasan Nipu, Tasfia Nuzhat Ornee, Rajib Rana, Niloofar Yousefi  

**Abstract:**  
**摘要：**  

Tool-using LLM agents increasingly execute high-impact actions, making runtime safety monitoring essential.  
使用工具的LLM智能体正越来越多地执行高影响操作，这使得运行时安全监控变得至关重要。  

We present NEXUS (Neural EXecution Utility and Safety), a structured-plan safety monitor that applies a formal intervention policy to select among four actions: allow, block, request confirmation, or request revision.  
我们提出了NEXUS（神经执行实用程序与安全），这是一个结构化计划安全监控器，它应用形式化干预策略，在四种操作中进行选择：允许、阻止、请求确认或请求修订。  

NEXUS combines deterministic safety rules, argument-level inspection, and a calibrated logistic-regression risk score for graded escalation.  
NEXUS结合了确定性安全规则、参数级检查以及用于分级升级的校准逻辑回归风险评分。  

On a 128-instance synthetic benchmark, NEXUS achieves an F1 score of 0.949 and a 4-class intervention accuracy of 0.6406, outperforming rule-only intervention selection by 27.3 percentage points.  
在一个包含128个实例的合成基准测试中，NEXUS达到了0.949的F1分数和0.6406的4类干预准确率，比仅使用规则的干预选择高出27.3个百分点。  

It also improves over rule-only on R-Judge (F1 = 0.861 vs. 0.849), matches rule-only on AgentHarm due to threat-model limits, and achieves 0% ASR at 99% control allow on IPI.  
它在R-Judge上优于仅规则的方法（F1 = 0.861 vs. 0.849），在AgentHarm上因威胁模型限制而与仅规则的方法持平，并在IPI上实现了99%控制允许率下0%的攻击成功率（ASR）。  

On the rule-blind NEXUS-Stress benchmark, NEXUS reaches an F1 score of 0.881, highlighting the difficulty of fine-grained intervention routing.  
在规则盲区的NEXUS-Stress基准测试中，NEXUS达到了0.881的F1分数，突显了细粒度干预路由的难度。  

With 0.205 ms median latency, NEXUS adds under 0.1% overhead to typical agent loops.  
其中位延迟仅为0.205毫秒，NEXUS为典型智能体循环增加的 overhead 低于0.1%。  

Code, benchmarks, and the calibrated risk scorer are publicly released.  
代码、基准测试和校准的风险评分器均已公开。
