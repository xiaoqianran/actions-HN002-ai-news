**Abstract:**  
**摘要：**

Air pollution causes an estimated 7.9 million premature deaths annually, making accurate forecasting a critical public health priority.  
空气污染每年导致约790万人过早死亡，使得准确预测成为一项关键的公共卫生优先事项。

Machine learning is increasingly being applied to forecast air pollution levels, yet existing benchmarks remain narrow in both geographic scope and pollutant coverage, and fail to evaluate the latest generation of time series foundation models (TSFMs) on real world, large scale data.  
机器学习正日益被应用于预测空气污染水平，但现有的基准在地理范围和污染物覆盖面上仍然狭窄，并且未能在大规模真实世界数据上评估最新一代的时间序列基础模型（TSFMs）。

We present Air Quality Arena (AQA), a large scale multi-country and multi-pollutant dataset (AQA-Data) and benchmark (AQA-Bench) to address this gap.  
我们提出了空气质量竞技场（AQA），一个大规模多国家和多污染物数据集（AQA-Data）与基准（AQA-Bench），以弥补这一空白。

AQA covers 6 major pollutants over a three year period across 7 diverse countries and 4 continents, with more than 14,000 station-pollutant series, aiming to provide a comprehensive benchmark for air quality tasks.  
AQA 涵盖了6种主要污染物，时间跨度为三年，覆盖7个不同国家和4个大洲，拥有超过14,000个监测站-污染物序列，旨在为空气质量任务提供一个全面的基准。

We benchmark this dataset across 11 leading time series foundation models and classical baselines to assess performance on short-term air quality forecasting.  
我们使用11个领先的时间序列基础模型和经典基线对该数据集进行基准测试，以评估短期空气质量预测的性能。

Our results demonstrate that TSFMs are effective zero-shot forecasters and consistently outperform classical baselines, with our top-performing model employing a cross-modal architecture that leverages a vision foundation model for time series forecasting.  
我们的结果表明，TSFMs 是有效的零样本预测器，并且持续优于经典基线，我们表现最好的模型采用了一种跨模态架构，该架构利用视觉基础模型进行时间序列预测。
