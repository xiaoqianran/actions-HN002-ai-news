# Title: Sony's decision to ditch discs was practically inevitable, data shows  
**标题：数据表明，索尼放弃光盘的决定几乎是不可避免的**

---

Many gamers have been lamenting Sony’s recently announced decision to halt sales of physical game discs in 2028. However, new data revealed by Circana analyst Mat Piscatella highlights how years of stark economic trends helped lead Sony to that decision.  
许多玩家对索尼近期宣布的2028年停止销售实体游戏光盘的决定表示惋惜。然而，Circana分析师Mat Piscatella公布的新数据突显了多年来严峻的经济趋势如何促使索尼做出这一决定。

In a social media thread Thursday morning, Piscatella shared insights into Circana’s tracking data on physical game sales in the US dating back to 2003. A graph of those trends shows physical sales peaking in the 12 months ending June 2009, when 297 million units were sold across the US. Those physical unit sales have been on a steady downward trajectory since then, leading to just 37 million physical units selling in the US in the last 12 months.  
在周四早间的社交媒体帖子中，Piscatella分享了Circana自2003年以来对美国实体游戏销售跟踪数据的洞察。趋势图显示，实体销售在截至2009年6月的12个月内达到峰值，当时全美售出2.97亿份。此后实体单位销量持续下降，导致过去12个月全美仅售出3700万份实体游戏。

The newly revealed data follows on an earlier post from Piscatella highlighting that only seven PlayStation games had sold over 100,000 physical units so far in 2026. Today, Piscatella followed up on that stat with a graph showing how this baseline used to be common; 100 PlayStation games sold at least 100,000 physical units in 2008. The ceiling for physical PlayStation game sales isn’t all that impressive these days either; Piscatella shared that the top-selling physical PlayStation game has only sold a paltry 275,000 units in the US so far this year.  
新公布的数据延续了Piscatella早前的帖子，该帖子强调截至2026年仅有7款PlayStation游戏实体销量超过10万份。今天，Piscatella用一张图表跟进这一数据，显示这一基准曾经很普遍；2008年有100款PlayStation游戏至少售出10万份实体版。如今实体PlayStation游戏销售的“天花板”也不容乐观；Piscatella分享称，今年到目前为止美国最畅销的实体PlayStation游戏仅售出微不足道的27.5万份。

Physical game sales in the US have fallen dramatically since their heyday in the late ’00s. Only a small handful of PlayStation games sell significant physical numbers these days.  
自2000年代末全盛期以来，美国实体游戏销量已大幅下降。如今只有极少数PlayStation游戏能实现可观的实体销量。

There are some small signs of hope for the physical game market, if you squint. Piscatella noted that aggregate spending on physical video games in the US was actually up 4 percent year-to-date, compared to the year before. But that small increase is a mere blip following 16 straight years of sizable declines in physical game spending in the US, reducing a peak of $11.5 billion in physical spending in 2009 to a mere $1.6 billion for the 12 months ending in May.  
如果仔细看，实体游戏市场仍有一些微弱的希望迹象。Piscatella指出，美国实体视频游戏的总支出实际上同比年初增长了4%。但这一微小增长在连续16年实体游戏支出大幅下降的背景下不过是昙花一现——从2009年115亿美元的峰值降至截至5月的12个月内仅16亿美元。

And Piscatella attributed the recent growth in physical spending to “the Switch 2 and physical sales growth on Nintendo platforms,” while other platforms (such as PlayStation) were still seeing “percentage drops from the teens to the mid 30s” in that spending.  
Piscatella将近期实体支出的增长归因于“Switch 2和任天堂平台实体销售的增长”，而其他平台（如PlayStation）在该支出上仍出现“从十几%到三十几%的百分比下降”。

The beginning of the end  
终结的开端

Looking back, it’s easy to see why sales of physical games seemed to peak in the late 2000s. At that time, services like Xbox Live Arcade, PSN and WiiWare were already showing console gamers’ willingness to pay for downloads of smaller indie games, and the concept was expanding to downloadable versions of major releases like Infamous and LittleBigPlanet.  
回顾过去，很容易理解为何实体游戏销售似乎在2000年代末达到顶峰。当时，Xbox Live Arcade、PSN和WiiWare等服务已表明主机玩家愿意为小型独立游戏的下载付费，这一概念正扩展到《声名狼藉》和《小小大星球》等大作的下载版。

By the time the PlayStation Vita launched in 2012, Sony required that all publishers that make physical game cartridges also offer a downloadable option. Subsequent consoles like the Wii U, PS4, and Xbox One continued that trend, and by the end of the 2010’s physical-exclusive console releases were practically nonexistent.  
到2012年PlayStation Vita推出时，索尼要求所有制作实体游戏卡带的发行商也必须提供下载选项。随后的Wii U、PS4和Xbox One等主机延续了这一趋势，到2010年代末，实体独占的主机游戏发行几乎已不复存在。

In recent years, some big-budget games have been skipping a retail release altogether or offering “download in a box” retail options like Switch 2’s Game Key Cards or Grand Theft Auto VI‘s printed download codes.  
近年来，一些大预算游戏完全跳过零售发行，或提供“盒装下载”零售选项，如Switch 2的游戏密钥卡或《侠盗猎车手VI》的印刷下载码。

Over the last 15 years or so, console gamers have overwhelmingly voted with their wallets in favor of all these digital download options, preferring the convenience of not leaving the house to purchase a game (or leaving the couch to switch to a new title) over the permanence of games on discs and cartridges.  
在过去15年左右，主机玩家 overwhelmingly 用钱包投票支持所有这些数字下载选项，他们宁愿享受不用出门买游戏（或不用离开沙发切换新游戏）的便利，也不愿拥有光盘和卡带上的永久所有权。

The shift has been so strong that many of today’s console gamers are fine skipping the disc drive entirely; Piscatella shared today that 27 percent of all PS5 hardware and 52 percent of all Xbox Series hardware sold in the US so far has been “Digital Only” editions.  
这种转变如此强烈，以至于许多当今主机玩家完全跳过光驱；Piscatella今日分享称，迄今为止美国售出的所有PS5硬件中有27%、所有Xbox Series硬件中有52%是“仅数字版”。

None of this data will be of much comfort to the small but passionate group of console gamers who still buy games they can hold in their hands. But the numbers pretty clearly explain why Sony doesn’t seem worried about losing what is, financially speaking, a rather small segment of its current gaming business.  
这些数据对仍然购买能握在手中的实体游戏的那一小部分但充满热情的主机玩家来说并无多大安慰。但数字清晰地解释了为何索尼似乎并不担心失去——从财务角度看——其当前游戏业务中相当小的一部分。
