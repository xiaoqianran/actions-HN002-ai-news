**Title: Bayesian Wind Tunnels for Model Selection**  
标题：贝叶斯风洞用于模型选择

**Authors: Siddhartha R Dalal, Vishal Misra, Abhay Parekh**  
作者：Siddhartha R Dalal, Vishal Misra, Abhay Parekh

**Computer Science > Machine Learning arXiv:2607.19379 (cs) [Submitted on 1 Jul 2026]**  
计算机科学 > 机器学习 arXiv:2607.19379 (cs) [提交于 2026年7月1日]

**Prior work has shown that transformers can perform exact Bayesian filtering within a fixed hypothesis class. Can they also perform Bayesian model selection -- identifying the correct hypothesis class from data? We introduce model-selection Bayesian wind tunnels: controlled environments where ground-truth posteriors over hypothesis classes are available in closed form.**  
先前的工作表明，变换器（transformers）可以在固定的假设类中执行精确的贝叶斯滤波。它们是否也能执行贝叶斯模型选择——从数据中识别出正确的假设类？我们引入了模型选择贝叶斯风洞：一种受控环境，其中假设类上的真实后验分布以闭式解提供。

**Using fixed-point-free involutions -- whose defining property f(f(x))=x is purely relational -- a 2.8M-parameter transformer achieves 0.01-bit entropy agreement with the Bayesian optimum (3 seeds), with both integer tokens and opaque symbols whose meanings change every episode.**  
使用无不动点的对合（其定义性质 f(f(x))=x 纯粹是关系性的）——一个具有280万参数的变换器达到了与贝叶斯最优解0.01比特的熵一致性（3次随机种子），同时使用整数标记和每轮含义变化的模糊符号。

**This extends to non-nested comparisons: involutions vs. 3-cycles (where neither class is a subset of the other) achieve class-posterior MAE under 0.001, demonstrating genuine model selection beyond simplicity/subset bias.**  
这扩展到非嵌套比较：对合与3-循环（其中两个类互不为子集）的类后验平均绝对误差低于0.001，展示了超越简单性/子集偏见的真实模型选择。

**We then identify a sharp perceptual access condition: when the discriminative statistic requires arithmetic -- modular addition (rotations) or multiplication (f(x)=cx mod p) -- model selection succeeds with integer tokens but fails completely with opaque symbols, and this boundary persists under 112x scaling (2.8M to 316M parameters).**  
随后我们确定了一个尖锐的感知访问条件：当判别统计需要算术运算——模加（旋转）或乘法（f(x)=cx mod p）时——模型选择在整数标记下成功，但在模糊符号下完全失败，且这一边界在112倍扩展（从280万到3.16亿参数）下仍然存在。

**A stationarity control confirms the operative factor: opaque tokens with a fixed relabeling succeed (0.009-bit MAE), showing that stable semantics, not integer identity, enable circuit compilation.**  
一个平稳性控制证实了关键因素：具有固定重标记的模糊标记成功（0.009比特MAE），表明稳定的语义而非整数标识启用了电路编译。

**Header subtask diagnostics localize the failure to the composition of header inversion with arithmetic rather than header parsing itself.**  
头部子任务诊断将失败定位到头部反转与算术的组合，而非头部解析本身。

**Probing frontier LLMs on the same tasks shows qualitative Bayesian behavior but a large calibration gap (~55x), measured through lossy probes and therefore directional rather than exact.**  
对前沿大语言模型进行相同任务的探测显示出定性的贝叶斯行为，但存在较大的校准差距（约55倍），通过有损探测测量，因此是方向性的而非精确的。
