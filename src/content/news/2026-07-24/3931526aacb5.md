# MythX shut down in March and nothing replaced it so I built something  
# MythX在三月份关闭后无人替代，于是我亲手打造了一个

If you were using MythX before March 31st, you already know. ConsenSys quietly sunset the entire MythX suite. One day it was there, the next your CI pipeline was just... failing silently.  
如果你在3月31日之前使用过MythX，那你已经知道了。ConsenSys悄然关闭了整个MythX套件。前一天它还在，后一天你的CI流水线就……静默失败了。

They pointed people toward Diligence Fuzzing, which is a great property-testing tool but it's not an audit tool. Writing fuzz harnesses isn't the same as pasting a contract and getting a vulnerability report back.  
他们引导用户转向Diligence Fuzzing，这是一个优秀的属性测试工具，但不是审计工具。编写模糊测试工具与粘贴合约并获取漏洞报告是两回事。

I've been building in Web3 security tooling for a while, and I kept running into the same wall: traditional audits cost $10K–$150K and take weeks, open-source scanners like Slither are powerful but raw, and the affordable SaaS middle ground basically disappeared when MythX went dark.  
我从事Web3安全工具开发已有一段时间，始终遇到同样的障碍：传统审计费用在1万至15万美元之间且耗时数周，像Slither这样的开源扫描器功能强大但原始，而平价SaaS中间地带在MythX关闭后基本消失了。

So I spent several months building SmartContractAuditor.ai an AI-powered audit tool that fills that gap.  
因此，我花了数月时间打造了SmartContractAuditor.ai——一个填补这一空白的AI驱动审计工具。

What it does: Paste your Solidity, Vyper, or Rust contract and get a categorized report in under 60 seconds  
它的功能：粘贴你的Solidity、Vyper或Rust合约，60秒内即可获得分类报告

Findings are split into Critical / High / Medium / Low severity  
发现结果按严重性分为：严重/高/中/低

Each finding includes a plain-English explanation of why it's dangerous and how to fix it (not just "SWC-107 detected" with a link to a spec)  
每个发现都包含通俗易懂的解释，说明其危险性及修复方法（不只是“检测到SWC-107”并附规范链接）

Fix verification: apply the fix, re-paste, confirm it's gone  
修复验证：应用修复后，重新粘贴，确认问题已消失

PDF report export for sharing with your team or investors  
可导出PDF报告，与团队或投资者分享

It catches the stuff that causes real losses: reentrancy, access control issues, oracle manipulation patterns, flash loan attack surfaces, tx.origin misuse, signature replay, unchecked external calls.  
它能捕捉导致实际损失的漏洞：重入攻击、访问控制问题、预言机操纵模式、闪电贷攻击面、tx.origin误用、签名重放、未检查的外部调用。

It's free to try 3 scans, no signup required. Just go to the site and paste a contract.  
免费试用3次扫描，无需注册。直接访问网站并粘贴合约即可。

I'm not posting this as a finished, polished product launch. I'm posting because I want feedback from people who actually write smart contracts.  
我发布这个并非作为最终完善的产品发布。我发布是因为我想从实际编写智能合约的人那里获得反馈。

Specifically:  
具体来说：

What does the output miss that Slither/Mythril would catch?  
输出遗漏了哪些Slither/Mythril能捕捉到的东西？

Is the severity categorization reasonable, or is it over/under-flagging?  
严重性分类是否合理？是否存在过度或不足标记？

What would make this actually fit into your dev workflow?  
什么能让它真正融入你的开发流程？

Is there a specific vulnerability class you'd want it to prioritize?  
是否有你希望它优先检测的特定漏洞类别？

If you've been looking for something to replace MythX in your pipeline, give it a shot and tell me what you think either in the comments here or on the site itself.  
如果你一直在寻找替代流水线中MythX的工具，请尝试一下，并在评论区或网站上告诉我你的想法。

The tool is at smartcontractauditor.ai no account needed for the first 3 scans.  
工具地址：smartcontractauditor.ai，前3次扫描无需账户。
