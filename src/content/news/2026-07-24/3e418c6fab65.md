# Forgot your Google password? Now you can log in with a selfie.  
# 忘记谷歌密码？现在可以用自拍登录了。

Getting locked out of an account is no fun. Google has a few ways to help you regain access if you happen to forget your password or lose an authenticator, including recovery contacts and backup codes. Now, Google has a completely new option: your face. You can now give Google a video record of your face and sign into your account with a selfie, which sounds like something people are going to just love.  
被锁在账户外可不是什么有趣的事。如果你不小心忘记了密码或丢失了验证器，谷歌提供了一些方法帮助你重新获得访问权限，包括恢复联系人和备用验证码。现在，谷歌推出一个全新的选项：你的脸。你现在可以给谷歌录制一段面部视频，并通过自拍登录账户，这听起来像是人们会非常喜欢的功能。

You will have to set this feature up ahead of time if you want the option of regaining account access with a selfie later on. To get started, verify your account type is supported. You won’t be able to configure selfie sign-ins for Workspace accounts, child accounts, or any account enrolled in Google’s Advanced Protection Program.  
如果你想以后使用自拍来恢复账户访问权限，就需要提前设置此功能。首先，请确认你的账户类型受支持。你无法为 Workspace 账户、儿童账户或任何已加入谷歌高级保护计划的账户配置自拍登录。

Configuring selfie sign-in requires you to record a video, which Google will store on its servers. Google’s selfie sign-in landing page includes the typical disclaimers about privacy and data access, promising that the company will keep the video encrypted and won’t use it for any other purposes unless you opt in. Selfie videos can be used for login purposes, to verify your age for accessing certain account features, and to create an AI avatar.  
配置自拍登录需要你录制一段视频，谷歌会将其存储在其服务器上。谷歌的自拍登录落地页包含有关隐私和数据访问的典型免责声明，承诺公司将对视频进行加密，且除非你选择加入，否则不会将其用于任何其他目的。自拍视频可用于登录、验证年龄以访问某些账户功能，以及创建 AI 虚拟形象。

Currently, Google does have an optional toggle in the setup flow that allows the company to use your selfie video to improve its facial recognition tech. A Google spokesperson confirms this is not required to use the new account recovery feature, and the company will not use the video for anything else if you leave the box unchecked.  
目前，谷歌在设置流程中确实有一个可选开关，允许公司使用你的自拍视频来改进其人脸识别技术。一位谷歌发言人确认，使用新的账户恢复功能并不需要开启此选项，如果你不勾选该选项，公司不会将视频用于任何其他用途。

Assuming you’re okay with all that, you will use the camera on your phone or computer to record yourself looking around as instructed. This allows Google’s models to accurately map your face so the system can compare that to selfies you take in the future to verify your identity. Google notes it may ask users to update their selfie videos on occasion.  
假设你同意以上所有条款，你将使用手机或电脑的摄像头按照指示录制自己转动头部的视频。这使谷歌的模型能够精确地绘制你的面部地图，以便系统将来可以将你拍摄的自拍与此进行比对以验证你的身份。谷歌指出，可能会偶尔要求用户更新他们的自拍视频。

When you use a selfie to access your account, Google will have you move your head around to ensure you’re a living human. It matches this data against the original video, and boom, you’re logged in.  
当你使用自拍访问账户时，谷歌会让你转动头部以确保你是一个活生生的人。系统会将此数据与原始视频进行比对，然后——砰！你就登录成功了。

However, in the age of AI, it’s not very hard to make deepfake videos of someone. It’s even possible to do that in near real-time, as would be required for Google account recovery. Google claims it has multiple layers of security measures to detect deepfakes, but this method may not be as secure as other options. After all, Google won’t allow those using Advanced Protection to use selfie sign-ins. Advanced Protection also requires you to use a security key, restricts third-party apps from accessing your account, and conducts more Gmail scans to detect phishing.  
然而，在人工智能时代，制作某人的深度伪造视频并不难。甚至可能以接近实时的方式完成，而这正是谷歌账户恢复所需要的。谷歌声称其拥有多层安全措施来检测深度伪造，但这种方法可能不如其他选项安全。毕竟，谷歌不允许使用高级保护的用户使用自拍登录。高级保护还要求你使用安全密钥，限制第三方应用访问你的账户，并进行更多的 Gmail 扫描以检测钓鱼攻击。

If you don’t mind giving Google your face, you can record a selfie video in just a few minutes. Google notes that you can choose to delete the video from its servers at any time from your account settings.  
如果你不介意将你的面部数据交给谷歌，只需几分钟就能录制一段自拍视频。谷歌指出，你可以随时在账户设置中选择从服务器删除该视频。
