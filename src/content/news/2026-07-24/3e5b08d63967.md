# For Taylor Swift, Madison Square Garden’s Controversial Cameras Briefly Went Dark  
# 泰勒·斯威夫特婚礼期间，麦迪逊广场花园争议性摄像头一度被关闭

 Madison Square Garden owner James Dolan has insisted that the face-recognition system and array of surveillance cameras deployed at his venues is “very, very useful for security”—a big reason why MSG “is the most secure venue in the country.”  
 麦迪逊广场花园老板詹姆斯·多兰一直坚称，其场馆部署的人脸识别系统和监控摄像头阵列“对安全非常、非常有用”——这也是MSG“成为全国最安全场馆”的一大原因。

 On the night of July 2, security in and around the Garden was particularly intense. Roughly 130 cops were deployed to the area. New York City officials were preparing to shut down the surrounding blocks to both vehicles and pedestrians the next day for a major, major event.  
 7月2日晚，花园内外安保格外森严。约130名警察被部署到该区域。纽约市官员正准备在次日因一场重大活动而封锁周边街区，禁止车辆和行人通行。

 July 2 was, of course, the kickoff to Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce’s wedding celebration, an approximately 100-person rehearsal dinner at Madison Square Garden’s Infosys Theater. And yet, on that extremely sensitive evening, Dolan’s MSG locked nearly all of its security team out of the network of surveillance cameras inside the arena, according to an internal document reviewed by WIRED. The move functionally prevented MSG security from fully operating the venue’s controversial face-recognition program that evening, two sources familiar with the Garden’s operations say.  
 当然，7月2日是泰勒·斯威夫特和特拉维斯·凯尔西婚礼庆典的启动日，一场约100人的预演晚宴在麦迪逊广场花园的Infosys剧院举行。然而，据《连线》杂志审阅的一份内部文件显示，在那个极其敏感的夜晚，多兰的MSG几乎将全部安保团队锁在了场馆内的监控摄像头网络之外。两位熟悉花园运营的消息人士称，此举实际上阻止了MSG安保当晚全面运行其备受争议的人脸识别程序。

 While the shutdown order reviewed by WIRED applied only to July 2, those sources add that they were told the lockout was repeated on July 3, Swift and Kelce’s wedding night, which drew roughly 1,000 of the biggest names in media, music, business, and sports.  
 虽然《连线》审阅的关闭命令仅适用于7月2日，但上述消息人士补充说，他们被告知该锁定措施在7月3日——斯威夫特和凯尔西的婚礼之夜——再次实施，当晚吸引了约1000位媒体、音乐、商业和体育界顶尖人物。

 At every stage of their planning, Swift’s and Kelce’s teams went to extraordinary lengths to keep private what happened inside the Garden. Guests had to sign a nondisclosure agreement before they could receive a digital invitation. Tents were set up for cars to park in, so people could move in and out in relative secrecy (and, presumably, avoid the biometric surveillance gear positioned at the standard entrances). Wedding attendees were told there was a no-phone policy. The only detailed description of the event posted by a wedding night guest was quickly deleted.  
 在策划的每个阶段，斯威夫特和凯尔西的团队都采取了非凡措施来保守花园内发生的一切。宾客在收到电子邀请前必须签署保密协议。帐篷被搭建起来供车辆停放，以便人们可以相对秘密地进出（ presumably，以避免标准入口处的生物识别监控设备）。婚礼参加者被告知有禁用手机政策。一位婚礼之夜宾客发布的唯一详细活动描述很快被删除。

 According to the internal document, an MSG executive ordered on July 2 a seven-hour lockdown of the Garden’s video management system, made by the security tech company Genetec, “due to a client privacy request.” Security staffers who ordinarily monitor the building’s internal cameras and the accompanying face-recognition software were barred from doing so. The memo states that only a handful of company leaders and engineers were allowed into their Genetec accounts. The rest were “disabled.”  
 据内部文件显示，一名MSG高管于7月2日下令，因“客户隐私要求”，对由安防科技公司Genetec制造的花园视频管理系统进行了七小时锁定。通常监控建筑内部摄像头及相关人脸识别软件的安保人员被禁止这样做。备忘录指出，只有少数公司领导者和工程师被允许进入其Genetec账户，其余人员账户均被“禁用”。

 “If you somehow still have access to cameras after 3PM, you will be forcibly logged off,” the document warned MSG security personnel.  
 该文件警告MSG安保人员：“如果你在下午3点后仍 somehow 拥有摄像头访问权限，你将被强制登出。”

 Since 2018, Dolan’s company has experimented with biometric surveillance at its venues, which include Radio City Music Hall and the Beacon Theater in New York, as well as the Sphere in Las Vegas. The organization contends that the technology is a security measure, but there are many publicly documented cases where the face-recognition system appears to have been for something else: retribution.  
 自2018年以来，多兰的公司在其场馆（包括纽约的无线电城音乐厅和灯塔剧院，以及拉斯维加斯的球形剧场）试验了生物识别监控。该组织声称这项技术是安全措施，但有许多公开记录的案例显示，人脸识别系统似乎用于其他目的：报复。

 Fans who’ve been critical of Dolan have had their faces captured and then put on watch lists. A lawyer representing a cop was banned in April because his client is suing MSG. A mom trying to take her then 9-year-old Girl Scout to a Christmas show at Radio City Music Hall had her face flagged and was blocked from entering. The reason: She was an attorney, and her firm was in a legal dispute with one Dolan-controlled entity.  
 批评过多兰的粉丝面部被捕捉并列入观察名单。一名代表警察的律师在4月被禁止入内，因为其客户正在起诉MSG。一位试图带她当时9岁的女童军去无线电城音乐厅观看圣诞演出的母亲面部被标记并遭拒绝入场。原因：她是一名律师，其事务所正与一家多兰控制的实体发生法律纠纷。

 Genetec’s software serves as the backbone for MSG’s video security system, monitoring feeds from the various cameras and allowing security personnel to manage the applications that rely on those feeds, including face recognition. Cameras at the guest entrances are tied into a face-recognition system made by eConnect. If someone’s face triggers an alert, Genetec then allows that person to be easily tracked across the camera network. According to a person familiar with the security surveillance setup, “This makes it much easier to track a person, because we can jump from camera to camera in real time and follow them.”  
 Genetec的软件是MSG视频安全系统的骨干，监控来自各个摄像头的画面，并允许安保人员管理依赖这些画面的应用程序，包括人脸识别。客人入口处的摄像头与eConnect制造的人脸识别系统相连。如果某人的面部触发警报，Genetec随后允许该人在摄像头网络中被轻松追踪。据一位熟悉安防监控设置的人士称：“这使得追踪一个人容易得多，因为我们可以实时在摄像头之间切换并跟随他们。”

 As WIRED previously reported, Garden security used these系统 to monitor a transgender woman second-by-second when she was in the arena. The cameras even noted when she went in and out of a bathroom.  
 正如《连线》此前报道，花园安保曾用这些系统逐秒监控一名在馆内的跨性别女性。摄像头甚至记录了她进出卫生间的时间。

 Attendees at the Swift-Kelce pre-wedding celebration were spared such prying eyes. Civil libertarians say they understand why the couple might not want Dolan’s surveillance system deployed in full. But those activists see the accommodation as more than a one-off. It’s a preview of “a future where privacy is a luxury for the elite few, and the rest of us are subjected to pervasive surveillance everywhere we go,” says Evan Greer, director of the digital rights group Fight for the Future.  
 斯威夫特-凯尔西婚前庆典的参加者免于此类窥探。公民自由主义者表示，他们理解这对夫妇可能不希望多兰的监控系统全面部署。但这些活动人士认为，这种通融不仅仅是一次例外。这是“一个未来的预演，在那里隐私是少数精英的奢侈品，而我们其余人则在所到之处都受到普遍监控”，数字权利组织“为未来而战”主任埃文·格里尔说。

 MSG’s venues host private events not infrequently. NBCUniversal takes over Radio City Music Hall every year to preview its shows for the next season; Warner Bros. Discovery goes to other Dolan-owned properties. But those customers did not ask to disable any aspect of the Garden’s security system, according to a source with direct knowledge of the matter. “It has never been done,” the source says, reflecting on their years at MSG, “and the request wouldn’t have been entertained.”  
 MSG的场馆并非不常举办私人活动。NBC环球每年都会接管无线电城音乐厅预览其下季节目；华纳兄弟探索公司也会使用其他多兰拥有的场所。但据一位直接了解此事的人士称，这些客户并未要求禁用花园安全系统的任何方面。该人士回顾其在MSG的岁月时说：“这从未发生过，而且这样的请求也不会被考虑。”

 Even when the New York Police Department takes over Madison Square Garden, the cameras have stayed on. The NYPD regularly holds its graduation ceremonies from the Police Academy within the arena. As WIRED previously reported, the MSG face-recognition system contained a photograph apparently taken from one of those events. It shows an officer in his Police Academy dress uniform.  
 即使当纽约警察局接管麦迪逊广场花园时，摄像头也一直开着。纽约警察局经常在馆内举行其警察学院的毕业典礼。正如《连线》此前报道，MSG人脸识别系统中包含一张显然取自此类活动的照片，显示一名穿着警察学院礼服制服的警官。

 Which would make this celebration an outlier.  
 这使得这场庆典成为一个例外。

 There’s the argument that Swift and Kelce, two of the planet’s most famous people, bought some modicum of privacy for themselves and their guests when they spent the estimated $1.2 to $1.6 million to book the Garden. It’s a venue with “some unique advantages,” former NY
