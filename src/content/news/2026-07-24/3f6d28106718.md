The Echo Show 21 can act as the centerpiece of any burgeoning smart home. The 1080p smart display is a Matter and Thread controller, meaning you can locally control compatible devices using voice commands or a range of widgets. Its built-in auto-framing camera also facilitates video calls. Plus, it has decent speakers for music and other features shared among Alexa displays.  
Echo Show 21 可以成为任何新兴智能家居的核心。这款 1080p 智能显示屏是 Matter 和 Thread 控制器，意味着你可以通过语音命令或一系列小组件本地控制兼容设备。其内置的自动取景摄像头还能方便视频通话。此外，它配备了不错的扬声器，可用于播放音乐，并共享 Alexa 显示屏的其他功能。

The Echo Show 21’s smart home controls are unique to it and the smaller Echo Show 15 that’s just $20 cheaper. They boast support for Zigbee, Matter, and Thread, providing seamless control for numerous devices. The included voice remote can’t interact with widgets or Alexa skills, but you can use it to control your streaming services or other media. There’s a built-in 13-megapixel camera, too, in case you want to video chat with a pal, with auto-framing to keep you centered even if you’re rushing around the house.  
Echo Show 21 的智能家居控制功能是它和更小、仅便宜 20 美元的 Echo Show 15 所独有的。它们支持 Zigbee、Matter 和 Thread，可为众多设备提供无缝控制。附带的语音遥控器无法与小组件或 Alexa 技能交互，但你可以用它控制流媒体服务或其他媒体。它还内置了 1300 万像素摄像头，方便你与朋友视频聊天，自动取景功能即使你在家里匆忙走动也能让你保持在画面中央。
