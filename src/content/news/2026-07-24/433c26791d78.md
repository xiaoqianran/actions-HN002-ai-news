**Title: Tesla’s door handles may spur new US safety rules**  
**标题：特斯拉门把手可能促使美国出台新安全规定**

---

U.S. regulators will begin developing new requirements for automakers to ensure drivers and passengers can safely exit their vehicles.  
美国监管机构将开始为汽车制造商制定新要求，以确保驾驶员和乘客能够安全离开车辆。

The new rule-making process, outlined in a regulatory filing and first reported by Bloomberg, follows a series of incidents, including fatal ones, in which people have become stuck inside cars with flush, electronically operated door handles like those found on Tesla vehicles.  
这一新的规则制定程序在一份监管文件中被概述，并由彭博社首次报道。它源于一系列事故，包括致命事故，人们因使用与车门齐平的电子操作门把手（如特斯拉车辆上的那种）而被困在车内。

The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) announced the new rule-making in response to a petition that asked the agency to open a safety defect investigation into the emergency mechanical door release design on 2022 Tesla Model 3 vehicles.  
美国国家公路交通安全管理局（NHTSA）宣布启动新的规则制定，以回应一份请愿书，该请愿书要求该机构对2022款特斯拉Model 3的紧急机械门释放装置设计展开安全缺陷调查。

The petition argued that door release doesn’t comply with federal motor vehicle standards. NHTSA denied taking the action that the petitioners wanted, saying that the issue would be best addressed through rulemaking rather than a defect investigation.  
请愿书认为该门释放装置不符合联邦机动车标准。NHTSA拒绝了请愿者要求的行动，表示通过规则制定而非缺陷调查来解决此问题更为合适。

If the agency adopts new rules, all automakers will need to follow them. However, it’s important to note that “commencing” rulemaking doesn’t mean new ones will be developed, according to NHTSA.  
如果该机构通过新规则，所有汽车制造商都必须遵守。但重要的是，根据NHTSA的说法，“启动”规则制定并不意味着一定会制定出新规则。

The decision comes less than a year after NHTSA opened an investigation into Tesla’s door handles after receiving nine reports from owners who were unable to get into their cars, sometimes with children still inside.  
该决定是在NHTSA对特斯拉门把手展开调查不到一年后做出的。此前，NHTSA收到了九起车主无法进入车辆的报告，有时车内还有儿童。

The probe followed Bloomberg’s own investigation into a series of incidents in which Tesla drivers and passengers became trapped inside their vehicles following a crash.  
该调查紧随彭博社自身对一系列事件的调查之后，在这些事件中，特斯拉驾驶员和乘客在车祸后被锁在车内。

While Tesla vehicles do have manual door releases, they are located only inside the car.  
尽管特斯拉车辆确实有手动门释放装置，但它们仅位于车内。

In an initial review by NHTSA, investigators found the handles may not work if the electronic door locks don’t receive enough voltage from the vehicle’s battery system.  
在NHTSA的初步审查中，调查人员发现，如果电子门锁无法从车辆电池系统获得足够电压，这些把手可能无法工作。

Tesla designer Franz von Holzhausen said last year that the company was working on a redesign of its door handles.  
特斯拉设计师弗朗茨·冯·霍尔豪森去年表示，公司正在重新设计其门把手。

Rivian said last year it was changing the interior door handle design on its R2 SUV to put the manual release in a more visible location, closer to the electrically powered door handles.  
Rivian去年表示，正在更改其R2 SUV的内部门把手设计，将手动释放装置放置在更显眼、更靠近电动门把手的位置。
