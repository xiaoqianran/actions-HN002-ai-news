# US government says Iran-linked hackers are disrupting American water and energy providers  
# 美国政府称与伊朗有关的黑客正在扰乱美国水务和能源供应商

The U.S. government is warning that Iranian state-backed hackers are actively breaking in and disrupting industrial control systems at American water and energy providers.  
美国政府警告称，伊朗国家支持的黑客正在积极入侵并扰乱美国水务和能源供应商的工业控制系统。

This new alert comes months after federal agencies warned of an escalation in hacking from Iranian actors amid the ongoing war.  
这一新警告是在联邦机构数月前警告伊朗行为体在持续战争期间加剧黑客活动之后发布的。

In an advisory updated Wednesday, the FBI, the NSA, the Department of Energy, and CISA said Iranian hackers were targeting programmable logic controllers on internet-connected operational networks, allowing them to manipulate data on their displays, causing outages and disruption.  
在周三更新的一份咨询报告中，FBI、NSA、能源部和CISA表示，伊朗黑客正针对联网运营网络上的可编程逻辑控制器，使其能够操纵显示屏上的数据，导致停机和中断。

The Iranian hackers were initially discovered earlier this year to be targeting controllers made by Rockwell, but the advisory has now expanded the types of industrial control systems under attack to include products from Schneider Electric and Siemens.  
今年早些时候，伊朗黑客最初被发现针对罗克韦尔制造的控制器，但该咨询报告现已将受攻击的工业控制系统类型扩大至包括施耐德电气和西门子的产品。

The agencies warn that “potentially all internet exposed” industrial control systems may be affected, and urged critical infrastructure owners to take action.  
这些机构警告称，“可能所有暴露在互联网上的”工业控制系统都可能受到影响，并敦促关键基础设施所有者采取行动。

Per the advisory, the Iranian-backed hackers were “conducting this activity to cause disruptive effects within the United States,” likely in response to the ongoing war between Iran and the U.S. and Israel.  
根据该咨询报告，伊朗支持的黑客“开展此活动是为了在美国境内造成破坏性影响”，这很可能是对伊朗与美国及以色列之间持续战争的回应。

According to the FBI, the hackers broke into one critical infrastructure provider and changed the controllers’ programming logic to disable processes that handled critical shutdowns and alarms.  
据FBI称，黑客入侵了一家关键基础设施提供商，并更改了控制器的编程逻辑，以禁用处理关键关闭和警报的流程。

The feds said this allowed “systems to enter unsafe conditions without notifying operators of the anomalies.”  
联邦官员表示，这使“系统能够在未向操作员通报异常情况的情况下进入不安全状态”。

This is the latest in a series of cyberattacks launched by Iranian government hackers and their proxies across the region since the start of the war in February.  
这是自2月战争开始以来，伊朗政府黑客及其代理人在该地区发起的一系列网络攻击中的最新一起。

The hacks have ranged from the country’s typical espionage and hack-and-leak operations, such as leaking the contents of FBI director Kash Patel’s personal email account, to more atypical destructive hacks that have caused large-scale damage or disruption.  
这些黑客行动从该国典型的间谍和黑客泄露行动（如泄露FBI局长Kash Patel个人邮箱账户内容）到更不典型的破坏性黑客攻击（已造成大规模损害或中断）不等。

Among the more notable incidents was a hack on the U.S. medical tech giant Stryker, which allowed the Iranian hacking group “Handala” to remotely wipe tens of thousands of employee devices.  
较值得注意的事件之一是对美国医疗科技巨头史赛克（Stryker）的黑客攻击，它使伊朗黑客组织“Handala”能够远程擦除数万名员工的设备。

Handala also took credit for a data breach affecting California water provider Cal Water in June, and claimed it could have disrupted the water supply (without providing evidence).  
Handala还声称对6月影响加州供水商Cal Water的数据泄露事件负责，并声称其本可以扰乱供水（但未提供证据）。

The water provider said that it saw no evidence of unauthorized access to its operational networks, which control the water supplies.  
该供水商表示，未发现其控制供水的运营网络存在未经授权的访问证据。
