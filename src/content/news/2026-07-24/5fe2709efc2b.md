**FDA reports new outbreak of explosive diarrhea with 72 cases identified**  
**FDA报告新发爆发性腹泻疫情，已确认72例病例**

The Food and Drug Administration announced on Wednesday that it’s investigating yet another outbreak of Cyclospora, the unicellular foodborne parasite behind a nationwide surge in explosive watery diarrhea illnesses. The FDA said 72 cases have been identified in this outbreak, but the agency provided no details on the situation.  
美国食品药品监督管理局周三宣布，正在调查另一起环孢子虫疫情。这种单细胞食源性寄生虫导致全国范围内爆发性水样腹泻病例激增。FDA表示，此次疫情已确认72例病例，但未提供具体情况细节。

This announcement marks the sixth Cyclospora outbreak investigation the FDA has opened this year. Overall, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention has gotten reports of more than 11,500 cases (including 4,173 confirmed and over 7,400 probable) from 41 states. Among the cases, there have been 308 hospitalizations and no deaths.  
此 announcement 标志着FDA今年启动的第六起环孢子虫疫情调查。总体而言，疾病控制与预防中心已收到来自41个州的超过11,500例报告（包括4,173例确诊和超过7,400例 probable）。其中，有308人住院，无死亡病例。

This year’s tally so far—just in July—greatly exceeds the normal number of cases the US sees in a whole year. In recent years, the US has reported between 2,000 and 5,000 cases in a year, according to CDC data. With a new outbreak announced, the tally of cases is likely to continue climbing.  
今年截至目前——仅7月份——的病例数已远超美国全年通常报告的病例数。根据CDC数据，近年来美国全年报告病例数在2,000至5,000例之间。随着新疫情的宣布，病例总数可能继续上升。

Nevertheless, when asked about the national surge in cases earlier this week, anti-vaccine Health Secretary Robert F. Kennedy Jr. said the situation was “under control.”  
然而，本周早些时候被问及全国病例激增情况时，反疫苗的卫生部长小罗伯特·F·肯尼迪称局势“已受控”。

Outbreak responses  
疫情应对措施

Last week, the FDA and CDC announced that one of the previously identified outbreaks was linked to shredded iceberg lettuce grown in Mexico and distributed by Taylor Farms. Some of the lettuce was served at Taco Bell locations and linked cases had been identified in five states: Indiana, Kentucky, Michigan, Ohio, and West Virginia. Michigan alone has reported 7,664 cases and 106 hospitalizations as of July 23.  
上周，FDA和CDC宣布，此前确认的疫情之一与墨西哥种植、由Taylor Farms分销的切碎冰山生菜有关。部分生菜曾在Taco Bell餐厅供应，相关病例已在五个州被发现：印第安纳州、肯塔基州、密歇根州、俄亥俄州和西弗吉尼亚州。仅密歇根州截至7月23日就报告了7,664例病例和106例住院。

A recall Taylor Farms issued led to the removal of iceberg lettuce the company distributed to 27 states. But the recall list was vague, not providing consumers with information on where the lettuce was sold or how consumers could avoid it. The FDA reported that some of the lettuce was sold at Walmart stores, while media reports found the lettuce also went to Jack in the Box restaurants, and food distributors Sysco and US Foods. It was also sold in blends with romaine lettuce.  
Taylor Farms发布的召回导致该公司分销至27个州的冰山生菜被下架。但召回名单模糊，未向消费者提供生菜销售地点或如何避免购买的信息。FDA报告称，部分生菜在沃尔玛商店销售，而媒体报道发现这些生菜也流向了Jack in the Box餐厅以及食品分销商Sysco和US Foods。这些生菜还以与长叶生菜混合的形式销售。

Whether the new outbreak the FDA identified Wednesday is ultimately from an entirely different source or linked to that outbreak remains unclear. The FDA did not provide information on where the cases were identified or how they may be linked. Similarly, it’s unclear what potential food sources are being investigated, though the FDA said a traceback investigation is underway.  
FDA周三确认的新疫情最终是否源于完全不同的源头，或与上述疫情相关，目前尚不明确。FDA未提供病例发现地点或可能关联方式的信息。同样，正在调查的潜在食品来源也不清楚，尽管FDA表示溯源调查正在进行中。

The Department of Health and Human Services, which houses both the FDA and CDC, did not immediately respond to requests for comment from Ars Technica. Other outlets, including the New York Times, have also reported that the FDA and CDC did not respond to questions about the new outbreak.  
下属FDA和CDC的卫生与公众服务部未立即回应Ars Technica的置评请求。包括《纽约时报》在内的其他媒体也报道称，FDA和CDC未回应关于新疫情的提问。

Both agencies have been hit hard by budget and staffing cuts by the Trump administration. In addition to the six Cyclospora outbreaks this year, the FDA has 10 other open investigations on foodborne outbreaks, including outbreaks caused by Salmonella, E. coli, Clostridium botulinum, and Listeria monocytogenes.  
这两个机构都因特朗普政府的预算和人员削减而受到重创。除今年的六起环孢子虫疫情外，FDA还有10起其他食源性疫情调查正在进行中，包括由沙门氏菌、大肠杆菌、肉毒杆菌和李斯特菌引起的疫情。
