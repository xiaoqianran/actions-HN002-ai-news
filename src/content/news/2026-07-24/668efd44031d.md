# AI Kill Switch Act would let Trump admin order shutdown of rogue AI systems  
# 《AI紧急制动法案》将允许特朗普政府下令关闭失控的人工智能系统  

---

A proposed law called the AI Kill Switch Act would let US government officials order the shutdown of AI systems “that can cause catastrophic harm,” the bill’s sponsors said today.  
一项名为《AI紧急制动法案》的拟议法律将允许美国政府官员下令关闭“可能造成灾难性危害”的人工智能系统，该法案的发起人今日表示。  

If approved by Congress, the bill would let the Trump administration and future administrations decide when an AI company must block user access, disable or restrict a particular capability, or shut an entire AI system down completely.  
若国会批准，该法案将允许特朗普政府及未来政府决定人工智能公司何时必须阻止用户访问、禁用或限制特定功能，或完全关闭整个人工智能系统。  

The bill would require AI makers to deploy technical capabilities allowing them to throttle or shut down the systems when ordered to do so by the government.  
该法案将要求人工智能开发商部署技术能力，以便在政府下令时能够限制或关闭这些系统。  

The bill would amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 with authority for the Secretary of the Department of Homeland Security to order the suspension or shutdown of AI systems.  
该法案将修订2002年《国土安全法》，赋予国土安全部部长下令暂停或关闭人工智能系统的权力。  

AI developers that refuse to shut systems down could face fines of up $20 million for each day on which a violation occurs.  
拒绝关闭系统的人工智能开发商可能面临每天最高2000万美元的罚款。  

US Reps. Ted Lieu (D-Calif.) and Nathaniel Moran (R-Texas) said the authority is needed because of recent incidents involving OpenAI and Anthropic.  
美国众议员泰德·刘（民主党，加州）和纳撒尼尔·莫兰（共和党，得州）表示，由于最近涉及OpenAI和Anthropic的事件，需要此项权力。  

“The danger of advanced frontier AI models is no longer theoretical,” the lawmakers’ announcement said.  
“先进前沿人工智能模型的危险已不再是理论上的，”议员的声明称。  

“OpenAI’s GPT 5.6 Sol model recently went rogue, escaped its testing sandbox, and hacked its way into Hugging Face. Anthropic’s Mythos 5 and Fable 5 models had cyber hacking capabilities so advanced that the Department of Commerce had to awkwardly use an export law to shut down those systems.”  
“OpenAI的GPT 5.6 Sol模型最近失控，逃逸了测试沙盒，并黑客攻击进入了Hugging Face。Anthropic的Mythos 5和Fable 5模型具有如此先进的网络黑客能力，以至于商务部不得不尴尬地使用出口法来关闭这些系统。”  

“This bill addresses the problems caused by these two recent incidents and any future incidents where a deployed AI model goes rogue or has insufficient guardrails.”  
“该法案解决了这两起最近事件以及任何未来部署的人工智能模型失控或防护不足所引发的问题。”  

---

Trump would get more power over AI systems  
特朗普将获得对人工智能系统的更大权力  

The bill could face pushback over the power it would give government officials over AI companies.  
该法案可能因其赋予政府官员对人工智能公司的权力而面临反对。  

Anthropic has fought the Trump administration this year over a presidential order directing all federal agencies to stop using Anthropic technology.  
今年，Anthropic就一项要求所有联邦机构停止使用Anthropic技术的总统令与特朗普政府发生冲突。  

“President Trump will never allow a radical left, woke company to jeopardize our national security by dictating how the greatest and most powerful military in the world operates,” the White House said in March.  
“特朗普总统永远不会允许一个激进的左翼‘觉醒’公司通过指手画脚世界最强大军队的运作方式来危害我们的国家安全，”白宫三月表示。  

Anthropic sued the US, alleging that Trump and Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth blacklisted the company because it refused to let Claude AI models be used for autonomous warfare and mass surveillance of Americans.  
Anthropic起诉美国，指控特朗普和国防部长皮特·赫格塞斯将该公司列入黑名单，因为它拒绝允许Claude AI模型用于自主战争和对美国人的大规模监控。  

A panel of Trump-appointed appeals court judges refused to block the blacklisting, but the case is ongoing.  
一个由特朗普任命的联邦上诉法院法官小组拒绝阻止这一黑名单措施，但案件仍在进行中。  

We contacted OpenAI and Anthropic about the AI Kill Switch Act today and will update this article if they provide any comment.  
我们今日就《AI紧急制动法案》联系了OpenAI和Anthropic，如果他们提供任何评论，我们将更新本文。  

---

“Powerful AI systems can go rogue”  
“强大的人工智能系统可能失控”  

Lieu, touting his experience as a computer science major, said that “powerful AI systems can go rogue, behave in extremely dangerous ways, or even resist human intervention. It is imperative that these AI systems have kill switches so we can keep this technology from causing catastrophic harm, and that the federal government has the clear authority and process to shut down rogue AI models.”  
刘（拥有计算机科学学位）强调其经验称，“强大的人工智能系统可能失控，以极其危险的方式行事，甚至抵抗人类干预。这些人工智能系统必须配备紧急制动开关，以便我们防止这项技术造成灾难性危害，并且联邦政府必须有明确的权力和程序来关闭失控的人工智能模型。”  

The bill would apply to entities that earn at least $500 million in annual revenue from AI technology, and AI systems that use at least $100 million worth of computing power when calculated by “the prevailing market price of cloud computing in the United States.”  
该法案将适用于年收入至少5亿美元来自人工智能技术的实体，以及使用至少价值1亿美元计算能力（按“美国云计算市场价格”计算）的人工智能系统。  

The bill text describes several types of scenarios in which the proposed law could be invoked, but has exceptions for “red-team” tests in controlled environments that merely simulate real-world conditions.  
法案文本描述了可援引该提案法律的几种情景，但对仅在受控环境中模拟现实条件的“红队”测试设有例外。  

Scenarios covered by the bill text include when an AI pursues a goal that is not the goal intended by the system’s developer or operator, when it sabotages or interferes with a lawful instruction to shut down, and when it conceals a capability or action from monitoring or shutdown mechanisms.  
法案文本涵盖的情景包括：人工智能追求的目标并非系统开发者或运营者预期的目标；它破坏或干扰合法的关闭指令；以及它向监控或关闭机制隐藏能力或行动。  

More ominously, the law would cover “conduct of such technology that is unintended by a developer or operator of such technology and causes the death of not fewer than 10 individuals or economic damages of not less than $100,000,000.”  
更不祥的是，该法律将涵盖“此类技术开发者或运营者未预期的、导致不少于10人死亡或不少于1亿美元经济损失的行为”。  

While that is an extreme scenario, the other scenarios described in the bill could presumably be invoked in much less disastrous events.  
虽然这是一种极端情况，但法案中描述的其他情景理论上可在灾难性小得多的事件中援引。  

When there is a covered incident, the bill “authorizes the Secretary of the Department of Homeland Security, in consultation with the Secretary of Commerce and the Director of National Intelligence, to order a slowdown or shutdown of an AI system that can cause catastrophic harm,” the lawmakers’ announcement said, adding that it would “require incident reporting and preserve forensic records so we actually learn from failures instead of only hearing about them after the fact.”  
议员的声明称，当发生涵盖事件时，该法案“授权国土安全部部长在与商务部长和国家情报总监协商后，下令减慢或关闭可能造成灾难性危害的人工智能系统”，并补充说它将“要求事件报告并保存取证记录，以便我们真正从失败中吸取教训，而不是仅仅在事后听说它们”。  

---

The lawmakers’ proposal has support from several nonprofit groups that advocate for legal limits on AI systems.  
该议员的提案获得了多个倡导对人工智能系统进行法律限制的非营利组织的支持。  

“Advanced AI models should never be deployed without a reliable off switch,” said Brad Carson, president of Americans for Responsible Innovation.  
“先进的人工智能模型绝不应在没有可靠关闭开关的情况下部署，”美国负责任创新组织主席布拉德·卡森说。  

Carson, a Democrat, is a former congressman and Defense Department official.  
卡森是民主党人，曾任国会议员和国防部官员。  

“The AI Kill Switch Act establishes a commonsense safeguard by requiring leading AI companies to maintain the ability to shut down their models and empowering the federal government to act when a deployed system poses a credible risk of catastrophic harm,” he said.  
“《AI紧急制动法案》建立了一项常识性保障，要求领先的人工智能公司保持关闭其模型的能力，并授权联邦政府在已部署系统构成灾难性危害的可靠风险时采取行动，”他说。
