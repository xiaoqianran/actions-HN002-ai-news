Title: Information Discernment in Large Language Models  
标题：大语言模型中的信息辨识能力

Authors: Joshua Ashkinaze, Laura Kurek, Alina Faisal, Tongyuan Miao, Mariam Joseph, Ceren Budak, Eric Gilbert  
作者：Joshua Ashkinaze, Laura Kurek, Alina Faisal, Tongyuan Miao, Mariam Joseph, Ceren Budak, Eric Gilbert

Abstract: LLMs are increasingly used with external knowledge sources like the internet. Do they weigh information appropriately -- updating more for reliable sources (source discernment) and more when claims bring priors closer to the truth (truth discernment)? We formalize this as information discernment and introduce Learn2Discern (L2D), an experimental framework and benchmark grounded in three normative axioms with interpretable metrics.  
摘要：大语言模型（LLMs）越来越多地与互联网等外部知识源结合使用。它们是否能恰当地权衡信息——对可靠来源（来源辨识）给予更高权重，并在声明使先验更接近真相时（真相辨识）更大幅度更新？我们将此形式化为“信息辨识”，并提出了 Learn2Discern（L2D），这是一个基于三条规范性公理且具有可解释指标的实验框架与基准。

To establish external validity, a pre-registered, quota-matched user study (n=299) confirms that real LLM users endorse all three axioms and report that violations reduce their trust and usage intent.  
为确立外部效度，一项预先注册、配额匹配的用户研究（n=299）证实，真实LLM用户认可所有三条公理，并报告称违反这些公理会降低他们的信任度和使用意愿。

Across 13 models and nearly 670K trials, we find consistent failures across both dimensions: models perform near chance on source and truth discernment, rely on source popularity twice as much as source reliability, and update roughly equally whether a claim improves or worsens their position relative to the ground truth.  
在13个模型和近67万次试验中，我们在两个维度上都发现了持续性的失败：模型在来源辨识和真相辨识上的表现接近随机水平；它们对来源流行度的依赖是来源可靠性的两倍；无论声明是改善还是恶化其相对于真实情况的位置，模型的更新幅度大致相同。

Models integrate external knowledge most effectively on datasets where their priors are already the most accurate.  
模型在那些其先验已最准确的数据集上，整合外部知识的效果最好。

Newer and larger models improve truth discernment but not source discernment, a blind spot that model complexity does not address.  
更新、更大的模型改善了真相辨识，但未改善来源辨识——这是一个模型复杂度未能解决的盲点。

We identify simple inference-time interventions that improve both forms of discernment.  
我们识别出简单的推理时干预措施，可同时改善这两种辨识能力。

We release our dataset and survey as a testbed for a core alignment property that scales in importance as LLMs replace traditional search.  
我们发布了数据集和调查问卷，作为核心对齐属性的测试平台，该属性随着LLMs取代传统搜索而日益重要。
