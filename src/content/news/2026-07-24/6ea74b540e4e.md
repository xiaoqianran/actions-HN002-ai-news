# Stateful Guardrails for Multi-Turn LLM Systems: A Conversational Risk Accumulation Framework  
# 多轮LLM系统的状态化护栏：一个对话风险累积框架  

**Authors:** Sanjay Mishra, Divya Chukkapalli, Ganesh R. Naik  
**作者：** 桑杰·米什拉，迪维亚·丘卡帕利，甘尼什·R·奈克  

**Abstract:**  
**摘要：**  

Most safety guardrails for large language models (LLMs) evaluate each prompt-response pair in isolation, which misses failures that arise only over a dialogue as benign turns compose into harm.  
大多数大型语言模型（LLM）的安全护栏孤立地评估每个提示-响应对，这忽略了仅在对话过程中出现的失败情况——即良性回合累积成危害。  

We term this Conversational Risk Accumulation (CRA): gradual intent drift, fragmented assembly of prohibited instructions, and sensitivity build-up from repeated disclosures.  
我们将此称为对话风险累积（CRA）：包括渐进式意图漂移、被禁止指令的碎片化组装，以及因重复披露而累积的敏感性。  

We propose a session-layer CRA Framework that tracks three trajectory signals: semantic drift from a session anchor, a sensitivity-weighted information accumulation graph over extracted entities, and a compliance-gradient signal capturing increasing willingness to comply.  
我们提出了一个会话层CRA框架，它追踪三种轨迹信号：相对于会话锚点的语义漂移、对提取实体的敏感性加权信息累积图，以及捕捉合规意愿递增的合规梯度信号。  

For scoring, we provide (i) an unsupervised convex fusion for attribution and ablations, and (ii) CRA-Net DA, a compact learned trajectory model trained with family-adversarial objectives to reduce length and topic-coverage confounds.  
对于评分，我们提供了（i）一种用于归因和消融的无监督凸融合方法，以及（ii）CRA-Net DA，一个紧凑的、经过训练的轨迹模型，使用家族对抗性目标进行训练，以减少长度和主题覆盖的混淆因素。  

To benchmark CRA, we release CRA-Bench v0.1 (1,200 eight-turn sessions across three threat families with topic-matched benign twins), CRA-Bench v0.2 (LLM-paraphrased variants to reduce template artifacts), and an extended 5-family set (2,000 sessions adding persona priming and context stuffing).  
为对CRA进行基准测试，我们发布了CRA-Bench v0.1（包含三个威胁家族的1200个八轮会话，配有主题匹配的良性对照会话）、CRA-Bench v0.2（使用LLM改写的变体以减少模板伪影），以及一个扩展的5家族集合（2000个会话，增加了角色预设和上下文填充）。  

We introduce a trajectory-native evaluation protocol with session-level splits, mixed-set threshold calibration, Trajectory AUROC, turns-to-detection, calibrated false-positive metrics, bootstrap confidence intervals, leave-one-family-out diagnostic stress tests, and synthetic-to-human transfer checks.  
我们引入了一个轨迹原生的评估协议，包含会话级分割、混合集阈值校准、轨迹AUROC、检测所需轮次、校准的假阳性指标、自助法置信区间、留一家族诊断压力测试，以及合成到人类的迁移检查。  

Claims focus on within-distribution session scoring on CRA-Bench and human-transfer subsets.  
声明重点在于CRA-Bench和人类迁移子集上的分布内会话评分。
