**Title:** OpenEvoShield: Dual Non-Stationary Continual Defense for Open-World Multi-Agent System Attacks  
**标题：** OpenEvoShield：面向开放世界多智能体系统攻击的双重非平稳持续防御  

**Authors:** Litian Zhang, Chaozhuo Li, Yuting Zhang, Zejian Chen, Bingyu Yan, Qiwei Ye  
**作者：** 张立天、李超卓、张宇婷、陈泽健、严冰玉、叶奇伟  

**Abstract:**  
LLM-based multi-agent systems (LLM-MAS) are increasingly deployed in safety-critical applications, where adversaries inject malicious instructions through inter-agent communication to propagate harmful behaviors. Unlike static threats, these attacks are doubly dynamic: adversaries refine injection strategies against deployed defenses while normal-agent behavior drifts with system expansion.  
**摘要：**  
基于大语言模型的多智能体系统（LLM-MAS）正日益部署于安全关键型应用中，其中攻击者通过智能体间通信注入恶意指令以传播有害行为。与静态威胁不同，这些攻击具有双重动态性：攻击者针对已部署的防御持续优化注入策略，而正常智能体的行为则随系统扩展发生漂移。  

Existing defenses treat deployment as a closed-world problem and degrade rapidly once either distribution shifts beyond training coverage.  
现有防御方法将部署视为封闭世界问题，一旦任一分布偏移超出训练覆盖范围，性能便会迅速退化。  

We propose OpenEvoShield, a co-evolutionary continual defense framework for LLM-MAS. An asymmetric rate controller (M1) decouples fast attack-side and slow normal-side learning rates from dual drift signals. A normal-boundary updater (M2) maintains a dynamic behavioral boundary at the slow rate, while an EWC-regularized policy ensemble (M3) fast-adapts without catastrophic forgetting. An energy-based multi-granularity detector (M4) fuses node-, subgraph-, and graph-level evidence to classify novel attacks as out-of-distribution.  
我们提出 OpenEvoShield，一个针对 LLM-MAS 的协同演化持续防御框架。非对称速率控制器（M1）将快速攻击侧与缓慢正常侧的学习率从双重漂移信号中解耦。正常边界更新器（M2）以缓慢速率维护动态行为边界，而 EWC 正则化策略集成（M3）则能快速适应且避免灾难性遗忘。基于能量的多粒度检测器（M4）融合节点、子图和图级证据，将新型攻击分类为分布外样本。  

Experiments over 100 deployment rounds across five benchmarks and four MAS topologies show that OpenEvoShield outperforms static and continual baselines, detecting most previously unseen attacks while keeping false positive rates low.  
在五个基准测试和四种 MAS 拓扑上进行的 100 轮部署实验表明，OpenEvoShield 优于静态和持续基线方法，在保持低误报率的同时检测出大多数先前未见过的攻击。
