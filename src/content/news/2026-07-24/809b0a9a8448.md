**Title:** Detect Early, Escalate Rarely: Anytime Detection of AI-Generated Video from the Compressed Bitstream  
**标题：** 及早检测，极少升级：从压缩比特流中实现AI生成视频的任意时刻检测  

**Authors:** Mert Onur Cakiroglu, Mehmet Dalkilic, Hasan Kurban  
**作者：** Mert Onur Cakiroglu, Mehmet Dalkilic, Hasan Kurban  

**Abstract:**  
Detectors for AI-generated video are evaluated offline. A clip is decoded to pixels and scored once, increasingly by a large vision-language model. Detection, however, is deployed online.  
AI生成视频的检测器通常在离线环境下评估。视频片段被解码为像素，并由大型视觉-语言模型进行一次评分。然而，检测任务实际部署于在线场景。  

We recast the task as streaming perception and score the motion field the codec already wrote into the bitstream. Reading that field is a parse, not a pixel-domain forward pass. Because the running aggregate is monotone, one end-calibrated threshold is anytime-valid at the data-dependent decision time. Recalibrating at each prefix is not.  
我们将任务重新定义为流式感知，并对编码器已写入比特流的运动场进行评分。读取该字段属于解析过程，而非像素域的前向传播。由于运行聚合是单调的，一个端校准阈值在数据依赖的决策时刻始终有效。而在每个前缀处重新校准则不然。  

Escalation is priced in closed form. A compute budget maps to a deferral window, on a frontier monotone exactly where the deferral condition holds. On matched GenVidBench the codec stage reaches full-length AUC 0.64 at five orders of magnitude less compute than a pixel CNN, on CPU.  
升级代价以闭式表达。计算预算映射到一个延迟窗口，该窗口在延迟条件成立的边界上严格单调。在匹配的GenVidBench数据集上，编码器阶段在CPU上以比像素CNN低五个数量级的计算量，达到全长AUC 0.64。  

Its gate holds the stopping-time false-positive rate at target while the real data match its calibration, and drifts above it under distribution shift. Deferring 15% of clips lifts accuracy from 0.75 to 0.78 at 7× less compute (paired: McNemar p<10⁻⁶). The stage-1 ordering replicates on AIGVDBench.  
其门控机制在目标水平保持停止时间假阳性率，同时真实数据匹配其校准，但在分布偏移下会漂移至目标之上。延迟15%的片段可将准确率从0.75提升至0.78，同时计算量减少7倍（配对：McNemar检验 p<10⁻⁶）。第一阶段排序在AIGVDBench上可复现。  

We introduce no new detector. The contribution is the reframing, two guarantees, and the measured frontiers. Code, configurations, and evaluation splits: this https URL.  
我们未引入新检测器。贡献在于重新定义框架、两项保证以及测量的前沿结果。代码、配置及评估划分：此https链接。
