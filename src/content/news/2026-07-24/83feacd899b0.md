**Title:** AegisAI, founded by former Google security execs, lands $36M to stop AI-driven spear phishing  
由前谷歌安全高管创立的AegisAI获得3600万美元融资，用于阻止AI驱动的鱼叉式网络钓鱼

Hackers are increasingly using AI to launch attacks on a massive scale, with email emerging as a primary target. AI can quickly aggregate personal information — such as information about co-workers, active projects, and recent travel itineraries — allowing bad actors to instantly craft convincing messages that look authentic.  
黑客正越来越多地使用人工智能发起大规模攻击，而电子邮件已成为主要目标。人工智能可以快速汇总个人信息——如同事信息、进行中的项目和最近的旅行计划——让恶意行为者能够立即制作出看起来真实可信的诱人信息。

Last year, former Google security executives Cy Khormaee and Ryan Luo, who previously worked on developing safe browsing technology and reCAPTCHA, teamed up to launch AegisAI, a startup that uses AI agents to stomp out these threats, known as spear phishing. With a decade of experience preventing email hacks, the AegisAI co-founders realized that existing rule-based systems for preventing hacks — relying on “if-then” logic — are too slow and limited to catching AI-crafted malicious emails. So they developed AI agents that quickly analyze each message as a human would, paying attention to small anomalies that even the most elaborate checklist wouldn’t catch.  
去年，曾参与开发安全浏览技术和reCAPTCHA的前谷歌安全高管Cy Khormaee和Ryan Luo联手创立了AegisAI，这是一家利用AI代理来消除鱼叉式网络钓鱼威胁的初创公司。凭借十年的防邮件黑客经验，AegisAI的联合创始人意识到，现有基于规则的黑客防御系统——依赖“如果-那么”逻辑——速度太慢且局限性大，无法捕获AI crafted的恶意邮件。因此他们开发了AI代理，能像人类一样快速分析每封邮件，关注连最详尽的清单都无法捕捉的细微异常。

Less than a year after its launch, AegisAI says its tech has been adopted by dozens of customers, including crypto payments company Mesh, AI startup LangChain, and privacy compliance platform Lokker. That demand has just helped AegisAI raise a $36 million Series A led by Battery Ventures, with participation from existing backers Accel and Foundation Capital. The fresh funding brings the startup’s total capital to $49 million.  
AegisAI表示，成立不到一年，其技术已被数十家客户采用，包括加密货币支付公司Mesh、AI初创公司LangChain和隐私合规平台Lokker。这一需求帮助AegisAI刚刚完成了由Battery Ventures领投的3600万美元A轮融资，现有投资者Accel和Foundation Capital也参与其中。新资金使这家初创公司的总资本达到4900万美元。

“AI-powered attacks bypass existing controls more than half the time now, which means they’re almost twice as effective as they used to be,” Khormaee told TechCrunch. “They’ve researched you, they understand everything about you, and they’re targeting attacks that are perfectly bespoke to you.”  
“目前，AI驱动的攻击超过一半的时间能绕过现有防护措施，这意味着它们的有效性几乎是以前的两倍，”Khormaee告诉TechCrunch。“他们已经研究过你，完全了解你的一切，并针对你量身定制攻击。”

Khormaee claims that AegisAI’s agents can spot threats traditional email security systems may miss entirely. For instance, the startup’s AI can catch malicious PDF attachments that look legitimate at first, including ones with built-in passwords and CAPTCHA, which are often used to fool standard spam filters.  
Khormaee声称，AegisAI的代理能发现传统电子邮件安全系统可能完全遗漏的威胁。例如，该初创公司的AI能捕获最初看起来合法的恶意PDF附件，包括内置密码和CAPTCHA的附件，这些常被用来欺骗标准垃圾邮件过滤器。

When Dharmesh Thakker, general partner at Battery Ventures, noticed an increase in email attacks, he set out to invest in a startup that could defend against AI with AI, one aiming to replace legacy email security tools with agentic-driven defense. “The bad guys are using email to attack us using AI at a much faster pace than we can keep up with,” Thakker told TechCrunch. “Defending against that is going to be a number one priority for a lot of companies.”  
当Battery Ventures的普通合伙人Dharmesh Thakker注意到邮件攻击增加时，他决心投资一家能用AI防御AI的初创公司，旨在用代理驱动的防御取代传统邮件安全工具。“坏人在用AI通过邮件攻击我们的速度远快于我们应对的速度，”Thakker告诉TechCrunch。“对此的防御将成为许多公司的首要任务。”

AegisAI isn’t the only startup using AI to analyze the context of every incoming email to detect fraud and impersonation attempts. Lightspeed-backed Ocean is also trying to displace established vendors like Proofpoint and Mimecast, along with newer players like Abnormal Security. However, given that AegisAI is led by experts who helped secure Gmail, the most popular email system in the world, Thakker believes the startup has the best shot at becoming the leading new hack-prevention company.  
AegisAI并非唯一一家使用AI分析每封入站邮件上下文以检测欺诈和冒充企图的初创公司。Lightspeed支持的Ocean也在试图取代Proofpoint和Mimecast等老牌厂商，以及Abnormal Security等新晋参与者。然而，鉴于AegisAI由曾帮助保护全球最流行邮件系统Gmail的专家领导，Thakker认为这家初创公司最有希望成为领先的新型防黑客公司。

While AegisAI is starting with email, the startup has its sights on eventually expanding to other areas of defense, such as data security. “The core idea of building customized, highly advanced agents that can do investigations is going to [determine] who becomes the next dominant security company,” Khormaee said.  
尽管AegisAI从邮件防护起步，但该公司目光长远，计划最终扩展到数据安全等其他防御领域。“构建能执行调查的定制化、高度先进的代理这一核心理念，将决定谁成为下一个主导的安全公司，”Khormaee说。
