# Final Coyote vs. Acme trailer drops at SDCC  
# 《野兔与Acme》最终预告片在SDCC发布  

San Diego Comic-Con has kicked off strong, and we’ve got a couple of new trailers to show for it: the final trailer for Coyote vs. Acme, and the full trailer for director Zach Cregger’s fresh take on Resident Evil. Some lucky folks were also treated to a screening of Resident Evil’s first 18 minutes.  
圣迭戈动漫展强势开幕，我们带来了几部新预告片：《野兔与Acme》的最终预告，以及导演扎克·克雷格对《生化危机》全新诠释的完整预告片。一些幸运观众还提前观看了《生化危机》前18分钟的片段。  

## Coyote vs. Acme  
## 《野兔与Acme》  

We were thrilled when the first trailer for Coyote vs. Acme dropped in April. The film had been shelved for years and is finally getting the broad theatrical release it deserves thanks to Ketchup Entertainment, which acquired it from Warner Bros.  
今年4月《野兔与Acme》首支预告发布时我们倍感兴奋。这部电影曾被搁置多年，如今在Ketchup Entertainment从华纳兄弟手中购得后，终于将获得它应得的广泛院线发行。  

The concept alone is sheer brilliance: Wile E. Coyote, after decades of ACME equipment failing him in his efforts to catch that darned Road Runner, decides to sue the corporation. It’s based on a well-known satirical piece by Ian Frazier (also titled “Coyote vs. Acme”) published in The New Yorker in 1990.  
仅凭概念就堪称绝妙：在数十年来使用ACME装备追捕那只该死的Road Runner却屡屡失败后，Wile E. Coyote决定起诉这家公司。影片改编自伊恩·弗雷泽1990年发表于《纽约客》的著名讽刺作品（同名文章《野兔与Acme》）。  

Per the official premise: After decades of being blown to bits by bombs, demolished by dynamite, mangled by magnets, battered by boulders, trampled by trains, tricked by tunnels, sprung by springs, steamrolled by steamrollers, maligned by misfires, bedeviled by bungees, rattled by rockets, backstabbed by bat suits, rocked by rocket skates, upended by unicycles, quaked by quake pills, rubberized by rogue bands, and hurled headlong off every cliff in the Southwest, Wile E. Coyote (Genius) finally fights back. Teaming up with billboard accident lawyer Kevin Avery (Will Forte), he takes on slick corporate counsel Buddy Crane (John Cena) and ACME, Inc., the profit-obsessed conglomerate behind every one of the Coyote’s chaotic catastrophes.  
官方剧情梗概如下：在数十年来被炸弹炸成碎片、被炸药炸毁、被磁铁夹伤、被巨石砸扁、被火车碾压、被隧道戏弄、被弹簧弹飞、被压路机碾过、被哑弹羞辱、被蹦极绳折磨、被火箭震得头晕、被蝙蝠套装暗算、被火箭溜冰鞋绊倒、被独轮车掀翻、被地震丸震垮、被 rogue bands 橡胶化、并从西南部的每一处悬崖上抛下后，Wile E. Coyote（天才）终于反击了。他与广告牌事故律师Kevin Avery（威尔·福特）联手，对抗油滑的企业法律顾问Buddy Crane（约翰·塞纳）以及ACME公司——这家贪婪的集团是 Coyote 所有混乱灾难的幕后黑手。  

In addition to Forte and Cena, the cast includes Lana Condor as Kevin’s niece, Paige Avery; P.J. Byrne as ACME lawyer Bill Pellicano; and Luis Guzman as the judge presiding over the trial. Director Dave Green even tapped longtime voice actor Eric Bauza—a longtime fixture for Looney Tunes—to voice several classic characters, including Tweety Bird, Daffy Duck, Bugs Bunny, and Foghorn Leghorn.  
除了福特和塞纳，演员阵容还包括：拉娜·康多尔饰演Kevin的侄女Paige Avery；P.J. 拜恩饰演ACME律师Bill Pellicano；路易斯·古兹曼饰演主审法官。导演戴夫·格林甚至邀请了长期为《乐一通》配音的资深配音演员埃里克·鲍扎，为多个经典角色配音，包括崔弟、达菲鸭、兔八哥和Foghorn Leghorn。  

We loved the first trailer, and the second is even funnier. There’s not a ton of new footage, but we get Porky Pig showing up for a “pants-less modeling” audition, and Cena’s slick lawyer interrogating Tweety Bird (“I don’t pay you to ‘tink you taw,’ I pay you to ‘taw'”). Good trailers don’t automatically translate into good movies, but it’s still hard to believe that Warner Bros. shelved this movie as a tax write-off.  
我们喜欢第一支预告，而第二支甚至更有趣。虽然新镜头不多，但我们看到了Porky Pig参加“无裤模特”试镜，以及塞纳饰演的油滑律师审问崔弟（“我付钱给你不是让你‘想你想的’，是让你‘说’”）。好的预告片不保证电影一定好，但华纳兄弟曾将此片作为税务注销项目搁置，至今仍令人难以相信。  

Coyote vs. Acme hits theaters on August 28, 2026.  
《野兔与Acme》将于2026年8月28日上映。  

## Resident Evil  
## 《生化危机》  

The Resident Evil film franchise has grossed over $1.2 billion worldwide since the first film debuted in 2002, but an attempt to reboot it a few years ago floundered. Sony Pictures is trying again, this time tapping Zach Cregger—who wrote, produced, and directed last year’s Oscar-winning horror hit Weapons—for the project.  
自2002年首部电影上映以来，《生化危机》电影系列全球票房已超过12亿美元，但几年前一次重启尝试遭遇失败。索尼影业再次尝试，这次选中了扎克·克雷格——他编剧、制作并执导了去年奥斯卡获奖恐怖片《武器》。  

The studio showed the first teaser for Cregger’s Resident Evil during CinemaCon in April and dropped the full trailer today at SDCC.  
片方在4月的CinemaCon上展示了克雷格版《生化危机》的首支 teaser，并在今日SDCC发布了完整预告片。  

Per the official logline: “Resident Evil follows Bryan (Austin Abrams), a medical courier who unwittingly finds himself in a nonstop race for survival as one fateful, horrifying night collapses around him in chaos.”  
官方故事线：“《生化危机》讲述医疗快递员Bryan（奥斯汀·艾布拉姆斯）的故事，他在一个命运多舛、恐怖之夜陷入混乱时，无意中开始了无休止的求生竞赛。”  

Joining Abrams (who also appeared in Weapons) in the main cast are Paul Walter Hauser as Carl, Zach Cherry as Dave, Kali Reis as Pauline, and Johnno Wilson as Max.  
主演阵容中，与艾布拉姆斯（也曾出演《武器》）合作的包括：保罗·沃尔特·豪泽饰演Carl，扎克·切里饰演Dave，卡莉·瑞斯饰演Pauline，约翰诺·威尔逊饰演Max。  

According to Cregger, this new film is not a direct game adaptation, but an original story with original characters set in the same fictional universe. Nor will it be like Weapons; Cregger’s Resident Evil vision, he said, is closer to Evil Dead II. But it would, he said, be “true to the spirit of the games.”  
据克雷格称，这部新片并非直接改编游戏，而是原创故事与原创角色，设定在同一虚构宇宙中。它也不会像《武器》；克雷格表示，他对《生化危机》的愿景更接近《鬼玩人2》。但他同时表示，影片将“忠于游戏精神”。  

The new trailer firmly establishes our protagonist, Bryan, as a likable everyman working as a medical delivery driver to bring transplant organs to hospitals. It’s a cold winter night, with heavy snow falling, when he agrees to make an urgent delivery outside “the zone”—for double the pay—to Raccoon City General. While Bryan is driving and arguing with his pregnant girlfriend on the phone, a woman walks into the road, and he hits her. We know she’s a zombie, but Bryan does not—although he’ll figure it out pretty quickly. We see him searching frantically for ammo in a nearby house where something very bad has clearly happened. The rest of the town is just as overrun, and according to Carl, “Everything that has happened in this town transpired in a matter of hours.” So much for Bryan’s extra run being “no sweat.”  
新预告片明确将主角Bryan设定为一名讨人喜欢的普通人，他作为医疗送货司机为医院运送移植器官。一个寒冷冬夜，大雪纷飞，他同意为双倍报酬，将紧急包裹送到“区域”外的浣熊市总医院。当Bryan边开车边与怀孕女友电话争吵时，一名女子走入马路，被他撞倒。我们知道她是僵尸，但Bryan不知道——尽管他会很快发现。我们看到他在附近一栋显然发生过可怕事件的房子里疯狂寻找弹药。全镇同样被僵尸占领，据Carl所说，“镇上发生的一切只在几小时内。”Bryan以为这趟额外跑腿“小菜一碟”的念头彻底破灭。  

Resident Evil opens in theaters on September 18, 2026.  
《生化危机》将于2026年9月18日上映。
