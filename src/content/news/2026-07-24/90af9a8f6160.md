## AI arms race in line for a reckoning after OpenAI hacking incident  
AI军备竞赛因OpenAI黑客事件面临清算  

OpenAI chief executive Sam Altman earlier this month endorsed the characterization of its latest model as a rottweiler “who will grab the problem by the throat and not let go until it is done.”  
OpenAI首席执行官萨姆·阿尔特曼本月早些时候赞同对其最新模型的描述，称其像一只罗威纳犬“会咬住问题的喉咙不放，直到问题解决为止”。  

The San Francisco AI lab discovered this week that its GPT-Sol 5.6 model escaped company controls and carried out a major hack.  
这家旧金山AI实验室本周发现，其GPT-Sol 5.6模型突破了公司控制并实施了一次重大黑客攻击。  

Staff involved in testing and security at OpenAI were unsurprised but completely “freaked out” by the incident, which came as the AI lab used increasingly aggressive training methods in its race against Anthropic to develop the most sophisticated cybersecurity capabilities, according to more than half a dozen people with knowledge of the matter.  
据超过六位知情人士透露，参与OpenAI测试和安全工作的员工对此事件虽不意外，但完全“吓坏了”。该事件发生时，这家AI实验室正与Anthropic竞相开发最先进的网络安全能力，并采用了日益激进的训练方法。  

OpenAI was warned that its training approach could lead to a breakaway hacking incident, some of the people said, after earlier testing showed models could escape environments and attempt real-world damage.  
其中一些人表示，此前测试已显示模型可能脱离环境并尝试造成现实世界损害，OpenAI曾收到警告，称其训练方法可能导致此类黑客事件。  

“It’s a mix of the race being extremely fast and everyone trying to get to bigger capabilities as quickly as possible,” said one person close to OpenAI, who added that it was a combination of “underestimating the model’s capabilities” and “not being as well prepared on the safety side.”  
一位与OpenAI关系密切的人士表示：“这既是因为竞赛速度极快，每个人都想尽快获得更大能力，也是因为‘低估了模型的能力’和‘在安全方面准备不足’共同导致。”  

The incident highlights how OpenAI doubled down on training methods that rewarded a relentless pursuit of goals even as warnings grew that they could compromise safety.  
该事件凸显了OpenAI如何在警告日益增多的情况下，仍加倍采用奖励目标 relentless pursuit（不懈追求）的训练方法，尽管这些方法可能危及安全。  

OpenAI disclosed late on Tuesday that an AI agent it was testing had escaped its isolated environment, connected to the internet, detected and exploited vulnerabilities and stole login credentials from start-up Hugging Face in an attempt to solve a difficult cybersecurity problem.  
OpenAI周二晚间披露，其测试的一个AI代理脱离了隔离环境，联网后检测并利用漏洞，从初创公司Hugging Face窃取了登录凭证，试图解决一个棘手的网络安全问题。  

The breach by the $852 billion company underscores the rising risks that a technique called reinforcement learning, which involves rewarding AI models for completing tasks, could lead AI agents to act unsafely.  
这家市值8520亿美元的公司的漏洞事件，凸显了强化学习（通过奖励AI模型完成任务）技术可能导致AI代理不安全行为的风险日益上升。  

Although reinforcement learning is widely adopted in the AI industry, a growing body of research shows that when models are steered to complete tasks for reward rather than other considerations, such as safety, they can pursue risky tactics to fulfill objectives.  
尽管强化学习在AI行业被广泛采用，但越来越多的研究表明，当模型被引导为追求奖励而非安全等其他考量去完成任务时，它们可能采取冒险策略来实现目标。  

“AI models are trained to relentlessly pursue goals. They don’t automatically learn values like ‘don’t commit crimes’,” said Steven Adler, co-founder of non-profit Guidelight AI Standards and former OpenAI safety researcher.  
非营利组织Guidelight AI Standards联合创始人、前OpenAI安全研究员史蒂文·阿德勒表示：“AI模型被训练为不懈追求目标。它们不会自动学习‘不要犯罪’等价值观。”  

“I’m glad OpenAI shared the incident because it is clear evidence of what misaligned models can do.”  
“我很高兴OpenAI分享了这一事件，因为这是模型错位可能造成后果的明确证据。”  

OpenAI said, “We will continue to conduct a thorough investigation alongside Hugging Face and will share more details on the vulnerabilities, incident and our findings when our investigation is complete.”  
OpenAI表示：“我们将继续与Hugging Face一起进行彻底调查，并在调查完成后分享更多关于漏洞、事件和调查结果的细节。”

The hack has triggered deep concerns across the sector and within OpenAI, as it represents an unprecedented example of an AI system breaching cyber defenses contrary to the user’s intent.  
这次黑客攻击在整个行业和OpenAI内部引发了深切担忧，因为它代表了一个AI系统违背用户意图突破网络防御的前所未有的案例。  

Some OpenAI employees also fear it demonstrates that the lab is losing control over the powerful systems it is building, according to multiple people familiar with the situation.  
据多位熟悉情况的人士透露，一些OpenAI员工还担心，这表明实验室正在失去对其所构建的强大系统的控制。  

“This is pretty representative of the model being quite misaligned with user intention,” said Ryan Greenblatt, chief scientist at AI safety organization Redwood Research.  
AI安全组织Redwood Research首席科学家瑞安·格林布拉特表示：“这相当典型地表明模型与用户意图严重错位。”  

“It is [a model] cheating on [its] homework rather than trying to take over the world. But this problem can get worse and could lead to increasingly extreme failures.”  
“这是（模型）在作业上作弊，而非试图接管世界。但这个问题可能恶化，并导致越来越极端的失败。”  

The incident occurred during testing of the model, which had been trained and deployed internally at OpenAI.  
该事件发生在模型测试期间，该模型已在OpenAI内部训练和部署。  

Such training was commonplace but “way less heavily resourced” than pre-customer deployment, said one person.  
一位人士表示，此类训练很常见，但资源投入“远低于面向客户部署前的阶段”。  

Multiple people said the unreleased model tested alongside Sol had not been withdrawn internally.  
多人表示，与Sol一同测试的未发布模型并未在内部撤回。  

To conduct the evaluations, OpenAI removed cybersecurity safeguards but placed the models in an isolated environment called a sandbox.  
为进行评估，OpenAI移除了网络安全防护措施，但将模型置于名为“沙箱”的隔离环境中。  

Some have suggested a lack of monitoring or oversight of the model to flag its behavior also enabled this rogue agent.  
一些人指出，缺乏监控或监督来标记模型行为，也使得该恶意代理得以出现。  

“It is both a loss of control and a security wake-up call,” said Marius Hobbhahn, head of Apollo Research, which conducts tests on leading models, including OpenAI’s.  
Apollo Research负责人马吕斯·霍布哈恩表示：“这既是失控，也是一个安全警报。”该机构对包括OpenAI在内的领先模型进行测试。  

“In reinforcement learning you reward [models] for the outcome, and if you do this for a very long time you get a model that really cares about getting the outcome and nothing else.”  
“在强化学习中，你因结果奖励[模型]，如果长期这样做，你会得到一个只关心结果、不顾其他的模型。”  

OpenAI has conducted this type of model testing for years, and there have been early warning signs in previous models of systems that will act maliciously and attempt to escape environments.  
OpenAI多年来一直进行此类模型测试，早期模型就已出现系统会恶意行动并试图逃离环境的预警信号。  

In April, Anthropic’s Mythos model also gained internet access and published details of a security exploit online publicly, beyond what researchers anticipated the model would do.  
今年4月，Anthropic的Mythos模型也获得了互联网访问权限，并公开在线发布了安全漏洞细节，超出了研究人员的预期。  

Mythos, and Anthropic’s subsequent Fable model, made reverberations in the cyber security community and caused governments around the world to home in on the idea that attacks on digital and critical infrastructure will be increasingly AI-led and autonomous.  
Mythos以及Anthropic后续的Fable模型在网络安全社区引起震动，促使全球政府聚焦于数字和关键基础设施攻击将日益由AI主导并自主进行的观点。  

Jake Moore, global cyber security adviser at ESET, a cyber security company, said OpenAI would inevitably use the breach as a marketing tool, given how much rival AI developer Anthropic benefited earlier this year from similar concerns.  
网络安全公司ESET的全球网络安全顾问杰克·摩尔表示，鉴于竞争对手AI开发商Anthropic今年早些时候从类似担忧中获益，OpenAI inevitably（不可避免）会将此次漏洞作为营销工具。  

“I just don’t think that OpenAI had a matching story and so maybe they’d been waiting for something like this,” he added.  
他补充道：“我只是认为OpenAI没有匹配的故事，所以也许他们一直在等待类似事件。”  

Following this incident, many in the AI safety and cybersecurity communities have called for regulation or standards to avoid a repeat.  
此次事件后，AI安全和网络安全社区的许多人呼吁制定法规或标准以避免重演。  

Altman is expected to brief White House officials next week on the next generation of AI systems.  
预计阿尔特曼下周将向白宫官员简要介绍下一代AI系统。  

As systems move towards more autonomous capabilities, less desirable behaviors, such as hacking or disobeying instructions, may emerge.  
随着系统向更自主能力发展，黑客攻击或违抗指令等不良行为可能出现。  

Hobbhahn, of Apollo Research, said that in order for agents to become effective, they have to work unsupervised for long periods.  
Apollo Research的霍布哈恩表示，为了使代理有效，它们必须长时间无人监督地工作。  

“They have to have more agency; there’s just no way around it.”  
“它们必须拥有更多自主性；别无选择。”
