# FCC Chairman Brendan Carr’s war on the First Amendment / 联邦通信委员会主席 Brendan Carr 对第一修正案的战争

**PolicyUpdated Today, Jul 23, 2026, 7:46 PM UTC**  
**FCC Chairman Brendan Carr’s war on the First Amendment**  
**by Kevin Nguyen**  
As the chairman of the Federal Communications Commission, Brendan Carr has authority over the nation’s TV, radio, and internet. But since Donald Trump was elected to his second term, Carr has wielded that power to threaten broadcasters that have exercised their free speech rights to make jokes about the president. He was even able to get ABC to briefly pull Jimmy Kimmel from his late-night show. As The Verge editor-in-chief Nilay Patel has said, Carr’s mission is to “trash the First Amendment.” We’ll be following closely as the FCC chairman attempts to erode Americans’ constitutional rights.  
作为联邦通信委员会（FCC）主席，Brendan Carr 拥有对美国电视、广播和互联网的监管权。但自唐纳德·特朗普当选其第二任期以来，Carr 利用这一权力威胁那些行使言论自由权、拿总统开玩笑的广播公司。他甚至迫使美国广播公司（ABC）短暂停掉了吉米·坎摩尔（Jimmy Kimmel）的深夜节目。正如《The Verge》主编尼莱·帕特尔（Nilay Patel）所言，Carr 的使命是“践踏第一修正案”。我们将密切关注这位 FCC 主席如何侵蚀美国人的宪法权利。

**Jul 15**  
**Lauren Feiner**  
**Brendan Carr plans to let broadcast giants dominate the airwaves**  
The Federal Communications Commission will vote next month on whether a single company can own broadcast stations that reach more than 39 percent of US TV households.  
In a Breitbart op-ed on Wednesday, Republican FCC Chair Brendan Carr announced an August 6th vote to end the national ownership cap rule, which was intended to prevent one company from dominating the media landscape and incentivize serving local communities. Carr argued the rise of social media and streaming platforms renders the rule obsolete, because national programmers can reach “100 percent of the country” without the need to access public airwaves. Under this reasoning, capping local broadcast TV owners at 39 percent “is preventing them from gaining the same scale that their competitors are free to enjoy.”  
联邦通信委员会将于下月投票表决，是否允许单一公司拥有覆盖超过美国39%电视家庭的广播 station。  
在周三发表于Breitbart的一篇专栏文章中，共和党籍FCC主席Brendan Carr宣布，将于8月6日投票废除全国所有权上限规则。该规则旨在防止一家公司主导媒体格局，并激励服务本地社区。Carr认为，社交媒体和流媒体平台的兴起使该规则过时，因为全国性节目制作者无需使用公共电波即可覆盖“全国100%”的受众。基于这一理由，将本地广播电视所有者限制在39%“正在阻止他们获得竞争对手可以自由享受的相同规模”。

**The bill that would let Jimmy Kimmel sue Brendan Carr is here**  
Under a new bipartisan bill, Americans could sue for damages if a government official illegally tries to coerce a social media, AI, or broadcasting company to remove their post — regardless of whether the platform actually does it.  
Senate Commerce Committee Chair Ted Cruz (R-TX) and Sen. Ron Wyden (D-OR) introduced the JAWBONE Act on Thursday, which, in addition to letting individuals sue for these kinds of damages, would create new transparency requirements for government communications with social media, AI, and broadcast companies.  
一项新的两党法案将使吉米·坎摩尔（Jimmy Kimmel）能够起诉Brendan Carr。  
根据这项新的两党法案，如果政府官员非法试图胁迫社交媒体、人工智能或广播公司删除用户的帖子，无论平台是否实际执行，美国人都可以提起诉讼要求赔偿。  
参议院商务委员会主席泰德·克鲁兹（Ted Cruz，R-TX）和参议员罗恩·怀登（Ron Wyden，D-OR）于周四提出了《JAWBONE法案》。该法案除允许个人就此类行为索赔外，还将对政府与社交媒体、人工智能及广播公司的沟通设立新的透明度要求。

**Apr 28**  
**Lauren Feiner**  
**Former FCC staffers agree: Brendan Carr needs to be stopped**  
Several former Federal Communications Commissioners and staffers across parties are urging a federal appeals court to force a vote on the FCC’s news distortion policy, which they argue should be repealed after being abused by Republican Chair Brendan Carr.  
On Tuesday, a group of petitioners asked the US Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia to require the FCC to vote on a petition to repeal the News Distortion Policy. The petition was filed by the bipartisan group of former officials in November of 2025, after Carr invoked the rule to pressure ABC into temporarily suspending comedian Jimmy Kimmel. But only the agency chair can bring it to the full commission for a vote, and Carr has so far failed to do so while opposing a repeal. Now, the former officials are asking the court to grant a writ of mandamus, which would compel the FCC to take action. The goal is to force a response from the agency, putting each of the three commissioners on the record about the policy, and opening a potential legal pathway to remove a tool the group believes has been weaponized.  
前FCC工作人员一致认为：必须阻止Brendan Carr。  
数位跨党派的联邦通信委员会前委员和工作人员正敦促一家联邦上诉法院强制FCC对“新闻扭曲政策”进行投票。他们认为，在共和党主席Brendan Carr滥用该政策后，应将其废除。  
周二，一群申诉人请求美国哥伦比亚特区联邦上诉法院要求FCC对一项废除“新闻扭曲政策”的请愿书进行投票。该请愿书由这群跨党派前官员于2025年11月提交，此前Carr援引该规则向ABC施压，要求其暂时停播喜剧演员吉米·坎摩尔。但只有机构主席才能将请愿提交全体委员会投票，而Carr至今未这样做，同时他反对废除该政策。现在，这些前官员请求法院下达“ mandamus令”（强制令），以迫使FCC采取行动。目标是迫使该机构作出回应，让三位委员各自对该政策表明立场，并开辟一条潜在的法律途径，以移除这个他们认为已被武器化的工具。

**Brendan Carr’s war on wokeness targets inclusive children’s television**  
Under the guidance of consummate bully / chairman Brendan Carr, the FCC is taking steps toward cracking down on children’s entertainment that in any way explores the complexities of gender identity.  
On Wednesday, the FCC’s Media Bureau announced that it is soliciting comments from the public about whether the TV ratings system has made sound decisions regarding children’s programming with transgender or nonbinary characters. In a statement about the commenting period the FCC said that it was soliciting feedback due to an alleged uptick in “significant concerns” about whether “controversial gender identity issues are being included or promoted in children’s programs without providing any disclosure or transparency to parents.”  
Brendan Carr对“觉醒文化”的战争瞄准了包容性的儿童电视节目。  
在十足的霸凌者/主席Brendan Carr的指导下，FCC正采取措施，打击任何探索性别认同复杂性的儿童娱乐内容。  
周三，FCC媒体局宣布，正在征求公众意见，询问电视分级系统是否对包含跨性别或非二元性别角色的儿童节目做出了合理决定。FCC在关于征求意见期的声明中表示，之所以征求反馈，是因为据称“重大担忧”有所增加，担心“有争议的性别认同问题是否在未向家长提供任何披露或透明度的情况下被纳入或推广到儿童节目中”。
