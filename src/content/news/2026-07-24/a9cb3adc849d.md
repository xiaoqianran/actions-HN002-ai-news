**Title: OpenAI makes ChatGPT Health available to all US users**  
**标题：OpenAI 向所有美国用户开放 ChatGPT 健康功能**

OpenAI said today it is making ChatGPT Health, a feature that helps users with health-related queries, available to all U.S.-based users over 18 across all plans.  
OpenAI 今日宣布，其健康功能 ChatGPT Health 现面向所有年满 18 周岁的美国用户开放，适用于所有套餐。

The announcement comes a day after a Florida-based pastor sued the company for giving a near-fatal suggestion not to consult a doctor.  
此消息公布前一天，一名佛罗里达州的牧师因该公司建议其“不要咨询医生”而提起诉讼，该建议险些导致致命后果。

The company started testing the feature through a dedicated hub earlier in January, allowing users to integrate data from other services and their personal information from services such as Apple Health, Function, and MyFitnessPal.  
今年 1 月初，该公司通过一个专属中心开始测试该功能，允许用户整合来自 Apple Health、Function 和 MyFitnessPal 等服务的个人健康数据。

At that time, OpenAI said users were asking 230 million health-related queries each week. That number has now gone up to 300 million.  
当时 OpenAI 表示，用户每周提出约 2.3 亿次健康相关查询。目前这一数字已上升至 3 亿次。

Users can also integrate their medical records from hospital systems like Epic and Oracle Health, and health platforms like One Medical and Function Health.  
用户还可以整合来自 Epic 和 Oracle Health 等医院系统，以及 One Medical 和 Function Health 等健康平台的医疗记录。

OpenAI said earlier that users needed to use the health hub for health-related queries. Now they can choose to draw insights from connected information in the health section in all queries.  
OpenAI 此前表示，用户需通过健康中心提交健康相关查询。现在用户可在所有查询中选择从健康板块的关联信息中获取洞察。

The company said during the testing it observed that 70% of health-related queries took place outside the dedicated hub.  
该公司在测试期间发现，70% 的健康相关查询发生在专属中心之外。

Through this new feature, users can use their health information to get information on food or allergies in the general chat.  
通过这一新功能，用户可在普通对话中利用其健康信息获取食物或过敏相关资讯。

The company noted that its models have made progress in answering health-related queries.  
该公司指出，其模型在回答健康相关查询方面取得了进展。

The company noted that the smallest model from its latest release, GPT 5.6-Luna, outperforms GPT 5.5 on HealthBench evaluation, an open source benchmark developed by the company to evaluate large language models (LLMs) on health queries.  
该公司表示，其最新发布的最小模型 GPT 5.6-Luna 在 HealthBench 评估中优于 GPT 5.5。HealthBench 是该公司开发的开源基准测试，用于评估大语言模型在健康查询方面的表现。

OpenAI said that it doesn’t use any user data to train its model, and it works with physicians to improve its models on health queries.  
OpenAI 表示，其不使用任何用户数据训练模型，并与医生合作以提升模型在健康查询方面的表现。

Despite these performance gains, the company maintains in its terms that its services are “not intended for use in the diagnosis or treatment of any health condition.”  
尽管性能有所提升，但该公司在服务条款中仍强调，其服务“不适用于任何健康状况的诊断或治疗”。

The company cited these clauses in response to the above lawsuit, and also told The New York Times that it is working on making health- or medicine-related answers safer.  
针对上述诉讼，该公司援引了这些条款，并告知《纽约时报》其正致力于使健康或医学相关回答更安全。

With the latest roll out, OpenAI said it wants people to verify information and take medical decisions based on professional advice.  
随着最新功能推出，OpenAI 表示希望用户核实信息，并基于专业建议做出医疗决策。

Several studies have highlighted that AI bots are not reliable for medical advice. However, this has not deterred companies like Anthropic and Google from launching health-related AI features.  
多项研究指出，AI 机器人提供的医疗建议并不可靠。然而，这并未阻止 Anthropic 和 Google 等公司推出健康相关的 AI 功能。

Health in ChatGPT is rolling out to logged-in U.S. users with free, Go, Plus, and Pro plans on the web and iOS this week.  
ChatGPT 健康功能本周将向已登录的美国用户开放，支持免费版、Go、Plus 和 Pro 套餐，覆盖网页和 iOS 平台。
