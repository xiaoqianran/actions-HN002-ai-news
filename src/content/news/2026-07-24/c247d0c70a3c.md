**Title:** FineServe: A Fine-Grained Dataset and Characterization of Global LLM Serving Workloads  
**标题：** FineServe：一个细粒度数据集与全球LLM服务负载特征分析

**Authors:** Tiancheng Zhang, Shaoyuan Huang, Mingyuan Wang, Yunfeng Zhao, Xiaofei Wang, Wenyu Wang  
**作者：** 张天成，黄绍远，王明远，赵云峰，王晓飞，王文宇

**Abstract:** Large language models (LLMs) are increasingly deployed as always-on online services, making efficient LLM serving a critical systems challenge. Achieving low latency and high throughput under volatile demand requires deep understanding of real-world serving workloads, yet existing studies often rely on proxy traces or coarse-grained characterizations that fail to capture the heterogeneity of modern multi-model LLM platforms.  
**摘要：** 大型语言模型（LLM）正日益作为常驻在线服务进行部署，使得高效的LLM服务成为一个关键的系统挑战。在需求波动的情况下实现低延迟和高吞吐量，需要深入理解真实世界的服务负载，但现有研究往往依赖代理跟踪数据或粗粒度特征分析，无法捕捉现代多模型LLM平台的异构性。

We present FineServe, an in-the-wild, multi-model LLM serving workload dataset collected from a global commercial marketplace, enabling fine-grained characterization of real-world serving dynamics across heterogeneous models and tasks.  
我们介绍了FineServe，这是一个从全球商业市场收集的、真实环境中的多模型LLM服务负载数据集，能够对跨异构模型和任务的真实服务动态进行细粒度特征分析。

Leveraging FineServe, we conduct a comprehensive analysis of arrival dynamics and token behavior, revealing fundamentally different fluctuation regimes across model architectures, scales and task intents.  
利用FineServe，我们对到达动态和词元行为进行了全面分析，揭示了不同模型架构、规模和任务意图下根本不同的波动模式。

Building on these insights, we develop the FineServe workload generator, which composes fine-grained model-aware workloads into configurable mixtures tailored for benchmarking multi-model serving platforms.  
基于这些见解，我们开发了FineServe负载生成器，它将细粒度的、感知模型的负载组合成可配置的混合负载，专为多模型服务平台基准测试而设计。

By exposing these fine-grained workload dynamics, FineServe provides a realistic foundation for evaluating routing, scheduling, and capacity-planning strategies in LLM serving systems.  
通过揭示这些细粒度的负载动态，FineServe为评估LLM服务系统中的路由、调度和容量规划策略提供了现实基础。
