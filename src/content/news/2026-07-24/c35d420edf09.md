# Insurance startup Corgi reportedly raised more money at $4B — its third round in 8 weeks  
# 保险初创公司Corgi据传再融数亿美元，估值达40亿美元——八周内第三轮融资

Insurance tech, data room software, and coffee-shop startup Corgi is reportedly raising yet another round that closely follows its last raise and would double its valuation, sources have told Forbes.  
据福布斯消息，保险科技、数据室软件及咖啡店初创公司Corgi正在筹集新一轮资金，此轮融资紧随上一轮之后，将使公司估值翻倍。

The round is said to be a second extension of its Series B round and has already closed.  
据悉，此轮融资是B轮的第二笔追加融资，且已宣告完成。

Corgi announced its last round, the B1 round of $106 million at a $2.6 billion valuation at the end of May, about eight weeks ago.  
约八周前的五月底，Corgi宣布了上一轮B1轮融资，金额为1.06亿美元，估值26亿美元。

In the AI-funding frenzy, many startups are raising back-to-back rounds at ever-increasing valuations — but even by those standards, Corgi stands out.  
在人工智能融资热潮中，许多初创公司正以不断攀升的估值连续融资——但即便以此为标准，Corgi的表现也尤为突出。

The Y Combinator alum (summer 2024) raised a $108 million Series A in January at an undisclosed valuation. (PitchBook estimates $630 million post-money.)  
这家Y Combinator（2024年夏季班）校友公司于1月完成了1.08亿美元的A轮融资，估值未公开。（PitchBook估算其投后估值约为6.3亿美元。）

Four months later, in early May, it raised its Series B: $160 million at a $1.3 billion valuation.  
四个月后，即5月初，该公司完成B轮融资：1.6亿美元，估值13亿美元。

Just three weeks later, it announced a B1 round from the same investors, saying they’d invested $106 million at a $2.6 billion valuation.  
仅三周后，公司宣布由相同投资方参与的B1轮融资，称其投资1.06亿美元，估值达26亿美元。

Now, eight weeks later, sources tell Forbes there’s a B2.  
如今，八周后，消息源向福布斯透露，已有B2轮融资。

Forbes did not report the amount of money that Corgi raised, though, and the company declined to comment on the potential funding.  
然而，福布斯未披露Corgi此轮融资金额，该公司也拒绝对潜在融资置评。

Corgi is backed by TCV and Kindred Ventures.  
Corgi的资方包括TCV和Kindred Ventures。

Kindred’s Kanyi Maqubela cited the startup’s momentum to TechCrunch as justification for the last valuation leap.  
Kindred的Kanyi Maqubela曾向TechCrunch表示，该公司的发展势头是上一轮估值跃升的理由。

The apparent justification for the new valuation is the startup’s revenue trajectory.  
新一轮估值看似合理的依据是该公司的收入增长轨迹。

When Corgi announced its Series A seven months ago, the founders said their company had already hit $40 million in annualized revenue run rate.  
七个月前Corgi宣布A轮融资时，创始人表示公司年化收入运行率已达4000万美元。

Sources told Forbes it’s now on track to increase run rate to $450 million by the end of the year.  
消息源告诉福布斯，目前公司有望在今年年底将年化收入运行率提升至4.5亿美元。

Corgi offers AI-powered insurance.  
Corgi提供由人工智能驱动的保险服务。

It uses AI to give prospects fast quotes and to speed payments on claims.  
该公司利用人工智能为客户提供快速报价，并加速理赔付款。

It offers startups various types of liability insurance like general liability, coverage of tech-related incidents, and employment liability, as well as business renters’ and auto insurance.  
该公司为初创企业提供多种责任保险，包括一般责任险、科技相关事件险、雇佣责任险，以及商业租户险和汽车保险。

Insurance is an inherently cash-intensive business but perhaps even more so for Corgi because it uses a type of insurance structure known as a Risk Retention Group (RRG).  
保险业本质上是资本密集型行业，而对Corgi而言可能更是如此，因为它采用了一种名为“风险保留集团”（RRG）的保险结构。

This is a way for people in the same industry, or those facing similar liabilities, to pool their resources and self-insure collectively.  
这是一种让同一行业或面临类似风险的人群共同 pooling 资源、集体自保的方式。

According to Corgi’s website, RRGs aren’t subject to all the same state regulations as traditional, rated, underwritten insurance carriers.  
根据Corgi官网信息，RRG不受与传统评级、承保保险公司相同的所有州法规约束。

Corgi has adopted some different structures for different types of insurance, however, a spokesperson says.  
不过，一位发言人表示，Corgi已针对不同保险类型采用了不同的结构。

In addition to RRGs, some policies might use state-regulated carriers, for instance.  
例如，除RRG外，部分保单可能使用州监管的承保机构。

However, in an RRG insurance business, claims are paid out of the pool, and a steep claim can impact how much is left in the kitty to pay other claims.  
然而，在RRG保险业务中，理赔款来自共同资金池，大额理赔会直接影响池中可用于支付其他理赔的余额。

RRGs aren’t backed by state guaranty funds, so if the pool can’t pay, members bear the loss.  
RRG不受州担保基金支持，因此若资金池无力支付，成员将承担损失。

If the claims are big enough, they can even bankrupt the RRG.  
若理赔金额足够大，甚至可能导致RRG破产。

Perhaps it’s not surprising, then, that Corgi wants to grow its coffers.  
因此，Corgi希望扩大资金规模或许并不令人意外。

But even as the startup has expanded the types of insurance it offers, it has also branched out in other ways.  
但即便在拓展保险类型的同时，该公司也在其他领域多元化发展。

Corgi now offers data room software, having weathered a recent uproar over how that software was vibe coded.  
Corgi如今还提供数据室软件，此前该软件的“氛围编码”方式曾引发争议。

Plus, the startup operates two 24-hour coffee shops with cute and sometimes ad-sponsored drink names like “Brexspresso.”  
此外，这家初创公司运营着两家24小时营业的咖啡店，提供可爱且有时由广告赞助的饮品，如“Brexspresso”。

Its shops are located in San Francisco and Atlanta, but it says it’s planning to launch five more soon, including several in New York and one in London.  
其店铺位于旧金山和亚特兰大，但公司表示计划 soon 再开设五家，包括纽约的数家及伦敦一家。

Opening up physical cafe locations also requires cash.  
开设实体咖啡馆同样需要资金。

Meanwhile, Corgi has a reputation in Silicon Valley for having a demanding corporate culture after founder-CEO Nico Laqua said he expects his employees to work seven days a week.  
与此同时，由于创始人兼CEO Nico Laqua曾表示期望员工每周工作七天，Corgi在硅谷以要求严苛的企业文化著称。
