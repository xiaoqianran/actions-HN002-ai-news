# Patreon lays off 20% of its workforce  
# Patreon裁员20%的员工

Patreon is laying off 20% of its workforce, or 93 people, CEO Jack Conte told employees on Thursday.  
Patreon首席执行官Jack Conte周四告知员工，公司将裁员20%，即93人。

In a memo to staff that was shared online by the company, Conte said Patreon’s core business is strong but that the platform has to respond to market changes and adjust its cost structure to remain stable, which is why it needs to make the “painful” but necessary cuts.  
在公司向员工发布并上传至网络的一份备忘录中，Conte表示，Patreon的核心业务依然强劲，但平台必须应对市场变化并调整成本结构以保持稳定，因此需要进行“痛苦但必要”的裁员。

Conte wrote that “AI has fundamentally transformed the tech industry,” and that the pace of change has “never been more intense.”  
Conte写道，“人工智能从根本上改变了科技行业”，变化的节奏“从未如此激烈”。

However, Conte went on to note that Patreon isn’t making the cuts because it wants to replace employees with AI.  
然而，Conte接着指出，Patreon裁员并非因为想用AI取代员工。

“To be clear about the impact of AI on today’s decision: we are not making the above changes because we believe AI replaces humans,” he wrote.  
他写道：“为了明确AI对今日决定的影响：我们做出上述调整并非因为我们相信AI能取代人类。”

“The more we have learned to use these new tools, the clearer it has become that they are not substitutes for the creativity, judgment, detail orientation, or craftsmanship that our teammates have in spades, nor do they replace the desire for human connection that all of us cherish so deeply. That’s my personal opinion, but more importantly, it’s the foundation of Patreon’s strategy: our product vision and business are both predicated on the value of human creativity and human connection.”  
“我们越学习使用这些新工具，就越清楚它们无法替代我们团队成员所拥有的丰富创造力、判断力、细致入微的观察力或精湛技艺，也无法取代我们所有人深为珍视的人际联结渴望。这是我的个人观点，但更重要的是，这是Patreon战略的基石：我们的产品愿景和业务都建立在人类创造力与人类联结的价值之上。”

He continued, “AI has fundamentally transformed the tech industry, though, including how we work, how we build products, how we communicate, and more. That does have an impact on how we operate and organize.”  
他继续说道：“然而，AI确实从根本上改变了科技行业，包括我们的工作方式、产品构建方式、沟通方式等。这确实对我们的运营和组织方式产生了影响。”

Beyond the headcount reduction, Conte said Patreon is also restructuring how it operates, “flattening” its organizational chart and refocusing teams around its top priorities.  
除了裁员，Conte表示Patreon还在重组运营方式，“扁平化”组织架构，并将团队重心重新聚焦于最高优先事项。

Affected employees will receive at least 16 weeks of severance pay, plus an additional week for every year worked, healthcare coverage through the end of the year, and a $1,500 stipend to replace their company laptop.  
受影响的员工将获得至少16周的遣散费，外加每工作一年增加一周的补偿，医疗保险覆盖至年底，以及1500美元用于更换公司笔记本电脑的津贴。

Last week, Patreon announced that it was partnering with internet infrastructure provider Cloudflare to directly block access to AI bots designed to train their AI models on creators’ work without permission.  
上周，Patreon宣布与互联网基础设施提供商Cloudflare合作，直接屏蔽旨在未经许可使用创作者作品训练AI模型的AI机器人。

The company said it had to enhance its efforts on this front because AI scraping has become more sophisticated.  
公司表示，由于AI数据抓取已变得更加复杂，因此必须加强这方面的努力。

The move came as online publishers and creators are grappling with AI companies using their work to train AI models.  
此举正值在线出版商和创作者们正面临AI公司利用其作品训练AI模型的问题。

Patreon’s latest round of layoffs is the platform’s largest since it cut 17% of its staff back in 2022.  
Patreon最新一轮裁员是该平台自2022年裁员17%以来规模最大的一次。

During that earlier round of layoffs, Patreon also closed its offices in Berlin and Dublin.  
在早前的裁员中，Patreon还关闭了其柏林和都柏林的办公室。
