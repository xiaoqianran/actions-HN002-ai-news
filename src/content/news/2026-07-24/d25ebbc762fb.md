# Geekbench 7 will push your computer or phone even harder for better benchmarking  
# Geekbench 7 将通过更严苛的测试，为你的电脑或手机提供更精准的基准测试

Primate Labs is releasing Geekbench 7, the latest generation of its popular benchmarking tool. Geekbench 7 features new video and audio encoding / decoding tests, a redesigned multi-core test, and larger, more demanding datasets for CPUs and GPUs to crunch through. It otherwise looks and acts just like Geekbench 6, and a Pro license will cost the same $99 (discounted to $79 until August 6th).  
Primate Labs 正在发布 Geekbench 7，这是其广受欢迎的基准测试工具的最新版本。Geekbench 7 具备新的视频和音频编码/解码测试、重新设计的多核测试，以及供 CPU 和 GPU 处理更大、要求更高的数据集。在其他方面，它看起来和用起来都与 Geekbench 6 相同，Pro 许可证价格仍为 99 美元（8月6日前折扣价为 79 美元）。

A Geekbench 7 Pro license allows for offline benchmarking that doesn’t get uploaded to the Geekbench Browser, and it still covers all compatible desktop platforms of Windows, macOS, and Linux.  
Geekbench 7 Pro 许可证允许进行离线基准测试（结果不会上传至 Geekbench Browser），并继续支持所有兼容的桌面平台：Windows、macOS 和 Linux。

Geekbench be looking like Geekbench. Image: Primate Labs  
Geekbench 看起来还是那个 Geekbench。图片：Primate Labs

Geekbench is one of the mainstay synthetic benchmarks we use in reviewing laptops here at The Verge. I’ve always liked how simple and fast Geekbench is to run, but its short duration also means it’s a better test of quick bursts of computing rather than sustained loads that can throttle some CPUs and GPUs. (One of its tests involves rendering a 1.5MB PDF file, which must be near-instantaneous for most modern chips.)  
Geekbench 是我们在 The Verge 评测笔记本电脑时使用的主要综合基准测试之一。我一直喜欢 Geekbench 运行起来简单快捷，但其短暂的运行时间也意味着它更适合测试计算的快速爆发，而非可能导致某些 CPU 和 GPU 降频的持续负载。（其中一个测试涉及渲染一个 1.5MB 的 PDF 文件，这对大多数现代芯片来说必须是近乎瞬时的。）

Primate Labs claims Geekbench 7 uses “more demanding data sets to more accurately capture the硬件 demands of modern applications.” The new audio / video tests also now include AV1, Opus, model video conferencing, streaming, and content consumption workloads. And multi-core benchmarks are designed to better reflect real software behavior since the new workloads will only run multi-threaded when the modeled tasks run that way.  
Primate Labs 声称 Geekbench 7 使用“要求更高的数据集，以更准确地捕捉现代应用的硬件需求”。新的音频/视频测试现在还包括 AV1、Opus、模拟视频会议、流媒体和内容消费等工作负载。多核基准测试的设计也更好地反映了真实软件行为，因为新工作负载仅在模拟的任务以多线程方式运行时才会以多线程运行。

These all sound like marked improvements, even if Geekbench 7 tests still only take a few minutes to run. Scores from Geekbench 6 are of course not compatible with Geekbench 7. So if you’re the testing type, feel free to spiritually join me in a whole bunch of new benchmark testing and retesting. Always be testing.  
这些听起来都是显著的改进，即使 Geekbench 7 测试仍然只需几分钟就能完成。Geekbench 6 的分数当然与 Geekbench 7 不兼容。所以，如果你是测试爱好者，欢迎在精神上加入我，进行大量新的基准测试和重测。永远保持测试。
