**Title:**  
Task Competence Is Not Instruction Following: Evaluating Instruction-Conflicting Behavior in Small Language Models  
**标题：**  
任务能力不等于指令遵循：评估小型语言模型中的指令冲突行为  

**Authors:**  
Mahdiyeh Farajidizaji, Vatsal Raina  

**Abstract:**  
Instruction tuning is meant to make language models follow user requests, yet it is unclear whether small models comply when an instruction conflicts with their usual task behavior.  
指令微调旨在使语言模型遵循用户请求，但尚不清楚当指令与模型通常的任务行为冲突时，小型模型是否会遵从。  

We study this across three tasks - multiple-choice question answering (MCQA), sentiment classification, and mathematical question answering - by pairing a standard instruction with a conflicting non-standard one (select an incorrect option, output the opposite sentiment, or return twice the answer). This cross-task design allows us to test whether resistance to conflicting instructions is tied to specific task characteristics or reflects a broader behavioral tendency.  
我们通过三个任务研究这一点——多项选择题回答（MCQA）、情感分类和数学问答——通过将标准指令与非标准指令配对（选择错误选项、输出相反情感或返回两倍答案）。这种跨任务设计使我们能够测试对冲突指令的抵抗是否与特定任务特征相关，还是反映了更广泛的行为倾向。  

As all predictions are scored against the original ground truth, a model that ignores the non-standard instruction still appears accurate.  
由于所有预测都是根据原始真实标签评分的，忽略非标准指令的模型仍然显得准确。  

Using standard accuracy, non-standard accuracy, and an Instruction-Following Failure Rate (IFFR), we evaluate instruction-tuned Qwen models across sizes.  
我们使用标准准确率、非标准准确率和指令遵循失败率（IFFR）来评估不同规模的指令微调Qwen模型。  

Both standard accuracy and instruction following generally improve with scale, although the pattern is not consistent across all tasks and datasets.  
标准准确率和指令遵循通常随规模提高，尽管在所有任务和数据集中的模式并不一致。  

Small models stay competent yet routinely ignore the non-standard instruction, while larger models show a clear gap between the two settings.  
小型模型保持能力但通常忽略非标准指令，而大型模型在这两种设置之间显示出明显差距。  

These findings suggest that gains in task capability do not automatically provide reliable control over model behavior.  
这些发现表明，任务能力的提升并不自动提供对模型行为的可靠控制。  

Task competence and instruction following are therefore distinct abilities, and reporting only standard accuracy hides instruction-following failures.  
因此，任务能力和指令遵循是不同的能力，仅报告标准准确率会隐藏指令遵循失败。
