**Title: Microsoft responds to LG monitors installing McAfee ads on Windows**  
**标题：微软回应LG显示器在Windows上安装McAfee广告问题**

LG is reportedly pulling a McAfee pop-up ad from a controversial app that some of its monitors have stealthily installed on connected computers.  
据报道，LG正从其部分显示器悄然安装在连接电脑上的争议应用中移除McAfee弹窗广告。

The app, LG Monitor App Installer, installs itself through a Windows Update alongside monitor driver updates. LG Monitor App Installer then pushes ads for a 30-day free trial for McAfee, as well as other software, via a pop-up on affected systems, multiple users have reported.  
该应用名为“LG Monitor App Installer”，会通过Windows Update随显示器驱动程序更新自动安装。随后，该应用会在受影响的系统上推送弹窗广告，推广McAfee 30天免费试用及其他软件，多名用户已报告此问题。

This has drawn negative attention recently, with complaints surfacing on Reddit and a subsequent video from YouTube channel Gamers Nexus. In the video, editor-in-chief Steve Burke said that the publication paid $1,200 for an LG UltraGear 3GX900A-B gaming monitor (the monitor’s price dropped to $600 two days later, Burke said) for testing and replicated the behavior “several times” across “multiple” Windows 11 systems.  
此事近期引发负面关注，Reddit上出现投诉，YouTube频道Gamers Nexus随后发布视频。视频中，主编Steve Burke表示，该媒体以1200美元购买了一台LG UltraGear 3GX900A-B电竞显示器（两天后该显示器价格降至600美元）进行测试，并在多台Windows 11系统上“多次”复现了此行为。

After LG Monitor App Installer was installed, a pop-up appeared on the screen’s lower-right corner “on every single boot,” Burke said. Most of the time, the pop-up was a McAfee ad. The publication also reported seeing ads for LG Switch, LG Calibration Studio, LG Dual Controller, and LG Channels on rare occasions.  
Burke称，LG Monitor App Installer安装后，每次开机屏幕右下角都会出现弹窗，且大多数时候是McAfee广告。该媒体还报告称，偶尔会看到LG Switch、LG Calibration Studio、LG Dual Controller和LG Channels的广告。

An example of the pop-up ad is seen in a still from Gamers Nexus’ video. Credit: Gamers Nexus/Windows/YouTube  
弹窗广告示例可见于Gamers Nexus视频截图。来源：Gamers Nexus/Windows/YouTube

Following Gamers Nexus’ video, a Microsoft representative stated that the LG Monitor App Installer will no longer show pop-up ads for McAfee. In response to a social media post about the app, Pavan Davuluri, EVP of Windows and devices at Microsoft, said this week:  
继Gamers Nexus视频发布后，微软代表表示LG Monitor App Installer将不再显示McAfee弹窗广告。微软Windows和设备执行副总裁Pavan Davuluri本周在社交媒体上回应称：

“We’ve connected with the team at LG and as an immediate next step, they have agreed to disable the McAfee pop-up from their app. We appreciate LG working with us toward a shared goal of a better experience for our mutual customers. We will keep improving here with our ecosystem partners.”  
“我们已与LG团队沟通，作为立即的后续步骤，他们已同意在其应用中禁用McAfee弹窗。我们感谢LG与我们合作，共同致力于为双方客户提供更好的体验。我们将继续与生态合作伙伴共同改进。”

As mentioned, some LG monitors appear to have been installing LG Monitor App Installer onto Windows computers for months. Publication Windows Latest noted that the app recently got an update, “and its changelog mentions McAfee as an additional app,” which could be what prompted more people to see the ads…and then complain about them.  
如前所述，部分LG显示器似乎已数月来在Windows电脑上自动安装LG Monitor App Installer。媒体Windows Latest指出，该应用最近获得更新，“其更新日志提到McAfee作为附加应用”，这可能促使更多人看到广告并随后投诉。

However, the removal of McAfee doesn’t address the problem of a peripheral installing ad-pushing software onto people’s computers.  
然而，移除McAfee并未解决外设向用户电脑强制安装广告推送软件的问题。

Once the app is installed, “LG technically possesses permission to use ‘all system resources,’” as well as to “collect geolocation, device data, online activity, contacts, user credentials, transactions, and more,” Burke said.  
Burke表示，一旦该应用安装，“LG在技术上拥有使用‘所有系统资源’的权限”，并可“收集地理位置、设备数据、在线活动、联系人、用户凭证、交易记录等更多信息”。

It’s also concerning that the practice has been going on for years and has seemingly expanded recently. Burke said he recently started seeing the pop-up ad on LG monitors he purchased three years ago.  
更令人担忧的是，这种做法已持续数年，且近期似乎有所扩大。Burke称，他最近在三年前购买的LG显示器上也看到了弹窗广告。

The incident also brings questions about what Windows permits from peripherals’ companion apps. LG Monitor App Installer’s description on the Microsoft Store states that the app is for users to “easily install and use apps supported by your monitor through LG Monitor App Installer” and lists McAfee as one of those apps.  
此事件也引发了对Windows对外设配套应用许可范围的质疑。LG Monitor App Installer在Microsoft Store的描述称，该应用旨在让用户“通过LG Monitor App Installer轻松安装和使用显示器支持的应用”，并将McAfee列为其中之一。

Further, Microsoft allows Universal Windows Platform device apps “to automatically install when the user connects their device to the PC” if the user hasn’t opted out of Recommended Settings during installation and is connected to the Internet and signed into the Microsoft Store, per a Microsoft support page for Windows developers spotted by Windows Latest.  
此外，根据Windows Latest发现的微软开发者支持页面，如果用户在安装时未退出“推荐设置”，且已连接互联网并登录Microsoft Store，微软允许通用Windows平台设备应用“在用户连接设备到电脑时自动安装”。

The page reads: “The automatic installation feature does not provide a notification to the user when the app is installed. Some users may find this experience confusing and frustrating and give your app a bad rating.”  
该页面写道：“自动安装功能在应用安装时不会向用户提供通知。部分用户可能会觉得此体验令人困惑和沮丧，从而给你的应用打低分。”

Other hardware vendors have been criticized for their devices automatically installing unwanted software when connected to a computer. Examples include Razer (gaming peripherals), Logitech (gaming and productivity peripherals), Asus (motherboards), and Gigabyte (motherboards).  
其他硬件厂商也因设备连接电脑时自动安装不受欢迎的软件而受批评。例如Razer（游戏外设）、Logitech（游戏及生产力外设）、华硕（主板）和技嘉（主板）。

Considering that most monitors don’t necessarily require a companion app and can be rather expensive, the forcible installation of LG Monitor App Installer may seem particularly egregious.  
考虑到大多数显示器未必需要配套应用且价格不菲，强制安装LG Monitor App Installer的行为可能显得尤为恶劣。

Ars Technica reached out to LG about the purpose of LG Monitor App Installer, affected monitors, and privacy concerns. A company representative acknowledged receipt of the questions but didn’t provide a response ahead of publication.  
Ars Technica就LG Monitor App Installer的目的、受影响显示器型号及隐私问题联系了LG。公司代表确认收到问题，但在发布前未提供回复。

Citing user complaints, Gamers Nexus listed the following monitors as auto-installing LG Monitor App Installer onto PCs. But with Microsoft approving of the practice, there may be more monitors installing LG’s bloatware:  
基于用户投诉，Gamers Nexus列出了以下自动向电脑安装LG Monitor App Installer的显示器型号。但由于微软批准此做法，可能有更多显示器正在安装LG的臃肿软件：
