**Title: Amazon puts Luna cloud-streamed games like Fallout 4 inside Prime Video**  
**标题：亚马逊将《辐射4》等Luna云流式游戏整合至Prime Video**

Now Prime Video has a games section, with Amazon’s cloud gaming platform Luna built in to let you start playing right away.  
现在Prime Video拥有了游戏专区，内置亚马逊云游戏平台Luna，让用户可以立即开始游玩。

by Stevie Bonifield  
Jul 23, 2026, 4:39 PM UTC  
作者：Stevie Bonifield  
2026年7月23日

The new strategy that Amazon gaming exec Jeff Gattis talked to The Verge about last month is getting clearer now that Prime Video has added a new Games tab on Fire TV devices.  
亚马逊游戏高管Jeff Gattis上月向The Verge透露的新战略如今更加清晰——Prime Video已在Fire TV设备上新增了“游戏”标签页。

With Prime Gaming, Amazon Game Studios, and Luna now combined into one organization, it’s aiming at the more casual audience of people already using Prime Video to stream movies, TV shows, and sports, instead of trying to compete directly with “core” gamers playing MMOs and shooters.  
随着Prime Gaming、Amazon Game Studios和Luna合并为一个组织，其目标转向了那些已在使用Prime Video流媒体观看电影、剧集和体育节目的休闲用户，而非直接与玩MMO和射击游戏的“核心”玩家竞争。

Luna was already included in Prime, but now users in the US and UK can access it directly within Prime Video to play titles like Dispatch, Fallout 4, Hogwarts Legacy, EA Sports FC 26, and Clue.  
Luna此前已包含在Prime会员中，但如今美国和英国的用户可直接在Prime Video内访问，游玩《Dispatch》《辐射4》《霍格沃茨遗产》《EA Sports FC 26》和《Clue》等游戏。

Amazon says that “details on additional devices, countries, and games” will be announced later.  
亚马逊表示，“关于更多设备、国家和游戏的细节”将在后续公布。

The expansion is similar to Netflix’s recent additions of streaming games you can play on your TV, and in an interview with The Hollywood Reporter, Gattis admitted Amazon has “never established” itself as a gaming platform so far, and emphasized the key role Amazon IP like Tomb Raider and James Bond will have.  
此次扩展与Netflix近期新增的电视可玩游戏类似。在接受《好莱坞报道者》采访时，Gattis承认亚马逊迄今“从未确立”其游戏平台地位，并强调了《古墓丽影》《詹姆斯·邦德》等亚马逊IP将发挥的关键作用。

The Games tab is also a place for Amazon to promote tie-in games to people who watched its content on Prime Video, like the Masters of the Universe: Legends Unite co-op card battler game, or its “AI-powered party game” Courtroom Chaos: Starring Arnold Schwarzenegger ahead of his new holiday movie for Amazon later this year.  
“游戏”标签页也是亚马逊向观看过Prime Video内容的用户推广联动游戏的平台，例如《宇宙的巨人：希曼——传奇联合》合作卡牌对战游戏，或今年假日季阿诺·施瓦辛格主演新片前的“AI派对游戏”《法庭混乱：施瓦辛格主演》。
