# Tesla’s robotaxi promises are clashing with reality  
特斯拉的无人驾驶出租车承诺正与现实产生冲突  

---

Mileage is flat, the number of vehicles are shrinking, and safety is an open question.  
行驶里程停滞不前，车辆数量正在减少，而安全性仍是一个悬而未决的问题。  

by Andrew J. Hawkins  
作者：Andrew J. Hawkins  

Jul 23, 2026, 5:09 PM UTC  
2026年7月23日，下午5:09（协调世界时）  

In an earnings call yesterday, Tesla CEO Elon Musk did his best to paint a positive portrait of the company’s robotaxi program. New cities are being added, more miles are being driven, and more people are experiencing Tesla’s unsupervised vehicles.  
在昨天的财报电话会议上，特斯拉CEO埃隆·马斯克尽力为公司无人驾驶出租车项目描绘一幅积极图景。新城市正在增加，行驶里程不断上升，越来越多的人正在体验特斯拉的无人监管车辆。  

But Musk’s typical bullishness seemed to be absent from the call, as the occasional trillionaire sought to temper expectations about the program’s future.  
但马斯克典型的乐观情绪似乎并未出现在此次会议上，这位偶尔的万亿富翁试图为项目的未来预期降温。  

“We’re going as fast as humanly possible in scaling Robotaxi,” Musk said, “while trying to ensure that we do not harm anyone at all and ideally do not even run over a pet.”  
“我们正在以人力所及的最快速度扩大Robotaxi的规模，”马斯克说，“同时力求确保绝不伤害任何人，理想情况下甚至不碾压到宠物。”  

Musk’s insistence that Tesla’s robotaxi service was growing is clashing with the reality of the situation: Mileage is basically flat, the number of vehicles appears to be shrinking, and despite the Tesla executive team’s claims of “zero notable incidents,” the safety of the company’s technology appears to be an open question.  
马斯克坚持称特斯拉的无人驾驶出租车服务正在增长，但这与实际情况相冲突：里程基本停滞，车辆数量似乎在减少，尽管特斯拉管理团队声称“零重大事故”，但公司技术的安全性仍是一个未知数。  

Tesla did its best to frame the situation as headed in the right direction. Ashok Elluswamy, the company’s head of AI, said that Tesla’s unsupervised robotaxis have driven 380,000 miles across six cities in two different states. And according to Musk, that was increasing at a rate of 10 percent a week in terms of miles driven.  
特斯拉尽力将情况描述为朝着正确方向发展。公司人工智能负责人阿肖克·埃卢斯瓦米表示，特斯拉的无人监管机器人出租车已在两个州的六个城市行驶了38万英里。据马斯克称，按行驶里程计算，这一数字正以每周10%的速度增长。  

Elluswamy couldn’t help but take a swipe at Tesla’s competitors who use lidar sensors, as opposed to Tesla’s camera-only approach. “Historically, the so-called experts have always claimed that you need lidars, radars, HD maps, and the entire kitchen sink to drive safely,” he said. “Here we show that such is not true. You can have safe, comfortable, and affordable autonomy with just cameras. This record should be a huge validation of Tesla’s entire AI approach.”  
埃卢斯瓦米忍不住对使用激光雷达传感器（而非特斯拉纯视觉方案）的竞争对手进行了抨击。“历史上，所谓的专家一直声称你需要激光雷达、雷达、高清地图和所有家当才能安全驾驶，”他说，“而我们在此证明事实并非如此。仅用摄像头就能实现安全、舒适且可负担的自动驾驶。这一记录应该是对特斯拉整个AI方法的巨大验证。”  

But even the numbers that Tesla reported seem to betray that message. Waymo, which uses lidar sensors and radar as redundant sensors in addition to cameras, typically drives around four million miles a week. Tesla’s total mileage, a year into its robotaxi program, is less than 10 percent of that figure. This reality is starting to spook investors, with BNP Paribas Equity Research senior analyst James Picariello calling it “an alarming decline” in a research note to clients.  
但即使特斯拉报告的数据也似乎背叛了这一说法。Waymo除了摄像头外，还使用激光雷达和雷达作为冗余传感器，通常每周行驶约400万英里。特斯拉在无人驾驶出租车项目启动一年后的总里程还不到这一数字的10%。这一现实开始令投资者恐慌，法国巴黎银行股票研究高级分析师詹姆斯·皮卡里洛在给客户的研究报告中称其为“令人警醒的下降”。  

Tesla uses a mix of unsupervised and supervised vehicles as part of its fleet, but that ratio appears to be in a constant state of flux. According to the crowdsourced Robotaxi Tracker, the company currently only has 16 unsupervised vehicles in Austin, four in Dallas, and one in Houston.  
特斯拉的车队混合使用无人监管和有人监管车辆，但这一比例似乎处于不断变化中。根据众包的Robotaxi Tracker数据，该公司目前在奥斯汀仅有16辆无人监管车辆，达拉斯4辆，休斯顿1辆。  

During the earnings call, Elluswamy was asked what was preventing Tesla from flooding the zone in Austin, where it first launched its robotaxis a year ago, as opposed to dropping a small number of vehicles in new cities.  
在财报电话会议上，有人问埃卢斯瓦米，是什么阻止了特斯拉在一年前首次推出无人驾驶出租车的奥斯汀大量投放车辆，而非在新城市投放少量车辆。  

“The reason we have been expanding across different cities, instead of just doubling down on a single city, is that we want to make sure that our stack is a very general one,” Elluswamy said. “It is a general one. We just want to like, you know, both prove to ourselves and to other folks that it is working across a lot of different cities without too much effort per city.”  
“我们之所以在不同城市扩张，而不是只在一个城市加倍投入，是因为我们希望确保我们的技术栈非常通用，”埃卢斯瓦米说，“它确实是通用的。我们只是想，你知道，向自己和他人证明它能在许多不同城市运行，且每个城市无需太多努力。”  

Another limiting factor is state regulations. New Jersey is considering a bill that would essentially ban robotaxis without multiple sensors, like radar and lidar. Morgan Stanley analyst Andrew Percoco flagged this on the call, noting “there seems to be some evolving regulations at the state level, potentially around sensor requirements.” To which Tesla’s vice president of vehicle engineering Lars Morvay acknowledged “obviously the stuff in New Jersey, you know, is a little bit disheartening.”  
另一个限制因素是州法规。新泽西州正在考虑一项法案，该法案将 essentially 禁止没有多种传感器（如雷达和激光雷达）的无人驾驶出租车。摩根士丹利分析师安德鲁·佩尔科科在会议上指出了这一点，指出“州层面似乎有一些不断演变的法规，可能围绕传感器要求。”对此，特斯拉车辆工程副总裁拉尔斯·莫雷承认“显然新泽西州的事情，你知道，有点令人沮丧。”  

A more restrictive regulatory environment could limit the total addressable market for Tesla robotaxis, which would clash with the company’s message about a more generalized service that can drive anywhere, under any conditions.  
更严格的监管环境可能会限制特斯拉无人驾驶出租车的总可触达市场，这将与公司关于一种更通用的服务、能在任何条件下 anywhere 行驶的信息相冲突。  

Safety is another obstacle. Musk’s comment about wanting to avoid running over anyone’s pet appeared to be a subtle swipe at Waymo, which has hit and killed a number of animals over the past several years. But Tesla’s safety record is a mixed bag. We don’t have a lot of great insight into Tesla’s robotaxi crashes because the company tends to redact a lot of the important details.  
安全是另一个障碍。马斯克关于不想碾压任何人宠物的评论，似乎是对Waymo的微妙抨击，后者在过去几年里撞死并杀死了许多动物。但特斯拉的安全记录好坏参半。我们对特斯拉无人驾驶出租车的碰撞事故知之甚少，因为公司倾向于 redact 许多重要细节。  

According to the Robotaxi Tracker, the company’s fleet has been involved in approximately 18 incidents, 56 percent of which were the fault of another driver. And Tesla’s Full Self-Driving is under investigation by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, with the potential for a future recall. Tesla’s robotaxis are currently running version 15 of FSD, as compared to version 14.3.6 on its customer vehicles.  
根据Robotaxi Tracker，该公司车队已卷入约18起事故，其中56%是其他驾驶员的过错。特斯拉的完全自动驾驶（FSD）正接受国家公路交通安全管理局的调查，未来可能召回。特斯拉的无人驾驶出租车目前运行FSD第15版，而客户车辆上运行的是14.3.6版。  

Tesla is asking investors, supporters, and customers to look past these shortcomings as it continues to push the message that its robotaxi program is growing. But the company continues to run into a reality that contradicts that message.  
特斯拉要求投资者、支持者和客户忽视这些缺陷，同时继续推动其无人驾驶出租车项目正在增长的信息。但该公司不断遭遇与这一信息相悖的现实。
